## Cross-Referencing Guidelines

1. **Two-source rule**: Any factual claim should appear in at least 2 independent sources before being reported as confirmed
2. **Direct quote verification**: Prefer official transcripts or video sources for direct quotes
3. **Timeline awareness**: News cycles move fast; always check publication timestamps
4. **Social media caution**: Treat social media posts as primary sources for Trump's own statements, but verify any claims made within them against news reporting
