## Cross-Processing Looks

| Effect | What Happens |
|--------|-------------|
| C-41 → E-6 | Colors shift, contrast increases, unexpected hues |
| E-6 → C-41 | Muted, pastel, low contrast |
| B&W → C-41 | Grain increases, contrast shift |
| Positive → Negative | Inverted tones, surreal colors |

## Slide Film Push/Pull

| Technique | Effect |
|-----------|--------|
| Push +1 | Increased contrast, more grain, saturated colors |
| Push +2 | Dramatic, gritty, punchy, heavy grain |
| Pull -1 | Reduced contrast, muted, smoother grain |
| Pull -2 | Very flat, pastel, minimal grain |

## Prompting Film Looks

```
General film look: 
"shot on [film name], film grain visible, [contrast level] contrast,
 [color characteristics], authentic [era] film aesthetic"

Without naming specific film:
"film grain, muted colors, soft contrast, slight color cast,
 documentary photography aesthetic, analog film quality"

With specific qualities:
"high contrast black and white, prominent grain, punchy blacks,
 classic street photography film look, Kodak Tri-X style"
```
