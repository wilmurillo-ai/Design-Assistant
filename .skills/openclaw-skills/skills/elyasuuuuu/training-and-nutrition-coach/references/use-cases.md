# Common use cases

## Fat loss beginner
Priorities:
- modest deficit
- high protein
- easy meal repetition
- simple full-body training 2-4x/week

## Muscle gain beginner
Priorities:
- modest surplus
- high protein
- progressive overload
- simple meals that are easy to eat consistently

## Recomposition
Priorities:
- calories near maintenance
- high protein
- consistent strength training
- patience over scale obsession

## Home workout limited equipment
Bias toward:
- squats or split squats
- push-ups or dumbbell press
- rows
- hinges or glute work
- planks / carries / core

## Small budget nutrition
Bias toward:
- eggs
- oats
- rice
- pasta
- frozen vegetables
- chicken thighs
- canned tuna
- yogurt / skyr / fromage blanc
- legumes

## Low cooking motivation
Bias toward:
- repeatable breakfasts
- bowl meals
- wraps
- sheet-pan meals
- one-pot meals
- simple snacks with protein

## Recipe note
When recipe output becomes actionable, prefer concrete recipes instead of stopping at abstract meal ideas.
