# Proposal

> A proposal's job is to answer every question the buyer will ask—before they ask it.

Proposal is an approval-acceleration skill for late-stage commercial opportunities.

## What it does

This skill helps you:
- reduce approval friction
- clarify scope, pricing, and timeline
- surface likely objections before the buyer raises them
- strengthen the yes-path
- turn a late-stage opportunity into a cleaner decision package

## What it does NOT do

- qualify whether an opportunity is worth pursuing
- manage the full deal book
- find pre-contact targets
- replace legal or procurement review
- handle post-sale client management

## Works Well With

- `@AGIstack/lead` for deciding whether an opportunity deserves proposal-stage effort
- `@dpetcr/pipeline` for identifying which deals are truly ready for proposal
- `@dpetcr/client` for post-sale handoff after approval
