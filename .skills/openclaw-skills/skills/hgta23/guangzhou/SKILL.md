# Guangzhou City Guide Skill

## Overview
This skill provides a comprehensive guide to Guangzhou, the capital of Guangdong Province in China. It offers detailed information about the city's attractions, food, culture, history, transportation, and more. Whether you're planning a trip to Guangzhou or just want to learn about this vibrant city, this skill has you covered.

## Key Features

### Attractions
- **Canton Tower**: Learn about the iconic 600-meter tower with observation decks, restaurants, and entertainment facilities
- **Shamian Island**: Explore the historical island with European architecture and scenic views
- **Chen Clan Ancestral Hall**: Discover traditional Chinese architecture and cultural artifacts
- **Yuexiu Park**: Information about the largest park in Guangzhou with historical sites
- **Pearl River Cruise**: Details about evening cruises along the Pearl River

### Food & Dining
- **Cantonese Cuisine**: Introduction to famous dishes like dim sum, roast goose, and congee
- **Local Food Streets**: Recommendations for popular food areas like Shangxiajiu Pedestrian Street
- **Tea Culture**: Information about traditional Cantonese tea drinking customs
- **Restaurant Recommendations**: Curated list of top restaurants for different budgets

### Culture & History
- **Historical Timeline**: Overview of Guangzhou's development from ancient times to present
- **Cultural Traditions**: Information about local customs, festivals, and traditions
- **Lingnan Culture**: Introduction to the unique culture of southern China
- **Museums**: Details about major museums in the city

### Transportation
- **Getting Around**: Information about public transportation, taxis, and ride-sharing options
- **Airport Information**: Details about Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport
- **Train Stations**: Information about major train stations and high-speed rail connections
- **Travel Tips**: Practical advice for navigating the city

### Practical Information
- **Weather**: Seasonal weather patterns and best times to visit
- **Accommodation**: Recommendations for different types of lodging
- **Shopping**: Information about shopping areas and local products
- **Safety Tips**: Advice for tourists visiting Guangzhou

## About Guangzhou
Guangzhou, also known as Canton, is a major metropolis in southern China with a history dating back over 2,200 years. It is a hub of commerce, culture, and transportation, and serves as an important gateway to China for international trade. The city blends traditional Chinese culture with modern development, offering visitors a unique and enriching experience.
