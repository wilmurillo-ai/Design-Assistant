# Haikou Skill

Your ultimate companion for exploring Haikou, the tropical capital city of Hainan Province, China.

## Overview

Haikou (海口) is the capital and largest city of Hainan Province, located on the northern tip of Hainan Island. Known as the "City of Coconut" and "Summer Capital of China", Haikou offers a unique blend of natural beauty, historical sites, and modern urban amenities. This skill provides comprehensive information about all aspects of Haikou.

### 🍜 Local Cuisine

Haikou is famous for Hainan's diverse culinary traditions:

- **Hainan Chicken Rice (文昌鸡)**: The iconic dish - steamed chicken with fragrant rice
- **Qingbuliang (清补凉)**: Refreshing coconut milk-based dessert with toppings
- **Wenchang Chicken (文昌鸡)**: Tender chicken raised in Wenchang area
- **Hainan Hot Pot (椰子鸡)**: Fresh chicken cooked in coconut water
- **Sea Urchin Rice**: Local specialty with fresh sea urchin
- **Tropical Fruits**: Mango, durian, lychee, longan, papaya, coconuts
- **Old Street Snacks**: Gaodong Street and South Lake area street food

**Recommended Food Districts**:
- South Lake Area (南湖片区) - Local snacks
- Golden/long Street (金龙路) - Diverse cuisines
- Haikou University Area - Affordable local food

### 🌤️ Weather & Climate

Haikou enjoys a tropical oceanic monsoon climate:

- **Best Visiting Season**: October to April (dry season, comfortable temperatures)
- **Summer (May-Sep)**: Hot (28-35°C), humid, typhoon season possible
- **Winter (Nov-Feb)**: Mild (18-25°C), dry, perfect for escape from cold
- **Rainy Season**: May-October with afternoon thunderstorms
- **What to Bring**: Light breathable clothing, sunscreen, umbrella, mosquito repellent

### 📋 Practical Information

**Visa & Entry**:

- Hainan 59-country visa-free transit (up to 30 days)
- Sanya/Haikou离岛免税购物政策
- Bring passport, valid for 6+ months

**Money & Payments**:

- RMB (CNY) currency
- WeChat Pay and Alipay widely accepted
- Cash still needed for some local markets
- ATMs available at banks and shopping centers

**Language**:

- Mandarin Chinese primary language
- Local Hainanese dialect (闽南语系)
- English not widely spoken outside tourist areas
- Translation apps recommended

**Health & Safety**:

- Tap water not drinkable, use bottled water
- Hospital: Haikou People's Hospital (海南省人民医院)
- Police: 110 | Ambulance: 120 | Fire: 119
- Generally safe city, normal precautions apply

## Tips for Visitors

1. **Visit during dry season** (Oct-Apr) for best weather
2. **Rent an electric scooter** for convenient city exploration
3. **Try the night markets** for authentic local food experiences
4. **Bring sunscreen** - tropical sun is strong year-round
5. **Visit Xixiu Beach** for cleaner, less crowded beaches
6. **Take a day trip** to Wenchang to see the original Hainan chicken
7. **Shop at duty-free stores** for luxury goods at reduced prices
8. **Respect local customs** during temple visits
