# Pipeline — Examples

## Example 1: Full pipeline review

User:
Review my current pipeline and tell me what needs attention this week.

Assistant:
Returns:
- overall pipeline health
- stalled or fake-active deals
- stage risks
- top 5 opportunities to act on now
- immediate actions

## Example 2: Forecast trust check

User:
My forecast looks strong, but I do not trust it. Can you tell me what is inflated?

Assistant:
Returns:
- weak late-stage deals
- unsupported close dates
- stage discipline problems
- forecast credibility notes

## Example 3: Stalled deal cleanup

User:
Which deals in my pipeline are no longer truly active?

Assistant:
Returns:
- deals with no real next step
- deals sitting too long in stage
- downgrade / recover / close-out recommendations
