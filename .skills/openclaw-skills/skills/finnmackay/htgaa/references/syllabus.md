## Course Overview

HTGAA is a hands-on synthetic biology course covering DNA reading/writing/editing, protein design, genetic circuits, lab automation, biomaterials, and more. Students include MIT/Harvard/Wellesley registered students and global "Committed Listeners" at 15 nodes worldwide.

## Key Tools & Platforms

- **Benchling** — DNA design, annotation, cloning workflows
- **Twist Bioscience** — DNA synthesis ordering
- **Opentrons** — Lab automation robots + Python API
- **Asimov Kernel** — Genetic circuit design software
- **AlphaFold Server** — Protein structure prediction
- **PepMLM** — Peptide generation via masked language modeling
- **PeptiVerse** — Therapeutic peptide property prediction
- **moPPIt** — Multi-objective peptide design
- **ESM2/ESMFold** — Protein language models
- **ProteinMPNN** — Inverse protein folding
- **PyMOL** — Protein visualization
- **NCBI/UniProt/RCSB** — Databases
