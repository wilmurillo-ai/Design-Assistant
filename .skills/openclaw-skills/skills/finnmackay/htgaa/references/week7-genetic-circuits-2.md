# Week 7: Genetic Circuits Part 2 — Applications

**Date:** Mar 17, 2026
**Lecturer:** Ron Weiss (Professor of Biological Engineering, MIT; Director, MIT Synthetic Biology Center)

## Topics
- Advanced genetic circuits
- Neuromorphic circuits in cells
- Artificial neural networks in biological systems
- Synthetic biology applications

## Lab
- Design, build, and test neuromorphic circuits (led by PhD student Evan Holbrook)
- Biomaterials: hands-on mycelium exercise (led by PhD student Ren Ramlan)

## Key Concepts
- **Neuromorphic circuits** — Computing architectures inspired by the brain, implemented in living cells
- **Cellular neural networks** — Using genetic circuits to implement neural network-like computation
- **Mycelium** — Fungal biomaterial with applications in manufacturing, packaging, construction
