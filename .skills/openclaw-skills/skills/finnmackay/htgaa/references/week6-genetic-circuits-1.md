## Key Concepts
- **Repressilator** — Classic oscillating genetic circuit (Elowitz & Leibler, 2000)
- **Gibson Assembly** — Isothermal, single-reaction DNA assembly method
- **Golden Gate Assembly** — Type IIS restriction enzyme-based assembly
- **PCR** — Polymerase Chain Reaction for DNA amplification
- **Primer Design** — Designing oligos for PCR and assembly
