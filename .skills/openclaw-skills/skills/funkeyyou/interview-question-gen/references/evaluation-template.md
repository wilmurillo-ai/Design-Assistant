## Scoring Guide

| Stars | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Exceeds expectations, strong differentiator |
| ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Meets expectations, solid performance |
| ⭐⭐⭐ | Adequate, some gaps |
| ⭐⭐ | Below expectations, significant gaps |
| ⭐ | Critical weakness |

## Tips for Writing Good Evaluations

- **Be specific, not generic**: Quote actual interview moments ("When asked about ROI, she initially described it as a user ratio rather than revenue/cost") rather than vague summaries ("data skills were weak").
- **Separate facts from interpretations**: State what happened, then what it suggests.
- **Address the job requirements directly**: The role is full-cycle activity planning (策划→上线→复盘), not execution support. Evaluate against that bar.
- **Note what was NOT covered**: If certain competencies weren't tested (e.g., Japanese oral not done), say so.
