# 5-Email Drip Sequence

## Email 1: The Intro (Day 0 — immediately after deploy)

**Subject:** I built [Business Name] a website — take a look

> Hi [First Name],
>
> I noticed [Business Name] doesn't have a website (or could use an upgrade), so I went ahead and built one for you.
>
> **Here's your preview:** [live demo link]
>
> It's mobile-friendly, shows up on Google, and has your phone number front and center so customers can call you directly.
>
> This is yours to keep if you want it — no obligation.
>
> — Ryan Romero
> Romero Automation

## Email 2: Social Proof (Day 3)

**Subject:** Quick question about your website preview

> Hi [First Name],
>
> Just checking in — did you get a chance to look at the website I built for [Business Name]?
>
> I've helped several businesses in the [City] area get online, and the ones who did saw an average of 30-40% more calls within the first month.
>
> Happy to walk you through it if you have questions. No pressure either way.

## Email 3: Urgency (Day 7)

**Subject:** Your [Business Name] website preview expires soon

> Hi [First Name],
>
> Heads up — the website preview I created for [Business Name] will be taken down in about a week.
>
> If you'd like to keep it, I can transfer it to your own domain. Pricing starts at $997 with everything included — hosting, SSL, updates, the works.

## Email 4: Last Chance (Day 14)

**Subject:** Last day for your [Business Name] website

> Hi [First Name],
>
> This is the last day your preview website will be live. After today, it'll be taken down.
>
> If you change your mind later, I can always rebuild it — but I wanted to give you a heads up.

## Email 5: Breakup (Day 30)

**Subject:** Closing the loop on [Business Name]

> Hi [First Name],
>
> Just a final note — the website preview I built for [Business Name] was taken down a couple weeks ago.
>
> If you ever decide you want a professional website, my door is always open.

## Psychology Behind the Sequence

- **Day 0:** No-pressure gift. They see their business on a real website before paying.
- **Day 3:** Social proof. Others like them got results.
- **Day 7:** Scarcity. The preview is temporary.
- **Day 14:** Loss aversion. Last chance to keep what they've already seen.
- **Day 30:** Clean break. Leaves the door open without being pushy.
