Each Member shall have voting power proportional to their membership interest. Except as otherwise provided in this Agreement, all decisions shall be made by a majority vote of the membership interests.

### Section 3.3 — Actions Requiring Unanimous Consent
The following actions require unanimous consent of all Members:
- Admission of new Members
- Amendment of this Agreement
- Dissolution of the Company
- Sale of all or substantially all Company assets
- Incurrence of debt exceeding ${{DEBT_THRESHOLD}}
- Any transaction between the Company and a Member

### Section 3.4 — Officers
The Manager(s) or Members may appoint officers (President, Secretary, Treasurer, etc.) as needed. Officers serve at the pleasure of the appointing authority.

## ARTICLE IV — ALLOCATIONS AND DISTRIBUTIONS

### Section 4.1 — Allocation of Profits and Losses
Net profits and net losses shall be allocated to the Members in proportion to their membership interests, unless otherwise agreed by unanimous consent.

### Section 4.2 — Distributions
Distributions of available cash shall be made to the Members in proportion to their membership interests at such times and in such amounts as determined by {{DISTRIBUTION_AUTHORITY}}.

### Section 4.3 — Tax Distributions
The Company shall make distributions to Members sufficient to cover their estimated tax liability arising from Company income, based on the highest applicable marginal tax rate.

## ARTICLE V — TRANSFER OF INTERESTS

### Section 5.1 — Restrictions on Transfer
No Member may sell, assign, or transfer their membership interest without the prior written consent of {{TRANSFER_APPROVAL}}.

### Section 5.2 — Right of First Refusal
Before transferring any interest to a third party, the transferring Member must first offer the interest to the remaining Members on the same terms.

### Section 5.3 — Permitted Transfers
A Member may transfer their interest to a trust, estate plan, or wholly-owned entity without consent, provided the transferring Member remains in control.

## ARTICLE VI — DISSOLUTION

### Section 6.1 — Events of Dissolution
The Company shall be dissolved upon:
- Unanimous vote of all Members
- Entry of a judicial decree of dissolution
- Any event that makes it unlawful for the Company to continue

### Section 6.2 — Winding Up
Upon dissolution, the Company's affairs shall be wound up by the Manager(s) or, if none, the Members. Assets shall be applied in the following order:
1. Payment of debts and obligations to creditors
2. Setting up reserves for contingent liabilities
3. Distribution to Members in accordance with their capital accounts

## ARTICLE VII — INDEMNIFICATION

The Company shall indemnify each Member, Manager, and officer to the fullest extent permitted by the laws of the State of {{STATE}} against any losses, claims, or liabilities incurred by reason of their role in the Company, except for willful misconduct or fraud.
