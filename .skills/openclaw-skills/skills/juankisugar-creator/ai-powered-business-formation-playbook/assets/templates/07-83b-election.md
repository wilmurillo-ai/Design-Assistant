# SECTION 83(b) ELECTION

## Instructions

1. **TIMING:** Must be filed with the IRS within 30 days of stock transfer. NO extensions. Missing this deadline is irreversible.
2. **WHERE TO FILE:** Mail to the IRS Service Center where you file your federal income tax return. Send via certified mail, return receipt requested.
3. **COPIES:** Provide a copy to {{COMPANY_NAME}}. Keep a copy for your records.
4. **TAX RETURN:** Attach a copy to your federal income tax return for the year of transfer.

---
