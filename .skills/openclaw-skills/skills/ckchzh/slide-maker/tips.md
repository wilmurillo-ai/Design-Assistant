# 💡 Slide Maker Tips

1. **Outline first, slides second**: Unsure about structure? Run `outline` to clarify your thinking, then `slides` to fill in content.

2. **Keep pitch decks under 12 slides**: Investors have short attention spans. 10-12 pages is the sweet spot for a Pitch Deck.

3. **One core idea per slide**: Don't cram content. One slide, one message — your audience will actually remember it.

4. **Speaker notes include timing**: `speaker` allocates 2-3 minutes per slide so your total presentation time stays on track.

5. **Markdown is portable**: Output Markdown imports directly into Marp, Slidev, or Reveal.js — no vendor lock-in.

6. **Check design before building**: Run `design` first for color and typography recommendations — saves rework later.

7. **Reports need numbers**: Every report slide should have 1-2 key metrics. Data beats paragraphs of text.

8. **Add interaction to training**: Training slides should include a question or exercise every 3-4 pages to keep engagement up.
