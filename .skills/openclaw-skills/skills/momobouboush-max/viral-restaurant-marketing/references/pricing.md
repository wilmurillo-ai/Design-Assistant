## ROI Calculation for Restaurants

**Scenario: Restaurant doing €15,000/month in revenue**

With 1 viral video (100K views, local audience):
- Estimated new visitors: 200-400
- Average spend per table: €35-€60
- Estimated revenue uplift: €7,000-€24,000 one-time

With consistent monthly content (3-4 posts/week):
- Follower growth: +500-2,000/month
- Google Reviews boost: +20-50 reviews/month
- Website traffic: +30-50% from social
- Monthly revenue impact: +€2,000-€8,000

**Break-even point:** Typically month 2-3
