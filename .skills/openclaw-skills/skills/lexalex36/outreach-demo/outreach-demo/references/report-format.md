# Outreach Demo Report Format

Use this structure for each prospect report.

## 1. Business snapshot
- Business name
- Website
- What they appear to do
- Likely audience

## 2. Website observations
- clear strengths
- unclear/missing signals
- operational/marketing/service observations grounded in visible evidence

## 3. OpenClaw fit
List up to 3 concrete ways OpenClaw could help.
For each:
- use case name
- why it fits this business
- likely business value
- lowest-friction starting point

## 4. Priority move
Recommend the single first move you would prioritize.
Include:
- what to do first
- why it should come first
- expected benefit

## 5. Why it matters
Frame the business consequence without inventing numbers.
Examples:
- saves owner time
- reduces slow follow-up
- catches regressions faster
- improves lead-response consistency

## 6. Suggested demo angle
Recommend one specific live demo angle that would resonate with this business.

## 7. Outreach summary
Two to four sentences suitable for email body reuse.

Rules:
- Do not claim internal business pain unless visibly supported.
- Phrase uncertain findings as likely or apparent.
- Prefer evidence from homepage/about/services/contact pages.
- Keep total report concise and decision-useful.
