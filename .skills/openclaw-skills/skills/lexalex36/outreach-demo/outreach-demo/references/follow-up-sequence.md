# Follow-Up Sequence

Use only after explicit user approval.

## Follow-up 1
Send 2 to 4 business days later.

Pattern:
- mention one additional observed opportunity
- keep it under 90 words
- ask a soft question

Template:

Subject: Re: {{subject}}

Hi {{contact_name}},

One additional thing I noticed while looking at {{business_name}}: {{additional_observation}}.

If useful, I can send a revised brief or walk through the priority recommendation live.

Best,
{{sender_name}}
{{sender_email}}

## Follow-up 2
Send 4 to 7 business days after follow-up 1.

Pattern:
- short close-the-loop note
- no pressure

Template:

Subject: Re: {{subject}}

Hi {{contact_name}},

Closing the loop on the brief I sent for {{business_name}}.

If this is not a priority right now, no problem. If useful later, I can revisit it with updated observations.

Best,
{{sender_name}}
{{sender_email}}
