# Outreach Demo Email Template

Default to value-first outreach.

Lead with the finished mini-asset, not the meeting ask.

Subject options:
- I made this 1-page brief for {{business_name}}
- I put together 3 ideas for {{business_name}}
- Quick OpenClaw brief for {{business_name}}

Plain-text body pattern:

Hi {{contact_name}},

I pulled together a short **OpenClaw Opportunity Brief** for {{business_name}}.

One thing that stood out: {{specific_observation}}.

Inside:
- 3 concrete OpenClaw opportunities
- the priority move I would test first
- a simple live-demo angle

Attached if you want to skim it. If useful, I can walk through it live in 10 minutes.

Best,
{{sender_name}}
{{sender_brand_line}}
{{sender_email}}

Rules:
- Keep under ~170 words.
- Prefer short paragraphs and 3 bullets max.
- Make the value feel concrete: brief, teardown, mini-audit, or 3 ideas.
- No hype.
- No unverifiable claims.
- Ask for a light next step, not a heavy commitment.
- Draft first; send only after explicit user approval.
- If HTML is allowed, keep styling light and mobile-readable.
