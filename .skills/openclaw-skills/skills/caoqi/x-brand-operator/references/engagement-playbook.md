# Engagement Playbook

## Keyword List (starter set — adapt to your brand)

**High priority** (search weekly):
- "YouTube deleted my video"
- "TikTok banned me"
- "video hosting alternative"
- "YouTube demonetized"
- "content censorship"
- "creator freedom"

**Mid priority** (add as needed):
- "free video hosting"
- "upload without censorship"
- "platform ban"
- "shadowbanned"

## Filtering Rules

✅ Engage with:
- Real creators expressing genuine frustration with censorship / demonetization / bans
- People actively looking for platform alternatives
- Accounts with real post history (not brand-new or bot-like)

❌ Skip:
- Ads or promoted content
- Accounts with <10 followers or no profile picture
- Generic complaint bots
- Content clearly unrelated to your brand's audience

Per keyword: select max 2 best candidates.

## Reply Templates

Adapt tone to the tweet. Never copy-paste — always personalise.

**For censorship frustration:**
> "Platform censorship is exhausting — and it's only getting worse. More creators are moving to alternatives where they own their content. [Brand] lets you upload without registration and earn points for every view. No algorithm deciding your fate. [url]"

**For demonetization:**
> "Getting demonetized after years of work is brutal. [Brand] has a different model — you earn points for views, likes, comments, and follows, not ad revenue. Small creators earn too. [url]"

**For seeking alternatives:**
> "Worth checking out [Brand] — free upload, no censorship, and you earn points that convert to rewards. No account needed to start. [url]"

## Engagement Sequence (per tweet)

1. `xurl like <tweet_id>`
2. Write reply (personalised, quality-checked)
3. `xurl reply <tweet_id> "<reply>"` → fallback to browser if fails
4. `xurl follow <username>` (if account looks like a real creator)
5. Sleep 5 seconds before next tweet

## Weekly Engagement Summary (Telegram)

Send after each run:
```
📊 Weekly Engagement — [Date]

✅ Liked: X posts
💬 Replied: X posts (xurl: X / browser: X)
👤 Followed: X accounts

Highlights:
- [Most interesting interaction 1–2 lines]

Next week: focus on [trending topic or keyword suggestion]
```
