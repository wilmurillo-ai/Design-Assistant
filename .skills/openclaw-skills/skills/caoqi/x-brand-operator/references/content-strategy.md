# Content Strategy

## Content Pillar Architecture

Define 5–7 pillars for your brand and assign them to days of the week. Example rotation (adapt to your brand):

| Day | Pillar | Purpose |
|-----|--------|---------|
| Monday | Brand positioning | What the product is and why it exists |
| Tuesday | Core value / reward | Key feature or benefit |
| Wednesday | Engagement / question | Ask the audience, spark replies |
| Thursday | Comparison | Brand vs. incumbent platforms |
| Friday | Pain point / anti-censorship | Resonate with frustrated users |
| Saturday | Feature highlight | Specific capability showcase |
| Sunday | Community | Invite people in, celebrate users |

Morning posts: informative / authoritative tone.  
Evening posts: lighter, conversational, question-driven.

## Tweet Format

```
[Hook — stops the scroll]
[1–2 sentences of core value]
[CTA] [brand URL]
[1–2 hashtags]
```

All in one paragraph, no line breaks, ≤ 280 chars.

## Hook Patterns

- Stat / surprising claim: "94% of creators have had a video removed..."
- Contrarian: "YouTube rewards advertisers. We reward creators."
- Question: "What happens to your content when a platform bans you?"
- Story: "A creator lost 3 years of uploads overnight. Here's what they did."

## CTA Examples

- "Upload free → [url]"
- "See how it works → [url]"
- "Join the creators → [url]"
- "Start earning → [url]"

## Tone Guide

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Bold, direct | Corporate / fluffy |
| Creator-first language | Platform jargon |
| Specific numbers | Vague claims |
| Empathetic to pain points | Dismissive |
| Short sentences | Run-on paragraphs |
