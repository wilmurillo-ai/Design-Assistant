# IU Skill

## Overview

This skill provides comprehensive knowledge about **IU (아이유)**, whose real name is **Lee Ji-eun (이지은)**. Born on May 16, 1993, in Seoul, South Korea, IU is one of the most beloved and influential singer-songwriters and actresses in the Korean entertainment industry. Known as the "Nation's Little Sister (국민 여동생)", she has captivated audiences worldwide with her angelic voice, exceptional songwriting talent, and versatile acting skills.

## Early Life & Debut

- **Birth**: May 16, 1993, Seoul, South Korea.
- **Training**: Endured a challenging trainee period before debuting under **LOEN Entertainment** (now **EDAM Entertainment** under Kakao Entertainment).
- **Debut**: September 18, 2008, at the age of 15, with the single "Lost Child (미아)".
- **Breakthrough**: Gained massive popularity in 2010 with the hit song "Good Day (좋은 날)", famous for its iconic three-octave high note sequence.

## Achievements & Awards

- 🏆 **Golden Disc Awards**: Multiple Daesangs (Grand Prizes) for digital music
- 🏆 **Melon Music Awards**: Artist of the Year, Best Album
- 🏆 **Korean Music Awards**: Best Pop Song, Best Pop Album
- 🏆 **Baeksang Arts Awards**: Best Actress nomination for "My Mister"
- 🏆 **Billboard**: Charted on Billboard 200 and Hot 100
- 📊 **Records**: Known as the "Digital Queen" for consistently topping Korean music charts; holds multiple all-kill records (topping all major Korean music charts simultaneously)

## Philanthropy

IU is widely recognized for her generous charitable activities:

- Donated billions of Korean Won to various causes including children's welfare, elderly care, and disaster relief.
- Regular donations on her birthday and album release anniversaries.
- Named one of the top celebrity donors in South Korea by the Beautiful Foundation.
- Advocates for mental health awareness and youth education.

## Fun Facts

- 🎤 She is a prolific songwriter who writes and composes many of her own songs.
- 🐰 Her fan club is called **UAENA (유애나)**, a combination of "U" (you), "Ae" (love in Korean), and "Na" (me).
- 📚 Known for her love of reading and literature, which often inspires her music.
- 🎭 She selects acting roles carefully, preferring complex and emotionally challenging characters.
- 🌟 Close friends with many K-pop idols and actors, known for her warm and humble personality.
