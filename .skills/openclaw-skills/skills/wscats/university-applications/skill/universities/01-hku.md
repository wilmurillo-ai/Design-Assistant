## Collection Instructions

### Primary Data Sources (Official Only)

1. **Programme List**: https://www.gradsch.hku.hk/gradsch/prospective-students/programmes-on-offer
   - Navigate through each faculty section
   - Collect all taught master's programs (ignore PhD/MPhil research programs)

2. **Admission Requirements**: https://www.gradsch.hku.hk/gradsch/prospective-students/admission-requirements
   - General English requirements for HKU
   - Program-specific requirements

3. **Fee Information**: https://www.gradsch.hku.hk/gradsch/prospective-students/fees-scholarships-and-financial-support
   - Tuition fee schedule by program

4. **Application Timeline**: https://www.gradsch.hku.hk/gradsch/prospective-students/application-and-admission-timeline
   - Application opening and closing dates

### General English Requirements for HKU

HKU's general minimum English requirements (programs may require higher):

| Test | Minimum Score |
|------|--------------|
| IELTS (Academic) | Overall 6.0, no sub-score below 5.5 |
| TOEFL iBT | 80 |
| TOEFL pBT | 550 |
