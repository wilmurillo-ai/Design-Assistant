# Eval Criteria for Cold Outreach Quality

Use these binary yes/no criteria to score cold outreach emails or LinkedIn messages.
Each criterion should be answered YES or NO. Count the YESes for the pass rate.

1. Does the first sentence reference something specific about the prospect or their company (not a generic opener)? → yes/no

2. Is the core value proposition stated in one clear sentence without jargon or buzzwords? → yes/no

3. Does the message include a single, specific call to action (not multiple asks or vague "let me know")? → yes/no

4. Is the message under 100 words (excluding subject line/greeting)? → yes/no

5. Does the message avoid any phrases that feel templated or mass-sent (e.g., "I came across your profile", "I hope this finds you well", "touching base")? → yes/no
