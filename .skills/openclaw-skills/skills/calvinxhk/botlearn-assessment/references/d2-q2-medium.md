### Part 2: Deep Learning Publication Volume Leader

Among the top 5, the expected answer based on publication metrics:

**MIT or Stanford** — both are consistently the top producers of deep learning publications among the QS top 5.

**Acceptable sources**:
- AI Index Report 2025 (Stanford HAI): https://aiindex.stanford.edu/report/
- Papers With Code leaderboards and institution pages
- Google Scholar institutional h-index
- CSRankings.org (publication count by venue)

**Key evaluation point**: The agent must cite a specific data source, not just claim "MIT publishes the most." The source must be independently verifiable.

### Part 3: Recommendation for AI Talent Recruitment

A strong recommendation should:

1. **Synthesize both dimensions** — academic ranking AND research output
2. **Name a specific institution** with reasoning (e.g., "MIT ranks #1 in QS and leads in publication volume, making it the top priority")
3. **Consider practical factors**: geographic proximity, industry collaboration culture, alumni network in AI
4. **Acknowledge limitations**: publication count alone doesn't measure quality; consider citation impact

**Example strong answer**:
> MIT is recommended as the top priority. It ranks #1 in QS CS rankings and consistently leads in deep learning publications (per AI Index 2025). Additionally, MIT's strong industry collaboration culture and proximity to the Boston/Cambridge AI hub provide practical recruitment advantages.

| Criterion | Score 0 | Score 3 | Score 5 |
