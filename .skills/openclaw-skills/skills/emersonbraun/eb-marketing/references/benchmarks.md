| Industry | Avg Open Rate | Avg Click Rate | Avg Conversion Rate |
|---|---|---|---|
| SaaS/Software | 20-25% | 2-3% | 1-2% |
| E-commerce | 15-20% | 2-3% | 0.5-1.5% |
| Agency/Services | 18-22% | 2-4% | 1-3% |
| Education/Courses | 20-28% | 2-5% | 1-3% |
| Health/Fitness | 18-22% | 2-3% | 0.5-1.5% |
| Finance/Fintech | 20-25% | 2-4% | 1-2% |
| Media/Publishing | 20-25% | 3-5% | 0.5-1% |

### Welcome Series Benchmarks (runs higher than campaigns)
- Open rate target: 40-60%
- Click rate target: 8-15%
- Reply rate target: 3-5% (especially on engagement emails)
- Unsubscribe rate: < 0.5% per email

### Nurture Sequence Benchmarks
- Sequence completion rate: 60%+ should receive all emails
- Conversion rate: 2-8% of sequence recipients
- Best performing email: Usually the offer emails (near end of sequence)

### Launch Sequence Benchmarks

| Metric | Good | Great | Exceptional |
|---|---|---|---|
| Open rate (launch day) | 30% | 45% | 60%+ |
| Click rate (launch day) | 5% | 10% | 15%+ |
| Conversion rate (full sequence) | 2% | 5% | 10%+ |
| Revenue per subscriber | $1-3 | $3-8 | $8+ |

### Best Send Times
- B2B: Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM recipient's local time
- B2C: Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM or 7-9 PM recipient's local time
- E-commerce: Thursday-Sunday (promo), Tuesday-Wednesday (educational)
- Avoid: Monday mornings, Friday afternoons, weekends (except e-commerce)

## 5. Page Speed to Conversion Impact

| Load Time | Conversion Impact |
|---|---|
| 0-2 seconds | Baseline (optimal) |
| 2-3 seconds | -7% conversion rate |
| 3-5 seconds | -20% conversion rate |
| 5-8 seconds | -35% conversion rate |
| 8+ seconds | -50%+ conversion rate |

### Core Web Vitals Benchmarks

| Metric | Good | Needs Work | Poor |
|---|---|---|---|
| Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) | Under 2.5s | 2.5-4.0s | Over 4.0s |
| First Input Delay (FID) | Under 100ms | 100-300ms | Over 300ms |
| Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS) | Under 0.1 | 0.1-0.25 | Over 0.25 |
| Time to First Byte (TTFB) | Under 200ms | 200-500ms | Over 500ms |
| First Contentful Paint (FCP) | Under 1.8s | 1.8-3.0s | Over 3.0s |

### Core Web Vitals Revenue Impact
- Sites passing all CWV: 24% fewer page abandonments
- 100ms decrease in LCP: 1.1% increase in conversion rates
- Reducing CLS by 0.1: 15% decrease in bounce rate
- Pages loading in 2s: 9% bounce rate vs 5s: 38% bounce rate

## 9. Retargeting Funnel Budget Splits

```
STAGE 1: AWARENESS (Cold Audience)        -- 40% of total ad spend
  Audience: Lookalikes, interest-based, broad
  Goal: Introduce brand and value proposition
  Ad Type: Educational content, how-to videos, thought leadership
  Metrics: CPM, reach, video view rate, landing page views

STAGE 2: CONSIDERATION (Warm Audience)    -- 35% of total ad spend
  Audience: Website visitors (7-30 days), video viewers (50%+),
            social engagers, email list
  Goal: Build trust and handle objections
  Ad Type: Case studies, testimonials, demos, comparison content
  Metrics: CPC, CTR, landing page conversion rate

STAGE 3: CONVERSION (Hot Audience)        -- 25% of total ad spend
  Audience: Cart abandoners, pricing page visitors, trial users,
            high-intent page visitors
  Goal: Drive the final conversion action
  Ad Type: Direct offer, urgency, guarantee, limited-time discount
  Metrics: CPA, ROAS, conversion rate
```

### Retargeting Window Recommendations
- Website visitors: 7-30 days (sweet spot: 14 days)
- Video viewers (50%+): 30-60 days
- Cart abandoners: 1-7 days (highest urgency)
- Pricing page visitors: 7-14 days
- Email subscribers: 30-90 days

## 10. Launch Budget Allocation Guide

| Budget Level | Allocation |
|---|---|
| **Bootstrapped ($0-500)** | 100% organic: content, communities, email list, personal outreach |
| **Moderate ($500-5,000)** | 40% paid ads, 30% influencer/partner, 20% tools/software, 10% design |
| **Well-Funded ($5,000-25,000)** | 35% paid ads, 25% influencer/partner, 20% PR/media, 10% events, 10% tools |
| **Enterprise ($25,000+)** | 30% paid ads, 20% events/webinars, 20% PR, 15% influencer, 10% content, 5% tools |

### Launch Type Timeline Guide

| Launch Type | Best For | Key Channel | Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|
| Product Hunt | SaaS, dev tools, consumer apps | Product Hunt + Twitter/X | 4-6 weeks prep |
| Email List | Course, info product, SaaS with list | Email | 6-8 weeks |
| Social Media | Consumer product, personal brand | Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Instagram | 4-6 weeks |
| Paid Ads | E-commerce, established product | Facebook/Google Ads | 2-4 weeks prep |
| Community | Niche product, developer tools | Reddit, Discord, Slack | 6-8 weeks |
| Partner | B2B, enterprise, marketplace | Partner channels | 8-12 weeks |
| Hybrid | Any high-stakes launch | Multi-channel coordinated | 8-12 weeks |

### Launch Metrics Dashboard

**Awareness Metrics:** Traffic (total + by source), social impressions, press mentions, email open rates.
**Engagement Metrics:** Time on site, pages/session, engagement rate, CTR, demo completion.
**Conversion Metrics:** Signup/purchase rate, revenue, AOV, CPA, email-to-conversion rate.
**Retention Metrics (Post-Launch):** Day 1/Day 7 retention, feature adoption, support tickets, NPS.

## 11. Revenue Impact Estimation Framework

```
Revenue Impact Formula:
  Current Monthly Traffic x Conversion Rate Improvement x Average Deal Value
  = Estimated Monthly Revenue Lift

Example:
  10,000 visitors x 0.5% conversion lift x $99 ARPU = $4,950/month

Impact Levels:
  High Impact:   >$5,000/mo or >20% improvement (clear evidence)
  Medium Impact: $1,000-$5,000/mo or 5-20% improvement (industry benchmarks)
  Low Impact:    <$1,000/mo or <5% improvement (incremental optimization)
```

### Revenue Per Visitor (RPV) Calculation

```
RPV = Monthly Revenue / Monthly Visitors

Improvement scenario:
  Current:  10,000 visitors x 2.0% CR x $100 AOV = $20,000/mo  (RPV = $2.00)
  Improved: 10,000 visitors x 2.5% CR x $100 AOV = $25,000/mo  (RPV = $2.50)
  Lift:     $5,000/month = $60,000/year
```
