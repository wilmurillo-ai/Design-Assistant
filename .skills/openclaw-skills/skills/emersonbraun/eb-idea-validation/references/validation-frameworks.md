# Validation Frameworks Reference

## The Mom Test — Question Bank

Rules: Never ask "Would you use X?" — ask about past behavior and real pain.

### Pain Discovery Questions
- "What's the hardest part about [doing X]?"
- "Tell me about the last time you dealt with [problem]. Walk me through it."
- "How are you solving this today? What do you use?"
- "What have you tried that didn't work?"
- "How much time/money does this cost you per [week/month]?"
- "If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing about [process], what would it be?"

### Commitment Questions
- "Would you be willing to try a beta if I built this?"
- "Would you pay $X/month for this?" (Only AFTER establishing pain)
- "Can you introduce me to 3 other people who have this problem?"
- "Would you be willing to pre-pay for early access?"

### Red Flag Responses
- "That sounds cool!" — Opinion, not commitment. Dig deeper.
- "I'd definitely use that" — Future promise. Ask what they do TODAY.
- "Sure, send it when it's ready" — No skin in the game. Ask for pre-payment.

## Jobs To Be Done Canvas

"People don't buy products — they hire them to do a job."

| Element | Question |
|---------|----------|
| **Job** | What job is the customer trying to get done? |
| **Circumstance** | When and where does this job arise? |
| **Current Solution** | What do they hire today to do this job? |
| **Pain with Current** | Why is the current solution frustrating? |
| **Desired Outcome** | What does "done well" look like? |
| **Switching Cost** | What would it take to switch from current to new? |

## Market Sizing Worksheet

### Bottom-Up (Primary)
```
Number of potential customers: ___
x Percentage you can reach (SAM): ___%
= Reachable customers: ___
x Average revenue per customer/year: $___
= SAM: $___
x Realistic capture rate Year 1 (1-5%): ___%
= SOM: $___
```

### Top-Down (Sanity Check)
```
Industry total market: $___
x Percentage in your segment: ___%
= TAM: $___
x Percentage you can serve: ___%
= SAM: $___
```

If bottom-up and top-down differ by more than 5x, investigate why.
