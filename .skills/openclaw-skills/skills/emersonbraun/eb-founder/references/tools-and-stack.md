## Recommendation by Phase

### Pre-MVP (Validation)
- **Landing**: Carrd or Framer ($0-20)
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics + Hotjar free
- **Email**: Mailerlite free
- **Design**: Figma free + Canva free
- **Forms**: Google Forms or Formspree
- **Total**: $0-20/mo

### MVP (Build)
- **Stack**: Next.js + Supabase + Vercel ($0-25)
- **Analytics**: PostHog free
- **Payments**: Stripe or Pagar.me
- **Email**: Postmark ($10)
- **Project**: Linear free
- **Monitoring**: Sentry free + UptimeRobot free
- **Total**: $10-60/mo

### Growth (Scale)
- **Analytics**: PostHog/Mixpanel + ChartMogul
- **CRM**: HubSpot or Intercom
- **Automation**: N8N or Zapier
- **Email**: ConvertKit or Klaviyo
- **Total**: $100-500/mo

### Series A+ (Enterprise)
- Upgrade to paid tiers for everything
- Datadog, Amplitude, Zendesk enterprise
- **Total**: $1K-10K/mo
