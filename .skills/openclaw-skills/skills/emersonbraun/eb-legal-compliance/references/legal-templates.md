# Legal Templates Reference

> ⚠️ ALL templates below are starting points. Have a qualified lawyer review before using.

## Privacy Policy Template

```markdown
# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** [DATE]

## What We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:
- **Account information**: Email address, name, password (hashed)
- **Usage data**: Pages visited, features used, timestamps
- **Payment information**: Processed by [Stripe/etc.] — we never store card numbers

## Why We Collect It

| Data | Purpose | Legal Basis |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Email | Account creation, communication | Contract |
| Usage data | Product improvement, bug fixing | Legitimate interest |
| Payment info | Process transactions | Contract |

## Cookies and Tracking

We use:
- **Essential cookies**: Required for the product to function (no consent needed)
- **Analytics cookies**: [PostHog/etc.] to understand usage (requires consent)

You can reject non-essential cookies via the cookie banner.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data (request an export)
- **Delete** your data (delete your account)
- **Correct** your data (edit your profile)
- **Object** to processing (contact us)
- **Withdraw consent** (cookie preferences)

To exercise these rights, email: [EMAIL]

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your data. We share data only with:
- **Payment processor**: [Stripe] for payment processing
- **Hosting provider**: [Vercel/Railway] for running the service
- **Email provider**: [Resend] for transactional emails

## Data Retention

- Account data: Kept until you delete your account
- Usage data: Aggregated and anonymized after 24 months
- Payment records: Kept for 7 years (tax requirements)

## Security

We use industry-standard security: HTTPS, encrypted storage, access controls.

## Contact

Questions? Email [EMAIL] or write to [ADDRESS].
```

## Refund Policy Template

```markdown
# Refund Policy

## Free Trial
Cancel during your trial and you won't be charged.

## Monthly Plans
No refunds for the current month. Cancel anytime — you'll keep access until the billing period ends.

## Annual Plans
Full refund within 14 days of purchase. After 14 days, you can cancel but no refund for the remaining period.

## How to Request
Email [EMAIL] with your account email and reason for cancellation.
```

## Cookie Policy Template

```markdown
# Cookie Policy

## What Are Cookies
Small files stored on your device to make websites work and to help us understand usage.

## Cookies We Use

| Cookie | Type | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|------|---------|----------|
| session | Essential | Keep you logged in | Session |
| cookie-consent | Essential | Remember your cookie preference | 1 year |
| _ph_* | Analytics | Usage analytics (PostHog) | 1 year |

## Managing Cookies
- Use the cookie banner to accept/reject non-essential cookies
- Clear cookies in your browser settings
- Note: rejecting essential cookies may break the product

## Changes
We'll update this page if we add new cookies.
```
