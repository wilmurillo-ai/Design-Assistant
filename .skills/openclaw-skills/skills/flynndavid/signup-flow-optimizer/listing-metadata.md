**Name:** Signup Flow Optimizer — SaaS Friction Audit & Redesign
**Tagline:** Turn your leaky signup into a conversion machine in one session.
**About:** Signup Flow Optimizer gives your AI assistant a structured framework to audit your SaaS signup flow and produce a complete redesign recommendation. Paste or describe your current flow — fields, screens, redirects — and get back a friction-scored map, your #1 estimated drop-off point, the top 3 cuts to make immediately, and a redesigned flow you can hand directly to engineering. Built around the Friction Audit Method, which scores every field and step on necessity, timing, and cognitive load. Ideal for SaaS founders, growth teams, and product managers who suspect their signup is leaking users but don't know where or why. Every recommendation includes a directional conversion lift estimate so you can prioritize with confidence.

**Capabilities:**
- Maps your entire current signup flow into a structured, auditable format
- Scores every field and step on necessity, timing, and cognitive load (High/Medium/Low)
- Identifies your #1 estimated drop-off point with a clear explanation
- Delivers the top 3 highest-impact cuts or simplifications, ranked by priority
- Produces a complete redesigned flow spec — ready to hand to a designer or engineer
- Provides a directional conversion lift estimate with A/B testing guidance
