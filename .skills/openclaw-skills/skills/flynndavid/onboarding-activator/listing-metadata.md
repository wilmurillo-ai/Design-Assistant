The Onboarding Activator skill guides your AI assistant through the Activation Path Method: a structured framework to define your aha moment, audit every delay between signup and first value, and produce a concrete redesign plan. You'll walk away with a minimum viable activation path, a trigger-based email sequence, and a prioritized implementation list — not generic advice, but a specific redesign built around your product. Built for SaaS founders, growth PMs, and onboarding teams who want higher D7 retention without a major engineering lift.

- Define or sharpen your aha moment — the single action that predicts long-term retention
- Map current time-to-aha and identify your top 3 activation delays (blank canvas, setup-first flows, feature tours, and more)
- Generate a Minimum Viable Activation Path (MVAP) — the shortest route from signup to first value
- Design a trigger-based first-week email sequence with subject lines, send logic, and CTAs
- Get specific UX recommendations for empty states, coach marks, demo data, and milestone celebrations
- Receive a prioritized implementation plan with directional activation improvement projections
