**About:**
Most SaaS upgrade screens fail for the same reasons: feature lists instead of outcomes, generic plan names, no risk reversal, and a CTA that says "Upgrade Now" to people who don't yet believe they should. This skill fixes all of it. Paste in your current paywall copy, answer a few questions about your product and buyer, and the AI produces a complete conversion-optimized upgrade screen — headline, plan names, CTA variations, risk reversal copy, social proof placement, and a full ready-to-implement spec. Built on the Upgrade Moment Method: a framework that treats every paywall as a sales conversation that happens inside your product. Ideal for SaaS founders, product managers, and growth teams who want a conversion lift without a redesign agency.

**Capabilities:**
- Diagnoses exactly what's killing conversion in your current upgrade screen
- Rewrites your headline, subheadline, and plan names using outcome-first copy
- Delivers 3 CTA variations (outcome-focused, access-focused, risk-reversal-embedded)
- Recommends the right risk reversal (trial, guarantee, no-CC) with exact copy and placement
- Produces a full redesigned upgrade screen spec — ready to hand to a designer or developer
- Advises on trigger placement, soft vs. hard paywalls, and downgrade path design
