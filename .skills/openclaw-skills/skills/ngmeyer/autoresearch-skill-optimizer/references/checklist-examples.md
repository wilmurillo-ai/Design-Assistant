# Scoring Checklist Examples by Skill Type

## Cold outreach email
- Does it mention the prospect's business name specifically?
- Is it under 75 words?
- Does it end with a specific question (not "let me know if interested")?
- Is it free of buzzwords (revolutionary, cutting-edge, synergy, game-changing)?
- Does the first line reference a specific pain point?

## Content/writing skill
- Is output free of em-dashes? (AI detector signal)
- Does the CTA use a specific verb phrase?
- Is the headline specific (number or result) vs. vague promise?
- Is the copy under the target word count?

## Research/analysis skill
- Does every claim have a source URL or ⚠️ inference flag?
- Is there a concrete recommendation section?
- Is output under 800 words?
- Are contradicting sources noted?

## Thread/content extraction
- Does output contain actual content (not an error)?
- Is author/metadata correctly extracted?
- Is UI chrome absent from output?
- Is output structured with clear sections?

## Universal structural quality (add to any checklist)
- Does the skill have a Gotchas section?
- Is the description a trigger condition?

## Process/meta-skills
- Did the process produce a clear result?
- Were there moments of confusion where instructions were ambiguous?
- Did any step feel unnecessary or redundant?
- Could someone follow this without prior context?
