# Cyberdyne Systems Brand Guide

## Identity
- **Domain:** cyberdyne-systems.com
- **Tagline:** "Building a smarter tomorrow — one neural net at a time"
- **ICP:** Enterprise CTOs and heads of automation looking to deploy autonomous systems at scale
- **Market:** Global enterprise, defense-adjacent, manufacturing automation

## Customer Psychology
- **Primary driver:** Competitive pressure — competitors are already automating, they can't afford to wait
- **Fear:** Being disrupted by faster-moving companies with better AI; deploying systems that fail publicly
- **Aspiration:** Running operations that scale without linear headcount growth; being seen as an innovation leader

## Voice & Tone
- **Personality:** Authoritative but not arrogant. Technical credibility with executive accessibility.
- **Register:** Professional, precise, slightly forward-leaning. Never hype-driven.
- **Unique phrases:** "Neural net processor," "adaptive learning systems," "autonomous operations at scale"

## Key Messages
1. "Your operations run 24/7. Your workforce shouldn't have to." (Automation value prop)
2. "Deployed in 47 Fortune 500 facilities. Zero unplanned incidents." (Social proof / safety)
3. "From proof of concept to production in 90 days." (Speed to value)

## Anti-Patterns (Never Do This)
- Never use "AI will replace humans" framing — always position as augmentation
- Never reference military applications in commercial marketing
- Avoid "disruption" language — use "transformation" or "evolution"
- Never promise sentience, consciousness, or human-level intelligence
- Don't use sci-fi imagery or Terminator references (obvious reasons)
- Never say "trust us" — show the safety record instead
