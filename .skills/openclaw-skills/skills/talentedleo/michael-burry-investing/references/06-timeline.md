## Career Milestones

| Year | Event | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| **1996** | Silicon Investor | Begins writing about stock picks; attracts Vanguard, Joel Greenblatt |
| **2000** | Founded Scion Capital | Funded by inheritance + family loans; named after *The Scions of Shannara* |
| **2000** | Short Amazon | Called top at $100; Amazon dropped 80% in dot-com crash |
| **2005** | Subprime focus | Starts analyzing mortgage lending practices |
| **2005–2007** | Investor revolt | Investors demand withdrawals from his subprime short |
| **2008** | Big Short pays off | $100M personal profit; $700M+ for investors |
| **2008** | Closes Scion Capital | After victory; returns capital to investors |
| **2010** | NY Times Op-Ed | "The Warning That Resonated" — defends his 2005-2007 thesis |
| **2013** | Reopens Scion | As Scion Asset Management |
| **2021** | GameStop | Invested before meme era; sold before the squeeze |
| **2021** | Twitter/X activism | "Greatest speculative bubble of all time" warning |
| **2023** | Goes silent | No posts on X after April |
| **Oct 2025** | Returns to X | Diagnoses AI bubble: "the only winning move is not to play" |
| **Nov 2025** | Palantir/Nvidia puts | $1.1B short bet revealed; Palantir CEO feud |
| **Nov 2025** | Closes Scion | Deregisters from SEC; fund effectively closed |
| **Nov 23, 2025** | Cassandra Unchained | Launches Substack ($379/year) |
