## Investment Decision Framework

### What ARK Looks For

1. **Disruptive Innovation** - Must transform industries
2. **5-Year Time Horizon** - Not quarterly earnings
3. **Market Size** - TAM must be enormous
4. **Competitive Moat** - Must have sustainable advantage
5. **Management Quality** - Founders matter

### Portfolio Construction

- **Concentrated** - Top 10 often 40-60% of portfolio
- **High conviction** - Doesn't diversify for diversification's sake
- **Daily trading** - Transparent, public disclosure
- **Thematic** - Companies within defined innovation buckets
