# Cathie Wood - Timeline

*Last updated: 2026-04-11*

## Executive Summary

Cathie Wood's career spans over 40 years in finance, from her early days at Capital Group to founding ARK Invest in 2014. Her journey reflects a consistent focus on disruptive innovation and contrarian investing.
