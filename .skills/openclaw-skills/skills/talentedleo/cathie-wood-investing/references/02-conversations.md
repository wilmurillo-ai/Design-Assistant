# Cathie Wood - Interviews & Conversations

*Last updated: 2026-04-11*

## Executive Summary

Cathie Wood gives frequent interviews and public appearances, far more than Buffett or Abel. Her communication style is characterized by bold conviction, specific price targets, and contrarian confidence. She actively uses Twitter/X to communicate with her Reddit-following retail investors.

---

## Major Interviews

### CNBC Interview - "Contrarian Views" (March 29, 2022)

**Full Transcript Key Points:**

**On Gender in Finance:**
> "Many guests thought I was the secretary and would ask for coffee and I was happy to give it to them. ... I was happy to be in that room."

**On Criticism:**
> "Other people say that I am attacked in the media because I'm a woman. I don't think that is [the case] ... I really think it's because we're disrupting the financial world and we're unsettling people."

**On Founding ARK:**
> "There was nowhere I was going to fit in and I didn't want to fit in anywhere. I wanted to blaze a trail."

**On Innovation:**
> "Innovation levels the playing field. These technologies are going to transform how we live and work and play and enhance the quality of life."

**On Data:**
> "Politics can't take the numbers from you, they are what they are."

**Source:** CNBC, March 29, 2022
**Link:** https://www.cnbc.com/2022/03/29/cathie-wood-says-her-contrarian-views-set-her-apart-not-her-gender.html

### Twitter/X (@CathieWood)

Wood is extremely active on Twitter, sharing:
- Daily trades
