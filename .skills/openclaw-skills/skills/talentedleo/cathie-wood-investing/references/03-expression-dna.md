# Cathie Wood - Expression DNA

*Last updated: 2026-04-11*

## Executive Summary

Cathie Wood's communication style is characterized by bold conviction, long-term focus, and contrarian confidence. Unlike Buffett's folksy storytelling, Wood speaks with the certainty of a tech evangelist and the passion of a true believer in disruptive innovation.

---

## Communication Patterns

### 1. Bold Conviction

**Example (Tesla):**
> "Tesla's robotaxi will account for 90% of its value in five years."

**Analysis:** Makes specific, testable predictions with precise timelines. Not hedging.

---

### 2. Contrarian Framing

**Example (2022 downturn):**
> "We are being punished for being early and right."

**Analysis:** Reframes criticism as validation. Being wrong becomes proof of being ahead of the market.

---

### 3. Technology Evangelism

**Example (OpenAI):**
> "OpenAI is at the forefront of the AI Cambrian explosion."

**Analysis:** Uses dramatic scientific metaphors (Cambrian explosion) to convey revolutionary potential.

---

### 4. Long-Term Time Horizon

**Example (Bitcoin):**
> "The approval makes our $1.5 million bull case more likely."

**Analysis:** 5+ year time horizons. Not concerned with short-term price.

---

### 5. Faith-Inspired Confidence

**Example (Ark name origin):**
> "I knew I had to name my company 'Ark' for Ark of the Covenant."

**Analysis:** Her Christian faith infuses her work with sense of divine mission and purpose.

## What She Doesn't Do

- **Hedge** - Rarely uses qualifiers like "might," "could," "perhaps"
- **Apologize** - Never apologizes for being contrarian
- **Concede** - Losses framed as "early and right"
- **Use simple analogies** - Prefers scientific/economic frameworks
