### Bear Case Arguments

**1. Concentration Risk**
Critics argue ARKK's concentrated bets (often 40-60% in top 10 holdings) create excessive volatility.

**2. High Fees**
ARK's active ETFs charge higher fees than passive alternatives.

**3. Performance Chasing**
The 2020 success came after years of mediocre performance.

**4. Narrative Over Fundamentals**
Critics say Wood prioritizes compelling stories over financial analysis.

---

## Bull Case Arguments

### ARK Defense Points

**1. Long-Term Vision**
ARK evaluates companies on 5-year horizons, not quarters.

**2. Innovation Exposure**
Provides unique exposure to transformative technologies unavailable elsewhere.

**3. Transparency**
Daily disclosure of all trades.

**4. Rebound in 2023/2025**
ARKK was top performer in 2023 and 2025.
