# Greg Abel - Timeline

*Last updated: 2026-04-10*

## Executive Summary

Greg Abel's journey from Edmonton, Alberta to CEO of Berkshire Hathaway spans over 40 years. His career is marked by operational excellence, loyal service, and eventual selection as Warren Buffett's successor.
