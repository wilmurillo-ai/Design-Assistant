# Seers User Guide (English)

## What is Seers?

Seers is a multi-chain decentralized prediction market platform that allows users to:

- Create prediction events (pay 1000 USDT as deposit)
- Participate in event trading (buy/sell YES/NO positions)
- Submit evidence for arbitration
- Earn rewards based on event outcomes

Seers uses a pool-based market mechanism for predicting and settling future events. Users place bets to form pools for different options, and rewards are ultimately distributed based on the proportion of bets in the correct option pool.

## Is Seers a Market Maker?

No. Seers is not a market maker.

On Seers, users are the core drivers—

- Users are responsible for creating events
- Users bear the event deposit
- Users can profit from event trading fees

Seers official only handles:

- Arbitration
- Security maintenance
- On-chain mechanism execution

The platform remains strictly neutral, does not bet on or manipulate market outcomes, and only intervenes in rare disputed events requiring secondary rulings to maintain overall fairness and user interests.

## How to Create an Event?

Any user can create an event by:

1. Connecting a supported wallet (e.g., MetaMask / Phantom)
2. Selecting a chain (Solana / Polygon / Ethereum, etc.)
3. Setting up:
   - Event title
   - Description
   - Rules and resolution criteria
   - Expiration time

To meet different needs, we offer two creation methods:
- Traditional Mode: No deposit required, suitable for most regular events.
- Maker Mode: Creators pay a configurable deposit to incentivize higher quality events and maintain platform ecosystem.

## Why Pay a Deposit?

The deposit is used for:

- Preventing malicious event creation
- Providing initial market credibility
- Compensating for disputed or voided events
- Ensuring participants have real cost involvement

The deposit is automatically refunded after final settlement (unless the event violates rules or incurs dispute costs).

## How to Trade?

In any event, you can choose:

- YES (believe the event will happen)
- NO (believe the event won't happen)

Buy shares of the corresponding option and join the pool. The market shows the current fund ratio of each option, and at settlement, winners receive rewards based on their share in the correct pool.

When the event ends:

- Correct prediction → Receive payout
- Wrong prediction → Position goes to zero

## Who Decides the Outcome?

Seers uses a hybrid arbitration mechanism:

### 1. User Evidence Submission

Anyone can upload:

- On-chain data screenshots
- Official announcements
- News media
- Sports results
- Trusted third-party information

### 2. Official Arbitration (Final Arbitration)

The Seers arbitration team publishes the final ruling on-chain.

This helps avoid:

- Malicious event creators
- Third-party manipulation
- Data falsification

## Can Seers Manipulate Results?

No.

Seers' responsibilities are:

- Executing on-chain rules
- Ensuring fair and transparent mechanisms
- Providing final arbitration

Seers does not participate in market trading or bet on events, only maintaining platform operations through reasonable fees.

## Which Chains are Supported?

Currently supported:

- Solana
- Polygon
- Ethereum
- BSC
- Base
- BTC (partial features)

More chains coming soon.

## What Events Can Be Created?

Any event with clear, verifiable outcomes, such as:

- Crypto markets (Will BTC break 100k)
- Sports events
- Tech launches and product events
- Policy or social trends
- Real-time hot topics
- Crypto ecosystem data (Gas, TVL, active users, etc.)

Prohibited:

- Content involving personal privacy
- High-risk political inflammatory content
- Content that cannot be objectively verified

Seers reserves the right to reject or terminate violating events in extreme cases.
