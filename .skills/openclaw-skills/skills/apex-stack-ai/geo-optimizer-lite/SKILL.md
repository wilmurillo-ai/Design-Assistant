## What Is GEO?

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of optimizing content so AI search engines cite it in their responses. Traditional SEO gets you into Google's index. GEO gets you cited by AI.

## How to Use

1. Provide a URL or paste content
2. I score it on 4 GEO dimensions

## GEO Scorecard (Lite — 4 of 8 Dimensions)

| Dimension | What It Measures | Score |
|-----------|-----------------|-------|
| **Factual Density** | Data points, statistics, specific claims per paragraph | /10 |
| **Source Attribution** | Citations, references, methodology transparency | /10 |

## Scoring Guide

- **32-40**: Excellent — high citability
- **24-31**: Good — minor improvements needed
- **16-23**: Fair — significant gaps
- **Below 16**: Poor — major rewrite recommended
