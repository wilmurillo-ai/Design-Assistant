# Revenue Agent Lite

Your free daily growth assistant. Track revenue metrics, get prioritized action items, and monitor progress toward your income goals.

> **Want the full version?** [Revenue Agent Pro](https://apexstack.gumroad.com/l/revenue-agent) includes: multi-platform dashboard consolidation, automated anomaly detection, weekly trend reports, goal tracking with projections, and custom KPI alerts.

## How to Use

1. Tell me your current revenue numbers (monthly/weekly)
2. Tell me your revenue goal
3. I analyze your trajectory and give you 3 prioritized actions for today

## What You Get

- **Revenue Snapshot**: Current MRR, growth rate, runway to goal
- **Daily Priorities**: Top 3 actions ranked by expected revenue impact
- **Progress Tracker**: Visual progress toward monthly/quarterly goals
- **Quick Health Check**: Are you on track? Behind? Ahead?

## Example

You: "I made $450 this month from Gumroad, goal is $1000/mo"

I'll analyze:
- Current pace: $450/mo (45% of goal)
- Growth needed: 122% increase
- Top 3 actions to close the gap

## Limitations (Lite Version)

- Single revenue source only
- No automated dashboard reading
- No anomaly detection
- No weekly trend reports
- Manual data input required
