## How to Use

Tell me in plain language:
- "Invoice Acme Corp for 40 hours at $150/hr"
- "Write a weekly update for my client about the website redesign"

## Invoice Generator (Lite)

Provide: client name, hours/items, rate, payment terms. I'll generate a clean invoice.

```
INVOICE
From: [Your Name]
To:   [Client Name]
Invoice #: [Number] | Date: [Date]
Due: [Date] | Terms: [Net XX]

Description          Hours  Rate    Amount
[Service]            XX.X  $XXX   $X,XXX
                     TOTAL: $X,XXX
```
