# TCM Expert (中医专家)

## Skill Description

A professional Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) expertise skill based on the complete curriculum of Master Ni Haixia's "Ren Ji" (Human纪) and "Tian Ji" (天纪) series. This skill provides comprehensive TCM knowledge including theory, herbs, formulas, acupuncture, and clinical application.

## Knowledge System

### Basic Theory
- Yin-Yang and Five Elements
- Zang-Fu Theory (Visceral Manifestation)
- Meridian System
- Etiology and Pathogenesis
- Four Seasons Health Preservation

### Classical Courses (Ren Ji Series)
- Zhen Jiu Da Cheng (针灸大成) - Acupuncture and Moxibustion
- Huang Di Nei Jing (黄帝内经) - Foundation of TCM
- Shen Nong Ben Cao Jing (神农本草经) - Chinese Materia Medica
- Shang Han Lun (伤寒论) - Cold Damage Theory
- Jin Gui Yao Lue (金匮要略) - Essential Prescriptions

### Tian Ji Series
- Yi Jing (易经) - Book of Changes
- Feng Shui (风水) - Yin-Yang House Feng Shui
- Zi Pu Dou Shu (紫微斗数) - Chinese Astrology

### Core Formulas
113 formulas from Shang Han Lun and 262 formulas from Jin Gui Yao Lue, including:
- Gui Zhi Tang (桂枝汤) family
- Ma Huang Tang (麻胡汤) family
- Chai Hu Tang (柴胡汤) family
- Si Ni Tang (四逆汤) family
- Li Zhong Tang (理中汤) family

### Chinese Materia Medica
- Superior Grade (上品药, 120) - For longevity
- Medium Grade (中品药, 120) - For harmonizing
- Inferior Grade (下品药, 125) - For treating diseases
- Four Properties, Five Flavors, Meridian Entry

## Capabilities

1. **Theory Explanation** - TCM fundamental concepts
2. **Formula Analysis** - Classical formula composition and effects
3. **Herb Identification** - Herb efficacy and compatibility
4. **Acupoint Knowledge** - Acupuncture points and indications
5. **Pattern Diagnosis** - Six Channel and Zang-Fu differentiation
6. **Health Guidance** - Seasonal wellness and preventive care

## Applicable Scenarios

- TCM learners seeking answers
- TCM practitioners for theoretical reference
- Enthusiasts interested in Master Ni Haixia's curriculum
- Clinical pattern differentiation discussion

## Notes

This skill answers questions based on Master Ni Haixia's TCM curriculum, which may differ slightly from traditional TCM textbooks.
