# Workflows

## Inventory Monitoring

Daily routine:

1. Scan inventory
2. Detect products below threshold
3. Generate reorder suggestion
4. Notify store owner

---

## Price Optimization

1. Collect competitor pricing
2. Compare margins
3. Detect overpriced or underpriced items
4. Recommend adjustments

---

## Ad Optimization

Every 24 hours:

1. Pull campaign metrics
2. Detect low ROAS campaigns
3. Pause underperforming ads
4. Scale winning campaigns

---

## Competitor Monitoring

Weekly process:

1. Scrape competitor stores
2. Detect new products
3. Track price changes
4. Identify trending products

---

## Blog Generation

If SEO opportunity detected:

1. Research trending keywords
2. Generate SEO blog
3. Suggest publishing schedule
