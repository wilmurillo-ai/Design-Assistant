# Google Ads Algorithm & Policy Tracker

## Performance Max (PMax) — 2025 Transformation

PMax went from "black box" to significantly more transparent and controllable in 2025.
Over 1 million advertisers now use PMax.

### PMax 2025 Updates (Chronological)

**January 2025**
- ✅ **Campaign-level negative keywords** (10,000 limit) — Search and Shopping only
  - Does NOT block Display, YouTube, Gmail, Discover (use placement/topic exclusions there)
  - Immediate impact: One retailer saw 15% cost reduction adding "free" and "used" as negatives
  - Access: Campaign → Keywords → Negative keywords

**April–May 2025 (Google Marketing Live)**
- ✅ **Channel-level performance reporting** (beta → all accounts by Nov 2025)
  - Shows performance across: Search, Shopping, YouTube, Display, Gmail, Maps, Discover
  - Includes clicks, conversions, conversion value, cost per channel
  - Access: Campaign → Channel performance tab

- ✅ **Full Search Terms Reporting** for PMax Search & Shopping placements
  - Same granularity as standard Search campaigns
  - Access: Reports → Predefined Reports → Search Terms

- ✅ **Asset-level reporting** with impressions, clicks, cost, conversions per asset
  - No longer just "Learning/Good/Best" ratings — actual performance data

- ✅ **Search themes** expanded 25 → 50 per asset group

- ✅ **AI Max** launched — new Search campaign type (part of the "Power Pack")
  - Works alongside PMax and Demand Gen
  - Demand Gen handles awareness; AI Max captures Search intent; PMax scales full funnel

**November 2025**
- ✅ **Channel reporting extended to ALL accounts** (was beta before)
- ✅ **Waze inventory** added for PMax store goals campaigns (US only; international 2026)
  - Appears as "Promoted Places in Navigation" pin to 150M+ active drivers
  - No extra setup needed; uses existing assets

**2026 Rollout (underway)**
- ✅ **First-party audience exclusions** — exclude specific customer lists (e.g., existing buyers)
- ✅ **Budget projection report** — end-of-month spend projection inside PMax
- ✅ **Placement reports by network** — brand safety visibility
- ✅ **New PMax steering features** — more ways to guide AI

### PMax Optimization Checklist
1. ☐ Review Search Terms report weekly; add high-volume irrelevant terms as negatives
2. ☐ Check Channel Performance — if YouTube CPA >> Search CPA, adjust asset mix
3. ☐ Upload Customer Match lists for audience signals (quality > quantity: 1k similar > 10k diverse)
4. ☐ Refresh creative assets every 4–6 weeks
5. ☐ Enable first-party audience exclusions (converted customers) when available
6. ☐ Use 50 search themes per asset group to guide toward valuable traffic
7. ☐ For retail: ensure catalog/product feed is healthy — PMax uses it for Shopping

## Google AI Power Pack (2025 Onward)

Google's recommended three-campaign setup:

| Campaign Type | Role | When to Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Demand Gen** | Awareness + consideration (YouTube, Discovery, Gmail) | Top-of-funnel; showed 26% conversion lift in tests |
| **AI Max** | Search intent capture | Replace or supplement standard Search campaigns |
| **Performance Max** | Full-funnel scale across all Google properties | Scale; need sufficient conversion volume |

**Note:** Real-world results are mixed for the full Power Pack. Test in A/B before full commitment.

## Smart Bidding & Algorithm Updates

**Broad Match Evolution**
- Broad match + Smart Bidding now strongly recommended by Google
- Matching behavior has expanded significantly — monitor Search Terms report closely
- Best with Target CPA or Target ROAS bidding

**Customer Acquisition Mode (Rolling out 2025–2026)**
- Bid more aggressively for new customers vs. returning customers
- Requires Customer Match list of existing customers
- Useful when LTV of new customers justifies higher CPA

**Attribution**
- Data-driven attribution (DDA) is now default for most accounts
- Last-click still available but Google increasingly deprioritizes it
- Meridian (Google's MMM tool) released 2025 for cross-channel measurement

**Ad Strength Updates (2025)**
- RSA (Responsive Search Ad) now reports individual headline/description performance
- Brand guidelines feature: control colors, fonts, tone, off-limits phrases for AI copy
- Text customization and final URL expansion assets can be reviewed/removed

## Google Ads Policy Changes (2025)

**Search Partner Network**
- Placement reports now available showing which Search Partner Network sites served your ads
- Currently shows impressions only (not clicks/conversions/cost) — still major improvement
- Exclusion lists available via DoubleVerify, IAS, Zefr for brand safety

**Brand Controls**
- Brand exclusions available for Search text ads (while keeping Shopping active)
- Device and demographic targeting controls added to PMax
- Enhanced geo-targeting to reduce undesirable location traffic

**Special Categories (ongoing)**
- Healthcare, financial services, political: restricted targeting, disclaimer requirements
- Similar to Meta — sensitive verticals face tighter data/audience rules

## Recommended Action Matrix

| Issue | Likely Cause | Action |
|---|---|---|
| PMax spending on irrelevant queries | No negative keywords | Add campaign-level negatives via Search Terms report |
| PMax blowing budget on YouTube | Channel imbalance | Check channel report; add more search-focused assets |
| Declining ROAS in PMax | Poor asset quality or signals | Refresh creative, upload better Customer Match lists |
| Smart bidding not hitting targets | Insufficient conversion data | Ensure 50+ conversions/month; consolidate campaigns |
| Brand queries cannibalizing in PMax | No brand exclusion | Set brand exclusions; consider separate Brand campaign |
| AI Max/Broad Match runaway spend | Keywords matching too broadly | Review Search Terms, add negatives, use TROAS/TCPA |
