# Source Gathering

Use this reference when collecting source material for the day’s WeChat article.

## Goal

Build a compact but high-signal source pool for article drafting.

## Recommended collection process

1. Gather 8-15 raw items
2. Narrow to 3-5 items worth writing about
3. Record for each kept item:
   - title
   - source
   - time
   - one-line summary
   - linked topic or sector

## What to gather

### Macro and market environment
- overseas market movement
- rates, bonds, and central-bank signals
- oil, gold, and major commodities
- geopolitics
- policy and regulatory changes

### Sector and theme signals
Typical candidates:
- energy
- chemicals
- technology
- AI infrastructure
- optical/network themes
- power-grid themes
- unexpected breakout themes

## Market-angle compression

Before drafting, compress the day into 3-5 observations, such as:
- whether the market is trend-driven or rotation-driven
- whether the setup is risk-on or risk-off
- what cross-market signals matter most
- what retail-reader emotional hook is strongest today

## Output expectation

The drafting step should receive:
- a filtered source list
- a short market judgment
- one or two primary directions worth expanding
