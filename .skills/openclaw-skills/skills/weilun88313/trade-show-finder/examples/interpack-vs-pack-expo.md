# Example: Interpack vs. PACK EXPO International

**Prompt:** "Compare Interpack and PACK EXPO International for a DACH packaging SaaS vendor targeting enterprise manufacturers."

---

## Executive Recommendation

Choose **Interpack** as the stronger primary exhibit decision for this profile. Keep **PACK EXPO International** as the North America expansion option, but it is not the best first priority for a DACH-centered enterprise motion.

## ICP / Goal Snapshot

- Company / offer: Packaging SaaS
- ICP: Enterprise manufacturers
- Buyers: Plant leadership, operations, packaging engineering, digital transformation
- Goal: Pipeline generation
- Region: DACH first, broader Europe second
- Motion: Exhibit

## Execution Readiness

`Conditional` — the prompt implies an exhibitor motion, but no budget band or North America expansion plan was provided. Interpack is easier to justify operationally for a DACH-based team; PACK EXPO becomes stronger only if U.S. expansion is already funded.

## Top Recommendation(s)

**1. Interpack** — Best primary exhibit choice.  
It matches the regional strategy, has the right packaging-manufacturing gravity, and gives a stronger immediate commercial case for a DACH-based enterprise sales motion.

**2. PACK EXPO International** — Strong secondary option if North America is a real priority.  
This is a serious show, but it should not displace Interpack unless the company is already committed to U.S. pipeline or channel expansion.

## Why Not / Tradeoffs

- **Interpack**: premium flagship event; requires pre-show planning to avoid expensive but shallow traffic
- **PACK EXPO International**: commercially strong, but too geography-heavy for a DACH-first plan unless U.S. expansion is already in scope
