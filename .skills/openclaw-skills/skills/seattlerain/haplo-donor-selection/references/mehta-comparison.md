### Donor Age Thresholds (Exploratory)
- BL-M/DRB1-MM haplo ≤45y → better than 30y PBM-matched MMUD
- BL-M/DRB1-MM haplo >45y → advantage may disappear
- BL-MM haplo ≥55y → may be worse than 30y PBM-matched MMUD

## Decision Logic Summary
- **Young (<35) + BL-M + DRB1-MM** = best of all donor types
- **BL-M + DRB1-MM (any age ≤45)** > MMUD
- **BL-M + DRB1-MM (>45)** ≈ young MMUD (depends on MMUD age/PBM)
- **DRB1-M** = higher relapse (HR 1.56-1.86 vs MMUD)
- **All Haplo+PTCy** = lower GVHD/NRM vs MMUD+CNI
