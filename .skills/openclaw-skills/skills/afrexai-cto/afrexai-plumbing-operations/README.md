# Plumbing Business Operations

AI operations assistant for plumbing companies. Handles the back-office work that eats into your billable hours.

## What It Does

- **Job Estimation** — Generate professional quotes with labor, materials, and permit costs calculated from job parameters (type, fixture count, access difficulty, emergency status)
- **Schedule Optimization** — Route techs by geographic zone, prioritize emergencies, minimize windshield time, output daily route sheets
- **Parts Inventory** — Track high-turn items (PVC, copper, PEX, valves, water heater parts) with reorder alerts based on weekly burn rate
- **Customer Comms** — Templates for quotes, confirmations, completion summaries, follow-ups, and annual maintenance reminders
- **Profitability Analysis** — Per-job margins, tech utilization rates, revenue per truck, callback tracking
- **Warranty & Compliance** — Track labor/parts warranties, license renewals, backflow certifications

## Who It's For

Owner-operators and small plumbing companies (1-15 techs) who want to run tighter operations without hiring office staff.

## Quick Start

1. Install the skill in your AI agent (OpenClaw, etc.)
2. Start with "Create an estimate for a tankless water heater install"
3. Or "Optimize tomorrow's schedule for 3 techs with these 9 jobs"

## Example Prompts

- "Estimate a whole-house repipe, 2-story, 8 fixtures, copper to PEX"
- "What's my inventory reorder list this week?"
- "Draft a follow-up email for the Johnson job completed Tuesday"
- "Show profitability by job type for this month"
- "When is my contractor license up for renewal?"
