# AI Compliance Readiness Assessment

**By AfrexAI** | [clawhub.com](https://clawhub.com)

Evaluate how prepared your organization is for AI governance regulations. Scores readiness across 8 dimensions and generates an actionable compliance roadmap.

## What It Does

Assesses compliance posture against major AI regulations:

- **EU AI Act** (Aug 2026 deadline)
- **NIST AI Risk Management Framework**
- **HHS AI mandates** (healthcare)
- **State bar AI rules** (legal)

Scores 8 dimensions: governance, risk assessment, transparency, data management, human oversight, security, documentation, and training.

## Output

- Overall readiness score with risk level
- Dimension-by-dimension gap analysis
- Prioritized action plan with timelines
- Quick wins vs long-term investments
- Audit preparation checklist

## Usage

```
Assess AI compliance readiness for:
Organization: Acme Health, 200 employees, healthcare SaaS
AI Usage: Patient triage chatbot, claims processing automation
Regulations: HIPAA, EU AI Act, HHS mandates
Timeline: Audit in Q3 2026
```
