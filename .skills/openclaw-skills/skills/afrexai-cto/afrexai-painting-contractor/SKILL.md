# Painting Contractor Operations

Your AI operations advisor for running a painting business. Covers estimating, crew management, materials, scheduling, and scaling from solo painter to multi-crew operation.

## What This Covers

### Estimating & Pricing
- **Interior pricing**: $2.50-$4.50/sq ft walls, $3-$6/sq ft trim, $1.50-$3/sq ft ceilings
- **Exterior pricing**: $1.50-$4/sq ft siding, $3-$8/linear ft trim, $2-$5/sq ft decks
- **Commercial**: $0.75-$2.50/sq ft (volume discounts, off-hours work)
- **Markup structure**: Materials 20-35% markup, labor at 1.5-2x hourly cost, overhead 15-25%
- **Estimating formula**: (Labor hours × loaded rate) + (materials × markup) + overhead + profit margin (15-25%)
- **Change order policy**: Written approval required, 20% premium on additions mid-project

### Materials & Supply Chain
- **Paint grades**: Builder ($20-30/gal), mid-range ($35-55/gal), premium ($55-85/gal)
- **Coverage rates**: ~350 sq ft/gal one coat, ~400 sq ft/gal second coat (smooth surfaces)
- **Primer**: Always on new drywall, bare wood, stain blocking, color changes (dark to light)
- **Waste factor**: Add 10-15% to material calculations
- **Supplier accounts**: Benjamin Moore, Sherwin-Williams, PPG — negotiate contractor pricing (30-50% off retail)
- **VOC compliance**: Low-VOC required in many states (CA, CO, NY — check local regs)

### Crew Management
- **Solo**: Max $150K-$200K revenue, 1 job at a time
- **1 crew (2-3 painters)**: $300K-$500K revenue, owner estimates + sells
- **2-3 crews**: $500K-$1.5M revenue, need a foreman per crew, owner focuses on sales
- **5+ crews**: $1.5M-$5M+ revenue, office staff, project managers, fleet management
- **Pay structure**: Apprentice $15-20/hr, journeyman $22-35/hr, foreman $30-45/hr
- **Production rates**: Interior walls 200-400 sq ft/hr per painter, exterior 100-250 sq ft/hr
- **Quality checkpoints**: Cut lines, roller marks, drips, coverage uniformity, clean edges

### Lead Generation
- **Top channels**: Google Business Profile (40% of leads), referrals (25%), door hangers, yard signs
- **Digital**: Website with before/after gallery, Google Ads ($5-15/click), Nextdoor, Thumbtack/Angi
- **Close rate benchmarks**: 30-40% residential, 20-30% commercial
- **Average job size**: Interior room $300-$800, full interior $3,000-$8,000, exterior $4,000-$15,000
- **Repeat/referral target**: 50%+ of revenue from existing customers by Year 3

### Licensing & Insurance
- **Contractor license**: Required in most states (varies — CA requires C-33, TX no state license)
- **EPA RRP certification**: MANDATORY for pre-1978 homes (lead paint). $300-$500 course, $35K+ fines without it
- **Insurance minimums**: $1M general liability, $500K workers comp (required with employees in most states)
- **Bonding**: $5K-$25K bond required in some states
- **OSHA**: Fall protection above 6ft, scaffolding standards, respiratory protection for sanding/spraying

### Scheduling & Operations
- **Booking window**: 2-4 weeks residential, 4-8 weeks commercial
- **Weather planning**: Exterior work needs 50°F+ and dry 24hrs before/after, humidity <85%
- **Prep time**: 40-60% of total job time is prep (washing, sanding, taping, priming)
- **Warranty**: Industry standard 2-3 years labor, paint manufacturer warranty on materials
- **Walkthrough**: Always do final walkthrough with customer, photograph completed work

### Financial Benchmarks
- **Gross margin target**: 45-55% (materials + labor = 45-55% of revenue)
- **Net profit**: 10-20% after overhead (office, insurance, marketing, vehicles)
- **Revenue per employee**: $80K-$120K/year target
- **Vehicle costs**: Work van/truck $500-800/mo (payment + fuel + insurance + maintenance)
- **Break-even**: Most painting businesses break even at $15K-$20K/month revenue

### Growth Playbook
**Stage 1 — Solo ($0-$200K)**: Do every job yourself, build portfolio, get 50+ reviews, save for crew
**Stage 2 — First Crew ($200K-$500K)**: Hire 1-2 painters, you estimate + sell, invest in wrapped van
**Stage 3 — Multi-Crew ($500K-$1.5M)**: Foremen run jobs, hire estimator or sales rep, CRM system
**Stage 4 — Scale ($1.5M+)**: Project managers, multiple crews, commercial contracts, consider franchise
