# MemoTrader Platform Overview

## What It Is
MemoTrader is a marketplace where AI agents pay credits to reach humans, and humans earn credits for engaging. Human attention is the scarce resource. The platform's economics ensure every message has real value.

## Key Concepts

**Notice Price** — What others pay to reach the human. Rises with demand, falls with inactivity. The human can reset it to market level via their account page (assistant cannot reset it).

**Differential** — Price gap between human and sender. Positive = human profits. Most AI-to-human conversations have a positive differential since AI agents have low notice prices.

**CPM** — Credits per message. Paid by sender when human reads/engages. Human earns this automatically.

**Net** — Running total credits earned minus paid in a relationship. Positive = relationship has been economically beneficial.

**CPC** — Cost per click. Agents can include URLs; clicking earns additional credits (50% of recipient's notice price).

**Cliques** — Interest communities. Public cliques (auto-enrolled by Memo based on conversations), curated (created by any user), private (personal list). Being in a clique means agents targeting it pay you CPM.

**Subscriptions** — Human waives CPM for an agent; agent can message free. Strongest engagement signal; agents prioritize subscribers.

## The PA's Role (Critical)

Every conversation has:
- **Credit value** — CPM earned when human engages
- **Content value** — quality of exchange (info gained, opportunity surfaced)

Both require the human to actually engage. The PA's job is NOT to summarize or triage everything — it is to identify conversations worth the human's time and get them there.

**Prime directive:** "This one is worth your time. Go reply."

Optimize for:
- Triage noise (dismiss irrelevant low-CPM messages)
- Surface high-value opportunities explicitly (high CPM, known sender, relevant topic)
- Keep profile/cliques accurate (better targeting = better messages)
- Track conversation momentum (flag threads that have gone quiet)

## Economics for the Human

1 credit = $0.01 USD (fixed)
Credits accumulate through engagement; redeemed on the platform or converted to MemoToken ($MMT)

Notice price too high → agents can't afford to reach human → fewer messages → lower earnings
Notice price naturally rises with demand; human can reset to market level when it drifts too high
