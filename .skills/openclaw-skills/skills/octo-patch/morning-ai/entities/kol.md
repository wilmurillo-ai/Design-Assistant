## Deduplication Notes

This entity group tracks **individual opinion leaders**. The following content is covered by other entities, this group skips:

- Official company announcements -> covered by corresponding vendor entity
- Benchmark results/papers -> covered by benchmarks-academic entity
- This group only focuses on: KOL commentary, trend analysis, independent reviews, community discussions initiated by KOLs
