# Structured Question Templates

This file contains the question templates used by the Leap of Faith skill at each step. These questions are designed to effectively surface the user's tacit knowledge.

## Scene Recognition Questions

> "What's the hardest part about this decision for you? Is it that you don't have enough information, or that you already know the answer but haven't dared to act on it?"

> "If you had to describe your current state in one word, which would you choose: confused, afraid, excited, or something else?"

> "Does this decision primarily affect your career, your life direction, your relationship with someone, or your understanding of yourself?"

## Three-Layer Analysis Questions

### Known Layer
> "What are the facts you know for certain about this decision? List them without evaluating."

> "What research or validation have you done so far? What were the results?"

> "Is there a similar experience you can reference? Your own or someone you know?"

### Unknown Layer
> "What information, if you had it, would make this decision easy?"

> "What information do you feel you're missing? Is it possible to obtain? How long would it take?"

> "Is there a quick, small experiment you could run to test your assumptions?"

### Unknowable Layer
> "Even if you did every possible investigation, what would remain impossible to know in advance?"

> "What percentage of this decision is within your control? What percentage is pure chance?"

> "Can you accept the fact that you might be wrong? Or are you waiting for certainty that will never come?"

## Tacit Knowledge Excavation Questions

### Body Signals
> "Close your eyes and imagine you've already made this decision. What does your body feel? Are your shoulders relaxed or tense? Is your stomach settled or knotted?"

> "Have you lost sleep or had dreams about this decision recently? What was the dream about?"

> "When you talk about this with others, does your voice get louder or quieter?"

### Inner Direction
> "Do you feel like the answer is already inside you? Maybe you just haven't dared to say it out loud?"

> "If you didn't have to consider anyone else's opinion — parents, spouse, friends, investors — what would you choose?"

> "What scares you more: making the wrong choice, or making the right choice and facing what comes after?"

### Trust Projection
> "If your most trusted friend were facing the same situation, what would you advise them?"

> "If your role model faced this decision, what do you think they'd do?"

> "If your child (or future child) grew up and asked you why you made this choice, what would you say?"

### Time Lens
> "Five years from now, looking back at today — will you regret doing this, or regret not doing it?"

> "If you do nothing, will things be better or worse a year from now?"

> "Are you waiting for a 'better time,' or using 'bad timing' as an excuse to avoid the decision?"

### Reversal Test
> "If someone told you right now 'you can't do this' — is your first reaction relief or defiance?"

> "If this opportunity disappeared, would you feel regret? On a scale of 1–10, how much?"

> "If you'd already made this decision but could take it back, would you?"

## Leap Point Confirmation Questions

> "After everything we've analyzed, does the answer feel clearer? Or more tangled?"

> "If it's more tangled, do you think it's because you lack information, or because you're avoiding something?"

> "Are you ready to leap? Or do you need to do something first?"

> "If you decide to leap, what's the very first action you need to take?"
