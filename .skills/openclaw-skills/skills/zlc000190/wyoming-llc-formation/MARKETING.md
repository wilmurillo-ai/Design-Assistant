# Wyoming LLC Formation - Complete Guide

## Why Choose Wyoming for Your LLC?

Wyoming has become the **#1 choice for privacy-focused entrepreneurs** and small business owners. Here's why:

### 🔒 **Maximum Privacy Protection**
- No public disclosure of member names
- Anonymous LLC ownership allowed
- Strong asset protection laws

### 💰 **Lowest Formation Costs**
- **$100** filing fee (vs $300+ in other states)
- **$60/year** annual report fee
- No franchise tax
- No state income tax

### ⚡ **Fast Processing**
- 5-7 business days standard processing
- Same-day expedited service available

### 🏛️ **Business-Friendly Laws**
- Flexible operating agreement requirements
- Single-member LLCs allowed
- Series LLC structure available

## Formation Process Made Simple

### Step 1: Choose Your LLC Name
Your business name must:
- Include "LLC" or "Limited Liability Company"
- Be distinguishable from existing Wyoming businesses
- Not include restricted words without approval

**Need help?** Try our [AI-Powered LLC Name Generator](https://github.com/zlc000190/llc-name-generator)

### Step 2: Appoint a Registered Agent
Required: A Wyoming registered agent with a physical address in the state.

**Cost**: $50-150/year for professional service

### Step 3: File Articles of Organization
Submit to Wyoming Secretary of State:
- Business name and address
- Registered agent information
- Management structure
- Effective date

**Filing Fee**: $100

### Step 4: Create Operating Agreement
While not required by Wyoming law, an operating agreement:
- Defines ownership structure
- Establishes management rules
- Protects limited liability status

### Step 5: Obtain EIN
Free from IRS - required for:
- Opening business bank accounts
- Hiring employees
- Filing taxes

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to live in Wyoming to form an LLC there?
**No.** You can form a Wyoming LLC from anywhere in the world. You only need a registered agent with a Wyoming address.

### Can I be my own registered agent?
**Yes**, but you must have a physical address in Wyoming (not a P.O. Box). Most out-of-state owners use a professional registered agent service.

### How long does formation take?
**5-7 business days** for standard processing. Expedited same-day service is available for an additional fee.

### What's the difference between Wyoming and Delaware?
**Wyoming** is better for:
- Privacy (no member disclosure)
- Lower costs ($60/year vs $300/year)
- Asset protection

**Delaware** is better for:
- Venture capital funding
- Complex corporate structures
- Established case law

### Do I need a business license?
Depends on your business type and location. Wyoming doesn't require a general business license, but specific industries may need permits.
