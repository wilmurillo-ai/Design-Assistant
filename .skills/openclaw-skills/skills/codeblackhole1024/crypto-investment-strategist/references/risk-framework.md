# Risk Framework

## Non-negotiables

- Protect capital first
- Avoid all-in deployment
- Avoid oversized leverage
- Do not confuse luck with edge

## Risk questions

Before recommending action, check:
- What is the downside if invalidated?
- Is the stop level logical or arbitrary?
- Is the position too large for the volatility?
- Is this setup tradable, or just interesting?

## Risk labels

- **Low**: clear trend, clear invalidation, modest volatility
- **Medium**: acceptable setup with some uncertainty
- **High**: elevated volatility, weak structure, or crowded positioning
- **Extreme**: leverage-sensitive, event-driven, or structurally unclear

## Capital protection actions

When risk is too high, prefer:
- Wait
- Reduce size
- Move to stables
- Tighten exposure
- Skip leverage entirely
