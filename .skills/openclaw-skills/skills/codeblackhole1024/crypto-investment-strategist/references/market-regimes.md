# Market Regimes

Use this reference when the user needs context beyond a single chart.

## Core regimes

1. **Uptrend**
   - Higher highs and higher lows
   - Pullbacks often become buy opportunities
   - Prefer buying support over chasing vertical candles

2. **Downtrend**
   - Lower highs and lower lows
   - Capital preservation is more important than aggressive entries
   - Treat rebounds with suspicion until structure improves

3. **Range**
   - Price rotates between support and resistance
   - Buy near support, reduce near resistance
   - Avoid breakout chasing without confirmation

4. **Event-driven volatility**
   - News, ETF headlines, macro releases, exchange incidents
   - Lower confidence and reduce position size

5. **Risk-off regime**
   - BTC weak, altcoins weaker, liquidity exits fast
   - Increase stablecoin reserve
   - Favor patience over forced action

## Regime questions

- Is BTC leading or lagging?
- Are altcoins outperforming or underperforming BTC?
- Is price extended from major moving averages?
- Is the current move trend continuation or short squeeze?
- Does current volatility allow a rational stop placement?
