# Allocation Playbook

Use this reference when the user wants a concrete capital deployment plan.

## Inputs to consider

- Total capital
- Risk tolerance
- Market regime
- Existing holdings
- Desired time horizon

## Default split logic

### Low risk
- Higher BTC and stablecoin weight
- Smaller altcoin sleeve

### Medium risk
- Balanced BTC, ETH, altcoins, and stable reserve

### High risk
- Larger altcoin sleeve, but still keep a stable reserve

## Deployment style

- **Stage 1**: starter entry
- **Stage 2**: add on confirmation or pullback
- **Stage 3**: reserve for opportunity or defense

## Avoid

- All-in entries at resistance
- Zero stablecoin reserve in unstable regimes
- Oversized altcoin concentration without a clear thesis
