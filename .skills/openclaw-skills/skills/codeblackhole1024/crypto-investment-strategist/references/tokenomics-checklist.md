# Tokenomics Checklist

Use this reference when evaluating altcoins beyond chart structure.

## Checklist

- What is the circulating supply versus fully diluted valuation?
- Are major token unlocks approaching?
- Is insider or treasury sell pressure likely?
- Does the token have real demand, or only narrative demand?
- Are fees, usage, or TVL supporting the valuation?
- Is the coin liquid enough for the user's size?

## Red flags

- Very high FDV with low circulating supply
- Large unlock schedule near current price strength
- Weak real usage despite strong social hype
- Thin liquidity that makes exits difficult

## Practical use

If tokenomics are weak, reduce conviction even if the chart looks good.
