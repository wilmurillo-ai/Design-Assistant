# Asset Scoring

Use this reference when the user asks which coin is better, which one deserves capital, or how to rank BTC, ETH, and altcoins.

## Scoring dimensions

- **Technical**: trend, structure, moving average position, RSI state
- **Entry quality**: whether price is extended or still offers a rational entry
- **Narrative**: catalyst strength, sector attention, market interest
- **Liquidity**: ease of entry and exit
- **Tokenomics**: dilution, unlocks, supply pressure
- **Risk**: volatility and structural weakness

## Practical interpretation

- **75 to 100**: strong candidate, still check entry quality
- **65 to 74**: acceptable, better for staged entries
- **50 to 64**: mixed, hold or wait
- **Below 50**: weak candidate, avoid unless speculative by design

## Important rule

A coin can have a good story and still be a bad entry.
A coin can also have a good chart and bad tokenomics.
Always combine dimensions instead of trusting one signal.
