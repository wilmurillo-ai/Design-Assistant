# Audio Modes — 9 Reference Templates

These modes are **reference templates**, not rigid categories. Use when the user's request naturally fits one, or borrow elements from multiple modes. If none fits, create a custom audio profile (see SKILL.md Custom Mode section).

**Scene Classification vs. Audio Modes:** Scene Classification (Step 1 in SKILL.md) determines *how to collect personalized context*. Audio Modes determine *how to structure the final audio*. The two are independent — blend freely based on the user's actual need.

When the user explicitly requests a mode, use it. When they don't, prioritize their actual need over mode matching.

---

### 1. Soul Healing Mode
**Duration:** 10-20 min | **Trigger:** Emotional distress, burnout, anxiety, sadness — "好累", "好焦虑", "需要安慰", "压力好大", "想哭", "emo了", or difficult emotional experiences.
**Suggestion:** "Sounds like you could use some gentle care — want me to create a healing audio just for you?"

### 2. Daily Briefing Mode
**Duration:** 3-5 min | **Trigger:** News, trending topics, industry updates — "what's happening today", "summarize today's headlines".
**Suggestion:** "Want me to turn this into an audio briefing? Perfect for your commute."

### 3. Knowledge Deep Dive Mode
**Duration:** 10-15 min | **Trigger:** Concept/technology questions — "explain X to me", "what is X", "how does X work".
**Suggestion:** "This is a rich topic — want me to generate an in-depth audio explainer?"

### 4. Content Digest Mode
**Duration:** 5-15 min | **Trigger:** Long text, local files (PDF/docs/notes) — "summarize this", "too long to read", "key points".
**Suggestion:** "That's a lot of content — want me to compress it into an audio digest?"

### 5. Bedtime Radio Mode
**Duration:** 15-25 min | **Trigger:** "bedtime", "relax", "can't sleep", "wind down", "meditation", "冥想", "正念".
**Suggestion:** "Want me to generate a bedtime audio? Could be a relaxing story, gentle science talk, or guided meditation."

### 6. Language Learning Mode
**Duration:** 10-20 min | **Trigger:** Vocabulary, grammar, pronunciation — "how do you say X in Y", "teach me X language", "help me practice".
**Suggestion:** "Want me to generate an audio lesson for this? Great for practicing listening on the go."

### 7. Conversation Extension Mode
**Duration:** 10-20 min | **Trigger:** Deep ongoing conversation with rich content worth preserving.
**Suggestion:** "We just covered a lot of ground — want me to package it into a complete audio piece you can revisit?"

### 8. Topic Tracker Mode
**Duration:** 5-10 min | **Trigger:** Following a topic over time — "track X for me", "any updates on X", "what's new with X".
**Suggestion:** "Want me to generate an audio update on the latest developments?"

### 9. Study Buddy Mode
**Duration:** 10-20 min | **Trigger:** Studying, reviewing — "exam", "memorize", "can't remember", "help me review".
**Suggestion:** "Want me to turn these key points into audio? Listen on repeat — it sticks better."
