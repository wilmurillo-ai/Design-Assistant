# 示例

## 示例 1：牙槽窝缺损修复支架图

### 图意

展示一体化多层 RSF 支架如何从植入早期封闭创口并促进血管生成，最终引导新骨形成和牙槽骨再生。

### 分镜建议

- `A`：拔牙后牙槽窝缺损，显示牙龈、牙槽骨、血凝块
- `B`：支架爆炸或单独展示，说明上层、中间屏障层、下层放射状通道
- `C`：植入后早期牙龈封闭、血管生成、下方新骨形成

### 英文 prompt

```text
Create a clean publication-quality biomedical schematic illustration for a journal figure about alveolar socket defect repair using an integrated RSF scaffold. Use a 3-panel horizontal layout labeled A, B, and C on a white background.

Panel A: cross-sectional view of an extraction socket defect with gingiva, alveolar bone, and a blood clot in the socket.
Panel B: enlarged view of a pushpin-shaped integrated RSF scaffold, clearly showing an upper layer, a middle barrier layer, and a lower layer with radial channels.
Panel C: implanted scaffold in the socket, with early gingival sealing and angiogenesis in the upper region, and later osteogenesis and new bone formation in the lower region.

Use a high-end medical illustration style with vector-like clarity, soft pastel pink for gingiva, bone beige for alveolar bone, light cyan-blue for the scaffold, teal for new bone, and muted red for blood vessels. Keep labels external with thin leader lines, use subtle arrows, maintain consistent anatomy across panels, and make the figure elegant and suitable for a biomaterials paper.
```

### 负面约束

```text
Avoid photorealism, gore, surgical instruments, dramatic lighting, glossy 3D rendering, cluttered labels, dark background, oversaturated colors, comic-book style, and anatomically inconsistent teeth or bone.
```

## 示例 2：可迁移到其他医学材料主题

把上面的结构替换成下面这个骨架即可：

```text
Create a publication-quality biomedical schematic illustration for a journal figure.
Topic: [material system and target tissue].
Use a [2-4]-panel horizontal layout with panel letters.
Show: [baseline defect or disease], [material architecture], [early response], [late regeneration].
Style: vector-like medical illustration, soft pastel colors, subtle gradients, precise outlines, white background, minimal clutter, external labels, scientific elegance.
Avoid: photorealism, glossy 3D render look, busy background, excessive text, messy arrows, oversaturated colors.
```
