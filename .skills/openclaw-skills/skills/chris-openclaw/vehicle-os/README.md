# Vehicle OS

A conversational OpenClaw skill that tracks everything about your vehicles: service history, maintenance schedules, mechanics, registration, insurance, and more. Built-in maintenance knowledge for cars, trucks, motorcycles, boats, and RVs with both mileage and time-based reminders. Supports multiple vehicles.

## What It Does

- **Vehicle Profiles** -- year, make, model, mileage, nickname, and notes for every vehicle you own
- **Service History** -- every oil change, repair, inspection, and DIY job with dates, mileage, costs, and mechanic info
- **Smart Maintenance Schedules** -- built-in knowledge for cars, trucks, motorcycles, boats, and RVs. Auto-suggests intervals when you add a vehicle.
- **Mechanic Directory** -- shop names, specialties, contact info, and quality notes. Reusable across vehicles.
- **Admin Tracking** -- registration expiration, insurance renewal, inspection/emissions due dates
- **Cost Tracking** -- per-vehicle spending, per-mechanic totals, cost-per-mile, and category breakdowns
- **Proactive Reminders** -- nudges for overdue maintenance, upcoming registration, and mechanic follow-ups

## Example Usage

**Add a vehicle:**
> "I have a 2019 Subaru Outback. About 67,000 miles."

**Log service:**
> "Got the oil changed today at Main Street Auto. $85."

**Check what's due:**
> "What maintenance is coming up?"

**Admin check:**
> "When does my registration expire?"

**Cost check:**
> "How much have I spent on the Subaru this year?"

## Installation

Copy the `vehicle-os` folder into your OpenClaw skills directory and restart your agent.
