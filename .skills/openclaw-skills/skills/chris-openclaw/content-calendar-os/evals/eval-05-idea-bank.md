# Eval 05: Capture and Promote Ideas

## Input (two messages)

**Message 1:** "Oh, idea: what if I interviewed a pastor who uses AI for sermon prep?"

**Message 2 (later):** "Let's schedule that pastor interview idea as a blog post for next Thursday."

## Expected Behavior

**After Message 1:**
- Adds to idea bank with description, suggested theme, potential channels
- Confirms it's saved

**After Message 2:**
- Promotes the idea from the bank to the calendar
- Creates a content entry: blog post, next Thursday, status: planned
- Links it back to the original idea
- Asks about outline or angle

## What to Watch For
- Does the idea-to-calendar promotion work smoothly?
- Does it preserve the context from the original idea?
- Does it suggest next steps for the now-scheduled piece?
