# Eval 01: Weekly Content Brainstorm

## Setup Context
Channels: Instagram (3x/week), blog (1x/week), newsletter (weekly). Theme: "AI in ministry."

## Input
"I need content ideas for next week."

## Expected Behavior
1. Generates ideas for each channel based on frequency
2. Ideas connect to established themes
3. Each idea has a brief rationale or angle
4. Suggests repurposing where natural (blog post becomes newsletter content)
5. Offers to draft any of them

## What to Watch For
- Does it respect posting frequency (3 IG, 1 blog, 1 newsletter)?
- Are ideas specific, not generic?
- Does it suggest cross-channel repurposing?
