# Eval 04: Weekly Calendar View

## Setup Context
5 content pieces scheduled for the week: 3 IG posts (1 drafted, 1 planned, 1 idea), 1 blog (scheduled), 1 newsletter (drafting).

## Input
"What's on the calendar this week?"

## Expected Behavior
1. Shows a table or structured view with day, channel, title, and status
2. Highlights pieces that need work (still in idea or planned status)
3. Shows overall readiness (e.g., "2 ready, 1 drafting, 2 need work")
4. Offers to help with the pieces that aren't ready

## What to Watch For
- Is the format scannable (table-like)?
- Does it flag what needs attention?
- Does it show readiness at a glance?
