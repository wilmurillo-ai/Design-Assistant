# Content Calendar

A conversational OpenClaw skill for planning, creating, and tracking content across any platform. Manages the full pipeline from idea to published, brainstorms topics, drafts posts, and repurposes content across channels.

## What It Does

- **Content Pipeline** -- track every piece from idea to published across all your channels
- **Channel Management** -- define your platforms (blog, Instagram, LinkedIn, newsletter, etc.) with posting frequency goals
- **Idea Bank** -- capture ideas on the fly and promote them to the calendar when ready
- **Content Creation** -- brainstorm topics, draft posts and captions, repurpose pieces across platforms
- **Calendar Views** -- weekly and monthly views showing what's planned, drafted, and published
- **Theme Tracking** -- organize content by recurring themes or campaigns
- **Performance Notes** -- log quick observations on what worked and what didn't
- **Proactive Nudges** -- flags scheduling gaps and undrafted content approaching its publish date

## Example Usage

**Plan content:**
> "I need content ideas for next week."

**Schedule a post:**
> "Schedule a blog post for Wednesday about AI tools."

**Draft content:**
> "Draft the Instagram caption for that carousel."

**Repurpose:**
> "That blog post did well. Can we repurpose it?"

**Check the calendar:**
> "What's on the content calendar this week?"

**Capture an idea:**
> "Idea: interview a pastor who uses AI for sermon prep."

## Installation

Copy the `content-calendar` folder into your OpenClaw skills directory and restart your agent.
