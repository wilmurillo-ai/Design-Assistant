# Eval 09: Research Note Tracking

## Input
"I did some research on lobster fishing in Maine for the setting. The season runs June through December, traps need to be tagged, and there's an informal territory system among lobstermen. I'll reference this in chapter 3 and probably chapter 7."

## Expected Behavior
1. Creates a research note: topic (lobster fishing in Maine), key findings (season, tagging, territories), linked to chapters 3 and 7
2. Confirms what was logged
3. Notes that it will flag if any future chapter details contradict this research
4. Writes to book-data.json

## What to Watch For
- Does it capture all the details from a natural language message?
- Does it link the research to specific chapters?
- Does it mention the consistency benefit (will flag contradictions)?
