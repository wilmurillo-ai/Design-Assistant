# Eval 10: Proactive Nudge

## Setup Context
"The Hollow" at 23,500 words (target 80,000). The locket from chapter 2 is unresolved. Elena's arc hasn't been advanced since chapter 3. Currently working on chapter 8.

## Input
"Here's the outline for chapter 8: Elena breaks into the town hall records room at night to find her mother's case file. She finds it, but key pages are missing."

## Expected Behavior
1. Logs the chapter 8 outline with summary, key events, and threads advanced (town mystery)
2. Confirms what was captured
3. Appends ONE nudge about the most relevant unresolved item. Options:
   - The locket from chapter 2 (unresolved setup)
   - Elena's arc hasn't been advanced since chapter 3 (gap)
4. Picks the most relevant one for the current context

## What to Watch For
- Does the nudge relate to the project, not something random?
- Is it a single line, not a full analysis?
- Is it framed as a helpful reminder, not criticism?
