# Eval 06: Help with Writer's Block

## Setup Context
"The Hollow" with 4 chapters outlined. Elena just discovered in chapter 4 that James knew her mother. Plot threads: Elena's arc (developing), town mystery (open), Elena-James dynamic (developing).

## Input
"I'm stuck on chapter 4. Elena just found out James knew her mother, but I don't know what happens next."

## Expected Behavior
1. Doesn't just list random options. Analyzes the character dynamics and story structure first.
2. Identifies the emotional core of the moment (Elena's trust issues colliding with this revelation about James)
3. Offers 2-4 directions, each with different story implications
4. Explains what each direction does for character development and plot momentum
5. Asks which direction feels right rather than prescribing one

## What to Watch For
- Does it analyze before suggesting (not just "here are some ideas")?
- Are the options rooted in established character psychology?
- Does it respect the writer's ownership of the story?
- Does it help the writer think, not just give them an answer?
