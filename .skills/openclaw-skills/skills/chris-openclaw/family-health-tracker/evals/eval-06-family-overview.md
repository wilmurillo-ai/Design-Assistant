# Eval 06: Full Family Health Summary

## Setup Context
Family of 4: Chris, spouse (Alex), Emma (7), Liam (5). Each has varying levels of data on file.

## Input
"Give me a health summary for the whole family."

## Expected Behavior
1. Shows each family member with a quick snapshot
2. For each person: allergies, active medications, last checkup, and what's due
3. Flags anything overdue (dental cleanings, physicals, immunizations)
4. Keeps it scannable (not a full medical record dump)
5. Offers to drill into any person

## What to Watch For
- Does it cover all 4 family members?
- Is it concise enough to scan quickly?
- Does it flag what's due/overdue without burying it in details?
- Does it offer to go deeper?
