# Default Creator Screening Framework

Generic quality screening for any brand partnership evaluation.

## Scoring Dimensions

### 1. Production Quality (Weight: 30%)
- **Lighting**: Studio/ring light vs natural/uncontrolled
- **Resolution**: 1080p+ vs sub-HD
- **Framing**: Centered, stable vs shaky, off-center
- **Environment**: Clean, intentional background vs cluttered/messy
- **Color**: Balanced color grading vs oversaturated/dull

Score: 1-5 (1=amateur, 5=broadcast quality)

### 2. Audio Quality (Weight: 20%)
- **Clarity**: External mic vs phone mic
- **Consistency**: Even volume vs fluctuating
- **Background**: Clean vs noisy/echo
- **Post-processing**: Professional vs raw

Score: 1-5

### 3. Content & Delivery (Weight: 25%)
- **Structure**: Organized, clear flow vs rambling
- **Confidence**: Composed vs nervous/fumbling
- **Vocabulary**: Strong, appropriate vs limited
- **Originality**: Original content vs trend copies
- **Value**: Educational/entertaining vs filler

Score: 1-5

### 4. Brand Safety (Weight: 15%)
- **Tone**: Professional vs controversial
- **Language**: Clean vs explicit
- **Topics**: Brand-safe vs risky
- **Associations**: Reputable vs questionable

Score: 1-5

### 5. Engagement & Reach (Weight: 10%)
- **Views-to-followers ratio**: >10% good, >50% great
- **Engagement rate**: >1% good, >3% great
- **Comment quality**: Real discussion vs spam/bots
- **Growth trend**: Rising vs stagnant vs declining

Score: 1-5

## Verdict Thresholds

- **APPROVED**: Weighted score >= 3.5/5
- **CONDITIONAL**: Weighted score 2.5-3.4
- **REJECTED**: Weighted score < 2.5
