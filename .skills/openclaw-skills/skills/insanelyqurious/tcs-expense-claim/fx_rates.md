## Notes

- Rates are approximate and should be verified for the actual travel dates
- TCS finance may apply their own standard monthly rate — check with your
  finance team if the converted amount differs significantly
- For INR bills claimed in India: no conversion needed
- For USD bills claimed from India: convert at the rate on the bill date
- EUR bills at Amsterdam Schiphol: use EUR→USD or EUR→INR as appropriate
- Always note: "Converted at ₹XX/USD (approx. [Month] [Year] rate)"
