# 广告文案模板和示例

本文档提供不同风格的广告文案模板，帮助生成高质量的产品广告。

## 文案风格指南

### 1. 高端奢华风 (Luxury & Premium)

适用于：高端产品、奢侈品、精品、艺术品

**核心特征**：
- 简约、克制、意境深远
- 使用高级感词汇：匠心、臻选、典藏、至臻
- 强调品质、工艺、传承

**Slogan 模板**：
```
[品牌/产品] ｜ [核心价值]
例：
- 时间的艺术，指尖的奢华
- 匠心独运，为你而生
- 不止于美，更在于臻
- 至简之美，至臻之选
```

**解说词模板**：
```
每一个细节，都是时间的馈赠。
[产品名]，以[核心工艺/材质]诠释[品质/格调]。
不随波逐流，只为懂得欣赏的你。

例：
"每一道光泽，都是时间的馈赠。以瑞士顶级机芯诠释时间的艺术，为追求极致的你而来。"
```

**视频提示词模板**：
```
The product elegantly rotates on a pure white background with professional studio lighting.
Soft shadows create depth. Camera slowly dollies in, highlighting premium textures and details.
Cinematic quality, luxury advertising aesthetic, warm tones, 1080p, 24fps.
```

---

### 2. 科技未来风 (Tech & Innovation)

适用于：科技产品、电子设备、智能硬件、软件服务

**核心特征**：
- 简洁、未来感、数据驱动
- 使用科技词汇：智能、创新、突破、革新
- 强调功能、效率、前沿科技

**Slogan 模板**：
```
[动词] + [未来/可能性]
例：
- 让未来，触手可及
- 智能重塑，生活升级
- 科技无界，可能无限
- 创新驱动，智见未来
```

**解说词模板**：
```
打破边界，重新定义[场景/体验]。
[产品名]搭载[核心技术]，
让[功能/体验]变得前所未有的[形容词]。

例：
"打破边界，重新定义智能家居。搭载最新AI语音系统，让每一次对话都精准响应。"
```

**视频提示词模板**：
```
The product emerges from darkness with blue-white tech lighting accents.
Holographic UI elements appear around it. Camera orbits slowly revealing sleek surfaces.
Futuristic style, clean lines, neon reflections, 1080p, smooth motion.
```

---

### 3. 自然清新风 (Natural & Fresh)

适用于：食品、护肤品、健康产品、家居用品

**核心特征**：
- 温暖、亲切、治愈感
- 使用自然词汇：纯净、天然、新鲜、呵护
- 强调健康、安心、生活品质

**Slogan 模板**：
```
[自然元素] + [生活/情感]
例：
- 源于自然，呵护每一天
- 纯净如初，温柔以待
- 取自天然，给你最好
- 自然的力量，生活的美好
```

**解说词模板**：
```
每一份[产品]，都来自[自然/产地]的精心馈赠。
不添加[负面元素]，只为给你[正面价值]。
[产品名]，让[场景]更加[形容词]。

例：
"每一滴精华，都来自高山雪域的精心馈赠。不添加防腐剂，只为给你最纯净的呵护。"
```

**视频提示词模板**：
```
The product sits on a natural wooden surface with soft morning light streaming in.
Gentle camera movement, shallow depth of field, natural elements like leaves or flowers.
Warm tones, cozy atmosphere, organic feel, 1080p, gentle transitions.
```

## 品牌保护视频提示词模板

### 高端品牌广告（强调品牌保护）

```
Ultra-premium product advertisement featuring [产品名] by [品牌名].

The product stands majestically on pure white background.
Professional studio lighting creates elegant highlights and subtle shadows.
Camera performs a slow, cinematic dolly-in revealing exquisite details.

BRAND REQUIREMENTS:
- The [品牌名] LOGO at [位置] must remain crystal clear throughout
- Brand colors [色值] must be perfectly preserved
- Premium, luxury aesthetic aligned with brand identity

CONSISTENCY REQUIREMENTS:
- Product shape: UNCHANGING throughout
- Product size: CONSISTENT (natural perspective only)
- LOGO clarity: SHARP in every frame
- Colors: EXACT match throughout
- Lighting: UNIFORM, professional studio

Motion: Elegant, refined, befitting a luxury brand
Quality: Cinematic, 1080p, smooth 24fps
```

### 科技产品广告（品牌+科技感）

```
Cutting-edge technology product showcase for [产品名].

The device emerges with subtle tech-inspired lighting.
Clean lines and surfaces catch professional studio lights.
Camera smoothly orbits, revealing innovative design details.

BRAND REQUIREMENTS:
- [品牌名] LOGO must be clearly visible and sharp
- Tech-forward aesthetic while protecting brand identity
- Product design integrity is paramount

CONSISTENCY REQUIREMENTS:
- Device shape: RIGID, no warping or morphing
- Display/screens: Consistent brightness and content
- Material finishes: Consistent reflections
- LOGO: Clear and legible throughout

Motion: Smooth, precise, technological
Quality: Crisp, modern, 1080p
```

### 生活美学产品（品牌+温馨感）

```
Lifestyle product advertisement for [产品名] by [品牌名].

The product rests on clean white surface with warm, inviting lighting.
Soft shadows create depth and approachability.
Camera gently approaches, emphasizing natural beauty and quality.

BRAND REQUIREMENTS:
- [品牌名] branding visible and clear
- Warm, authentic aesthetic
- Product authenticity is key

CONSISTENCY REQUIREMENTS:
- Product appearance: Natural and consistent
- Colors: True to life, no artificial shifts
- Textures: Authentic material representation
- LOGO: Naturally integrated but readable

Motion: Gentle, organic, inviting
Quality: Warm, authentic, 1080p
```

## 品牌一致性关键词速查

### 产品一致性关键词

| 英文关键词 | 用途 | 示例用法 |
|-----------|------|---------|
| `EXACT shape` | 精确形状 | Product MUST maintain EXACT shape |
| `no morphing` | 禁止变形 | No morphing, warping, or deformation |
| `consistent proportions` | 一致比例 | Maintain consistent proportions throughout |
| `rigid` | 刚性/不变形 | Product must remain rigid |
| `identical` | 完全相同 | Look identical from first to last frame |

### LOGO 保护关键词

| 英文关键词 | 用途 | 示例用法 |
|-----------|------|---------|
| `pixel-perfect` | 像素级完美 | LOGO must be pixel-perfect sharp |
| `undistorted` | 无变形 | LOGO remains undistorted throughout |
| `legible` | 可读的 | All text must remain legible |
| `crisp` | 清晰的 | Keep LOGO crisp and clear |
| `visible` | 可见的 | Brand LOGO clearly visible in every frame |

### 颜色一致性关键词

| 英文关键词 | 用途 | 示例用法 |
|-----------|------|---------|
| `color accuracy` | 颜色准确 | Maintain color accuracy throughout |
| `no color shift` | 无色偏 | Zero tolerance for color shift |
| `exact match` | 精确匹配 | Brand colors must be exact match |
| `consistent` | 一致的 | Colors must remain consistent |
| `preserve` | 保留 | Preserve original brand colors |

### 场景一致性关键词

| 英文关键词 | 用途 | 示例用法 |
|-----------|------|---------|
| `uniform lighting` | 统一灯光 | Uniform lighting throughout |
| `consistent background` | 一致背景 | Background must stay consistent |
| `no flickering` | 无闪烁 | No lighting flickering |
| `steady` | 稳定的 | Steady, smooth motion |
| `continuous` | 连续的 | Single continuous shot feel |
