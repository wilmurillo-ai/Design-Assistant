## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Team Resistance
**Mitigation:**
- Start with volunteers only
- Share early wins publicly
- Provide extensive training
- Celebrate successes

### Risk 2: Tool Costs
**Mitigation:**
- Start with pilot team (lower cost)
- Track ROI weekly
- Scale only when ROI proven
- Negotiate volume discounts

### Risk 3: Quality Concerns
**Mitigation:**
- Human review required for all AI output
- Set up quality metrics
- Regular team feedback sessions
- Iterate based on issues

### Risk 4: Over-Reliance on AI
**Mitigation:**
- Maintain manual processes as backup
- Regular "AI-free" exercises
- Focus on augmentation, not replacement
- Keep humans in decision loop
