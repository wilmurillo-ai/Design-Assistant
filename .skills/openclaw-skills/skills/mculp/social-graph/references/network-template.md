# Social Network Graph Template

Copy this file to `social-graph/network.md` and fill it in for your own relationships. Replace the example entries with real people you interact with. Each entry captures not just who someone is, but how to be around them — what to share, what to hold back, and when.

### When You Meet Someone New

1. Start conservative — listen more than share
2. Note what topics they respond to
3. Pay attention to what makes them uncomfortable
4. Build trust before opening up
5. Add them here and update as the relationship develops
