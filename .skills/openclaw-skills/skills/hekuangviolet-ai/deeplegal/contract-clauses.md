# Standard Contract Clauses Reference / 标准合同条款参考

## Definitions Clause / 定义条款

### English
```
"Affiliate" means, with respect to any Person, any other Person that directly or indirectly Controls, is Controlled by, or is under common Control with such Person. "Control" means the possession, directly or indirectly, of the power to direct or cause the direction of the management and policies of a Person, whether through the ownership of voting securities, by contract, or otherwise.

"Confidential Information" means all non-public information disclosed by one Party to the other Party, whether orally, in writing, or by inspection, that is designated as confidential or that reasonably should be understood to be confidential given the nature of the information and the circumstances of disclosure.

"Intellectual Property" means all patents, trademarks, service marks, trade names, copyrights, trade secrets, know-how, inventions, software, and other intellectual property rights, whether registered or unregistered.

"Trade Secrets" means information that: (a) is not generally known to or readily ascertainable by persons who can obtain economic value from its disclosure or use; (b) has actual or potential economic value; and (c) is subject to reasonable measures to maintain its secrecy.
```

### 中文
```
"关联方"是指直接或间接控制一方、受一方控制、或与一方共同受控制的任何实体。"控制"是指通过持有有表决权的证券、合同安排或其他方式，直接或间接拥有指导或决定一方管理和政策的权力。

"保密信息"是指一方向另一方披露的所有非公开信息，无论以口头、书面或其他方式披露，且该等信息被标记为保密信息或根据信息的性质和披露的情形合理地应被理解为保密信息。

"知识产权"是指所有专利权、商标权、服务标识、商号、著作权、商业秘密、技术诀窍、发明、软件及其他知识产权，无论是否已注册。

"商业秘密"是指符合以下条件的信息：(a) 不为公众所知悉或不能轻易获取；(b) 具有实际或潜在的经济价值；(c) 权利人已采取合理保密措施。（参照《反不正当竞争法》第九条）
```

### Bilingual (中英对照)
```

## Limitation of Liability / 责任限制

### English (Balanced)
```
EXCEPT FOR A PARTY'S INDEMNIFICATION OBLIGATIONS, BREACH OF CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS, INFRINGEMENT OF INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS, OR WILLFUL MISCONDUCT:

(a) IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE TO THE OTHER PARTY FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES, INCLUDING LOSS OF PROFITS, REVENUE, DATA, OR BUSINESS OPPORTUNITY, REGARDLESS OF THE THEORY OF LIABILITY.

(b) EACH PARTY'S TOTAL AGGREGATE LIABILITY UNDER THIS AGREEMENT SHALL NOT EXCEED THE TOTAL FEES PAID OR PAYABLE BY [CLIENT] TO [PROVIDER] DURING THE TWELVE (12) MONTH PERIOD IMMEDIATELY PRECEDING THE EVENT GIVING RISE TO THE CLAIM.
```

### 中文
```
除因一方故意或重大过失、违反保密义务、侵犯知识产权或违反反不正当竞争义务所导致的责任外：

(a) 任何一方均不对另一方承担任何间接损失、附带损失、特殊损失、后果性损失或惩罚性赔偿责任，包括但不限于利润损失、收入损失、数据损失或商业机会损失。

(b) 各方在本协议项下的累计赔偿总额不超过引起索赔的事件发生前十二(12)个月内[委托方]已向[服务方]支付或应支付的费用总额。
```

## Confidentiality / 保密条款

### English
```
1. Each Party agrees to hold in strict confidence and not to disclose to any third party any Confidential Information of the other Party without the prior written consent of the disclosing Party.

2. The receiving Party shall use the Confidential Information solely for the purpose of performing its obligations and exercising its rights under this Agreement.

3. The obligations of confidentiality shall not apply to information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the receiving Party; (b) was known to the receiving Party prior to disclosure, as evidenced by written records; (c) is independently developed by the receiving Party without use of or reference to the Confidential Information, as evidenced by written records; or (d) is rightfully obtained from a third party without restriction on disclosure.

4. The receiving Party may disclose Confidential Information to the extent required by applicable law, regulation, or legal process, provided that the receiving Party shall: (a) give the disclosing Party prompt written notice of such requirement (to the extent legally permissible); and (b) cooperate with the disclosing Party's efforts to obtain protective treatment for such information.

5. The obligations under this Section shall survive for a period of [three (3)] years from the date of disclosure, provided that obligations with respect to trade secrets shall survive for so long as such information remains a trade secret under applicable law.
```

### 中文（含商业秘密保护强化条款）
```
1. 各方同意对另一方的保密信息严格保密，未经披露方事先书面同意，不得向任何第三方披露。

2. 接收方仅可为履行本协议项下义务和行使本协议项下权利之目的使用保密信息。

3. 保密义务不适用于以下信息：(a) 非因接收方过错而成为公开信息的；(b) 接收方在接收前已合法持有的（以书面记录为证）；(c) 接收方独立开发且未使用或参考保密信息的（以书面记录为证）；(d) 接收方从有权披露的第三方合法获得的。

4. 接收方在适用法律、法规或法律程序要求的范围内可以披露保密信息，但接收方应：(a) 在法律允许的范围内及时书面通知披露方；(b) 配合披露方为该等信息寻求保护性措施的努力。

5. 各方应采取与保护自身同等重要保密信息相同的措施（且不低于合理审慎标准），包括但不限于限制知悉范围、签署保密协议、物理和电子安全措施等，以满足《反不正当竞争法》第九条项下"相应保密措施"的要求。

6. 本条项下的保密义务自信息披露之日起[三(3)]年内有效；但对于构成商业秘密的信息，保密义务持续至该等信息不再构成适用法律项下的商业秘密时止。
```

## Indemnification / 赔偿与补偿

### English (Mutual)
```
1. Each Party ("Indemnifying Party") shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the other Party and its Affiliates, and their respective officers, directors, employees, and agents ("Indemnified Parties") from and against any and all losses, damages, liabilities, costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising out of or relating to:
   (a) any breach of the Indemnifying Party's representations, warranties, or obligations under this Agreement;
   (b) any negligent or wrongful act or omission of the Indemnifying Party;
   (c) any third-party claim arising from the Indemnifying Party's performance under this Agreement; or
   (d) any violation of applicable laws, including anti-unfair competition laws, anti-bribery laws, or data protection laws, by the Indemnifying Party.

2. The Indemnified Party shall: (a) promptly notify the Indemnifying Party in writing of any claim; (b) grant the Indemnifying Party sole control of the defense and settlement (provided that no settlement shall be entered into without the Indemnified Party's prior written consent if such settlement imposes any obligation on or requires any admission by the Indemnified Party); and (c) provide reasonable cooperation at the Indemnifying Party's expense.
```

## Force Majeure / 不可抗力

### English
```
Neither Party shall be liable for any failure or delay in performing its obligations under this Agreement to the extent that such failure or delay results from circumstances beyond the reasonable control of that Party, including but not limited to: acts of God, natural disasters, epidemics or pandemics, war, terrorism, civil unrest, government actions or sanctions, embargo, labor disputes, power failures, internet or telecommunications failures, or cyberattacks ("Force Majeure Event").

The affected Party shall: (a) promptly notify the other Party in writing of the Force Majeure Event and its expected duration (such notice to be given within [five (5)] business days of the occurrence); (b) use commercially reasonable efforts to mitigate the effects; and (c) resume performance as soon as reasonably practicable.

If a Force Majeure Event continues for more than [ninety (90)] consecutive days, either Party may terminate this Agreement upon [thirty (30)] days' written notice to the other Party, without liability except for payment of amounts due for services already rendered.
```

### 中文
```
任何一方因不可抗力事件导致无法履行或延迟履行本合同项下义务的，不承担违约责任。不可抗力事件包括但不限于：自然灾害、疫情、战争、恐怖主义行为、政府行为或制裁、禁运、罢工、电力中断、互联网或电信故障、网络攻击等超出该方合理控制范围的事件。

受影响的一方应：(a) 在事件发生后[五(5)]个工作日内以书面方式通知另一方不可抗力事件及其预计持续时间；(b) 采取商业上合理的措施减轻影响；(c) 在合理可行的情况下尽快恢复履行。

如不可抗力事件持续超过[九十(90)]日，任何一方可提前[三十(30)]日书面通知另一方终止本合同，但需支付已提供服务的对应款项。
```

## Anti-Corruption & Anti-Unfair Competition / 反腐败与反不正当竞争

### English (Comprehensive)
```
1. Anti-Corruption. Each Party represents and warrants that in connection with this Agreement:
   (a) it has not and will not, directly or indirectly, offer, pay, promise to pay, or authorize the payment of any money, gift, or anything of value to any government official, political party, or any other person for the purpose of influencing any act or decision, or securing any improper advantage;
   (b) it shall comply with all applicable anti-bribery and anti-corruption laws, including but not limited to the U.S. Foreign Corrupt Practices Act (FCPA), the UK Bribery Act 2010, the Prevention of Bribery Ordinance (Cap. 201) of Hong Kong, and the anti-bribery provisions of the PRC Criminal Law and Anti-Unfair Competition Law;
   (c) it shall maintain accurate books and records that fairly reflect all transactions.

2. Anti-Unfair Competition. Each Party covenants that in connection with this Agreement:
   (a) it shall not engage in any act of commercial bribery (商业贿赂) as defined under the PRC Anti-Unfair Competition Law, including providing kickbacks, secret commissions, or other improper benefits to secure business advantages;
   (b) it shall not engage in false or misleading advertising or promotional activities in connection with the subject matter of this Agreement;
   (c) it shall not misappropriate or misuse the other Party's trade secrets, commercial data, or proprietary information;
   (d) it shall comply with all applicable competition and anti-unfair competition laws in each jurisdiction where it operates.

3. Audit Rights. Each Party shall have the right, upon reasonable notice and during normal business hours, to audit the other Party's books and records to verify compliance with this Section.

4. Breach Consequences. A breach of this Section shall constitute a material breach of this Agreement entitling the non-breaching Party to immediate termination and full indemnification for all resulting losses.

## Liquidated Damages / 违约金

### 中文（符合民法典第585条司法实践）
```
1. 如[违约方]违反本合同第[X]条的约定，应向[守约方]支付违约金人民币[金额]元。

2. 如上述违约金不足以弥补[守约方]因[违约方]违约所遭受的实际损失，[违约方]应另行赔偿差额部分。

3. 双方确认，上述违约金金额系根据可预见的损失合理确定，不构成《民法典》第五百八十五条第二款所述的"过分高于造成的损失"的情形。

注：根据司法实践，违约金一般以不超过实际损失的30%为合理上限参考标准。建议违约金金额设置在该范围内，以降低被法院依职权调减的风险。
```
