# Finance Issues Log

Reconciliation breaks, control failures, forecast misses, regulatory findings, and cash flow anomalies captured during finance operations.

**Triggers**: reconciliation | close_review | audit_finding | variance_analysis | control_test | aging_review | regulatory_update
**Areas**: accounting | treasury | tax | audit | budgeting | reporting | accounts_payable | accounts_receivable

## Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `pending` | Not yet addressed |
| `in_progress` | Actively being remediated |
| `resolved` | Issue resolved, prevention added |
| `wont_fix` | Determined immaterial or accepted risk (reason in Resolution) |
| `promoted` | Elevated to close checklist, control matrix, or reconciliation procedure |
| `promoted_to_skill` | Extracted as a reusable skill |
