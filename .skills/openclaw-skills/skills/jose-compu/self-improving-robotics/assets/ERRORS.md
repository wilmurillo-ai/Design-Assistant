# Robotics Issues Log

Recurring robotics failures, autonomy regressions, runtime incidents, and subsystem-level defects captured during development and field operations.

**Triggers**: telemetry_alert | simulation_failure | hil_test | field_test | safety_event | hardware_diagnostics | code_review | postmortem
**Areas**: perception | localization | mapping | planning | control | manipulation | navigation | safety | simulation | hardware_integration

## Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `pending` | Not yet addressed |
| `in_progress` | Actively being fixed |
| `resolved` | Issue fixed, tested, and validated |
| `wont_fix` | Decided not to address (reason in Resolution) |
| `promoted` | Elevated to runbook, checklist, or engineering standard |
| `promoted_to_skill` | Extracted as a reusable skill |
