# Negotiation Issues Log

Negotiation failures, concession breakdowns, unresolved redlines, escalation misalignment, and agreement quality risks captured during deal execution.

**Triggers**: deadlock | repeated_objection | scope_creep | concession_exceeded_threshold | batna_not_defined | approval_missing | term_ambiguity | redline_unresolved
**Areas**: preparation | discovery | framing | bargaining | concessions | closing | stakeholder_alignment | escalation | contract_terms | follow_up

## Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `pending` | Not yet addressed |
| `in_progress` | Being actively addressed |
| `resolved` | Mitigation implemented and verified |
| `wont_fix` | Intentionally not addressed (reason required) |
| `promoted` | Promoted to playbook/library/checklist/template |
| `promoted_to_skill` | Promoted into reusable standalone skill |
