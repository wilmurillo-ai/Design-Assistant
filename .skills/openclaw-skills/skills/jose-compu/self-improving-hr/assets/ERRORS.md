# HR Process Issues Log

Compliance risks, process failures, candidate experience issues, policy violations, and regulatory findings captured during HR operations.

**Triggers**: audit | complaint | hris_alert | manager_escalation | exit_interview | benefits_error
**Areas**: recruiting | onboarding | compensation | compliance | performance | offboarding | dei

## Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `pending` | Not yet addressed |
| `in_progress` | Actively being remediated |
| `resolved` | Issue fixed, prevention added |
| `wont_fix` | Decided not to address (reason in Resolution) |
| `promoted` | Elevated to policy document, compliance calendar, or checklist |
| `promoted_to_skill` | Extracted as a reusable skill |

**CRITICAL**: NEVER log PII (names, SSNs, salary details, medical info, performance ratings for specific individuals). Always anonymize.
