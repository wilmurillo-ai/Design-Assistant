# Legal Issues Log

Contract disputes, compliance failures, clause risks, regulatory impacts, and litigation matters.

**Categories**: clause_risk | compliance_gap | precedent_shift | contract_deviation | regulatory_change | litigation_exposure
**Areas**: contracts | compliance | litigation | intellectual_property | privacy | regulatory | corporate_governance
**Statuses**: pending | in_progress | escalated | resolved | wont_fix | promoted | promoted_to_skill

## Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `pending` | Not yet triaged or reviewed |
| `in_progress` | Actively being negotiated, reviewed, or remediated |
| `escalated` | Referred to senior counsel, outside counsel, or executive |
| `resolved` | Matter closed, clause revised, compliance gap addressed |
| `wont_fix` | Risk accepted with documented justification and approver |
| `promoted` | Elevated to clause library, compliance checklist, or contract playbook |
| `promoted_to_skill` | Extracted as a reusable legal skill |

## CRITICAL REMINDER

**NEVER log privileged attorney-client communications, specific case strategy, or confidential settlement terms in this file.**
Abstract all entries to process-level lessons. Describe the type of issue and the process improvement, not the confidential details.

## Severity Definitions

| Severity | Criteria |
|----------|----------|
| `critical` | Active litigation or regulatory enforcement, material breach, injunction |
| `high` | Compliance deadline approaching, material contract risk, IP infringement claim |
| `medium` | Contract deviation with workaround, process gap with limited exposure |
| `low` | Template update, documentation improvement, informational precedent |
