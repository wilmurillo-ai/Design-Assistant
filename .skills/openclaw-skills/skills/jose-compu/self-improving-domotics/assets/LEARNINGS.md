# Domotics Learnings

Reliability findings, safety guardrails, integration lessons, occupancy insights, and energy optimization patterns from smart-home automation.

**Categories**: automation_conflict | sensor_drift | device_unreachable | integration_break | energy_optimization | safety_rule_gap | occupancy_mismatch | latency_jitter
**Areas**: lighting | climate | security | access_control | energy | sensors | routines | voice_assistants | integrations | network
**Statuses**: pending | in_progress | resolved | wont_fix | promoted | promoted_to_skill

## Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `pending` | Not yet addressed |
| `in_progress` | Under active analysis/remediation |
| `resolved` | Verified correction applied |
| `wont_fix` | Accepted limitation with rationale |
| `promoted` | Elevated to playbook/matrix/library/safety automation |
| `promoted_to_skill` | Converted into reusable skill |

## Skill Extraction Fields

When a learning becomes a skill, include:

```markdown
**Status**: promoted_to_skill
**Skill-Path**: skills/skill-name
```

## Safety Reminder

This workflow is documentation and reminder guidance only.
Do not use entries to directly actuate physical devices.
Human confirmation is mandatory for high-impact actions.
