# Business Learnings

Business administration learnings across process governance, KPI alignment, decision quality, and cross-functional execution.

**Categories**: process_breakdown | policy_gap | kpi_misalignment | decision_latency | handoff_failure | stakeholder_misalignment | documentation_drift | compliance_oversight
**Areas**: planning | execution | governance | reporting | operations | budgeting | procurement | vendor_management | risk_management | communication
**Statuses**: pending | in_progress | resolved | wont_fix | promoted | promoted_to_skill

## Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `pending` | Not yet addressed |
| `in_progress` | Active remediation |
| `resolved` | Validated improvement in place |
| `wont_fix` | Risk accepted with documented rationale |
| `promoted` | Elevated to playbook/checklist/registry/RACI/cadence |
| `promoted_to_skill` | Extracted as reusable skill |

## Skill Extraction Fields

When a learning is promoted to a skill, add:

```markdown
**Status**: promoted_to_skill
**Skill-Path**: skills/skill-name
```

## Reminder-Only Boundary

Entries are documentation and reminders only.
No approvals, commitments, or transactions are executed from this file.
