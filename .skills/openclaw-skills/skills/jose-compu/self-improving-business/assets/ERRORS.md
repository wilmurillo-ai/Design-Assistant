# Business Issues Log

Operational execution issues, governance gaps, policy inconsistency, KPI risk, and cross-functional coordination failures.

**Categories**: process_breakdown | policy_gap | kpi_misalignment | decision_latency | handoff_failure | stakeholder_misalignment | documentation_drift | compliance_oversight
**Areas**: planning | execution | governance | reporting | operations | budgeting | procurement | vendor_management | risk_management | communication
**Statuses**: pending | in_progress | escalated | resolved | wont_fix | promoted | promoted_to_skill

## Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `pending` | Not yet triaged |
| `in_progress` | Actively being remediated |
| `escalated` | Raised to governance or executive owner |
| `resolved` | Issue closed with verified process update |
| `wont_fix` | Risk accepted with documented human approval |
| `promoted` | Converted into permanent operating artifact |
| `promoted_to_skill` | Extracted into reusable skill |

## Reminder-Only Boundary

This file is for documentation and reminders only.
Do not treat entries as approvals or transactional instructions.

## Severity Definitions

| Severity | Criteria |
|----------|----------|
| `critical` | Material operational or compliance impact |
| `high` | Repeated SLA misses or significant execution risk |
| `medium` | Contained process or governance gap |
| `low` | Minor documentation or coordination cleanup |
