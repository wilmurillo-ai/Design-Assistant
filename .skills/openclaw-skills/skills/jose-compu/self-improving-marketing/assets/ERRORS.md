# Campaign Issues Log

Campaign failures, channel problems, messaging errors, deliverability issues, and performance drops captured during marketing operations.

**Triggers**: performance_alert | manual_review | a_b_test | customer_complaint | deliverability_report | attribution_audit | brand_audit
**Areas**: content | campaigns | seo | social | email | paid_media | analytics

## Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `pending` | Not yet addressed |
| `in_progress` | Actively being optimized or fixed |
| `resolved` | Issue fixed, prevention added |
| `wont_fix` | Decided not to address (reason in Resolution) |
| `promoted` | Elevated to brand guidelines, channel playbook, or attribution model |
| `promoted_to_skill` | Extracted as a reusable skill |
