# Deal Issues Log

Lost deals, pipeline leaks, pricing errors, forecast misses, and deal execution failures captured during sales operations.

**Triggers**: pipeline_review | deal_review | win_loss_analysis | forecast_review | CRM_audit | call_transcript
**Areas**: prospecting | qualification | discovery | proposal | negotiation | closing | renewal

## Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `pending` | Not yet addressed |
| `in_progress` | Actively being worked on (process change or deal rescue) |
| `resolved` | Issue addressed, prevention added |
| `wont_fix` | Decided not to address (reason in Resolution) |
| `promoted` | Elevated to battle card, pricing playbook, or qualification framework |
| `promoted_to_skill` | Extracted as a reusable skill |
