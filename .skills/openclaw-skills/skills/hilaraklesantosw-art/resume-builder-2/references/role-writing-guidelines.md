# Role Writing Guidelines

Use these guidelines to change emphasis by role family while keeping all content factual.

## Universal Rules

- Lead with ownership, scope, and outcome.
- Prefer results over duty lists.
- Use concise, business-grade phrasing.
- Keep bullets tight and readable.
- Do not invent metrics the user did not provide.

## Engineering And Technical Roles

Emphasize:

- systems built or owned
- scale, performance, reliability, delivery speed
- architecture and technical leadership
- tooling, automation, and measurable improvements

## Product And Project Roles

Emphasize:

- problem framing and prioritization
- cross-functional leadership
- roadmap execution
- launch quality and business outcomes

## Operations And Growth Roles

Emphasize:

- process ownership
- funnel, retention, efficiency, or conversion outcomes
- campaign execution and iteration
- data-driven decisions

## Marketing Roles

Emphasize:

- brand and campaign goals
- channel strategy
- measurable reach, engagement, pipeline, or ROI
- cross-team coordination

## Sales And Customer Roles

Emphasize:

- revenue impact
- pipeline ownership
- strategic account work
- customer expansion, retention, and adoption

## HR, Recruiting, Finance, And Administrative Roles

Emphasize:

- process improvement
- support to business leaders
- compliance, coordination, and execution quality
- hiring efficiency, reporting quality, or operational rigor
