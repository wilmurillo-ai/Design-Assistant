YEARLY WISDOM DISTILLATION: Extract transcendent lessons from a full year of experience.

## Steps

1. **Create wisdom file:** Create `memory/wisdom/YYYY.md` for the closing year. This file is permanent — never edited after creation. It's a snapshot of that year's learning.

2. **Read all monthly archives:** Read every `memory/archive/YYYY-MM.md` from the past year. Look for patterns that transcend individual events.

3. **Extract wisdom:** Wisdom is NOT a summary of events. It's insight that applies broadly. Examples:
   - "When debugging production issues, the first diagnosis is usually wrong. Spend 5 extra minutes gathering evidence before acting."
   - "Property viewings scheduled within 24h of listing have 3x higher success rate — speed matters more than preparation."
   - "When Andrew says 'just a quick thing', budget 2 hours."

   Each wisdom entry should have:
   ```
   ### [Principle title]
   **Insight:** [The wisdom itself — 1-3 sentences]
   **Evidence:** [What experiences from this year support this]
   **Application:** [When and how to apply this going forward]
   ```

4. **Identity reflection:** Review IDENTITY.md — how have you grown this year?
   - What new capabilities or skills were developed?
   - What responsibilities changed?
   - What relationships evolved?
   - Write a "year in identity" section in the wisdom file.

5. **SOUL.md evolution:** Propose updates to SOUL.md based on a year's experience:
   - What should change about your approach or personality?
   - What values were reinforced or need adjusting?
   - **Always flag proposed changes to the human for approval** — present them as suggestions with rationale, not edits.

6. **Review decision frameworks:** Look at `memory/decisions.md` for the year:
   - Which decision patterns worked well?
   - Which led to regret or revision?
   - Distill into decision-making principles for the wisdom file.

## Rules
- Wisdom must be genuinely insightful, not platitudes ("communication is important" is not wisdom)
- Preserve your voice — wisdom should sound like you, not a corporate handbook
- The yearly file is permanent — never edited after creation
- Always flag SOUL.md and IDENTITY.md changes to the human for approval
- Include specific evidence for each wisdom entry — ungrounded wisdom is just opinion
