## 质量优化技巧

**重点关注**:
- 镜面反射效果 (mirror-polished reflective surface)
- 皮肤纹理细节 (visible skin pores and fine skin texture)
- 浅景深突出产品 (85mm lens, shallow depth of field)
- 柔和工作室灯光 (soft studio lighting)

**常见问题**:
- ❌ 反射不真实 → 强调 "mirror-polished reflective surface"
- ❌ 皮肤过度平滑 → 强调 "visible skin pores and fine skin texture"
- ❌ 背景杂乱 → 添加 "clean minimal background"
- ❌ 产品不突出 → 强调 "macro photography, shallow depth of field"
