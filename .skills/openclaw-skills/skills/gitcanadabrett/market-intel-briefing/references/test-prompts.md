# Test Prompts

## 1. Ecosystem weekly brief
Create a weekly market intel brief on the OpenClaw ecosystem for the last 7 days. Focus on releases, partnerships, notable builds, and adoption signals. Keep it lean and separate confirmed product changes from community speculation.

## 2. Alberta energy-data center brief
Build a 14-day market intel brief on Alberta data centers and power. Focus on grid access, behind-the-meter generation, gas supply, policy, and project announcements. Show what matters for timing and project risk.

## 3. Competitive watch brief
Create a 30-day competitive brief for three edge AI infrastructure vendors. Focus on launches, partnerships, pricing signals, hiring, and enterprise traction. End with follow-up questions a commercial team should answer next.
