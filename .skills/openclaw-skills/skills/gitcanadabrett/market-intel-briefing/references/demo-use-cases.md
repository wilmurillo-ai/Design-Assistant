# Demo Use Cases

## 1. OpenClaw ecosystem weekly brief
Input:
- Topic: OpenClaw ecosystem
- Time window: last 7 days
- Themes: releases, funding, partnerships, standout community builds
- Sources: project docs, GitHub, Discord summaries, major AI infra coverage

Output:
- weekly operator brief with links
- clear split between shipped changes and community speculation
- watch-next section for product and ecosystem signals

## 2. Alberta energy and data-center market brief
Input:
- Topic: Alberta data-center and power market
- Time window: last 14 days
- Themes: grid access, BTM generation, gas supply, policy, project announcements
- Sources: AESO, AUC, company releases, government announcements, trade press

Output:
- concise market brief
- confirmed vs unverified claims ledger
- implication section focused on timing, project risk, and commercial positioning

## 3. Competitive company watch brief
Input:
- Topic: edge AI infrastructure vendors
- Company set: three named competitors
- Time window: last 30 days
- Themes: pricing, hiring, launches, partnerships, enterprise traction

Output:
- comparison brief
- evidence table by company
- suggested follow-up questions for sales or product teams
