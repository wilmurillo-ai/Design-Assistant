# LinkedIn Connection & Outreach

Generate LinkedIn connection requests and follow-up messages.

## Connection Request Templates

### Template 1: Mutual Interest
```
Hi [Name], I saw your post about [topic] and found your insights valuable. Would love to connect and follow your content.
```

### Template 2: Industry Peer
```
Hi [Name], fellow [industry] professional here. Always looking to expand my network with people doing great work in our space.
```

### Template 3: Specific Reason
```
Hi [Name], I've been following [Company]'s work on [project/initiative] and would love to connect and learn more.
```

## Follow-Up Message Sequence

### After Accept (Day 1)
```
Thanks for connecting, [Name]!

I noticed [specific thing about their work/background]. That's really interesting because [relevant insight].

I'd love to learn more about [specific topic]. Would you be open to a quick chat?
```

### Value-Add Message (Day 3-5)
```
Hi [Name], came across this [article/resource] and thought you might find it relevant given your work on [topic].

[Link]

No response needed - just wanted to share!
```

### Soft Ask (Day 7-10)
```
Hi [Name], been enjoying following your posts about [topic].

I wanted to mention - I help [target audience] with [solution]. If you ever know someone who might benefit, I'd appreciate an intro.

Happy to return the favor anytime!
```

## Profile Research Checklist

Before reaching out, check:
- [ ] Recent posts (last 30 days)
- [ ] Company news/updates
- [ ] Shared connections
- [ ] Featured section
- [ ] Activity comments

## Engagement Tips

1. **Comment first** - Engage with their content before connecting
2. **Be specific** - Reference exact posts or achievements
3. **Keep it brief** - LinkedIn messages should be shorter than emails
4. **Don't pitch immediately** - Build rapport first
