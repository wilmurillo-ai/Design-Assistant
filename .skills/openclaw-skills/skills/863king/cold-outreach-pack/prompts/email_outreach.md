# Cold Email Outreach Prompt

Generate a high-converting cold email based on these inputs:

## Input Variables

- **Target Name**: [Name]
- **Company**: [Company Name]
- **Their Pain Point**: [Problem they face]
- **Your Solution**: [How you help]
- **CTA**: [Desired action]

## Email Structure

### Subject Line Options
1. [Name], quick question about [Company]
2. [One-sentence benefit]
3. [Curiosity gap headline]

### Email Template

```
Hi [Name],

[Hook - 1-2 sentences about them or their company]

I noticed [specific insight about their business/work].

[One sentence on what you do + key benefit].

[Social proof or credibility sentence].

[Low-commitment CTA].

Best,
[Your name]
```

## Personalization Tips

- Research their recent posts, company news, or speaking engagements
- Mention specific metrics or achievements
- Reference mutual connections if applicable

## Follow-Up Timing

| Touch | Timing | Purpose |
|-------|--------|---------|
| Email 1 | Day 0 | Initial outreach |
| Email 2 | Day 3 | Value add (share useful resource) |
| Email 3 | Day 7 | Break pattern with new angle |
| Email 4 | Day 14 | Final attempt with time pressure |

## Key Principles

1. **Keep it short** - Under 150 words
2. **Focus on them** - Not you
3. **One CTA** - Make it easy to respond
4. **Be specific** - Generic = ignored
