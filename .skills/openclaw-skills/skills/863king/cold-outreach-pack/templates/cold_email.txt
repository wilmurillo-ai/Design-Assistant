# Cold Email Templates

## B2B SaaS Template

```
Subject: [Name], quick question about [Company]

Hi [Name],

I noticed [specific insight about their company or role].

Most [role] struggle with [common pain point] - we've helped [similar company] achieve [specific result].

Would a 10-minute call make sense to explore if there's a fit?

Best,
[Your name]
```

## Investor Outreach Template

```
Subject: [Company] founder reaching out

Hi [Name],

I'm building [company] - [one sentence on what you do].

I know you're invested in [sector], and I'd love to get your perspective on [specific question].

No pitch - just genuinely curious about your thoughts.

Best,
[Your name]
```

## Partnership Template

```
Subject: Partnership idea for [Company]

Hi [Name],

I've been following [Company]'s work on [project/initiative] - impressive stuff.

We work with companies like [similar companies] to [value proposition].

Would you be open to a quick call to explore if there's a natural fit?

Best,
[Your name]
```

## Freelancer Client Template

```
Subject: Quick question about [Company]'s [need]

Hi [Name],

I help [target companies] with [service] - currently working with [2-3 examples].

Saw your post about [topic] and had some ideas that might help.

Worth a quick 10-minute chat?

Best,
[Your name]
```

## Job Seeker's Outreach Template

```
Subject: [Role] opportunity question

Hi [Name],

I'm reaching out about potential opportunities at [Company].

[One sentence on background + why this company specifically].

Would you be open to a brief call to learn more about the team?

Best,
[Your name]
```
