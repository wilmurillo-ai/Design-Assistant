# Supported styles

Use this file only when selecting a style or strengthening a retry prompt.

## 1. 热血少年/少女感 (Shonen/Shojo Anime)

- Core: `Anime style, vibrant colors, dynamic pose, bold lines`
- Style: `90s retro anime, studio masterpiece, high contrast`
- Constraints: `Sharp focus, expressive eyes, cinematic action`
- Scene: `Summer sunlight, school rooftop, wind blowing hair`

## 2. 吉卜力/清新风 (Studio Ghibli Style)

- Core: `Studio Ghibli inspired, hand-drawn animation, Hayao Miyazaki style`
- Style: `Soft edges, watercolor texture, nostalgic`
- Constraints: `Natural lighting, lush greenery, pastoral, simple facial features`
- Scene: `Meadow, rustic kitchen, blue sky with fluffy white clouds`

## 3. 新海诚式唯美 (Makoto Shinkai Style)

- Core: `Makoto Shinkai style, Your Name aesthetic, hyper-detailed scenery`
- Style: `Lens flare, emotional lighting, purple and orange sunset`
- Constraints: `Highly detailed sky, realistic reflection, romantic atmosphere`
- Scene: `Train station, city skyline at twilight, raindrops on window`

## 4. 赛博朋克 (Cyberpunk)

- Core: `Cyberpunk, futuristic, sci-fi, high-tech`
- Style: `Neon lights, pink and blue palette, synthwave, gritty`
- Constraints: `Night city, glitch effect, robotic parts, metallic textures`
- Outfit: `Techwear, oversized hoodie, glowing accessories`

## 5. 美漫/波普风 (Comic & Pop Art)

- Core: `American comic book style, DC/Marvel style, Pop art`
- Style: `Halftone dots, Ben-Day dots, high saturation, ink outlines`
- Constraints: `Onomatopoeia accents, flat color, dramatic shadows`
- Scene: `Urban street, comic panel layout`

## 6. Y2K/千禧风 (Y2K Aesthetic)

- Core: `Y2K style, Year 2000 aesthetic, retro-futurism`
- Style: `Glossy, iridescent, low-rise jeans, butterfly clips`
- Constraints: `Fisheye lens, glitter, bright candy colors, CD-ROM aesthetic`
- Outfit: `Velour tracksuit, tinted sunglasses`

## 7. 3D 粘土/皮克斯风 (Clay & 3D Pixar)

- Core: `3D render, Disney Pixar style, Claymation`
- Style: `Octane render, C4D, soft shadows, rounded shapes`
- Constraints: `Subsurface scattering, matte texture, cute proportions, big eyes`
- Detail: `Chunky clothes, plastic-like hair`

## 8. 国风/古风插画 (Traditional Chinese Art)

- Core: `Traditional Chinese painting, Dunhuang style, ink wash`
- Style: `Guofeng, elegant, intricate patterns, flowing silk`
- Constraints: `Meticulous brushwork, gold foil, soft pastel colors`
- Outfit: `Hanfu, crane motifs, jade accessories`

## 9. 简约 Q 版 (Chibi / Kawaii)

- Core: `Chibi, super deformed, kawaii, tiny body big head`
- Style: `Minimalist, pastel colors, sticker art`
- Constraints: `Simple lines, 2-head tall, oversized head, dot eyes`
- Detail: `Small limbs, simple background, clean white border`

## 10. 手绘/油画感 (Hand-drawn / Oil Painting)

- Core: `Oil painting, impressive style, thick brushstrokes`
- Style: `Canvas texture, artistic, Van Gogh style, Monet`
- Constraints: `Visible paint strokes, heavy impasto, rich color blending`
- Outfit: `Woolen textures, classic silhouettes`
