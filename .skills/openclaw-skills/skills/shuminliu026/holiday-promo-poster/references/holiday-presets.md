# Holiday Promo Poster Presets

## Double 11

- Best for: explosive e-commerce promotion, low-price communication, urgency
- Palette: red, black, bright white, optional neon magenta accent
- Motifs: sale tags, price bursts, countdown feeling, bold commerce graphics
- Tone: aggressive, high-conversion, headline-first
- Avoid: soft lifestyle illustration, weak discount emphasis, luxury gift calmness

## Christmas

- Best for: gifting, festive packs, warm seasonal promotion
- Palette: deep red, pine green, gold, warm cream
- Motifs: ribbon, ornaments, gift boxes, snowfall glow, holiday lights
- Tone: warm, premium, celebratory
- Avoid: bargain-market chaos, harsh neon sale board look

## 618

- Best for: mid-year shopping campaigns and broad product pushes
- Palette: vivid red, orange, electric blue accents, clean white grounding
- Motifs: platform-sale energy, discount labels, dynamic commerce blocks
- Tone: lively, efficient, deal-driven
- Avoid: over-romantic holiday styling, winter gift visuals

## New Year

- Best for: year-end or new-year kickoff campaigns
- Palette: red, gold, bright cream, festive sparkle
- Motifs: fireworks, lucky cloud shapes, festive light, prosperity cues
- Tone: celebratory, refreshed, auspicious
- Avoid: flat corporate deck look, weak seasonal identity

## Valentine's Day

- Best for: gift products, beauty, fragrance, jewelry, lifestyle goods
- Palette: rose pink, crimson, soft blush, ivory
- Motifs: ribbons, elegant hearts, glow, gift presentation
- Tone: romantic, soft, polished
- Avoid: hard-edged bargain visual noise, masculine industrial styling

## Black Friday

- Best for: aggressive discount hero campaigns
- Palette: black, gold, white, optional neon green or neon red
- Motifs: bold numbers, premium sale plate, spotlight contrast
- Tone: urgent, premium, deal-heavy
- Avoid: cute seasonal decor, pastel softness, festive warmth

## Generic Holiday Prompt Rules

- Make the product image the hero asset.
- Use the holiday style to frame the product and the discount message.
- Keep one dominant campaign promise.
- Use 2 to 4 selling points max.
- If the result looks generic, strengthen palette, motif, and tone all at once.

## Poster Style Presets

### mesh-gradient

- Best for: refined techy youth brands, premium but soft campaign boards
- Visual cues: diffused color glow, mesh-like light blooms, frosted text blocks, airy depth
- Must-see: soft irregular light blooms, translucent text carrier, elegant airy openness
- Avoid: cluttered sticker sale-board style, hard-edged geometry overload

### memphis

- Best for: loud promotions, trendy youth campaigns, banner-like event energy
- Visual cues: dots, zigzags, rings, irregular shapes, saturated contrast palette, dense composition
- Must-see: obvious geometric decorations, lively clash palette, filled negative space, playful visual noise
- Avoid: too much whitespace, quiet luxury calmness, weak celebration feel

### c4d-render

- Best for: skincare, cosmetics, electronics, appliances, premium physical goods
- Visual cues: 3D display stage, floating props, strong lighting, polished materials, rendered depth
- Must-see: display pedestal or spatial scene, premium material rendering, dimensional light-shadow, object depth
- Avoid: flat poster background only, plain magazine layout, hand-drawn casual feel

### editorial-layout

- Best for: fashion, home, furniture, lifestyle, aesthetic brand campaigns
- Visual cues: strong typography hierarchy, whitespace, large color blocks, magazine layout rhythm
- Must-see: visible layout grid, elegant typography contrast, editorial whitespace, restrained art direction
- Avoid: noisy promo clutter, heavy 3D prop overload, childish doodles

### doodle-illustration

- Best for: snacks, stationery, mother-baby, casual lifestyle, friendly direct-to-consumer brands
- Visual cues: real product photo plus hand-drawn lines, icons, handwritten words, warm playful rhythm
- Must-see: hand-drawn intervention around product photo, doodle marks, playful warmth, approachable handmade feel
- Avoid: hyper-polished luxury render, rigid minimal severity, overbuilt sale geometry

## Retry Hints

- If `mesh-gradient` is weak, strengthen `airy mesh light blooms`, `frosted translucent text cards`, and `soft diffused color field`.
- If `memphis` is weak, strengthen `dense dots zigzags rings`, `high-saturation clash colors`, and `full celebratory composition`.
- If `c4d-render` is weak, strengthen `clear 3D pedestal`, `premium rendered materials`, and `obvious dimensional light-shadow`.
- If `editorial-layout` is weak, strengthen `large whitespace`, `editorial typography hierarchy`, and `magazine composition blocks`.
- If `doodle-illustration` is weak, strengthen `hand-drawn doodles`, `handwritten marks`, and `real product plus sketch overlay`.
