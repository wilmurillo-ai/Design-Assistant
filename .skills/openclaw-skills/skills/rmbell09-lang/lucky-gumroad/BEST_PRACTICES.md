# Gumroad Best Practices

## Product Creation

### Digital Products (Templates, Guides, etc.)
- **Pricing sweet spot:** $5-$15 for individual templates, $19-$49 for bundles
- **Bundle strategy:** 5-10 templates per bundle increases perceived value 3-5x
- **Cover images:** 1280x720px minimum, show actual product previews (not mockups)
- **Product descriptions:** Lead with benefits, include what's included list, show use cases
- **File formats:** Always include multiple formats (PSD, AI, Canva link, PNG)

### Product Listing Optimization
- **Title format:** `[Product Type] - [Niche/Style] | [Key Benefit]`
  - Example: "Instagram Story Templates - Minimalist | 30 Editable Designs"
- **Tags:** Use all available tag slots. Mix broad ("social media templates") and specific ("instagram story minimalist")
- **Description structure:**
  1. One-line hook (what problem it solves)
  2. What's included (bullet list with quantities)
  3. Who it's for (target audience)
  4. How to use (quick instructions)
  5. File formats and compatibility
- **Price anchoring:** Show original price crossed out for bundles

### Product Types That Sell on Gumroad
1. **Social media templates** (Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest)
2. **Notion/productivity templates**
3. **E-books and guides**
4. **Design assets** (icons, fonts, graphics)
5. **Educational content** (courses, workshops)

## Store Optimization

### Profile Setup
- **Display name:** Professional, memorable, niche-relevant
- **Bio:** Focus on what you create and who it helps (not about you)
- **Profile image:** Clean logo or brand mark
- **Cover image:** Showcase best products

### SEO & Discovery
- Gumroad has internal search — optimize titles and descriptions for keywords
- External SEO: Gumroad product pages rank in Google
- Use descriptive URLs (custom slugs when possible)

## Marketing (Free Channels)

### Launch Checklist
1. Product page fully optimized
2. 3-5 preview images showing the product in use
3. Share on social media with direct link
4. Post in relevant Reddit/Facebook communities (provide value, not spam)
5. Create a free sample/lead magnet version

### Pricing Psychology
- **$0+ (pay what you want):** Use for lead magnets and building audience
- **$5-$10:** Low barrier, impulse purchases
- **$15-$29:** Mid-tier, needs strong value proposition
- **$30-$49:** Premium bundles, courses
- **Odd pricing:** $7, $12, $19 outperform round numbers

### Reviews & Social Proof
- First 5 reviews are critical — give free copies to get initial reviews
- Use Gumroad's "offer code" feature for review copies
- Display customer count on product page

## Automation Notes

### Product Upload Flow
1. Navigate to `gumroad.com/products/new`
2. Fill: Name, Price, Description
3. Upload cover image and product files
4. Set URL slug, tags, and categories
5. Preview → Publish

### Monitoring
- Check `gumroad.com/dashboard` for sales
- Revenue data at `gumroad.com/dashboard/sales`
- Customer emails available for follow-up marketing

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- ❌ Launching without preview images
- ❌ Generic descriptions ("great template pack")
- ❌ Pricing too low (devalues product) or too high without social proof
- ❌ Not offering a free/cheap entry product
- ❌ Ignoring SEO in titles and descriptions
- ❌ Not bundling related products
