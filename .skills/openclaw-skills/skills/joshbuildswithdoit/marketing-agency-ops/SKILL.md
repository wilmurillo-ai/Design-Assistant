# Marketing Agency Operations

Run a tighter agency with less admin overhead.

## Client Reporting
- Generate weekly/monthly performance summary templates per client
- Pull key metrics into report format (input numbers → formatted report)
- Draft executive summary paragraphs from raw data
- Send automated report reminders to team members

## Campaign Management
- Track active campaigns, deadlines, and deliverables per client
- Alert 48 hours before deliverable due dates
- Generate creative brief templates
- Maintain asset library index (by client, campaign, file type)

## New Business Pipeline
- Track leads from first contact to signed contract
- Draft proposal outlines from a discovery call summary
- Follow-up sequence templates for warm prospects
- Win/loss tracking with reason codes

## Retainer & Billing
- Monthly retainer reminder 5 days before invoice date
- Track hours logged vs retainer budget per client
- Flag scope creep early (hours trending over)
- Generate invoice summary notes

## Team Operations
- Weekly capacity check across team members
- Onboarding checklist for new clients
- Offboarding checklist (asset transfer, access removal)
- Meeting agenda templates (kickoff, monthly review, QBR)

## Strategy Support
- Research competitor campaigns for client verticals
- Draft creative concepts from a brief
- Generate A/B test ideas for ads, subject lines, landing pages
- Summarize industry news relevant to client's audience

## Setup
Tell your agent:
- Your active client roster and retainer amounts
- Team member names and roles
- Reporting cadence per client
- Key deliverable types you produce
