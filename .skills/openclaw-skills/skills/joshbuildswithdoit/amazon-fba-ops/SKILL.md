# Amazon FBA Operations

Run your Amazon business tighter without more headcount.

## Inventory Management
- Daily inventory health check (days of stock remaining per ASIN)
- Reorder point alerts (flag when stock < 30 days)
- Generate purchase orders from reorder quantities
- Stranded inventory report and fix recommendations
- FBA fee estimate for new products

## Listing Optimization
- Monthly listing audit (title, bullets, description, backend keywords)
- A+ Content improvement suggestions
- Competitor listing comparison (input ASINs)
- Image improvement checklist per listing

## PPC & Advertising
- Weekly PPC performance summary (input ACoS, spend, sales)
- Flag campaigns with ACoS > target
- Keyword harvesting reminders (move winners from auto to manual)
- Budget pacing check (daily spend vs monthly budget)

## Reviews & Ratings
- Daily new review digest (flag 1-3 star reviews)
- Draft seller response templates for negative reviews
- Review velocity tracking (goal: consistent new reviews)
- Vine enrollment reminder for new ASINs

## Financials
- Monthly P&L template (revenue, COGS, FBA fees, PPC spend, net margin)
- Unit economics calculator (input landed cost → margin at each price point)
- Reimbursement audit checklist (lost/damaged inventory)
- Q4 inventory planning timeline

## Competitor Intelligence
- Weekly competitor price monitoring (input competitor ASINs)
- New entrant alerts in your category
- Buy Box win rate tracker

## Setup
Tell your agent:
- Your top 10 ASINs and reorder lead times
- Target ACoS per campaign
- Supplier contacts and MOQs
- Current monthly ad spend budget
