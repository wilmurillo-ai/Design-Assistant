# Climbing Gym Revenue Optimization Playbook

## Revenue Mix Targets (Industry Benchmarks)
| Stream | Target % | Avg $/mo (300 members) | Notes |
|--------|----------|----------------------|-------|
| Memberships | 70% | $25,500 | Core revenue, focus on retention |
| Day passes | 15% | $5,460 | Conversion funnel to membership |
| Youth programs | 10-15% | $3,600-5,400 | Teams, camps, parties |
| Retail & rental | 5-8% | $1,800-2,900 | 80%+ margin on rentals |
| Events/corporate | Variable | $1,000-4,000 | Lumpy but high-margin |
| Food & bev | 3-5% | $1,000-1,800 | Vending or café partnership |

**Total target range:** $36,000-$42,000/mo at 300 members

## 1. Membership Tier Design

### Recommended Tiers
| Tier | Price | Includes | Target |
|------|-------|----------|--------|
| Basic | $65/mo | Climbing only, off-peak hours | Students, budget-conscious |
| Standard | $85/mo | Unlimited climbing, all hours | Core members |
| Premium | $110/mo | Climbing + yoga/fitness + guest pass/mo + retail discount | High-value members |
| Family | $150/mo | 2 adults + kids under 16 | Families, weekend warriors |
| Annual | $799/yr | Same as Standard, ~22% discount | Retention play |

### Key Principles
- Annual plans reduce churn by 40-60% vs monthly
- Premium tier should be 15-20% of members (high LTV)
- Student/off-peak tier fills dead hours without cannibalizing prime time
- Freeze option (1-2 months/year) reduces cancellations — member stays on file

## 2. Day Pass → Membership Conversion

### Funnel
1. **Day pass purchase** ($18-25)
2. **On-site experience** — staff introduces themselves, offers beta, suggests routes
3. **Exit conversation** — "Did you have fun? We have an intro month for $49"
4. **Intro month** ($49, no commitment) — 30 days unlimited
5. **Day 7 check-in** email/text: "How's the climbing going?"
6. **Day 21 conversion offer** — "Your intro month ends in 9 days. Lock in $85/mo — first month already paid."
7. **Day 28 urgency** — "Last 3 days of your intro. After this, day passes only."

### Benchmarks
- Day pass → intro month: target 25-30%
- Intro month → membership: target 50-60%
- Overall day pass → member: 12-18%
- Intro class attendees convert 20% better than walk-ins

## 3. Churn Reduction Strategies

### Early Warning System (At-Risk Triggers)
| Signal | Days | Action |
|--------|------|--------|
| No visit | 14 | Friendly check-in text: "Miss you at the gym! New routes just went up." |
| No visit | 21 | Email with content: route video, community event invite |
| No visit | 30 | Personal call from staff: "Everything OK? Can we help?" |
| Payment failure | Day 1 | Auto-retry + soft notification |
| Payment failure | Day 5 | Personal reach-out — don't let billing kill a relationship |
| Freeze request | — | Offer 1-month freeze (better than cancel), schedule return date |

### Retention Boosters
- **Community events:** Monthly meetups, outdoor trips, film nights — builds social bonds that prevent quitting
- **Route-setting freshness:** Stale routes are the #1 silent churn driver. Reset sections every 4-6 weeks minimum
- **Milestone recognition:** 100th visit, 1-year anniversary, first V5/5.11 — public shoutout + small reward
- **Buddy system:** "Bring a friend free" once/month for members — accountability partner
- **Feedback loops:** Quarterly NPS survey, act on top 3 complaints within 30 days

## 4. Youth Program Scaling

### Revenue Potential
- Youth programs can reach 15% of total revenue
- Summer camps: $250-400/week, 8-12 kids per session, 8-10 weeks = $20K-48K/summer
- Birthday parties: $250-400/party, 2-4 per weekend = $2K-6.4K/mo
- Climbing team: $100-150/mo per kid, 20-40 kids = $2K-6K/mo

### Optimization
- Waitlist management: don't leave money on the table — offer additional sessions before turning away
- Early-bird pricing: 15% off if registered 30+ days early (locks revenue, aids planning)
- Sibling discount: 10-15% for second child (increases family LTV)
- School partnerships: after-school programs, PE field trips, scout badges

## 5. Corporate Events

### Pricing Model
| Group Size | Per Person | Min Spend | Includes |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|----------|
| 10-20 | $45-55 | $500 | 2hr climb, gear, basic instruction |
| 20-40 | $40-50 | $900 | 2hr climb, gear, instruction, group photo |
| 40+ | $35-45 | $1,500 | Custom event, multiple activities, catering coordination |

### Pipeline Management
- Track all inquiries (even declined — follow up next quarter)
- Send post-event feedback survey within 48 hours
- Offer 10% rebook discount if they schedule next event within 30 days
- Quarterly email to past corporate clients: "Team building season is here"
