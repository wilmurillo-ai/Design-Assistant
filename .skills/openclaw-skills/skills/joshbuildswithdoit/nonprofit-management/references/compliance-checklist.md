# 501(c)(3) Annual Compliance Checklist

## Federal (IRS)

### Annual Filing (Due 5/15 for calendar year orgs)
- [ ] Determine correct form: 990-N (<$50K), 990-EZ (<$200K rev & <$500K assets), or 990
- [ ] Gather financial statements (audited if required by state or funders)
- [ ] Complete Schedule A (public charity status — most small orgs: 170(b)(1)(A)(vi))
- [ ] Complete Schedule B (contributor schedule — confidential, not public)
- [ ] Complete Schedule O (supplemental information — required explanations)
- [ ] Review functional expense allocation (program vs management vs fundraising)
- [ ] Review compensation reporting (Part VII — officers, directors, key employees)
- [ ] File Form 8868 for extension if needed (automatic 6-month extension)
- [ ] **CRITICAL:** 3 consecutive years of non-filing = automatic revocation of tax-exempt status

### Ongoing Federal Requirements
- [ ] Maintain contemporaneous written acknowledgments for gifts ≥$250
- [ ] Issue Form 1099-NEC for independent contractors paid ≥$600
- [ ] File Form 990-T if unrelated business income exceeds $1,000
- [ ] Maintain written conflict of interest policy (asked on 990 Part VI)
- [ ] Maintain document retention/destruction policy
- [ ] Make 990, application (1023/1023-EZ), and determination letter available for public inspection
- [ ] Track lobbying activities (substantial part test or 501(h) election)
- [ ] **Zero political campaign activity** (absolute prohibition for 501(c)(3))

## Ohio State Requirements

### Ohio Attorney General
- [ ] File CT-12 (Charitable Trust Registration) annually
- [ ] Due date: same as federal 990 (5/15 for calendar year)
- [ ] Filing fee: $0 for organizations under $25K revenue; $50 for $25K-$1M
- [ ] Include copy of federal 990/990-EZ

### Ohio Secretary of State
- [ ] File biennial report (every 2 years, due by anniversary of incorporation)
- [ ] Filing fee: $25 online
- [ ] Update registered agent if changed

### Ohio Department of Taxation
- [ ] Maintain sales tax exemption certificate (blanket exemption for qualifying nonprofits)
- [ ] File CAT (Commercial Activity Tax) exclusion if applicable (most 501(c)(3)s excluded)
- [ ] Property tax exemption renewal if owning real property

### Ohio Gaming/Raffle
- [ ] Obtain nonprofit gaming license if conducting raffles, bingo, or games of chance
- [ ] Annual license: $50 (Type A — small games) or varies by type
- [ ] Quarterly gaming reports to Ohio AG

## Board Governance Checklist (Annual)
- [ ] Hold minimum required board meetings (check bylaws — typically 2-4/year)
- [ ] Maintain quorum at all meetings (check bylaws — typically majority)
- [ ] Annual conflict of interest disclosures signed by all board members
- [ ] Review and approve annual budget
- [ ] Review and approve CEO/ED compensation (document in minutes)
- [ ] Review D&O insurance coverage
- [ ] Review bylaws (amend if needed — majority vote typically required)
- [ ] Board self-evaluation / governance assessment
- [ ] Review and update strategic plan

## Financial Controls
- [ ] Annual financial review or audit (required by some funders over $500K)
- [ ] Segregation of duties: different people authorize, record, and reconcile
- [ ] Monthly bank reconciliation by someone other than check signer
- [ ] Board review of financial statements at each meeting
- [ ] Annual review of internal controls and procedures
- [ ] Maintain fidelity bond / employee dishonesty insurance
- [ ] Review investment policy (if holding investments)

## Donor & Fundraising Compliance
- [ ] Acknowledgment letters sent within 48 hours of all gifts
- [ ] Year-end tax receipts for all donors (especially ≥$250)
- [ ] Quid pro quo disclosure for gifts >$75 where goods/services provided
- [ ] State charitable solicitation registration current (if soliciting out-of-state)
- [ ] Review fundraising expense ratio (target: <25% of total fundraising revenue)

## Employment & HR
- [ ] Worker classification review (employee vs independent contractor)
- [ ] All required employment posters displayed
- [ ] I-9 verification for all employees
- [ ] Workers' compensation insurance current (Ohio BWC)
- [ ] Unemployment insurance contributions current (ODJFS)
- [ ] Employee handbook review and update
- [ ] Background checks for positions working with minors/vulnerable populations
