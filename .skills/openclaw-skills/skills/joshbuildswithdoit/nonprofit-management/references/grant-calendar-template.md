## Grant Lifecycle Stages
1. **Research** — Identify funder, confirm eligibility, assess fit
2. **LOI** — Letter of inquiry (if required) — typically 1-2 pages
3. **Application** — Full proposal with budget, narrative, attachments
4. **Review** — Funder review period (4-12 weeks typical)
5. **Award/Decline** — Notification, agreement signing, fund setup
6. **Implementation** — Deliver program, track expenses against grant budget
7. **Reporting** — Interim and final reports per funder schedule
8. **Renewal** — Reapply or steward for next cycle

## Key Documents to Keep Ready
- IRS determination letter (501(c)(3) status)
- Most recent 990
- Current year budget (board-approved)
- Audited financial statements (if available)
- Board roster with affiliations
- Organization overview / boilerplate (1 paragraph, 1 page, 3 page versions)
- Logic model / theory of change
- Letters of support (refresh annually)
