# Nonprofit Financial Health Benchmarks

## Key Ratios & Targets

### Program Expense Ratio
- **Formula:** Program Expenses / Total Expenses
- **Target:** ≥75% (IRS and major charity watchdogs benchmark)
- **Good:** 75-85%
- **Excellent:** >85%
- **Concern:** <65% (triggers donor and watchdog scrutiny)
- **Note:** Reported on 990 Part IX. Functional expense allocation must be reasonable and documented.

### Fundraising Efficiency
- **Formula:** Fundraising Expenses / Total Contributions
- **Target:** <25% (i.e., spend less than $0.25 to raise $1)
- **Good:** 10-20%
- **Excellent:** <10%
- **Concern:** >35% (BBB Wise Giving Alliance threshold)

### Operating Reserve Ratio
- **Formula:** Unrestricted Net Assets / Annual Operating Expenses
- **Target:** 3-6 months of operating expenses
- **Minimum:** 3 months (covers seasonal fluctuations, unexpected costs)
- **Healthy:** 6 months
- **Excess:** >12 months may raise questions about hoarding

### Revenue Concentration Risk
- **Metric:** % of total revenue from single source
- **Target:** No single source >30% of total revenue
- **Risk threshold:** >50% from any one funder = high dependency risk
- **Diversification targets:**
  - Individual donors: 30-50%
  - Grants/foundations: 20-30%
  - Earned income/program fees: 10-20%
  - Events: 5-15%
  - Government: varies (can be higher for direct-service orgs)

### Current Ratio (Liquidity)
- **Formula:** Current Assets / Current Liabilities
- **Target:** ≥1.5
- **Minimum:** 1.0 (can meet short-term obligations)
- **Concern:** <0.8 (cash flow problems likely)

### Debt Ratio
- **Formula:** Total Liabilities / Total Assets
- **Target:** <0.5 (assets exceed liabilities by 2:1)
- **Concern:** >0.8 (heavily leveraged — unusual for small nonprofits)

## Red Flags to Monitor

### Financial
- [ ] Expenses exceed revenue for 2+ consecutive years
- [ ] Cash balance declining 3+ consecutive months
- [ ] Restricted funds used for unrestricted purposes (serious compliance issue)
- [ ] Large year-end adjustments or restatements
- [ ] Payroll taxes not deposited timely (personal liability for responsible persons)
- [ ] Unrelated business income >$1,000 without filing 990-T

### Governance
- [ ] Board meetings fewer than required by bylaws
- [ ] No independent financial review or audit when required
- [ ] Executive compensation not reviewed/approved by independent board members
- [ ] Conflict of interest policy not enforced
- [ ] Related-party transactions not disclosed on Schedule L

### Operational
- [ ] Key person dependency (only one person knows financials, passwords, donor relationships)
- [ ] No succession plan for ED/CEO
- [ ] Insurance gaps (D&O, general liability, property, workers' comp)
- [ ] Grant reporting deadlines missed (risks future funding)
