# Federal + Ohio Tax Deadline Calendar

## Federal Deadlines (Calendar Year Taxpayers)

| Return Type | Due Date | Extension To | Notes |
|-------------|----------|-------------|-------|
| 1065 (Partnership) | March 15 | September 15 | K-1s due to partners by original deadline |
| 1120-S (S-Corp) | March 15 | September 15 | K-1s due to shareholders by original deadline |
| 1041 (Trust/Estate) | April 15 | September 30 | K-1s to beneficiaries by original deadline |
| 1040 (Individual) | April 15 | October 15 | Estimated tax still due by 4/15 even if extended |
| 1120 (C-Corp) | April 15* | October 15 | *Fiscal year: 15th day of 4th month after FYE |
| 990 (Nonprofit) | May 15 | November 15 | 990-N (e-Postcard) same deadline, no extension needed |
| 5500 (Retirement Plan) | July 31 | October 15 | Solo 401(k) exempt if <$250K assets |

## Quarterly Estimated Taxes (1040-ES / 1120-W)

| Quarter | Period | Due Date |
|---------|--------|----------|
| Q1 | Jan 1 – Mar 31 | April 15 |
| Q2 | Apr 1 – May 31 | June 15 |
| Q3 | Jun 1 – Aug 31 | September 15 |
| Q4 | Sep 1 – Dec 31 | January 15 (following year) |

## Payroll Tax Deposits

| Schedule | Frequency | Rule |
|----------|-----------|------|
| Monthly | 15th of following month | Total payroll tax liability <$50K in lookback period |
| Semi-weekly | Wed/Thu payroll → following Wed; Fri-Sun payroll → following Fri | Total payroll tax liability >$50K in lookback period |
| Quarterly (Form 941) | Last day of month following quarter end | All employers |
| Annual (Form 940 - FUTA) | January 31 | All employers with FUTA liability |

## Ohio-Specific

| Filing | Due Date | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| Ohio IT 1040 (Individual) | April 15 | Automatic extension to October 15 (no form needed, but pay by 4/15) |
| Ohio CAT (Commercial Activity Tax) | May 10 (annual) or quarterly | $150K+ gross receipts threshold |
| Municipal Income Tax | April 15 (most cities) | Cleveland, Columbus, Cincinnati all April 15; rates vary 1.5-2.5% |
| Ohio Annual Report (SOS) | Varies by entity | Not required for LLCs since 2024; corps must file biennially |
| Ohio BWC (Workers Comp) | Varies | True-up reports due annually |

## Key Retirement Deadlines

| Action | Deadline |
|--------|----------|
| Solo 401(k) establishment | December 31 of tax year |
| Solo 401(k) employee contribution | December 31 (or fiscal year end) |
| Solo 401(k) employer contribution | Tax filing deadline (including extensions) |
| SEP-IRA establishment + contribution | Tax filing deadline (including extensions) |
| Traditional/Roth IRA contribution | April 15 (no extension) |
| SDIRA investment | Any time (must be funded first) |

## S-Corp Election (Form 2553)

| Timing | Deadline |
|--------|----------|
| New entity (current year) | Within 75 days of formation |
| Existing entity (next year) | By March 15 of the year election takes effect |
| Late election relief | Attach to first S-Corp return, reasonable cause statement |
