# Document Request Templates by Return Type

## Individual (1040)

### Standard Document Request
```
Subject: [Year] Tax Document Request — [Client Name]

Hi [Client Name],

It's that time of year! To prepare your [Year] tax return, I'll need the following:

INCOME:
□ W-2(s) from all employers
□ 1099-NEC / 1099-MISC (freelance/contract income)
□ 1099-INT / 1099-DIV (interest and dividends)
□ 1099-B (stock/investment sales)
□ K-1(s) from partnerships, S-Corps, trusts, or estates
□ 1099-R (retirement distributions)
□ 1099-SSA (Social Security)
□ 1099-G (unemployment or state refunds)
□ Rental income summary (if applicable)

DEDUCTIONS:
□ Mortgage interest statement (1098)
□ Property tax bills paid
□ State/local income taxes paid (beyond withholding)
□ Charitable donation receipts ($250+ need written acknowledgment)
□ Medical expenses (if significant — 7.5% AGI threshold)
□ Student loan interest (1098-E)
□ Tuition (1098-T)

BUSINESS (if self-employed):
□ Gross income summary
□ Business expense summary by category
□ Home office square footage (if applicable)
□ Vehicle mileage log
□ Health insurance premiums paid
□ Retirement contributions (SEP, Solo 401k, SIMPLE)

OTHER:
□ Prior year tax return (if new client)
□ Estimated tax payments made during the year
□ Any IRS or state correspondence received
□ Life changes: marriage, divorce, new baby, home purchase, home sale

Please upload documents to [portal link] or reply to this email with attachments.
Deadline for on-time filing: [date — typically March 15 for complete docs].

Thanks!
[Firm Name]
```

## S-Corporation (1120-S)

### Document Request
```
Subject: [Year] S-Corp Tax Document Request — [Entity Name]

Hi [Client Name],

To prepare the [Year] Form 1120-S for [Entity Name], I'll need:

INCOME & EXPENSES:
□ Year-end P&L (profit and loss statement)
□ Year-end balance sheet
□ Bank statements (December + January for outstanding items)
□ Credit card statements (if not in bookkeeping)
□ Loan statements (mortgage, equipment, lines of credit)

PAYROLL:
□ Year-end payroll summary (or access to payroll provider)
□ W-2s issued (copies)
□ Owner compensation total for the year
□ Payroll tax deposits made (941 summaries)

ASSETS:
□ Asset purchase receipts (equipment, vehicles, improvements)
□ Asset dispositions (anything sold or scrapped)
□ Vehicle mileage logs (if business vehicles)

OWNERSHIP:
□ Any ownership changes during the year
□ Shareholder loan activity (advances to/from shareholders)
□ Distribution amounts per shareholder

OTHER:
□ Prior year tax return (if new client)
□ State-specific filings needed (list states of operation)
□ Any IRS or state correspondence received
□ Related party transactions (payments to/from other entities you own)

Please send by [date] for on-time March 15 filing.

Thanks!
[Firm Name]
```

## Partnership (1065)

### Document Request
```
Subject: [Year] Partnership Tax Document Request — [Entity Name]

Hi [Client Name],

For the [Year] Form 1065 for [Entity Name]:

□ Year-end P&L and balance sheet
□ Bank and credit card statements
□ Partner capital account activity (contributions, distributions)
□ Guaranteed payment amounts per partner
□ Asset purchases and dispositions
□ Loan statements
□ Partnership agreement (if new client or amended)
□ Partner allocation percentages (if changed)
□ Prior year return (if new client)
□ State filing requirements

Due: March 15 for K-1 delivery to partners.

Thanks!
[Firm Name]
```

## Nonprofit (990 / 990-EZ)

### Document Request
```
Subject: [Year] Form 990 Document Request — [Organization Name]

Hi [Client Name],

To prepare the [Year] Form 990 for [Organization Name]:

FINANCIALS:
□ Year-end financial statements (P&L + balance sheet)
□ Functional expense allocation (program vs. management vs. fundraising)
□ Bank statements (all accounts)

GOVERNANCE:
□ Board member list with titles and compensation
□ Key employee list with compensation
□ Board meeting minutes (or summary of meetings held)
□ Updated conflict of interest policy (if revised)

REVENUE:
□ Contribution/donation detail (top donors, grants received)
□ Program service revenue detail
□ Fundraising event income and expenses
□ Investment income

COMPLIANCE:
□ Any changes to mission, bylaws, or articles
□ Any related party transactions
□ Any new programs or discontinued programs
□ Lobbying or political activity (if any)
□ Foreign activities or grants (if any)

PUBLIC DISCLOSURE:
□ Confirm website URL is current
□ Confirm Form 990 is available for public inspection

Due: May 15 (or November 15 with extension).

Thanks!
[Firm Name]
```

## Trust (1041)

### Document Request
```
Subject: [Year] Trust Tax Document Request — [Trust Name]

Hi [Trustee Name],

For the [Year] Form 1041 for [Trust Name]:

□ Trust document (if new client)
□ 1099s received in the trust's name (INT, DIV, B, MISC)
□ K-1s from partnerships or S-Corps owned by the trust
□ Rental income and expense summaries (if trust owns property)
□ Distributions made to beneficiaries (amounts and dates)
□ Trustee fees paid
□ Legal and accounting fees paid
□ Investment management fees
□ Property tax payments
□ Any trust principal transactions (asset sales, purchases)
□ Beneficiary information (names, SSNs, addresses)
□ Prior year return (if new client)

Due: April 15 (or September 30 with extension).

Thanks!
[Firm Name]
```

## Reminder Sequence Templates

### Day 7 (Polite)
```
Subject: Friendly Reminder — [Year] Tax Documents

Hi [Name], just checking in! I sent over your document request list on [date]. 
No rush yet, but getting docs in early means a smoother filing season for everyone.
Let me know if you have any questions about what's needed.
```

### Day 14 (Firm)
```
Subject: Documents Needed — [Year] Tax Return

Hi [Name], I'm following up on your [Year] tax documents. 
We're about [X weeks] from the filing deadline and I still need several items.
Could you prioritize getting these to me this week? [List top 3 missing items]
```

### Day 21 (Urgent)
```
Subject: URGENT — Documents Needed for [Year] Filing

Hi [Name], your [return type] is due [date] and I'm still missing key documents.
Without them by [cutoff date], I'll need to file an extension.
An extension gives us more time to file but does NOT extend the payment deadline.
Please send what you have ASAP — even partial documents help me get started.
```

### Day 28 (Final / Extension Notice)
```
Subject: Extension Filed — [Year] [Return Type]

Hi [Name], since I haven't received your remaining documents, 
I've filed an extension for your [Year] [return type].
New deadline: [extension date]. Please get documents to me by [new target] 
so we have time for a thorough review before the extended deadline.
```
