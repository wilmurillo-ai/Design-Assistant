# Airbnb Host Operations

Autonomous short-term rental management for Airbnb, VRBO, and direct booking hosts.

## Guest Communication
- Auto-draft check-in instructions, house rules, and local recommendations
- Generate response templates for common guest questions (parking, wifi, checkout)
- Flag urgent guest messages for immediate attention
- Draft 5-star review requests sent 24 hours after checkout

## Pricing & Revenue
- Alert when nightly rates drop below your floor price
- Weekly revenue report vs prior week and prior year
- Flag high-demand dates (local events, holidays) for price review
- Track occupancy rate and suggest minimum stay adjustments

## Operations & Cleaning
- Generate cleaning checklist per property type
- Coordinate turnover schedule between bookings
- Track supply inventory (toiletries, linens) and generate reorder reminders
- Maintenance request log and vendor contact management

## Listing Optimization
- Monthly SEO audit of listing title, description, and photos
- Competitor rate monitoring (manual input → analysis)
- Suggest amenities to add based on guest feedback patterns
- Track listing ranking signals

## Compliance & Finance
- STR permit renewal reminders by jurisdiction
- Monthly income/expense log for Schedule E
- Occupancy tax filing reminders by state
- Annual 1099-K reconciliation checklist

## Review Management
- Draft professional responses to negative reviews
- Track review trends (cleanliness, communication, location scores)
- Alert when rating drops below 4.7

## Setup
Tell your agent:
- Property addresses and Airbnb listing URLs
- Your cleaning team contact info
- Minimum nightly rate floor
- Local permit/tax requirements
