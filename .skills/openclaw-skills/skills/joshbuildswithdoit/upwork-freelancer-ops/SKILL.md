# Upwork Freelancer Operations

Scale your Upwork income with less time on the platform.

## Job Scanning & Proposals
- Daily scan brief: summarize best-fit jobs posted in last 24 hours (input: job search URL)
- Draft tailored proposals from a job description (just paste the posting)
- Score job fit 1-10 based on your skills, rate, and history
- Track proposal → interview → contract conversion rates

## Client Management
- New client onboarding checklist (kickoff call, access, deliverables)
- Weekly status update templates per active contract
- Milestone completion checklist
- Contract closeout and review request sequence

## Income Tracking
- Weekly earnings summary vs target
- Monthly income report by client
- 90-day earnings forecast based on active contracts
- Flag if weekly earnings are tracking below target

## Rate & Positioning
- Track your hourly rate history and suggest increase timing
- Monitor competitor rates in your skill category (manual input)
- Draft updated profile bio and portfolio descriptions
- Identify skill gaps based on highest-paying job categories

## Repeat Business
- 30/60/90 day follow-up templates for past clients
- Holiday check-in messages for top clients
- Case study drafts from completed projects

## Platform Health
- Job Success Score monitoring alerts
- Review request timing (best: within 48 hours of contract end)
- Response time tracker (flag if you haven't replied within 24 hours)

## Setup
Tell your agent:
- Your Upwork profile URL and skill categories
- Current hourly rate and target rate
- Active contracts and milestones
- Top 5 past clients you want to re-engage
