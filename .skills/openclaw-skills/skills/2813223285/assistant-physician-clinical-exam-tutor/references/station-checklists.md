# Station Checklists

## Usage

Use these checklists to evaluate answers in mock examiner mode. Score each item as:

- `2` = complete and correct
- `1` = partially correct
- `0` = missing or unsafe

## 1) History Taking (问诊)

1. Confirm identity, main complaint, and duration.
2. Clarify symptom evolution, severity, and aggravating/relieving factors.
3. Ask associated symptoms and red-flag symptoms.
4. Review past history, medication, allergies, and surgery history.
5. Ask personal, family, and epidemiological history where relevant.
6. Summarize findings and verify with patient.

## 2) Physical Examination (体格检查)

1. Explain procedure and obtain consent.
2. Ensure hand hygiene and patient positioning.
3. Perform inspection, palpation, percussion, and auscultation in logical order.
4. State key positive and key negative findings explicitly.
5. Protect privacy and comfort throughout.
6. Close with concise interpretation.

## 3) Clinical Operation Skills (操作)

1. Verify indication, contraindication, and preparation.
2. Perform aseptic setup and instrument check.
3. Execute steps in correct order without skipping safety points.
4. Monitor patient response and complication signs.
5. Complete disposal, documentation, and post-procedure guidance.

## 4) Objective Question Review (客观题复盘)

1. Confirm tested knowledge point and blueprint module.
2. Identify why wrong option looked attractive.
3. Mark decisive clue that excludes distractors.
4. Summarize one-line rule for similar questions.
5. Attach one immediate variant question for retest.
6. Record to weak-point queue if repeated within 7 days.
