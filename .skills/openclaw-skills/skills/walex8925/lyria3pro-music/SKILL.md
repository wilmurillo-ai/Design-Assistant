# Lyria 3 Pro — Free AI Music Generator

Create full-length AI music tracks from text prompts, powered by Google's Lyria 3 Pro model.

**Website**: https://www.dh3-seedream.com

## What it does

Lyria 3 Pro is a free AI music generation tool that lets you create high-quality music tracks simply by describing what you want. Built on Google's flagship Lyria 3 Pro model, it supports structure-aware composition with intro, verse, chorus, and bridge sections — up to 3 minutes per track.

## Key features

- **Text-to-music** — describe your track in natural language and generate it instantly
- **Structure-aware** — generates compositions with proper musical structure (intro/verse/chorus/bridge)
- **Up to 3 minutes** — create full-length tracks, not just short clips
- **Multiple genres** — pop, rock, electronic, classical, jazz, ambient, and more
- **No music theory required** — just describe the mood, style, and instruments you want
- **Download ready** — export your tracks for use in videos, podcasts, or projects

## Quick start

1. Visit https://www.dh3-seedream.com
2. Enter a prompt describing your desired music
3. Choose a generation mode
4. Click Create and wait for your track
5. Preview and download
