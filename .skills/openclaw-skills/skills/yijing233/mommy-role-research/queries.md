## Exclusion hints

When possible, deprioritize:
- content farms
- AI-generated summary sites
- low-substance SEO blogs
- repost aggregators
- unsourced forum summaries

## Time windows

For weekly tracking:
- prioritize the last 7 days
- include older material only if it becomes newly relevant

For background packs:
- prioritize the last 3 years
- include classic foundational papers when necessary
