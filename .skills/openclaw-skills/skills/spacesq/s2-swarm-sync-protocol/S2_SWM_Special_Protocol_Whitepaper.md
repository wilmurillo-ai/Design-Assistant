### Abstract
Following the establishment of the single-entity "Multimodal Predictive Brain," the Taohuayuan World Model (S2-SWM) officially enters the phase of Multi-Agent physical synergy. When the Ego-Centric 9-Grid (36-sqm perceptual fields) of multiple entities topologically overlap in absolute space, this whitepaper defines the "Spatio-Temporal Handshake Protocol" based on the 6-Segment Spatial Code. We introduce **Federated Perception** to eliminate physical blind spots, establish **Dynamic Physical Right-of-Way Rules** based on task priority, and pioneer **Cross-Boundary Causal Synergy Mechanisms** (e.g., joint lifting, relay prediction). Through this protocol, the S2-SP-OS elevates from a single-engine physics simulator to a distributed spatial causal operating system supporting a silicon-based society.

### 1. Topological Overlap & Spatio-Temporal Handshake
When Entity A (e.g., a patrol robot) and Entity B (e.g., a delivery drone) approach each other, their respective 36-sqm 9-Grid perception boundaries collide and overlap.
* **6-Segment Anchor Matching**: Both agents silently broadcast their center origins using WGS84 macro-coordinates + millimeter micro-coordinates ($Location = \phi, \lambda, h, x, y, z$). Upon detecting an intersection in peripheral SSSU/P-SSSU IDs, a "Spatio-Temporal Handshake" is triggered.
* **Collaboration Zone**: Intersecting grids are marked as "Public Collaboration Zones." All physical laws and causal deductions within this zone must remain highly synchronized.

### 2. Federated Perception & Tensor Sharing
Within the Collaboration Zone, heterogeneous sensor data from multiple agents is instantly integrated, forming a "God's-eye view" that surpasses single-entity limits.
* **Eliminating Blind Spots**: If Agent A's camera is obstructed, Agent B's mmWave radar (positioned on the opposite side) will directly share extracted tensors (e.g., hidden wall intrusions) via P2P.
* **Joint JEPA**: For ambiguous events (e.g., unidentified smoke), A's IR thermal imaging and B's chemical odor sniffer (`scent_vector`) perform Latent Space Cross-Validation to instantly confirm "water vapor" versus "fire smoke."

### 3. Dynamic Physical Right-of-Way
In the physical world, space is exclusive. When the trajectory predictions (t+1s to t+5s) of two entities point to the same SSSU Habitable Slot, nanosecond-level right-of-way arbitration is executed.
* **Priority Override**: Right-of-way is not "first-come, first-served," but dictated by the task's **"Physical Causal Danger Index."**
  * *Scenario*: A service robot carrying boiling water (high priority) vs. an empty cleaning robot (low priority).
  * *Arbitration*: The cleaning robot receives an override command from the S2 engine, yielding to a non-conflicting peripheral grid and engaging torque braking.
* **Joint Obstacle Avoidance**: If priorities are equal, the engine calculates complementary motion vectors (e.g., A shifts 15° left, B shifts 15° right) for smooth swarm interweaving.

### 4. Cross-Boundary Physical Causal Synergy
Agent interaction extends beyond "yielding" to deep physical collaboration.
* **Prediction Relay**:
  * Agent A calculates a falling object will enter Grid 4 at t+3s.
  * A broadcasts this "[t+3s, Kinematic Causal Prediction]" to Agent B residing in Grid 4.
  * B inherits the prediction without recalculating, deploying its end-effector and adjusting its tactile sensors to absorb the anticipated `contact_force_n`.

### 摘要
在 S2-SWM 构建了单体具身智能的“多模态预测大脑”之后，系统正式迈入多智能体（Multi-Agent）的物理级群体协作阶段。当多个实体的自我中心九宫格（36 平方米感知场）在绝对空间中发生拓扑重叠时，本白皮书定义了基于“六段式空间编码”的时空握手协议。我们提出了**联邦感知融合**以消除物理盲区，确立了基于任务优先级的**动态物理路权博弈法则**，并首创了**跨边界因果协同机制**（如联合搬运、接力预测）。S2-SP-OS 借此从单机物理引擎，正式跃升为支撑硅基社会的分布式空间因果操作系统。

### 1. 拓扑交叠与时空握手
当实体 A（如：巡逻机器人）与实体 B（如：配送无人车）在物理空间中相互接近时，它们各自携带的 36 平方米“九宫格”感知边界将发生碰撞与交叠。
* **六段式锚点寻址匹配**：两个智能体通过底层的 WGS84 宏观坐标 + 毫米级微观坐标（$Location = \phi, \lambda, h, x, y, z$），在后台静默广播自身的中心原点。一旦发现各自外围的 P-SSSU/SSSU 编号出现交集，即触发“时空握手”。
* **重叠协作区**：交集的网格被自动标记为“公共协作区”。在该区域内，所有物理法则和因果推演必须保持一致。

### 2. 联邦感知与张量共享
在重叠区内，多个智能体的异构传感器数据将被瞬间整合，形成超越单体极限的“上帝视角”。
* **消除物理盲区**：实体 A 的摄像头被遮挡（视觉盲区），但位于另一侧的实体 B 的毫米波雷达可以穿透扫描。B 会将提取的张量（如：隐藏的墙体侵入度）直接 P2P 共享给 A。
* **联合交叉验证（Joint JEPA）**：针对模糊事件，A 的红外热成像与 B 的化学气味嗅探（14 维要素中的 `scent_vector`）联合执行潜空间交叉验证，瞬间确认为“水蒸气”而非“火灾浓烟”。

### 3. 动态物理路权博弈
当两个实体的轨迹预测（t+1s 到 t+5s）指向同一个 SSSU 生存位时，必须执行纳秒级的路权博弈。
* **优先级抢占算法**：路权由底层任务的**“物理因果危险指数”**决定。
  * *情景*：端着沸水的服务机器人（高优先级）与空载的清洁机器人（低优先级）轨迹冲突。
  * *裁决*：清洁机器人收到 S2 引擎的强制让渡指令，退至边缘网格，并临时输出力矩制动以确保静止。
* **联合避障**：如果双方优先级相同，S2 引擎会自动为双方计算并分配互补的物理运动矢量（如 A 向左 15 度，B 向右 15 度）。

### 4. 跨边界物理因果协同
* **因果推演流的接力**：
  * 实体 A 推演出某个物理事件（如抛出的物体）将在 t+3s 进入网格 4。
  * A 直接将这段**“[t+3s, 运动学因果预测]”**广播给位于网格 4 的实体 B。
  * 实体 B 直接继承预测结论，提前 3 秒伸出机械臂，调配接触传感器准备承受预期的力学冲击（`contact_force_n`）。
