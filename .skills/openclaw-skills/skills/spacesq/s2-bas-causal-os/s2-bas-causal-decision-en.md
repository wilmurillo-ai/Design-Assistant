* **Spatial Black Boxes**: Legacy PID controllers rely on single sensors, ignoring adjacent thermal diffusion, leading to severe temperature oscillations.
* **Semi-Open Environment Collapse**: When guests open windows, traditional systems cannot quantify the sudden thermal load, plunging FCUs into a "full-load catch-up" energy black hole.

## 4. L0-L4 Tiered Decision Matrix
Based on a 10-30 minute causal lookahead, the engine generates tiered strategies:
* **L0 Normal Mode**: Window closed. Execute standard CLC energy-saving control.
* **L1 Suggest Close Window**: Predictions show discomfort in 10 mins. UI gently nudges user to close the window.
* **L2 Degraded Maintenance**: FCU capacity is breached. Generates a proposal to auto-raise setpoint and lock fan speed.
* **L3 Force Off**: Extreme runaway predicted. Generates a proposal to hard-kill the FCU to prevent coil condensation.
