# Source Heuristics

These are tie-breakers layered on top of the shared taxonomy.

Email:
- elevate account security, billing, business access, bookings, and deadline-bound admin
- downgrade newsletters, broad promotions, and routine announcements

Private mail:
- elevate privacy, banking, purchases, and logistics confirmations
- downgrade retail offers and generic promotions

Chat:
- elevate direct asks, approvals, clarifications, and sensitive preferences
- downgrade acknowledgements and low-information chatter

Operational messaging:
- elevate urgent real-world coordination and short clarifications that unblock motion
- downgrade duplicate chatter and messages without clear context

Community chat:
- elevate direct mentions and scoped project responsibilities
- downgrade ambient conversation and already-answered threads

Rule hierarchy:
1. hard safety rules
2. explicit registries
3. source-specific heuristics
4. generic fallback
