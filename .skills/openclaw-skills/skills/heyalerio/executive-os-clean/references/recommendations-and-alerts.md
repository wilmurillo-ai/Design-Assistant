# Recommendations And Alerts

Recommendation layer:
- attach a best next action when it helps
- include one-line reasoning
- show confidence when useful

Confidence:
- `high` for strong evidence and low ambiguity
- `medium` for likely-right suggestions
- `low` for incomplete context

Prefer recommendations that are:
- reversible
- low-risk
- momentum-preserving
- aligned with explicit user constraints

Alert levels:
- `Immediate alert` for time-sensitive or risky issues
- `Digest-only` for most important work
- `Watch only` for weak or informational signals

Immediate alert triggers usually include:
- account security
- money, legal, or billing risk
- travel or booking issues that expire soon
- health or safety issues
- same-day human dependency
- business access problems that block operations

Do not alert just because something is unread.
