# Screenshot Plan

## Goal

Prepare a small but strong visual set that explains the product in under 10 seconds on GitHub and ClawHub.

## Built Promo Art

The repository now includes ready-to-use SVG renders:

- `assets/github-hero.svg`
- `assets/robot-showcase.svg`
- `assets/visualization-showcase.svg`
- `assets/clawhub-cover.svg`

These can carry the page visually even before final live screenshots are captured.

## Required Live Shots

1. Hero dashboard
- Show avatar, title area, status, and score
- Keep the theme balanced and readable
- Use a robot style that feels friendly but competent

2. Trend and metrics
- Show the efficiency chart with clear card hierarchy
- Keep score and token-related signals visible together
- Include one or two surrounding stat cards

3. Chat and control
- Show the chat panel plus nudge / heal controls
- Make it obvious that this is interactive, not just decorative

4. Customization
- Open the settings drawer
- Show avatar controls, headwear, hands, wings, and background effects

5. Personality shot
- Focus on the avatar rail or a tight hero crop
- This is the cute robot marketing image for listings and social sharing

## Visual Rules

- Avoid noisy particle states in the final screenshots
- Keep layout clean and premium
- Prefer alive-looking robot states over exaggerated comedy poses
- Do not capture broken experiments or clipped limb states
