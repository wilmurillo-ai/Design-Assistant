# Jupiter

> When choices multiply, bad decisions usually come from bad routing.

Jupiter is a gravity center for best-path decision making.

## What it does

This skill helps you:
- compare multiple options
- identify the strongest route
- expose hidden tradeoffs
- reduce fragmented decision-making
- choose under real constraints instead of surface appeal

## What it does NOT do

- execute trades
- replace legal or tax advice
- provide regulated investment advice
- guarantee best outcome under uncertainty
