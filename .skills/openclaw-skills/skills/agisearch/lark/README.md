# Lark Digital Command Center (v2.0.1)

> Navigate the organizational landscape with precision. Don't just act—resolve.

Version 2.0.1 adds a **Coordination Diagnosis Engine**, turning Lark from a high-quality automation layer into a tactical command center that understands where friction actually lives.

## New in v2.0.1: Coordination Diagnosis

Before acting, the skill now:
1. **Detects friction** — is this a chat overload problem, an approval bottleneck, a meeting follow-through gap, or a stakeholder sensitivity issue?
2. **Selects the right layer** — Chat, Approval, Meeting, Spreadsheet, Calendar, or Docs
3. **Calibrates the action** — Summarize, Draft, Nudge, Sync, Escalate, or Hold

## Core Pillars

- **Handshake Protocol**: choose operating mode (Counselor vs Executive)
- **Diagnosis before action**: understand the coordination problem before moving
- **Red-line protection**: upward, cross-functional, and other high-sensitivity actions remain guarded
- **Context recovery**: prioritize what was decided, not just what was said

## Safety

Instruction-only design. No bundled network code. Depends on the host platform’s trusted Lark connector.

## Best Fit

- teams with heavy chat, meetings, docs, approvals, and spreadsheet coordination
- managers who need both speed and stakeholder sensitivity
- organizations where collaboration friction is often hidden inside normal workflow noise
