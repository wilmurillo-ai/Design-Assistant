# Meeting: The Execution Operator (v2.0.0)

> Transform synchronous chaos into structured execution. Stop just attending meetings—start operating them.

## The 2.0.0 Shift
Meeting 2.0.0 moves from a passive recorder to a **Diagnostic Operator**. It helps you identify why meetings fail and ensures that decisions are captured and tracked without needing a human scribe.

## File Structure
- `skill.md`: The core logic, persona, and diagnostic engine.
- `skill.json`: Metadata and capability declarations.
- `examples.md`: Practical scenarios for pre-meeting, capture, and tracking.

## Core Pillars
- **Coordination Diagnosis**: Identifying gaps in prep or follow-through.
- **Draft-First Policy**: Nothing is distributed or tracked without your "Go."
- **Institutional Memory**: Remembering what was decided six meetings ago.

## Safety
No autonomous recording. No data leakage. Purely logic-driven orchestration via your trusted platform connectors.
