# Platform Guidelines - Social Media Posting Rules

## Twitter/X

### Posting Rules
- **Character Limit**: 280 characters (hard limit)
- **Media Limit**: Up to 4 images, 1 GIF, or 1 video per tweet
- **Link Preview**: Automatically generated, counts as ~23 characters
- **Thread Limit**: Up to 25 tweets in a thread

### Best Practices
- Use hooks in first 50 characters
- Add 1-2 relevant hashtags
- Include 1 call-to-action
- Post 3-5 times daily maximum
- Optimal times: 9am, 12pm, 5pm (your audience's timezone)

### Content Guidelines
- Be concise and punchy
- Use emojis sparingly
- Share insights, not promotions
- Engage with replies within 1 hour
- Retweet relevant content daily

### What's Not Allowed
- Spam behavior (excessive posting, automation abuse)
- Duplicate content across accounts
- Misinformation
- Harassment or hate speech
- Copyrighted content without permission

## Instagram

### Posting Rules
- **Caption Limit**: 2,200 characters (hashtags included)
- **Hashtag Limit**: 30 hashtags maximum
- **Image Size**: 1080 x 1080 pixels (square), 1080 x 1350 (portrait)
- **Video Length**: 60 seconds (feed), 15 seconds (stories)
- **Reel Length**: 90 seconds maximum

### Best Practices
- Write hooks in first 125 characters (visible before "more")
- Use 11 hashtags for optimal reach
- Post to stories daily
- Use 5-10 relevant hashtags in first comment
- Respond to comments within 2 hours

### Content Guidelines
- High-quality, visually consistent images
- Carousel posts for educational content
- Reels for reach (favored by algorithm)
- User-generated content builds community
- Save-worthy content drives engagement

### Engagement Rate Benchmarks
- <1%: Low
- 1-3.5%: Average
- 3.5-6%: High
- >6%: Very High

### What's Not Allowed
- Mass following/unfollowing
- Comment pods (fake engagement)
- Watermarked content
- Misleading captions
- Excessive promotional content

## LinkedIn

### Posting Rules
- **Post Length**: 3,000 characters maximum
- **Optimal Length**: 700-1,500 characters
- **Hashtag Limit**: 3-5 hashtags recommended
- **Document Size**: PDF up to 100MB
- **Video Length**: 10 minutes maximum

### Best Practices
- Write personal stories or insights
- Use professional, authentic tone
- Post 2-5 times per week
- Include 3 relevant hashtags at end
- Engage with comments within 4 hours

### Content Types That Work
- Personal professional stories
- Industry insights and trends
- How-to guides and tips
- Company announcements
- Employee spotlights
- Industry memes (occasionally)

### Engagement Rate Benchmarks
- <1%: Low
- 1-2%: Average
- 2-4%: High
- >4%: Very High

### What's Not Allowed
- Fake engagement or bots
- Promotional spam
- Misleading content
- Harassment
- Copyright infringement

---

## Facebook

### Posting Rules
- **Post Length**: 63,206 characters (practically unlimited)
- **Optimal Length**: 80-150 words for organic reach
- **Video Length**: 240 minutes maximum
- **Hashtag Limit**: 1-3 hashtags (optional)
- **Image Size**: 1200 x 630 pixels (link), 1080 x 1080 (image)

### Best Practices
- Post 1-2 times daily maximum
- Use community-friendly tone
- Ask questions to drive comments
- Share behind-the-scenes content
- Respond within 2 hours

### Content Guidelines
- Short, scannable posts perform best
- Native video > shared links
- Images drive engagement
- Stories for daily presence
- Events for promotions

### Engagement Rate Benchmarks
- <0.5%: Low
- 0.5-1.5%: Average
- 1.5-3.5%: High
- >3.5%: Very High

### What's Not Allowed
- Engagement bait ("like if...")
- Misleading content
- Spam behavior
- False news
- Unauthorized copyrighted content

## Global Platform Rules

### Content That Violates All Platforms
- Hate speech and harassment
- Violence and gore
- Spam and scams
- Misinformation
- Copyrighted material (without license)
- Privacy violations
- Sexually explicit content
- Illegal activities

### General Best Practices
1. **Consistency** > Frequency
2. **Quality** > Quantity
3. **Engage** > Broadcast
4. **Authentic** > Perfect
5. **Value** > Promotion
