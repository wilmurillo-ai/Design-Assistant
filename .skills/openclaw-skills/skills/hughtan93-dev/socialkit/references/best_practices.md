# Social Media Best Practices - Content Strategy Tips

## Content Strategy Fundamentals

### The 80/20 Rule
- **80% Value**: Educational, entertaining, inspiring content
- **20% Promotion**: Product/service promotion
- Balance builds trust before selling

### Content Pillars
Establish 3-5 content pillars:
1. **Educational**: Tips, how-tos, tutorials
2. **Inspirational**: Motivation, success stories
3. **Entertainment**: Memes, fun facts, behind-the-scenes
4. **Promotional**: Products, services, offers
5. **Community**: UGC, questions, polls

## Content Creation Tips

### Writing Hooks That Work
```
Before: "Here are 5 tips for better productivity"
After: "I tried 5 productivity hacks for 30 days - here's what happened"

Before: "Our new feature is launching"
After: "We spent 6 months building this feature. Here's why it almost didn't happen."

Before: "Tips for better marketing"
After: "99% of businesses make this marketing mistake"
```

### Creating Scroll-Stopping Headlines
- Use numbers ("7 ways...", "5 reasons...")
- Create curiosity gaps
- Make bold claims
- Use power words: Secret, Ultimate, Proven, Essential
- Address pain points directly

### Call-to-Actions That Convert
- **Save-worthy**: "Save this for later!"
- **Comment-driven**: "What's your take? Drop it below"
- **Share-friendly**: "Tag someone who needs to see this"
- **Question-based**: "Which one resonates with you?"

## Platform-Specific Tips

### Twitter/X
- Tweet when your audience is active (analyze your metrics)
- Engage in conversations, don't just broadcast
- Use threads for complex topics
- Retweet with comment adds value
- Reply to others to build relationships

### Instagram
- Stories = daily presence (post 3-5 daily)
- Reels = reach (create weekly)
- Carousels = saves (educational content)
- Live = engagement (weekly)
- DMs = relationships (respond to all)

### LinkedIn
- Personal brand > Company page
- Document posts perform well
- First 3 lines matter most
- Comments drive more reach than posts
- Connect with purpose (not just numbers)

### Facebook
- Groups > Page (for community)
- Video > Images > Links
- Questions drive comments
- Respond to ALL comments
- Post when fans are online

## Engagement Best Practices

### Responding to Comments
- Respond within 1-2 hours
- Be personal, not generic
- Ask follow-up questions
- Add value to the conversation
- Show personality

### Building Community
- Ask questions regularly
- Feature user content
- Celebrate community members
- Host giveaways/contests
- Create专属 hashtags

### Handling Negative Feedback
- Don't delete (unless abusive)
- Respond professionally
- Take it offline when needed
- Learn from criticism
- Thank them for feedback

## Hashtag Strategy

### Finding Right Hashtags
1. Check competitors' hashtags
2. Use platform's suggested tags
3. Research niche-specific tags
4. Mix broad + medium + niche
5. Track performance over time

### Hashtag Formulas
```
Post engagement: [broad hashtag] + [niche hashtag] + [1 branded]

Educational: [topic] + [education] + [tips] + [learning]

Business: [industry] + [business] + [motivation] + [brand]

Location: [broad] + [location] + [niche]
```

### Platform-Specific Limits
- Twitter: 1-3 hashtags
- Instagram: 5-15 hashtags (30 max)
- LinkedIn: 3-5 hashtags
- Facebook: 1-3 hashtags

## Content Calendar Best Practices

### Planning Frequency
- **Monthly**: Theme + campaigns
- **Weekly**: Content pillars + themes
- **Daily**: Actual posting

### Weekly Content Mix (Example)
| Day | Content Type | Platform Focus |
|-----|--------------|----------------|
| Monday | Motivation/Goals | LinkedIn, Twitter |
| Tuesday | Educational | LinkedIn, Instagram |
| Wednesday | Product/Feature | All platforms |
| Thursday | Community/UGC | Facebook, Instagram |
| Friday | Engagement/Poll | Twitter, Instagram |
| Saturday | Behind-the-scenes | Instagram, Facebook |
| Sunday | Curated/Planning | Repurpose content |

### Batch Creating Content
- Create content in batches (1 day/week)
- Use templates for consistency
- Repurpose across platforms
- Schedule in advance
- Leave room for trending topics

## Analytics & Optimization

### Key Metrics to Track
- **Reach**: How many saw it
- **Engagement Rate**: Interactions/Reach
- **Follower Growth**: Net new followers
- **Click-through Rate**: Link clicks/Reach
- **Save Rate**: Saves/Reach (Instagram)

### What to Test
- Posting times
- Content formats
- Hashtag combinations
- CTA styles
- Caption lengths

### Optimization Loop
1. Post content
2. Track metrics
3. Identify top performers
4. Analyze what worked
5. Apply learnings
6. Repeat

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Content Mistakes
- ❌ Only promotional content
- ❌ Posting without clear CTA
- ❌ Ignoring comments/messages
- ❌ Inconsistent posting schedule
- ❌ Not repurposing content

### Strategy Mistakes
- ❌ Chasing vanity metrics
- ❌ Ignoring analytics
- ❌ Not defining target audience
- ❌ Trying to be everywhere at once
- ❌ Not having a brand voice

### Engagement Mistakes
- ❌ Buying followers
- ❌ Using engagement pods
- ❌ Generic responses
- ❌ Not replying to DMs
- ❌ Only responding to positive feedback

## Pro Tips

### Content Repurposing
- 1 long-form content = 5+ social posts
- 1 video = Reel + Story + Twitter thread
- 1 blog post = Infographic + Quotes + Carousel

### Building Personal Brand
- Share professional journey
- Be consistent with posting
- Engage with industry leaders
- Provide value freely
- Show behind-the-scenes

### Automation Best Practices
- Automate scheduling (not engagement)
- Personalize automated responses
- Maintain human touch
- Don't over-automate
- Monitor all automated actions
