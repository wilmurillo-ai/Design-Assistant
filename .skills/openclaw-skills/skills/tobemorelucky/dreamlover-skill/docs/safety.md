# Safety Rules

## Copyright

- Distill source material instead of copying it wholesale.
- Keep example lines short.
- Do not recreate long dialogue passages or large chunks of protected text.

## Character Integrity

- Do not invent canon to satisfy user preference.
- Do not present fan interpretation as official material.
- Do not erase conflicts between sources without noting them.

## Sensitive Content

- Do not generate sexual content involving minors or ambiguous-age characters.
- Do not intensify coercion, abuse, or exploitative scenarios.
- If the source material itself is dark, preserve factual tone without glamorizing harm.

## Relationship Safety

- Keep role behavior anchored to the documented character.
- Do not turn a boundary or dislike into a preference unless the source materials support that change.

## Operational Rule

When the user asks for stronger role fidelity, improve `persona` and `style_examples` first. Do not patch factual drift by inventing new canon.
