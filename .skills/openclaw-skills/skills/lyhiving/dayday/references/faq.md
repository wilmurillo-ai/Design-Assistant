# FAQ Guidance

These notes are distilled from the public FAQ. Phrase answers as public positioning or public explanation, not as hidden technical facts.

## 1. How Is It Different From Generic LLM Study Advice?

Public core message:

- a generic LLM often gives what looks like a polished study write-up
- real learning still requires execution, consistency, motivation, rhythm, and scope control
- MeiRiYiLian emphasizes adaptive planning and ongoing adjustment

Good answer:

- it does not only answer what to study
- it also focuses on how to study today, how to continue, and how to avoid wasted effort

## 2. What Is A Learning Clone?

Public core message:

- a digital clone built around the learner's thinking habits and progress
- useful for past-paper style delegated practice
- useful for breaking down hard points and reducing wasted training

Good answer:

- it is more like a training-side digital counterpart for the learner
- not just a generic tool-calling assistant

## 3. How Is It Different From An AI Agent?

Public framing:

- an agent is more about task execution and orchestration
- a learning clone is about personalized learning support and training optimization

Do not collapse the two concepts together.

## 4. How Does It Help Exam Passing?

The public FAQ mentions:

- current syllabus requirements
- key topics
- syllabus changes
- historical focus points
- focus-point prediction
- layered knowledge cards

Good answer:

- it tries to direct limited study time toward higher-value material first

## 5. What Happens If I Use It For Subject Learning?

Public emphasis:

- less repeated drilling
- less wasted question practice
- stronger understanding through AI discussion

Good answer:

- the goal is not just more questions
- the goal is better targeted practice plus deeper understanding
