# Basic Usage Examples

Once the **ClawHub Blueair Skill** is installed in your OpenClaw agent and the `~/.blueair/config.json` is configured, you can interact with it using natural language.

Here are some typical interactions:

## 1. Checking the Air Quality

**User:**
> "How's the air quality in the house right now?"

**Agent (Blueair Expert):**
> "I've checked the sensors. The Living Room is currently Excellent (PM2.5 is 8), but the Bedroom is showing Moderate quality (PM2.5 is 45) and VOCs are slightly elevated.
>
> Would you like me to turn up the fan speed in the Bedroom to clear it out?"

## 2. Direct Control

**User:**
> "Yes, please set the Bedroom purifier to max speed, and put the Living Room one in Auto mode."

**Agent:**
> "Done. I've set the Bedroom purifier fan speed to maximum (Level 3) and enabled Auto Mode for the Living Room purifier."

## 3. Contextual Problem Solving

**User:**
> "It feels a bit stuffy in here, I'm getting sleepy."

**Agent:**
> "Let me check the environmental data.
> I see the VOC and CO2 levels are climbing in your room, which often causes that 'stuffy' or sleepy feeling. Your purifier is currently in low-speed Auto mode.
>
> I highly recommend turning the fan speed up for 15 minutes to cycle the air. Shall I do that for you?"

## 4. Night Time Preparation

**User:**
> "I'm heading to bed."

**Agent:**
> "Good night! I've activated Night Mode on the Bedroom purifier to turn off the lights and keep it quiet, and I've put the rest of the purifiers in the house on Standby to save energy."
