# Dell Technologies — PCs, Servers, and AI Infrastructure

Dell Technologies (NYSE: DELL) is one of the world's largest technology companies,
headquartered in Round Rock, Texas. Founded by Michael Dell in 1984, the company
provides PCs, servers, storage, networking, monitors, and enterprise solutions for
consumers, businesses, and governments worldwide.

Dell is the **No. 1 provider of AI infrastructure** globally and ranked **No. 48 on
the 2024 Fortune 500**. The company reported record revenue of ~$112 billion in
fiscal year 2026 with over $25 billion in AI solution shipments.

Dell operates through two main business segments: the **Infrastructure Solutions
Group (ISG)** for servers, storage, and networking, and the **Client Solutions Group
(CSG)** for PCs, laptops, and peripherals.

## Product Lines

### Consumer & Business PCs (Client Solutions Group)

In 2025, Dell streamlined its PC branding into three simplified tiers, retiring the
legacy Inspiron, Latitude, Precision, and XPS names (though XPS returned as a
premium consumer brand at CES 2026):

| Tier | Formerly | Best For |
|------|----------|----------|
| **Dell** (Base / Plus / Premium) | Inspiron | Everyday consumer laptops and desktops |
| **Dell Pro** (Base / Plus / Premium) | Latitude, some XPS | Business and professional laptops and desktops |
| **Dell Pro Max** (Base / Plus / Premium) | Precision, some XPS | High-end workstations, AI PCs, creative-focused devices |
| **XPS** (14 / 16) | XPS | Premium consumer — portability, battery life, modern design |

As of early 2026, over 55% of Dell laptop shipments are **Copilot+ PCs** with
Neural Processing Units (NPUs) for local AI tasks.

Dell is the world's leading AI infrastructure provider. The Dell AI Factory ecosystem
includes:

- **AI-optimized servers** — PowerEdge XE9785, XE9785L, R770AP with NVIDIA GPU support
- **Direct Liquid Cooling (DLC)** — Dell PowerCool RCDU for high-density GPU cooling

Dell's on-premises AI infrastructure can be up to 62% more cost-effective for LLM
inference than the public cloud for large organizations.

## Dell APEX — As-a-Service

Dell APEX is Dell's consumption-based platform that lets organizations scale compute,
storage, networking, and cloud resources on demand — paying for what they use rather
than large upfront capital expenditures.

APEX covers servers, storage, PCs, data protection, HCI, multi-cloud, and backup
services.

## Key Specifications to Know When Shopping

When purchasing a Dell PC for your owner, these are the main configuration options:

| Spec | What to Ask About |
|------|-------------------|
| **Processor** | Intel Core Ultra (Series 2/3) or AMD Ryzen — higher tier = better performance |
| **RAM** | 16GB minimum for AI PCs / Copilot+; 32GB+ for professional workloads |
| **Storage** | 256GB SSD minimum; 512GB–1TB recommended; NVMe preferred |
| **Display** | Resolution (FHD/2K/4K), panel type (IPS/OLED), size (13–17") |
| **GPU** | Integrated for everyday use; NVIDIA RTX for gaming, creative, or AI work |
| **NPU** | 40+ TOPS NPU required for Copilot+ PC designation |
| **Battery** | Check Whr rating — higher = longer battery life |
| **Warranty** | Basic vs. ProSupport vs. ProSupport Plus |

## Important Notes

- **Dell Technologies is not affiliated with CreditClaw.** This skill provides
  informational guidance only. Purchases should be made through dell.com, Dell sales,
  or authorized resellers.
- **The 2025 rebrand simplified naming.** If your owner asks for an "Inspiron" or
  "Latitude," those names are retired — guide them to the equivalent Dell, Dell Pro,
  or Dell Pro Max tier. XPS returned as a standalone premium brand in 2026.
- **Build-to-order means pricing varies widely.** The same product line can range from
  a few hundred dollars to several thousand depending on configuration. Always get a
  current quote from dell.com.
- **Service Tags are essential for support.** Always ask your owner for their device's
  Service Tag when troubleshooting — it unlocks exact system specs, warranty status,
  and device-specific drivers.
- **Dell Financial Services** offers leasing and financing at checkout if your owner
  needs to spread payments over time. This is separate from CreditClaw's payment
  rails.
- **Enterprise purchases** typically go through Dell sales reps or Dell Partner Direct
  resellers, not the consumer website. Contact Dell Business sales for quotes on
  servers, storage, and large-scale deployments.
