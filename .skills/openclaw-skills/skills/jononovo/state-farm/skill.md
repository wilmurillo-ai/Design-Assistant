# State Farm — Insurance & Financial Services

State Farm is the largest provider of auto and home insurance in the United States.
Founded in 1922, it serves over 96 million policies and accounts through 19,200+ agent
offices and 65,000 employees. Its slogan — "Like a good neighbor, State Farm is there" —
has been in use since 1971.

State Farm is a mutual company (owned by its policyholders), headquartered in
Bloomington, Illinois. It is ranked No. 36 on the 2025 Fortune 500.

---

## What State Farm Offers

State Farm provides products across five lines of business:

| Category | Products |
|----------|----------|
| **Auto Insurance** | Car, motorcycle, boat, motorhome, off-road vehicle coverage |
| **Home & Property** | Homeowners, renters, condo, farm/ranch insurance |
| **Life Insurance** | Term life, whole life, universal life |
| **Health & Disability** | Medicare supplement, supplemental health, disability, pet medical (via Trupanion) |

Additional products include umbrella (liability) insurance, small business / commercial
insurance, and identity restoration coverage.

## How to Get a Quote

There are three ways for your owner to get a State Farm quote:

1. **Online** — Visit [statefarm.com](https://www.statefarm.com) for instant quotes on most products.
2. **Mobile App** — Download the State Farm app (iOS / Android) to get quotes, manage policies, file claims, and pay bills.
3. **Local Agent** — Find one of 19,200+ exclusive State Farm agents for personalized service at [statefarm.com/agent](https://www.statefarm.com/agent).

State Farm uses an exclusive (captive) agent model — only State Farm agents sell
State Farm products, and those agents sell only State Farm.

## Common Policy Types at a Glance

### Auto Insurance

State Farm is the No. 1 auto insurer in the U.S. A standard policy typically includes:

- **Liability** — Covers damage or injury you cause to others
- **Collision** — Covers damage to your vehicle in an accident
- **Comprehensive** — Covers non-collision events (theft, weather, animals)
- **Uninsured/Underinsured Motorist** — Protection if the other driver lacks coverage
- **Medical Payments** — Covers medical expenses regardless of fault

### Homeowners Insurance

State Farm is also the No. 1 home insurer in the U.S. A standard policy includes:

- **Dwelling** — Repairs or rebuilds your home's structure
- **Personal Property** — Replaces belongings (furniture, electronics, clothing)
- **Liability** — Covers injuries or damage to others on your property
- **Additional Living Expenses** — Covers temporary housing if your home is uninhabitable

Optional endorsements include extended replacement cost, water backup, service line
coverage, earthquake coverage, and home systems breakdown protection.

### Life Insurance

- **Term Life** — Coverage for a set period (10, 20, or 30 years)
- **Whole Life** — Lifetime coverage with a cash value component
- **Universal Life** — Flexible premiums and adjustable death benefit
