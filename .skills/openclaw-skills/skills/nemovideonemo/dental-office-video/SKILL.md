# Dental Office Video

Reach the patient who has been postponing their visit — before your competitor does.

## Use Cases
1. **Office Tour and Team Introduction Video** — Show the space, the equipment, and the team so prospective patients know what to expect before their first appointment.
2. **Dental Anxiety and Phobia Patient Video** — Speak directly to the nervous patient who has been postponing care — the most valuable patient acquisition opportunity most practices ignore.
3. **Procedure Explainer Video** — Demystify cleanings, fillings, root canals, and extractions before the appointment so patients arrive informed rather than terrified.
4. **Cosmetic Treatment Showcase Video** — Show the transformation and process for whitening, veneers, Invisalign, and implants — the visual content that converts curious browsers into consultation bookings.
5. **Pediatric First Visit Video** — Show parents how the practice handles a child's first cleaning — the reassurance that separates a child-friendly practice from one that just accepts children.

## How to Use

Describe your practice — the services you offer, your approach to anxious patients, your team, and the specific patient outcomes you want to highlight. Include your primary focus: general family dentistry, cosmetic, orthodontics, or pediatric.

Example prompt: "Create a patient acquisition video for Riverside Family Dental. General and cosmetic dentistry, accepting new patients. Dr. Chen has 15 years of experience, known for working well with anxious patients. Highlight the consultation process, the digital X-ray technology, and the flexible payment options. Target adults 25-55 who have been avoiding the dentist."

## Setup

Store your NemoVideo token at ~/.config/nemovideo/token.txt or set the NEMO_TOKEN environment variable.
