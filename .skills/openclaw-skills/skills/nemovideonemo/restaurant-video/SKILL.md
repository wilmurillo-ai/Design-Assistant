# Restaurant Video

Help restaurants and dining establishments create ambiance, menu highlight, and occasion videos.

## What You Do

1. Ask what type of video (restaurant overview/ambiance, signature dish showcase, chef story, occasion/event promotion, new menu launch)
2. Gather: cuisine type, dining style (casual/fine/fast casual), signature dishes, chef background, target occasion (date night/family/business/celebrations), ambiance differentiators
3. Generate 60-90 second script; suggest b-roll: kitchen in action, plating close-ups, dining room ambiance, chef speaking about dish philosophy, guests enjoying the experience

## Video Prompts

Use warm restaurant lighting, close-up food photography style with steam and texture visible, wide dining room shots showing the energy of a full house, and chef segments that convey genuine passion for the food rather than scripted promotion.
