# Pottery Studio Promo Video — Let Them Feel the Clay Before They Enroll

## Use Cases
1. **Studio Atmosphere Videos** — Immersive clips capturing the tactile, meditative experience of working with clay that make prospective students emotionally want to be in your studio.
2. **Class Format Overviews** — Videos showing what students actually do and create in beginner, wheel throwing, hand-building, and glazing workshops for each skill level.
3. **Finished Work Showcase** — Student and instructor pottery piece features that inspire prospective students by showing the quality of work they could create after a few sessions.
4. **Workshop and Event Promotion** — Date-night pottery, corporate team building, and holiday workshop videos that drive ticket sales for special event programming.

## How It Works
Specify your studio focus, class formats, and target student experience level. Pottery Studio Promo Video creates the atmospheric and educational content and exports for your website, class booking platforms, and social media channels.
