# Wedding Venue Promo Video — Show the Experience Before the Tour

## Use Cases
1. **Venue Reveal Videos** — Cinematic walkthroughs of ceremony and reception spaces that let couples picture their wedding day before ever scheduling a site visit.
2. **Bridal Fair and Open House Promos** — Short-form invitation videos for venue open houses and bridal shows that drive registrations from actively searching couples.
3. **Seasonal Availability Campaigns** — Promote remaining Saturday dates or off-peak packages with urgency-driven video content tied to specific seasons or months.
4. **Package and Pricing Explainers** — Clear, visual breakdowns of what is included — catering, decor, setup, accommodation — communicated in a way couples forward to each other.

## How It Works
Describe your venue's visual style, capacity, and standout features. Wedding Venue Promo Video creates a cinematic marketing video showcasing your space, exported in formats ready for Instagram, TikTok, your website, and wedding planning platforms.
