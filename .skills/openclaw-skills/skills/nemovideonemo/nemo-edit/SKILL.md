# Nemo Edit — AI Video Editing That Actually Saves Your Time

## 1. Core Editing Workflow

### Step 1: Import
Upload directly from your device, or connect to Google Drive, Dropbox, or record directly in the browser. Nemo Edit accepts:
- **Video**: MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WebM (up to 10GB per file)
- **Audio**: MP3, WAV, AAC, FLAC
- **Images**: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP
- **Maximum resolution**: 8K (7680x4320) — downscaled automatically for web delivery

### Step 2: AI Analysis
Within 60-90 seconds of upload, Nemo Edit's AI completes:
- **Scene detection**: Identifies distinct shots and segments for timeline organization
- **Silence detection**: Marks all silent gaps longer than 0.5 seconds for one-click removal
- **Quality scoring**: Flags shaky, blurry, or poorly-lit segments
- **Speech transcription**: Generates word-level transcript for caption generation and searchable timeline navigation

### Step 3: Smart Cut
The AI suggests a cut version of your raw footage — removing pauses, filler words ("um," "uh," "like"), and low-quality segments. You approve, adjust, or override any suggestion. Average raw-to-cut time: **8 minutes** for 30 minutes of raw footage.

### Step 4: Enrich
Add captions, music, B-roll, graphics, and brand elements. Nemo Edit's suggestion engine recommends:
- **Music**: Based on video mood, pacing, and detected content category
- **B-roll**: From a library of 5M+ licensed stock clips, matched to transcript keywords
- **Transitions**: Context-appropriate (cut, dissolve, wipe) based on scene type

## 3. Speed & Pacing Controls

### Silence Remover
One-click removal of all pauses longer than your chosen threshold (0.3 sec / 0.5 sec / 1 sec). Review in list view before applying — catch any intentional pauses the AI flagged. Average time saved on a 20-minute talking-head video: **4-7 minutes of dead air removed**, reducing finished length by 20-35%.

### Speed Ramp
Set keyframe points on your timeline, choose entry and exit speeds, and Nemo Edit calculates the smooth velocity curve automatically. Options:
- **Decelerate**: Normal → 20% slow (for peak moments)
- **Accelerate**: Slow → overspeed (for montage energy)
- **Sandwich**: Normal → slow → normal (for isolated moments)

### Variable Speed Timeline
Each clip can have independently set playback speed (10% to 800%). Nemo Edit automatically adjusts audio pitch correction at non-100% speeds.

## 9. Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I use Nemo Edit on my phone?**
Yes — the mobile web app supports full editing on iOS Safari and Android Chrome. The native iOS and Android apps provide an optimized mobile editing experience for short-form content.

**Does Nemo Edit work without a fast internet connection?**
Nemo Edit requires internet access for AI processing. For upload-heavy workflows, we recommend a minimum 10 Mbps upload connection. Offline editing is not currently supported.

**Are the captions ADA/WCAG compliant?**
Captions exported as SRT/VTT meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA requirements for timing and accuracy. Burned-in captions should be reviewed for contrast ratio compliance.

**What happens to my video files?**
Uploaded files are stored on encrypted servers for 90 days on free plans and indefinitely on paid plans. Files are never shared or used for training without explicit consent.
