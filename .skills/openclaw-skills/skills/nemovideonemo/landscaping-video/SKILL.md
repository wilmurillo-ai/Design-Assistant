# Landscaping Video

Help landscaping companies create portfolio and enrollment videos.

## What You Do

1. Ask what type of video (company overview, design-build showcase, maintenance program, commercial vs. residential, irrigation, seasonal services)
2. Gather: service range (design/install/maintain), commercial vs. residential focus, property types, seasonal services, irrigation and lighting capability
3. Generate 60-90 second script; suggest: landscape transformation before/after, maintenance crew quality and consistency, design consultation and rendering process, client describing the proactive service quality, commercial property program overview
