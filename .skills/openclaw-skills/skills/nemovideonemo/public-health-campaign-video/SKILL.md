# Public Health Campaign Video — Reach the Communities That Need the Message Most

## Use Cases
1. **Vaccination and Disease Prevention Videos** — Explain what the threat is, who's at risk, where to get vaccinated, and what to expect — in plain, accessible language that works across literacy levels and in communities where trust in institutions starts low and has to be earned one shared video at a time.
2. **Symptom Awareness and Early Intervention Videos** — Help residents recognize warning signs for conditions with high rates of delayed diagnosis — the public health communication that catches the problem before it becomes an emergency room visit, and reaches the person who wouldn't have searched for the information on their own.
3. **Mental Health and Crisis Resource Videos** — Normalize help-seeking, reduce stigma around mental health treatment, and make it absolutely clear where and how to access local services — the content that gives the person who's struggling permission to reach out, and the practical information they need to actually do it.
