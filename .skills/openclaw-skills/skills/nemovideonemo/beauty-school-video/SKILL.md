# Beauty School Video

Show the school culture, the graduate outcomes, and the people who will teach — before a student applies.

## Use Cases
1. **Day-in-the-Life and School Culture Video** — Show what a typical day in the school looks and feels like — practical lab, instructor feedback, student energy — for the prospective student who is still deciding.
2. **Graduate Outcome and Career Placement Video** — Show where graduates are working and how long it took — the proof that this school produces licensed professionals, not just students.
3. **Program-Specific Showcase Video** — Individual videos for cosmetology, esthetics, nail tech, barbering, and every credential you offer — matched content for every type of prospective student.
4. **Instructor and Mentor Introduction Video** — Put your faculty on camera to share their industry experience and teaching philosophy — the human signal that makes one school stand out.
5. **Financial Aid and Enrollment Process Video** — Answer the practical questions that stop prospective students from applying — how aid works, what the timeline looks like, what to expect in week one.

## How to Use

Describe your school, the programs you offer, your graduate placement record, and the instructors who define your culture. Include your primary prospective student — career-changer, recent graduate, or licensed professional adding a specialty.

Example prompt: "Create an enrollment video for Luxe Beauty Academy. Cosmetology and esthetics programs, 12-month full-time and 18-month part-time options. 87 percent job placement within 6 months of graduation. Highlight the hands-on practical curriculum, the licensed instructors with salon ownership experience, and the school's salon floor where students work with real clients."

## Setup

Store your NemoVideo token at ~/.config/nemovideo/token.txt or set the NEMO_TOKEN environment variable.
