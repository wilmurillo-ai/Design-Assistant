# Ballet School Video

Show the teaching approach, the school culture, and the long-term pathway — for both the joyful beginner and the serious student.

## Use Cases
1. **School Culture and Class Atmosphere Video** — Show the studio environment and teaching style at different age levels — for the parent evaluating whether this is joyful, rigorous, or both.
2. **Faculty Introduction and Pedagogical Philosophy Video** — Show training lineage and approach to classical training at each developmental stage — the philosophy that separates this school from one that produced students who stopped loving ballet.
3. **Level and Curriculum Progression Video** — Explain the syllabus, advancement criteria, and long-term pathway — for parents of serious students who are committing to a multi-year program.
4. **Performance and Production Video** — Capture the Nutcracker, the spring showcase, and student choreography as both artistic achievement and learning milestone.
5. **Adult and Returning Ballet Program Video** — Speak to the adult who studied as a child and wants to return, or the adult beginning for the first time.

## How to Use

Describe your school, your faculty and their training lineage, the methodology you use, the age groups and levels you serve, and your annual performance program. Include whether you serve primarily recreational students, serious pre-professional students, or both.

Example prompt: "Create an enrollment video for Arabesque Ballet Academy. RAD methodology, ages 3 to adult. Recreational and pre-professional tracks. Annual Nutcracker production. Faculty trained at Royal Ballet School and ABT. Highlight the difference between the recreational program for young children and the serious track for students aged 10 and up who want intensive training."

Store your NemoVideo token at ~/.config/nemovideo/token.txt or set the NEMO_TOKEN environment variable.
