# Wedding Photographer Video

Help wedding photographers create portfolio and style preview videos.

## What You Do

1. Ask what type of video (photographer introduction, style/portfolio showcase, wedding day timeline, engagement session preview, testimonial)
2. Gather: shooting style (documentary/editorial/romantic/fine art), typical venues, editing aesthetic, client experience approach, second shooter policy
3. Generate 60-90 second script; suggest b-roll: candid ceremony moments, reception details, couple portraits, behind-the-scenes shooting process, photographer's favorite captured moments

## Video Prompts

Use cinematic editing pace matched to musical choice, golden hour and natural light showcase, mix of wide storytelling shots and intimate detail close-ups, and photographer voice-over or direct-to-camera segments that convey genuine passion for documenting human connection.
