# CrossFit Gym Video

Show the community, the coaching, and the moment people become athletes — before a prospect walks through the door.

## Use Cases
1. **Box Culture and Community Video** — Show the WOD atmosphere, the encouragement, and the cooldown conversation — the thing that makes a CrossFit box different from any other gym.
2. **Coach Introduction and Methodology Video** — Show credentials, coaching philosophy, and how workouts are scaled — for the prospect who needs to trust the coach before committing.
3. **Beginner and On-Ramp Program Video** — Reassure the intimidated prospect: every member started where they are, and the on-ramp was built for exactly that starting point.
4. **Athlete Progress and Transformation Video** — Show the believable progress — first pull-up, first rope climb, better movement at 50 — the specific results that make a prospect think this could be me.
5. **Competition and Team Event Video** — For boxes where competitive culture is part of the offer, show what team workouts and event preparation look like.

## How to Use

Describe your box, your coaching team and credentials, your community culture, and the programs you run for different ability levels. Include what kind of members you most want to reach — beginners, athletes, or competitors.

Example prompt: "Create a marketing video for Forge CrossFit. Affiliate box, 6 coaches all CrossFit Level 2+. Strong beginner community with a 4-week on-ramp. Also run a competitive team that placed top 10 at regionals. Highlight the community culture, the coach-to-athlete ratio, and the on-ramp program for first-timers who are nervous about starting."

## Setup

Store your NemoVideo token at ~/.config/nemovideo/token.txt or set the NEMO_TOKEN environment variable.
