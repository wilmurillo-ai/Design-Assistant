## How the Strategy Router Works

Tell your agent your market outlook, IV rank, and account size. It recommends the optimal strategy with exact entry criteria, position sizing, risk management, and exit rules.

```
High IV + Bullish → Sell puts (Cash-Secured Puts, Bull Put Spreads)
High IV + Neutral → Iron Condors, Covered Calls, Wheel
Low IV + Bullish → Buy calls (LEAPS, Debit Spreads)
Earnings → Straddles, Iron Butterflies
```

Each template includes entry criteria, position sizing, stop-loss rules, profit targets, rolling criteria, and a complete example trade with numbers.
