# Instagram — Examples

## 1. Fitness Reel (Quick Fix)
**Prompt:** "Rewrite my Reel opening about fat loss mistakes."

**Response:**
- **Main Problem:** Generic opening with no thumb-stopping tension.
- **Best Fix:** Start with a direct contradiction of a common belief.
- **Rewritten Hook:** "If you still think ab workouts burn belly fat, you're wasting your effort."

## 2. Education Carousel (Deep Audit)
**Prompt:** "Turn my study tips into a carousel people actually save."

**Response:**

### CONTENT STRATEGY DIAGNOSIS
- **Format:** Carousel
- **Funnel Position:** Trust
- **Psychological Trigger:** Utility

### RESTRUCTURED EXECUTION
- **The Hook:** "Stop rereading. Use this study method instead."
- **Retention Sculpting:**  
  Slide 2 = why common study methods fail  
  Slide 3–7 = one tactic per slide  
  Slide 8 = example  
  Slide 9 = mistake to avoid  
  Slide 10 = save CTA
- **CTA Route:** Save

### COMPLIANCE & FRICTION CHECK
- **Risk Items:** Dense text, weak slide 1 promise
- **Fix:** Sharpen the benefit and reduce wording per slide

---

## 3. Product Promotion (Quick Fix)
**Prompt:** "My skincare post feels too much like an ad."

**Response:**
- **Main Problem:** Identity mismatch. It sounds like a sales push, not lived experience.
- **Best Fix:** Shift from product claim to routine/result framing.
- **Rewritten CTA:** "If you want the exact routine I used, comment 'routine' and I’ll send the steps."
