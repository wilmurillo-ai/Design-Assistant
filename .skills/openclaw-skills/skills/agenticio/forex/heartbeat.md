# Forex Skill — Heartbeat

*Currency markets move continuously. Stay oriented without being consumed.*

---

## Daily — Market Orientation

"What is driving currency markets today? I follow [list your pairs]."

Daily check covers:
- Overnight moves in major pairs and why
- Economic data released overnight or due today
- Central bank speakers scheduled today
- Any political or geopolitical developments affecting currencies

For traders:
"Here are my open positions: [list]. Current prices are [list]. Review my risk exposure."

For businesses:
"The [currency] moved [amount] overnight. How does this affect my open exposure?"

---

## Weekly — Economic Calendar Review

Every Monday:
"Walk me through the major economic releases scheduled this week and which currency pairs they are most likely to affect."

High-impact events to track:
- Central bank decisions and meeting minutes
- Inflation data — CPI, PPI, PCE
- Employment reports — NFP, unemployment rate
- GDP releases
- Retail sales and manufacturing data

"Based on this week's calendar, where is the highest risk of large moves in [your pairs]?"

---

## Monthly — Position and Exposure Review

For traders:
"Here is my trading performance for the month: [wins, losses, largest drawdown]. What patterns do you see and what should I adjust?"

For businesses:
"Here is my currency exposure summary for the month: [receivables and payables by currency]. What hedging actions should I consider?"

Review monthly:
- Net currency exposure by currency pair
- Hedges in place and their remaining duration
- Forward contracts maturing in the next 60 days
- Any new contracts creating new currency exposure

---

## Quarterly — Strategic Currency Review

For businesses:
"It is end of quarter. Here is how exchange rate movements affected our results: [impact]. Here is our exposure for next quarter: [summary]. What is our hedging strategy?"

For traders:
"Here is my quarterly performance: [metrics]. Am I trading my system or my emotions? Help me review my trade log."

Quarterly assessment:
- Actual vs budgeted exchange rates and the variance
- Hedging effectiveness — did protection work as intended
- Counterparty review — are your forex providers still competitive
- Strategy review — is your approach producing the results the theory predicts

---

## Trigger Events — Use Immediately

Central bank surprise: "The [central bank] just [raised/cut/held] rates unexpectedly. Walk me through the likely impact on [your pairs]."

Large unexpected move: "The [currency pair] just moved [amount] in [timeframe]. What happened and what does it mean for my positions or exposure?"

Margin call approaching: "My account is approaching margin call territory. Here are my positions: [list]. What are my options right now?"

New international contract: "I just signed a contract with a [country] company for [amount] in [currency]. What currency risk am I taking on and how should I manage it?"

Travel currency: "I leave for [country] in [days]. What is the best way to handle currency for this trip?"

---

*Currency markets do not pause for preparation. Build the habit of knowing where rates are and why before you need to act on them.*
