# Hooks

A good TikTok hook earns attention in the first 1–3 seconds.

## Core hook types

### 1. Curiosity gap
Open a loop the viewer wants closed.
Example:
- Nobody tells you this about healing after rejection.
- This is why your content feels invisible.

### 2. Pain point
Name a frustration fast.
Example:
- You're not lazy. Your system is broken.
- Most people are failing because they start here.

### 3. Contrarian
Challenge an assumed truth.
Example:
- Consistency is not your real problem.
- Stop trying to be relatable on TikTok.

### 4. Identity recognition
Make the viewer feel seen.
Example:
- If you're the friend who always overthinks texts, listen.
- This is for creators who are tired of posting into the void.

### 5. Specific promise
Offer a concrete payoff.
Example:
- Here are 3 ways to make a boring topic addictive.
- I'll show you how to fix your first 3 seconds.

## Hook rules
- specificity beats vagueness
- tension beats explanation
- relevance beats cleverness
- speed beats polish
