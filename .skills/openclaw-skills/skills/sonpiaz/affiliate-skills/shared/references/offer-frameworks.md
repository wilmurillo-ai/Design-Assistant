# Offer Frameworks Reference

Frameworks for creating irresistible affiliate offers. Used by S4 Landing skills.

## Hormozi Grand Slam Offer Formula

**Value = Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood ÷ Time Delay ÷ Effort & Sacrifice**

To maximize perceived value:
1. **Increase Dream Outcome** — Paint the vivid after-state. What does life look like after using this product?
2. **Increase Perceived Likelihood** — Social proof, guarantees, demonstrations, case studies
3. **Decrease Time Delay** — Quick wins, immediate access, fast onboarding
4. **Decrease Effort & Sacrifice** — Done-for-you, templates, automation, no learning curve

### Dream Outcome Prompts
- "What is the #1 thing your audience is trying to achieve?"
- "What would they pay anything for?"
- "What keeps them up at night?"

### Likelihood Boosters
- Specific numbers: "1,247 users saved 3.2 hours/week"
- Named case studies: real user, real result, real timeline
- Demonstrations: screenshots, videos, before/after
- Guarantees: your personal guarantee on top of the product's

### Time Delay Reducers
- "Get started in under 5 minutes"
- "See first results within 24 hours"
- "No setup required — works out of the box"

### Effort Reducers
- "Copy-paste templates included"
- "One-click integration"
- "Works with tools you already use"

## Bonus Stack Framework

Bonuses are the #1 differentiator for affiliate marketers. The product is the same through every affiliate link — YOUR bonuses are what makes YOUR link the obvious choice.

### Bonus Types (ranked by perceived value)
1. **Done-for-you assets** — Templates, swipe files, configurations (highest value)
2. **Private access** — Community, group coaching, office hours
3. **Educational content** — Course, workshop, tutorial series
4. **Tools & calculators** — Spreadsheets, checklists, audit tools
5. **Personal support** — Email access, review, feedback (highest effort but most compelling)

### Bonus Stack Rules
- Each bonus should solve a specific objection to the main product
- Total bonus value should exceed the product price (perceived)
- Include at least one "exclusive" bonus (only through your link)
- Time-limit at least one bonus for urgency
- Bonuses must be deliverable — don't promise what you can't fulfill

### Bonus Value Calculation
```
Perceived Value = What you'd charge if sold separately
Example:
  - Product: $49/mo
  - Bonus 1: Setup templates ($97 value)
  - Bonus 2: Private community access ($47/mo value)
  - Bonus 3: 1-on-1 setup call ($150 value)
  - Total bonus value: $294
  - Message: "Get $294 in bonuses FREE when you start through my link"
```

## Guarantee Framework

Your guarantee sits ON TOP of the product's guarantee. It addresses the gap: "What if I buy through your link and the product doesn't work for me?"

### Guarantee Types
1. **Results guarantee** — "If you don't [specific result] in [timeframe], I'll [action]"
2. **Support guarantee** — "If you get stuck, I'll personally help you [specific help]"
3. **Satisfaction guarantee** — "If you're not happy with my bonuses, I'll [refund/replace]"
4. **Risk-reversal guarantee** — "Try it for [period]. If it's not for you, I'll [help with cancellation + bonus]"

### Guarantee Rules
- Be specific about the outcome and timeframe
- Only guarantee what you can deliver (YOUR bonuses and support, not the product's features)
- Include a clear claim process
- Make it feel generous, not legalistic

## Value Ladder Framework

Map the entire customer journey from free to premium:

```
FREE (awareness)
  → Blog post, social content, free tool
  → Goal: build trust, demonstrate expertise

TRIPWIRE ($1-$49, impulse buy)
  → Your bonus pack, mini-course, template kit
  → Goal: convert from reader to buyer, get email

CORE ($50-$499, main recommendation)
  → The affiliate product itself
  → Goal: solve their main problem, earn commission

UPSELL ($500+, high-ticket)
  → Premium version, annual plan, enterprise tier
  → Goal: maximize LTV for recurring programs
```

### Value Ladder Rules
- Each step must deliver standalone value (P4: standalone principle)
- Lower steps sell the next step naturally (not forcefully)
- The affiliate product is always the CORE — not the upsell
- Your content (blog, social, email) is always the FREE layer
- Tripwire is optional but dramatically increases conversion

## Pricing Psychology

### Anchoring
- Show the "without this" cost first: "Hiring a consultant: $5,000"
- Then show the product price: "Or get [product] for $49/mo"
- Then show your bonus stack: "Plus $294 in free bonuses"

### Framing
- Per-day pricing: "$49/mo = $1.63/day — less than a coffee"
- ROI framing: "If it saves you 3 hours/week at $50/hr, it pays for itself in 2 days"
- Comparison: "10x cheaper than [alternative] with 80% of the features"

### Urgency (ethical only)
- Real deadlines: "Price increases on [date]"
- Real scarcity: "I can only support 20 1-on-1 calls this month"
- Never fake urgency — it destroys trust and violates FTC guidelines
