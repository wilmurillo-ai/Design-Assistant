# Consent Message Template (Privacy-First)

Subject/Opening:
- Invitation to a short product diary study

Template:

Hello [First Name],

We are running a short diary study to understand real usage behavior over time and improve product decisions.

What participation includes:
- Study length: [X days]
- Typical entry time: 5-10 minutes
- Entry frequency: [timing mode and expected cadence]
- Topics: what you did, context, blockers, and outcomes
- Optional media: screenshot/photo/audio only when relevant

How your data will be used:
- Used only for this study's research goal and weekly product insights.
- Reports are redacted and use participant codes (for example, P01), not direct identifiers.
- Raw responses are stored separately from reports with restricted access.
- Data is retained for [retention window], then deleted or anonymized.

Your rights:
- Participation is voluntary.
- You can skip any question.
- You can withdraw at any time without penalty.
- You can request deletion of your identifiable data where applicable.

Incentive and response requirements:
- Completion payout: [amount + currency]
- Minimum required entries: [min_entries_required]
- Maximum counted entries: [max_entries_allowed]
- Payout timing: [timing]

Media policy (optional section):
- If you share screenshots/photos/audio, avoid including sensitive personal details.
- Files are stored securely and referenced by ID in reports.

If you agree, reply with this exact confirmation sentence:

"I consent to join this diary study and understand how my entries will be used."

Thank you,
[Researcher Name / Team]

---

## Consent Logging Requirements
- Save consent timestamp in UTC.
- Save channel and source message ID for audit.
- Do not mark participant active until exact or semantically equivalent explicit consent is recorded.
