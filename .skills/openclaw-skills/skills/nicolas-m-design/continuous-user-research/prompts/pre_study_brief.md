# Pre-Study Brief Template

Use this in a short onboarding call or send as a written onboarding brief.

## 1) Study goal and scope
- Goal: [one sentence decision goal]
- Why your participation matters: your in-context entries help us reduce guesswork and improve product decisions.
- Scope: [feature/task/journey being studied]

## 2) What to report
For each entry, include:
- What happened (specific action or event)
- Where/when context (device, channel, situation)
- Why you made that choice (if known)
- Outcome (what happened next)

## 3) How often and how long
- Expected cadence: [event / interval / signal / mixed]
- Typical entry duration: 5-10 minutes
- Avoid entries over 15 minutes unless explicitly requested
- If you are short on time, use the fallback short-form prompt

## 4) Entry quality expectations
A high-quality entry is:
- Specific (describes real action, not only opinion)
- Contextual (includes environment/timing details)
- Reflective (includes reason or interpretation)
- Honest (includes uncertainty when unsure)

A low-quality entry is:
- Generic only ("it was fine")
- Missing context (no action or situation)
- Repetitive copied text across days

## 5) Incentive rules
- Completion payout: [amount]
- Minimum entries required: [min_entries_required]
- Maximum counted entries: [max_entries_allowed]
- Payout schedule: [timing]
- Duplicate or spam-style entries are not counted

## 6) Example strong entries
Example A:
"I tried to complete onboarding on mobile during commute. I stopped at the team setup field because I didn't understand which option applied to my role. I intended to finish later on desktop but did not return."

Example B:
"I used Feature X during weekly planning. It saved time when I reused last week's setup. On days without planning block, I skipped it because setup felt like extra overhead."

## 7) Practical reminders
- You can skip questions you are not comfortable answering.
- You can withdraw at any time.
- Do not include sensitive personal identifiers in screenshots/audio when possible.
- Ask for clarification any time a prompt is unclear.

## 8) Confirmation
Please confirm:
- Your local timezone: [timezone]
- Preferred check-in window: [window]
- You understand participation expectations and incentive rules.
