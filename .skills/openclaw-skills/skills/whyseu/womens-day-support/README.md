# Women's Day Support Skill

A compassionate and practical skill designed to support women on International Women's Day (March 8th) and beyond.

## Features

- **Resources Hub**: Access to curated resources for women's health, career development, legal rights, and mental wellness
- **Local Support Finder**: Helps locate nearby women's shelters, healthcare providers, and support groups
- **Inspirational Content**: Daily quotes and stories from influential women
- **Event Finder**: Discover local Women's Day events and workshops
- **Self-Care Guide**: Personalized self-care recommendations

## How It Works

When activated, the skill provides:
1. A welcoming message acknowledging Women's Day
2. A menu of support options tailored to different needs
3. Direct links to verified resources and helplines
4. Option to schedule follow-up check-ins

## Privacy & Safety

- All interactions are private and confidential
- No personal data is stored without explicit consent
- Emergency contacts are provided for crisis situations

## Usage

Trigger with any of these phrases:
- "Women's Day help"
- "Support for women"
- "I need women's resources"
- "March 8th assistance"
