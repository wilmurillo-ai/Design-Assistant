# Platycerium Hybrid & Cultivar Reference

## Overview
400+ registered hybrid cultivars. Convention: Parent A = spore (mother), Parent B = pollen (father).

## Major Lineage Categories

### DW — Diversifolium x Willinckii
Most popular hybrid line. Produces compact plants with dramatic forking.
Notable cultivars: Full Moon, New Moon, Blue Moon, Oh My God, Paul Vespa, White Hawk, Pegasus, Snow Moon, Selene, Blue Sapphire

### BW — Bifurcatum x Willinckii
Combines bifurcatum hardiness with willinckii elegance.

### Lewis — Mt. Lewis Based
Derived from P. bifurcatum 'Mt. Lewis' — a high-altitude form with silver trichomes.

### Veitchii — Veitchii Selections
Intraspecific selections of P. veitchii. Valued for silver coloring and sun/cold tolerance.

### VW — Veitchii x Willinckii
Combines veitchii hardiness with willinckii forking pattern.

### FSQ — Foongsiqi Based
P. foongsiqi hybrid line.

### VB — Veitchii x Bifurcatum
Hardy crosses with silver trichome expression.

### Hillii — Hillii Based
P. hillii selections and crosses. Upright, structured plants.

### WH — Willinckii x Hillii
Combines willinckii forking with hillii structure.

### Willinckii — Willinckii Selections
Intraspecific: Bali form, Bogor form, and named clones. Extremely variable.

### Pewchan — Pewchan Line
Named breeder line.

### Other — Multi-species / Rare
Various interspecific crosses including unusual parent combinations.

## Key Parent Species Traits
- **P. willinckii**: dramatic forking, variable forms, cornerstone parent
- **P. veitchii**: cold hardiness, silver trichomes, sun tolerance
- **P. hillii**: structural upright form, stress tolerance
- **P. bifurcatum**: resilience, versatility, breeding workhorse
- **P. bifurcatum 'Mt. Lewis'**: silver form, high-altitude traits

## Notable Breeders
Wanna, Tonkla, Banna, Yot, Stem, Nook, PN, KFL, CK, KHAOKHO, Nam Ferns (primarily Thailand-based breeding community)
