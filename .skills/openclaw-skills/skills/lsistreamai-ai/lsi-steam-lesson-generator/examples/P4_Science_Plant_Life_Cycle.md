## 3. Teaching Steps

```
Minute 0-5: Introduction (引入)
- Play HeyGen video with "Plant Guide" avatar
- Ask students: "Have you ever planted a seed? What happened?"
- Show real plant sample if available

Minute 5-12: Direct Instruction (直接教學)
- Present the 4 stages of plant life cycle using slides
- Use bilingual terms: Seed (種子), Germination (發芽), Growth (生長), Flowering/Reproduction (開花/繁殖)
- Demonstrate Canva AI on projector: generate an image of "seed sprouting"

Minute 12-27: Guided Practice (引導練習)
- Students log into Canva in pairs
- Walk through creating first AI-generated image together
- Students create infographic with 4 stages
- Teacher circulates to support

Minute 27-37: Independent Practice (獨立練習)
- Students complete their infographic
- Add 2 facts about each stage using knowledge from lesson
- Share with partner for feedback

Minute 37-40: Closure & Assessment (總結與評估)
- Quick quiz: "What comes after germination?"
- Show 2-3 student infographics on projector
- Exit ticket: Draw the 4 stages in order
```

## 4. Student Activities

```
Activity 1: AI Infographic Creation
- Type: Pair work
- Tool: Canva AI
- Instructions:
  1. Open Canva and search "infographic template"
  2. Use AI image generator (type: "seed in soil", "sprouting plant", "growing plant", "flowering plant")
  3. Insert images into 4 sections
  4. Add Chinese and English labels
  5. Add one interesting fact about each stage
- Output: Completed infographic with 4 stages and facts

Activity 2: Plant Detective
- Type: Individual
- Tool: None (observation)
- Instructions:
  - Look at the real plant sample
  - Identify which stage the plant is in
  - Write your answer and reasoning
- Output: Written answer with reasoning
```
