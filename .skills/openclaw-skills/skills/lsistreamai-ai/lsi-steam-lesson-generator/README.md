## Why This Skill Sells

### For Hong Kong Teachers (EDB Grant Requirement)

The Education Bureau's **AI for Empowering Learning & Teaching Funding** gives schools **HK$500,000** — but they must:
- Implement AI in **at least 3 subjects**
- Develop **6 teaching examples/resources** using AI

**This skill solves that problem.** Every lesson plan output CAN be submitted as one of the 6 required AI teaching resources.

### For International Teachers

Even outside the EDB grant, teachers need:
- Lesson plans that integrate AI meaningfully (not just "play with ChatGPT")
- Practical guidance on HOW to use AI tools in the classroom
- Assessment help (AI prompts for grading student work)
