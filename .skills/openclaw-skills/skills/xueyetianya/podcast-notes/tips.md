# Podcast Tips & Best Practices

## Production Essentials
- **Audio quality** is non-negotiable — invest in a decent mic (Blue Yeti, ATR2100x, SM7B)
- **Room treatment**: Record in a quiet, carpeted room. Closets work surprisingly well
- **Record at 44.1kHz/16-bit** minimum (standard for podcast hosting platforms)
- **Leave 2-3 seconds of silence** at the start for noise floor sampling
- **Always have a backup recording** (record locally AND in the cloud)

## Episode Structure
1. **Cold Open** (30-60s): Start with the most interesting moment or a teaser
2. **Intro** (30s): Theme music + "You're listening to [show], I'm [host]"
3. **Context** (1-2 min): Why this topic, why now, why this guest
4. **Main Content** (bulk): The meat of the episode
5. **Key Takeaways** (2-3 min): Summarize the most important points
6. **CTA** (30s): Subscribe, review, share, visit website
7. **Outro** (15s): Thank + theme music

## Guest Interview Tips
- Research the guest for 1+ hours before recording
- Send 3-5 questions in advance (but not all of them)
- Follow-up questions > scripted questions
- "Tell me more about that" is your best tool
- Let silences breathe — guests often fill them with gold
- Always ask: "What's something I should have asked but didn't?"

## Show Notes Best Practices
- **Summary**: 2-3 sentences describing the episode
- **Key timestamps**: Let listeners jump to topics
- **Links**: Everything mentioned in the episode
- **Guest info**: Bio, social links, website
- **Transcript**: Full or summary — great for SEO
- **CTA**: What to do next

## Growth Strategies
1. **Cross-promotion**: Appear on other podcasts in your niche
2. **Clip marketing**: 60-90 second clips on social media (Reels, TikTok, Shorts)
3. **Newsletter**: Weekly email with episode + bonus content
4. **Community**: Discord/Slack for engaged listeners
5. **SEO**: Optimize show notes as blog posts
6. **Consistency**: Same day, same time, every week

## Monetization Tiers
- **0-1K downloads/ep**: Focus on growth, not money
- **1K-5K**: Affiliate marketing, Patreon
- **5K-10K**: Small sponsor deals ($18-25 CPM)
- **10K-25K**: Mid-tier sponsors, premium content
- **25K+**: Multiple sponsors, live events, courses

## Distribution Platforms
- Apple Podcasts (still the largest)
- Spotify (fastest growing, exclusive deals)
- Google Podcasts → YouTube Podcasts
- Amazon Music / Audible
- Overcast, Pocket Casts, Castro (power listeners)
- YouTube (video podcast — growing fast)
