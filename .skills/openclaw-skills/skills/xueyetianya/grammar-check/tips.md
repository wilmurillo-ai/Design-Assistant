# Grammar Check Tips 📖

## 中国学生常见语法错误 Top 10
1. **冠词遗漏**: ~~I am student~~ → I am **a** student
2. **时态混乱**: ~~I go there yesterday~~ → I **went** there yesterday
3. **主谓不一致**: ~~He don't~~ → He **doesn't**
4. **可数/不可数**: ~~informations~~ → information
5. **介词错误**: ~~arrive to~~ → arrive **at/in**
6. **从句语序**: ~~I don't know where is he~~ → where **he is**
7. **动词形式**: ~~enjoy to do~~ → enjoy **doing**
8. **比较级**: ~~more better~~ → **better**
9. **there be**: ~~there have~~ → **there is/are**
10. **代词**: ~~Let I~~ → Let **me**
