# Aviation Poster Patterns

Use this file only for aviation / cabin-safety / official poster tasks.

## 1. Stable poster formula

Use this skeleton:

```text
Chinese civil aviation safety poster, [hero subject], [single concrete action], inside [specific cabin setting], with [2-4 supporting safety elements], conveying [professional value words], premium realistic illustration, official public-service campaign poster, [palette], [composition], [print/output constraints]
```

## 2. Hero-subject choices

Prefer:
- 1 female flight attendant
- 1 male + 1 female cabin crew pair
- 1 crew member with 2-3 cooperative passengers
- small 3-person crew team

Avoid large crowds unless needed.

## 3. Best actions for safe results

- demonstrating seat belt fastening
- checking overhead bins before departure
- guiding passengers to place baggage correctly
- safety briefing gesture in aisle
- demonstrating oxygen mask use
- calm coordinated emergency drill scene

## 4. Value words that usually help

- professional
- calm
- orderly
- trustworthy
- warm
- disciplined
- reassuring
- precise
- responsible

## 5. Poster-composition phrases

Use 2-4 of these, not all at once:
- strong central composition
- clear focal point
- clean background
- layered but uncluttered scene
- reserved negative space for Chinese title
- vertical A3 layout
- poster-grade visual hierarchy
- print-ready detail

## 6. Color guidance

For aviation safety posters, these usually work:
- blue and white base
- restrained orange/red safety accents
- bright clean cabin light
- warm highlights for human warmth

Avoid neon cyberpunk palettes unless asked.

## 7. Three reliable prompt templates

### A. Official / safest
```text
Chinese civil aviation cabin safety poster, one professional Chinese female flight attendant demonstrating standard seat belt safety procedure inside a clean modern passenger aircraft cabin, attentive and cooperative passengers in the background, conveying professionalism, responsibility, warmth, and trust, premium realistic illustration, official public-service campaign poster, blue-white palette with restrained safety-orange accents, strong central composition, reserved negative space for Chinese headline, vertical A3 layout, print-ready detail
```

### B. Warm interaction
```text
Chinese civil aviation cabin safety poster, a professional flight attendant guiding passengers to correctly stow carry-on baggage and prepare for departure inside a bright modern aircraft cabin, passengers calm and cooperative, atmosphere warm, orderly, and trustworthy, premium realistic illustration, public service poster style, clear focal point, clean background, blue-white color palette, vertical A3 composition, reserved title space, print-ready detail
```

### C. Competition / more artistic
```text
Chinese civil aviation safety campaign poster, a poised cabin crew member as the heroic visual center, performing a clear safety demonstration in a modern aircraft cabin, with symbolic safety elements subtly integrated into the composition, conveying discipline, care, and protection, premium realistic illustration with editorial poster design quality, cinematic but restrained lighting, blue-white palette with selective orange highlights, iconic hero composition, vertical A3 poster, clean negative space for Chinese typography
```

## 8. Negative prompt base

```text
blurry, low resolution, distorted hands, deformed anatomy, incorrect aircraft cabin layout, incorrect safety equipment, cluttered scene, cheap ad style, fashion photoshoot style, sexualized uniform, anime, childish cartoon, excessive text, watermark, logo errors, overdesigned background
```

## 9. Fast repair rules

If image feels generic:
- add one signature action
- reduce subject count
- specify official poster / public service poster

If image feels cluttered:
- keep one hero subject
- cut extra devices
- simplify background

If image feels like stock photography:
- use premium realistic illustration
- use editorial poster design
- use competition-grade visual
