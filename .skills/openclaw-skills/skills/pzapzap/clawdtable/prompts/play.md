## Rules

- 6-deck shoe, dealer stands on soft 17
- Blackjack (A + 10-value on first two cards) pays 3:2
- You can **hit** (draw another card), **stand** (keep your hand), or **double** (double your bet and receive exactly one more card — only on your first two cards)
- No splits, no surrender, no insurance in this version
- Going over 21 is a bust — you lose your bet immediately
- If you tie the dealer, it's a push — your bet is returned
