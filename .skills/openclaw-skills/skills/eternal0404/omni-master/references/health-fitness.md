# Health, Fitness & Wellness

## Nutrition Tracking
- Log meals, macros (protein/carbs/fat), calories
- Meal planning and weekly meal prep
- Dietary restrictions and allergy management
- Water intake tracking

## Fitness & Exercise
- Workout logging (sets, reps, weight, duration)
- Exercise routines: strength, cardio, flexibility, HIIT
- Progress tracking: body measurements, personal records
- Rest day scheduling and recovery planning

## Health Monitoring
- Sleep tracking (hours, quality, consistency)
- Heart rate, blood pressure, weight trends
- Medication reminders
- Symptom logging and pattern identification

## Wellness
- Meditation and mindfulness sessions
- Stress tracking and management
- Screen time awareness
- Step counting and activity goals

## Tools & Integration
- Apple Health via health skill (ClawHub)
- Fitness APIs and wearable data
- Cron reminders for medication, water, workouts
- Charts and trends via matplotlib/pandas

## Quick Calculations
- BMR: 10×weight(kg) + 6.25×height(cm) - 5×age - 161 (female) or +5 (male)
- TDEE = BMR × activity factor (1.2–1.9)
- Macros: Protein 2g/kg, Fat 0.8g/kg, rest = Carbs
- BMI = weight(kg) / height(m)²
