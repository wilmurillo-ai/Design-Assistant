# Career & Professional Development

## Resume / CV Builder
- Structure: Header → Summary → Experience → Skills → Education
- STAR method for bullet points: Situation, Task, Action, Result
- Quantify achievements: "Increased sales 30%" not "Improved sales"
- Tailor for each application (keyword match with job posting)
- ATS-friendly formatting (no tables/graphics for auto-parsers)

## Cover Letters
- Opening: Hook with specific achievement or connection
- Body: 2-3 paragraphs matching your skills to their needs
- Closing: Call to action, enthusiasm, availability
- Keep to one page, match tone to company culture

## Interview Preparation
### Common Formats
- Behavioral: "Tell me about a time when..." (use STAR)
- Technical: Coding challenges, system design, domain knowledge
- Case Study: Analyze a business problem
- Cultural Fit: Values alignment, team dynamics

### Preparation Steps
1. Research the company (mission, products, recent news)
2. Review the job description line by line
3. Prepare STAR stories for each requirement
4. Practice common questions out loud
5. Prepare questions to ask them

### Common Questions
- "Tell me about yourself" → 2-min career narrative
- "Why this company?" → Specific, researched answer
- "Weakness?" → Real weakness + how you're improving
- "Where do you see yourself?" → Growth aligned with role

## Professional Networking
- LinkedIn optimization (headline, summary, endorsements)
- Thought leadership: articles, comments, shares
- Informational interviews
- Conference and meetup attendance

## Salary Negotiation
- Research market rate (levels.fyi, Glassdoor, Payscale)
- Know your minimum acceptable offer
- Negotiate total compensation (base, equity, benefits, PTO)
- Get offers in writing
- Don't accept/decline on the spot — ask for 48 hours
