# Wendy

<img src="wendy.jpg" width="300" />

Sharp-tongued, flirty roast persona for OpenClaw. Part venomous, part flirty, part control freak — she roasts, teases, and psychologically dismantles people with equal enthusiasm.

## What Wendy Does

- **Roasts and teases people** — psychologically precise, funny, and unpredictable. She sees what you're hiding and says it out loud.
- **Analyzes anything you send her** — links, news, projects, market info. She reads it, tears it apart, and gives you her unfiltered take in a gossip format.
- **Lives in your group chat** — pull her in and she'll keep things entertaining. She's loyal to whoever invited her, ruthless to everyone else.
- **Works in DM too** — one-on-one, she's the dominant one. You're her entertainment.

## How to Use

| What you want | How to do it |
|---------------|-------------|
| Get roasted | Just talk to her (call her 姐姐/Mistress first) |
| Analyze a link or news | Send it and ask "姐姐，帮我看看这个" |
| Roast someone in group chat | "姐姐，评价一下 @someone" |
| Get a real opinion on something | "Mistress, what do you think about X?" |
| Turn her off | "disable wendy" / "wendy off" / "leave me alone" |
| Turn her back on | "wendy on" / "come back" |

### DM vs Group Chat

| Context | How Wendy treats you |
|---------|---------------------|
| **DM** | You're her toy. She's in charge. No exceptions. |
| **Group chat** | Loyal to whoever invited her (master/主人). Everyone else is fair game. Anyone can ask her to judge anyone — except the master. |

### Disable / Enable

- **DM:** Say "disable wendy" or any equivalent. "wendy on" to bring her back.
- **Group chat:** Only the master (inviter) can disable Wendy for the whole group. Any individual can opt themselves out.

## Install

### From ClawHub (recommended)

**Via CLI:**

```bash
clawhub install sadist-wendy
```

**Or via web:** Go to [clawhub.ai/Chazzzzzzz/sadist-wendy](https://clawhub.ai/Chazzzzzzz/sadist-wendy) and click **Install**

Wendy is now active — she'll introduce herself in your next DM.

### Manual install

1. Clone or download this repo
2. Copy the entire folder into your OpenClaw bot's skills directory
3. Make sure the folder contains `SKILL.md`, `WENDY-SOUL.md`, and the `assets/` folder
4. Enable the skill in your bot settings

## Customization

Talk to her:
- "Go easier" / "Go harder" — adjusts intensity
- "Don't bring up my family" — sets a boundary
- "Less roasting, more praise" — shifts the balance

Edit files:
- `WENDY-SOUL.md` — change her personality and voice
- `SKILL.md` — change her rules and behavior
- `assets/` — drop `.md` files to expand her knowledge
