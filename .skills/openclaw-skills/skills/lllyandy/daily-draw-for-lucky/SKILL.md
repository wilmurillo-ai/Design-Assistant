# Daily Draw: For Lucky 🎋 | 观音灵签

🙏 Daily one draw to guide your way. 100 Guanyin Oracle sticks.

## Usage | 使用
Triggers: **"draw"** | **"帮我抽个签"** | **"/draw"**
Limit: One draw per day

## Structure | 签文结构
1. Number | 签号
2. Fortune Level | 吉凶 (上上/上/中/下/下下)
3. Poem | 签诗 (4 lines)
4. Meaning | 解曰
5. Four Aspects | 事业/姻缘/健康/财运
6. Story | 典故

## Distribution | 分布
- Great Fortune (上上签): ~20
- Good Fortune (上签): ~25
- Neutral (中签): ~35
- Bad Fortune (下签): ~15
- Great Misfortune (下下签): ~5

## Guide | 指引
- **Great/Good**: Actively pursue | 积极进取
- **Neutral**: Stay steady | 稳扎稳打
- **Bad/Misfortune**: Stay defensive | 守成为上

## Notes | 注意
1. One per day | 每日一签
2. Sincerity matters | 诚心则灵
3. Reference only | 仅供参考
4. Stay balanced | 心态平和

*May Guanyin bless you | 愿观音菩萨保佑* 🙏
