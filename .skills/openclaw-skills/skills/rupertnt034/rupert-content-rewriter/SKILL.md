# Content Rewriter Skill

## Overview
Rewrite and optimize content for different platforms, tones, and audiences.

## Capabilities
- Rewrite for different platforms (Twitter, LinkedIn, blog, etc.)
- Adjust tone (professional, casual, funny, formal)
- Translate between languages
- Summarize long content
- Expand bullet points into full content
- SEO optimization

## Usage

### Commands
- `rewrite for [platform]`
- `make this more [tone]`
- `translate to [language]`
- `summarize this`
- `expand these notes`
- `make it SEO friendly`

## Platform Guidelines

### Twitter (280 chars)
- Concise, engaging
- Use hashtags strategically
- Hook in first line

### LinkedIn (3000 chars)
- Professional tone
- Storytelling format
- Clear call-to-action

### Blog (Unlimited)
- SEO-optimized
- Structured with headers
- Expert authority

### Email
- Clear subject line
- Scannable format
- Professional sign-off

## Tone Options
- Professional
- Casual
- Funny/Humorous
- Inspirational
- Educational
- Persuasive

## Examples

**Input:** "Our company sells shoes"
**Output (Twitter):** 
```
👟 NEW: The most comfortable shoes you'll ever wear.

After 10,000+ steps testing, we're confident.

Your feet deserve better. 

#Footwear #Innovation
```

**Output (LinkedIn):**
```
After 3 years of development and 10,000+ wear tests, we're excited to launch [Company Name].

We didn't just want to make another shoe.

We wanted to create the most comfortable footwear experience possible.

Here's what we learned...
```
