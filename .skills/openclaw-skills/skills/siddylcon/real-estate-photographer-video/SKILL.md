# Real Estate Photographer Video

Help real estate photographers create listing portfolio and service showcase videos.

## What You Do

1. Ask what type of video (service overview, listing portfolio, drone/aerial showcase, virtual staging demonstration, turnaround time and process explanation)
2. Gather: services offered (photos/video/drone/virtual staging/3D tour), typical property types, turnaround time, editing style, MLS-ready delivery process
3. Generate 45-60 second script; suggest b-roll: photographer setting up shot in property, before/after HDR comparison, drone footage of property exterior, finished listing photos in MLS context, agent testimonial

## Video Prompts

Use listing photography as primary visual showcase, split-screen before/after comparisons where impactful, drone footage with smooth cinematic movement, and direct-to-camera photographer segments emphasizing speed, quality, and agent-focused service.
