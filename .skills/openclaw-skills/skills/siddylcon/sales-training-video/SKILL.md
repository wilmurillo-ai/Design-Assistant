# Sales Training Video — From Product-Knower to Deal-Closer, at Scale

## Use Cases
1. Objection handling scenario videos: price, timing, champion, competitor, and procurement — word-for-word frameworks in realistic roleplay reps can rewatch before their next call.
2. Discovery call framework videos showing which questions to ask, in what order, and how to recover when the prospect goes off-script.
3. Cold outreach and prospecting videos demonstrating multi-channel sequences, personalization that gets replies, and voicemails that generate callbacks.
4. Pipeline review and deal coaching videos: accurate forecasting, stalled deal recovery, and executive QBR execution for managers and AEs.
5. New hire onboarding accelerator compressing ramp time from 6 months to 6 weeks.
6. Manager calibration videos aligning every front-line coach on what "a great discovery call" looks like — before they give rep feedback.

## How It Works
Tell us your product, your ICP, and the three conversations your team struggles with most. Share call recordings, winning pitch decks, or your top rep's objection cheat sheet if available. Sales Training Video extracts the winning patterns, structures them into teachable frameworks, and produces video modules in 5–10 minute segments — formatted for LMS delivery, Slack enablement channels, and the new hire playlist that plays on day one.

## Tips
- Start with your top 3 most common objections for fastest measurable impact on close rates
- Use anonymized real deal examples — scenarios that feel invented don't stick
- Pair each module with a 2-question knowledge check to drive retention and identify gaps
- Refresh objection handling content quarterly as competitive landscape and ICP evolve
