# AI Podcast Clip Editor — Find the Best Moments, Ship Them Everywhere

## Use Cases

1. **Highlight Clip Extraction** — Analyze a full podcast episode and automatically identify the most quotable, emotionally resonant, and shareable moments — then cut them into precise 30-90 second clips with clean in and out points, ready for every platform.

2. **Auto-Captioned Social Clips** — Generate word-highlighted captions formatted for silent autoplay on Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and LinkedIn — because 85% of social video plays without sound and uncaptioned clips lose that audience entirely.

3. **Guest Quote Cards and Interview Clips** — Package the best moments from interview episodes into branded clips with the guest's name — the clips guests repost to their own following and the ones that bring new listeners into your back catalog.

4. **Audiogram and Waveform Video** — Turn audio-only podcast episodes into visual social assets with animated waveforms, episode artwork, and speaker identification — shareable content from a recording setup with no video camera.

5. **Episode Teaser for Launch Day** — Cut a 60-90 second preview optimized for YouTube, Spotify, and Apple Podcasts to drop at launch — the algorithm-friendly preview that drives episode opens in the first 48 hours when platform promotion is highest.

## How It Works

Upload your podcast episode audio or video and the episode transcript (or let AI Podcast Clip Editor generate one). Specify your show format, target platforms, and clip style. The editor identifies the high-value moments, makes the cuts, adds captions, and exports platform-ready clips for every distribution channel simultaneously — so every episode ships with a full week of social content attached.
