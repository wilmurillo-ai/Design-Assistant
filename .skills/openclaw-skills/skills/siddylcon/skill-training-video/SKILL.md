# Skill Training Video — Training Content That Workers Actually Learn From

## Use Cases
1. **Step-by-Step Procedure and Compliance Training Video** — Show exactly how a task is performed in the correct sequence — the safety checks, the common failure points, the standard that must be met — built for the worker who needs to execute the procedure after watching, not just pass a quiz, because compliance training that doesn't change behavior is a liability disguised as a checkbox.
2. **Onboarding and Role Introduction Video** — Get a new hire from first day to productive faster by showing the workflow, tools, team structure, and performance expectations in a format they can pause and rewatch during their first week — the onboarding content that replaces the handbook nobody reads with video context that actually sticks when someone needs it most.
3. **Microlearning and Performance Support Video** — Three-minute modules designed for a mobile device between tasks — just-in-time training for warehouse floors, retail environments, and field service contexts where no one has forty-five minutes for a desktop training session — the format that delivers skill reinforcement where the work actually happens instead of in a conference room two months before it matters.

## How It Works
Describe the skill or procedure to be trained, the target learner (frontline workers, new hires, managers, or certified professionals), the delivery platform (LMS, mobile app, internal portal), and the performance outcome you need the training to produce — behavior change, compliance certification, or role readiness. Skill Training Video builds the procedure-clear, scenario-realistic training content that employees complete because it respects their time and delivers usable knowledge: compliance procedures that show the right way, onboarding videos that accelerate ramp time, and microlearning modules that support performance in the moment it's needed. Compatible with SCORM-export for LMS integration, optimized for Workday Learning, Cornerstone, TalentLMS, and mobile-first training delivery environments.
