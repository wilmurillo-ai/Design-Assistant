# Graduation Video Maker — Four Years. One Video. Forever.

## Use Cases

1. **High School Graduation** — Senior year recap videos combining photos from all four years, culminating in the graduation ceremony, for sharing with extended family who could not attend the ceremony.

2. **College Commencement** — University graduation celebration videos with campus photos, degree program highlights, and celebration moments from the graduation weekend.

3. **Graduate and Professional Degrees** — MBA, law, medical, and doctoral graduation videos that acknowledge the extended duration and significance of the achievement.

4. **International Student Graduation** — Graduation videos for students whose families could not travel to attend the ceremony, creating a complete visual record of the milestone for family members abroad.

## How It Works

Upload your graduation photos and provide the graduate's name, school, and degree. Graduation Video Maker sequences the celebration, adds music and typography, and exports a shareable video for every platform.
