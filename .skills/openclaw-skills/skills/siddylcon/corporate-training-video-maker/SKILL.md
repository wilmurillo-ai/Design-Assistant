# Corporate Training Video Maker — The L&D Production Tool Your Team Has Been Waiting For

## Use Cases
1. Compliance and regulatory training videos converting policy documents into engaging scenario-based learning content employees actually complete.
2. Product knowledge refresh modules keeping sales teams, customer success reps, and channel partners current after product launches and updates.
3. Manager and leadership development videos covering the skills frontline managers need: coaching conversations, performance reviews, delegation, and team feedback.
4. New hire onboarding video libraries that compress institutional knowledge transfer into a structured watch-and-learn curriculum for the first 30 days.
5. Soft skills and professional development modules that HR teams need in volume but rarely have budget to produce with external agencies.

## How It Works
Describe the training topic, target employee audience, and the behavior change or knowledge outcome you need. Provide any existing materials — policy documents, slide decks, subject matter expert notes, or process diagrams. Corporate Training Video Maker builds the instructional sequence, selects the right visual format for each content type (scenario, explainer, demonstration, or reference card), adds on-screen reinforcement of key learning points, and exports SCORM-compatible modules ready for your LMS.

## Key Features
- Scenario-based learning sequences showing the right decision in realistic workplace contexts
- Animated process walkthroughs for complex compliance and procedural content
- Branded templates matching your company's visual identity for consistent training library look and feel
- Knowledge check integration at key learning intervals to reinforce retention
- LMS-ready export formats for all major corporate learning platforms

## Output Formats
- SCORM 1.2 and SCORM 2004 packages for LMS upload
- MP4 standalone modules for Slack, SharePoint, and intranet distribution
- Short-form reference clips (under 3 minutes) for just-in-time performance support
- Manager guide videos for team meeting facilitation
