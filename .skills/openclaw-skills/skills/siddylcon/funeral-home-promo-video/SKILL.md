# Funeral Home Promo Video — Trust Built Before the Phone Rings

## Use Cases
1. **Facility Tour Videos** — Compassionate walkthroughs of chapel spaces, family rooms, and grounds that help families envision a meaningful farewell before visiting.
2. **Pre-Planning Education** — Videos explaining the emotional and financial benefits of advance funeral arrangements for families who want to protect their loved ones.
3. **Service Options Overview** — Clear explanations of burial, cremation, celebration of life, and green burial options for families navigating choices under emotional stress.
4. **Community Trust Building** — Staff introduction and community involvement videos that establish the funeral home as a trusted, compassionate presence in the local community.

Specify your service offerings and community focus. Funeral Home Promo Video creates respectful, compassionate content in appropriate tone and exports for your website, Google Business Profile, and preneed marketing.
