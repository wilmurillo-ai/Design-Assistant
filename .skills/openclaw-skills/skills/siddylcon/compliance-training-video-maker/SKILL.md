# Compliance Training Video Maker — Training That Protects, Not Just Completes

## Use Cases
1. OSHA and workplace safety training videos showing workers the specific hazard, the correct protective behavior, and the consequence — in the actual work environment context, not a generic warehouse.
2. Harassment prevention and anti-discrimination training videos using scenario walkthroughs that put employees at the decision point so they can recognize the situation in real interactions.
3. HIPAA, FERPA, GDPR, and data privacy training videos animating the specific data handling requirements in the workflow contexts where violations actually happen.
4. Code of conduct and ethics training videos covering conflicts of interest, gift policies, social media conduct, and information handling with industry-calibrated examples.
5. Annual compliance refresh modules that update only the sections where regulations changed — without requiring a full course rebuild or a new vendor contract.

## How It Works
Describe the compliance area, regulatory framework, industry context, and the specific behavioral outcomes you need employees to demonstrate. Provide any existing policy documents, incident case studies, or regulatory guidance materials. Compliance Training Video Maker builds scenario-based content showing the decision point, the available choices, and the outcomes, adds regulatory citations and documentation hooks for audit readiness, and exports completion-trackable modules ready for your LMS.

## Key Features
- Scenario-based decision sequences showing employees the situation, the choice, and the consequence — not just the rule
- Industry-calibrated examples for healthcare, financial services, manufacturing, retail, and professional services
- Regulatory citation overlays for audit documentation and legal defensibility
- Modular architecture allowing annual section updates without full course rebuilds
- Multi-language export support for organizations with multilingual workforces

## Output Formats
- SCORM 1.2 / SCORM 2004 packages with completion tracking for LMS audit trails
- Standalone MP4 modules for intranet, digital signage, and pre-shift safety briefings
- Short scenario clips under 3 minutes for team meeting facilitation and toolbox talks
- Manager discussion guide companion videos for team-level compliance conversations
