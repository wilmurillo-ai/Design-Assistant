# Maid Service Promo Video — Trust That Books the First Clean

## Use Cases
1. **Service Process Videos** — Show the actual cleaning process, products, and quality standards so prospects can visualize the service before they book their first appointment.
2. **Team Introduction Videos** — Introduce your cleaning staff and company story to build the personal trust essential when clients are welcoming cleaners into their home.
3. **Pricing and Package Explainers** — Videos explaining service frequencies, deep clean options, and add-on services that help clients self-select the right package without a phone call.
4. **Neighborhood Targeting** — Localized videos for Nextdoor and neighborhood social media showing cleaning results in homes like those of your prospective clients.

## How It Works
Specify your service area, cleaning specializations, and target homeowner profile. Maid Service Promo Video creates the trust-building content and exports for your website, Google Business Profile, and local social channels.
