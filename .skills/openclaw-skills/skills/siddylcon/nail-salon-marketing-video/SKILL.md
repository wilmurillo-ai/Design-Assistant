# Nail Salon Marketing Video — Turn Your Best Work Into Your Next Booking

## Use Cases
1. Nail art showcase videos capturing the detail and texture of your best sets — gel, acrylic, chrome, 3D art — in close-up format that stops scrollers who are actively looking for a reference.
2. Service menu explainer videos showing the visual and price difference between basic, gel, extension, and custom art services so clients book the right appointment and arrive with the right expectations.
3. Seasonal collection videos — Valentine's, summer, Halloween, holiday glam — driving advance bookings for your highest-demand periods before the slots fill.
4. Nail technician introduction and specialty spotlight videos helping clients choose the tech whose aesthetic matches what they want before they walk in.
5. Pinterest and Instagram portfolio videos that rank in nail art searches and drive organic discovery from clients you have never interacted with before.

## How It Works
Upload your nail photos, finished set shots, or short clips from your chair. Describe your specialty services and the booking message you want clients to act on. Nail Salon Marketing Video assembles your portfolio content into professional short-form videos with close-up sequencing, adds service callouts and booking prompts, and exports versions sized for every platform where nail clients discover their next tech.

## Key Features
- Macro close-up sequencing that showcases nail art detail at the scale clients need to see
- Seasonal collection template library ready to drop in your existing photos
- Multi-tech portfolio format for salons with multiple specialists and styles
- Pinterest-optimized aspect ratio exports alongside standard Reels and TikTok formats

## Output Formats
- Instagram Reels and TikTok vertical video (9:16)
- Pinterest video pin format (2:3 and 1:1)
- Google Business Profile video
- Story and highlight cover formats
