# Martial Arts School Video

Show the teaching environment, the student relationships, and the character development — not just the techniques.

## Use Cases
1. **School Culture and Instructor Introduction Video** — Show the teaching environment, how instructors relate to young students, and how this school builds discipline and confidence — the answer to every parent's real question.
2. **Program-Specific Showcase Video** — Individual videos for kids karate, adult BJJ, Muay Thai, women's self-defense, and competitive training — matched content for every audience you serve.
3. **Belt and Rank Progression Video** — Show the achievement structure, the ceremonial moments, and what a student earns — the motivation that keeps families enrolled through the years.
4. **Adult Self-Defense Program Video** — Speak to the adult who wants practical skills for real scenarios, told through what the program covers and what a graduate can do.
5. **Competitive Team Video** — Show the record, the coaching, and what serious competitive training looks like for the student whose goal is tournament results.

## How to Use

Describe your school, the disciplines and programs you offer, your instructors and their credentials, and the student outcomes you prioritize — character development, practical self-defense, fitness, or competitive performance.

Example prompt: "Create an enrollment video for Iron Tiger Martial Arts. Karate for kids ages 4-12, BJJ and Muay Thai for adults. Instructors have combined 40 years of experience. Emphasis on discipline and confidence building for kids. Adult competitive team with 3 national champions. Highlight the after-school program and the family atmosphere."

## Setup

Store your NemoVideo token at ~/.config/nemovideo/token.txt or set the NEMO_TOKEN environment variable.
