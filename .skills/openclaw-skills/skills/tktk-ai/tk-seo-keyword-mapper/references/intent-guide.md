# Search Intent Classification Guide

## The 4 Intent Types

### Informational (60-70% of searches)
**Signal words**: how, what, why, when, guide, tutorial, tips, examples, learn
**User goal**: Learn something, understand a concept
**Content format**: Blog posts, guides, tutorials, explainers
**Monetization**: Ads, affiliate links, email capture → nurture → sell
**Example**: "how to automate social media posting"

### Navigational (10-15% of searches)
**Signal words**: [brand name], login, pricing, reviews, [brand] vs
**User goal**: Find a specific page or brand
**Content format**: Landing pages, brand pages, documentation
**Monetization**: Direct if it's your brand, comparison pages if competitor
**Example**: "OpenClaw documentation", "Buffer login"

### Commercial Investigation (15-20% of searches)
**Signal words**: best, top, review, comparison, vs, alternative to, for [use case]
**User goal**: Research before buying, compare options
**Content format**: Comparison posts, reviews, roundups, "best X for Y"
**Monetization**: Affiliate links, lead capture, product pages
**Example**: "best ai writing tools for agencies 2026"

### Transactional (5-10% of searches)
**Signal words**: buy, price, discount, coupon, free trial, download, sign up
**User goal**: Complete a purchase or action
**Content format**: Product pages, pricing pages, landing pages
**Monetization**: Direct sales, signups, downloads
**Example**: "openclaw pricing", "ai content tool free trial"

## Intent → Content Type Matrix

| Intent | Best Content Type | Avg Word Count | CTA Type |
|--------|-------------------|---------------|----------|
| Informational | Blog post, guide | 1,500-3,000 | Email signup, related content |
| Navigational | Landing page | 500-1,000 | Direct action (login, docs) |
| Commercial | Comparison, review | 2,000-4,000 | Affiliate link, free trial |
| Transactional | Product/pricing page | 500-1,500 | Buy button, signup form |

## Priority Order for New Sites

1. **Low-competition informational** — Build traffic and authority
2. **Commercial comparison** — Capture decision-stage visitors
3. **Transactional** — Convert existing brand awareness
4. **High-competition informational** — Long-term authority play
