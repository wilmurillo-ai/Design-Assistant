# Cold Email Frameworks — Detailed Guide

## AIDA — Attention, Interest, Desire, Action

**Best for**: First touch emails, warm prospects, well-known pain points

**Structure**:
- **Attention** (1 sentence): Hook with personalized observation or trigger event
- **Interest** (1-2 sentences): Connect to a relevant problem or opportunity
- **Desire** (1-2 sentences): Paint the outcome — social proof, specific result
- **Action** (1 sentence): Clear, low-friction CTA

**Example**:
```
Hi Sarah,

Saw DataFlow just closed Series B — congrats. [ATTENTION]

Scaling analytics companies often hit a content bottleneck right after funding — new product features outpace your ability to communicate them. [INTEREST]

We helped CloudMetrics go from 4 to 25 blog posts/month within 3 weeks of their Series B, without hiring a single writer. [DESIRE]

Worth a 15-min call Thursday to see if we could do the same? [ACTION]
```

---

## PAS — Problem, Agitate, Solve

**Best for**: Pain-heavy industries, urgent problems, prospects who are struggling

**Structure**:
- **Problem** (1 sentence): Name their specific pain
- **Agitate** (1-2 sentences): Make the pain feel urgent — what happens if they don't fix it?
- **Solve** (1-2 sentences): Position your service as the relief + CTA

**Example**:
```
Hi Mike,

Noticed CloudScale is hiring 3 content writers — that tells me content demand is outpacing your team. [PROBLEM]

In my experience, that gap gets worse before it gets better. The hiring takes 2-3 months, onboarding another 1-2, and meanwhile your content calendar falls further behind. [AGITATE]

We can fill that gap this week. Our AI-augmented team produces 50+ SEO posts/month with voice matching. Happy to show you how in 10 minutes? [SOLVE]
```

## BAB — Before, After, Bridge

**Best for**: Transformation stories, case studies, follow-up emails

**Structure**:
- **Before** (1-2 sentences): Describe their current painful situation
- **After** (1-2 sentences): Paint the better future
- **Bridge** (1-2 sentences): Your service is how they get there + CTA

**Example**:
```
Hi Lisa,

Right now GrowthLab probably has 3-5 blog posts in draft purgatory, a social calendar that's never fully loaded, and a newsletter that goes out "when we get to it." [BEFORE]

Imagine publishing 20+ pieces/week across all channels, with your exact brand voice, and spending just 2 hours reviewing instead of 20 hours writing. [AFTER]

That's what our content engine does for agencies your size. 15 minutes — I'll show you the system. Tuesday work? [BRIDGE]
```

---

## QVC — Question, Value, CTA

**Best for**: Short follow-ups, LinkedIn messages, text-based outreach

**Structure**:
- **Question** (1 sentence): Ask something they'll want to answer
- **Value** (1 sentence): Drop one compelling insight or stat
- **CTA** (1 sentence): Specific next step

**Example**:
```
Quick question — how long does a typical blog post take your team from brief to published?

For our agency clients, we got that from 8 hours to 45 minutes.

Worth a chat? I'm free Thursday afternoon.
```

---

## Framework Selection Guide

| Situation | Best Framework |
|-----------|---------------|
| First cold email, strong trigger | AIDA |
| Prospect has obvious pain point | PAS |
| Follow-up with case study | BAB |
| Short follow-up or LinkedIn DM | QVC |
| Breakup/last email | Direct statement + door-open CTA |
