# 25 Proven Cold Email Subject Line Formulas

## Trigger-Based (Highest Open Rate)
1. `Congrats on [trigger event]`
2. `[Trigger event] → quick thought`
3. `Saw [Company] is [trigger event]`
4. `Re: [Company]'s [trigger event]`
5. `[Mutual connection] suggested I reach out`

## Question-Based
6. `[First name], quick question about [topic]`
7. `How does [Company] handle [problem]?`
8. `Curious about [Company]'s approach to [topic]`
9. `[Problem] at [Company]?`
10. `What if [desirable outcome]?`

## Value-Based
11. `[Specific result] for [company type]`
12. `How [similar company] achieved [result]`
13. `[Number]% improvement in [metric] — here's how`
14. `Idea for [Company]'s [specific goal]`
15. `[Stat] about [their industry]`

## Curiosity-Based
16. `[First name] — thought about this`
17. `Quick idea for [Company]`
18. `Something I noticed about [Company]`
19. `This might help with [goal]`
20. `Not what you'd expect`

## Follow-Up Specific
21. `Re: [original subject]` (for reply chains)
22. `[Resource] I mentioned`
23. `Thought you'd find this useful`
24. `One more thing about [topic]`
25. `Last note from me`

## Rules
- 4-7 words optimal (40% higher open rate than 8+)
- No ALL CAPS words
- No excessive punctuation (!!!, ???)
- Personalization in subject = +22% open rate
- Questions outperform statements by 10-15%
- "Re:" only if there was a prior exchange (otherwise deceptive)
