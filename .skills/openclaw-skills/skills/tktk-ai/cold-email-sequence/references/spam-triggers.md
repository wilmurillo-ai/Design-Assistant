# Spam Trigger Words & Patterns

## High-Risk Words (Avoid in Subject Lines)
- Free, free trial, free access
- Guaranteed, guarantee
- No obligation, no cost, no fee
- Act now, limited time, urgent
- Click here, click below
- Buy now, order now
- Congratulations, you've been selected
- Make money, earn money, income
- Discount, sale, deal, offer
- 100%, double, triple

## Medium-Risk Patterns
- ALL CAPS words (even one)
- Multiple exclamation marks (!!!)
- Dollar signs ($$$)
- Percentage symbols with large numbers (500%)
- RE: or FW: when no prior conversation exists
- Excessive links (more than 2)
- HTML-heavy formatting in cold emails
- Image-heavy emails (especially first touch)
- Tracking pixels from unknown domains
- URL shorteners (bit.ly, tinyurl)

## Safe Practices
- Plain text format for cold emails
- Max 1 link (ideally your website, not a tracking link)
- No images in first email
- Short subject lines (4-7 words)
- Personalized first line
- Under 150 words for email 1
- Under 100 words for follow-ups
- Natural language (write like a human, not a marketer)
- Match sender name to email address
- Warm up new email domains (2-4 weeks)

## Deliverability Checklist
- [ ] SPF record configured
- [ ] DKIM signing enabled
- [ ] DMARC policy set
- [ ] Domain age > 30 days
- [ ] Sending volume ramped gradually (start with 10/day)
- [ ] Bounce rate below 3%
- [ ] Unsubscribe link included (for bulk sends)
- [ ] Reply-to address monitored
- [ ] No purchased or scraped email lists
