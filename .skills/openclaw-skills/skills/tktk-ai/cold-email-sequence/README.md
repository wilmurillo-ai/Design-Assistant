# Cold Email Sequence — ClawHub Skill

> Generate complete cold email campaigns with personalized sequences, A/B variants, follow-up logic, and deliverability optimization.

## The Problem

Cold email is the highest-ROI sales channel for B2B — but writing good sequences is painful. Most cold emails get ignored because they're generic, too long, or trigger spam filters.

## The Solution

This skill generates professional cold email sequences that actually get replies:

- **Personalized** — Merge fields for prospect-specific details
- **Multi-framework** — AIDA, PAS, BAB, or custom per email
- **Complete sequences** — 3-5 emails with timed follow-ups
- **A/B ready** — 2 subject lines + 2 body variants per email
- **Deliverability-optimized** — Spam trigger warnings, length checks
