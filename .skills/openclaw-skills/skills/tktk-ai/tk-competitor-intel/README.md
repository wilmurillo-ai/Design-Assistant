# Competitor Intelligence — ClawHub Skill

> Deep competitive analysis — profiles, battle cards, SWOT, pricing intelligence, and exploitable market gaps.

## Why This Skill?

Competitive intelligence agencies charge $2,000-10,000 per report. Most founders and teams skip it entirely because of cost. This skill produces professional-grade competitive analysis in minutes.

**Replaces**: Crayon ($25K+/year), Klue ($15K+/year), manual research (20+ hours per competitor)

