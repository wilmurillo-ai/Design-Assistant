# Content Calendar Template

**Weekly totals**: 20 tweets + 3 threads + 5 LinkedIn + 2 blogs + 1 newsletter = 31 pieces

## Content Type Rotation

### Tweets (Daily Mix)
- 1 value/educational
- 1 engagement/question
- 1 personal/story or hot take

### Threads (3x/week)
- Monday: How-to / Tutorial
- Wednesday: Story / Case study
- Friday: Listicle / Roundup

### LinkedIn (Daily)
- Mon: Industry insight
- Tue: Personal lesson
- Wed: Behind the scenes
- Thu: Data/research share
- Fri: Weekend thought / reflection

### Blog (2x/week)
- Tuesday: SEO-focused long-form (target keyword)
- Thursday: Thought leadership / opinion piece

## Batch Production Workflow (Sunday)

1. **Review performance** — What worked last week? (15 min)
2. **Select topics** — 5 topics for the week (15 min)
3. **Generate pyramids** — 1 pyramid per topic via Content Engine (30 min)
4. **Review and edit** — Quality check all output (45 min)
5. **Schedule** — Load into scheduling tool (15 min)

**Total: 2 hours** for a full week of content across all platforms.
