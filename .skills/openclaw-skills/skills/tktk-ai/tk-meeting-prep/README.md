# Meeting Prep — ClawHub Skill

> Complete meeting preparation in 5 minutes — attendee research, talking points, objection handling, follow-ups. Works for sales, investor, client, and interview meetings.

## Why This Skill?

The #1 predictor of meeting success is preparation. But most people wing it. This skill does in 5 minutes what takes 30-60 minutes of manual research — and produces better results.

**Time saved**: 30-60 minutes per meeting  
**Win rate**: Prepared meetings convert 2-3x higher  
**Coverage**: Sales, investor, client, job interview

## Quick Start

```bash
clawhub install meeting-prep
```

Then:
```
Prepare me for a sales call tomorrow with Sarah Chen, CMO at DataFlow. I'm selling AI content services. Give me everything I need.
```

## Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Attendee Research | Background, role, recent activity, style |
| Company Intel | Overview, news, challenges, opportunities |
| Talking Points | Customized to their situation |
| Objection Handling | Predicted objections + responses |
| Smart Questions | Show preparation, build rapport |
| Follow-Up Template | Send within 2 hours of meeting |
| 2-Min Cheat Sheet | Quick review right before you walk in |

## Meeting Types Supported

- Sales calls (discovery, demo, negotiation, close)
- Investor meetings (pitch, follow-up, due diligence)
- Client meetings (kickoff, check-in, renewal, upsell)
- Job interviews (technical, behavioral, executive)
- Partnership discussions
- Board meetings
