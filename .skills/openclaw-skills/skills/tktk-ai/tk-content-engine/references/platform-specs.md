# Platform Specifications

## X / Twitter
- **Character limit**: 280 (threads: unlimited tweets)
- **Optimal thread length**: 7-12 tweets
- **Media**: Images, GIFs, video (up to 2:20)
- **Hashtags**: 1-2 max (or none — they reduce reach)
- **Best posting times**: 8-10 AM EST, 12-1 PM EST, 5-6 PM EST
- **Tone**: Conversational, punchy, opinionated
- **What works**: Hot takes, threads, personal stories, data

## LinkedIn
- **Character limit**: 3,000 (posts), 700 (articles preview)
- **Optimal post length**: 800-1,200 characters
- **Media**: Images, documents (PDF carousels), video
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 relevant ones
- **Best posting times**: 7-9 AM EST Tue-Thu
- **Tone**: Professional but human, story-driven
- **What works**: Career lessons, industry insights, behind-the-scenes

## Blog / SEO
- **Optimal length**: 1,500-3,000 words
- **Structure**: H1 → H2s → H3s, short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
- **Meta description**: 150-160 characters
- **Title tag**: 50-60 characters
- **Images**: 1 per 300 words, with alt text
- **Internal links**: 2-5 per post
- **What works**: How-to guides, listicles, case studies, comparisons

## Newsletter / Email
- **Subject line**: 40-60 characters
- **Preview text**: 40-100 characters
- **Body**: 500-1,000 words (keep scannable)
- **CTA**: 1 primary, 1 secondary max
- **Best send times**: Tue-Thu, 10 AM recipient timezone
- **What works**: Personal stories, curated links, exclusive insights

## Instagram
- **Caption limit**: 2,200 characters
- **Optimal caption**: 125-150 characters (short) or 800-1,000 (long)
- **Hashtags**: 5-15 in first comment
- **Best posting times**: 11 AM-1 PM, 7-9 PM
- **Tone**: Visual-first, casual, emoji-friendly
- **What works**: Carousels, reels, story-driven captions
