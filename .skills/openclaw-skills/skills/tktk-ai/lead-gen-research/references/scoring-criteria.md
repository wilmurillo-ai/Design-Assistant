# Prospect Scoring Criteria

## Score Range: 1-10

### Company Fit (0-4 points)
- **Industry match** (0-2): How well does the prospect's industry align with your offer?
  - 2 = Direct match (e.g., selling content services to marketing agencies)
  - 1 = Adjacent (e.g., selling content services to SaaS companies)
  - 0 = Poor fit
- **Company size** (0-1): Is the company in your ideal customer profile range?
  - 1 = Within ICP range
  - 0 = Too small or too large
- **Stage/funding** (0-1): Can they afford your solution?
  - 1 = Funded / profitable / growing
  - 0 = Pre-revenue / downsizing

### Timing Signals (0-3 points)
- **Recent trigger event** (0-2): Has something happened that creates urgency?
  - 2 = Strong trigger (new funding, new CMO, competitor launch)
  - 1 = Moderate trigger (job postings, content increase)
  - 0 = No visible triggers
- **Growth indicators** (0-1): Is the company actively growing?
  - 1 = Hiring, expanding, launching new products
  - 0 = Stable or contracting

### Contact Quality (0-3 points)
- **Decision maker access** (0-2): Can you reach the right person?
  - 2 = Direct contact info for decision maker
  - 1 = Can identify but no direct contact
  - 0 = No clear decision maker identified
- **Engagement potential** (0-1): Is the contact active on reachable channels?
  - 1 = Active on LinkedIn/X, engages with relevant content
  - 0 = Low online presence

## Priority Tiers
- **8-10**: High priority — reach out within 24 hours
- **5-7**: Medium priority — include in weekly batch
- **1-4**: Low priority — nurture list or skip
