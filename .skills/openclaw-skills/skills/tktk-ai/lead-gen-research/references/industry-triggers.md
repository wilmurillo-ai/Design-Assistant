# Common Trigger Events by Industry

## SaaS / Tech
- New funding round announced
- New VP of Marketing / CMO hired
- Product launch or major feature release
- Competitor acquisition or shutdown
- IPO preparation signals
- Layoffs (often followed by outsourcing needs)
- Series A/B/C transitions

## E-Commerce / D2C
- New product line launch
- Seasonal ramp-up (Q4, back-to-school, etc.)
- Platform migration (Shopify, BigCommerce)
- International expansion
- Influencer partnership announcements
- Supply chain disruption (creates content/marketing needs)

## Professional Services (Agencies, Consulting)
- New client wins announced
- Office expansion / new hires
- Service line expansion
- Partner/leadership changes
- Award wins or certifications
- Mergers and acquisitions

## Healthcare / Biotech
- FDA approvals or trial results
- New facility openings
- Partnership with health systems
- Regulatory changes affecting operations
- Digital health initiative launches

## Financial Services
- New product launches (cards, accounts, tools)
- Regulatory compliance deadlines
- Fintech partnership announcements
- Branch expansion or digital transformation
- Leadership transitions

## How to Use Triggers in Outreach

**Template**: "I noticed [Company] just [TRIGGER EVENT]. When companies [do this], they typically need [YOUR SERVICE] because [REASON]. We helped [SIMILAR COMPANY] achieve [RESULT] in a similar situation."

**Key principle**: The trigger creates urgency. Your outreach should connect the trigger to a pain point your service solves.
