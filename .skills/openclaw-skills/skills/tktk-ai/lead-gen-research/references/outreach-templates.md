# Outreach Templates

## Email Opener Templates

### Trigger-Based
```
Subject: [Trigger Event] → quick question

Hi [First Name],

Saw [Company] just [trigger event]. Congrats — that's a big move.

When companies [hit this milestone], [common challenge] usually follows. We help [type of company] handle that by [your service in one sentence].

Worth a 10-min call this week?
```

### Pain Point-Based
```
Subject: [Pain point] at [Company]?

Hi [First Name],

[Type of companies] at your stage typically struggle with [specific pain point].

We built [service/tool] specifically for this — helped [similar company] go from [before] to [after] in [timeframe].

Happy to share how. Free Thursday?
```

### Social Proof-Based
```
Subject: How [similar company] solved [problem]

Hi [First Name],

[Similar company in their industry] was spending [X hours/dollars] on [problem]. We cut that to [Y] in [timeframe].

Given [Company]'s focus on [their priority], thought this might be relevant.

Quick call worth it?
```

## LinkedIn Connection Templates

### Warm
```
Hi [Name] — saw your post about [topic]. Really resonated with [specific point]. I work on [related area] and would love to connect.
```

### Direct
```
Hi [Name] — I help [their role] at [company type] with [specific outcome]. Noticed [Company] is [trigger/growth signal]. Would love to share an idea.
```

## Follow-Up Sequence

**Day 0**: Initial outreach (trigger or pain point based)
**Day 3**: Value-add follow up (share relevant article, case study, or insight)
**Day 7**: Social touch (engage with their LinkedIn/X content)
**Day 10**: Direct follow up ("Circling back — still relevant?")
**Day 21**: Breakup email ("Last note from me — here if timing changes")
