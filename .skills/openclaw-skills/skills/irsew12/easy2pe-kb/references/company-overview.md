# Easy2Pe — Company Overview

**Website:** https://easy2pe.com  
**Type:** Multi-service recharge & payments platform (India)  
**Tech Stack:** ASP.NET MVC (.NET Framework 4.x)

## About

Easy2Pe provides India's largest multi-recharge platform. The company offers comprehensive payment services for consumers and merchants, serving over 5,000 consumers with seamless mobile payments via cards, bank accounts, and digital credit. Investors and partners span all over India.

## Core Services

### 1. Prepaid & DTH Recharge
- All prepaid mobile and DTH (Direct-to-Home) recharge
- Live response with fast processing

### 2. Utility Bill Payments (BBPS)
- Postpaid mobile bills
- Landline bills
- Broadband bills
- Electricity bills (via BBPS — Bharat Bill Payment System)
- Insurance premium payments
- Water bill payments

### 3. M-POS (Mobile Point of Sale)
- Mini ATM device services
- 24×7 fast transaction processing
- Maximum commission for agents/retailers

### 4. AEPS (Aadhaar Enabled Payment System)
- Aadhaar-linked banking services

### 5. Travel Booking
- **Bus booking:** Domestic routes, 8,020+ routes, partnerships with premier bus operators (launched 2018)
- **Flight booking:** Domestic and international flights, lowest airfares, Fare Calendar, MyRewards, MyWallet features, 24/7 helpline, 99%+ customer satisfaction
- **Hotel booking:** Domestic and international hotels, budget to luxury, secure payment gateway

### 6. PAN Card Services
- New PAN registration
- PAN card edits/corrections
- PAN is a permanent, lifetime-valid tax identification number for Indian taxpayers (similar to US SSN for tax purposes)

### 7. Digital Gift Cards
Categories offered:
- E-commerce & Online
- Entertainment
- Grocery & Home Needs
- Health & Hospitality
- Fashion & Lifestyle
- Sportswear & Footwear
- Electronics
- Travel & Restaurants

### 8. E-Commerce
- India's largest e-commerce marketplace (as claimed)
- Minimum price, maximum standard
- Custom e-commerce development services and website design

## Key Features
- Single platform for multiple services ("Multi Service with Single Platform")
- Mobile app available for download
- Dedicated support team accessible to all customers
- Secure payment gateway for all transactions

## Target Audience
- Individual consumers making bill payments and recharges
- Merchants and retailers (M-POS, AEPS)
- Travel bookers (flights, buses, hotels)
- Businesses needing PAN services
