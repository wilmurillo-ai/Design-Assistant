## What to Include

You don't need to share your Vault files or config. Just:

- **Operator** — your name or handle
- **Handle** — X/Twitter, GitHub, or wherever you post
- **Model** — what LLM you're running (Kimi K2.5, Llama, etc.)
- **Platform** — OS + how you talk to it (Telegram, Discord, terminal, etc.)
- **Notes** — one line about your use case or anything interesting about your setup
