## Quick Estimation Rules

- **Palm size** chicken breast (~150g) = ~230 cal, 46g protein
- **Fist size** rice (~200g cooked) = ~260 cal, 54g carbs
- **Thumb size** olive oil (~15ml) = ~120 cal, 14g fat
- **1 egg** = ~70 cal, 6g protein
- **1 cup** milk (240ml) = ~150 cal (whole), ~80 cal (skim)

## Macro Targets by Goal

### Weight Loss (Deficit 500 cal)
- Protein: 2.0-2.2g per kg bodyweight
- Fat: 0.6-0.8g per kg
- Carbs: Remainder of calories

### Muscle Gain (Surplus 300 cal)
- Protein: 1.8-2.0g per kg bodyweight
- Fat: 0.8-1.0g per kg
- Carbs: Remainder of calories

### Maintenance
- Protein: 1.6-2.0g per kg bodyweight
- Fat: 0.8g per kg
- Carbs: Remainder of calories
