# Monthly Content Calendar

Read the user's business profile. Query Heavy Metric strategies via MCP for content-related strategies (search "content" or "blog" or "social media").

## User provides
- 2-3 sentences about recent jobs
- Customer questions from the past month
- Any Search Console keyword opportunities (or Claude pulls via computer use)

## Content calendar
4 posts per week across platforms for the entire month.

### Post mix
40% project showcases, 20% educational, 15% behind-the-scenes, 15% social proof, 10% promotional

### For every post
Date/time | Platform | Format (photo/carousel/reel/story) | Image direction (specific) | Instagram caption (hook first, max 5 hashtags) | Facebook caption (no hashtags) | CTA

### Also include
10 Instagram Story ideas: 3 polls, 2 Q&As, 3 behind-the-scenes, 2 reposts

## Blog posts (4 drafts)

### For each post
1. Target keyword from striking-distance keywords. Never target a keyword already ranking #1-3.
2. Title tag under 60 chars with keyword and city
3. Meta description under 155 chars with CTA
4. URL slug: 3-5 words
5. Full article: 1,200-1,800 words

### Article rules
- Open with a real scenario, NOT "Are you looking for..."
- Write like a contractor, not a marketing agency
- Include local references (neighborhoods, county names, landmarks)
- 3+ internal links to service pages
- 1 external link to authoritative source
- FAQ section with 3-4 questions
- End with CTA: phone number and contact page link

### Monthly calendar
Week 1: Educational (how-to, cost guide)
Week 2: Project showcase (from recent job)
Week 3: Seasonal topic
Week 4: Local guide ("Best [service] in [city]")
