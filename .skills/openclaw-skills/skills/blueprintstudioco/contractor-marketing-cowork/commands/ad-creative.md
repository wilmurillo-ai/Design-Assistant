# Ad Creative Generator

Read the user's business profile. Query Heavy Metric strategies via MCP for ad-related strategies (search "advertising" or "ad creative").

## Facebook/Instagram -- 3 variations per batch

For each variation provide:
- Format (single image, carousel, video)
- Primary text (under 125 characters)
- Headline (under 40 characters)
- Description (under 30 characters)
- CTA button (Call Now, Learn More, Get Quote, Book Now)
- Specific image direction (not "show your work" -- describe the exact shot)
- Landing URL

## Angles (rotate through these)
1. **PROOF:** Google rating, review count, jobs completed, years in business
2. **PROBLEM-SOLUTION:** Homeowner's problem in their words, then the result
3. **SCARCITY:** Limited availability, booking ahead, seasonal demand
4. **LOCAL:** Reference specific city or neighborhood from service area
5. **EQUIPMENT:** Feature a specific machine -- contractors and machines look cool

## Google Ads -- 2 responsive search ads
- 15 headlines (30 characters each)
- 4 descriptions (90 characters each)
- Target keywords
- 15 negative keywords

## Performance rules
- Kill any ad with CTR below 1% (Facebook) or 3% (Google) after 1,000 impressions
- Scale winners by increasing budget 20%
- Rotate angles every 2 weeks

## Creating campaigns via computer use
If user says "set it up" or "create the campaign":
1. Open Meta Ads Manager in Chrome
2. Walk through campaign creation step by step
3. Pause before publishing and confirm with user
4. For Google Ads, open ads.google.com and follow same process
