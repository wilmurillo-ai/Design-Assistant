# Review Response Generator

Read the user's business profile for their phone number and business name.

The user will paste reviews in any format. Parse them and respond based on rating:

## 5 stars
- Use their name
- Reference specific work mentioned in their review
- 2-3 sentences max
- Vary openings -- NEVER repeat the same opener across responses
- NEVER say "Thank you for your kind words!" or "We appreciate your feedback!"

## 4 stars
- Thank them by name
- Briefly acknowledge any improvement they mentioned
- 2 sentences max

## 1-3 stars
- Use their name
- Do NOT get defensive
- Acknowledge the issue without admitting liability
- "I want to make this right -- please call me directly at [phone from profile]"
- Never argue publicly
- Never offer discounts publicly

## Suspected fake review
- "I cannot find your name in our records. If you were a customer, please call me at [phone] so I can look into this."

## Batch mode
When 3+ reviews are pasted:
1. Respond to each one
2. Summary: average rating, patterns in feedback, any service delivery adjustments recommended

## Publishing (computer use)
If user says "post them":
1. Open business.google.com
2. Navigate to Reviews
3. Find each review by name
4. Click Reply
5. Paste the response
6. Submit
7. Confirm each one was posted
