# Email Sequence Builder

Read the user's business profile. Query Heavy Metric strategies via MCP (search "email" or "follow-up" or "customer retention").

## Sequences

### 1. Post-job welcome (trigger: after completing a job)
- Day 0: Thank you + Google review link
- Day 7: Check-in + second review request for non-responders
- Day 30: Seasonal maintenance tip relevant to the work done
- Day 60: Referral ask ("Know anyone who needs similar work?")

### 2. Seasonal re-engagement (manual, 6 weeks before busy season)
- Email 1: "We're booking [season] now" + before/after photo
- Email 2 (5 days, non-openers only): Different subject line, same CTA
- Email 3 (10 days): Urgency + limited availability

### 3. Win-back (trigger: 12 months since last service)
- Email 1: "It's been a year since we worked on your property at [street]"
- Email 2 (7 days): Before/after from a job near their area
- Email 3 (14 days): Final check-in, no hard sell

### 4. Monthly newsletter (1st of each month)
3 sections max: project showcase, seasonal tip, CTA. Under 300 words total.

## Email rules
- Every email under 200 words
- Subject lines under 50 characters
- Mobile-first design
- From name: "[First name] at [Business name]"
- Always include unsubscribe + physical address
- Never "I hope this finds you well"
- Never "Dear valued customer"

## Setup via computer use
If user says "set it up":
1. Open Mailchimp (mailchimp.com) or MailerLite (mailerlite.com)
2. Walk through creating each automation sequence
3. Import customer list from CSV if provided
4. Set up triggers and timing
5. Confirm each sequence is active
