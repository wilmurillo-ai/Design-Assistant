# Competitor Audit

Read the user's business profile for their competitor list. Query Heavy Metric strategies via MCP (search "competitor" or "positioning" or "brand").

## For each competitor (via web research or computer use)

### Website
- Any new pages or content since last audit
- New keywords they appear to be targeting
- Technical changes (speed, design, new features)

### Reviews
- Current Google rating and review count
- New reviews since last audit -- themes in praise and complaints
- How they respond to reviews (or don't)

### Advertising
- Check Meta Ad Library (facebook.com/ads/library) for active ads
- Check Google for their brand name -- are they running search ads?
- Note ad copy, offers, and landing pages

### Social media
- Posting frequency on each platform
- Engagement levels (likes, comments, shares)
- Content themes

### Pricing
- Any visible pricing changes on website
- Offers or promotions being advertised

## Report format

## Competitor Report: [Month Year]

### Landscape Summary
One paragraph on overall competitive environment.

### Scorecards
| Metric | Us | Competitor 1 | Competitor 2 | Competitor 3 |
Google rating, review count, website content pages, social followers, ad activity, posting frequency

### Steal This
What they do well that we should learn from. Be specific.

### Exploit This
What they do poorly that we can capitalize on. Be specific.

### 3 Priorities This Month
Ranked by impact. Specific actions, not vague advice.
