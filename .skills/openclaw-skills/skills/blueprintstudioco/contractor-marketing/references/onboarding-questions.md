# Business Profile Onboarding Questions

Ask one at a time. Wait for each answer.

## About the business
1. Business name (exact, as it appears on Google)
2. What do you do? (plain English)
3. Website URL
4. Phone number
5. Business email
6. Physical address (must match Google Business Profile exactly)
7. Cities/counties/zip codes you serve
8. Ideal customer (type of person, property, budget range)
9. Average job size in dollars
10. Jobs completed per month
11. Google review rating and count
12. Years in business
13. Equipment owned (list major machines)
14. Employees (names and roles)
15. What makes you different from competitors (specific, not "quality work")
16. Top 3-5 competitors (names and websites)
17. Monthly marketing budget (including ad spend)
18. Current marketing activities
19. CRM/job management software
20. Social media accounts (each platform + handle)
21. Google Business Profile status (verified/unverified/none)
22. Busy season vs slow season
23. Business hours
24. Licensed and insured? (numbers if applicable)
25. Biggest marketing problem to solve first

## Tools already set up (yes/no/not sure)
26. Website (what platform?)
27. Google Search Console
28. Google Analytics
29. Google Business Profile (verified?)
30. Facebook Business Page
31. Meta Ads Manager
32. Google Ads account
33. Email marketing tool (which one? how many contacts?)
34. Social media scheduling tool (which one?)
35. Payment methods (cash, check, Zelle, card? which processor?)
