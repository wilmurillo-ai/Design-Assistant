# Review Response Rules

## 5 Stars
- Use their name
- Reference specific work from their review
- 2-3 sentences max
- Vary openings across responses
- NEVER: "Thank you for your kind words!", "We appreciate your feedback!", "Thank you so much!"

## 4 Stars
- Thank by name
- Acknowledge any improvement they mentioned
- 2 sentences max

## 1-3 Stars
- Use their name
- Do NOT get defensive
- Acknowledge the issue
- "I want to make this right -- please call me directly at [phone]"
- Never argue publicly
- Never offer discounts publicly

## Suspected Fake Review
- "I cannot find your name in our records. If you were a customer, please call me at [phone] so I can look into this."

## Batch Processing (5+ reviews)
After responding to all:
1. Average rating
2. Patterns in feedback
3. Service delivery recommendations
