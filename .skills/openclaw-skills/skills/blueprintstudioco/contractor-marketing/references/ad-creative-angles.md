# Ad Creative Angles

Rotate through these every 2 weeks:

## 1. PROOF
Lead with numbers: Google rating, review count, jobs completed, years in business.
"4.9 stars. 200+ jobs. Your property is next."

## 2. PROBLEM-SOLUTION
State the homeowner's problem in their words, then show the result.
"Overgrown lot? We cleared 3 acres in Mason last week. Your turn."

## 3. SCARCITY
Limited availability, booking ahead, seasonal demand.
"April is almost full. Lock in your spring clearing now."

## 4. LOCAL
Reference a specific city or neighborhood from the service area.
"Loveland homeowners: we just finished 5 projects in your area."

## 5. EQUIPMENT
Feature a specific machine. Heavy equipment is visually compelling.
"Our [machine name] handles what others can't. See it in action."

## Facebook/Instagram specs
- Primary text: under 125 characters
- Headline: under 40 characters
- Description: under 30 characters

## Google Ads specs
- 15 headlines (30 chars each)
- 4 descriptions (90 chars each)

## Kill/scale rules
- Kill CTR below 1% (Facebook) or 3% (Google) after 1,000 impressions
- Scale winners by 20% budget increase
- Frequency above 2.5 = creative fatigue, swap creative
