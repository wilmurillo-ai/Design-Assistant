# Campaign Calendar Template

## Purpose
Produce a 4-week execution calendar with specific dates, owners, and content tasks. The calendar must be immediately actionable — no vague entries like "post something" or "engage community."

---

## Format Rules

- Use actual week labels (Week 1, Week 2, etc.) mapped to real days (Mon/Tue/Wed/Thu/Fri/Sat/Sun)
- Each entry must have: Platform | Content Type | Topic/Hook | Notes
- Cluster heavy content on Tue–Thu (highest Web3 engagement days)
- Keep weekends light: community check-ins only, no major announcements
- Never schedule two major announcements on the same day

---

## Adaptation Rules

- **DeFi projects**: Add Thursday "yield/liquidity" education posts in Week 2+
- **NFT projects**: Replace Thursday Discord event (Week 3) with mint party / whitelist reveal
- **GameFi**: Add daily in-game event posts on Discord; reduce X frequency to 2–3x/week
- **No token**: Replace any "token" CTAs with product-native actions (subscribe, connect wallet, use feature)
- **Small community (<100)**: Prioritize Discord depth over X breadth; skip Telegram until Week 3

---

## Content Do's and Don'ts

| ✅ Do                                          | ❌ Don't                                      |
|------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| Use specific numbers in posts                  | Say "huge announcement" without details       |
| Acknowledge bugs or issues publicly            | Delete critical feedback                      |
| Cross-post summaries (not duplicates)          | Post identical copy on all channels           |
| Schedule posts for 9–11am EST or 2–4pm EST     | Post at 3am UTC with no timezone awareness    |
| Ask one clear question per community post      | Ask 3 questions in one post (no one answers)  |
