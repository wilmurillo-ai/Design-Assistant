## Tier 4: Local/Regional Directories

### City Portal Networks
- californiaonline.us (and regional equivalents) — one account covers all city portals
- City/county government business directories
- Local "best of" lists and community sites

### Chambers of Commerce (Paid But High Value)
- Local chamber membership typically provides dofollow links
- Competitor backlink analysis often reveals chamber links as top sources
- Prioritize the chamber for the city where the business is located, then expand

### Community/Newspaper Directories
- Local newspaper websites often have free business directories (Locable-powered, etc.)
- Community sites (eldohills.com, zoomfolsom.com, etc.)
- Regional business directories

## Competitor Backlink Analysis Process

When the client provides a competitor backlink export (CSV from Ahrefs, SEMrush, etc.):

1. **Parse all rows** — extract referring domain, DR/DA, nofollow status, spam flag, page title, URL
2. **Deduplicate by domain** — take highest DR per domain
3. **Filter:** DR ≥ 10, not spam
4. **Separate dofollow vs nofollow** — prioritize dofollow
5. **Cross-reference** against client's existing listings
6. **Categorize** new opportunities: free directory, paid directory, editorial mention, chamber, etc.
7. **Submit** to all free qualifying directories
8. **Flag** paid opportunities for client decision
