# Traffic Strategy Framework

---

## Traffic Hierarchy (Priority Order)

Execute in this order. Higher tiers have lower cost and higher conversion.

| Tier | Source | Cost | Conversion | Do First |
|------|--------|------|------------|----------|
| 1 | Existing email list | $0 | Highest | Yes |
| 2 | Organic community amplification | $0 | High | Yes |
| 3 | Partner / affiliate sends | Revenue share | High | Yes |
| 4 | Paid ads | Budget | Medium | After organic |

---

## Tier 1 — Email List

**Send cadence (10-day campaign):**
- Day 1: Announcement email (longest, most story-driven)
- Day 3: Value-forward email (teach something, pitch at end)
- Day 5: Social proof email (testimonials + registration push)
- Day 7: Urgency email (seats / deadline framing)
- Day 9: Day before reminder
- Day 10: Day-of sequence (3 emails: morning / 1hr before / 10min before)

**List segmentation:**
- Engaged (opened in last 30 days) → full sequence
- Cold (not opened in 90+ days) → send Day 1 and Day-of only
- Buyers (previous purchasers) → personalized sequence referencing their purchase

---

## Tier 2 — Organic Community Amplification

**The Meme Militia Model:**
- 25–35 active community members
- Each posts minimum 1 time per day across their platforms
- Coordinated daily themes so content doesn't feel repetitive
- 10-day campaign = 350+ organic posts total

**Content Mix:**
| Type | % of Posts | Job |
|------|------------|-----|
| Memes (funny, relatable, shareable) | 50% | Virality |
| Testimonials (real results) | 20% | Social proof |
| Countdown posts | 15% | Urgency |
| Behind-the-scenes | 10% | Authenticity |
| Direct registration prompts | 5% | Conversion |

**Expected reach:**
- Conservative: 500 impressions per post
- 35 posts/day × 500 = 17,500 impressions/day
- 10 days = 175,000 organic impressions
- Estimated registrations: 50–150

**Daily theme schedule:**
- Day 1: Announcement (who is this training for?)
- Day 2: The problem (pain amplification)
- Day 3: The possibility (dream outcome)
- Day 4: Social proof (testimonials, results)
- Day 5: Behind the scenes (prep, energy, excitement)
- Day 6: FAQ (answer the top 3 objections)
- Day 7: Countdown (3 days)
- Day 8: Countdown (2 days)
- Day 9: Tomorrow (urgency + last chance)
- Day 10: TODAY (multiple posts, maximum urgency)

## Tier 3 — Paid Ads

### Platform Selection by Audience

| Audience | Best Platform |
|----------|--------------|
| Entrepreneurs / business owners | X/Twitter, LinkedIn |
| Creators / side hustlers | Instagram, TikTok |
| Professionals (35–55) | Facebook, LinkedIn |
| Crypto / Web3 | X/Twitter |
| General consumer | Facebook, Instagram |

### 10-Day Budget Framework

| Phase | Days | % of Budget | Goal |
|-------|------|------------|------|
| Test | 1–3 | 30% | Run 5 variations. Identify top 2 by CTR and cost per registration. |
| Scale | 4–7 | 50% | Kill bottom 3. Double spend on top 2. |
| Push | 8–10 | 20% | Final urgency push. Add retargeting layer. |

**Example at $400 total:**
- Days 1–3: $30/day ($90 total)
- Days 4–7: $50/day ($200 total)
- Days 8–10: $37/day ($110 total)

### Retargeting Layer (Add Day 4–10)

Target: People who visited registration page but did NOT register.
Copy angle: *"Still thinking about it? Spots are filling."*
Budget: 15–20% of daily spend.

### The 5 Ad Copy Variations

Always produce all 5 before launching. Test all 5 in Days 1–3. Scale top 2.

**Variation 1 — Problem-Focused**
Hook: Name the pain they feel right now.
Body: Agitate the problem. Show consequences of staying stuck.
CTA: Register to learn the solution.
Best for: Cold audiences who know the problem but haven't found the solution.

**Variation 2 — Credibility-Driven**
Hook: Your most impressive result or credential.
Body: Brief story of how you achieved it. What you're teaching.
CTA: Join free training.
Best for: Audiences who need to know you're real before clicking.

**Variation 3 — Direct Benefit**
Hook: The outcome they want most.
Body: Bullet list of exactly what they'll walk away with.
CTA: Register free.
Best for: Audiences already aware of the solution, deciding between options.

**Variation 4 — Short + Punchy**
Hook: 5 words maximum. Extreme curiosity or contrarian claim.
Body: 2–3 sentences only. No explanations.
CTA: One link, one action.
Best for: Scrollers. First exposure to cold traffic.

**Variation 5 — Identity / Values**
Hook: Speak to who they want to become (not what they want to have).
Body: The values and operating principles of the person they aspire to be.
CTA: This training is for [identity statement] only.
Best for: Audiences with strong identity attachment to your niche.

### Ad Copy Rules

- **Headline:** Must create unanswerable curiosity OR name an irresistible outcome
- **Image/video:** Pattern interrupt over polish. Curiosity over pretty.
- **Registration page hook must match ad hook** — mismatched messaging destroys conversion
- **Never explain what they'll learn** — curiosity drives clicks, not information
- **Specific beats vague:** "$4,000 in one day" beats "make money with AI"

---

## Registration Page Principles

**Brunson's rule:** The only copy on the registration page should be:
1. The hook (one bold curiosity-driven claim)
2. The 3 secrets / what they'll learn (written as curiosity, not topics)
3. The form

**Fladlien's rule:** The hook must be unanswerable without attending. If they can figure out the answer in their head, they won't register.

**Conversion benchmarks:**
- Cold traffic: 25–40% registration rate
- Warm traffic (email list): 40–60% registration rate
- If below 25% on cold: test a new hook
- If below 40% on warm: test subject lines and email copy

## Show-Up Rate Optimization

Industry average show-up rate: 30–35%.
Target: 40%+.

**Tactics to increase show-up rate:**
1. Send day-before + 1-hour + 10-minute reminder emails (non-negotiable)
2. SMS reminder if you collected phone numbers (20–30% lift)
3. Go To Webinar trick (Brunson): Change webinar title to "★ STARTING NOW ★" 10 minutes before — GTW sends email notification to all registered attendees
4. Short pre-webinar indoctrination videos (Day 1 / Day 2 / Day 3 after registration) — builds anticipation and makes attendance feel urgent
5. Community posts the morning of: *"Going live in [X] hours"* — social proof creates FOMO
6. Organic amplifiers post "joining now" content during the first 10 minutes — creates wave of live energy

**Show-up rate impact on revenue:**
- 500 registrants × 30% show-up = 150 live attendees × 10% conversion = 15 sales
- 500 registrants × 40% show-up = 200 live attendees × 10% conversion = 20 sales
- Increasing show-up rate from 30% to 40% = 33% more sales from the same registrations
