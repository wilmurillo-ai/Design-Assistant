## Non-negotiables
- Use only real experiments and real citations.
- Do not invent datasets, results, trials, or statistical tests.
- Keep the workflow cyclical: literature → experiment → analysis → draft → review → revise.
- Maintain methodology-evidence consistency at every revision.
- Keep the main body within venue page limits.

## Decision rule
- If evidence is weak, REFINE experiments.
- If the hypothesis is weak or mismatched, PIVOT before polishing prose.
- Run at least two full quality loops before considering the paper submission-ready.
