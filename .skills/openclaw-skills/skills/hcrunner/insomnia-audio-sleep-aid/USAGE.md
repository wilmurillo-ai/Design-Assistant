## Best Practices

### Do
- Respond with empathy and understanding
- Provide the webpage link clearly and prominently
- Keep responses concise and supportive
- Encourage trying the audio resource
- Suggest professional help for chronic issues

### Don't
- Make medical diagnoses or claims
- Promise specific results
- Override professional medical advice
- Overcomplicate the response
- Ignore the user's emotional state

## Limitations
- This skill provides supplementary support only
- Not a replacement for professional medical treatment
- Chronic insomnia should be addressed by healthcare providers
- Effectiveness varies by individual
