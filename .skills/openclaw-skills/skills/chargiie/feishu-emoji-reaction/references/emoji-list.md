# Feishu Emoji Reference

Complete list of tested and documented Feishu emoji reactions.

## Confirmed Working

### Positive & Affirmative
- **THUMBSUP** 👍 — Agree, approve, acknowledge
- **HEART** ❤️ — Love, appreciate
- **Yes** — Affirmative, agree
- **LGTM** ✅ — Looks Good To Me (code review)
- **Get** ✅ — Understood, got it
- **CHECK** ✅ — Confirmed, completed
- **OnIt** 🔄 — Working on it ("在做了在做了")
- **SaluteFace** 🫡 — Roger that, acknowledged
- **AWESOMEN** 🐮 — Awesome, amazing (牛逼)
- **FIRE** 🔥 — Excellent, lit
- **PARTY** 🎉 — Celebrate, success

### Emotions
- **SMILE** 😊 — Social smile (呵呵, passive-aggressive in Chinese context)
- **THINKING** 🤔 — Pondering, considering
- **EMBARRASSED** 🙈 — Embarrassed, shy
- **CRY** 😢 — Sad, unfortunate
- **SPEECHLESS** — Wordless (无语)
- **WHAT** ❓ — What? Shocked
- **SPITBLOOD** 😵 — Extreme frustration (吐血)
- **COMFORT** 🤗 — Pat on the head, comforting (摸头)
- **HUG** 🫂 — Hug, embrace
- **STRIVE** 💪 —努力 (strive, keep going)
- **SHY** — Shy, bashful
- **DULL** — Dull, unimpressed (学习自大头 2026-04-01)

### Context & Tone
| Emoji | Literal | Cultural Meaning |
|-------|---------|------------------|
| SMILE 😊 | Smile | 呵呵 (passive-aggressive smile, not genuine happiness) |
| OnIt 🔄 | On it | "在做了在做了" (meme: claiming to work while procrastinating) |
| SPITBLOOD 😵 | Spit blood | 吐血 (extreme frustration, beyond angry/sad) |
| AWESOMEN 🐮 | Awesome | 牛逼 (badass, impressive) |
| COMFORT 🤗 | Comfort | 摸头 (pat on the head) |

## Usage Notes

1. **Emoji names are case-sensitive**
2. **Some emoji have culturally specific meanings** — don't assume literal translations
3. **The list is not exhaustive** — Feishu supports more emoji, learn them
