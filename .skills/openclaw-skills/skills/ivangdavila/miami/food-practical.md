| Store | Vibe | Best For | Price |
|-------|------|----------|-------|
| **Publix** | Florida institution | Everything, subs famous | $$ |
| **Whole Foods** | Upscale, prepared foods | Health food, hot bar | $$$ |

### Publix Culture
- **Pub subs** — Legendary sandwiches, build your own
- **Buy one get one** — Famous BOGO deals
- **Publix brand** — Quality store brand
- **Boar's Head** — Premium deli meats

- **Sedano's** — Cuban groceries, best in Little Havana
- **Presidente** — Dominican/Caribbean focus
- **La Preferida** — Mexican products
- **Bravo** — General Latin

## Food Delivery

### Apps That Work
| App | Coverage | Notes |
|-----|----------|-------|
| **Uber Eats** | Best coverage | Most restaurants |
| **DoorDash** | Good | Growing |
| **Postmates** | Limited | Being absorbed into Uber |
| **Grubhub** | Limited | Not strong in Miami |

### Tips
- Delivery fees + service fees add up ($7-15 extra)
- Traffic makes delivery times unpredictable
- Restaurant pickup often faster and cheaper
- Some Cuban spots don't do delivery — call directly

## Tipping & Paying

### Standard Tips
| Service | Tip |
|---------|-----|
| Sit-down restaurant | 18-22% |
| Counter service | $1-2 or 10-15% |
| Delivery | $3-5 minimum, 15-20% |
| Bartender | $1-2/drink |

### Watch For
- Many restaurants add 18% gratuity automatically
- Check bill before adding more tip
- "Service charge" is NOT always tip — ask

### Payment
- Cards accepted almost everywhere
- Some Cuban spots cash-only (getting rare)
- Venmo common for smaller spots
- Apple Pay/Google Pay growing
