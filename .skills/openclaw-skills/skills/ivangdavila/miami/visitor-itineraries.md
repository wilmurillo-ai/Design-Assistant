# Miami Itineraries

## 1-Day Itinerary (First-Timer)

**Morning (9am-12pm)**
- Start at Versailles in Little Havana for Cuban breakfast + cafecito
- Walk Calle Ocho, watch domino players at Máximo Gómez Park
- Browse cigar shops, fruit stands

**Afternoon (12pm-5pm)**
- Uber to Wynwood (~15 min)
- Lunch at Wynwood Kitchen or Zak the Baker
- Explore Wynwood Walls + surrounding galleries
- Walk to Design District (15 min), browse luxury stores + art installations

**Evening (5pm-10pm)**
- Uber to South Beach
- Walk Lincoln Road (pedestrian mall)
- Sunset drinks at Juvia rooftop or The Setai
- Dinner at Joe's Stone Crab (season only) or Prime 112
- Walk Ocean Drive (just to see, don't sit)

## 3-Day Itinerary

### Day 1: Art Deco & Beach
- Morning: Art Deco Walking Tour (start at Welcome Center, 10th & Ocean)
- Late morning: Beach time at South Beach (south of 5th for less crowds)
- Lunch: Big Pink (classic diner) or Pura Vida (healthy)
- Afternoon: PAMM or Frost Science Museum
- Evening: Sunset at South Pointe Park, dinner in SoBe

### Day 2: Cuban Culture & Neighborhoods
- Morning: Versailles breakfast, Little Havana exploration
- Lunch: Azucar ice cream + Cuban sandwich
- Afternoon: Wynwood Walls + Design District
- Evening: Dinner at KYU (Asian BBQ) or Mandolin (Greek), drinks at Broken Shaker

### Day 3: Nature & Upscale
- Morning: Vizcaya Museum & Gardens
- Lunch: Coconut Grove (Green Street Café, Lulu)
- Afternoon: Key Biscayne beach (Crandon Park) or kayaking
- Evening: Coral Gables exploration, dinner at Biltmore or Ortanique

## 7-Day Itinerary

### Days 1-3: Follow 3-day itinerary above

### Day 4: Day Trip — Everglades
- Book airboat tour (Everglades Safari Park or Gator Park)
- 3-4 hours in the Glades
- Return via Shark Valley bike trail (optional)
- Evening: Chill in Brickell, rooftop drinks

### Day 5: Day Trip — Keys
- Drive to Key Largo (1.5h) or Islamorada (2h)
- Snorkeling at John Pennekamp Coral Reef State Park
- Lunch at local fish shack
- Return for dinner in Miami

### Day 6: Local Life
- Morning: Farmers market (Lincoln Road Sat, or Coconut Grove Sat)
- Brunch: Mandolin, Blue Collar, or Salty Donut
- Afternoon: Miami Beach Botanical Garden (free) + shopping
- Evening: Explore Brickell nightlife or Faena Hotel

### Day 7: Beach Day & Departure
- Morning: One last beach session (try Haulover or Bill Baggs for variety)
- Lunch: La Mar (Peruvian) at Mandarin Oriental
- Pack up, head to airport

## Budget Itinerary (1 Day)

**Total Cost: ~$50**

- Breakfast: Cafecito + pastelito at any ventanita ($4)
- Morning: Free Wynwood street art
- Lunch: Cuban sandwich from La Camaronera ($12)
- Afternoon: Free beach at South Beach + free South Pointe Park
- Sunset: Free views from South Pointe pier
- Dinner: Taco stand in Little Havana or food truck in Wynwood ($15)
- Evening: Walk Lincoln Road, Ocean Drive (free people watching)

**Tips for Budget Travel:**
- Use Metromover (free!) in downtown/Brickell
- Beach is free (just bring towel, don't rent chairs)
- Happy hours have great deals (5-7pm most places)
- Cuban food is cheap and filling
