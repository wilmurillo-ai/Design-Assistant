# Portuguese Experiences

## Cultural Experiences

### Fado in Lisbon ⭐
**What:** Portugal's soul music. Melancholic, passionate, UNESCO heritage.
**Where:** Alfama, Mouraria, Bairro Alto
**When:** Evening shows start 21:00-22:00

**Authentic venues:**
| Venue | Style | Cost | Notes |
|-------|-------|------|-------|
| Tasca do Chico | Spontaneous | Free (order drinks) | Tiny, magical |
| Mesa de Frades | Intimate | €5 cover | Former chapel |
| Clube de Fado | Professional | €25 minimum | Higher quality |
| Maria da Mouraria | Local | Free (drinks) | In Mouraria |

**Fado etiquette:**
- Silence during performance (mandatory)
- Clap between songs, never during
- Dress smart casual
- No photos during songs

**Skip:** Tourist dinner shows (€50+, formulaic)

### Port Wine Cave Tour
**Where:** Vila Nova de Gaia (opposite Porto)
**Cost:** €12-25
**Time:** 1-1.5 hours

**Best caves:** Taylor's (best overall), Graham's (views), Ramos Pinto (intimate)

**Pro tip:** Book Taylor's ahead for terrace lunch with view.

### Surfing
**Where:** Ericeira, Peniche, Costa da Caparica, Algarve
**Cost:** Lessons €30-50, board rental €15-25/day
**Season:** Year-round (wetsuit needed except summer)

**Beginner beaches:** Carcavelos, Baleal (Peniche), Matosinhos, Praia do Amado (Algarve)

### Azulejo Workshop
**Where:** Lisbon (various ateliers)
**What:** Paint your own traditional tiles
**Cost:** €40-80 for 2-3 hours
**Recommended:** Cerâmica de São Pedro, Oficina de Azulejos

### Cooking Classes
**What:** Learn traditional dishes
**Where:** Lisbon, Porto
**Cost:** €60-120

**Classes:**
- **Cooking Lisbon** — Market tour + cooking
- **Taste of Lisboa** — Food tour + petiscos class
- **Oporto Food Tours** — Porto-focused

## Adventure Experiences

### Big Wave Surfing (Spectating)
**Where:** Nazaré
**When:** October-March (winter swells)
**What:** Watch from Forte de São Miguel lighthouse

**How it works:** WSL Big Wave Tour when conditions right. Waves 20-30m. Free to watch. Dramatic.

### Dolphin & Whale Watching
**Where:** Azores (best), Setúbal, Algarve
**When:** Year-round (species vary)
**Cost:** €50-80

**Azores:** Best in Atlantic. See sperm whales, dolphins, sometimes blue whales.
**Setúbal:** Dolphins in Sado estuary, resident population.

### Kitesurfing
**Where:** Guincho, Algarve lagoons
**Best:** Ria Formosa (flat water), Guincho (waves)
**Season:** April-October
**Cost:** Lessons €80-150

### Canyoning
**Where:** Peneda-Gerês, Madeira
**What:** Waterfall rappelling, swimming, jumping
**Cost:** €50-80 half day
**Season:** May-October

### Paragliding
**Where:** Serra da Arrábida (Lisbon area), Madeira
**What:** Tandem flights over coast/mountains
**Cost:** €90-130
**Duration:** 15-30 minutes flight

## Unique Experiences

### Pastéis de Nata Class
**Where:** Lisbon
**What:** Make Portugal's famous custard tarts
**Cost:** €35-50
**Where:** Pastelaria Batalha, various cooking schools

### Cork Factory Visit
**Where:** Alentejo
**What:** See world's largest cork producer
**Cost:** Free-€10
**Recommended:** Amorim (largest), or smaller family operations

### Wine Harvest (Douro)
**When:** September
**What:** Pick grapes, traditional treading (sometimes)
**Where:** Various quintas
**Book:** Months ahead

### Swimming in Hot Springs
**Where:** Azores
**Free:** Ferraria (ocean hot spring, tide-dependent)
**Paid:** Terra Nostra (€8), Poça da Dona Beija (€8)

### Levada Walks (Madeira)
**What:** Walk along irrigation channels through laurel forest
**Best:** 25 Fontes, Caldeirão Verde
**Cost:** Free (guided €40-60)
**Unique:** Tunnels, waterfalls, endemic plants

## Festivals

### Santos Populares (June)
**Where:** Lisbon (Santo António), Porto (São João)
**When:** Throughout June, peak 12-13 (Lisbon), 23-24 (Porto)
**What:** Street parties, grilled sardines, plastic hammers (Porto), paper decorations

**Lisbon:** Alfama decorations, sardines, arraiais (street parties)
**Porto:** Hit people with plastic hammers (seriously), release balloons, party all night

### Carnaval (February/March)
**Where:** Torres Vedras (biggest), Loulé, Ovar
**What:** Parades, costumes, satire
**Vibe:** Less famous than Rio, more satirical/political

### Festa da Flor (May)
**Where:** Madeira
**What:** Flower festival, parades, carpet of flowers
**When:** After Easter, usually May

### Romaria de Nossa Senhora da Agonia (August)
**Where:** Viana do Castelo
**What:** Traditional costumes, folk culture, fireworks
**When:** Third weekend of August

## Food & Drink Experiences

### Market Tours
**Lisbon:**
- Mercado da Ribeira (Time Out Market) — Tourist but good food court
- Mercado de Santa Clara — Antiques + food (Saturday)
- Mercado de Arroios — Local, no tourists

**Porto:**
- Mercado do Bolhão — Under renovation, temporary market
- Mercado Bom Sucesso — Modern food court

### Wine Tasting Experiences
**Douro:** Quinta visits with lunch (book ahead)
**Lisbon:** Wines of Portugal Tasting Room (Praça do Comércio)
**Porto:** Same concept near Palácio da Bolsa

### Ginjinha Tasting
**Where:** A Ginjinha bar (Rossio, Lisbon)
**What:** Sour cherry liqueur shots
**Cost:** €1.50-2
**Order:** "Com" (with cherries) or "sem" (without)

## Day Experiences

### Sintra + Cascais
**Duration:** Full day
**Route:** Train to Sintra (early), palaces, train/bus to Cascais, sunset, train back
**See:** sintra.md

### Arrábida + Setúbal
**Duration:** Full day
**What:** Beaches, Serra da Arrábida, seafood in Setúbal
**Need:** Rental car
**Best for:** Beach day + nature

### Óbidos + Nazaré
**Duration:** Full day
**What:** Walled medieval village + fishing town/big waves
**From:** Lisbon (1-1.5h each)
**Need:** Car or organized tour

### Douro Valley
**Duration:** Full day (or overnight)
**What:** Wine tastings, scenic train, quintas
**From:** Porto
**Options:** Train (scenic, limited), tour (easy), car (freedom)

## Booking Tips

- **Fado houses:** Many don't take reservations—arrive early (20:00)
- **Quinta visits in Douro:** Book 1-2 weeks ahead
- **Cooking classes:** Book 3-7 days ahead
- **Festival accommodation:** Book months ahead
- **Whale watching in Azores:** Book 1-2 days ahead (weather dependent)
