# Portugal Itineraries

**Pace:** Comfortable

| Day | Where | Highlights |
|-----|-------|------------|

| 3 | Sintra | Full day: Pena Palace + Quinta da Regaleira |

## Planning Tips

### Domestic Flights
- Lisbon ↔ Porto: 1h, from €20
- Lisbon ↔ Faro: 45min, from €25
- Lisbon ↔ Funchal: 1h30
- Lisbon ↔ Ponta Delgada: 2h15

### Trains (cp.pt)
- Lisbon → Porto: 3h Alfa Pendular, €35
- Lisbon → Faro: 2h30, €25
- Porto → Douro (Pinhão): 3h, €14 (scenic!)

### Car Rental
**Need for:** Alentejo, Algarve exploring, islands, Rota Vicentina, Centro
**Don't need for:** Lisbon, Porto, train routes

### Best Seasons
- **Spring (Apr-May):** Perfect weather, fewer crowds
- **Summer (Jun-Aug):** Beach weather, crowded, expensive
- **Fall (Sep-Oct):** Great weather, wine harvest
- **Winter (Nov-Mar):** Mild south, rainy north, good for Madeira
