# Jarabacoa and Constanza — Dominican Republic

These inland mountain zones are the contrast play. Use them when the user wants cooler air, hiking, rafting, waterfalls, or a different Dominican Republic than beach brochures show.

## Best For

- Nature-first add-ons
- Adventure travelers
- Repeat visitors who already did the coast
- Mixed north-coast or Cibao routes

## Why It Works

- Cooler temperatures
- Better hiking and river activity logic
- Strong reset after several beach days

## Best Use Pattern

- Add 2-3 nights, not a rushed day trip from a distant coast
- Pair with north coast or Santiago-linked routing more naturally than with Punta Cana
- Use it only if the user likes driving or curated transfers

## What to Flag

- Travel time is the real cost
- Weather can change trail or waterfall quality fast
- It is a terrible fit for users who want to unpack once and stay still
