# Beaches — Dominican Republic

Do not talk about beaches as if they all deliver the same water, wind, and vibe. Matching the coast to the user's expectation matters more than naming the most famous stretch.

## Fast Beach Map

| Coast or area | Best For | Reality |
|---------------|----------|---------|
| Punta Cana and Bavaro | Easy resort beach time | Best for convenience, conditions vary by season |
| Bayahibe side | Calmer southeast logic and island access | Good for balanced trips |
| Samana peninsula | Scenic beaches and greener feel | Higher reward, more logistics |
| Las Galeras side | Quieter, lower-density beauty | Better for slower travelers |
| Cabarete | Wind sports and active beach days | Not the calm-water default |
| Puerto Plata area | Mixed-use beaches and easy north-coast routing | More about varied trip shape than one iconic beach |

## Choose by User Type

| User type | Best beach logic |
|-----------|------------------|
| Wants easiest swimmable resort week | Bayahibe or selected Punta Cana areas |
| Wants beach town plus restaurants | Las Terrenas |
| Wants wind sports | Cabarete |
| Wants scenic quieter beach add-on | Samana or Las Galeras |

## Reality Checks

- Seaweed and swell can change the quality of the same beach.
- A famous beach in photos may be wrong for families, weak swimmers, or users who want calm mornings.
- East coast and north coast often feel very different even when both are called "beach vacation" areas.
