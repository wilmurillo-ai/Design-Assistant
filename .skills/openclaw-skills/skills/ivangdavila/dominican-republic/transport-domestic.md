# Transport Domestic

## Best Transport Model by Trip Type

| Trip | Better transport model |
|------|------------------------|
| Punta Cana or Bayahibe resort stay | Airport transfer plus selected excursions |
| Santo Domingo city stay | Walking in key districts plus app rides |
| North-coast town trip | Airport transfer or rental car depending base hops |
| Samana or Jarabacoa independent loop | Private transfer or rental car if user accepts daylight driving |

## Airports

- Choose the airport that matches the coast. East-coast resort trips do not need a capital-city arrival unless pricing or routing clearly justifies it.
- Santo Domingo works for city trips and some southeast pairings.
- Puerto Plata works best for north-coast stays.
- Samana or eastern arrivals can reduce road friction when available.

## Buses and Long Transfers

- Caribe Tours, Metro, and Expreso Bavaro cover many intercity moves, but they solve only part of the trip because hotel-last-mile transport still matters.
- Buses work best for light packers who are staying in cities or town centers, not isolated villas or large resort compounds.

## City Movement

- Santo Domingo traffic is the country's main urban friction point. Keep airport, hotel, and dinner geography compact.
- In resort corridors, hotel transfers are often cleaner than improvising roadside taxis.
- Moto-based informal transport is not the default recommendation for most travelers.

## Rule

In Dominican Republic planning, transport failure usually happens in the last mile, not on the headline route, so always finish the movement plan from terminal to hotel and back.
