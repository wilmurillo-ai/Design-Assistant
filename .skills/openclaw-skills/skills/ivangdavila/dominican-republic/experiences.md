# Experiences — Dominican Republic

Good experience planning should reduce randomness. Choose experiences by trip shape, not by a giant generic excursion menu.

## Best Experience Types

| Experience | Best For | Watch out for |
|------------|----------|---------------|
| Saona or island day | Southeast bases | Early starts, crowding, weather |
| Whale watching | Seasonal Samana trips | Conditions and timing matter |
| Diving or snorkeling | Bayahibe and some southeast stays | Skill level and sea conditions |
| Waterfalls, rafting, mountain days | Jarabacoa-led routes | Transfer time and physical effort |
| Colonial Zone cultural time | Santo Domingo trips | Heat and pacing |
| Wind sports | Cabarete | Works only if the user actually wants active beach days |

## How to Choose

- Pick one signature experience per 3-4 days of trip length, not one every day.
- If the user is staying in a resort to rest, do not overload the schedule with bus or boat days.
- If the user is in Samana or the mountains, keep a weather backup ready.

## What to Avoid

- Selling every boat trip as mandatory
- Combining long transfer days with physically demanding activities
- Recommending adventure-heavy days to families with small kids or users with mobility limits
