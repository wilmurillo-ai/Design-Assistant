## By Profile

### Young Professionals
1. **Kallio** - Nightlife, cafes, affordable
2. **Punavuori** - Design scene, restaurants
3. **Vallila** - Emerging, good value

### Families
1. **Toeoeloe** - Parks, schools, safe
2. **Lauttasaari** - Island life, community
3. **Tapiola** - Planned city, nature nearby

### Students
1. **Arabia** - Near Aalto Arts, cheap
2. **Kallio** - Lively, budget-friendly
3. **Otaniemi** - Aalto main campus (Espoo)

### Tech Workers
1. **Ruoholahti** - Tech offices nearby
2. **Keilaniemi** - Nokia, tech cluster (Espoo)
3. **City Center** - Easy commute anywhere

### Expats
1. **Toeoeloe** - International schools nearby
2. **Lauttasaari** - Expat community established
3. **Westend (Espoo)** - Upscale, international

### Budget Priority
1. **Itaekeskus** - Metro access, cheap
2. **Vuosaari** - Far east, affordable
3. **Vantaa suburbs** - Lowest prices

## Rent Trends (Feb 2026)

- **Rising**: Kallio, Vallila (gentrification)
- **Stable**: Toeoeloe, Lauttasaari
- **Affordable**: East (Itaekeskus, Vuosaari), Vantaa

## Safety

All neighborhoods are safe. Finland consistently ranks among safest countries.
Some late-night areas in Kallio can be lively (bars) but not dangerous.
