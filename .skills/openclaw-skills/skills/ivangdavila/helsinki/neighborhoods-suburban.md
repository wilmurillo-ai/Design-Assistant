# Helsinki Suburban Neighborhoods

## Espoo Overview

Second largest city in Finland, part of Helsinki metro area. More space, more nature, lower rents. Well-connected by metro and trains.

### Tapiola

**The planned garden city.** Built in 1950s-60s as ideal modern community. Cultural center, Aalto architecture, excellent services.

| Aspect | Rating | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Rent (1BR) | EUR 1,000-1,400 | Good value |
| Transit | Excellent | Metro direct to Helsinki |
| Groceries | Excellent | Ainoa shopping center |
| Dining | Good | Local options |
| Green space | Excellent | Gardens, Nuuksio nearby |

**Best for**: Families, nature lovers, design enthusiasts, Aalto campus students

**Landmarks**: Tapiola Garden City, WeeGee Exhibition Centre, Aalto main campus

### Otaniemi

**Aalto University main campus.** Tech hub, student housing, Nokia headquarters nearby.

| Aspect | Rating | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Rent (1BR) | EUR 800-1,200 | Student area pricing |
| Transit | Good | Metro |
| Campus life | Strong | University amenities |

**Best for**: Students, academics, tech workers

### Keilaniemi

**Business district.** Nokia HQ, tech companies, modern offices. Limited residential.

| Aspect | Rating | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Rent (1BR) | EUR 1,000-1,400 | Business area |
| Transit | Excellent | Metro |
| Work access | Excellent | Major employers |

**Best for**: Tech workers, business professionals

### Westend/Haukilahti

**Upscale waterfront.** Villas, international families, beaches.

| Aspect | Rating | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Rent (1BR) | EUR 1,200-1,600 | Upscale |

### Leppaevaara

**Espoo's commercial center.** Sello shopping mall, services, modern apartments.

| Aspect | Rating | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|

## Vantaa Overview

Third largest city, north of Helsinki. Airport location. Most affordable option in metro area.

### Tikkurila

**Vantaa's center.** Train station hub, shopping, services. Main administrative area.

| Aspect | Rating | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Rent (1BR) | EUR 800-1,100 | Affordable |
| Transit | Excellent | Train direct to Helsinki (20 min) |
| Groceries | Excellent | Dixi shopping center |

**Best for**: Budget seekers, commuters, practical living

### Myyrmaki

**Dense residential.** Good services, train connection, multicultural.

| Aspect | Rating | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Rent (1BR) | EUR 750-1,000 | Very affordable |
| Transit | Good | Train |
| Character | Diverse | Multicultural area |

**Best for**: Budget priority, diversity seekers

| Aspect | Rating | Notes |

## Comparison: Helsinki vs Espoo vs Vantaa

| Factor | Helsinki | Espoo | Vantaa |
|--------|----------|-------|--------|
| **Rent** | Highest | Middle | Lowest |
| **Transit** | Best | Good (metro) | Good (train) |
| **Services** | Most | Good | Basic |
| **Nature** | Parks | Best (Nuuksio) | Moderate |
| **Character** | Urban | Suburban | Practical |
| **Nightlife** | Yes | Limited | None |

### Recommendation

- **Helsinki proper**: Urban life, willing to pay premium
- **Espoo**: Families, nature, tech workers
- **Vantaa**: Maximum savings, practical priorities
