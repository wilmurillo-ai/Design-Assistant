# Helsinki Lodging

## Area Guide

### City Center (Keskusta)
**Best for**: First-timers, short stays, business
- Walking distance to everything
- Highest prices
- Busy, less local atmosphere

**Hotels**: Kamp, Haven, Scandic Grand Central
**Budget**: EUR 150-300/night

### Kamppi
**Best for**: Shopping, transport hub, convenience
- Above/near bus terminal
- Good metro/tram access
- Commercial area

**Hotels**: Original Sokos Hotel Presidentti, GLO Hotel Art
**Budget**: EUR 120-200/night

### Punavuori/Design District
**Best for**: Design lovers, boutique experience
- Trendy area with character
- Great restaurants and bars
- Slightly south of center

**Hotels**: Hotel Fabian, Klaus K
**Budget**: EUR 130-220/night

### Kallio
**Best for**: Budget travelers, nightlife, local vibe
- Hipster neighborhood
- Best bars and cafes
- 10-15 min tram to center

**Hotels**: Scandic Kallio, hostels
**Budget**: EUR 80-150/night

### Katajanokka
**Best for**: Quiet, waterfront, unique
- Former prison hotel (Best Western Premier Hotel Katajanokka)
- Near ferry terminals
- Peaceful, residential

**Budget**: EUR 120-180/night

## Alternative Accommodation

### Apartments (Airbnb/Booking.com)
- Good for 4+ nights or groups
- Kitchen saves on expensive restaurants
- Book neighborhoods: Kallio, Punavuori, Kruununhaka
- EUR 80-150/night for 1BR

### Summer Options (Jun-Aug)
- University dorms as hotels (Domus Academica)
- Lower prices, basic amenities
- Book early, popular period

## Booking Tips

### When to Book
- **Christmas/New Year**: 2-3 months ahead
- **Summer (Jun-Aug)**: 1-2 months ahead
- **Conferences/Events**: Check Helsinki events calendar
- **Regular times**: 1-2 weeks usually OK

### Price Patterns
- **Cheapest**: January-February (dark season)
- **Highest**: June-August, Christmas
- **Weekends**: Often cheaper than weekdays (business city)

### Breakfast Notes
- Finnish breakfast: Hearty, includes bread, fish, porridge
- Hotel breakfast: EUR 15-25 if not included
- Alternative: Cafes nearby often cheaper and better

## Near Airport (Vantaa)

Only if early flight or late arrival:
- **Clarion Hotel Helsinki Airport**: Connected to terminal
- **Scandic Helsinki Airport**: 5 min walk
- **Break Sokos Hotel Flamingo**: Near, with spa
- **Train to city**: 30 min, runs 5:00-24:00, EUR 5

No reason to stay at airport otherwise. City is 30 min away.
