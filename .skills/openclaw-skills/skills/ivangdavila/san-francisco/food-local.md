# SF Local Specialties

## Mission Burritos

**What makes it SF:** Rice inside, giant size, foil-wrapped, meant to be eaten immediately.

| Spot | Location | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| **El Farolito** | 24th & Mission | Late night, cash only, super burrito |
| **La Taqueria** | 25th St | No rice (purist), award-winning |
| **Taqueria Cancún** | Mission | Reliable, good salsa bar |
| **Papalote** | 24th St | Great salsas, slightly upscale |

**Order:** Super burrito (beans, rice, meat, cheese, sour cream, guac)

## Irish Coffee

Invented at Buena Vista Café in 1952. The original recipe.

**Where:** Buena Vista Café, Fisherman's Wharf
**Cost:** ~$12
**Note:** It's a tourist thing, but the original is worth trying once
