# Buenos Aires — Playbook

## Best Use Case

First landing zone, culture and food base, short premium city stay, or urban half of a city-plus-nature Argentina trip.

## Core Advantage

High density of neighborhoods, dining, museums, and useful flight connectivity without needing a long stay.

## Route Strategy

- Cluster days by neighborhood instead of zigzagging across the city.
- Keep one lighter day for parks, markets, or recovery before the next flight.
- Use Buenos Aires as a soft landing or final decompression point, not automatic center of gravity for the whole trip.

## Stay Strategy

- Prioritize neighborhood fit and evening return comfort over a tiny nightly discount.
- Short trips should favor walkability and reliable airport-transfer logic.

## Transport Notes

- Public transport uses SUBE and works well for many tourist movements.
- Car rental is usually a mistake for city-only stays.

## Common Mistake

Spending too many days in Buenos Aires on a short country trip and then compressing the region that actually motivated the trip.
