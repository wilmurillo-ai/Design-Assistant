# Border Hops and Neighbor Countries — Argentina

## When This Matters

Use this file when user wants to add the Brazil side of Iguazu, drive to Chile, or stack neighboring-country logistics onto an Argentina route.

## Brazil Side of Iguazu

- The Brazilian side is often worth it, but it should be treated as a separate planning decision.
- Passport, visa, and queue logic can turn a simple waterfall add-on into a full-day operation.
- For U.S., Canadian, and Australian travelers, Brazil visa assumptions changed after 2025; verify before promising the side trip.

## Chile Crossings

- Chile mountain crossings from Mendoza and some Patagonian routes are weather and queue sensitive.
- Never connect a mountain border day to a hard non-refundable deadline on the other side.

## Self-Drive Border Rule

- Rental-car cross-border paperwork must be solved before pickup.
- If the vehicle cannot cross, redesign the route rather than improvising at the border.

## Common Mistake

Selling a border hop as "just a quick add-on" when it actually needs passport, weather, and transfer planning.
