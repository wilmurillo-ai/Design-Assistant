# South Patagonia — Playbook

## Best Use Case

Glaciers, dramatic landscapes, serious hiking interest, and the classic El Calafate plus El Chalten model.

## Core Advantage

This is the Patagonia choice for users who want glacier scale and iconic hiking rather than general scenic comfort.

## Route Strategy

- Treat El Calafate and El Chalten as distinct bases with different jobs.
- Use El Calafate for glacier logistics and arrival stability.
- Use El Chalten only if weather, energy, and hiking appetite justify it.

## Reservation and Access Notes

- Peak windows require earlier flight and lodging discipline.
- Los Glaciares north-zone access is online-first and card-based.
- Camp reservations and remote-area registration are not optional details.

## Risk Notes

- Wind can make a perfect-looking itinerary collapse quickly.
- Build at least one flex window if hiking is central to the trip.

## Common Mistake

Selling El Calafate and El Chalten as a single easy stop when they need separate pacing, transport, and weather logic.
