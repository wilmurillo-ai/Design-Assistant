# Salta and Jujuy — Playbook

## Best Use Case

Cultural north, desert color palettes, slow scenic driving, and travelers who want something radically different from Buenos Aires and Patagonia.

## Core Advantage

High visual payoff and regional identity with strong food and landscape variation.

## Route Strategy

- Plan by altitude and driving tolerance, not only by map loops.
- One strong base plus selected excursions often beats a rushed multi-town chain.
- Use extra caution if user is sensitive to altitude, long drives, or motion fatigue.

## Transport Notes

- Self-drive can be excellent here for confident drivers.
- For slower travelers or mixed ages, driver-supported days can reduce fatigue.

## Common Mistake

Packing Salta, Jujuy, and mountain loops into too few days and turning a scenic region into a windshield exercise.
