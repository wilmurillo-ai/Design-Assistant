# Food Guide — Argentina

## Food Strategy by Region

| Region | Strongest angle |
|--------|-----------------|
| Buenos Aires | Parrillas, pizza, cafe culture, pastries, modern dining |
| Mendoza | Wine lunches, vineyard dining, slower premium meals |
| Salta/Jujuy | Regional empanadas, stews, Andean flavors |
| Patagonia | Lamb, trout, chocolate, comfort food after outdoor days |
| Atlantic coast | Seafood when season and location fit |

## Practical Rules

- Dining hours can run later than many visitors expect.
- Great Argentina trips are not built on steak alone; regional food adds more value than repeating the same city-center grill format.
- Weekend reservations matter more in top neighborhoods and winery circuits.

## Common Mistake

Only chasing famous parrillas and missing the regional identity that actually differentiates the trip.
