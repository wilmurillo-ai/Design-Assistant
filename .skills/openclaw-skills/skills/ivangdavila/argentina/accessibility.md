# Accessibility — Argentina

## Planning Rule

Accessibility should be solved at the base-and-routing level, not as a note added after attractions are chosen.

## Best-Case Patterns

- Buenos Aires for the widest range of urban services and transport options
- Iguazu for viewpoint-led nature impact without mandatory hard trekking
- Mendoza for slower premium stays with controlled movement

## High-Friction Patterns

- Historic areas with uneven sidewalks or cobblestones
- Hike-led Patagonia plans
- Same-day airport plus excursion schedules
- Boats, boardwalks, or nature access points with boarding constraints

## What to Confirm Early

- Elevator and shower details
- Distance from hotel to evening dining
- Airport assistance if flights are involved
- Transfer type for luggage and mobility equipment

## Common Mistake

Treating "nature trip" as automatically incompatible with low mobility instead of redesigning it around scenic access and better bases.
