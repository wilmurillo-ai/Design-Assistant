# Nightlife — Argentina

## Nightlife by Trip Type

| Destination | Best for | Watch-out |
|-------------|----------|-----------|
| Buenos Aires | Late dinners, bars, live culture, some club routes | Very late start times and return logistics |
| Mendoza | Wine bars and softer evenings | Less about all-night energy, more about pacing |
| Bariloche / resort towns | Seasonal bar scenes and relaxed nights | Can be weather- and season-dependent |
| Atlantic coast in summer | Domestic holiday energy | Peak-season crowd and price spikes |

## Planning Rule

- Nightlife should follow neighborhood and return-safety logic, not just hype.
- For short trips, one or two strong evenings beat trying to "sample everything."

## Common Mistake

Booking a far-away hotel, dining too early, and then losing the evening to transit dead time.
