# Itineraries — Argentina

## 7 Days: One Contrast Pair

### Option A: Buenos Aires + Iguazu
- Days 1-3: Buenos Aires by neighborhood clusters
- Days 4-5: Iguazu with one full Argentina-side park day
- Days 6-7: Brazil-side option or return buffer

### Option B: Buenos Aires + Mendoza
- Days 1-2: Buenos Aires soft landing
- Days 3-6: Mendoza city plus winery/Andes split
- Day 7: Buffer + departure

## 10 Days: City + One Big Nature Cluster

### Option A: Buenos Aires + South Patagonia
- Buenos Aires: 2-3 nights
- El Calafate: glacier and logistics base
- El Chalten or extra Calafate days: only if hiking/weather fit

### Option B: Buenos Aires + Patagonia Lakes
- Buenos Aires: 2 nights
- Bariloche/Villa La Angostura cluster: 6-7 nights
- Keep one flex day for weather and driving logic

## 14 Days: Two Strong Regions

### Option A: Buenos Aires + Mendoza + Iguazu
- Works when user values contrast over deep Patagonia
- Use flights, not heroic overland transfers

### Option B: Buenos Aires + South Patagonia + Mendoza
- Strong premium mix if user accepts multiple flights
- Do not add Iguazu unless trip length and tolerance are unusually high

## 21 Days: Three-Region Maximum

- Region 1: Buenos Aires or Mendoza
- Region 2: One Patagonia cluster
- Region 3: Iguazu or Salta/Jujuy
- Keep the final night in the departure city

## Itinerary Quality Checks

- Every weather-sensitive region gets a fallback or flex day
- Every airport change is priced with real transfer time
- Every border hop is optional, not critical path
- Every short trip cuts destinations before cutting sleep
