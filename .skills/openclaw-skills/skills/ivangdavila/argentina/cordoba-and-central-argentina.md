# Cordoba and Central Argentina — Playbook

## Best Use Case

Domestic-style pacing, sierras escapes, city plus slower inland rhythm, or travelers who want less tourist pressure than the headline circuits.

## Core Advantage

Good value and easier pacing for users who do not need Patagonia-level spectacle on every day.

## Route Strategy

- Works best as a calm regional trip or recovery block, not as a frantic add-on to every Argentina itinerary.
- Pair with Buenos Aires only if user explicitly wants a lower-intensity second region.

## Common Mistake

Including Cordoba only because it is on the way, without a real trip reason.
