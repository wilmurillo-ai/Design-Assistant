# Peninsula Valdes and Puerto Madryn — Playbook

## Best Use Case

Wildlife-led trip, family nature travel, lower-hiking Patagonia add-on, or users who care more about fauna than about glacier trekking.

## Core Advantage

Strong nature payoff with relatively approachable physical demands compared with harsher Patagonia trekking models.

## Planning Notes

- Wildlife value depends heavily on season.
- Keep expectations species-specific instead of promising "all animals, all year."
- A car can help, but the trip still needs paced wildlife windows rather than constant moving.

## Common Mistake

Treating Peninsula Valdes as a generic beach stop instead of a seasonal wildlife product.
