## Dietary Restrictions

### Vegetarian/Vegan
Challenging but improving:
- **Temple food** (사찰음식) — Buddhist, naturally vegan
- **Bibimbap** — Request no meat (고기 빼주세요)
- **Plant Café** — Vegan café, Itaewon
- **Osegye Hyang** — Vegan Korean, Insadong

### Halal
- **Itaewon mosque area** — Multiple halal restaurants
- **Busan-style halal** — Limited but exists
- **Masjid Al-Madinah area** — Halal Korean

### Gluten-Free
Difficult — soy sauce (contains wheat) is everywhere
- **Rice-based dishes** — Safer options
- **Korean BBQ** — Plain meat is usually safe
- Communicate clearly: "밀가루 알레르기" (wheat allergy)

### Allergies
- Sesame, nuts, shellfish common in Korean cuisine
- English allergen info rare outside international restaurants
- Learn your allergen in Korean and show written note
