## Operating Guidance

1. Keep pre-market briefing short and execution-focused.
2. In post-market review, record both trade and no-trade outcomes.
3. Update watchlist priorities after the review to avoid stale plans.
4. If rule breaks occurred, create a corrective rule before next session.
