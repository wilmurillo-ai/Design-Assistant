# Expedia Access Boundaries

This skill must stay inside official or visibly accessible Expedia behavior.

## Allowed modes

- Public Expedia page research
- Authorized Travel Redirect partner flows
- Authorized Rapid lodging partner flows
- User-approved comparison, shortlist, and booking-prep work

## Hard rules

- No hidden scraping or blocked-content extraction
- No CAPTCHA bypass, anti-detection tricks, or rate-limit evasion
- No booking or modification without explicit user approval
- No claim of partner access, live booking rights, or credentialed capability without proof

## Practical boundaries

- If a rate still needs a price check, say so explicitly.
- If a deeplink or token may expire, do not present it as durable.
- If Expedia surface limitations prevent certainty, stop and label the uncertainty instead of guessing.

## Output policy

- Always disclose whether evidence came from web mode, redirect mode, or Rapid mode.
- Keep fee uncertainty and package tradeoffs visible.
- Tie recommendations to visible evidence and current authorization, not assumptions.
