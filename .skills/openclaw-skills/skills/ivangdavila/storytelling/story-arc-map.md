# Story Arc Map

Use this file when the draft feels disconnected, flat, or hard to follow.

## Arc Blueprint

1. Situation
- What world are we entering?
- Why should the audience care right now?

2. Friction
- What is not working?
- What cost appears if nothing changes?

3. Choice
- What decision changes the trajectory?
- Why this option and not the alternatives?

4. Execution
- What actions were taken?
- What obstacles appeared during execution?

5. Outcome
- What changed in measurable or visible terms?
- What remains unresolved?

6. Transfer
- What should the audience do with this story now?

## Arc Quality Checks

- Every section should create pressure for the next section.
- If a section can be removed without changing meaning, merge or cut it.
- If the ending does not map back to the opening tension, the arc is incomplete.

## Repair Moves

- No tension: add one meaningful stake with a time, cost, or consequence.
- No decision moment: isolate the turning point and name the rejected alternative.
- No proof: add one concrete observation, metric, or quote-level detail.
- No transfer: add one explicit implication for the audience.
