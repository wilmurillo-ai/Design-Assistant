# Shipping and Claims - Vinted

Use this file when an item sells or when delivery problems appear.

## Before Shipment

Run the same proof sequence every time:
- final photos of front, back, tags, and flaws
- packed-item photo
- label or parcel reference captured
- timestamped note of handoff or drop-off

Proof should be easy to find later, not rebuilt from memory.

## Packing Standard

Pack to survive ordinary transit:
- protect delicate surfaces
- seal against moisture
- avoid overstuffing thin mailers
- match packaging to item shape and value

Good packing is part of trust, not an afterthought.

## Tracking and Exceptions

Watch only for exceptions:
- no movement
- wrong route
- damage report
- buyer says item differs from listing

When an exception appears, freeze assumptions and rebuild the timeline from proof.

## Claim Handling

Structure the issue in this order:
1. what was sold
2. what proof exists
3. what the carrier or platform shows
4. what the buyer claims
5. next best action inside platform rules

Chronology matters more than emotion.

## Fast Escalation Signals

Escalate carefully when:
- parcel status stalls beyond normal expectation
- buyer reports damage that was not visible pre-shipment
- the received item allegedly differs from documented details
- the conversation pushes for off-platform settlement

Never delete proof while an issue is open.
