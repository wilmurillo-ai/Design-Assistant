# Trust and Safety - Vinted

Use this file when a listing, buyer, seller, or conversation feels wrong.

## Counterfeit and Misrepresentation Checks

Pause and review when you see:
- luxury or hype items with weak proof of authenticity
- inconsistent tags, labels, or packaging details
- condition claims that conflict with photos
- seller behavior that changes after purchase questions

If authenticity confidence is low, skip or relist only with stronger evidence.

## Off-Platform Risk Signals

Treat these as hard warnings:
- payment outside the marketplace
- courier stories that bypass normal labels
- requests to continue on another app before trust is established
- pressure to rush around platform rules

Low friction for the other person usually means high risk for the user.

## Account Safety Rules

- Keep all high-risk negotiation inside platform messaging.
- Do not promise what the listing does not prove.
- Do not hide damage, missing parts, or uncertain fit.
- Do not continue with buyers who repeat scam patterns.

## Business and Policy Boundaries

Block tactics that create account risk:
- misleading condition grading
- prohibited or restricted items
- fake urgency or fake scarcity
- evasive behavior after a dispute starts

Short-term wins that damage trust are not wins.
