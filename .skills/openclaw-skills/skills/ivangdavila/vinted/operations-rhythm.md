# Operations Rhythm - Vinted

Use this cadence to keep the closet clean, pricing rational, and incidents visible.

## Daily Checks

- reply to serious buyer questions and unresolved offers
- check sold items waiting for shipment
- review new issues or suspicious conversations
- watch only exceptions, not every listing

Daily work should be short and focused on movement.

## Weekly Checks

- review stale listings and choose keep, refresh, markdown, or bundle
- inspect offer acceptance rate versus floor discipline
- review category or brand pockets with strongest sell-through
- log dispute patterns and prevention rules

Weekly cadence is where most resale quality improves.

## Monthly Checks

- assess what categories deserve more effort
- identify what stock keeps trapping time with weak return
- tighten pricing rules and bundle policy
- decide whether casual mode is drifting into pro mode

Monthly reviews stop clutter from becoming strategy.

## Incident Rhythm

When a problem appears:
1. stabilize the active issue
2. rebuild the evidence timeline
3. choose the smallest safe next action
4. record the prevention rule

Fast action matters, but repeated prevention matters more.
