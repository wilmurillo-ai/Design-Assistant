# Fiordland and Southland Playbook

## Core Rule

If Fiordland matters, give it real structure. This is not a casual add-on.

## Best Base

### Te Anau
- easiest practical base
- far better than trying to do everything from Queenstown
- good for early starts and calmer pacing

## Milford Sound vs Doubtful Sound

| Option | Best For |
|--------|----------|
| Milford Sound | First-timers, iconic scenery, easier booking logic |
| Doubtful Sound | Quieter, longer, more immersive day |

## Key Planning Reality

- Rain is common and can actually improve the waterfall drama
- Clear weather is not guaranteed
- Road conditions, avalanche controls, and coach timings can reshape the day
- Sandflies are part of the deal

## Best Way to Do Milford

### Option 1: Base in Te Anau
Best all-round choice for most users.

### Option 2: Coach-cruise-fly from Queenstown
Good when the user hates long drives and accepts premium cost plus weather risk.

### Option 3: Self-drive day trip from Queenstown
Possible, but only if the user understands it is a long day and not relaxed.

## Southland Beyond Fiordland

- **Invercargill** is functional, not the main reason to come
- **Bluff** matters most to oyster fans and trip-completion types
- **Stewart Island** is excellent for slow wildlife and deep-nature travelers with time

## Common Mistakes

- Booking only one night and expecting perfect weather
- Doing Milford on the same day as a major transfer
- Leaving the car with no repellent, layers, or food
- Treating Fiordland like a quick scenic stop instead of a weather-led region
