# Costs and Taxes — Texas

Use this file for budget comparisons, tax framing, and hidden recurring costs.

## What "No State Income Tax" Does and Does Not Mean

- Texas has no personal state income tax.
- That does not mean low overall cost.
- For many households, the real counterweights are sales tax, property tax pressure, insurance, tolls, and high summer electricity use.

## Sales Tax Reality

- Texas state sales and use tax is 6.25%.
- Local additions can raise the combined rate up to 8.25%.
- Do not quote a single number without noting local variation.

## Business and Self-Employment Reality

- Texas business costs still include formation, permits, local compliance, payroll obligations, and franchise-tax reporting where applicable.
- "No income tax" is not a substitute for asking what the user sells, where they operate, and whether local permits apply.

## Household Budget Categories That Surprise Newcomers

| Category | Why it hurts |
|----------|--------------|
| Property tax pressure | Raises ownership cost and can flow into rent over time |
| Home and auto insurance | Hail, wind, flood, and driving exposure matter |
| Toll roads | Common in DFW, Austin, and Houston routines |
| Power bills | Summer cooling can materially change the monthly picture |
| Car dependence | Insurance, fuel, maintenance, and parking stack together |

## Budget Rules

### 1. Compare monthly life, not only rent or mortgage
- If the user is comparing Texas against California, New York, Florida, or another Texas metro, price the whole routine.

### 2. Ask whether the user is owner, renter, or operator
- Property tax logic, homestead workflows, and insurance exposure change by role.

### 3. Distinguish state tax from local burden
- Sales tax, appraisal districts, and utility structure all create local spread inside the same metro.

## Common Mistakes

- Selling Texas on tax reputation alone.
- Forgetting local sales tax variation.
- Ignoring insurance and power when discussing affordability.
- Giving founder advice without mentioning franchise-tax or permit workflows.
