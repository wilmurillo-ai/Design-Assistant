# Sources — Texas

Use these sources when precise Texas rules or current workflows matter.

## State Services and Resident Tasks

- Texas.gov resident services: https://www.texas.gov/
- Texas DPS driver license services: https://www.dps.texas.gov/section/driver-license
- TxDMV new-to-Texas vehicle guidance: https://www.txdmv.gov/motorists/new-to-texas

## Taxes and Property

- Texas Comptroller taxes portal: https://comptroller.texas.gov/
- Texas sales and use tax overview: https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/sales/
- Texas property tax help and exemptions: https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/property-tax/

## Family and Education

- Texas Education Agency: https://tea.texas.gov/
- Texas school district locator tools and district resources: start from TEA and district sites

## Emergency and Preparedness

- Texas Ready preparedness guidance: https://www.texasready.gov/
- FEMA flood maps when flood risk matters: https://msc.fema.gov/

## Utilities and Power

- Power to Choose for deregulated electricity areas: https://www.powertochoose.org/
- ERCOT public grid conditions: https://www.ercot.com/

## Travel and Regions

- Official Texas travel portal: https://www.traveltexas.com/

## Usage Notes

- Use official state or local sources for unstable rules.
- Use county, district, or city portals when state pages point to local execution.
- If a portal requires an address, send it only when the user wants location-specific help.
