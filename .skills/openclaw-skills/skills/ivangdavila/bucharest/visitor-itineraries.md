# Bucharest Itineraries

## 1 Day: Essential Bucharest

### Morning (9am-1pm)
1. **Start at Revolution Square** (30 min)
   - See the balcony where Ceaușescu gave his last speech
   - Former Royal Palace (now National Art Museum)
   - Memorial to 1989 revolution victims

2. **Walk down Calea Victoriei** (30 min)
   - Beautiful historic boulevard
   - Pass Romanian Athenaeum (photo stop)
   - Architecture from Belle Époque to communist

3. **Palace of Parliament** (2 hours)
   - Book morning tour in advance
   - English tours available hourly
   - Bring ID/passport

### Lunch (1pm-2pm)
- **Caru' cu Bere** in Old Town — traditional Romanian, touristy but worth it
- Or **Lacrimi și Sfinți** — upscale Romanian cuisine

### Afternoon (2pm-6pm)
4. **Old Town / Lipscani** (2 hours)
   - Wander cobblestone streets
   - Stavropoleos Church (hidden gem)
   - Carturesti Carusel bookstore
   - Pasajul Macca-Vilacrosse

5. **Cișmigiu Gardens** (1 hour)
   - Relax in oldest park
   - People watching, peaceful

### Evening (7pm onwards)
6. **Dinner in Old Town**
   - Terrace dining in summer
   - Bar hopping if you're up for it
   - Try mici and wine

## 3 Days: Deep Dive

### Day 1: History & Culture

**Morning**
- Revolution Square + National Art Museum (if interested)
- Walk to Romanian Athenaeum
- Calea Victoriei stroll

**Lunch**: Shift Pub or Energiea (central, good value)

**Afternoon**
- Palace of Parliament tour
- Walk down Bulevardul Unirii (communist axis)
- See contrast of old neighborhoods

**Evening**
- Old Town for dinner and drinks

### Day 2: Parks & Museums

**Morning**
- Village Museum (Muzeul Satului) — arrive early
- Herăstrău Park walk

**Lunch**: Hard Rock Cafe Herăstrău or park restaurants

**Afternoon**
- National Museum of Romanian History (optional)
- Shopping at Băneasa Shopping City or AFI Cotroceni

**Evening**
- Floreasca area for dinner (more upscale, less touristy)
- Expirat or Control for live music

### Day 3: Relaxation or Day Trip

**Option A: Therme Day**
- Therme București (thermal spa)
- Spend half day minimum
- Return for evening in city

**Option B: Day Trip to Snagov**
- Snagov Monastery (Dracula's tomb allegedly)
- Lake scenery
- 30 min from city

**Option C: Neighborhood Exploration**
- Cotroceni district (Presidential Palace area)
- Drumul Taberei Park
- Local feel away from tourists

**Evening**
- Fine dining: Kaiamo, The Artist, or Joseph

## Practical Timing Tips

| Activity | Best Time |
|----------|-----------|
| Palace of Parliament | Morning (first tours) |
| Old Town | Afternoon/evening |
| Parks | Morning or late afternoon |
| Museums | Weekday mornings |
| Day trips | Start by 8am |
| Therme | Weekday to avoid crowds |

### Seasonal Adjustments

**Summer (Jun-Aug)**
- Start early, heat peaks 2-5pm
- Terrace dining everywhere
- Parks and lakes ideal
- Old Town very crowded at night

**Winter (Dec-Feb)**
- Museums and indoor attractions
- Christmas markets (December)
- Therme even better
- Layer up, it's cold
- Some outdoor attractions limited

**Spring/Fall**
- Best overall weather
- All attractions accessible
- Fewer tourists than summer
