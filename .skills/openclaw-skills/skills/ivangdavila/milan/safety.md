# Milan Safety and Legal Basics

## Overall Safety Profile

Milan is generally safe by European big-city standards, with most issues concentrated in petty theft and tourist-targeted scams.

## Main Risk Types

| Risk | Typical Context | Mitigation |
|------|-----------------|------------|
| Pickpocketing | Crowded metro, stations, tourist clusters | Keep valuables front and zipped |
| Bag or phone snatch | Busy transit transitions | Avoid visible loose devices |
| Tourist overcharging | Landmark-adjacent services | Check menu and fare clarity first |
| Rental scams | High-demand housing market | Verify contract and legal details |

## Area Awareness

- Major station areas can vary by block quality.
- Nightlife zones require normal late-night caution.
- High-footfall tourist points attract opportunistic theft.

## Legal and Compliance Notes

1. Carry valid identification.
2. Respect transport ticket validation rules.
3. Follow local traffic and restricted-zone regulations.
4. Confirm short-term rental compliance requirements.

## Safety Workflow for Newcomers

- Map safe route alternatives for late returns.
- Prefer licensed transport channels at night.
- Keep emergency contacts and copies of key documents.
