# Milan Cost of Living (2025-2026)

## Monthly Budget Snapshot

| Profile | Monthly Range |
|---------|----------------|
| Student (shared housing) | EUR 1,000-1,600 |
| Single professional | EUR 1,800-3,200 |
| Couple | EUR 2,800-4,800 |
| Family of 4 | EUR 4,200-7,500 |

## Housing (Largest Cost Driver)

| Type | Typical Monthly Range |
|------|------------------------|
| Studio | EUR 800-1,700 |
| 1 Bedroom | EUR 950-2,500 |
| 2 Bedroom | EUR 1,500-3,800 |
| 3 Bedroom | EUR 2,000-5,000 |

## Everyday Costs

| Item | Typical Range |
|------|----------------|
| Espresso | EUR 1.20-1.80 |
| Casual lunch | EUR 10-18 |
| Mid-range dinner | EUR 25-45 |
| Grocery basket (single) | EUR 250-450 per month |
| Mobile plan | EUR 8-20 per month |
| Home internet | EUR 25-40 per month |

## Hidden or Missed Costs

- Building charges in rentals.
- Agency fees and deposits.
- Event-week accommodation spikes.
- Intercity train and weekend leisure spend.

## Cost Control Tactics

1. Optimize neighborhood by commute, not only central prestige.
2. Use monthly transport pass if commuting regularly.
3. Book event-period travel and lodging early.
4. Track housing total cost including fees.
