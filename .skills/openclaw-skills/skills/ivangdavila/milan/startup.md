# Milan Startup Ecosystem

## Ecosystem Snapshot

Milan has Italy's strongest concentration of startups, VC activity, corporate innovation programs, and startup events.

## Strong Verticals

| Vertical | Why Milan Works |
|----------|------------------|
| Fintech | Finance and enterprise concentration |
| B2B SaaS | Corporate buyer density |
| Fashion tech | Direct link to fashion industry |
| Food and retail tech | Large commerce and logistics base |
| Climate and mobility | Growing policy and investment focus |

## Funding Reality

- Pre-seed and seed options are improving.
- Later-stage rounds are possible but often need international reach.
- Network quality and traction metrics matter heavily.

## Founder Priorities

1. Validate segment with paying customers quickly.
2. Build distribution channels before scaling team.
3. Use event calendar for partnership and investor meetings.
4. Keep legal and accounting setup clean from day one.

## Common Startup Traps

- Hiring too fast before repeatable sales motion.
- Ignoring unit economics while chasing visibility.
- Over-indexing on events instead of customer calls.
- Choosing expensive office footprint too early.
