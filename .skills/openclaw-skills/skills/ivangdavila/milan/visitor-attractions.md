# Milan Visitor Guide: Attractions (2025-2026)

## Must-See Attractions

| Attraction | Why It Is Worth It | Typical Cost | Time Needed | Best Slot |
|------------|--------------------|--------------|-------------|-----------|
| Duomo + Rooftop | Iconic skyline and city orientation | EUR 16-30 | 2-3h | Early morning or sunset |
| Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II | Architecture, shopping, central location | Free entry | 30-60m | Morning before crowds |
| Santa Maria delle Grazie (Last Supper) | World-class art experience | EUR 15-20 | 1h | Book weeks ahead |
| Brera + Pinacoteca | Best mix of art and walkable district | EUR 15-20 | 2-4h | Late morning |
| Navigli Canals | Social atmosphere and aperitivo culture | Free to walk | 2-3h | Evening |
| Castello Sforzesco + Sempione | History plus park in one area | Free castle exterior, museums extra | 2-3h | Afternoon |
| Fondazione Prada | Contemporary art and design | EUR 15-20 | 2-3h | Weekday afternoon |
| Cimitero Monumentale | Unique sculpture and architecture | Free | 1-2h | Morning |

## Worth It With Caveats

| Attraction | Caveat | Recommendation |
|------------|--------|----------------|
| San Siro Tour | Match days change logistics | Check calendar before booking |
| Fashion district shopping | High spend pressure | Treat as walk, not mandatory buying |
| Last Supper | Very limited slots | Build itinerary around ticket time |
| Day trip to Como | Midday crowds heavy | Go early or sleep one night there |

## Tourist Traps To Avoid

1. Taxi queues outside major landmarks when metro is faster.
2. Restaurants with photo menus right at Duomo square.
3. Overpacked one-day itineraries crossing the city repeatedly.
4. Buying "skip the line" packages from unofficial street sellers.

## High-Value Free Experiences

- Walk from Duomo to Brera through side streets.
- Sunset in Parco Sempione with skyline views from Torre Branca area.
- Street art and design storefront walk in Isola.
- Sunday local markets in neighborhood zones beyond center.

## Booking Priority Order

1. Last Supper tickets.
2. Duomo rooftop preferred slot.
3. Dinner in high-demand neighborhoods on weekends.
4. Intercity trains if planning Como, Turin, or Bologna day trips.

## Practical Planning Notes

- Monday closures are common for museums.
- Rain can be intense in spring and autumn; keep indoor backup plans.
- During Design Week and Fashion Week, reserve everything earlier than usual.

*Last updated: February 2026. Prices are approximate and vary by season and event weeks.*
