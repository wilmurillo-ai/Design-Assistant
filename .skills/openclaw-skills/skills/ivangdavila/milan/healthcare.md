# Milan Healthcare Guide

## Healthcare Landscape

Milan offers strong public and private healthcare options, with major hospitals and specialist care availability compared to many other Italian cities.

## Access Paths

| Path | Best For | Trade-Off |
|------|----------|-----------|
| Public system | Cost control, long-term residents | Wait times for some specialties |
| Private clinics | Speed and flexibility | Higher direct cost |
| Employer-supported plans | Professionals with benefits | Depends on company coverage depth |

## Typical Private Costs

| Service | Typical Range |
|---------|----------------|
| GP consultation | EUR 80-150 |
| Specialist consultation | EUR 120-250 |
| Basic diagnostics | EUR 100-300 |
| Dental cleaning/check | EUR 70-150 |

## Setup Priorities for New Residents

1. Identify nearest emergency and general care options.
2. Clarify insurance scope and reimbursement workflow.
3. Keep digital records for prescriptions and reports.

## Practical Advice

- Book non-urgent specialist visits early.
- Use pharmacy support for minor routine guidance.
- Keep language support options if Italian is limited.
