# Moving and Settling in Milan

## First 30 Days Priorities

1. Secure valid housing contract and confirm registration pathway.
2. Obtain tax code (codice fiscale) if not already done.
3. Set up banking, SIM, and transport pass.
4. Confirm work or study paperwork status.
5. Build neighborhood routines: groceries, pharmacy, healthcare access.

## Setup Checklist

| Task | Why It Matters |
|------|----------------|
| Tax code | Required for contracts and services |
| Registered rental contract | Needed for legal residency workflows |
| Local SIM and data | Required for app-based city services |
| Public transport pass | Immediate cost and time savings |
| Basic bank account access | Salary, rent, utilities workflow |

## Housing Process Reality

- Good apartments in central zones move quickly.
- Documentation quality strongly affects approval speed.
- Ask upfront about building charges and utilities.

## Common Newcomer Mistakes

- Underestimating paperwork lead times.
- Choosing location only by price, not commute quality.
- Signing without verifying legal contract registration steps.
- Ignoring August and holiday slowdowns.

## Practical Onboarding Routine

Week 1: documents and mobility setup.
Week 2: housing stability and banking.
Week 3: healthcare and local admin cleanup.
Week 4: optimize commute and budget.
