# Family Travel — Indonesia

Family trips work best when transfer stress, heat, and uneven walking are designed out early. The right route is usually slower and more base-driven than the adults first imagine.

## Best Family Bases

| Base | Why it works |
|------|--------------|
| Sanur | Calmer Bali rhythm |
| Nusa Dua | Easier beach-resort comfort |
| Ubud | Good if hotel access and stairs are checked |
| Yogyakarta | Strong culture stop with manageable city scale |
| Gili Air | Best Gili fit when island conditions are acceptable |

## Rules

1. Never stack boat day plus flight day.
2. Use car transfers over complexity whenever possible.
3. Start heat-heavy sites early.
4. Label stairs, boat boarding, rough roads, and clinic access explicitly.
5. Keep a quiet indoor or pool fallback for rain or fatigue days.

## Poor Fits for Many Families

- Nusa Penida
- Bromo and Ijen on the same short route
- Overstuffed multi-island plans
- Remote islands without strong medical backup

## Trust Signals

Good advice includes toilet access, shade, walking load, medical fallback, and exact transfer pain. Generic "great with kids" language is not enough.
