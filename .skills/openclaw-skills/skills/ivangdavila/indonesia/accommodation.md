# Accommodation — Indonesia

The right base matters more than chasing the cheapest nightly rate. Traffic, stairs, beach access, and transfer friction can erase any savings.

## Best Base by Travel Style

| Style | Best base |
|-------|-----------|
| First Bali trip | Seminyak, Sanur, or Ubud depending priorities |
| Surf and nightlife | Canggu or Uluwatu |
| Quiet family Bali | Sanur or Nusa Dua |
| Culture-first Java | Yogyakarta |
| Urban gateway | Central or South Jakarta |
| Slow beaches | Senggigi, Kuta Lombok, or Gili Air |
| Komodo access | Labuan Bajo |

## Property Rules

- Check exact access road, not just the marketing neighborhood.
- In Bali and Ubud areas, verify stairs, slope, and car access.
- In Gilis, verify how luggage reaches the property from the harbor.
- In Labuan Bajo or hill areas, check for steep access and sunset-noise tradeoffs.
- In volcano areas, location should reduce pre-dawn transfer time more than maximize aesthetics.

## Hotel Type Guidance

| Need | Best type |
|------|-----------|
| Family simplicity | Full-service hotel or villa with driver access |
| Low-risk first trip | Hotel with front desk and airport-transfer support |
| Surf flexibility | Guesthouse or villa near main break, but not above noisy bars |
| Luxury reset | Resort with clear transport support |
| Mixed-age group | Ground-floor or elevator hotel, not cliff villa |

## Traps

- Booking trendy Canggu while needing calm sleep and easy airport runs
- Booking a beautiful Ubud villa that requires steep stairs and long rides everywhere
- Choosing Nusa Penida for comfort travel
- Choosing Gilis without considering boat landing, sand roads, and clinic limitations
