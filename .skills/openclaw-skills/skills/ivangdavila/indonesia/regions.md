# Regions — Indonesia

Indonesia works best when the route is shaped around one or two compatible island clusters. The wrong combination turns a dream trip into a chain of transfer days.

## Best Region by Trip Goal

| Goal | Best fit |
|------|----------|
| Easiest first trip | Bali |
| Culture and temples | Yogyakarta and Central Java |
| Volcano drama | East Java |
| Surf + social + easy logistics | Bali south coast |
| Quieter beaches | Lombok and Gilis |
| Diving and boat trips | Komodo and Flores |
| Orangutans and wilderness | Sumatra |
| Urban gateway with food and shopping | Jakarta |

## Cluster Logic

| Cluster | Why it works |
|---------|--------------|
| Bali + Nusa | Best short tropical trip with manageable logistics |
| Bali + Lombok/Gilis | Beach-first route with different energy levels |
| Yogyakarta + East Java | Culture plus volcanoes without overflying the whole country |
| Flores + Komodo | Strong nature route if boat days are acceptable |
| Sumatra only | Best for wildlife, trekking, and bigger-adventure pacing |

## Region Character

### Bali
Best for first-timers, mixed travel styles, surf, yoga, villas, day trips, and reliable tourist infrastructure. Weakest when the user wants low crowds or hates traffic.

### Java
Best for culture, volcanoes, food, train-friendly routing, and better urban depth. Strong when the user wants Indonesia beyond beach clichés.

### Lombok and Gilis
Best for quieter beach time, beginner-to-intermediate surf zones, and slower pace. Weaker when medical backup or mobility ease is the priority.

### Flores and Komodo
Best for diving, liveaboards, dramatic landscapes, and a more expedition feel. Bad fit for users who hate long sun-heavy boat days.

### Sumatra
Best for orangutans, jungle, volcano lakes, and travelers who want less polished tourism. Bad fit for travelers who need low-friction movement and polished resort comfort.

## Planning Rule

For 7-10 days, keep one cluster. For 12-16 days, two clusters are usually enough. Three clusters only work when one is very light and all flight and weather buffers are accepted.
