# Yogyakarta and Central Java

Yogyakarta is the best culture-first Indonesia stop for many users. It works when the user wants temples, batik, food, and a strong sense of place without Bali traffic logic.

## Why Go

| Priority | Strength |
|----------|----------|
| Borobudur and Prambanan | Signature cultural draw |
| Food and street life | Strong local identity |
| Train-linked route | Good Java backbone |
| Better value | Easier than Bali premium zones |

## Planning Rules

- Start temple visits early for heat and crowd control.
- Keep Borobudur and Prambanan on separate high-energy mornings if traveling with kids or older adults.
- Use Yogyakarta as a base, not a frantic one-night stop.

## Best Fit

- First-time user who wants real culture
- Pairing with East Java volcanoes
- Travelers who want Indonesia beyond beaches

## Traps

- Treating temple visits as easy all-day walking
- Ignoring heat, stairs, and crowd density
- Arriving late and leaving early, losing the city's food and atmosphere
