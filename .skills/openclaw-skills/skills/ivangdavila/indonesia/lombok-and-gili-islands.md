# Lombok and Gili Islands

This cluster works when the user wants beach time with less Bali intensity. It also creates boat, sand-road, and clinic-access tradeoffs that generic paradise advice ignores.

## Island Fit

| Place | Best for |
|-------|----------|
| Kuta Lombok | Surf, scenery, slower pace |
| Senggigi | Simpler base and mainland comfort |
| Gili Trawangan | Social and nightlife |
| Gili Air | Softer, mixed-age fit |
| Gili Meno | Quiet couple retreat |

## Planning Rules

- Use Gili Air for the broadest comfort range.
- Use Kuta Lombok when surf and scenery matter more than nightlife.
- Add buffer around fast boats in rougher seasons.
- Explain that "no cars" means luggage handling and sandy roads, not pure convenience.

## Bad Fits

- Older travelers who need easy clinic access
- Families who hate boat uncertainty
- Users expecting Bali-level dining density everywhere

## Traps

- Choosing Gili T for calm family sleep
- Underplaying harbor chaos or luggage friction
- Treating island transfers as weather-proof
