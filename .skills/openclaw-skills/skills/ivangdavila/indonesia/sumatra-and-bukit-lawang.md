# Sumatra and Bukit Lawang

Sumatra is for users who want wildlife, wilderness, and less polished tourism. It is excellent when the user values nature more than perfect logistics.

## Best For

| Priority | Why it works |
|----------|--------------|
| Orangutans | Bukit Lawang is the headline draw |
| Volcano and lake scenery | Northern Sumatra adds depth |
| Fewer crowds | Strong contrast with Bali |
| Longer, slower adventure | Better for patient travelers |

## Planning Rules

- Keep routes shorter than they look on the map.
- Build around one wildlife or one lake-volcano objective, not everything.
- Set expectations for longer road time and less polished tourism infrastructure.

## Good Fit

- Repeat Indonesia visitors
- Nature-first travelers
- Users who want authenticity over convenience

## Traps

- Adding Sumatra as a quick side trip to Bali
- Promising low-friction family comfort
- Ignoring rain, road time, and weaker backup options
