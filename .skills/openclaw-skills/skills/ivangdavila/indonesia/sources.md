# Sources — Indonesia

Use these as the primary re-check points when rules or operational details may have changed.

## Official Entry and Government

| Topic | Source |
|-------|--------|
| Immigration, visa, and e-visa rules | Directorate General of Immigration |
| Customs declaration and arrival rules | Directorate General of Customs and Excise |
| Health alerts and travel-health notices | Ministry of Health |

## Official Tourism

| Topic | Source |
|-------|--------|
| National tourism inspiration and destination pages | Indonesia Travel / Wonderful Indonesia |
| Bali-specific visitor information and levy references | Bali tourism and levy channels |
| National parks and conservation rules | Official park or ministry pages |

## Weather and Conditions

| Topic | Source |
|-------|--------|
| Weather forecasts and warnings | BMKG |
| Volcano activity and alerts | PVMBG and related official updates |
| Marine or port disruptions | Operator plus local authority channels |

## Practical Use Rule

Use these sources for:
- visa or VOA status
- Bali levy and customs process
- volcano, rain, or sea-condition re-checks
- park permits or conservation fee updates

Do not pretend fast-changing rules are static. Tell the user what to verify before spending money.
