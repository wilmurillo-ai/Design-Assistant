# Bali and Nusa Islands

Bali is the easiest first-trip Indonesia base, but it is not one market. Match the user to the right sub-area before recommending anything.

## Base Logic

| Base | Best for |
|------|----------|
| Ubud | Culture, rice terraces, slower rhythm |
| Seminyak | Easy dining and polished comfort |
| Canggu | Surf-social mix and remote-work energy |
| Uluwatu | Serious surf, cliffs, beach clubs |
| Sanur | Families, calmer beach, easier pacing |
| Nusa Lembongan | Short island add-on with lighter logistics than Penida |

## Decision Rules

- Choose Sanur over Canggu when the user wants smooth family logistics.
- Choose Ubud for culture and recovery, not for beach access.
- Choose Uluwatu for surf or clifftop views, not for easy movement.
- Prefer Lembongan over Penida for lower-friction island add-ons.

## Nusa Warning

Nusa Penida is a visual win but an operational trap for many users:
- rough roads
- cliff-edge viewpoints
- queueing
- limited comfort infrastructure
- weaker fit for kids, grandparents, or low-mobility travelers

## Bali Traps

- Overpacking Ubud, Canggu, Uluwatu, and Nusa into four days
- Ignoring Nyepi or ceremony impacts
- Assuming "near the beach" means easy walking or calm swimming
- Forgetting traffic when booking spa, dinner, and airport plans
