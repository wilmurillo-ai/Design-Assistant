# Itineraries — Indonesia

These are route shapes, not rigid scripts. Adjust by month, sea conditions, and flight times.

## 7 Days

### Option A: Easy First Trip
- Days 1-3: Bali south or Ubud base
- Day 4: Ubud or east Bali day
- Days 5-6: Nusa Lembongan or Uluwatu
- Day 7: Return buffer and departure

### Option B: Culture + Volcanoes
- Days 1-3: Yogyakarta
- Day 4: Train or flight east
- Days 5-6: Bromo or Ijen base
- Day 7: Departure from Surabaya or Banyuwangi

## 10 Days

### Option A: Bali + Lombok/Gilis
- 5-6 days Bali
- 3-4 days Gilis or south Lombok
- 1 buffer day before international flight

### Option B: Bali + Komodo
- 4-5 days Bali
- 3 days Labuan Bajo and Komodo day or boat trip
- 1-2 buffer days around flights and sea conditions

## 14 Days

### Option A: Java Backbone
- Jakarta arrival if needed
- 4 days Yogyakarta and temples
- 3-4 days East Java volcano route
- 4-5 days Bali recovery or beach finish

### Option B: Bali + Flores + Lombok
- Bali opener
- Flores and Komodo nature block
- Lombok or Gilis softer finish
- Keep at least one extra weather or flight buffer day

## Family Rule

If traveling with kids or older relatives:
- never plan same-day boat plus flight
- keep one recovery day every 3-4 active days
- choose fewer hotels than you first want
- avoid stacking heat-heavy temples, boats, and cliff viewpoints back to back
