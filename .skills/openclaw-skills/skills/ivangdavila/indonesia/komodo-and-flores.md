# Komodo and Flores

This is one of Indonesia's highest-payoff nature routes. It is also sun-heavy, boat-heavy, and less forgiving than Bali or Java.

## Best For

| Priority | Why it works |
|----------|--------------|
| Diving and snorkeling | Strong marine payoff |
| Boat-life adventure | Core appeal |
| Dragons and dramatic scenery | Signature Indonesia memory |
| Premium nature trip | Strong upscale potential |

## Planning Rules

- Base in Labuan Bajo when the user wants easier logistics.
- Use liveaboard only if the user truly wants boat life, close quarters, and variable sea conditions.
- For mixed-age travelers, choose selective day trips rather than maximum-island boat marathons.
- Protect one weather or flight buffer before international departure.

## Hard Truths

- Boat boarding can be awkward.
- Heat and shade exposure matter.
- Some viewpoints involve stairs or steep paths.
- Medical fallback is weaker than in major hubs.

## Traps

- Treating Komodo as a casual day trip add-on
- Hiding ladder, heat, and motion realities
- Booking tight onward flights after boat days
