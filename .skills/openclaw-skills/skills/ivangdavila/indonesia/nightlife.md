# Nightlife — Indonesia

Nightlife varies sharply by island. Give nightlife advice only after clarifying whether the user wants clubs, beach bars, rooftop cocktails, or simply social restaurants.

## Best Destinations by Night Style

| Style | Best fit |
|-------|----------|
| Beach clubs and polished social scene | Seminyak and Canggu |
| Surf and sunset scene | Uluwatu |
| Backpacker social nights | Gili Trawangan |
| Big-city cocktails and lounges | Jakarta |
| Low-key evenings | Ubud, Sanur, Yogyakarta |

## Rules

- Do not route sleep-sensitive users to party areas.
- Separate sunset drinking zones from late-night club zones.
- Add transport safety and late-night return logic.
- During religious or local event periods, operating rhythm can shift.

## Traps

- Booking calm family hotels in loud nightlife strips
- Assuming nightlife equals easy transport home at midnight
- Recommending Gili T or Canggu to users who primarily want rest
