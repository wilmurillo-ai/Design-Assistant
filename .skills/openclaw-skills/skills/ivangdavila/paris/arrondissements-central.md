| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Vibe** | Historic, touristy, luxury |
| **Landmarks** | Louvre, Tuileries, Palais Royal |

| **Best For** | Tourists, wealthy residents |

## Living in Central Paris

### Pros
- Walk everywhere
- Best restaurants, bars, culture
- Safe neighborhoods
- Beautiful architecture

### Cons
- Expensive rent
- Tourist crowds
- Small apartments
- Noise (some streets)
- Limited parking (irrelevant if no car)

### Who Should Live Here
- Those prioritizing location over space
- No car needed/wanted
- Higher budget (€2,000+/month for 1BR)
- Short-term visitors (experience the heart)
