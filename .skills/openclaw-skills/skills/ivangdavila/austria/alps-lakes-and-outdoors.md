# Alps, Lakes, and Outdoors — Austria

## Choose the Outdoor Product First

| User wants | Best Austria pattern |
|-----------|----------------------|
| Easy alpine views | Innsbruck or Salzburg-region lifts with city backup |
| Swim-and-relax summer | Salzkammergut or Carinthia lake base |
| Serious hiking | One mountain region with weather-flex days |
| Scenic driving | Short selective loops, not a whole-country road mission |

## Summer Alps Logic

- Mountain weather can flip faster than city users expect.
- Build one low-altitude backup per hiking day.
- Cable cars and panoramic roads are not interchangeable; verify what experience the user actually wants.

## Lakes Logic

- Salzkammergut is better for classic postcard density and day-trip variety.
- Carinthia works better for warmer-water, slower-pace summer stays.
- Do not combine too many lakes unless the user explicitly wants sampling over depth.

## Hut and Trail Reality

- Start times matter more than total distance because storms and cloud can erase views or margin.
- Good shoes, layers, and water are the baseline even on popular routes.
- If the user is inexperienced, keep hikes well-marked and cable-car assisted rather than selling big vertical days.

## Safety Rule

Avalanche and mountain-rescue context should be raised proactively for winter sports, off-piste ideas, or shoulder-season snow exposure.
