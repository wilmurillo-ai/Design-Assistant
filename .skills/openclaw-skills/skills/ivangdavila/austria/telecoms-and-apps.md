# Telecoms and Apps — Austria

## Connectivity Basics

- EU roaming rules help many travelers already inside the EU.
- Non-EU travelers should check eSIM or local-SIM value if they need constant navigation and messaging.
- City coverage is rarely the problem; mountain valleys and hikes are where offline planning matters.

## Useful App Categories

- OBB for rail planning and tickets
- City transit apps such as WienMobil when Vienna is the backbone
- Weather and warning apps for mountain or lake-heavy trips
- Offline maps for hiking, scenic drives, and weak-signal valleys

## Practical Advice

- Download tickets, reservations, and hotel confirmations offline.
- Save station names in German when they differ from the user's mental shorthand.
- Carry power during long rail days or mountain excursions.

## Payment and Connectivity Link

If the user depends on mobile payments or app tickets, remind them that battery loss is a real transport failure mode during full-day outings.
