### What Affects Price
- **AC** — Essential in summer, adds EUR 50-100/month
- **Furnished** — Usually included in rentals
- **Terrace** — Premium of 10-20%
- **Building age** — Historic = charm but worse insulation
- **Floor** — Higher = quieter, often more expensive

### Finding Apartments

| Platform | Best For |
|----------|----------|
| **Idealista** | Main listing site |
| **Fotocasa** | Second option |
| **Milanuncios** | Cheaper, riskier |
| **Facebook groups** | Expat/student rooms |
| **Badi** | Room shares |

### Rental Warnings
- **Agencies charge 1 month fee** — Negotiate or find direct
- **Deposit** — Usually 2 months
- **Photos can lie** — Always visit in person
- **Scams exist** — Never pay before seeing
- **Short-term premium** — Under 1 year costs more
- **No AC in old buildings** — Check before summer

| **Phone** | EUR 15-25 |

### Summer Warning
AC can double electricity bills. Budget EUR 150+ for summer electricity.

### Providers
- **Electricity:** Iberdrola, Endesa, Naturgy
- **Internet:** Movistar, Vodafone, Orange, Digi (cheapest)
- **Phone:** Digi, Pepephone (budget); Movistar, Vodafone (main)
