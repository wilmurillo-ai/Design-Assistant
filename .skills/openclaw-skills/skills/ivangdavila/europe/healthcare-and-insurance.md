# Healthcare and Insurance in Europe

Use this for travel cover, EHIC/GHIC questions, resident enrollment, and cross-border care concerns.

## Core Distinction

- Short-stay travel cover
- Public healthcare entitlement
- Private insurance
- Long-term resident enrollment

These are not interchangeable.

## Europe-Level Reality

- EHIC or GHIC can help some users access medically necessary public care in certain contexts.
- EHIC or GHIC is not the same as full travel insurance and does not solve every private-cost or repatriation issue.
- Residency often changes the correct health pathway from travel cover to local enrollment.

## Practical Use Cases

- EU citizen moving within the EU
- UK traveler or resident using GHIC logic
- Non-EU student choosing required private cover
- Remote worker splitting time across countries
- Family choosing cities partly through healthcare depth

## Good Advice Pattern

1. Identify nationality and current healthcare status.
2. Identify trip vs move vs residency.
3. Identify target country.
4. Explain what Europe-level rights may exist.
5. Then verify country enrollment steps if residency is involved.
