# Weekend Trips and Multi-Country Routes

Use this for shorter Europe travel, repeat trips, and users who want to use one city as a base.

## Weekend Logic

- Europe is unusually strong for 2-4 day trips if the base city has good rail or low-friction airport access.
- The key is not country count. It is transfer quality and city-center access.

## Good Route Shapes

- one major city + one day trip
- two nearby cities linked by fast rail
- one base with open-jaw flights only if airports are easy
- one non-Schengen segment only when the user is document-ready

## Bad Route Shapes

- three countries in three days
- late-night flight in plus early morning border-heavy connection
- separate tickets with zero buffer
- backpacking pace for users who actually need comfort, family rhythm, or work hours

## Resident-Useful Angle

Use this file not just for tourists, but for:
- people deciding where to live based on weekend reach
- remote workers designing low-burn travel patterns
- families comparing how usable a city is as a base for school breaks
