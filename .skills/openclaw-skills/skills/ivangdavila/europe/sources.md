# Sources — Europe

Use these sources when precise Europe-wide rules or current workflows matter.

## Core Europe Rights and Guidance

- Your Europe portal: https://europa.eu/youreurope/
- Your Europe citizens section: https://europa.eu/youreurope/citizens/
- Your Europe business section: https://europa.eu/youreurope/business/
- EUR-Lex: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/

## Travel, Borders, and Mobility

- Your Europe travel and documents: https://europa.eu/youreurope/citizens/travel/
- Schengen area guidance via the Council of the EU: https://www.consilium.europa.eu/en/policies/schengen-area/
- Europe emergency number 112: https://europa.eu/112
- Rail passenger rights overview: https://europa.eu/youreurope/citizens/travel/passenger-rights/rail/
- Air passenger rights overview: https://europa.eu/youreurope/citizens/travel/passenger-rights/air/

## Money, Consumers, and Problem Solving

## Usage Notes

- Use Europe-level sources to explain the framework, then move to the target country's official portal for execution.
- If nationality, country, route, or regulated profession changes the answer, do not stop at EU-level guidance.
- If a current fee, threshold, date, or permit condition matters, verify before giving a precise answer.
