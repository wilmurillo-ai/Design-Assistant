# Las Vegas Cost of Living

## The Bottom Line

Las Vegas is cheaper than California and the coasts, but NOT cheap. Prices have risen significantly since 2020.

**Compared to national average**: About 5-10% higher
**Compared to Los Angeles**: About 30-35% cheaper
**Compared to Phoenix**: About the same

## Taxes

### The Big Advantage: No State Income Tax
Nevada has NO state income tax. This is significant.

**What you save vs California** (example):
- $100K income in CA: ~$6,000-8,000 state tax
- $100K income in NV: $0 state tax

### What Nevada DOES Tax
- **Sales tax**: 8.375% (Clark County)
- **Property tax**: generally low by major-metro standards
- **Gaming tax**: Built into casino odds
- **Car registration**: Based on vehicle value

### Federal Taxes
You still pay federal income tax. Nevada residency doesn't change this.

## Hidden Costs

### Summer Survival
- Higher electricity (can't avoid A/C)
- Sunscreen, skincare
- More restaurant meals (too hot to cook)
- Indoor entertainment premium

### Car Extras
- Battery replacement: Heat kills batteries (every 2-3 years)
- Tire replacement: Heat degrades rubber
- Sunshades, tinting
- Windshield chips (common, cheap to fix)

### Housing Extras
- Pool maintenance (if you have one): $100-200/month
- Landscaping: Desert-scape saves money
- A/C maintenance: Annual tune-up essential

### Quality of Life
- Road trip escapes (escape the heat): Gas + lodging
- Flights home (many Vegas residents are transplants)
- Visitors (they will come): Entertainment + hosting

## Money-Saving Tips

### Housing
- West side (Summerlin) is pricier than east (Henderson)
- North Las Vegas is cheapest (trade-offs apply)
- Summer is cheapest time to move
- Negotiate rent (competition exists)

### Utilities
- Smart thermostat: Saves 10-20% on cooling
- Keep blinds closed in summer
- Use fans to supplement A/C
- Solar panels pencil out (lots of sun)

### Food
- WinCo, Costco for groceries
- Chinatown for cheap, great dining
- Happy hours at nice restaurants
- Cook at home (especially summer)

### Entertainment
- Player's cards = comps
- myVEGAS app = free stuff
- Local resident discounts at shows
- Off-Strip is cheaper (drinks, gambling, food)

### Transportation
- Costco gas
- Combine trips (heat ruins frequent short trips)
- Car share with coworkers if commuting same direction
