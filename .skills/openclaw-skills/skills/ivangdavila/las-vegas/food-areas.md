# Best Areas for Dining in Las Vegas

## The Strip: Where to Eat by Location

### South Strip (Mandalay Bay to Park MGM)

**Best restaurants**:
- **Libertine Social** (Mandalay) — Gastropub, Shawn McClain
- **Fleur** (Mandalay) — Hubert Keller, French-American
- **Border Grill** (Mandalay) — Mexican, Mary Sue Milliken
- **Aureole** (Mandalay) — Fine dining, wine tower
- **Bavette's** (Park MGM) — Steakhouse, beautiful
- **Eataly** (Park MGM) — Italian marketplace

**Vibe**: Conference crowd, fewer tourists than mid-Strip, solid options

**Quick eats**: Shake Shack (Park MGM), Best Friend (Park MGM)

### Mid-Strip (Aria to Bellagio/Caesars)

**Best restaurants**:
- **Carbone** (Aria) — Italian-American icon
- **Catch** (Aria) — Seafood, sceney
- **Jean Georges** (Aria) — Modern American
- **Bardot Brasserie** (Aria) — French
- **Le Cirque** (Bellagio) — Classic French
- **Spago** (Bellagio) — Wolfgang Puck, fountain views
- **Hell's Kitchen** (Caesars) — Ramsay flagship
- **Guy Savoy** (Caesars) — Ultimate French
- **Old Homestead** (Caesars) — Steakhouse
- **Nobu** (Caesars) — Japanese-Peruvian
- **Giada** (Cromwell) — Light Italian

**Vibe**: Tourist central, most famous names, highest prices

**Quick eats**: Bobby's Burgers (Caesars), The Henry (Cosmopolitan)

### Cosmopolitan (Special Mention)

The Cosmo has arguably the best restaurant concentration on the Strip:
- **é by José Andrés** — 8-seat omakase (if you can get in)
- **Jaleo** — Spanish tapas
- **Beauty & Essex** — Hidden entrance, creative American
- **STK** — Steakhouse club
- **Scarpetta** — Italian
- **Blue Ribbon** — Sushi, fried chicken
- **Milk Bar** — Desserts
- **Eggslut** — Breakfast sandwiches
- **The Chandelier** — Bar with flowers

### North Strip (Venetian to Wynn)

**Best restaurants**:
- **CUT** (Palazzo) — Wolfgang Puck steakhouse
- **Carnevino** (Palazzo) — Mario Batali's steakhouse
- **Bouchon** (Venetian) — Thomas Keller bistro
- **Delmonico** (Venetian) — Emeril
- **TAO** (Venetian) — Asian-fusion, club
- **SW Steakhouse** (Wynn) — Best overall steakhouse
- **Mizumi** (Wynn) — Japanese, beautiful
- **Lakeside** (Wynn) — Seafood, lake views
- **Wing Lei** (Wynn) — Upscale Chinese
- **Sinatra** (Encore) — Italian, Rat Pack vibe

**Vibe**: Higher-end, business crowd, conference attendees

**Quick eats**: Bouchon Bakery (Venetian), Urth Caffé (Wynn)

## Chinatown (Spring Mountain Road)

**The layout**: Strip malls along Spring Mountain, west of Strip. Don't judge by exteriors.

**By cuisine**:

| Block | Best Options |
|-------|--------------|
| Near Strip | Chengdu Taste, Raku, EDO |
| Mid-Spring Mountain | China Mama, Hwaro, District One |
| Further west | Lotus of Siam, Archi's |

**Parking**: Strip mall lots. Free, easy.

**Strategy**: Park once, walk between several spots.

## Proximity Planning

### If Staying at Bellagio
Walk to: Le Cirque, Spago, Lago, Jasmine
Short walk: Aria (Carbone, Catch), Cosmopolitan (everything)
Uber: Wynn properties, Chinatown

### If Staying at Venetian/Palazzo
Walk to: CUT, Bouchon, TAO, Delmonico
Short walk: Wynn properties
Uber: Mid-Strip, Chinatown

### If Staying at Cosmopolitan
Walk to: Jaleo, é, STK, all Cosmo restaurants
Short walk: Aria, Bellagio, Planet Hollywood
Uber: Wynn, Chinatown

### If Staying Downtown
Walk to: Carson Kitchen, Container Park, Oscar's
Uber required: Everything else (Chinatown 15 min, Strip 15-20 min)

## The Strategic Meal Plan

### First Vegas Trip (3 Days)
- **Day 1**: Lunch at Bouchon, Dinner at mid-range celebrity (Hell's Kitchen, Giada)
- **Day 2**: Brunch at Hash House, Dinner splurge (Carbone, SW, or Bazaar Meat)
- **Day 3**: Chinatown lunch (Lotus of Siam), Quick dinner before departure

### Budget Vegas Trip
- Breakfast: Bouchon Bakery or hotel coffee
- Lunch: Chinatown or food courts
- Dinner: Happy hour at celebrity restaurant OR Chinatown
- Late night: Tacos El Gordo

### Foodie Vegas Trip
- **Reservations needed**: é, Carbone, SW Steakhouse, Bazaar Meat
- **No reservation**: Raku, Lotus of Siam (wait), Chinatown spots
- **Don't miss**: One omakase, one Chinatown night, one steakhouse
