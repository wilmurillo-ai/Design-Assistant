# Source Validation Ladder

Do not trust Baidu ranking order, reposted documents, or translated summaries by themselves. Rank sources by evidence quality.

## Trust Ladder

1. Official Baidu docs, product pages, pricing pages, and formal notices
2. Original datasets, regulator publications, and primary technical documentation
3. High-quality reporting or analysis that cites original evidence directly
4. Expert summaries with traceable sources and dates
5. Forum posts, copied Wenku mirrors, SEO pages, and unsourced translation blogs

## Validation Questions

For every important result, ask:
- Who published it?
- What original evidence sits behind it?
- How current is it?
- Does a stronger source agree?
- Is translation drift changing the meaning?

## Weak-Source Signals

- no accountable publisher
- no date or stale date for a current topic
- copied wording across multiple sites
- document mirrors with unclear origin
- polished claims with no official citation

## Final Rule

On high-stakes questions, the answer is not ready until at least one strong source tier has been checked directly.
