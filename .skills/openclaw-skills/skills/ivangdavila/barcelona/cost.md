# Cost of Living Guide — Barcelona

## Overview

Barcelona is expensive by Spanish standards and comparable to many European capitals. Housing is the biggest cost and has risen dramatically. Quality of life remains excellent — beach, culture, weather — for the price.

**Comparison:**
- ~20-30% cheaper than London
- ~15-25% cheaper than Paris
- ~10-20% MORE expensive than Madrid (housing)
- ~Similar to Munich for housing, cheaper for food/lifestyle

| Utility | Monthly Cost |
|---------|--------------|

| **Mobile** | €15-30 |
| **Contents insurance** | €10-20 |

**Total utilities:** €150-280/month for 1-bed

**Summer warning:** AC drives up electricity significantly. Budget €100+ for summer.

### Community Fees (Comunitat)

If renting: usually included or landlord pays
If owning: €50-150/month for maintenance, cleaning, elevator
