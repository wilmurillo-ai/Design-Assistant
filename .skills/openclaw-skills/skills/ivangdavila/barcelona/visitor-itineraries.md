# Itineraries — Barcelona

## 1-Day Itinerary: Essential Barcelona

For visitors with very limited time. Hits the highlights.

### Morning (9am-1pm)
| Time | Activity | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| 9:00 | **Sagrada Família** | Book 9am slot in advance |
| 11:00 | Walk to **Passeig de Gràcia** | See Casa Batlló & La Pedrera exteriors |
| 12:00 | Coffee in **Gràcia** | Plaça del Sol or Plaça de la Virreina |

### Afternoon (1pm-5pm)
| Time | Activity | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| 13:00 | **La Boqueria market** | Lunch (avoid front stalls, go inside) |
| 14:30 | Walk **Las Ramblas → Gothic Quarter** | Quick walk, don't linger on Ramblas |
| 15:00 | Explore **El Born** | Best neighborhood, Picasso Museum optional |
| 16:30 | Coffee/break | Santa Maria del Mar church (free) |

### Evening (5pm-11pm)
| Time | Activity | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| 17:30 | **Barceloneta Beach** | Quick beach walk |
| 19:00 | Take metro/taxi to **Bunkers del Carmel** | Arrive for sunset |
| 20:30 | Return to center | |
| 21:00 | Tapas dinner | El Born or Gràcia |

## Beach Day Itinerary

Perfect for summer when you need sand and sea.

| Time | Activity | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| 9:30 | **Bogatell Beach** | Less crowded than Barceloneta |
| 13:00 | Lunch at **Poblenou** | Walk inland 5 min |
| 15:00 | Return to beach | |
| 17:30 | Walk to **Mar Bella** | Nudist section if interested |
| 19:00 | Sunset drinks at xiringuito | Beach bar |
| 21:00 | Dinner in **Poblenou** | Rambla del Poblenou |

**Beach tips:**
- Barceloneta = most crowded, most atmosphere
- Nova Icària = family-friendly
- Bogatell = younger, less crowded
- Mar Bella = nudist section, LGBTQ+ friendly
- NEVER leave belongings unattended
