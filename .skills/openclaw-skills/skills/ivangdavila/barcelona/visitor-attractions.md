# Attractions Guide — Barcelona

| Attraction | Why | Cost | Tip |
|------------|-----|------|-----|
| **Sagrada Família** | Gaudí's masterpiece, unlike anything | €26-36 | BOOK ONLINE (weeks ahead in summer) |
| **Park Güell** | Gaudí's public park, views, mosaics | €10 (monumental zone) | Book morning slot, free zone is free |
| **La Boqueria** | Best food market in Spain | FREE | Go before 10am (avoid tourist crush) |
| **El Born** | Best neighborhood for wandering | FREE | Evening best, great bars |
| **Bunkers del Carmel** | Best panoramic views, free | FREE | Sunset essential |
| **Gothic Quarter** | Medieval streets, cathedral | FREE | Early morning (empty) |
| **Picasso Museum** | Excellent, chronological journey | €12 | FREE Thursdays 4-7pm, first Sunday |
| **Casa Batlló** | Gaudí's residential masterpiece | €35-45 | Worth it, book evening for lights |
| **La Pedrera** | Another Gaudí gem, rooftop | €25-30 | Night visits excellent |
| **Barceloneta Beach** | City beach, atmosphere | FREE | Morning for swimming, evening for drinks |
| **Montjuïc** | Castle, gardens, views | Various | Take cable car up |
| **Camp Nou** | FC Barcelona temple | €28 tour | Under renovation until 2025 |
| **MACBA** | Contemporary art, Raval vibe | €11 | FREE Saturdays 4-8pm |

## Tourist Traps to SKIP

| Skip | Why | Instead |
|------|-----|---------|
| **Las Ramblas restaurants** | 200-300% markup, tourist quality | Walk to El Born or Gràcia |
| **Barceloneta beachfront paella** | Overpriced, mediocre, tourist trap | Can Paixano, inland restaurants |
| **Wax Museum** | Overpriced, boring | Picasso Museum |
| **Columbus Monument** | Just a column | Bunkers del Carmel for views |
| **Zoo Barcelona** | Dated, better options elsewhere | Day trip to Montserrat |
| **"Authentic" flamenco** | Not Catalan culture, tourist shows | Rumba catalana is local |
| **Airport taxis without meter** | Scam | Insist on meter (~€40-45 to center) |
| **Pickpocket "helpers"** | Distraction tactic | Ignore, keep walking |
| **Free roses/bracelets** | Scam, demand payment | Firmly refuse |

## Gaudí Deep Dive

### Sagrada Família

| What | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Must-see** | Nativity façade, forest interior, light through stained glass |
| **Time needed** | 1.5-2 hours |
| **Best time** | Morning (light), sunset (colors) |
| **Tip** | Tower access adds 30 min + €10. Book weeks ahead in peak season |
| **Skip if** | You can't book in advance (queues 2-3 hours) |

### Park Güell

| What | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Must-see** | Dragon fountain, mosaic bench, views |
| **Time needed** | 1.5 hours |
| **Best time** | Early morning (8:30am slot) |
| **Tip** | Book specific time slot. Free zone has good views too |
| **Getting there** | Bus 116 or long uphill walk |

### Casa Batlló

| What | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Must-see** | Dragon rooftop, bone balconies, light wells |
| **Time needed** | 1 hour |
| **Best time** | Evening (illuminated) |
| **Tip** | Expensive but worth it. Audio guide included. |

### La Pedrera (Casa Milà)

| What | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Must-see** | Rooftop chimneys, attic space |
| **Time needed** | 1 hour |
| **Best time** | Night experience excellent |
| **Tip** | Less crowded than Casa Batlló |

### Other Gaudí Worth Visiting

| Site | What | Time | Cost |
|------|------|------|------|
| **Palau Güell** | Raval mansion | 1 hour | €12 |
| **Casa Vicens** | First major work | 1 hour | €18 |
| **Colònia Güell** | Industrial village church | Half day | €10 |

## Museums Deep Dive

### Picasso Museum

| What | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Must-see** | Las Meninas series, Blue Period, chronological progression |
| **Time needed** | 2 hours |
| **Best time** | Free Thursday afternoons (queue early) |
| **Tip** | Combined with El Born neighborhood exploration |

### MACBA (Contemporary Art)

| What | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Must-see** | Rotating exhibitions, the building itself |
| **Time needed** | 1-1.5 hours |
| **Best time** | Saturdays free 4-8pm |
| **Tip** | Plaza is skateboarding hub — fun to watch |

### Fundació Joan Miró

| What | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Must-see** | Largest Miró collection, building by Sert |
| **Time needed** | 1.5 hours |
| **Best time** | Morning |
| **Tip** | Combine with Montjuïc exploration |

### Other Museums Worth Considering

| Museum | What | Time | Cost |
|--------|------|------|------|
| **MNAC** | Romanesque art, views | 2 hours | €12 |
| **CCCB** | Cultural exhibitions | 1.5 hours | €6 |
| **Fundació Tàpies** | Abstract art | 1 hour | €8 |
| **Maritime Museum** | Ships, port history | 1.5 hours | €10 |
| **CosmoCaixa** | Science (great for kids) | 2-3 hours | €6 |
