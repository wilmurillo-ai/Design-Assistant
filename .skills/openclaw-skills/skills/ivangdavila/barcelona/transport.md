# Transport Guide — Barcelona

## System Overview

Barcelona has excellent public transport managed by multiple operators under ATM (Autoritat del Transport Metropolità).

| Mode | Operator | Coverage | Best For |
|------|----------|----------|----------|
| **Metro** | TMB | 12 lines, 187 stations | Central Barcelona |
| **Bus** | TMB + others | 100+ routes | Local trips, airport |
| **FGC** | Ferrocarrils | Sarrià, Tibidabo, suburbs | Upper Barcelona |
| **Rodalies** | Renfe | Airport, suburbs, coast | Day trips, airport |
| **Tram** | TRAM | Diagonal, coastal | Specific areas |
| **Bicing** | Ajuntament | E-bikes, 500+ stations | Residents only |
| **Telefèric** | TMB | Montjuïc, port | Tourist |

**Key Insight:** T-Casual (10 trips) or Hola BCN! for tourists. T-usual for residents.

## Zone System

Barcelona uses concentric zones.

| Zone | Coverage |
|------|----------|
| **Zone 1** | Barcelona city (most people need only this) |
| **Zone 2** | Near suburbs (Hospitalet, Badalona) |
| **Zone 3-6** | Further suburbs, airport |

**Airport:** Zone 4 on metro (L9 Sud), Zone 1+ on Aerobus, varies on Rodalies.

## Bicing (Residents Only)

Public e-bike share for Barcelona residents.

### Pricing (2026)

| Plan | Cost | Includes |
|------|------|----------|
| **Annual** | €50/year | 30 min free per trip |
| **Per trip** | €0.35/30 min | After free period |

### Requirements
- Barcelona residence (empadronament)
- Bank account
- Register at bicing.barcelona

### Tips
- Electric + mechanical bikes
- Stations throughout center
- App shows availability
- Popular = stations empty/full at rush hour

**Tourists cannot use Bicing.** Use Donkey Republic or electric scooters instead.

## Driving — Why NOT to Drive

### ZBE (Low Emission Zone)

Barcelona has strict ZBE (Zona de Baixes Emissions):

| Vehicle Label | Access |
|---------------|--------|
| **Zero emissions** | Always allowed |
| **ECO** | Always allowed |
| **C** | Allowed (for now) |
| **B** | Restricted during pollution episodes |
| **No label** | Banned in ZBE |

**Check:** zfrurbanes.barcelona

### Parking

| Zone | Color | Cost |
|------|-------|------|
| **Blue Zone (Àrea Blava)** | Blue | €2-3/hr, max 2-4 hrs |
| **Green Zone (Residents)** | Green | Residents cheap, visitors expensive |
| **Underground** | Various | €2-4/hr |

### When Driving Makes Sense
- Day trips outside Barcelona
- IKEA/big box shopping
- Moving furniture
- Costa Brava exploration

## Late Night Transport

### Metro Hours
- **Weekdays:** 5am-midnight
- **Fridays:** 5am-2am
- **Saturdays:** 24 hours
- **Sundays/holidays:** 5am-midnight

### Night Buses (NitBus)

When metro closes, NitBus operates:

| Hub | Routes |
|-----|--------|
| **Plaça Catalunya** | All night bus routes radiate |
| **Frequency** | Every 20-30 min |
| **Fare** | Same as day (T-Casual works) |

### Taxi/Rideshare at Night

| Service | Notes |
|---------|-------|
| **Taxi** | Black/yellow, green light = available |
| **Cabify** | Popular in Barcelona |
| **Uber** | Works here |
| **Bolt** | Also available |

**Night supplement:** Taxis charge extra 9pm-7am.

## Quick Decision Rules

| Situation | Do This |
|-----------|---------|
| Airport → Center | Aerobús (easiest), Rodalies R2 (cheapest from T2) |
| Moving around center | Metro + walking |
| Short trip (<2km) | Walk |
| Beach areas | L4 or walk |
| Sarrià/Tibidabo | FGC |
| Late night | Metro (Sat 24h), NitBus, Cabify |
| Under 30? | Get T-Jove (€45.50/3 months!) |
