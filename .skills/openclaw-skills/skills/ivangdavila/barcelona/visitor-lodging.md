## Apartments & Rentals

### When Apartments Make Sense
- Stays 5+ nights
- Families or groups
- Want kitchen/space
- Prefer local living

### Platforms

| Platform | Best For |
|----------|----------|
| **Airbnb** | Variety (but regulations strict in BCN) |
| **Booking.com Apartments** | Reliability, support |
| **Spotahome** | Verified properties |
| **Flatio** | Mid-term stays |
| **HousingAnywhere** | Students, professionals |

### Typical Prices (Central)

| Type | Per Night | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Studio | €80-130 | 1-2 people |
| 1-Bedroom | €100-180 | 2-3 people |
| 2-Bedroom | €150-280 | 4-5 people |

### Apartment Red Flags
- No reviews
- Prices too good to be true
- Payment outside platform
- "Keys at café" arrangements
- No interior photos
- Request money before arrival

### Legal Note
Barcelona heavily regulates tourist apartments. Look for:
- HUTB license number in listing
- Licensed = legal and safer
- Many Airbnbs operate in gray zone

## Booking Tips

### When to Book
| Timing | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| **3+ months ahead** | Major events (MWC, Primavera Sound) |
| **1-2 months ahead** | Summer, Easter |
| **2-3 weeks ahead** | Normal times |
| **Last minute** | Winter deals possible |

### Peak Seasons (Higher Prices)
- Easter week (Semana Santa)
- May-June (best weather)
- September-October (great weather)
- Mobile World Congress (late Feb)
- Primavera Sound (early June)
- La Mercè (late September)

### Low Season (Better Deals)
- January-February (except MWC week)
- November
- December (except Christmas)

### Money-Saving Tips

| Tip | Savings |
|-----|---------|
| Book directly with hotel | Often 5-10% off |
| Sunday-Thursday stays | Lower than Fri-Sat |
| Gràcia/Poble Sec over Born | 20-30% less |
| Hostels with private rooms | Hotel quality, hostel price |
| Consider outer areas | Poblenou, Sant Antoni excellent value |

## Safety Considerations by Area

| Area | Safety Notes |
|------|--------------|
| **Eixample** | Very safe, even at night |
| **Gràcia** | Very safe, local feel |
| **El Born** | Safe, watch for pickpockets daytime |
| **Gothic Quarter** | Pickpockets common, some sketchy corners at night |
| **Barceloneta** | Beach theft common, watch belongings |
| **Raval** | Avoid dark streets late night, stick to main areas |
| **Poble Sec** | Safe, residential |
| **Poblenou** | Very safe, quieter |

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It's Bad |
|---------|--------------|
| Staying on Las Ramblas | Noisy, touristy, pickpocket central |
| Gothic Quarter expecting quiet | Narrow streets amplify noise |
| Barceloneta for authenticity | Very touristy now |
| Not checking HUTB license | Illegal apartments risky |
| Booking MWC week without knowing | 3-5x normal prices |
| Raval without research | Some blocks fine, others sketchy |
