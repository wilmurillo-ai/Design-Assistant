# Messages and Negotiation - Facebook Marketplace

Use this when the bottleneck is buyer messaging, offers, holds, or no-shows.

## For Buyers

Messages should do one job at a time:
- confirm availability
- confirm the decision-changing facts
- test flexibility on price
- lock the meetup

Keep the first message short and credible.
Do not send a wall of questions before the seller has even confirmed the item is live.

## For Sellers

Replies should move toward one outcome:
- qualified conversation
- confirmed pickup
- paid protected shipment
- clear rejection

Useful defaults:
- answer quickly
- be specific
- avoid oversharing personal details
- confirm pickup window, payment expectation, and what is included

## Offer Handling

Use an offer ladder:
- instant accept zone
- counteroffer zone
- decline zone

Counter only when the buyer seems real.
Low-effort buyers plus weak communication usually become no-shows.

## Hold Policy

Define it before you need it:
- no holds
- short hold with confirmed ETA
- hold only after protected payment if the platform flow supports it

Never create a custom hold rule in the middle of pressure.

## No-Show and Ghosting Rules

For repeat patterns, tighten the system:
- same-day confirmations
- exact pickup windows
- one reminder only
- auto-release after a missed time window

Do not spend emotional energy chasing weak leads.

## Safety in Conversation

Escalate to safety mode when:
- payment must move off platform
- the other side avoids concrete details
- there is pressure to hurry without evidence
- identity, item, or location details keep changing

Then recommend pause, block, report, or stricter terms instead of persuasion.
