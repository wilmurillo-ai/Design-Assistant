# Boquete and Chiriqui Highlands

## Why Go

- Cooler temperatures, coffee, hiking, birding, and a slower pace than the coasts.
- Strong contrast after Panama City or island humidity.

## Best for

- 3 to 4 nights
- Coffee-focused trips
- Soft adventure
- Travelers who want comfort plus nature

## Strong Plan Components

- Coffee estate visit
- One hike or waterfall day
- One flexible weather day
- Early starts for nature instead of overfull afternoons

## Common Mistakes

- Packing only beach clothes
- Assuming every viewpoint or hike works in wet weather
- Forcing sunrise or summit plans on users who do not enjoy cold, dark starts

## Best Pairings

- Panama City + Boquete
- Boquete + Bocas if time is generous
- Boquete + Pacific road trip for slower travelers
