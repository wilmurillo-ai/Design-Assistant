# Accessibility

## Best Practical Bases

- Modern Panama City hotels and malls generally offer the most predictable accessibility support.
- Central Boquete can work better than remote lodges for users needing smoother logistics.

## Harder Destinations

- San Blas
- Boat-heavy Bocas itineraries
- Remote beaches with sand access and uneven roads
- Some historic zones with cobblestones, steps, or uneven sidewalks

## Planning Rules

- Solve the whole movement chain, not just the hotel room.
- Confirm vehicle type, pier access, bathroom setup, and distance from drop-off to room.
- Avoid assuming island day trips are accessible because the water looks calm.
