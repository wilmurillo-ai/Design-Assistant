# Azuero and Pacific Coast

## Best For

- Surf, beach downtime, folklore, fishing, and slower overland travel.
- Good for users who want a local-feeling coast instead of an urban trip.

## Main Stops

### Playa Venao
- Best for surf energy, social atmosphere, and younger beach rhythm.

### Pedasi
- Better for a calmer town base, fishing, and mixed beach access.

### Cambutal
- Better for remote, nature-first, low-friction beach time if the user is happy being truly out of the way.

## Planning Rules

- Car or private transfer usually improves the trip.
- Do not promise urban convenience.
- Great fit for 3 to 5 nights, weak fit for one-night hopping.

## Tradeoff

- Better beach rhythm and local pace than the city
- More transfer commitment and fewer backup options if weather or energy changes
