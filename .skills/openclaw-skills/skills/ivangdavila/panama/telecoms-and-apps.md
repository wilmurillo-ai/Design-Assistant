# Telecoms and Apps

## Connectivity Reality

- Panama City is the easiest place to solve SIM, eSIM, ATM, and ride-hailing basics.
- Remote islands and beach zones should be planned as lower-connectivity environments even if the hotel advertises Wi-Fi.

## Useful Planning Habits

- Download offline maps before heading to islands, beaches, or highland trails.
- Keep confirmations and transfer contacts available offline.
- Do not assume card readers or strong mobile data in every remote stop.

## Payments

- Cards are common in the city and stronger tourism corridors.
- Cash becomes more important on islands, in smaller towns, and for water-linked logistics.
- Break larger bills before leaving Panama City or a major hub.
