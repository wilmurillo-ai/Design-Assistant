# Food Guide

## What to Prioritize

- Seafood on both coasts
- Sancocho, fried snacks, and market breakfasts
- Afro-Caribbean flavors in the Caribbean corridor
- Coffee experiences in Boquete and Chiriqui

## By Region

### Panama City
- Best for variety, modern dining, and one-trip sampling.
- Good place for the broadest restaurant planning.

### Caribbean Side
- Look for stronger Afro-Caribbean identity and seafood-forward meals.

### Highlands
- Coffee matters as much as food.
- Cooler weather supports slower breakfasts and cafe-heavy days.

### Pacific Coast
- Simple seafood and beach-town timing often beat overplanned reservations.

## Practical Rules

- In beach and island zones, eat where logistics are easy instead of chasing long detours.
- For short city breaks, book one anchor dinner and let the rest stay flexible.
- Keep hydration and lighter lunches in mind in humid zones.
