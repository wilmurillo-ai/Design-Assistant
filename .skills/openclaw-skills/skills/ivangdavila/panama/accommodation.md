# Accommodation

## Panama City

- Best for convenience: central neighborhoods with restaurants, easy car access, and practical airport links.
- Best for charm: Casco area, but confirm noise profile, parking, and luggage logistics.
- Best for longer stays: central business-residential zones with supermarkets and easier taxi pickup.

## Bocas del Toro

- Isla Colon works best for restaurants, tours, and nightlife.
- Bastimentos or outer islands work better for quieter stays and nature, but transfers become part of every day.
- Confirm power, Wi-Fi, and boat transfer timing before booking remote lodges.

## Guna Yala / San Blas

- Treat most stays as rustic experiences, not classic beach resorts.
- Confirm what is included: transport, meals, island transfers, beds, bathroom style, power, and cash needs.

## Boquete

- Central Boquete is best for walkability and tours.
- Outskirts are quieter and greener, but usually work better with a car or arranged transfers.
- Ask whether the user wants town access or mountain-lodge atmosphere.

## Pacific Coast

- Playa Venao and Santa Catalina are activity-driven stays; location relative to surf, dive shops, or departure piers matters more than generic star rating.
- On remote beaches, confirm road access, late-arrival procedure, and food options nearby.
