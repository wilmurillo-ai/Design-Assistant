# Bocas Del Toro

## Why Go

- Caribbean islands, surf, snorkeling, boat days, and a looser social rhythm.
- Strong for travelers who accept humidity, boats, and flexible weather plans.

## Best Base Logic

- Isla Colon for convenience, nightlife, and tour departures.
- Bastimentos or quieter islands for nature and slower stays.
- Split stays only if the user truly wants two moods; otherwise stay simple.

## Good Trip Shapes

- 3 to 5 nights as a main beach block
- 4 to 6 nights for surf or dive focus
- Pair with Panama City, not with too many other bases

## Common Friction

- Heavy rain and shifting sea conditions
- Cash dependence in smaller spots
- Water taxi costs and last-boat timing
- Remote lodges that look idyllic but complicate meals and movement

## Best User Fit

- Social beach traveler
- Surf traveler
- Couple wanting relaxed island energy
- Mixed nature and nightlife traveler
