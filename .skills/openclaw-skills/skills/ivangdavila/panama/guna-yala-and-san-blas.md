# Guna Yala and San Blas

## What It Is

- A simple island experience built around turquoise water, small-island scenery, and Guna territory.
- Best treated as a logistics-sensitive experience, not a luxury beach default.

## Best Use

- One day trip for users who want the look without rustic overnight tradeoffs
- One or two nights for users prioritizing scenery over comfort

## Hard Constraints

- Early departures and long transfer days
- Cash-first expectations
- Limited power, connectivity, and comfort
- Luggage and packing constraints
- Weather and sea state matter more than travelers expect

## User Fit

- Great fit: flexible, low-maintenance traveler who wants tropical simplicity
- Weak fit: remote worker, luxury seeker, mobility-limited traveler, or anyone sensitive to bumpy rides

## Respect Rules

- Frame it as indigenous territory, not a generic resort zone.
- Respect local norms, photos, timing, and inclusion rules from the operator or host community.
- Confirm exactly what is included before recommending any package.
