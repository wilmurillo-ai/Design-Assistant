# Family Travel

## Best Family Shapes

- Panama City plus one easy add-on
- Boquete for cooler weather and gentler pace
- Taboga or a simple beach block rather than overcomplex island hopping

## Usually Good With Kids

- Short city stays with clear rest windows
- Canal visit if the child likes ships or engineering
- Wildlife and gentle outdoor time near the city or highlands

## Usually Hard With Kids

- Tight multi-base routes
- Early and bumpy boat logistics
- Rustic island overnights with limited facilities
- Long same-day transfer chains after flights

## Family Planning Rules

- Keep one reliable food and sleep base before adventurous segments.
- Do not build every day around transfers.
- In humid regions, prioritize shade, hydration, and shorter outdoor blocks.
