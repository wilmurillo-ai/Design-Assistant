# Santa Catalina and Coiba

## Why Go

- Best for diving, marine life, snorkeling, fishing, and a more remote Pacific experience.
- Coiba is the headline; Santa Catalina is the practical base.

## Best Use

- Nature-first travelers
- Divers and snorkelers
- Users happy to trade convenience for marine value

## Planning Reality

- Coiba access depends on operator, weather, and departure point.
- Santa Catalina is not a place to overstuff with side missions; it works best when the marine day is the priority.
- Build buffer around boat departure and return windows.

## Weak Fit

- Travelers wanting lots of nightlife
- Users uncomfortable with long transfers
- Very short trips without schedule slack

## Strong Combo

- Panama City + Santa Catalina
- A slower Pacific route with Azuero only if the user accepts extra driving
