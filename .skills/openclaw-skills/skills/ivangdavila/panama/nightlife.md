# Nightlife

## Best Nightlife Bases

### Panama City
- Best overall variety: rooftops, bars, dining-driven nights, and late options.
- Strongest fit for users who want nightlife without sacrificing comfort.

### Bocas Del Toro
- Best for informal island nightlife and social traveler energy.
- Better for spontaneous nights than polished city evenings.

### Playa Venao
- Surf-town social scene, smaller scale, and activity-linked nights.

## Weak Nightlife Bases

- San Blas
- Boquete, unless the user wants very low-key evenings
- Santa Catalina, except for simple dive-town socializing

## Planning Rule

- If nightlife is a core priority, anchor at least part of the trip in Panama City or Bocas rather than hoping every beach stop delivers it.
