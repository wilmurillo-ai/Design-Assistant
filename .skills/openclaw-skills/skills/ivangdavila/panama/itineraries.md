# Itineraries

## 4 Days: Panama City First Trip

1. Arrive and settle in a practical city base.
2. Canal + Casco + skyline night.
3. Rainforest, Panama Viejo, or Taboga depending on weather and energy.
4. Easy final half-day before departure.

## 6 Days: City + San Blas

1. Two or three nights in Panama City.
2. One or two nights in Guna Yala or a day trip if comfort needs are higher.
3. Final city buffer before departure.

Why it works:
- Minimal flight friction
- Good contrast between urban and island Panama
- Easy fallback if sea conditions change

## 7 Days: City + Boquete

1. Two or three nights Panama City.
2. Fly or bus to Chiriqui.
3. Three or four nights in Boquete.
4. Keep the last night near the departure airport if the schedule is tight.

## 8 Days: Bocas Del Toro Focus

1. Fly or connect into Bocas early.
2. Four or five nights split only if the user wants quiet + town contrast.
3. Finish with one or two nights in Panama City.

## 10 to 12 Days: City + Highlands + Pacific or Caribbean

- Panama City
- Boquete
- One coastal add-on: Bocas or Santa Catalina or Azuero

Choose one coast, not two, unless time is generous and flights are easy.

## Buffer Rules

- Keep one logistics-light night before the international departure.
- Add weather buffer before or after boat-heavy segments.
- Do not stack same-day international arrival with long road or sea transfers unless the user explicitly accepts the risk.
