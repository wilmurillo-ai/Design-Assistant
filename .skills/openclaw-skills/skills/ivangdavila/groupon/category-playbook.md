# Category Playbook

Use the category-specific checks that match the deal type.

## Dining and drinks

- Compare against the merchant's current menu or recent public pricing if available.
- Check whether the voucher behaves like a credit or a fixed bundle.
- Penalize deals that push the user into a much larger total bill just to unlock the stated value.

## Wellness and beauty

- Confirm service duration, provider tier, and whether upgrades are required for a normal experience.
- Watch for weekday-only availability and heavy gratuity expectations.

## Activities and entertainment

- Check weather dependence, equipment rental, age limits, and group minimums.
- Downrank offers that look cheap but require expensive on-site extras.

## Travel and getaways

- Validate the same property, dates, and fees outside Groupon before trusting the claimed savings.
- Treat parking, resort fees, and availability windows as first-class factors.

## Goods and shipped products

- Compare the shipped price against normal retail and marketplace alternatives.
- Check shipping time, return conditions, and whether the merchant rather than Groupon handles fulfillment.

## Gifts

- Verify whether the voucher is transferable and whether the recipient will face awkward booking friction.
- A gift deal is weak if the recipient still has to spend heavily or fight for availability.
