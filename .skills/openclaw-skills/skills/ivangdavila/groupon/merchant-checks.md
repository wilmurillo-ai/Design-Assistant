# Merchant Checks

Use this file when the discount looks attractive but the merchant or fine print may ruin the experience.

## Merchant Trust Checklist

Check these before recommending a voucher:

| Signal | What good looks like | Red flag |
|--------|----------------------|----------|
| Review recency | multiple recent reviews | mostly old reviews or long silence |
| Complaint pattern | minor issues, resolved quickly | repeated "could not book", "upsold hard", or "closed" |
| Booking path | easy online booking or responsive phone flow | phone tag, no response, impossible availability |
| Location reality | practical distance and parking/transit fit | inconvenient route for small savings |
| Offer clarity | clear inclusions and exclusions | hidden add-ons or vague exclusions |

## Fine Print Checklist

Read the restriction block line by line:
- valid days and hours
- blackout dates and holiday exclusions
- one per person, one per table, or one per visit rules
- new-customer only or only-valid-for-selected-services language
- mandatory gratuity, taxes, or fees
- appointment requirements and cancellation terms
- whether advance booking is required before viewing or redeeming the voucher

## Quick Red Flags

Treat these as serious warnings:
- "subject to availability" with no online calendar
- repeated review complaints about being treated differently on Groupon bookings
- very small service scope plus expensive add-ons
- the merchant seems active elsewhere but unavailable for Groupon redemptions
- the terms are written to look broad, then narrow the deal to one time slot or one provider

## Merchant Types That Need Extra Skepticism

### Spa and beauty
- Verify service length, provider level, gratuity rules, and add-on pressure.
- Watch for weekday-only redemption or "new clients only."

### Dining
- Check whether drinks, tax, and tip are excluded.
- Verify dine-in vs takeout, reservation method, and blackout windows.

### Activities and events
- Check age limits, equipment fees, weather risk, and rescheduling policy.
- Prefer merchants with clear scheduling and quick support responses.

### Auto and home services
- Confirm what is actually included.
- Watch for bait-and-switch pricing on materials, disposal, upgrades, or travel fees.

### Hotels and getaways
- Confirm resort fees, parking, taxes, and date restrictions.
- Validate the same property or package outside Groupon to test the claimed savings.

## Confidence Language

Use explicit confidence markers in the recommendation:

| Confidence | When to use it |
|------------|----------------|
| High | recent reviews, clear terms, easy booking, true savings |
| Medium | mostly good fit but one unresolved caveat remains |
| Low | weak merchant signals or incomplete restriction data |
