# Accommodation - Paraguay

Choose accommodation by trip shape, not by nightly rate alone. The wrong base creates avoidable transfer time, heat exposure, and border stress.

## Best Base by Trip Type

| Trip Type | Best Base | Why |
|-----------|-----------|-----|
| First Paraguay trip | Asuncion in Carmelitas, Villa Morra, or Las Mercedes | Easiest dining and transport |
| Summer leisure | Encarnacion near the Costanera or Playa San Jose | Lets the waterfront do the work |
| Border shopping | Functional hotel near the retail corridor, not the noisiest core | Better sleep, easier resets |
| Jesuit missions route | Encarnacion or south-road lodge | Keeps day trips shorter |
| Chaco road trip | Filadelfia, Loma Plata, or a lodge | Cuts long daily backtracking |
| Family pool weekend | Resort-style property outside the dense core | Better for heat management |

## What to Prioritize

| Priority | Why It Matters |
|----------|----------------|
| Air conditioning that actually works | Heat changes the trip quality fast |
| Parking if doing self-drive | Useful in south routes and the Chaco |
| Reliable breakfast timing | Important before transfers or border days |
| Quiet over "central" in Ciudad del Este | Sleep matters more than location prestige |
| Pool or shaded outdoor area | High value in warm months |

## Area Guidance

### Asuncion
- Carmelitas and Villa Morra are easiest for short stays.
- Centro can be fine for a purposeful historic stay, but it is rarely the simplest first base.

### Encarnacion
- Waterfront proximity matters if the trip is leisure-first.
- If the trip is only mission-focused, center-city practicality can beat beachfront hype.

### Ciudad del Este
- Use a hotel as a recovery tool, not as part of a romantic city narrative.
- Favor secure parking, late check-in reliability, and controlled access.

## Traps

- Booking a bargain hotel far from the useful neighborhood and paying for it in daily transport.
- Choosing a border-hub hotel for a family leisure trip.
- Ignoring heat and booking a pretty place with weak cooling.
