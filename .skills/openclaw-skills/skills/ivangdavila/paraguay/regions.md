# Regions - Paraguay

Paraguay is easier to plan when reduced to travel-relevant corridors rather than administrative detail.

## Macro-Regions That Matter for Travelers

| Region | Why Go | Best For |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Asuncion and Central | Food, museums, logistics, short escapes | First-time and short trips |
| Cordillera / Lake corridor | Aregua, San Bernardino, warm-weather leisure | Weekend add-ons |
| South / Itapua | Encarnacion, missions, waterfront | Balanced 5-8 day trips |
| East / Alto Parana | Itaipu, shopping, tri-border logic | Tactical add-ons, east corridor |
| Northern parks | Road trips, history, wider-open landscapes | Longer trips |
| Chaco | Wildlife, ranches, colony history | Dedicated nature or road trips |

## Fast Region Selection

| User Says | Send Them Toward |
|-----------|------------------|
| "I only have a few days" | Asuncion and maybe Cordillera |
| "I want relaxed summer energy" | Encarnacion or lake corridor |
| "I need electronics or border shopping" | East corridor |
| "I want wildlife and space" | Chaco |
| "I want history and a route" | South missions plus Asuncion |

## What the Country Is Not Great For

- Ultra-dense sightseeing itineraries with zero transit
- Huge museum depth in multiple cities
- Ocean-coast beach trips
- Casual all-in-one loops in under a week
