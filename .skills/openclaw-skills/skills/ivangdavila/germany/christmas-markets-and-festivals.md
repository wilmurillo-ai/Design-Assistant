# Christmas Markets and Festivals — Germany

## Use This Module When

User mentions Christmas markets, Advent trips, Oktoberfest, major beer festivals, or calendar-specific atmosphere as a core reason to visit.

## Event Logic

| Event type | Best planning posture |
|------------|-----------------------|
| Christmas markets | Pick 1-2 city clusters, book early, expect cold and short daylight |
| Oktoberfest | Treat Munich as premium-demand and high-friction |
| Big city festivals or fairs | Check hotel demand before committing route |
| Smaller market trips | Choose one base and day-trip rather than hauling luggage nightly |

## Christmas Market Rule

- The win is atmosphere, not market count.
- Nuremberg, Dresden, Munich, Cologne, Berlin, and smaller Franconian towns all offer different tradeoffs in scale, crowding, and charm.
- Cold-weather routing should be simpler than summer routing.

## Oktoberfest Rule

- If user wants Munich during Oktoberfest, reframe the entire trip around price, booking windows, and tolerance for crowds.
- It is not a normal Bavaria week with one extra evening event.

## Trap to Flag

Designing an Advent trip like a summer city-hopping route and discovering that darkness, cold, and hotel pressure make it exhausting.
