# Berlin — Germany Playbook

## Best For

- First-time Germany history and museum trips
- Nightlife and modern-city energy
- Strong day trips without changing hotels

## Why Berlin Works

- Berlin rewards longer stays better than rushed checklists.
- Museums, neighborhoods, history layers, and nightlife all compete for time in different ways.
- Potsdam is the easiest high-value add-on for first-time visitors.

## Planning Rules

- Do not overload museum bookings on consecutive days.
- Build by neighborhoods and themes, not just landmarks.
- Stay transit-friendly and avoid assuming a car helps anything inside the city.

## Trap to Flag

Trying to do Berlin in two nights while also forcing multiple day trips and nightlife.
