# Rhine, Moselle, and West — Germany Playbook

## Best For

- River scenery, castles, wine towns, and west-Germany city contrast
- Cologne add-on with smaller scenic bases
- Shoulder-season and autumn travel

## Why This Cluster Works

- The west is strongest when users mix one major gateway with one scenic base.
- Cologne, Dusseldorf, Heidelberg, Koblenz, or Moselle villages should not all be crammed into the same short route.

## Planning Rules

- Decide if the trip is city-first, castle-first, or wine-first.
- Rail can handle many backbone moves, but river-village depth may push toward selective car use.
- Market weekends and wine-season weekends need stronger booking discipline.

## Trap to Flag

Building a west-Germany route that looks short on the map but becomes a constant hotel-moving exercise.
