# Munich and Upper Bavaria — Germany Playbook

## Best For

- First-time Bavaria with polished city base
- Beer gardens, classic food, and easy Alpine day trips
- Castle, lake, and mountain contrast without changing hotels constantly

## Why This Cluster Works

- Munich gives the smoothest gateway for Bavaria.
- Day trips can cover lakes, castles, or nearby Alpine scenery if the user avoids doing all of them at once.
- Oktoberfest or peak summer changes price and crowd math dramatically.

## Planning Rules

- One Munich base often beats a fragmented Munich plus village split on short trips.
- Car helps only when the route is village-heavy or lake-heavy beyond easy rail logic.
- Castle or Alpine days need early starts and realistic travel windows.

## Trap to Flag

Treating Munich, Neuschwanstein, Salzburg, and the Alps as a no-friction same-day chain.
