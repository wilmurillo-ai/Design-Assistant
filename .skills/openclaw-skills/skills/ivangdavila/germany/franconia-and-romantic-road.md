# Franconia and Romantic Road — Germany Playbook

## Best For

- Medieval towns, smaller-city charm, and slower pacing
- Wine, Christmas markets, and scenic road logic
- A contrast to Berlin or Munich without another giant city

## Why This Cluster Works

- Nuremberg, Wurzburg, Rothenburg, Bamberg, and nearby towns give a distinct Germany feel that major cities do not replace.
- The Romantic Road is better as a selective route than as a trophy checklist.

## Planning Rules

- Use a car if the user truly wants villages and scenic stops.
- Use rail if the trip is more about Nuremberg plus one or two other bases.
- In December, book early and simplify movement.

## Trap to Flag

Trying to "complete" the entire Romantic Road in a rushed rail-first itinerary.
