# Beer and Wine Regions — Germany

## Use This Module When

User wants brewery culture, beer halls, scenic wine routes, tavern-style evenings, or a trip shaped around drinking context rather than only monuments.

## Region Map

| Region | Strongest fit |
|--------|---------------|
| Munich and Upper Bavaria | Beer halls, beer gardens, classic festival atmosphere |
| Franconia | Smaller-city beer identity plus strong local wine pockets |
| Moselle | Scenic wine routes and village pacing |
| Rhine and Rheingau | Wine-country day trips with stronger city gateways |
| Baden and southwest | Useful wine extension on Black Forest or Freiburg routes |

## Planning Rules

- Beer-focused trips work best with city bases and walking or transit.
- Wine-focused trips often reward a smaller river or village base, but not too many one-night moves.
- If alcohol is part of the trip identity, be stricter about car-versus-rail planning.

## Trap to Flag

Trying to combine all of Bavaria, Rhine wine, and Black Forest drinking culture into one short itinerary without accepting that they are different products.
