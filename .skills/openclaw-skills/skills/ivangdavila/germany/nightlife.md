# Nightlife — Germany

## Destination Types

| Destination | Nightlife character |
|-------------|---------------------|
| Berlin | Deep, late, scene-driven, strongest for club culture |
| Hamburg | Lively port-city mix, bars plus nightlife zones |
| Munich | More polished, beer-hall and bar rhythm, less about extreme late-night scene |
| Cologne | Social and lively, often stronger in groups or event periods |
| Smaller towns | Cozy, seasonal, and much less all-night |

## Planning Rules

- Match nightlife to city identity instead of promising the same experience everywhere.
- Late-night plans should not sit directly before early rail or museum-heavy mornings.
- Winter or festival trips need even more recovery and transport logic than summer ones.

## Tourist Trap to Flag

Assuming Berlin-style hours and density exist across the whole country.
