Many high-end restaurants:

- 24-48h cancellation required

## Dress Code

### Fine Dining
- Smart casual minimum
- No beachwear
- Men: collared shirt, nice shoes
- Women: dress/smart outfit
- Some venues more relaxed outdoors

### Farm-to-Table/Agroturismo
- Smart casual
- Cleaner than beach
- Relaxed but presentable
