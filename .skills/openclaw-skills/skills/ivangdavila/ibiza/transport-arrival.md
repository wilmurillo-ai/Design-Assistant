# Getting to Ibiza

## By Air

### Ibiza Airport (IBZ)

**Location**: 7km southwest of Ibiza Town

**Airlines (Summer)**
- Major: Vueling, Ryanair, EasyJet, Iberia, British Airways
- Charter: TUI, Jet2
- Private: Growing market

**Airlines (Winter)**
- Reduced schedule
- Mainly: Vueling, Ryanair, Iberia
- Connections via Barcelona/Madrid common

### Flight Routes

**Direct Flights (Summer)**

| From | Airlines | Frequency | Flight Time |
|------|----------|-----------|-------------|
| Barcelona | Vueling, Ryanair | Multiple daily | 45 min |

**Winter**: Reduced to Barcelona, Madrid, London, few others

### Booking Tips

**When to Book**
| Timing | Price Level | Notes |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| 3+ months ahead | Best | Peak weeks book early |
| 1-2 months | Good | Most availability |
| 2-4 weeks | Higher | Less choice |
| Last minute | Variable | Sometimes deals, often expensive |

**Best Days**
- Tuesday/Wednesday: Usually cheapest
- Friday/Saturday: Premium
- Sunday: Mixed

**One-way vs Return**
- Low-cost: Often cheaper as two one-ways
- Legacy carriers: Usually return cheaper

### From Airport to Island

**Taxi**
- Fixed prices from airport
- Ibiza Town: €20-25
- Playa d'en Bossa: €15-20
- San Antonio: €30-35
- Santa Eulalia: €35-40
- North coast: €50-60
- Night surcharge: +20%
- Queue outside arrivals

**Bus**
- Line L10: Airport ↔ Ibiza Town
- €3.50, every 20-30 min
- Limited hours
- Fine for Ibiza Town stays

**Pre-booked Transfer**
- €30-60 depending on distance
- Private vehicle
- Useful for groups/villas
- Book via hotels or transfer companies

**Car Rental**
- All major companies at airport
- Book ahead in summer (sells out!)
- Compare: RentalCars, AutoEurope, direct
- Typical summer week: €400-700
- Off-season week: €150-250

## By Sea

### From Mainland Spain

**Barcelona to Ibiza**
- Companies: Trasmediterránea, Baleària, GNV
- Duration: 8-9 hours (overnight) or 5-6h (fast ferry)
- Price: €50-150 depending on cabin/season
- Advantage: Bring your car
- Book: Direct or DirectFerries

**Valencia to Ibiza**
- Companies: Trasmediterránea, Baleària
- Duration: 6-7 hours (fast ferry)
- Price: €40-100
- Often cheaper than Barcelona

**Denia to Ibiza**
- Company: Baleària
- Duration: 3.5-5 hours
- Price: €50-100
- Popular for Spanish travelers

### From Other Balearics

**Mallorca (Palma) to Ibiza**
- Frequent ferries
- 2-4 hours depending on vessel
- €30-60
- Easy island-hopping option

**Formentera Connection**
- Not for arrival, but for day trips
- See Formentera section

### Ferry Tips

**With a Car**
- Book early in summer
- Prices based on vehicle length
- Arrive 1-2 hours before
- Sleeping cabin recommended for overnight

**Without a Car**
- Much cheaper
- Seats fine for day crossings
- Bring food/water
- Consider overnight to save hotel

**Seasickness**
- Mediterranean usually calm
- Worse in winter/storms
- Bring medication if prone

## By Private Boat/Yacht

Popular arrival method for wealthy:
- Marinas: Ibiza Town (Marina Ibiza, Ibiza Magna), Santa Eulalia, San Antonio
- Berth booking essential in summer
- Yacht charter: €5,000-100,000+/week

## Arrival Tips

### Peak Season Chaos

**Airport in Summer**
- Long queues immigration (non-Schengen)
- Baggage delays common
- Car rental queues
- Taxi queues 30+ min peak
- Solution: Book transfer, arrive off-peak

**What to Pre-arrange**
- Car rental (book months ahead)
- Airport transfer (if not renting)
- First night accommodation (don't arrive without)

### Late Night Arrivals

**Challenges**
- Limited public transport
- Taxi availability varies
- Car rental desks may close

**Solutions**
- Pre-book transfer
- Pre-book car (24h pickup available some companies)
- Stay near airport first night

### Luggage Considerations

**Club Gear**
- Heels, outfits take space
- Consider smaller carry-on (low-cost fees)

**Beach Supplies**
- Buy snorkel gear there (cheap)
- Bring reef-safe sunscreen (expensive locally)

**What to Leave**
- Warm clothes (summer)
- Formal wear (rarely needed)
- Too many shoes
