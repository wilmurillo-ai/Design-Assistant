# Ibiza Itineraries

## 3-Day Party Focus

### Day 1: Arrival + Sunset + Club
- **Morning**: Arrive, check in, recover from travel
- **Afternoon**: Playa d'en Bossa beach, lunch at Nassau Beach Club
- **Sunset**: Café del Mar, San Antonio (book terrace)
- **Night**: Pacha (arrive 00:00, classic first-night choice)

### Day 2: Beach + Pool Party + Club
- **Morning**: Recovery, late breakfast
- **Midday**: Ushuaïa pool party (from 16:00, day ticket)
- **Evening**: Shower, dinner in Marina Botafoch
- **Night**: Hï Ibiza or Amnesia (depending on lineup)

### Day 3: Cala + Sunset + Underground
- **Morning**: Rent car, drive to Cala Comte
- **Afternoon**: Beach, lunch at Sunset Ashram or Cala Comte restaurants
- **Sunset**: Benirràs or back at Cala Comte
- **Night**: DC-10 (if Monday) or free evening for recovery

## 3-Day Chill/Couples Focus

### Day 1: Ibiza Town Exploration
- **Morning**: Arrive, settle in, stroll Dalt Vila (UNESCO old town)
- **Lunch**: La Oliva or S'Escalinata in old town
- **Afternoon**: Figueretas beach or Talamanca bay
- **Sunset**: Drinks at Sunset Ashram or Experimental Beach
- **Dinner**: Es Torrent (book ahead) or La Brasa

### Day 2: North Coast Discovery
- **Morning**: Drive to Santa Gertrudis, breakfast at Musset
- **Late morning**: Cala Xarraca (mud baths, swimming)
- **Lunch**: El Bigotes at Cala Mastella (arrive 13:30, cash only)
- **Afternoon**: Benirràs beach
- **Sunset**: Benirràs with wine
- **Dinner**: Can Domingo or back to Ibiza Town

### Day 3: South + Las Salinas
- **Morning**: Las Salinas beach (arrive early for good spots)
- **Lunch**: Sa Trinxa or Jockey Club (book)
- **Afternoon**: Es Cavallet walk, natural park area
- **Late afternoon**: Ses Salines salt flats (photo stop)
- **Sunset**: Kumharas or Café Mambo
- **Dinner**: Can Berri Vell (San Agustín) or La Paloma (San Lorenzo)

## 5-Day Balanced Experience

### Day 1: Arrival + Ibiza Town
- Arrive, check in
- Explore Dalt Vila old town
- Sunset drinks at La Torre
- Dinner in Ibiza Town
- Optional: gentle drinks at port

### Day 2: West Coast Beaches
- Morning: Cala Bassa (arrive early)
- Lunch: Cala Bassa Beach Club or pack picnic
- Afternoon: Cala Comte
- Sunset: Sunset Ashram
- Night: Casual dinner San Antonio, optional Ibiza Rocks

### Day 3: Party Day
- Late wake-up
- Midday: Pool time at hotel
- Afternoon: Ushuaïa (from 17:00)
- Night: After-party at Hï or back to hotel

### Day 4: North + Culture
- Morning: Santa Gertrudis village, coffee + boutiques
- Late morning: Drive to Portinatx (three beaches)
- Lunch: Local restaurant in Portinatx
- Afternoon: Swim, relax
- Drive via San Juan (charming village)
- Evening: Dinner at wild card like Atzaró (book ahead) or Can Mimosa

### Day 5: Flexible + Departure
- If departing late: morning beach (Talamanca easy)
- If full day: return to favorite cala
- Lunch in Ibiza Town
- Final stroll
- Airport

## 7-Day Complete Ibiza

### Day 1: Arrival + Ibiza Town
- Explore Dalt Vila
- Dinner in old town
- Night: Optional Pacha or drinks at port

### Day 2: East Coast
- Santa Eulalia (town beach, promenade)
- Es Canar (if Wednesday: hippie market!)
- Lunch seafront
- Afternoon: Cala Llenya or Cala Nova

- Late morning: Playa d'en Bossa
- Afternoon: Ushuaïa

- Morning: Yoga class (Ibiza Yoga, Six Senses if budget allows)

- Full day: Cala Comte, Cala Bassa, Cala Salada triangle

## Seasonal Adjustments

**Shoulder Season (May, October)**
- Clubs starting/ending season — check what's open
- Beaches much quieter
- Restaurants mostly open
- Car rental cheaper, parking easier
- Water still warm enough for swimming

**Off-Season (November-April)**
- Most clubs closed
- Many beach restaurants closed
- Focus on: hiking, cycling, village exploration
- Excellent for wellness retreats
- Local life more visible
- Christmas/New Year can be magical (small celebrations)
