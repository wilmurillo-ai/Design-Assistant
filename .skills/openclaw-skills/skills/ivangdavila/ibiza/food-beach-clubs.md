## Booking Guide

### When to Book

| Venue Type | Weekday | Weekend |
|------------|---------|---------|
| Major beach clubs | 2-3 days | 1 week+ |
| Chiringuitos | Usually walk-in | Day before |
| Sunset tables | 1 week | 2 weeks |
| Peak August | 2 weeks | 3-4 weeks |

### How to Book
- **Apps**: Many clubs have dedicated apps
- **WhatsApp**: Common booking method
- **Website**: Standard for chains
- **Phone**: Still works for traditional spots

### What's Included

**Sunbed booking usually includes:**
- Sunbed (1-2 per booking)
- Umbrella/shade
- Table service
- Sometimes minimum spend required

**What costs extra:**
- Food and drinks (always)
- Premium positioning
- Additional sunbeds
- Towels (sometimes)

## Dress Code

### Beach Clubs (Upscale)
- Cover-up over swimwear
- Stylish beachwear
- Wedges/sandals for women
- No football shirts or cheap flip-flops
- Some venues more relaxed post-sunset

### Chiringuitos
- Swimwear fine
- Casual
- No dress code really

## Price Guide

### Typical Spend (Per Person)

| Category | Beach Club | Chiringuito |
|----------|------------|-------------|
| Sunbed only | €50-150 | €10-30 |
| + Light lunch | €80-200 | €30-50 |
| + Full experience | €150-400 | €50-80 |
| + Bottles/premium | €500+ | N/A |

### Tips to Save
- Weekdays cheaper
- Arrive early (better spots, sometimes discounts)
- Share sunbeds (some allow)
- Skip sunbeds, use free beach sections
- Lunch specials at some venues

## Seasonal Notes

### Peak Season (Jul-Aug)
- Book everything ahead
- Prices at maximum
- Can feel crowded
- Best DJs and parties

### Shoulder (May-Jun, Sep-Oct)
- Easier bookings
- Better value
- Pleasant weather
- Some venues opening/closing

### Off-Season (Nov-Apr)
- Most beach clubs closed
- Some chiringuitos remain
- Very quiet beaches
- Bring your own everything
