# Ibiza Nightlife Guide

## The Big Six Clubs

### Pacha
- **Location**: Ibiza Town (Marina)
- **Capacity**: 3,000
- **Vibe**: Glamorous, fashion-forward, iconic
- **Music**: House, tech-house, commercial dance
- **Signature**: Cherry logo, sexy dancers, mirror ball room
- **Dress code**: Smart — no beachwear, no sportswear; heels encouraged
- **Key nights**: Solomun (+1), Fisher, various residencies
- **Entry**: €50-80
- **Drinks**: €15-20 spirits, €8-12 beer
- **Local tip**: The terrace is the spot for a breather; main room gets hot

### Amnesia
- **Location**: San Rafael (between Ibiza Town and San Antonio)
- **Capacity**: 5,000
- **Vibe**: Underground, purist, legendary
- **Music**: Techno, tech-house, trance
- **Signature**: Terrace (open-air), Main Room, CO2 cannons at sunrise
- **Dress code**: More relaxed than Pacha; casual acceptable
- **Key nights**: Pyramid (techno), various residencies
- **Entry**: €50-90
- **Drinks**: €15-18 spirits
- **Local tip**: Terrace for techno, Main Room for big room sound; stay for sunrise moment

### Hï Ibiza (formerly Space)
- **Location**: Playa d'en Bossa
- **Capacity**: 5,000
- **Vibe**: Modern, massive production, LED walls everywhere
- **Music**: House, tech-house, EDM
- **Signature**: Theatre room with huge LED screen, Club room
- **Dress code**: Smart casual; more relaxed than Pacha
- **Key nights**: Fisher, Black Coffee, various residencies
- **Entry**: €50-80
- **Drinks**: €15-20
- **Local tip**: Theatre room has better sound; Club room more intimate

### Ushuaïa
- **Location**: Playa d'en Bossa (beachfront)
- **Capacity**: 8,000
- **Vibe**: Open-air, pool party, daytime luxury, see-and-be-seen
- **Music**: House, tech-house, commercial EDM
- **Signature**: Poolside stages, sunset sessions, hotel rooms overlooking party
- **Schedule**: Daytime/sunset (doors 16:00-17:00, ends 00:00)
- **Dress code**: Glamorous beachwear acceptable; swimwear with cover-up
- **Key nights**: Calvin Harris, David Guetta, Martin Garrix (residencies)
- **Entry**: €60-120+
- **Drinks**: €18-25
- **Local tip**: Book VIP table for pool access; general admission can be far from stage. Sunscreen essential.

### DC-10
- **Location**: Near airport
- **Capacity**: 1,500
- **Vibe**: Underground, raw, purist, legendary after-hours
- **Music**: Techno, house, minimal
- **Signature**: Planes flying overhead, industrial setting, Garden terrace
- **Dress code**: Very relaxed; jeans and t-shirt fine
- **Key nights**: Circoloco (Monday — the legendary after-party), Paradise
- **Entry**: €30-60
- **Drinks**: €12-15
- **Local tip**: Monday Circoloco is a religious experience; starts early afternoon. The Garden is where the party really is.

### Privilege (legendary but currently closed)
Note: Closed in 2023. May reopen under new management. Was the world's largest club (10,000 capacity).

## Smaller/Underground Venues

**Pikes**
- **Location**: San Antonio hills
- **Vibe**: Bohemian, intimate, magical gardens
- **Signature**: Ex-hotel turned venue, famous pool, sunset sessions
- **Dress code**: Creative; express yourself
- **Local tip**: Book for Freddie Rocks (Saturday); incredible production in intimate setting

**Las Dalias**
- **Location**: San Carlos
- **Vibe**: Hippie market by day, world music/eclectic by night
- **Signature**: Night Market (summer evenings), garden parties
- **Local tip**: Thursday Night Market is iconic; Namaste party on Fridays

**Cova Santa**
- **Location**: San José
- **Vibe**: Restaurant by day, underground venue by night, cave setting
- **Signature**: WooMooN (full moon parties), theatrical productions
- **Local tip**: The cave acoustics are special; dress up a bit

**Club Chinois**
- **Location**: Ibiza Town
- **Vibe**: Intimate house music, quality over spectacle
- **Capacity**: 900
- **Local tip**: Great for a proper dance without mega-club overwhelm

## Ticket Strategy

### Buying Tickets

| Method | Pros | Cons |
|--------|------|------|
| **Resident Advisor** | Reliable, compare prices | Fees |
| **Club websites** | Official, often cheapest | Less comparison |
| **Ibiza Spotlight** | Good deals, local info | Variable |
| **Hotel concierge** | Convenience | Often overpriced |
| **Ticket touts/beach sellers** | Available last-minute | Scams common, overpriced |
| **PRs at clubs** | Sometimes guest list | Can be unreliable |

**Key tips:**
- Book 1-2 weeks ahead for big nights
- Prices increase closer to date
- Free/reduced entry often available before midnight (ask PRs)
- Closing parties (September) sell out weeks ahead

### Guest List
- Available for many nights via PRs or online
- Usually means free entry before midnight or reduced entry
- Worth arranging for non-peak nights
- Major headliner nights: rarely available

## Getting There

**From Ibiza Town:**
- Pacha: Walking distance (5 min)
- Amnesia: Taxi €15-20 or Disco Bus
- DC-10: Taxi €10-15
- Hï/Ushuaïa: Taxi €10-15 or Disco Bus

**From San Antonio:**
- Amnesia: Taxi €15-20 or Disco Bus
- Pacha: Taxi €25-30 or Disco Bus
- Hï/Ushuaïa: Taxi €25-30 or Disco Bus

**Disco Bus:**
- Runs between main towns and clubs (summer only)
- €4 per journey
- Runs until 6am+
- Can be packed; allow time

**Taxis:**
- Limited supply after 3am
- Book ahead via app (PideTaxi) or ask club to call
- Expect 30-60 min waits at peak times
- Agree price before getting in

## Dress Code Guide

### Strict (Pacha, Ushuaïa VIP)
- **Men**: Smart shoes (no trainers), chinos/smart jeans, collared shirt
- **Women**: Heels encouraged, dress/smart outfit
- **Avoid**: Shorts, sandals, flip-flops, sportswear, vests

### Smart Casual (Hï, most venues)
- **Men**: Clean trainers OK, jeans/chinos, nice t-shirt
- **Women**: Flexible; dress up or dress down
- **Avoid**: Beachwear, very casual

### Relaxed (DC-10, Amnesia, underground)
- Jeans, t-shirts, trainers all fine
- Express yourself
- No formal requirements

### Typical Night Timeline
- **18:00-20:00**: Pre-drinks at hotel/apartment
- **20:00-22:00**: Dinner
- **22:00-00:00**: Drinks in town (Ibiza Town port/San Antonio strip)
- **00:00-01:00**: Arrive at club
- **01:00-06:00**: Party
- **06:00+**: After-hours (DC-10 Monday, various)

## Survival Tips

**Hydration**
- Water costs €10-15 inside; still buy it
- Pre-hydrate before going out
- Clubs have water fountains (ask staff)
- Dehydration is real in summer heat + dancing

**Pacing**
- 5-6 hour club sessions are normal
- Eat properly before going out
- Don't peak too early
- Know your limits

**Safety**
- Drugs are illegal; clubs are strict
- Watch drinks being poured
- Stay with friends
- Have a meeting point if separated
- Share location with someone

**Recovery**
- Next-day beach plans: be realistic
- Brunch culture exists for a reason
- Siesta tradition makes sense
- Pool days beat beach days post-clubbing
