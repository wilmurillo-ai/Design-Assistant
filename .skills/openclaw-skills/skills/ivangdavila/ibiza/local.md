# Local Services & Practicalities

## Banking & Money

### Currency
- Euro (€)
- Cards widely accepted
- Cash useful for markets, small venues, tips

### ATMs
- Throughout towns
- Airport
- Fees vary (€2-5 for foreign cards)
- Use bank ATMs over standalone (lower fees)

### Banks
- Main Spanish banks present: Santander, BBVA, CaixaBank, Sabadell
- Hours: Mon-Fri 8:30-14:00
- Some open Sat morning
- Currency exchange: Banks or exchange offices (compare rates)

### Opening a Bank Account
For residents:
- NIE required
- Proof of address
- Passport
- Takes 1-2 weeks
- Online banks (N26, Revolut) work for EU residents

## Phone & Internet

### SIM Cards
**Options:**
- **Vodafone**: Good coverage, stores everywhere
- **Orange**: Similar
- **Movistar**: Spanish Telecom
- **Yoigo**: Budget option

**Tourist SIMs:**
- Available at airport, supermarkets
- €15-30 for data package
- Bring unlocked phone

**EU Roaming:**
- EU phones: Use home plan (roaming included)
- Check fair use policy for longer stays

### WiFi
- Hotels: Standard, quality varies
- Cafés: Common in towns
- Beach clubs: Usually available
- Speed: Generally good
- Mobile data: Fast LTE/5G coverage

### Coworking
Limited dedicated spaces:
- Some cafés suitable
- Hotel lobbies
- Check Facebook for pop-ups
- Santa Gertrudis has café culture

## Post & Shipping

### Post Office (Correos)
- Main office: Ibiza Town
- Smaller offices in other towns
- Hours: Mon-Fri 8:30-14:30, Sat 9:30-13:00

### Shipping
- DHL, UPS, FedEx available
- From/to mainland: 2-4 days
- International: Standard times

## Healthcare

### Pharmacies (Farmacias)
- Green cross sign
- Found in all towns
- Many medications available without prescription
- Staff often speak English
- Duty pharmacy (farmacia de guardia) for nights/weekends

### Public Healthcare
- EU citizens: EHIC/GHIC covers public healthcare
- Centro de Salud in each town
- Hospital Can Misses: Main public hospital

### Private Healthcare
- Clínica Nuestra Señora del Rosario
- Various private clinics
- Faster service, English common
- Insurance or pay (reasonable by US standards)

### Common Needs
| Need | Where |
|------|-------|
| Sunburn treatment | Pharmacy |
| Hangover help | Pharmacy (electrolytes, vitamins) |
| Minor injury | Centro de Salud |
| Dental | Private clinics |
| Emergency | Hospital Can Misses / 112 |

## Shopping

### Supermarkets
| Chain | Character | Locations |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| Mercadona | Best prices, good quality | Ibiza Town, Santa Eulalia, San Antonio |
| Eroski | Wide selection | Throughout |
| Lidl | Cheapest | Limited |
| Spar | Convenience | Towns, resorts |

### Opening Hours
- Supermarkets: 9:00-21:00 (some close 14:00-17:00)
- Small shops: Siesta break common (14:00-17:00)
- Sunday: Limited (some supermarkets open)

### Shopping Areas
- **Ibiza Town**: Boutiques, fashion, souvenirs
- **Santa Eulalia**: Marina shops, local crafts
- **Santa Gertrudis**: Boutiques, galleries
- **Markets**: Hippie crafts, local goods

### What to Buy
- Hierbas Ibicencas (local liqueur)
- Adlib fashion (white Ibiza style)
- Handmade jewelry
- Local olive oil
- Flor de sal (sea salt)
- Hippie market crafts

## Laundry

### Options
- Hotel laundry service
- Self-service laundromats (limited)
- Dry cleaners in towns
- Accommodation with washer (best for long stays)

### Costs
- Self-service: €5-10/load
- Hotel service: €15-30
- Dry cleaning: Variable

## Childcare

### Options
- Some hotels offer kids clubs
- Private nannies (agencies and Facebook)
- Expect €15-25/hour for babysitting

## Pets

### Bringing Pets
- EU pet passport required
- Microchip
- Rabies vaccination
- Many accommodations pet-friendly
- Beaches: Pets restricted on tourist beaches in summer

### Vet Services
- Veterinary clinics in main towns
- Emergency vet available

## Utilities (For Residents)

### Setting Up
- **Electricity**: Endesa, Iberdrola
- **Water**: Local municipality
- **Gas**: Bottled (butane) common; no mains gas
- **Internet**: Movistar, Vodafone, Orange

### Costs (Monthly Average)
| Utility | 1BR | 2BR |
|---------|-----|-----|
| Electricity | €50-100 | €70-150 |
| Water | €30-50 | €40-70 |
| Internet | €40-60 | €40-60 |
| Gas (cooking) | €10-20 | €15-25 |

Summer AC: Can double electricity bill.

## Language

### What's Spoken
- **Spanish (Castilian)**: Official, everyone speaks
- **Catalan/Ibizan**: Local dialect, older locals
- **English**: Widely spoken in tourist areas
- **German**: Common (large German community)

| English | Spanish |
