# Itineraries — Finland

## 4 Days: Compact First Trip

### Option A: Helsinki City Break
- Days 1-2: central neighborhoods, design, one museum block, one sauna session
- Day 3: side trip such as Porvoo, Nuuksio, or Suomenlinna
- Day 4: buffer and departure

### Option B: One Lapland Base
- Day 1: transfer north and low-friction evening
- Days 2-3: aurora window plus one guided activity and one backup indoor slot
- Day 4: return without forcing extra sightseeing

## 6-7 Days: Two-Base Finland

### Option A: Helsinki + Lapland
- Base 1: Helsinki for 2-3 days
- Transfer: flight or sleeper train with honest fatigue math
- Base 2: Rovaniemi, Levi, Yllas, or Saariselka for 3-4 days

### Option B: Helsinki + Lakeland
- Base 1: Helsinki for 2 days
- Base 2: Tampere or a lake-area cabin for 3-4 days
- Works best from late spring to early autumn

## 10-14 Days: Seasonal Mixed Trip

- South base: Helsinki or Turku for city and food
- Nature base: Lakeland or archipelago in summer, Lapland in winter
- Keep one full flex day if the route depends on ferries, aurora, or weather-sensitive outdoor plans

## Itinerary Quality Checks

- Last night before international departure stays in the departure city unless rail reliability is proven and risk tolerance is high
- Every outdoor-heavy stretch has an indoor or weather-safe fallback
- Winter trips protect at least one easy day after long transfer or late aurora nights
- Family trips avoid stacking airport, gear rental, and activity booking on the same day
