# Romania Black Sea — Coast and Delta Logic

## When The Coast Makes Sense

Recommend the coast mainly from June to early September. Outside that window, it is usually better as a walk-and-sea-air stop than a true beach holiday.

## Main Choices

| Place | Best for | Watch out for |
|-------|----------|---------------|
| Constanta | History plus coast access | More city stop than resort |
| Mamaia | Clubs, big-resort energy, broad beach | Loud, flashy, expensive by local standards |
| Eforie | Family and midrange beach logic | Less style, more practicality |
| Vama Veche | Alternative, younger crowd | Not for quiet rest |
| Sulina and Delta edge | Slow nature and water landscape | Harder logistics |

## Coast vs Delta

Choose the Black Sea if the user wants:
- beach time
- easy seafood stops
- lighter planning

Choose the Delta if the user wants:
- birdlife
- boat days
- quiet nature
- slower pacing

Do not sell them as the same product.

## Good Advice

- weekdays beat weekends for calmer beach time
- Mamaia is a mood, not a universal recommendation
- verify water and weather conditions before promising sea days
- private transfer or car becomes more useful once the route leaves the main rail spine

## Common Traps

- choosing Mamaia for a quiet restorative trip
- forcing the coast into May or October and expecting peak-summer atmosphere
- treating the Delta like a quick half-day detour
