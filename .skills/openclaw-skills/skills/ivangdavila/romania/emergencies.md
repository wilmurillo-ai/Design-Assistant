# Romania Emergencies and Safety

## Emergency Number

- General emergency: `112`

Tell the user to say "English" immediately if language is a concern.

## Main Risk Profile

Romania is generally manageable for travelers. The bigger issues are practical, not dramatic:
- petty theft in busy urban areas
- taxi scams if using street pickups
- mountain weather and wildlife
- road safety on long drives

## Health and Help

- private clinics in larger cities are often the easiest path for non-critical care
- pharmacies are common in cities and towns
- travel insurance matters more for mountain or road-heavy trips

## Mountain-Specific Safety

- use marked routes
- check weather before leaving town
- respect bear warnings and food discipline
- do not assume rescue is instant

## Beach and Delta Safety

- weather and water conditions can shift quickly
- boat or nature trips should use operators the user trusts
- mosquito prep matters in Delta logic

## Good Safety Advice

- use ride apps in cities
- avoid isolated shortcuts late at night in unfamiliar districts
- keep some cash, but do not carry a full-trip amount
- if driving, plan generous daylight buffers
