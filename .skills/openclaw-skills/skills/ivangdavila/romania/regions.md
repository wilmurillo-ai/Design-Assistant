# Romania Regions — Route Logic

Use this file to choose the right Romania shape before naming hotels or day trips.

| Region | Best for | Reality check |
|--------|----------|---------------|
| Bucharest and Wallachia | First entry point, food, museums, easy first nights | Big-city energy, not the prettiest Romania |
| Southern Transylvania | Pretty towns, castles, mountain access | Best first-timer corridor |
| Cluj and the west | Cafes, younger vibe, festivals, remote-work feel | Better urban trip than classic postcard trip |
| Maramures | Wooden churches, village life, slower rhythm | Needs more time and stronger logistics |
| Bucovina | Painted monasteries, rural scenery | Better with a car or driver |
| Dobrogea and Black Sea | Beaches, Delta access, seafood | Strongly seasonal |
| Carpathian mountains | Hiking, cabins, alpine scenery | Weather and bear awareness matter |

## Best First Shapes

### 1. Classic First Trip
- Bucharest
- Brasov
- optional Sinaia or Sibiu

This gives the easiest balance of city, old-town atmosphere, and mountain scenery.

### 2. Slower Culture Trip
- Bucharest
- Sibiu
- Brasov or Sighisoara

Best for users who want less transit and more time in walkable historic centers.

### 3. Urban Romania Without Dracula Cliches
- Bucharest
- Cluj-Napoca
- optional Sibiu

Good when the user wants restaurants, cafes, nightlife, and contemporary city energy.

### 4. Summer Coast Split
- Bucharest or Constanta
- Black Sea coast
- optional Danube Delta

Only recommend this when the dates support beach weather and open-season energy.

## What Usually Does Not Work

- Romania plus Black Sea plus Delta plus Cluj in one week
- treating every historic town as equal when one good base can cover several day trips
- building the whole route around Bran Castle branding
