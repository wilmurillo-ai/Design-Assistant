# Sibiu — Slow, Walkable, and Strong for Southern Transylvania

## Why Sibiu Works

Sibiu is one of the best Romania bases for users who want:
- elegant old-town atmosphere
- a calmer pace than Bucharest
- easier access to southern Transylvania
- a city that feels rewarding without nonstop movement

## Best Uses

### 1. Slower Couple Trip
Sibiu rewards 2 nights well. It is excellent for strolling, eating, and taking shorter excursions.

### 2. Cultural Base
- old town squares
- ASTRA Museum for open-air heritage
- nearby villages and artisan routes

### 3. Southern Route Anchor
Useful on Bucharest -> Brasov -> Sibiu or Cluj -> Sibiu corridors.

## Good Add-Ons

| Add-on | Best for | Caveat |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Marginimea Sibiului villages | Rural food and village rhythm | Better with a car or driver |
| Transfagarasan approach | Scenic-road users in season | Seasonal, not year-round |
| Sighisoara | Day trip for fortified-town feel | Better as a stop than a second base |

## Stay Logic

- stay in or near the old town if the user values atmosphere
- stay outside center if parking and larger rooms matter more
- Sibiu suits users who dislike noisy nightlife districts

## Sibiu Traps

- expecting it to replace mountain planning by itself
- overcommitting in winter when daylight is short and some side trips feel thin
- using it for long day trips that would be better as overnight stops elsewhere
