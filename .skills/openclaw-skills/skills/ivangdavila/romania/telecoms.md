# Romania Telecoms and Connectivity

## Why Romania Is Easy Here

Romania is one of the easiest parts of the trip:
- cheap mobile data
- strong urban internet
- decent connectivity even on many travel routes

## Main Carriers

| Carrier | Best for |
|---------|----------|
| Orange | Broad coverage and easy tourist familiarity |
| Vodafone | Strong mainstream option |
| Digi | Good value, widely used locally |

## Practical Guidance

- local SIMs are usually affordable enough that the user should not overthink them
- eSIM can be cleaner when the phone supports it
- city Wi-Fi is usually fine, but remote mountain stays are less predictable
- confirm signal expectations before selling a deep-rural workcation

## Roaming Notes

- EU users often have smoother roaming logic, but local SIMs can still be better value
- non-EU users should compare roaming costs before arrival

## Common Traps

- assuming one guesthouse claim of "good Wi-Fi" means video-call grade internet
- treating remote cabins and mountain villages like city coverage zones
