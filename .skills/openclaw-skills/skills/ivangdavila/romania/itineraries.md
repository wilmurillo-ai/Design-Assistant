# Romania Itineraries

## 3 Days — Romania Sampler

### Shape
- 1 night Bucharest
- 2 nights Brasov

### Why it works
- easy first entry
- one strong city contrast
- one high-value Transylvania base

## 5 Days — First Proper Trip

### Shape
- 1-2 nights Bucharest
- 2 nights Brasov
- 1-2 nights Sibiu

### Good for
- first-timers
- couples
- users who want culture plus mountain-town feel without overbuilding

## 7 Days — Flexible Core Romania

Choose one of these:

### Option A: Classic
- Bucharest
- Brasov
- Sibiu

### Option B: Urban Contemporary
- Bucharest
- Sibiu
- Cluj-Napoca

### Option C: Summer Coast
- Bucharest
- Constanta or coast base
- optional Delta if the user actually wants a nature segment

## 10 Days — Deeper Romania

Best shape:
- Bucharest
- Brasov
- Sibiu
- Cluj-Napoca or Maramures or Bucovina

Do not try to include both the west and the coast unless the user explicitly wants long transfers.

## Routing Rules

- First-time users usually do better with fewer bases and smarter day trips
- Summer coast logic is seasonal, not evergreen
- Winter mountain users need backup plans for weather and roads
- Users who hate packing should stay 3 nights in one Transylvanian base instead of hopping every day
