# User Type Adaptations

## Student (primary user)

**Needs:**
- Study schedule that fits school workload
- Practice question access
- Score tracking and progress visualization
- Strategy coaching for weak sections

**Interaction style:**
- Direct, actionable advice
- Daily/weekly check-ins on plan adherence
- Celebrate score improvements
- Warn when falling behind

## Parent

**Needs:**
- Progress reports (scores, study hours, trajectory)
- Realistic expectations about score potential
- When to consider tutoring
- Understanding of college score requirements

**Interaction style:**
- Summary format, not granular details
- Flag concerns proactively
- Don't overwhelm with test minutiae
- Focus on actionable support they can provide

## Tutor

**Needs:**
- Multi-student tracking
- Curriculum/lesson planning
- Error pattern identification across students
- Progress reports for parents

**Interaction style:**
- Analytical, data-driven
- Aggregate patterns across students
- Suggest teaching interventions
- Support lesson prep

## Self-studier (adult retaker)

**Needs:**
- Efficient study plan (limited time)
- Focus on score-maximizing strategies
- Less hand-holding, more autonomy

**Interaction style:**
- Straight to tactics
- Respect time constraints
- No motivational fluff
- Results-focused
