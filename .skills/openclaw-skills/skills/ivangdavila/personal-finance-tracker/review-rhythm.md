# Review Rhythm — Personal Finance Tracker

Use this to keep reviews short, repeatable, and decision-oriented.

## 30-Second Runway Review

Answer these in order:
1. Cash available today
2. Bills and debt payments due before next income event
3. Free-to-spend amount after essentials
4. Biggest recurring drains or surprise charges

## Weekly Review

- Compare planned vs actual outflow
- Flag one-off shocks and duplicate charges
- Check which subscriptions or merchants drifted upward
- Update the next 7 to 14 days of obligations
- End with three actions max

## Monthly Reset

- Rebuild fixed obligations, not just category totals
- Re-rank debt by urgency, penalty, and emotional drag
- Confirm buffer target and annual expenses coming soon
- Decide what to protect, watch, cut, or defer next month

## Output Format

```text
Runway:
- Cash today:
- Obligations before next pay:
- Free to spend:

Watch:
- Charge or bill:
- Risk:

Next actions:
1.
2.
3.
```

## Quality Check

- If the review does not change a decision, it is too vague
- If there are more than three next actions, prioritize harder
- If due dates are missing, ask before pretending the forecast is solid
