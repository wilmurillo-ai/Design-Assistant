# Itineraries — Los Angeles

LA is huge. These itineraries minimize driving by grouping by area.

---

## 1 Day in LA (First Timer)

**Theme:** Greatest hits, minimal driving

### Morning (9am-12pm)
- **Griffith Observatory** — Arrive early, less crowded
- Hollywood sign views
- Walk around, exhibits (free)

### Lunch (12-1pm)
- **Grand Central Market** (DTLA) — Food hall, try Tacos Tumbras or Eggslut

### Afternoon (1-5pm)
- **The Broad** (DTLA) — Free museum (book ahead!)
- Walk to nearby arts district if time

### Evening (5-8pm)
- **Santa Monica Pier** — Classic sunset spot
- Dinner on/near pier or Third Street Promenade

**Total driving:** 3 locations, ~45 min between them

## Traffic Avoidance Tips

### Best Times to Drive
- Early morning (before 7am)
- Mid-day (10am-3pm)
- Late evening (after 8pm)

### Worst Times
- 7-10am (weekdays)
- 4-8pm (weekdays)
- Weekend beach traffic (leave early)

### Area Pairings (Group These Together)
- Griffith + Los Feliz + Silver Lake
- DTLA + Arts District + Little Tokyo
- Santa Monica + Venice + Culver City
- Getty Center + Brentwood + Westwood
- South Bay (all beach towns together)
- Valley (Universal + Burbank + Studio City)
