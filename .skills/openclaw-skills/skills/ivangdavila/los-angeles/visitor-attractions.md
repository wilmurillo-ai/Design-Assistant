# Attractions Guide — Los Angeles

## Worth Your Time

| Attraction | Why | Cost | Tip |
|------------|-----|------|-----|
| **Griffith Observatory** | FREE, best city views, Hollywood sign | FREE | Sunset is magical |
| **Getty Center** | World-class art, stunning architecture | FREE | Book parking ($20) ahead |
| **The Broad** | Free contemporary art, infinity room | FREE | Book timed entry weeks ahead |
| **Santa Monica Pier** | Classic beach pier, sunset | FREE | Skip the rides, just walk |
| **Getty Villa** | Roman/Greek art, Malibu setting | FREE | Requires reservation |
| **Huntington Library** | Gardens, art, architecture | $29-35 | Full day experience |
| **LACMA** | Major art museum, Urban Light | $20-25 | Free second Tue |
| **Universal Studios** | Theme park, Harry Potter | $109-200 | Weekday for smaller crowds |
| **Descanso Gardens** | Beautiful gardens, peaceful | $15 | Great for photos |
| **Natural History Museum** | Dinosaurs, kid-friendly | $18 | DTLA area |
| **Academy Museum** | Oscars history, film buffs | $25 | New, well-designed |

## Tourist Traps to SKIP

| Skip | Why | Instead |
|------|-----|---------|
| **Hollywood Walk of Fame** | Dirty, sketchy characters, disappointing | Griffith Observatory, Hollywood sign hike |
| **Hollywood Wax Museum** | Overpriced, tacky | The Broad (FREE, actually cool) |
| **Venice Beach Boardwalk** | Aggressive vendors, sketchy at night | Venice Canals (quiet, beautiful) |
| **Driving Lombard St equivalent** | Mulholland Drive is just... a road | Griffith Park hike instead |
| **Beverly Hills "tour"** | Just rich people houses | Walk Rodeo Drive briefly, move on |
| **Celebrity home tours** | Can't see anything, waste of time | Any free museum |
| **TMZ Hollywood Tour** | Expensive, embarrassing | Literally anything else |
| **Mann's Chinese Theatre** | Just handprints, crowded | See a movie there if you must |
| **Madame Tussauds** | $35 for wax figures | Getty Center (FREE, world-class) |
| **Hollywood Sign** | Getting to it is illegal/hard | View from Griffith Observatory |

## Best Itineraries

### If You Have 1 Day

**Morning:** Griffith Observatory (early, less crowded)
**Lunch:** Grand Central Market (DTLA)
**Afternoon:** The Broad or LACMA
**Sunset:** Santa Monica Pier
**Dinner:** Venice/Santa Monica

### If You Have 3 Days

**Day 1:** Griffith Observatory + Hollywood sign views + The Broad + DTLA dinner
**Day 2:** Getty Center + Santa Monica + Venice Canals + beach sunset
**Day 3:** Universal Studios OR Malibu day trip (PCH drive, beach, seafood)

### If You Have 7 Days

Add:
- Huntington Gardens (full day)
- Joshua Tree day trip
- Disneyland (if interested)
- Palm Springs day trip
- More beach time (Manhattan Beach, Malibu)
- Food exploration (Koreatown, Little Tokyo)
