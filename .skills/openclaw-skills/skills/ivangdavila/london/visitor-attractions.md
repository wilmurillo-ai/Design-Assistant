# Attractions Guide — London

## Worth Your Time

| Attraction | Why | Cost | Tip |
|------------|-----|------|-----|
| **Tower of London** | Crown Jewels, Beefeater tours | £34.80 | Arrive at opening |
| **British Museum** | FREE, Rosetta Stone, world-class | FREE | Book timed entry |
| **Natural History Museum** | Stunning building, blue whale | FREE | Exhibition Rd entrance |
| **Westminster Abbey** | Coronations, poet's corner | £30 | Book online |
| **St Paul's Cathedral** | Climb dome for views | £26 | 500+ steps to top |
| **Tate Modern** | FREE modern art, river views | FREE | Floor 6 views free |
| **Sky Garden** | FREE panoramic views | FREE | Book weeks ahead |
| **Horizon 22** | FREE views, less known | FREE | Book online |
| **Borough Market** | Best food market | FREE | Go weekday |
| **West End Shows** | World-class theatre | £25-150 | TodayTix for deals |
| **Churchill War Rooms** | WWII bunkers | £32 | Book ahead |
| **Greenwich** | Maritime Museum, observatory | Mixed | Take Thames Clipper |
| **Hampstead Heath** | Nature, city views | FREE | Clear day for views |

## Tourist Traps to SKIP

| Skip | Why | Instead |
|------|-----|---------|
| **London Eye** | £40+, long queues, dated | Sky Garden, Horizon 22 (FREE) |
| **Madame Tussauds** | £35+, gimmicky | Any FREE museum |
| **Leicester Square** | Tourist trap restaurants | Walk to Soho/Chinatown |
| **Piccadilly Circus** | Nothing to do | Pass through quickly |
| **Camden Market** | Overpriced junk now | Columbia Road, Spitalfields |
| **Oxford Street** | Chaotic, pickpockets | Regent St, Marylebone High St |
| **Hop-On Buses** | Stuck in traffic, £40+ | Bus #11 (£1.75, same views) |
| **Westminster Bridge** | Overcrowded, scammers | Waterloo or Millennium Bridge |
| **Platform 9¾** | 30+ min queue | Skip unless with HP fans |
| **Abbey Road Crossing** | Just a crosswalk | Any zebra crossing |
| **Changing of Guard** | Crowded, can't see | Household Cavalry Museum |
