# Best Areas for Food — London

## Brick Lane (E1)

**Known for:** Curry houses, bagels, street food

| Spot | What |
|------|------|
| **Beigel Bake** | 24hr bagels, legendary salt beef |
| **Bengal Village** | Reliable curry |
| **Aladin** | BYO curry classic |

**Tip:** Sunday market brings street food stalls.

---

## Soho (W1)

## Coffee Culture

London has exceptional specialty coffee. Flat white is the default order.

**Roasters:** Monmouth, Square Mile, Origin, Ozone

**Best shops:**
- **Shoreditch:** Allpress, Ozone, The Attendant
- **Soho:** Flat White, Monmouth
- **South:** Assembly (Brixton), Federation

**Tip:** Most specialty shops don't do large sizes — order flat white or cortado.
