# Entry and Documents — United Kingdom

## Fast Decision Tree

1. Confirm whether the traveler can enter visa-free, needs a visitor visa, or now needs an ETA.
2. Verify passport validity and whether the trip includes the Republic of Ireland as well as the UK.
3. Check whether the user is flying, arriving by train/ferry, or crossing via the Common Travel Area.
4. Only then lock flights, rail passes, and non-refundable lodging.

## Visitor Pathways

| Path | Typical use | Main caution |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| Visa-free visitor | Short tourism, family visits, meetings | Nationality-specific rules still apply |
| ETA + visitor entry | Many non-visa nationals | ETA is permission to travel, not admission guarantee |
| Standard Visitor visa | Nationalities that require a visa | Processing time can shape the trip window |

## ETA and Border Control

- ETA coverage expanded across many nationalities in 2025 and remains a first check for 2026 travel.
- Travelers should verify the exact rule for their passport before booking.
- eGates can speed entry for eligible travelers, but eligibility depends on passport type and border point.

## Passport and Proof Logic

- Keep the passport valid for the whole trip and ideally beyond it.
- Carry onward or return travel proof if the route looks open-ended.
- Be ready to explain the tourism purpose, lodging plan, and basic budget.

## Ireland and Northern Ireland

- UK travel can become more complex if the user combines Great Britain, Northern Ireland, and the Republic of Ireland.
- The Common Travel Area reduces visible border friction, but immigration permission still matters.
- If the route includes Dublin plus Belfast or vice versa, tell the user to confirm both UK and Ireland entry logic before spending money.

## Children and Family Documents

- Minor travelers should carry the same core identity documents as adults.
- If a child is traveling with only one parent or with another adult, supporting consent paperwork can reduce friction.

## Before Locking Non-Refundables

- Entry pathway confirmed for the exact passport
- ETA or visa status checked where relevant
- Passport validity confirmed
- Ireland/Northern Ireland combination checked if relevant
- Return/onward logic and lodging proof ready
