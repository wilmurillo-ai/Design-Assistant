# Family Travel — Chile

## Strong Family Fits

| Region | Why it works |
|--------|---------------|
| Santiago + Vina | Easy services, short hops, urban comfort |
| Lake District | Scenic, flexible, easier pacing |
| Atacama | Good for older kids who handle dry air and early starts |
| Patagonia | Better for active families with budget and weather tolerance |

## Family Planning Rules

- Reduce base changes.
- Avoid stacking altitude, dawn departures, and long drives together.
- Keep one indoor or low-effort fallback in urban zones.
- Be explicit about wind, cold, and bathroom/logistics reality in remote regions.

## Good Family Pairings

- Santiago + Vina
- Santiago + Puerto Varas
- Santiago + San Pedro for families with older children

## Weak Family Fits

- Short trips trying to cover too much ground
- Hard Patagonia trekking without tested appetite from the whole group
- Carretera Austral unless the family genuinely enjoys road-trip uncertainty
