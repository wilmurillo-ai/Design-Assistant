# Itineraries — Chile

## 5 Days: Santiago + Coast

- Base 1: Santiago for 3 nights
- Base 2: Vina del Mar or Valparaiso for 2 nights
- Best for: first-time visitors, food, museums, easy logistics
- Skip if: traveler wants nature over city texture

## 7 Days: Santiago + Atacama

- Nights 1-2: Santiago
- Nights 3-6: San Pedro de Atacama
- Best for: classic first Chile trip with strong contrast
- Critical notes: Calama transfer, altitude pacing, cold desert nights

## 8-9 Days: Santiago + Lake District

- Nights 1-2: Santiago
- Nights 3-8: Puerto Varas base with optional Chiloe add-on
- Best for: scenic but lower-stress nature trips
- Critical notes: rain planning and a car helps outside town centers

## 10-12 Days: Patagonia Focus

- Night 1: Santiago buffer
- Nights 2-4: Punta Arenas or Puerto Natales approach
- Nights 5-10: Torres del Paine / Puerto Natales structure
- Best for: trekking, wildlife, iconic Patagonia
- Critical notes: lock refugios, park tickets, wind-ready backup plan

## 12-14 Days: Carretera Austral Lite

- Fly into Puerto Montt or Balmaceda
- Build 3-4 bases max
- Best for: self-drive travelers who accept slower roads
- Critical notes: ferry bookings, daylight, fuel discipline, weather slack

## 14+ Days: Desert or Patagonia Plus Central

- Option A: Santiago + Valparaiso/Vina + Atacama
- Option B: Santiago + central coast + Patagonia
- Best for: travelers who want one urban block plus one iconic nature block
- Avoid: adding a third distant zone unless the user explicitly values movement over depth
