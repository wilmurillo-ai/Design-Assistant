# International Cuisine - Seattle

## Vietnamese

Seattle has excellent Vietnamese food, especially pho.

### Best Pho

| Restaurant | Location | Notes |
|------------|----------|-------|
| Pho Bac | International District | The original, cash only |
| Tamarind Tree | ID | Upscale, great happy hour |
| Ba Bar | Capitol Hill | Modern, full menu |
| Stateside | Capitol Hill | Trendy, cocktails |

### Beyond Pho
- **Banh mi:** Seattle has good options everywhere
- **Bun:** Vermicelli bowls common
- **Banh xeo:** Crispy crepes
