# Beaches — Taiwan

Taiwan has better beach variety than many first-timers expect, but the right answer depends on season, current conditions, and whether the traveler wants swimming, surfing, snorkeling, or just sea atmosphere.

## Best Beach Choices by Goal

| Goal | Best fit | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Easy north-coast access | Fulong or nearby north-coast beaches | Best as day trips from Taipei |
| Southern sand and beach-town feel | Kenting area | Works best with enough time |
| Snorkeling and turtle-focused island time | Xiaoliuqiu | Better as a dedicated side trip |
| Island beach vacation | Penghu | Strong seasonal destination |
| Scenic coast more than swim culture | East coast spots | Beautiful, but not always the best swimming answer |

## Beach Logic by Region

| Region | Reality |
|--------|---------|
| North coast | Convenient but weather-sensitive |
| South | Better for classic beach energy |
| East coast | More dramatic scenery, stronger sea conditions |
| Offshore islands | Highest payoff when the trip is built around them |

## When to Go

- Early summer and shoulder periods are often easier than peak-holiday crush
- Typhoon season means plans must stay flexible
- Winter is better for southern coastal atmosphere than for a classic all-day swimming trip in the north

## Best for Different Travelers

| Traveler | Best move |
|----------|-----------|
| First-timer with one beach day | Keep it simple from Taipei or Kaohsiung |
| Snorkel lover | Build around Xiaoliuqiu or island options |
| Family | Pick calmer access and fewer transfers |
| Surfer | North/east options can work better than stereotypical beach resorts |

## Important Cautions

- Sea conditions matter more than the postcard
- Some iconic coastal spots are better for walking and photos than for swimming
- Island ferries and weather windows can reshape the day

## Common Mistakes

- Treating a full Kenting side trip like a tiny detour
- Assuming every pebble or scenic beach is made for swimming
- Planning offshore islands without enough weather slack
