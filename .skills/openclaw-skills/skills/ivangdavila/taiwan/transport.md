# Transport — Taiwan

Taiwan transport is excellent if the user understands the layers. The clean rule is simple: HSR for long west-coast jumps, TRA for local and east-coast travel, MRT and bikes for city movement, taxi for the last mile.

## Rail Layers

| Layer | Best For | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Taiwan High Speed Rail (THSR) | West-coast long jumps | Fastest way to move between major west-side cities |
| Taiwan Railways (TRA) | Local routes, east coast, smaller-city connections | Broader network, slower but more flexible |
| MRT / metro systems | Taipei, Taoyuan airport link, Taichung, Kaohsiung city movement | Big-city ease |

## Best West-Coast Rail Moves

| Route | Best method | Notes |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Taipei-Taichung | HSR | Fast and easy |
| Taipei-Tainan | HSR | Big time saver |
| Taipei-Kaohsiung | HSR | Clean long move |
| Tainan-Kaohsiung | HSR or TRA | Pick by hotel and station logic |

## East-Coast and Local Logic

- Use TRA for the east coast and many smaller stops
- Reserve seats early for busy weekends and holidays
- Do not assume east-coast travel works like the HSR west-coast sprint

## Airport Access

| Airport | Best move |
|---------|-----------|
| Taoyuan International Airport | Airport MRT, bus, or taxi depending on arrival time and luggage |
| Kaohsiung International Airport | MRT is the clean answer for most city stays |

## City Movement

| City | Best move |
|------|-----------|
| Taipei | MRT first, taxi second, YouBike where appropriate |
| Taichung | Bus, taxi, limited MRT usefulness by route |
| Tainan | Walk + taxi + local bus; last mile matters |
| Kaohsiung | MRT + light rail + short taxi or bike |

## Stored-Value Cards

- EasyCard is the easiest default stored-value answer in much of Taiwan
- iPASS is also common, especially in the south
- Stored-value cards reduce friction for urban transport and convenience spending

## Driving Rules

| Good for | Bad for |
|----------|---------|
| Rural routes, mountain areas, some beach or island-heavy plans | Taipei, short city breaks, rail-first west-coast routes |

## Common Mistakes

- Using a car for a classic Taipei-Tainan-Kaohsiung trip
- Ignoring the last mile from HSR station to hotel or attraction
- Treating east-coast timings like west-coast HSR timings
- Leaving holiday rail booking too late
