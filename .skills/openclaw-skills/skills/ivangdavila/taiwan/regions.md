# Regions — Taiwan

Taiwan planning is easier when the island is broken into functional travel regions instead of a long county list.

## Fast Regional Map

| Region | Best For | Core bases | Reality |
|--------|----------|------------|---------|
| North | First trips, transit, museums, easy day trips | Taipei, New Taipei | Easiest entry point |
| Northwest / Hsinchu-Taoyuan | Airports, logistics, some culture and tea side trips | Taoyuan, Hsinchu | More useful than romantic |
| Central west | Cafes, lakes, tea, slower city pace | Taichung | Good repeat-visitor region |
| Southwest | Food and history | Tainan | Best for slowing down |
| South | Harbor city, winter sun, beach branching | Kaohsiung | Strong all-round southern base |
| East coast | Scenery, ocean, Indigenous context, rail journeys | Hualien, Taitung | Needs weather and time |
| Offshore islands | Beach or culture-led side trips | Penghu, Xiaoliuqiu, Kinmen, Matsu | Best when intentional |

## Which Region Fits Which Traveler

| Traveler | Best region logic |
|----------|-------------------|
| First-timer | North plus one west-coast contrast |
| Food-first | Tainan, Taipei, then select other stops |
| Scenic rail traveler | West spine first, east coast only if time allows |
| Beach traveler | South or islands, not generic all-island sampling |
| Repeat visitor | Central Taiwan, east coast, or islands |

## Seasonal Bias

- Summer: higher heat and humidity, better for coast or altitude if weather is stable
- Winter: stronger for Kaohsiung, Tainan, hot springs, and many urban trips
- Rain and typhoons: keep the east coast and mountains flexible

## Regional Trade-Offs

| Choice | Trade-off |
|--------|-----------|
| All west coast | Easiest transport, less dramatic scenery |
| East coast add-on | High scenic reward, more fragile logistics |
| Offshore islands | Great contrast, adds weather dependency |

## What People Misjudge

- Taiwan is small enough for rail, but not small enough for careless daily zigzags
- The east coast is not just "another city stop"
- Islands should support the trip theme, not just tick the map
