# Food Guide — Taiwan

Taiwan rewards city-by-city eating. The island is small, but breakfast styles, noodle habits, sweets, and market culture shift meaningfully by region.

## Signature Foods by Region

| Region | Signature foods | Notes |
|--------|-----------------|-------|
| Taipei / north | Beef noodles, scallion pancakes, soy milk breakfasts, dumplings | Broadest range and easiest first-time entry |
| Taichung / central west | Bubble tea, sun cakes, sweets, creative cafes | Strong on snacks and desserts |
| Tainan | Beef soup, milkfish, shrimp rolls, coffin bread, old-school sweets | Best breakfast city |
| Kaohsiung / south | Duck rice, seafood, papaya milk, hot-pot and casual night eating | Relaxed evening food city |
| East coast | Indigenous flavors, seafood, mountain vegetables | More route-dependent and seasonal |
| Islands | Seafood and destination-specific specialties | Best when tied to the trip, not forced as a checklist |

## Breakfast Culture

| Style | What to expect |
|-------|----------------|
| Soy milk shop | Savory pastries, egg crepes, warm soy milk |
| Tainan breakfast | Beef soup, milkfish, rice bowls, much heavier than a cafe start |
| Cafe breakfast | Easy fallback in Taipei and Taichung |

## Night Market Logic

| City | Best use |
|------|----------|
| Taipei | Variety and easy one-stop grazing |
| Taichung | Big student-energy markets and snack wandering |
| Tainan | Good if the user already understands which market runs when |
| Kaohsiung | Easygoing evenings with less pressure than Taipei |

## Good Food Questions to Answer

- Is the traveler willing to queue?
- Are they more excited by classics, coffee, sweets, seafood, or grazing?
- Do they want iconic names or smoother local wins?
- Can they handle very early starts for famous breakfast places?

## What to Order by Mood

| Mood | Best move |
|------|-----------|
| Easy first meal | Dumplings, noodles, soy-milk breakfast |
| Late-night snack crawl | Taipei or Kaohsiung night-market loop |
| Food-obsessed city day | Tainan old-town grazing |
| Dessert and cafe day | Taichung |
| Refined local souvenir | Tea, pineapple cake, sun cakes, specialty sweets |

## Food Reality Checks

- Service fees are common in sit-down restaurants; tipping is not usually the main point
- The hottest famous stall is not always the best stall for a short trip
- Queue tolerance should shape recommendations more than fame alone
- Breakfast matters more in Taiwan than many travelers expect

## Common Mistakes

- Sleeping through Taiwan's best food window in Tainan
- Forcing one giant night market when the user would prefer a smaller focused area
- Chasing only internet-famous stalls and ignoring neighborhood eating
- Planning too many heavy meals without rest, coffee, or walking time
