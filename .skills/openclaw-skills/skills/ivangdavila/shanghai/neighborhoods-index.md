# Shanghai Neighborhoods Index (2026)

Start here to choose where to stay, explore, or relocate.
Each linked section goes deeper by district style and tradeoffs.

| Neighborhood type | Core examples | Best for | Cost signal | Speed to center |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Downtown core | People's Square, Jing'an, Xintiandi edge | First-time visitors, short stays | Mid to high | Fast |
| Tech corridors | Zhangjiang, West Bund tech belt, Hongqiao business zones | Tech workers, business travelers | Mid to high | Medium |
| Historic zones | Former French Concession, Old City edges, Hongkou heritage pockets | Walkable culture, architecture | Mid to high | Fast to medium |
| Suburban districts | Minhang, Songjiang, Jiading, Qingpu | Families, larger homes, lower pressure pace | Low to mid | Medium to slow |

## Quick routing

- If your top priority is convenience, start with Downtown.
- If your top priority is office access, compare Tech zones by commute.
- If your top priority is character streets, review Historic areas.
- If your top priority is space/value, review Suburban districts.

## Decision checklist

- [ ] Define max one-way commute target.
- [ ] Set realistic monthly budget band.
- [ ] Rank nightlife vs quiet nights.
- [ ] Decide if weekend park access is essential.
- [ ] Test at least one trial commute before committing.
