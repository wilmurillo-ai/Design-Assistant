# Source Ledger - Parking Radar

This file records the official sources used to shape the parking market map.

| Source | Confirmed signal | Why it matters |
|--------|------------------|----------------|
| Parkopedia official site and business pages | Global parking discovery plus commercial data and payments products | Parkopedia is the safest worldwide discovery fallback |
| SpotHero official site and developer pages | Large North American reservation inventory plus developer access | Strong reservation-first choice for US airports, events, and downtowns |
| ParkWhiz official `parking.dev` docs | Public booking-oriented API documentation | Useful evidence that some North American booking flows are API-friendly |
| BestParking official help and site | User-facing parking search tied to bookable inventory in supported markets | Good comparison layer, especially when ParkWhiz inventory is present |
| ParkMobile official site | Parking payments, reservations, and operator technology | Important distinction between municipal payment and marketplace booking |
| JustPark official consumer and EventPass pages | Driveway marketplace, venue parking, and partner integrations | Core UK and event-booking surface |
| RingGo official site | Consumer payment app for city parking | Strong payment-first signal in the UK |
| Q-Park official sites | Pre-book and operator-native reservation flows | Reliable operator-first booking surface in several European markets |
| APCOA Connect official pages | Operator-native parking payment and booking flows | Important for station and airport parking in Europe |
| PayByPhone official site | Large payment-app footprint across cities and countries | Good default for municipal or operator payment when reservations are not needed |
| EasyPark official consumer and developer pages | Cross-country payment coverage plus developer access | Important for Europe and selected other markets |
| Parkster official site | Nordic and European payment-heavy parking flows | Useful when the market is payment-first rather than reservation-first |
| Parclick official site | Cross-border parking reservations in Southern Europe | Strong airport and city-center reservation surface |
| Onepark official site | Reservations centered on France and nearby markets | Useful for rail, airport, and dense-city parking |
| Secure Parking and Wilson Parking official sites | Operator-native booking in Australia and New Zealand | Shows that ANZ parking is often operator-fragmented |
| Parking.sg official site | Singapore public-space parking payment app | Strong example of payment-first city parking |
| Parkin and RTA official Dubai pages | City payment and district parking flows in Dubai | Confirms operator and city-app dominance in the UAE |
| Park+ official site | India-focused parking and car-services platform | Useful first check for Indian urban markets |
| Hong Kong government data portal | Public parking-vacancy APIs | Rare example of official real-time public parking data |

## Maintenance Rule

When new evidence appears:
- prefer official operator, app, city, or developer pages
- record whether the source proves discovery, payment, reservation, or API access
- update local notes only after the provider role is clear
