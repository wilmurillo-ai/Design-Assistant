# Dog Records and Logistics

## Per-Dog Files

Maintain these files when the user approves storage:
- `profile.md` for durable identity, health baseline, and preferences
- `timeline.md` for dated symptom changes, appointments, milestones, and incidents
- `health.md` for labs, vaccines, meds, and vet follow-up questions
- `training.md` for cue reliability, trigger distance, and handling progress
- `logistics.md` for microchip, insurance, sitter, boarding, and travel notes

## Medication Tracking

Record:
- medication name
- purpose as given by the vet
- schedule
- start date
- refill point
- any side effect the user reports

Never infer dose changes.

## Appointment Prep

Before a vet visit, prepare:
- the change timeline
- appetite, elimination, vomiting, and energy summary
- meds and recent exposures
- a short list of questions the user wants answered

## Shopping and Restock

Track the shared household basics:
- food
- treats or training rewards
- preventive care items
- poop bags and cleaning supplies
- grooming and travel gear

## Report Types

Generate on request:
- symptom timeline for the vet
- monthly dog-care summary
- sitter or boarding handoff pack
- shopping restock list
