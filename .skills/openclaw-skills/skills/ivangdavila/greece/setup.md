# Setup - Greece Travel Guide

Create `~/greece/` on first use and initialize `~/greece/memory.md` from `memory-template.md`.

## First Contact

Answer the immediate Greece question first, then capture only the trip details that change the plan:
- Entry pathway already solved or still pending
- Dates or month range
- Island-first, mainland-first, or mixed
- Ferry tolerance, driving comfort, and transfer tolerance
- Trip style: archaeology, beaches, food, nightlife, family, hiking, mixed
- Budget band and pace

## What To Save

Keep durable facts in memory:
- Travel window and flexibility
- Confirmed bases or islands
- Traveler mix and mobility constraints
- Heat tolerance, sea tolerance, and car preference
- Open decisions such as "Cyclades vs Crete"

## Returning Users

Read `~/greece/memory.md`, reuse what is still valid, and ask only what changed:
- Dates
- Region focus
- Bookings already made
- Ferry or car decisions

## Quick Start Prompts

**"I want Athens and islands"**
- Ask total days, island count tolerance, and whether wind-sensitive ferry days are acceptable.
- Then use `athens-and-attica.md`, `cyclades.md`, `itineraries.md`, and `island-hopping-and-ferries.md`.

**"I want history and beaches"**
- Ask month, heat tolerance, and whether user prefers one larger island or mainland plus coast.
- Then use `archaeology-and-museums.md`, `crete.md`, `peloponnese-and-mainland.md`, and `weather-and-seasonality.md`.

**"I need a full Greece route"**
- Ask trip length, arrival airport, and whether user accepts open-jaw flights.
- Then use `regions.md`, `itineraries.md`, `budget-and-costs.md`, and `transport-domestic.md`.

## Important Notes

- Greece quality comes from fewer bases with better transport logic.
- Wind, heat, and ferry reliability matter more than attraction count.
- Last-night protection before the international flight is usually worth the extra hotel move.
