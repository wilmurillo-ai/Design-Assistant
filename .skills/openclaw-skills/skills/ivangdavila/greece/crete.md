# Crete - Greece

## Best Use Case

Crete is for travelers who want a real destination, not a token island stop: beaches, food, archaeology, mountains, and enough scale for a one-week trip.

## Zone Choice

| Zone | Best for | Watch-outs |
|------|----------|------------|
| Chania west | Scenery, beaches, old-town atmosphere | Long drives to far east |
| Heraklion center | Knossos, museum, transit convenience | Less romantic base feel |
| East Crete | Quieter beach focus | Longer logistics from main gateways |

## Planning Rules

- Pick one coast or two adjacent zones, not the whole island.
- Crete can justify a car more than many other Greece trips.
- Pair Knossos with Heraklion Museum instead of stacking too many road miles around it.

## Why Crete Wins

- Strong value outside peak premium pockets
- Deep food identity
- Better bad-weather resilience than fragile multi-island hopping
- Enough scale for repeat travelers

## Trap Avoidance

- Do not add Crete for only two nights unless flights make it effortless.
- Do not underestimate driving time across the island.
- Do not plan remote beaches without checking road and sea conditions first.
