# Regions - Greece

## Region Selection Grid

| Region | Best for | Watch-outs |
|--------|----------|------------|
| Athens and Attica | First-time trips, archaeology, city plus coast mix | Heat, traffic, cruise crowds |
| Cyclades | White-village islands, classic Aegean look, ferry-linked hopping | Wind, premium pricing, overhopping |
| Crete | Longer stays, beaches plus history plus food | Long internal distances |
| Ionian Islands | Greener scenery, calmer water, family or drive-and-base trips | Car often helps, shoulder-season services vary |
| Peloponnese and mainland south | Archaeology, road trips, lower crowd pressure | Best with car, spread-out sights |
| North and Meteora | Mountains, monasteries, Thessaloniki, cooler shoulder travel | More transfer time from island-heavy routes |

## Planning Rule of Thumb

- 5 to 7 days: Athens plus one nearby region or one major island
- 8 to 12 days: Athens plus one island group or one deep mainland loop
- 14 plus days: Two clusters maximum unless traveler accepts transfer-heavy logistics

## Route Logic

- Cyclades reward fewer islands with longer stays.
- Crete works best as a destination, not a quick add-on.
- Peloponnese is stronger by car than by fragmented public transport.
- North Greece is better paired with Thessaloniki than with a rushed Athens-islands loop.

## First-Time Greece Suggestions

- Classic first trip: Athens plus one Cyclades island pair
- History plus coast: Athens plus Peloponnese
- Relaxed beach trip: Crete only, or Ionian only
- Cooler shoulder season: Athens plus Thessaloniki and Meteora
