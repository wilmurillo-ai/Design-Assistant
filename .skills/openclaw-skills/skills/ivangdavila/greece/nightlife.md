# Nightlife - Greece

## Destination Styles

| Destination | Nightlife pattern |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Athens | Bars, rooftops, late dinners, neighborhood variety |
| Mykonos | Scene-heavy clubs and premium beach-club carryover |
| Santorini | More drinks-with-view than club-first intensity |
| Thessaloniki | Strong student and local bar energy |
| Crete larger towns | Mixed local bars and summer resort energy |

## Timing Reality

- Dinners start later than many northern-European users expect.
- Beach-club destinations often begin in daylight and spill into night.
- Transport back to hotel matters as much as venue choice on islands.

## Strategy

- Match nightlife to the user's real goal: cocktails, dancing, sunset, live music, or late food.
- Keep one recovery day after a big party night if ferries or long drives follow.
- In Athens, choose the neighborhood first, then the venue list.

## Trap Avoidance

- Do not promise seamless taxi availability after every late island night.
- Do not pair sunrise clubbing with early archaeological start times.
- Do not assume all famous islands offer the same music scene or crowd profile.
