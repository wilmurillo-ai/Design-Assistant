# Family Travel - Greece

## Best Family Fits

| Trip shape | Why it works |
|------------|--------------|
| Athens plus one beach island | Simple logistics, balanced activities |
| Crete only | Broad activity range and longer-stay flexibility |
| Ionian one-island base | Easier swimming and lower hop fatigue |
| Peloponnese road loop | Works for older kids who tolerate drives |

## What Families Need Most

- Shorter transfer chains
- Shade and midday slowdown in warm months
- Accommodation with easy beach or dinner access
- Realistic snack, bathroom, and rest-stop planning

## Better Choices

- One larger island beats three small islands for most families.
- Calm-water beaches often matter more than iconic sunset villages.
- Museum plus swim day often works better than ruins plus ruins plus ruins.

## Trap Avoidance

- Do not put kids through repeated port check-ins unless the family enjoys movement.
- Do not overbook archaeology in peak heat.
- Do not rely on last-minute restaurant spontaneity in very popular destinations.
