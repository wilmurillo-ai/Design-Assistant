# Ionian Islands - Greece

## Best Use Case

The Ionian Islands fit travelers who want greener scenery, calmer water, easier family swimming, and a less wind-dominated feel than the Aegean.

## Selection Logic

| Island style | Better fit |
|--------------|------------|
| Easy first stop with airport and broad appeal | Corfu |
| Beach scenery and road exploration | Lefkada |
| Relaxed coves and sailing mood | Kefalonia or Ithaca |

## Transport Pattern

- Ionian trips often work better as one island plus a car than as repeated ferry hops.
- Airport access can simplify the route compared with Cyclades ferry chains.
- Shoulder season can be excellent if services are still running at the needed frequency.

## Strengths

- Good for couples, families, and slower pacing
- Often better swim comfort than windier island groups
- Easier to build a drive-and-base trip

## Trap Avoidance

- Do not expect the same white-village visual style as the Cyclades.
- Do not skip car planning if hotel and beach goals are spread out.
- Do not mix Ionian and Cyclades in one short trip unless flights are extremely clean.
