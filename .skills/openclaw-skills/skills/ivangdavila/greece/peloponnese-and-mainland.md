# Peloponnese and Mainland South - Greece

## Best Use Case

This region suits travelers who want archaeology, smaller towns, coast, and a road-trip rhythm without paying island premium pricing.

## High-Value Bases

| Base | Good for |
|------|----------|
| Nafplio | Epidaurus, Mycenae, easy first mainland base |
| Monemvasia area | Historic atmosphere plus coastal routes |
| Kalamata or Mani side | Food, coastline, village driving |
| Olympia corridor | Classical history with inland pacing |

## Why It Works

- Strong balance of history and scenery
- Better value than headline islands in many periods
- Easier to build a coherent 5-7 day driving loop

## Requirements

- Car usually improves the trip
- Users should accept moderate driving days
- Heat planning still matters for exposed sites and beaches

## Trap Avoidance

- Do not reduce the Peloponnese to a single rushed day trip from Athens if the user likes history.
- Do not plan too many far-flung bases.
- Do not assume coastal roads are always fast just because the distances look short.
