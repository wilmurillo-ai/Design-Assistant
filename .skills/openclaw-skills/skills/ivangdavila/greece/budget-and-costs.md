# Budget and Costs - Greece

## Daily Budget Bands

| Style | Typical daily pattern |
|-------|-----------------------|
| Budget | Hostel or simple room, bakery lunches, buses, selective paid sites |
| Mid-range | Private room or hotel, one solid dinner, ferries or car on some days |
| Premium | View hotel, private transfers, better beach setup, premium dining |

## What Changes the Budget Fast

- July-August island pricing
- Santorini and Mykonos room premiums
- Ferry cabin or car transport
- Airport and port taxi dependence
- Beach club or organized beach spend

## Cost Areas Users Miss

- Port transfers on both ends
- Checked bags on domestic flights
- Parking in high-demand coastal or old-town areas
- Site tickets for archaeology-heavy routes
- Extra hotel night to protect against ferry risk

## Best Value Patterns

- Travel in May, June, September, or early October when weather still works
- Pick one bigger island instead of multiple premium small-island hops
- Use Athens or Thessaloniki as gateway cities, not as rushed transit-only stops
- Spend on location and transport logic before spending on room size

## Money-Saving Tradeoff

The cheapest-looking island chain often becomes expensive once ferry timing, transfer taxis, and missed-day risk are included.
