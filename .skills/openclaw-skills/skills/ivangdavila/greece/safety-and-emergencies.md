# Safety and Emergencies - Greece

## Core Risks

| Risk | Why it matters |
|------|----------------|
| Heat | Summer sightseeing can become a medical issue quickly |
| Wildfire | Access roads, beaches, and island movements can change fast |
| Wind and sea state | Ferry comfort and reliability can shift |
| Petty theft | Mostly city and transit awareness issue |
| Driving fatigue | Mountain and coastal roads can be slower than expected |

## Practical Rules

- Use 112 for emergency help.
- Check civil protection and weather updates during heat or fire periods.
- Carry water, hat, shade plan, and offline reservation details.
- If ferries matter for the route, monitor conditions the day before and the morning of travel.

## Health Notes

- Serious heat plus exposed ruins is a common avoidable failure mode.
- Travel insurance becomes more important when the route depends on ferries or remote driving.
- Save pharmacy and hospital options near the active base, not just near arrival airport.

## Theft and Scam Level

- Greece is usually a planning-and-conditions safety problem more than a violent-crime destination.
- Watch belongings in crowded Athens transit or ferry boarding zones.
- Be cautious with very aggressive transfer offers in ports and airports.
