# Telecoms and Apps - Greece

## Connectivity Setup

- Prefer eSIM or a ready SIM plan before island movement starts.
- Keep offline maps and hotel addresses saved before ferry days.
- Assume ports and remote beaches are worse moments for low battery than city museums.

## Useful App Categories

| Use case | Tool type |
|----------|-----------|
| City navigation | Maps with offline support |
| Ferry monitoring | Operator or ferry-booking app with alerts |
| Athens transport | Official transit or ticketing tools |
| Taxis and transfers | Licensed taxi dispatch or prebooked transfer apps |
| Weather and safety | Forecast plus fire or civil protection checks |

## Device Hygiene

- Screenshot ferry, hotel, and transfer confirmations.
- Save passport copy and insurance details securely.
- Carry a power bank on long transit days.

## Practical Rule

No battery means no route. Greece logistics depend heavily on tickets, maps, and fast re-checks when transport shifts.
