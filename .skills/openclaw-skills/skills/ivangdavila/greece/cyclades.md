# Cyclades - Greece

## Best Use Case

Cyclades suit travelers who want classic Aegean villages, sea views, ferries, and a balanced mix of swim time and evening atmosphere.

## Island Selection Logic

| Priority | Better fit |
|----------|------------|
| First-time postcard feel | Santorini or a well-chosen pair around it |
| Beach plus village balance | Naxos or Paros |
| Nightlife and scene | Mykonos |
| Slower food-led pace | Sifnos or Serifos |

## Routing Rule

- Two islands are usually enough for a 7-10 day Cyclades block.
- Neighboring islands beat famous islands that require awkward connections.
- Santorini is better as one committed stop than as a rushed photo detour.

## Seasonal Reality

- Meltemi wind matters in summer.
- Premium islands book out fast in July and August.
- Shoulder season often gives better overall value and less ferry stress.

## Trap Avoidance

- Do not pair the most famous islands just because the names are famous.
- Do not assume every Cycladic island has the same vibe, beach quality, or transport ease.
- Do not treat port arrival time as the same thing as hotel arrival time.
