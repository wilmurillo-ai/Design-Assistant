# Itineraries - Greece

## 7 Days: One City Plus One Strong Base

### Option A: Athens plus Naxos or Paros
- Days 1-2: Athens core archaeology and walkable neighborhoods
- Days 3-6: One Cyclades base with one slow day and one optional day trip
- Day 7: Return to Athens or depart from island airport if route allows

### Option B: Athens plus eastern Peloponnese
- Days 1-2: Athens and Acropolis zone
- Days 3-6: Nafplio base with Epidaurus and Mycenae logic
- Day 7: Buffer and departure

## 10 Days: Two-Base Greece

### Option A: Athens plus Crete
- Base 1: Athens for 2-3 nights
- Base 2: Crete for 6-7 nights, one coast zone only unless user likes driving

### Option B: Athens plus two Cyclades islands
- Use a neighboring pair such as Paros-Naxos, not islands in opposite directions
- Keep at least one buffer half-day for ferry changes

## 14 Days: History Plus Sea

### Athens plus Peloponnese plus one island
- Athens 3 nights
- Peloponnese 5-6 nights by car
- One island or Crete 4-5 nights
- Last night near departure airport

## 21 Days: Two Clusters With Depth

- Cluster 1: Athens plus mainland or Peloponnese
- Cluster 2: One island group or one large island
- Avoid three separate island groups unless traveler explicitly values movement over ease

## Quality Checks

- No same-day international arrival plus essential ferry unless there is a backup
- Every 3-4 active days include one lighter beach or cafe day
- Final night is in the departure city unless open-jaw flights make that unnecessary
