# I-Drive, Dr. Phillips, Lake Buena Vista, Celebration

## Best For

- Visitors who want convenience first
- Service and hospitality workers
- Households that want park access or convention access
- Residents who care more about southwest location than city culture

## Area Personality

| Area | Strength | Watchout |
|------|----------|----------|
| International Drive | Hotels, conventions, chain dining, attractions | Feels tourist-heavy and traffic-loaded |
| Dr. Phillips | Strong restaurant cluster and polished suburban feel | Expensive and still car-dependent |
| Lake Buena Vista | Disney access and resort adjacency | Mostly tourism infrastructure |
| Celebration | Attractive design and Disney-adjacent order | Can feel curated and premium-priced |

## Good Fit

- Conference traveler who needs fast venue access
- Disney-heavy household
- Resident working in southwest hospitality or tourism nodes

## Bad Fit

- User wanting local-city authenticity
- Budget renter assuming tourism-zone apartments are cheap
- Family that hates traffic and seasonal spikes
