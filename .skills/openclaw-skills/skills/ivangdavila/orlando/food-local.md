# Orlando Local and Florida Staples

## What Counts as Local Here

Orlando local food is not one singular cuisine. Think of it as Florida plus Central Florida plus migration:
- Cuban and Puerto Rican influence
- Southern comfort
- Fresh seafood from nearby coasts
- Citrus-forward desserts and drinks
- Florida brunch culture

## High-Value Things to Try

- Cuban sandwich or Tampa-vs-Orlando variation talk
- Puerto Rican roast pork, mofongo, pastries
- Fresh Gulf or Atlantic fish depending on the menu
- Key lime dessert
- Strong brunch instead of weak hotel breakfast

## Best Settings

| Goal | Better Area |
|------|-------------|
| Puerto Rican and Latin flavor | Kissimmee side, parts of south Orlando |
| Upscale Florida dinner | Winter Park, Dr. Phillips |
| Brunch | Winter Park, College Park, Mills 50 |

## Mistake to Avoid

Do not ask Orlando to behave like New Orleans or Charleston. Its local flavor is more hybrid and spread out.
