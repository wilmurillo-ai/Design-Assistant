# Orlando Food Practicalities

## Reservations and Timing

- Reserve top places on weekends, school-break weeks, and major convention windows.
- Eat earlier than usual with kids in summer.
- Do not expect easy spontaneous seating in the tourism corridor during peak seasons.

## Tipping and Budgeting

- Standard full-service tipping remains 18-20 percent.
- Resort and park food can blow budgets faster than tickets if unmanaged.
- Grocery breakfast plus one anchor dinner often beats trying to optimize every meal.

## Dietary Fit

- Orlando is generally good for gluten-free, dairy-free, and kid-sensitive dining if the user plans ahead.
- Disney is especially strong at handling allergens when declared properly.

## Kids and Families

- Prioritize air conditioning and predictable menus on hard park days.
- One memorable meal is usually better than chasing every reservation trend.
