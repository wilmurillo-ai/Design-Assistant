# Winter Garden, Windermere, Oviedo, Apopka, Clermont

## Best For

- Families wanting space and school options
- Buyers looking beyond the urban core
- Households that accept car dependence in exchange for larger homes

## Area Personality

| Area | Strength | Watchout |
|------|----------|----------|
| Winter Garden / Horizon West | Fast-growing family favorite near Disney side | Traffic and price creep |
| Windermere | Prestige and large-home appeal | Expensive, not much walkability |
| Oviedo | Strong family reputation on the northeast side | Long cross-metro drives |
| Apopka | Better value and access north-west | Less polished, commute matters |
| Clermont | Space and relative value west of core | Commute can be rough |

## Main Tradeoff

These areas often win on house, school, and neighborhood feel, but daily life becomes fully car and toll dependent.
