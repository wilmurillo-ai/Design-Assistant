# Orlando Startup and Founder Guide

## What Orlando Is Good For

- Service businesses with disciplined operations
- Travel-tech and hospitality-adjacent products
- Bootstrapped founder life with lower burn than major coastal hubs
- Community-driven founders who like Central Florida quality of life

## What Orlando Is Not

- A giant venture market with nonstop dense founder collisions
- The best place to raise a large institutional round by default

## Good Founder Assets

- UCF talent pipeline
- Coworking and business-community nodes
- Convention and visitor economy for certain customer segments
- Easier lifestyle for families than many larger startup hubs

## Better Founder Advice

| Founder Type | Better Move |
|--------------|-------------|
| Bootstrapped operator | Orlando can work well |
| Hard-tech VC seeker | Probably needs a broader national network |
| Family-first founder | Orlando often shines |
| Solo remote founder | Winter Park, Baldwin Park, and Lake Nona fit well |
