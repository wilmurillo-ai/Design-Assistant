# How to Choose an Orlando Area

## Ask These Questions First

1. What matters most: parks, schools, airport, nightlife, or a clean commute?
2. How many days per week will someone cross I-4 or go toward Disney?
3. Is the household okay with toll roads?
4. Does the user want polished suburbia or local-city texture?
5. Is walkability a nice extra or a real requirement?

## Best Decision Tree

| Priority | Best Starting Areas |
|----------|---------------------|
| Disney and southwest jobs | Celebration, Dr. Phillips, Horizon West, Lake Buena Vista side |
| Urban living | Thornton Park, South Eola, Mills 50, Ivanhoe |
| Family polish | Winter Park, Baldwin Park, Winter Garden, Oviedo |
| Airport and Medical City | Lake Nona, Conway |
| Lower-cost outer ring | Apopka, east Orlando, Clermont, parts of Kissimmee |

## Red Flags

- Choosing by straight-line distance
- Ignoring toll budgets
- Assuming school quality is uniform across a city name
- Optimizing only for rent while creating a bad commute
