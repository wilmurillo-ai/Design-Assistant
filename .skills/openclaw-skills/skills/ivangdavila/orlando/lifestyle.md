# Orlando Lifestyle Guide

## What Daily Life Actually Feels Like

- Errand-heavy and car-oriented
- Strong for family logistics if the neighborhood is chosen well
- Better for sunshine and convenience than for dense urban stimulation
- Surprisingly good for dining, fitness, golf, and planned recreation

## Good Lifestyle Fits

| Profile | Why Orlando Works |
|---------|-------------------|
| Family with kids | Activities, schools, space, airport, weather |
| Remote worker | Space and airport access |
| Active retiree | Weather and amenities |
| Urban maximalist | Works only in limited pockets |

## Best Quality-of-Life Moves

- Pay for a better daily location, not just a bigger unit
- Choose a neighborhood with one or two routines you can do without a long drive
- Build weather-aware exercise and kid routines
