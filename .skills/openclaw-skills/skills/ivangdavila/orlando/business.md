# Orlando Business Setup

## Best Fit for Orlando

- Hospitality-adjacent services
- Healthcare and wellness
- Home and family services
- Events and convention support
- Education, sports, and youth programming
- Specialized B2B services for Central Florida growth corridors

## Florida Setup Basics

| Item | Typical Reality |
|------|-----------------|
| LLC filing | Handled through Florida Sunbiz |
| Annual report | Required to keep the entity active |
| State income tax | None for individuals |
| Sales tax and local compliance | Still matter depending on the business |

## Orlando-Specific Business Truths

- Location matters more than people expect because the metro is spread out.
- The convention economy creates opportunity, but not every business benefits from tourism.
- Southwest Orlando and airport-adjacent corridors behave differently from Winter Park or east-side neighborhoods.

## Common Mistakes

- Choosing a cheap location that kills operational convenience
- Assuming a Florida LLC alone solves licensing or permit needs
- Building for tourists when the real margin is in residents or B2B services
