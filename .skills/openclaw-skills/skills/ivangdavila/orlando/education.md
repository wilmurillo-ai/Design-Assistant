# Orlando Education Guide

## Main Education Lanes

| Segment | Reality |
|---------|---------|
| Public K-12 | Strongly neighborhood and district dependent |
| Private schools | Good options, but costs rise fast |
| Higher education | UCF is the major anchor |
| Young-kid care | Childcare and after-school access can shape housing choice |

## Family Planning Rules

1. Choose the school path before choosing the house.
2. Verify boundaries and programs directly.
3. Budget for childcare separately from housing.

## College and University Context

- UCF gives Orlando real student and research energy.
- Rollins adds value on the Winter Park side.
- Valencia College is important for practical local pathways.

## Education Mistakes

- Assuming all "good suburb" schools are equally good
- Ignoring commute to school, not just commute to work
- Choosing far-out housing that complicates after-school logistics
