# Orlando Driving and Car Ownership

## Core Reality

If a resident asks whether they need a car, the answer is usually yes.

## What Driving Feels Like

- Congestion around I-4, Disney corridors, and airport approaches
- Tolls are common enough to matter
- Parking is routine but often paid in tourist zones
- Distances feel short until traffic turns them into real commutes

## Good Driving Advice

| Situation | Better Move |
|-----------|-------------|
| Family move | Budget tolls and insurance from day one |
| Park vacation | Compare rental-car total versus hotel-plus-shuttle strategy |
| Remote worker | Live where daily driving friction stays low |

## Common Mistakes

- Renting a car without understanding toll billing
- Choosing a house that looks close to work but crosses the wrong corridor
- Ignoring airport or school driving in the weekly schedule
