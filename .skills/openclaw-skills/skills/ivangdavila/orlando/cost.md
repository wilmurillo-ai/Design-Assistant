# Orlando Cost Guide 2025-2026

## Quick Summary

| Profile | Typical Monthly Cost |
|---------|----------------------|
| Single person, local life only | USD 2,700-4,100 |
| Couple, comfortable renter life | USD 4,200-6,200 |
| Family of 4, renter | USD 5,800-8,800 |
| Park-heavy family trip | USD 450-900 per day all-in |

## Housing

| Item | Typical Range |
|------|---------------|
| 1BR in tourism corridor | USD 1,500-2,100 |
| 1BR Downtown or Winter Park | USD 1,650-2,400 |
| 3BR suburban rental | USD 2,500-4,000 |
| Median closed home price | Around USD 380,000-400,000 |

## Resident Budget Traps

- Car insurance
- Toll roads
- Electricity spikes in hot months
- Childcare
- Home and flood-related insurance exposure

## Visitor Budget Traps

- Date-based tickets
- Hotel parking
- Resort fees
- Park food and premium line-skipping products
- Rideshare churn between districts

## Good Framing

Orlando can be a value if the user keeps the trip or move geographically coherent. It gets expensive when the user tries to optimize every category separately and ends up paying in transport, time, and add-ons.
