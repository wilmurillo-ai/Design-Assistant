# Downtown Orlando, Thornton Park, Milk District, Ivanhoe

## Best For

- Young professionals
- People who want some nightlife and event density
- Residents working near Downtown, hospitals, or creative districts
- Short local-style weekends

## Area Personality

| Area | Strength | Watchout |
|------|----------|----------|
| Thornton Park / South Eola | Best-looking urban pocket, walkable around Lake Eola | Smaller inventory, higher price |
| Milk District | Quirky food and bar scene | Not truly walkable for every errand |
| Ivanhoe Village | Good local identity and lake adjacency | Patchy block-to-block feel |
| Central Downtown | Event access and apartments | Noise, parking, and uneven night feel |

## Good Fit

- Wants bars, coffee shops, and events more than school-zoned suburbia
- Does not need Disney access daily
- Can accept some parking friction and a more urban feel

## Bad Fit

- Daily Disney worker commuting southwest
- Family wanting top suburban school reputation
- User imagining NYC-scale walkability
