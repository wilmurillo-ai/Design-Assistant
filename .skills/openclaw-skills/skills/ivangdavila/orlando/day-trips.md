# Orlando Day Trips

## Best Full-Day Options

| Trip | Why Go | Drive Time | Best For |
|------|--------|------------|----------|
| Kennedy Space Center | Best science and space day in the region | About 1h | Families, adults, repeat Orlando visitors |
| Winter Park + Maitland + museums | Most polished local-city day | 20-35 min from core Orlando | Adults, lighter days |
| Wekiwa or Kelly Park / Rock Springs | Springs and heat relief | 30-50 min | Summer, outdoors, kids |
| Cocoa Beach | Closest beach reset | About 1h | Casual beach day |
| New Smyrna Beach | Better town feel and surf culture | 1h-1h15 | Couples, adults |
| Bok Tower Gardens | Calm inland day with great landscaping | 1h15 | Older travelers, couples |

## What Actually Works

- Kennedy Space Center plus beach stop if the group has energy
- Springs day when the forecast is hot but not storm-dominated
- Winter Park day if the user wants "real Orlando" and not another ticketed attraction

## What Usually Fails

- Beach day on a partial departure day
- Springs day without checking reservations, capacity, or storm forecast
- Trying to combine a beach, an outlet mall, and Disney Springs in one day

## Best Add-On by Visitor Type

| Visitor Type | Best Add-On |
|--------------|-------------|
| First family trip | One springs or Winter Park half-day, not both |
| Adults on a second Orlando trip | Kennedy or a better coast day |
| Conference traveler | Winter Park or Park Avenue evening |
| Theme-park maximalist | No day trip needed unless trip is 5+ days |
