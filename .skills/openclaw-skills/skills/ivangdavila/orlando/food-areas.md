# Best Orlando Dining Areas

## Best Areas by Use Case

| Area | Best For | Vibe |
|------|----------|------|
| Winter Park / Park Avenue | Date night, polished dining | Upscale, walkable pocket |
| Mills 50 / Audubon Park | Local flavor and variety | Creative, casual, local |
| Dr. Phillips | Group dinners near parks or conventions | Polished suburban strip |
| Disney Springs | Good branded variety without park ticket | High-energy, tourist-heavy |
| Celebration | Family-friendly off-park dinner | Clean, easy, curated |

## If the User Has One Night

- Adults: Winter Park or Mills 50
- Family near Disney: Disney Springs or Celebration
- Conference dinner: Dr. Phillips

## If the User Has Three Nights

1. One polished dinner
2. One local casual neighborhood meal
3. One convenience-first night near lodging
