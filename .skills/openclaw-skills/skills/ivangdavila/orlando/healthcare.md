# Orlando Healthcare Guide

## Main System Reality

Orlando is strong by Florida standards because of major hospital systems, Medical City in Lake Nona, and a large healthcare workforce.

## Good Anchors

| Need | Better Move |
|------|-------------|
| Complex care or major system depth | Orlando Health or AdventHealth network review |
| Family primary care | Choose based on home base, not brand alone |
| Airport-linked executive travel | Lake Nona and southeast side can be convenient |

## Important Resident Moves

- Check provider network before choosing insurance based only on premium.
- Pick primary care early because appointment lead times can stretch.
- For families, measure pediatric and urgent-care access from the actual neighborhood.

## Visitor Advice

- Theme-park trips should still include urgent-care awareness for heat, dehydration, or minor injuries.
- Travel insurance matters more when the trip cost is ticket-heavy and nonrefundable.
