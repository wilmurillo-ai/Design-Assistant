# Winter Park, Audubon Park, College Park

## Best For

- Families and couples wanting polish
- Remote workers who want better daily quality
- Buyers or renters prioritizing schools, aesthetics, and dining

## Area Personality

| Area | Strength | Watchout |
|------|----------|----------|
| Winter Park | Best all-around prestige + lifestyle balance | Expensive, tight inventory |
| Audubon Park | Strong local food and neighborhood feel | Smaller scale, limited housing variety |
| College Park | Popular family choice close to Downtown | Price creep and older housing stock |

## Why People Choose This Belt

- Tree canopy and older charm
- Better local restaurant density
- Strong private-school and family-adjacent ecosystem
- Feels more like a real city than the tourism corridor

## Main Tradeoff

You pay for vibe, schools, and convenience, but park commutes are worse than the map suggests.
