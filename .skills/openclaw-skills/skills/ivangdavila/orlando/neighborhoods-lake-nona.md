# Lake Nona, Conway, Baldwin Park, Southeast Side

## Best For

- Airport-heavy travelers
- Healthcare and medical-city workers
- Newer housing seekers
- Families who like planned environments

## Area Personality

| Area | Strength | Watchout |
|------|----------|----------|
| Lake Nona | Clean, modern, polished, healthcare hub | Expensive and master-planned feeling |
| Conway / Hourglass-adjacent | Good airport access with more established housing | Less prestige than Lake Nona |
| Baldwin Park | Highly polished, family-friendly, walkable pocket | Premium pricing |

## Best Use Cases

- Frequent MCO access
- Work in southeast employment nodes
- Want newer homes and easier day-to-day logistics
- Need a cleaner line between city convenience and family order

## Weak Spots

- Not ideal for Disney-heavy daily life
- Can feel engineered rather than organic
- Car dependence remains high despite nice internal planning
