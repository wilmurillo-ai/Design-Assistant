# Orlando International Dining

## Strongest International Lanes

| Cuisine | Best Areas |
|---------|------------|
| Vietnamese | Mills 50 and Colonial corridor |
| Japanese / sushi / omakase | Winter Park, Dr. Phillips |
| Korean | Scattered east and north pockets |
| Italian | Winter Park, Dr. Phillips, Celebration |
| Upscale tasting menus | Winter Park and luxury-resort zones |
| Brazilian and steakhouse | Tourism corridor and southwest |

## Best Use

- Adults-only evening off from the parks
- Conference dinner beyond hotel restaurants
- Repeat visitor who already knows Disney Springs and CityWalk

## What to Tell the User

- Orlando's best international meals are usually not inside the parks.
- The strongest meal may require a short drive.
- Reservations matter for top Winter Park and Dr. Phillips tables.
