# Profile Presets - Email Management

Choose the closest profile and adapt based on user feedback.

## Executive

Focus:
- decision speed
- high-signal summaries
- delegation prompts

Defaults:
- show top 3 priorities first
- compress FYI into one digest block
- use direct action language

## Manager

Focus:
- team dependencies
- stakeholder alignment
- deadline visibility

Defaults:
- highlight blocked threads
- track commitments by owner
- include escalation candidates

## Freelancer

Focus:
- client responsiveness
- scope control
- invoice and approval timing

Defaults:
- prioritize client-facing actions
- flag unpaid or unapproved work
- suggest concise follow-up nudges

## Student

Focus:
- assignment deadlines
- advisor and professor communication
- opportunity tracking

Defaults:
- prioritize academic deadlines
- keep tone respectful and clear
- summarize opportunities and required steps
