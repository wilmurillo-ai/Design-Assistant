# Best Eating Areas — Madrid

## La Latina

**The tapas capital.** Cava Baja and surrounding streets have the highest concentration of quality tapas bars in Madrid.

| Vibe | Traditional + trendy mix |
|------|-------------------------|
| Best for | Tapas crawl, Sunday post-Rastro |
| Price | €€ |
| Best time | Sunday lunch, evening any day |

### Must-Visit

| Bar | Specialty | Price |
|-----|-----------|-------|
| Casa Lucio | Huevos rotos | €€€ |
| Juana la Loca | Creative tortilla | €€ |
| Txirimiri | Basque pintxos | €€ |
| El Viajero | Rooftop | €€ |
| Taberna Tempranillo | Wine + tapas | €€ |

**Crawl route:** Start Cava Baja → La Perejila → Juana la Loca → El Viajero rooftop

## Malasaña

**Hipster food scene.** Brunch culture, specialty coffee, international options, traditional vermuterías side by side.

| Vibe | Young, creative, eclectic |
|------|--------------------------|
| Best for | Brunch, casual dinner, late night |
| Price | €-€€ |
| Best time | Weekend brunch, evening |

### Must-Visit

| Place | Specialty | Price |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| Casa Julio | Croquetas (legendary) | € |
| Bodega de la Ardosa | Vermut | € |
| Federal Café | Brunch | €€ |
| Pez Tortilla | Tortillas | €€ |
| La Barraca | Traditional tapas | €€ |
| HanSo Café | Korean | €€ |

**Tip:** Calle Pez and surrounding streets for density.

| Vibe | Classic, residential, quality |
|------|------------------------------|
| Best for | Quality without pretension |
| Price | €€-€€€ |
| Best time | Lunch, evening |

### Must-Visit

| Place | Specialty | Price |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| Casa Mingo | Asturian cider + chicken | € |

## Usera (Chinatown)

**Authentic Chinese (not European Chinese).** Largest Chinese community in Spain.

| Vibe | Authentic, zero tourists |
|------|-------------------------|
| Best for | Real Chinese food |
| Price | € |
| Best time | Weekend dim sum |

### Must-Visit

| Place | Specialty | Price |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| Chifa Dashi | Peruvian-Chinese | €€ |
| Royal Cantonés | Dim sum | € |
| Restaurante Chino 168 | Hand-pulled noodles | € |
| Floating Islands | Sichuan | € |

**Tip:** Calle Marcelo Usera for concentration. Weekend dim sum is excellent value.
