# Attractions Guide — Madrid

## Worth Your Time

| Attraction | Why | Cost | Tip |
|------------|-----|------|-----|
| **Museo del Prado** | World-class collection: Velázquez, Goya, El Greco | €15 | FREE Mon-Sat 6-8pm, Sun 5-7pm |
| **Reina Sofía** | Picasso's Guernica + modern masters | €12 | FREE Mon, Wed-Sat 7-9pm, Sun 12:30-2:30pm |
| **Retiro Park** | Gorgeous gardens, rowboats, Crystal Palace | FREE | Sunday morning best |
| **Royal Palace** | Europe's largest royal palace | €14 | See changing of guard (Wed, Sat 11am) |
| **Templo de Debod** | Egyptian temple, best sunset spot | FREE | Go 1h before sunset |
| **Thyssen Museum** | Private collection, Impressionists to Pop | €14 | FREE Mondays 12-4pm |
| **El Rastro** | Massive Sunday flea market | FREE | Go early (9-10am) or late (1-2pm) |
| **Gran Vía** | Iconic avenue, beautiful at night | FREE | Walk after sunset for lights |
| **Plaza Mayor** | Historic central square | FREE | Morning (empty) or evening |
| **Mercado de San Miguel** | Beautiful building (but overpriced) | FREE | Quick photo, don't eat there |
| **Matadero** | Former slaughterhouse, now culture center | FREE | Check event calendar |
| **Caixa Forum** | Vertical garden, exhibitions | €6 | Vertical garden is free to see |

## Tourist Traps to SKIP

| Skip | Why | Instead |
|------|-----|---------|
| **Mercado de San Miguel** | 200-300% markup, tourist prices | Mercado de San Fernando or Antón Martín |
| **Plaza Mayor restaurants** | Overpriced, mediocre | Walk to La Latina (5 min) |
| **Casa Botín** | "Oldest restaurant" hype, not worth price | La Bola for cocido, Casa Lucio for huevos |
| **Sol restaurants** | Tourist trap central | Walk 2 blocks any direction |
| **Bernabéu match scalpers** | Scam prices | Official site or reputable resellers |
| **Segway tours** | Overpriced, annoying | Walk or BiciMAD |
| **"Free" walking tours** | Pressure to tip €15-20 | Pay for quality guided tour or self-guide |
| **Taxi from airport (no meter)** | Should be €33 fixed | Insist on meter or fixed rate |

## Museums Deep Dive

### Museo del Prado

| What | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Must-see** | Las Meninas (Velázquez), Black Paintings (Goya), Garden of Earthly Delights (Bosch) |
| **Time needed** | 2-3 hours minimum |
| **Best time** | Early morning or free evening hours |
| **Tip** | Download app for free audio guide |

### Reina Sofía

| What | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Must-see** | Guernica (Picasso), Dalí collection, Miró |
| **Time needed** | 2 hours |
| **Best time** | Free evening hours |
| **Tip** | Guernica room is always crowded — go early |

### Thyssen-Bornemisza

| What | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Must-see** | Van Gogh, Monet, Hopper, chronological journey |
| **Time needed** | 1.5-2 hours |
| **Best time** | Free Mondays |
| **Tip** | Good café in garden |

### Other Museums Worth Considering

| Museum | What | Time | Cost |
|--------|------|------|------|
| **Sorolla Museum** | Impressionist painter's house | 1 hour | €3 (free Sat after 2pm, Sun) |
| **Archaeology Museum (MAN)** | Iberian artifacts, Dame of Elche | 2 hours | €3 |
| **Lázaro Galdiano** | Private collection, house | 1.5 hours | €8 |
| **Real Academia de Bellas Artes** | Goya, Velázquez | 1 hour | €8 (free Wed) |

## Day Trips

| Destination | Distance | Why Go | How |
|-------------|----------|--------|-----|
| **Toledo** | 30 min train | Medieval city, El Greco | AVE from Atocha |
| **Segovia** | 30 min train | Roman aqueduct, cochinillo, castle | AVE from Chamartín |
| **El Escorial** | 1 hr train | Royal monastery, massive | Cercanías C3 |
| **Ávila** | 1.5 hr train | Medieval walls | Regional train |
| **Aranjuez** | 45 min train | Royal palace, gardens | Cercanías C3 |
| **Chinchón** | 45 min bus | Plaza, garlic, anís | Bus from Conde de Casal |

**Best day trips:** Toledo and Segovia are unmissable if you have 3+ days.
