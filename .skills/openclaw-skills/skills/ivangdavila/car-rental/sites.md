## Price Comparison Strategy

### For Short Rentals (1-7 days)
1. Check aggregators first (Kayak, Rentalcars)
2. Note best price
3. Check direct with that company (sometimes cheaper)
4. Check AutoEurope/DiscoverCars for budget options
5. Factor in insurance and total cost

### For Medium-term (1-4 weeks)
1. Ask aggregators for "monthly" rates
2. Check subscription services
3. Compare total cost vs daily rate x days
4. Consider pickup from city vs airport

### For Long-term (1+ months)
1. Subscription services first
2. Local dealers for lease quotes
3. Traditional rental with monthly rate
4. Calculate break-even vs buying

## Red Flags

- **Too cheap** — Check what's excluded (insurance, mileage, fees)
- **Unknown company** — Read recent reviews carefully
- **Prepay required** — Less flexibility, harder refunds
- **Pickup location unclear** — Might be shuttle bus required
- **"Free cancellation" details** — Check the actual terms
