# Playbooks

Use these when the user needs execution, not theory.

## 1. Program Launch Sprint

- lock partner types, payouts, and restricted behaviors
- create onboarding and disclosure pack
- validate links, attribution, and landing paths
- recruit only partners that fit one clear launch use case

## 2. Tracking Recovery Sprint

- audit links, coupons, UTMs, and attribution windows
- isolate where affiliate credit conflicts with paid or CRM
- fix broken paths before approving more scale
- rerun partner QA after every structural change

## 3. Fraud Containment Sprint

- pause suspicious payouts
- review sources, coupon spread, and lead quality
- classify abuse vs tracking error vs normal variance
- document permanent rule changes before reactivation

## 4. Margin Rescue Sprint

- review payout ceilings, refunds, and partner mix
- cut partners that only work on irrational commission
- shift budget to partners with cleaner intent and lower leakage
- redesign the offer before expanding volume again
