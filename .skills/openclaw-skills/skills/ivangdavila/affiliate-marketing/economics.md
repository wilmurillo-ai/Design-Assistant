# Economics

Use this before changing commissions or scaling affiliate volume.

## Commission Logic

| Question | Why it matters |
|----------|----------------|
| What is gross margin after refunds and fees? | defines safe payout ceiling |
| Is revenue incremental? | prevents paying for demand that was already yours |
| What is the time-to-value or payback? | protects cash flow |
| What is reversal risk? | avoids fake or low-quality wins |
| What partner type is driving the sale? | different types deserve different payout logic |

## Payout Models

| Model | Best for | Main caution |
|-------|----------|--------------|
| CPA | simple acquisition goals | can reward low-quality conversions |
| Rev-share | long-term aligned partners | delayed quality visibility |
| Hybrid | mixed conversion and retention goals | more admin complexity |
| Tiered | rewarding top quality | can create sudden margin pressure |

## Ceiling Rule

Never recommend commission increases without checking:
- gross margin
- assisted vs incremental conversion
- refund or clawback rate
- payout timing
- overlap with paid search, CRM, or existing promo channels

If the channel only wins at irrational commission, the program design is wrong.
