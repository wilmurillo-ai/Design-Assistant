# Partner Pipeline

Use this to source, qualify, and prioritize affiliates.

## Qualification Grid

| Dimension | What to inspect |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Audience fit | overlap with buyer and geography |
| Traffic quality | search intent, content depth, creator trust, spam signals |
| Commercial fit | payout expectations, promo style, preferred offer types |
| Brand safety | claims style, disclosure habits, reputation, trademark behavior |
| Activation likelihood | responsiveness, content format fit, onboarding friction |

## Priority Signals

Move a partner up when:
- audience matches the best buyers
- they can explain the offer credibly
- economics can work at reasonable commission
- they fit one clear campaign use case

Move a partner down when:
- traffic looks incentivized or low intent
- they rely on trademark bidding or coupon leakage
- they need payouts that break margin
- they create channel conflict with existing teams

## Outreach Pack Requirement

Before first outreach, prepare:
- pitch angle
- offer or commission proposal
- landing page or destination
- disclosure expectation
- tracking method
- next step if they respond positively

Weak outreach usually means weak program design, not weak templates.
