# Program Design

Use this before recruiting or approving affiliates.

## Core Choices

| Decision | Why it matters |
|----------|----------------|
| Partner types | creators, content publishers, coupon sites, SaaS referrers, agencies, communities |
| Payout model | CPA, rev-share, hybrid, tiered, launch bonus |
| Attribution window | controls payout fairness and conflict with paid/CRM |
| Approval rules | stops low-quality or unsafe partners early |
| Restricted traffic sources | prevents brand abuse, spam, and channel conflict |
| Clawback policy | protects margin from refunds, fraud, and fake leads |

Do not open recruitment while these are undefined.

## Partner Type Logic

| Type | Best for | Main risk |
|------|----------|-----------|
| Content affiliates | review and comparison traffic | weak commercial intent |
| Creator affiliates | trust and reach | disclosure failures |
| Coupon partners | end-of-funnel lift | cannibalization |
| B2B referral partners | higher trust and deal quality | long sales cycles |
| Media-buying affiliates | rapid scale | fraud and margin pressure |

## Offer Design Rule

An affiliate offer should define:

1. Who should sell it
2. What behavior gets paid
3. What cannot be promoted
4. When the payout is valid
5. When the payout can be reversed

If one of these is fuzzy, the program will leak.
