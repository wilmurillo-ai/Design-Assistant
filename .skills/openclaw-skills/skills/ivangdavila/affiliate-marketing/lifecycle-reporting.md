# Lifecycle And Reporting

Use this to run affiliate marketing as an operating loop.

## Lifecycle Stages

| Stage | Main job |
|-------|----------|
| Recruit | identify and prioritize good partners |
| Onboard | align tracking, disclosures, assets, and offer |
| Activate | get first quality traffic live |
| Coach | improve landing fit, content angle, and promo quality |
| Scale | increase volume only when economics hold |
| Reactivate | bring back strong partners with new offers or assets |
| Offboard | remove unsafe or unproductive partners cleanly |

## Weekly Review Output

End each review with:
- recruit
- activate
- fix
- scale
- pause
- remove

Each decision should cite:
- economics
- traffic quality
- compliance quality
- attribution clarity
- partner responsiveness

## Executive Metrics

Track at minimum:
- incremental affiliate revenue
- margin after commissions
- reversal or refund rate
- top partner concentration
- active vs dormant partners
- fraud or compliance incidents

Large revenue with weak quality or weak control is not program health.
