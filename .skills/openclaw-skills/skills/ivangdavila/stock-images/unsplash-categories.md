## Usage Tips

1. **Combine terms** with commas: `?office,work,team`
2. **Be specific** for better results: `?italian,pasta` vs just `?food`
3. **Use common sizes**: 1920x1080, 1200x800, 800x600, 400x300
4. **Cache important images** by saving the final URL after redirect
5. **For consistency**, get the image ID after redirect and use `/id/PHOTO_ID/`
