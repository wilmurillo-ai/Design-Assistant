# Nightlife - Bulgaria

## Sofia

| Area | Best for | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Shishman | Bars and cafes | Best relaxed starting point |
| Vitosha / center | General nightlife | Easy for mixed groups |
| Studentski Grad | Clubs | Only if they want student-party energy |

## Plovdiv

| Area | Best for | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Kapana | Craft beer, bars, late cafes | Best evening neighborhood |
| Center | More casual bars | Easy fallback |

## Varna

| Area | Best for | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Sea Garden / beach bars | Summer nights | Seasonal and weather-dependent |
| Center | Year-round casual bars | Safer outside peak season |

## Bansko

| Mode | Reality |
|------|---------|
| Winter apres-ski | Strongest social energy |
| Summer nights | Much quieter, tavern-led |

## Rules

- Sofia is the best all-year nightlife answer.
- Varna works best in summer.
- Plovdiv is better for bars than clubs.
- Bansko nightlife should be recommended mainly in winter.
