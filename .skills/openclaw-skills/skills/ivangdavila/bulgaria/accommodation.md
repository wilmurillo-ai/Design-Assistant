# Accommodation - Bulgaria

## Quick Price Bands

| Type | Typical range | Best for |
|------|---------------|----------|
| Hostel | 20-50 BGN | Solo and budget travel |
| Simple guesthouse | 50-110 BGN | Short local stays |
| Mid-range hotel | 110-220 BGN | Most city trips |
| Boutique / spa hotel | 180-350 BGN | Couples and comfort trips |
| Resort package hotel | 180-500+ BGN | Only if they want resort life |

## Where to Stay by Base

### Sofia

| Area | Best for | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Center / Serdika | First visit | Easiest without a car |
| Oborishte | Calmer stylish stay | Good cafes and walks |
| Lozenets | Longer stays | Good restaurants, local feel |

### Plovdiv

| Area | Best for | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Kapana / center | Easy evening plan | Best overall pick |
| Old Town edge | Character | Beautiful, less convenient with luggage |
| Wider center | Value | Fine when price matters more than charm |

### Varna

| Area | Best for | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Center | City break | Good balance |
| Sea Garden side | Summer atmosphere | Higher prices |
| Golden Sands | Resort stay | Only if resort is intentional |

### Bansko

| Area | Best for | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Near gondola | Ski-first trips | Best winter convenience |
| Old Town | Food and character | Better atmosphere |
| Spa outskirts | Quiet | Better with a car |

## Booking Rules

- City hotels are usually better value than coast resorts outside peak season.
- On the coast, shoulder season brings a much better price-to-experience ratio.
- In Bansko winter weekends, book early if staying close to the gondola.
- In small towns, guesthouses can outperform chains on local advice and breakfast.

## Common Traps

- Booking Sunny Beach or Golden Sands by default without checking trip style
- Choosing very cheap coast hotels far from any good beach or town
- Staying outside Sofia to save little and lose walkability
- Staying far from the lift in Bansko on a short ski trip
