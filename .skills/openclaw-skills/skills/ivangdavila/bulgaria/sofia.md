# Sofia

## Neighborhoods

| Area | Best for | Reality |
|------|----------|---------|
| Center / Serdika | First-time visitors | Walkable, practical, busy |
| Oborishte | Cafes, calmer stay | Elegant, residential, good balance |
| Lozenets | Restaurants, parks | Local feel, short taxi/metro rides |
| Vitosha Blvd area | Shopping, nightlife | Handy but more touristy |
| Studentski Grad | Clubbing | Party-heavy, weak for culture trips |

## What to Prioritize

### Core sights
- Alexander Nevsky Cathedral
- St. Sofia Basilica
- Largo and Serdica Roman remains
- National Palace of Culture area
- Boyana Church if there is time for a half-day detour

### Strong city rhythm
- Walk from Alexander Nevsky through the center to Vitosha Boulevard
- Use Sofia as the easiest base for day trips to Rila, Plovdiv, or the mountains
- Pair museums with a late coffee stop, not a full marathon day

## Food and Drink Zones

| Area | Best for | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Shishman Street | Cafes, bars, brunch | Easy first-night area |
| Rakovska / center | Traditional restaurants, theaters | Convenient but mixed quality |
| Zhenski Pazar side streets | Markets, casual eats | More local, rougher edges |
| Lozenets | Better modern dining | Good for repeat visitors |

## Practical Notes

- Metro from the airport is the easiest budget arrival if staying near the center.
- Taxis should be ordered by app or taken from official ranks.
- Sofia is compact enough for mixed walking + metro days.
- Vitosha Mountain is close enough for a same-day nature break.

## Day Trips from Sofia

| Trip | Time | Why go |
|------|------|--------|
| Rila Monastery | Full day | The iconic cultural trip |
| Seven Rila Lakes | Full day | Best summer mountain day |
| Plovdiv | Full day | Strong if they want a second city |
| Boyana Church + Vitosha | Half day | Easy culture + nature combo |

## Common Traps

- Staying too far from the center to save very little money
- Taking random airport taxis instead of metro or app taxi
- Cramming museums and mountain time into the same rushed half day
- Using Studentski Grad unless nightlife is the explicit goal
