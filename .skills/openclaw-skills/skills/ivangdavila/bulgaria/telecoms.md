# Telecoms - Bulgaria

## If They Are From the EU

Roaming is typically the easiest option. For short trips, this usually beats the hassle of buying a local SIM.

## If They Need a Local SIM or eSIM

| Option | Best for |
|--------|----------|
| eSIM before arrival | Fast setup and no store visit |
| Airport or city shop SIM | Longer stays or heavy data use |

## Main Mobile Networks

- A1
- Yettel
- Vivacom

## Coverage Reality

- Sofia, Plovdiv, Varna, and major roads are generally straightforward.
- Beach zones are easy.
- Mountain routes and remote villages can weaken quickly.

## Advice

- Download offline maps for mountain or remote coast trips.
- Use WhatsApp or other data messaging as default.
- Do not rely on perfect signal for long hiking days.
