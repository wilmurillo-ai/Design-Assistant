# With Kids - Bulgaria

## Best Base Types

| Type | Best for |
|------|----------|
| Sofia | City breaks with parks and museums |
| Varna / Albena | Family beach trips |
| Bansko in summer | Light mountain breaks |
| Plovdiv | Short culture trip with easy walking zones |

## Good Family Picks

### Sofia
- Borisova Gradina
- Muzeiko and family-friendly museums
- Vitosha half days when weather is kind

### Varna
- Sea Garden
- Dolphinarium area and easy beach days
- Albena for cleaner resort logistics

### Plovdiv
- Old Town in shorter segments
- Kapana for relaxed evening walks
- rowing canal parks if staying longer

### Bansko
- Gondola rides in summer
- light trail walks
- spa hotel rhythm for mixed-age families

## Food Wins

- banitsa
- grilled meats
- fries and salads
- simple grilled fish on the coast

## Common Traps

- booking party resorts instead of family beaches
- overbuilding hot-weather sightseeing days
- attempting serious mountain routes with very young kids
