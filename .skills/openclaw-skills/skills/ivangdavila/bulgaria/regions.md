# Regions - Bulgaria

## Regional Differences That Matter

### Sofia Region
- Best transport hub and easiest first base
- Strongest city infrastructure
- Best launch point for Rila and Vitosha

### Plovdiv and the Thracian Plain
- Best balance of culture, wine, and slower pace
- Warmer feel and easier second-city recommendation
- Strong for food-and-wine travelers

### Black Sea North
- Varna, Balchik, Albena
- Better for mixed city-and-sea trips
- Easier for families and calmer resort profiles

### Black Sea South
- Burgas, Sozopol, Pomorie, Sinemorets
- Better for prettier town beaches and longer coast routes
- Sunny Beach belongs only to party-oriented users

### Rila and Pirin
- Best for hiking, lakes, monasteries, and ski bases
- Weather and road conditions matter more here
- Bansko is the main practical base

### Rhodopes
- Slower, more atmospheric, village-and-nature Bulgaria
- Better for road trips than for fast public transport plans

### Veliko Tarnovo and North-Central Bulgaria
- Good for history-focused detours
- Strong as a stop on a longer route, weaker as a first priority on a short trip

## Matching Rules

| User wants | Send them toward |
|------------|------------------|
| First trip | Sofia + Plovdiv |
| Sea but not chaos | Varna, Sozopol, Sinemorets, Albena |
| Ski or alpine scenery | Bansko, Pirin, Rila |
| Wine and food | Plovdiv, Thracian Valley, Melnik |
| Quiet road trip | Rhodopes, south coast, smaller towns |
