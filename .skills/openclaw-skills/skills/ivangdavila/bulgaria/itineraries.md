# Itineraries - Bulgaria

## 4 Days - Sofia + Plovdiv

| Day | Plan |
|-----|------|
| 1 | Sofia center, cathedral, Roman remains, Shishman dinner |
| 2 | Boyana or Vitosha half day, evening train or bus to Plovdiv |
| 3 | Plovdiv Old Town, Ancient Theatre, Kapana |
| 4 | Slow morning, Roman Stadium, return to Sofia |

Best for: first-time visitors who want culture without long transfers.

## 6 Days - Sofia + Rila + Plovdiv

| Day | Plan |
|-----|------|
| 1-2 | Sofia base |
| 3 | Rila Monastery or Seven Rila Lakes day trip |
| 4-5 | Plovdiv |
| 6 | Return to Sofia and depart |

Best for: classic intro trip with city + mountain contrast.

## 7 Days - Sofia + Plovdiv + Varna

| Day | Plan |
|-----|------|
| 1-2 | Sofia |
| 3-4 | Plovdiv |
| 5-7 | Varna and northern Black Sea |

Best for: mixed city-and-coast trip, especially June or September.

## 8 Days - Bulgaria Coast Without Party Chaos

| Day | Plan |
|-----|------|
| 1-2 | Varna |
| 3 | Balchik or Albena |
| 4 | Drive or transfer south |
| 5-6 | Sozopol |
| 7 | Sinemorets or Strandzha edge |
| 8 | Burgas departure or return |

Best for: people who want sea, towns, and calmer beaches instead of resort overload.

## 7 Days - Winter Bulgaria

| Day | Plan |
|-----|------|
| 1 | Sofia arrival |
| 2-5 | Bansko skiing or winter spa rhythm |
| 6 | Return to Sofia |
| 7 | Sofia departure |

Best for: value ski trip with easy city bookends.

## Routing Rules

- Bulgaria is better with fewer bases and smarter day trips.
- Use buses or private transfers where rail is slow.
- Coast + mountains + multiple cities in one week is too much for most people.
