# Bansko

## What Bansko Does Well

Bansko works in two different modes:
- winter ski base
- summer hiking base for Pirin

It is not only for package ski weekends.

## Areas

| Area | Best for | Reality |
|------|----------|---------|
| Gondola area | Ski convenience | Most practical in winter, more modern |
| Old Town | Character, taverns | Better atmosphere, less lift convenience |
| Outskirts / spa hotels | Quiet stays | Good with car, less walkable |

## Winter Use

- Best if the user wants value skiing, not Alpine polish
- Book near the gondola if first lifts matter
- Warn about gondola queues in peak holiday periods
- Spa hotel add-ons can make more sense than pure ski-in fantasy

## Summer Use

- Strong base for Vihren hut area, lake walks, and Pirin scenery
- Better for active travelers than for people wanting only a cute town
- Pair with Banya or Dobrinishte if they want hot springs or slower rhythm

## Food and Evenings

| Type | Best for | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Mehana taverns | Traditional dinners | Good after hiking or skiing |
| Modern ski-town spots | Easy group meals | Mixed quality |
| Spa hotel dining | Convenience | Fine when they prioritize recovery |

## Good Add-ons

| Place | Why add it |
|-------|-------------|
| Banya | Hot springs and spa time |
| Dobrinishte | Softer, less polished alternative |
| Pirin hikes | Best summer reason to stay |

## Common Traps

- Booking far from the gondola on a short winter trip
- Going only for nightlife when Sofia is stronger
- Underestimating peak-season queues
- Treating summer Bansko like a pure sightseeing town instead of a trail base
