# Apps - Bulgaria

## Core Set

| App | Use |
|-----|-----|
| Google Maps | General navigation and reviews |
| Moovit | Public transport backup in major cities |
| Booking | Hotels and guesthouses |
| Google Translate | Menus, signs, and simple phrase support |

## Sofia

| App | Use | Notes |
|-----|-----|-------|
| Sofia Traffic | Public transport planning | Good for metro, tram, bus routing |
| TaxiMe or Yellow app | Ordered taxis | Better than hailing random cabs |

## Intercity

| App / site habit | Use |
|------------------|-----|
| BDZ tools | Train times and tickets |
| Bus company sites or station desks | Long-distance buses |

## Coast and Mountains

| Tool | Use |
|------|-----|
| Weather app | Mountain weather changes fast |
| Offline maps | Useful for Pirin, Rila, Rhodopes, and remote beaches |

## Practical Rules

- In Bulgaria, transport info is sometimes clearer on the operator site than in aggregator apps.
- For taxis in Sofia, app ordering reduces pricing friction.
- For mountain days, offline maps matter more than restaurant apps.
