# Experiences - Bulgaria

## Standout Experiences

| Experience | Best base | Why it is worth it |
|-----------|-----------|--------------------|
| Rila Monastery day trip | Sofia | The classic cultural landmark |
| Seven Rila Lakes hike | Sofia | Best summer mountain day for many travelers |
| Ancient Theatre and Kapana evening | Plovdiv | Best culture-to-night transition |
| Sea Garden sunset walk | Varna | Easy city-coast experience |
| Pirin hiking day | Bansko | Strong summer use of the resort base |
| Melnik wine stop | Southwest trip | Best for slower food-and-wine travelers |

## Seasonal Winners

### Spring
- Plovdiv and Sofia city breaks
- Rhodope and lower mountain drives
- Rose Valley if timed carefully

### Summer
- Black Sea coast outside the loudest strips
- Rila and Pirin hiking
- Mixed city + beach routes

### Winter
- Bansko ski trips
- Sofia city breaks with spa or mountain add-ons

## Strong Day Trip Logic

| From | Day trip |
|------|----------|
| Sofia | Rila Monastery, Seven Rila Lakes, Boyana + Vitosha |
| Plovdiv | Bachkovo Monastery, winery visits, Rhodope edge |
| Varna | Balchik, Albena, quieter northern beaches |
| Bansko | Banya, Dobrinishte, Pirin trailheads |

## Weak Experiences to Filter Out

- Generic folklore dinner packages unless the user explicitly wants that
- Full resort-strip days when they want culture or local food
- Oversold "authentic village" stops with no real reason to be there
