# Hiking - Bulgaria

## Best Regions

| Region | Best for | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| Vitosha | Easy Sofia add-on | Best quick mountain access |
| Rila | Big mountain days | Seven Lakes is the headline route |
| Pirin | Stronger alpine feel | Best from Bansko |
| Rhodopes | Gentler hiking + villages | Better for slower road trips |

## Good Hiking Matches

### First-timers
- Vitosha from Sofia
- easier Rila options in summer

### Regular hikers
- Seven Rila Lakes in good weather
- Pirin trail days from Bansko

### Scenic but not intense
- Rhodope viewpoints and village routes

## Planning Rules

- Mountain weather changes quickly even in summer.
- Start early, especially on famous routes.
- Lift access can help, but check if it is operating before building the day around it.
- In shoulder months, ask whether the user wants a serious hike or only mountain scenery.

## Common Traps

- Treating the Seven Lakes as a casual city walk
- Going without layers, water, or offline navigation
- Assuming all lifts run every day outside high season
- Mixing a hard hike with long intercity transfers on the same day
