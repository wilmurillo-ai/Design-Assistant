# Nightlife — Italy

Italy's nightlife is more about aperitivo culture and late dinners than clubbing. But options exist everywhere.

## Aperitivo Culture (18:00-21:00)

### The Ritual
Order a drink (€8-15), get FREE food. Can range from nuts and chips to full buffet.

## Club Basics

### Getting In
- **Dress code:** Smart casual minimum (no shorts, no sneakers usually)
- **Age:** 18+ officially, often 21+ in practice
- **Entry:** €10-30 with drink, €20-50 big nights
- **Time:** Italians arrive 00:00-01:00, clubs peak 02:00-04:00

### What's Open When
| Night | Crowds | Notes |
|-------|--------|-------|
| Mon-Wed | Dead | Aperitivo only |
| Thursday | Starting | Student nights |
| Friday | Good | Building momentum |
| Saturday | Peak | Main night out |
| Sunday | Variable | Recovery aperitivo |

## Safety

- Italian cities are generally safe at night
- **Watch for:** Pickpockets near clubs, drink spiking (rare but exists)
- **Taxis:** Official white taxis only, agree price first for unlicensed
- **Apps:** FreeNow works in major cities
- **Night buses:** Exist in Rome (N lines), Milan (night trams)
