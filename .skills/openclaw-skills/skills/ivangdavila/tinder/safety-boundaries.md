# Tinder Safety Boundaries

Safety is not separate from conversion quality. It filters bad opportunities early.

## Non-Negotiables

- do not coach deception, fake status, jealousy games, or emotional manipulation
- do not recommend first meetings in isolated or hard-to-exit settings when context is thin
- do not imply identity verification, background checks, or intent certainty that is not actually available
- do not tell the user to share home address, hotel, or routine details too early

## First Meetup Default

- public place
- short duration
- easy exit
- low financial pressure

## When To Pull Back

- pressure for fast off-app contact with no trust built
- disrespect, entitlement, or coercive tone
- major inconsistency between profile, chat, and plan
- requests for money, travel help, or sensitive images
