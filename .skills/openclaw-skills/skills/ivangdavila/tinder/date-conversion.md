# Tinder Date Conversion

Turn a good chat into a plan with low friction and low ambiguity.

## Preconditions

- enough reciprocity to believe the plan will not feel random
- at least one topic or vibe lane that can become a date idea
- no obvious safety concern or hidden mismatch

## Good Date Proposal Pattern

- reference the thread naturally
- offer one simple public idea
- suggest a narrow but flexible time window
- make the ask easy to answer yes, no, or alternative

## Examples of Good Framing

- coffee, wine, walk, market, or one-stop activity
- weekday evening or weekend window instead of endless scheduling
- "if not, another day works too" instead of over-explaining

## When Not To Push

- they ignored two different attempts to make a plan
- the chat still feels interview-like
- logistics are unclear and the user is forcing momentum to rescue ego
- the proposal would require unusual cost, travel, or privacy risk
