# NYC Food Classics

## Pizza

### Top Picks by Style

| Spot | Style | Price | Location | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|----------|-------|
| **Joe's Pizza** | NY Slice | $4.50-6.50 | Multiple (orig: West Village) | THE classic. Open til 3-4am. |
| **Scarr's Pizza** | NY Slice | $5-7 | LES | Grinds own flour. Perfect slice, long lines. |
| **L'Industrie** | Upscale Slice | $4-7.50 | Williamsburg, West Village | Quality toppings. Burrata slice. |
| **Lucali** | Whole Pie | $25-32 | Carroll Gardens | Cash only. No slices. Worth hype. |
| **Una Pizza Napoletana** | Neapolitan | $$$$ | LES | Thu-Sat only. Hard to get. Purist perfection. |
| **John's of Bleecker** | Coal-Fired | $$$ | West Village | Since 1929. Pies only. |
| **Prince Street Pizza** | Square | $5-8 | Nolita | Pepperoni square iconic. Long lines. |

**Budget hack:** Dollar slice shops (2 Bros, 99 Cents Fresh) are $1.50-2 now. Spend $4-5 for quality.

## Pastrami & Deli

| Spot | Price | Location | Notes |
|------|-------|----------|-------|
| **Katz's Delicatessen** | $29+ | LES | Since 1888. THE icon. Tip carver $2-3 BEFORE slicing. |
| **2nd Ave Deli** | $28.95 | Midtown East, UES | Better rye than Katz's. Thin-sliced perfection. |
| **Pastrami Queen** | $27 | UES, UWS, Moynihan | Bourdain favorite. Slightly smoky. |

**Katz's ordering:**
1. Take ticket at door (DON'T lose it = $50 charge)
2. Go to counter, tip carver $2-3 BEFORE he slices
3. Get pastrami on rye, full sour pickles
