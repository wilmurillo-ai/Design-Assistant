## Central Park Highlights (All Free)

1. **Belvedere Castle** — Panoramic views
2. **Bethesda Terrace & Fountain** — Iconic movie location
3. **The Mall & Literary Walk** — Cathedral of American elms
4. **Strawberry Fields** — John Lennon memorial
5. **Bow Bridge** — Most photographed spot
6. **Conservatory Garden** — 3 formal gardens

**Best times:** Weekday mornings. Avoid weekend afternoons.

## The High Line

- **Cost:** FREE
- **Hours:** 7am-10pm (Apr-Nov), 7am-8pm (Dec-Mar)
- **Length:** 1.45 miles, 25-40 min walk
- **Best entrance:** Gansevoort St (south) or 30th St (Hudson Yards)
- **Tip:** Weekday evenings least crowded. Walk south to north for sunset.

## Brooklyn Bridge Walk

- **Cost:** FREE
- **Direction:** Walk Brooklyn → Manhattan for skyline views ahead
- **Time:** 25-40 minutes
- **Best time:** Sunrise or sunset. Early morning = fewer crowds
- **Combine with:** DUMBO, Brooklyn Heights Promenade

## Staten Island Ferry

**THE BEST FREE ATTRACTION IN NYC**

- 100% FREE (both directions)
- 25 minutes each way, runs 24/7
- Views: Statue of Liberty, Ellis Island, Manhattan skyline
- **Best views:** Right side (starboard) going TO Staten Island
- Much better than $30+ harbor cruises

## Tourist Traps to SKIP

### Times Square
- Visit 15 min max, see lights, leave
- AVOID: Chain restaurants (Olive Garden, Applebee's), costumed characters (aggressive tips), CD hustlers, souvenir shops

### Madame Tussauds
- $36-45+, wax figures often look off
- **Better:** Museum of Broadway ($39)

### Statue of Liberty Reality
- **Ferry ticket:** $24.50 (reserve via Statue City Cruises)
- **Crown access:** Books 3-6 MONTHS ahead
- **Better option:** FREE Staten Island Ferry for views

### Little Italy
- Now just 2 blocks, overpriced tourist traps
- **Exceptions:** Ferrara Bakery (cannoli), Di Palo's (cheese)
- **Better Italian:** Arthur Avenue in the Bronx

## Hidden Gems Locals Love

- **The Cloisters** — Medieval art museum, upper Manhattan, far from crowds
- **Governors Island** — FREE ferry weekends before noon (May-Oct)
- **Roosevelt Island Tram** — FREE with MetroCard, amazing views
- **DUMBO** — Washington Street photo spot, less crowded than bridge
