# Cotai

Cotai is a resort strip, not a normal neighborhood.
It works brilliantly for comfort, scale, nightlife, shows, and integrated entertainment.
It works poorly if the user thinks it is the full story of Macau.

## Best For

- Resort weekends
- Conferences and events
- High-comfort short stays
- Travelers who want nightlife, pools, shopping, and shows in one zone

## Strengths

- Best room product in Macau
- Strong indoor weather resilience
- Easy shuttle logic if staying in a resort
- Convenient for users who want a polished, low-friction trip

## Weaknesses

- Detached from everyday local life
- Long internal walking distances
- Expensive food if the user never leaves resorts
- Can feel interchangeable with other global resort corridors

## Use It Correctly

- Sleep here if comfort matters.
- Leave it at least once if the user wants actual Macau.
- Do not market it as "walkable city life"; it is walkable only inside the resort ecosystem.
