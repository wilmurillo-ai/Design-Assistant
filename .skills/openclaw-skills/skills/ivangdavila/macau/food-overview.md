# Macau Food Overview

Macau's food strength is not only "Portuguese tarts."
The city works because Cantonese everyday food, Macanese fusion, casino-hotel dining, and snack culture all sit close together.

## What Makes Macau Worth Eating In

- Macanese cuisine with Lusophone influence
- Strong Cantonese seafood and banquet tradition
- Good old-school cafes and bakeries
- High-end hotel dining far above what the city size suggests

## Best Strategy

- One local breakfast or cha chaan teng style stop
- One Macanese meal
- One Taipa Village or old-town snack session
- Optional one resort dinner if budget allows

## What Tourists Get Wrong

- Overpaying for average food inside resorts only
- Eating one egg tart and claiming they "did Macau cuisine"
- Ignoring Taipa and local streets for dinner
