# Macau International and Hotel Dining

Macau over-indexes on hotel and resort dining because the integrated resorts carry huge food infrastructure.

## Where It Shines

- Cantonese fine dining
- Portuguese fine dining
- Steakhouses and celebratory dinners
- Buffet and brunch formats

## Good Reasons to Use Resort Dining

- Weather-proof plans
- Business meals
- Anniversary or premium weekend plans
- Large-group convenience

## Trade-Offs

- Resort dining can be excellent, but it can also be expensive and generic.
- If the user wants Macau flavor, do not let the whole trip stay indoors.

## Best Use Pattern

- One premium resort meal
- One Taipa Village or peninsula local meal
- One casual snack crawl

That structure gives range without wasting the city.
