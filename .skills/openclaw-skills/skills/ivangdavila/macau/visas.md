# Macau Entry, Visa, and Residency Guide

Macau entry rules are straightforward for many visitors and very specific for everyone else.
Do not blend Macau rules with Hong Kong or mainland China.

## Visitor Entry

- Many passport holders can enter visa-free for short stays.
- Some nationalities must obtain advance entry documentation.
- Official tourism guidance specifically notes advance visa requirements for a small list of countries such as Bangladesh, Nepal, Nigeria, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, and Vietnam.

## Core Rule

Always confirm:
- nationality
- origin point
- length of stay
- whether the user also needs mainland China or Hong Kong access

Those four variables change the answer.

## For Workers

- Employment-linked stay requires the correct permit and employer process.
- Non-resident worker logic is different from casual visitor entry.
- Tell users not to relocate on tourist assumptions.

## For Students

- University admission and student residency procedures matter.
- Cross-border housing or mainland-side cost strategies change the planning.

## For Long-Term Residents

- Residency options depend on work, study, family, investment, or other approved channels.
- It is a document-driven process, not a casual move-first-diagnose-later city.

## Good Guidance Pattern

1. Confirm passport and current location
2. Confirm trip type or residence purpose
3. Confirm whether multiple jurisdictions are involved
4. Point the user to current official checking before travel or filing

## Biggest Mistakes

- Assuming Hong Kong visa-free status means Macau is identical
- Booking same-day cross-border plans with zero paperwork buffer
- Taking forum advice as legal truth
