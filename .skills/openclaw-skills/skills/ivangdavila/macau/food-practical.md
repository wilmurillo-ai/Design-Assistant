# Macau Food - Practical Rules

## Tipping

- Service charges are common in hotels and upscale venues.
- Casual local places often do not work on heavy tipping logic.
- Do not apply US-style assumptions blindly.

## Reservations

- Fine dining and popular weekend resort restaurants should be booked ahead.
- Local casual places often work best by showing up a little early.

## Timing

- Some local restaurants close between lunch and dinner.
- Late-night food exists, but not every district delivers equally.
- Taipa and resort zones are easier for evening continuity.

## Dietary Notes

- Resorts handle vegetarian, vegan, and allergy requests better.
- Small local kitchens may need simpler, clearer requests.

## Best Practical Advice

- Eat one main local lunch before the heaviest afternoon crowds.
- Use dinner for Taipa or a resort meal.
- Carry cash for smaller snack stops.
