# Macau Districts - Quick Comparison

Macau is compact, but the lived experience changes fast by district.

## Fast Comparison

| Area | Best For | Cost Level | Walkability | Character |
|------|----------|------------|-------------|-----------|
| Historic peninsula | First-time local Macau, heritage, snacks | Mid | Good in pockets | Dense and layered |
| NAPE / Outer Harbour | Ferry access, business, practical stays | Mid | Moderate | Functional urban |
| Taipa | Balanced base, food, families, easier pace | Mid to high | Good | Livable and mixed |
| Cotai | Resorts, luxury, events, shows | High | Internal walkability only | Spectacle |
| Coloane | Quiet, village feel, greenery | Mid to high | Good locally | Slow and low-rise |

## Best by Persona

| Persona | Area |
|--------|------|
| One-day visitor | Historic peninsula |
| Weekend couple | Taipa or Cotai |
| Family with kids | Taipa or Coloane |
| Budget-conscious short stay | Peninsula |
| Luxury and nightlife | Cotai |
| Slow traveler | Taipa plus Coloane |

## Core Decision Rule

If the user cares about Macau itself, steer toward peninsula or Taipa.
If they care about resort comfort, steer toward Cotai.
If they need quiet, steer toward Coloane.
