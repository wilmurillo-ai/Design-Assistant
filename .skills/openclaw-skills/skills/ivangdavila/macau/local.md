# Macau Local Services

## Payments

- MOP is official, HKD commonly accepted in many tourist-facing places.
- Card acceptance is strongest in resorts, hotels, chain stores, and better restaurants.
- Cash still smooths many old-town transactions.

## SIM and Data

- Airport, convenience-store, and telecom options are straightforward for visitors.
- eSIM can be easiest for short stays.
- Cross-border users should verify roaming behavior before assuming one setup covers all movement.

## Banking

- For residents and workers, bank setup may depend on status, employer, and address proof.
- Cross-border salary and card behavior can matter if income touches Hong Kong or mainland systems.

## Electricity and Daily Basics

- Daily life is easy once housing is solved.
- The real service friction is less about utilities and more about documentation, landlords, and border-linked routines.

## Best Advice

- Visitors: solve cash, data, and port transport first.
- Residents: solve status, housing, bank, and phone before optimizing anything else.
