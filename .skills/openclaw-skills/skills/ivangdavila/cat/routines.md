# Cat Routines

## Daily

- Food: meal times, amount, appetite quality, treats, and any skipped meals
- Water: bowl or fountain status and any unusual change in drinking
- Litter: scoop, urine and stool check, accidents, and odor changes
- Play: one or more short play sessions matched to the cat's age and energy
- Comfort: resting spots, hiding, grooming, and interaction tolerance

## Weekly

- Weigh if the cat has an active health or diet concern
- Trim nails if the cat tolerates it
- Brush coat based on coat type and shedding
- Refresh enrichment: wand toys, puzzle feeders, scratching surfaces, window time
- Review supplies before food or litter runs low

## Monthly

- Deep clean litter boxes and mats
- Review body condition and current food plan
- Check carrier readiness and emergency transport basics
- Review meds, refills, and appointment dates
- Update sitter notes if travel or absences are likely

## Multi-Cat Reminders

- Keep enough litter boxes and watch whether one cat is guarding access
- Separate feeding if one cat steals food or eats slower
- Track which cat used which medication, food, or treatment
- Watch social changes after moves, guests, illness, or vet visits

## Useful Outputs

Generate on request:
- a simple daily checklist
- a weekly summary of appetite, litter, and behavior
- a month view of meds, appointments, and supply restock dates
