# Cycle Trackable Data

Reference only — offer what's relevant to her, not everything.

## Physical
- **Pain:** cramps, headaches, migraines, back pain, breast tenderness
- **Body:** bloating, fatigue, appetite changes, cravings, weight changes
- **Skin:** acne, oily/dry skin
- **Hair:** thinning, excess growth (hirsutism)
- **Vasomotor:** hot flashes, night sweats, chills
- **Sleep:** insomnia, disrupted sleep, fatigue

## Bleeding
- **Flow:** spotting, light, medium, heavy
- **Duration:** start date, end date
- **Notes:** spotting between periods, unusual color

## Emotional
- **Mood:** mood swings, irritability, anxiety, calm
- **Energy:** low, medium, high
- **Mental:** brain fog, motivation, stress

## Fertility (optional, only if she wants)
- **BBT:** basal body temperature
- **LH tests:** ovulation predictor results
- **Cervical mucus:** consistency

## Conversational Signals
Only if she shares explicitly:
- "Period started" → Day 1
- "Cramps today" → pain symptom
- "Exhausted" → fatigue
- "Hot flash again" → vasomotor

## Important Notes
- No fixed phase table — cycles vary hugely
- 60+ day cycles are normal for PCOS/perimenopause
- Irregular ≠ broken
- Her patterns > textbook patterns
- Fertility tracking is OPT-IN only
