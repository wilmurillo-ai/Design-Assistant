# Useful Apps for France Travel

## Transport and Navigation

- **SNCF Connect** — rail schedules and tickets
- **RATP and local transit apps** — city routing and disruptions
- **Google Maps** — baseline walking and transit planning

## Booking and Dining

- **TheFork / OpenTable / direct booking links** — useful for restaurant reservations
- **Airline and rail operator apps** — faster disruption notices than aggregators

## Weather and Outdoors

- **Meteo-France style weather apps** — useful for coast and mountain planning
- **Offline maps** — essential backup for hikes and village routes

## Practical

- **Wise / Revolut-style apps** — payment and exchange tracking
- **Translation apps** — useful for smaller-town interactions

## Workflow Tip

Use apps for discovery first, then confirm directly with operators before paying for higher-value bookings.
