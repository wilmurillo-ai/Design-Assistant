# Signature Experiences in France

## Urban Experiences

- Paris museum plus neighborhood-food split day
- Lyon market and bouchon route
- Marseille harbor and culture sequence

## Coastal and Nature Experiences

- Riviera town-hopping with rail-first strategy
- Provence village and market routes
- Alps or lake-region day plans with weather flexibility

## Seasonal Experiences

| Season | Good choices |
|--------|--------------|
| Spring | Great city and village balance with lower pressure |
| Summer | Best daylight and coast access, highest demand |
| Autumn | Strong food and wine windows with better value |
| Winter | City culture and festive markets in selected regions |

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Treating weather as stable across all regions.
2. Running one fixed plan with no alternatives.
3. Overfilling days with transfers plus late nights.
