# France Wine Guide

## Main Wine Regions

| Region | What to Expect |
|--------|----------------|
| Bordeaux | Large-appellation ecosystem and strong winery tourism |
| Burgundy | Village-driven nuance, high demand, premium pricing |
| Champagne | Sparkling-focused houses and cellar tours |
| Loire Valley | Broad styles, castle routes, strong value opportunities |
| Rhone Valley | Northern and southern contrasts, food-pairing depth |
| Alsace | Aromatic whites and compact tasting routes |

## Planning Tips

- Reserve tastings early in peak periods.
- Keep 2-3 tastings max per day with meal buffer.
- Plan designated-driver strategy for rural routes.

## City Pairings

### Paris
Strong wine bar density and curated pairing programs.

### Lyon
Excellent food and wine pairing potential with nearby Rhone access.

### Bordeaux city base
Best launch point for winery-day routing.

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Overpacking winery visits in one day.
2. Assuming all houses accept walk-ins.
3. Driving after multi-stop tastings.
4. Ignoring transfer times between appellation clusters.
