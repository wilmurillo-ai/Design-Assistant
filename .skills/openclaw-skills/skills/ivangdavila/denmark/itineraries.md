# Itineraries

## 3 Days - Copenhagen Core

- Stay in one Copenhagen base for all nights.
- Build around one museum or design block, one food or market block, and one evening neighborhood.
- Good for first-timers, shoulder season, and users who want low-friction Denmark.
- Do not add Aarhus or Bornholm just to "see more country."

## 5 Days - Copenhagen Plus One Extension

- Use Copenhagen for 3 nights and add one focused extension: North Zealand, Roskilde, or south-Zealand coast.
- Best for users who want city depth plus one scenic or historical contrast.
- Works rail-first or with one targeted day rental.

## 7 Days - Capital Plus Funen

- Copenhagen 3 nights, Odense or south-Funen 2-3 nights, then return or onward departure.
- Strong for first-timers who want city, culture, and a softer island-and-small-city feel.
- Keep the route simple; do not also force Aarhus or Skagen into the week.

## 7 Days - Aarhus Plus North Jutland

- Aarhus 2-3 nights, then one north-Jutland base for coast, dunes, or Skagen.
- Best for users who prefer fewer big-city days and more open landscape.
- Usually works best with a car or a rail plus selective car-rental model.

## 7 Days - Bornholm Focus

- Use 1-2 bases only, usually one south or central base plus one north base if needed.
- Good for cycling, beaches, food, and family rhythm in late spring through early autumn.
- Weak fit if the user expects a dense city trip and headline attractions every hour.

## 10 Days - Copenhagen, Funen, and Aarhus

- Copenhagen 3 nights, Odense or Funen 2 nights, Aarhus 3 nights, with one flex night.
- Strong fit for users who want a coherent east-Denmark route without long backtracking.
- Works rail-first; add a car only for targeted nature or coast days.

## 12-14 Days - Denmark Deep Dive

- One Zealand base, one Funen or east-Jutland base, and one specialty region such as north Jutland, Wadden Sea, or Bornholm.
- This is the first shape that can justify both country breadth and real time on the ground.
- Pick one nature product, not all of them.

## Downgrade Rules

- If the user has under 5 days, choose one core base.
- If islands are the dream, cut city count first.
- If the user wants both Copenhagen and Bornholm, remove west-Jutland ambitions.
- If the month is cold or dark, shift from coast-counting to city, food, museum, and hygge-weighted plans.
