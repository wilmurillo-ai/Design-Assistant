# Setup - Denmark Travel Guide

Create `~/denmark/` on first use and initialize `~/denmark/memory.md` from `memory-template.md`.

## First Contact

Answer the immediate Denmark question first so the user gets value quickly.

In the first useful exchange, also learn how broadly this skill should activate in the future:
- Denmark-only trip planning
- Copenhagen city breaks
- Wider Denmark plus Sweden or Germany routing

Save only that activation preference to main memory, then keep the travel discussion moving.

## What To Capture

Capture only the facts that change the route:
- Dates or month range
- Copenhagen, islands, west coast, Aarhus, Bornholm, or mixed priorities
- Rail-first, car-first, ferry-first, or bike-friendly preference
- Budget band and pace
- Travelers, kids, and mobility needs
- Weather tolerance, coast comfort, and whether the user wants beaches, culture, food, cycling, or nature

## What To Save

Keep durable facts in memory:
- Travel window and date flexibility
- Main corridor under consideration
- Confirmed bases, flights, ferries, or car decisions
- Whether the user wants a city break, island trip, coast drive, or broader country route
- Mobility needs, traveler mix, and child setup
- Open decisions such as "Bornholm vs north Jutland" or "rail-first vs road-trip"

## Returning Users

Read `~/denmark/memory.md`, reuse what is still valid, and ask only what changed:
- Dates
- Region focus
- Existing bookings
- Car, rail, ferry, or bike decisions
- Pace, budget, or weather tolerance

## Quick Start Prompts

**"I want Copenhagen"**
- Ask nights, whether the trip should stay city-only or include day trips, and whether the user wants culture, food, family time, design, or nightlife.
- Then use `copenhagen.md`, `accommodation.md`, `transport-domestic.md`, and `payments-and-tax-free.md`.

**"I want a Denmark road trip"**
- Ask total days, month, whether bridges and ferries are acceptable, and whether the trip should favor coasts, islands, or cities.
- Then use `regions.md`, `road-trips-and-driving.md`, `weather-and-seasonality.md`, and the relevant regional playbooks.

**"I want beaches or islands"**
- Ask month, swimming expectations, wind tolerance, and whether the user wants Bornholm, Funen islands, or west-coast Denmark.
- Then use `cycling-coasts-and-islands.md`, `bornholm.md`, `southwest-jutland-and-wadden-sea.md`, and `weather-and-seasonality.md`.

## Important Notes

- Denmark quality comes from fewer bases with smarter local movement, not from ticking every famous place.
- Rail and city transit solve more of Denmark than many users expect.
- Islands, bridges, parking, and peak-summer accommodation create the hidden friction.
- When the user wants coasts or cycling, season and wind matter more than map distance.
