# Swedish Lapland - Playbook

## Best Use Case

Winter aurora and snow-activity trips, or summer midnight-sun and hiking plans, for users who accept remote logistics and strong seasonality.

## Base Selection

| Base type | Best for | Watch-out |
|-----------|----------|-----------|
| Kiruna area | Icehotel style trips, winter packages, flight access | Activity pricing and transfer timing |
| Abisko area | Aurora reputation and hiking access | Weather exposure and fewer fallback options |
| Lulea or coastal north | Easier mixed trip with urban comfort | Less "deep Arctic" feeling |

## Planning Rules

- Ask if the user wants aurora, dogsledding, Sami context, trains, or hiking before naming a base.
- Aurora is never guaranteed; build activities that still work under clouds.
- Winter trips need explicit cold tolerance and clothing logic.
- Summer north-Sweden trips trade aurora away for long daylight and easier hiking.

## Booking Reality

- Peak winter inventory can go early.
- Activity pickup location often matters more than hotel style.
- Sleeper trains are useful only when the user wants the experience and accepts timing risk.

## Trap to Flag

Do not let a user treat Lapland as a casual add-on to a south-Sweden city loop. It needs its own weather, budget, and transfer logic.
