# Gotland - Playbook

## Best Use Case

Summer island trip built around Visby, cycling, beaches, ruins, and a slower Baltic rhythm.

## Core Planning Logic

- Decide whether the trip is Visby-first, beach-first, or bike-and-rural-first.
- In peak summer, book lodging and transport before treating Gotland as a casual add-on.
- Gotland works best as its own short trip or a focused extension, not as a side quest in a packed route.

## Movement Rules

- Visby is the easiest first base.
- A car or bike can improve reach outside town depending on trip style.
- Ferry or flight timing should be checked before locking mainland connections.

## Trap to Flag

Gotland is seasonal. A plan that works beautifully in July can feel thin, quiet, or logistically different in shoulder months.
