# Entry and Documents - Sweden

## Fast Decision Tree

1. Confirm whether the traveler enters Sweden as an EU-EEA-Swiss citizen, a visa-free third-country visitor, or a Schengen visa holder.
2. Count Schengen days across the whole trip, not just Sweden.
3. Check passport validity before locking flights or ferries.
4. If the route crosses Denmark or another Schengen state first, make sure entry rules are still calculated for the full Schengen stay.

## Visitor Pathways

| Path | Typical use | Core limit |
|------|-------------|------------|
| EU-EEA-Swiss travel | Tourism with passport or national ID | Stay rights follow EU rules |
| Visa-free short stay | Tourism or short visits | Up to 90 days in any 180-day Schengen period |
| Schengen visa | Tourism when visa-free access does not apply | Length limited by visa decision |

## Passport and Stay Basics

- For many non-EU visitors, the passport should be issued within the last 10 years and remain valid for at least 3 months after planned Schengen departure.
- Sweden uses Schengen stay counting, so time in Denmark, France, Italy, or other Schengen states counts against the same 90-in-180 limit.
- Keep hotel bookings, onward plans, and sufficient-funds logic easy to show if border questions arise.

## EES and ETIAS Context

- The EU Entry-Exit System is in rollout and affects how short-stay non-EU travelers are recorded at the border.
- ETIAS is still not operational as of March 9, 2026; travelers cannot apply yet.
- If user guidance online mentions ETIAS, verify that it is future-state information rather than a current requirement.

## South Sweden and Cross-Border Reality

- Malmo and Skane plans often connect through Copenhagen Airport.
- Even on routine bridge or rail crossings, travelers should carry the same ID or passport they used for the trip.
- Do not assume a city-break style day trip means border checks are impossible.

## Checklist Before Non-Refundables

- Correct stay pathway confirmed
- Passport validity and Schengen day count checked
- EES or visa context understood when relevant
- Cross-border south-Sweden plans mapped with correct ID in hand
- Backup routing if a visa or documentation step slips
