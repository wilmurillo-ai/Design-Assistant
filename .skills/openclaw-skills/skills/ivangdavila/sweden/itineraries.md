# Itineraries - Sweden

## 5 Days: Stockholm Deep Dive

- Days 1-3: Stockholm by island clusters, museums, food, and one ferry segment
- Day 4: Archipelago day trip or Drottningholm-style add-on
- Day 5: Buffer and departure

## 7 Days: Two-Base South Sweden

### Option A: Stockholm + Gothenburg
- 3-4 days Stockholm
- Fast rail transfer
- 2-3 days Gothenburg with one west-coast excursion

### Option B: Stockholm + Dalarna
- 4 days Stockholm
- 2 days central Sweden for lakes, cabins, or folk-culture rhythm
- 1 flex day for transfer or weather

## 10 Days: Stockholm + Skane

- Base 1: Stockholm for 4 days
- Transfer day by rail or flight
- Base 2: Malmo-Lund-Skane for 4 days
- Keep one day flexible for Copenhagen Airport access or weather

## 10 Days: Gothenburg + Bohuslan + Stockholm

- 3 days Gothenburg
- 2-3 days west-coast islands or fishing towns
- 3-4 days Stockholm
- Use rail unless the user specifically wants a coast road trip

## 14 Days: South and Far North Split

- 5-6 days Stockholm, Gothenburg, or Skane corridor
- 1 transfer day
- 5-6 days Lapland for winter or midnight-sun logic
- Do not add Gotland or another island unless the trip is unusually long

## Itinerary Quality Checks

- Every transfer day has one backup option
- The final night stays near the actual departure airport
- Lapland plans include a cloud-weather or storm downgrade option
- Peak summer island plans include ticket or lodging checks before flights are fixed
