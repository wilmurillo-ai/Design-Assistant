# Gothenburg and West Coast - Playbook

## Best Use Case

Seafood-driven city break with a calmer rhythm than Stockholm plus strong access to Bohuslan villages, islands, and summer coast routes.

## Regional Split

| Subarea | Core strengths | Typical base |
|---------|----------------|--------------|
| Gothenburg city | Food, trams, walkable center, museums | Central station corridor or inner tram zone |
| Southern archipelago | Easy island texture without huge logistics | Gothenburg base |
| Bohuslan coast | Fishing villages, boating, summer scenery | One coast base or light road trip |

## Planning Rules

- Gothenburg works well without a car; Bohuslan often becomes easier with one.
- Seafood and summer demand create price spikes on weekends.
- One island or one fishing-town corridor is usually enough for a short trip.

## Good 5-Day Pattern

- 2-3 days Gothenburg
- 1-2 days island or coast excursion
- 1 flex day for weather, food, or return transfer

## Trap to Flag

The west coast looks compact, but ferry timing, bridge routing, and coastal detours make same-day overstuffing easy to regret.
