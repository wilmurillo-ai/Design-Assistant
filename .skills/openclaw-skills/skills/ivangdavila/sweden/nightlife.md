# Nightlife - Sweden

## City Pattern

| City type | Typical vibe | Watch-out |
|-----------|--------------|-----------|
| Stockholm | Broadest choice, from bars to clubs | Higher prices and queue pressure |
| Gothenburg | More relaxed and pub-friendly | Best on weekends |
| Malmo | Smaller scene but easy to pair with Copenhagen | Late-night transport matters |

## Alcohol and Going-Out Rules

- Budget for drinks early; bar spend escalates fast.
- ID checks are normal.
- A nice dinner reservation can outperform club-chasing for many Sweden trips.
- Public transport closing rhythm and taxi fallback matter more outside Stockholm.

## Seasonal Pattern

- Summer nightlife expands with terraces and waterfront energy.
- Winter can still be strong, but users should anchor around one neighborhood and warm transfer logic.
- Midsummer is culturally important, but many normal city routines change.

## Trap to Flag

Do not let the user assume stronger take-away alcohol can be bought casually late at night. Plan ahead if that changes the evening.
