# Malmo and Skane - Playbook

## Best Use Case

South-Sweden base for easy urban movement, lower friction than Stockholm, and natural pairing with Lund, beaches, castles, or Copenhagen-linked travel.

## Why It Wins

- Compact center and bike-friendly feel
- Fast regional access within Skane
- Excellent route fit when Copenhagen Airport is the actual arrival or departure point

## Movement Rules

- Keep passport or ID handy for any Denmark-linked segment.
- Use Malmo as the sleep base unless a rural Skane stay changes the trip style on purpose.
- If the user wants Copenhagen and Malmo together, build one integrated plan instead of two competing city breaks.

## Good Add-Ons

- Lund for an easy academic-town contrast
- Coastal or beach stretches in warm months
- Ystad or Osterlen style south-coast adds when the user accepts slower movement

## Tourist Trap to Flag

South Sweden can feel so easy that users forget border, airport, and final-night logic. That is where the trip breaks, not in the city center.
