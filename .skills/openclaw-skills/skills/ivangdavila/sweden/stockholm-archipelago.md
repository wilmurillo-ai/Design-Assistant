# Stockholm Archipelago - Playbook

## Best Use Case

Short summer or shoulder-season escape from Stockholm for ferry scenery, slower pace, and light nature without committing to a full island trip elsewhere in Sweden.

## Planning Logic

- Decide early between day trip, one-night island stay, or multi-island sampling.
- For first-time users, one island done well beats trying to "see the archipelago."
- Weather and ferry frequency matter more than raw distance.

## Good Fit by Style

| Style | Best approach |
|-------|---------------|
| Relaxed first-timer | One ferry day trip from Stockholm |
| Outdoor-focused | Overnight with walking and lower urban density |
| Family | Easy island with food and return flexibility |
| Premium summer | Overnight island hotel with early booking |

## Common Mistakes

- Leaving island selection until after lodging is sold out
- Chaining the last ferry with a fixed airport move
- Treating shoulder-season service like peak summer service

## Practical Rule

Archipelago planning works best as a Stockholm extension, not as an extra checkbox layered onto an already dense Sweden route.
