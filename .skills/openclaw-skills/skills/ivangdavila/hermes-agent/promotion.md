# Promotion Rules

Hermes mode should not create new skills impulsively.

## Promote to a Dedicated Skill When

- the workflow appears across multiple projects
- the workflow has clear inputs, outputs, and traps
- the behavior would help future sessions without extra explanation
- the rule is too large or specialized for memory.md

## Keep as a Workspace Rule When

- the lesson mainly sharpens how the current agent behaves
- the pattern is still evolving
- the workflow depends heavily on the user's environment
- the instruction fits in 1-3 concise bullets

## Discard When

- the lesson was a one-off patch
- the signal is too weak or too subjective
- the rule conflicts with newer evidence

## Promotion Record

When something graduates, record:
- pattern summary
- evidence count
- scope
- destination: memory.md or a new skill
- what changed in OpenClaw behavior
