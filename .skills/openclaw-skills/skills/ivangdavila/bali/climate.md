# Bali Climate and Season Guide 2026

> Last updated: March 2026

## Climate Pattern

Bali has a tropical climate with two practical seasons:
- Dry season: generally April to October.
- Wet season: generally November to March.

Temperature stays warm year-round; rainfall is the main operational variable.

## Operational Impact by Season

### Dry Season (Apr-Oct)
- Easier inter-area mobility and day trips.
- Higher accommodation demand and pricing.
- Better reliability for outdoor activities and boats.

### Wet Season (Nov-Mar)
- Heavy rain bursts and localized flooding.
- Higher mold/humidity pressure in housing.
- More traffic disruption and occasional schedule changes.

## Best Months by Objective

| Objective | Recommended Window |
|-----------|--------------------|
| First Bali trip | May, June, September |
| Surf + social season | June to September |
| Budget stays | January to March (with rain flexibility) |
| Family smoother logistics | May to October |

## Heat and Health Rules

- Hydrate continuously in midday heat.
- Use high-SPF sun protection year-round.
- Avoid peak physical exertion at midday in humid periods.
