# Moving to Bali: Resident Setup Guide

> Last updated: March 2026

## 30-Day Relocation Sequence

### Week 1: Landing and Baseline
- Temporary accommodation in target zone.
- Validate internet, transport, and food routines.
- Confirm visa timeline and extension windows.

### Week 2: Area Validation
- Test commutes at real hours.
- Shortlist long-stay housing options.
- Compare costs using real spending behavior.

### Week 3: Commitments
- Sign lease only after full inspection.
- Finalize mobility model (scooter, app, driver).
- Secure healthcare/insurance baseline.

### Week 4: Stabilization
- Optimize routines, backup plans, and monthly budget.
- Set compliance reminders for visa and payments.

## New Resident Priorities

| Priority | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Visa compliance system | Avoid legal and financial penalties |
| Housing quality checks | Prevent mold, noise, and maintenance issues |
| Mobility and safety plan | Reduce biggest daily risk exposure |
| Healthcare access | Fast response when needed |
| Budget discipline | Bali inflation by lifestyle is real |

## 90-Day Success Markers

- Housing and transport are stable.
- Monthly spend within planned range.
- No visa compliance uncertainty.
- Reliable social and professional routines established.
