# Bali International and Premium Dining

> Last updated: March 2026

## International Strength Areas

| Area | Strength |
|------|----------|
| Canggu/Berawa | Trendy global cafes, brunch, fitness-food crossover |
| Seminyak | Premium Asian fusion and fine dining |
| Ubud | Plant-based, conscious cuisine, destination dining |
| Uluwatu | Scenic dining with sunset focus |

## Price Positioning

| Segment | Typical Ticket |
|---------|----------------|
| International casual | IDR 120k-300k per person |
| Mid-premium | IDR 300k-700k per person |
| Premium tasting/signature | IDR 800k-2.5M+ per person |

## Booking and Timing

- Sunset tables in cliff/surf zones should be booked early.
- Popular brunch formats can require weekend waiting windows.
- Match reservations with realistic transfer times.

## Who Gets Best Value

- Users staying in-area rather than cross-island dining runs.
- Users prioritizing one premium meal and local meals otherwise.
