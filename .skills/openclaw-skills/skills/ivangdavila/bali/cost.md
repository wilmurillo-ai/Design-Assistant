# Bali Cost of Living Guide 2026

> Last updated: March 2026 | Currency: IDR

## Snapshot (Denpasar Benchmarks + Bali Tourist Areas)

Based on Numbeo and market observations, Bali cost depends heavily on area and lifestyle.

## Core Monthly Benchmarks (Numbeo Denpasar)

| Item | Typical Value |
|------|---------------|
| Single person (excl. rent) | ~IDR 11.2M |
| Family of 4 (excl. rent) | ~IDR 39.9M |
| 1BR city center | ~IDR 13.2M (range 7.2M-25M) |
| 1BR outside center | ~IDR 7.7M (range 5.0M-12.5M) |
| 3BR city center | ~IDR 35.8M |

| Profile | Budget Range | Notes |
|---------|--------------|-------|

## Hidden Costs to Budget

- Visa fees and extensions.
- Bali tourist levy (international visitors).
- Deposits for long stays.
- Wet-season maintenance and laundry spikes.
- Private insurance and healthcare out-of-pocket.

## Cost Traps

- Assuming Bali is always cheap because local food is cheap.
- Choosing high-rent area then commuting long distances daily.
- Ignoring school and healthcare costs for families.
- Under-budgeting visa and compliance costs for multi-month stays.
