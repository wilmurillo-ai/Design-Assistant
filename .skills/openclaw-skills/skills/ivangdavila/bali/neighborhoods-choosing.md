# How to Choose a Bali Base

> Last updated: March 2026

## 5-Question Framework

1. What is your main day structure? (work, surf, family, culture, social)
2. What is your monthly all-in budget?
3. How much traffic stress can you tolerate daily?
4. Do you need walkability or are you comfortable with scooter/car dependency?
5. Is this a trial stay or a long commitment?

## Profile-Based Picks

| Profile | First Choice | Backup |
|---------|--------------|--------|
| Digital nomad social | Canggu/Berawa | Pererenan/Seminyak edge |
| Couple premium short stay | Seminyak | Nusa Dua |
| Family with kids | Sanur | Jimbaran |
| Wellness and culture | Ubud | Nyuh Kuning area |
| Surf-first | Uluwatu/Bingin | Canggu |

## Budget Anchors (Monthly)

| Budget Level | What It Usually Supports |
|--------------|--------------------------|
| Under IDR 20M | Room/guesthouse + local routine |
| IDR 20M-40M | Solid 1BR + mixed local/international lifestyle |
| IDR 40M-80M | Comfortable villa lifestyle in many areas |
| IDR 80M+ | Premium villa and frequent premium services |

## Commit-After-Test Rule

For stays over 2 months:
- Book first 7-14 days flexible.
- Run commute tests during peak traffic.
- Validate internet reliability, water pressure, and noise at night.
