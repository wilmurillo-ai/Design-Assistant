# Bali Food Scene Overview

> Last updated: March 2026

## Food Landscape

Bali has four parallel dining worlds:
1. Local warung economy (best value, high flavor).
2. Mid-range international cafes and bistros.
3. Premium dining and chef-driven venues.
4. Wellness and dietary-specialized ecosystems.

## Typical Price Bands

| Experience | Typical Price |
|------------|---------------|
| Local warung meal | IDR 25k-70k |
| Casual cafe meal | IDR 70k-180k |
| Mid-range dinner | IDR 150k-350k per person |
| Premium dinner | IDR 500k-1.5M+ per person |

## Where the Density Is

- Canggu/Berawa: highest international and trend-driven density.
- Seminyak: established upscale restaurants and lounges.
- Ubud: wellness, plant-forward, and conscious dining.
- Sanur: calmer mixed local + mid-range scene.

## Recommendation Logic

- Budget users: prioritize trusted warungs and local chains.
- Health-driven users: Ubud and Canggu have the strongest options.
- Celebrations: Seminyak/Jimbaran/Uluwatu premium cluster.

## Operational Tips

- Book premium places 1-3 days ahead in high season.
- Keep hydration and food safety standards high in hot weather.
- Check traffic before locking dinner times across distant zones.
