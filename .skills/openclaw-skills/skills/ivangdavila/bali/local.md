# Bali Local Setup: Banking, SIM, Apps, and Daily Admin

> Last updated: March 2026

## Connectivity First

| Need | Practical Baseline |
|------|--------------------|
| Mobile internet | Local SIM/eSIM with strong data package |
| Home/work internet | Test speed at actual usage times |
| Backup for remote work | Second data provider or tethering fallback |

## Core Apps for Daily Life

- Maps/navigation app for routing.
- Ride-hailing and delivery apps.
- Messaging app widely used for local coordination.
- Banking/payment apps depending on account eligibility.

## Money Flow Options

| Mode | Best For |
|------|----------|
| International card + ATM | Short to medium stays |
| Local bank account | Long stays with legal eligibility |
| Multi-currency fintech | Reducing FX friction |

## Housing Admin Checklist

1. Written lease terms and deposit conditions.
2. Utility responsibility and payment method.
3. Internet SLA expectations (if remote work dependent).
4. Maintenance response process and contacts.

## Practical Daily Friction Reducers

- Keep small cash for parking/tolls/small shops.
- Maintain digital copies of key documents.
- Use rain-season transport buffers.
- Keep local emergency and landlord contacts pinned.
