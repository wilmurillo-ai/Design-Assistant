# Bali Healthcare and Insurance Guide 2026

> Last updated: March 2026

## Healthcare Pattern in Bali

Bali has a mix of private hospitals/clinics and public facilities. For many expats and long-stay visitors, private care and robust insurance are the practical default.

## Facility Strategy

| Need | Typical Choice |
|------|----------------|
| Routine care | Local private clinics/hospitals |
| Specialist care | Major private hospitals in Denpasar/Kuta area |
| High-complexity cases | Referral to larger Indonesian hubs when needed |

## Insurance Baseline

- Maintain travel/health insurance that explicitly covers motorbike incidents if riding.
- Confirm emergency evacuation terms.
- Verify deductible, network, and cashless treatment process.

## Typical Out-of-Pocket Direction

| Service | Typical Range |
|---------|---------------|
| GP/private consultation | IDR 250k-800k |
| Specialist consult | IDR 500k-1.5M |
| ER visit | IDR 1M-5M+ depending on tests |

## Health Risks to Plan For

- Traffic-related injury risk.
- GI and hydration issues.
- Mosquito-borne illness risk in some periods.
- Heat and humidity fatigue.

## Practical Readiness Checklist

1. Keep insurer hotline and policy number offline.
2. Know nearest capable private hospital from your base.
3. Maintain personal medication stock and prescriptions.
4. Keep emergency contacts accessible for driver/hotel/landlord.
