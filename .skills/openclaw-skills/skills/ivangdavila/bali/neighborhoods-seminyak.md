# Seminyak and Kerobokan Guide

> Last updated: March 2026

## Positioning

Seminyak is one of Bali's premium leisure zones: strong restaurants, beach clubs, shopping, and upscale hotels. Kerobokan gives nearby access with somewhat broader pricing.

## Best For

- Couples and premium holiday users.
- Short stays prioritizing dining and convenience.
- Users who want polished hospitality over local grit.

## Constraints

- Premium pricing for accommodation and dining.
- Traffic pressure around dining and beach-club peaks.
- Less practical for budget-first long-term stays.

## Typical Housing Ranges (Monthly)

| Unit Type | Typical Range |
|-----------|---------------|
| Guesthouse room | IDR 7M-14M |
| 1BR serviced apartment | IDR 12M-30M |
| Villa 1-2BR | IDR 20M-55M |

## Practical Advice

- Keep expectations realistic on beach quality versus resort photos.
- For long stays, compare with Canggu/Sanur for better value.
- Prioritize walkability if not planning to drive daily.
