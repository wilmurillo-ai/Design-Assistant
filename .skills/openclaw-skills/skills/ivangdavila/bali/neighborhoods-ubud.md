# Ubud Area Guide

> Last updated: March 2026

## Ubud Profile

Ubud is Bali's culture and wellness center with temple access, rice-field landscapes, yoga studios, and arts communities. It suits slower routines better than nightlife-centered lifestyles.

## Best For

- Wellness and recovery stays.
- Culture-focused travelers.
- Writers/creatives wanting quieter evenings.

## Challenges

- Narrow roads and local bottlenecks.
- Rain and humidity can affect mold-prone properties.
- Less direct beach access.

## Typical Housing Ranges (Monthly)

| Unit Type | Typical Range |
|-----------|---------------|
| Local room/guesthouse | IDR 5M-12M |
| 1BR apartment/studio | IDR 8M-20M |
| Villa with garden/pool | IDR 15M-40M |

## Sub-Area Direction

| Pocket | Typical Use |
|--------|-------------|
| Central Ubud | Walkability, cafes, tourism density |
| Penestanan | Quieter creative pocket |
| Nyuh Kuning | Family and mixed long-stay feel |
| Tegallalang edge | Nature-heavy, more transport dependence |

## Selection Rule

For stays above 1 month, inspect humidity, ventilation, and mold management before signing.
