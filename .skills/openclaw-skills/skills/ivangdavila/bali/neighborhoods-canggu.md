# Canggu and Berawa Neighborhood Guide

> Last updated: March 2026

## Who It Fits

- Remote workers needing dense cafe/coworking options.
- Solo travelers wanting quick social integration.
- Users okay with traffic and premium rents.

## Strengths

- Highest concentration of coworking and digital services.
- Strong health/fitness and international food options.
- Active surf and social event ecosystem.

## Trade-Offs

- Peak-hour road congestion is significant.
- Noise and construction can affect focus and sleep.
- Rents often priced above value in top pockets.

## Typical Housing Ranges (Monthly, Long-Stay)

| Unit Type | Typical Range |
|-----------|---------------|
| Room in guesthouse/coliving | IDR 6M-15M |
| 1BR apartment/studio | IDR 10M-25M |
| 1-2BR villa | IDR 18M-45M |

## Micro-Area Notes

| Pocket | Note |
|--------|------|
| Berawa | Balanced for cafes, coworking, schools, and beach access |
| Batu Bolong | Most social and congested corridor |
| Pererenan | Slightly calmer, still rising in price |
| Seseh (edge) | Quieter but less walkability |

## Daily-Life Advice

- Choose lodging within 10-20 min of your repeated destinations.
- Prioritize flood and drainage quality in wet season.
- Ask for real internet speed tests at peak hours.

## Who Should Avoid

- Users wanting silence and easy driving.
- Families needing large green public spaces and low traffic stress.
