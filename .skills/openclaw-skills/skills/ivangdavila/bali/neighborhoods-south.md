# South Bali: Sanur, Nusa Dua, Jimbaran, Uluwatu

> Last updated: March 2026

## Sanur
- Calm shoreline with sunrise promenade.
- Strong family and mature expat profile.
- Easier day-to-day mobility than Canggu in many periods.

## Nusa Dua
- Resort-oriented, curated, cleaner roads.
- Less organic local street life.
- High suitability for short premium vacations.

## Jimbaran
- Strategic airport-adjacent base.
- Good seafood and family villa pockets.
- Balanced option between logistics and comfort.

## Uluwatu and Bingin
- Cliff views, surf culture, sunset strengths.
- Lower walkability, more transport dependence.
- Popular for scenic short stays and creator lifestyles.

## Cost Direction (Relative)

| Zone | Relative Cost |
|------|---------------|
| Sanur | Mid |
| Jimbaran | Mid |
| Nusa Dua | High |

## Decision Shortcut

- Choose Sanur/Jimbaran for practical long stays and family routines.
- Choose Nusa Dua for resort convenience.
- Choose Uluwatu/Bingin for surf + views if mobility constraints are acceptable.
