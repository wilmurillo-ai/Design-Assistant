# Telecoms and Apps - Turkey

## Connectivity Logic

- Urban Turkey is easy to navigate with a phone-first trip stack.
- International roaming or eSIM is often the simplest short-trip option.
- Local SIM can make sense for longer trips, but setup effort is not always worth it for short stays.

## Useful Digital Stack

| Need | What to use |
|------|-------------|
| Offline navigation | Maps with offline download before long drives |
| Translation | Turkish offline phrase support for menus and drivers |
| Flight and airport handling | Official airline and airport apps or mobile sites |
| Rail and museum planning | Official TCDD and museum portals |
| Weather and alerts | Official weather and emergency pages |

## Practical Rules

- Download maps before entering mountain, coast, or eastern driving regions.
- Keep hotel addresses in both map and plain-text form for arrival-night transfers.
- Save passport, visa, tickets, and reservation proofs in offline-access form.
- Carry one backup charging option on flight-heavy or bus-heavy days.
