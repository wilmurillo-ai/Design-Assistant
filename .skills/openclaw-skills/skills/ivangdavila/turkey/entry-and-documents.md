# Entry and Documents - Turkey

## Fast Decision Tree

1. Confirm whether the traveler is visa-free, e-Visa eligible, or needs a consular visa.
2. Check passport validity and exact name matching before booking flights, trains, and balloon activities.
3. Verify arrival airport and whether the route uses one Istanbul airport or a different arrival/departure pair.
4. Keep hotel, onward, and insurance proof easy to surface if airline or border staff ask for supporting documents.

## Visitor Pathways

| Path | Typical use | Core limit |
|------|-------------|------------|
| Visa-free short stay | Many tourism passports | Stay length depends on nationality |
| e-Visa | Eligible nationalities through official portal | Conditions depend on passport and trip purpose |
| Consular visa | Non-eligible or more complex cases | Timing depends on consular processing |

## Practical Rules

- Use only official channels in `sources.md` for visa checks. Aggregator blogs drift fast.
- Some nationalities have extra conditions for e-Visa or are routed to consular processing instead.
- The visa outcome should be solved before locking non-refundable domestic flights.
- Keep passport access easy on arrival day and for airport check-ins on internal flights.

## Booking Name Logic

- Match the passport spelling exactly across international flights, domestic flights, and tours.
- If user has dual nationality or multiple passports, anchor the whole trip to one document set.
- If route includes Greece or another nearby country, re-check Schengen vs Turkey document logic separately.

## Before Locking Non-Refundables

- Entry pathway confirmed
- Passport validity checked
- Airport pair confirmed
- Name spelling aligned across all bookings
- First and last night logic decided
