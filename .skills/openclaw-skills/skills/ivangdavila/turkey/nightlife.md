# Nightlife - Turkey

## Nightlife Strength by Destination

| Destination type | Best fit | Limits |
|------------------|----------|--------|
| Istanbul | Broadest range from cocktail bars to clubs to Bosphorus scenes | Traffic and district choice matter |
| Bodrum and premium coast | Summer club and beach-nightlife culture | Cost and seasonality spike hard |
| Antalya and larger resort zones | Resort nightlife and mixed visitor crowd | Less distinctive local-city feel |
| Kadikoy / Izmir / student areas | Relaxed bar and live-music rhythm | More neighborhood-specific than iconic |

## Planning Rules

- Match nightlife advice to the user's tolerance for crowds, cost, and late transfers.
- Istanbul works best when nightlife is matched to the hotel district.
- Premium coast nightlife is strongest in summer and weakest as a year-round assumption.
- In quieter or more conservative areas, position nightlife as selective rather than central.

## Common Mistakes

- Booking far from the evening district and assuming rides back are frictionless.
- Treating every coast town like Bodrum.
- Ignoring dress code or reservation logic in premium venues.
- Recommending nightlife-heavy routes to users who mainly want dawn activities next day.
