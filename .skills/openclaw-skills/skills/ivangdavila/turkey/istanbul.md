# Istanbul - Turkey

## Best Fit

Best for first-time Turkey trips, food-heavy city breaks, Bosphorus days, shopping, and layered history.

## Base Selection

| Base | Strong for | Watch-outs |
|------|------------|------------|
| Sultanahmet / Sirkeci | Landmark-first first trips | Tourist density and evening energy drop |
| Karakoy / Galata / Beyoglu | Balanced sightseeing, food, nightlife | Hills and crowd density |
| Besiktas / Nisantasi | Repeat visitors, local-city feel, dining | Less old-city immediacy |
| Kadikoy | Asia-side cafe and nightlife rhythm | More ferry and cross-city logic for classic sightseeing |

## Stay Length

- 3 nights: efficient landmark sampler
- 4-5 nights: proper first-time city trip
- 6+ nights: works only if mixing districts, shopping, Bosphorus, and day-trip margin

## Planning Rules

- Confirm IST vs SAW first; it changes the whole arrival and departure day.
- Split old-city, Bosphorus, and modern-city days instead of crossing the city repeatedly.
- Use ferries as part of the experience when weather and timing fit.
- If Friday prayer timing matters for the user, avoid overpacking mosque-heavy midday plans.

## Common Mistakes

- Staying in a postcard zone with hard luggage access and then taking constant taxis.
- Trying to see both sides plus every major museum in one rushed weekend.
- Underestimating line time for icon sites.
- Adding a rental car to an Istanbul-based itinerary.
