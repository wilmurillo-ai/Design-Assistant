# Food Guide - Turkey

## Food Planning Logic

Turkey is not one single kebab menu. Route the food plan by region and meal style.

## Regional Food Map

| Region | Focus |
|--------|-------|
| Istanbul | Wide national spread, seafood, meyhane, modern dining |
| Southeast | Kebab depth, pistachio desserts, liver, breakfast, intense savory identity |
| Aegean | Olive-oil dishes, herbs, seafood, meze and slower coast meals |
| Black Sea | Anchovy culture, corn-based dishes, breakfast and tea-country comfort |
| Central Anatolia | Doughs, grills, hearty comfort foods |

## Meal Strategy

- Turkish breakfast can be a headline experience, not just hotel filler.
- Meze and grilled mains suit slower dinner pacing better than checklist ordering.
- Dessert matters most in southeast routes and in select Istanbul specialists.
- If the user drinks alcohol, note that nightlife and alcohol culture vary a lot by district and region.

## Practical Rules

- Ask whether the user wants street food, classic restaurants, or special-occasion dining.
- Build heat-aware lunch logic for southeast or summer coast trips.
- Travelers with dietary limits need more planning in smaller towns than in Istanbul.
- If food is a top priority, give it route power, not just restaurant side notes.
