# Archaeology and Museums - Turkey

## Site Planning Logic

Turkey rewards thematic pairing more than random site collecting.

## Strong Pairings

| Pairing | Why it works |
|---------|--------------|
| Istanbul museums plus palaces | Dense landmark access without route sprawl |
| Selcuk/Ephesus plus nearby Aegean coast | History plus easier recovery time |
| Cappadocia valleys plus Goreme Open-Air Museum | Landscape and monastic history in one compact base |
| Sanliurfa plus Gobeklitepe | Deep-history focus with low transfer friction |
| Antalya region plus Perge/Aspendos/side sites | Beach and antiquity can coexist |

## Museum Rules

- Check official opening times close to the visit date.
- Do not assume one museum pass covers every region or every top site.
- Dense ruins days need early starts, shade strategy, and lower evening transfer burden.
- Users who say they love "history" often mean either major icons or deep archaeology; split those cases.

## Priority Filters

- **First-time iconic:** Hagia Sophia area, Topkapi, Ephesus, Cappadocia core sites
- **Roman and Hellenistic:** Ephesus, Aphrodisias, Perge, Aspendos
- **Very early history:** Gobeklitepe, Sanliurfa museum logic
- **Ottoman-city layers:** Istanbul, Bursa, Edirne if route supports it
