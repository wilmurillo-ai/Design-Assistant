# Accessibility - Turkey

## Accessibility Reality

Accessibility quality varies sharply by region, old-town fabric, and hotel type.

## Easier Choices

- Modern Istanbul hotels in flatter districts
- Larger resort properties on the Mediterranean coast
- Direct-transfer city breaks with fewer intercity moves
- Private transfer routing when rail or bus station handling would be stressful

## Harder Choices

- Cave hotels in Cappadocia
- Steep or cobbled old towns
- Small harbor or hillside coastal bases
- Dense archaeology days with uneven terrain and limited shade

## Planning Rules

- Ask first about walking distance, stairs, wheelchair use, bathroom needs, and transfer tolerance.
- Prioritize room and neighborhood suitability over postcard value.
- One accessible base with hired transport beats multiple romantic but difficult properties.
- Do not assume museum and site access is uniform even within the same city.
