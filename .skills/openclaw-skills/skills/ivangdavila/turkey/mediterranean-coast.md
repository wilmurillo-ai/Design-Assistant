# Mediterranean Coast - Turkey

## Best Fit

Best for beach time, resort families, scenic drives, boat days, and users who want a warmer and more resort-forward Turkey route.

## Route Logic

| Cluster | Strong for | Watch-outs |
|---------|------------|------------|
| Antalya city and old town | Short cultural beach mix | Not the same product as a quiet coast escape |
| Lara / Belek | Resort convenience and family comfort | Can feel detached from local texture |
| Kas / Kalkan | Scenic coast, couples, small-town base | Hills, steps, and car or taxi dependence |
| Fethiye / Oludeniz | Paragliding, lagoons, family and activity mix | Busy in peak season |

## Planning Rules

- Decide early whether the user wants resort ease or small-town coast character.
- The D400 corridor is beautiful but should not be overscheduled.
- One boat day can replace multiple rushed beach hops.
- Peak summer heat changes old-site tolerance and walking comfort.

## Common Mistakes

- Picking a resort strip when the user actually wants old-town evenings.
- Driving too much coast in too few days.
- Ignoring school-holiday crowd patterns.
- Forgetting that scenic boutique stays often mean stairs and hills.
