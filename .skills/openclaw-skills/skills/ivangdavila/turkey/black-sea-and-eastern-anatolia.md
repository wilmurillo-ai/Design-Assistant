# Black Sea and Eastern Anatolia - Turkey

## Best Fit

Best for lush landscapes, mountain villages, tea-country scenery, long-form rail stories, winter mood, and travelers who want a less standard Turkey trip.

## Region Selection Grid

| Zone | Strong for | Watch-outs |
|------|------------|------------|
| Trabzon / Rize | Green mountains, yaylas, Sumela area | Fog, rain, winding roads |
| Kars | Winter atmosphere, rail arrival stories, eastern architecture | Cold logistics and seasonal transport |
| Van and far east | Big landscapes and distinctive history | Long distances and fewer route shortcuts |

## Planning Rules

- Treat the Black Sea as its own weather system.
- Eastern Anatolia works better with a targeted flight than with a heroic overland stretch.
- Road scenery is part of the product, so do not pair it with an overcompressed pace.
- In winter, build extra resilience for snow, delayed roads, and early darkness.

## Common Mistakes

- Adding this region as a casual side trip to a west-coast holiday.
- Expecting beach-style infrastructure or same-hour flexibility.
- Underestimating the effect of rain and fog on viewpoints.
- Planning too many mountain transfers in one day.
