# East Coast — Caniço, Santa Cruz, Machico

## Overview

The east coast from Caniço to Machico offers a different pace than Funchal: more residential, better beaches, and closer to the airport. Popular with families and those seeking quieter stays while still being connected.

---

## Caniço

### Quick Facts
| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Distance from Funchal | 15 min drive |
| Distance from airport | 10 min drive |
| Population | ~23,000 |
| Vibe | Beach-focused, residential, international mix |

### Character
Caniço is essentially two areas:
- **Caniço de Baixo** (lower): Tourist hotels, beach access, restaurants
- **Caniço center** (upper): Local town, services, more affordable

Popular with German and British expats. Good balance of convenience and beach lifestyle.

### Beaches & Swimming
| Spot | Type | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| Lido Galomar | Lido complex | Pool + sea access, facilities, €4 entry |
| Praia dos Reis Magos | Pebble beach | Free, small but nice |
| Galo resort beach | Hotel lido | Guests only |

### Cost of Living
| Item | Range |
|------|-------|
| 1BR apartment | €550-800/month |
| Meal out (local) | €8-12 |
| Supermarket | Pingo Doce, Spar available |

### Pros
- Best beach access near Funchal
- Quieter than city
- Quick airport access
- International community
- Good value vs. Funchal

### Cons
- Need car for exploring
- Limited nightlife
- Can feel touristy (Caniço de Baixo)
- Fewer restaurant options

## Santa Cruz

### Quick Facts
| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Distance from Funchal | 20 min drive |
| Distance from airport | 5 min drive |
| Population | ~10,000 |
| Vibe | Local, authentic, working town |

### Character
Santa Cruz is a real Madeiran town—not a tourist destination but a place people actually live and work. Has a nice promenade, local market, and genuine community feel. The airport is right here, which is both convenient (close to flights) and occasionally noisy.

### Highlights
| Spot | What |
|------|------|
| Promenade | Pleasant seafront walk |
| Church | Historic, pretty square |
| Market | Local produce, authentic |
| Aquaparque | Water park (families) |

### Cost of Living
| Item | Range |
|------|-------|
| 1BR apartment | €500-700/month |
| Meal out (local) | €7-10 |
| Supermarket | Pingo Doce |

### Pros
- Most affordable east coast option
- Authentic local life
- Good schools
- Closest to airport
- Nice promenade

### Cons
- Not walkable to Funchal
- Limited tourist infrastructure
- Airport noise (some areas)
- Fewer expat services

## Machico

### Quick Facts
| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Distance from Funchal | 30 min drive |
| Distance from airport | 10 min drive |
| Population | ~11,000 |
| Vibe | Historic, local, beach town |

### Character
Machico was the first place Europeans settled on Madeira (1419). Today it's a pleasant town with the island's only golden sand beach (sand imported from Morocco). More local feel than Caniço, more facilities than Santa Cruz. Good option for those wanting beach + authenticity.

### Beaches
| Spot | Type | Notes |
|------|------|-------|
| Praia de Machico | Golden sand | Imported sand, breakwater protected, calm |
| Praia da Banda d'Além | Pebble | Locals' beach, natural |

### Highlights
| Spot | What |
|------|------|
| Historic center | Old churches, colonial architecture |
| Fort São João Baptista | Historic, views |
| Bay | Protected swimming, kayaking |
| Nearby levadas | Good hiking access |

### Cost of Living
| Item | Range |
|------|-------|
| 1BR apartment | €500-700/month |
| Meal out (local) | €7-11 |
| Supermarket | Pingo Doce, small shops |

### Pros
- Only real beach with sand
- Historic charm
- Very affordable
- Local restaurants excellent value
- Gateway to east coast levadas

### Cons
- 30+ minutes from Funchal
- Limited nightlife
- Fewer expat services
- Need car for most things

## Who Should Choose East Coast

**Caniço if:**
- Beach access is priority
- Want international community
- Have budget for mid-range
- Don't mind some tourists

**Santa Cruz if:**
- Budget is tight
- Want real local life
- Flying frequently (airport close)
- Have kids (schools, water park)

**Machico if:**
- Must have sand beach
- Appreciate history
- Want affordability + character
- Like hiking (good levada access)

**All three require a car** for exploring the island, though local life is walkable in each town center.
