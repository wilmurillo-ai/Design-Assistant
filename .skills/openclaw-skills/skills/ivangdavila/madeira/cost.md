# Madeira Cost of Living

## Quick Summary

| Category | Range |
|----------|-------|
| Monthly budget (modest single) | €1,200-1,500 |
| Monthly budget (comfortable single) | €1,800-2,200 |
| Monthly budget (couple) | €2,200-3,000 |
| Monthly budget (family of 4) | €3,000-4,500 |

**Context**: Cheaper than most Western European cities, similar to mainland Portugal, more expensive than it was pre-2020.

### What's Cheaper in Madeira
- Local food and restaurants
- Wine and alcohol
- Healthcare (public system)
- Entertainment/activities
- Public transport

### What's More Expensive
- Imported goods
- Cars and fuel
- International brands
- Some electronics
- Amazon delivery (slower, shipping costs)

## Saving Money Tips

1. **Shop at Pingo Doce** — Best supermarket prices
2. **Eat prato do dia** — €6-9 for full lunch
3. **Live outside center** — 20-30% cheaper rent
4. **Bus over car** — If Funchal-based
5. **Cook at home** — Massive savings vs. eating out
6. **Local SIM** — Much cheaper than roaming
7. **Public healthcare** — Register once eligible
8. **Farmer's markets** — Cheaper than supermarket for fresh
9. **Happy hour drinks** — If going out
10. **Long-term rental** — Better rates than Airbnb
