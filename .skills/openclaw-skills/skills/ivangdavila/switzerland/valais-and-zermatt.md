# Valais and Zermatt — Switzerland

## Best For

- Matterhorn-focused trips
- High-Alps scenery, glacier logic, and dramatic mountain identity
- Users willing to pay for altitude and scenery

## Core Planning Rules

- Zermatt is a destination, not a casual add-on.
- Treat the transfer in and out as part of the trip design.
- Altitude, visibility, and season matter more here than in softer lake routes.

## Good Use Cases

- Dedicated 2-4 night Zermatt stay
- Valais as the mountain anchor in a 10-14 day trip
- Pairing with Lake Geneva for contrast

## Watch-Outs

- Premium pricing and low-inventory pressure
- Weak fit for users who dislike weather risk or frequent transfers
- Poor choice for people who only want one quick scenic photo stop
