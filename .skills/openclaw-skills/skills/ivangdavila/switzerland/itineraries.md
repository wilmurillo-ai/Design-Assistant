# Itineraries — Switzerland

## 4 Days: One Strong Base

### Option A: Zurich + Easy Day Trips
- Days 1-2: Zurich neighborhoods, lake, museums, food
- Day 3: Lucerne or Rhine Falls day trip
- Day 4: Buffer and departure

### Option B: Lucerne + Central Switzerland
- Lake promenade and old town
- One mountain day
- One boat or nearby town day
- Final buffer before departure

## 7 Days: Classic Two-Base Route

### Option A: Zurich + Lucerne or Bernese Oberland
- City arrival base first
- Mountain or lake base second
- Keep one flex day for weather-sensitive lifts

### Option B: Geneva or Lausanne + Montreux or Bernese Oberland
- Lake Geneva urban start
- Shift inland only once
- Protect final night near departure city

## 10 Days: Scenic Rail and Mountain Balance

- Base 1: Zurich or Geneva, 2-3 days
- Base 2: Lucerne or Bernese Oberland, 3-4 days
- Base 3: Valais or Graubunden, 3-4 days
- Build at least one weather downgrade day into the middle

## 14 Days: Grand Switzerland Without Overkill

- Zurich or Basel entry
- Central Switzerland or Bernese Oberland
- Valais or Graubunden
- Lake Geneva or Ticino finish
- Do not exceed four bases unless the user explicitly enjoys transfer-heavy travel

## Itinerary Quality Checks

- Every mountain day needs a lower-altitude backup
- Every scenic-train day needs a light evening
- Last night before an international flight stays near the departure airport unless the user accepts risk
- If the user wants both Zermatt and St. Moritz, verify that the transfer itself is part of the experience
