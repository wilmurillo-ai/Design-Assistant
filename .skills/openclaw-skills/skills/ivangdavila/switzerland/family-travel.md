# Family Travel — Switzerland

## Strong Family Patterns

- One stable base with easy day trips
- Short transfer days
- Mountain plans with low-altitude backup
- Apartment or family room near groceries and transit

## What To Ask

- Ages of children
- Stroller, carrier, or independent walkers
- How much luggage the group will move
- Whether the family actually wants snow, hikes, or simply views

## Good Family Fits

- Lucerne and Central Switzerland
- Selected Bernese Oberland bases with conservative pacing
- Lake Geneva for mixed indoor and outdoor options

## Watch-Outs

- Too many lift changes with strollers
- Scenic-train days that are long for small children
- Famous mountain towns with evening food and grocery friction
- School-holiday windows that distort price and availability
