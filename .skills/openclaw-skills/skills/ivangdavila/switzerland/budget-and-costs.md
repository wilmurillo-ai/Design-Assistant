# Budget and Costs — Switzerland

## Broad Budget Bands

| Style | Daily pattern |
|------|---------------|
| Budget | Hostel or simple guesthouse, supermarket meals, selective paid sights, rail discipline |
| Mid-range | 3-star hotel or apartment, cafe and restaurant mix, one or two premium excursions |
| Premium | Central or scenic hotels, fine dining, flexible transport, major mountain experiences |

## Where Money Moves Fast

- Bernese Oberland in summer
- Zermatt and Engadin in ski season
- Scenic-train trips with multiple premium add-ons
- Lake Geneva festival or congress periods

## What To Price Honestly

- Airport transfers and intercity rail backbone
- Mountain lifts and peak-to-peak excursions
- Resort taxes, parking, luggage, and breakfast
- Ski gear, spa, or panoramic-restaurant spending
- CHF pricing when the user is mentally budgeting in euros

## Good Value Patterns

- Fewer bases with deeper local day trips
- One iconic mountain day instead of stacking expensive lifts daily
- Shoulder-season city plus lake or low-altitude routes

## Poor Value Patterns

- One-night alpine stops
- Renting a car for station-friendly classic routes
- Building the trip around the most famous villages every single night

## Budget Guidance Rule

Give budget advice as a route model, not a Swiss national average. Costs swing more by altitude, fame, and season than by canton label alone.
