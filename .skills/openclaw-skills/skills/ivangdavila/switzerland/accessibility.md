# Accessibility — Switzerland

## Why Switzerland Can Work Well

- Rail and station infrastructure are often strong by European standards.
- Cities are usually easier than steep resort villages.
- Boats, lifts, old hotels, and mountain paths vary much more than the rail backbone.

## Planning Rules

- Confirm the real last-mile path from station to hotel.
- Separate step-free city routing from high-altitude excursion ambitions.
- If wheelchair or low-mobility access matters, verify the exact lift, boat, or platform instead of relying on destination reputation.

## Better Fits

- Zurich, Geneva, Lausanne, Bern, and Basel city stays
- Lucerne if the trip is selective about excursions

## Higher-Friction Cases

- Car-free mountain villages with slopes or stairs
- Old chalets and historic hotels
- Ambitious multi-connection scenic days
