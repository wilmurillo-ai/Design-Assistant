# Alps, Lakes, and Scenic Trains — Switzerland

## What This Module Is For

Use this when the user wants the classic Switzerland product: mountain panoramas, lake boats, cable cars, car-free villages, and train rides chosen for experience rather than speed.

## Scenic Experience Rules

- Scenic trains are best treated as anchor experiences, not everyday transport.
- One major panoramic day usually beats stacking multiple prestige rides back-to-back.
- Mountain visibility can matter more than the exact brand name of the train or lift.

## Good Use Cases

- Glacier Express or Bernina-style travel when the journey itself is the headline
- Lucerne or Interlaken with one high-value boat-plus-mountain day
- Engadin or Valais routes where altitude and scenery are the reason to go

## Watch-Outs

- Cloud and wind can erase the value of expensive mountain days
- Some routes need reservations or careful luggage logic
- Boat, bus, and lift timetables can force early starts and conservative pacing

## Nature Planning Rule

If the user wants hiking, lakes, and panoramic transport in one trip, choose one altitude-heavy region and one easier recovery base instead of chasing every postcard spot.
