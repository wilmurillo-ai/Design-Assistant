# Weather and Seasonality — Switzerland

## Switzerland Is Not One Weather Product

| Context | Typical planning consequence |
|--------|------------------------------|
| Zurich, Basel, Bern | Easier year-round city logic, weaker snow guarantee |
| Lake Geneva and Ticino | Softer climate, different shoulder-season value |
| Bernese Oberland and Central mountains | Strong visibility and cloud dependence |
| Valais and Engadin | Higher-altitude logic, stronger winter identity |

## Best Use Windows

- Late spring and early autumn often give the cleanest city-plus-scenery balance.
- High summer is strong for lakes and hiking, but crowds and pricing rise sharply.
- December works for atmosphere, festive markets, and lower-elevation city routes.
- Deep winter is best when the trip is genuinely built around snow.

## Weather Rules

- Ask the month before recommending region mix.
- Keep indoor or low-altitude backups for every mountain-heavy route.
- Verify pass, lift, and trail status close to travel dates.
- Be honest when the best-looking plan depends on rare blue-sky luck.
