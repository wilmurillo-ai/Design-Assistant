# Geneva, Lausanne, and Lake Geneva — Switzerland

## Best For

- Soft-climate city plus lake trips
- Wine, food, museums, and elegant pacing
- Users arriving by Geneva airport or linking with France

## Route Logic

- Geneva is strongest for international arrival and short urban stays.
- Lausanne is better for a livable multi-night base.
- Montreux works when the user wants lake views and a resort feel.

## Why It Works

- Good year-round city value
- Easier low-altitude fallback when mountain weather is poor elsewhere
- Strong fit for slower travelers and couples

## Watch-Outs

- Event and congress spikes can distort hotel prices
- Users may underplay transfer time to deep alpine regions
- Cross-border assumptions with France need explicit checking
