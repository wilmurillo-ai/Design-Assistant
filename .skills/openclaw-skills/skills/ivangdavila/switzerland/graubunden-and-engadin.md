# Graubunden and Engadin — Switzerland

## Best For

- Scenic rail users
- St. Moritz, Davos, and Engadin valleys
- Users who want a more spacious, less standard Switzerland trip

## What Makes It Different

- The journey itself often carries the value
- Distances are longer than they look
- National-park and high-valley logic reward patient pacing

## Good Use Cases

- Scenic rail plus one stable base
- Summer hiking with clear weather flexibility
- Winter users who want Engadin or Davos rather than the default icons

## Watch-Outs

- High transfer cost if crammed into a short classic trip
- Shoulder-season gaps can reduce what feels possible
- Weak fit if the user only wants the simplest first-time highlights
