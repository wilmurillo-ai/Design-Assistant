# Basel and Northwest — Switzerland

## Best For

- Art, museums, and Rhine urban energy
- Easy tri-border positioning
- Users who want a city-forward Switzerland start or finish

## Why It Can Win

- Strong airport and rail access
- Good fit with Alsace, Germany, or Jura add-ons
- Easier and often cheaper than forcing every trip through the classic alpine spine

## Watch-Outs

- Not the right anchor if the user's main dream is Jungfrau or Zermatt
- Border-heavy planning can create hidden admin and roaming assumptions
- Short trips should not combine Basel with too many distant mountain icons
