# Best Toronto Areas for Dining

## King West (Entertainment District)

**Vibe**: Trendy, date night, see-and-be-seen
**Price**: CAD 50-100/person
**Best For**: Special occasions, impressing visitors, foodies

### Top Restaurants
- **Byblos** — Mediterranean, beautiful space
- **Pai** — Northern Thai, always packed
- **KOST** — Rooftop, views, Mediterranean
- **Wvrst** — Sausages, 100+ beers
- **Buca** — Italian, basement space
- **Richmond Station** — Canadian, seasonal
- **Ki Modern** — Japanese, sleek

### Tips
- Reservations essential, especially weekends
- Dress smart casual
- Expect waits at popular spots
- Best Friday/Saturday energy

---

## Ossington Strip

**Vibe**: Cocktail bars, chef-driven, locals
**Price**: CAD 40-80/person
**Best For**: Cocktails + food, foodie discoveries

### Top Restaurants
- **Union** — French bistro
- **Enoteca Sociale** — Italian, wine
- **PrettyUgly** — Cocktails, bar food
- **Bellwoods Brewery** — Craft beer
- **Bar Isabel** — Spanish tapas
- **Grand Electric** — Tacos, whiskey

### Tips
- Walk the strip, pop into bars
- Less corporate than King West
- Great cocktail scene
- Some spots cash-only

---

## Leslieville (Queen East)

**Vibe**: Brunch capital, neighborhood feel
**Price**: CAD 25-50/person
**Best For**: Weekend brunch, casual dinner

### Top Restaurants
- **Lady Marmalade** — Brunch institution
- **Descendant** — Detroit-style pizza
- **Ascari Enoteca** — Italian
- **Ruby Watchco** — Set menu, changing
- **Ed's Real Scoop** — Ice cream
- **Loaded Pierogi** — What it says

### Tips
- Brunch lines Saturday/Sunday
- Coffee shops excellent
- Family-friendly
- Streetcar access (504, 501)

---

## Kensington Market / Chinatown

**Vibe**: Bohemian, cheap eats, diverse
**Price**: CAD 10-30/person
**Best For**: Budget eating, exploration, late night

### Top Restaurants
- **Seven Lives** — Tacos
- **Rasta Pasta** — Jamaican-Italian
- **Golden Turtle** — Vietnamese
- **Wah Sing** — Seafood
- **King's Noodle** — BBQ duck/pork
- **Rol San** — Dim sum, late night

### Tips
- Pedestrian-only Sundays (summer)
- Street food vibe
- Cash helpful at small spots
- Great for vegetarians

---

## Little Italy (College Street)

**Vibe**: Classic Toronto, patios, people-watching
**Price**: CAD 30-60/person
**Best For**: Patios, casual Italian, date night

### Top Restaurants
- **Bar Isabel** — Spanish, excellent
- **Pizzeria Libretto** — Neapolitan pizza
- **Cafe Diplomatico** — Patio icon
- **Trattoria Taverniti** — Classic Italian
- **Bang Bang** — Ice cream

### Tips
- Best patios in summer
- Walk from Bathurst to Shaw
- Casual dress
- Good late-night options

---

## Danforth / Greektown

**Vibe**: Greek heritage, family-friendly
**Price**: CAD 25-45/person
**Best For**: Greek food, family dining

### Top Restaurants
- **Mezes** — Traditional Greek
- **Asteria** — Souvlaki
- **Mamakas** — Modern Greek (Ossington)
- **Pantheon** — Classic taverna
- **Christina's** — Greek bakery

### Tips
- Taste of the Danforth (August)
- Subway accessible (Pape, Donlands)
- BYOB some spots
- Kids welcome

---

## Scarborough (Asian Food)

**Vibe**: Authentic Asian, strip malls, incredible value
**Price**: CAD 15-35/person
**Best For**: Chinese, South Asian, Filipino, Korean

### Top Restaurants
- **Federick** — Chinese seafood
- **Buk Chang Dong Soon Tofu** — Korean tofu
- **Lahori Karahi** — Pakistani
- **Iqbal Kebab** — Halal, kababs
- **Casa Manila** — Filipino

### Tips
- Car helpful
- Strip mall parking
- Cash helpful some spots
- Worth the trip for food alone

---

## Yorkville

**Vibe**: Upscale, fine dining, expense accounts
**Price**: CAD 80-200/person
**Best For**: Fine dining, impressing, special occasions

### Top Restaurants
- **Alo** — Toronto's best (get reservations early)
- **Cafe Boulud** — Daniel Boulud's Toronto outpost
- **Sotto Sotto** — Italian, celebrity sightings
- **Momofuku** — David Chang group

### Tips
- Reservations required
- Dress well
- Valet parking available
- After-dinner drinks at hotel bars

---

## North York (Korean)

**Vibe**: Suburban Koreatown, authentic
**Price**: CAD 20-50/person
**Best For**: Korean BBQ, late-night Korean

### Top Restaurants
- **Korean Village** — BBQ
- **Owl of Minerva** — 24-hour
- **Buk Chang Dong** — Multiple locations
- **Arisu** — BBQ

### Tips
- Yonge/Finch intersection hub
- Late hours common
- Subway accessible
- Korean grocery stores nearby

---

## The Waterfront / Harbourfront

**Vibe**: Tourist, views, chains
**Price**: CAD 40-80/person
**Best For**: Views, tourists, casual

### Notable Spots
- **Amsterdam Brewhouse** — Patio, views
- **Against the Grain** — Urban winery
- **Pearl Diver** — Seafood

### Tips
- Touristy, higher prices
- Views compensate
- Better for drinks than dinner
- Harbourfront Centre events
