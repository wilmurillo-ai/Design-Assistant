# Japan Itineraries

## 6 Days — Tokyo + One Extension

### Days 1-3 Tokyo
- Neighborhood and food split days
- One culture or museum block

### Days 4-6 Kyoto or Osaka
- One heritage day
- One food and nightlife day
- One flexible half-day before transfer

**Best for:** First-time trip with low transfer fatigue.

## 9 Days — Tokyo + Kyoto + Osaka

### Days 1-3 Tokyo
### Days 4-6 Kyoto
### Days 7-9 Osaka (with optional Nara day)

**Best for:** Balanced city, culture, and food route.

## 12 Days — Extended Classic Route

### Days 1-4 Tokyo
### Days 5-7 Kyoto
### Days 8-9 Osaka
### Days 10-12 Hiroshima/Miyajima or regional add-on

**Best for:** Travelers wanting depth without overloading daily transfers.

## Seasonal Notes

- Cherry blossom and autumn foliage windows need earlier booking.
- Golden Week and New Year need extra transport buffer.
- Summer requires heat-aware pacing and indoor backup options.
