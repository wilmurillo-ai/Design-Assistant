# Scouting Grid

Evaluate players through role fit, not vibes.

## Scorecard

| Area | What to inspect | Score |
|------|-----------------|-------|
| Skill | handle, passing, finishing, shooting versatility, touch | /10 |
| Tactical | spacing, timing, reads, help awareness, role discipline | /10 |
| Physical | burst, lateral movement, repeatability, contact tolerance, durability | /10 |
| Mental | poise, competitiveness, response to mistakes, coachability | /10 |
| Role fit | how well the player solves this exact role in this exact system | /10 |
| Risk | injuries, sample size, level jump, shooting variance, decision volatility | low | medium | high |

## Role-Fit Questions

- What is the exact offensive job?
- What is the exact defensive job?
- Which lineup or partner makes the role work?
- What limitation must the team hide?
- Is the player a current starter, developmental piece, or specialist?

## Red Flags

- Highlight-only evaluation
- Role fit described without lineup context
- "High IQ" used instead of naming concrete reads
- Ignoring point-of-attack defense, rebounding, or decision speed for core rotation roles
