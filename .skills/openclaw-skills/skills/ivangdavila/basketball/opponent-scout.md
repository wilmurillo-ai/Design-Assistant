# Opponent Scout Blueprint

Use this when the user needs a coach-ready opponent brief.

## Core Questions

- What do they want their offense to feel like?
- Which actions create their best shots?
- Which player combinations generate the most stress?
- Where are they most vulnerable if pressed, switched, helped, or forced late in the clock?

## Fast Flags

- Bigs in deep drop -> punish with pull-up and pocket timing
- Weak nail help -> attack middle and spray to corners
- Turnover-prone guards -> heat them with loaded gaps and ball pressure
- Poor transition floor balance -> run after every live-ball win
- Strong offensive rebounding -> assign early hit-first rules
