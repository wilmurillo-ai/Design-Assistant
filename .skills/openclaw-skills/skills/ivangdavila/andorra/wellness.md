# Wellness — Andorra

Wellness is one of Andorra's strongest non-ski, non-hike plays. It works especially well on short breaks and on bad-weather days.

## Core Strategy

| Goal | Best move |
|------|-----------|
| Signature spa time | Build one evening around Caldea |
| Recover after skiing | Book late afternoon, not the first slot |
| Couple's reset | Stay in Escaldes and walk to the spa |
| Rainy-day save | Combine spa + shopping + good dinner |

## Booking Advice

- Reserve ahead for weekends, school holidays, and winter peaks.
- If the user hates crowds, avoid the most obvious evening slots on Saturdays.
- Pair the spa with a nearby hotel if the group wants the day to feel easy.

## Best Uses

- One full wellness block in a short trip
- One recovery session after a hard ski or hike day
- The backup plan when weather kills panoramic routes

## What Not to Do

- Cram a spa session between two rushed outdoor plans
- Treat wellness as the whole trip if the user actually wants mountain identity
- Forget that thermal plans can be tiring after a long border drive
