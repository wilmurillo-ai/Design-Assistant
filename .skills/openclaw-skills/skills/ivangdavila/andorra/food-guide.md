# Food Guide — Andorra

Andorra food is mountain food with Catalan and French influence. The good move is to lean into borda cooking, seasonal menus, and hearty dishes instead of chasing generic international comfort food every night.

## What to Order

| Dish or style | What it is | Best moment |
|---------------|------------|-------------|
| Escudella | Hearty stew | Cold weather lunch |
| Trinxat | Potato and cabbage mountain classic | Winter dinner |
| Grilled meats | Common in bordas | Snow or hiking recovery day |
| Mountain rice dishes | Rich, filling | Group dinners |
| Local cured meats and cheeses | Easy starter play | Aperitif or lunch board |

## Best Contexts

| Context | Advice |
|---------|--------|
| First dinner | Book a borda and order local, not generic pizza |
| Ski lunch | Keep it lighter unless the afternoon is done |
| Summer lunch | Terrace plus one local dish works better than full heavy menus |
| Short trip | Do one mountain meal, one modern central meal |

## Borda Strategy

- Bordas are the clearest local identity move.
- Book ahead on weekends and winter holidays.
- They are better for slow dinners than quick turnarounds before nightlife.
- If the user hates heavy food, steer them toward central bistro-style options one night and only one traditional mountain dinner.

## What to Avoid

- Eating every meal in the busiest shopping streets
- Assuming "duty free" means cheap restaurant pricing
- Doing a huge mountain lunch right before afternoon skiing or a long drive

## Good Ordering Advice

| Situation | What to say |
|-----------|-------------|
| Want local but not too heavy | Ask for one traditional starter and shared mains |
| Traveling with kids | Keep one familiar dish, then add local sides |
| Mixed group | Share cured meats, cheese, and one traditional hot dish |

## Dessert and Drink Notes

- Mountain desserts are richer and make more sense after a cold day than after a spa afternoon.
- For drinks, local wine exists but the broader selection is often imported. Recommend pairing by style, not only by origin.
