# Emergencies — Andorra

Andorra is straightforward in emergencies, but mountain conditions and cross-border logistics make preparation worth it.

## Emergency Numbers

| Service | Number | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|
| General emergency | 112 | Main emergency number |
| Ambulance | 116 | Medical support |
| Fire | 118 | Fire and rescue |
| Police | 110 | Police support |

## Medical Reality

- The main hospital reference is in Escaldes-Engordany.
- Mountain issues are common enough that hikers and skiers should not improvise.
- Travel insurance matters more for non-EU visitors and for anyone doing mountain activities.

## When to Escalate Fast

| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Chest pain, major trauma, severe breathing issue | Call 112 immediately |
| Ski or hiking injury | Stop moving, share location, call emergency support |
| Winter road problem in dangerous conditions | Do not keep forcing the drive |

## Practical Preparedness

- Save hotel address offline in case signal is weak.
- Carry a small layer and water even on easy mountain days.
- If a document is stolen, file a police report before trying to solve insurance or embassy steps.

## Common Mistakes

- Treating a mountain weather turn as a minor inconvenience
- Hiking without enough battery or offline navigation
- Delaying a medical check because the country feels small and close to the border
