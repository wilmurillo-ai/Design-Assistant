# Andorra la Vella

The capital is best for short stays focused on shopping, easy bus access, and evenings out. It is not the smartest base for first-lift skiing unless convenience matters more than slope time.

## Best For

| Traveler | Why it works |
|----------|--------------|
| Weekend shoppers | Meritxell corridor and central stores on foot |
| Car-free stays | Main bus connections and walkable center |
| Mixed city + mountains | Easy pivot to Escaldes, old town, museums |
| Night owls | Best concentration of bars and late dinners |

## Best Zones

| Zone | Choose it for | Watch out for |
|------|---------------|---------------|
| Barri Antic | Character, Casa de la Vall, quieter nights | Less hotel stock, uphill walking |
| Meritxell axis | Shopping-first, central location | More traffic, less charm |
| Parc Central side | Practical access, easier parking | Can feel functional rather than scenic |

## What to Do

- Walk the Barri Antic early or late, not only midday when it feels busiest.
- Use the capital as a base for shopping, museums, and one relaxed dinner night.
- Pair it with Escaldes on foot, since the two centers blend into one urban strip.
- Use it as the fallback plan when weather ruins a mountain day.

## Strong Plays

| Plan | Why it works |
|------|--------------|
| Afternoon arrival + old town walk + dinner | Makes a short stay feel fuller |
| Shopping block before lunch, not after | Streets and fitting rooms get busier later |
| One night here, next nights near slopes | Best of both city and ski access |

## Weak Plays

- Booking here for a pure ski trip and then commuting daily to Canillo or Pas de la Casa
- Assuming every central hotel has easy parking access
- Staying only for the skyline; the capital is about convenience, not postcard views

## Food and Night Notes

- Dinner starts later than in France but earlier than big Spanish cities. Around 20:00 to 21:00 works well.
- Central areas have the broadest range of international food, but mountain dishes feel more convincing in traditional bordas outside the main shopping strip.
- For drinks, start in the capital if the group wants variety rather than apres-ski energy.
