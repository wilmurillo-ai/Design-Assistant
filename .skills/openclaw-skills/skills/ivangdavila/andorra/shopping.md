# Shopping — Andorra

Shopping is a real reason to come, but it works best with categories and price discipline. The bad version is walking the whole center without a list and buying the first "deal" you see.

## Best Shopping Areas

| Area | Best for | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| The Shopping Mile | General shopping | Main central axis |
| Meritxell and nearby streets | Cosmetics, fashion, gifts | Best for first-timers |
| Vivand / Carlemany | Walkable browsing | Easy to combine with Escaldes stay |
| Resort-side sports shops | Ski and mountain gear | Better for technical categories |

## Smart Strategy

- Go with a short list: cosmetics, electronics, outdoor gear, or gifts.
- Compare prices before buying electronics or premium goods.
- Buy technical sports gear where staff understands the category.
- Keep purchase limits and customs allowances in mind when returning to Spain or France.

## Best Timing

| Timing | Why it works |
|--------|--------------|
| Morning | Easier walking, calmer fitting rooms |
| Weekdays | Less packed than weekend afternoons |
| Shoulder season | More comfortable browsing |

## Common Mistakes

- Assuming every product is cheaper because Andorra is duty free
- Doing all shopping after a tiring ski day
- Forgetting car space if buying bulky gear
- Ignoring customs reality when carrying alcohol, tobacco, or high-value items
