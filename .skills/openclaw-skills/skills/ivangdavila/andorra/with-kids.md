# With Kids — Andorra

Andorra is excellent for families when the trip has structure. The key is to mix one outdoor highlight with one easy fallback.

## Best Family Bases

| Base | Why it works |
|------|--------------|
| Canillo | Best all-round family answer |
| Escaldes | Good for short comfort-first breaks |
| Ordino | Great for calmer outdoorsy families |

## Good Family Activities

| Activity | Best season | Notes |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| Mon(t) Magic style mountain parks | Summer | High payoff for mixed ages |
| Simple snow activities | Winter | Good for non-ski siblings |
| Caldea-style wellness windows | Year-round | Check family access rules |
| Easy valley walks | Summer | Better than forcing a hard hike |
| Naturland side plan | Year-round | Works best with transport planning |

## Family Rules

- Avoid overpacking the day.
- Keep snacks, layers, and one indoor option ready.
- If the children are new to snow, keep the first day lighter than the adults want.
- Pick the hotel for logistics, not only for pool photos.

## Common Mistakes

- Long car shuttles every morning
- Too much shopping time for small kids
- One ambitious hike chosen only because the photos looked good
