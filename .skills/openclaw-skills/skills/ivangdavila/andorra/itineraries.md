# Itineraries — Andorra

Andorra rewards shorter, cleaner plans. Most people enjoy it more when each day has one anchor and one backup.

## 1 Night — Shopping + Spa Weekend

- Sleep in Escaldes or central Andorra la Vella.
- Day 1: arrive, old town or shopping walk, early dinner.
- Day 2: Caldea or half-day shopping before leaving.

## 2 Nights — First-Timer Mixed Trip

- Base in Escaldes or Canillo.
- Day 1: easy arrival, central walk, local dinner.
- Day 2: mountain highlight like Roc del Quer or a ski day.
- Day 3: shopping or Meritxell stop before departure.

## 3 Nights — Winter Split

- Night 1 in the center if the group wants a city evening.
- Nights 2 and 3 closer to Canillo or Ordino side.
- Use one ski day, one flexible snow or spa day, and one short shopping block.

## 3 Nights — Summer Mountain Split

- Base in Ordino or Canillo.
- Day 1: scenic arrival and village evening.
- Day 2: signature hike.
- Day 3: viewpoint or UNESCO valley plus long lunch.
- Day 4: capital stop on the way out.

## Family Version

- Sleep in Canillo if activities matter more than shopping.
- Keep one indoor backup every day.
- Avoid overdriving after late dinners or heavy mountain afternoons.
