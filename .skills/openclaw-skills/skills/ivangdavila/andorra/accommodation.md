# Accommodation — Andorra

The right base matters more than the star rating. Pick the parish for the trip first, then the hotel.

## Best Base by Goal

| Goal | Best area |
|------|-----------|
| Shopping + spa | Escaldes-Engordany |
| City convenience | Andorra la Vella |
| Ski-first trip | Canillo, Encamp, resort corridors |
| Quiet mountain stay | Ordino |
| Family activity mix | Canillo |

## Budget Guidance

| Budget | Best move |
|--------|-----------|
| Budget | Functional hotels outside the center or in resort corridors |
| Mid-range | Escaldes, Canillo, or solid village hotels |
| Premium | Spa-linked stays or polished mountain hotels |

## Booking Rules

- Book winter weekends early.
- Check parking before booking if bringing a car.
- If skiing is the main goal, count transfer time, not only room rate.
- If the trip is short, central location often beats a small nightly saving.

## Common Mismatches

- Capital hotel for a pure slope trip
- Remote mountain hotel without a clear dinner or transport plan
- Cheap room far from the real focus of the trip
