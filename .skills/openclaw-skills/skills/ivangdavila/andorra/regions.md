# Regions — Andorra

Andorra has 7 parishes. Distances are short, but the trip style changes a lot depending on where you sleep.

| Parish | Best For | Vibe | Notes |
|--------|----------|------|-------|
| Andorra la Vella | Shopping, short stays, buses | Busy, practical | Best central logistics |
| Escaldes-Engordany | Spa weekends, comfort | Polished, easy | Caldea and Vivand |
| Canillo | Grandvalira, families | Mountain-accessible | Good all-rounder |
| Encamp | Ski access, value | Functional | Useful for resort links |
| Ordino | Nature, quieter stays | Refined, calm | Strong summer appeal |
| La Massana | Bike and mountain trips | Active | Good adventure base |
| Sant Julia de Loria | Southern access, Naturland | Lower, calmer | Best for arrivals from Spain and activity parks |

## Fast Recommendations

| If they want... | Send them to... |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| Shopping + walkability | Andorra la Vella or Escaldes |
| Best family balance | Canillo |
| Quiet mountain elegance | Ordino |
| Bike and outdoor energy | La Massana |
| Easy first/last stop from Spain | Sant Julia de Loria |

## Seasonal Bias

- Winter favors Canillo, Encamp, La Massana, and Ordino-side bases depending on the ski plan.
- Summer brings Ordino, Canillo, and La Massana forward because hiking and cycling dominate.
- Shoulder season often works best in the center if the user wants flexibility and backups.

## What People Misjudge

- The country is small, but repeated daily transfers still eat time.
- Central does not always mean best. For ski or hike priorities, sleep closer to the activity.
- Parish choice matters more than people expect because roads funnel traffic at peak times.
