# Colonial Cities and Boyaca — Playbook

## Best Use Case

Cool-weather road loops, heritage towns, food stops, and users who want a calmer Colombia.

## Core Strengths

- Villa de Leyva and surrounding heritage stops
- Boyaca small-town rhythm
- Easy pairing with Bogota for a first or second trip

## Planning Rules

- Best by car or organized transfer, not by fragmented same-day public transport chains.
- Keep at least one overnight in the main town instead of treating the whole region as a day trip.
- Good fit for couples, families, and travelers who dislike tropical heat.

## Trap

Trying to squeeze Boyaca into one long out-and-back day usually removes exactly what makes it worth doing.
