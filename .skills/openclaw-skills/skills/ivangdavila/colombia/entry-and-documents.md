# Entry and Documents — Colombia

## Fast Decision Tree

1. Check whether the passport nationality is visa-exempt or needs a visitor visa.
2. Confirm intended stay length and whether a simple tourist stay is enough.
3. Complete `Check-Mig` inside the official window before departure when required.
4. Verify passport validity, onward-plan proof, and any vaccine-sensitive routing.

## Visitor Pathways

| Path | Typical use | Core limit |
|------|-------------|------------|
| Visa-free visitor entry | Tourism for many nationalities | Commonly up to 90 days initially, subject to officer decision |
| Visitor visa (Type V) | Tourism when nationality or case needs prior approval | Terms depend on the issued visa |

## Stay-Length Notes

- A typical tourist admission is 90 days, with extension possibilities that can reach 180 days in a calendar year when eligible.
- Entry permission is always granted by immigration at arrival, not by airfare or hotel booking alone.
- If the user needs a long stay, route early to visa or extension guidance instead of assuming a border-run fix.

## Check-Mig Reality

- Use the official Migracion Colombia portal, not copycat forms.
- Complete it close to departure, because doing it too early can create confusion if plans change.
- Keep a screenshot or PDF confirmation available offline.

## Health and Vaccine Triggers

- Yellow-fever planning matters most for Amazon, Llanos, jungle-edge areas, and some national-park routes.
- If traveler is arriving from a yellow-fever-risk country or routing into high-risk Colombian zones, verify current health notices before ticket finalization.
- Vaccine timing matters; last-minute jungle or island changes can become impossible without it.

## Checklist Before Locking Non-Refundables

- Visa-free or visa-needed pathway confirmed
- Passport validity checked
- Stay-length math checked against the actual plan
- Check-Mig timing understood
- Vaccine or health-sensitive segments verified
- Backup option if entry timing slips
