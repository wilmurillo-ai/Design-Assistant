# Itineraries — Colombia

## 7 Days: One City Pair

### Option A: Bogota + Medellin

- Days 1-3: Bogota by zones, with one day for museums and one for food or Monserrate timing
- Day 4: Midday transfer
- Days 5-7: Medellin plus one Antioquia day trip

### Option B: Cartagena + Rosario or nearby coast

- Days 1-3: Walled city and Getsemani pacing around heat
- Day 4: Lighter day or transfer
- Days 5-7: Beach or island segment with weather backup

## 10 Days: City + Nature Hybrid

### Option A: Medellin + Coffee Region

- Base 1: Medellin (4 days)
- Transfer: short flight or road, depending on tolerance
- Base 2: Pereira/Armenia/Salento band (4 days)
- Keep one flex day for weather or road delays

### Option B: Bogota + Cartagena

- Base 1: Bogota (4 days)
- Transfer day with airport slack
- Base 2: Cartagena (4 days)
- Use the final day as heat or departure buffer

## 14 Days: Three-Base First Trip

- Base 1: Bogota (3-4 days)
- Base 2: Medellin or Coffee Region (4-5 days)
- Base 3: Cartagena or island/coast add-on (4-5 days)
- Keep one or two flex slots for operational disruptions

## 21 Days: Deep Colombia

- Andes core: 6-7 days
- Culture or coffee loop: 5-6 days
- Caribbean, Pacific, or frontier finish: 6-7 days
- Do not exceed 3 major region jumps without explicit flight tolerance

## Itinerary Quality Checks

- Every 3-4 active days should include one lighter day
- Last night before international departure stays in the departure city
- High-rain or frontier segments get backup indoor or alternate-city plans
