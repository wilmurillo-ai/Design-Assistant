# Nightlife — Colombia

## Nightlife by City Type

| City type | Stronger model | Watch-out |
|-----------|----------------|-----------|
| Bogota | Neighborhood-based night with planned return | Long cross-city rides late at night |
| Medellin | Social, club, rooftop, and cafe mix | Weekend crowding and tourist-price zones |
| Cartagena | Rooftops, bars, and heat-aware pacing | Aggressive street selling and high markups |
| Cali | Salsa-first planning | Best enjoyed with lighter next-morning scheduling |

## Practical Rules

- Use licensed transport or known pickup points at night.
- Avoid moving between far nightlife zones unless the user explicitly wants a long night.
- Keep phones and payment methods managed conservatively in crowded areas.

## Trip-Design Rule

If nightlife matters, reduce next-day transfer load. Colombia nightlife pairs badly with dawn buses, long road legs, or island departures.
