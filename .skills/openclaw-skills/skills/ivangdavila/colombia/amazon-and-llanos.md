# Amazon and Llanos — Playbook

## Best Use Case

Wildlife, river, jungle, or frontier-style travel for users who accept more logistics and less predictability.

## Planning Reality

- Entry logistics, vaccine timing, weather, and operator quality matter more than headline attractions.
- These regions are not casual add-ons to a standard city itinerary.
- The trip should be built around one gateway and one operator logic, not stacked loosely.

## Core Rules

- Verify current health guidance before final booking.
- Keep one strong weather buffer.
- Do not place this segment at the end of a fragile international itinerary.

## Better Use Cases

- 3-5 days as a dedicated extension
- Users who actively want wildlife, guides, and lower-comfort conditions
- Repeat Colombia visitors who already covered the major cities
