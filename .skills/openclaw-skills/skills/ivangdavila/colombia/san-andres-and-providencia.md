# San Andres and Providencia — Playbook

## Best Use Case

Island-focused trips for users who prioritize reef, sea color, and slower beach rhythm over urban variety.

## Island Logic

- San Andres is easier for first-time island travelers and shorter stays.
- Providencia is more special but requires more weather and seat tolerance.
- Pairing both can be excellent, but only when the user accepts operational fragility.

## Planning Rules

- Book flights early in constrained periods.
- Keep sea and wind conditions in mind before promising boat-dependent activities.
- Do not schedule the final international departure immediately after the most fragile island segment.

## Cost Trap

Island Colombia is not "cheap Colombia." Flights, logistics, and limited inventory push costs up quickly.
