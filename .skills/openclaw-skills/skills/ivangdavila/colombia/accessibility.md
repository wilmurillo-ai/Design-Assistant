# Accessibility — Colombia

## Accessibility Reality

Accessibility quality varies sharply by city, neighborhood, and building age.

## Better Bets

- Newer parts of Bogota and Medellin generally offer better hotel, mall, and transport infrastructure.
- Medellin's metro system can be more workable than fragmented surface movement in other cities.
- Modern airports and upscale hotels are easier than colonial centers, island docks, or hill neighborhoods.

## Harder Environments

- Cobblestone colonial towns
- Beach or island transfers involving boats and uneven docks
- Steep neighborhoods with stairs or limited sidewalk quality
- Smaller bus terminals and rural viewpoints

## Planning Rules

- Confirm elevator, step-free entry, and bathroom setup directly before booking.
- Cluster days to minimize repeated transfers.
- If wheelchair or low-mobility travel is involved, avoid casual island or nature add-ons unless logistics are confirmed.
