# Family Travel — Colombia

## Good Family Fits

- Medellin for mild weather, parks, and manageable urban movement
- Coffee Region for scenery, short drives, and farm-style stays
- Cartagena only when heat tolerance and pool or beach downtime are built in
- Boyaca or smaller Andean towns for calmer pacing

## What Families Need More Of

- Midday rest in hot regions
- Fewer hotel changes
- Shorter transfer days
- Air-conditioned transport when traveling on the coast

## Common Mistakes

- Stacking late nights and early flights
- Treating island or jungle segments as easy add-ons with young children
- Booking steep-hill lodging with strollers or tired kids

## Planning Rule

Family Colombia trips improve dramatically when one activity per day is enough and the accommodation can absorb downtime.
