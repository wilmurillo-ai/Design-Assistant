# Santander and Eastern Andes — Playbook

## Best Use Case

Adventure travelers, canyon landscapes, and users who want Colombia beyond the standard city triangle.

## Region Profiles

| Zone | Best for | Watch-out |
|------|----------|-----------|
| Bucaramanga base | Practical logistics | Less romantic than smaller towns |
| San Gil corridor | Adventure sports and day trips | Heat and bus dependence |
| Barichara and nearby towns | Slow heritage pacing | Better with at least one overnight |

## Planning Rules

- Keep heat exposure in mind; canyon country can feel harsher than expected.
- Do not promise a quick add-on from major cities unless flights and transfers are clean.
- This region shines with 3-5 days, not a one-night squeeze.

## Good Pattern

- Base in Bucaramanga or San Gil
- One adventure day
- One heritage-town day
- One buffer for transport or recovery
