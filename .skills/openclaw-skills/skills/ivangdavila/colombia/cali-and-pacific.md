# Cali and Pacific — Playbook

## Best Use Case

Salsa, Afro-Colombian food, and travelers who want a different Colombia than the standard Bogota-Medellin-Cartagena path.

## Region Split

| Zone | Core strengths | Typical caution |
|------|----------------|-----------------|
| Cali city | Salsa, food, urban energy | Traffic and late-night pacing |
| Pacific coast links | Surf, whales, nature, remoteness | Rain, operational variability, harder logistics |

## Planning Rules

- Cali works best when nightlife and dance are treated as core activities, not side notes.
- Pacific extensions need strong flexibility and lower comfort expectations.
- Do not place a fragile Pacific transfer right before international departure.

## Good Pattern

- 3 days Cali
- 2-4 days Pacific only if weather, budget, and logistics tolerance are aligned
