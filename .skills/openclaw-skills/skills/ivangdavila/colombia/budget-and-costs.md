# Budget and Costs — Colombia

## Useful Budget Bands (Per Person / Day)

| Style | Typical range | Notes |
|------|---------------|-------|
| Budget | USD 45-90 | Hostels or simple hotels, buses or low-cost flights, selective paid activities |
| Mid-range | USD 90-180 | Comfortable hotels, some flights, strong food spend, guided day trips |
| Premium | USD 180+ | Prime stays, island or jungle logistics, private transfers, top dining |

## Major Cost Drivers

- Internal flights booked late
- Caribbean islands and Amazon logistics
- Holiday peaks and long weekends
- Premium neighborhoods or boutique stays
- Private transport for scattered rural plans

## Cost Control Levers

- Reduce region count before cutting daily comfort
- Use one strong domestic flight instead of two long surface transfers
- Stay longer in each base
- Book island or frontier segments earlier than city hotels

## Hidden Costs Checklist

- Airport transfers and checked baggage
- Cash-only purchases and ATM fees
- Boat or park fees
- Tolls, fuel, and parking in road-trip regions
- Air conditioning or resort-style surcharges on the coast

## Planning Rule

Colombia can look cheap on headline prices and still become expensive when flights, islands, or remote logistics are stacked carelessly.
