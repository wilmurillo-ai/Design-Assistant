# Cartagena and Caribbean — Playbook

## Best Use Case

History, heat, beaches, and short high-contrast trips where the user wants atmosphere more than low-friction comfort.

## Region Profiles

| Zone | Best for | Caution |
|------|----------|---------|
| Walled city and Getsemani | Architecture, bars, walking, classic first-timer energy | Heat, markups, and street sellers |
| Bocagrande and nearby coast | Easier hotel stock and beach access | Less historic atmosphere |
| Rosario and island add-ons | Water days and resort rhythm | Sea conditions and limited flexibility |
| Santa Marta / Tayrona extension | Nature plus coast | Heat, transfers, closure checks |

## Seasonal Rule

Coastal heat and humidity are always part of the plan; sea and wind conditions decide whether island add-ons are worth the risk.

## Execution Notes

- Use early mornings and late afternoons for heavy walking.
- Keep island plans away from departure days.
- Push users to decide whether they want historic city depth or pure beach time, because those are different trip styles.
