# Coffee Region — Playbook

## Best Use Case

Scenic small-town trip with coffee estates, viewpoints, hikes, and softer-paced travel.

## Core Bases

- Salento/Cocora for iconic scenery and hiking access
- Pereira or Armenia for smoother transport and hotel practicality
- Filandia or smaller towns for slower rhythm and repeat meals

## Planning Rules

- Do not turn the region into a daily hotel-hop circuit.
- Weather matters more than the photo list; keep one flexible outdoor day.
- Pair coffee experiences with town time, not just plantation tours.

## Good 4-6 Day Pattern

- One town-focused base
- One coffee-estate or farm day
- One scenery or hike day
- One lighter food or market day

## Trap

Visitors often underestimate rain, winding roads, and how much better the region feels when it is not rushed.
