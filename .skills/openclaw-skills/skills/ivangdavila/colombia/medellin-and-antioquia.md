# Medellin and Antioquia — Playbook

## Best Use Case

Mild-climate city trip with strong nightlife, urban design interest, and easy access to Antioquia day trips.

## Region Split

| Zone | Core strengths | Caution |
|------|----------------|---------|
| Medellin urban core | Cafes, nightlife, city viewpoints, metro-based movement | Tourist-price pockets and weekend crowding |
| Eastern Antioquia | Guatape, reservoir scenery, short escapes | Day-trip crowding and road timing |
| Southwest Antioquia | Coffee and pueblo rhythm | Longer access and fewer shortcuts |

## Planning Rules

- Medellin works best with neighborhood-based days, not citywide zig-zagging.
- Keep one slower morning after a nightlife-heavy evening.
- If Guatape is included, treat it as an early-start day, not a casual add-on.

## Good 5-Day Pattern

- 3 days Medellin neighborhoods and cultural movement
- 1 Antioquia day trip
- 1 flex day for weather, rest, or a second regional excursion
