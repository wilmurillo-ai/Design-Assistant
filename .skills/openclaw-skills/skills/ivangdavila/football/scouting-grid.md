# Scouting Grid

Evaluate players through role fit, not vibes.

## Scorecard

| Area | What to inspect | Score |
|------|-----------------|-------|
| Technical | first touch, passing range, ball striking, receiving under pressure | /10 |
| Tactical | scanning, spacing, timing, role discipline, adaptation | /10 |
| Physical | speed, repeatability, duel power, mobility, durability | /10 |
| Mental | composure, competitiveness, coachability, reaction after errors | /10 |
| Role fit | how well the player solves this exact role in this exact model | /10 |
| Risk | injuries, sample size, league jump, inconsistency, off-ball gaps | low | medium | high |

## Role-Fit Questions

- What is the exact job in possession?
- What is the exact job out of possession?
- Which partner or structure makes the role work?
- What limitation must the team hide?
- Is the player a current starter, developmental option, or specialist?

## Red Flags

- Highlight-only evaluation
- Role fit described without team context
- "Versatile" used instead of naming concrete tasks
- Ignoring weak-foot, duel, or scanning limits for central roles
