# Role Cards

Use these as football shorthand, then adapt to the user's real model.

## Back Line

| Role | What good looks like | Common mismatch |
|------|----------------------|-----------------|
| Center-back stopper | wins duels, protects depth, attacks first balls | asked to be the main progresser without support |
| Center-back builder | breaks lines, attracts pressure, opens angles | exposed in large spaces with weak cover |
| Full-back runner | provides width and repeated overlap | team has no rest-defense cover behind them |
| Inverting full-back | gives central support and circulation | receives with closed body and kills tempo |

## Midfield

| Role | What good looks like | Common mismatch |
|------|----------------------|-----------------|
| 6 anchor | protects center, scans early, connects safely | isolated with two 8s both jumping high |
| 8 runner | attacks space, presses hard, supports both boxes | asked to dictate every build-up action |
| 10 connector | finds pockets, combines, plays final pass | forced to defend wide lanes without cover |

## Front Line

| Role | What good looks like | Common mismatch |
|------|----------------------|-----------------|
| Winger isolator | beats full-back, creates separation, threatens box | receives too low and never gets final-third touches |
| Wide playmaker | comes inside, links, creates overloads | team loses width because no overlap supports it |
| Stretch striker | pins center-backs and threatens behind | team never finds the run early enough |
| Link striker | sets, lays off, attacks second actions | left alone with no runners beyond |

## Pairing Logic

- Builder center-back pairs well with stopper center-back
- Inverting full-back needs a winger or center-back to hold width or cover
- One 8 runner plus one calmer connector usually balances midfield better than two identical 8s
- Stretch striker and connector 10 can unlock depth if the winger attacks the far post

## Use This Verdict Formula

`Role demand -> current evidence -> support needed -> decision`

Example:
`Progressive 6 -> scans well but slow turning -> needs double-pivot cover -> useful against mid-blocks, risky alone against high press`
