# Road Trips and Visiting in Florida

Florida travel works best when you plan around heat, storms, theme-park intensity, and corridor spread.

## Good Trip Shapes

- South Florida city + Keys extension
- Orlando/Central Florida family base with recovery days
- Tampa Bay + Gulf Coast beach loop
- Panhandle beaches with lower-density pacing

## Practical Rules

- Do not stack full-heat outdoor plans without rest and hydration windows.
- Treat hurricane season as a flexibility problem, not just a weather note.
- Use realistic drive times; Florida corridors can feel longer than the map suggests.
- If the trip includes theme parks, cruises, or Keys driving, build buffer time.

## Resident-Useful Angle

This file is not only for tourists. Use it for:
- showing new residents what is actually drivable for weekends
- comparing base-city access to beaches, parks, and airports
- deciding whether a region fits the user's preferred weekend life
