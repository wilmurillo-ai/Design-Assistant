# Family and Schools in Florida

Florida family choices usually combine school fit, commute, housing cost, storm exposure, and childcare availability.

## Ask First

1. Public, charter, magnet, private, or mixed strategy?
2. Need after-school coverage or summer coverage?
3. Full-time resident household or split-year arrangement?
4. Prioritizing quiet suburb, walkability, sports, or healthcare access?

## Decision Logic

- School district and commute should be evaluated together.
- Newer suburban areas can solve one problem while creating long daily drive chains.
- Coastal appeal may conflict with budget, evacuation stress, or school match.
- Seasonal families need clarity on whether Florida is primary school base or just part of the year.

## What to Verify

- District boundaries
- School calendars
- Transportation eligibility
- Childcare waitlists
- Special-program or language-support availability
