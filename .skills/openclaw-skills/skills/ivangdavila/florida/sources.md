# Sources — Florida

Use these sources when precise Florida rules or current workflows matter.

## State Services and Resident Tasks

- Florida state services: https://www.myflorida.gov/
- Florida Highway Safety and Motor Vehicles: https://www.flhsmv.gov/
- County tax collector finder via FLHSMV local offices: https://www.flhsmv.gov/locations/

## Taxes and Business

- Florida Department of Revenue: https://floridarevenue.com/
- Florida new business resources: https://dos.fl.gov/sunbiz/

## Storms and Insurance

- Florida Division of Emergency Management: https://www.floridadisaster.org/
- Florida insurance consumer resources: https://www.myfloridacfo.com/division/consumers
- FEMA flood map service center: https://msc.fema.gov/portal/home

## Health and Education

- Florida Department of Health: https://www.floridahealth.gov/
- Florida Department of Education: https://www.fldoe.org/
- HealthCare.gov for Florida marketplace coverage: https://www.healthcare.gov/
- Medicare: https://www.medicare.gov/

## Travel and Regions

- Official Florida travel portal: https://www.visitflorida.com/
- Florida State Parks: https://www.floridastateparks.org/

## Usage Notes

- Use official state or local sources for unstable rules.
- Use county, district, tax collector, or emergency-management portals when state pages point to local execution.
- If a portal requires an address, county, or flood-zone context, send it only when the user wants location-specific help.
