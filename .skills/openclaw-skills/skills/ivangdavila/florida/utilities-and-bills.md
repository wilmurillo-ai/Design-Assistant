# Utilities and Bills in Florida

Florida recurring costs are shaped by cooling demand, storm outages, and local infrastructure, not just advertised rent.

## What to Price Early

- Electricity and summer cooling load
- Water, sewer, and trash
- Internet quality and backup options
- HOA or condo fees
- Pest control, parking, tolls, and optional storage

## Florida-Specific Patterns

- A/C runs hard for much of the year.
- Older buildings can be less efficient and more moisture-prone.
- Storm season can turn backup power and phone-charging plans into part of normal budgeting.
- Waterfront or seasonal communities may have extra service fees and rules.

## Ask Before Budgeting

1. House, apartment, or condo?
2. Inland or coastal?
3. Full-time occupancy or seasonal?
4. Need strong home internet for remote work or telehealth?
5. HOA-covered utilities or fully separate billing?
