# Seasonal Living and Snowbirds

Use this whenever the user lives in Florida part of the year, owns a second place, or wants to become a snowbird.

## Core Questions

1. Which months are spent in Florida?
2. Is Florida the primary residence, secondary home, or a trial season?
3. Where are mail, doctors, vehicles, and key documents anchored?
4. Who handles storms or maintenance when the home is empty?

## What Changes for Seasonal Households

- Insurance and property checks matter even when the home is vacant.
- Healthcare continuity can be harder than housing setup.
- Vehicles, toll accounts, and airport strategy need to match actual stay patterns.
- Condo and HOA rules may affect rentals, absentee ownership, and storm access.

## Advice Pattern

- Keep recommendations reversible if the user is still testing Florida.
- Optimize for simplicity and resilience, not just winter weather.
- Treat storm planning for an empty home as mandatory, not optional.
