# Emergencies — India

Most India emergencies are solved with preparation: good hotel locations, transport buffers, hydration, and knowing where serious hospitals are.

## Important Basics

| Need | Best Move |
|------|-----------|
| Immediate emergency | Call `112` |
| Medical issue in major city | Use a reputable private hospital |
| Lost card / payment problem | Contact bank first, hotel second |
| Heat or food issue | Hydrate, rest, and escalate early if symptoms build |

## Strong Hospital Logic

- In major cities, serious travelers usually do better with established private hospitals
- Ask the hotel concierge or insurer for the nearest reputable option
- Keep travel insurance details accessible offline

## Common Risk Areas

- Heat exhaustion and dehydration
- Upset stomach from over-ambitious first-day eating
- Long-transfer fatigue
- Traffic-related delays that become missed connections

## Emergency Traps

- Waiting too long on heat or stomach symptoms
- Assuming every clinic is equal
- Carrying no cash backup during a payment outage
- Leaving passport and critical documents without a hotel-safe plan
