# With Kids — India

India with children works when the route is shorter, hotel quality is higher, and daily plans leave room for rest.

## Best Family Patterns

| Pattern | Why it works |
|---------|--------------|
| Delhi + Jaipur + one resort finish | Variety without too many transfers |
| Mumbai + Goa | Easy urban start plus beach recovery |
| Kerala slow route | Boats, greenery, softer pacing |

## What Helps Most

- Non-stop or short-connection arrival where possible
- Hotels with reliable breakfast, pool, and solid hygiene
- One major sight per half day, not three
- Drivers or transport plans that avoid repeated negotiation

## Family Traps

- Overdoing monuments in heat
- Booking too many one-night stays
- Assuming every child will handle spice, noise, and crowds the same way
- Picking a cheap hotel over a calm, clean, well-located one
