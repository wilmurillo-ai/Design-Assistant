# Apps — India

Keep the toolkit small and reliable. Most users need navigation, rides, tickets, food delivery, and one backup for payments.

## Core Apps

| Need | Best Option | Backup |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Navigation | Google Maps | Maps.me offline areas |
| Cabs | Uber | Ola |
| Rail info / booking | IRCTC | A reputable OTA with clear seat display |
| Flights / hotels | Major OTA or direct booking | Booking directly with hotel for premium stays |
| Food delivery | Zomato | Swiggy |
| Messaging | WhatsApp | Normal SMS may fail on roaming |

## High-Value Setup

- Download offline maps for the arrival city
- Save hotel names in English exactly as the property lists them
- Screenshot train, flight, and driver details in case connectivity drops
- Keep one payment backup beyond the primary card

## App Traps

- Relying on live data with no offline backup
- Assuming every app supports foreign cards equally well
- Booking trains through a random reseller without checking class and boarding point
- Using delivery apps as restaurant quality filters instead of local knowledge
