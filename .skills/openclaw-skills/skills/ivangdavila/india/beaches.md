# Beaches — India

India has good beach time, but not every coastal stop is for the same traveler.

## Best Beach Areas by Goal

| Goal | Best Area | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Nightlife + restaurants | North Goa | Assagao, Vagator, Anjuna orbit |
| Quiet resort time | South Goa | Best for families and recovery |
| Laid-back alternative | Gokarna | Better for slower, simpler beach trips |
| Palm-lined coast + backwaters | Kerala coast | Better for softness than partying |
| Clearer water, island feel | Andamans | Great if the traveler can commit time |

## Reality Check

- Goa is the easiest beach add-on for most itineraries
- Monsoon changes beach quality, sea conditions, and mood dramatically
- "Beachfront" does not always mean swimmable or peaceful

## Beach Traps

- Adding a beach just because the trip feels intense
- Choosing party North Goa for a family-rest trip
- Underestimating transfer time to quieter beach properties
- Expecting Mediterranean-style beach clubs everywhere
