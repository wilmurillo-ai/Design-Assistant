# Accommodation — India

Where you stay matters more in India than shaving a little off the nightly rate.

## Best Stay Styles

| Style | Best For | Watch For |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| International business hotel | First night, late arrivals, families | Less local character |
| Boutique city hotel | Food-led city breaks | Room sizes and noise vary a lot |
| Heritage haveli / palace stay | Rajasthan | Charm can hide maintenance issues |
| Beach resort | Goa, Kerala | Location can be isolated |
| Homestay | Repeat visitors, slower trips | Needs stronger vetting on comfort |

## City Base Rules

| City | Best Base |
|------|-----------|
| Delhi | Aerocity, Connaught Place, or South Delhi |
| Mumbai | Colaba/Fort or Bandra West |
| Jaipur | C-Scheme, MI Road, or a carefully chosen heritage stay |
| Goa | Assagao/Anjuna for energy, South Goa for calm |

## What to Prioritize

- Location before room size
- Reliable AC in hot months
- Good reviews that mention cleanliness and water pressure, not just decor
- Airport or station logistics on arrival and departure days

## Accommodation Traps

- Booking deep inside chaotic market areas for a first trip
- Choosing a heritage property because it looks beautiful, not because it runs well
- Staying too far from restaurants to save a little money
- Assuming "sea view" or "fort view" means walkable to what matters
