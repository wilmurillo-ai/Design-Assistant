# Nightlife — India

India nightlife is uneven by city. Put the energy where it actually exists instead of assuming every destination works after dark.

## Best Cities for Nights Out

| City | Best For | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| Mumbai | Bars, lounges, late dinners | Best all-round nightlife city |
| Goa | Beach clubs, villa parties, cocktail spots | Strongest nightlife destination overall |
| Delhi | Lounges, restaurants, polished bars | More venue-driven than street energy |
| Bengaluru | Brewpubs and relaxed group nights | Best if the route already includes it |

## Good Night Logic

- Use **Mumbai** for one polished city night
- Use **Goa** for nightlife-centric days
- In Jaipur or heritage routes, keep nights more about courtyards, cocktails, and dinners than clubbing

## Nightlife Traps

- Long cross-city transfers before dinner in Mumbai or Delhi
- Expecting spontaneous late-night walkability like Europe
- Choosing nightlife-heavy Goa bases for travelers who want sleep
- Underestimating dress codes and entry filters at some higher-end venues
