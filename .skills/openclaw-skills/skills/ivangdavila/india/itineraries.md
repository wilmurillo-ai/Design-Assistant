# Itineraries — India

Keep India trips narrower than instinct suggests. Quality comes from fewer bases and better sequencing.

## 7 Days — First India Trip

**Route:** Delhi -> Agra -> Jaipur -> Delhi

- **Why it works:** Classic, varied, and realistic for a first-timer
- **Best for:** First visit, heritage-heavy trip, winter travel
- **Do not add:** Mumbai or Goa unless the user drops Agra or Jaipur

## 10 Days — First Trip With Breathing Room

**Route:** Delhi -> Agra -> Jaipur -> Udaipur

- Adds a softer finish after Delhi and Jaipur
- Better for couples and slower travelers
- Fly out of Udaipur if possible instead of backtracking hard

## 10 Days — City + Beach

**Route:** Delhi -> Jaipur -> Goa

- Best for travelers who want one heritage segment and one decompression segment
- Strong option for honeymoon-style or premium trips
- Avoid if traveling during peak monsoon for beach expectations

## 14 Days — India Sampler

**Route:** Delhi -> Agra -> Jaipur -> Mumbai -> Goa

- Best for travelers who want North India, one major city, and a coast finish
- Requires flights between Jaipur/Mumbai and Mumbai/Goa
- Keep hotel quality high because pace is already full

## 14 Days — South India Reset

**Route:** Kochi -> Munnar -> Alleppey -> Fort Kochi / Marari -> Goa or Bengaluru out

- Best for repeat visitors, families, and slower pacing
- Better during months when North India is too hot
- Do not pair with a rushed Golden Triangle unless the traveler loves constant movement
