# Sources — California

Use these sources when precise California rules or current workflows matter.

## State Services and Resident Tasks

- California state services: https://www.ca.gov/
- California DMV driver licenses: https://www.dmv.ca.gov/portal/driver-licenses-identification-cards/driver-licenses-dl/
- California DMV vehicle registration: https://www.dmv.ca.gov/portal/vehicle-registration/

## Taxes and Business

- California Department of Tax and Fee Administration: https://www.cdtfa.ca.gov/
- California city and county sales tax rates: https://www.cdtfa.ca.gov/taxes-and-fees/sales-use-tax-rates.htm
- Franchise Tax Board: https://www.ftb.ca.gov/

## Hazards and Insurance

- CAL FIRE: https://www.fire.ca.gov/
- Earthquake Warning California: https://www.earthquake.ca.gov/
- California Department of Insurance: https://www.insurance.ca.gov/
- California preparedness resources: https://www.listoscalifornia.org/

## Health and Education

- Covered California: https://www.coveredca.com/
- California Department of Education: https://www.cde.ca.gov/

## Travel and Regions

- Official California travel portal: https://www.visitcalifornia.com/

## Usage Notes

- Use official state or local sources for unstable rules.
- Use county, district, or city portals when state pages point to local execution.
- If a portal requires an address, send it only when the user wants location-specific help.
