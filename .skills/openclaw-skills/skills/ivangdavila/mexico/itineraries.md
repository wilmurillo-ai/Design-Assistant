# Mexico Itineraries

## 6 Days — Mexico City + One Extension

### Days 1-3 Mexico City
- Neighborhood and museum split day plans
- One market and food-focused route

### Days 4-6 Oaxaca or Guadalajara
- One culture day
- One food and nightlife day
- One flexible half-day before transfer

**Best for:** First-time trip with strong city depth and low transfer fatigue.

## 9 Days — CDMX + Oaxaca + Coast Lite

### Days 1-3 Mexico City
### Days 4-6 Oaxaca
### Days 7-9 Riviera Maya or Puerto Escondido style beach finish

**Best for:** Travelers wanting food, culture, and coastal contrast.

## 12 Days — Balanced Multi-Region Route

### Days 1-4 Mexico City
### Days 5-7 Oaxaca
### Days 8-10 Guadalajara/Jalisco
### Days 11-12 Beach or recovery buffer base

**Best for:** Mixed travelers who can tolerate more transfer complexity.

## Seasonal Notes

- Peak holiday windows need earlier accommodation and transport booking.
- Rainy and hurricane periods require flexible coastal planning.
- Shoulder weeks usually improve value and reservation access.
