# Budapest Itineraries

## 1 Day — Essential Highlights

**Theme**: See the icons, taste the culture

### Morning (9:00-13:00)
- **9:00** Start at Széchenyi Thermal Bath (2 hours)
  - Arrive at opening to avoid crowds
  - Experience outdoor pools, steam rooms
- **11:30** Walk through City Park
  - Vajdahunyad Castle exterior
  - Heroes' Square photos
- **12:30** Lunch in District VI
  - Recommendation: Menza (Hungarian classics, modern twist)
  - Or grab lángos from street vendor

### Afternoon (13:30-18:00)
- **13:30** Walk down Andrássy Avenue
  - Opera House exterior (or quick tour if time)
  - Terror House (exterior only if 1 day)
- **14:30** St. Stephen's Basilica
  - Interior + tower for views (30-45 min)
- **15:30** Walk to Parliament
  - Exterior photos, Shoes on the Danube memorial
- **16:30** Cross Chain Bridge to Buda
- **17:00** Funicular or walk up to Castle District
  - Fisherman's Bastion for sunset views
  - Matthias Church exterior

### Evening (18:30+)
- **18:30** Dinner in Castle District or District V
  - Upscale: Halászbástya Restaurant (views + Hungarian)
  - Budget: walk back to Pest
- **20:30** Szimpla Kert ruin bar
  - One drink minimum at the original
- **22:00** Night walk along Danube
  - Parliament illuminated

## 3 Days — Proper Exploration

### Day 1: Buda & Castle

**Morning**
- **9:00** Buda Castle District
  - Matthias Church (interior)
  - Fisherman's Bastion (early = no crowds)
  - Walk the medieval streets
  - Hospital in the Rock tour (book ahead)
- **12:30** Lunch: Baltazár or Zone for views

**Afternoon**
- **14:00** Gellért Hill climb
  - Cave Church on the way
  - Citadella and Liberty Statue
  - Panoramic views of entire city
- **16:00** Gellért Bath
  - Art Nouveau splendor
  - 2 hours minimum

**Evening**
- **19:00** Dinner in District XI
  - Hadik Kávéház for local vibe
- **21:00** Walk Chain Bridge at night

### Day 2: Pest & Culture

**Morning**
- **9:00** Great Market Hall
  - Browse ground floor
  - Breakfast upstairs (lángos, kolbász)
- **10:30** Dohány Street Synagogue + Jewish Quarter walk
  - Book tour in advance
- **12:30** Lunch: Mazel Tov or Kőleves

**Afternoon**
- **14:00** Walk Andrássy Avenue
  - House of Terror (2 hours, intense)
- **16:30** Heroes' Square & City Park
  - Vajdahunyad Castle walk

**Evening**

  - Start Szimpla Kert
  - Continue Instant-Fogas
  - End at Ellátó Kert (more local)

Follow 3-day itinerary above.

## Practical Routing Tips

### Walking Distances
- Castle District circuit: 2-3 km
- Pest center (Parliament to Market Hall): 2 km
- Andrássy Avenue full length: 2.5 km
- Gellért Hill climb: 1 km but steep

### Transit Notes
- Metro closes ~23:30, night buses replace
- Tram 2 is tourist-scenic (Danube view)
- HÉV to Szentendre: 40 min, use monthly pass
- Airport: Bus 200E + Metro or miniBUD shuttle

### Energy Management
- Thermal baths = recovery, schedule after walking days
- Castle District is uphill — do morning when fresh
- Museum days = rest legs
- Ruin bars = late night, don't plan early next day

### Weather Adjustments
- **Rain**: Museums, covered market, thermal baths
- **Heat**: Morning activities, afternoon baths, evening walks
- **Cold**: Thermal baths become the activity, warm cafés
- **Snow**: Castle District magical but slippery
