# Interview Preparation — Job Search

## Pre-Interview Checklist

1. **Company research** — funding, news, product, competitors
2. **Interviewer lookup** — LinkedIn, their background, shared connections
3. **Role deep-dive** — re-read JD, map your experience to each requirement
4. **Questions prepared** — 3-5 thoughtful questions showing research

## Common Questions by Type

### Behavioral (STAR Format)
| Question | What They Want |
|----------|---------------|
| "Tell me about yourself" | 2-min narrative: past → present → why here |
| "Biggest challenge" | Problem-solving, resilience |
| "Conflict with colleague" | Communication, maturity |
| "Why leaving current role" | No badmouthing, positive framing |

### Career Changer Questions
| Question | Strategy |
|----------|----------|
| "Why the switch?" | Authentic story + transferable skills |
| "How will you ramp up?" | Concrete learning plan |
| "What do you bring?" | Unique perspective from old industry |

### Senior-Level Questions
| Question | Strategy |
|----------|----------|
| "Why leave after X years?" | Growth framing, not escape |
| "Management style" | Specific examples, not theory |
| "Disagree with leadership" | Diplomatic but with backbone |

## Red Flags FROM Companies

- Interviewer unprepared or late
- Can't explain role clearly
- Evasive about compensation
- Negative talk about former employees
- "We're like a family" (boundary issues)
- Pressure to decide immediately

## Post-Interview

1. Send thank-you within 24h (personalized, not template)
2. Log notes while fresh: questions asked, vibes, concerns
3. Update `applications.md` with status and next steps
4. Set follow-up reminder if no response in stated timeline
