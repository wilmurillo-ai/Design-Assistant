# Useful Apps for Ireland Travel

## Transport and Navigation

- **TFI Live** — public transport real-time support
- **TFI Journey Planner / Go tools** — route and ticket support
- **Google Maps** — baseline walking and driving utility

## Booking and Dining

- **OpenTable / local booking links** — useful in high-demand cities
- **Airline and rail official apps** — more reliable disruption notices

## Weather and Outdoors

- **Met Eireann** — official weather reference
- **AllTrails / offline map tools** — useful for hikes and route backup

## Practical

- **Revolut/Wise-style apps** — payment and exchange management
- **Taxi apps by city** — useful after late-night events

## Workflow Tip

Use aggregator discovery first, then confirm on official operator pages before payment for high-value bookings.
