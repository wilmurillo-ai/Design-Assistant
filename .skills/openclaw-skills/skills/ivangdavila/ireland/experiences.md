# Signature Experiences in Ireland

## Urban Experiences

- Dublin literary and pub-culture split day
- Cork food-market and harbor-town pairing
- Galway music-session night with coastal daytime walk

## Coastal and Nature Experiences

- West-coast scenic drives with structured stop windows
- Clifftop viewpoints with early/late crowd strategy
- Connemara and Kerry day routes built around weather flexibility

## Seasonal Experiences

| Season | Good choices |
|--------|--------------|
| Spring | Lower crowds, improving daylight |
| Summer | Peak festivals and long days, highest demand |
| Autumn | Great balance of atmosphere and lower pressure |
| Winter | Strong city-culture and pub-focus trips |

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Scheduling every day as weather-perfect.
2. Using one fixed plan with no alternatives.
3. Overfilling days with long drives plus late nights.
