# XMoney

> Turn messy monetization ideas into clearer revenue decisions.

XMoney is a monetization and revenue-logic skill for creators, operators, and internet businesses.

## What it does

This skill helps you:
- improve monetization strategy
- compare pricing options
- simplify payout logic
- diagnose conversion friction
- design cleaner revenue paths
- make monetization decisions easier to act on

## What it does NOT do

- process payments
- replace accounting or tax advice
- move real money
- provide regulated financial sign-off

## Works Well With

- `@ethagent/content` for content monetization
- `@ethagent/brand` for commercial positioning
- `@ethagent/launch` for offer rollout planning
