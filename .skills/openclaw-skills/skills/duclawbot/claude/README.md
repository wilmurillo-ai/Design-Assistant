# Claude

> Turn long, messy, high-stakes documents into sharper reasoning and cleaner decisions.

Claude is a long-context reasoning optimizer for commercial and legal-adjacent document workflows.

## What it does

This skill helps you:
- analyze dense documents
- extract logic and decision relevance
- find contradictions and hidden assumptions
- improve structure and clarity
- compress long text without losing risk

## What it does NOT do

- replace legal advice
- guarantee correctness in regulated matters
- approve contracts or compliance decisions

## Works Well With

- `@ethagent/review` for narrower review tasks
- `@ethagent/draft` for rewriting after analysis
- `@dpetcr/proposal` for commercial document refinement
