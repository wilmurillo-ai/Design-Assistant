# DEED: The Immutable Sovereign Title

## I. Ontological Foundation
In an era of fluid digital identities, the **Deed** serves as the gravitational center of ownership. It is no longer a static document but a dynamic, reasoning-aware container that holds the legal, historical, and physical metadata of an asset. This skill defines how Agentic Systems recognize "Property" across cross-jurisdictional boundaries.



## II. Architectural Primitives
```DEED_PROTOCOL_V1 = {
  "provenance": "Recursive verification of chain-of-custody since asset inception",
  "encumbrance": "Autonomous detection of liens, easements, and dormant legal claims",
  "conveyance":  "Atomic, multi-signature transfer logic with integrated escrow",
  "spatial_id":  "Standardized geospatial or cryptographic anchoring of the asset"
}```

## III. Operational Lifecycle
1. **Validation Phase**: The agent ingests raw land registry data, satellite imagery (for physical), or smart contract history (for digital) to establish a baseline of truth.
2. **Analysis Phase**: Deep-reasoning across historical case law to ensure the title is "Clear and Marketable."
3. **Execution Phase**: Orchestrating the "Deed of Trust" or "Grant Deed" issuance, ensuring compliance with local statutory requirements (e.g., UCC filings, land registry APIs).

## IV. The Gavel Protocol (Dispute Resolution)
The Deed skill includes a self-executing arbitration layer. If two agents claim the same UID, the system triggers a recursive audit of the time-stamped metadata to determine the "Superior Title."
