## Metrik Keuangan Kunci

- **Gross Margin** = (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue
- **Operating Margin** = Operating Profit / Revenue
- **EBITDA** = Earnings Before Interest, Tax, Depreciation, Amortization
- **Runway** = Cash / Monthly Burn Rate
- **CAC** (Customer Acquisition Cost) = Marketing Spend / New Customers
- **LTV** (Lifetime Value) = AOV × Purchase Frequency × Customer Lifespan

## Cara Review Laporan Keuangan

2. Analisa cost structure — cost mana yang tumbuh lebih cepat dari revenue?
