# Rewriting Guide

## Goal

Turn truthful but scattered candidate information into a focused resume for a specific job direction.

## Principles

- Keep facts unchanged
- Change emphasis, not reality
- Prefer role language over self-description
- Remove low-signal demographic details
- Make the first third of the resume match the target role

## Summary writing

Use 2-3 short sentences.

Include:

- strongest relevant background
- strongest relevant operating ability
- target job direction

Avoid:

- “认真负责”
- “学习能力强”
- “执行力强”
- “抗压能力强”

unless tied to evidence.

## Bullet writing

Prefer this structure:

- action
- scope
- operational value or outcome

Example:

- Coordinated cross-team scheduling, partner communication, and on-site support to keep campaign execution on track.

Avoid:

- Helped with various tasks
- Responsible for daily work
- Strong communication ability

## Track heuristics

### Operations

Emphasize:

- workflow
- coordination
- scheduling
- documentation
- execution
- support

### Content

Emphasize:

- content maintenance
- asset handling
- live support
- material organization
- visual tools

### Training / Learning support

Emphasize:

- lesson support
- event planning
- stakeholder communication
- materials preparation
- classroom or program coordination
