# Industry Map

Use this file to normalize industry labels, expand search terms, and keep each scan focused on commercial signal instead of generic news volume.

## Search Method

For each industry:
- Search in Chinese and English when relevant.
- Combine one industry keyword with one change keyword.
- Add exact date constraints when the platform supports them.
- Prefer official, investor, regulatory, and top industry media sources.

Common change keywords:
- 趋势
- 新需求
- 商业模式
- 采购变化
- 定价
- 合规
- 发布
- financing
- funding
- raised
- launch
- pricing
- demand
- regulation
- procurement

## Signal Filters

Promote a signal when it changes one of these:
- customer urgency
- budget owner
- buying criteria
- deployment architecture
- compliance requirement
- pricing model
- partner/channel strategy
- ROI story

Demote a signal when it is only:
- a vague thought-leadership article
- a repeated headline with no market consequence
- a funding story without product or customer impact
- a rumor with no credible source
