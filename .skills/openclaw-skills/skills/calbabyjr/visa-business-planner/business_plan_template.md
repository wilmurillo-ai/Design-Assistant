# Business Plan Template for Japanese Business Manager Visa

This template is designed for applicants with tech, media, or consulting backgrounds applying for Japan's Business Manager Visa. Ensure your plan demonstrates stability, continuity, and meets the visa requirements (e.g., 30M JPY capital, full-time employee, Japanese proficiency).

## 1. Executive Summary
- Business name and location
- Mission statement
- Overview of products/services
- Key financial highlights (e.g., projected revenue)
- Visa-specific: How this business meets investment and employment criteria

## 2. Company Description
- Legal structure (e.g., KK or GK)
- Business objectives
- Target market (e.g., bilingual content creation in Japan)
- Unique value proposition (e.g., tech-enabled vlogging with IT coaching)

## 3. Market Analysis
- Industry overview (use data from Statista, Japanese government sites)
- Target audience (e.g., expats in Japan needing bilingual media services)
- Competitive analysis
- SWOT analysis
- Market size and trends (e.g., Japan's digital media market projected at X billion JPY)

## 4. Organization and Management
- Management team (your background in tech/media/consulting)
- Organizational chart
- Hiring plan (at least 1 full-time Japanese employee)
- Your Japanese language proficiency and management experience

## 5. Products/Services
- Detailed description (e.g., vlogging agency offering bilingual content and IT coaching)
- Pricing strategy
- Development roadmap

## 6. Marketing and Sales Strategy
- Marketing channels (social media, partnerships)
- Sales forecast
- Customer acquisition plan

## 7. Funding Request
- Capital investment (minimum 30M JPY)
- Use of funds
- Investment sources

## 8. Financial Projections
- Income statement (3-5 years)
- Cash flow projections
- Break-even analysis
- Use the provided financial_projection.py script for assistance

## 9. Legal Requirements
- Compliance with Japanese laws
- Visa-specific documents (office lease, employee contracts)
- Risk management

## 10. Appendix
- Resumes
- Market research data
- Legal documents
- References
