# CRM Pipeline — Your Sales Team in a Chat Window

**Manage your entire sales pipeline with plain English. No Salesforce. No HubSpot. No $50K/year CRM.**

"Add lead: John from Acme, $5K deal, interested in enterprise" — tracked, staged, ready to close.

---

## The Problem

CRMs are built for sales teams of 50. You're a founder, a solo seller, a small team. You need to track 20-100 leads, not configure Salesforce for 6 months.

You forget follow-ups. Deals go stale. You lose track of who said what. And the CRM you're paying $89/month for? You stopped updating it 3 weeks ago.

## The Solution

CRM Pipeline turns your AI assistant into a sales ops teammate. Everything through natural language:

- **"Add lead: Sarah at TechCo, $12K, came from the webinar"** — created, categorized, tracked
- **"Sent proposal to Sarah, final number $11,500"** — stage updated, interaction logged
- **"Remind me to follow up with Sarah Friday"** — reminder set
- **"Sarah signed!"** — deal closed, revenue booked, stats updated
- **"How's my pipeline looking?"** — instant report with forecasts

### Why CRM Pipeline?

- **Conversational.** Talk to it like you'd talk to a sales assistant. No clicking through 15 fields.
- **Zero config.** No pipelines to set up, no custom fields to define, no workflows to configure. Works in 10 seconds.
- **Never forget a follow-up.** Every lead gets tracked, every promise gets a reminder.
- **Local-first.** Your prospect data stays on your machine. Not on someone else's server.
- **Actually useful reports.** Pipeline value, conversion rates, revenue forecast, stale deal alerts — the stuff that matters.
