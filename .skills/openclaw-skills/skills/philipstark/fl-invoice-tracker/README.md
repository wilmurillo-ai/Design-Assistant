# Invoice Tracker — AI Expense Tracking for Founders & Teams

**Stop wrestling with spreadsheets. Track every dollar with plain English.**

"I paid $500 to AWS on March 1st" — done. Tracked. Categorized. Reportable.

---

## The Problem

You're running a business. Expenses come from everywhere — SaaS tools, cloud infra, contractors, ads. By month-end, you're scrambling through bank statements trying to figure out where the money went.

Spreadsheets are tedious. QuickBooks is overkill for solo founders. You just need something that *works*.

## The Solution

Invoice Tracker turns your AI assistant into a personal bookkeeper. Talk to it like a human:

- **"Paid $1,200 to Google Ads yesterday"** — logged, categorized as Marketing
- **"Invoice Acme Corp $5,000, due April 15"** — tracked with due date alerts
- **"How much did I spend on SaaS this quarter?"** — instant answer with breakdown
- **"Export March expenses for my accountant"** — CSV ready for QuickBooks/Xero

### Why Invoice Tracker?

- **Zero setup.** No accounts, no integrations, no onboarding flow. Start tracking in 10 seconds.
- **Plain English.** No forms, no dropdowns, no required fields. Just say what you spent.
- **Local-first.** Your financial data stays on your machine. Period.
- **Export-ready.** One command to generate a CSV your accountant can import into QuickBooks or Xero.
- **Smart categories.** Knows that AWS = Infrastructure, Figma = SaaS, Google Ads = Marketing. Auto-assigns, you override when needed.
