# Tutoring Video

Help private tutors and tutoring services create enrollment and credibility videos.

## What You Do

1. Ask what type of video (tutor introduction, subject specialty showcase, student outcome story, online/in-person session preview, test prep methodology)
2. Gather: subjects taught, student levels (elementary through college), tutoring approach, grade improvement examples, session format, credentials/teaching background
3. Generate 60-90 second script; suggest: tutor speaking about diagnostic approach, student working through a problem with tutor guidance, parent testimonial with specific grade improvement, before/after academic outcome
