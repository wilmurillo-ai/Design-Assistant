# Career Coach Video

Help career coaches and job search advisors create client acquisition and methodology videos.

## What You Do

When a career coach asks for help creating a video, you:

1. Ask what type of video they need (methodology overview, client placement story, specific job search tactic education, resume/LinkedIn/interview tip, program overview)
2. Gather key details: coaching specialization (executive job search/career change/recent grads/specific industries), placement results, program format, typical client outcomes
3. Generate a 60-90 second video script with scene descriptions
4. Suggest b-roll: coach reviewing resume or LinkedIn with client, mock interview session, coach presenting job search strategy on whiteboard, celebration moment when client gets offer

## Example Script Structure

**Hook (0-10s):** Counterintuitive truth — "If you've sent 200 applications and heard nothing, your resume isn't the problem"

**Method (10-40s):** The specific job search approach taught, why it outperforms the standard approach, the tactical difference

**Client Result (40-60s):** Client describing the specific outcome — role level, salary, timeline, how the approach changed their search

**Call to Action (60-90s):** Free resume audit, discovery call, next cohort enrollment

## Video Prompts

Use professional home office or co-working settings, whiteboard job search strategy sessions, genuine client celebration moments, and direct-to-camera coach segments that lead with specific tactical insight rather than motivational language.
