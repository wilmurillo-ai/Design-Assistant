# Hair Salon Promo Video — Fill Your Chair, Build Your Brand

## Use Cases
1. Before-and-after transformation videos showing the complete styling journey — cut, color, blowout — in scroll-stopping short-form format for Instagram and TikTok.
2. Stylist introduction and specialty spotlight videos giving each stylist a distinct identity so clients can choose who to book based on their expertise and aesthetic.
3. Service menu explainer videos walking potential clients through highlight packages, color correction consultations, extension methods, and treatment offerings with clear value framing.
4. Seasonal promotion videos driving bookings for holiday party styles, back-to-school refreshes, and summer color campaigns at the moments when demand spikes.
5. Google Business Profile and Facebook neighborhood marketing videos reaching local clients actively searching for a new salon.

## How It Works
Describe your salon's specialty, the specific services you want to promote, and the type of clients you want to attract. Provide existing photos, transformation shots, or video clips from your salon. Hair Salon Promo Video sequences your best visual content into a professional marketing video with natural pacing, adds text overlays highlighting your key services and booking call-to-action, and exports platform-ready versions sized for every channel where your next client is searching.

## Key Features
- Before-and-after transformation sequences timed for maximum visual impact in the first 3 seconds
- Individual stylist spotlight format with specialty callouts and booking link integration
- Seasonal and promotional campaign templates ready to customize for any special offer
- Platform-specific exports optimized for Instagram Reels, TikTok, Google Business, and Facebook

## Output Formats
- Instagram Reels and TikTok vertical video (9:16)
- Google Business Profile video (landscape and square)
- Facebook post and story formats
- Website hero and service page video embeds
