# Sales Enablement Video — Close the Gap Between Your Best Reps and Everyone Else

## Use Cases
1. Objection handling video libraries giving every rep on-demand access to demonstrated response frameworks for the objections that kill deals before they close.
2. Discovery and qualification coaching videos walking through the question sequencing that separates qualified pipeline from activity metrics.
3. Competitive positioning and battlecard videos keeping the entire team aligned on differentiation messaging after competitive landscape shifts.
4. Product and pricing update videos ensuring reps have current positioning within days of a launch — not weeks after the PDF playbook gets emailed out.
5. New hire onboarding accelerators packaging top rep knowledge into a structured watch-and-practice curriculum that compresses ramp from months to weeks.

## How It Works
Describe your sales motion, target buyer, and the specific knowledge gap or performance problem you need to address. Provide any existing materials — call recordings, win/loss notes, competitive intel, or product documentation. Sales Enablement Video identifies the right format for each content type, builds the instructional sequence, adds on-screen frameworks and key talking points, and exports a complete video package ready for your sales stack.

## Key Features
- Role-play scenario videos demonstrating the actual conversation — not just describing what to say
- Competitive battlecard videos organized by competitor with clear differentiation positioning
- Short-form reference clips under 5 minutes designed for pre-call prep, not classroom learning
- Manager coaching guide versions of every module for team meeting facilitation
- Integration-ready exports for Highspot, Seismic, Guru, Notion, and Slack

## Output Formats
- MP4 modules for LMS and enablement platform upload
- Short-form clips for Slack channel distribution and pre-call prep playlists
- Screen-and-talk coaching videos for asynchronous rep feedback
- Interactive scenario modules with branching decision points for advanced rep development
