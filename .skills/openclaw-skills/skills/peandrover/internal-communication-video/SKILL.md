# Internal Communication Video — Leadership That Actually Reaches Everyone

## Use Cases

1. **CEO and Leadership Messages** — Recorded leadership communications for company milestones, strategic pivots, and organizational changes. Video conveys tone and authenticity that written memos cannot.

2. **Town Hall Editing** — Raw town hall recordings edited to 10-15 minute highlight reels with chapter markers, removing Q&A dead time and surfacing the key announcements for employees who missed the live session.

3. **Policy and Compliance Updates** — Video explanations of new policies, regulatory changes, and procedure updates that communicate the why behind the change — not just the change itself.

4. **Remote Team Alignment** — Weekly or monthly video updates from department heads for distributed teams that need to maintain alignment without adding more meetings.

## How It Works

Upload your recording or script and key talking points. Internal Communication Video edits the content, adds lower-thirds and chapter markers, and exports videos optimized for your internal communication channels.
