# Lead

> Turn engaged prospects into clear next actions.

Lead is a decision skill for handling commercial opportunities after engagement begins.

## What it does

This skill helps you:
- judge whether an engaged lead is worth active pursuit
- identify missing information
- detect risks and friction
- choose the next best action
- draft follow-up that has a real reason to exist

## What it does NOT do

- filter pre-contact targets
- manage the whole deal book
- write full proposals
- redesign your entire sales process

Use Prospect before contact.  
Use Lead after engagement begins.  
Use Pipeline when you need system-level control across many active deals.

## Works Well With

- `@dpetcr/prospect` for filtering and prioritizing targets before outreach
- `@dpetcr/pipeline` for reviewing the health of the whole active opportunity system
