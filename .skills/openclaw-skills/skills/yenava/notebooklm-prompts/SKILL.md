# NotebookLM Prompts

## Overview

This skill provides curated prompt templates for generating presentation slides using NotebookLM or Kael.im. Use when users need to create visually appealing slides for business, creative, or academic purposes.

## Quick Start

1. Ask user about their presentation topic and target audience
2. Help them choose a suitable style from the reference prompts
3. Customize the prompt with their content
4. Guide them to use with NotebookLM or Kael.im

## Available Styles

### A. Editorial & Business Styles
- **Modern Newspaper**: Swiss/Bauhaus style, asymmetrical layout, business professional
- **Sharp-edged Minimalism**: Grid-based, whitespace, architectural feel
- **Yellow × Black Editorial**: Bold, fashion magazine aesthetic
- **Black × Orange Creative Agency**: Dynamic, agency-style
- **For Seminar Use**: Clean, minimal text, high-sensibility

### B. Pop, Youth, and Street Styles
- **Manga Style**: Comic/book format for engaging storytelling
- **Magazine Style**: Mature-cute, sophisticated editorial design
- **Pink Street-style**: Pop, deformed illustrations, street vibe
- **Neo-Retro Dev Deck**: Pixel-art, developer-centric, 90s tech aesthetic

### C. Typography / Font-driven
- **Mincho × Handwritten Mix**: Serif + handwritten combo, stylish

### D. Artistic & Avant-Garde
- **Royal Blue × Red Watercolor**: Artistic, wet watercolor feel

### E. Professional / Premium
- **Studio / Mockup / Premium**: Apple device mockups, premium UI showcase

### F. Sports & High-energy
- **Sports / Athletic / Energy**: Dynamic, fast-paced, impactful

## Usage Tips

1. **Choose style**: Based on audience and topic
2. **Customize prompt**: Replace [Source Information] with actual content
3. **Language preference**: Add "the language should be what users said in the prompt" for multilingual support
4. **Generate**: Feed to NotebookLM or Kael.im
