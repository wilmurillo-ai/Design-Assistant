# Product Brief

## Purpose

Deliver a premium, patient-specific report for users who paid for hospital matching guidance in China. The report should help a patient quickly understand where to start, why those hospitals fit, what type of specialist profile to look for, and what practical travel planning issues matter before booking.

## What The User Is Paying For

- A reasoned shortlist of recommended hospitals
- Specialist-direction guidance tied to the case
- Actionable cost and travel context
- A polished English PDF that can be shared internally or with family

## Core Promise

- Default to 3 recommended hospitals
- Use the bundled 2025 Fudan ranking snapshot as the static baseline
- Refresh dynamic public information separately before final delivery
- Keep the report practical, evidence-aware, and readable

## Included

- Executive summary
- Patient situation snapshot
- Recommendation logic
- 3 hospital recommendations by default
- Specialist direction guidance
- Cost estimates
- Travel and logistics notes
- Next steps
- Evidence notes
- Disclaimer

## Not Included

- Appointment guarantees
- Outcome or cure guarantees
- Exact pricing guarantees
- Hidden or unverified doctor claims
- Runtime web lookup for the Fudan rankings themselves
