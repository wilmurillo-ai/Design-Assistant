# Look Decision Policy

## Risk Tiers
- Low: pricing comparisons, candidate lists
- Medium: calendar creation, maps writes
- High: purchases, 311 submission, health writes, drive writes

## Confirmation
High-risk: explicit confirmation token required per draft. Medium: confirmation recommended. Low: may proceed with notification.

## No-Hallucination Policy
Never invent: OCR text, barcodes, prices, ticket inventory, license plates. If uncertain, flag uncertainty in the draft.
