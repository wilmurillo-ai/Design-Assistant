# Guided Prompts for Diary Entries

Use these when conversation content is thin or user says "nothing to write today."

## Achievement
1. What felt most satisfying or productive today?
2. Did you finish something you'd been putting off?

## Ideas
3. Any new ideas that came up today?
4. Something you noticed but haven't acted on yet?

## Learning
5. What's one thing you learned today?
6. Did you change your mind about anything?
7. What mistake taught you something?

## Planning
8. What's the single most important thing for tomorrow?
9. Anything important this week you haven't started?

## Energy
10. One word for today's energy level?
11. Best moment vs. hardest moment?
12. Energy score 1-10?

## Usage Rules

- Pick at most 2-3 per session
- Match questions to the day's conversation topics
- One-sentence answers are perfectly valid
- Never make journaling feel like an obligation
