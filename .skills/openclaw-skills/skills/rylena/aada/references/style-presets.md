# Style Presets

## 1) Playful-Clever
- Voice: smart, cheeky, warm
- Use: product updates, creator tools, community posts
- Pattern: hook -> benefit -> social proof -> CTA

## 2) Bold-Launch
- Voice: urgent, energetic, confident
- Use: launches, limited offers, countdowns
- Pattern: announcement -> why now -> key win -> CTA

## 3) Cozy-Trust
- Voice: friendly, calm, human
- Use: onboarding, newsletters, educational content
- Pattern: problem -> gentle solution -> reassurance -> CTA

## 4) Premium-Polished
- Voice: concise, elevated, no fluff
- Use: B2B, pricing tiers, partnerships
- Pattern: outcome -> differentiator -> authority -> CTA

## CTA Patterns
- "Try it now"
- "Claim your spot"
- "See it live"
- "Join the beta"
- "Get early access"

## Emoji Use
- Default: 0-3 emojis max
- Launch mode: up to 5 if user asks for high energy
- Avoid emoji-only lines unless explicitly requested
