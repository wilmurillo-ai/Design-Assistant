## IV. Backlink Strategy

### 4.1 Link Types

- **Internal Link**: Links within the same site
- **External Link**: Links pointing to external sites
- **Backlink**: Links from other sites pointing to us
  - Source: "Internal Links Are as Important as External Links"

### 4.2 Methods to Acquire Backlinks

1. **Free submission for backlinks**: Sites add our link in exchange for free inclusion
2. **Post links**: Reddit, forums, and other platforms
3. **Submit to AI directories**: Proactively submit to AI navigation sites
4. **Build a tool site matrix**: Each tool site links to the main site
   - Be careful to avoid being classified as a PBN
   - Source: "What is PBN? Why Does Google Penalize PBNs?"

### 4.3 Avoiding PBN Classification

- Each tool site should have real value and genuine traffic
- Use different templates
- Each site has independent branding and logo
- Diverse and natural backlink sources
  - Source: "How Do We Prevent Being Classified as a PBN?"
