## X. Monetization Methods

### 10.1 Adsense Advertising

- Suitable for content sites and tool sites
- Simplest way to monetize traffic
- Application requirements: Complete website with sufficient content
  - Source: "Details for Submitting Adsense Application"

### 10.2 Paid Subscriptions

- Tool sites are more suitable for charging
- Western users have high hourly wages and are willing to pay
- Pricing: $9.9-$49.9/month is common
  - Source: "Plain Language Explanation of Overseas Website Revenue Logic"
