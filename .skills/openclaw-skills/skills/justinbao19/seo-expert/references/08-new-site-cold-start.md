## VIII. New Site Cold Start

### 8.1 Time Expectations

- **New keywords**: Launch today, might have traffic tomorrow
- **Medium competition keywords**: 1-3 months
- **High competition keywords**: 6+ months
  - Source: "How Long Does It Take for a Page to Rank After Going Live"

### 8.2 New Site Honeymoon Period

- Google gives new sites short-term test rankings
- Rankings may drop after the honeymoon period
- Continuous optimization needed to maintain rankings
  - Source: "Retrospective: Recovery After Impressions Dropped from 700+ to 40+"

### 8.3 Bulk Page Publishing for New Sites

- **New sites should NOT publish pages in bulk**: Will be classified as spam sites
- Correct approach: Create quality pages one by one
- After getting indexed with rankings, gradually add more
  - Source: "Bulk Publishing Pages on New Sites is a Death Sentence"
