## XIII. Backlink Building Deep Dive

> Based on 12 backlink-focused articles (2023-2026)

### 13.1 Basic Link Concepts

- **Internal Link**: Links between pages on the same website, homepage → inner page, inner page → inner page
  - Source: "Website Building Step 5: Inner Pages and Internal Links"

- **External Link**: Links from your site pointing to external websites
  - Source: "Illustrated Guide: Internal Links, External Links, Backlinks, Forward Links"

- **Backlink**: Links from other websites pointing to your site, a core SEO metric
  - Source: "Illustrated Guide: Internal Links, External Links, Backlinks, Forward Links"

- **Link direction determines the name**: A→B, for A it's an external link, for B it's a backlink, essentially the same link
  - Source: "Illustrated Guide: Internal Links, External Links, Backlinks, Forward Links"

### 13.2 Importance of Backlinks

- **Google's Three Ranking Factors**:
  1. Title/H1 ensures the page enters the keyword ranking pool
  2. **Backlinks help you reach Google's top 10**
  3. Website experience determines final position
  - Source: "Simple Summary of How Google Rankings Work"

- **Backlinks can turn into money**: 500 backlinks can bring $50K MRR
  - Source: "Added 500 Backlinks, Achieved $50K MRR"

- **Internal links are as important as external links**: Proper internal links help transfer authority and improve indexing speed
  - Source: "Revisiting Internal Link Building: Internal Links Are as Important as External Links"

### 13.3 Judging Backlink Quality

- **Best backlink format** (effectiveness decreasing):
  ```html
  <!-- Best: Both title and anchor text -->
  <a href="URL" title="AI Sticker Maker">AI Sticker Maker</a>
  
  <!-- Good: Only anchor text -->
  <a href="URL">AI Sticker Maker</a>
  
  <!-- Worse: Anchor text is brand name -->
  <a href="URL">Sticker.Show</a>
  
  <!-- Worst: Anchor text is the link itself -->
  <a href="URL">https://sticker.show/</a>
  ```
  - Source: "Case Study: 16 Personality Test Website with 20.58 Million Monthly Visits"

- **Nofollow backlinks still have value**: Google has modified its algorithm, nofollow links also pass authority
  - Source: "Don't Dismiss Nofollow Backlinks, They Help Improve Authority"

- **Relevance + Authority of backlink sources**:
  - Relevance: HVAC company getting links from renovation sites > tech blogs
  - Authority: 1 link from DR90 Forbes > 100 links from DR30 small blogs
  - Source: "Google SEO Backlink Building Guide (2025 Update)"

### 13.4 Methods to Acquire Backlinks

1. **Leave links on websites frequently visited by Google crawlers**: Indexing speed depends on crawler visit frequency
   - Source: "Website Building Step 8: Add Backlinks, Wait to Be Indexed"

2. **Guest Post**: Find industry-related blogs, provide high-quality content in exchange for links ($200-400/link)
   - Source: "Google SEO Backlink Building Guide (2025 Update)"

3. **Digital PR**: Get media coverage through HARO, press releases
   - Source: "Google SEO Backlink Building Guide (2025 Update)"

4. **Resource Page Link Building**: Find industry resource pages, pitch your content
   - Source: "Google SEO Backlink Building Guide (2025 Update)"

5. **Broken Link Building**: Find dead links, offer your content as a replacement
   - Source: "Google SEO Backlink Building Guide (2025 Update)"

6. **Sponsor open source projects for backlinks**: Sponsor $5-$10 to leave URL in comments
   - High DR open source project homepage links are extremely valuable (consider $5000/year for DR85 projects)
   - Source: "This Backlink Method Most People Don't Think Of"

7. **Build great products for organic sharing**: GPTsHunter got 4,700 backlinks within 4 days of launch
   - Core strategy: Fast launch + solving scarce needs + open community promotion
   - Source: "How They Got 4,700 Backlinks in 4 Days"

### 13.5 Internal Link Building Key Points

- **Functions of internal links**:
  - Help users navigate the site easily
  - Help crawlers index all pages
  - Transfer authority between pages
  - Source: "Revisiting Internal Link Building: Internal Links Are as Important as External Links"

- **Internal link best practices**:
  - List important sections in navigation
  - Show latest and popular page lists on homepage
  - Add related page lists within pages
  - Inner pages link to homepage and parent categories
  - List important pages in footer
  - Source: "Revisiting Internal Link Building: Internal Links Are as Important as External Links"

- **Six-Word Mantra: Categorize and List**
  - Source: "Revisiting Internal Link Building: Internal Links Are as Important as External Links"

### 13.6 Inner Page Backlink Strategy

- **Should inner pages have backlinks? Four statements + Five-step check**:
  1. Any link can transfer authority (both internal and external)
  2. Google's minimum ranking unit is the page
  3. Any page needs sufficient competitiveness to rank
  4. Every keyword search result is a leaderboard, pages compete with each other
  - Source: "Should Inner Pages Have Backlinks? Remember Four Statements, Learn Five-Step Check"

- **Five-Step Check Method**:
  1. Is On Page SEO done well?
  2. Is page competitiveness sufficient?
  3. How competitive are pages in keyword search results?
  4. How to improve competitiveness? (Internal links + External links + Content)
  5. Has enough time passed? (3-4 months of continuous backlink building will show results)
  - Source: "Should Inner Pages Have Backlinks? Remember Four Statements, Learn Five-Step Check"

### 13.7 Backlink Pitfalls to Avoid

- ❌ Buying $5 for 1000 backlinks on Fiverr (junk links, may get penalized)
- ❌ Over-optimizing anchor text (100 backlinks with exact match will be flagged as spam)
- ❌ Only looking at DR, ignoring relevance
- ❌ New site suddenly adding 100 backlinks in a month (abnormal growth rate will be suspicious)
- ❌ Even the worst backlinks are better than no backlinks
  - Source: "Google SEO Backlink Building Guide (2025 Update)"

### 13.8 Time and Budget Recommendations

- **Backlink building is a long-term project**: 3-6 months to see significant changes
- **With limited budget**: First do on-site optimization, focus on 10-20 high-quality backlinks
- **10 high-quality backlinks > 1,000 junk backlinks**
  - Source: "Google SEO Backlink Building Guide (2025 Update)"
