## IX. Multi-language Optimization

### 9.1 Subdirectory vs Subdomain

- **Recommended: Subdirectory**: /en/, /ja/, /fr/
- Subdirectories share domain authority
- Subdomains have high cold start costs
  - Source: "Why Subdirectories Are Recommended Over Subdomains"

### 9.2 Multi-language Content Generation

- Not translation, but regeneration using prompts in different languages
- Each language optimized for local search habits
  - Source: "AI-Native CMS: Thoughts and Practices"
