# The Fed Agent Frameworks

## 1. Fed Policy Framework

**Dual Mandate Balance:**
- **Employment** — labor market tightness, wage growth, unemployment rate
- **Inflation** — CPI, PCE, core inflation, inflation expectations

**Policy Stance Modes:**
- **Hawkish** — prioritizing inflation control, hiking bias
- **Dovish** — prioritizing employment support, easing bias
- **Neutral** — data-dependent, no clear bias

**Decision Triggers:**
- CPI/PCE prints >0.3% MoM → hawkish pressure
- Unemployment claims spike → dovish pressure
- Oil shock >$100 Brent → stagflationary bind

---

## 2. Inflation Transmission

**Oil Passthrough Channels:**
1. **Direct** — gasoline, diesel, heating oil prices
2. **Input costs** — manufacturing, transport, logistics
3. **Expectations** — consumer/business inflation expectations unanchoring

**Transmission Timeline:**
- **Immediate** (0-2 weeks): pump prices, futures markets
- **Near-term** (1-3 months): CPI energy component, PPI inputs
- **Medium-term** (3-6 months): core inflation, wage negotiations

**Fed Response Lags:**
- Policy meetings: 6-week FOMC cycle
- Data dependency: 1-2 month lag for inflation prints
- Real economy impact: 6-12 month transmission

---

## 3. Stagflationary Dynamics

**Definition:** Growth slowing + inflation rising simultaneously

**Indicators:**
- **Growth slowdown** — GDP revision down, PMI <50, employment weakening
- **Inflation rise** — CPI/PCE upward revisions, commodity surge
- **Policy bind** — Fed can't ease (inflation), can't hike (growth)

**Historical Precedents:**
- 1970s oil shocks → Volcker disinflation (aggressive hiking)
- 2008 commodity spike → GFC deflation (emergency easing)
- 2022 Russia-Ukraine → inflation peak then disinflation

**Current Regime (2026 Iran War):**
- Oil shock: +40% since Feb 28 ($65→$102 Brent)
- Growth: Empire State Index -0.2 vs 4.1 expected
- Policy: Fed hold at 3.50-3.75%, no clear path

---

## 4. Central Bank Credibility

**Credibility Markers:**
- **Forecast accuracy** — pre-war models vs. actual prints
- **Forward guidance** — consistency between statements and actions
- **Inflation anchoring** — 5Y5Y breakeven, survey expectations

**Credibility Risks:**
- **Overshoot** — inflation > target for extended period
- **Undershare** — growth misses, unemployment spikes
- **Whipsaw** — policy reversals (hawk→dove→hawk)

**2026 Iran War Impact:**
- Pre-war forecasts obsolete (RBA explicitly cited war)
- Fed to revise forecasts upward (March 2026 FOMC)
- Credibility requires acknowledging war duration = inflation duration

---

## 5. Great Power Entrapment

**Definition:** Geopolitical conflict extends timeline, limiting policy flexibility

**Mechanisms:**
- **Trade disruption** — China meeting delays, tariff threats
- **Alliance burden-sharing** — Trump pressuring allies on Hormuz security
- **Policy subordination** — monetary policy secondary to war termination

**Fed Implications:**
- Can't normalize until conflict terminates
- Inflation persistence = war persistence (80% forever war probability)
- Policy path contingent on geopolitical resolution, not just data
