# 🌿 Organic Nature (Style ID: nature-organic)

## Vibe
Grounded, Sustainable, Gentle, "Earth Knows Best".
Best for: Wellness brands, organic food, eco-packaging, botanical studios, yoga/meditation apps, sustainable fashion.

## 1. Color Palette (System)

| Token | Hex | Role |
|-------|-----|------|
| `bg-page` | `#F2EDE4` | Raw linen / unbleached cotton |
| `bg-surface` | `#FAFAF6` | Near-white with warm undertone |
| `text-primary` | `#2D2A24` | Dark bark brown |
| `text-secondary` | `#7C7061` | Weathered driftwood |
| `accent-moss` | `#5A7052` | Deep moss green |
| `accent-clay` | `#B8694A` | Terracotta / fired clay |
| `accent-sand` | `#D4B896` | Desert sand |
| `border` | `rgba(90,112,82,0.15)` | Subtle green line |

## 2. Typography
- **Headings**: `Freight Display`, `Canela`, `Recoleta`. (Weight: 400/500, Tracking: -0.01em, Mixed serif weights).
- **Body**: `Jost`, `DM Sans`, `Nunito`. (Weight: 300/400, Line-height: 1.75 — breathable).
- **Accent**: Hand-lettering style fonts (`Caveat`, `Sacramento`) for small callouts only.

## 3. Visual Treatments
- **Radius**: `12px` base, but prefer organic blob shapes over perfect circles/squares.
- **Shadows**: Warm-tinted, very soft.
    - `shadow-sm`: `0 2px 8px rgba(45,42,36,0.06)`
    - `shadow-lg`: `0 10px 40px rgba(45,42,36,0.10)`
- **Texture**: Subtle linen or watercolor paper grain overlay.
- **Borders**: Thin, slightly imperfect strokes — avoid perfectly crisp lines.
- **Illustration Style**: Hand-drawn botanical line art as decorative elements.

## 4. Layout Principles
- **Generous White (Linen) Space**: Let content breathe — never dense.
- **Asymmetric but Balanced**: Slight irregularity feels intentional, not broken.
- **Photography**: Lifestyle shots with natural light, slight overexposure, no harsh flash.
- **No Hard Geometry**: Prefer soft dividers, wavy separators, or botanical motifs over straight lines.
