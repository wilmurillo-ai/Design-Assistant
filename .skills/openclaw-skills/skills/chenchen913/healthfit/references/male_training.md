# Male-Specific Training Guide

> **Purpose:** Training recommendations tailored for male physiological characteristics  
> **Last Updated:** 2026-03-17  
> **Evidence Base:** NSCA, ISSN position stands

---

## M1: Comprehensive Muscle Building

**Focus:** Compound movements for overall muscle development

**Core Movements:**
- Squat (barbell, goblet, front squat variations)
- Deadlift (conventional, Romanian, sumo)
- Bench Press (flat, incline, decline)
- Overhead Press (barbell, dumbbell)
- Rows (bent-over, seated cable, single-arm dumbbell)
- Pull-ups/Chin-ups

**Training Frequency:** 4-5 times/week

**Split Recommendation:**
- Day 1: Chest + Triceps
- Day 2: Back + Biceps
- Day 3: Rest or Active Recovery
- Day 4: Legs (Quads + Hamstrings)
- Day 5: Shoulders + Abs
- Day 6-7: Rest

**Rep Range:** Primarily 6-12RM for hypertrophy

**Progressive Overload:**
- Increase weight by 2.5-5kg when hitting top of rep range
- Track all workouts in training log

## M2: Male Sexual Function Enhancement

**Focus:** Pelvic floor muscles, testosterone promotion

### Pelvic Floor Training (Kegel Exercises)

**Identification:**
- Stop urine flow mid-stream (only for identification, not regular practice)
- The muscles you use are pelvic floor muscles

**Basic Kegel:**
1. Contract pelvic floor muscles
2. Hold for 5 seconds
3. Relax for 5 seconds
4. Repeat 10-15 times
5. Perform 3 sets daily

**Advanced Progression:**
- Quick Kegels: Rapid contractions (1 second hold)
- Long Holds: Up to 10 seconds
- Reverse Kegels: Push out (relaxation focus)

### Testosterone-Promoting Exercises

**Compound Movements:**
- **Squat:** Heavy loads stimulate testosterone production
  - 4-5 sets × 5-8 reps at 80-85% 1RM
- **Deadlift:** Full body compound movement
  - 3-4 sets × 4-6 reps at 85-90% 1RM
- **Farmer's Walk:** Grip strength + core stability
  - 3-4 sets × 30-60 seconds

**Training Tips:**
- Train legs at least 2 times/week
- Allow 48-72 hours recovery between heavy sessions
- Sleep 7-8 hours/night for optimal hormone production

## M3: Cardiovascular Endurance

**Focus:** Vascular elasticity, endurance improvement

### LISS (Low-Intensity Steady State)

**Protocol:**
- Intensity: 60-70% maximum heart rate
- Duration: 30-60 minutes
- Frequency: 2-3 times/week
- Activities: Jogging, cycling, swimming, brisk walking

**Benefits:**
- Improves cardiovascular health
- Enhances recovery between strength sessions
- Burns calories without excessive fatigue

### HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)

**Protocol:**
- Work: 30 seconds at 85-95% maximum effort
- Rest: 30-60 seconds active recovery
- Repeat: 8-12 rounds
- Frequency: 1-2 times/week

**Example Workout:**
```
Warm-up: 5 minutes light jogging

Main Set:
- Sprint 30 seconds
- Walk 30 seconds
- Repeat 10 times

Cool-down: 5 minutes walking
Total: 20 minutes
```

**Progression:**
- Week 1-2: 8 rounds
- Week 3-4: 10 rounds
- Week 5-6: 12 rounds

## M4: Core and Lower Back Strengthening

**Focus:** Lumbar protection, postural endurance

### Core Exercises

**Plank Variations:**
- Standard Plank: 3 sets × 60 seconds
- Side Plank: 3 sets × 45 seconds/side
- Plank with Shoulder Tap: 3 sets × 20 taps
- Plank Jacks: 3 sets × 30 seconds

**Anti-Rotation:**
- Pallof Press: 3 sets × 12 reps/side
- Cable Woodchop: 3 sets × 12 reps/side

**Dynamic Core:**
- Dead Bug: 3 sets × 12 reps/side
- Bird-Dog: 3 sets × 10 reps/side
- Ab Wheel Rollout: 3 sets × 10-15 reps

### Lower Back Exercises

**Extension:**
- Back Extension: 3 sets × 15 reps
- Superman: 3 sets × 15 reps (hold 2 seconds)
- Good Morning: 3 sets × 12 reps (light weight)

**Stability:**
- Suitcase Carry: 3 sets × 30 seconds/side
- Single-Arm Farmer's Walk: 3 sets × 30 meters/side

## M5: Flexibility-Specific

**Focus:** Hip mobility, postural adaptation

### Hip Mobility

**Dynamic Stretches (Pre-workout):**
- Leg Swings: 2 sets × 15 reps/side
- Hip Circles: 2 sets × 10 reps/direction
- World's Greatest Stretch: 2 sets × 5 reps/side

**Static Stretches (Post-workout):**
- Hip Flexor Stretch: 2 sets × 30 seconds/side
- Pigeon Pose: 2 sets × 30 seconds/side
- Butterfly Stretch: 2 sets × 30 seconds

### Thoracic Spine Mobility

**Exercises:**
- Foam Roller Thoracic Extension: 2 sets × 10 reps
- Cat-Cow: 2 sets × 10 reps
- Thread the Needle: 2 sets × 30 seconds/side

### Ankle Mobility

**Exercises:**
- Ankle Circles: 2 sets × 10 reps/direction
- Calf Stretch: 2 sets × 30 seconds/side
- Knee-to-Wall: 2 sets × 10 reps/side

## Weekly Training Template

### Beginner (0-6 months experience)

```
Monday: Full Body A
- Squat: 3×10
- Bench Press: 3×10
- Row: 3×10
- Plank: 3×30 seconds

Wednesday: Full Body B
- Deadlift: 3×8
- Overhead Press: 3×10
- Pull-up (assisted): 3×8
- Side Plank: 3×30 seconds/side

Friday: Full Body C
- Leg Press: 3×12
- Incline Dumbbell Press: 3×12
- Lat Pulldown: 3×12
- Dead Bug: 3×12/side
```

### Intermediate (6-24 months experience)

```
Monday: Chest + Triceps
- Bench Press: 4×6-8
- Incline Dumbbell Press: 3×8-10
- Dips: 3×10-12
- Tricep Pushdown: 3×12-15

Tuesday: Back + Biceps
- Deadlift: 4×4-6
- Pull-ups: 3×8-10
- Barbell Row: 3×8-10
- Bicep Curl: 3×12-15

Thursday: Legs
- Squat: 4×6-8
- Romanian Deadlift: 3×8-10
- Leg Press: 3×12-15
- Calf Raise: 4×15-20

Friday: Shoulders + Abs
- Overhead Press: 4×6-8
- Lateral Raise: 3×15-20
- Face Pull: 3×15-20
- Hanging Leg Raise: 3×12-15
```

### Advanced (2+ years experience)

```
Follow periodized program with:
- Mesocycles (4-6 weeks)
- Deload weeks every 4th week
- Peaking phases for competition
```

## Recovery Recommendations

### Sleep
- Target: 7-9 hours/night
- Consistency: Same bedtime and wake time
- Environment: Cool, dark, quiet

### Nutrition
- Protein: 1.6-2.2g/kg body weight
- Carbohydrates: Timing around workouts
- Hydration: 3-4 liters water/day

### Active Recovery
- Light walking: 20-30 minutes
- Foam rolling: 10-15 minutes
- Stretching: 10-15 minutes
