## When to Seek Medical Attention

**Consult Healthcare Provider If:**

**For Men:**
- Sudden onset of erectile dysfunction
- Persistent difficulties (>3 months)
- Pain during intercourse
- Blood in semen

**For Women:**
- Persistent pain during intercourse
- Unusual bleeding
- Sudden changes in cycle
- Suspected infections

**Important:** This module provides wellness guidance, not medical diagnosis or treatment.
