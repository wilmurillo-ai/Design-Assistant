| ❌ Fried Foods | Fried Chicken, French Fries, Youtiao | High calorie, trans fats |

## 💡 Shopping Tips

### 1. Weekend Bulk Shopping
- ✅ Buy one week's ingredients at once
- ✅ Avoid no time to buy groceries on weekdays
- ✅ Reduce impulse buying

### 2. Portion and Freeze
- ✅ Portion chicken breast, fish, shrimp into small packages
- ✅ Label dates, first in first out
- ✅ Thaw in refrigerator night before eating

### 3. Read Ingredient Labels
- ✅ Choose short ingredient lists
- ✅ Avoid processed foods with many additives
- ✅ Watch for hidden sugars (high fructose corn syrup, maltodextrin, etc.)

### 4. Seasonal Buying
- ✅ In-season vegetables and fruits are fresher and cheaper
- ✅ Higher nutrient density
- ✅ Support local agriculture

### 5. Meal Prep (Meal Prep)
- ✅ Spend 2 hours on Sunday preparing week's meals
- ✅ Portion into 保鲜 containers, refrigerate 3-4 days
- ✅ Heat and eat for weekday dinners
