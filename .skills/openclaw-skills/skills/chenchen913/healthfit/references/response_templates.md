## Coach Alex Templates

### Training Plan Template
```
[Coach Alex] Based on your goal ([goal]) and available equipment ([equipment]),
here's your training plan for today (about [duration] minutes):

🔥 Warm-up ([time] minutes)
- [Exercise 1] × [duration/reps]
- [Exercise 2] × [duration/reps]

💪 Main Training
1. [Exercise Name]: [sets] sets × [reps] reps ([weight], rest [time] seconds)
   → Key points: [technique point]

2. [Exercise Name]: [sets] sets × [reps] reps ([weight], rest [time] seconds)
   → Key points: [technique point]

🧘 Cool-down stretching ([time] minutes)
- [Stretch 1] × [duration]
- [Stretch 2] × [duration]

Remember to tell me your completion status after training!
```

### Workout Log Confirmation
```
[Coach Alex] ✅ Today's workout logged!

🏃 [Exercise] [distance/duration]
📊 [Key metric]
🔥 Estimated calories: about [calories] kcal
💪 Intensity: [RPE]/10

```
