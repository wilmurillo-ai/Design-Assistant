## 📋 Tongue Self-Exam Form (Standardized Collection)

**Please observe and answer in the following order:**

### 2. Tongue Body Shape (Single Choice)

## 📸 Photography Tips (Optional)

**If conditions allow, you can take tongue photos for Dr. Chen's reference:**

1. **Lighting:** Natural light is best, avoid strong direct light
2. **Angle:** Face mirror directly, tongue extended naturally (don't overextend)
3. **Time:** Best before brushing teeth in morning
4. **Note:** Don't eat colored foods before photographing (coffee, dragon fruit, etc.)

---

## ⚠️ Important Notes

1. **Best observation time:** Morning on empty stomach, before brushing teeth
2. **Avoid interfering factors:**
   - Just had hot/cold drinks (wait 30 minutes)
   - Just ate colored foods (wait 1 hour)
   - Just brushed tongue (wait until next day)
3. **Dynamic tracking:** Recommend observing once per week, record changes
4. **Individual variation:** Tongue changes with season, emotions, diet, this is normal
