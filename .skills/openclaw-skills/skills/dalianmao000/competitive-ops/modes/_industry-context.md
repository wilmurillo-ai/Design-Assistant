## Industry-Specific SWOT Questions

### AI / Machine Learning

**Strengths:**
- What model capabilities make this competitor stand out?
- How competitive is their pricing (tokens per dollar)?
- What is their context window size and does it enable new use cases?
- How fast is their inference speed?
- What safety measures and red-teaming do they have in place?

**Weaknesses:**
- What are their model limitations or known failure modes?
- Do they lack pricing tiers for different enterprise needs?
- What enterprise features are they missing (SSO, audit logs, etc.)?
- How limited is their multimodal capability?

**Opportunities:**
- Are there new model releases or updates coming?
- What underserved markets or verticals could they expand into?
- How strong is their API ecosystem and developer adoption?
- Are there partnerships or integrations that could accelerate growth?

**Threats:**
- How does open-source models (Llama, Mistral) threaten their positioning?
- Are big tech companies commoditizing the space?
- What regulatory risks exist around AI safety and data usage?
- Could new entrants with better pricing disrupt them?

---

### SaaS / B2B Software

**Strengths:**
- What are their retention metrics (>100% NRR indicates strong product-market fit)?
- What is their NPS score and customer satisfaction?
- How extensive is their integration ecosystem?
- What is their feature release velocity?

**Weaknesses:**
- What is their churn rate and why do customers leave?
- What feature gaps exist compared to requirements?
- Where is there UX friction or complexity?
- How confusing is their pricing structure?

**Opportunities:**
- Can they expand vertically into new industries?
- Are there partnership GTM opportunities?
- Could pricing tier optimization improve revenue?
- What adjacent features could they add?

**Threats:**
- Are competitors achieving feature parity?
- Is big tech entering their market segment?
- Are switching costs eroding due to standardization?
- Could a well-funded startup displace them?

---

### FinTech

**Strengths:**
- What regulatory licenses do they hold (banking, money transmitter, etc.)?
- What security certifications do they have (SOC2, PCI-DSS, ISO 27001)?
- How strong are their bank partnerships and integrations?
- How advanced is their fraud detection and prevention?

**Weaknesses:**
- How limited is their geographic coverage?
- What compliance gaps exist in regulated markets?
- What enterprise features are they missing?
- How narrow is their product offering?

**Opportunities:**
- How can open banking regulations create new opportunities?
- What underbanked markets could they serve?
- Is crypto or blockchain integration a viable path?
- Can they expand their financial product suite?

**Threats:**
- How might regulatory tightening affect their business?
- Are big banks competing directly in their space?
- What security vulnerabilities could be exploited?
- Could a tech giant with better distribution enter the market?
