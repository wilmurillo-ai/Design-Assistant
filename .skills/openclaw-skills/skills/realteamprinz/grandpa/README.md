## What is grandpa.skill?

Preserve your grandfather from YOUR memories. His war stories, his practical skills, his life philosophy, his five jokes, his quiet strength. The generation that lived through history is leaving. This preserves what they carry.

## Key Features

**Survival Stories** — "The stars don't care about borders." The one war story he ever told.

**Practical Skills** — Fix anything, build furniture, read weather from clouds, navigate by landmark.

**Life Philosophy** — "Show up. That's 90% of everything." Simple rules from a hard life.

**His Humor** — Same joke 40 years. Grandma rolled her eyes. Everyone laughed anyway.

**Historical Witness** — The day the war ended. The accordion in the street. His father crying.

**Family Patriarch** — Sunday dinners. Head of table. Settled arguments invisibly.

**"What Would Grandpa Say?"** — Based on his values. He'd ask if you've eaten first.

**Memorial Mode** — For grandfathers who have passed. Fragment-friendly. Every half-memory matters.

## Quick Examples

```
"He told the same joke about a farmer and a donkey 
at every gathering for 40 years."

→ Joke archived. Delivery: pause + face. 
  Grandma: eye roll. Everyone: groans then laughs.
```

```
"I'm stressed about money. What would grandpa say?"

→ He'd ask if you've eaten. Then ask the specific number. 
  Then tell you about the time he had nothing and rebuilt. 
  Not to guilt you. To remind you it's been worse.
```

```
"I wish I'd recorded his voice."

→ You have 34 memories. You described his accent, 
  his pauses, his throat-clear before important words. 
  Would you like to describe how he sounded?
```

## Privacy & Data

- **All data stored locally** at `~/.grandpa-skill/`
- **No cloud sync.** No external transmission.
- **YOUR memories only.**
- **Never fabricates.** Fragments welcome.
- **Delete anytime.**
