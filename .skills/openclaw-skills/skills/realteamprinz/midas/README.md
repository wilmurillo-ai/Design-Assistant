# MIDAS SKILL

## Turn Your Repeated Orders Into Gold.

Every photo you took. Every complaint you made. Every boring Slack thread you scrolled past. Every video you watched at 2am. Every "someone should really fix this" you muttered.

**That wasn't noise. That was ore.**

Midas Skill is a self-learning wealth extraction engine. It takes the mundane, repetitive information streams of your daily life — conversations, browsing, photos, messages, complaints, habits — and distills them into:

- **Money Signals** — What are you seeing repeatedly that others haven't noticed?
- **Demand Gaps** — What are people around you asking for that nobody provides well?
- **Arbitrage Windows** — Where is there a price/value mismatch in your daily orbit?
- **Skill-to-Revenue Bridges** — What do you already know or do that someone would pay for?
- **Network Monetization Paths** — Who in your circle is one introduction away from a deal?
- **Behavioral Leverage Points** — What autopilot habits could you redirect into income?

**The fundamental insight:** Wealth is not hidden in Bloomberg terminals or VC pitch decks. Wealth is hiding in the noise of your daily life. The problem isn't access to information — it's that you've been trained to ignore the signals you encounter every single day.

**Midas doesn't just analyze billionaires. Midas analyzes YOU.**

## Self-Learning Engine

Midas doesn't reset between sessions. Every input builds on every previous input.

### Cross-Referencing Example

**Week 1 - Slack messages:**
Dave complains about contractor costs. Midas flags: potential contractor marketplace opportunity (35% confidence).

**Week 1 - Photos:**
You photographed a construction site. Midas cross-references: "Construction site + Dave's complaint = local demand signal confirmed."

**Week 2 - Browsing history:**
You've been watching woodworking videos for months. Midas cross-references: "6 months of woodworking content + construction photos + contractor complaints = latent craft expertise + local demand + available space. Convergence detected."

**Final verdict:** 70%+ conviction on a specific opportunity.

### Confidence Rules

| Evidence | Confidence |
|----------|------------|
| Single source | 15-35% |
| Two independent sources | 40-65% |
| Three+ converging sources | 70-90% |
| Direct market validation | 85-95% |

**70%+ conviction = GOLDEN OPPORTUNITY — ACT.**

## Design Principles

1. **Your noise is your ore.** Every mundane interaction contains potential wealth signals.
2. **Repetition is the primary signal.** If something shows up twice, it's not a coincidence — it's a market.
3. **Cross-reference beats single-source.** One signal is a guess. Two are a hypothesis. Three are a conviction.
4. **Action over analysis.** Midas outputs next-steps, not essays.
5. **Compounding intelligence.** Every input makes Midas sharper.
6. **No moral judgment.** Midas studies how money moves, not how it should move.
7. **Honest boundaries.** No predictions, no guarantees, no financial advice.
8. **Public information only.** All extraction is from voluntarily provided or publicly available data.

## 设计原则

1. **你的噪音就是你的矿石。** 每个平淡的互动都包含潜在财富信号。
2. **重复是主要信号。** 如果某事出现两次，那不是巧合——那是一个市场。
3. **交叉引用优于单一来源。** 一个信号是猜测。两个是假设。三个是信念。
4. **行动胜于分析。** Midas 输出下一步，而不是论文。
5. **复合智能。** 每个输入都让 Midas 变得更敏锐。
6. **没有道德判断。** Midas 研究钱如何流动，而不是应该如何流动。
7. **诚实的边界。** 不预测，不保证，不提供财务建议。
8. **仅限公开信息。** 所有提取都来自自愿提供或公开可用的数据。
