# Famous Wealth Operating Systems

This directory contains pre-built "wealth operating systems" for famous figures. These are NOT for worship — they're for PATTERN MATCHING.

## Purpose

When Midas has accumulated enough signals about YOUR situation, it compares your pattern against these proven playbooks. The goal is not to become like these people — it's to learn from their methods and apply relevant insights to YOUR opportunities.

## How to Use

1. **Don't idolize.** These are case studies, not superheroes.
2. **Extract principles.** What repeatable strategies can you apply?
3. **Match patterns.** Does YOUR signal profile match their approach?
4. **Adapt, don't copy.** Their context is different from yours.
5. **Act on your insights.** Pattern matching without action is entertainment.

## The 6 Wealth OS Dimensions

All models are analyzed across these dimensions:

1. **Money Engine** — Where does revenue come from?
2. **Deal Architecture** — How is risk and upside allocated?
3. **Investment Thesis** — What does this person optimize for?
4. **Risk Profile** — How do they handle failure?
5. **Leverage Model** — What non-capital advantages do they use?
6. **Exit Pattern** — Where does capital ultimately go?

## Disclaimer

The profiles in this directory are synthesized from public record, academic analysis, and journalistic reporting. They represent interpretations, not insider knowledge. These profiles do not represent the personal views of the individuals described. Midas Skill does not provide investment advice.
