## The Meta-Pattern

**What's happening:**

This friend group is urban professionals (28-35) with:
- Money (willing to pay $100-400 for solutions)
- Time poverty (2 hours for dentist = whole afternoon)
- Complex lives (stock options, rental properties, pets)
- Career anxiety (transitioning, growing)

**They're all frustrated with:**
1. Services that don't respect their time
2. Services they can't trust
3. Life admin that takes mental energy

**The pattern:**
> Urban professionals will pay premiums for time savings and trust guarantees.

**This is worth BILLIONS** — which is why Angie's List, Thumbtack, TaskRabbit, Rover, and others exist. But they're all imperfect. The gap is: **guaranteed quality + time savings.**
