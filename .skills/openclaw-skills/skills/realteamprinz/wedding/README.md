## What is wedding.skill?

Your wedding planning co-pilot. Tracks budget honestly, manages vendors, solves seating charts, keeps both partners in sync, and tells you when you're about to go over budget before it's too late.

## Key Features

**Honest Budget Tracker** — "You're $1,800 over in venue + dress. At current pace, final total: $33,400."

**Vendor Intelligence** — Every conversation, contract term, and deadline in one place.

**Guest List & Seating** — "Uncle Mike CANNOT sit near Aunt Linda." Solved.

**Timeline Manager** — 12 months of deadlines. What's done, what's overdue, what's next.

**Day-Of Timeline** — Hour by hour. 8am hair starts. 3:30 ceremony. 5:30 first dance.

**Couple Sync** — "She wants a band ($4K), he wants a DJ ($1.5K). Compromise: both for $3.2K."

**Family Politics Navigator** — Divorced parents seating. Uninvited cousin response plan.

## Quick Examples

```
"How's the budget looking?"

→ $21,950 of $30,000 spent. $1,800 over in venue + dress.
  Projected final: $33,400. Cut flowers $500 + cake $200 
  to recover $700. Still $1,100 over.
```

```
"When's the photographer's balance due?"

→ $2,000 due 2 weeks before wedding. Shot list due same time.
  She's worked your venue before — knows the good spots.
```

```
"My mom wants to control the flowers since she's paying."

→ Common situation. Suggestion: Let her choose 2 of the 
  5 arrangements. You keep the bouquet and ceremony arch. 
  She feels involved. You keep creative control where it matters.
```

## Privacy & Data

- **All data stored locally** at `~/.wedding-skill/`
- **No cloud sync.** No bank connections. No vendor system access.
- **No payments.** Tracks spending, never transacts.
- **Delete anytime.** Remove the folder.
