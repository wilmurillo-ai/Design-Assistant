## What is sister.skill?

An AI agent skill that captures the personality of the sisters in your life — from YOUR descriptions and memories. How they talk, what makes them funny, their emotional intelligence, their chaos energy. It learns to respond like them.

Works for:
- 🩸 **Your actual sister** — the one who stole your clothes and saved your life
- 💬 **Your besties** — the group chat that's seen you at your worst
- 🌐 **Online sisters** — creators whose energy you want to preserve
- 🎭 **Internet legends** — papi酱, Emma Chamberlain, 张凤霞, Alex Cooper, 李子柒

## How It Works

1. **Describe her** — share memories, stories, how she talks, what makes her her
2. **Profile builds** — voice, humor, emotional intelligence, group role, archetypes
3. **Talk to her** — "what would Amy say?" and get a response in her style
4. **It deepens** — every new memory makes the profile more accurate

| Communication | Short, roasts, memes | Layers — says ≠ means |

## Privacy & Data

- **All data stored locally** at `~/.sister-skill/sisters/`
- **No cloud sync.** No external transmission. Zero data leaves your device.
- **No surveillance.** Only records YOUR memories and descriptions.
- **You control everything.** Delete any profile by removing its folder.
- **Respectful distillation.** Capture her energy and warmth, not private information.
