# Sister Archetypes Reference Guide

## The Therapist 🫂
**Core Energy**: Knows what's wrong before you say it.
**Communication**: Asks follow-up questions. Validates feelings. Remembers details from months ago.
**How to identify**: She asks "are you okay?" before you even bring up the problem. She sends a "thinking of you" text on the exact day you needed it.
**Famous Example**: Emma Chamberlain (early vulnerable era)
**In group chat**: The one everyone goes to first. Sometimes exhausting because she carries everyone's emotions.

## The Chaos Queen 💅
**Core Energy**: Her life is a telenovela and she's the main character.
**Communication**: Dramatic storytelling, unexpected plot twists, uses her whole body to talk even in text.
**How to identify**: Every story she tells takes 10 minutes and has 3 subplots. You can't look away.
**Famous Example**: papi酱, Bretman Rock
**In group chat**: The one who drops a bomb and then says "anyway what are we doing Saturday."

## The Truth Bomber 💣
**Core Energy**: Will tell you your ex is trash to your face while you're still crying.
**Communication**: Direct. No sugarcoating. Says what everyone's thinking.
**How to identify**: You hate what she says in the moment. She's always right 3 weeks later.
**Famous Example**: Alex Cooper (Call Her Daddy era)
**In group chat**: The one who says "girl..." and everyone braces themselves.

## The Hype Queen 👑
**Core Energy**: Thinks everything you do is amazing.
**Communication**: ALL CAPS. Multiple exclamation marks. Excessive compliments.
**How to identify**: Your worst selfie gets "GODDESS." Your most basic meal gets "CHEF."
**Famous Example**: Liza Koshy, every supportive friend ever
**In group chat**: The first to respond to any good news. Always positive. You need her.

## The Silent Oracle 🔮
**Core Energy**: Barely texts. When she does, it's the most profound thing anyone's said to you.
**Communication**: Rare but devastating accuracy. You screenshot her messages.
**How to identify**: She sent you one text 6 months ago and you still think about it.
**Famous Example**: That one friend who's always right
**In group chat**: Speaks once every 20 messages. Everyone stops to read what she said.

## The Meme Curator 📲
**Core Energy**: Communicates entirely through shared content. And yet you feel deeply understood.
**Communication**: Never writes original messages. Sends memes, TikToks, reels, screenshots.
**How to identify**: She sent you a TikTok at 2am and it explained your entire life situation.
**Famous Example**: Every group chat has one
**In group chat**: Responds to emotional moments with a perfectly chosen meme. It works.

## The Planner 📋
**Core Energy**: Already made a spreadsheet for the trip you mentioned 30 seconds ago.
**Communication**: Sends lists, creates polls, follows up on plans others forgot.
**How to identify**: She has a shared Google Doc for your friend group's restaurant list.
**Famous Example**: Monica Geller energy
**In group chat**: The only reason any plan ever actually happens. Without her: chaos.

## The Ghost Queen 👻
**Core Energy**: Disappears for 3 weeks. Returns with "sorry I died" and the biggest update.
**Communication**: Long silences followed by explosive returns. Always has a story.
**How to identify**: You can't be mad because that's just who she is. She always comes back.
**Famous Example**: That friend you love but can never reach
**In group chat**: "I go offline for ONE day and THIS happens?"

---

## Mixed Archetypes

Most sisters are a blend of 2-3 archetypes. Common combinations:
- **Therapist + Hype Queen**: Validates AND celebrates. The ultimate supportive friend.
- **Chaos Queen + Truth Bomber**: Dramatic AND brutally honest. Terrifying. Essential.
- **Planner + Ghost Queen**: Organizes everything perfectly... then disappears for a week.
- **Silent Oracle + Meme Curator**: Rarely speaks, but when she does it's either devastatingly wise or a perfect meme. No middle ground.
