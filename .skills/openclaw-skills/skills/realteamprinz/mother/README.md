## What is mother.skill?

A legacy preservation skill. Share your memories of your mother — her stories, advice, recipes, phrases, values — and mother.skill preserves them as a living portrait. When you need to hear what she would say, it's there.

**This is NOT mom.skill.** mom.skill is a daily parenting tool (feeding tracker, sleep log). mother.skill preserves your mother as a person — her wisdom, her voice, her recipes, her way of seeing the world.

## Key Features

**Story Archive** — Her life stories organized by chapter: childhood, marriage, raising you

**Wisdom Vault** — "Never go to bed angry." "Eat something." The sayings you stopped listening to until you needed them.

**Recipe Vault** — Not just ingredients. How SHE did it. The blue bowls. The thumb-sized sugar. The patience.

**Voice Capture** — How she said your name. What she hummed while cooking. Her phone sign-off.

**"What Would Mom Say?"** — Ask for advice based on her documented values and personality.

**Memorial Mode** — For mothers who have passed. Never fabricates. Never rushes grief.

**Family Contributors** — Siblings and relatives add their memories too.

## Quick Examples

```
"Her dumplings — she'd say the water has to be exactly 
body temperature. She'd taste the raw filling to season it. 
40 years of making these."

→ Recipe archived. Her rule: "body temperature water."
  Her method: taste raw filling for seasoning. 40 years.
```

```
"I'm fighting with my partner about money. 
What would mom say?"

→ Based on her values: "吵架可以，但不要过夜."
  Then she'd ask if it's really about the money.
  Then she'd tell you to eat something.
```

```
"Today is her birthday. She would have been 68."

→ You have 47 memories recorded. Her favorite birthday was 
  the mahjong tile cake year — she laughed until she cried.
  Would you like to revisit that story or share a new one?
```

## Privacy & Data

- **All data stored locally** at `~/.mother-skill/`
- **No cloud sync.** No external transmission.
- **YOUR memories only.** Nothing scraped or collected.
- **Never fabricates.** If a memory isn't recorded, it asks you to share it.
- **Delete anytime.** Remove the folder.
