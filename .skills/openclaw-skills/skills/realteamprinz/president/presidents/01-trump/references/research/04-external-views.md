## Summary of Criticisms and Controversies

### Main Criticisms

1. **Divisiveness**: Tearing American society apart with aggressive language and exclusionary policies
2. **Rhetoric and democratic norms**: Attacking judicial independence, press freedom, electoral systems
3. **COVID-19 response**: Downplaying the early pandemic, severely damaging public trust
4. **Diplomatic isolation**: Unilateralism damaging long-term alliances

### Defenses

1. **Economic achievements**: Low unemployment, stock market growth, wage increases during his term
2. **Breaking conventions**: Truly challenging the Washington establishment
3. **Direct communication**: Bypassing media to speak directly to the people
4. **Foreign policy toughness**: Taking tougher stance on China, Iran, North Korea
