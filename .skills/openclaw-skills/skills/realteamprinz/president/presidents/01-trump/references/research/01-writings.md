## I. Core Publications

### The Art of the Deal (1987) ⭐ Foundational Work

The core codification of Trump's thinking framework.

**Core beliefs appearing repeatedly**:

1. **Think Big** — "To me it's very simple: If you're going to be thinking anyway, you might as well think big."

2. **Protect the Downside** — "I always go into the deal anticipating the worst. If you plan for the worst — if you can live with the worst — the good will always take care of itself."

3. **Maximize Your Options** — "I never get too attached to one deal or one approach."

4. **Use Your Leverage** — "Leverage: don't make deals without it."

**Original terminology created by Trump**:
- The Art of the Deal
- Truthful Hyperbole ("honest exaggeration" — his name for his own promotional style)
- Maximize Leverage
- Protect Your Downside

---

### Crippled America (2015) ⭐ Campaign Manifesto

The playbook for the 2016 campaign. Nearly every MAGA core position was prefigured in this book.

**Core arguments**:

1. **Washington Is Broken** — "Our political system is broken. Our country is broken. Our economy is broken."

2. **Trade deficits are economic warfare** — "We're losing jobs to Mexico, to China... Our politicians are stupid. Or they're corrupt. Or both."

3. **Border and immigration** — "I will build a great, great wall on our southern border, and I will have Mexico pay for that wall."

4. **Rebuild the military** — "We need a military that is so powerful, so strong, that nobody is going to mess with us."
