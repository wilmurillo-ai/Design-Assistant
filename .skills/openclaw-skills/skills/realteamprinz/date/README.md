## What is date.skill?

Your dating memory bank. After every date, dump what you remember — their favorite restaurant, their sister's name, the trip they loved, the allergy that matters. date.skill organizes it, reminds you before the next date, and suggests thoughtful gestures based on what THEY told you.

Not a manipulation tool. Not a pickup guide. Just a second brain for people who listen but can't hold it all when they're nervous and charming at the same time.

## How It Works

**After a date** → Share what you remember → Profile builds

**Before next date** → Quick refresh → Walk in prepared

**Need a date idea** → Personalized suggestions based on THEIR preferences

**Wondering how it's going** → Green/yellow/red flag summary

## Quick Examples

```
"First date with Kai — she's a UX designer, has a cat named 
Mochi, allergic to shellfish, wants to try the Thai place on 5th"

```

```
"One month — how's it going?"

→ 🟢 Consistent, introduced you to friends, talks about future
  🟡 Cancelled once (had a reason)
  🔴 None observed
  Suggestion: pottery class she mentioned
```

## Key Features

**Date Memory Bank** — dump everything after each date, it organizes

**Pre-Date Refresh** — 30-second brief before you walk in

**Thoughtful Gesture Engine** — not "buy flowers" but "she loved Lisbon, try the Portuguese place"

**Green/Yellow/Red Flag Tracker** — honest pattern tracking when emotions make it hard to see clearly

**Allergy & Important Date Flags** — the things you absolutely cannot forget

## Privacy & Data

- **All data stored locally** at `~/.date-skill/people/`
- **No cloud sync.** No external transmission.
- **No access to anyone's social media, dating apps, or messages.**
- **Not a stalking tool.** Only records what YOU remember from conversations.
- **Delete when it's over.** Relationship ends → delete their folder.
