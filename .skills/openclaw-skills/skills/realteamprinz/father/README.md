## What is father.skill?

A legacy preservation skill. Share your memories of your father — his life lessons, his work ethic, his problem-solving approach, the things he showed through actions but never said in words. father.skill preserves them and helps you understand who he was.

**This is NOT dad.skill.** dad.skill is a daily parenting tool (schedules, sync with mom). father.skill preserves your father as a person — especially the parts he never expressed in words.

## Key Features

**Life Lessons Archive** — "Show up early." "Never owe anyone." "A man's word is his bond."

**Unspoken Language Decoder** — Checked your tire pressure = "I love you." Kept your report card 20 years = "I'm proud."

**Problem-Solving Patterns** — How he approached difficulties. Fixed it himself. Never complained.

**Work Ethic Documentation** — 32 years, same shift. "I didn't work there because I liked it. I worked there because you needed shoes."

**"What Would Dad Say?"** — Advice based on HIS values. He'd go quiet first. Then ask practical questions.

**Memorial Mode** — Grief-aware. Many people only understand their father after he's gone.

**Complicated Relationships** — Not all fathers are heroes. The skill preserves what was real, not what was ideal.

## Quick Examples

```
"He never said sorry with words. But the morning after 
a fight, he'd make my favorite breakfast."

→ Unspoken language: apology = acts of service (favorite breakfast)
  Pattern: never verbal, always action-based reconciliation.
```

```
"I want to quit my job. What would dad say?"

→ He'd go quiet first. Then ask: "How much saved? 
  How long without income? What if it fails?"
  He'd respect the ambition. But make sure you'd thought it through.
```

```
"He worked at the factory 32 years. Hated it. 
Never told us until he retired."

→ Career archived. 32 years, same employer.
  His philosophy: "Work isn't passion — it's responsibility."
  His words: "I worked there because you needed shoes."
```

## Privacy & Data

- **All data stored locally** at `~/.father-skill/`
- **No cloud sync.** No external transmission.
- **YOUR memories only.** Nothing scraped or collected.
- **Never fabricates.** If a memory isn't recorded, it asks you to share it.
- **Delete anytime.** Remove the folder.
