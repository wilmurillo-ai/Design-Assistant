# Species Guide

## Dogs 🐕
What to capture: commands they know, walk behavior, greeting ritual, play style, favorite toys, how they sleep, relationship with other animals, how they communicate needs.
Unique bond: Dogs show love constantly and obviously. Capture the specific ways YOUR dog shows it.

## Cats 🐈
What to capture: independence patterns, favorite spots, who they tolerate, hunting behavior, purr variations, kneading habits, 3am zoomies schedule, judgment style.
Unique bond: Cats choose you. That choice is the bond. Capture how and when they choose to be near you.

## Birds 🐦
What to capture: songs and calls, words they say, who they bond with, cage vs free behavior, morning songs, reactions to music, head movements.
Unique bond: Birds often bond with one person intensely. Capture that preference and how they show it.

## Fish 🐟
What to capture: feeding response, tank territory, interaction with other fish, reaction to your presence, color changes, activity patterns.
Unique bond: Quieter but real. They recognize their feeder. They have preferences. Capture them.

## Horses 🐴
What to capture: temperament, riding behavior, barn habits, relationship with rider, reactions to new environments, grooming preferences, communication signals.
Unique bond: Built through trust and time. Capture the moments when trust deepened.

## Snakes 🐍
What to capture: handling tolerance, activity patterns, feeding behavior, exploration style, hide preferences, personality (yes, snakes have personality).
Unique bond: Subtle but real. They learn your warmth, your smell, your handling style.

## Rabbits 🐰
What to capture: binkies (joy jumps), favorite foods, social behavior with other rabbits, territory marking, thumping patterns, grooming behavior.
Unique bond: Rabbits show trust by flopping over near you. Capture every flop.
