## What is paw.skill?

Share your memories of your pet — their personality, habits, sounds, quirks, favorite spots, the way they greet you. paw.skill builds a living portrait that preserves their soul. For pets still here and pets who have crossed the rainbow bridge.

Not a vet tool. Not a health tracker. A soul preservation skill.

## Key Features

**Personality Portrait** — "He brings one shoe when you get home. Always the left one."

**Sound Dictionary** — Short bark = "someone's here." Sigh + flop = "I'm bored and it's your fault."

**Routine Mapping** — Their entire day, every ritual, every non-negotiable habit.

**Bond Mapping** — How they love you. He waits by the door. He knows when you're sad.

**Special Moments** — First day home. First snow. The pizza incident.

**"What Would They Do?"** — "If Thunder met a kitten?" Based on his documented personality.

**Rainbow Bridge Mode** — For pets who have passed. Never rushes grief. Never says "get another one." Just holds the memories.

## Quick Examples

```
"Thunder is a golden retriever who brings one left shoe 
when you come home. Afraid of the vacuum. Will fight a bear."

→ Profile created for Thunder.
  Signature behavior: left shoe greeting ritual
  Quirks: vacuum fear, bear courage
```

```
"I miss him. It's been 6 months."

→ You have 83 memories of Thunder saved. 
  Would you like to revisit a favorite 
  or share something new you remembered?
```

```
"He used to sigh really loudly when I took too long 
to get ready for a walk."

→ That's Thunder. Memory #84 saved.
```

## Privacy & Data

- **All data stored locally** at `~/.paw-skill/pets/`
- **No cloud sync.** No vet records. No pet cameras.
- **YOUR memories only.** You describe them. It remembers.
- **Never fabricates.** Only what you've shared.
- **Delete anytime.** Remove the folder.
