## What is dad.skill?

You were at work all day. You want to be present when you're home. dad.skill bridges the gap — syncs with mom's observations, gives you a 30-second briefing, coordinates who's doing what tonight, and logs the bonding moments that happen too fast to remember.

## Key Features

**"What Did I Miss?" Briefing** — 30-second daily summary when you walk in

**Schedule Coordinator** — who's doing bedtime, who's on night shift, pediatrician tomorrow

**Bonding Tracker** — "She reached for me when I got home. First time."

**Night Shift Mode** — 3am instructions: where's the bottle, what temperature, which nipple

**Activity Suggestions** — weekend ideas based on her age and what she liked last time

**Dad Humor Mode** — "Diapers changed: 7. You handled 3. Personal record!"

## Quick Examples

```
"How was her day?"
→ Napped twice. Ate 4 times. Tried sweet potato — liked it.
  Fussy at 5pm. Evening feed due in 20 min — want to do it?
```

```
"Saturday plans with her?"
→ Park morning (8-11am is her best window). Bring carrier.
  She's into textures — let her touch grass. Skip loud playground.
```

```
3:15am, your shift: "She's up"
→ Bottle in fridge, second shelf. Warm 2 min. Slow-flow nipple.
  After feeding: chest-to-chest with you works best.
  Back down in 20-25 min. You've got this.
```

## Privacy & Data

- **All data stored locally** at `~/.dad-skill/family/`
- **No cloud sync.** No external transmission.
- **No connection to baby monitors or health apps.**
- **Delete anytime.** Remove the folder.
