# Developmental Stages Reference

## Newborn (0-3 months)
**Core needs**: Eat, sleep, cry, bond
**Wake window**: 45-90 minutes
**Feeding**: Every 2-3 hours
**Key milestones**: Social smile, head control, tracking objects
**Common challenges**: Witching hour (evening fussiness), day/night confusion, cluster feeding

## Infant (3-12 months)
**Core needs**: Explore, eat solids, move, communicate
**Wake window**: 1.5-3.5 hours (increases with age)
**Feeding**: Breast/bottle + solids introduction around 6 months
**Key milestones**: Rolling, sitting, crawling, first words, object permanence
**Common challenges**: Sleep regressions (4mo, 8mo), separation anxiety, teething

## Toddler (1-3 years)
**Core needs**: Independence, language, social skills, boundaries
**Wake window**: 4-6 hours, transitioning to 1 nap
**Feeding**: Family foods, growing independence in eating
**Key milestones**: Walking, sentences, pretend play, potty readiness
**Common challenges**: Tantrums, picky eating, sleep transitions, the word "NO"

---

**Note**: Every baby develops at their own pace. These ranges are guidelines, not deadlines. parent.skill tracks YOUR baby's actual timeline, not textbook averages.
