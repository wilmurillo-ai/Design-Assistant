## Chocolate Chip Cookies

### Ingredients
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup butter, softened
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 cup chocolate chips
- 1/2 tsp baking soda

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 375°F
2. Mix butter and sugar until creamy
3. Add eggs and vanilla
4. Combine flour and baking soda, add to mixture
5. Fold in chocolate chips
6. Drop cookies onto baking sheet
7. Bake cookies for 9-11 minutes
8. Let cookies cool on rack

### Tips for Perfect Cookies
- Don't overbake — cookies should look slightly underdone
- Chill cookie dough for 30 minutes for thicker cookies
- Use room temperature butter for better cookie texture
- Store cookies in airtight container

## Session Planning
For a baking session with friends:
- Plan 2 hours for a full session
- Each session produces about 48 cookies
- Schedule sessions on weekends for best results
