# WeChat Viral Content Frameworks

## High-Performance Article Structures

### Framework 1: The "Shock Stat → Deep Dive" Structure
1. **Opening Hook**: Start with 1 surprising statistic or counter-intuitive claim
2. **The Reveal**: Explain why the stat is true / what it means
3. **Evidence Stack**: 3-5 supporting data points (YouTube, reports, expert quotes)
4. **Actionable Takeaway**: What the reader should do differently
5. **Closing**: Elevate to broader trend or implication

**Best for**: Trend analysis, industry insights, AI tool comparisons

### Framework 2: The "Case Study → Blueprint" Structure
1. **Opening**: Name a specific success/failure case
2. **Backstory**: What happened, step by step
3. **The Turning Point**: What made the difference
4. **The Blueprint**: Actionable framework extracted from the case
5. **CTA**: Encourage reader to apply framework

**Best for**: Tutorials, how-to guides, service business topics

### Framework 3: The "Myth-Bust → Reality" Structure
1. **The Common Belief**: What most people think
2. **The Reality**: What actually happens (with evidence)
3. **Why the Myth Exists**: incentive alignment, survivorship bias
4. **The Nuanced Truth**: What the picture really looks like
5. **What To Do Instead**: Practical alternatives

**Best for**: Debunking misconceptions, AI tool advice, productivity tips

### Framework 4: The "Future Vision → Today Action" Structure
1. **The Vision**: Where the industry/topic is heading in 3-5 years
2. **The Gap**: Why most people aren't ready for this future yet
3. **The Early Signals**: Evidence the vision is already happening
4. **Today's First Step**: One concrete action to start preparing
5. **The Stakes**: What happens if you don't adapt

**Best for**: Strategic topics, AI trends, business transformation

## Viral Hook Formulas

### Number-Based Hooks
- "X个理由..." (X reasons why...)
- "从X到X，我只用了..." (From X to X, I only used...)
- "X%的人不知道..." (X% of people don't know...)

### Question Hooks
- "为什么XXX？" (Why XXX?)
- "XXX和XXX，区别在哪里？" (What's the difference between XXX and XXX?)
- "如果你知道这个..." (If you knew this...)

### Contrast Hooks
- "别人XXX，你却XXX" (Others XXX, but you XXX)
- "不再是XXX，而是XXX" (No longer XXX, but XXX)
- "看起来XXX，实际上XXX" (Looks like XXX, actually XXX)

### Authority Hooks
- "Sam Altman最新演讲透露..." (Sam Altman's latest talk reveals...)
- "看完这个AI报告，我决定..." (After reading this AI report, I decided...)
- "这位创始人说了一句让全场沉默的话..." (This founder said something that silenced the whole room...)

## Angle Diversification Checklist

When generating topics, ensure variety across:
- [ ] Audience level: Beginner vs. advanced users
- [ ] Tone: Analytical vs. inspirational vs. cautionary
- [ ] Format: Case study vs. framework vs. myth-busting vs. trend analysis
- [ ] Specificity: Broad industry trends vs. specific tool tutorials
