# Stock Analysis Workflow

This reference documents the standard procedures and data requirements for generating a deep stock analysis report.

## Data Points to Collect
- **Valuation & Efficiency**: PE Ratio, ROE (Return on Equity).
- **Yields**: Dividend, Dividend Yield (DY).
- **Price Range**: 52-week High, 52-week Low.
- **Financial History**: Net Income, EPS (Earnings Per Share), Revenue trends.

## Technical Analysis (TA) Checklist
1. **Support & Resistance**: Identify key horizontal levels and psychological barriers.
2. **Moving Averages**: Check 20, 50, and 200-day SMAs.
3. **Momentum**: Analyze RSI (14) for overbought/oversold and MACD for trend strength.
4. **Volume**: Confirm price moves with volume spikes or institutional accumulation.

## Catalyst & Macro Research
- **Recent News**: Search for earnings, regulatory changes, or sector-specific news within the last 72 hours.
- **Macro**: Monitor Central Bank (Fed/Bank Negara) interest rate decisions and inflation data.
- **Institutional Flow**: Check for major buy/sell actions by big funds (e.g., EPF, ASB, BlackRock).

## Report Structure
1. Fundamental Metrics
2. Technical Analysis
3. News & Catalysts
4. Macro Context
5. Actionable Advice (Entry, Target, Stop Loss)
