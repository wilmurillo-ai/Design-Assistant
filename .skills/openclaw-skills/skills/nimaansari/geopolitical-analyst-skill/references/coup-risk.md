# Coup Prediction & Intervention Risk Assessment

## Purpose

Coups follow patterns. Predict them by identifying precursor signals in the right sequence.

---

## Coup Phases

### Phase 1: Crisis Emergence (Weeks to Months)
- Government facing legitimacy crisis
- Military observing government weakness
- Officers beginning confidential conversations

**Signals:** Government criticism increasing; military statements becoming political

### Phase 2: Conspiracy Formation (Days to Weeks)
- Core group of officers plots coup
- Coalition building (which units join? which officers?)
- External patron coordination (if applicable)

**Signals:** Unusual officer meetings; military movement; communications restrictions

### Phase 3: Execution Window (Hours to Days)
- Coup attempt launched
- Key positions seized (capital, government buildings, media)
- Leader's response determines success/failure

**Signals:** Military mobilization; unusual orders; communications breakdown

### Phase 4: Consolidation (Days to Weeks)
- New regime establishes control
- Legitimacy challenge (domestic and international)
- Revenge/purges begin

---

## Coup Success Factors

| Factor | Favors Success | Favors Failure |
|--------|---|---|
| Military cohesion | Unified military | Fragmented military |
| Public support | Public wants coup | Public opposes coup |
| Speed | Quick execution | Government prepares defense |
| Elite support | Other elites support | Other elites oppose |
| International | Patrons support | Patrons intervene against |
