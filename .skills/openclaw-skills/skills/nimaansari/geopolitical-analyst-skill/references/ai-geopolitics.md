# AI & Future Technology Geopolitics

## Purpose

AI is reshaping geopolitics. Predict how:
- Who controls advanced AI?
- How does it shift power?
- What's at stake?
- What conflicts will it create/resolve?

---

## AI Dominance Implications

### Military Dimension
- Autonomous weapons systems
- Swarm attacks
- Faster decision-making
- First-mover advantage becomes critical

**Risk:** AI miscalculation in weapons systems could trigger unintended war

### Economic Dimension
- AI productivity gains
- Job displacement
- Wealth concentration
- Competitive advantage for AI leaders

**Risk:** AI-enabled countries grow wealthy; others stagnate or decline

### Information Dimension
- Deepfake technology
- Perfect disinformation
- Personalized propaganda
- Truth becomes unverifiable

**Risk:** Information warfare becomes much more effective

### Governance Dimension
- AI enables surveillance
- Facial recognition/mass monitoring
- Behavior prediction
- Authoritarian control enhanced

**Risk:** Authoritarian regimes become harder to oppose

---

## AI Competition

**Current leaders:** US (OpenAI, Google), China (Alibaba, Baidu)
**Catching up:** EU, Russia, India, others

**Key question:** Who will dominate AI in 2030?

**Implication:** AI leader likely dominates geopolitics
