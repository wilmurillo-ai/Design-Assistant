# Atrocity Risk & Genocide Early Warning

## Purpose

Predict genocide/war crimes before they escalate:
- What are the warning signs?
- Which conflicts have highest risk?
- What can stop mass atrocities?

---

## Atrocity Precursors

### Precursor 1: Dehumanization
- Victim group described as less than human
- Propaganda equating target group to animals, disease, pests
- Media campaign depicting target as threat

**Risk level:** LOW (necessary but not sufficient)

### Precursor 2: Historical Grievance Framing
- Narrative: "They did this to us; now we do it to them"
- Revenge motivation

**Risk level:** MEDIUM (combined with others, increases risk)

### Precursor 3: Group Identification System
- Victims identified by ethnicity, religion, nationality on ID cards
- Easy to target specific group
- System in place for systematic killing

**Risk level:** HIGH (enables logistics of genocide)

### Precursor 4: Military Formation / Weapons Gathering
- Militia formation preceding atrocity
- Weapons distribution
- Military training in preparation

**Risk level:** CRITICAL (signals preparation)

### Precursor 5: Checkpoint/Segregation System
- Separation of victim group from perpetrator group
- Movement restrictions
- Concentration in specific areas

**Risk level:** CRITICAL (precedes mass violence)

### Precursor 6: Leadership Rhetoric / Orders
- Leaders calling for violence against group
- Orders issued to kill specific group
- Official statements framing group as enemy/threat

**Risk level:** CRITICAL (explicit incitement)

---

## Genocide Warning Signs

**Risk is HIGH when 4+ precursors present:**
- Dehumanization + historical grievance + ID system + military formation
- = genocidal potential activated

**Risk becomes CRITICAL when:**
- Leadership explicitly incites genocide
- Military formations mobilizing for violence
- First killings beginning (signals escalation likely)

```markdown
### Precursor Checklist
- [ ] Dehumanization present
- [ ] Historical grievance framing
- [ ] Group identification system
- [ ] Military formation / weapons
- [ ] Checkpoint/segregation system
- [ ] Leadership rhetoric / orders

**# of precursors present:** [Count]

### Risk Assessment
**Atrocity risk:** CRITICAL / HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW
**Genocide risk:** CRITICAL / HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW

### Timeline
**If precursors continue, escalation within:** [Days / Weeks]

### Prevention Opportunities
**What would reduce risk:**
- [ ] Leadership change
- [ ] International intervention
- [ ] Third-party deployment
- [ ] Sanctions on perpetrators
- [ ] ICC investigation/arrest warrants

### Probability of Prevention
**Intervention by [country/organization]:** [%]
**Successful prevention:** [%]
**If not prevented, estimated deaths:** [Range]
```
