# Cyber Operations & Digital Warfare Doctrine

## Purpose

Cyber is now a primary warfighting domain alongside military, economic, information.

Understanding cyber operations means predicting:
- Which actors have cyber capability?
- What targets are at risk?
- What's the threshold for cyber as "act of war"?
- How do cyber attacks integrate with military/economic conflict?

---

## Cyber Capability Tiers

### Tier 1: Advanced Cyber Nations (US, China, Russia)
- Offensive & defensive capability
- Intrusions into foreign government/military networks
- Critical infrastructure sabotage capability
- Attribution-evading operations

### Tier 2: Capable Nations (Israel, UK, France, Germany, Japan, India)
- Significant offensive capability
- Limited to specific targets
- Some critical infrastructure capability
- Regional focus

### Tier 3: Developing Nations
- Limited capability
- Mostly defensive or commercial espionage
- Require external support for major ops
- Low sophistication

### Tier 4: Non-state Actors
- Depends on external state support
- Ransomware, DDoS, data theft
- Cannot do sophisticated operations alone

---

## Cyber Attack Types

### Type 1: Espionage
- Stealing data
- No damage to target
- Hard to detect
- Escalation: LOW

### Type 2: Sabotage
- Destroying/disabling systems
- Temporary or permanent damage
- Detectable
- Escalation: MEDIUM-HIGH

### Type 3: Critical Infrastructure Attack
- Power grids, water systems, hospitals down
- Widespread civilian impact
- Potential for mass casualties
- Escalation: CRITICAL (acts like military attack)

### Type 4: Election Interference
- Changing votes or preventing voting
- Delegitimizing democratic process
- Hard to detect/prove
- Escalation: MEDIUM (political, not kinetic)

---

## Cyber-Military Integration

Cyber operations often precede military operations:
```
Phase 1: Cyber reconnaissance (mapping networks)
Phase 2: Cyber preparation (disabling air defense, communications)
Phase 3: Military operations (strikes against blinded/confused enemy)
```

**Signal:** If cyber attacks on military networks increasing → military operation likely in coming weeks
