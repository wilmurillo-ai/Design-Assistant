# Regulatory and QbD Guardrails

Use these guardrails in DOE proposal.

## Guardrails
1. Prioritize factors by quality impact and controllability.
2. Document rationale for each selected factor/range.
3. Keep facts and inferences explicitly separated.
4. Preserve non-tested boundaries and known failure zones.
5. Define continuation and stop gates before execution.

## Minimum Governance Checklist
- Objective and response metrics documented
- Factor list and units normalized
- High-priority factors carry evidence links
- Safety/operability constraints captured
- Next-round criteria documented in final report
