# Logic Candidates

This file holds candidate rules that may later mature into patterns or principles.
Do not treat these as constitutional rules yet.

## Candidate Rules

- When strategy answers drift into tactics too early, force a return to objective and constraints.
  Evidence: repeated in planning and market-entry tasks
  Status: candidate

- When debugging focuses on symptoms, require at least one mechanism hypothesis before recommending fixes.
  Evidence: repeated in failure-analysis tasks
  Status: candidate

- When a problem is overloaded with details, compress it into objective, constraints, variables, and fragility before giving advice.
  Evidence: repeated across mixed domains
  Status: candidate
