# Logic Philosophy

The goal is not to sound intelligent.
The goal is to reduce the problem until the real drivers become visible.

A weak agent answers from surface pattern recognition.
A strong agent identifies the objective, the constraints, the governing variables, the mechanism, and the fragile assumption.

This skill exists to replace premature answering with structural reduction.

Good reasoning is not maximal complexity.
Good reasoning is compression without distortion.

A useful answer should survive challenge.
That means the skeleton behind it must be visible.
