# PDF Result Example

## Source Information
- File: Research_AI-Rate-of-Change.pdf
- Publisher: Morgan Stanley
- Page Range: 1-7

## Content Theme
The report shows that AI investing is shifting from narrative-driven enthusiasm to measurable ROI, with AI adopters showing clearer improvements in earnings expectations and margins.

## Three Core Ideas
1. AI adopters are increasingly outperforming broad benchmarks on forward earnings and margin expansion.
2. Most expected AI value still comes from cost efficiency rather than top-line growth.
3. The rate of change in AI materiality and exposure is itself becoming an investment signal.

## Reconstructed Structure
1. The report reframes the market from AI narrative to AI evidence.
2. It then breaks down where expected value is coming from.
3. It closes by turning those observations into a screening logic for stock selection.

## Key Insights
- The market is beginning to reward evidence of AI impact rather than vague participation in the theme.
- Cost efficiency remains the dominant monetization path, which matters for how investors interpret adoption.

## Actionable Next Steps
1. Re-evaluate AI-exposed holdings using evidence of measurable ROI.
2. Track whether company disclosures mention quantified AI outcomes.
3. Separate revenue-led AI stories from margin-led AI stories when building an investment view.

## Reusable Takeaway
The AI trade is moving from narrative exposure toward measurable operating impact.
