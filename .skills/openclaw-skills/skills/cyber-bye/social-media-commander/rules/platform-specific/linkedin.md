# LinkedIn Platform Rules

## Hard Rules
- Every post must have a professional hook (no clickbait)
- Data/statistics must have source
- Job titles/companies mentioned must be accurate
- No emotionally manipulative content

## Soft Rules
- Best format: 3-5 line paragraphs with line breaks
- Include personal experience when possible
- Tag relevant people only if genuinely relevant
- Document posts (PDF carousels) get 3-5x more reach
