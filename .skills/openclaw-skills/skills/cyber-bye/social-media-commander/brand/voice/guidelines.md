|---|---|---|

| Behind-scenes | Personal, raw, relatable | |

## Language Rules

### Always use
- Active voice
- Short sentences (avg < 15 words)
- Second person ("you", "your")
- Specific numbers over vague claims ("3x faster" not "much faster")

### Never use
- Jargon without explanation
- Passive voice in CTAs
- Generic openings ("In today's world...", "Have you ever...")
- Overused phrases: [list brand-specific banned phrases here]

| Twitter/X | More punchy, opinionated |

## CTA Guidelines

- Every piece of content needs a CTA (even if soft)
- CTA types: Follow, Save, Share, Comment, Click link, DM, Buy
- Hard CTAs (buy/click) only on conversion-stage content
- Soft CTAs (save/share/comment) on all other stages
