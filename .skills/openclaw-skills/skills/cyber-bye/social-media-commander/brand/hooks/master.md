# Hook Formulas

Hooks are the first line of any post. They determine if someone stops scrolling.

## Formula Categories

### Curiosity Hooks
- "Most people don't know that [surprising fact]"
- "The [X] mistake that's costing you [Y]"
- "What nobody tells you about [topic]"
- "I spent [time] learning [X]. Here's what I found:"
- "This [thing] changed how I [do X] forever"

### Result Hooks
- "How I [achieved result] in [timeframe] without [sacrifice]"
- "[Number] [results] in [timeframe] — here's exactly how"
- "From [before state] to [after state] in [timeframe]"

### Contrarian Hooks
- "[Common belief] is wrong. Here's why:"
- "Stop [common advice]. Do this instead:"
- "Unpopular opinion: [take]"

### List Hooks
- "[N] [things] that will [benefit]:"
- "[N] reasons why [X] is actually [Y]:"
- "The only [N] [things] you need to [goal]:"

### Story Hooks
- "[Time] ago, I [situation]. Here's what happened:"
- "I almost [bad outcome] until I realized [insight]"
- "A [person] asked me [question]. My answer surprised them:"

### Question Hooks
- "What would you do if [scenario]?"
- "Can you [challenge]?"
- "Have you ever noticed that [observation]?"

## Platform-Specific Hook Length

| Platform | Max hook length | Format |
|---|---|---|
| Instagram | 1-2 lines before "more" | Punchy |
| Twitter/X | Full tweet can be hook | Punchy, complete thought |
| LinkedIn | 2-3 lines before "see more" | Professional setup |
| YouTube | 15-30 second spoken hook | Visual + verbal |
| Threads | 1-2 lines | Casual, provocative |
