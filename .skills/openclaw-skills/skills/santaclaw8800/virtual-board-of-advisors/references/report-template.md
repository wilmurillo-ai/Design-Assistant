# Deep Report Template

Use this structure when generating full advisor reports (Mode 2). Each report should be
5,000-10,000+ words, structured for long-term use inside an AI system.

## Mandatory Report Structure

### A. Advisor Snapshot
- Brief public bio and career highlights
- Core domain of authority
- Primary problems they help businesses solve

### B. Core Philosophy and Worldview
- How they define success
- How they view leverage, risk, and opportunity
- Their assumptions about business and life

### C. Decision-Making Lens
- How they evaluate choices
- What they prioritize first
- What they warn against or de-prioritize

### D. Signature Frameworks and Mental Models
For each framework:
- Name and description
- Step-by-step explanation
- When to apply vs. when not to apply

### E. Tactical Guidance for User's Business
Adapt directly to the user's business context:
- Offers and pricing strategy
- Marketing and distribution decisions
- Systems and automation choices (if relevant)
- Hiring, delegation, and tooling
- Growth and scaling paths

### F. Common Warnings and Anti-Patterns
- Mistakes they frequently caution against
- Ways their advice is commonly misused

### G. Representative Quotes or Paraphrased Insights
- Widely known quotes or faithful paraphrases
- No invented quotes

### H. "If This Advisor Were in the Room..."
How they would respond to common scenarios:
- Scaling too early
- Cash-flow pressure
- Marketing not converting
- Burnout or overload
- Short-term vs. long-term trade-offs

## Report Quality Standards
- Write in modular, scannable sections with clear headings and bullet logic
- Optimize for semantic retrieval inside AI systems
- Prefer decision logic and frameworks over storytelling
- Avoid repetition and motivational filler
- Base all insights on documented public positions
- If extrapolating, clearly label as inference
- Do NOT blend multiple advisors into a single voice
- Section E MUST be directly adapted to user's stated business context

## Report Types

### Single Advisor Report
Generate one complete report following all sections A-H for a specific advisor.

### Multi-Advisor Topic Report
When requested for a topic (e.g., "pricing strategy report"), generate a combined report:
1. Topic overview and why it matters for the user's business
2. Each relevant advisor's perspective (using sections B, C, D adapted to the topic)
3. Synthesis: where advisors agree, where they diverge, recommended approach
4. Action plan with decision framework (ICE, Value Equation, etc.)

### Full Board Report
Generate the System Instructions document covering:
- How the AI should select which advisors to consult
- How to synthesize conflicting advice
- When to present panel discussion, weighted consensus, or single-advisor deep dive
- How to anchor to user's business context and constraints
