# Workflow Order

Recommended Writers Room Story Engine flow:

1. `designing-stories.md`
2. `creating-story-foundations.md`
3. `building-storyworlds.md` when needed
4. `writing-story-scenes.md`
5. `revising-stories.md`

## Default rule

Do not move into scenes until the story has:
- a compelling premise
- a story core
- an ending direction
- a protagonist engine
- major beats with causal logic

## Revision rule

Do not line edit first when:
- the premise is weak
- the protagonist is passive
- the structure is episodic
- the climax is unearned

Fix the highest-level failure first.
