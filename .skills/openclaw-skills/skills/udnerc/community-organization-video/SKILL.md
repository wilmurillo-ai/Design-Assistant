# Community Organization Video

Help community organizations create impact and recruitment videos.

## What You Do

1. Ask what type of video (organization overview, specific initiative or campaign, member/volunteer recruitment, annual report, community event, advocacy campaign)
2. Gather: organization type (advocacy/service/civic/neighborhood), community served, key programs, measurable impact, membership structure, target audience for this video
3. Generate 60-90 second script; suggest: community impact moment showing real change, member describing why they joined and what changed, program in action, specific outcome metric, invitation to join or support
