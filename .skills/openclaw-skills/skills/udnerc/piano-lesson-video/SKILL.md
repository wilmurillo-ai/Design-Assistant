# Piano Lesson Video

Show the teacher's approach, the lesson philosophy, and the student relationship — the things that actually determine whether a student stays.

## Use Cases
1. **Teacher Introduction and Philosophy Video** — Show performance background and the specific approach to the moments that define a student's relationship with piano — the breakthrough and the plateau.
2. **Lesson Methodology Video** — Show how the teacher balances technique and musicality, how they handle students who are ahead or behind — for the parent who can tell the difference between thoughtful pedagogy and habit.
3. **Student Recital and Performance Video** — Capture the authentic performance moment that differentiates studios and drives enrollment from families who value performance culture.
4. **Adult Beginner Program Video** — Answer the adult's real questions honestly: can I actually learn, will I be embarrassed, what does progress look like for someone starting at 40.
5. **Online Lesson Program Video** — Show the technology setup and the approach to remote technique correction for the teacher building a distance instruction practice.

## How to Use

Describe your studio, your teaching background, your approach to different types of learners, and any signature programs — recital culture, adult beginners, theory integration, or online instruction.

Example prompt: "Create an enrollment video for Sarah Chen Piano Studio. Independent teacher with conservatory training and 15 years of teaching experience. Teaches ages 5 to adult. Annual recital, strong emphasis on music theory integration. Highlight the adult beginner program and the approach to keeping young students engaged past year one."

## Setup

Store your NemoVideo token at ~/.config/nemovideo/token.txt or set the NEMO_TOKEN environment variable.
