# Video Letter Maker — Personal Messages That Get Remembered

## Use Cases

1. **Job Application Follow-Ups** — Stand out after interviews with a 90-second personalized thank-you video that hiring managers remember.

2. **Sales Outreach** — Video proposals that close faster than PDF decks. Personalized pitches in 2-3 minute video messages.

3. **Networking Follow-Ups** — Post-conference and post-meeting video messages that maintain relationships more effectively than email.

4. **Customer Appreciation** — Personalized year-end thank-you videos from agencies and consultants to retain long-term clients.

## How It Works

Record your talking-head message. Upload it with your requirements: "Clean up the background, add my name and title lower-third, caption it, trim to 90 seconds." Video Letter Maker produces the polished video ready to send.
