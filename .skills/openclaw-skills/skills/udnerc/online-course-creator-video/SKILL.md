# Online Course Creator Video — Course Videos That Make Students Finish (and Come Back)

## Use Cases
1. Course introduction videos establishing instructor authority and previewing the transformation students will experience.
2. Module walkthrough videos animating complex frameworks, processes, or skills into visual sequences students can follow and retain.
3. Student outcome testimonial videos featuring real completers describing specific results — revenue earned, skills gained, certifications achieved.
4. Course launch promo videos optimized for marketplace listings (Udemy, Coursera, Teachable) with outcome-first hooks in the first 6 seconds.
5. Corporate L&D module videos for onboarding, compliance training, and internal certification programs.

## How It Works
Describe your course topic, target learner, and the core outcome students achieve. Provide any slides, screen recordings, or concept notes you already have. The tool selects the highest-impact visual approach for each content type, adds motion graphics and on-screen text reinforcing key learning points, and exports video packages formatted for your LMS, landing page, and launch campaign.

## Output
- Course intro and enrollment CTA videos (60–90 seconds)
- Module lesson videos with animated key concepts
- Student testimonial and transformation showcase videos
- Platform-optimized launch promo (Udemy 2-min, Teachable/Kajabi landing page)
- Social teaser clips (Instagram Reels, TikTok, LinkedIn)

## Example Prompt
"Create a course intro video for my 6-week copywriting course targeting freelancers who want to charge $150/hour. The outcome: students land their first premium client within 30 days of completing module 4."
