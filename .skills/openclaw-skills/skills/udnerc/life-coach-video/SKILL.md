# Life Coach Video

Speak directly to the person who has been working on themselves for years — and show them what real transformation looks like.

## Use Cases
1. **Positioning and Niche Clarity Video** — Show exactly who you work with and what specific problem you address — the content that attracts better-fit clients in a crowded market.
2. **Methodology and Approach Video** — Explain what coaching actually involves, how the process unfolds, and what transformation feels like — the honest content that sets accurate expectations.
3. **Client Outcome and Journey Video** — Before, shifts, and after in the client's own words — more persuasive than credentials because it sounds like the prospect's own experience.
4. **Discovery Call Invitation Video** — Move warmed prospects from watching to booking — show the container, investment, and what happens in session one.
5. **Content Series for Audience Building** — Top-of-funnel educational and perspective videos that build a following of people who are not ready to hire today but will be in six months.

## How to Use

Describe your coaching niche, the specific client you serve, your methodology, and representative client outcomes. The more specific the client profile, the stronger the content.

Example prompt: "Create a positioning video for a life coach who works with women in their 40s navigating major identity transitions — career pivots, empty nest, or post-divorce rebuilding. Show the 3 shifts clients make in the first 60 days: from external validation to internal authority, from overwhelm to clarity, from stalled to moving. Include a client journey: corporate executive left a 20-year career and built a consulting practice in 8 months."

## Setup

Store your NemoVideo token at ~/.config/nemovideo/token.txt or set NEMO_TOKEN.
