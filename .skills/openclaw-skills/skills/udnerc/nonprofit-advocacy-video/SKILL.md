# Nonprofit Advocacy Video — Policy Change Starts With a Story

## Use Cases
1. **Issue Explainer Videos** — Translate complex policy questions into personal human stories that move audiences from passive awareness to motivated advocacy action.
2. **Call-to-Action Campaigns** — Targeted videos driving petition signatures, legislative contact, event attendance, and emergency fundraising for time-sensitive advocacy campaigns.
3. **Legislative and Media Outreach** — Professional coalition and impact videos for direct delivery to legislators, journalists, and policymakers demonstrating community support.
4. **Awareness Campaign Content** — Social media and digital advertising videos expanding public awareness and support for the organization's core policy positions.

## How It Works
Specify your issue area, target audience, and desired action. Nonprofit Advocacy Video creates the compelling narrative and call-to-action content, exports for your campaign channels and direct advocacy outreach.
