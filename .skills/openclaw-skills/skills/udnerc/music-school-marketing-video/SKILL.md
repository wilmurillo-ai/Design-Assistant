# Music School Marketing Video — Fill Fall. Hold Spring. Run the Recital Clip Year-Round.

## Use Cases
1. Teacher introduction and teaching philosophy videos putting a face and personality to the instructor before a family calls — converting "I wonder if my child will like their teacher" into confident enrollment.
2. Instrument and program differentiation videos showing what beginner piano looks like versus teen guitar versus high school voice prep — for parents who have a specific child with a specific goal.
3. Student milestone and progress videos capturing the first complete song, the first recital performance, the first moment a student plays something recognizable — the emotional proof point that sells lessons better than any description.
4. Enrollment and trial lesson explainer videos walking families through how the first lesson works, what to bring, and what the first three months of progress looks like.
5. Seasonal and recital promotion videos timed to the August enrollment spike, January re-enrollment window, and spring recital season for maximum annual coverage.

## How It Works
Describe your studio's instruments, age range, teaching philosophy, and enrollment calendar. Provide any existing recital footage, teacher bios, or student progress stories. Music School Marketing Video builds the year-round video presence that fills your fall roster and keeps it full through spring.

## Key Features
- Teacher-forward introduction format building the personal relationship trust that music lesson enrollment depends on
- Recital highlight activation turning your May performance footage into your highest-converting August enrollment content
- Instrument differentiation sequences for multi-instrument studios serving different age groups and skill levels
- Seasonal campaign structure: August enrollment surge → January re-enrollment → spring recital conversion

## Output Formats
- Google Business Profile clips for "music lessons near me" and "piano lessons near me" local search
- Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts for student milestone and recital highlight organic reach
- Website homepage and teacher bio page videos with trial lesson booking CTA
- Paid social ad creative for August and January enrollment season campaigns
