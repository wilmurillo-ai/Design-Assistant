# Church Sermon Video — Weekly Sermons Published on Time, Every Time

## Use Cases

1. **Weekly Sermon Publishing** — Process Sunday morning sermon recordings into polished YouTube uploads by Tuesday. Multi-camera sync, audio replacement, branding overlays, and chapter markers for consistent weekly publishing.

2. **Sermon Series Archiving** — Consistent formatting across a complete sermon series so the archive looks professional for new visitors and longtime members who revisit past teaching.

3. **Short Clip Extraction** — 60-90 second highlight clips from the full sermon for Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok outreach, capturing the most quotable moments from each message.

4. **Podcast Audio Export** — Clean audio extraction from sermon recordings for church podcast distribution on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, and Google Podcasts without requiring a separate audio recording setup.

## How It Works

Upload your raw sermon recordings. Church Sermon Video handles the technical editing — sync, audio, branding, chapters — and exports finished videos for every platform your church uses to reach your congregation.
