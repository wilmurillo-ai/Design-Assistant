# AI source starter set

Use this file when the user wants a high-quality AI source list, a monitoring dashboard, or a reduced watchlist from a noisy master list.

## Selection logic

Build from roles, not fame.

### 1. Official / primary sources
Use for first-order facts: launches, model updates, papers, product changes, API changes.

- OpenAI — product and model releases
- Google DeepMind — research and flagship model updates
- Anthropic — Claude, safety, API, product updates
- Meta AI — open-source model ecosystem and research
- DeepSeek — open-source / pricing / model disruption
- Alibaba Qwen — major open-source and multimodal releases
- Kimi / Moonshot — long-context and product movement in China
- MiniMax — multimodal and video movement in China
- NVIDIA / NVIDIA AI — infrastructure, hardware, and ecosystem direction

### 2. Builders / operators
Use for informed interpretation from people shipping products or research.

- Sam Altman — OpenAI strategy and market direction
- Dario Amodei — Anthropic strategy, safety, and product direction
- Demis Hassabis — DeepMind strategy and science direction
- Andrej Karpathy — technical explanation with unusually high clarity
- Yann LeCun — contrarian and anti-hype perspective from a top researcher
- Ilya Sutskever — rare but important signals on frontier research
- Jeff Dean — infrastructure and research depth
- Junyang Lin — Qwen technical direction
- Peter Steinberger — agent / developer workflow perspective

### 3. Explainers / interpreters
Use for turning scattered developments into usable understanding.

- Andrew Ng — practical trend interpretation
- Thomas Wolf — open-source ecosystem perspective
- Fei-Fei Li — spatial intelligence and broader AI direction
- Allie K. Miller — industry pattern spotting (use selectively)

### 4. Critics / risk voices
Use to test assumptions and reduce hype intoxication.

- Gary Marcus — sharp critic of overclaiming
- Eliezer Yudkowsky — extreme but important safety lens
- ethics / governance researchers when the task concerns policy or deployment risk

## Usage notes

- Prefer official accounts for “what happened?”
- Prefer builders for “why does this matter?”
- Prefer critics for “what could be wrong with this narrative?”
- Do not let aggregators dominate the list unless speed matters more than signal purity.

### Pruning rule

- Remove if mostly reposting
- Remove if engagement bait overwhelms information value
