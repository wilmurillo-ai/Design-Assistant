# AI Wedding Video Editor — Eight Hours of Footage, One Perfect Highlight Reel

## Use Cases

1. **Wedding Highlight Reel** — Automatically identify the ceremony kiss, first dance, vows, cake cutting, and reception moments from hours of raw footage and assemble a 3-5 minute highlight video paced to music — the shareable wedding video that goes out to family and lands on Instagram the week after the wedding.

2. **Full Ceremony Multi-Camera Edit** — Sync footage from two or more cameras, replace camera audio with the cleaner lapel or audio recorder feed, apply consistent warm color grading across indoor and outdoor shots, and deliver a complete ceremony recording couples can share with guests who weren't in the room.

3. **Same-Day Edit / Reception Teaser** — Cut a 60-90 second teaser from ceremony and cocktail hour footage fast enough to screen at the reception — the surprise moment that makes the room go quiet and the clip that drives the most Instagram shares in the 24 hours after the wedding.

4. **Speech and Toast Clips** — Isolate toasts and speeches from reception footage, clean up the audio, add lower-thirds with each speaker's name, and export standalone clips for sharing — the content that gets forwarded to everyone who wasn't there.

## How It Works

Upload your raw wedding footage. Describe the couple, the key moments to prioritize, and the style — cinematic, documentary, upbeat, emotional. AI Wedding Video Editor handles the assembly: identifying the moments that matter, syncing cameras, cleaning audio, pacing to music, and exporting finished video ready for client delivery, Vimeo, Instagram, and anywhere else the couple wants to share the best day of their lives.
