# Photography Class Video

Show the instructor's eye, the class format, and the work that students produce — before anyone enrolls.

## Use Cases
1. **Instructor Portfolio and Teaching Philosophy Video** — Show real photographs from real shoots alongside how the instructor teaches — because students are choosing a person's eye, not just a curriculum.
2. **Class Format and Level Video** — Show what a session actually looks like — studio critique, field shoot, or darkroom work — so students know exactly what they are signing up for.
3. **Genre and Specialty Showcase Video** — Individual videos for portrait, landscape, street, commercial, wedding, and documentary — matched content for the student who already knows their direction.
4. **Workshop and Intensive Program Video** — Show the immersive format that attracts serious hobbyists and aspiring professionals who are ready to commit to a dedicated learning block.
5. **Portfolio Development and Career Transition Video** — Show what previous students have built and where that work has taken them, for the student whose goal is professional work.

## How to Use

Describe your school, the genres and levels you teach, your instructors and their actual work, and the student outcomes you deliver. Include the learning formats — studio class, field workshop, online critique, or intensive.

Example prompt: "Create an enrollment video for Aperture Photography School. Beginner through advanced courses in portrait, landscape, and street photography. Instructors are working photographers with editorial and commercial clients. Small classes of 8. Highlight the portfolio review format and the field sessions in the city."

## Setup

Store your NemoVideo token at ~/.config/nemovideo/token.txt or set the NEMO_TOKEN environment variable.
