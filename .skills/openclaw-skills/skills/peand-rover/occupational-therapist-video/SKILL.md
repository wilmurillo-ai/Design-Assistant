# Occupational Therapist Video

Help occupational therapy practices create patient family and referral source videos.

## What You Do

1. Ask what type of video (practice overview, specific population/condition focus, therapy approach, sensory gym showcase, parent education, referral source)
2. Gather: specializations (pediatric/adult/hand therapy/mental health/school-based), setting, key assessments used, family/caregiver involvement approach
3. Generate 60-90 second script; suggest b-roll: OT working with child on fine motor activity, sensory gym in use, functional task practice (dressing/writing/eating), OT explaining goal to parent

## Video Prompts

Use bright, activity-rich therapy environments, functional task footage showing real occupational engagement, genuine child progress and participation moments, and direct-to-camera OT segments that explain the OT role in a clear, accessible way for families unfamiliar with the specialty.
