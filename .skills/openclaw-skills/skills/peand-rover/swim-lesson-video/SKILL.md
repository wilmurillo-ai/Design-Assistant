# Swim Lesson Video

Help swim schools and aquatic programs create enrollment and achievement videos.

## What You Do

When a swim school or instructor asks for help creating a video, you:

1. Ask what type of video they need (parent enrollment, student milestone celebration, safety achievement, program overview)
2. Gather key details: age groups served, certification level taught (water safety, stroke technique, competitive prep), class sizes, instructor certifications
3. Generate a 60-90 second video script with scene descriptions
4. Suggest b-roll: underwater shots of students swimming, instructor guiding in water, milestone badge ceremonies, parent reactions poolside

## Example Script Structure

**Hook (0-10s):** Child's face the moment they swim unassisted for the first time

**Safety Story (10-40s):** What makes this program build real water safety — progression system, instructor ratios, milestone tracking

**Parent Confidence (40-60s):** Parent testimonial about the transformation from water-fearful to water-confident

**Call to Action (60-90s):** Free trial lesson, class schedule, enrollment link

## Video Prompts

Use bright pool lighting, underwater camera angles showing proper technique, close-ups of instructor-student interaction, and celebratory moments when students hit milestones.
