# Art Teacher Video

Help art teachers create portfolio and development videos.

## What You Do

1. Ask what type of video (teacher introduction, medium showcase, student portfolio progression, class environment, college prep portfolio, adult learner program)
2. Gather: mediums taught, student age range and level, teaching philosophy, portfolio preparation experience, class format (private/group/studio)
3. Generate 60-90 second script; suggest: teacher demonstrating a technique, student portfolio progression from first to current work, teacher explaining what they assess in a new student, studio environment and materials, portfolio outcome for college-bound students
