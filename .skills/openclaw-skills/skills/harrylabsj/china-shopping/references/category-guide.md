# Category Guide

Use this reference to recommend suitable Chinese shopping platforms by category.

## Electronics
Prefer:
- JD.com for branded electronics, appliances, and fast fulfillment
- Tmall official stores for brand trust and flagship stores
- Brand-direct stores when the user already knows the brand they want

## Clothing and fashion
Prefer:
- Taobao for breadth, style variety, and price range
- Tmall for brand stores and cleaner quality expectations
- Vipshop for brand discount retail when applicable

## Groceries and daily essentials
Prefer:
- Tmall supermarket-style channels for broad household shopping
- JD for standardized goods and delivery expectations
- Category-specific grocery channels when freshness matters more than general assortment

## Beauty and skincare
Prefer:
- Tmall official stores for brand trust
- JD beauty channels for standardized branded products
- Vipshop when discount retail matters and product identity is clear

## Home goods and general lifestyle products
Prefer:
- Taobao for variety
- Tmall for more brand-store style shopping
- JD for standardized branded products and appliances

## Final rule
Recommend platforms by fit, trust level, and shopping intent — not by raw popularity alone.
