# Output Patterns

- 可直接优先看
- 建议继续比店

## Best Candidate

Name the strongest listing or seller and why it stands out.

## Risks

Mention the most meaningful Taobao-specific concerns:
- variant ambiguity
- weak seller signals
- suspiciously low price
- confusing pricing conditions

## Next Step

Tell the user what to do next inside Taobao:
- compare more sellers
- verify spec
- open store page
- avoid and search a narrower term
