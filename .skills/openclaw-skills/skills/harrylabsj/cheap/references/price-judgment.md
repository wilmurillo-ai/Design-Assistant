# Price Judgment Reference

Use this reference to decide which visible price should count as the meaningful cheapest price.

## Prefer the lowest meaningful visible price
Prefer the lowest visible price only when the listing is genuinely comparable.

## Treat these with caution
Flag clearly when the visible low price depends on:
- coupon-only discounts
- membership-only pricing
- deposit or pre-sale pricing
- bundle pricing that changes what is included
- different quantity or pack size
- variant mismatch
- seller subsidy that may not apply generally

## Price hierarchy
When comparing prices, prefer this interpretation order:
1. directly visible comparable sale price
2. visible discounted price with normal checkout conditions
3. coupon or member price, if clearly labeled as conditional
4. deposit/pre-sale or heavily conditional price, with strong warning

## Do not mislead
Do not present a heavily conditional or distorted price as the straightforward cheapest option without caveat.

The cheapest visible price is not automatically the best buying choice when seller trust, bundle quality, or price conditions differ materially.

## Final rule
The cheapest trustworthy comparable price matters more than the lowest raw number.
