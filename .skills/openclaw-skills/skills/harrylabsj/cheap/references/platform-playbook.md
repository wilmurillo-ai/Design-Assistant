# Platform Playbook Reference

Use this reference when searching Chinese shopping platforms for the same product.

## Core platforms
Check the most relevant platforms first:
- Taobao
- Tmall
- JD.com
- Pinduoduo
- Vipshop when brand discount retail is relevant

## Platform notes

### Taobao
- Broad supply and many sellers
- Price may be low but seller quality varies more
- Watch for accessory, used-like, or lightly different variants

### Tmall
- More brand-store or official-store style listings
- Often cleaner comparability for branded goods
- Price may not be the absolute lowest, but listing trust can be higher

### JD.com
- Strong for branded electronics, appliances, and standardized goods
- Watch for self-operated vs marketplace seller differences
- Promotions, coupons, and membership benefits may change the real payable price

### Pinduoduo
- Often aggressive visible prices
- Check carefully for variant mismatch, bundle differences, and seller quality risk
- Low price can be meaningful, but only when the listing is truly comparable

### Vipshop
- Useful for branded apparel, beauty, and discounted retail items
- Inventory may be seasonal or limited

## Search rule
Do not force every platform if the product clearly belongs to fewer relevant channels.

## Coverage rule
If some platforms are inaccessible or weakly readable, say so explicitly.
