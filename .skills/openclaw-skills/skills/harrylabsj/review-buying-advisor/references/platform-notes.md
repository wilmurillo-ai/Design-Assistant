# Platform Notes Reference

Use this reference to adjust review reading strategy by platform.

Platform notes help calibrate evidence. They do not replace evidence.

## General rule

Across all platforms:
- do not trust star ratings alone
- do not treat review count alone as proof
- prioritize concrete review content
- watch for short, generic, or logistics-heavy comments

## Tmall / Taobao

Common characteristics:
- many short comments
- strong influence from photos and first impressions
- seller differences may matter on generic products

Useful signals:
- buyer photos
- comments after actual use
- comments about size, color, smell, material feel
- complaints about mismatch between listing and reality

Reading advice:
- downweight generic praise
- focus on detailed comments and photo-backed evidence
- separate product issues from seller fulfillment issues

## JD.com

Common characteristics:
- often more product-focused for electronics, appliances, and branded goods
- better signal for function and reliability than style-heavy categories

Useful signals:
- performance after several days or weeks
- comparison with older models or alternatives
- defect, durability, setup, and after-sales details

Reading advice:
- prioritize function details
- do not overcount logistics comments
- pay extra attention to recent low-rated reviews

## Pinduoduo

Common characteristics:
- strong price sensitivity
- many short reviews
- seller and batch variation may matter more

Useful signals:
- comments comparing price and actual received quality
- comments showing whether the product is acceptable only for light or temporary use
- repeated defect complaints

Reading advice:
- interpret praise through price expectation
- distinguish “good enough for the money” from “actually good”
- watch carefully for repeated hard-failure complaints

## Amazon-like marketplaces

Common characteristics:
- more long-form review content
- higher risk of review mixing across variants or generations

Reading advice:
- compare recent reviews with top helpful reviews
- check whether reviews belong to the exact variant
- avoid relying only on older high-visibility reviews

## Xiaohongshu-style commerce content

Common characteristics:
- strong visual and lifestyle framing
- weaker separation between review and recommendation content

Reading advice:
- prioritize detailed first-person use reports
- downweight polished but vague praise
- use cautiously for durability or long-term reliability judgments

## Confidence adjustment

Raise confidence when:
- reviews are detailed
- multiple independent reviews point to the same strengths or risks
- recent reviews align with older patterns

Lower confidence when:
- reviews are mostly generic
- logistics comments dominate
- seller and product effects are mixed together
- the listing combines multiple variants
