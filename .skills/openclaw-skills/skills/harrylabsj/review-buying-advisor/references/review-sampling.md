# Review Sampling Reference

Use this reference to collect a representative review set before giving a buying recommendation.

Do not rely only on the first few visible reviews or only on one rating band.

## Minimum target

If enough reviews are available, try to read:
- 12-20 reviews total

Include:
- several positive reviews
- several negative reviews
- several mixed or moderate reviews, if visible

If fewer than 12 useful reviews are available:
- continue with what is available
- lower confidence
- state the limitation

## Preferred balance

When possible, aim for:
- 6-10 positive reviews
- 4-8 negative reviews
- 2-6 mixed reviews

If star ratings are visible, include:
- 5-star
- 1-star and 2-star
- 3-star when available, because they often contain the best trade-off language

## Sort order

If possible, check more than one view:
- most relevant or default
- lowest rated
- most recent

If only one view is accessible:
- use it
- note that coverage may be skewed

## Recency

If timestamps are visible:
- pay extra attention to recent reviews
- note whether recent reviews are better or worse than older ones

If recency cannot be checked:
- do not speculate

## Prefer these reviews
Give more weight to reviews that mention:
- specific use case
- concrete benefit or defect
- comparison to expectation or another product
- repeat use or long-term use
- performance, durability, fit, or compatibility
- return, regret, or repurchase

## Downweight these reviews
Downweight reviews that are mainly:
- generic praise
- generic blame
- shipping-only comments
- emotional but vague complaints
- suspiciously promotional wording

## Evidence sufficiency

### Medium-to-high confidence
- at least 12 useful reviews
- several specific reviews
- repeated signals are visible
- little contradiction on core function

### Tentative recommendation only
- fewer than 12 useful reviews
- many vague reviews
- high contradiction
- weak access to recent or low-rated reviews

### Not enough evidence for a strong recommendation
- almost no usable reviews
- only generic snippets
- unclear whether reviews refer to the same product variation
- no access to actual review content
