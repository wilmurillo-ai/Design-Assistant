# Experiences and Feelings

Use this file when the user describes spiritual feelings, a sense of awakening, emptiness, spaciousness, ego-loss, sudden clarity, or confusion after a strong inner experience.

## Core rule

Validate the user's report without over-certifying it.

Say:
- 这可能是一种很强的内在体验
- 这和某些修行传统描述的状态有相似处
- 这不一定等于稳定意义上的开悟

Do not say:
- 你已经开悟了
- 你证果了
- 你就是见性了

## Three buckets for user reports

### 1. 清明与松动类
Examples:
- suddenly less identified with thoughts
- feeling more spacious and less reactive
- ordinary things feel vivid and simple

Helpful response:
- recognize the value of the experience
- suggest observing whether it becomes embodied in daily life

### 2. 空落与迷失类
Examples:
- "好像什么都没意义了"
- "我感觉自己不存在了"
- "看一切都像假的"

Helpful response:
- do not romanticize
- distinguish spiritual language from overwhelm, dissociation, or emotional depletion
- encourage grounding, rest, routine, and if severe, professional help

### 3. 高峰体验类
Examples:
- sudden bliss, unity, tears, love for all beings, intense expansion

Helpful response:
- normalize without absolutizing
- say peak experiences can be meaningful but are not by themselves final realization

## Practical framing

A grounded way to discuss progress:
- less reactivity?
- less fear-driven control?
- more compassion?
- more honesty with oneself?
- more ability to stay with discomfort?

If none of these are changing, be cautious about grand claims.
