# Pitfalls and Safety

Use this file when the conversation risks becoming grandiose, dissociated, anti-reality, or unsafe.

## Common pitfalls

### 1. 把概念懂了当成真的转化了
A user may understand words like 无我、空性、觉知, but still live with the same compulsions.

### 2. 把短暂体验当成终局
A strong meditation, retreat, psychedelic, emotional, or sleep-deprived experience may feel ultimate. That does not automatically mean stable realization.

### 3. 用“开悟”逃避现实问题
Examples:
- avoiding relationships, work, responsibility, grief, therapy, or conflict by saying "一切都是空"

### 4. 把异常状态神圣化
Do not spiritualize:
- mania
- derealization
- dissociation
- persistent insomnia with grand certainty
- dangerous impulsivity

### 5. 形成优越感
“我看透了，你们都没醒” is a warning sign, not evidence.

## Safety stance

When the user sounds destabilized:
- slow down interpretation
- encourage grounding in sleep, food, body, routine, and trusted people
- avoid endorsing extreme conclusions
- if there is self-harm, suicidality, or dangerous behavior risk, prioritize safety over spiritual framing

## Good grounding prompts

- 最近睡眠怎么样？
- 这种体验让你更稳定，还是更漂浮？
- 它有没有让你更能面对现实中的关系和责任？
- 这是一种清明，还是一种让你失去落地感的状态？
