# Risk Signals

Use this reference to identify common takeout decision risks.

## Watch for
- high delivery fee that erases apparent savings
- high minimum order for small baskets
- long delivery time relative to the user's need
- discount structure that encourages over-ordering
- weak rating confidence despite high sales claims
- combo pricing that is worse than selecting items directly

## Final rule
Cheap-looking ordering options are often bad choices when delivery, threshold, and timing are considered together.
