# Promotion Judgment

Use this reference to judge whether a Meituan promotion is actually worth it.

## Check the true cost
Do not judge an offer by the discount label alone. Also consider:
- delivery fee
- minimum order threshold
- whether the basket naturally reaches the threshold
- whether extra filler items are needed to unlock the discount

## Common traps
- strong full-reduction discount with unrealistic threshold
- low item price but high delivery fee
- bundle that looks cheap but contains low-value filler
- platform coupon that only works under narrow conditions

## Final rule
A smaller but realistic discount can be better than a larger but awkward discount.
