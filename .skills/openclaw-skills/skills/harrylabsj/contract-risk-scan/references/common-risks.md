# Common Contract Risk Patterns

## 1. Liability & Indemnification Risks

### 🔴 Critical: Unlimited Liability
**Pattern**: "Party A shall be liable for all damages, losses, and expenses..."
**Risk**: No financial cap on liability exposure
**Suggestion**: Negotiate a liability cap (e.g., 12 months of fees or contract value)

### 🔴 Critical: Broad Indemnification
**Pattern**: "Party B shall indemnify and hold harmless Party A from any and all claims..."
**Risk**: One-sided indemnification without carve-outs
**Suggestion**: Limit indemnification to direct damages caused by indemnifying party

### 🟡 Warning: No Liability Cap Mentioned
**Pattern**: Absence of any liability limitation clause
**Risk**: Uncapped exposure under applicable law
**Suggestion**: Add mutual liability cap clause

## 2. Termination Risks

### 🔴 Critical: No Termination for Convenience
**Pattern**: Contract can only be terminated for cause/breach
**Risk**: Locked into unfavorable relationship
**Suggestion**: Add termination for convenience with reasonable notice (30-90 days)

### 🟡 Warning: Excessive Notice Period
**Pattern**: "Either party may terminate with 180 days written notice"
**Risk**: Long commitment period
**Suggestion**: Reduce to 30-60 days for standard contracts

### 🟡 Warning: Harsh Termination Penalties
**Pattern**: "Early termination requires payment of all fees for remaining term"
**Risk**: Prohibitive exit costs
**Suggestion**: Negotiate reasonable termination fee or prorated payment

## 3. Payment Term Risks

### 🟡 Warning: Extended Payment Terms
**Pattern**: "Payment due within 60/90 days of invoice"
**Risk**: Cash flow impact
**Suggestion**: Standard net 30 terms; consider early payment discounts

### 🟡 Warning: No Late Payment Penalties
**Pattern**: Silent on late payment consequences
**Risk**: No incentive for timely payment
**Suggestion**: Add late fee clause (e.g., 1.5% per month)

### 🟢 Advisory: Unclear Payment Schedule
**Pattern**: "Payment upon completion" without milestone definition
**Risk**: Disputes over when payment is due
**Suggestion**: Define specific milestones or deliverables triggering payment

## 4. Intellectual Property Risks

### 🔴 Critical: Work-for-Hire Without Limitation
**Pattern**: "All work product shall be work-made-for-hire..."
**Risk**: May inadvertently transfer background IP
**Suggestion**: Limit to specific deliverables only; exclude background IP

### 🟡 Warning: Broad IP Assignment
**Pattern**: "Contractor assigns all rights, title, and interest in all inventions..."
**Risk**: Captures inventions unrelated to work
**Suggestion**: Limit to inventions conceived during work on specific project

### 🟡 Warning: Missing License Grant
**Pattern**: No explicit license for pre-existing IP needed to use deliverables
**Risk**: Cannot legally use delivered work
**Suggestion**: Add license for necessary background IP

## 5. Confidentiality Risks

### 🟡 Warning: Indefinite Confidentiality
**Pattern**: "Confidential information shall be kept confidential indefinitely"
**Risk**: Perpetual obligation may be unenforceable
**Suggestion**: Set reasonable term (3-5 years) or specify trade secret exception

### 🟡 Warning: Overbroad Confidentiality Definition
**Pattern**: "All information disclosed is confidential"
**Risk**: Unworkable in practice
**Suggestion**: Define specific categories and marking requirements

### 🟢 Advisory: Missing Return/Destruction Clause
**Pattern**: No requirement to return/destroy confidential info upon termination
**Risk**: Information remains in possession
**Suggestion**: Add return/destruction obligation

## 6. Dispute Resolution Risks

### 🔴 Critical: Unfair Venue Selection
**Pattern**: "All disputes shall be resolved in [Counterparty's home jurisdiction]"
**Risk**: Home court advantage to counterparty
**Suggestion**: Negotiate neutral venue or mutual agreement

### 🟡 Warning: No Alternative Dispute Resolution
**Pattern**: Silent on mediation/arbitration
**Risk**: Immediate litigation costs
**Suggestion**: Add mandatory mediation before litigation

### 🟡 Warning: One-Sided Attorney Fees
**Pattern**: "Prevailing party shall be entitled to attorney fees" (without mutuality)
**Risk**: Chilling effect on enforcing rights
**Suggestion**: Make attorney fee provision mutual

## 7. General Contract Risks

### 🔴 Critical: Automatic Renewal Without Notice
**Pattern**: "This agreement automatically renews for successive one-year terms"
**Risk**: Unintended renewal
**Suggestion**: Add notice requirement (30-60 days before renewal)

### 🟡 Warning: Unclear Governing Law
**Pattern**: No governing law clause
**Risk**: Uncertainty about applicable law
**Suggestion**: Specify governing law (typically your jurisdiction)

### 🟡 Warning: Assignment Restrictions
**Pattern**: "This agreement may not be assigned without prior written consent"
**Risk**: Limits business flexibility
**Suggestion**: Allow assignment to affiliates or in M&A transactions

### 🟢 Advisory: Missing Force Majeure
**Pattern**: No force majeure clause
**Risk**: No protection for unforeseeable events
**Suggestion**: Add standard force majeure provision

## Risk Assessment Matrix

| Risk Category | Critical | Warning | Advisory |
|--------------|----------|---------|----------|
| Liability | Unlimited liability, Broad indemnification | No liability cap | - |
| Termination | No termination right | Long notice, Harsh penalties | - |
| Payment | - | Extended terms, No late fees | Unclear schedule |
| IP | Work-for-hire issues | Broad assignment, No license | - |
| Confidentiality | - | Indefinite term, Overbroad def | Missing return clause |
| Dispute | Unfair venue | No ADR, One-sided fees | - |
| General | Auto-renewal | Unclear law, Assignment restriction | Missing force majeure |

## Usage Notes

This database covers common risk patterns but is not exhaustive. Contract risks vary by:
- Industry and business context
- Jurisdiction and applicable law
- Counterparty relationship
- Specific transaction terms

Always consult qualified legal counsel for important contracts.
