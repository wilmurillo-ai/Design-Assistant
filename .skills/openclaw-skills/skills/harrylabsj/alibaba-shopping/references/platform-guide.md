# Platform Guide

Use this reference to recommend suitable Alibaba ecosystem platforms.

## Platform Comparison

| Platform | Best For | MOQ | Price Level | Trust Level |
|----------|----------|-----|-------------|-------------|
| **Tmall (天猫)** | Brand shopping, authentic guarantee | 1 | Higher | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ |
| **Taobao (淘宝)** | Variety, small sellers, bargains | 1 | Medium | ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ |
| **1688** | Wholesale, factory sourcing, resellers | 10-100+ | Lowest | ⭐⭐⭐☆☆ |

## Decision Tree

```
User wants to buy:
  │
  ├─ Brand item (iPhone, Nike, etc.)
  │   └─→ Recommend: Tmall 旗舰店
  │
  ├─ Generic item, best price
  │   └─→ Recommend: Taobao (compare sellers)
  │
  ├─ Bulk/wholesale
  │   └─→ Recommend: 1688
  │
  └─ Unique/handmade
      └─→ Recommend: Taobao
```

## When to Use Each

### Tmall
- Electronics and appliances
- Brand clothing and accessories
- Cosmetics and skincare
- Items where authenticity is critical
- When buyer protection matters most

### Taobao
- Unique or niche products
- Handmade or custom items
- Price-sensitive purchases
- Variety and selection
- Small quantity orders

### 1688
- Bulk purchasing for resale
- Factory direct sourcing
- Custom manufacturing
- B2B procurement
- When MOQ is acceptable

## Final Rule
Recommend the platform that fits the user's quantity needs, trust requirements, and price sensitivity.
