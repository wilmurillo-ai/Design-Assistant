# Channel Guide

Use this reference to recommend suitable Alibaba ecosystem channels.

## Prefer 1688
- for wholesale and factory-oriented sourcing
- when low unit price matters and minimum order is acceptable

## Prefer Tmall/Taobao ecosystem
- for consumer retail buying
- when smaller quantity, convenience, and buyer protection matter more

## Final rule
Recommend the channel that fits the user's quantity, trust, and sourcing needs.
