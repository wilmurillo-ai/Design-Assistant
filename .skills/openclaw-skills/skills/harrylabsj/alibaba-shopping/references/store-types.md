# Store Types Guide

Understanding store badges and trust levels on Alibaba platforms.

## Tmall Store Types

| Store Badge | Chinese | Trust Level | Best For |
|-------------|---------|-------------|----------|
| **天猫旗舰店** | Flagship Store | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Brand authenticity, official products |
| **天猫专营店** | Specialized Store | ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ | Authorized brands, specific categories |
| **天猫超市** | Tmall Supermarket | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Daily necessities, groceries |
| **天猫国际** | Tmall Global | ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ | Imported goods, overseas products |

### Identification
- Look for red "天猫" badge
- Store name often includes brand name
- Higher entry requirements for sellers
- Better buyer protection

## Trust Level Summary

| Level | Badge | Meaning |
|-------|-------|---------|
| ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Highest | Official brand, verified factory, best protection |
| ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ | High | Verified business, good track record |
| ⭐⭐⭐☆☆ | Medium | Standard seller, check reviews carefully |
| ⭐⭐☆☆☆ | Low | New or small seller, higher risk |

## Red Flags

Avoid sellers with:
- No sales history
- Low rating (< 4.6)
- No reviews or photos
- Stock images only
- Prices too good to be true
- Poor communication

## Green Flags

Prefer sellers with:
- High rating (4.8+)
- Many positive reviews with photos
- Detailed product descriptions
- Responsive customer service
- Clear return policy
- Long operating history
