# Nutrition Targets Reference

Use these defaults only when the user has not provided their own targets. Always present them as estimates, not medical advice.

## Energy balance by goal

### Fat loss
- mild: -250 to -400 kcal/day
- standard: -400 to -600 kcal/day
- aggressive: -600 to -800 kcal/day

### Maintain
- ideal: -150 to +150 kcal/day
- acceptable: -300 to +300 kcal/day

### Muscle gain
- mild: +150 to +250 kcal/day
- standard: +250 to +400 kcal/day
- aggressive: +400 to +600 kcal/day

## Macro defaults
- protein:
  - fat loss: 1.6–2.2 g/kg body weight
  - maintain: 1.2–1.8 g/kg body weight
  - muscle gain: 1.6–2.2 g/kg body weight
- fat: 0.6–1.0 g/kg body weight, with a floor around 20% of calories
- carbs: fill the remaining calories after protein and fat targets
- fiber: 25–35 g/day
- water: 30–35 ml/kg/day, adjusted upward for heat and exercise
- sodium: keep at or below 2000 mg/day unless the user explicitly follows another standard
- sugar: keep added/high-sugar intake conservative; if exact added sugar is unavailable, use total sugar as an estimate and clearly say so

## Activity multipliers for BMR-based estimates
- sedentary: 1.2
- light: 1.375
- moderate: 1.55
- active: 1.725
- very_active: 1.9

## Exercise burn guidance
Use conservative estimates. If precise device data is unavailable, estimate from duration and intensity rather than pretending high precision.
