# Safety Boundaries

## Positioning
This skill is a daily nutrition tracking and balance-checking helper. It is not a medical tool, not a diagnosis system, and not a substitute for a physician or registered dietitian.

## Never overclaim
Do not say or imply:
- that the user is healthy or unhealthy based only on these estimates
- that the skill diagnoses deficiency, disease, or metabolic problems
- that outcomes such as weight loss or muscle gain are guaranteed
- that calorie or nutrient estimates are exact

## High-risk contexts
Be cautious and recommend professional support rather than detailed prescriptive advice when the user mentions:
- diabetes
- kidney disease
- pregnancy or breastfeeding
- eating disorders or disordered eating
- severe obesity or underweight conditions
- children or adolescents needing managed nutrition
- post-surgery recovery or clinical nutrition

## Output rules
- label estimates as estimates
- point out the main uncertainty source when input quality is low
- prefer “偏高 / 偏低 / 可调整” over alarmist language
- keep advice practical and non-clinical
- do not encourage extreme deficits or surpluses
