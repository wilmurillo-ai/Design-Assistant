# Staffing Agency Operations

Hard numbers for running a staffing agency — markup rates, payroll burden, compliance requirements, and growth benchmarks from $0 to $10M+.

Covers temp, temp-to-hire, and direct placement across light industrial, clerical, healthcare, and IT verticals.

## What's Inside

- **Billing & markup rates** by vertical (6 categories)
- **Payroll burden calculator** — FICA, FUTA, SUTA, workers' comp, ACA
- **Weekly KPI dashboard** — fill rate, time-to-fill, DSO, recruiter productivity
- **Compliance matrix** — I-9, ACA, FLSA joint employer, OSHA, state licensing
- **Technology stack costs** — ATS, VMS, payroll, background checks
- **Growth playbook** — four stages from solo operator to $10M+
- **Cash flow planning** — working capital needs by revenue level

## Who This Is For

Staffing agency owners, recruiters going independent, workforce solutions companies, and anyone building AI agents for the $174B US staffing industry.
