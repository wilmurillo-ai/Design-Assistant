# Spend Intelligence Framework

Turn raw transaction data into cost savings. Categorize spending, flag waste patterns, benchmark against 2026 industry data, and generate prioritized action plans.

Built for operations teams, finance leads, and founders who want to stop guessing where money goes.

## What It Does

- Classifies spending into fixed/variable/discretionary/revenue-generating
- Flags 6 common waste patterns (duplicate SaaS, zombie subscriptions, price creep, vendor concentration, timing penalties, overprovision)
- Benchmarks against 2026 industry ratios (SaaS, services, manufacturing)
- Generates 90-day optimization calendar with dollar-specific targets
- Projects cash runway before and after optimizations

## Who It's For

- CFOs and finance teams at 15-200 person companies
- Founders tracking burn rate
- Operations leads running vendor reviews
- Anyone who just got a "why is our spend up 30%?" Slack message
