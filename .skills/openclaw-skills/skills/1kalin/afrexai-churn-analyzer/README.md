# Churn Risk Analyzer

Stop losing customers you could have saved.

This skill turns your usage data, support tickets, and engagement signals into a prioritized churn risk scorecard. Each account gets a 0-100 risk score with specific retention actions.

## What You Get
- Churn risk scoring across 6 behavioral dimensions
- Accounts ranked by revenue at risk
- Specific retention plays for each critical account
- 30/60/90 day action calendar

## Works With
- CSV exports from any CRM or billing system
- Manual account descriptions
- Support ticket logs
- Usage/analytics data

## No Data? No Problem.
Describe your customers qualitatively and the skill adapts — you don't need a data warehouse to start identifying risk.
