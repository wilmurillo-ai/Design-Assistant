# Plumbing Business Operations

Everything you need to run a more profitable plumbing company — pricing, dispatching, licensing, inventory management, and a 4-stage growth playbook from owner-operator to $10M+.

## What's Inside

- **Pricing & estimating** — flat-rate structures, markup targets, commercial bidding process
- **Dispatching** — zone-based scheduling, tech utilization targets, software stack
- **Licensing & compliance** — state requirements, permits, OSHA, EPA Lead-Safe, insurance
- **Inventory & fleet** — truck stock standards, par levels, vendor management
- **Marketing** — highest ROI channels for plumbers, key metrics, maintenance agreements
- **Growth playbook** — 4 stages from $0 to $10M+ with benchmarks at each level
- **Financial benchmarks** — revenue per tech, margins, overhead targets

## Who It's For

Plumbing company owners, service managers, and anyone using AI agents to automate plumbing business operations.
