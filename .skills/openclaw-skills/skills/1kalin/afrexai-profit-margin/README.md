# Profit Margin Analyzer

Analyze, benchmark, and optimize profit margins across your business. Built for founders, CFOs, and ops leaders who want to stop guessing and start measuring.

## What You Get
- Gross, operating, and net margin calculations by product/service line
- 2026 industry benchmarks across 10 sectors
- Margin erosion checklist (the 7 silent killers)
- The 1% pricing rule — why small price moves create outsized profit impact
- Pricing power assessment framework
- 90-day margin optimization roadmap
- Contribution margin templates to identify what to kill vs scale

## Why This Matters
Most businesses track revenue. Few track margin by product line. The ones that do consistently outperform — because they know which dollars are worth chasing and which ones cost more than they earn.
