# Reading framework

Use this file when you need a sharper structure for a specific BaZi reading type.

## Required inputs

- Date of birth
- Time of birth if known
- Place of birth
- Requested focus

If time is unknown, say so explicitly and reduce certainty.

## Global disclaimer patterns

Use one short honesty line near the top. Examples:
- "This is a reflective BaZi-style reading, not a precise chart audit."
- "Without a dedicated Chinese calendar calculator in front of me, treat this as a high-level traditional reading."
- "Good for insight; not for betting the farm."

## Personality reading

Focus on:
- internal authority
- social style
- emotional style
- gifts
- shadow traits
- how the person behaves under strain

Useful themes:
- discernment
- standards
- pattern recognition
- command energy
- guardedness vs openness
- how they relate to weak structure or false hierarchy

End with:
- one distilled sentence
- 2-3 follow-up options such as shadow traits, relationships, or how others experience them

## Current luck cycle reading

Focus on:
- what the current phase is asking from the person
- what gets rewarded vs punished
- where energy wants to consolidate or expand
- authority, timing, and selective pressure
- practical behavior changes for the next stretch

Useful themes:
- restructuring
- leverage
- clarity
- reducing noise
- stronger boundaries
- selective aggression

## Money reading

Focus on:
- how wealth tends to build for this type of person
- where leakage happens
- what kinds of financial environments fit or do not fit
- the difference between opportunity and noise
- what strengthens disciplined capital deployment

Useful themes:
- structure over hustle
- command money vs busy money
- conviction with rules
- hidden leakage
- protecting capital from chaos

## Career reading

Focus on:
- where the person creates value
- whether they fit operator, builder, strategist, advisor, allocator, or specialist patterns
- whether they need authority, autonomy, or architecture influence
- what work environments nourish or poison them

Useful themes:
- clarity under complexity
- shaping systems
- monetising judgment
- avoiding high-responsibility / low-control traps

## Career + money reading

Blend:
- value creation
- authority
- leverage
- compounding systems
- ownership vs mere contribution

Emphasize:
- how commercial upside improves when judgment becomes repeatable
- why strategic labor should not be donated endlessly into broken systems

## Relationships reading

Focus on:
- intimacy style
- trust thresholds
- attraction patterns
- what creates security vs friction
- where standards help or isolate

Avoid deterministic soulmate nonsense.

## Year-specific reading

Focus on:
- major theme of the year
- pressure points
- where caution helps
- what to cultivate
- which types of action are timely vs premature

Keep the tone directional rather than prophetic.

## Tone guide

Aim for:
- elegant
- grounded
- a little dramatic
- psychologically literate
- practical

Avoid:
- dense technical jargon unless requested
- fake certainty
- doom prophecy
- generic positivity sludge
