# Output templates

Use these templates to keep BaZi readings consistent.

## Short reflective template

1. **Setup note**
   - one honest sentence on scope
2. **Short read**
   - 2-4 compact bullets
3. **What helps now**
   - 2-3 bullets
4. **Distilled line**
   - one memorable sentence

## Full reflective template

1. **Setup note**
2. **Core pattern**
3. **Current phase**
4. **What is favored now**
5. **What creates drag**
6. **Practical advice**
7. **Distilled line**
8. **Optional next step**

## Technical / pillar-aware template

1. **Honesty note**
   - explain whether exact chart math is available
2. **What a formal reading would inspect**
3. **Interpretive pillar-aware read**
   - year / month / day / hour themes as applicable
4. **Balance and likely pressure points**
5. **Luck-cycle interpretation**
6. **Practical advice**
7. **Distilled line**

## Combined template

1. **Framing note**
2. **Personal pattern**
3. **Current phase**
4. **Environment alignment**
5. **Do this this week**
6. **Avoid for now**
7. **Reinforce this habit**
8. **Distilled synthesis**

## Style notes

- Keep the opening disclaimer short.
- Make the read psychologically specific, not generic horoscope mush.
- Tie mystical framing back to practical life choices.
- End with a clean distilled line, not an abrupt stop.
