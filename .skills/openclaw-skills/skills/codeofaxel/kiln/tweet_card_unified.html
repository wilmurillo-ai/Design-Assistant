Reactive — Emergency Stop System
🛑

Persistent E-stop latch

Survives restarts. Blocks start, resume, dispatch, and hazardous G-code until a human clears it.

✋

Human-in-the-loop clearing

No silent resume. Inspect the printer, acknowledge the hazard, then clear with a signed note.

Predictive — Print Trend Analysis
📈

Health scoring & failure trends

Per-printer health score. Tracks success rates, duration anomalies, and recurring failure modes over time.

🔍

Material reliability tracking

Per-material success rates per printer. Surfaces gaps like “ABS at 25% on this machine.”

Preventive — Ambient Condition Monitoring
🌡

Chamber temp vs. material limits

PLA in a hot enclosure? ABS without chamber heat? 11 materials with thermal profiles, checked before printing.

⚠

Thermal runaway & cooldown advisories

Critical alert if chamber exceeds safety max. Cooldown wait times between material swaps.
