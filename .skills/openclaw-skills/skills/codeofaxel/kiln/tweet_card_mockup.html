■ Safety Release
Safety hardening for
AI-controlled printers
When AI agents control physical machines, the emergency stop needs to be more than a software flag.
🛑

Persistent E-stop latch

Survives software restarts. The printer stays stopped until a human says otherwise.

✋

Explicit acknowledge-to-clear

No silent resume. A human must inspect, acknowledge, and clear before operations resume.

🚫

Hard safety gates while latched

Start, resume, dispatch, and hazardous G-code are all blocked. No exceptions, no workarounds.

🔌

External trip input

Bridge physical E-stop buttons or PLC signals directly into the safety system.
