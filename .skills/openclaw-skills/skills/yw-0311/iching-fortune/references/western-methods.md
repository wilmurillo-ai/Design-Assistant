# Western/International Divination Methods

## Tarot

### Overview
Card-based divination system originating from 15th century Italy. Uses 78 cards divided into Major Arcana (22 trump cards) and Minor Arcana (56 suit cards).

### Process
1. **洗牌**: Shuffle the deck while focusing on question
2. **切牌**: Cut the deck (optional)
3. **布局**: Choose and lay out cards in a spread
4. **读牌**: Interpret each card in position
5. **综合**: Synthesize into narrative

### Required Information
- Question or area of life
- Deck preference (Rider-Waite-Smith, Thoth, etc.)
- No birth data required

### Popular Spreads
| Spread | Cards | Use |
|--------|-------|-----|
| One Card | 1 | Quick daily guidance |
| Three Card | 3 | Past/Present/Future |
| Celtic Cross | 10 | Comprehensive reading |
| Horseshoe | 7 | Extended situation |

- 0 The Fool: New beginnings, innocence
- I The Magician: Manifestation, power
- II The High Priestess: Intuition, secrets
- III The Empress: Abundance, nurturing
- IV The Emperor: Authority, structure
- V The Hierophant: Tradition, guidance
- VI The Lovers: Choice, union
- VII The Chariot: Determination, victory
- VIII Justice: Fairness, truth
- IX The Hermit: Introspection, guidance
- X Wheel of Fortune: Fate, cycles
- XI Strength: Courage, patience
- XII The Hanged Man: Surrender, new perspective
- XIII Death: Transformation, endings
- XIV Temperance: Balance, patience
- XV The Devil: Shadow, bondage
- XVI The Tower: Upheaval, revelation
- XVII The Star: Hope, renewal
- XVIII The Moon: Illusion, fear
- XIX The Sun: Joy, success
- XX Judgement: Reflection, rebirth
- XXI The World: Completion, accomplishment

### Minor Arcana Suits
- Wands/Cups/Swords/Pentacles
- Each suit: Ace-10 + Page/Knight/Queen/King

## Astrology

### Overview
Ancient system interpreting planetary positions to understand personality and predict events. Major traditions include Western, Chinese, Hindu (Vedic), and Mayan astrology.

### Process
1. **收集数据**: Birth date, time, location
2. **计算星盘**: Calculate planetary positions
3. **分析相位**: Evaluate aspects between planets
4. **论断**: Provide interpretation

### Required Information
- ✅ 出生日期
- ✅ 出生时间 (精确到分钟)
- ✅ 出生地点 (经纬度)

### Key Components
- 十二星座: 12 Zodiac signs
- 十大行星: Sun, Moon, Mercury, Venus, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune, Pluto
- 十二宫位: 12 Houses (life areas)
- 相位: Aspects (conjunction, opposition, trine, square, sextile)

### Chart Types
- Natal Chart: Birth map
- Transit Chart: Current planetary positions
- Progressed Chart: Evolutionary development
- Synastry: Relationship compatibility

## Numerology

### Overview
Using numbers derived from name and birth date to reveal character traits and life patterns. Based on Pythagorean principles.

### Process
1. **获取数字**: From name and birth date
2. **计算命数**: Calculate core numbers
3. **解读**: Interpret meanings

### Required Information
- 出生日期
- 姓名 (身份证上的名字)

### Core Numbers
| Number | Name | Meaning |
|--------|------|---------|
| 1 | Life Path | Purpose and direction |
| 2 | Expression | Talents and abilities |
| 3 | Soul Urge | Inner desires |
| 4 | Personality | How others see you |
| 5 | Birthday | Special talents |
| 6-9 | Master | Special significance |

### Letter Values (Pythagorean)
```
A J S = 1    B K T = 2    C L U = 3
D M V = 4    E N W = 5    F O X = 6
G P Y = 7    H Q Z = 8    I R = 9
```

## Crystal Gazing (Scrying)

### Overview
Ancient practice of gazing into a reflective surface to receive visions or information. Surfaces include crystal balls, mirrors, water, or dark stones.

### Process
1. **准备**: Cleanse the scrying surface
2. **放松**: Enter meditative state
3. **凝视**: Softly gaze without focusing
4. **记录**: Note visions and impressions
5. **解读**: Interpret received information

### Required Information
- Question or focus
- Clear scrying surface
- Darkened, quiet environment

### Scrying Surfaces
- Crystal ball (最经典)
- Black mirror (黑镜)
- Water (水池)
- Obsidian stone (黑曜石)
- Bowl of milk or ink

## Tasseography (Tea Leaf Reading)

### Overview
Reading tea leaves to divine the future. Popular in Middle East, UK, and Greece. Residual patterns in empty cup reveal symbols.

### Process
1. **泡茶**: Brew loose leaf tea
2. **饮用**: Drink until only dregs remain
3. **翻转**: Turn cup upside down on saucer
4. **等待**: Let sediment settle
5. **解读**: Read patterns formed

### Required Information
- Loose leaf tea (not bagged)
- Question or intent
- Clear cup (white interior best)

### Common Symbols
- Animals: Various meanings
- Objects: House, tree, boat, etc.
- Letters: Form initials
- Numbers: Timeline indicators

## Oracle Cards

### Overview
Modern divination using decks with various themes (Angels, Goddess, Oracle). More flexible than Tarot, often simpler interpretations.

### Process
1. **洗牌**: Shuffle while focusing
2. **选择**: Draw cards
3. **解读**: Read card meanings

### Required Information
- Question
- Specific deck (various themes available)

### Popular Oracle Deck Types
- Angel Cards (Doreen Virtue)
- Goddess Cards
- Faery Cards
- Animal Spirit Guides
- Archetype Cards
