# Chinese Divination Methods

## 易经 (I Ching) - Book of Changes

### Overview
One of the oldest Chinese classic texts, dating back to 1000 BCE. Uses 64 hexagrams (八卦) composed of yin (broken lines) and yang (solid lines) to divine the future.

### Process
1. **工具**: 3 coins OR 50 yarrow stalks
2. **投掷**: Throw coins 6 times to form one hexagram
3. **记录**: Each throw creates one line (bottom to top)
4. **查卦**: Look up hexagram in the I Ching text
5. **变卦**: Check for changing lines (optional)

### Required Information
- Question/intent clearly defined
- Date and time of reading (optional, for hexagram timing)
- No personal birth data needed

### Key Concepts
- 阴阳 (Yin/Yang): 6 lines per hexagram
- 八卦 (Ba Gua): 8 trigrams
- 卦辞 (Gua Ci): Hexagram judgment text
- 爻辞 (Yao Ci): Individual line meanings
- 变爻: Changing lines indicate transformation

## 八字 (Ba Zi) - Four Pillars of Destiny

### Overview
"Four Pillars" uses the year, month, day, and hour of birth to create a命运的 chart. Each pillar consists of one heavenly stem and one earthly branch.

### Process
1. **收集信息**: Exact birth date, time, location
2. **排盘**: Calculate the Four Pillars (年柱、月柱、日柱、时柱)
3. **起大运**: Determine major luck cycles
4. **分析**: Evaluate relationships between elements and pillars
5. **论命**: Provide interpretation

### Required Information
- ✅ 出生年月日时 (精确到小时)
- ✅ 出生地点 (用于真太阳时)
- ✅ 性别 (部分流派需要)

### Key Concepts
- 十天干: 10 Heavenly Stems (甲乙丙丁戊己庚辛壬癸)
- 十二地支: 12 Earthly Branches (子丑寅卯辰巳午未申酉戌亥)
- 五行: Five Elements (木火土金水)
- 十神: Ten Gods (relationships)
- 大运: 10-year fortune cycles
- 流年: Annual fortune

### Difficulty Notes
- Self-study: 3-5 years to become proficient
- Professional reading requires deep knowledge of Chinese metaphysics

## 紫微斗数 (Zi Wei Dou Shu) - Purple Star Astrology

### Overview
Chinese astrological system that maps 14 major stars onto the fate plate (命盘). Considered one of the "Three Mountains" of Chinese destiny studies.

### Process
1. **定命盘**: Create natal chart based on birth data
2. **排星耀**: Place stars in the 12 palaces
3. **论格局**: Identify fate patterns
4. **看运势**: Analyze current and future periods

### Required Information
- 出生年月日时 (精确)
- 出生地点 (计算命宫)

### Key Concepts
- 命宫: Life Palace (self)
- 身宫: Body Palace
- 十二宫: 12 Palaces (each representing life aspect)
- 主星: Major stars (紫微、天机、太阳等)
- 辅星: Supporting stars
- 四化: Transformation (禄权科忌)

## 手相 (Shou Xiang) - Palmistry

### Overview
Reading the lines and mounts of the palm to understand character and destiny. Cross-cultural practice with roots in ancient India.

### Process
1. **观察**: Study hand shape and size
2. **读线**: Analyze major lines (生命线、感情线、事业线、智慧线)
3. **看丘**: Evaluate mounts (丘)
4. **查纹**: Check special marks and secondary lines

### Required Information
- 双手清晰照片 (最好自然光)
- 性别 (影响部分解读)

### Key Lines
- 生命线 (Life Line): Vitality, health, life journey
- 感情线/爱情线 (Heart Line): Love, emotions
- 事业线/命运线 (Career Line): Career, ambition
- 智慧线/脑线 (Head Line): Intellect, thinking
- 婚姻线 (Marriage Lines): Romantic history

### Key Mounts
- 拇指根部: Venus mount
- 小指下方: Mercury mount
- 食指下方: Jupiter mount
- 无名指下方: Saturn mount
- 中指下方: Sun/Apollo mount

## 面相 (Mian Xiang) - Physiognomy

### Overview
Reading facial features to determine character and fortune. Based on the principle that the face reflects the inner self.

### Process
1. **观整体**: Overall face shape and proportions
2. **分区**: Analyze specific areas (三庭五眼)
3. **看五官**: Detailed reading of features
4. **察气色**: Observe complexion and energy

### Required Information
- 清晰正面照片 (最好素颜)
- 年龄参考

### Key Areas
- 三庭: Upper/Middle/Lower court
- 五眼: Five-eye proportion
- 十二宫: Face as a map of life
- 五官: Ears, eyebrows, eyes, nose, mouth

## 风水 (Feng Shui) - Geomancy

### Overview
"Wind and Water" - ancient Chinese practice of harmonizing with environmental energy (气).

### Process
1. **勘测**: Survey the space
2. **定向**: Determine compass directions
3. **布局**: Recommend adjustments
4. **化煞**: Address negative influences

### Required Information
- 地址或平面图
- 房屋/办公室朝向
- 居住者出生日期 (用于命理配合)

### Key Concepts
- 八卦: Eight directions
- 九宫: Nine palaces grid
- 罗盘: Feng Shui compass (罗盘)
- 气: Qi (life force energy)
- 形煞: Physical sha (negative energy forms)

## 梅花易数 (Mei Hua Yi Shu) - Plum Blossom Numerology

### Overview
Flexible divination system using any random numbers, sounds, or observations to generate hexagrams. Known for its spontaneity and accessibility.

### Process
1. **起卦**: Generate hexagram from random numbers, sounds, or observations
2. **分析**: Interpret the hexagram and changing lines
3. **应事**: Connect to the question

### Required Information
- Question or intent
- Number, sound, or object as catalyst

### Key Feature
- 可以用任意数字起卦
- 听声音、观景象皆可
- 灵活多变，不拘形式

## 抽签 (Chou Qian) - Drawing Lots

### Overview
Simple method using drawn lots (签) to receive guidance. Found in temples, divination books, and various systems.

### Process
1. **请神**: Invoke divine presence
2. **祈求**: Formulate the question
3. **求签**: Draw a签
4. **解签**: Interpret the签文

### Required Information
- 明确的祈求或问题
- 签筒或数字系统

### Types
- 观音签: Guanyin lots (49 or 100签)
- 关帝签: Guan Yu lots
- 妈祖签: Mazu lots
- 灵签: Generic spiritual lots
