# Fortune-Telling Preparation Guide

## General Principles

### Before Any Reading

1. **明确目的**: Have a clear question or area of focus
2. **保持开放**: Approach without rigid expectations
3. **记录时间**: Note the exact date/time of the reading
4. **环境整洁**: Choose quiet, comfortable space

### Mental Preparation

1. **静心**: Take a few deep breaths
2. **专注**: Focus on your question
3. **诚心**: Approach with genuine intent
4. **接受**: Be open to unexpected answers

## Method-Specific Preparation

### 🔮 I Ching (易经)

**Before:**
- Clear your mind
- Have 3 coins (or 50 yarrow stalks if traditional)
- Write down your question clearly
- Note the date and time

**During:**
- Focus intently while casting
- Record each result immediately
- Take notes on your intuition

**After:**
- Meditate on the reading
- Research the hexagram meanings
- Consider how changing lines relate to your situation

### 📊 Ba Zi (八字)

**Essential Information:**
| Item | Required | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| 出生年份 | ✅ 必填 | 农历还是公历? |
| 出生月份 | ✅ 必填 | 需确认是否精确到节气 |
| 出生日期 | ✅ 必填 | 公历日期 |
| 出生时间 | ✅ 必填 | 精确到时辰(2小时) |
| 出生地点 | 建议 | 用于真太阳时 |
| 性别 | 建议 | 影响部分分析 |

**Important Notes:**
- Time accuracy is crucial (四柱命理学以时辰划分)
- 夏令时要还原为标准时间
- 跨时区出生要注意

### 🃏 Tarot

**Before:**
- Clean your deck (optional purification)
- Hold cards while thinking of question
- Shuffle until feel ready
- Decide on spread

**During:**
- Focus on one clear question
- Trust your first impressions
- Note any intuitive hits

**After:**
- Journal your reading
- Consider card interactions
- Look for patterns across cards

---

### ⭐ Astrology

**Essential Information:**
| Item | Required | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| 出生日期 | ✅ | 公历 |
| 出生时间 | ✅ | 精确到分钟 |
| 出生地点 | ✅ | 城市名或经纬度 |

**Time Accuracy Matters:**
- Birth time determines house placements
- 4-minute difference can change house
- Hospital records often have approximate times

---

### ✋ Palmistry (手相)

**Photo Requirements:**
- Good lighting (natural light best)
- Clear focus on palm
- Both hands (traditionally left=inherited, right=current)
- Fingers spread comfortably
- Avoid shadows on palm lines

**Optional Information:**
- Age
- Gender (for some traditions)

---

### 🎭 Face Reading (面相)

**Photo Requirements:**
- Straight-on, level photo
- Neutral expression
- No sunglasses or heavy makeup
- Natural lighting
- Full face visible (ears to chin)

---

### 🏠 Feng Shui (风水)

**Information to Prepare:**
- 地址或房屋平面图
- 房屋朝向 (用罗盘测量或从地图确定)
- 主要居住者出生日期 (可选,用于命理配合)
- 问题区域描述

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Wrong
- Asking vague questions ("What will happen to me?")
- Asking multiple questions at once
- Expecting absolute answers
- Ignoring contradictory information

### ✅ Right
- One clear question at a time
- "Should I...?" or "What should I know about...?"
- Accepting guidance, not prophecy
- Using reading for insight, not excuses

---

## Questions by Category

### Love/Relationships
- "What should I know about my current relationship?"
- "What energy is affecting my love life?"
- "Should I pursue this connection?"

### Career/Work
- "What career path suits me?"
- "What阻碍 my professional growth?"
- "When is a good time to job search?"

### Decision Making
- "What should I consider in this decision?"
- "What are the likely outcomes if I choose X?"
- "What energy surrounds this situation?"

### General Guidance
- "What do I need to know right now?"
- "What lesson is appearing in my life?"
- "What should I focus on this month/year?"

## Interpretation Guidelines

### Important Reminders

1. **不是预言**: Fortune-telling shows trends, not fixed futures
2. **可变**: Energy changes, so can outcomes
3. **配合**: Reading works with free will, not against it
4. **参考**: Use guidance to make informed choices

### When Results Seem Negative

1. Don't panic - energy can be transformed
2. Look for growth opportunities
3. Consider timing factors
4. Ask for clarification in follow-up reading

### Integration Steps

1. Reflect on the message
2. Consider how it applies to your situation
3. Take inspired action if indicated
4. Check back in future readings

## Finding a Professional Reader

### What to Look For

- Clear explanation of their method
- Reasonable pricing (avoid extremes)
- Willingness to explain process
- Not making guarantees
- References or reviews

### Red Flags

- Guarantees specific outcomes
- Pressuring for expensive packages
- Refusing to explain method
- Claiming supernatural powers
- Asking for excessive personal information

### Questions to Ask

1. What method do they use?
2. How long have they practiced?
3. Can they explain the reading?
4. What if I don't understand?
