# 30-Day TikTok Posting Calendar

## Week 1-2: Test Phase (14 days)
Goal: Find what works. Post 2x/day = 28 videos.

### Daily Schedule
| Time | Content Type | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 7:30 AM | Comedy/Story (AI image series) | Morning commute viewers |
| 8:30 PM | Trend/Reaction | Evening peak traffic |

### Content Rotation (7-day cycle)
| Day | Morning | Evening |
|---|---|---|
| Mon | Comedy Story | Behind-the-scenes |
| Tue | Comedy Story | Trend-riding |
| Wed | Educational (how AI works) | Comedy Story |
| Thu | Comedy Story | Engagement poll |
| Fri | Comedy Story | Comedy Story |
| Sat | Compilation/Best-of | Trend-riding |
| Sun | Comedy Story | Q&A / Comment reply |

### Metrics to Track Week 1-2
- 3-second view rate (goal: >40%)
- Average watch time (goal: >60% of video length)
- Comment rate (goal: >1% of views)
- Share rate (goal: >0.5% of views)

---

## Week 3-4: Double Down Phase (14 days)
Goal: Scale what works. Post 3x/day on winning formats.

Post 3x/day:
- 7:30 AM
- 12:30 PM  
- 8:30 PM

Focus exclusively on top 2 performing content types from Week 1-2.

---

## Month 2-3: Optimization Phase

### A/B Test Hooks
Same video body, 2 different hooks. Post both, compare performance.

### Series Format
Create named series (viewers come back for next episode):
- "The Adventures of [Character]" — ongoing story
- "Mission: [X]" — new mission each week
- "What happens when [Character] tries [thing]" — format people expect

### Engagement Tactics
- Reply to every comment in first hour (algorithm boost)
- Pin best comment on each video
- Create videos responding to comments ("You asked if the hamster succeeded...")
- Duet popular videos with your character

---

## Hashtag Strategy

### Per Video Mix
```
#[YourCharacter]           (e.g., #hamsterheist)
#AIart                     (broad AI audience)  
#funny                     (broad comedy)
#[ContentType]             (e.g., #storytelling)
#[TrendingSound/Challenge] (1 trending tag only)
```

### What NOT to Do
- ❌ Don't use #fyp or #foryoupage (oversaturated, no benefit)
- ❌ Don't use more than 5 hashtags
- ❌ Don't repeat the same hashtags on every video

---

## Optimal Video Specs
- Resolution: 1080 x 1920 (9:16)
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Length: 15-30 seconds (sweet spot for completion rate)
- File size: Under 500MB
- Format: MP4
- Caption: 1-2 sentences max + call-to-action + hashtags
