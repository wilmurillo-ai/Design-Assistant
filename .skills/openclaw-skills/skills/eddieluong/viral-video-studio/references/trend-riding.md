# Trend-Riding Playbook

## How TikTok Trends Work
Trends = sounds, challenges, or formats that TikTok is actively boosting.
Using a trending sound gives 2-3x more reach than original audio.

## Finding Trends
1. TikTok app → Discover → Trending sounds
2. Check what sounds top creators in your niche use this week
3. Look for sounds with 100K-1M uses (sweet spot — not oversaturated)

## Adapting Trends to Your Character
Formula: [Trending sound/format] + [Your character doing it unexpectedly]

Examples:
- Trending: "girl dinner" format → Your version: "hamster dinner" (tiny pile of seeds)
- Trending: dramatic reveal sound → Your version: character reveals "plan" that's obviously going to fail
- Trending: "expectation vs reality" → Your version: character's plan vs what actually happens

## Trend Adaptation Prompt
```
Current trending TikTok sound/format: [DESCRIBE TREND]
My character: [CHARACTER DESCRIPTION]
Generate 3 ways my character could participate in this trend in a funny/unexpected way.
Each idea: setup + character action + twist
```

## Sound Selection Priority
1. Trending sound from past 7 days (highest boost)
2. Trending sound from past 30 days (still effective)
3. Evergreen funny sounds (no boost but timeless)
4. Original audio (lowest reach, but builds brand sound identity)

## Stitch/Duet Strategy
Stitch or Duet popular videos of real cats/animals with your AI character reacting.
This hijacks the original video's audience.

Format: "POV: [your character] watches this happen 👀"
