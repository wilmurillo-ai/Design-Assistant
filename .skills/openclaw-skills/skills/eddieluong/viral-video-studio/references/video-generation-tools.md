## Kling AI Workflow

### Account Setup
1. Go to **klingai.com** → Sign up
2. Plans: Free (limited) / Standard ~$10/month / Pro ~$35/month
3. Free tier: ~5 short clips/day (enough to test)

### Kling AI Pricing (Credits)
- Standard: 660 credits/month = ~$10
- 1 clip (5s, standard quality) = 10 credits
- 1 clip (5s, professional quality) = 35 credits
- 1 clip (10s, professional) = 70 credits

**For 1 video (15 clips × 5s, professional):**
15 × 35 = 525 credits ≈ **$8/video** on Standard plan

**For scale (30 videos/month):**
30 × 525 = 15,750 credits → Pro plan (~$35) = **$1.17/video**

## Kling AI Prompt Formula

Kling works best with **specific, visual prompts**. Formula:

```
[CHARACTER DESCRIPTION], [ACTION], [CAMERA MOVEMENT], [SETTING], [MOOD/LIGHTING], [STYLE]
```

### Camera Movement Keywords
- `camera slowly zooms in on` — Ken Burns effect
- `camera follows character from behind`
- `low angle shot looking up at`
- `close-up on face showing expression`
- `wide establishing shot`
- `slow motion` — slows down action

### Motion Keywords
- `sneaking cautiously`
- `running frantically`
- `flying through the air`
- `falling slowly`
- `shaking with effort`

## Kling AI Tips for Character Consistency

1. **Save character as "Reference Image"** — upload 1 Hammy image as character reference
2. **Use Image-to-Video** instead of Text-to-Video for better consistency
3. **Workflow:**
   - Step 1: Generate 1 perfect Hammy image in DALL-E
   - Step 2: Upload to Kling as character reference
   - Step 3: Generate each clip using that reference image + motion prompt
4. **Negative prompt:** `deformed, blurry, different character, changing outfit`
