# TikTok Monetization Roadmap

## Milestone Map

```
0 → 1K followers      (~30-60 days at 2-3 posts/day)
1K → 10K followers    (~60-90 days)
10K → 50K followers   (~90-120 days)
50K → 100K followers  (~120-180 days)
```

---

## Revenue Streams by Milestone

### At 1,000 Followers
**TikTok Creator Rewards Program**
- Earn per 1,000 qualified views
- Rate: ~$0.02-0.04 per 1,000 views
- Requirement: 18+, 10,000 views in last 30 days, 1,000 followers
- Realistic: $50-200/month at this stage

**Start now (no follower minimum):**
- **Midjourney affiliate**: midjourney.com/affiliate — 20% recurring commission on $10-$30/month plans
- **CapCut Pro affiliate**: In-app creator program
- **Amazon affiliate**: Link AI drawing tablets, lighting equipment in bio

### At 10,000 Followers
**Brand Deals (AI Comedy Niche)**
- AI art tools (Midjourney, Adobe Firefly, Canva AI)
- Pet products (ironic given animal characters)
- Mobile apps targeting young audiences
- Rate: $100-500 per sponsored video

**How to get brand deals:**
1. Email: `partnerships@[brand].com`
2. Subject: "TikTok collaboration — [X]K followers, AI comedy niche"
3. Include: follower count, avg views, engagement rate, 3 best videos

### At 50,000 Followers
**Affiliate Marketing (Serious Revenue)**
- Commission from product links in bio/videos
- Best products for AI comedy niche:
  - AI tools: $5-30/conversion
  - Online courses: $20-100/conversion
  - Digital products: $10-50/conversion
- Realistic: $500-2,000/month

**Digital Products (Create Once, Sell Forever)**
- Prompt pack: "100 Viral AI Image Prompts" — sell for $9-29
- Template pack: CapCut templates for AI video series — sell for $19-49
- Mini-course: "Make Viral AI TikToks in 1 Hour" — sell for $29-99
- Sell via: Gumroad, Payhip, or direct link in bio

### At 100,000 Followers
**Sponsored Content**
- Rate: $1,000-5,000 per sponsored video
- Frequency: 2-4 deals/month
- Annual potential: $24,000-240,000

**Merchandise**
- Print-on-demand with your character designs (Printful + Shopify)
- T-shirts, stickers, phone cases with hamster/cat character
- Margin: $8-15 per item
- Realistic: $500-3,000/month at 100K

**Live Gifts**
- TikTok LIVE allows viewers to send virtual gifts
- Convert to real money at ~$0.005-0.01 per coin
- Realistic: $200-1,000/month with engaged community
