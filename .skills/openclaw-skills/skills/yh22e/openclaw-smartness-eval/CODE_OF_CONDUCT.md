# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- giving constructive feedback,
- being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences,
- focusing on what is best for the project and users.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments,
- personal or political attacks,
- public or private harassment.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.
