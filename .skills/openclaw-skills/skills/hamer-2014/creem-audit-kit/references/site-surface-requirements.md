# Site surface requirements (where to place what)

## Footer (public, every page)
- Links: Privacy, Terms, Refund, Content Policy, Contact/Support
- Support email visible or one-click away

## Pricing page
- Plan details (what customer gets)
- Billing period + auto-renew disclosure (if subscription)
- Refund summary (one-liner) + link to full Refund Policy
- Cancellation summary (one-liner) + link to Terms / billing portal

## Checkout entry points / paywall
- Inline links to Terms + Privacy + Refund before payment
- If subscription: “Renews automatically until canceled” language

## Account / Billing
- How to cancel / manage subscription
- How to contact support
- Link to Refund policy (especially if credits-based)

## Post-purchase confirmation
- Receipt/confirmation includes support contact + how to manage billing
