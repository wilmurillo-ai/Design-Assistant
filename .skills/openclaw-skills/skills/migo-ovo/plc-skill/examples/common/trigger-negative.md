# Negative trigger examples

## Example 1
User:
“PLC 是什么？”

Why not trigger:
- too broad
- no FX3U / GX Works2 / ST context

## Example 2
User:
“帮我选一个断路器型号”

Why not trigger:
- electrical selection, not PLC programming skill scope

## Example 3
User:
“西门子 S7-1200 怎么写这个逻辑？”

Why not trigger:
- non-Mitsubishi platform

## Example 4
User:
“这个急停线路这样接是否绝对安全？”

Why not trigger by default:
- safety-critical
- wiring/fail-safe dependent
- should not give confident conclusion without field confirmation
