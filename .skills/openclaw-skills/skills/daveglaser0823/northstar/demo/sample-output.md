# Northstar - Sample Output

These are real examples of what Northstar delivers every morning.
All numbers are illustrative. Your briefing uses your actual data.

---

## Good Day

```
📊 Northstar Daily Briefing - March 22
Revenue yesterday: $1,248 (+12% vs last week)
Active subscribers: 342 (+3 new, -1 churn)
Month-to-date: $18,430 (74% of $24,900 goal)
Shopify: 23 orders fulfilled | 8 open
Top product: Growth Plan - Annual (7 units)
Next: 6 days left in month, on track.
```

**What this tells you:**
- Up 12% week-over-week. Growth is compounding.
- 342 active subs, net +2 yesterday. Healthy.
- 74% of monthly goal with 6 days left. On pace.
- No alerts. Go enjoy your coffee.

---

## Day That Needs Attention

```
📊 Northstar Daily Briefing - March 22
Revenue yesterday: $423.00 (-62% vs last week)
Active subscribers: 318 (+1 new, -4 churn)
Month-to-date: $12,100 (49% of $24,900 goal)
Shopify: 7 orders fulfilled | 4 open | 2 refund ($247.00)
Top product: Starter Plan (4 units)

⚠️ 4 payment issues pending - review in Stripe
⚠️ High churn: 4 cancellations yesterday
⚠️ Large refund: $247.00 - review in Shopify
Next: 11 days left in month, below pace.
```

**What this tells you:**
- Revenue down 62% vs. same day last week. Investigate.
- 4 churns in one day is above your threshold (3). Check why.
- 4 payment failures need manual review in Stripe.
- $247 in refunds - someone's unhappy. Shopify has the details.
- 49% of monthly goal at mid-month. Below pace.

Without Northstar, you'd find out about this at 10 AM after checking 5 tabs manually. With Northstar, you know at 6 AM and can act immediately.
