## Combining References

Good combinations for CanMarket:
- "Swiss Style typography meets James Turrell light"
- "Hiroshi Sugimoto's meditative quality with Bauhaus structure"
- "Apple product clarity with Tadao Ando's material honesty"
- "Kinfolk editorial restraint with Peter Zumthor atmosphere"

Avoid combinations that conflict with brand:
- Wes Anderson (too quirky/colored)
- Pop Art (too saturated)
- Maximalist designers (too busy)
- Street photography (too chaotic)
