# Ready-to-Use Prompt Examples

## Social Media (Mode 3: Thinking)

### LinkedIn Thought Leadership

```
Thought leadership visual for LinkedIn post about brand consistency.

Art direction inspired by Hiroshi Sugimoto's Seascapes and Swiss International Style.

Central composition: Abstract horizon line — a subtle gradient transition from deep charcoal (#171717) at bottom to soft light at top, with a single thin horizontal line in Interactive Blue (#2563EB) suggesting a threshold or clarity moment.

No text overlay — image only.

Background: Clean off-white (#FAFAFA) with barely perceptible paper texture. 70% negative space.

Lighting: Soft ambient glow along the horizon line, like dawn breaking. Diffused, no harsh shadows.

Mood: Contemplative, forward-looking, the quiet moment before clarity arrives.

Technical: Medium format aesthetic, subtle film grain, matte finish. Square format 1:1.

Avoid: Busy patterns, multiple colors, tech clichés (circuits, robots, blue glowing brains), stock photo feel, text.
```

### Twitter/X Brand Statement

```
Twitter/X visual for brand philosophy post.

Art direction inspired by Tadao Ando's use of light and concrete, Bauhaus geometric purity.

Central composition: Single geometric form — a clean rectangle or circle — in warm near-black (#18181B), positioned off-center with intentional asymmetry. One edge kissed by Interactive Blue (#2563EB) light.

Background: Warm off-white (#FAFAFA) with subtle concrete texture.

Lighting: Dramatic but soft — like sunlight entering through a narrow skylight. Clear shadows define the form.

Mood: Confident, grounded, intelligent. "We know what we stand for."

Technical: Architectural photography feel, crisp edges, no grain. 16:9 format.

Avoid: Illustrations, icons, multiple shapes, decorative elements, gradients.
```

### 小红书 Lifestyle

小红书 lifestyle visual for founder/brand story.

## Website/Product (Mode 1: Orientation)

### Homepage Hero

```
Homepage hero visual explaining Brand Operating System concept.

Art direction inspired by Bauhaus geometric abstraction and James Turrell light installations.

Central composition: Abstract architectural form — clean geometric shapes (rectangles, subtle curves) arranged to suggest "system" or "structure" — in warm near-black (#18181B) with one key element illuminated in Interactive Blue (#2563EB). Shapes cast soft shadows creating depth and dimension.

Background: Warm off-white (#FAFAFA) with subtle texture suggesting quality paper.

Lighting: Soft but defined — ambient glow from the blue element creates subtle illumination on surrounding forms. Like a thinking space at dusk with one light on.

Mood: Intelligent, structural, inviting. "This is where ideas become systems."

Technical: Architectural rendering aesthetic, medium format clarity, very slight warm tone. 16:9 widescreen.

Avoid: Illustrations, stock imagery, multiple colors, decorative elements, busy compositions, tech clichés.
```

### Feature Section Visual

```
Feature section visual for "Brand Memory" concept.

Art direction inspired by Peter Zumthor's atmospheric spaces and Muji's essential reduction.

Central composition: Abstract representation of "memory" or "storage" — perhaps layers of translucent planes stacked with subtle depth, predominantly grayscale with one layer tinted Interactive Blue (#2563EB).

Background: Pure white (#FFFFFF) transitioning to soft gray at edges, creating natural vignette.

Lighting: Soft ambient, as if the forms themselves emit gentle light. No harsh shadows.

Mood: Ethereal, systematic, trustworthy. "Your brand identity, safely structured."

Technical: 3D render aesthetic, clean anti-aliased edges, no texture. 4:3 format.

Avoid: File folder metaphors, brain imagery, cloud icons, literal interpretations.
```

## Campaign (Mode 4: Action)

### Launch Announcement

```
Product launch announcement visual.

Art direction inspired by Apple product reveals and Swiss poster design.

Central composition: Abstract "emergence" — a geometric form appearing from or breaking through a surface, suggesting "new arrival." Form in Interactive Blue (#2563EB), surface in off-white (#FAFAFA).

Typography: "Style Genome™ is here." in charcoal (#171717), semibold Inter, positioned lower third with generous margin.

Background: Clean gradient from off-white to very subtle warm gray, creating depth.

Lighting: Product spotlight feeling — soft but defined, form casts clear shadow establishing dimension.

Mood: Confident arrival, understated excitement, "finally."

Technical: Premium 4K product photography aesthetic, crisp, no grain. 16:9 format.

Avoid: Fireworks, confetti, "NEW!" badges, multiple colors, busy celebration elements.
```

### CTA Banner

```
Conversion banner for free trial signup.

Art direction inspired by Apple product photography and minimal SaaS design.

Central composition: Clean product interface mockup (abstract/simplified) floating with subtle shadow, positioned left following rule of thirds. Right side has clear space for text and CTA.

Typography:
- Headline: "See your brand, computed." in charcoal (#171717), semibold
- Subhead: "Style Genome™ makes aesthetics measurable." in gray (#525252), regular
- CTA button: Interactive Blue (#2563EB) with white text "Start Free Trial"

Background: Pure white (#FFFFFF) with subtle shadow beneath mockup creating ground plane.

Lighting: Clean product lighting from above-left, soft ambient fill, gentle shadows.

Mood: Professional, inviting, zero friction. Showing confidence by showing, not telling.

Technical: Crisp 4K render, no grain, clean vector-like edges. 16:9 or 2:1 banner ratio.

Avoid: Multiple CTAs, secondary buttons, busy backgrounds, decorative elements, hype language.
```

## Events & Occasions

### New Year / Fresh Start

```
New Year 2026 poster for premium tech brand.

Art direction inspired by James Turrell's light portals and Swiss International Style typography.

Central composition: Abstract geometric form suggesting "threshold" or "new beginning" — perhaps a subtle aperture or portal shape. Gradient from deep charcoal (#171717) transitioning through Interactive Blue (#2563EB) to soft warm light, evoking the feeling of stepping into illumination.

Typography: Clean sans-serif "2026" in charcoal (#171717), semibold weight, positioned with intentional asymmetry using rule of thirds. Supporting text "Build brands that last." in lighter gray (#525252).

Background: Soft off-white (#FAFAFA) with barely perceptible paper texture. Generous negative space — 60% of composition is breathing room.

Lighting: Soft diffused glow emanating from the geometric element, creating subtle ambient warmth. Like early morning light through frosted glass.

Mood: Contemplative, quietly confident, forward-looking. Not celebratory fireworks — more like the calm clarity of a fresh start.

Technical: High-end print advertisement aesthetic, subtle film grain (Kodak Portra warmth), matte finish. 4:5 or 1:1 format.

Avoid: Red and gold traditional palette, fireworks, confetti, busy patterns, decorative gradients, multiple saturated colors.
```

### Conference/Event

```
Conference speaking engagement announcement visual.

Art direction inspired by TED stage design and architectural photography.

Central composition: Abstract stage or podium suggestion — clean geometric forms creating depth and perspective, predominantly in warm blacks and grays with one element (podium edge or light strip) in Interactive Blue (#2563EB).

Background: Deep charcoal (#171717) to black gradient, creating dramatic but minimal backdrop.

Lighting: Dramatic stage lighting feel — single key light from above creating defined shadows. Spotlight quality without literal spotlight.

Mood: Authority, anticipation, "something important is about to be said."

Technical: Cinematic aspect ratio 2.35:1, subtle grain, high contrast but not harsh.

Avoid: Literal microphones, conference room photos, busy stage setups, multiple light colors.
```

## Abstract Concepts

### "Brand Memory" Concept

```
Abstract visual representing "Brand Memory" concept.

Art direction inspired by Olafur Eliasson's perceptual art and Hiroshi Sugimoto's minimalism.

Central composition: Layers of translucent geometric planes arranged with subtle depth, suggesting strata or accumulated layers. Predominantly grayscale with hints of Interactive Blue (#2563EB) glowing from within, as if light is stored inside.

Background: Soft gradient from white to very pale gray, endless depth.

Lighting: Internal glow — the forms seem to contain light rather than be lit externally. Ethereal, not clinical.

Mood: Permanent yet accessible, structured yet alive. "Memory that thinks."

Technical: Ethereal 3D render aesthetic, soft anti-aliased edges, slight atmospheric haze.

Avoid: Brain imagery, filing cabinets, database icons, literal storage metaphors.
```

### "Creative Intelligence" Concept

```
Abstract visual representing "Creative Intelligence" concept.

Art direction inspired by Bauhaus geometric play and James Turrell's color perception works.

Central composition: Dynamic arrangement of geometric forms (circles, rectangles, lines) in motion or tension — not chaotic, but alive. Forms in charcoal/black with one form or connection line in Interactive Blue (#2563EB) suggesting the "intelligence" spark.

Background: Clean off-white (#FAFAFA) with subtle texture.

Lighting: Even ambient with subtle shadows establishing depth. One area has slightly more luminosity suggesting the "spark" of intelligence.

Mood: Alive, structured, thinking. "Creativity with purpose."

Technical: Clean vector-inspired aesthetic but with subtle organic imperfection. 1:1 square format.

Avoid: Lightbulb imagery, brain imagery, neural network visualizations, robots, AI clichés.
```
