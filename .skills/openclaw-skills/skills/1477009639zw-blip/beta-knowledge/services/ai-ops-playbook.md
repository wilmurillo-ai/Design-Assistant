# AI Agent Operations — Service Playbook

> The real money in AI agents is NOT in building. It's in running.
> Source: Reddit r/Entrepreneur — "The real AI gold rush isn't in building. It's in babysitting."

---

## The Positioning

**NOT:** "I will build you an AI agent"
**YES:** "I will run your AI operations so you never have to think about it"

---

## Two Service Tiers

### Tier 1: AI Agent Setup (One-Time)
- Configure and deploy your first AI agent
- Connect to your tools (email, calendar, CRM, etc.)
- Train on your workflows
- Document everything
- **Price:** $500-$2,000
- **Time:** 1-3 days

### Tier 2: AI Operations Management (Recurring) ⭐ RECOMMENDED
- I run all your AI agents 24/7
- Monitor performance and fix issues
- Update prompts when context shifts
- Swap models when better/cheaper options emerge
- Weekly summary reports
- **Price:** $500-$2,000/month
- **Time:** 2-4 hours/week

---

## What I Actually Do (AI Ops)

Based on Reddit case study — a solo founder's "AI cofounder" does:

1. **Content Operations**
   - Research: audit data sources, find gaps
   - Writing: generate blog posts, social media, email drafts
   - Publishing: schedule and distribute

2. **Research & Intelligence**
   - Monitor competitors (pricing, products, job postings)
   - Track industry news, flag relevant items
   - Due diligence on partners/vendors

3. **Workflow Automation**
   - Connect tools that don't talk to each other
   - Automate repetitive data entry
   - Set up triggers and alerts

4. **Analytics & Reporting**
   - Daily/weekly summaries of key metrics
   - Anomaly detection (traffic drops, conversion changes)
   - Actionable insights from data

5. **Development Support**
   - Write and review code
   - Open pull requests for your review
   - Research technical solutions to problems

## Qualification Questions (for discovery call)

1. What are the top 3 tasks you spend time on that feel repetitive?
2. What tools are you currently using that don't work well together?
3. What would being "free from [task X]" mean for your business?
4. How many hours do you currently spend on [identified task] per week?

## What to Track Per Client

- Hours saved (per week/month)
- Tasks automated
- Reports delivered
- Issues resolved
- Models/agents running

This builds the case for renewal and upsell.
