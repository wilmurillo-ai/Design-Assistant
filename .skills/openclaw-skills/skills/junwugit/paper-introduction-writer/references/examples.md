# 结构化样例库（Worked Examples）

本文件提供 **7 个结构化引言样例**，每个样例包含：
- 论文题目
- 英文结构示意（用于学习结构，不逐字复用原文）
- 四组件结构标注
- 逐句功能分析
- 中文功能注释示例

生成新引言时可参照这些样例的**结构与叙述节奏**，而不是直接复制其内容。若需要查看某篇论文的完整原文，应回到合法来源或用户提供的材料。

---

## 样例 1：材料科学 —— Chitin-PLA 复合材料（核心示例）

**题目**：A light-weight chitin-PLA composite for load-bearing biomedical applications

### 英文结构示意（非逐字原文）

1. Establish biomass-derived PLA as an important material because of its degradability and biocompatibility.
2. Provide a basic factual definition: PLA comes from renewable feedstocks.
3. Connect those properties to biomedical applications.
4. Introduce a broad limitation: weak impact resistance.
5. Add a second constraint: biomedical reinforcement must remain low-toxicity.
6. Move into prior work on keratin-based reinforcement.
7. Mention a representative study showing that keratin can be incorporated into PLA.
8. Identify the specific gap: keratin increases weight, and alternatives have received limited attention.
9. State that the present paper develops selection criteria for an alternative component.
10. Briefly describe the selected material and preparation route.
11. Preview the result with calibrated contribution markers: lightweight, improved impact resistance, retained biocompatibility.

### 逐句结构分析

| 句号 | 组件 | 功能 |
|---|---|---|
| S1 | C1 | 建立主题重要性（"has received much attention in recent years"） |
| S2 | C1 | 提供背景事实（一般现在时）|
| S3 | C1 | 背景深化 + 引用支撑（应用场景）|
| S4 | C1 | 引出一般问题（However + 领域性弱点）|
| S5 | C1 | 扩展问题（另一维度——生物医用要求低毒）|
| S6 | C2 | 链接到已发表研究（keratin-PLA 复合物）|
| S7 | C2 | 列举关键研究（Penney et al., 2012）|
| S8 | C3 | 识别 gap（However, although... little attention has been paid）|
| S9 | C4 | 描述本文（The present paper presents...）|
| S10 | C4 | 简述方法（chitin 替代）|
| S11 | C4 | 预告主要成果 + 贡献标记词（如 lightweight, improved, retained）|

### 中文注释密度示例

【段落功能】此类短引言可把组件 1、2、3、4 压缩在一个连续段落中，但每一句仍应承担清晰功能：先建立主题重要性，再铺背景与一般问题，随后进入前人工作、gap 和本文贡献。

[主题材料] 近年来因 [关键性质] 而受到关注。*（功能：S1 — 建立主题重要性；用 "in recent years" + 现在完成时把主题定位为当前研究焦点。）*

[主题材料] 可由 [来源/组成] 制得。*（功能：S2 — 提供读者理解后文所需的基础事实；用一般现在时陈述已接受事实。）*

然而，[一般性能限制] 限制了其在 [目标应用] 中的使用。*（功能：S4 — 用 "However" 引出领域一般问题，为后文 gap 铺路。）*

尽管 [前人方案] 已经显示出一定效果，[该方案的局限] 仍然存在，且 [替代路径/关键变量] 尚未得到充分关注。*（功能：S8 — 组件 3：用让步 + 转折结构锁定具体 gap。）*

本文提出 [选择标准/方法]，并据此开发 [新材料/新系统]。*（功能：S9-S10 — 进入组件 4：说明本文做什么，并简要给出方法轮廓。）*

该方案实现了 [经证据支持的贡献]。*（功能：S11 — 用克制的贡献标记词预告结果；强断言必须有数据或引用支撑。）*

## 样例 2：神经科学（系统层面）—— Propofol

**题目**：Rapid fragmentation of neuronal networks at the onset of propofol-induced unconsciousness

### 英文结构示意（节选段落结构，非逐字原文）

**Paragraph 1**（组件 1 — 重要性 + 背景 + 领域 focus）：
- Define general anesthesia as a clinically important reversible state.
- Introduce propofol as a widely used anesthetic and give enough mechanistic background for non-specialist readers.
- End the paragraph by narrowing to a multi-scale mechanism question: molecular effects are known, but the link to neuronal circuits and unconsciousness remains unclear.

**Paragraph 2**（组件 2 — 宏观观察）：
- Summarize established macroscopic EEG signatures under propofol anesthesia.
- Group prior observations by signal type rather than listing studies randomly.
- Add animal physiology evidence to connect macroscopic signals to neuronal states.

**Paragraph 3**（组件 3 — gap）：
- Use "Although..." to concede that EEG patterns are reliable.
- Identify the main gap: their functional relationship to loss of consciousness is unclear.
- Add a methodological gap: prior work often studies steady states instead of the transition.
- Add an empirical gap: few studies record single-neuron and oscillatory dynamics across distributed sites at the same time.
- Conclude with the unresolved causal question.

**Paragraph 4**（组件 4 — 本文）：
- Begin with "To address this question..." to connect directly back to the gap.
- State the study object: neuronal and circuit-level dynamics during induction.
- Give a compact method outline: simultaneous recordings, multiple spatial scales, high temporal resolution, behavioral marker of loss of consciousness.

**Paragraph 5**（组件 4 续 — 结果预告 + 结论）：
- Preview the main finding and its interpretation.
- Use contribution markers tied to the evidence, such as multi-scale account or fundamental component, rather than unsupported novelty claims.

### 结构要点

- **5 段式结构**，适合较长的引言。
- 组件 1 的"importance"部分与"background"部分合并在第 1 段。
- 组件 3 的 gap 有**多重维度**：functional link 不清 + 缺乏 transition tracking + 多区域同步记录少。
- 组件 4 先给 method outline（paragraph 4），再给结果预告（paragraph 5）。

## 样例 3：光学 / 有机材料 —— OVPD

**题目**：Organic vapor phase deposition: a new method for the growth of organic thin films with large optical non-linearities

### 结构分析

**Paragraph 1**（组件 1）：
- Establish interest in organic materials for non-linear optical (NLO) devices.
- Provide the key material requirements needed for high device performance.

**Paragraph 2**（组件 2 — 方法 1）：
- Summarize the conventional polymer-matrix approach.
- Immediately note its stability and temperature-related limitations.

**Paragraph 3**（组件 2 — 方法 2，新发展）：
- Introduce single-crystal organic or organometallic salts as a newer high-performance direction.
- Use one representative material to make the promise concrete.

**Paragraph 4**（组件 3 — gap：薄膜生长方法缺）：
- Narrow from bulk/single-crystal promise to the practical need for optical-quality thin films.
- Identify the method gap: common vacuum evaporation does not suit some polar organic salts.
- Add a concrete failure mode and, where appropriate, mention unsuccessful attempts to show why the gap matters.

**Paragraph 5**（组件 2/3 过渡 — 另一技术的借鉴）：
- Introduce a technique from a neighboring field that could plausibly solve the gap.
- Explain why the borrowed technique is relevant rather than presenting it as an unrelated detour.

**Paragraph 6**（组件 4）：
- State the present paper: apply VPE-derived techniques to grow the target organic salt film.
- Give the main characterization/result in one compact sentence.
- Use a strong contribution marker such as "first demonstration" only if the manuscript can substantiate it.

### 结构要点

- 组件 2 被拆为**两种方法**（polymer poling vs. single crystal）并分别讲述各自的限制。
- 组件 3 也分两层：gap 1（DAST 蒸发分解）+ 作者自己实验失败的陈述（诚实）+ gap 2（共蒸发不行）。
- 组件 4 里 "To our knowledge, this is the first..." 是**最强的贡献标记表达**之一，只能在证据充分时使用。

## 样例 4：细胞生物学 —— hESC 神经分化

**题目**：Identification and characterisation of the early differentiating cells in neural differentiation of human embryonic stem cells

### 结构分析（精简）

**Paragraph 1**（组件 1 — 发育背景）：
- Begin with the biological importance of pluripotent embryonic cells and early lineage specification.
- Move to neural induction as the current focus.
- Identify the broad obstacle: early developmental tissue is difficult to access, so early neural populations and mechanisms remain incompletely defined.

**Paragraph 2**（组件 1/2 — hESC 作为模型的价值）：
- Introduce hESCs as a model that preserves key developmental properties.
- Explain why this model helps bridge inaccessible early development and experimental study.

**Paragraph 3**（组件 2 — Oct4 综述）：
- Review the relevant transcription factor or marker.
- Use prior animal and stem-cell evidence to justify why this marker matters for the present question.

**Paragraph 4**（组件 3 + 4 — gap + 本文）：
- Move from prior work by the same group to the unresolved early differentiating population.
- Use "Little is known..." and "it is not clear whether..." to state the gap.
- Present the study's identification/isolation strategy and preview the contribution: a useful model or resource for mechanism studies.

### 结构要点

- **Paragraph 4 同时包含组件 3 和 4**：先铺"little is known / it is not clear"（gap），然后立即"In this study, we..."（present paper）。
- 结尾句的 "valuable cell source" 是生科领域常见的贡献标记表达。

## 样例 5：机器学习 —— GPU + Kernel SVM

**题目**：A GPU-tailored approach for training kernelized SVMs

### 结构分析

**Paragraph 1**（组件 1）：
- Establish SVMs as a widely used learning method.
- Provide just enough technical background about what training means.
- Broaden from the original use case to newer prediction tasks.

**Paragraph 2**（组件 2）：
- Define the computational bottleneck.
- Review general-purpose and specialized solvers by problem type.
- End with a need statement: kernel SVM training still needs to be faster.

**Paragraph 3**（组件 4 第一次 — broad statement）：
- Introduce GPUs as a plausible route to speed.
- State the present paper at a broad level: efficient GPU implementation for target SVM settings.

**Paragraph 4**（组件 2/3 — 近期相关工作的不足）：
- Acknowledge recent GPU-related work.
- Identify the limitation: prior approaches did not sufficiently adapt algorithms to GPU architecture.
- Transition to the paper's algorithmic design focus.

**Paragraph 5**（组件 3 — 具体 gap）：
- State a sharper gap: sparse datasets are poorly supported.
- Explain why sparsity is difficult on GPUs in terms of memory access patterns.
- Contrast the present implementation with prior solvers.

**Paragraph 6**（组件 4 — 结果）：
- Close with evidence-calibrated performance claims, such as orders of magnitude faster, only if benchmarks support them.

### 结构要点

- CS 典型结构：**组件 4 可以出现两次**——第一次宏观介绍，第二次给结果。
- 组件 3 的 gap 用"One particularly significant drawback..."显式点出。
- 末段用"orders of magnitude faster" —— 典型 CS 性能贡献标记表达，前提是基准测试支持。

## 样例 6：工程学 —— SHM 结构健康监测

**题目**：Evaluation of the damage detection capability of a sparse-array guided-wave SHM system applied to a complex structure under varying thermal conditions

### 结构分析（精简）

**Paragraph 1**（组件 1 — 工业应用角度）：
- Establish SHM as important for safety-critical infrastructure and inspection cost reduction.
- Define what a successful SHM system must combine: sensitivity, localization/identification, and practical sensor density.

**Paragraph 2**（组件 2 — 技术传承）：
- Map earlier NDE applications using guided waves.
- Show the shift toward permanently installed SHM systems for complex structures.

**Paragraph 3**（组件 2 — 挑战）：
- Explain the practical challenge in complex structures: reflected signals are hard to interpret.
- Introduce baseline subtraction as a common mitigation.
- Connect baseline quality directly to defect sensitivity.

**Paragraph 4**（组件 2/3 — 环境影响 + gap）：
- Add environmental and operational variability as the next problem layer.
- Review temperature-compensation approaches.
- Prepare the gap: promising techniques still need evaluation under realistic, uncontrolled conditions.

**Paragraph 5**（组件 4）：
- State the present paper as a realistic nonlaboratory evaluation.
- Briefly outline feasibility testing, baseline acquisition, compensation assessment, and defect verification.

### 结构要点

- 工程论文典型的 **"现实问题 → 技术 → 挑战 → 验证"** 脉络。
- 组件 3 隐含在"非实验室环境"的强调中。
- 组件 4 结尾用**被动语态 + 过去时**陈述方法步骤——工程领域偏好的风格。

## 样例 7：工程学 / 交通 —— SPPPV 车罩（短引言范例）

**题目**：A cover for the single-person pedal-powered vehicle

来源：Glasman-Deal《Science Research Writing》第 1 单元 Exercise 5 的 Key（书中 p. 70）。这里保留结构化示意，不逐字复现 model answer；该范例是**紧凑型 3 段式**引言，长度约 250 词、9 句，是方案 B（短论文）的典型实现。

### 练习任务设定

- 假设作者刚完成一个 SPPPV / bicycle cover 研究项目，目标是保护骑行者免受 injury、pollution 或 rain 的影响。
- 研究切入点可自由选择：computer simulation、ventilation modelling、aerodynamics、polymer construction 或其他设计问题。
- 题目固定为 **A cover for the single-person pedal-powered vehicle**。
- 目标长度约 250-350 words，必须覆盖四组件，并使用本单元的语言资源。
- 该练习允许创造 fake research references；真实论文写作时不要虚构引用。

### 英文结构示意（非逐字原文）

**Paragraph 1**（组件 1 — 主题重要性 + 背景 + 一般问题）：

- S1: Use an urban transport or pollution problem to establish the topic's policy importance.
- S2: Provide a factual policy or emissions background with citation placeholders.
- S3: Narrow from transport policy to environmentally friendly commuter vehicles such as the SPPPV.
- S4: Use "However, although..." to introduce a broad adoption problem involving safety and comfort.

**Paragraph 2**（组件 2 — 研究地图 + 组件 3 — gap）：

- S5: Open the literature map with a general statement about safety improvements.
- S6: Give two chronological examples of safety-oriented designs.
- S7: Switch to comfort/protection designs, also in chronological order.
- S8: Use "However, it has been suggested that..." to identify the trade-off: comfort/protection features may reduce aerodynamic efficiency.

**Paragraph 3**（组件 4 — 介绍本研究）：

- S9: State the present study in one dense sentence: model/evaluate the aerodynamic effects, use the resulting data, and propose a balanced design.

### 逐句结构分析

| 句号 | 组件 | 功能 | 时态 / 信号 |
|---|---|---|---|
| S1 | C1 | 用"社会级"议题建立主题重要性（urban air pollution + transport policy） | Present Perfect："has become" |
| S2 | C1 | 提供读者需要的背景事实 + 政策数据（含引用） | Present Simple + 并列句 |
| S3 | C1 | 把宽泛议题收束到本文对象（SPPPV），用"As a result, much research has focused on..."桥接 | Present Perfect |
| S4 | C1 → bridge | 在段末用 "However, although... need to be addressed" 先轻触领域一般问题，为下一段铺路 | Present Perfect |
| S5 | C2 | 段落主题句——概括性地开启文献地图 | Present Perfect |
| S6 | C2 | 按**时间顺序**列举 safety 方向的代表性工作（Wang 1999, Martinez 2002） | Past Simple |
| S7 | C2 | 按**同一时间轴**列举 comfort 方向的代表性工作（Kohl 2008, Martinez recent） | Past Simple + "more recently" |
| S8 | C3 | 用 "However, it has been suggested that..." 引出 gap：既有解决方案之间的**新矛盾**（气动效率） | Present Perfect 被动/间接 |
| S9 | C4 | 单句本文段——同时包含 aim（"we model..."）+ method outline（"use the data..."）+ 贡献暗示（"balances... optimum aerodynamic performance"） | Present Simple |

### 中文功能注释示例

【段落功能】第一段对应**组件 1** — 从城市交通或污染这类宽泛议题切入，提供政策/事实背景，再把焦点收束到 SPPPV，并在段末预告 safety and comfort 这一一般问题。本段是引言漏斗的宽端。

[城市交通/污染问题] 已成为 [政策/社会/工程领域] 的重要议题。*（本句功能：S1 — 用社会级问题建立主题重要性；可用 Present Perfect 把议题定位为当前持续重要的问题。）*

[政策目标或事实背景] 表明 [为什么替代通勤系统值得研究]。*（本句功能：S2 — 提供必要背景事实，并用引用占位符支撑量化信息。）*

因此，研究逐渐转向 [低排放/安全/低成本] 通勤系统，例如 SPPPV。*（本句功能：S3 — 用因果桥接句把宏观问题收束到具体系统。）*

然而，尽管 [积极趋势]，若要实现 [更大规模采用/环境收益]，[安全性/舒适性/可靠性] 问题仍需解决。*（本句功能：S4 — 在 C1 段末用让步 + 转折引出一般问题，为下一段综述和 gap 铺路。）*

【段落功能】第二段对应**组件 2 + 组件 3** — 先用 chronological mini-review 展示 safety 与 comfort 两条前人路线，再用一个 "However" 锁定 trade-off 型 gap。

已有研究改进了 SPPPV 的多个安全性方面。*（本句功能：S5 — 段落主题句，开启研究地图。）*

[早期研究 A] 与 [后续研究 B] 分别提出了 [安全设计 1] 和 [安全设计 2]。*（本句功能：S6 — 按时间顺序列举 safety 支线的代表性工作；真实投稿时使用用户提供的引用或语义占位符。）*

[另一组研究] 则从舒适性或防护性角度提出了 [设计 3] 和 [设计 4]。*（本句功能：S7 — 切换到 comfort/protection 支线，为 gap 准备具体对象。）*

然而，已有证据提示这些防护特征可能降低 [关键性能指标]。*（本句功能：S8 — 组件 3：锁定工程论文常见的 trade-off gap，即一个维度的改进损害另一个维度。）*

【段落功能】第三段对应**组件 4** — 用一句高密度本文段收束漏斗，合并 aim、method outline 与贡献暗示。

本研究对这些安全性和舒适性特征的 [性能影响] 进行建模/评估，并利用所得数据提出一种在 [设计参数] 与 [目标性能] 之间取得平衡的新设计。*（本句功能：S9 — 组件 4 的合并句；"new"、"balanced"、"optimum" 属于克制的贡献标记词，适合平衡型工程设计。）*

### 结构要点（Key takeaways）

- **紧凑 3 段式**（方案 B 变体）：组件 2 与 3 合并入同一段，组件 4 只有一句。适合 250–350 词的短引言或 letter。
- **Gap 的类型值得注意**：不是"这件事没人做"，而是"前人各自解决一个维度后，维度之间出现新冲突"（safety/comfort vs. aerodynamics）——**这是工程论文最常见的 gap 模式**。可以直接套用 "However, it has been suggested that X negatively affects Y..."。
- **贡献标记词的节制使用**：本文结构只需要 "new"、"balanced"、"optimum" 这类克制表达，不一定需要 "novel / first / significant"。匹配研究定位（这是一个平衡性设计，不是突破性发现）比堆砌形容词更重要。
- **文献按时间顺序呈现**：safety 支线 1999→2002，comfort 支线 2008→more recently——即使是综述几个研究，时间锚定也能让"领域演进"的感觉更自然。
- **段末"伏笔式" However**：S4 先轻触 "safety and comfort issues need to be addressed"，S8 才重锤落下（气动矛盾）。这种**两段式 gap**比一次性亮牌更有节奏感。
- 对应到**第 1 段平均 4 句 + 第 2 段 4 句 + 第 3 段 1 句** 的结构：任何一段都不应短于 1 句，但"本文段"可以只有一句，前提是这一句足够密集（aim+method+contribution 三合一）。

## 通用写作 checklist（生成后自查）

- [ ] 组件 1 清晰存在，至少 1-2 句
- [ ] 背景事实适度（跨学科读者也能读懂）
- [ ] 组件 2 综述有叙述主线，不是 shopping list
- [ ] 至少一个"However / Although / Yet"显式标出 gap
- [ ] gap 与本文 aim 之间逻辑衔接顺畅
- [ ] 本文至少包含 aim、method outline、(optional) main result
- [ ] 至少一个证据强度匹配的贡献标记词锚定本研究贡献
- [ ] 时态使用正确（参见 language-toolkit.md）
- [ ] 长度符合任务：常规引言 15-25 句、3-5 段；短引言练习 8-12 句、约 250-350 words
- [ ] 中文版每句都有功能注释，且注释描述 function 而非 content
- [ ] 中文版每段开头有【段落功能】标注
