# 怎么写一条 Task Statement

贯穿第 2 章的一项"可迁移技能"：写任务句。FJA 与 task inventory 都靠它吃饭，CIT 的"行为"部分也能用相近的笔法。

## 基本句法（DOL FJA 版）

> **[Verb] + [Immediate Object] + [to + Infinitive (work-field verb) + Object of Infinitive]**

- **主语（worker）省略**。
- **动词**说"做了什么"（worker function）。
- **直接宾语**承接动作（MTEWA、data、people 中的某一方）。
- 可选不定式短语说明"如何 / 为何"——第一部分是不定式（多对应 work-field 动词），第二部分是其宾语（MPSMS）。

## 课本范例（Table 2.7）

### 驯兽师
- "Evaluates ability, behavior, and performance of animals to train animals."
- "Trains wild animals to entertain audience."
- "Manipulates objects to train animals."

### 正片检验员
- "Compares positive film with reference prints to detect film irregularities."
- "Signals operator to project film reference prints."
- "Handles films and prints to reject defective films."

### Painting 任务样本（Table 2.6）
- "Paints landscapes, portraits, still life, abstract designs, or other compositions in oils, watercolors, or tempera."
- "Draws cartoons for publication to depict news topics in satirical or humorous manner."
- "Devises layout, and paints letters and designs on wood or cardboard blank to make sign."
- "Paints designs on rug surfaces, using spray gun and stencils."
- "Paints or inks flower designs on enameled surface of jewelry or buttons, using brush or pen."

注意：task 里自然嵌入了工具（spray gun, stencils）、subject matter（landscapes, portraits）、目的（make sign）。

## Fine 的六问句模板（Fine & Cronshaw, 1999, p. 50）

写一条任务句时自问：

1. **Who?** (subject)
2. **Performs what actions?** (action verbs)
3. **To whom or what?** (object of verb)
4. **Upon what instructions or sources of information?** (phrase)
5. **Using what tools, equipment, or work aids?** (phrase)
6. **To produce/achieve what output?** (in order to …)

组合后就是一条完整描述了"谁、做什么、对象、依据、工具、目的"的任务句。

## Task Inventory 里的任务句结构（同根异枝）

结构与 FJA 一致，但典型写得**更短更机械**（便于问卷列表阅读）：

- 最精简："Make refunds."
- 中度："Call patient about scrip not picked up after 7 days."
- 较详："Brief personnel on unit security or safety rules."

若目的需要更多信息（如设计培训），可在任务后加一句 qualifier；但尽量**别让每条都变叙事**。

## Specificity（粒度）的黄金准则

Gael (1983, p. 9) 定义：**A task = a discrete organized unit of work, with a definite beginning and end, performed by an individual to accomplish the goals of a job.**

### 过粗 vs 过细

| 过粗（只说目标） | 合适 | 过细（降到动作） |
|---|---|---|
| "Takes care of customers." | "Call customers to notify them that merchandise has arrived." | "Picks up phone, dials numbers, waits for answer, …" |
| "Conducts research." | "Evaluates chemical and physical properties utilizing chromatography." | "Pipettes 5 mL of sample into flask, …" |

### 判断经验法则

- 能否**再拆成有意义的单元**而不掉到"具体肢体动作"层面？能 → 还太粗；不能 → 合适。
- 看 purpose：培训设计偏细；绩效评估或岗位描述偏粗。

## 常见错误与修正

| 错误 | 说明 | 修正 |
|---|---|---|
| 只说目标不说行为 | "Takes care of customers." | "Answers customer questions about products and services." |
| 只说品质不说行为 | "Showed good initiative." | "Noticed an item in a letter that didn't appear to be right, checked it, and corrected it." |
| 主语出现 | "The worker trains wild animals…" | "Trains wild animals to entertain audience."（省略主语） |
| 动宾不清 | "Welds steel pipes." | "Welds steel pipes using TIG process to produce pressure vessels." |
| 粒度过细 | "Grasps mortar from board." | "Lays bricks using mortar and trowel to build wall." |
| 把推断混入行为 | "The surgeon was inattentive…" | "The surgeon responded to the nurse's question twice before returning to suturing."（以行为示意） |

## 从 "what gets done" 改写为 "what the worker does"

Fine (1988, p. 1027) 的经典改写：

- **原**：*"Determines the qualifications that employees must possess in order to fill vacant positions."*
- **改**：*"Reviews/analyzes job description data (e.g., training manuals and performance requirements) drawing on experience and psychological background in order to determine employee qualifications for vacant positions."*

改写要点：
1. 把"determines"这种**目标动词**换成**描写 worker 实际动作的动词**（reviews/analyzes）。
2. 显化 **依据信息源**（job description data, training manuals）。
3. 显化 **能力/知识依据**（experience and psychological background）。
4. 用"in order to"把原目标接回来——不丢"为了什么"。

## 练习题思路（教学引导）

如果用户要练笔，可以按这个阶梯给提示：

1. 给一个"过粗目标句" → 让 TA 改成 FJA 句。
2. 给一个 FJA 句 → 让 TA 推断 worker function (data/people/things 各为哪一级)。
3. 给一个"推断品质句"（critical incident）→ 让 TA 拆成 context + behavior + consequence。
4. 给一个岗位，让 TA 列出 3 条 duty，每 duty 下 3 条 task。
5. 给 2 条"同目标不同 worker function"的任务，比较其 training / selection 含义。
