# Creator Content Pillar Builder

Build a practical content pillar system for ecommerce creators so they stop posting random one-off ideas and start compounding around repeatable themes.

Use this skill when a creator, operator, or growth lead needs a content structure that ties audience pain points, product angles, proof assets, and conversion goals into a simple pillar map. It is especially useful for TikTok Shop, creator-led ecommerce brands, affiliate programs, and small teams trying to turn scattered content into a repeatable engine.

## Problem it solves
Many creator teams have lots of ideas but no stable content system. They jump between trends, offers, and hooks without knowing which themes deserve repetition. This skill turns scattered inputs into a small set of content pillars that can be reused across videos, posts, lives, and campaign briefs.

## Use when
- A creator account feels random or inconsistent
- You need 3-5 repeatable content pillars tied to commerce goals
- A brand wants creators to balance growth content, proof content, and sales content
- You are planning a content calendar, creator brief, or testing backlog
- You want clearer mapping from audience pain point to content angle

## Do not use when
- The user only needs a single post idea
- There is no audience, product, or offer context at all
- The task is pure brand strategy unrelated to creator content execution

## Inputs to ask for
- Product, category, and price point
- Target audience and buyer awareness level
- Main product promise and top objections
- Proof assets available: testimonials, demos, creator experience, data, UGC
- Current content channels: TikTok, Reels, Shorts, live, email, PDP
- Brand goals: reach, trust, conversion, retention
- Existing themes that have worked or failed

## Workflow
1. Clarify the product, buyer, and business goal
2. Identify the core audience jobs-to-be-done, pains, desires, and objections
3. Group those into 3-5 durable pillar themes
4. Define what each pillar is for: awareness, trust, conversion, retention, or community
5. For each pillar, generate angle territories, example formats, proof types, and CTA fit
6. Check for balance so the system is not all education or all hard sell
7. Output a reusable pillar map plus next-step content ideas

## Output requirements
Return:
1. Recommended pillar set with short names
2. Why each pillar matters
3. Audience/problem/offer mapping per pillar
4. Suggested formats and recurring series ideas
5. Proof assets or content ingredients needed
6. CTA guidance by pillar
7. Optional testing priority and weekly mix recommendation

## Quality standards
- Pillars should be durable, not trend-dependent
- Must connect content themes to real business goals
- Should feel creator-native, not brand-deck jargon
- Avoid generic buckets like “education” unless made specific
- The output should be reusable in future briefs and calendars

## Example prompts
- Build 4 content pillars for a TikTok Shop skincare creator account
- Turn this product, audience, and offer into a repeatable creator content system
- We have random videos and no structure. Create content pillars we can actually reuse
