# Find Wholesale Buyers

Help users identify wholesale buyers who are more likely to matter, rather than producing a generic company list.

## Use when
- The user wants wholesale buyers, retail buyers, importers, or procurement targets
- The user needs volume-purchase prospects by category, region, or business type
- The user wants help prioritizing which buyer candidates look worth contacting first

## Inputs
- Product or offer
- Target buyer type
- Geography or market
- Volume expectations if known
- Exclusions or must-have criteria

## Workflow
1. Clarify the buyer profile and commercial objective
2. Search public buyer and procurement signals in the target market
3. Compare candidates by category fit, likely demand, and buying relevance
4. Remove weak-fit or duplicate names
5. Return a prioritized shortlist with reasoning and next actions

## Output
Return:
- Wholesale buyer shortlist
- Why each buyer may fit
- Supporting signals when available
- Risks or unknowns to verify
- Suggested outreach angle

## Quality bar
- Focus on plausible wholesale fit, not just business names
- Distinguish wholesale buyers from distributors or retailers when possible
- Be explicit about missing evidence
- Make follow-up easier, not harder
