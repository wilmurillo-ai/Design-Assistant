# Trend Evaluation Matrix — Scoring Framework

Use this matrix to score any TikTok or short-form trend before committing production resources. Score each factor and total up before deciding to proceed.

---

## Scoring Instructions

Rate each factor from 0 to the maximum points shown. Be conservative — the purpose of this matrix is to prevent wasted production effort, not to justify executing every trend you encounter.

## Total Score Interpretation

**Maximum possible — 100 points**

| Total Score | Decision |
|---|---|
| **85–100** | 🟢 Execute immediately — this is a high-priority trend opportunity |
| **70–84** | 🟡 Proceed with strong execution — address any flagged factors |
| **55–69** | 🟠 Proceed with caution — at least one significant gap exists |
| **40–54** | 🔴 Marginal — only proceed if production cost is minimal |
| **Below 40** | ⛔ Skip — the combination of poor fit and/or timing makes participation net-negative |

## Section Score Benchmarks

Use these benchmarks to identify where the trend opportunity is strongest or weakest:

| Section | Below This = Problem | Above This = Good |
|---|---|---|
| Timing (/ 25) | < 10 | > 17 |
| Product–Trend Fit (/ 30) | < 15 | > 22 |
| Brand Safety (/ 20) | < 12 | > 16 |
| Production Feasibility (/ 25) | < 10 | > 18 |

A score below the "Problem" threshold in **any single section** warrants re-evaluation even if the total score is high. A poor timing score (trend already saturated) cannot be compensated by a perfect product fit score.

## Quick Evaluation (Under 2 Minutes)

If you're in the field and need a fast go/no-go, use these three questions:

1. **Is the use count under 500k and have you seen it fewer than 3 times in your niche?** (Yes = timing is viable)
2. **Can you explain in one sentence how your product fits this trend without it sounding forced?** (Yes = product fit is viable)
3. **Can you film and post this within 48 hours?** (Yes = production is feasible)

**All 3 Yes → Proceed to full evaluation and brief**
**Any No → Full evaluation to identify whether the gap can be resolved or the trend should be skipped**
