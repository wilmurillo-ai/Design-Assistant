# Payment Methods by Market — Ecommerce Reference

This reference documents the dominant payment methods by geography for ecommerce sellers. Use it to identify gaps in your payment method stack when entering new markets.

**Update note**: Payment landscape evolves quickly. Verify current market share data with your PSP before making integration decisions.

---

## Southeast Asia

### Indonesia
| Method | Type | Market Share | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| GoPay | E-wallet | ~30% | Gojek ecosystem; dominant for app-native shoppers |
| OVO | E-wallet | ~25% | Tokopedia partnership; strong across demographics |
| Dana | E-wallet | ~20% | Ant Financial backed; growing |
| QRIS | QR code | Growing | National QR standard; accepted across wallets |
| BCA Virtual Account | Bank transfer | ~15% | Most common bank transfer method |
| BNI/BRI/Mandiri VA | Bank transfer | ~10% | Other bank VA options |
| Visa/Mastercard | Card | ~15% | Lower penetration than other SEA markets |
| Indomaret/Alfamart | Cash at store | ~10% | Significant for unbanked population |

**Integration options for non-Indonesian businesses**: Midtrans, Xendit, Doku, or international PSPs with Indonesia coverage

### Malaysia
| Method | Type | Market Share | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| FPX (Financial Process Exchange) | Bank transfer | ~40% | National online banking standard; most popular |
| Touch 'n Go eWallet | E-wallet | ~30% | Dominant wallet; linked to highway toll system |
| GrabPay | E-wallet | ~20% | Strong among urban users |
| Boost | E-wallet | ~15% | Growing; Axiata backed |
| Visa/Mastercard | Card | ~25% | Relevant but often secondary to FPX |
| DuitNow | QR/transfer | Growing | National real-time payment system |

**Integration options**: iPay88, Billplz, Stripe (with FPX), Razer Merchant Services

### Singapore
| Method | Type | Market Share | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| PayNow | Bank transfer (real-time) | ~45% | Dominant for P2P and increasingly ecommerce |
| GrabPay | E-wallet | ~25% | Strong urban adoption |
| PayLah! (DBS) | E-wallet | ~20% | Major bank wallet |
| Visa/Mastercard | Card | ~60% | Higher card penetration than rest of SEA |
| Atome | BNPL | Growing | Pay-in-3; strong for fashion/beauty |

**Integration options**: Stripe (PayNow, cards), HitPay (Singapore-focused), Adyen

### Thailand
| Method | Type | Market Share | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| PromptPay | QR/bank transfer | ~50% | Government-backed; very high adoption |
| TrueMoney Wallet | E-wallet | ~25% | CPGroup ecosystem; strong offline + online |
| Rabbit LINE Pay | E-wallet | ~20% | LINE messaging app integration |
| Visa/Mastercard | Card | ~30% | Relevant, especially for higher-income segment |
| Bank transfer (SCB, Kasikorn, BBL) | Manual transfer | ~20% | Still used for larger purchases |

**Integration options**: 2C2P, Omise (now Opn Payments), Stripe (cards only)

### Vietnam

| ZaloPay | E-wallet | ~20% | Zalo (messaging app) ecosystem |
| Visa/Mastercard | Card | ~20% | Growing but still secondary |
| COD (Cash on Delivery) | Cash | ~40% | Still dominant for many product categories |

### Philippines

## Europe

### Netherlands
| Method | Type | Market Share | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| iDEAL | Bank transfer | ~70% | Dominant; non-negotiable for NL ecommerce |
| Visa/Mastercard | Card | ~25% | Secondary to iDEAL for most Dutch buyers |
| Klarna | BNPL | ~15% | Growing for fashion/electronics |

### Germany
| Method | Type | Market Share | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| SEPA Direct Debit | Bank debit | ~35% | Very common for recurring and one-off purchases |
| PayPal | Wallet | ~50% | Dominant for online shopping |
| Klarna (invoice) | BNPL/invoice | ~30% | "Buy now, pay after delivery" very popular in DE |
| Visa/Mastercard | Card | ~25% | Secondary to PayPal and Klarna |
| Sofort (Klarna) | Bank transfer | ~20% | Real-time bank transfer |

### UK
| Method | Type | Market Share | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|

| PayPal | Wallet | ~30% | Strong for online purchases |
| Apple Pay / Google Pay | Digital wallet | ~25% | Growing strongly on mobile |
| Klarna | BNPL | ~20% | Strong in fashion/beauty verticals |
| Open Banking | Bank transfer | Growing | New but emerging; strong regulatory support |
