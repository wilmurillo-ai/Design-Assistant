# Find Creators

Help users find creator candidates worth contacting, not just a random list of accounts.

## Use when
- The user wants creators for TikTok Shop, affiliate campaigns, UGC, whitelisting, or seeding
- The user needs a shortlist of creators by niche, geography, audience size, or style
- The user wants help comparing which creators are a better fit for a product or campaign

## Inputs
- Product or category
- Target audience
- Platform and geography
- Preferred creator size or style
- Budget or collaboration model if known
- Any exclusions or must-have filters

## Workflow
1. Clarify the product, audience, platform, and collaboration goal
2. Search public creator signals across the specified sources
3. Compare candidates by relevance, style, audience match, and likely partnership fit
4. Remove weak or duplicate candidates
5. Return a prioritized shortlist with clear reasons

## Output
Return:
- Creator shortlist
- Links or source references when available
- Why each creator looks relevant
- Risks or unknowns to verify
- Suggested next outreach angle

## Quality bar
- Do not dump a raw list with no reasoning
- Explain why each candidate made the shortlist
- Separate strong fits from weaker maybes
- Be honest about missing information or uncertainty
