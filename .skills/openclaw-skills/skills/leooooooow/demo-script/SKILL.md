# Demo Script Builder for E-Commerce

Create professional, conversion-focused demo scripts for product videos, live sales presentations, and training materials. This skill guides you through structuring demos that showcase product benefits, address customer pain points, and drive purchasing decisions.

## Quick Reference Decision Matrix

| Scenario | Demo Length | Focus | Script Type |
|----------|------------|-------|------------|
| Social media clip | 15-30 sec | Features & wow factor | Hook-driven |
| Product page video | 60-90 sec | Problem/solution | Story-arc |
| Live sales presentation | 5-10 min | Objection handling | Conversation guide |
| Customer onboarding | 3-5 min | Setup & best practices | Tutorial |
| Trade show/event | 2-3 min | Differentiation | Elevator pitch with demo |
| Email sequence | 30-60 sec per video | Specific benefits | Modular segments |
| Webinar | 15-20 min | Deep education | Educational + conversions |
