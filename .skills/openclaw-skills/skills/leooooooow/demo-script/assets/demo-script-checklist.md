# Demo Script Quality Checklist

Use this checklist to review your demo script before recording or presenting. A great demo hits most of these points. An excellent demo hits all of them.

---

## Section 1: Opening & Hook (10-15% of demo)

- [ ] **Opens with customer problem, not product features**
  - Does not start with "This is [Product Name]"
  - Starts with customer pain point or desired outcome
  - Example: "You lose 70% of sales at checkout" NOT "We have a fast checkout system"

- [ ] **Establishes emotional connection**
  - Acknowledges customer frustration or goal
  - Uses "you" language to make it personal
