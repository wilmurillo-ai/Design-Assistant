# Global Tax Guide — Compliance Quality Checklist

Use this checklist to verify the quality of a completed tax obligation assessment before sharing it with a seller. A complete, accurate assessment should pass all items in each category.

---

## Category 1 — Input Completeness (6 items)

- [ ] Seller has provided all required selling platforms (not just one)
- [ ] Revenue figures provided per market, not just total global revenue
- [ ] Product categories are specific (not just "products" or "goods")
- [ ] Ship-to markets listed individually, not grouped as "international"
- [ ] Current compliance status confirmed (existing registrations noted)
- [ ] Order/transaction counts provided where relevant for dual-trigger US states

**Category score: __ / 6**

---

## Category 2 — Jurisdiction Coverage (8 items)

- [ ] All markets with revenue above 50% of registration threshold are included
- [ ] US assessment distinguishes between marketplace-facilitated sales and own-website sales
- [ ] EU assessment uses current €10,000 OSS threshold (not old per-country thresholds)
- [ ] UK is assessed separately from EU (post-Brexit rules)
- [ ] Australia threshold ($75,000 AUD) applied correctly for both physical and digital goods
- [ ] Singapore OVR threshold ($100,000 SGD) checked for digital services
- [ ] Marketplace facilitator coverage confirmed for each platform × each market combination
- [ ] Digital products assessed under digital services rules where applicable

**Category score: __ / 8**

---

## Category 3 — Product Classification (5 items)

- [ ] Each product category checked against reduced/zero rate eligibility in applicable markets
- [ ] Food/beverage products classified correctly (UK zero-rating, EU reduced rates)
- [ ] Children's clothing checked (UK zero-rating, other market variations)
- [ ] Medical/supplement products flagged where rates vary
- [ ] Digital products distinguished from physical goods where rules differ

**Category score: __ / 5**

---

## Category 4 — Risk Assessment Quality (6 items)

- [ ] Risk flags cover all markets where seller appears above threshold without registration
- [ ] Risk flags are prioritized by revenue exposure (highest exposure first)
- [ ] Retroactive liability period estimated for each exposed market
- [ ] Marketplace facilitator reliance confirmed in writing (not assumed)
- [ ] FBA/inventory storage nexus issue flagged for Amazon sellers with EU warehousing
- [ ] Filing frequency requirements specified for each active market

**Category score: __ / 6**

---

## Category 5 — Output Completeness (7 items)

- [ ] Market Summary Table includes all four required columns (tax type, threshold status, MF coverage, registration status)
- [ ] Obligations Narrative written in plain English (not jargon-heavy)
- [ ] Product-specific rate variations called out explicitly where they apply
- [ ] Risk Flags section present with severity ratings
- [ ] Recommended Next Steps ordered by urgency + revenue exposure
- [ ] Professional support type specified for each recommended action (advisor vs. platform service)
- [ ] Educational disclaimer present at end of output

**Category score: __ / 7**

---

## Category 6 — Accuracy Verification (5 items)

- [ ] EU OSS eligibility conditions checked (B2C only, not B2B)
- [ ] IOSS scope limited to imports under €150 (not all cross-border sales)
- [ ] US economic nexus rules applied correctly (revenue OR transactions, not AND in most states)
- [ ] UK non-established seller rules applied (£85,000 threshold, not UK resident threshold)
- [ ] All threshold amounts confirmed against jurisdiction reference (not from memory)

**Category score: __ / 5**

---

## Category 7 — Professional Standards (4 items)

- [ ] Assessment explicitly labeled as educational, not legal or tax advice
- [ ] Professional consultation recommended for any market above threshold
- [ ] Seller advised to verify current rules with a licensed tax professional
- [ ] Assessment date recorded (rules change; date stamps matter for compliance records)

**Category score: __ / 4**

---

## Total Score

**Total: __ / 41**

- **38–41**: High quality — ready to share
- **32–37**: Good — minor gaps, review flagged items before sharing
- **25–31**: Needs revision — multiple gaps, revise before sharing
- **Below 25**: Significant gaps — do not share without substantial revision
