# Find Affiliates

Help users identify affiliates who are more likely to drive relevant traffic or sales, rather than producing a generic partner list.

## Use when
- The user wants affiliates, publishers, review sites, or performance partners
- The user needs promotion partners by audience, niche, or region
- The user wants help prioritizing which affiliate prospects look worth contacting first

## Inputs
- Product or offer
- Target audience
- Geography or market
- Affiliate model or payout preference if known
- Exclusions or must-have criteria

## Workflow
1. Clarify the offer, audience, and affiliate objective
2. Search public affiliate and publisher signals in the target niche
3. Compare candidates by audience fit, content fit, and promotion relevance
4. Remove weak-fit or duplicate names
5. Return a prioritized shortlist with reasoning and next actions

## Output
Return:
- Affiliate shortlist
- Why each affiliate may fit
- Supporting signals when available
- Risks or unknowns to verify
- Suggested outreach angle or partnership framing

## Quality bar
- Focus on plausible promotion fit, not just websites or creators
- Distinguish affiliates from generic media lists
- Be explicit about missing evidence
- Make follow-up easier, not harder
