# Deal Scorecard (Lightweight)

## Decision fields

- Objective clarity (1-5)
- Scope clarity (1-5)
- Timeline realism (1-5)
- Commercial upside (1-5)
- Payment safety (1-5)
- Strategic fit (1-5)

## Decision rule

- **Accept**: avg >= 4.0 and no red-flag item
- **Negotiate**: avg 3.0-3.9 or one fixable risk
- **Decline**: avg < 3.0 or major rights/payment red flag

## Mandatory red flags

- No written usage rights
- Unlimited revisions without fee protection
- Vague deliverables + urgent deadline
- Payment terms without due date
