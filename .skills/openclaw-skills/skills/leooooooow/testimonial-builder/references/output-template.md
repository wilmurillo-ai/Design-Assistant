## 2. Testimonial Scoring Matrix

| Dimension | 1 Star | 3 Stars | 5 Stars |
|-----------|--------|---------|---------|
| **Credibility** (25%) | No verification | Standard details | Full verification + media |
| **Persuasion Power** (30%) | Generic praise | Good specificity | Emotional + specific + narrative |
| **Platform Fit** (20%) | Wrong format | Adequate length | Perfect for platform |
| **Uniqueness** (15%) | Duplicate claim | Somewhat unique | Highly differentiated |
| **Visual/Media** (10%) | No media | Text only | Pro video or photo |

**Formula:** (Cred × 0.25) + (Persuasion × 0.30) + (Platform × 0.20) + (Unique × 0.15) + (Media × 0.10) = Total/5.0

**Thresholds:**
- 4.5+ = Hero/Featured
- 4.0+ = Primary Placements
- 3.5+ = Secondary Placements
- 4.2+ = Ad Creative
- Below 3.0 = Reject

## 4. Platform Placement Guide

### Amazon Product Page
| Position | Format | Limit | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|-------|
| Hero overlay | Text overlay | 100 chars | Rotate monthly |
| A+ Content 1 | 3-5 quotes + images | 150 chars each | Lifestyle images |
| A+ Content 2 | "Customers say" | 80 chars each | Stack vertically |
| Review highlights | Callout badge | 50 chars | Auto-highlight |

**Best Practices:** Prioritize verified badges, include name+location, rotate hero every 4 weeks.

### TikTok Shop / Short-Form Video
| Position | Format | Duration | Notes |
|----------|--------|----------|-------|
| Shop feed carousel | Video clip | 15-30 sec | Customer on camera |
| Video pin | Full testimonial | 30-60 sec | 15% engagement lift |
| Live stream | Live customer | 2-3 min | Real-time interaction |

**Best Practices:** Video 3x outperforms text, include product in frame, 3-5 new clips/month.

### Shopify / DTC Website
| Section | Format | Char Limit | Rotation |
|---------|--------|---|---|
| Homepage hero | Quote + name | 80 chars | Monthly |
| Homepage slider | Quote + headshot | 120 chars | Weekly |
| Product page | Quote + CTA | 100 chars | Per product |
| Checkout | Trust badge | 50 chars | Static |

**Best Practices:** 400x400px headshots, include 4-5 stars, test photo vs no photo (+8% CTR).

### Facebook/Instagram Ads
| Format | Placement | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Static image + text | Feed | CPC; broad audience |
| Video testimonial | Reels/Feed | 15-30 sec; CPV; detailed targeting |
| Carousel | Stories+Feed | 3-5 cards; lookalike audience |

**Best Practices:** Customer face prominent, text overlay (sound off), test 3-4 per campaign, pause underperformers at 10K impressions.

### Email Marketing
| Sequence | Position | Format | Trigger |
|----------|----------|--------|---------|
| Welcome 1 | Body | Quote + name + image | Subscribe |
| Welcome 3 | CTA reinforcement | 2-3 short testimonials | 48h post-signup |
| Abandoned cart | Urgency section | "Join X customers who..." | 4+ hours |
| Post-purchase 1 | Confirmation | How-to tips from customer | Order confirmation |
| Post-purchase 2 | Engagement hook | Success story | 7 days post-delivery |

**Best Practices:** 80-120 chars max, include headshot (+12% open), segment by product.
