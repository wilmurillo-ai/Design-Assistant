# Find Partners

Help users find promising partnership targets for growth, distribution, or collaboration.

## Use when
- The user wants channel, distribution, affiliate, or brand collaboration partners
- The user needs help identifying likely-fit business partners
- The user wants a partnership shortlist with reasoning, not just names

## Inputs
- Partnership goal
- Target market or niche
- Preferred partner type
- Geography or channel constraints
- Commercial model if known
- Any must-have or exclusion criteria

## Workflow
1. Clarify the partnership objective and target partner profile
2. Search public sources for relevant partner candidates
3. Compare candidates by fit, channel relevance, and collaboration logic
4. Remove weak or duplicate options
5. Return a ranked shortlist with next-step guidance

## Output
Return:
- Partner shortlist
- Why each partner looks relevant
- Likely partnership angle
- Risks or unknowns
- Suggested next outreach direction

## Quality bar
- Prioritize strategic fit over surface similarity
- Explain why the partnership could make sense
- Call out weak assumptions and open questions
- Make the output usable for immediate outreach planning
