# Creator Market Digest

Turn scattered creator-market signals into one concise business digest.

## Problem it solves
Creator commerce teams often react too late because signals are fragmented across posts, creators, launches, trend bursts, and platform chatter. This skill helps condense those signals into a practical market digest instead of forcing operators to manually piece everything together.

## Use when
- A team wants a periodic summary of what is shifting in the creator market
- An operator needs a quick read on creator-side signals before planning outreach or content
- The goal is to identify patterns, not just collect links
- A user wants the “what matters” version, not raw feed noise

## Do not use when
- The task requires primary-source reporting from a specific platform API in real time
- The user wants a full research report instead of a concise digest
- There are no meaningful signals to synthesize

## Inputs
- Recent creator-side observations, links, notes, screenshots, headlines, or examples
- Time window (24h / 7d / this week / this month)
- Market focus: creators, TikTok Shop, UGC, category-specific, etc.
- Optional region, niche, or commerce angle

## Workflow
1. Gather candidate signals and sort out noise.
2. Cluster similar movements into themes.
3. Identify what changed, why it matters, and who should care.
4. Separate temporary chatter from actionable pattern shifts.
5. Summarize the market into a concise operator-ready digest.

## Output
Return:
1. Top market shifts
2. Why each matters
3. Who is affected
4. Immediate implications for creator/ecommerce teams
5. Watchlist for what to monitor next

## Quality bar
- Focus on signal over volume
- Make implications practical, not vague
- Distinguish hype from meaningful movement
- Write like an operator briefing, not a content recap
