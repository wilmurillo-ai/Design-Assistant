# Creator Payment Terms Template

## Skill Card

- **Category:** Creator Operations
- **Core problem:** Payment ambiguity causes friction, disputes, and delays in creator campaigns.
- **Best for:** Teams standardizing creator payout expectations before launch.
- **Expected input:** Collaboration model, deliverables, acceptance rules, payment timing, extra terms.
- **Expected output:** Payment structure summary + risk points + copy-ready terms.

## Workflow

1. Identify payment model.
2. Tie payment timing to acceptance milestones.
3. Flag ambiguous or risky clauses.
4. Produce a creator-facing payment terms template.

## Output format

1. Payment structure summary
2. Risk points
3. Copy-ready payment terms
4. Short creator-facing explanation

## Quality and safety rules

- Keep language clear and practical.
- Reduce disputes, do not replace legal contracts.
- Link money terms to delivery and acceptance.
