# Find Distributors

Help users identify distributors who are more likely to move product effectively, rather than producing a generic company list.

## Use when
- The user wants regional distributors, importers, or channel partners
- The user needs distribution options in a new country or market
- The user wants help prioritizing which distributors look worth contacting first

## Inputs
- Product or category
- Target market or geography
- Preferred channel type
- Ideal distributor profile if known
- Exclusions or must-have criteria

## Workflow
1. Clarify the distribution goal and market context
2. Search public distributor and channel signals in the target market
3. Compare candidates by category fit, reach, and likely relevance
4. Remove weak-fit or duplicate names
5. Return a prioritized shortlist with reasoning and next actions

## Output
Return:
- Distributor shortlist
- Why each distributor may fit
- Supporting signals when available
- Risks or unknowns to verify
- Suggested first contact angle

## Quality bar
- Focus on plausible channel fit, not just company names
- Distinguish distributors from retailers or service providers
- Be explicit about missing evidence
- Make follow-up easier, not harder
