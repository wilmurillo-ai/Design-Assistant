# Contract Variant Templates

Five base patterns for the most common ecommerce creator deals. Pick the variant that matches the collaboration type, then customize.

## Variant A — Product-for-Post (Gifted)

**Use when**: No cash changes hands; Brand sends product worth under ~$200 in exchange for organic content.

**Must-have clauses**:
- Deliverables (1 post is typical)
- Required disclosure (#gifted or #ad in first 3 lines)
- Organic usage rights, perpetual on Brand-owned channels with Creator credit
- 30-day non-deletion clause (post must remain live)
- No exclusivity (or category-only, 14 days)
- No kill fee

**Skip**: paid rights, performance bonuses, complex revision rounds.

## Variant B — Flat-Fee UGC

**Use when**: Brand pays a flat fee for one or more pieces of content. May or may not include posting on Creator's own channel.

**Must-have clauses**:
- Specific deliverables with format/length spec
- 2 revision rounds with 48-hour SLA each
- Full transfer of usage rights for Brand-owned channels (organic + paid for N months)
- Kill fee schedule (50% pre-shoot, 100% post-shoot)
- Payment schedule (50% on signing, 50% on approval)

## Variant C — Affiliate Partnership

**Use when**: Compensation is variable, tied to tracked sales via discount code or affiliate link.

**Must-have clauses**:
- Commission rate, attribution window (typically 30 days), payment cadence
- Net Sales definition (gross less returns/refunds/tax/shipping)
- Posting cadence (e.g., minimum 1 post per month for 3 months)
- Code/link issuance and tracking platform (e.g., Refersion, Impact)
- Termination: either side may end with 30 days' notice

**Often skipped**: kill fee (no upfront fee to kill), exclusivity.

## Variant D — Whitelisted Paid Ads

**Use when**: Brand will run the content as paid ads via Creator's handle (Spark Ads, Branded Content tool, etc.).

**Must-have clauses**:
- Whitelisting access mechanism and duration (Spark Ads code maintained for full window)
- Paid usage rights for [N] months, optional renewal at agreed rate
- Specific platforms (TikTok, Instagram, both)
- Higher fee than equivalent organic-only deal (paid rights are valuable)
- Asset handover: raw footage, vertical and square crops

## Variant E — Hybrid (Multi-Component)

**Use when**: Combination of product gift + flat fee + paid usage + affiliate commission.

**Must-have clauses**: All of Variants B and D, plus:
- Affiliate commission terms layered on top (modest %, since flat fee already paid)
- Performance bonus tied to attributable sales or specific view threshold
- Clear stack of compensation: product + fee + commission + bonus

## Variant Selection Quick Test

| Brand pays cash? | Brand wants paid ads? | Variable comp? | → Variant |
|------------------|----------------------|----------------|-----------|
| No | No | No | A — Gifted |
| Yes | No | No | B — Flat-Fee UGC |
| Optional | No | Yes | C — Affiliate |
| Yes | Yes | No | D — Whitelisted |
| Yes | Yes | Yes | E — Hybrid |
