# Output Template — Creator Collaboration Agreement

Use this section structure for every contract draft. Each numbered heading should be followed by a one-line plain-English summary, then the contract language.

---

## 1. Parties and Scope

*Plain-English: Identifies who is signing and what work is covered.*

This Creator Collaboration Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on [DATE] between [BRAND NAME], a [STATE] [ENTITY TYPE] ("Brand"), and [CREATOR NAME / LEGAL ENTITY] ("Creator"). The Agreement covers the deliverables described in Section 2 and the rights, obligations, and compensation set forth herein.

## 2. Deliverables and Timeline

*Plain-English: What the creator must produce and by when.*

| Deliverable | Platform | Format | Quantity | Posting Window |
|-------------|----------|--------|----------|----------------|
| [e.g., Product demo] | TikTok | Video, 30–60s | 1 | Within 14 days of product receipt |

Creator shall submit a draft to Brand at least [X] business days before each scheduled posting date.

## 3. Content Specifications and Approvals

*Plain-English: Quality requirements and the review process.*

- Creator shall include the following hashtags / disclosures: [list]
- Creator shall mention the following talking points: [list]
- Approval rounds: [N] rounds, [SLA] business days for Brand review and Creator revision per round
- Final asset format and resolution: [spec]

## 4. Usage Rights

*Plain-English: Where, how, and how long Brand can use the content.*

Creator grants Brand:
- (a) **Organic rights**: [perpetual / N months] on Brand-owned channels
- (b) **Paid rights**: [N months] for paid social media, including [whitelisting tool, e.g., Spark Ads / Branded Content]
- (c) **Repurposing rights**: [list channels — website, email, retail signage]

## 5. Exclusivity

*Plain-English: Categories or competitors the creator can't work with during a window.*

For [N] days from first publication, Creator shall not promote: [category definition] OR [named competitor list].

## 6. Compensation and Payment

*Plain-English: How and when the creator gets paid.*

- Flat fee: $[X], paid [50% on signing / 100% on approval / etc.]
- Product value: $[X], shipped within [N] days
- Performance bonus (if applicable): [tier table tied to UTM/discount-code sales or view threshold]
- Affiliate commission (if applicable): [%] of Net Sales, [N]-day cookie, paid monthly net [N]
- Kill fee: [% pre-shoot] / [% post-shoot]

## 7. Representations and Warranties

*Plain-English: Promises both sides make about the content.*

Creator represents that the Content will be original, will not infringe third-party rights (including music), and will comply with applicable disclosure rules (FTC 16 CFR Part 255 in the US, ASA/CAP in the UK, equivalent rules elsewhere). Creator shall include #ad in the first three lines of caption and verbal disclosure where format permits.

## 8. Termination and Kill Fees

*Plain-English: How either side ends the deal early.*

Either party may terminate for material breach with [N] days' written notice and opportunity to cure. If Brand terminates without cause after Creator has begun production but before publication, Brand shall pay [%] of the flat fee. If terminated after publication, full fee applies.

## 9. General Provisions

*Plain-English: Standard legal boilerplate.*

Governing law: [STATE/COUNTRY]. Disputes: [arbitration / courts]. Amendments must be in writing. This Agreement is the entire agreement between the parties.
