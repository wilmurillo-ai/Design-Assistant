# Platform Fee Comparison Guide

This reference compares fee structures across seven major global marketplaces. Use it during Step 3 (Dimension A: Platform Fees) of the marketplace evaluation workflow.

**Important**: Fees change frequently. Treat the figures below as baseline estimates for modeling purposes. Always verify current rates on each platform's official seller fee page before making final decisions.

**Last reviewed**: Q1 2026

---

## Fee Structure Overview

Every marketplace charges some combination of the following fee types:

| Fee Type | Description |
|---|---|
| **Referral / Commission** | Percentage of the sale price taken by the marketplace. Varies by product category. |
| **Fulfillment Fee** | Charged when using marketplace fulfillment (FBA, SFP, etc.). Covers pick, pack, and ship. |
| **Storage Fee** | Monthly fee for inventory stored in marketplace warehouses. Often increases for aged inventory. |
| **Subscription / Account Fee** | Monthly or annual fee for a professional seller account. |
| **Payment Processing** | Fee for processing the buyer's payment. Sometimes bundled into the referral fee. |
| **Advertising** | Not a platform fee per se, but practically mandatory. Modeled separately as a cost of doing business. |
| **Currency Conversion** | Applied when the seller's payout currency differs from the marketplace transaction currency. |
| **Other / Misc** | Category-specific surcharges, high-volume listing fees, or program-specific fees. |

## Marketplace-by-Marketplace Breakdown

### 1. Amazon (US, EU, JP)

| Fee Component | US | EU (DE/FR/IT/ES) | UK | JP |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Referral Fee** | 8-15% (most categories 15%) | 7-15% (most 15%) | 7-15% (most 15%) | 8-15% |
| **FBA Fulfillment (standard item)** | $3.22-$6.90 | EUR 2.60-5.90 | GBP 2.20-5.10 | JPY 290-600 |
| **FBA Storage (standard, per cu ft/mo)** | $0.87 (Jan-Sep), $2.40 (Oct-Dec) | EUR 0.72-1.80 | GBP 0.65-1.60 | JPY 80-160 |
| **Professional Account** | $39.99/mo | EUR 39.00/mo (covers all EU) | GBP 25.00/mo | JPY 4,900/mo |
| **Payment Processing** | Included in referral | Included in referral | Included in referral | Included in referral |
| **Currency Conversion** | N/A | 1.5% (if paid in non-local currency) | 1.5% | 1.5% |

**Key Notes**:
- Pan-European FBA allows storing inventory in one EU country and fulfilling across all EU marketplaces. Removes per-country fulfillment setup but adds EUR 0.50-1.00 per cross-border unit.
- Long-term storage surcharge applies to inventory stored over 180 days: $6.90/cu ft or $0.15/unit, whichever is greater (US).
- Amazon also charges a closing fee on media items ($1.80/unit in US).
- Refund administration fee: Lesser of $5.00 or 20% of the referral fee on returned items.

**Typical all-in platform fee (excluding ads)**: 20-25% of revenue for standard items using FBA.

### 2. Shopee (Southeast Asia)

| Fee Component | Cross-Border Seller | Local Seller |
|---|---|---|
| **Commission Fee** | 4-8% (varies by category and country) | 3-6.5% |
| **Transaction Fee** | 2.0% | 1.5-2.0% |
| **Service Fee** | 2.0% (cross-border service) | N/A |
| **Shipping Fee Subsidy** | Shopee subsidizes shipping; seller co-pays $0.30-$1.00/order depending on country | Varies by Shopee Supported Logistics rates |
| **Fulfillment (Shopee Fulfillment)** | $0.80-$2.50/order (country-dependent) | $0.50-$2.00/order |
| **Account Fee** | Free | Free |
| **Currency Conversion** | 2.0-3.0% (built into exchange rate) | N/A |

**Key Notes**:
- Shopee charges no monthly subscription fee, making it low-risk for testing.
- Cross-border sellers from China benefit from Shopee's SIP (Seller Incentive Program) with reduced commissions for the first 90 days.
- Shopee frequently runs platform-wide promotions (9.9, 11.11, 12.12) where sellers are expected to offer discounts. Factor 5-15% promotional discounting into margin models.
- Free shipping vouchers are partly funded by sellers through the "Free Shipping Program" co-pay.

**Typical all-in platform fee (excluding ads)**: 10-16% for cross-border sellers; 6-12% for local sellers.

### 4. TikTok Shop (US, UK, Southeast Asia)

| Fee Component | US | UK | Southeast Asia |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Commission Fee** | 8% (standard); 2-4% first 90 days | 5-8% | 3-5% (introductory); 5-8% standard |
| **Payment Fee** | Included in commission | 1.0% | 1.0-2.0% |
| **Shipping** | Seller-arranged or TikTok Shipping ($3-$7) | Seller-arranged or TikTok Shipping (GBP 2.50-5.00) | Platform logistics $0.50-$2.00 |
| **Account Fee** | Free | Free | Free |
| **Affiliate Commission** | 10-20% (if using affiliate program) | 10-20% | 5-15% |

**Key Notes**:
- TikTok Shop's introductory commission rates (first 90 days) are significantly lower than standard rates. Model post-introductory rates for long-term projections.
- The affiliate program is a major sales driver on TikTok Shop. Affiliates (content creators) promote your products for a commission you set. This is an additional cost on top of platform fees but drives discovery.
- Content creation costs should be factored in. Budget $500-$2,000/month for video production if creating in-house content.
- TikTok Shop Fulfillment (warehouse services) is expanding but not yet available in all markets.
- Live shopping is a significant revenue channel, especially in SEA. Requires dedicated staff for live sessions.

**Typical all-in platform fee (excluding ads and affiliates)**: 8-12%. Including affiliates: 18-30%.

### 5. Walmart Marketplace (US)

| Fee Component | Value |
|---|---|
| **Referral Fee** | 6-15% (category-dependent; most categories 15%) |
| **WFS Fulfillment (standard item)** | $3.45-$7.50 |
| **WFS Storage (per cu ft/mo)** | $0.75 (standard); $1.50 (Nov-Dec peak) |
| **Account Fee** | Free (no monthly subscription) |
| **Payment Processing** | Included in referral fee |
| **Currency Conversion** | N/A (US-only, paid in USD) |

**Key Notes**:
- No monthly subscription fee is a significant advantage over Amazon for lower-volume sellers.
- Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS) is optional but gives listings a "Fulfilled by Walmart" badge and eligibility for Walmart+ 2-day shipping.
- Referral fee percentages are similar to Amazon but Walmart has no closing fees or high-volume listing fees.
- Walmart's seller base is smaller than Amazon's (~150K active sellers vs Amazon's ~2M), meaning less competition.
- Approval required: Walmart reviews seller applications and may reject based on product quality, fulfillment capability, or brand reputation.

**Typical all-in platform fee (excluding ads)**: 18-23% with WFS; 8-15% with self-fulfillment.

### 6. eBay (Global)

| Fee Component | Value |
|---|---|
| **Final Value Fee** | 3-15% (most categories 13.25%) |
| **Per-Order Fee** | $0.30/order |
| **Store Subscription** | $0 (no store) to $299.95/mo (Enterprise) |
| **Payment Processing** | Included in final value fee (Managed Payments) |
| **International Fee** | Additional 1.65% for cross-border transactions |
| **Promoted Listings** | 2-20% ad fee (cost per sale model) |

**Key Notes**:
- eBay's fee structure has simplified since Managed Payments replaced PayPal. The final value fee now includes payment processing.
- Store subscriptions provide free listing allowances. Starter store ($4.95/mo) includes 250 free listings; Basic ($21.95/mo) includes 1,000.
- eBay's Promoted Listings Standard is a cost-per-sale model (you only pay when the ad leads to a sale), making it lower risk than CPC advertising.
- Strong in used goods, collectibles, and parts/accessories. Less effective for commodity new products competing with Amazon.
- eBay's Global Shipping Program (GSP) enables international sales with minimal seller effort -- eBay handles customs and international shipping from a US hub.

**Typical all-in platform fee (excluding ads)**: 13-16% for most categories.

### 7. Mercado Libre (Latin America)

| Fee Component | Brazil | Mexico | Argentina |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Commission Fee** | 11-19% (varies by category and listing type) | 12.5-20% | 13-21% |
| **Shipping (Mercado Envios)** | Seller-subsidized or buyer-paid; $1.50-$5.00 | Seller-subsidized; $1.00-$4.00 | Seller-subsidized |
| **Payment Fee (Mercado Pago)** | 4.99% + R$0.40 | 3.99% + MXN 4.00 | 4.99% + ARS fees |
| **Account Fee** | Free | Free | Free |
| **Currency Conversion** | 3.0-4.0% for international sellers | 3.0-4.0% | 4.0-5.0% |

**Key Notes**:
- Mercado Libre is the dominant marketplace in Latin America, with 148M+ active users.
- Fee structures are complex: commission rates depend on listing type (Classic vs Premium) and seller reputation level. Premium listings get better visibility but cost 5-6% more in commission.
- Mercado Envios (integrated logistics) is practically mandatory for competitive visibility. Sellers ship to a local hub; MeLi handles last-mile delivery.
- Currency risk is significant, especially in Argentina (high inflation) and Brazil (BRL volatility). Model worst-case exchange rate scenarios.
- Cross-border selling is possible via Mercado Libre's CBT program, but local inventory is strongly preferred by the algorithm.
- Tax complexity: Brazil has ICMS (state-level VAT), PIS/COFINS, and ISS. Budget for a local tax advisor.

**Typical all-in platform fee (excluding ads)**: 20-28% in Brazil; 18-25% in Mexico.

## How to Use This Guide

1. **Identify applicable marketplaces** from the long list.
2. **Calculate per-unit fees** for your hero product using the relevant marketplace section above.
3. **Include all fee components** -- do not compare referral fees alone.
4. **Model with advertising costs separately** -- use category average ACoS/ROAS from marketplace advertising tools.
5. **Account for promotional costs** -- platform-wide sale events may require 10-20% discounts.
6. **Verify current rates** on the platform's official seller fee documentation before finalizing any financial model.
