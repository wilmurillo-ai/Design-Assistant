# Marketplace Expander Quality Checklist

Use this checklist to verify completeness and quality of your marketplace expansion evaluation before presenting recommendations. Every item should be checked before finalizing the expansion roadmap.

---

## Category 1: Expansion Objectives (5 items)

- [ ] Revenue target is specific, measurable, and time-bound (e.g., "$500K incremental revenue within 12 months")
- [ ] SKU scope is defined (full catalog vs. hero SKUs, with specific SKU count)
- [ ] Geographic scope is explicitly stated with priority ranking of regions/countries
- [ ] Budget envelope is documented including breakdown of how it will be allocated
- [ ] Team capacity constraints are quantified in hours/week or FTE available

## Category 2: Marketplace Research (6 items)

- [ ] Long list includes at least 5 marketplace candidates with documented rationale for inclusion
- [ ] Each marketplace has verified data on GMV, active buyers, and primary categories (source cited)
- [ ] Seller onboarding requirements are documented for each marketplace (entity type, tax IDs, bank accounts)
- [ ] Marketplaces requiring a local entity are flagged and evaluated against seller's geographic constraints
- [ ] Competitor presence analysis completed -- identified where top 5 competitors are selling
- [ ] Eliminated marketplaces have documented rationale for exclusion

## Category 3: Fee Analysis (7 items)

- [ ] All-in fee calculation completed for hero product on each shortlisted marketplace
- [ ] Fee calculation includes all components: referral, fulfillment, storage, payment processing, subscription, currency conversion
- [ ] Advertising cost modeled separately using category-average ACoS or ROAS benchmarks
- [ ] Promotional discount impact estimated (platform sale events like 11.11, Prime Day, etc.)
- [ ] Fee calculations verified against current official marketplace documentation (not outdated sources)
- [ ] Net margin per unit calculated after all fees and COGS
- [ ] Fee comparison table created showing apples-to-apples across shortlisted marketplaces

## Category 4: Audience and Competition (6 items)

- [ ] Target marketplace's buyer demographics documented (age, income, geography, device usage)
- [ ] Category-level search volume and growth trend verified with marketplace-specific tools
- [ ] Number of direct competitors counted in specific subcategory (not broad category)
- [ ] Top 10 competitor review counts and ratings documented
- [ ] Price range of competing products mapped against seller's target price point
- [ ] Advertising saturation assessed (estimated CPC/CPM for top keywords where data is available)

## Category 5: Fulfillment and Logistics (6 items)

- [ ] Fulfillment method selected for each shortlisted marketplace (self-fulfill, 3PL, marketplace fulfillment)
- [ ] Shipping cost per unit calculated for the primary destination country
- [ ] Delivery time expectation of marketplace buyers documented and compared to seller's capability
- [ ] Return handling process defined including return address in target market
- [ ] For cross-border: customs duties, import taxes, and importer-of-record requirements documented
- [ ] Fulfillment integration timeline estimated (weeks from decision to first order shipped)

## Category 6: Compliance and Regulatory (5 items)

- [ ] Product certifications required by target market identified (safety, environmental, ingredient)
- [ ] Labeling requirements documented (language, format, required disclosures)
- [ ] Tax registration requirements identified (VAT, GST, sales tax) with estimated timeline and cost
- [ ] Restricted or prohibited product categories checked for the target marketplace and country
- [ ] Data privacy and consumer protection obligations reviewed (e.g., EU GDPR, Brazil LGPD)

## Category 7: Financial Modeling (6 items)

- [ ] 12-month P&L projection completed with monthly granularity for each shortlisted marketplace
- [ ] Three scenarios modeled: conservative, base, and optimistic (with stated assumptions for each)
- [ ] Break-even month identified for each scenario
- [ ] Setup costs itemized: inventory, listing creation, compliance, photography, software, hiring
- [ ] Working capital requirement calculated: total cash outlay before first payout received
- [ ] Cash flow impact on existing operations assessed -- confirms expansion does not jeopardize current channels

## Category 8: Operational Readiness (6 items)

- [ ] Expansion Readiness Framework scorecard completed with scores for all 6 dimensions
- [ ] No dimension scores below 2 (any score of 1 is a launch blocker requiring remediation)
- [ ] Technology integration path confirmed -- current tools support the target marketplace or migration plan exists
- [ ] Team capacity plan includes specific assignments (who owns the new marketplace, with hours allocated)
- [ ] Content readiness confirmed -- product images, descriptions, and video assets meet marketplace requirements
- [ ] Customer service plan in place with language capability and SLA awareness for target marketplace

## Category 9: Roadmap and Risk (5 items)

- [ ] Expansion roadmap is phased (not simultaneous multi-marketplace launch) with clear timelines per phase
- [ ] Each phase has defined KPIs and go/no-go criteria for advancing to the next phase
- [ ] Exit criteria defined -- specific metrics and timeline that would trigger marketplace withdrawal
- [ ] Risk register includes at least 5 identified risks with probability, impact, and mitigation actions
- [ ] Next steps are documented with specific owners and deadlines for each action item

---

## Scoring

- **Total items**: 52
- **Minimum passing score**: 44/52 (85%)
- **Items marked critical** (must be completed regardless of overall score):
  - All-in fee calculation completed for hero product (Category 3)
  - Net margin per unit calculated after all fees (Category 3)
  - 12-month P&L projection completed (Category 7)
  - Break-even month identified (Category 7)
  - No readiness dimension scores below 2 (Category 8)
  - Exit criteria defined (Category 9)

If any critical item is incomplete, the evaluation should not be considered final regardless of the overall score.
