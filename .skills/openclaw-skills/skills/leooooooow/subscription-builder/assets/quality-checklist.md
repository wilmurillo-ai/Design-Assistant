# Subscription Program Quality Checklist

Verify every item before launch or during program audits.

---

## 1. Pricing (6 items)

- [ ] Discount is 5-20% and does not exceed 40% of gross margin per unit
- [ ] Subscription price displayed on PDP alongside one-time price
- [ ] Savings shown in both percentage and dollar amount
- [ ] Per-unit or per-serving cost displayed for countable products
- [ ] Loyalty escalator tiers modeled for margin impact (if used)
- [ ] Prepay refund policy documented and communicated (if offered)

## 2. Frequency (5 items)

- [ ] Default frequency based on consumption data or defensible estimates
- [ ] 3-4 frequency options available per product
- [ ] Frequency changeable at any time via self-service portal
- [ ] Skip-next-order is 1-2 clicks with no penalty
- [ ] Pause available with defined resume date

## 3. PDP and Conversion (5 items)

- [ ] Subscription option pre-selected with clear one-time alternative
- [ ] "How it works" explainer visible (ships automatically, manage anytime, cancel anytime)
- [ ] Frequency selector visible without additional clicks
- [ ] Annual savings shown for frequently reordered products
- [ ] Mobile widget fully functional without horizontal scroll

## 4. Onboarding (5 items)

- [ ] Confirmation email includes subscription details, next ship date, portal link
- [ ] Welcome email sent day 3-5 introducing self-service controls
- [ ] Product education email sent day 10-14
- [ ] Pre-shipment notification sent 2-3 days before processing
- [ ] No aggressive upsell in first two onboarding emails

## 5. Subscriber Portal (5 items)

- [ ] Accessible via direct link, not buried in account settings
- [ ] All self-service actions available: skip, pause, cancel, change frequency, swap, update payment/address
- [ ] Loads under 3 seconds on mobile
- [ ] Next order date and contents clearly displayed
- [ ] Add-on purchases supported for next shipment

## 6. Churn Reduction (6 items)

- [ ] Cancellation flow collects reason (required single-select)
- [ ] At least 2 reason-matched save offers before cancellation processes
- [ ] Visible "cancel now" link on every save screen
- [ ] Win-back sequence configured at Day 7, Day 45, Day 75 post-cancel
- [ ] At-risk signals monitored (consecutive skips, email disengagement)
- [ ] Automated re-engagement triggers active for medium/high risk subscribers

## 7. Payment and Dunning (5 items)

- [ ] 3-4 automatic retries over 7-8 days with notification at each step
- [ ] Dunning emails deep-link to payment update page
- [ ] Payment updater service active (Visa/Mastercard ABU)
- [ ] Subscription paused (not cancelled) after final failed retry
- [ ] Recovery rate tracked monthly, target 50%+

## 8. Operations (4 items)

- [ ] Subscription orders flagged for priority fulfillment
- [ ] Inventory allocation accounts for upcoming subscription shipments
- [ ] Subscription forecast feeds demand planning
- [ ] Support team trained on subscription policies and portal

## 9. Analytics (4 items)

- [ ] Dashboard tracks attach rate, cohort retention, churn, LTV, revenue share
- [ ] Cohort retention curves generated monthly
- [ ] Cancellation reasons aggregated and reviewed monthly
- [ ] A/B testing infrastructure in place for discount, frequency, and PDP layout
