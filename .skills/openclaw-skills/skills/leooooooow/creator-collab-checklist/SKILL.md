# Creator Collab Checklist

## Skill Card

- **Category:** Creator Operations
- **Core problem:** Collaboration issues come from unclear roles, deadlines, and quality standards.
- **Best for:** Teams launching creator campaigns at scale.
- **Expected input:** Collaboration goals, deliverables, timeline, revision and payment rules.
- **Expected output:** Readiness check + risk list + launch checklist.

## Workflow

1. Check if core collaboration info is complete.
2. Flag ambiguity and conflict risks.
3. Produce a copy-ready launch checklist.
4. Add alignment message for both sides.

## Output format

1. Readiness check
2. Risk points
3. Launch checklist
4. Alignment message

## Quality and safety rules

- Keep checklist concrete and executable.
- Reduce revision and delay risk.
- Do not replace legal contracts.
