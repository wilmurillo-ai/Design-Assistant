# Hook Patterns (Quick Bank)

1. **Contrarian**: "Everyone says X. For creators under 50k, that usually backfires."
2. **Proof-first**: "This script format lifted our hold rate in one week — here’s the exact opener."
3. **Mistake/fix**: "If your reels die after 2 seconds, you’re probably doing this."
4. **Checklist**: "3 lines to write before you record any TikTok ad script."
5. **Timing**: "Right now this topic is peaking — post this angle before it gets saturated."
6. **Observation**: "Creators who grow fastest in this niche all do one thing in their first sentence."
