## 1. Belle Epoque Pioneers (1870s-1900s)

### Jules Cheret (1836-1932)
**"The Father of the Modern Poster"**

- **Visual traits**: Bright joyful colors, idealized feminine figures, dynamic movement, hand-drawn typography, lithographic mastery
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Jules Chéret style, Belle Époque poster, bright joyful colors, idealized feminine figure in motion, dynamic energy, hand-drawn typography, impressionist lithographic aesthetic, vintage 1890s Paris poster art`
- **Best for**: Celebrations, performances, entertainment, romantic comedies

### Henri de Toulouse-Lautrec (1864-1901)
**"Master of Montmartre"**

- **Visual traits**: Flat color blocks, Japanese woodblock influence, simplified forms, bold silhouettes, limited palette (3-5 colors), integrated text and image
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Toulouse-Lautrec style Mondo poster, flat color blocks, Japanese print influence, simplified bold silhouettes, 3-color lithograph: black, red, yellow, integrated typography, Montmartre cabaret aesthetic, vintage 1891 poster art`
- **Best for**: Nightlife, music venues, theatrical performances, bohemian themes

### Alphonse Mucha (1860-1939)
**"Art Nouveau Icon"**

- **Visual traits**: Ornate floral patterns, flowing organic lines (whiplash curves), Byzantine ornamentation, idealized female figures, decorative borders, pastel harmonies
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Alphonse Mucha Art Nouveau style, flowing organic curves, ornate floral patterns, idealized feminine figure, decorative byzantine border, pastel harmonies: soft pink, sage green, gold, elaborate frame design, vintage 1894 poster art`
- **Best for**: Period dramas, romantic films, fantasy, elegant designs

### Theophile-Alexandre Steinlen (1859-1923)
**"Le Chat Noir Master"**

- **Visual traits**: Social realism, expressive line work, strong contrast, feline motifs, working-class subjects, bold composition
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Steinlen style poster, social realist approach, expressive bold line work, high contrast, cat motif, working-class authenticity, 3-color print: black, red, cream, Le Chat Noir aesthetic, vintage 1896 Paris poster`
- **Best for**: Urban dramas, social commentary, noir films

## 2. Modernist Masters (1920s-1960s)

### Saul Bass (1920-1996)
**"Minimalist Film Poster King"**

- **Visual traits**: Radical simplification, symbolic abstraction, bold geometric shapes, limited color (2-4 colors), negative space mastery, kinetic energy, visual metaphors
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Saul Bass style Mondo poster, radical minimalist abstraction, bold geometric shapes, symbolic visual metaphor, 3-color screen print: burnt orange, black, cream, negative space mastery, kinetic energy, vintage 1959 film poster`
- **Best for**: Thrillers, psychological dramas, noir, any conceptual approach

### A.M. Cassandre (1901-1968)
**"Modernist Geometry Master"**

- **Visual traits**: Cubist/Futurist influence, dramatic perspectives, flat geometric planes, bold sans-serif typography, monumental scale, travel poster aesthetic
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Cassandre style Mondo poster, Cubist geometric planes, dramatic forced perspective, monumental scale, bold Art Deco typography, flat color blocks, 3-color print: navy, red, cream, modernist 1930s travel poster aesthetic`
- **Best for**: Travel, adventure, architectural films, Art Deco settings

### Milton Glaser (1929-2020)
**"Pop Art Typography Innovator"**

- **Visual traits**: Psychedelic color combinations, Art Nouveau revival, innovative typography, surrealist elements, vibrant joyful palettes
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Milton Glaser style poster, psychedelic color palette, Art Nouveau flowing lines, innovative typographic design, surrealist elements, vibrant joyful colors, vintage 1960s pop culture aesthetic, cultural iconography`
- **Best for**: Music, counterculture themes, biographical films, celebrations

### Josef Muller-Brockmann (1914-1996)
**"Swiss International Style Master"**

- **Visual traits**: Grid-based layouts, mathematical precision, Helvetica typography, objective/neutral approach, asymmetrical balance, primary colors, clean whitespace
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Josef Müller-Brockmann Swiss style, mathematical grid layout, Helvetica typography, objective minimal design, asymmetrical balance, primary colors: red, blue, yellow, black, clean whitespace, International Typographic Style, 1960s rationalist design`
- **Best for**: Documentaries, architectural films, technical subjects

### Paul Rand (1914-1996)
**"Corporate Modernism Pioneer"**

- **Visual traits**: Playful geometry, clever visual puns, bold simple forms, limited palettes, typography integration, wit and intelligence
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Paul Rand style poster, playful geometric forms, clever visual wordplay, bold simple shapes, limited palette: 2-3 colors, integrated typography, witty intelligent design, modernist corporate aesthetic, 1950s American design`
- **Best for**: Corporate themes, intelligent comedies, design-focused subjects

## 3. Film Legends (1970s-2000s)

### Drew Struzan (1947-)
**"Hollywood Blockbuster Legend"**

- **Visual traits**: Painted photorealism, epic cinematic compositions, warm nostalgic lighting, character-focused, multiple vignettes, signature glow effect
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Drew Struzan style Mondo poster, painted realism adapted for screen print, epic cinematic composition, character portraits integrated, warm nostalgic lighting glow, multiple scene vignettes, classic Hollywood poster tradition, 1980s adventure poster aesthetic`
- **Best for**: Adventure, blockbusters, franchise films, ensemble casts

### Olly Moss (1987-)
**"Contemporary Minimalism Master"**

- **Visual traits**: Ultra-minimal compositions, clever negative space, visual double meanings, 1-3 color palettes, figure-ground inversion
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Olly Moss style Mondo poster, ultra minimalist design, clever negative space, hidden imagery within silhouette, 2-color limited palette, geometric simplicity, visual double meaning, symbolic object-focused, Saul Bass influence, contemporary Mondo aesthetic`
- **Best for**: Films with iconic imagery, conceptual themes, noir

### Tyler Stout (1976-)
**"Maximalist Detail King"**

- **Visual traits**: Dense character collages, intricate line work, maximalist compositions, comic book influence, high contrast, organized chaos
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Tyler Stout style Mondo poster, densely layered composition, character collage, intricate line work, multiple characters integrated, high contrast black ink lines, organized maximalist design, comic book influenced, screen print with fine detail`
- **Best for**: Ensemble films, franchises, epic stories

### Martin Ansin (1977-)
**"Art Deco Elegance"**

- **Visual traits**: Art Deco influence, elegant line work, muted vintage palettes, sophisticated compositions, architectural elements
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Martin Ansin style Mondo poster, Art Deco influence, elegant flowing lines, muted vintage palette, sophisticated composition, architectural elements, refined line work, period styling, polished screen print aesthetic`
- **Best for**: Classic Hollywood, period pieces, gothic horror

### Laurent Durieux (1968-)
**"Visual Pun Master"**

- **Visual traits**: Clever hidden imagery, atmospheric lighting, symbolic compositions, subtle gradients, mysterious mood, narrative layers
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Laurent Durieux style Mondo poster, visual pun composition, hidden symbolic imagery, atmospheric lighting, subtle gradient within screen print style, clever composition trick, mysterious evocative mood, layered narrative`
- **Best for**: Thrillers, mysteries, films with twists

## 4. Contemporary (2000s-Present)

### Kilian Eng (1983-)
**"Geometric Futurism"**

- **Visual traits**: Precise technical line work, geometric sci-fi architecture, cool color palettes, isometric perspectives, atmospheric depth, neon accents
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Kilian Eng style Mondo poster, geometric futuristic design, precise technical line work, sci-fi architectural elements, cool teal and purple palette, angular isometric perspective, digital precision in screen print, atmospheric neon-lit depth, contemporary sci-fi aesthetic`
- **Best for**: Sci-fi, cyberpunk, futuristic settings

### Dan McCarthy (1973-)
**"Geometric Abstraction"**

- **Visual traits**: Ultra-flat color planes, geometric landscape abstraction, limited palettes (2-4 colors), no gradients, stark contrast
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Dan McCarthy style Mondo poster, ultra-flat geometric abstraction, simplified landscape forms, 2-3 solid color planes, no gradients, architectural nature shapes, stark minimalist contrast, contemporary geometric screen print`
- **Best for**: Westerns, road films, minimalist narratives

### Jock (1972-)
**"Gritty Dynamic Energy"**

- **Visual traits**: Rough expressive brushwork, dynamic action poses, high contrast lighting, gritty texture, comic book intensity, dramatic angles
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Jock style Mondo poster, rough expressive brushwork, dynamic action composition, high contrast chiaroscuro lighting, gritty textured surfaces, comic book intensity, dramatic forced perspective, raw kinetic energy, contemporary action poster aesthetic`
- **Best for**: Action films, thrillers, crime dramas, horror

### Shepard Fairey (1970-)
**"Street Art Revolutionary"**

- **Visual traits**: Bold propaganda style, red/black/cream palette, halftone portraits, political messaging, stencil aesthetic, urban texture
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Shepard Fairey style poster, bold propaganda aesthetic, red black cream palette, halftone portrait, political street art influence, stencil layers, urban gritty texture, OBEY aesthetic, contemporary activist poster design`
- **Best for**: Political films, biographical dramas, social justice themes

### Jay Ryan (1971-)
**"Folksy Handmade Charm"**

- **Visual traits**: Single focal image, bold simple shapes, textured backgrounds, warm earth tones, handmade feel, approachable aesthetic
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Jay Ryan style Mondo poster, single focal image, bold simple shapes, textured hand-printed background, folksy handmade aesthetic, warm limited palette, one symbolic element represents entire film, accessible charm`
- **Best for**: Coming-of-age, indie dramas, nostalgic stories

### Paula Scher (1948-)
**"Typographic Maximalism"**

- **Visual traits**: Typography as primary visual, layered text compositions, vibrant colors, information density, expressive letterforms
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Paula Scher style poster, typography as primary visual element, layered text composition, vibrant color palette, dense information design, expressive hand-drawn letterforms, grid-breaking dynamic layout, contemporary typographic maximalism`
- **Best for**: Music events, festivals, text-heavy subjects

## 5. Book Cover Designers

### Chip Kidd
**"Conceptual Book Cover"**

- **Visual traits**: Conceptual approach, single symbolic object, bold typography hierarchy, provocative visual metaphor, clean layout
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Chip Kidd style book cover, conceptual single symbolic object, bold typography hierarchy, provocative visual metaphor, clean modern layout, literary fiction aesthetic`
- **Best for**: Literary fiction, contemporary novels

### Peter Mendelsund
**"Abstract Literary"**

- **Visual traits**: Abstract interpretation, deconstructed typography, fragmentary imagery, intellectual design, modernist influence
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Peter Mendelsund style book cover, abstract literary interpretation, deconstructed typography, fragmentary imagery, intellectual modernist design, sophisticated minimalism`
- **Best for**: Literary fiction, philosophical works, translations

### Coralie Bickford-Smith
**"Penguin Clothbound Classics"**

- **Visual traits**: Decorative repeat patterns, Art Nouveau/Arts & Crafts influence, elegant symmetry, foil stamping aesthetic, vintage craftsmanship
- **Prompt modifiers**: `Coralie Bickford-Smith style book cover, decorative repeat pattern, Art Nouveau Arts and Crafts influence, elegant symmetrical design, gold foil stamping aesthetic, Penguin Clothbound Classics, vintage craftsmanship`

## Mixing Styles

Combine complementary approaches for unique results:

**Template:**
```
[Subject] in Mondo poster style, combining [Artist A]'s [technique] with
[Artist B]'s [color approach], [shared visual elements], [unified aesthetic]
```

**Proven combinations:**
- **Saul Bass + Toulouse-Lautrec** → Flat color noir thriller
- **Olly Moss + Cassandre** → Geometric sci-fi with hidden imagery
- **Mucha + Kilian Eng** → Art Nouveau meets cyberpunk
- **Jock + Tyler Stout** → Gritty action ensemble
- **Dan McCarthy + Jay Ryan** → Warm geometric indie western
- **Chinese National Trend + Saul Bass** → Modern Chinese minimalist
