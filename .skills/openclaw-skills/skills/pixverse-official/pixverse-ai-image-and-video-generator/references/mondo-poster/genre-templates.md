# Mondo Poster Genre Templates Reference

> Based on [qiaomu-mondo-poster-design](https://github.com/joeseesun/qiaomu-mondo-poster-design) by [@vista8](https://x.com/vista8).

Genre-specific prompt templates for creating Mondo-style posters, book covers, and album covers. Each template includes visual approach, color guidance, and a ready-to-fill prompt template.

**Placeholders:** Replace `[BRACKETED TEXT]` with your specific content.

---

## Film Genres

### Horror / Supernatural

**Visual approach:** Atmospheric dread through suggestion, NOT explicit gore. Shadows, negative space, and symbolic elements create unease. Gothic architecture, isolated settings, and eerie lighting.

**Color palettes:**
- Classic: black, blood red, bone white
- Gothic: deep purple, silver, charcoal
- Supernatural: midnight blue, sickly green, cream

**Prompt template:**
```
[HORROR SUBJECT] in Mondo poster style, vertical [ASPECT_RATIO],
atmospheric horror composition, [COMPOSITION: Gothic silhouette / lurking
shadow / ominous architecture], [COLOR_PALETTE] 3-color screen print,
suggestion over explicit imagery, vintage 1970s horror poster aesthetic,
halftone texture, sense of dread and unease
```

**Recommended artists:** Martin Ansin, Jock, Steinlen

---

### Science Fiction

**Visual approach:** Geometric precision, technical detail, retro-futuristic elements. Cool color temperatures. Architecture and technology as character. Isometric or dramatic perspective.

**Color palettes:**
- Retro sci-fi: teal, orange, black
- Cyberpunk: neon pink, electric blue, black
- Space: deep navy, white, gold

**Prompt template:**
```
[SCI-FI SUBJECT] in Mondo poster style, vertical [ASPECT_RATIO],
geometric sci-fi composition, [COMPOSITION: architectural vista / technology
close-up / cosmic scale], [COLOR_PALETTE] 3-color screen print, retro-futuristic
aesthetic, precise technical line work, vintage 1970s sci-fi poster,
screen print halftone texture
```

**Recommended artists:** Kilian Eng, Cassandre, Saul Bass

---

### Film Noir / Crime

**Visual approach:** High contrast shadows, urban settings, venetian blind light patterns, silhouetted figures in trench coats. Mystery and moral ambiguity through visual composition.

**Color palettes:**
- Classic noir: black, cream, single red accent
- Neo-noir: black, electric blue, gold
- Pulp: black, mustard yellow, crimson

**Prompt template:**
```
[NOIR SUBJECT] in Mondo poster style, vertical [ASPECT_RATIO],
high contrast noir composition, [COMPOSITION: shadow-split face / venetian
blind lighting / rain-slicked street], [COLOR_PALETTE] 3-color screen print,
dramatic chiaroscuro, vintage 1940s noir poster aesthetic, halftone shadow texture
```

**Recommended artists:** Saul Bass, Olly Moss, Laurent Durieux

---

### Western

**Visual approach:** Vast landscape horizons, lone figures against expansive skies. Warm earth tones. Simplicity and solitude. Horizontal compositions even in vertical format (sky dominance).

**Color palettes:**
- Classic: burnt sienna, dusty gold, dark brown
- Sunset: deep orange, purple, warm cream
- Desert: sand, rust red, turquoise sky

**Prompt template:**
```
[WESTERN SUBJECT] in Mondo poster style, vertical [ASPECT_RATIO],
vast landscape composition, [COMPOSITION: lone figure on horizon / desert
vista / frontier town silhouette], [COLOR_PALETTE] 3-color screen print,
vast open space, warm earth tones, vintage 1960s Western poster aesthetic
```

**Recommended artists:** Dan McCarthy, Cassandre, Toulouse-Lautrec

---

### Action / Thriller

**Visual approach:** Dynamic angles, motion lines, explosive energy. Bold contrasts and diagonal compositions suggest movement and tension. Character-driven with environmental context.

**Color palettes:**
- Explosive: red, black, bright yellow
- Tactical: dark grey, military green, white
- Chase: orange, dark blue, black

**Prompt template:**
```
[ACTION SUBJECT] in Mondo poster style, vertical [ASPECT_RATIO],
dynamic action composition, [COMPOSITION: dramatic angle / motion lines /
explosive diagonal], [COLOR_PALETTE] 3-color screen print, kinetic energy,
bold contrasts, vintage 1980s action poster aesthetic, high contrast halftone
```

**Recommended artists:** Jock, Drew Struzan, Tyler Stout

---

### Fantasy / Adventure

**Visual approach:** Mythical symbolism, epic scale, ornate detail. Rich palettes or restrained earth tones depending on tone. Creatures and landscapes as silhouettes or geometric forms.

**Color palettes:**
- Epic: gold, deep blue, crimson
- Dark fantasy: emerald green, black, silver
- Fairy tale: warm amber, forest green, cream

**Prompt template:**
```
[FANTASY SUBJECT] in Mondo poster style, vertical [ASPECT_RATIO],
epic fantasy composition, [COMPOSITION: mythical creature silhouette /
enchanted landscape / heroic figure], [COLOR_PALETTE] 3-color screen print,
ornate symbolic elements, vintage fantasy poster aesthetic, screen print texture
```

**Recommended artists:** Mucha, Drew Struzan, Kilian Eng

---

### Comedy

**Visual approach:** Bright cheerful colors, playful compositions, visual gags. Simplified character forms, exaggerated expressions through shape rather than detail. Witty and approachable.

**Color palettes:**
- Bright: sunny yellow, sky blue, red
- Retro: pink, mint green, orange
- Slapstick: bold primary colors

**Prompt template:**
```
[COMEDY SUBJECT] in Mondo poster style, vertical [ASPECT_RATIO],
playful cheerful composition, [COMPOSITION: visual gag / exaggerated prop /
absurdist scenario], [COLOR_PALETTE] 3-color screen print, bright joyful
aesthetic, simplified playful forms, vintage 1960s comedy poster
```

**Recommended artists:** Paul Rand, Milton Glaser, Cheret

---

### Drama / Indie

**Visual approach:** Emotional subtlety through muted palettes and restrained composition. Single powerful visual metaphor. Negative space conveys weight of emotion. Sophisticated and understated.

**Color palettes:**
- Melancholy: muted blue, warm grey, cream
- Intimate: dusty rose, soft brown, off-white
- Stark: black, single muted color, white

**Prompt template:**
```
[DRAMA SUBJECT] in Mondo poster style, vertical [ASPECT_RATIO],
emotionally subtle composition, [COMPOSITION: single visual metaphor /
isolated figure / contemplative scene], [COLOR_PALETTE] 3-color screen print,
muted sophisticated palette, understated design, vintage art house poster aesthetic
```

**Recommended artists:** Jay Ryan, Dan McCarthy, Josef Muller-Brockmann

---

### Animated Films

**Visual approach:** Simplified character silhouettes from the source material. Vibrant colors matching the animation's palette. Geometric simplification of the animated world into screen-print-friendly forms.

**Color palettes:**
- Vibrant: as per source animation palette
- Simplified: 3-4 key colors from the original
- Stylized: reinterpreted through vintage filter

**Prompt template:**
```
[ANIMATED SUBJECT] in Mondo poster style, vertical [ASPECT_RATIO],
simplified character silhouette, [COMPOSITION: iconic scene distilled /
character outline / key symbolic element], [COLOR_PALETTE] 3-color screen print,
geometric simplification, vibrant yet limited palette, vintage animated poster
```

**Recommended artists:** Olly Moss, Saul Bass, Dan McCarthy

## Book Cover Templates

### Literary Fiction
**Approach:** Sophisticated minimalism, conceptual symbolism, author prominence.
```
[BOOK TITLE concept] book cover in Mondo style, vertical 2:3, single
conceptual symbol, muted sophisticated palette: [colors], prominent author
typography, literary fiction aesthetic, Chip Kidd influence, clean design
```

**Approach:** Retro-futuristic elements, technical precision, cool colors.
```

pulp-influenced precision, screen print aesthetic
```

## Genre Mixing

When a film/book spans multiple genres, blend templates:

**Template:**
```
[SUBJECT] in Mondo poster style blending [Genre A] and [Genre B] aesthetics,
[Genre A visual element] combined with [Genre B color approach],
[shared composition technique], [unified mood]
```

**Example (Horror-Comedy):**
```
Shaun of the Dead in Mondo poster style blending horror and comedy aesthetics,
Gothic silhouette combined with bright playful colors: red, yellow, cream,
visual pun composition where weapon is also everyday object, darkly humorous
vintage poster, 3-color screen print
```
