# SRE/DevOps News Scoring Rubric

Rate each article from 1 to 5 stars from a **senior SRE/DevOps/Solution Architect** perspective.

## Rating Criteria

| Rating | Criteria |
|--------|----------|
| ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5) | Groundbreaking for SRE/DevOps practice; actionable insights; industry-defining reports or tools; directly impacts reliability, observability, or incident management at scale |
| ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4) | Highly relevant to SRE/DevOps; introduces meaningful new tools, practices, or platform capabilities; strong practical value |
| ⭐⭐⭐ (3) | Moderately relevant; useful context or updates but indirect impact on day-to-day SRE/DevOps work |
| ⭐⭐ (2) | Tangentially related; mostly developer-focused or vendor marketing with limited SRE/DevOps takeaways |
| ⭐ (1) | Minimal relevance to SRE/DevOps; general tech news or off-topic |

## Category Taxonomy

Assign one or two categories from this list (or create new ones if needed):

- **Observability Tools** — Monitoring, logging, tracing, APM platforms
- **AI in Observability** — AI/ML-driven monitoring, AIOps, anomaly detection
- **AI in Operations** — AI agents for incident response, automation, remediation
- **Platform Engineering** — Internal developer platforms, self-service infrastructure
- **Cloud Native** — Kubernetes, containers, service mesh, cloud-native tooling
- **Cloud SRE** — Cloud provider SRE features, managed reliability services
- **Incident Management** — On-call, incident response, postmortems, alerting
- **SRE Trends** — Industry reports, surveys, state-of-SRE analyses
- **SRE Philosophy** — Culture, principles, best practices, thought leadership
- **Security** — DevSecOps, supply chain security, identity management
- **Development Tools** — Languages, frameworks, CI/CD, build systems
- **DevOps Impact** — Cross-cutting DevOps implications of broader tech changes
- **Infrastructure** — IaC, networking, DNS, CDN, storage
- **Reliability Engineering** — SLOs, error budgets, chaos engineering, resilience
- **Performance Optimization** — Performance monitoring, optimization, capacity planning
- **FinOps** — Cost management, FinOps practices, cloud cost optimization
- **DevSecOps** — Security practices integrated into DevOps workflows
- **GitOps** — GitOps practices, tools, and case studies
- **ChatOps** — ChatOps tools, practices, and case studies
- **Containerization** — Docker, container runtimes, container security
- **Serverless** — Serverless platforms, best practices, case studies
