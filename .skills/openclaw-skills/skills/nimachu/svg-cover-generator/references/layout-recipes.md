# Layout Recipes

Choose one recipe before writing the SVG.

## 1. Left-Anchor Editorial

Use for:
- reports
- essays
- product strategy docs
- keynote title slides

Shape:
- strong text block on the left
- abstract geometry on the right
- calm but premium feel

Best when:
- title is medium or long
- readability matters most

## 2. Centered Poster

Use for:
- event posters
- manifesto covers
- launch announcements

Shape:
- centered title cluster
- symmetrical or near-symmetrical motif
- strong background gradient or halo

Best when:
- the title is short
- the user wants something dramatic

## 3. Diagonal Motion

Use for:
- AI topics
- startup decks
- futuristic or energetic themes

Shape:
- diagonals, slashes, repeated lines, offset blocks
- text locked to one edge or corner

Best when:
- the cover should feel dynamic
- the user wants movement without illustration

## 4. Modular Grid

Use for:
- data topics
- design systems
- engineering reports
- dashboard-style covers

Shape:
- grid base
- one or two broken modules for tension
- typography snapped to grid edges

Best when:
- the content should feel structured and modern

## 5. Framed Minimal

Use for:
- luxury feel
- founder letters
- polished internal docs

Shape:
- large negative space
- slim border or frame
- restrained accent details

Best when:
- the brief asks for elegant, minimal, premium output
