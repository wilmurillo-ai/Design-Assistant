# Emergency Go-Bag Checklist

Prepare a bag that you can grab in under 2 minutes. Keep it by the door.

## Essential (Always Ready)

### Documents (in waterproof bag)
- [ ] Passport (+ copies)
- [ ] National ID / residence permit
- [ ] Travel insurance documents
- [ ] Embassy registration confirmation
- [ ] Emergency contact list (printed — phones may die)
- [ ] Cash (local currency + USD, small denominations)
- [ ] Credit/debit cards
- [ ] Medical records / prescriptions
- [ ] Property documents / lease agreement (copies)

### Electronics
- [ ] Phone + charger + power bank (fully charged)
- [ ] USB drive with document scans
- [ ] Headphones (for receiving alerts)
- [ ] Small FM/AM radio (optional but valuable if networks go down)

### Water & Food
- [ ] Water bottles (minimum 2L per person)
- [ ] Energy bars / non-perishable snacks (3+ days)
- [ ] Water purification tablets (if available)

### Medical
- [ ] Prescription medications (7+ day supply)
- [ ] Basic first aid kit
- [ ] Any chronic condition supplies (insulin, inhaler, etc.)
- [ ] Pain relievers, antihistamines
- [ ] Hand sanitizer, masks

### Clothing & Shelter
- [ ] Change of clothes (weather-appropriate)
- [ ] Rain jacket / warm layer
- [ ] Comfortable walking shoes
- [ ] Hat / sun protection
- [ ] Small blanket or emergency mylar blanket

### Tools
- [ ] Flashlight + spare batteries
- [ ] Multi-tool or knife
- [ ] Dust masks / N95 (for debris, smoke)
- [ ] Whistle (for signaling)
- [ ] Duct tape (small roll)
- [ ] Plastic bags (waterproofing)
- [ ] Permanent marker + notepad

## Per-Person Add-ons

### Children
- [ ] Comfort item (small toy/blanket)
- [ ] Diapers / formula if applicable
- [ ] Children's medications
- [ ] ID / medical info card in pocket

### Pets
- [ ] Carrier / leash
- [ ] Pet food (3 days)
- [ ] Vaccination records
- [ ] Pet ID tags

## Vehicle Preparedness
- [ ] Fuel above half tank AT ALL TIMES
- [ ] Paper road map (GPS may not work)
- [ ] Jumper cables
- [ ] Extra water in trunk
- [ ] Blanket in trunk

## Digital Preparedness
- [ ] Offline maps downloaded (Google Maps / Maps.me)
- [ ] Emergency numbers saved in phone
- [ ] ICE (In Case of Emergency) contact set in phone
- [ ] Important documents photographed and in cloud storage
- [ ] VPN installed (communications may be restricted)
- [ ] Messaging apps with end-to-end encryption installed

---

**Review this checklist weekly during elevated threat periods.**
**Test your readiness: Can you leave in 2 minutes with everything you need?**
