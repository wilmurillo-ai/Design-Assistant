# Link Building Reference

Use this file for backlink quality review, outreach ideas, and link-risk guidance.

## Principles

- Favor durable, relevant, editorially defensible links.
- Quality and relevance matter more than raw quantity.
- Avoid recommending manipulative schemes.

## Safe guidance

Recommend:
- digital PR / useful assets
- relevant partnerships
- niche-relevant guest contributions
- resource inclusion where genuinely appropriate
- reclaiming broken/unlinked mentions
- strong internal linking as the first scalable authority lever

## Risk flags

Warn against:
- PBNs
- spam link packages
- irrelevant paid placements
- comment/forum spam
- bought reviews
- obvious reciprocal schemes designed only to game rankings

## Backlink review questions

- Is the linking domain topically relevant?
- Does the link appear editorial or manufactured?
- Is anchor text unnaturally optimized?
- Is there evidence of site-wide spam?
- Is the tactic repeatable without raising risk?

## Outreach advice

Keep outreach:
- specific
- low-spam
- value-based
- targeted

Do not imply guaranteed ranking gains from a single link.

## Reporting template

- current backlink/risk picture
- what is safe to pursue
- what to avoid
- 3–5 realistic acquisition angles
