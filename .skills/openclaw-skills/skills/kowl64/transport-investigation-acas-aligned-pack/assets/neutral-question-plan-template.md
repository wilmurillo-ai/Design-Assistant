# Neutral Question Plan (ACAS-aligned)

Case reference:
Meeting date:
Attendees:

## Opening
- Confirm purpose is fact-finding
- Confirm right-to-be-accompanied and confidentiality
- Confirm notes will be taken

## Broad account
1) “Talk me through your shift from start to finish on [date].”
2) “What was the plan, and what changed?”

## Specific probes (based on evidence)
- “The record shows [fact]. Help me understand what was happening at that point.”
- “What options did you consider?”

## Policy/process understanding
- “What is your understanding of the required process for [topic]?”
- “What training have you had on this?”

## Mitigation/context
- “Were there any factors that made compliance difficult?”
- “What support would have helped?”

## Closing
- “Is there anything else you want us to consider?”
- Agree next steps and timescales
