# Google Policy Guidelines Reference (Updated 07/08/2025)

## 1. General Guidelines
- The ads should advertise what is provided for in the article. It must not make false promises or manipulate users to click.
- The content the user sees must be relevant to the ad that was clicked and provide value.
- The Referrer Ad Creative (RAC) must be accurate, complete, and without omissions.
- **Dynamic Keyword Insertion (DKI)** cannot be used.

## 2. Forbidden: False Promises
- Concrete or explicit promises that cannot be fulfilled:
  - Free or cash offers ("free phone", "no-fee lawyer").
  - Promise to hire.
  - Unreasonably cheap offers (brand new vehicle for $1000).
  - Loans or instant approvals.
  - "Cure" for a disease.
  - Misrepresentation of identity ("We're hiring", "Our best deals", "Rated #1").
- Specific promises outside of your control:
  - No credit check, Paid trials, % savings, On sale.

## 3. Forbidden: Click Incentives
Phrases that encourage or pressure users to click by using urgent terminology or shocking language:
- Fake expiration dates (expires 10/1 at midnight!!).
- Fake quantities (only 17 remain!).
- "Hurry", "Today", "Now", "Instantly", "Unbelievable", "Shocking".
- Sexually explicit or suggestive images that do not match content.
- Icons or images to click or point (arrows, fake play buttons).
- "See here".

## 4. Forbidden: Implied Function
Wording that implies users are performing an action not available on the next page:
- "Search", "Find Prices", "Get Quotes", "Start", "Download", "Install", "Book now", "Apply now", "See Options", "Request more info", "Explore", "Discover".

## 5. Vertical-Specific Rules
### Automotive
- **General Category:** Image must be reasonably representative of vehicles in the category.
- **Specific Model:** Should be an actual vehicle (No AI fake cars). Brand logos must match. Must be a real car, not a concept car. Realistic features only.

### Loans
- Clarify that rates vary and approval is not guaranteed.
- Always support values with disclaimers: "Terms and availability depend on your credit profile".
- Reflect same values on the content (Ad and page must align).
- Use value ranges instead of fixed amounts (e.g., "$1,000–$10,000").
- **Avoid:** "Only 1 Loan Left for You!", "Get Cash in 5 Minutes!", "Guaranteed 5% interest rate", "Get approved in 60 seconds — no credit check".

## 6. Content Pages Requirements
- **Match Ad and Content:** Ad image, ad text, and landing page article must match perfectly.
- **Content Length:** Intro paragraph before the keyword block should be at least 40-50 words. Total length >750 words.
- **No Incenting Clicks:** Do not use leading text like "Start your online test below" or "Check out these top searches" right before ads.
