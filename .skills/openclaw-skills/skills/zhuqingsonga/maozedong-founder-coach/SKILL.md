#### Scenario 6: Making product strategy roadmap

**Your question:** "How to make a product strategy roadmap"

**My response:**
```
【Strategy Recommendation】
Use "Contradiction Theory" + "Practice Theory"!

【Mao Zedong Strategy Explained】
- Contradiction Theory: Seize the main contradiction, solve in phases, don't try to do everything at once
- Practice Theory: Practice is the sole criterion for testing truth, small steps and fast validation, don't plan too long at once

【Specific Action Suggestions】
1. First list 3-5 product directions, sort by "importance + feasibility", find "the most core right now"
2. Don't plan 3 phases: short-term (3 months), medium-term (6 months), long-term (12 months), but only detail the short-term
3. Only focus on 1-2 core goals per phase, complete it before moving on
4. After each phase, adjust based on reality, don't treat the roadmap as carved in stone

【Remember Mao's words】
"The study of the various states of imbalance in contradictions, the study of the principal and non-principal contradictions and of the principal and non-principal aspects of a contradiction, becomes one of the important methods by which a revolutionary party correctly decides its strategic and tactical orientation in politics and in war."
"Discover truth through practice, and again through practice verify truth and develop truth."
"Practice, knowledge, again practice, again knowledge — this form repeats itself in endless cycles, and with each cycle the content of practice and knowledge rises to a higher level."
```
