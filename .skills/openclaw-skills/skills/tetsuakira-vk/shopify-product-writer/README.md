# Shopify Product Writer

**Stop staring at a blank product page. Go from brief to live listing in under 60 seconds.**

Writing product listings is one of the most time-consuming parts of running a Shopify store — and most listings leave money on the table. Shopify Product Writer turns a rough product brief into a complete, SEO-optimised listing you can paste straight into your store.

---

## What it does

Give it a product brief. Get a complete Shopify listing back — instantly.

- **SEO product title** — Keyword-led, max 70 characters, formatted for Shopify and Google Shopping
- **Full product description** — 200–350 words, benefit-led copy with a natural keyword flow and a soft call to action
- **Meta title & meta description** — Optimised search snippets with character counts, ready to paste into your SEO fields
- **5 feature bullet points** — Scannable, benefit-first highlights for above-the-fold display
- **10–15 Shopify tags** — A full set of product, audience, and use-case tags to improve store search and filtering

Every output is structured with clearly labelled sections so you can copy each piece directly into the right Shopify field — no reformatting needed.

## Usage

### Example 1 — Minimal brief

**Input:**
```
Write a listing for a stainless steel water bottle, 32oz, double-walled, 
keeps drinks cold 24 hours or hot 12 hours. Targeting gym-goers and hikers.
```

**Output (excerpt):**
```
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
SHOPIFY PRODUCT LISTING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

【 PRODUCT TITLE 】
32oz Stainless Steel Water Bottle — Stays Cold 24 Hours
Character count: 56/70

【 FEATURE BULLET POINTS 】
• Stay hydrated longer: Double-walled insulation keeps drinks cold for 24 hours
• Hot drinks, handled: Keeps coffee and tea warm for up to 12 hours
• Built for the trail and the gym: Durable stainless steel resists dents and drops
• No more leaks mid-hike: Leak-proof lid seals tight every time
• The right size for a full day: 32oz capacity means fewer refill stops

【 SHOPIFY TAGS 】
water-bottle, stainless-steel-bottle, insulated-bottle, gym-water-bottle,
hiking-gear, 32oz-bottle, double-walled, hot-cold-bottle, hydration,
sports-bottle, outdoor-gear, reusable-bottle, leak-proof
```
