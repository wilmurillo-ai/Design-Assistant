# Srbija Voz keyword cues

Use these cues when classifying notices.

## Delay
- kašnjenje
- kasni
- zakašnjenje
- u kašnjenju

## Canceled or not running
- neće saobraćati
- izostaje
- otkazan
- otkazuje se

## Stopped or held
- stoje vozovi
- zbog smetnje
- do daljnjeg
- na licu mesta

## Operational change
- izmena saobraćaja
- privremena izmena
- operativna izmena
- tehnički razlozi

## Replacement bus service
- autobuski red vožnje
- organizovan besplatan prevoz
- autobus
- prevoz putnika autobusom

## Corridor note
- On the Petrovaradin - Novi Sad centar - Petrovaradin corridor, the bus notice is recurring service information and is expected to update regularly
- Do not classify it as a disruption by itself unless a notice explicitly says the bus service changed, stopped, or was canceled

## Novi Sad - Petrovaradin corridor
Watch for:
- Petrovaradin
- Novi Sad centar
- Batajnica
- Beograd centar
- Zemun
