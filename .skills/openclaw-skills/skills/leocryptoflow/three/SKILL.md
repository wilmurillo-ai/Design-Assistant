## 中文版本 / Chinese Version

### 1. 哲学与宗教意义

**西方宗教**：
- 基督教：三位一体（圣父、圣子、圣灵）
- 古希腊：三美神（优雅、美丽、欢乐）
- 西方童话中常见"三个愿望"、"三只小猪"等三元结构

**东方哲学**：
- 道家："二生三，三生万物"，3 代表万物生成的关键
- 儒家："三才"（天、地、人），"三纲五常"
- 佛教："三宝"（佛、法、僧），"三世"（过去、现在、未来）

### 2. 文化象征

**中国**：
- "三"代表多数，如"三思而后行"、"三人行必有我师"
- "三阳开泰"象征新年吉祥
- "三星高照"代表福、禄、寿
- 古代科举：乡试、会试、殿试三级考试

**西方**：
- 3 是幸运数字（lucky number）
- "Third time's the charm"（第三次就会成功）
- 颁奖典礼的前三名（金、银、铜牌）
- 故事的三段式结构（开始、发展、结局）

### 3. 现代意义

- 三维空间（长、宽、高）
- 三色原理（RGB 色彩系统）
- 三等奖、第三名的象征
- "三"代表稳定和完整（三角形最稳定）

## English Version

### 1. Philosophical & Religious Significance

**Western Religion**:
- Christianity: The Holy Trinity (Father, Son, Holy Spirit)
- Ancient Greece: The Three Graces (elegance, beauty, joy)
- Western fairy tales often feature triadic structures: "three wishes," "three little pigs"

**Eastern Philosophy**:
- Taoism: "Two produced Three; Three produced All Things," 3 represents the key to generating all things
- Confucianism: "Three Powers" (Heaven, Earth, Humanity), "Three Bonds and Five Constants"
- Buddhism: "Three Jewels" (Buddha, Dharma, Sangha), "Three Times" (past, present, future)

### 2. Cultural Symbolism

**Chinese Culture**:
- "三" represents multitude: "三思而后行" (think thrice before acting), "三人行必有我师" (in a group of three, one can be my teacher)
- "三阳开泰" symbolizes New Year auspiciousness
- "三星高照" represents Fortune, Prosperity, and Longevity
- Ancient imperial examinations: three levels (local, provincial, palace)

**Western Culture**:
- 3 is a lucky number
- "Third time's the charm"
- Top three in award ceremonies (gold, silver, bronze medals)
- Three-act story structure (beginning, development, conclusion)

### 3. Modern Significance

- Three-dimensional space (length, width, height)
- Three-color principle (RGB color system)
- Symbol of third place/bronze medal
- "Three" represents stability and completeness (triangle is the most stable shape)
