## 中文版本 / Chinese Version

### 1. 哲学与宗教意义

**西方哲学**：
- 毕达哥拉斯学派认为 2 代表"对立"与"差异"
- 2 是第一个偶数，象征平衡与和谐
- 在二元论哲学中，2 代表善与恶、光与暗的对立

**东方哲学**：
- 道家："一生二"，2 代表阴阳的分化
- 阴阳是宇宙最基本的对立统一关系
- 《易经》中，阴爻和阳爻构成八卦的基础

### 2. 文化象征

**中国**：
- "好事成双"，2 代表吉祥和圆满
- 婚礼中的"双喜"，象征美满婚姻
- "二龙戏珠"、"比翼双飞"等表达美好祝愿
- 但某些语境下"二"也有贬义（如"二愣子"）

**西方**：
- 2 代表 partnership（伙伴关系）和 cooperation（合作）
- 情侣、夫妻的"成双成对"
- 硬币的两面（two sides of a coin）象征事物的两面性

### 3. 现代意义

- 二进制系统的核心（0 和 1 之外的基础）
- 双人合作、团队协作的象征
- 选择与对比的代表（A 或 B）

## English Version

### 1. Philosophical & Religious Significance

**Western Philosophy**:
- Pythagoreans believed 2 represents "opposition" and "difference"
- 2 is the first even number, symbolizing balance and harmony
- In dualistic philosophy, 2 represents the opposition of good and evil, light and dark

**Eastern Philosophy**:
- Taoism: "One produced Two," 2 represents the division of Yin and Yang
- Yin-Yang is the most fundamental opposing yet unified relationship in the universe
- In the I Ching, Yin and Yang lines form the basis of the Eight Trigrams

### 2. Cultural Symbolism

**Chinese Culture**:
- "好事成双" (good things come in pairs), 2 represents auspiciousness and completeness
- "Double Happiness" (双喜) in weddings symbolizes a blissful marriage
- Expressions like "二龙戏珠" (two dragons playing with a pearl), "比翼双飞" (flying wing to wing) convey good wishes
- However, in some contexts "二" can have negative connotations

**Western Culture**:
- 2 represents partnership and cooperation
- Couples and spouses as "pairs"
- "Two sides of a coin" symbolizes the dual nature of things

### 3. Modern Significance

- Core of binary systems (foundation beyond 0 and 1)
- Symbol of双人 cooperation and teamwork
- Represents choice and contrast (A or B)
