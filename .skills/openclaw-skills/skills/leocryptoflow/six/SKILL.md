# 数字 6 的文化介绍 / Cultural Meaning of Number 6

## 中文部分

### 基本信息

**数字**: 6  
**汉字**: 六  
**拼音**: liù

### 中国文化中的 6

#### 吉祥寓意
- **六六大顺**: 最著名的吉祥语，象征一切顺利、万事如意
- **六合**: 天地四方，代表宇宙完整
- **六畜兴旺**: 马、牛、羊、鸡、狗、猪，象征农业繁荣

#### 传统文化概念

| 概念 | 内容 |
|-----|------|
| **六艺** | 礼、乐、射、御、书、数（古代教育六项技能） |
| **六经** | 《诗》《书》《礼》《易》《乐》《春秋》 |
| **六亲** | 父、母、兄、弟、妻、子 |
| **六腑** | 胆、胃、大肠、小肠、膀胱、三焦 |
| **六根** | 眼、耳、鼻、舌、身、意（佛教概念） |
| **六尘** | 色、声、香、味、触、法（佛教概念） |

#### 现代应用
- **电话号码**: 含 6 的号码被视为吉利，价格较高
- **车牌号**: 666 等连号备受追捧
- **结婚吉日**: 农历初六、十六、二十六常被视为好日子
- **商业定价**: 66 元、666 元等定价象征吉利

#### 西方文化中的 6
- **完美数字**: 在基督教中，6 被视为完美数字（上帝 6 天创造世界）
- **幸运数字**: 在许多文化中代表幸运
- **六边形**: 自然界常见结构（蜂巢、雪花）

### Number 6 in Chinese Culture

#### Auspicious Meanings
- **六六大顺 (Liù Liù Dà Shùn)**: Most famous blessing, meaning "everything goes smoothly"
- **六合 (Liu He)**: Heaven, earth, and four directions, representing cosmic completeness
- **六畜兴旺**: Six domestic animals thriving, symbolizing agricultural prosperity

#### Traditional Cultural Concepts

| Concept | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Six Arts (Liu Yi)** | Ritual, Music, Archery, Charioteering, Calligraphy, Mathematics |
| **Six Classics (Liu Jing)** | Book of Songs, Book of Documents, Book of Rites, I Ching, Book of Music, Spring and Autumn Annals |
| **Six Relations (Liu Qin)** | Father, Mother, Elder Brother, Younger Brother, Wife, Children |
| **Six Fu Organs** | Gallbladder, Stomach, Large Intestine, Small Intestine, Bladder, San Jiao |
| **Six Roots (Buddhism)** | Eye, Ear, Nose, Tongue, Body, Mind |
| **Six Dusts (Buddhism)** | Form, Sound, Smell, Taste, Touch, Dharma |

#### Modern Applications
- **Phone Numbers**: Numbers containing 6 are considered lucky, sold at premium prices
- **License Plates**: Repeating 6s (like 666) are highly sought after
- **Wedding Dates**: 6th, 16th, 26th of lunar calendar considered auspicious
- **Business Pricing**: 66 yuan, 666 yuan pricing symbolizes good fortune

#### Number 6 in Western Culture
- **Perfect Number**: In Christianity, 6 is considered perfect (God created the world in 6 days)
- **Lucky Number**: Represents luck in many cultures
- **Hexagon**: Common structure in nature (honeycombs, snowflakes)
- **Six Senses**: Sight, Hearing, Touch, Taste, Smell, Intuition
