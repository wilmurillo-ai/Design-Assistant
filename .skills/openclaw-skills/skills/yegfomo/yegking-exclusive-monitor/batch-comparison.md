## LJR Batch

**Specialty: Nike & Jordan Brand**

LJR (named after the factory initials) is widely considered the **top batch for Jordan 1s and Nike Dunks**. It's produced in a factory that has refined its molds and materials over many production cycles.

### Strengths:
- 🏆 Best stitching quality on leather Jordans
- ✅ Correct toe box shape on Jordan 1 High/Low
- ✅ Accurate Nike swoosh angles and proportions
- ✅ Correct wing logo embossing on Air Jordan
- ✅ Clean heel tab and tongue label printing

### Best Models:
- Air Jordan 1 Retro High OG (all colorways)
- Nike Dunk Low / High
- Air Jordan 4, 11, 3
- Nike Air Force 1

### Watch Out For:
- Glue marks near the sole on older production runs
- Slight color variation on dark colorways — check under natural light

---

## PK Batch

**Specialty: Adidas / Yeezy**

PK (Perfect Kicks) batch is the **gold standard for Adidas silhouettes**, particularly Yeezy 350 V2, Yeezy 500, and Ultraboost. The factory has extremely accurate molds for Adidas boost soles.

### Strengths:
- 🏆 Best boost sole texture — matches retail closely
- ✅ Accurate Primeknit upper pattern
- ✅ Correct translucent stripe on Yeezy 350 V2
- ✅ Proper outsole tread pattern (sharp, not blurry)
- ✅ Correct midsole height and proportions

### Best Models:
- Yeezy 350 V2 (all colorways)
- Yeezy 500 / 700
- Adidas Ultra Boost
- Adidas NMD

### Watch Out For:
- Boost can look slightly different in different lighting — always check under natural light
- Stripe semi-transparency varies — compare against reference photos

---

## OG Batch

**Specialty: Collector-Level Accuracy**

OG Batch represents the **highest tier of rep manufacturing** — produced with stricter quality control, better materials inspection, and closer attention to retail accuracy. Often described as "1:1" or "retail-level" by experienced collectors.

### Strengths:
- 🏆 Near-retail manufacturing standards
- ✅ Best material quality across multiple silhouettes
- ✅ Minimal excess glue or construction errors
- ✅ Most accurate box printing and packaging
- ✅ Passes most in-hand retail comparisons

### Best Models:
- High-profile Jordan colorways (Chicago, Bred, Royal)
- Limited Nike collab shoes (Travis Scott, Off-White)
- Any shoe where retail accuracy is critical

### Watch Out For:
- Higher price point — verify authenticity of OG label before purchase
- Supply can be limited on newer colorways
