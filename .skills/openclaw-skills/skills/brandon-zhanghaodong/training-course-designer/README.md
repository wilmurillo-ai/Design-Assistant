# Training Course Designer - Enhanced Edition

## Overview

This skill enables HR professionals and L&D specialists to design high-impact corporate training programs with **ONE-CLICK generation** of complete training packages.

## What's New in Enhanced Edition

### 🚀 One-Click Complete Package Generation

Generate 14 professional, ready-to-use documents in 10 minutes by providing just 5 key inputs:

1. Training Topic
2. Target Audience  
3. Training Duration
4. Learning Objectives
5. Training Format

### 📦 Complete Deliverables Package

**Course Design Materials (5 documents):**
- Course Outline Document
- Instructor Guide
- Participant Handbook
- Interactive Activity Sheets
- Evaluation & Follow-up Plan

**Marketing & Operations Copy (6 documents):**
- Enrollment Notice
- Pre-Training Reminder (1 Week)
- Pre-Training Reminder (1 Day)
- Welcome Message
- Post-Training Thank You
- 30-Day Follow-Up

**Assessment Materials (3 documents):**
- Pre-Training Knowledge Check
- Post-Training Quiz
- Level 1 Evaluation Survey

## Support & Troubleshooting

### Common Questions

**Q: Can I generate materials for compliance training?**
A: Yes, but ensure you add legally required content and have it reviewed by your legal/compliance team.

**Q: How do I customize the generated materials?**
A: All documents are provided in Markdown format, which can be easily edited. Add your examples, branding, and specific requirements.

**Q: Can I request additional materials not in the 14-document package?**
A: Yes! You can request PowerPoint slides, infographics, video scripts, manager briefings, etc.

**Q: What if the generated content doesn't fit my industry?**
A: Provide industry-specific context in your inputs, and customize the generated content with your own examples and terminology.
