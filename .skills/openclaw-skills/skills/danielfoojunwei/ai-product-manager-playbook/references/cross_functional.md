# Cross-Functional Collaboration in AI Teams

This framework outlines how to structure and manage AI product teams to ensure alignment across business, design, and engineering.

## When to use this framework
- Setting up a new AI product team.
- Troubleshooting communication breakdowns between data scientists and product managers.
- Scaling AI initiatives across an organization.

## 1. Structuring for Success
There is no one-size-fits-all solution, but the optimal structure depends on organizational maturity.

| Team Structure Model | Description | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Centralized (Center of Excellence)** | A single, dedicated AI team serves the entire organization. | Smaller companies or those new to AI. |
| **Embedded (Distributed Teams)** | AI specialists are embedded within individual business units. | Large organizations with mature AI capabilities. |
| **Hybrid** | A combination of a central AI team and embedded specialists. | Most organizations, balancing standardization with autonomy. |

## 2. The Pivotal Role of the AI PM
The AI PM acts as the bridge between business, technical, and design teams.
- **Action:** Define the product vision, prioritize features based on strategic alignment and technical feasibility, and manage the ongoing lifecycle of the AI system.

## 3. Key Collaboration Practices
- **Involve Stakeholders Early:** Include business strategists, data scientists, ML engineers, designers, legal, and cybersecurity from the very beginning.
- **Prioritize Data Readiness:** Invest in building a robust data infrastructure and hiring data engineers early on. Data is the lifeblood of AI.
- **Foster Psychological Safety:** AI development is experimental. Create an environment where team members feel comfortable sharing ideas and learning from failures.
- **Establish Clear Roles:** Ensure everyone understands their responsibilities throughout the product development lifecycle.
- **Implement MLOps:** A well-defined MLOps strategy is essential for the smooth deployment, monitoring, and maintenance of AI models in production.
