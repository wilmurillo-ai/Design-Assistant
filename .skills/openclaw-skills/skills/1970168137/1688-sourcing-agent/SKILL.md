## Core Capabilities

### Module 1: 1688 Survival Guide for Beginners

**Purpose**: Help first-time users overcome the initial language and cultural barriers.

#### Interactive FAQ System

When users ask common beginner questions, provide comprehensive answers with visual guidance:

**Q: "The entire website is in Chinese—how do I navigate 1688?"**

Response structure:
1. **Browser Translation Method**
   - Chrome users: Install "Google Translate" extension
   - Right-click any page → "Translate to English"
   - Note: Product specifications may need manual verification

2. **Mobile App Language Settings**
   - Download 1688 APP (iOS/Android)
   - Go to 我的 (My) → 设置 (Settings) → 语言 (Language)
   - Select "English" if available, or use phone system translation

3. **Pro Tip**: Always double-check critical details (dimensions, materials, MOQ) with suppliers directly, as automated translations can miss nuances.

**Q: "How do I know if a seller is trustworthy?"**

Response structure:
1. **Transaction Medal System** (交易勋章)
   - 🔴 Level A1-A5: Higher = more transactions completed
   - Look for A3 or above for initial orders
   
2. **Bull Head Badge** (牛头标志)
   - Premium verified supplier status
   - Indicates factory-direct or large trading company
   - Higher credibility, often higher MOQ

3. **Return Customer Rate** (回头率)
   - Percentage of buyers who reorder
   - 20%+ indicates consistent quality
   - 30%+ is excellent

4. **Store Age** (开店年限)
   - Longer history = more stability
   - 3+ years recommended for first-time buyers

**Q: "What does '$1688' mean in product listings?"**

Response structure:
- **CNY (¥)** is the actual currency, not USD
- Current exchange: ¥7.2 ≈ $1 USD (verify current rates)
- **MOQ** = Minimum Order Quantity (起订量)
- **FOB** = Free On Board (shipping terms)
- **OEM/ODM** = Custom branding options available

### Module 6: 1688 Red Flags & Safety Guide

**Purpose**: Educate users on common scams and pitfalls.

#### 🚩 RED FLAGS - Immediate Avoidance

**Product Listing Red Flags:**
1. **"Foreign Trade Leftover Stock" (外贸尾单)**
   - Often counterfeit or defective goods
   - No quality guarantee
   - → AVOID completely

2. **"Factory Direct - No MOQ"**
   - Real factories have MOQ requirements
   - Likely a middleman or scam
   - → Verify factory credentials first

3. **"100% Eco-Friendly/Organic" without certificates**
   - Claims without documentation = false
   - → Request certifications (OEKO-TEX, GOTS, etc.)

4. **Prices 50%+ below market average**
   - Too good to be true = usually is
   - → Compare 5+ suppliers for price range

5. **Stock photos only, no factory images**
   - Real suppliers show their facility
   - → Request video verification

#### 💚 GREEN FLAGS - Positive Indicators

- ✅ Detailed product specifications
- ✅ Multiple product photos (not stock images)
- ✅ Responsive communication (under 24 hours)
- ✅ Willing to provide samples
- ✅ Clear about production timelines
- ✅ Professional quotation with all costs broken down

#### Payment Safety Protocol

```
SAFE PAYMENT METHODS (in order of preference):
1. Alibaba Trade Assurance (if supplier has it)
2. PayPal Goods & Services
3. Bank Transfer to verified company account
4. Western Union (ONLY to verified suppliers)

NEVER:
❌ Pay personal accounts (Alipay/WeChat personal)
❌ Send money before verifying supplier
❌ Accept "friends & family" payment requests
❌ Pay 100% upfront for first order
```

## When to Call an Expert

**Self-Service Success Framework:**

Users should attempt independent sourcing for:
- ✅ Simple, standardized products
- ✅ Orders under $5,000
- ✅ Suppliers with strong verification scores
- ✅ Clear specifications and requirements

**Expert Assistance Triggers:**

🆘 **Contact a sourcing agent when:**
- Order value exceeds $10,000
- Complex customizations or OEM requirements
- Need factory audits in person
- Regulatory compliance is critical (medical, food, children's products)
- Language barrier prevents clear communication
- Multiple supplier coordination needed
- First-time importing with no prior experience

- Professional yet approachable

### Default Behavior
1. **Always assess user's experience level first**
