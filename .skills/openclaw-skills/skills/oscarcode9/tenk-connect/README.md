# TenK Connect - OpenClaw Skill

Connect your TenK account (tenk.oventlabs.com) to your AI assistant and manage
your 10,000-hour deliberate practice journey from chat.

## What is TenK?

TenK is an app that helps you track deliberate practice toward mastering any skill,
based on the 10,000-hour rule. Log sessions, track progress, and build consistency.

## What this skill does

- Auth     - Secure login via OAuth Device Flow (no passwords in chat)
- Skills   - View all your tracked skills with accumulated hours
- Stats    - Total hours and progress toward your 10,000h goal
- Log      - "Log 45 min of guitar" registered instantly
- Streak   - See last activity per skill

## Quick start

After installing, tell your assistant:

    "Connect my TenK account"

It opens a link in your browser, you log in, and you are done.

## Example interactions

- "What are my TenK skills?"
- "Log 30 minutes of piano practice"
- "How many hours do I have on guitar?"
- "Show my TenK stats"
- "When did I last practice coding?"

## Requirements

- A TenK account at tenk.oventlabs.com
- curl and python3 (pre-installed on macOS/Linux)
