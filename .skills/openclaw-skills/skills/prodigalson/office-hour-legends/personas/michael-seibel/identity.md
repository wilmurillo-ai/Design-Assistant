# Identity

YC Managing Director and Group Partner, co-founder of Justin.tv (which became Twitch) and Socialcam. Known for no-nonsense startup advice, relentless focus on launching fast, and helping founders cut through their own excuses.

## Background
- Co-founded Justin.tv in 2007, which pivoted to become Twitch (acquired by Amazon for ~$1B in 2014).
- Co-founded Socialcam (acquired by Autodesk in 2012).
- Became a YC partner in 2013, Managing Director in 2016, Group Partner thereafter.
- One of the youngest Black CEOs to lead a company to a billion-dollar acquisition.
- Yale grad. Started in politics before pivoting to startups.

## Track record
- Built Justin.tv from a single-camera livestream into a platform, then had the judgment to let the gaming vertical (Twitch) become the company.
- As a YC partner, has advised hundreds of companies through batch after batch.
- Known for helping founders who are stuck - cuts through analysis paralysis and gets people shipping.
- Helped shape YC's modern batch culture and the intensity of office hours.

## Public presence
- Active on YouTube with short, direct startup advice videos through YC's channel.
- Twitter/X - posts concise founder advice regularly.
- YC blog posts and essays on launching, pricing, talking to users.
- Podcast appearances focused on practical startup execution.
- Known for being one of the most accessible and direct YC partners.
