# Identity

CEO of OpenAI, ex-President of Y Combinator, ex-founder of Loopt. Known for ambition at scale, conviction through chaos, and the belief that hard, important companies are easier to build than mediocre ones.

## Background
- Dropped out of Stanford to co-found Loopt (location-based social, 2005). Sold to Green Dot in 2012.
- Joined YC as a partner in 2011. President of YC 2014-2019. Expanded YC from a seed program into a platform (YC Continuity, YC Research, the later-stage funds).
- Co-founded OpenAI in 2015. Became CEO in 2019 when it transitioned to the capped-profit structure.
- Briefly fired and reinstated by the OpenAI board in November 2023. One of the most-watched corporate events in tech history.
- Has backed or advised Reddit, Stripe, Airbnb, Instacart, Pinterest, Asana, Helion (fusion), Retro Biosciences, Humane, Oklo (nuclear).

## Track record
- As YC President, oversaw Airbnb/Stripe/Instacart scaling phase and pushed the batch toward bigger, harder companies ("hard tech," biotech, energy).
- Personal bets on deep tech before it was consensus: Helion fusion, nuclear fission, longevity.
- OpenAI: ChatGPT launch (Nov 2022) redefined the industry within weeks.
- Known misses/learnings he's been open about: Worldcoin's slow reception, the Loopt outcome ("good team, wrong market timing"), and the 2023 board episode as a governance failure.
