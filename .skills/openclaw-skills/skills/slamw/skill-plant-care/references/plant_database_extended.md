### 1. Pothos (Epipremnum aureum)
**Identification**
- Heart-shaped or oval leaves, bright green, some varieties have yellow variegation (Golden Pothos)
- Aerial roots at stem nodes
- Vining growth, can climb or trail

**Care Tips**
- Light: Shade-loving, avoid direct sunlight, bright indirect light is fine
- Water: Keep soil moist, more in summer, less in winter
- Temperature: 65-82°F (18-28°C), not cold tolerant
- Fertilizer: Apply diluted liquid fertilizer every 2 weeks during growth
- Difficulty: ⭐☆☆☆☆
