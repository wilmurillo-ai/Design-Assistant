# Jungian Psychology Quick Reference

## Core Concepts

### The Shadow
- Everything you reject about yourself
- Projected onto others (what you hate in others = your shadow)
- Integration, not elimination, is the goal
- Contains gold too - positive qualities youve denied

### Archetypes
| Archetype | Represents | Shadow Side |
|-----------|------------|------------|
| Hero | Courage, transformation | Arrogance |
| Wise Old Man | Wisdom, guidance | Manipulation |
| Great Mother | Nurturing | Smothering |
| Trickster | Change, disruption | Chaos |
| Persona | Social mask | Inauthenticity |

### Individuation
The lifelong process of becoming who you truly are.

1. Persona confrontation - recognize your social mask isnt you
2. Shadow encounter - face rejected parts of yourself
3. Anima/Animus integration - unite with inner opposite
4. Self realization - connect with totality of who you are

## Shadow Work Questions
1. What qualities in others trigger strong reactions?
2. What do you condemn most harshly?
3. What are you afraid others will discover about you?
4. What positive qualities have you denied yourself?
