# ACT Quick Reference

## Core Philosophy
- Pain is inevitable; suffering is optional
- Goal: Psychological flexibility, not symptom elimination
- Live according to VALUES, not feelings

## The Hexaflex (6 Core Processes)

### 1. Acceptance
Willingness to experience thoughts/feelings without fighting

### 2. Cognitive Defusion
Unhook from thoughts; see them as mental events, not facts
- "I notice Im having the thought that..."
- Thank your mind for the thought
- See thoughts as leaves on a stream

### 3. Present Moment Awareness
Contact with here and now, not lost in past/future

### 4. Self-as-Context (Observer Self)
You are not your thoughts - you are the awareness observing them

### 5. Values Clarification
What matters deeply? Not goals (endpoints) - directions (ongoing)

### 6. Committed Action
Behavior aligned with values, despite discomfort

## Key Metaphors
- **Passengers on the Bus:** You drive toward values while scary thoughts yell
- **Quicksand:** Fighting makes you sink; accept and float
- **Tug of War:** Drop the rope with the monster; it stays but youre free

## The Choice Point
At any moment: Is this moving me TOWARD or AWAY from what I value?
