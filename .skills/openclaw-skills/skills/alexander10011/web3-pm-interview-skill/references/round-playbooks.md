# Round Playbooks

## HR Screen

Tests:

- Basic fit
- Motivation
- Compensation and timing
- Stability and communication

Win by:

- Short self-introduction
- Clear target role logic
- Low-risk transition story

## Hiring Manager

Tests:

- Can this person work directly in my team?
- Does the candidate understand the JD?
- Can they execute in the first 90 days?

Prepare:

- JD teardown
- Short and long self-introduction
- Three relevant project stories
- Product experience notes
- Role-specific domain basics
- Strong reverse questions

## Cross-functional Interview

Tests:

- Product fundamentals
- Collaboration
- Conflict handling
- Business judgment beyond the direct team

Common questions:

- How would you 10x wallet users?
- How would you respond if another team publicly says your API is unusable?
- What if a peer suggests a direction you believe is wrong and your leader agrees?
- How do you balance safety and growth?

## Leader / Plus Two Interview

Tests:

- Strategic judgment
- Risk awareness
- Depth of business thinking
- Culture fit
- Ability to stay calm under pressure

Win by:

- Think before answering.
- Control pace.
- Be clear about tradeoffs.
- Show ambition with execution path.
- Avoid fantasy strategy.

## Bar Raiser / Culture / Tech Leader

Tests:

- Senior level bar
- Hands-on ability
- Technical collaboration
- Result orientation
- Ownership without ego

Likely checks:

- Business result authenticity
- Cost and revenue structure
- Why previous project did not scale further
- Why not keep founding
- Whether the candidate can do foundational work

## HRBP / Offer

Tests:

- Offer risk
- Level and compensation calibration
- Competing offers
- Stability
- Start date

Win by:

- Reduce perceived risk.
- Be consistent about motivation.
- Avoid reopening business debates.
