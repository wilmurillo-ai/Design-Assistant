# Interviewer Research

## Goal

Use public information to infer interviewer focus and tune preparation.

## Inputs

- Interviewer role
- Public LinkedIn
- Public X / Twitter
- Public talks, interviews, podcasts, articles
- Public GitHub or docs, if relevant
- HR-provided role context

## Output

- Likely focus areas
- Interview style hypothesis
- Questions they may ask
- How direct or cautious to be
- What to avoid
- Best reverse questions

## Privacy Boundary

Use only public information. Do not expose private contact details, personal life, non-public employment details, or sensitive personal inferences.

When creating public material, replace real names with roles:

- Direct manager
- Cross-functional lead
- Wallet business owner
- Tech leader
- Bar raiser
- HRBP
