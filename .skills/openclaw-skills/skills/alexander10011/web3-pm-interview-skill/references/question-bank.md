# Question Bank

Use questions selectively based on role, level, and interview stage. Do not dump the full bank unless the user asks.

## Universal

1. Introduce yourself.
2. Why this company?
3. Why this role?
4. Why now?
5. What is your strongest project?
6. What was your biggest business challenge?
7. What did you learn from a failed product or startup?
8. What would you do in your first 90 days?
9. What is your biggest weakness for this role?
10. What questions do you have for us?

## Wallet PM

1. How do you evaluate a wallet transaction flow?
2. What should a PM care about in an on-chain transaction?
3. How would you improve wallet activation?
4. How would you reduce wrong-chain or wrong-address mistakes?
5. How do you balance security and conversion?
6. How should wallet, DEX, Earn, and CEX flows connect?
7. What is the role of account abstraction in wallet UX?
8. How would you design authorization management?

## DeFi / On-chain Data PM

1. How do you evaluate whether a DeFi protocol should be integrated?
2. How do you define a DeFi position data model?
3. How would you ensure DeFi data accuracy?
4. What is the difference between APR and APY?
5. What is a health factor and why does it matter?
6. How would you explain impermanent loss to users?
7. Why do rebasing assets create data product challenges?
8. How would you define the north star metric for wallet DeFi?
9. If TVL grows but transaction count falls, how would you diagnose it?
10. How would you move from campaign-driven growth to platform capability?

## AI Wallet / Agentic Wallet

1. What can AI do in wallet products today?
2. What should not be automated by AI?
3. How do MCP and skills differ?
4. What is an Agentic Wallet?
5. How would you design permissions for an AI agent?
6. How do you prevent an agent from making unsafe transactions?
7. What is the first realistic AI Wallet use case?
8. How do you make AI useful without overpromising returns?

## Leadership And Organization

1. How do you handle conflict with a peer team?
2. What if your leader supports a direction you believe is wrong?
3. How do you prioritize when teams disagree?
4. How do you evaluate team performance?
5. How do you manage underperformers?
6. How do you build trust after joining a new org?
7. How do you balance short-term KPI and long-term platform work?

## Bar Raiser / Culture

1. Are your past business results real and repeatable?
2. Why did your startup not scale further?
3. Why return to a large organization?
4. Are you willing to do foundational work?
5. How do you work with engineers?
6. What does hands-on mean for a PM?
7. What would make you leave after six months?

## Answer Frame

Use this structure:

```text
Conclusion
Context
Framework
Example
Tradeoff
Execution path
Metric or validation
Risk control
```
