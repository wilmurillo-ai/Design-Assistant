## LinkedIn Post

I open-sourced a bilingual Web3 PM Interview Skill.

It is designed for product managers preparing for interviews in wallets, exchanges, DeFi, DEX, on-chain data, growth, AI Wallet, Agentic Wallet, Senior PM, Product Lead, and Product Director roles.

The problem it solves:

Most Web3 PM candidates do not fail because they know nothing about crypto. They fail because they cannot translate their experience into the target role's hiring model, cannot turn Web3 concepts into product judgment, and cannot present senior experience as a coherent interview mainline.

This repo includes:

- JD teardown framework
- Candidate-role fit diagnosis
- Interview narrative framework
- Round-specific playbooks
- Wallet / DeFi / on-chain data / AI Wallet references
- High-probability question bank
- Mock interview scoring rubric
- Case interview and 30/60/90 templates
- Privacy-safe anonymized examples

It supports five modes:

1. Quick JD diagnosis
2. Full interview battle plan
3. Mock interview review
4. Case / take-home prep
5. Post-interview debrief

The core idea:

> Help candidates answer like a role owner, not a job seeker.

Repo: [link]

Feedback from Web3 PMs, hiring managers, and recruiters is welcome.

## 中文发布文案

我开源了一套 **Web3 PM 面试作战系统（AI Skill）**。

它不是教你“Web3 是什么”，而是解决一个很现实的问题：

很多 Web3 PM 候选人懂 Crypto，但不会把自己的经历翻译成面试官愿意买单的能力。

这套 Skill 面向正在准备 Web3 产品经理面试的人，尤其适用于：

- Wallet
- Exchange
- DeFi
- DEX
- On-chain Data
- Growth
- AI Wallet
- Agentic Wallet
- Senior PM / Product Lead / Product Director

无论是 Web2 转行，还是 Web3 内部转岗，你只需要把简历和 JD 喂给 AI，它就能帮你：

1. 看透 JD 背后的真实岗位模型
2. 提炼属于你的面试主线和差异化卖点
3. 按轮次准备业务面、交叉面和终面
4. 生成 Mock 打分和高频反问
5. 输出 Case / 30-60-90 天计划框架

里面包括：

- JD 拆解框架
- 候选人匹配矩阵
- 面试主线框架
- 轮次作战手册
- 钱包 / DeFi / 链上数据 / AI Wallet 参考资料
- 高频问题库
- Mock 面试评分表
- Case 和 30/60/90 模板
- 脱敏案例和不同背景候选人的转岗样例

支持 ChatGPT / Claude / Cursor / Codex 开箱即用。

Repo: [link]

欢迎 Web3 PM、面试官、招聘方、候选人提 Issue 或 PR。
