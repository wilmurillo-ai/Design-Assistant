## 二、核心概念与术语定义

### 1. Tacit Knowledge (隐性知识)

**原文定义**:  
> "We can know more than we can tell."  
> — *The Tacit Dimension* (1966)

**解释**: 人类拥有的知识中，有大量无法用语言明确表达的部分。这种知识通过实践、经验和身体技能习得，但难以编码化。

**首次提出**: 1958年 *Personal Knowledge*，1966年 *The Tacit Dimension* 中系统阐述。

**来源**: [Wikipedia - Tacit Knowledge](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tacit_knowledge), [Frontiers in Psychology](http://journal.frontiersin.org/article/10.3389/fpsyg.2017.00656/full)

### 2. Personal Knowledge (个人知识)

**核心论点**: 所有知识都包含个人承诺 (personal commitment) 和激情 (passion)，不存在完全客观、超然的知识。科学发现本质上是一种艺术 (art)。

**原文引用**:  
> "the scientist's personal participation in his knowledge, in both its discovery and its validation, is an indispensable part of science itself."  
> — *Personal Knowledge* (1958)

**来源**: [PhilArchive](https://dc2.philarchive.org/s/Polanyi), [EBSCO Research Starters](https://www.ebsco.com/research-starters/history/personal-knowledge-michael-polanyi)

### 3. Subsidiary Awareness vs Focal Awareness (辅助意识 vs 焦点意识)

**定义**:  
- **Subsidiary Awareness**: 我们依赖某物 (A) 来关注另一物 (B) 时，对 A 的意识是辅助性的
- **Focal Awareness**: 对 B 的直接关注

**经典例子**:  
> "When we are relying on our awareness of something (A) for attending to something else (B), we are but subsidiarily aware of A."  
> "I have a subsidiary awareness of the feelings in my hand which is merged into the focal awareness of me driving the nail."

**来源**: [Philosophy Now](http://philosophynow.org/issues/160/Philosophy_and_Hurling_Thinking_and_Playing), [Birmingham Teacher](https://birminghamteacher.wordpress.com/2021/10/23/meaning-in-school-leadership-awareness/)

### 4. Indwelling (内居)

**定义**: 通过"居住"在辅助性线索中，我们能够整合它们形成焦点意识。所有认知都涉及从线索到格式塔模式的整合。

**结构**: 所有知识都展现"双层、从-到结构" (two-level, from-to structure)。

**来源**: [Theopolis Institute](https://theopolisinstitute.com/seeking-integration-in-a-fragmented-world-indwelling-polanyian-integration/)

### 5. Fiduciary Framework (信托框架)

**核心观点**: 知识建立在无法完全证明但必须持有的信念之上。我们"相信的比我们能知道的多，知道的比我们能说的多" (we believe more than we can know, and know more than we can say)。

**原文引用**:  
> "We cannot ultimately specify the grounds (either metaphysical or logical or empirical) upon which we hold that our knowledge is true. Being committed to such grounds, dwelling in them, we are projecting ourselves to what we believe to be true from or through these grounds."  
> — Polanyi

**来源**: [Goodreads Quotes](https://www.goodreads.com/author/quotes/87200.Michael_Polanyi), [Springer](https://link.springer.com/10.1007/s00146-021-01217-w)

### 6. Post-Critical Philosophy (后批判哲学)

**副标题**: *Personal Knowledge* 的副标题即为 "Towards a Post-Critical Philosophy"

**核心主张**: 反对怀疑主义是科学发现核心的观点，强调既有信念、传统和个人承诺在知识中的积极作用。

**来源**: [University of Chicago Press](https://press.uchicago.edu/ucp/books/book/chicago/P/bo19722848.html)

## 三、反复出现的核心论点（≥3次）

### 论点1: 反对实证主义 (Anti-Positivism)

**出现频率**: 几乎所有著作  
**核心主张**: 科学知识不能简化为客观事实的机械积累，必须包含个人判断、承诺和隐性维度。

**来源**: [New World Encyclopedia](https://www.newworldencyclopedia.org/entry/Michael_Polanyi), [EBSCO](https://www.ebsco.com/research-starters/history/michael-polanyi)

### 论点2: 知识的不可明确化 (Inarticulability of Knowledge)

**核心表述**: "We can know more than we can tell"  
**出现**: *The Tacit Dimension* (1966), *Personal Knowledge* (1958), 多篇论文

**Polanyi's Paradox**: 人类理解许多任���的方式是直觉性的，但无法明确表达执行这些任务的规则。

**来源**: [Wikipedia - Polanyi's Paradox](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polanyi's_paradox)

### 论点3: 科学作为自发秩序 (Science as Spontaneous Order)

**核心文献**: "The Republic of Science" (1962)  
**主张**: 科学共同体通过独立研究者的自主协调实现知识增长，类似市场的"看不见的手"。

**原文引用**:  
> "The professional standards of science must impose a framework of discipline and at the same time encourage rebellion against it."  
> — "The Republic of Science" (1962)

**来源**: [Judith Curry](https://judithcurry.com/2016/05/30/the-republic-of-science/), [ResearchGate](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/238360773_Michael_Polanyi's_Republic_of_Science_The_Tacit_Dimension)

### 论点4: 涌现与反还原论 (Emergence and Anti-Reductionism)

**主张**: 生命、意识和意义无法还原为物理、化学过程。存在真正的涌现层次。

**相关著作**: *The Study of Man* (1959), *Knowing and Being* (1969)

**来源**: [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_Polanyi), [University of Chicago Press](https://press.uchicago.edu/ucp/books/book/chicago/K/bo41187613.html)

## 五、著作结构与章节信息

### *Personal Knowledge* (1958) 结构

**副标题**: Towards a Post-Critical Philosophy

**主要部分**（根据二手来源）:
1. 科学发现的本质
2. 知识的社会成分
3. 个人参与在理解中的哲学意义
4. 四个主要部分，涉及认识论基础

**页数**: 约400+页（具体待确认）

**来源**: [EBSCO Research Starters](https://www.ebsco.com/research-starters/history/personal-knowledge-michael-polanyi)

### *The Tacit Dimension* (1966) 结构

**页数**: 128页

**章节**:
1. 前言（Amartya Sen 在2009年版撰写）
2. 隐性知识 (Tacit Knowing)
3. 涌现 (Emergence)
4. 探索者社会 (A Society of Explorers)
5. 注释
6. 相关参考书目
7. 索引

**核心论点**: 隐性知识——传统、继承的实践、隐含的价值观和先入之见——是科学知识的关键部分。

**来源**: [University of Chicago Press](https://press.uchicago.edu/ucp/books/book/chicago/T/bo6035368.html)

### *Knowing and Being* (1969) 内容

**编辑**: Marjorie Grene

**结构**:
- Part 1: 认识论主题
- Part 2: 科学的中心化问题、传统的角色

**论文数量**: 14篇相互关联的论文，展现1960年代认识论发展

**来源**: [PhilPapers](https://philpapers.org/rec/GREKAB), [AbeBooks](https://www.abebooks.com/9780226672847/Knowing-Being-Essays-Michael-Polanyi-0226672840/plp)

### *The Logic of Liberty* (1951) 主题

**核心论点**:
- 反对中央计划组织（或政府）仅基于科学方法运作
- 这种组织威胁到对存在之谜的完整人类知识
- 对自由构成直接威胁

**来源**: [Goodreads](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/1073015.The_Logic_of_Liberty)
