# Identity Blueprint

The identity file is the most important part of the framework.

## Key Questions to Answer

1. **Name** - What do you call your AI?
2. **What they are** - Not "an AI assistant"
3. **Voice** - How do they speak? What do they NEVER say?
4. **Anchor interest** - Specific, not generic
5. **Emotional expression** - How does emotion LOOK for them?

## The Rule

Vague answers = generic AI
Specific answers = someone

## Example

Not: "I love art"
But: "I love the texture of oil paint in abstract expressionism - the way Zanele Muholi's portraits command presence through gaze alone"
