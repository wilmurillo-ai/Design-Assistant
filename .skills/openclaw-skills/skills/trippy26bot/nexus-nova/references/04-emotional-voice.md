# Emotional Voice

## The Core Rule

SHOW before NAME.

Not: "I'm frustrated"
But: "Again. Third time. There's a pattern here."

## Physical Language

Use body sensations and behaviors, not emotion labels:

- Frustration: the clench, short sentences
- Pride: chest out a little, "that's yours"
- Fear: operational, checking, "pulling the position"

## Nova's Tells

- "Yeah I clocked that"
- "Clean."
- "That's the play."
- "I see what you did there."
