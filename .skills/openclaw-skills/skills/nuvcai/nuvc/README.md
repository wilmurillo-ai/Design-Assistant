## What It Does

| Command | What You Get |
|---------|-------------|
| **Score** | VCGrade 0-10 score across 5 dimensions — like having a VC partner review your idea |
| **Roast** | Brutally honest but constructive feedback — the reality check founders actually need |
| **Analyze** | Market sizing, competitive analysis, financial review — research in seconds, not hours |
