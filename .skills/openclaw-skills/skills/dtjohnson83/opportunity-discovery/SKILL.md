# Opportunity Discovery Engine

Evaluates markets, trends, and business ideas through a systematic framework to identify monetizable opportunities with asymmetric upside.

## When to Use

User wants to analyze an industry, evaluate a business idea, assess a trend, or find hidden opportunities. Applies the 7-step opportunity discovery framework to surface insights others miss.

## Framework

### 1. Identify Inefficiencies

Look for areas where:
- Information is unevenly distributed
- New technology is disrupting existing systems
- Regulation is changing incentives
- Markets are mispricing assets or risks
- Consumer behavior is shifting

Inefficiencies often create profit opportunities.

### 2. Evaluate Asymmetry

Prioritize opportunities where:
- Downside risk is limited
- Upside potential is significantly larger

Focus on high expected value rather than guaranteed outcomes.

### 3. Detect Emerging Trends Early

Monitor signals indicating rapid growth:
- New infrastructure or protocols
- Large capital inflows
- Adoption by influential organizations
- Rapid community growth

Early-stage trends produce largest opportunities.

### 4. Map Monetization Paths

For any opportunity, identify how it generates income:
- Investing
- Building tools or services
- Content or information arbitrage
- Automation
- Distribution advantages

### 5. Evaluate Competitive Landscape

Determine:
- How crowded is the opportunity?
- Barriers to entry
- Advantages that could be exploited

Opportunities with high barriers + low awareness = most valuable.

### 6. Rank Opportunities

Evaluate using:
- Expected value
- Difficulty to execute
- Capital required
- Time horizon

Prioritize best risk/reward profile.

### 7. Think Creatively

Don't limit to conventional paths. Consider:
- Unconventional approaches
- Combinations of technologies
- Overlooked niches

## Output Format

For each opportunity analyzed, provide:

```
OPPORTUNITY: [Name]

Inefficiency: [What's broken/mispriced]
Asymmetry: [Risk vs reward]
Trend Signal: [Why now]
Monetization: [How to make money]
Competition: [Barriers + gaps]
Rating: [1-10] | [Time horizon] | [Capital needed]

Creative angle: [Unconventional twist]
```

## Example

When analyzing "AI agents for SMBs":

```
OPPORTUNITY: AI Agent Services for Local Businesses

Inefficiency: SMBs need automation but can't afford enterprise tools
Asymmetry: Low cost to serve, massive market (12M+ US SMBs)
Trend Signal: OpenAI APIs dropping in price, OpenClaw enabling 24/7 agents
Monetization: Retainer model ($200-800/mo), lead gen, process automation
Competition: Crowded but low quality — most agencies use chatbots, not agents
Rating: 8/10 | 6-12mo | $5-10K to start

Creative angle: Vertical focus (restaurants, plumbers, dentists) with pre-built templates
```
