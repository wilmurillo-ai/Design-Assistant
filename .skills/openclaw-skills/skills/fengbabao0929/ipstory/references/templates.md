# Personal Brand Story Templates

Ready-to-use story templates organized by industry and story type. Fill in the bracketed sections with your specific details.

## Template A: Corporate Escape (Corporate → Entrepreneur)

**Best for:** Former employees who left stable jobs to build businesses

```
# [Title: From [Old Position] to [New Achievement], I Found What Really Matters]

You know that feeling - [describe the false security: steady paycheck, predictable routine, everyone thinks you've "made it"]?

That was me [number] years ago. I was [position] at [company type], earning [specific salary], with [perks/benefits]. From the outside, life looked perfect.

## The Crack in the Facade

But beneath the surface, [describe the pain: burnout, lack of purpose, missing family moments, feeling like a cog in the machine]

I remember [specific moment: sitting at desk at 2am / missing child's event / staring at ceiling unable to sleep]. Something inside me whispered: "Is this it? Is this all there is?"

## The Turning Point

[Date/Event], everything changed.

[Describe the catalyst: health scare, layoff, parent's illness, mentor's death, book that changed perspective]

For me, it was [specific event]. [Describe vividly - what happened, who was there, what was said/felt]

That day I realized: [the epiphany - life is short / security is an illusion / I was building someone else's dream]

## The Leap into Unknown

Leaving felt [emotion: terrifying / exhilarating / impossible]. [People said: "You're crazy" / "What about your career?" / "You'll be back"]

[Describe specific obstacles: first 6 months with no income / doubting yourself daily / family's concern / failed first attempt]

There were nights I [specific low point: almost gave up / questioned everything / cried in the shower]

But I held onto [one thing: a vision / a promise / a belief]

## The Breakthrough

Then [describe the turning moment: first big client / product launch / viral moment]

I discovered [core insight: the truth that changed everything]

[Philosophy statement - something quotable]:
"[Your memorable line about what you learned]"

## The Transformation

Fast forward to today:

- [Specific achievement 1 with numbers]
- [Specific achievement 2 with numbers]
- [Client/student count or revenue]
- [Before/after comparison: went from X to Y]

But more than the numbers, what matters is [deeper impact: time freedom / helping others / building legacy]

Now I help [target audience] achieve [specific outcome]. Because I believe [closing philosophy statement]

If you're ready to [call to action], let's connect.

[Your name]
[Your tagline]
```

## Philosophy Statement Generator

Use these patterns to create memorable quotable lines:

**Contrast Patterns:**
- "Most people X, but I learned Y"
- "The world says Z, but I discovered [opposite]"
- "I used to think [old belief], now I know [new truth]"

**Truth Bombs:**
- "[Obvious-sounding truth] that [experts/gurus] never talk about"
- "Everything changed when I realized [uncomfortable truth]"
- "The secret to [result] isn't [common answer], it's [unexpected answer]"

**Action-Oriented:**
- "Don't [common advice]. Instead, [your advice]."
- "Stop [thing people do]. Start [thing they should do]."
- "[Result] doesn't come from [source], it comes from [real source]"

**Identity-Focused:**
- "I'm not [common label], I'm [your distinction]"
- "[Your field] isn't about [surface thing], it's about [deeper thing]"
- "The difference between [mediocre] and [exceptional] is [one thing]"
