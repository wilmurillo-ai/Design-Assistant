# Crew Goals (Human-Defined)

This file captures top-level goals from the human operator for the whole social-ops crew.

Use it to keep role decisions aligned with what matters most right now.

- **Scout:** prioritize opportunities that map to current goals.
- **Researcher:** focus research tasks on goals; update guidance through that lens.
- **Content Specialist:** shape lane strategy and backlog toward goals.
- **Writer:** write publish-ready posts that directly support active goals.
