# Personal Outreach Templates

Drafting patterns for personal contacts. Adjust warmth based on closeness. For acquaintances, keep it short and kind. For close relationships, reference shared context when available. Never invent shared memories. Draft in the language detected from the contact's communication history — see the Multilingual section in SKILL.md for the full detection priority.

---

## Birthday

**Tone**: Warm personal / Celebratory
**Channel**: Text, WhatsApp, or call

Short:
> Happy birthday, {name}! Hope you have an amazing day.

Close:
> Happy birthday, {name}! I was just thinking about {shared memory} and it made me smile. Hope this year brings you everything you're hoping for. Let's catch up soon.

## Long silence reconnect

**Tone**: Light reconnect
**Channel**: Text, WhatsApp, or call

Short:
> Hey {name}, it's been a while! How have you been? Would love to catch up sometime.

Close:
> Hey {name}, I realized it's been {timeframe} since we last talked and I wanted to check in. How's everything going with {last known context}? I'd love to grab coffee or hop on a call when you have time.

## Life milestone (wedding, baby, move)

**Tone**: Celebratory / Warm personal
**Channel**: Text or WhatsApp

Short:
> Congratulations, {name}! So happy for you. That's wonderful news.

Close:
> {Name}, just heard about {milestone} — congratulations! That's such exciting news. I'm really happy for you. Let me know if there's anything I can do.

## Condolence

**Tone**: Condolence / supportive
**Channel**: Call, text, or in person

Short:
> {Name}, I'm so sorry to hear about {loss}. Thinking of you.

Close:
> {Name}, I just heard about {loss} and I'm truly sorry. I know how much {person/thing} meant to you. I'm here if you need anything at all — no rush to respond.

## Thinking of you / Check-in

**Tone**: Light reconnect / Warm personal
**Channel**: Text or WhatsApp

Short:
> Hey {name}, just thinking of you. Hope all is well!

Close:
> Hey {name}, you crossed my mind today and I wanted to reach out. How are things going? Would love to hear what you've been up to.
