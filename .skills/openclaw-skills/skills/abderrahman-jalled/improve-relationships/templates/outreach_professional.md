# Professional Outreach Templates

Drafting patterns for work contacts. Default channel: email or LinkedIn. No emojis unless the relationship is casual. Only state facts you can confirm from shared context. Never guess titles or companies. Draft in the language detected from the contact's communication history — see the Multilingual section in SKILL.md for the full detection priority.

---

## Promotion / New role

**Tone**: Respectful professional / Celebratory
**Channel**: LinkedIn message or email

Short:
> Congratulations on the new role, {name}! Well deserved.

Close:
> Hi {name}, I just saw that you've moved into {role} at {company} — congratulations! I've always admired {specific quality or collaboration reference}, and I'm sure you'll do great things. Would love to catch up and hear more.

## Job change

**Tone**: Respectful professional
**Channel**: LinkedIn message or email

Short:
> {Name}, congrats on joining {company}! Exciting move. Wishing you the best.

Close:
> Hi {name}, saw that you've joined {company} — that's a great move. I hope the transition is going smoothly. I'd love to reconnect and hear about what you're working on.

## Professional reconnect (long silence)

**Tone**: Light reconnect / Respectful professional
**Channel**: Email

Short:
> Hi {name}, it's been a while since we connected. Hope things are going well at {company}. Would love to catch up.

Close:
> Hi {name}, I was thinking about {shared project or event} and realized we haven't connected in a while. Would you be open to a quick call or coffee?

## Public accomplishment (award, publication, talk)

**Tone**: Celebratory / Respectful professional
**Channel**: LinkedIn or email

Short:
> {Name}, just saw {accomplishment} — impressive work. Congratulations!

Close:
> Hi {name}, I came across {accomplishment} and wanted to say congratulations. {Why it's impressive}. Really great to see.

## Company news (funding, launch, milestone)

**Tone**: Respectful professional
**Channel**: Email or LinkedIn

Short:
> {Name}, saw the news about {company event}. Congrats to you and the team!

Close:
> Hi {name}, just read about {company event} — that's a significant milestone. I know how much work goes into something like that. Congratulations.

## End-of-year / Professional holiday

**Tone**: Respectful professional
**Channel**: Email

Short:
> Happy holidays, {name}. Wishing you a restful break and a strong year ahead.

Close:
> Hi {name}, as the year wraps up I wanted to say it's been great staying connected. Wishing you a restful holiday season and an excellent {next year}. Let's catch up in the new year.
