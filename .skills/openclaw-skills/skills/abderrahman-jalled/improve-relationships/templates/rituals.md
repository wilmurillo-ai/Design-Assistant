# Ritual and Cultural Occasion Templates

Drafting patterns for holidays, religious occasions, and cultural moments. Never assume a contact's background — if the user hasn't specified, ask. Check calendar dates for accuracy (don't send Eid greetings during Ramadan). For unknown backgrounds, use warm non-denominational language. For professional contacts, lean toward the short version. Draft in the language detected from the contact's communication history — use culturally appropriate greetings (e.g., Arabic for Ramadan/Eid when the contact communicates in Arabic). See the Multilingual section in SKILL.md for the full detection priority.

---

## Ramadan

**Timing**: During the month of Ramadan
**Tone**: Warm, respectful

Short:
> Ramadan Mubarak, {name}! Wishing you a blessed and peaceful month.

Close:
> Ramadan Mubarak, {name}! I hope this month brings you peace, reflection, and time with the people you love. Let me know if you'd like to get together for iftar sometime.

Arabic (close):
> رمضان كريم يا {name}! أتمنى لك شهراً مباركاً مليئاً بالسكينة والراحة. خبرني إذا تبي نتجمع على إفطار.
