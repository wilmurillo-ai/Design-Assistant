# Ad Video Creator

> Generate scroll-stopping advertisement videos from product photos, brand assets, or existing footage — with AI-written hooks, platform-optimized captions, and conversion-focused CTAs. Produce ad creatives for TikTok Ads, Meta Ads, Google Ads, and Xiaohongshu in minutes, not days.

## What This Skill Does

Ad Video Creator turns your product assets into performance-ready video advertisements. It combines AI video generation, copywriting, and platform ad specs knowledge to produce creatives that are ready to upload to ad platforms. Each ad includes attention-grabbing hooks, benefit-driven copy, and clear CTAs optimized for the target platform's algorithm.

### Core Capabilities

1. **Product-to-Ad Pipeline** — Upload product photos → get finished video ads with music, captions, and CTA
2. **Hook Generation** — AI writes multiple opening hooks based on proven ad frameworks (PAS, AIDA, storytelling)
3. **Multi-Format Output** — Same ad in multiple formats: 9:16 (Stories/Reels), 1:1 (Feed), 16:9 (YouTube pre-roll)
4. **A/B Variant Generation** — Produce 5-10 creative variants from the same assets for split testing
5. **Platform Ad Specs** — Auto-comply with TikTok Ads, Meta Ads, Google Ads creative requirements
6. **Conversion Copy** — CTA overlays, end cards, and swipe-up prompts designed for click-through

### Ad Frameworks

- **Problem-Agitate-Solve** — Hook with pain point → amplify → present product as solution
- **Before/After** — Show transformation with product
- **Social Proof** — UGC-style with testimonial overlays
- **Trending Format** — Adapt viral templates to your product
