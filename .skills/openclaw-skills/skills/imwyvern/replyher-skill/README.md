# ReplyHer
> AI communication coach — decode messages & craft high-EQ replies.

An [OpenClaw](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw) Skill that helps you understand the real meaning behind messages and respond with emotional intelligence. Covers dating, workplace, and social scenarios.

## Features

- **🔍 Message Decoding** — Paste a message you received, get 2 likely interpretations with subtext analysis
- **💬 Smart Replies** — Get 5 strategically different reply options tailored to your situation
- **🎯 Tactical Coaching** — Describe your situation, get actionable advice (not therapy-speak)
- **📊 Conversation Analysis** — Paste a chat thread, get a full dynamic breakdown

## Scenarios

| Scenario | What You Get |
|----------|-------------|
| 💕 Dating | Push-pull dynamics, confidence-first replies, stage-aware strategy (early/building/close) |
| 💼 Workplace | Conclusion-first communication, hierarchy-aware tone, deadline management |
| 👥 Social | Energy-matched responses — banter with friends, warmth with family |

## Quick Start

```bash
clawhub install replyher
```

Then just talk to your AI naturally:

- *"She said 'whatever', what does she mean?"*
- *"My boss asked me to finish this by tonight but I have other priorities"*
- *"How do I decline my friend's loan request without ruining the friendship?"*

The skill auto-detects your language and the scenario — no configuration needed.

## Language Support

ReplyHer automatically detects your language and responds accordingly:

- 🇨🇳 Chinese — optimized for WeChat/微信 texting culture
