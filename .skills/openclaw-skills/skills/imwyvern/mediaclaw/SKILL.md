# MediaClaw

> MediaClaw is an AI-powered video content factory and media production platform that transforms one source video into dozens of branded, localized, platform-ready clips. Capabilities include brand video replacement, face swap, AI subtitle overlay, voiceover replacement, video repurposing, batch video creation, ad creative generation, and social media content automation. Supports TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Douyin, and Xiaohongshu. Built for brands, MCN agencies, e-commerce sellers, and marketing teams who need video content at scale.

## What This Skill Does

MediaClaw is a full-stack AI video production pipeline. Give it a source video and brand assets, and it handles the entire workflow: analyzing scenes, replacing brand elements (logos, packaging, text), generating new captions with culturally-adapted copy, swapping voiceovers with natural TTS, and outputting platform-optimized clips for TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Xiaohongshu, and Douyin.

### Core Capabilities

1. **Brand Video Replacement** — Swap product packaging, logos, and brand elements in existing videos using AI inpainting (Gemini frame editing + Kling/Seedance i2v generation)
2. **Batch Video Production** — Turn 1 hero video into 15+ unique variants with different hooks, captions, color grades, and aspect ratios
3. **AI Subtitle Engine** — Generate scroll-stopping captions with blue-word strategy, emoji hooks, and platform-specific formatting (Pillow frame-by-frame rendering)
4. **Voiceover Replacement** — Separate BGM from voice (demucs), generate new VO with matched pacing (TTS), remix seamlessly
5. **Video Deduplication** — 6-layer anti-duplicate system (prompt mutation, color shift, speed variation, crop jitter, subtitle style rotation, fingerprint perturbation)
6. **Copywriting Engine** — Platform-native ad copy generation with trending hooks, CTAs, comment-bait phrases, and de-duplication against posting history
7. **Multi-Platform Export** — Auto-resize and format for each platform's specs (9:16, 1:1, 16:9) with platform-specific watermark placement
