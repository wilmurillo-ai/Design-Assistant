# Ecommerce Video AI

> Turn product photos and listings into engaging e-commerce video ads — product demos, unboxing simulations, before-after comparisons, and lifestyle showcases. Optimized for Shopify, Amazon, Taobao, Pinduoduo, and social commerce platforms.

## What This Skill Does

Ecommerce Video AI bridges the gap between static product listings and video-first commerce. Upload product images and descriptions, and it generates video content that drives conversions — from quick product showcases to detailed feature demos with benefit-driven captions and purchase CTAs.

### Core Capabilities

1. **Product Photo to Video** — Animate still product images into dynamic showcases with camera movement and lighting effects
2. **Listing-to-Script** — Extract key selling points from product listings and generate video scripts
3. **Demo Video Generation** — Simulate product usage, unboxing, and feature demonstrations
4. **Before/After Comparison** — Create transformation videos for beauty, fitness, home improvement products
5. **Lifestyle Integration** — Place products into lifestyle scenes (kitchen, gym, office, outdoor)
6. **Multi-Platform Commerce** — Format for Shopify product pages, Amazon A+ content, Taobao detail pages, Douyin shop, Xiaohongshu notes

### E-commerce Formats

- **Hero Video** — 15-30s product showcase for listing pages
- **Social Ad** — 9:16 scroll-stopper for paid social
- **Feature Spotlight** — Individual feature demos (3-5s each)
- **Testimonial Style** — UGC-format social proof videos
- **Campaign** — Seasonal/promotional videos (618, Double 11, Black Friday)

## Usage

- "Create a product demo video from these Amazon listing photos"
- "Generate 5 Douyin shop videos for our new skincare line"
- "Make a before/after comparison video for this cleaning product"
- "Produce Xiaohongshu-style product review videos from these images"
