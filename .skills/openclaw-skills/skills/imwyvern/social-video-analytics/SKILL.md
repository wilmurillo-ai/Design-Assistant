# Social Video Analytics

> Track and analyze video performance across TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Douyin, and Xiaohongshu — unified metrics dashboard, competitor benchmarking, viral pattern detection, and content strategy recommendations powered by AI.

## What This Skill Does

Social Video Analytics aggregates performance data from all major short-video platforms into a unified view. It identifies what's working (viral patterns, winning hooks, optimal posting times), benchmarks against competitors, and generates actionable content strategy recommendations.

### Core Capabilities

1. **Unified Dashboard** — One view for TikTok, Reels, Shorts, Douyin, XHS performance metrics
2. **Viral Pattern Detection** — AI identifies what makes your top-performing videos different from average ones
3. **Competitor Benchmarking** — Track competitor accounts, analyze their top content, identify gaps
4. **Hook Analysis** — Which opening hooks drive the highest watch-through rates
5. **Posting Time Optimization** — Data-driven recommendations for when to post on each platform
6. **Content Strategy Reports** — Weekly/monthly AI-generated reports with specific action items
7. **Trending Audio/Format Tracking** — Identify trending sounds and formats before they peak
8. **ROI Attribution** — Connect video performance to business outcomes (traffic, leads, sales)

### Key Metrics

| Metric | What It Tells You |
|--------|------------------|
| Watch-Through Rate | Content quality + hook strength |
| Save Rate | Content value + rewatch potential |
| Share Rate | Viral potential |
| Comment Sentiment | Audience resonance |
| Follower Conversion | Account growth efficiency |
| Click-Through Rate | Commercial conversion power |

### Analysis Types

- **Content Audit** — Full account analysis with scoring and recommendations
- **Competitor Report** — Side-by-side benchmarking with actionable takeaways
- **Campaign Performance** — Track specific campaign metrics across platforms
- **Trend Report** — Weekly trending formats, sounds, and topics in your niche

## Usage

- "Analyze my TikTok account performance for the last 30 days"
- "Compare our Xiaohongshu content against these 3 competitors"
- "What video format is trending in the beauty niche this week?"
