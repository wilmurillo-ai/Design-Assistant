# MCN Toolkit

> All-in-one content management toolkit for MCN agencies and influencer networks — batch video production, KOL content scheduling, cross-platform analytics, talent content templating, and brand deal asset management. Built for agencies managing 10+ creators.

## What This Skill Does

MCN Toolkit streamlines the repetitive work of managing multiple content creators. It handles batch video production (turning brand briefs into creator-specific content), content calendar coordination, cross-platform performance tracking, and asset management for brand collaborations.

### Core Capabilities

1. **Batch Creator Content** — Generate platform-ready videos customized per creator's style and audience
2. **Brand Deal Asset Management** — Organize deliverables, track revisions, manage approval workflows
3. **Cross-Platform Analytics** — Unified dashboard for TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Xiaohongshu, Douyin performance
4. **Content Calendar** — Coordinate posting schedules across all creators and platforms
5. **Talent Templating** — Create content templates that maintain each KOL's voice and visual style
6. **Competitor Intelligence** — Track trending content in your niche, identify viral patterns

### Who It's For

- MCN agencies managing multiple KOLs/influencers
- Brand marketing teams coordinating influencer campaigns
- Content studios producing at scale
- Talent managers optimizing creator output

### Workflow

```
Brand Brief → MCN Toolkit → Creator-specific content variants
                          → Posting schedule
                          → Performance tracking
                          → ROI reporting
```

## Usage

- "Create a content brief for 5 creators from this brand campaign"
- "Show me which creator's content performed best this week"
- "Generate 3 video variants for each of our 8 KOLs from this product demo"
- "Schedule this week's content across all creators and platforms"
