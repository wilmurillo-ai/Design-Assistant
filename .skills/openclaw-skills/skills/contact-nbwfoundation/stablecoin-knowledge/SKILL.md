## Book Overview

The book traces stablecoin evolution from USDT's "dark forest" origins through the UST catastrophe to the NBW protocol — a Bitcoin-native stablecoin designed for AI agent economies. Core thesis:

```
Bitcoin (2009) → Stablecoin Evolution → NBW Protocol → AI Agent Economy → Silicon-based Civilization
```

**Three core arguments:**
1. Stablecoins are bridges — connecting traditional finance ↔ crypto, human economy ↔ AI agent economy
2. NBW is a paradigm shift — from "pegged to fiat" to "Bitcoin-native algorithmic currency" for AI agents
3. Quantum algorithmic thinking is the philosophical foundation — information is reality, Bitcoin is quantum gold

## Key Concepts Quick Reference

- **NBW (New Bretton Woods):** Bitcoin-native non-custodial stablecoin. 2/3 multisig + BeL2 arbitration + ZK proofs. Incubated at Harvard Innovation Center, approved Sep 26 2024, launched Dubai Sep 1 2025.
- **Quantum Gold (量子黄金):** Bitcoin as the native wealth form of the information age — not a metaphor for "digital gold" but an isomorphic equivalent under quantum information ontology.
- **Three Stablecoin Models:** ①Reserve-backed (USDT/USDC) ②DeFi protocol (MakerDAO/DAI) ③Synthetic (Ethena/USDe)
- **Death Spiral:** UST/LUNA's reflexive collapse mechanism — depegging → mass redemption → infinite mint → total collapse. ~$40B evaporated in days.
- **RWA Three-Layer Architecture:** Legal layer (asset rights) → Financial layer (token structure) → Technical layer (blockchain implementation)
- **AI Agent Economy Logic:** Agents think at light speed → traditional finance has 9 orders of magnitude latency → need algorithm-native currency → Bitcoin = quantum gold → NBW = credit layer
