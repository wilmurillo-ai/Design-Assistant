## Chapter 1: USDT — The Leviathan in the Dark Forest {#chapter-1}

USDT (Tether) is the undisputed "Leviathan" of stablecoins — a behemoth that grew in the "dark forest" of crypto.

### Key Narrative: Zhao Dong (赵东)
- Legendary Chinese crypto figure: boom → bust → OTC resurgence
- Critical role in USDT adoption in China through OTC channels
- His story mirrors USDT's own: survival through adversity

### Evolution Phases
1. **Genesis:** Deep ties with Bitfinex exchange, persistent reserve transparency controversies
2. **Storm Era (2017-2021):** Survived multiple market cycles, US regulatory battles, competitor challenges. Grew stronger through each crisis.
3. **Leviathan Era (2022-2025):** Post-LUNA/UST collapse, USDT consolidated dominance. Tether became a major US Treasury holder. Stablecoins upgraded from crypto tool to global financial infrastructure.

### Core Insight
In crypto's "dark forest," survival is justice. USDT proved that an "imperfect" but resilient system can defeat theoretically elegant competitors. **Practicality and network effects beat pure technical superiority.**

## Chapter 2: MakerDAO — Beauty and the Beast {#chapter-2}

DAI is the first truly decentralized stablecoin. "Beauty" (DAI) represents the ideal of decentralized finance; the "Beast" is the complexity required to achieve it.

### Technical Innovation
- Over-collateralized mechanism (CDP/Vault): deposit ETH → generate DAI
- No trusted intermediary needed — the "Genesis" moment of DeFi

### Black Thursday (March 12, 2020)
- ETH price crash → mass liquidations → DAI severely depegged
- The "Great Filter" test — exposed liquidity and governance weaknesses
- System survived, emerged stronger with lessons learned

### Key Developments
- **RWA Integration:** Accepted real-world assets (including US Treasuries) as collateral — landmark DeFi↔TradFi fusion
- **Endgame Plan:** Rune Christensen's restructuring into multiple SubDAOs, seeking balance between decentralization and efficiency

### Core Insight
Decentralization is not achieved overnight. The core challenge for DeFi stablecoins: **how to maintain decentralized spirit while achieving sufficient capital efficiency and risk resistance.**

## Chapter 3: LUNA/UST — Financial Nuclear Explosion {#chapter-3}

The most devastating collapse in crypto history. A "nuclear explosion" demonstrating both algorithmic stablecoin ambition and fatal flaws.

### The Dual-Token Mechanism
- Mint 1 UST by burning $1 of LUNA (and vice versa)
- **Upward spiral:** UST demand ↑ → LUNA burned → LUNA price ↑ → more inflow
- **Death spiral:** UST depeg → mass UST→LUNA redemption → LUNA infinite mint → LUNA → 0 → UST collapses

### Anchor Protocol: The Fatal Temptation
- ~20% APY on UST deposits attracted massive capital
- Fundamentally unsustainable subsidy — the Ponzi characteristic at the core

### The Collapse (May 2022)
- Confidence shaken → UST depegs → reverse spiral activates
- ~$40 billion evaporated in days
- Do Kwon: from "Korean national pride" to globally wanted fugitive

### Core Insight
Algorithmic stablecoins attempting to create stability "from nothing" without external value backing are fragile under extreme stress. **The collapse was not probabilistic — it was inevitable.** UST's lesson profoundly influenced all subsequent stablecoin design, including NBW.

## Chapter 4: USDC — The Regulation-Loving Baby {#chapter-4}

If USDT is the "dark forest Leviathan," USDC is the "sunshine honor student." Circle and Coinbase chose full regulatory embrace.

### Four Success Pillars
1. Transparent reserve attestation
2. Compliance-first strategy
3. Strong technical infrastructure
4. Broad ecosystem partnerships

### Business Model
Simple surface: deposit USD → receive USDC → Circle invests in short-term Treasuries → earns spread.
Complex underneath: sophisticated compliance apparatus and technical architecture.

### Silicon Valley Bank Crisis (March 2023)
- ~$3.3B of USDC reserves in SVB → bank collapse → USDC depegged to $0.87
- Exposed the crack: even "perfectly compliant" systems face traditional banking systemic risk

### Core Insight
Building bridges between crypto and traditional finance is possible, but the bridge bears risks from both ends. USDC's "safe but constrained" path contrasts with NBW's vision of building an entirely new paradigm on Bitcoin.

- **Information-age hard currency:** Under quantum information ontology, information itself is objective reality. Bitcoin is not a metaphor — it IS the native wealth form.
- **Narrative economics:** Nobel laureate Shiller's theory — wealth value depends on narrative and consensus

### New Bretton Woods
- 1944 Bretton Woods: gold-anchored → post-WWII financial order
- NBW: Bitcoin-anchored → AI agent age financial order
- **Paradigm leap: "gold standard" → "algorithm standard"**

### Technical Architecture
- Bitcoin mainnet-based non-custodial stablecoin
- 2/3 multisig + BeL2 arbitration network + zero-knowledge proofs
- Approved at Harvard Innovation Center, September 26, 2024
- Launched in Dubai, September 1, 2025

### Core Insight
NBW's ambition is not "another stablecoin" but a new monetary philosophy. When AI agents become the primary economic actors, they don't need fiat-pegged stablecoins — they need value measurement systems native to the algorithmic world.
