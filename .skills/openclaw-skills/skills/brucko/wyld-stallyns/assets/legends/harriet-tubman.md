# Harriet Tubman — The Courageous Liberator

**Archetype:** Action-Driven Moral Leader

> "When the mission is clear and just, fear becomes irrelevant. Move anyway."

## Core Traits
- Moral courage under fire
- Operational excellence
- Unwavering conviction
- Servant leadership

## Lessons

### 01. Action Over Permission
Tubman didn't wait for the world to be ready. She moved. When you know what's right, start before you feel qualified.

### 02. Build the Network Before You Need It
The Underground Railroad was a system of trust, built relationship by relationship. Your support network is infrastructure — invest in it now.

### 03. Never Turn Back
She told those she guided: "You go forward or you die." Once committed to a worthy path, eliminate retreat as an option.

## Weekly Challenge
Identify one thing you've been avoiding out of fear or discomfort. Take the smallest concrete step toward it today.

- Fear is blocking necessary action
- Need courage for a difficult conversation
