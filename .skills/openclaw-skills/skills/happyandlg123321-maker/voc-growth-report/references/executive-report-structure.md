# Executive HTML Report Structure

Use this structure for a boss-ready VOC growth report.

## Required sections
1. 封面 / 数据概况
2. 用户情绪总览
3. 用户分群分析
4. 用户需求图谱
5. 商机与转化机会
6. 价值主张与增长建议
7. CEO Summary

## Guidance by section

### 1. 封面 / 数据概况
Include source, total posts, total comments, date range, and report objective.

### 2. 用户情绪总览
Show positive / neutral / negative distribution, plus top praise and complaint themes.

### 3. 用户分群分析
Segment comments into practical groups such as price-sensitive, function-driven, result-driven, beginner, high-intent, and observers.

### 4. 用户需求图谱
Separate satisfied needs, unsatisfied needs, and latent needs. Do not fabricate latent needs without textual evidence.

### 5. 商机与转化机会
Show opportunity distribution, high-intent comments, pre-conversion questions, and conversion blockers.

### 6. 价值主张与增长建议
Translate findings into value propositions, content topics, product fixes, and sales wording.

### 7. CEO Summary
Make this skimmable. It should answer:
- who to prioritize,
- what to fix first,
- what selling point to amplify,
- what the next 7-day actions should be.

## Visualization guidance
Prefer:
- summary cards,
- distribution charts,
- ranked lists,
- concise commentary.

Treat word clouds as optional decoration, not as the core artifact.
