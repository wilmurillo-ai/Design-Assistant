# Film Budget — Structure Reference
*Compiled from Saturation.io, StudioBinder, No Film School, and industry standards*

## The Two Main Sections

### Above the Line (ATL)
Creative talent whose fees are negotiated BEFORE the budget is locked.
- Story & Screenplay
- Producer fees
- Director fee
- Principal Cast fees

### Below the Line (BTL)
Everything else — the technical and logistical cost of production.

## Full Budget Category Breakdown

### ABOVE THE LINE

| Account | Category | Line Items |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| 1100 | Story & Screenplay | Script purchase/option, WGA fees, rewrites |
| 1200 | Producers | Producer fee, Line Producer, Associate Producers |
| 1300 | Director | Director fee, DGA fees (if applicable) |
| 1400 | Cast | Principal cast fees, casting director, SAG minimums |

**SAG Rate Reference (2025-2026):**
- Ultra Low Budget (ULB): Under $300K — $232-241/day minimum
- Modified Low Budget: Under $700K — $335-350/day minimum
- Low Budget: Under $2M — $630/day minimum
- Theatrical scale: ~$1,085/day minimum

## Fringe Rate Guide

| Crew Type | Fringe Rate |
|-----------|------------|
| Contractors (1099) | 0% (they pay own taxes) |
| Employees (W-2), non-union | 18-22% (FICA, FUTA, state) |
| SAG-AFTRA Ultra Low Budget | +14.3% H&P on wages |
| IATSE (full union) | 30-45% depending on local |

## Budget Tier Benchmarks

| Tier | Range | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Micro-budget | $0 - $50K | Deferred pay, favors, skeleton crew |
| Ultra-low | $50K - $300K | SAG ULB, minimal paid crew |
| Low budget | $300K - $1M | SAG Modified, small departments |
| Mid-range indie | $1M - $5M | Full paid crew, limited VFX |
| Mid-budget | $5M - $20M | Union, full departments |
| Studio indie | $20M - $50M | Prestige indie territory |

## Indie Budget Rules of Thumb

1. **Camera package:** 1-2% of total budget (own = $0)
2. **Catering:** $15-25/person/meal for cast+crew
3. **Locations:** 5-10% of total budget for location-heavy scripts
4. **Post-production:** 20-30% of total budget
5. **Contingency:** ALWAYS 10% of BTL — never skip
6. **Overtime buffer:** Add 15-25% to labor if shooting long days
7. **Insurance:** $3,000-$8,000 for typical indie (check cast/location requirements)
