```
                                                              24.

INT. HAYES APARTMENT - KITCHEN - NIGHT

The kitchen is a mess. Dishes piled in the sink. A half-empty 
whiskey bottle on the counter. Rain hammers the window above the 
stove.

HAYES (52, weathered detective, tie loose, shirt still wet from 
the rain) stands by the fridge. Across from him, MARIA (35, 
sharp, frightened but trying not to show it) clutches a coffee 
mug with both hands.

                    HAYES
          Where is it, Maria.

Not a question. She knows what he's talking about.

                    MARIA
          I don't know what you're—

                    HAYES
          Don't. I've been doing this thirty 
          years. I know what a lie looks like.

He takes a step toward her. She doesn't move.

                    MARIA
          You're drunk, Detective.

                    HAYES
          Sober enough.

He reaches into his jacket and sets his BADGE on the counter 
between them. A declaration, not a threat.

                    HAYES (CONT'D)
          The evidence folder from the Reyes 
          case. It was in lockup at 9 AM. 
          By noon it was gone. Only three 
          people had access.

A long beat. Maria's jaw tightens.

                    MARIA
                (quietly)
          You don't know what you're getting 
          into, Marcus.

Hayes goes very still. She used his first name. That means 
something.

                    HAYES
          Then tell me.

Maria sets the mug on the counter. Carefully. Then suddenly — 
SMASH — she shoves it off the edge. It shatters on the tile.

Hayes doesn't flinch. Neither does she.

                    MARIA
          They'll kill you.

                    HAYES
                (a beat — then)
          Not the first time someone's tried.

He picks up his badge. Pockets it. Heads for the door.

                    HAYES (CONT'D)
          Pack a bag. Tonight.

He's gone. Maria stares at the broken mug on the floor. The 
rain keeps hammering.

                                                   END OF SCENE
```
