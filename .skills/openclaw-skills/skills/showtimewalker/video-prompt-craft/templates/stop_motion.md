# 定格动画模板

风格关键词：`stop-motion animation`、`claymation`、`cutout animation`

## Prompt 模板

```text
A stop-motion animation of [材质描述] [主体] [动作/状态].
The [主体特征/颜色/纹理]. [环境/背景描述].
[光影描述], [氛围关键词].
```

## 完整示例

```text
A stop-motion animation of a small clay character shaped like a
sleepy cat, slowly stretching and yawning on a cozy windowsill made
of felt fabric. The cat has rounded features with tiny closed eyes
and soft pink ears. As it stretches, small yarn balls begin to
unravel and roll around it. The background is a miniature paper
cityscape with soft warm lighting filtering through a tiny felt
curtain. The scene has a gentle, handmade charm with visible
texture on the clay surface. Soft diffused lighting creates a warm
and inviting atmosphere.
```

**结构拆解**：风格=`stop-motion animation` | 材质=粘土+毛毡 | 主体=困倦的小猫角色 | 环境=纸片微缩城市+毛毡窗帘 | 光影=`soft diffused lighting`, `warm` | 氛围=`gentle, handmade charm`

## 材质速查

```
clay                   — 粘土
paper cutout           — 纸片
felt / fabric          — 毛毡/布料
everyday objects       — 日常物品
Lego / building blocks — 积木
food ingredients       — 食材
```

## 引导提问要点

- "用什么材质做定格？（粘土/纸片/布料/日常物品/积木？）"
- "主体是什么？在做什么动作？"
- "场景是什么样的？（微缩城市/桌面/自然？）"
