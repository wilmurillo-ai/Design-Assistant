# 3D 动画模板

风格关键词：`3D animation`、`Pixar-style`、`whimsical`、`stylized 3D`

## Prompt 模板

```text
3D animation of [角色描述]. The character, [特征补充],
[动作/交互]. The [环境] is alive with [奇幻元素].
[角色与环境互动]. [色彩/光影氛围].
```

## 完整示例

```text
3D animation of a small, round, fluffy creature with big, expressive
eyes explores a vibrant, enchanted forest. The creature, a whimsical
blend of a rabbit and a squirrel, has soft blue fur and a bushy,
striped tail. It hops along a sparkling stream, its eyes wide with
wonder. The forest is alive with magical elements: flowers that glow
and change colors, trees with leaves in shades of purple and silver,
and small floating lights that resemble fireflies. The creature stops
to interact playfully with a group of tiny, fairy-like beings dancing
around a mushroom ring. The creature looks up in awe at a large,
glowing tree that seems to be the heart of the forest.
```

**结构拆解**：风格=`3D animation` | 角色=圆形蓝色毛绒生物（兔+松鼠混合体） | 环境=魔法森林（发光花、紫银树、萤火虫光点） | 叙事=跳跃探索→与仙女互动→仰望发光巨树 | 光影=`sparkling stream`, `glowing`

## 角色设计引导

3D 动画需要明确的角色描述：
- 外形：圆形/方形/细长？毛茸茸/光滑？
- 颜色：主色调 + 辅色调
- 特征：大眼睛/长尾巴/翅膀/角？
- 混合设定：像什么动物的混合体？

## 奇幻元素速查

```
flowers that glow and change colors    — 发光变色的花
trees with leaves in purple and silver  — 紫银色树叶的树
floating lights resembling fireflies    — 萤火虫般的漂浮光点
mushroom ring                          — 蘑菇环
fairy-like beings                      — 仙女般的小精灵
crystals that shimmer                  — 闪烁的水晶
enchanted stream                       — 魔法溪流
magical particles / sparkles           — 魔法粒子/闪光
```

## 引导提问要点

- "角色是什么样子的？（外形、颜色、大小、有没有什么特别特征？）"
- "角色是像什么动物的混合？还是完全原创的？"
- "场景是怎样的奇幻世界？（森林/海洋/天空/城堡？有什么魔法元素？）"
