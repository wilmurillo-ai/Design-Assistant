# 商业广告模板

风格关键词：`commercial cinematography`、`product advertisement`、`premium product showcase`、`lifestyle commercial`、`high-end advertising`

## 适用场景

品牌广告、产品展示、App 推广、生活方式广告、开箱体验

## Prompt 模板

```text
A [景别] of [产品/品牌主体] in [场景/环境]. The [产品外观/材质/质感].
[使用场景/交互动作 - 人物与产品或产品与环境].
[环境氛围 - 室内陈设/户外场景/光影装饰].
[光线描述], [品牌调性关键词]. [电影质感关键词].
```

## 常见子类型

### 产品特写型

聚焦产品细节、材质、工艺。适合珠宝、化妆品、电子产品、手表等。

```text
A cinematic close-up of [产品], the camera slowly pushes in as
[光线] highlights the [材质/纹理细节]. The [产品] sits on
[背景/底座], with [反射/折射/光影效果] creating a [氛围]
atmosphere. Shallow depth of field, bokeh, cinematic 35mm film.
```

### 生活方式型

产品融入生活场景，有模特使用或环境衬托。适合服装、饮品、家居、汽车等。

```text
A wide shot of [人物] [使用产品的动作] in [生活场景]. The [产品]
is [产品状态 - 自然融入/成为焦点]. [环境细节 - 布置/天气/氛围].
[光线描述]. [品牌调性关键词], cinematic 35mm film.
```

### 开箱/展示型

动态展示产品从包装到使用的过程。适合数码、美妆、食品等。

```text
A tracking close-up following [产品] being [开箱/组装/使用的动作].
The camera captures [产品细节] as [光影变化]. [环境背景].
[光线描述]. Premium product showcase, high-end advertising.
```

## 品牌调性关键词速查

| 调性 | 关键词组合 |
|------|-----------|
| 高端奢华 | `luxurious`, `elegant`, `premium`, `sophisticated` |
| 年轻活力 | `energetic`, `dynamic`, `vibrant`, `fresh` |
| 极简科技 | `sleek`, `minimalist`, `clean`, `modern` |
| 温馨居家 | `cozy`, `warm`, `inviting`, `comforting` |
| 户外冒险 | `rugged`, `adventurous`, `durable`, `powerful` |
| 自然有机 | `natural`, `organic`, `fresh`, `clean`, `botanical` |

## 推荐光影

| 产品类型 | 光线 | 效果 |
|---------|------|------|
| 金属/玻璃 | `rim light` + `soft reflections` | 勾勒轮廓，突出质感 |
| 皮肤/布料 | `soft diffused light` | 柔和，还原真实色彩 |
| 食品/饮品 | `backlight` + `warm tones` | 通透诱人 |
| 科技产品 | `cool-toned lighting` + `lens flare` | 未来感 |
| 珠宝/腕表 | `dramatic sidelight` + `bokeh` | 闪耀，强调光泽 |

## 完整示例

```text
A cinematic close-up of a sleek glass bottle of premium skincare
serum resting on a polished white marble surface. The camera slowly
pushes in as soft golden backlight filters through the translucent
liquid inside the bottle, creating a warm luminous glow. Tiny water
droplets on the glass catch the light, adding a sense of freshness.
The background is a minimalist luxury bathroom with soft white
towels and a single eucalyptus branch. Shallow depth of field blurs
the background into a creamy bokeh, keeping all focus on the product.
Elegant and sophisticated atmosphere, cinematic 35mm film, color
graded with soft warm tones.
```

**结构拆解**：风格=`cinematic` / `high-end advertising` | 镜头=`close-up` + `slowly push in` | 主体=高端精华液瓶（玻璃瓶、大理石台面） | 环境=极简奢华浴室（白毛巾、尤加利枝） | 光影=`golden backlight` + `luminous glow` | 调性=`elegant`, `sophisticated` | 质感=`cinematic 35mm film`, `bokeh`

## 引导提问要点

- "你想推广什么产品？（外观、材质、颜色？）"
- "广告想传达什么品牌调性？（高端/年轻/极简/温馨？）"
- "产品是单独展示，还是有模特在使用？"
- "场景是在室内还是户外？什么环境最能体现产品价值？"
