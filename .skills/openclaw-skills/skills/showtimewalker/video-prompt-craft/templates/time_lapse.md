# 延时摄影模板

风格关键词：`time-lapse`、`hyperlapse`

## Prompt 模板

```text
A dynamic time-lapse [tracking from / showing] [视角/场景].
The camera captures [移动元素] [运动方式].
[固定元素 - 窗框/地面/天空].
[速度/运动感描述]. [整体氛围], [景别].
```

## 完整示例

```text
A dynamic time-lapse showing the rapidly moving scenery from the
window of a speeding train. The camera captures various elements
such as lush green fields, towering trees, quaint countryside houses,
and distant mountain ranges passing by quickly. The train window
frames the view, adding a sense of speed and motion as the landscape
rushes past. The camera remains static but emphasizes the fast-paced
movement outside. The overall atmosphere is serene yet exhilarating,
capturing the essence of travel and exploration. Medium shot focusing
on the train window and the rushing scenery beyond.
```

**结构拆解**：风格=`time-lapse` | 镜头=`static camera`（窗框构图） | 移动元素=田野/树木/房屋/山脉 | 固定元素=火车窗框 | 氛围=`serene yet exhilarating`

## 核心技巧：静与动对比

延时摄影的魅力在于固定元素 + 快速运动元素的对比：
- 固定：建筑、山体、窗框
- 运动：云、车流、人群、光影、水面

引导时一定要明确哪部分在动、哪部分不动。

## 引导提问要点

- "你想拍什么内容在变化？（云、车流、人群、光影？）"
- "镜头固定还是缓慢移动？（固定机位更有延时感）"
- "有没有一个框架元素？（窗框、门框、桥拱增加构图感）"
