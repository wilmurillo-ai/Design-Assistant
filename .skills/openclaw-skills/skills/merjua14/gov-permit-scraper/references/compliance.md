# Cold Email Compliance

## CAN-SPAM Act (US)
- Include your physical mailing address
- Include a clear unsubscribe mechanism
- Honor opt-out requests within 10 business days
- Don't use deceptive subject lines
- Identify the message as an ad (if applicable)
- B2B cold email IS legal under CAN-SPAM (no prior consent required)

## GDPR (EU/UK)
- B2B cold email allowed under "legitimate interest" in most EU countries
- Must include: who you are, why you're emailing, how to opt out
- Keep records of where you got the data (public records = strong basis)

## Best Practices
- **Always use verified sending domains** (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
- **Rate limit:** Max 25-50 per domain per day for cold outreach
- **Suppress bounces immediately** — hard bounces hurt your domain reputation
- **Rotate domains** if sending at scale (50+ per day)
- **Include "unsubscribe" link** or "reply STOP" mechanism
- **Government permit data is public record** — fully legal to use for outreach
- **Timing matters:** Email within 48 hours of permit issuance for highest response rate
