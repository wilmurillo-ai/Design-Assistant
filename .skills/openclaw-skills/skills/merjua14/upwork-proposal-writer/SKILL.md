# Upwork Proposal Writer Skill

Automatically craft winning Upwork proposals for automation, bot development, and web scraping jobs.

## What This Skill Does

1. Analyzes an Upwork job posting (URL or pasted text)
2. Identifies key requirements, budget, and client pain points
3. Generates a personalized, high-converting proposal
4. Includes relevant portfolio examples and social proof
5. Suggests pricing strategy (hourly vs fixed)

## Usage

### Input
Provide a job posting URL or paste the job description:

```
Write a proposal for this job: [URL or description]
```

### Proposal Template Structure

```markdown
**Opening Hook** (2-3 sentences)
- Reference specific detail from their posting
- Show you understand their exact problem
- Mention relevant experience

**Solution Overview** (3-4 bullet points)
- Exactly how you'll solve their problem
- Tools/tech you'll use
- Timeline estimate

**Social Proof** (1-2 sentences)
- Similar projects completed
- Results achieved (numbers!)

**Next Step** (1 sentence)
- Clear, low-friction call to action
```

### Example Proposal

For a job posting: "Need help automating data entry from spreadsheets to CRM"

---

Hi [Client],

I noticed you're spending too much time on manual data entry between spreadsheets and your CRM — that's exactly what I automate for clients every week.

**Here's what I'd build for you:**
- ✅ Automated pipeline: Google Sheets → your CRM (Salesforce/HubSpot/Zoho)
- ✅ Real-time sync — new rows auto-import within minutes
- ✅ Data validation and dedup before import
- ✅ Error logging so nothing falls through cracks

I recently built a similar pipeline for an insurance agency that cut their data entry from 15 hours/week to zero. Happy to share a quick demo.

Can we hop on a 10-minute call this week? I can usually have this running within 48 hours of kickoff.

Best,
[Name]

---

### Pricing Guidelines
| Job Type | Suggested Rate |
|----------|---------------|
| Simple automation (1 workflow) | $150-300 fixed |
| Multi-step bot/scraper | $300-800 fixed |
| Full business automation | $50-75/hr or $1,000-3,000 fixed |
| Ongoing maintenance | $25-50/hr |

### Key Tips
- **Never start with "Dear Hiring Manager"** — use their name or company
- **First sentence must reference THEIR specific problem** — not your skills
- **Include a deliverable timeline** — "48 hours" beats "ASAP"
- **End with a micro-commitment** — "quick call" not "hire me"
- **Keep it under 150 words** — busy clients skim
