# Cold Email Best Practices

## Deliverability

- Send from a domain with SPF, DKIM, and DMARC configured
- Warm up new domains: start with 5/day, increase by 5 every 3 days
- Max 25 emails per domain per day for cold outreach
- Use a dedicated subdomain (e.g., hello@outreach.yourdomain.com)
- Include a physical address in footer (CAN-SPAM compliance)
- Always include an unsubscribe mechanism

## Subject Lines That Work

- "Quick question about {business_name}" (35-45% open rate)
- "Noticed something on your website" (30-40% open rate)
- "{first_name}, your website might be losing customers" (25-35% open rate)

## Template Structure

1. **Opening hook** — Reference something specific about THEIR business (1 sentence)
2. **Problem** — Name the specific issues you found (1-2 sentences)
3. **Credibility** — Brief proof you can fix it (1 sentence)
4. **CTA** — One clear ask (reply, call, link)
5. **Keep total length under 150 words**

## Follow-Up Sequence

- **Email 1** (Day 0): Initial outreach with specific findings
- **Email 2** (Day 3): "Just following up" + one new insight
- **Email 3** (Day 7): Break-up email: "Last note from me" + social proof

## Suppression Management

- Maintain a suppression list of unsubscribed emails
- Honor unsubscribe requests within 24 hours
- Never email the same person from multiple domains
- Remove bounced emails immediately
