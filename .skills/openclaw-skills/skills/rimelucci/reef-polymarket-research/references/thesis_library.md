## How to Use This Document

1. **Before trading**: Create thesis entry with unique ID
2. **Reference in trades**: Link journal entries to thesis ID
3. **After resolution**: Update with outcome and learnings
4. **Periodically review**: Look for patterns in successful vs failed theses

---

## Thesis ID Format

`TH-[CATEGORY]-[NUMBER]`

Categories:
- POL = Politics
- CRY = Crypto
- SPO = Sports
- ENT = Entertainment
- OTH = Other

Example: `TH-POL-001`, `TH-CRY-003`

## Thesis Templates by Category

### Politics Thesis Template

Key factors to always consider:
- Polling (with methodology assessment)
- Fundamentals (economy, approval, incumbency)
- Historical patterns (base rates)
- Structural factors (electoral system, demographics)
- Information environment (media, campaigns)

### Crypto Thesis Template

Key factors to always consider:
- On-chain metrics
- Developer activity
- Macro environment
- Technical setup
- Sentiment vs fundamentals

### Sports Thesis Template

Key factors to always consider:
- Advanced stats
- Injury situation
- Schedule/rest
- Weather (if relevant)
- Market efficiency (are sharps on this?)

### Entertainment Thesis Template

Key factors to always consider:
- Critical reception
- Industry buzz
- Historical voting patterns
- Campaign activity
- Category competitiveness
