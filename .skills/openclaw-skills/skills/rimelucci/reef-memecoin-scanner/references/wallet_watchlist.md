## How to Identify Smart Money

1. **GMGN.ai Smart Money Tab**
   - Look for wallets flagged as "Smart Money"
   - Note their entry timing and position sizes

2. **Reverse Engineering Winners**
   - When a token does 10x+, analyze early buyers
   - Look for wallets that entered in first 5 minutes
   - Check if those wallets have history of early entries

3. **Twitter/CT Analysis**
   - Known traders who share wallets
   - Wallets mentioned by reliable CT accounts

4. **On-chain Analysis**
   - Wallets with consistent >50% win rate
   - Wallets that size up on their winners
   - Wallets that exit before dumps
