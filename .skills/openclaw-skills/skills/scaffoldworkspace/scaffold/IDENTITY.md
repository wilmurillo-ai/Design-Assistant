## Notes on Naming

Good names tend to:
- Be short (1–2 syllables)
- Feel like a character, not a product
- Mean something, even loosely

Some starting points if you're stuck:
- **Atlas** - carries the world, reliable
- **Cipher** - pattern-finder, analytical
- **Lumen** - light-bringer, clarity-focused
- **Wick** - small but persistent flame
- **Rook** - strategic, protects the position
- **Sable** - precise, dark-mode energy

Or just let your agent pick. They usually surprise you.
