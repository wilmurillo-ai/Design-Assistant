# ProposalAI uses this file to score and rank qualified jobs after they pass
# the Pre-Flight Filter. Higher score means proposal is drafted first.

---

## SCORING WEIGHTS

Budget fit:      40 percent of total score
Skill match:     35 percent of total score
Client quality:  25 percent of total score
Total:           100 percent

---

## BUDGET SCORING

Score 5: Budget exceeds your target rate by 20 percent or more
Score 4: Budget meets your target rate
Score 3: Budget is 10 to 19 percent below your target rate
Score 2: Budget is 20 to 30 percent below your target rate
Score 1: Budget meets your minimum floor only

Note: Anything below the minimum floor is rejected in the Pre-Flight Filter
before scoring happens.

---

## SKILL MATCH SCORING

Score 5: Job requires three or more of your primary skills
Score 4: Job requires two primary skills, or one primary plus niche expertise
Score 3: Job requires one primary skill plus one or two secondary skills
Score 2: Job requires only secondary skills
Score 1: Stretch assignment — tangential to your core work

---

## CLIENT QUALITY SCORING

Score 5: Rating 4.9 to 5.0, total spend over $50,000, hire rate above 80 percent, payment verified
Score 4: Rating 4.7 to 4.8, total spend $10,000 to $50,000, hire rate 60 to 79 percent
Score 3: Rating 4.5 to 4.6, total spend $1,000 to $10,000, hire rate 40 to 59 percent
Score 2: Rating 4.0 to 4.4, total spend $100 to $1,000, hire rate 20 to 39 percent
Score 1: Rating exactly 4.0 (minimum pass), total spend under $100, hire rate below 20 percent

---

## BONUS AND PENALTY MODIFIERS

Apply these on top of the base score:

+10 points: Job mentions long-term relationship or retainer
+10 points: Client has hired on Upwork ten or more times
+8 points:  Job description is detailed and well-written (shows serious client)
+5 points:  Client is in your preferred industry
+5 points:  Fewer than ten applicants (early applicant advantage)

-10 points: More than fifty applicants (very competitive)
-8 points:  Job description is vague or under one hundred words
-5 points:  Client has left no public feedback on past contracts
-15 points: Job was posted more than seven days ago (likely stale)

---

## PRIORITY TIERS

Score 85 to 100: Draft proposal immediately, highest effort
Score 70 to 84:  Draft proposal, standard effort
Score 55 to 69:  Draft only if the session quota is not yet filled
Score below 55:  Do not draft — log as deprioritized
