# AI TikTok Ad Script Generator for Shopify

Generate scroll-stopping, high-converting TikTok ad scripts for your Shopify products — complete with hooks, UGC scripts, A/B test variations, CTAs, and production notes. Now with **Reali-TEA Mode**: scripts that feel genuinely human, imperfect, and impossible to ignore.

---

## What This Skill Does

Provide your product details and receive a complete TikTok advertising script package across six sections:

| Section | What You Get |
|---|---|
| Hook Bank | 5 pattern-interrupt hooks with reasoning |
| Main Scripts | 1-3 A/B script variations with timestamp + visual + VO breakdown |
| UGC Version | 1 authentic creator-style script for organic-feeling ads |
| CTA Library | 6 CTAs (urgency, value, soft, interactive, social proof, curiosity) |
| A/B Testing Roadmap | 3-priority test plan with metrics and week-1 checklist |
| Production Notes | Filming, editing, music, and Shopify link guidance |

---

## Reali-TEA Mode

Toggle **Reali-TEA Mode ON** to generate scripts with intentional human imperfections that make ads feel genuinely unscripted:

| What Changes | Example |
|---|---|
| Speech fillers added naturally | "okay so... I was literally about to return it" |
| Mid-sentence restarts | "It's — okay it's hard to explain, just watch" |
| Authentic language only | "my skin looked different in like a week" (not "amazing results") |
| Imperfect camera directions | "phone slightly off-center, hand visible adjusting" |
| Product revealed "accidentally" | appears in background before creator notices it |
| Forgotten-sounding CTA | "Oh — link in my bio if you want it. Okay bye." |
| "What NOT to Fix in Post" list | editor keeps the stumble, the laugh, the restart |

**Reali-TEA Intensity levels:**
- **Low** — Subtle touches, good for aspirational or educational brands
- **Medium** — Clearly UGC-native, recommended starting point
- **High** — Raw, chaotic energy for Gen Z and novelty products

When Reali-TEA is ON, a bonus **Priority Test #4** is added to compare a Reali-TEA version vs. a polished version of your top script.

## How to Use

Describe your product in plain language. Example:

> "My product is a portable electric milk frother called FoamFlex — $19.99, USB rechargeable, makes barista foam in 15 seconds. Home coffee lovers aged 22-38. Pain point: flat sad lattes at home. Benefits: fast, rechargeable, works for matcha and protein shakes. Buy 2 Get 1 Free deal. Tone: Humorous. In-Feed Ad. 2 scripts. Reali-TEA ON, intensity Medium."

---

## Example Output (Reali-TEA ON, Hook Bank excerpt)

**Hook 1 — Confession (Reali-TEA)**
"I spent $6 on lattes every single day until I found this thing."
Why it works: Triggers financial guilt and curiosity; "this thing" feels unplanned.

**Hook 3 — Reaction (Reali-TEA)**
"Okay. Okay. Wait. Is that foam from a $20 gadget?"
Why it works: Starts with a sound reaction, not a word — sounds mid-thought, not scripted.
