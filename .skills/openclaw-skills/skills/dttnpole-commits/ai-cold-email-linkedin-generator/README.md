# AI Cold Email & LinkedIn Outreach Generator

Generate high-reply-rate cold emails and LinkedIn DMs in seconds — personalized, human-sounding, and built for real B2B sales teams.

---

## What This Skill Does

- Cold emails with two subject line options
- LinkedIn direct messages under 80 words
- 2-round follow-up sequences (day 3–5 and day 7–10)
- A/B test variants with different opening angles
- 4 tone presets from casual peer to direct closer

---

## How to Use

Fill in the inputs, select your outputs, and copy the result directly into your email client or outreach tool.

### Inputs

| Field | Required | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Product / Service | Yes | What you sell — be specific |
| Target Audience | Yes | Job title, company type, size |
| Pain Points | Yes | 1–3 real problems they face |
| Offer / CTA | Yes | The one thing you want them to do |
| Your Name | Yes | Sender name for signature |
| Your Role / Company | Yes | Used in signature |
| Prospect Name | Optional | Adds personalization |
| Prospect Company | Optional | Adds personalization |
| Personalization Hook | Optional | A recent post, award, or news item |
| Tone | Yes | Choose from 4 tone presets |
| Output Type | Yes | Select which formats to generate |

### Tone Options

- Friendly and Professional — warm but credible (recommended)
- Direct and Confident — straight to the point, no fluff
- Curious and Consultative — question-led, low pressure
- Casual Peer — founder-to-founder, very short sentences

```
Product: AI scheduling tool that auto-books meetings from email threads

Pain Points: 2–3 hours per day on back-and-forth scheduling; meetings fall through cracks
```

## Reply Rate Tips

1. Add a real personalization hook — specific beats generic by 2–3x.
2. Send Tuesday–Thursday, 7–9am or 4–6pm in the recipient's timezone.
3. Follow up — 70% of replies come from follow-ups, not the first email.
4. A/B test subject lines over 50+ sends before picking a winner.
5. Keep your signature simple — name, title, one link maximum.
