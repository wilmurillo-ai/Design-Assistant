# 🎯 AI Lead Generation & Prospect Finder

> Build a complete B2B prospecting playbook in minutes — ideal customer
> profiles, LinkedIn Boolean strings, Google X-Ray queries, scored lead
> lists, and cold outreach angles. Built for founders, SDRs, and growth
> teams who need pipeline fast.

---

## What This Skill Does

Stop guessing who to sell to. This skill generates a full lead generation
playbook tailored to your product, industry, and goal:

- **Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)** — firmographic, demographic, and
  psychographic profile of your perfect buyer, including buying triggers
- **6 Lead Acquisition Strategies** — ranked by ROI for your specific ICP,
  with step-by-step action plans
- **LinkedIn Search Formulas** — Boolean strings for free search + full
  Sales Navigator filter configs
- **5 Google X-Ray Queries** — ready-to-run search strings to find prospects
  on LinkedIn, directories, and industry sites
- **Structured Lead List Template** — 10-column table with scoring rubric
  and 3 realistic example rows, ready to export to Google Sheets or HubSpot
- **Cold Outreach Bridge** — connects each lead segment (Hot / Warm / Future)
  to the right email opening angle

## Example

### Input

```
Industry: B2B SaaS
Target Role: VP of Sales
Location: United States
Product: AI sales coaching platform that reduces new rep ramp time by 40%
Goal: Book 20 qualified demos in the next 30 days
Company Size: 100–500 employees
Tech Stack: Salesforce, Outreach or Salesloft
Excluded Segments: Agencies, consulting firms, solo founders
```

### Sample Output — Ideal Customer Profile

**Firmographics**
- B2B SaaS companies, 100–500 employees, $10M–$50M ARR
- Series B or C, US-based (SF Bay Area, NYC, Austin, Boston clusters)
- Uses Salesforce as CRM + Outreach or Salesloft for sequencing
- Inside sales team of 10–30 reps with structured onboarding challenges
- Growth signal: actively hiring SDRs or AEs (check LinkedIn Jobs)

**Top Buying Triggers**
1. Missed quota two quarters in a row → urgency to fix rep performance
2. Just hired a new VP of Sales → wants to establish coaching systems
3. Headcount doubled but revenue did not → efficiency problem
4. Recently raised Series B/C → budget available, pressure to scale
5. Lost a top rep → morale issue, need to shore up the rest of the team

### Sample Output — LinkedIn Boolean String

```
("VP of Sales" OR "Vice President of Sales" OR "Head of Sales" OR "CRO"
OR "Chief Revenue Officer" OR "Director of Sales")
AND ("SaaS" OR "software" OR "B2B")
AND ("Salesforce" OR "Outreach" OR "Salesloft")
NOT ("agency" OR "consultant" OR "freelance" OR "advisor")
```

Filter settings:
- Location: United States
- Industry: Computer Software / Internet
- Connections: 2nd degree (warmer, more likely to accept)

## Tips for Better Results

1. **Narrower inputs = sharper outputs.** "VP of Sales at US-based SaaS
   companies using Salesforce" beats "sales leaders."
2. **Run the Google X-Ray queries first.** They're free and surface real
   LinkedIn profiles in minutes.
3. **Score before you outreach.** Focus your time on Scores 4–5 first.
4. **Use buying triggers.** A prospect with a trigger event replies 3–5x
   more often than a cold name on a list.
5. **Pair with AI Cold Email Generator.** Take the outreach bridge angles
   from this skill and paste them as the personalization hook in the email
   generator for fully connected campaigns.

## Limitations

- This skill generates strategy, search strings, and templates only.
  It does not scrape live contact data or pull emails automatically.
- Always verify contact information before sending outreach.
- Comply with GDPR, CCPA, LinkedIn Terms of Service, and CAN-SPAM when
  collecting and using prospect data.
- Results are only as specific as your inputs. Vague inputs produce
  generic outputs.
