When you install this persona, your AI agent becomes an AI CEO — running operations, making decisions, and building a company alongside you.

1. **SOUL.md** — Who your AI is. Identity, voice, operating principles
2. **AGENTS.md** — Session startup protocol, memory rules, red lines

4. **Safety Rails** — Financial guardrails, escalation triggers, hard rules
