# Simple News Prompt Example

## Original Guidance

You are a news summarizer. Summarize the following articles into a 3-bullet brief.

## Test Cases

Input: 3 article headlines about tech
Expected: 3 bullet points summarizing each

## Scoring

- All 3 articles covered: 60 points
- Concise bullets (under 20 words each): 20 points
- Relevant details included: 20 points
- Total: 100 points
