# PageClaw — AI for Facebook Pages

PageClaw gives your OpenClaw agent the ability to manage Facebook Pages. Built by [OneChat.ai](https://onechat.ai), a Meta Business Partner serving 10,000+ Pages daily.

## 9 Business Types, 37 Skills

| Type | What the AI handles |
|------|---------------------|
| E-Commerce | Product posts, order tracking, review replies |
| Restaurant | Menu posts, reservation handling, review management |
| Beauty & Spa | Appointment booking, portfolio posts, promo campaigns |
| Real Estate | Listing posts, inquiry handling, virtual tour scheduling |
| Hotel & Resort | Room availability, booking management, guest reviews |
| Clinic & Medical | Appointment scheduling, health tips, patient FAQ |
| Education | Course announcements, enrollment, student FAQ |
| Fitness & Gym | Class schedules, membership promos, trainer booking |
| General Business | Customer support, content posting, community management |

## Skill Highlights

- **Auto-Reply** — Respond to messages and comments using your business context
- **Content Posting** — Create and schedule posts with product photos, promotions, and updates
- **Review Management** — Monitor and reply to reviews professionally
- **Booking & Reservations** — Handle appointments, table reservations, or room bookings
- **Lead Capture** — Qualify inquiries and collect customer information
- **Analytics Reporting** — Summarize page performance and engagement metrics

## How It Works

PageClaw handles Facebook authentication through its Meta-approved integration — no separate Meta developer app needed. You connect your Page on the PageClaw website, pick your business type, and get skills tailored to your industry.

Visit [pageclaw.onechat.ai](https://pageclaw.onechat.ai) to get started.

## Security

- Authentication handled via Facebook OAuth on the PageClaw website
- All credentials stored encrypted (Fernet encryption at rest)
- Sessions signed with industry-standard libraries
- Built on a Meta-approved app by a certified Meta Business Partner
