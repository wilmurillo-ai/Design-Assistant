# AI Citation Optimization Framework

## How AI Systems Select Citations

AI Overviews, ChatGPT with search, and Perplexity all use similar signals to decide what to cite:

### Content Signals (what to write)
- **Passage length**: ideal extraction window is 134-167 words per section
- **Stat density**: include statistics with source attribution every 150-200 words
- **Definite language**: "X costs $29" beats "X may cost around $29"
- **Entity density**: mention specific tools, people, companies by name
- **Question-answer pairs**: AI systems extract Q&A format easily
- **Tabular data**: comparison tables get cited disproportionately often

### Structural Signals (how to format)
- **Clear H2/H3 hierarchy**: each section should be self-contained and answerable
- **Lead with the answer**: first 40-60 words of each section should directly answer the implied question
- **Short paragraphs**: 2-3 sentences max for easier extraction
- **Lists and bullet points**: scannable formats are preferred by AI systems
- **Summary sections**: explicit TL;DR or key takeaways sections

### Authority Signals (why to trust)
- **Multi-source corroboration**: brand mentioned positively on 3+ independent domains
- **Author credentials**: visible expertise signals (bio, credentials, experience)
- **E-E-A-T**: Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness
- **Freshness**: ~50% of AI-cited content is less than 13 weeks old
- **Content depth**: pages over 20,000 characters average 10.18 citations vs 2.39 for pages under 500 chars

## Citation Decoupling (critical insight)

Only 38% of pages cited in AI Overviews also rank in the top 10 organically (down from 76% in mid-2025). This means:
- You CAN get AI citations without ranking #1
- You CAN lose AI citations despite ranking #1
- AI citation optimization is a separate discipline from traditional SEO

## CTR Impact by Query Type

| Query Type | AI Overview Coverage | CTR Impact | Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|
| Informational | 70%+ | -30 to -40% | Citation optimization |
| How-to/Tutorial | 65%+ | -25 to -35% | Content depth + visuals |
| B2B/Technical | ~50% | Up to -70% | GEO + entity authority |
| Transactional | ~25% | -5 to -15% | Traditional SEO holds |
| E-commerce | ~15% | -4% | Product schema focus |

## The Silver Lining

Visitors who DO click through from AI Overview pages convert at ~23x the rate of standard search visitors. Lower traffic, but much higher quality.

## Actionable Checklist

- [ ] Lead every section with a direct answer (40-60 words)
- [ ] Include stats with source attribution every 150-200 words
- [ ] Structure sections as self-contained 134-167 word passages
- [ ] Keep paragraphs to 2-3 sentences
- [ ] Add comparison tables for any comparative data
- [ ] Include author credentials and expertise signals
- [ ] Update content every 8-12 weeks
- [ ] Get brand mentioned on 3+ independent domains
