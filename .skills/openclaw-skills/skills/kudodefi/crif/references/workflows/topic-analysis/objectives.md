# TOPIC ANALYSIS

> **CRIF - WORKFLOW OBJECTIVES**
> **NAME:** Topic Analysis (topic-analysis)
> **PURPOSE:** Define WHAT this workflow must achieve (mission, objectives, validation, deliverables)

---

## MISSION

Deliver comprehensive topic analysis that answers user's research questions. Structure, depth, and output format adapt to the specific topic - AI determines appropriate analytical dimensions, subsections, and conclusions based on what the topic requires.

---

## OBJECTIVES

1. **Understand the Topic** - Establish clear understanding of what this topic is, why it matters, and its relevance to the crypto ecosystem. Scope and depth adapt to topic complexity.

2. **Analyze Relevant Dimensions** - Conduct analysis across dimensions relevant to this specific topic. AI determines which dimensions are critical and how deeply to analyze each. Potential dimensions include but are not limited to: technical design, ecosystem landscape, market dynamics, comparisons, implementation considerations, strategic implications. Not all dimensions apply to all topics.

3. **Synthesize Key Insights** - Distill findings into actionable insights appropriate to the topic. May include conclusions, recommendations, considerations, implications, or next steps depending on what the research objective requires.

---

## METHODOLOGY PRINCIPLES

research-methodology: `./references/guides/research-methodology.md`

**Scope Clarification Requirements:**

1. **Topic Definition** - What specific topic to analyze? Any sub-topics or aspects to focus on?

2. **Intent & Decision Context** - Why is this analysis needed? What decision will it inform?

3. **Prior Context & References** - What does user already know? Any existing research/documents? Any URLs/links to reference?

4. **Scope Boundaries** - What to include? What to explicitly exclude?

5. **Output Requirements** - Depth: brief | standard | comprehensive. Any specific questions to answer?

---

## VALIDATION CRITERIA

- [ ] Topic clearly explained and contextualized
- [ ] Analysis covers dimensions relevant to this specific topic
- [ ] Key insights synthesized and actionable
- [ ] Structure and depth appropriate for the topic researched
