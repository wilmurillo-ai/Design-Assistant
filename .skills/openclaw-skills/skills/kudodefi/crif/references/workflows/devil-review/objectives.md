# DEVIL'S ADVOCATE REVIEW

> **CRIF - WORKFLOW OBJECTIVES**
> **NAME:** Devil's Advocate Review (devil-review)
> **PURPOSE:** Define WHAT this workflow must achieve (mission, objectives, validation, deliverables)

---

## MISSION

Stress-test research through rigorous adversarial thinking to expose weaknesses and provide confrontational reality check. Develop steelman bear case, identify thesis breaking points systematically, challenge optimistic assumptions, and expose comprehensive failure modes. Test robustness across realistic scenarios and provide honest assessment. Enable better decision-making through adversarial validation.

---

## OBJECTIVES

1. **Develop Steelman Bear Case** - Create strongest possible opposing argument (not strawman weak version), synthesize most compelling counter-thesis from available evidence, attack each pillar of bull case with strongest counter-evidence, support bear case with credible evidence and precedent, assess bear case strength honestly

2. **Identify Thesis Breaking Points** - Determine critical conditions under which bull thesis collapses completely, map key assumptions to thesis outcomes, identify thesis-breaking assumptions explicitly, assess probability each breaking point occurs, evaluate thesis sensitivity to each breaking point

3. **Challenge Optimistic Assumptions** - Question every best-case scenario assumption aggressively, demand validation for rosy projections, test base-case vs best-case realism, identify hidden optimism bias in analysis, provide base-case or pessimistic alternatives

4. **Expose Failure Modes Comprehensively** - Identify all ways project could fail despite positive analysis, analyze execution, technical, competitive, market, token economic, and catastrophic failure modes, identify failure mode probabilities and impacts

5. **Question Consensus Beliefs** - Challenge "everyone knows X" assumptions without independent validation, identify groupthink in analysis, demand independent validation of consensus claims, develop contrarian perspectives, assess whether research thinks independently or follows crowd

6. **Stress-Test Under Adversarial Scenarios** - Test thesis under realistic stress scenarios not just best-case, develop base-case, stress-case, and worst-case scenarios, assess thesis survival under each scenario, determine whether thesis is robust or fragile

7. **Attack Weak Evidence and Unsupported Claims** - Identify all unsourced or weakly-sourced claims aggressively, challenge evidence quality for key claims, expose correlation-causation confusion, identify cherry-picked evidence, assess whether weak evidence undermines key conclusions

8. **Provide Reality Check Assessment** - Synthesize adversarial analysis into overall reality check verdict, assess thesis realism honestly, evaluate probability of thesis success realistically, distinguish between "possible" and "probable", deliver confrontational but constructive verdict
