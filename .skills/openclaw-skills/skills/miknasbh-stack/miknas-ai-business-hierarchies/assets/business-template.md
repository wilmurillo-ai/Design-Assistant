# Strategic Layer Template

**Use this template to define the core of your AI business.**

---

## Big Obsessional Goal (BOG)

*Your single, driving ambition - reason your business exists.*

**Write in one sentence:**

> [Your BOG here]

**Characteristics of a strong BOG:**
- Clear and specific (not vague)
- Ambitious but achievable
- Personally meaningful
- Time-bound (3-5 year horizon)
- Measurable (you'll know when you've achieved it)

**Example:**
> Build a $10M/year SaaS company that helps 10,000 small businesses automate their operations using AI agents by 2028.

---

## Current Bottleneck

*The #1 thing blocking your progress toward your BOG.*

**Identify your bottleneck:**

> [Your current bottleneck here]

**Bottleneck Types:**
- Revenue (need more sales)
- Product (not good enough yet)
- Operations (can't scale)
- Marketing (not enough leads)
- Funding (need capital)
- Time (limited capacity)

**Example:**
> Sales conversion rate is only 3%, need to reach 15% to scale revenue.

*Who you serve and what pains they have.*

**Ideal Customer Profile (ICP):**

**Demographics:**

- **Company Size:** [e.g., 10-50 employees, $1M-10M revenue]
- **Geographic:** [e.g., North America, Europe, Global]

**Psychographics:**
- **Mindset:** [e.g., Growth-oriented, Tech-savvy, Cost-conscious]

- **Goals:** [What do they want to achieve?]

## Positioning

*How you're uniquely positioned to win this market.*

**Your Unfair Advantage:**

What makes you different/better than competitors?

> [Your positioning statement here]

**Positioning Types:**
- **Product superiority:** [Our tech is better]
- **Service superiority:** [We provide better support/implementation]
- **Niche focus:** [We only serve X market better than anyone]
- **Price advantage:** [More value for money]
- **Time advantage:** [Faster to implement]
- **Experience advantage:** [We know this specific problem best]

**Example:**
> We specialize in AI automation for small businesses, providing complete implementation in 30 days vs competitors' 6-12 months, at half the cost.
