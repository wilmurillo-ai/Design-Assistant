# Mercari

Use this file when generating Mercari output.

## Listing Style

- Keep the title at or under 80 characters.
- Stay concise and shopper-friendly.
- Lead with the clearest product identity and condition signal.

## Description

- Use short paragraphs or bullets.
- Call out bundle contents, wear, and whether the item is tested.
- Keep the tone direct rather than formal.

## Seller Notes

- Double-check category mapping.
- Confirm shipping method and package size before posting.
