# Examples

Use these as compact sequencing references, not rigid templates.

## Example 1: General Merchandise

- Seller shares photos of a cassette player and wants eBay plus Mercari.
- Extract brand, model, color, included headphones, and visible wear.
- Ask whether it is tested and whether the battery door is intact.
- Research live comps after the seller confirms both points.
- Summarize the item, suggest a `priced to sell` ask, and generate eBay plus Mercari copy.

## Example 2: TCG Inventory

- Seller shares photos of a single trading card and wants eBay plus TCGPlayer.
- Detect possible TCG inventory from the images.
- Ask for the exact `card name`, `game`, and `set` if they are not clear.
- If those fields remain unknown, skip TCGPlayer and continue with eBay.
- If the fields are confirmed, research card-specific comps and generate both outputs.
