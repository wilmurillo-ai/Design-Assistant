# Content patterns (1-6)

## 1. Significance inflation — MEDIUM

**Words to watch:** stands/serves as, is a testament, vital/crucial/pivotal role, underscores importance, reflects broader, symbolizing, marking/shaping, represents a shift, key turning point, evolving landscape, indelible mark

**Before:**
> The protocol marks a pivotal moment in the evolution of decentralized finance, setting the stage for broader adoption.

**After:**
> The protocol launched in March 2025 and processed $2B in volume within three months.

---

## 2. Promotional language — MEDIUM

**Words to watch:** groundbreaking, revolutionary, game-changing, breathtaking, stunning, vibrant, rich heritage, showcasing excellence, nestled, in the heart of

**Before:**
> This groundbreaking protocol is revolutionizing the DeFi landscape with its vibrant ecosystem.

**After:**
> The protocol handles cross-chain swaps without requiring wrapped tokens.

---

## 3. Superficial -ing analyses — MEDIUM

**Words to watch:** highlighting, underscoring, emphasizing, ensuring, reflecting, symbolizing, contributing to, cultivating, fostering, showcasing

**Before:**
> The architecture separates consensus from execution, enabling modularity while ensuring security, highlighting the team's commitment to scalability.

**After:**
> The architecture separates consensus from execution. This lets each layer scale independently.

---

## 4. Vague attributions — HIGH

**Words to watch:** Research shows, Studies indicate, Experts believe, Industry observers note, Sources say

**Before:**
> Research shows that inference costs consume 60-80% of operating expenses.

**After:**
> A 2025 a16z study found inference costs consume 60-80% of operating expenses at AI-first startups.

**Fix pattern:** Always add specific source (who, when, where published)

---

## 5. Formulaic challenges sections — MEDIUM

**Pattern:** "Despite its... faces challenges... Despite these challenges..."

**Before:**
> Despite its rapid growth, the protocol faces challenges typical of emerging technologies. Despite these challenges, adoption continues to accelerate.

**After:**
> Two problems slowed adoption: gas costs on mainnet and a confusing onboarding flow. The team shipped L2 support in November, cutting costs 90%.

---

## 6. Generic positive conclusions — MEDIUM

**Pattern:** "The future looks bright", "Exciting times ahead", "continues its journey"

**Before:**
> The future looks bright for AI agents. Exciting times lie ahead as the industry continues its journey toward excellence.

**After:**
> Three teams are shipping production agents this quarter. We'll know by summer if the unit economics actually work.
