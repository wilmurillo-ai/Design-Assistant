# Style Presets

## `plain` / `纪实简洁`

- Keep paragraphs short.
- Lead with events, not mood.
- Use direct wording.
- Best for work logs, standups, bug notes, and short recaps.

## `gentle` / `温柔叙事`

- Keep the voice warm and personal.
- Let the mood soften the transitions.
- Avoid heavy metaphor.
- Best for ordinary life records and relationship notes.

## `reflective` / `克制反思`

- Start from the day's overall state, then slowly move through concrete events and emotional changes.
- Keep the writing grounded, but allow one extra layer of feeling.
- Let the emotion come from the facts, transitions, and small shifts in mood.
- Make the sentences feel natural, with a little turning and inner flow, but do not push them into overtly literary language.
- This is the default style.

## `lyrical` / `轻文艺`

- Add more atmosphere, scene, or rhythm.
- Keep the wording readable and controlled.
- Use this only when the source material naturally supports it.
- Do not drift into florid or overly sentimental prose.
