# Mad SEO Writer: V2 Qualitative & Citation Standards

## 🔬 The "Pareto" Audit: Top 10 Citation Killers
Before the deep-dive EEAT audit, ensure these 10 high-impact factors are met. If any fail, citation likelihood in AI Search (Gemini/Perplexity) drops by >80%.

1.  **Entity Grounding (C01)**: Is the "What/Who" defined clearly in the first 50 words?
2.  **Direct Answer (C02)**: Is the main query answered directly within 60 words in the intro?
3.  **Experience Marker (X01)**: Does it contain "I/We tested" or "Our evidence"? (Google HCU Mandatory).
4.  **Sentence Chunking (S01)**: Are facts written in clear, extractable standalone sentences?
5.  **Scientific Summary (T01)**: Is there a 2-column "Key Takeaways" table in the opening H2?
6.  **Intent Matching (I01)**: Does the article fulfill the specific search intent (Informational/Commercial)?
7.  **Expert Entities (E01)**: Are specific brands, tools, and experts named throughout?
8.  **Freshness (F01)**: Is the content explicitly dated and updated for the current month/year?
9.  **Citation Integrity (R01)**: Are external claims linked to authoritative primary sources?
10. **Silo Navigation (L01)**: Do internal links stay within the topic cluster to build authority?

## 🏆 CORE-EEAT Benchmark V2.0 (Blogging Edition)
*Optimized for long-form articles, pillars, and tutorials.*

### I. Competence & Intent (C)
- **C01 Intent Alignment**: Title promise must match content delivery.
- **C02 Direct Answer**: Core answer (40-60 words) provided IMMEDIATELY following every H2/H3 header.
- **C06 Audience Hook**: Explicitly state who the article is for in the intro.
- **C10 Semantic Closure**: Conclusion answers the opening question and provides next steps.

### II. Objectivity & Structure (O)
- **O01 Heading Hierarchy**: Strict H1 -> H2 -> H3 structure.
- **O02 Summary Box**: Mandatory TL;DR or Key Takeaways table.
- **O06 Section Chunking**: Paragraphs must be 3-5 sentences.
- **O09 Density**: No filler; every sentence must carry information.

### III. Reliability & Proof (R)
- **R01 Data Precision**: At least 5 precise numbers/metrics per article.
- **R02 Citation Density**: 1 external primary source citation per 500 words.
- **R04 Evidence**: Every major claim must be backed by data or experience.
- **R07 Entity Precision**: Use full names for brands, products, and experts.

### IV. Experience (X) - HCU MANDATORY
- **X01 Personal Narrative**: Inclusion of "I tested..." or "Our team analyzed...".
- **X02 Originality**: Providing insights not found in the top 10 SERP results.
- **X03 Visual Proof**: Reference to original screenshots, data tables, or video timestamps.

## 📑 Feature Snippet Optimization Patterns

**For Definition Snippets**:
"[Term] is [clear, concise definition in 40-60 words]"

**For List Snippets**:
Create clear, numbered or bulleted lists under H2s.

**For Table Snippets**:
Use comparison tables (2-column preferred) with clear headers.

**For How-To Snippets**:
Number each step clearly: "Step 1:", "Step 2:", etc.
