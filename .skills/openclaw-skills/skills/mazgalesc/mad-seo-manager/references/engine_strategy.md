# Mad SEO: Omnichannel 3.0 Strategic Framework

## Phase 1: Entity Grounding & Relationship Mapping
1. Identify the **Primary Entity**.
2. Map **Knowledge Graph Relationships** (Is-a, Part-of, Owns).
3. Anchor entities to Wikidata/Wikipedia IDs.

## Phase 2: Omnichannel 3.0 Research (Beyond Google)
1. **SERP Gap**: Identify thin spots in Top 10.
2. **Social Discovery**: Scrape Reddit, Discord, and niche forums for:
   - "Unspoken" user pain points.
   - community-specific terminology.
   - Debunked myths or common debates.
3. **Evidence Audit**: Identify what original data or assets competitors lack.

## Phase 3: Multimodal Pillar & Spoke
1. **Pillar Page**: Comprehensive Entity Guide (3,000+ words).
2. **Cluster Spokes**: Addressing "Sub-Queries" found in Social Discovery.
3. **Multimodal Attribution**: Every piece of content must have a unique visual asset (Chart, Data Table, Evidence Screenshot).

## Phase 4: Share of AI Voice (KPI)
1. Use `analyze_share_of_voice` to track citation status.
2. Optimize for **"Relationship Fact"** snippets to displace current citations.
