# Mad SEO Writer: V6 Qualitative & Citation Standards

## 🔬 The "MAD" Audit: Top 10 Growth Killers
If any of these fail, the article loses its "Expert" status in AI discovery.

1.  **Entity Grounding (C01)**: Primary entity defined in the first 50 words.
2.  **Multimodal Asset (M01)**: Inclusion of a custom table, chart, or screenshot to add "Original Value".
3.  **Fabrication Check (F01)**: Verification that personal "Experience" markers are realistic and not hallucinated.
4.  **Omnichannel Citation (O01)**: Citing community sentiment or social proof (Reddit/Forums).
5.  **Direct Answer (A01)**: Direct, chunked answer (40-60 words) immediately after H2 headers.
6.  **Sentence Chunking (S01)**: Standalone factual sentences for easy AI extraction.
7.  **Knowledge Graph Anchoring (K01)**: Linking entities to their Wikidata/Wikipedia relationship parent.
8.  **Freshness Signal (F02)**: Current month/year mentioned and verified data source.
9.  **LSI Density (L01)**: Presence of 3-5 sub-entities discovered during Social Discovery.
10. **Interactive Potential (I01)**: Suggestion for a tool, calculator, or interactive walkthrough.

## 🏆 CORE-EEAT Benchmark V6.0 (Omichannel Edition)
*Score Range: 0-100 Points*

### I. Competence & Intent (25 pts)
- **C01 Intent Alignment**: Content matches search sub-queries.
- **C02 Direct Answer Efficiency**: Answer provided before the first scroll.

### II. Objectivity & Structure (25 pts)
- **O01 Relationship Mapping**: Using "A is a type of B" logic in drafting.
- **O02 Tabular Precision**: At least 1 rich data table per 1,000 words.

### III. Reliability & Proof (25 pts)
- **R01 Source Diversity**: Links to official docs + community discussions.
- **R02 Data Recency**: No data points older than 18 months.

### IV. Experience (X) - HCU MANDATORY (25 pts)
- **X01 Human-Centric Perspective**: Unique take not found in Top-10 competitors.
- **X02 Visual Evidence**: Reference to actual usage, testing, or screenshots.
- **X03 Community Context**: Acknowledging common user debates or myths.

"Compare [Entity A] and [Entity B] based on [Metric 1], [Metric 2], and [Metric 3]."

**For Search Generative Experience (SGE)**:
Lead with "The key relationship between [Entity A] and [Topic] is [Relationship Fact]."
