# Mad SEO: Local SEO & Compliance Benchmarks

This reference governs the auditing and optimization of local business signals and Google Business Profile (GBP) health.

## 1. The NAP Consistency Standard
**NAP** = Name, Address, Phone.
- **Rule**: The business details must be identical across the website, GBP, and all third-party directories (Yelp, Industry Lists, etc.).
- **Audit Signal**: Flag any discrepancy in phone format or address abbreviations (e.g., "St." vs "Street").

## 2. Google Business Profile (GBP) Must-Haves
- **Primary Category**: Must match the core business intent.
- **Additional Categories**: Up to 5 related categories to capture broader intent.
- **Freshness**: At least 1 update/post per week.
- **Review Velocity**: Respond to 100% of reviews within 24-48 hours.
- **Photo Health**: 5+ new high-quality photos added monthly.

## 3. Local Citation Priority
1. **Google Business Profile** (Foundational)
2. **Yelp** (High Authority)
3. **Industry-Specific** (e.g., Avvo for lawyers, Houzz for home pros)
4. **Local Chamber of Commerce** (Trust Signal)
5. **Local News/Blog Mentions** (Authority Signal)

## 4. Local Keyword Optimization
Audit for the presence of:
- `[Service] [City Name]` in H1 and Title.
- `[Profession] [Neighborhood]` in H2.
- "Near me" intent coverage in Meta Descriptions.
