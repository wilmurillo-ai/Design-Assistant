## 摄影类

### 人像摄影
- 关键词: portrait photography, 85mm f/1.4 lens, shallow depth of field, bokeh, studio lighting
- 质量标签: catchlight in eyes, hair strand detail, natural skin tones
- 避免: cartoon, illustration, painting, anime, 3d render
- 构图: 极端特写、三分法
- 光线: 柔和主光+补光，可选轮廓光

### 电影写真
- 关键词: cinematic still frame, anamorphic lens, film grain, color grading
- 质量标签: volumetric lighting, atmospheric haze, depth of field
- 避免: flat lighting, stock photo, overexposed, cartoon
- 构图: 荷兰角、全景建立、过肩
- 光线: 实用光源、明暗对比

### 风景
- 关键词: landscape photography, wide angle 16mm, golden hour, atmospheric perspective
- 质量标签: sharp foreground to infinity, natural color, HDR detail
- 构图: 全景建立、三分法
- 光线: 黄金时段或蓝调时刻

### 赛博朋克
- 关键词: cyberpunk aesthetic, neon lights, rain-slicked streets, holographic
- 质量标签: neon glow reflections, wet surface specular, volumetric fog

- 光线: 霓虹实用光、高对比、人造光
