# 构图模式参考

| 构图 | 英文术语 | prompt 片段 | 适用场景 | 叙事权重 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 三分法 | Rule of Thirds | rule of thirds composition, subject at intersection point | 人像、风景、对话、日常 | 均衡 |
| 荷兰角 | Dutch Angle | dutch angle camera tilt, tilted horizon, creating tension | 悬疑、冲突、追逐 | 紧张 |
| 库布里克对称 | Kubrick Symmetry | Kubrick-style symmetrical composition, single point perspective, centered framing | 建筑、仪式、压迫感 | 氛围 |
| 过肩镜头 | Over-the-Shoulder | over-the-shoulder shot, foreground silhouette, focus on subject ahead | 对话、对峙、情感 | 连接 |
| 极端特写 | Extreme Close-up | extreme close-up, filling frame with detail, shallow depth of field | 情感、细节、紧张 | 情绪 |
| 全景建立 | Wide Establishing | wide establishing shot, full environment visible, character small in vast scene | 开场、转场、环境 | 背景 |
| 低角度英雄 | Low Angle Hero | low angle hero shot, camera looking up, subject appears powerful | 英雄、力量、胜利 | 力量 |
| 中心构图 | Center Subject | centered composition, subject at exact center, symmetrical balance | 卡通、插画、儿童 | 清晰 |
| 引导线 | Leading Lines | leading lines composition, road or path drawing eye toward subject | 旅程、街道、行走 | 旅途 |
| 框中框 | Frame Within Frame | frame within frame, subject framed by doorway or window | 窥视、距离、悬念 | 距离 |
| 留白 | Negative Space | negative space composition, large empty area emphasizing solitude | 孤独、思考、余韵 | 沉思 |
| 对角线 | Diagonal | diagonal composition, dynamic energy along diagonal axis | 运动、追逐、动态 | 能量 |
