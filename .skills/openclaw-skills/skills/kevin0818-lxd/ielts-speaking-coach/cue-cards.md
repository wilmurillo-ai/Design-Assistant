# IELTS Part 2 Cue Cards

Standard cue card templates for Practice Session mode. Each card is tagged with a difficulty level (Band range) and topic category. Present one cue card at a time; the user has 1 minute to prepare and 1-2 minutes to speak.

**2025 May–August 题库**: See [cue-cards-2025-may-aug.md](cue-cards-2025-may-aug.md) for 15 official cue cards from the current question bank.

**Difficulty legend**: [Easy] = Band 4-5.5 | [Medium] = Band 5.5-7 | [Hard] = Band 7+

---

## 人物 Person

### 1. A person who influenced you [Easy]

**Describe someone who has had a significant influence on your life.**

You should say:
- who the person is
- how you know them
- what they have done that influenced you
- and explain why this influence was important to you

---

### 2. A teacher you remember [Easy]

**Describe a teacher who had a positive impact on you.**

You should say:
- who this teacher is/was
- what subject they taught
- what made them special
- and explain how they influenced your learning

---

### 3. A person you admire [Medium]

**Describe someone you admire who is not famous.**

You should say:
- who this person is
- what they do
- what qualities they have
- and explain why you admire them

---

### 4. A person who is good at their job [Medium]

**Describe someone you know who is very good at their job.**

You should say:
- who this person is
- what their job is
- how you know them
- and explain why you think they are good at their job

---

### 5. A person with a different opinion [Hard]

**Describe a person whose opinion you disagree with but still respect.**

You should say:
- who this person is
- what opinion they hold
- why you disagree
- and explain why you still respect them

---
