# IELTS Part 2 Cue Cards — 2025 May–August 题库

Standard cue cards from the 2025 May–August IELTS Speaking question bank. Use for Practice Session mode. Source: 老烤鸭雅思 / 鸭圈 2025年5–8月口语题库. Present one at a time; 1 minute preparation, 1–2 minutes to speak.

---

## 人物类 Person

### 1. Describe a person you have met who you want to work or study with 想要一起学习/工作的人

You should say:
- who this person is
- how you met this person
- how long you have known him/her
- and explain why you want to work or study with him/her

---

### 2. Describe a person who you think wears unusual clothes 奇装异服的人

You should say:
- who this person is
- how you know him/her
- what kind of clothes he/she wears
- and explain why you think his/her clothes are unusual

---

### 3. Describe a person from a different cultural background with whom you enjoy spending time 不同文化的朋友

You should say:
- who this person is
- how you met this person
- what you do together
- and explain why you enjoy spending time with him/her

---

### 4. Describe a friend from your childhood 发小/童年朋友

You should say:
- who this person is
- how you became friends
- what you used to do together
- and explain why you remember this person

---

### 5. Describe a person who inspired you to do something interesting 激励你做有趣事情的人

You should say:
- who this person is
- what he/she inspired you to do
- how he/she inspired you
- and explain why this was interesting to you

---

### 6. Describe a famous person you would like to meet 想见的名人

You should say:
- who this person is
- what he/she is famous for
- how you first learned about him/her
- and explain why you would like to meet this person

---

## 地点类 Place

### 7. Describe a popular place for sports (e.g. a stadium) that you've been to 运动场所

You should say:
- where it is
- when you went there
- what you did there
- and explain how you felt about this place

---

### 8. Describe a city that you have been to and would like to visit again 想再去一次的城市

You should say:
- which city it is
- when you went there
- what you did there
- and explain why you would like to visit it again

---

### 9. Describe a shop/store you enjoy visiting 商店

You should say:
- what kind of shop it is
- where it is
- how often you go there
- and explain why you enjoy visiting it

---

### 10. Describe a beautiful sky you enjoyed seeing 美丽天空

You should say:
- when and where you saw it
- what the sky looked like
- what you were doing at the time
- and explain why you enjoyed it

---

### 11. Describe a place in your country or part of your country that you would like to recommend to visitors 向游客推荐本国旅游地

You should say:
- where it is
- what it is like
- what visitors can do there
- and explain why you would recommend it

---

## 事件类 Event

### 12. Describe your first day at school that you remember 开学第一天

You should say:
- when it was
- where you were
- what happened
- and how you felt during that day

---

### 13. Describe a problem you had while shopping online or in a store 糟糕购物

You should say:
- what you bought
- what the problem was
- how you handled it
- and explain how you felt about this experience

---

### 14. Describe a time when you helped someone 帮助他人

You should say:
- who you helped
- when and where it happened
- what you did to help
- and explain how you felt about helping

---

### 15. Describe a time when you were very busy 忙碌时刻

You should say:
- when it was
- what you were busy with
- how you managed your time
- and explain how you felt about being busy
