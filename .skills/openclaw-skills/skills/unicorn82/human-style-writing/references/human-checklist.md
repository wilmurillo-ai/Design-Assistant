# Human-likeness QA Checklist (score 0–2 each)

Use as the final gate before sending.

1) **Purpose first**: Does the first line reveal intent quickly?
2) **Situation**: Is there at least one believable situational anchor (time/constraint/context) *when appropriate*?
3) **Specificity**: Are there concrete nouns/numbers/examples instead of vague words?
4) **Rhythm**: Are sentence lengths varied? Any overly uniform paragraphs?
5) **Voice**: Does it sound like one person (consistent persona), not a committee?
6) **No meta**: Removed assistant phrases ("希望对你有帮助" etc.)?
7) **No repetition**: Same point said once, not 3 times?
8) **Politeness calibration**: Correct 你/您, honorifics, sign-offs?
9) **Factual integrity**: No invented facts, quotes, or experiences?
10) **Genre correctness**: Matches register conventions (chat/email/paper/news) and avoids genre-specific AI tells (e.g., news/press boilerplate padding)?

**Interpretation**
- 16–20: good
- 12–15: revise once
- ≤11: rewrite with a clearer style card
