## Theoretical Frameworks

1. **Anti-Vision Concept** — Dan Koe
   - Technique for creating a visceral image of the life you don't want

2. **Teleological Psychology** — Alfred Adler
   - All behavior is goal-oriented
   - "Trust only movement. Life happens at the level of events, not of words."

3. **7-Step Identity Formation** — Dan Koe (synthesized)
   - Goal → Perception → Learning → Action → Conditioning → Identity → Defense

4. **Cybernetics** — Norbert Wiener
   - Goal → Act → Sense → Compare → Iterate
   - "The art of getting what you want"

5. **Ego Development Stages** — Synthesized from:
   - Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs
   - Loevinger's Ego Development Theory
   - Spiral Dynamics (Clare Graves, Don Beck)
   - Integral Theory (Ken Wilber)

6. **Flow State / Gamification** — Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi
   - "The optimal state of inner experience is one in which there is order in consciousness"

7. **Dissonance → Uncertainty → Discovery** — Dan Koe
   - Three phases of identity transformation
