## Love Tendencies

Aquarius needs plenty of personal space and intellectual stimulation in a relationship. They aren't drawn to conventional expressions of romance — what they truly value is a meeting of minds. They make loyal, non-clingy partners who love deeply on their own terms.

## Career Tendencies

Innovation and technology are Aquarius's natural playground — IT, social activism, aviation, and invention are all ideal paths. They are born reformers and futurists who can feel trapped in conventional or rigidly structured environments.

## Finance Tendencies

Aquarius is largely indifferent to wealth as a measure of success. Money is a means to an end — a tool for pursuing ideals and making an impact rather than personal luxury. Their investment style tends toward the cutting-edge and unconventional.
