# Niche Market Insight Skill

## Description

This skill provides in-depth market analysis for a specific industry and geographical region. It helps users understand market trends, consumer sentiment, emerging technologies, and identify potential business opportunities within a niche market.

## 功能描述 (Description)

本技能旨在为特定行业和地理区域提供深度市场分析。它帮助用户了解细分市场的趋势、消费者情绪、新兴技术，并识别潜在的商业机会。

## Usage

To use this skill, specify the `industry` and `region` you are interested in.

**English:**
`niche-market-insight --industry "AI Healthcare" --region "Southeast Asia"`

**Chinese:**
`niche-market-insight --industry "AI医疗" --region "东南亚"`

## 使用方法 (Usage)

使用本技能时，请指定您感兴趣的`行业 (industry)`和`地域 (region)`。

**中文示例:**
`niche-market-insight --industry "AI医疗" --region "东南亚"`

**English Example:**
`niche-market-insight --industry "AI Healthcare" --region "Southeast Asia"`

## Parameters

*   `--industry`: The specific industry to analyze (e.g., "Sustainable Fashion", "Renewable Energy", "AI Healthcare").
*   `--region`: The geographical region for the analysis (e.g., "Europe", "North America", "Southeast Asia", "China").

## 参数说明 (Parameters)

*   `--industry`: 需要分析的特定行业（例如："可持续时尚"、"可再生能源"、"AI医疗"）。
*   `--region`: 分析的地理区域（例如："欧洲"、"北美"、"东南亚"、"中国"）。
