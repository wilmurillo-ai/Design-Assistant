## Skill Name

## Tagline
Professional contracts for photographers, videographers, and creatives in minutes. Describe the project, get a complete contract with copyright protection, usage licences, and UK legal references -- ready for signature.

## Category
Creative & Legal

## Description

**The problem:** Most photographers and videographers use free templates they found online, or pay a solicitor GBP 200+ for bespoke contracts. Those free templates are usually generic, miss critical clauses (AI training prohibition, GDPR compliance, moral rights assertion), and often misstate who owns copyright. Meanwhile, copyright and usage rights are the single most misunderstood area in creative work -- clients routinely assume they own images they have paid for, when UK law says otherwise.

**What this skill does:** Describe a creative project in plain English. Get back a complete, UK-law-grounded contract:

- 9 contract types covering the full creative workflow: client agreements, model releases, property releases, usage licences, second shooter agreements, print sales terms, gallery/exhibition agreements, and social media usage agreements
- Copyright ownership correctly assigned to the creator by default (CDPA 1988 s.11)
- Moral rights asserted in every contract (CDPA 1988 s.77, s.78)
- AI/ML training prohibition clause included as standard
- GDPR-compliant data protection clauses for personal images
- Usage licence pricing guidance with multiplier tables
- Package pricing structure templates
- Consumer Rights Act 2015 compliance for B2C contracts
- Plain English model releases that non-lawyers can understand and sign

**Two modes:**
- **Full contract mode** -- complete numbered-clause contract with all sections, schedules, and signature blocks
- **Quick mode** -- concise single-purpose document for straightforward situations (e.g., a simple model release)

**Why creatives pay for this:** A single bespoke contract from a solicitor costs GBP 200-500. A photographer might need 5-10 different contract types across their business. That is GBP 1,000-5,000 in legal fees for documents that still need updating when circumstances change. This skill generates tailored contracts in minutes for a fraction of the cost.
