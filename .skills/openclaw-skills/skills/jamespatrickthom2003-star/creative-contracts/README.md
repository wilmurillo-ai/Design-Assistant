# Photography & Creative Contracts Generator

Professional contracts for photographers, videographers, and creative professionals. Describe your project, get a complete contract ready for signature.

## What It Does

Describe a shoot, project, or licensing situation. Get back a legally grounded contract, release form, or licence agreement based on UK law:

- Client contracts for photography and videography (wedding, commercial, corporate, portrait, product, real estate)
- Model release forms (adult, minor, group, sensitive content)
- Property and location releases (residential, commercial, heritage)
- Usage rights and licensing agreements (editorial, commercial, exclusive, buyout)
- Second shooter and assistant agreements with proper IP assignment
- Print sales terms and limited edition certificates
- Exhibition and gallery loan agreements
- Social media usage agreements with platform-specific terms

## Contract Types

1. **Photography Client Contract** -- event, portrait, commercial, product, real estate
2. **Videography Client Contract** -- wedding, corporate, music video, documentary, social content
3. **Model Release Form** -- adult, minor (guardian consent), group/crowd, sensitive content
4. **Property/Location Release** -- residential, commercial, public space, heritage/listed building
5. **Usage Rights and Licensing** -- editorial, commercial, exclusive/non-exclusive, full buyout
6. **Second Shooter/Assistant Agreement** -- IP assignment, delivery, payment, non-compete
7. **Print Sales Terms** -- fine art, limited editions, reproduction rights
8. **Exhibition/Gallery Agreement** -- loan terms, insurance, commission, return conditions
9. **Social Media Usage Agreement** -- platform-specific rights, tagging, duration, removal

## Key Features

- **Copyright-first by default.** Creator retains copyright in every contract (CDPA 1988). Rights are licensed, not transferred, unless explicitly requested.
- **UK law grounded.** References the Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1988, Consumer Rights Act 2015, UK GDPR, and Data Protection Act 2018.
- **AI training prohibition.** Every contract includes a clause preventing use of images in AI/ML model training.
- **GDPR compliant.** Data protection clauses included for any contract involving personal images.
- **Plain English releases.** Model and property releases are written so non-lawyers can understand them.
- **Pricing guidance.** Usage licence multiplier tables and package structure templates included.

## How It Works

1. Describe your project or situation
2. The skill identifies the correct contract type
3. If essential details are missing, it asks up to 3 questions
4. Generates a complete, numbered-clause contract in clean markdown
5. Includes all required legal clauses, adapted to your specific situation
6. Add your details, review, and send to the client
