# Agent37 Listing — UK AML/KYC Compliance Documentation Generator

## Skill Name
UK AML/KYC Compliance Documentation Generator

## Tagline
Generate complete AML/KYC compliance documentation for UK regulated businesses — CDD records, risk assessments, policies, SAR drafts, and full onboarding packs. 15 document types covering all 8 regulated sectors, citing MLR 2017, POCA 2002, and current UK legislation.

## Category
Legal & Compliance

## Description

**The problem:** Every estate agent, solicitor, accountant, and financial advisor in the UK must maintain AML/KYC documentation or face criminal prosecution, regulatory fines, and loss of authorisation. Compliance consultants charge thousands for template packs. Commercial verification services charge per check. Most small firms cobble together inadequate documentation and hope for the best — until the HMRC inspection letter arrives.

**What this skill does:** Tell it your business type and what you need. Get back complete, regulation-compliant AML documentation in seconds. 15 document types covering the full compliance lifecycle:

- Customer Due Diligence records (standard, enhanced, simplified)
- Source of Funds and Source of Wealth declarations
- PEP and Sanctions screening documentation records
- Suspicious Activity Report drafts (internal, for MLRO submission to NCA)
- Firm-Wide Risk Assessments (mandatory under Regulation 18 MLR 2017)
- AML/KYC Policy documents tailored to your sector and supervisory body
- Client Risk Assessment matrices with weighted scoring methodology
- Staff training records, beneficial ownership verification, ongoing monitoring
- Third-party reliance records for introduced business

Every document cites specific legislation — MLR 2017 regulation numbers, POCA 2002 sections, Terrorism Act 2000 provisions, SAMLA 2018, and the Economic Crime and Corporate Transparency Act 2023. Documents are tailored to your regulated sector and supervisory body.

**What it does not do:** This generates documentation, not verification. It cannot perform ID checks, screen PEP/sanctions databases, or conduct electronic verification. Those require specialist services (SmartSearch, Onfido, Thirdfort). The skill makes this limitation explicit on every output.

**Who it's for:** Any UK business in the regulated sector — estate agents, solicitors, accountants, IFAs, TCSPs, high-value dealers, art market participants, and gambling operators.

**Why it's better than buying templates:** Templates go stale. Legislation changes. This skill generates documents using current law, tailored to your specific sector, with the correct supervisory body references and risk factors. No annual template subscription. No outdated regulation numbers.
