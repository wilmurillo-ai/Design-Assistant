## Why This Exists

Every estate agent, solicitor, accountant, financial advisor, and high-value dealer in the UK is legally required to maintain AML/KYC documentation. The consequences of getting it wrong are severe:

- Criminal prosecution for failure to report suspicious activity (s.330 POCA 2002 — up to 5 years' imprisonment)
- Tipping-off offences (s.333A POCA 2002 — up to 5 years' imprisonment)
- Regulatory fines running into hundreds of thousands of pounds
- Loss of practising certificates and authorisation
- Reputational damage

Commercial verification services charge per check. Documentation templates from compliance consultants cost thousands. This skill generates the documentation framework so you can focus your budget on the actual verification services that matter.

## Important Limitations

This skill generates AML/KYC documentation templates and records. It does **not**:

- Perform identity verification or ID document checks
- Screen against PEP or sanctions databases
- Conduct electronic verification (e-verification)
- Replace specialist verification services (SmartSearch, Onfido, Thirdfort, Credas)
- Constitute legal advice

Always verify information through appropriate channels and seek qualified legal advice for complex situations.
