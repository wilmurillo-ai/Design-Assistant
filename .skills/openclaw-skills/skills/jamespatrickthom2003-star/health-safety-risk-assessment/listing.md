# Agent37 Listing -- UK Health & Safety Risk Assessment Generator

## Skill Name
UK Health & Safety Risk Assessment Generator

## Tagline
HSE-compliant risk assessments in minutes. Describe the work, get a complete assessment with hazards, controls, risk ratings, and legal references -- ready for review.

## Category
Health & Safety / Compliance

## Description

**The problem:** Every UK employer is legally required to have risk assessments. The HSE template is a blank table. Most small business owners stare at it, guess, and hope for the best. Health and safety consultants charge GBP 200-500 per assessment. With 5.5 million businesses in the UK, that is a lot of money spent on what is fundamentally a structured document.

**What this skill does:** Describe a workplace or activity in plain English. Get back a complete, legally referenced UK risk assessment:

- Hazard identification with specific, named controls (not generic waffle)
- Risk ratings using the standard likelihood x severity matrix
- Action plans with owners and deadlines
- Legal references to HSWA, MHSWR, COSHH, CDM, RRO, and more
- Formatted to the HSE 5-step method
- 10 assessment types: general, COSHH, fire, manual handling, DSE, lone working, working at height, construction method statements, new/expectant mothers, young workers
- 11 industry presets: office, construction, restaurant, retail, warehouse, manufacturing, school, salon, gym, farm, events

**Two modes:**
- **Quick mode** -- describe the activity, get an instant assessment
- **Detailed mode** -- guided questions for high-risk activities (construction, COSHH, working at height)

**Why businesses pay for this:** A single risk assessment from a consultant costs GBP 200-500. The HSE can fine businesses up to GBP 20,000 in a magistrates' court for failing to have suitable and sufficient risk assessments (MHSWR Reg 3). An unlimited fine in the Crown Court. The cost of this skill is a fraction of one consultant visit.
