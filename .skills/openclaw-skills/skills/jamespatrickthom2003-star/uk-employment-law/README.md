# UK Employment Law Calculator & Letter Generator

Instant statutory employment calculations and professional HR letters for UK businesses. Built for 2025/26 with full ERA 2025 coverage.

## The Problem

UK employment law changed dramatically in 2025. Day-one unfair dismissal protection, new paternity rights, mandatory harassment prevention duties, fire-and-rehire restrictions, zero-hours contract reforms. Every employer needs to know what these mean in practice.

HR consultants charge £75-150/hour. Employment solicitors charge more. Most small businesses wing it — and get it wrong.

Getting a redundancy calculation wrong costs you a tribunal claim. Sending a disciplinary letter without mentioning the right to be accompanied breaches the ACAS Code. Missing the new flexible working response deadline (now 2 months, not 3) opens you up to a complaint.

## What This Does

**Calculations** — give it employee details, get back exact statutory entitlements:
- Holiday entitlement (including the 12.07% method for irregular hours workers)
- Statutory Sick Pay (£116.75/week, qualifying days, waiting days)
- Notice periods (1 week per year, capped at 12)
- Redundancy pay (age-banded, capped at £700/week, max 20 years)
- Maternity pay (90% for 6 weeks, then £187.18 for 33 weeks)
- Paternity pay (£187.18/week, now a day-one right)
- Shared Parental Leave/Pay
- National Minimum/Living Wage by age band

**Letters** — describe the situation, get a compliant letter:
- Disciplinary invitations (with right to be accompanied)
- Written warnings (first and final)
- Dismissal letters (conduct, capability, redundancy, SOSR)
- Grievance acknowledgement and outcomes
- Flexible working responses (day-one right under ERA 2025)
- Redundancy consultation letters
- Settlement agreement covering letters
- Factual references
- Probation reviews (pass, fail, extend)
- Right to work check records

**ERA 2025 Guidance** — flags every relevant change so you do not get caught out.

```

Check what's changed:
```

```

```
