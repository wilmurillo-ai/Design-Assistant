# Agent37 Listing -- Property Listing & Rental Yield Calculator

## Skill Name
Property Listing & Rental Yield Calculator

## Tagline
Professional property listings and rental yield analysis for UK landlords and agents. Marketing copy, SDLT calculations, cash flow modelling, Section 24 tax impact, and ROI projections -- all from one prompt.

## Category
Property & Investment

## Description

**The problem:** 2.65 million UK landlords and 35,000 estate/letting agents juggle two constant needs: writing property listings and running yield calculations. Online calculators show gross yield but ignore Section 24 tax relief restrictions, void periods, leasehold costs, and the 5% additional property stamp duty surcharge. Writing a decent listing still takes 30-60 minutes per property.

**What this skill does:** Describe the property and get both the marketing copy and the investment numbers in one pass.

**Listing generation:**
- Rightmove/Zoopla-ready descriptions with keyword-rich headlines
- Key features, room-by-room walkthrough, local area highlights
- Five tone options: premium, family-friendly, investment, first-time buyer, professional let
- EPC, tenure, and council tax details structured for portal upload

**Investment analysis:**
- Gross and net rental yield with all costs itemised
- Stamp duty (SDLT) for standard, additional property (+5%), and first-time buyers
- Monthly cash flow breakdown: mortgage, agent fees, insurance, maintenance, voids
- Section 24 mortgage interest tax impact with worked examples for each tax band
- ROI projections at 5, 10, and 25 years with configurable assumptions
- HMO per-room yield analysis with licensing and enhanced management costs
- Comparable rental framework for local market benchmarking

**Why it's better than a spreadsheet or online calculator:** It handles the SDLT surcharge correctly (5% from October 2024, not the old 3%), models Section 24 impact by tax band, includes all the costs landlords forget (EICR, gas safety, void allowance, licence fees), and generates the listing copy alongside the numbers. One input, complete output.

**Who it's for:** Buy-to-let landlords, portfolio investors, letting agents, estate agents, property managers, and first-time landlords evaluating their first purchase.
