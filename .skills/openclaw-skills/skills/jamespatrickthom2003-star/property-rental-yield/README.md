# Property Listing & Rental Yield Calculator

Professional property listings and investment analysis for UK landlords and letting agents. Listing copy and yield calculations in one skill.

## What It Does

Two things that every landlord and agent needs, combined:

**Property Listings**
Give it the property details. Get back portal-ready marketing copy you can paste straight into Rightmove, Zoopla, or OnTheMarket:

- Keyword-rich headline
- Engaging opening paragraph
- Key features bullet list
- Room-by-room descriptions
- Local area highlights
- EPC and tenure details

Five tone options: premium/luxury, family-friendly, investment opportunity, first-time buyer, professional let.

**Investment Analysis**
Give it the purchase price and expected rent. Get back a full financial breakdown:

- Gross and net rental yield
- Stamp duty (SDLT) with additional property surcharge
- Monthly cash flow with all costs itemised
- Section 24 mortgage tax impact
- ROI projections at 5, 10, and 25 years
- Comparable rental framework
- HMO multi-let analysis

## Quick Start

Yield check:
```
/property-rental-yield 200k property, 950/month rent
```

Full analysis:
```
/property-rental-yield 3-bed semi in Leeds, 185k, rents for 850/month, I'm a higher-rate taxpayer
```

Listing only:
```
/property-rental-yield write a listing for a 2-bed flat in Manchester, recently refurbished, balcony, concierge
```

Stamp duty:
```
/property-rental-yield stamp duty on a 350k buy-to-let
```

Compare properties:
```
/property-rental-yield compare: 150k 2-bed at 700/month vs 250k 3-bed at 1100/month
```

## Why It Exists

2.65 million UK landlords and 35,000 estate/letting agents need two things constantly: marketing copy and yield calculations. Currently they use separate tools for each — generic online calculators that miss Section 24 implications, void period adjustments, and leasehold costs, plus hours writing listing descriptions from scratch.

This skill does both in one pass. Describe the property, get the listing copy and the investment numbers together.

## Who It's For

- Buy-to-let landlords evaluating purchases
- Portfolio landlords comparing opportunities
- Letting agents writing property descriptions
- Estate agents marketing rental investments
- Property investors running due diligence
- First-time landlords who need guidance on costs

## Key Features

- **2025/26 SDLT rates** including the 5% additional property surcharge (October 2024)
- **Section 24 tax analysis** showing real impact on higher-rate taxpayers
- **Net yield** calculations with all costs itemised (not just gross yield like most calculators)
- **Cash flow modelling** with mortgage payments, agent fees, insurance, maintenance, voids
- **ROI projections** with configurable capital growth and rent increase assumptions
- **HMO analysis** with per-room yields and licensing costs
- **Portal-ready listings** in multiple tones, structured for Rightmove/Zoopla

## Rates Used

All calculations use current 2025/26 figures:
- SDLT additional property surcharge: 5% (from October 2024)
- First-time buyer nil-rate band: £425,000
- Typical BTL mortgage rates: 5-7%
- Section 24: 20% tax credit on mortgage interest (not deductible)
- Corporation tax: 25% (for incorporation comparison)
