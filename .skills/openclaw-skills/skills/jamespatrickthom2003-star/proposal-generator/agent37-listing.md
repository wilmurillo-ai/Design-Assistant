# Agent37 Listing — Freelancer Proposal & SOW Generator

## Skill Name
Freelancer Proposal & SOW Generator

## Tagline
Client-ready proposals in minutes. Describe the project, get a complete proposal with scope, pricing, timeline, and terms — ready to send.

## Category
Business Operations

## Description

**The problem:** Freelancers spend 3-5 hours writing proposals. Most of that is formatting and structure, not thinking. And a bad proposal loses the project regardless of talent.

**What this skill does:** Describe a project opportunity in plain English. Get back a complete, professional proposal:

- Opening that addresses the client's specific problem
- Scope of work with phased deliverables, timelines, and review points
- Pricing options (project-based or day rate) with payment terms
- Terms, revision limits, IP transfer, and cancellation
- Industry-specific language for tech, design, marketing, and consulting
- Quick quote mode for simple jobs

**Why freelancers pay for this:** A good proposal can win a £5,000+ project. The ROI on a few quid is obvious.

## Example Prompts
- "Website redesign for a Leeds law firm, budget around £5k"
- "Quote for 5 landing pages with CMS, £2k"
- "6-month content strategy retainer for a SaaS startup"
- "Brand identity project for a new restaurant, they want logo, menu design, signage"
