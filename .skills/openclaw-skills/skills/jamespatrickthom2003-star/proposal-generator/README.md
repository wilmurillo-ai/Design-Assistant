# Freelancer Proposal & SOW Generator

Client-ready proposals in minutes, not hours. Describe the project, get a complete proposal with scope, pricing, timeline, and terms.

## What It Does

Give it a project description. Get back a professional proposal you can send to the client immediately:

- Opening that references their specific problem
- Scope of work with phased deliverables and timelines
- Pricing (project-based or day rate) with payment terms
- Terms, revision rounds, and next steps
- Industry-specific language (tech, design, marketing, consulting)

## Quick Start

```
/proposal-generator website redesign for a Leeds law firm, budget around £5k
```

Or for a quick quote:
```
/proposal-generator quote for 5 landing pages, £2k
```

## Why It Exists

The average freelancer spends 3-5 hours writing a proposal. Most of that time is formatting, not thinking. This skill handles the structure so you focus on the strategy.

A good proposal can win a £5,000+ project. Spending £5-10 to generate it is a no-brainer.

## Who It's For

- Freelancers responding to briefs
- Consultants pitching new clients
- Agencies quoting project work
- Anyone who hates writing proposals (everyone)
