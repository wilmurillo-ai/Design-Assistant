# UK Teacher Lesson Plans & Assessment Generator

Curriculum-aligned lesson plans, differentiated worksheets, assessment questions with mark schemes, and student report comments. Tell it what you are teaching and the year group -- get a complete, ready-to-use lesson plan.

## What It Does

Describe what you are teaching. Get back classroom-ready resources aligned to the National Curriculum, GCSE specifications, or A-Level specifications:

- Full lesson plans in standard UK format (objectives, starter, main, plenary, differentiation, SEND)
- Differentiated worksheets at three levels (Foundation, Core, Extension)
- Assessment questions with full mark schemes (multiple choice, short answer, extended response, exam-style)
- Bloom's Taxonomy progression across all assessments
- Student report comments (personalised, parent-friendly, target-focused)
- Curriculum maps and scheme of work overviews

## Output Types

1. **Lesson Plan** -- standard UK format with learning objectives (all/most/some), starter, main activity with 3-way differentiation, plenary, SEND considerations, and resources list
2. **Differentiated Worksheets** -- Foundation (scaffolded, visual, sentence starters), Core (standard expectations), Extension (open-ended, analytical, deeper thinking)
3. **Assessment Questions** -- multiple choice with distractors, short answer, extended response, exam-style with mark schemes
4. **Mark Schemes** -- levels-based or point-based depending on exam board, with indicative content
5. **Report Comments** -- personalised from name, subject, attainment, effort, and observations. Strengths, areas for development, targets
6. **Curriculum Map** -- term or year overview with weekly topics, NC references, vocabulary, and assessment points

## Exam Board Support

- **AQA** -- levels-based mark schemes, most popular in England
- **Edexcel (Pearson)** -- point-based marking, strong in maths and science
- **OCR** -- levels of response, AO-linked marking
- **WJEC / Eduqas** -- banded mark schemes, dominant in Wales

If no exam board is specified for GCSE/A-Level, defaults to AQA.

## How It Works

1. Tell it what you are teaching (subject, topic, year group)
2. Optionally specify: exam board, lesson duration, ability range, output type
3. Get a complete resource ready to use or adapt

**Quick mode** generates a full lesson plan immediately from a topic and year group.

**Detailed mode** asks up to 5 questions to tailor the output to your class (ability range, SEND provision, exam board, lesson focus).
