# Agent37 Listing -- UK Teacher Lesson Plans & Assessment Generator

## Skill Name
UK Teacher Lesson Plans & Assessment Generator

## Tagline
Curriculum-aligned lesson plans, worksheets, and assessments in minutes. Describe what you are teaching -- get classroom-ready resources with differentiation, mark schemes, and SEND support built in.

## Category
Education

## Description

**The problem:** UK teachers spend an average of 5-7 hours per week on lesson planning and resource creation. Existing platforms like TES and Twinkl offer static, pre-made resources that rarely match the specific topic, year group, ability range, and exam board a teacher needs. The result: teachers download a generic worksheet and spend another hour adapting it. GCSE and A-Level teachers have it worse -- they need exam-board-specific mark schemes that match AQA, Edexcel, OCR, or WJEC formats exactly.

**What this skill does:** Tell it what you are teaching. Get back a complete, ready-to-use resource:

- **Lesson plans** in standard UK format -- learning objectives (all/most/some), starter, main activity with 3-way differentiation, plenary, SEND considerations, resources, assessment opportunities
- **Differentiated worksheets** at three levels -- Foundation (scaffolded, visual, sentence starters), Core (standard expectations), Extension (open-ended, analytical, deeper thinking)
- **Assessment questions with mark schemes** -- multiple choice with plausible distractors, short answer, extended response, GCSE/A-Level exam-style with levels-based or point-based mark schemes
- **Student report comments** -- personalised from name, subject, attainment, and effort. Not bank comments. Strengths, areas for development, actionable targets in parent-friendly language
- **Curriculum maps** -- term or year overview with weekly topics, NC references, key vocabulary, and assessment points

**Coverage:**
- EYFS through KS5 (Reception to Year 13)
- 20+ subjects including English, Maths, Science, Humanities, MFL, Computing, and more
- All four major exam boards: AQA, Edexcel, OCR, WJEC/Eduqas
- National Curriculum 2014 programme of study references built in
- Bloom's Taxonomy progression across all assessments
- SEND-aware output with specific adaptations (not generic "differentiate" statements)

**Two modes:**
- **Quick mode** -- describe what you are teaching, get a complete lesson plan instantly
- **Detailed mode** -- guided questions to tailor output to your specific class, ability range, and focus

**Why teachers pay for this:** A single differentiated lesson plan with worksheets, an assessment, and a mark scheme takes 1-2 hours to create from scratch. TES premium resources cost GBP 2-5 each and still need adapting. This skill generates tailored content from a single sentence, aligned to the exact year group, topic, and exam board.

- **vs TES/Twinkl:** Those are static resource libraries. This generates tailored content from a topic and year group. No browsing, no adapting, no "close enough."
