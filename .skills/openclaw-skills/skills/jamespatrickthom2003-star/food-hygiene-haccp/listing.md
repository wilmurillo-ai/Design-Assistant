## Skill Name

## Tagline
Generate inspection-ready UK food safety documentation in seconds. HACCP plans, allergen matrices, cleaning schedules, temperature logs — everything an EHO expects to see, tailored to your business type.

## Category
Food & Hospitality / Compliance

## Description

**The problem:** Every UK food business needs food safety documentation — it is a legal requirement under the Food Safety Act 1990 and Regulation (EC) No. 852/2004. Food hygiene consultants charge GBP 200-500. Template packs cost GBP 30-100 and still need customising. An EHO finding no HACCP documentation can score you a 0 rating or close you down.

**What this skill does:** Tell it your business type and what you need. Get back complete, legislation-compliant food safety documents:

- Full HACCP plans following all 7 Codex Alimentarius principles
- Allergen matrices covering all 14 UK allergens (Food Information Regulations 2014 / Natasha's Law)
- Food Safety Management Systems based on Safer Food Better Business (SFBB)
- Cleaning schedules with chemicals, dilution rates, frequencies, and contact times
- Temperature monitoring logs for fridges, freezers, cooking, hot holding, and deliveries
- Supplier approval records, staff training logs, pest control documentation
- Opening/closing checklists, delivery acceptance forms, waste management records
- Traceability and recall procedures
- EHO inspection preparation guidance

**14 document types total** — request individually or generate the full pack.

**Why it is better than buying templates:** Documents are tailored to your specific business type (restaurant, takeaway, food truck, bakery, school kitchen, care home, manufacturer — 11 presets). Content references actual UK legislation. Critical limits and monitoring procedures follow FSA guidance. Not blank forms with "insert here" — actual, realistic documentation.

**Who it is for:**
- Restaurant, cafe, and takeaway owners preparing for EHO inspection
- Food truck and mobile catering operators needing portable documentation
- New food businesses building their HACCP system from scratch
- School, hospital, nursery, and care home kitchen managers
- Food manufacturers needing production-scale HACCP plans
- Food safety consultants wanting to accelerate document production
- Anyone who has received a low food hygiene rating and needs to improve
