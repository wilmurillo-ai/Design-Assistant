# Food Hygiene & HACCP Documentation Generator

Generate inspection-ready food safety documentation for UK food businesses. HACCP plans, allergen matrices, cleaning schedules, temperature logs, and everything else an EHO expects to see.

## What It Does

Tell it your business type and what you need. Get back fully structured, legislation-compliant food safety documents in seconds:

- **HACCP Plans** following all 7 Codex Alimentarius principles — hazard analysis, CCPs, critical limits, monitoring, corrective actions, verification, record-keeping
- **Allergen Matrices** covering all 14 UK allergens mapped against your menu items
- **Food Safety Management Systems** based on Safer Food Better Business (SFBB)
- **Cleaning Schedules** with chemicals, dilution rates, frequencies, and contact times
- **Temperature Monitoring Logs** for fridges, freezers, cooking, hot holding, and deliveries
- **EHO Inspection Preparation** — what they check, how to score well, common failures

Plus 8 more document types: supplier approvals, staff training records, pest control documentation, food safety policy, opening/closing checklists, delivery acceptance, waste management, and traceability/recall procedures.

## Why This Exists

500,000+ UK food businesses are legally required to have food safety documentation under the Food Safety Act 1990 and Regulation (EC) No. 852/2004 (retained). Food hygiene consultants charge GBP 200-500. Template packs sell for GBP 30-100. An EHO inspection failure can mean closure.

Most small food businesses:
- Know they need HACCP documentation but do not know how to write it
- Have never seen a properly structured allergen matrix
- Fill in temperature logs retrospectively (or not at all)
- Cannot explain their CCPs when an EHO asks

This skill generates the actual documents — tailored to the specific business type, referencing the correct legislation, with realistic content rather than empty templates.
