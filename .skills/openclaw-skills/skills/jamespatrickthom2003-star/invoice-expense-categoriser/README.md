# UK Invoice & Expense Categoriser (HMRC-Aligned)

Instant expense categorisation against HMRC Self Assessment categories. Paste your transactions, get back SA103-ready summaries with VAT and MTD support.

## What It Does

Give it your expenses — pasted from a bank statement, typed as a list, or described in plain English. Get back every transaction categorised into the correct HMRC box, with totals ready to drop into your Self Assessment.

- **SA103 box mapping** — every expense tagged to the correct HMRC category and box number
- **Capital vs revenue separation** — flags items that should go under Capital Allowances, not expenses
- **Mileage calculator** — 45p/25p split tracked automatically across the year
- **Home office allowance** — simplified expenses (£10/£18/£26 per month) or actual cost method
- **VAT summaries** — input/output VAT with net amounts for VAT-registered businesses
- **MTD quarterly position** — running profit and loss by quarter with deadline tracking
- **Deductibility checks** — "Is X deductible?" answered instantly with the correct category

## Why This Exists

2.9 million UK freelancers and 5.5 million small businesses need to categorise expenses for Self Assessment and MTD quarterly returns. FreeAgent costs £14.50/month, Xero £15/month — but millions still use spreadsheets and shoeboxes.

Most freelancers get the categorisation wrong:
- Claiming client meals (not deductible for sole traders)
- Missing home office simplified expenses
- Treating capital items as revenue expenses
- Not apportioning mixed-use costs

This skill gets it right, instantly, using the same HMRC categories your accountant uses.

```

```

Describe your month:
```
/invoice-expense-categoriser I spent about £200 on software, £300 on travel to clients, £150 on office supplies, and bought a camera for £800
```

## HMRC Categories Covered

| SA103 Box | Category | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| Box 17 | Cost of goods sold | Materials, stock, packaging |
| Box 20 | Car, van and travel | Fuel, trains, mileage, parking |
| Box 21 | Wages and staff costs | Subcontractors, freelancers hired |
| Box 23 | Rent, rates, power, insurance | Office rent, utilities, business insurance |
| Box 25 | Repairs and maintenance | Equipment repairs, servicing |
| Box 26 | Professional fees | Accountant, solicitor, subscriptions |
| Box 27 | Interest and bank charges | Bank fees, loan interest |
| Box 28 | Phone, stationery, office costs | Mobile, broadband, stationery |
| Box 29 | Advertising | Website, ads, marketing, domains |
| Box 31 | Other allowable expenses | Software, training, home office |

Plus Capital Allowances (AIA, WDA) for equipment, computers, and vehicles.
