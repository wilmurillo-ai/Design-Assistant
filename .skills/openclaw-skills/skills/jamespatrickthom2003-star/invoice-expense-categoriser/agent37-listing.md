# Agent37 Listing — UK Invoice & Expense Categoriser

## Skill Name
UK Invoice & Expense Categoriser (HMRC-Aligned)

## Tagline
Categorise business expenses against HMRC Self Assessment boxes instantly. Paste transactions, get SA103-ready summaries with VAT tracking, mileage calculations, and MTD quarterly reports.

## Category
Finance & Accounting

## Description

**The problem:** 2.9 million UK freelancers and 5.5 million small businesses need to categorise expenses for Self Assessment. Most use spreadsheets or guess at HMRC categories. FreeAgent is £14.50/month, Xero is £15/month — and both require you to learn accounting software. The result: miscategorised expenses, missed deductions, and panic in January.

**What this skill does:** Paste your transactions — from a bank statement, a CSV, or just a typed list. Every line is categorised into the correct HMRC Self Assessment box (SA103), with totals ready to file.

- All 10 HMRC expense categories mapped to SA103 boxes (17-31)
- Capital allowances separated from revenue expenses automatically
- Mileage allowance calculator (45p/25p per mile, tracked cumulatively)
- Home office simplified expenses (£10/£18/£26 per month based on hours)
- VAT input/output summaries for registered businesses
- MTD quarterly P&L position with deadlines
- Single-expense deductibility checks ("Is X deductible?")
- Flags common mistakes: client meals, clothing, mixed-use items, capital vs revenue

**Why it's better than a spreadsheet:** It knows the HMRC rules. Client lunches are flagged as non-deductible. A £1,200 laptop goes to Capital Allowances under AIA, not Box 31. Your phone bill gets apportioned. A spreadsheet does none of this.

**Who it's for:** UK sole traders, freelancers, self-employed contractors, small business owners — anyone who needs to categorise expenses for Self Assessment or MTD.
