## Why This Exists

Section 21 no-fault evictions are abolished from 1 May 2026. The Renters' Rights Act 2025 introduced a 37-ground possession system, new mandatory obligations, and penalties that start at £7,000 and escalate to £40,000. Rent Repayment Orders now cover 24 months.

2.65 million UK landlords need new documentation. Most are not prepared:

- Serving a Section 8 notice on the wrong ground or with the wrong notice period invalidates it entirely
- Missing a single compliance step (deposit protection, information sheet, gas certificate) can block a possession claim
- The new Decent Homes Standard, Awaab's Law extension, and EPC Band C requirements add layers of obligation that did not previously apply to private landlords
- Operating without a required licence now risks £40,000 fines and 24-month Rent Repayment Orders

This skill generates every document a compliant landlord needs, citing exact legislation and using prescribed forms where applicable.
