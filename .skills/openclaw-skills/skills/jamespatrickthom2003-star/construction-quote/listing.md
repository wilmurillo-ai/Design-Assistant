# Agent37 Listing -- Construction Quote & Job Costing Calculator

## Skill Name
Construction Quote & Job Costing Calculator

## Tagline
Itemised construction quotes in minutes. Describe the job, get a full material list, labour breakdown, and client-ready quote document -- with UK 2025 prices.

## Category
Construction & Trades

## Description

**The problem:** Inaccurate quoting is the number one margin killer for small construction businesses. Most builders price from memory, use back-of-envelope maths, or copy an old spreadsheet and hope the numbers still work. Underprice and you eat the loss. Overprice and you lose the job. With over 900,000 construction businesses in the UK -- mostly sole traders and micro firms -- there is no shortage of people who need better quoting tools.

**What this skill does:** Describe a construction job in plain English. Get back a complete, itemised quote:

- Full material list with quantities, unit prices, and totals (UK 2025 builders' merchant prices)
- Labour breakdown by trade with day rates and estimated durations
- Additional costs: skips, scaffolding, Building Control, planning, Party Wall, structural engineer
- Summary with 10% waste allowance, 20% profit margin, and VAT calculation
- Client-ready quote document with payment schedule, inclusions, exclusions, and terms and conditions
- CIS (Construction Industry Scheme) guidance for subcontracting scenarios
- Regional pricing adjustments (London +20-40%, South East +10-20%, North -5-10%)

**16 job types covered:**
1. Kitchen fitting and refurbishment
2. Bathroom fitting and refurbishment
3. Single-storey extension
4. Double-storey extension
5. Loft conversion (Velux, dormer, hip-to-gable, mansard)
6. Garage conversion
7. Roofing (new, repair, flat to pitched)
8. Plastering and rendering
9. Electrical rewire
10. Plumbing and central heating
11. Painting and decorating
12. Fencing and landscaping
13. Driveway and paving
14. Window and door replacement
15. Structural alterations (RSJs, knock-throughs)
16. Damp proofing and tanking

**Two outputs per quote:**
- **Detailed breakdown** -- the builder's internal working document with full material quantities, trade day rates, and markup calculations
- **Client-ready quote** -- a professional document formatted for sending to the homeowner with grouped costs, payment schedule, and terms

**Why builders pay for this:** A quantity surveyor charges GBP 500-1,500 for a detailed estimate. Even spending 2 hours pricing a job yourself costs real money in lost productive time. This skill produces a structured starting point in minutes -- not a replacement for a site visit, but a solid framework that stops you guessing.
