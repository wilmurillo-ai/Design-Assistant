# GDPR Privacy Policy & Compliance Document Generator

Comprehensive, tailored GDPR compliance documentation for UK and EU businesses. Privacy policies, cookie policies, DPIAs, ROPA, DSAR responses, breach notifications, and more -- generated in minutes, not weeks.

## What It Does

Describe your business and what you need. Get back implementation-ready GDPR compliance documents tailored to your sector, data processing activities, and risk profile.

**14 document types:**

- **Privacy Policy** (website) -- UK GDPR + PECR compliant, with processing purpose tables
- **Privacy Notices** -- separate versions for employees, customers, and suppliers
- **Cookie Policy** -- with cookie audit table and consent mechanism guidance
- **Data Processing Agreement** -- controller-processor, Art 28 compliant
- **Data Processing Addendum** -- for SaaS and cloud provider contracts
- **Records of Processing Activities (ROPA)** -- Art 30 register with all required fields
- **Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)** -- full risk assessment template
- **Legitimate Interest Assessment (LIA)** -- ICO three-part test
- **Consent Forms** -- granular, freely given, withdrawable, with record-keeping
- **DSAR Response Templates** -- 30-day deadline compliant, with exemption guidance
- **Data Breach Notification** -- ICO 72-hour report and data subject notification
- **Data Retention Schedule** -- by category with legal justifications
- **Data Protection Policy** -- internal staff guidance with breach and DSAR procedures
- **International Data Transfer documentation** -- UK adequacy decisions, IDTA, SCCs

## Why This Exists

Every UK and EU business processing personal data needs GDPR documentation. The reality:

- Solicitors charge GBP 500-2,000 for a privacy policy alone. A full compliance pack runs into thousands.
- Free online generators (Termly, iubenda, PrivacyPolicies.com) produce generic, surface-level policies that miss sector-specific requirements and rarely cover the full document set.
- Most small businesses have a privacy policy copied from another website and nothing else -- no ROPA, no DPIAs, no breach response plan, no staff training policy.
- The ICO can fine up to GBP 17.5 million or 4% of global turnover. Even smaller enforcement actions (reprimands, enforcement notices) are disruptive and public.

This skill generates the full set of GDPR compliance documents, customised to the business, citing actual legislation (DPA 2018, UK GDPR articles, PECR regulations, ICO guidance), with industry-specific presets for common business types.
