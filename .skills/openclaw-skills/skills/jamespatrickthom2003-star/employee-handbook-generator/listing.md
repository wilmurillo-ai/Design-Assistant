## Category

## Description

**The problem:** Every UK business with employees needs workplace policies. HR firms charge GBP 500-2,000+ for a bespoke handbook. The Employment Rights Act 2025 has introduced sweeping changes -- day-one unfair dismissal protection, mandatory sexual harassment prevention, zero-hours contract reforms -- making every existing handbook outdated overnight.

**What this skill does:** Describe your business (type, size, working arrangements). Get a complete employee handbook covering all 20 essential policy areas:

- Employment terms, probation, and notice periods
- Working hours, flexible working (day-one right under ERA 2025), and holiday
- Sickness absence, family leave (maternity, paternity, shared parental)
- Pay, benefits, and pension auto-enrolment
- ACAS Code-compliant disciplinary and grievance procedures (full 5-stage process)
- Anti-harassment and bullying (ERA 2025 mandatory prevention duty, including third-party harassment)
- Equal opportunities, GDPR, IT/social media, health and safety
- Whistleblowing, remote working, termination and exit procedures
- Version control header, table of contents, and employee sign-off page

**Industry-specific adaptations** for construction (PPE, CSCS, CDM), hospitality (tips, shifts, food hygiene), retail (cash handling, Sunday working), office/professional (remote working, CPD), creative/agency (IP, freelancer boundaries), and healthcare (DBS, safeguarding, infection control).

**ERA 2025 changes flagged throughout** with clear markers so businesses know exactly what has changed and what action is needed.

**Every section cites the relevant legislation:** ERA 1996, ERA 2025, Equality Act 2010, Working Time Regulations 1998, ACAS Code of Practice, Health and Safety at Work Act 1974, UK GDPR, and more.

**Scaled to company size:** micro (1-9), small (10-49), medium (50-249), and large (250+) -- each tier gets the right level of detail and mandatory provisions.

**Why businesses pay for this:** A compliant handbook protects against tribunal claims. Non-compliance with the ERA 2025 harassment prevention duty alone can result in a 25% compensation uplift. Generating a professionally structured handbook for a few quid versus paying GBP 1,500 to an HR consultant is straightforward arithmetic.
