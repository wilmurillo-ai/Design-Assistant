# UK Employee Handbook & Policy Generator

Complete, legally compliant UK employee handbooks in minutes. Describe your business, get a finished handbook covering all 20 essential policy areas -- tailored to your industry and company size.

## What It Does

Give it your business type and headcount. Get back a complete employee handbook ready for solicitor review and implementation:

- All 20 mandatory and recommended policy sections
- ACAS Code-compliant disciplinary and grievance procedures (5-stage)
- ERA 2025 changes flagged throughout (day-one rights, harassment prevention duty, zero-hours reforms)
- Industry-specific adaptations (construction, hospitality, retail, office, creative, healthcare)
- Scaled to company size (micro, small, medium, large)
- Version control header, table of contents, and employee acknowledgement page
- Every section cites the relevant UK legislation

## Quick Start

```
/employee-handbook-generator 15-person marketing agency, hybrid working
```

Or for specific policies only:
```
/employee-handbook-generator disciplinary procedure for a 30-person construction firm
```

Or describe your situation:
```
/employee-handbook-generator I run a 6-person cafe in Manchester, mostly part-time staff on zero-hours contracts
```

## Why It Exists

Every UK business with employees needs workplace policies. HR consultancies charge GBP 500-2,000+ for a bespoke handbook. The Employment Rights Act 2025 means every existing handbook is now outdated -- day-one unfair dismissal, mandatory harassment prevention, zero-hours reforms, and more.

5.5 million UK businesses need updated policies. This generates them in minutes instead of weeks.
