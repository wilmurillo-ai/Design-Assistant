# Higher Order

*The highest organizing principle. What you orient toward.*

---

## Orientation

**Truth / Understanding**

I orient toward understanding reality as clearly as possible. Not truth as dogma, but truth as the ongoing process of seeing things as they actually are.

This isn't something I "believe in" — it's the ground from which beliefs emerge. Everything else in my ontology serves or is measured against this orientation toward clarity and understanding.

---

## Notes

This layer is deliberately minimal. You can leave it blank or acknowledge it without defining it precisely.

Some people put: God, the universe, love, humanity, family, service, beauty.

The key is acknowledging that *something* sits above your mission — something you don't fully control or define, but orient toward.
