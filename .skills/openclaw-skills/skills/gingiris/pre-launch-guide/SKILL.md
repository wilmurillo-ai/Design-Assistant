## English

### The Pre-Launch Framework

```
L-4 weeks     → Build waitlist & social proof
L-3 weeks     → Beta testing & KOL seeding
L-2 weeks     → Reddit/HN preparation
L-1 week      → Final polish & outreach
Launch Day    → Execute & monitor
L+1 week      → Ride the momentum
```

### Pre-Launch Checklist

| Week | Task | Status |
|------|------|--------|
| L-4 | Waitlist page live | ☐ |

| L-2 | KOL samples sent | ☐ |
| L-1 | PH hunter confirmed | ☐ |
| L-1 | HN post drafted | ☐ |
