## Reddit 发帖模板

### 模板 1: 技术分享型 (r/LocalLLaMA, r/MachineLearning)

```
Title: [Project Name] - [核心价值主张] (Now Open Source!)

Hey everyone! 👋

I'm excited to share [Project Name], which we just open-sourced.

**What it does:**
[2-3 sentences describing the core functionality]

**Key features:**
- [Feature 1]
- [Feature 2]
- [Feature 3]

**Technical highlights:**
- [Tech detail 1]
- [Tech detail 2]

**Links:**
- GitHub: [Link]
- Docs: [Link]
- Demo: [Link]

We'd love to hear your feedback and suggestions!
```

### 模板 2: 工具分享型 (r/opensource, r/coolgithubprojects)

```
Title: I built [Project Name] to solve [问题] - now open source

After [X months] of development, I'm happy to share [Project Name] 
with the community.

**The problem:** [1-2 sentences]

**The solution:** [Project Name] is [description]

**Why open source:** [简短说明]

GitHub: [Link]

Looking forward to your feedback! Happy to answer any questions.
```
