## The 24-Hour Battle Plan

Product Hunt is a 24-hour sprint starting at 12:01 AM PT. Here's how to win:

### Pre-Launch (L-7 to L-1)
- Lock your hunter (if using one)
- Prepare all assets: tagline, description, media, maker comment
- Warm up your community without asking for upvotes

### Launch Day (Hour by Hour)
- **00:00-03:00 PT** — Initial push, engage early voters
- **06:00-09:00 PT** — Peak traffic window, maximum engagement
- **12:00-15:00 PT** — Afternoon push, social media activation
- **18:00-21:00 PT** — Final sprint, thank voters

### Post-Launch
- Follow up with everyone who engaged
- Convert traffic to signups
- Plan your next launch
