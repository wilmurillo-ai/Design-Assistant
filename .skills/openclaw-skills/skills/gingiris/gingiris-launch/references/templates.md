## Reddit发帖模板

### 模板一：产品发布型（r/SideProject / r/indiehackers）

```
Title: I spent [X months] building [Product Name] to [solve specific problem] — just launched, would love feedback

Content:

Hey everyone, I've been lurking here for a while and finally have something to share.

The problem: [描述具体用户痛点，1-2句话]

What I built: [Product Name] is [one-line description]. Unlike [competitor], it [key differentiator].

Key features:
• [Feature 1 that directly addresses a pain point]
• [Feature 2]
• [Feature 3]

Current status: [Beta/Live], [pricing info], [open source info]

Links: [Website] | [GitHub] | [Product Hunt]

I'd really appreciate any feedback, especially on [specific aspect].

What do you think? Has anyone else tried to solve this problem differently?
```

### 模板二：工具推荐讨论型（r/AItools / r/productivity）

```
Title: What's the most underrated AI tool in your stack that solves [specific pain point]?

Content:

I've been exploring a lot of AI tools lately to deal with [specific pain point], and stumbled upon some real hidden gems.

For me, it's [Product Name]. I use it for [specific use case] and it's been a game-changer because [specific reason].

What about you? What's your "silent time-saver" AI tool for this problem?
```

### 模板三：竞品对比型（r/ChatGPT / r/artificial）

```
Title: I compared [Product Name] vs [Competitor] for [specific use case] — here's what I found

Content:

I've been using [Competitor] for [X months] for [use case], but recently switched to [Product Name] because I was struggling with [specific pain point].

Here's my honest comparison:

[Competitor]:
✅ [Strength 1]
✅ [Strength 2]
❌ [Weakness 1 - the pain point your product solves]

[Product Name]:
✅ [How it solves the weakness above]
✅ [Additional strength]
❌ [Honest weakness - builds credibility]

Overall, I've found [Product Name] to be better for [specific scenario] because [reason].

Has anyone else made a similar switch? What's been your experience?
```
