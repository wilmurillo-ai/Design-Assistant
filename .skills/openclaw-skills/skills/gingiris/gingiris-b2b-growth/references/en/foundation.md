# Part 1: Building the Foundation

## 1. User Research & PMF Validation

### HeyGen Case: 1800 Interviews to Find PMF

**Step 1: Wizard of Oz MVP**
Team offered a service on Fiverr: "Create a talking digital human video for you." Validated two hypotheses with minimal cost:
1. Market demand exists for "making static images speak"
2. Customers are willing to pay

**Step 2: Massive User Interviews**
Over 1800 interviews helped the team transform from "interesting tool" to "business solution."

## 4. Product Launch Strategy

### Three-Phase Rhythm

**Phase 1: Private Beta**
- Open to carefully selected few users
- High-intensity usage and feedback

**Phase 2: Early Access**
- Expand via invite codes or waitlists
- Waitlist creates scarcity and anticipation

**Phase 3: Public Launch**
- Product Hunt launch
- Official blog and press releases
- Social media concentrated push

---

## 5. Community-Led Growth (CLG)

### Three-Phase Path

**Phase 1: Cold Start (0-100 members)**
- Founder-driven, personally answer every question
- Platform: Discord
- Core channels: `#general`, `#feedback`, `#showcase`, `#announcements`

**Phase 2: Content & Culture (100-1000 members)**
- Regular: changelogs, tutorials, AMAs
- Encourage UGC: use case collection, template sharing

**Phase 3: Scale & Ambassador Program (1000+ members)**
- Launch Ambassador Program
