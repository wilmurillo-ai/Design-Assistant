# Channel Content Templates

## Twitter/X Launch Tweet

```

We're thrilled to launch [Product Name] [on Product Hunt] today!

[One-sentence description highlighting differentiation].

🚀 Check it out: [Link]
⭐ Star us on GitHub: [GitHub Link]
💬 Join our community: [Discord Link]

We'd love to hear your feedback!

#AI #[ProductCategory] #ProductHunt

[Attach brand video]
```

## Reddit Post Templates

### Template 1: Product Launch (r/SideProject, r/indiehackers)

```
Title: I spent [X months] building [Product Name] to [solve problem] — just launched, would love feedback

Hey everyone, finally have something to share.

The problem: [1-2 sentences on pain point]

What I built: [Product Name] is [description]. Unlike [competitor], it [differentiator].

Key features:
• [Feature 1]
• [Feature 2]
• [Feature 3]

Links: [Website] | [GitHub] | [Product Hunt]

I'd appreciate any feedback, especially on [specific aspect].
```

### Template 2: Tool Discussion (r/AItools, r/productivity)

```
Title: What's the most underrated AI tool that solves [pain point]?

I've been exploring AI tools for [pain point] and found some gems.

For me, it's [Product Name]. I use it for [use case] and it's a game-changer because [reason].

What's your "silent time-saver"?
```
