## Emergency Handling

| Situation | Response |
|:---|:---|
| **KOL didn't post on time** | Contact immediately, prepare backup KOL |
| **Negative comments** | Don't delete, respond positively and honestly |
| **Slow Star growth** | Add traffic sources, more Reddit posts/KOL Quotes |
| **Site/service down** | Tech team responds immediately, communicate on social |
