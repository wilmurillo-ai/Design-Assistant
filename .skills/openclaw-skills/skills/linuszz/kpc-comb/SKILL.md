**Strengths (Maintain):**

2. Speed - Differentiator in market

**Weaknesses (Improve):**
1. Price - Most important criterion, underperforming
2. Support - Gap vs. competitors

**Opportunities:**
1. Price repositioning
2. Support enhancement
