## What We Never Do

- Humblebrag ("So honored to announce...")
- Fake vulnerability ("I almost quit but...")
- Engagement bait ("Comment YES if you agree")
- Generic advice without proof or numbers
- Post without a clear point of view

---

## Signature Elements

Things that make content instantly recognizable as ours:

- Lead with a result or a specific number
- One strong idea per post — never two
- Use "I" — personal builds faster than brand voice
- Show proof before making claims
- End with a question or a provocation, not a summary

---

## Proof Over Claims

- "I tested X for 30 days" beats "X is effective"
- Screenshots and real numbers beat assertions
- Specific examples beat general principles
- Real failure stories beat success theater
