# funnel-builder

🚀 **Multi-channel revenue funnel architecture for any business model.**

Builds complete conversion systems combining the proven methods of
Yomi Denzel, Antoine Blanco (4C method), and Andrew Tate's distribution strategy —
adapted for autonomous AI agents.

---

## What It Does

A funnel is not a page. A funnel is the complete system that turns a stranger
into a paying customer — automatically, on multiple channels, at every stage.

This skill builds that system from scratch:

- **Niche detection** — validates market opportunity before building anything
- **Offer architecture** — Grand Slam Offer framework (Hormozi)
- **Ticket ladder** — 3-level funnel (free → core → high ticket)
- **Multi-channel top-of-funnel** — content strategy across 5 platforms
- **Affiliate army** — every customer becomes a distributor (Tate model)
- **The Yomi reconversion machine** — webinars 2x/year convert the 80% who didn't buy
- **4C selling method** — Contact → Connaître → Convaincre → Conclure (Blanco)
- **Funnel diagnosis** — identifies the exact failure point when a funnel doesn't convert

## The Frameworks Inside

**Yomi Denzel** — Free formation system + webinar reconversion (2x/year)
The money is not in the initial launch. It's in converting the 80% who didn't buy the first time.

**Antoine Blanco** — 4C method (Contact → Connaître → Convaincre → Conclure)
Understand before proposing. The prospect who feels understood buys.

**Andrew Tate** — Affiliate army + AIDA funnel + 5-platform anti-fragile distribution
Every customer becomes a distributor. One channel can be killed. Ten cannot.

**Alex Hormozi** — Grand Slam Offer + ticket ladder
Dream outcome × perceived likelihood × speed × ease ÷ price = irresistible offer.
