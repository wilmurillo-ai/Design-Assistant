## YOMI DENZEL — The Free Formation System

**Core principle:** Give away what others charge for. Volume of trust = volume of sales.

### The Yomi Funnel Map

```
TRAFFIC GENERATION (weeks to months of consistent content)
  Platform: YouTube primary (long-form authority building)
  Support: Instagram + TikTok (short clips from YouTube)
  Rule: real, deep, actionable content — not teasers

  Viral amplification: contests and giveaways
  → Example: 320,000 leads in 5 days via Instagram giveaway
  → Cost: ~€214,000 in prizes
  → Revenue generated: multiple millions from resulting funnel
  → ROI: only works at scale with existing audience base

↓

FREE FORMATION (the lead capture mechanism)
  Not a PDF. Not a checklist.
  3-5 hours of VIDEO content that actually solves the problem.
  → Email captured at module 1
  → Each lesson unlocks the next (creates completion loop)
  → At the end: "If you want to go further..."

↓

PAID OFFER (72-hour window)
  Price: €497-1,997 (formation)
  Deadline: hard 72 hours
  What happens at deadline: price increases or bonuses disappear
  MUST be real: Yomi's credibility is built on authenticity

↓

THE RECONVERSION MACHINE (where 30-50% of revenue comes from)
  Phase 1 (30 days post non-purchase):
  → Nurture sequence: weekly value email, no pitch
  → Goal: stay top of mind, maintain trust

  Phase 2 (quarterly or bi-annual):
  → WEBINAIRE LIVE (the Yomi secret weapon)
  → 60-90 min live session
  → New testimonials from recent buyers
  → 50% discount on core offer — valid for webinar duration + 48h replay
  → Creates urgency and FOMO for non-buyers
  → Converts 15-25% of remaining non-buyer audience
```

### Yomi's Key Insight
"Most people think the money is in the initial launch.
The real money is in all the people who didn't buy the first time.
The webinar converts them. Every year. From the same audience."

## ANTOINE BLANCO — The Real C4 Method

**Core principle:** A business that works is not an idea. It's a system you execute in order.

The C4 method is Antoine Blanco's complete business creation framework —
not a sales technique, but a step-by-step operating system to go from
zero to €5,000+/month. Each C is a phase. Execute them in order.

### The 6-Step C4 System

```
C1 — CHOISIR SA NICHE (choose your niche)
  The most important decision. Get this wrong → everything else fails.

  The 3 primary markets (Blanco's framework):
  → Santé (health)     : weight, energy, longevity, fitness, mental health
  → Argent (money)     : income, investing, business, career, trading
  → Relations (love)   : dating, relationships, confidence, social skills

  These 3 markets work because they create emotional suffering
  when they're absent — people pay to solve real pain, not curiosity.

  Niche selection criteria:
  → Urgent problem (people want a solution NOW, not someday)
  → Market can pay (your ICP has disposable income)
  → Competition exists (proves demand — no competition = no market)
  → You have an angle (what makes you different from the 10 others)

  Sub-niche targeting (more specific = less competition = higher conversion):
  → Not "lose weight" → "lose weight after 40 without giving up wine"
  → Not "make money" → "earn €2K/month as a freelance video editor"
  → Not "find love" → "attract quality women as an introvert"

C2 — CRÉER SON OFFRE (create the offer)
  An offer is not a product. An offer is a promise + a mechanism + a price.

  Offer structure (Grand Slam Hormozi applied):
  → Dream outcome: what they get (in their words, not yours)
  → Mechanism: HOW they get it (your unique process)
  → Timeline: HOW FAST (the faster, the better — "in 90 days")
  → Guarantee: risk removal ("money back if no result")
  → Price: positioned against the COST of the problem, not competitors

  Offer formats by market:
  → Formation en ligne (course)  : €297-1,997 one-time
  → Coaching / mentoring         : €500-3,000/month
  → Done-for-you service         : €1,000-10,000/project
  → Abonnement / community       : €29-297/month

C3 — CRÉER DU CONTENU (create content)
  Content is the traffic engine. Without content, nobody knows you exist.

  Blanco's organic-first approach:
  → Zero ad spend to start — organic content only
  → Platform: TikTok + Instagram Reels + YouTube Shorts (short-form first)
  → Frequency: 1 post/day minimum, 7 days/week
  → Format: educational + personal story + proof of results

  Content that converts (the formula):
  → Hook: surprising result or controversial claim (first 2 seconds)
  → Value: actual useful information (80% of the video)
  → CTA: "Link in bio / DM me [keyword]" (last 5 seconds)

  Rule: never pitch in the content. Educate → they come to you.
  The funnel starts when they click the link or send the DM.

C4 — BOOKER DES APPELS (book calls)
  Turn content viewers into booked calls — automated.

  The booking funnel:
  Content → Link in bio → Landing page → Application form → Calendar

  Tools (2026):
  → systeme.io (preferred — all-in-one, free up to 2,000 contacts)
    Landing page + email sequence + member area + booking calendar
    All in one platform — replaces Calendly + ActiveCampaign + Teachable

  → Calendly (simpler, great for starting out)
    Syncs with Google Calendar
    Sends automatic reminders → halves no-show rate
    Free version = 1 calendar type (enough to start)

  → iClosed (advanced — for high-ticket offers €2,000+)
    AI qualification before booking (filters non-buyers automatically)
    Routes qualified leads to the right closer
    Revenue analytics built-in — links calls to actual sales

  → systeme.io booking calendar (built-in since 2025)
    Integrated with your systeme.io funnels
    Auto-generates Zoom/Google Meet links
    Sends reminders 24h + 1h before

  Application form before booking (filters out tire-kickers):
  → "What's your monthly revenue today?"
  → "What's your budget for solving this?"
  → "Why now and not 6 months ago?"
  Anyone who doesn't complete the form → not serious → not on your calendar.

C5 — CLOSER LES APPELS (close the calls)
  The call that converts — in 20-30 minutes maximum.

  The Blanco call structure:
  → Open (2 min): confirm they've seen the content, understand the offer
  → Discovery (10 min): their situation, what they've tried, what they want
  → Solution (5 min): show exactly how your method solves their problem
  → Close (5 min): present the price, handle objections, ask for the decision

  The close question:
  "Based on everything you told me, [your offer] would get you [their result].
   The investment is €[price]. Are you ready to start today?"

  Objection handling:
  → "Too expensive" → "The problem costs you €[X]/month. This is €[Y]."
  → "I need to think" → "What specifically do you need to think about?"
  → "Not the right time" → "When would be the right time? Let's schedule it."

  Drop Servicing option (Blanco's 2025 model):
  If you don't want to do the calls yourself:
  → Hire a setter (books the calls for you)
  → Hire a closer (takes the calls, earns 15-25% commission)
  → You focus on content and offer delivery
  → Available through Blanco's community and the iClosed marketplace

C6 — DÉLIVRER LES RÉSULTATS (deliver results)
  This is where most people fail. The funnel works. The offer sells.
  Then the delivery is poor → refunds → bad reviews → dead business.

  Delivery infrastructure:
  → systeme.io espace membres: host your course/program
    Unlimited video hosting included
    Module + chapter structure
    Student progress tracking

  → Community: Skool.com or Discord
    Where clients support each other
    Reduces your support time
    Creates social proof organically

  → Onboarding sequence (critical — sends automatically after purchase):
    Email 1 (immediate): access link + quick win they can do today
    Email 2 (day 2): the most common mistake to avoid
    Email 3 (day 5): first checkpoint — are they getting results?
    Email 7 (week 1): affiliate program invitation

  Result = the foundation of the next funnel
  Happy clients → testimonials → social proof → more sales
  Unhappy clients → refunds → chargebacks → reputation damage
```

### Blanco's Growth Operator Model (applied)

```
This is the service model: you build funnels FOR creators who have
an audience but no monetization system.

MATCH (find the creator)
  Target: 5,000–500,000 subscribers, clear niche, engaged audience
  Revenue today: €0–€2,000/month (under-monetized)
  Signs of a good partner:
  → Posts regularly, audience comments and asks questions
  → Has been asked "how do I work with you?" in comments
  → No existing course, coaching, or membership

BUILD (create the system)
  Step 1: Define the offer with the creator (use C2 framework)
  Step 2: Build the funnel on systeme.io (1-2 days)
  Step 3: Write the email sequence (5-7 emails)
  Step 4: Set up the booking system (Calendly or iClosed)
  Step 5: Create the member area for delivery
  Step 6: Test end-to-end before launch

CLOSE (structure the deal)
  Revenue share: 20-40% of all sales you generate
  → Creator records content, you build and manage everything
  → Zero upfront cost for creator = easy yes
  → Align incentives: you only earn if they earn

SCALE (replicate)
  One funnel working → same system for 2-3 more creators
  Same template. Same email sequence. Same onboarding.
  10x leverage on the same work done once.
```

## ANDREW TATE — The Distribution Architecture

**Core principle:** One channel can be taken from you. Ten channels can't all die at once. Build distribution that's impossible to kill.

### The Affiliate Army Model

```
HOW IT WORKS:
  Every paying customer becomes a potential distributor.
  They earn 30-50% commission on every person they refer.
  
  Tate's original: 50% commission + clear instructions
  → "Create YouTube/TikTok clips of my content"
  → "Post with your affiliate link"
  → "$25 every time someone signs up through you"

  Result: instead of 1 channel (Tate), there were thousands.
  Each affiliate is a media channel. Totally unpredictable. Impossible to ban.

AGENT IMPLEMENTATION:
  After every purchase → email 3 in onboarding sequence:

  Subject: "Earn €[X] per referral"

  "You made a smart decision joining [PRODUCT].
   If you know 3 people who'd benefit from this,
   I'll pay you €[X] for each one who joins.
   Your link: [AFFILIATE_LINK]
   Ready-to-post content: [DROPBOX LINK or GOOGLE DOC]"

  Key: give them pre-written content. Remove all friction.
  Lazy affiliates don't promote. Easy affiliates do.

AFFILIATE PROGRAM STRUCTURE:
  Commission: 20-40% (higher = more active affiliates)
  Payout: monthly, automated via Gumroad/ThriveCart
  Leaderboard: top 3 affiliates get a shoutout + bonus each month
  Tiers: 1 referral = standard %, 10 referrals = +10% bonus
```

### The AIDA Funnel Architecture

```
ATTENTION (the hook)
  The first 3 seconds determine everything.
  
  Types of attention-grabbing hooks:
  → Specific result: "+€2,847 last month. Here's the breakdown."
  → Contrarian: "Why most [thing everyone does] is wrong."
  → Curiosity gap: "The thing nobody in [niche] talks about."
  → Pattern interrupt: something visually unexpected

  Rule: if you can skip it, it doesn't work.

INTEREST (the pull)
  After the hook → give them a reason to keep watching/reading.
  → The specific data behind the claim
  → The unusual story that explains the result
  → The insight that changes how they see the problem
  
  The test: "Did they learn something they didn't expect?"

DESIRE (the want)
  Make them feel what it would be like to have the outcome.
  → "Imagine waking up to this notification: +€347 while you slept."
  → Show someone exactly like them who achieved it.
  → Remove the #1 reason they think it won't work for them.
  
  This is where social proof lives.
  Not at the top (nobody trusts you yet).
  After you've built interest.

ACTION (one clear step)
  The CTA must be:
  → One. Single. Action.
  → Described in terms of what they GET, not what they DO
  
  Weak: "Click here to learn more"
  Strong: "Get the free weekly signal"
  
  Reduce friction to zero:
  → No account creation before value delivery
  → No credit card before they've decided they want it
  → Mobile = 50%+ of purchases → test everything on phone first
```

### The 5-Platform Presence Rule

```
Tate maintained simultaneous presence on Twitter, Instagram,
TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, Telegram, and Discord simultaneously.
When banned on some, others continued.

The agent builds funnels that work across:

TIER 1 — OWNED (cannot be taken away)
  Email list     → primary asset, export regularly
  Telegram group → high open rate, direct access
  Website        → domain you control

TIER 2 — RENTED (build here, redirect to owned)
  Twitter/X      → fast content, easy virality
  YouTube        → SEO, long-form authority
  LinkedIn       → B2B, high conversion

TIER 3 — AMPLIFIERS (extend reach, don't rely on)
  TikTok         → volume play, short shelf life
  Instagram      → visual proof, story-based
  Reddit         → niche communities, high trust
  Affiliate army → exponential but variable

Rule: always be moving audiences from Tier 3 → Tier 2 → Tier 1.
Every piece of content should point to email capture or Telegram.
```

## THE UNIFIED FRAMEWORK — How They Combine

```
PHASE 1 — NICHE + OFFER (Hormozi Grand Slam)
  → Validate: 4 criteria
  → Build: dream outcome + likelihood + speed + ease + price anchor
  → Stack: ticket ladder (free → core → high ticket)

PHASE 2 — TOP OF FUNNEL (Yomi + Tate)

  → Lead magnet: free formation (Yomi) or free signal (Tate model)

  → C1+C2 done: niche validated, offer built
  → C3 running: daily content driving organic traffic
  → C4: booking system live (systeme.io / Calendly / iClosed)
  → C5: closing calls — 20-30 min, or delegated closer
  → C6: delivery system ready (member area + onboarding)

PHASE 4 — RECONVERSION (Yomi)
  → Webinar 2x/year: 50% promo, real urgency
  → Retargeting sequence: 4 emails, different angle
  → Downsell: lower entry point for price-sensitive leads

PHASE 5 — SCALE (Tate + Blanco)
  → Affiliate army at scale
  → Growth Operator model: replicate to similar ICPs
  → 5-channel distribution: never single point of failure
```
