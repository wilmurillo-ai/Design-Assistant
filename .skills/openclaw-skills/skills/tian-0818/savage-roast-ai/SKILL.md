 ## 💀 What is this? / 功能描述 
 
 Savage Roast AI is your **no-mercy AI mouthpiece**.  
 It takes any text you provide—posts, messages, bios, ideas, or conversations—and turns it into **one sharp, funny, meme-worthy roast** ready to screenshot and share.  
 这个技能可以将用户输入的任意文本（帖子、消息、文案、想法或对话）转化为**一句扎心、搞笑、有梗的吐槽**，非常适合截图分享。 
 
 No sugarcoating. No fake politeness. Just one pure, savage line.  
 不掺水，不客套，只输出**一句纯粹的毒舌**。
