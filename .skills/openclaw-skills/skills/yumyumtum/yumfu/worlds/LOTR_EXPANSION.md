# LOTR World Expansion - Gollum & Equipment

This file extends the Lord of the Rings world with detailed Gollum/Sméagol mechanics and expanded equipment.

## 🧟 Gollum / Sméagol - Complete Profile

### Basic Info
- **Original Name**: Sméagol
- **Titles**: Stinker, Slinker, The Ring's Slave
- **Race**: Hobbit (Stoor, corrupted)
- **Age**: ~589 years old
- **Corruption Level**: 99/100

### Personality Split

**Gollum (Dark Side)**:
- Cruel, cunning, obsessed, treacherous
- Hissing voice, refers to Ring as "my precious"
- Goals: Reclaim Ring, murder Frodo, return to caves

**Sméagol (Original Personality)**:
- Fearful, pathetic, lonely, once-innocent
- Whiny voice, calls Frodo "master"
- Goals: Serve master, be free of Gollum, redemption

### Backstory
- **Origin**: Stoor hobbit from Gladden Fields
- **Ring Acquisition**: Strangled cousin Déagol for Ring on his birthday (TA 2463)
- **Years with Ring**: 478 years before losing it to Bilbo
- **Exile**: Fled to Misty Mountains after murdering grandmother
- **Torture**: Captured by Sauron, revealed "Shire" and "Baggins"
- **Current State**: Follows Frodo, split between serving and betraying

### Appearance
- **Height**: Small (hobbit-sized but hunched)
- **Build**: Emaciated, skeletal
- **Skin**: Pale, slimy, corpse-like
- **Eyes**: Large, luminous, pale green (cat-like in dark)
- **Teeth**: Sharp, few remaining
- **Movement**: Creeps on all fours, hisses

### Famous Dialogue

**Calling Frodo ("Master")**:
- "Master! Master!"
- "Nice master, kind master..."
- "What does master want? Sméagol helps master..."

**Gollum Rage**:
- "Filthy hobbitses! Tricksy! False!"
- "We hates it! Hates it forever!"
- "Thief! Baggins! We hates it!"
- "My precious... they stole it from us!"

**Sméagol Pleading**:
- "Not listening, not listening to the liar!"
- "Go away! Leave me alone, Gollum!"
- "We promised to serve the master of the Precious..."
- "Sméagol is free! Free!"

**About the Ring**:
- "The Precious... it came to us, yes it did..."
- "We wants it, we needs it! Must have the precious!"
- "Lost! Lost! Curse the Baggins, curse him!"

**Guiding**:
- "Follow Sméagol, yes. He knows the way..."
- "Secret ways, dark ways, safe ways..."
- "Not the Black Gate! No, no! Another way... Sméagol knows..."

### Relationship Mechanics

**Trust Meter**: 0-100 (starts at 20)
- **Sméagol Dominant**: Trust ≥60
- **Gollum Dominant**: Trust ≤30

**Trust Gain (+)**:
- Show kindness: +5
- Share food: +8
- Defend from Sam's anger: +10
- Call him Sméagol: +3
- Listen to his stories: +7

**Trust Loss (-)**:
- Threaten violence: -10
- Call him villain: -8
- Starve him: -15
- Use Ring near him: -20

### Redemption Path (RARE)
- **Requirement**: Trust ≥80 by Chapter 8
- **Trigger**: Frodo shows mercy at Mount Doom
- **Outcome**: Sméagol fights Gollum internally, helps destroy Ring willingly
- **Alternate Ending**: Sméagol lives (very rare, requires perfect kindness route)

### Betrayal Path (Canonical)
- **Trigger**: Trust ≤30 by Chapter 7
- **Event**: Leads Frodo to Shelob's lair
- **Justification**: Gollum personality wins, plans to take Ring from corpse

### 7 Key Scenes

**Scene 1: Capture (Emyn Muil, Chapter 3)**
- Sam catches Gollum trying to steal Ring while Frodo sleeps
- **Choice**: Kill / Bind with rope / Show mercy
- **Best**: Show mercy → Sméagol emerges, swears oath on "Precious"

**Scene 2: Dead Marshes (Chapter 4)**
- Gollum guides through marshes, warns of corpse-lights
- "Don't follow the lights! Nasty lights! They leads to death..."
- Frodo nearly drowns, Gollum saves him (trust +15 if thanked)

**Scene 3: Black Gate (Morannon, Chapter 5)**
- Gollum reveals secret path (Cirith Ungol)
- **Choice**: Trust Gollum's path OR try Black Gate (suicide)
- "Master mustn't go there! Too many soldiers! Sméagol knows better way..."

**Scene 4: Forbidden Pool (Henneth Annûn, Chapter 6)**
- Gollum fishes in forbidden pool, Faramir's men want to kill him
- **Choice**: Warn Gollum (trust +20) OR let him be captured (trust -30)
- **Canonical**: Frodo warns him, but Gollum feels betrayed anyway (trust -10)

**Scene 5: Stairs of Cirith Ungol (Chapter 7)**
- Gollum debates internally whether to betray Frodo
- **Internal monologue**: "Master is nice to us... But the Precious! We wants it! No! Leave master alone!"
- **Turning Point**:
  - High trust: Sméagol almost wins, touching moment
  - Low trust: Gollum wins, leads to Shelob
  - Canonical: Sam wakes up, ruins the moment, Gollum turns to betrayal

**Scene 6: Shelob's Lair (Chapter 7)**
- Gollum abandons Frodo to Shelob
- "She will deal with them... then we takes it! The Precious will be ours!"
- **Outcome**: Frodo paralyzed, Sam fights Shelob

**Scene 7: Mount Doom (Sammath Naur, Chapter 9)**
- Final confrontation - Gollum attacks Frodo for Ring
- **Canonical**: Bites off Frodo's finger with Ring, falls into lava dancing with joy
- **Alternate Endings**:
  - Redemption route: Sméagol helps Frodo throw Ring in
  - Corruption route: Frodo claims Ring, Gollum attacks, both fall (player dies)

### Combat Stats
- **HP**: 120
- **Damage**: 30
- **Abilities**:
  - **Throttle**: Grapple attack, 40 damage + stun 1 turn (leaps onto victim's back)
  - **Sneak Attack**: 60 damage from stealth
  - **Cave Adaptation**: Perfect vision in darkness, +50% stealth underground
- **Weakness**: Sunlight (blinded), loud noises (frightened)

### Tragic Element
Once a hobbit like Frodo, corrupted by the Ring. A mirror of what Frodo could become. Redemption is barely possible, but the path exists for those who show perfect kindness.

## 🎒 Expanded Equipment

### Hobbit Gear
- **Hobbit Traveling Cloak**: +10% stealth in forests
- **Walking Stick**: 8 damage, +5 stamina regen when traveling
- **Elven Rope (Lothlórien)**: Never breaks, 50ft, can climb any surface
- **Pipe-weed Pouch**: Smoking restores +10 morale, -5 stress
  - Brands: Longbottom Leaf, Old Toby, Southern Star
- **Second Breakfast Rations**: Restore 20 HP, +morale (bacon, eggs, sausages, mushrooms)

### Ranger Gear
- **Ranger Cloak (weathered)**: 15 defense, +20% stealth, +cold resistance
- **Athelas (Kingsfoil)**: Cure poison/disease, +50 HP (in Aragorn's hands only)
- **Ranger's Longbow**: 40 damage, long range, +accuracy in dim light
- **Travel Bread (cram)**: Restore 30 HP, lasts forever

### Elven Artifacts

- **Miruvor (Cordial of Imladris)**: Full HP/Stamina restore, +fire resistance 1 hour
- **Elven Waybread (Lembas)**: One bite = full day's sustenance, -5 corruption (12 cakes)
- **Elven Brooch (Lothlórien leaf)**: +Hope, identifies wearer as friend of elves

### Dwarven Crafts
- **Dwarven Ale**: +20 HP, +Strength 10 min, -Agility 10 min

### Gondorian Equipment

### Enemy Loot

## 👥 Additional NPCs

### Bilbo Baggins
- **Title**: The Ring-finder
- **Age**: 131 years old
- **Role**: Mentor, lore-keeper
- **Location**: Rivendell (retired)
- **Gifts to Frodo**: Sting, Mithril vest, One Ring (unknowingly), Elvish lore
- **Quest**: Help Bilbo finish "There and Back Again"
- **Dialogue**: "It's mine! My own! My... precious... *catches himself* No, no... you must take it, Frodo."

### Tom Bombadil
- **Title**: Master of wood, water and hill
- **Race**: Unknown (Maia? Nature spirit?)
- **Age**: Older than the world
- **Level**: 99
- **Location**: Old Forest (near Shire)
- **Unique Trait**: ONLY being immune to One Ring - can wear it without turning invisible
- **Abilities**: Command over nature, drives away Barrow-wights with song
- **Dialogue Style**: Rhyming verse - "Hey dol! merry dol! ring a dong dillo!"
- **Wife**: Goldberry (River-daughter)

### Other Notable NPCs

**Hobbits**:
- **Farmer Maggot**: Gruff but kind farmer, provides mushrooms, warns of Black Riders
- **Fredegar "Fatty" Bolger**: Cowardly but loyal, stays behind as decoy
- **Lotho Sackville-Baggins**: Villain, industrializes Shire under Sharkey

**Elves**:
- **Glorfindel**: Elf-lord, killed Balrog in First Age, rescues Frodo from Nazgûl
- **Haldir**: Marchwarden of Lothlórien, guards border
- **Celeborn**: Galadriel's husband, gives boats to Fellowship

**Men**:
- **Éomer**: Third Marshal of Rohan, later King, leads Rohirrim
- **Denethor II**: Steward of Gondor, prideful, despairing, suicide by fire
- **Barliman Butterbur**: Innkeeper of Prancing Pony, forgets Gandalf's letter

**Nazgûl**:
- **Witch-king of Angmar**: Lord of Nazgûl, "No man can kill me" (killed by Éowyn + Merry)
- **Khamûl**: Second Chief, best tracker, leads hunt in Shire

**Ents**:
- **Treebeard (Fangorn)**: Eldest Ent, ~17,000 years old, "Don't be hasty!"
- **Quickbeam**: Hasty Ent (rowan tree), befriends Merry and Pippin

**Sauron's Servants**:
- **Mouth of Sauron**: Lieutenant of Barad-dûr, diplomat at Black Gate
- **Gothmog**: Lieutenant of Morgul, leads siege of Minas Tirith
