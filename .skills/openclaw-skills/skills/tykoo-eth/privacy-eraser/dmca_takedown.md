# DMCA Takedown Notice (版权投诉)

## 适用场景
- 你的原创内容被盗用
- 你的照片被未授权使用
- 你的文字被抄袭
- 可以"借用"版权框架来达到删除目的

## 发送对象
- 网站的 DMCA Agent（通常在网站 Legal/Copyright 页面）
- 或 abuse@domain.com / legal@domain.com
- 托管商的 abuse 邮箱

## DMCA Takedown Notice 模板

```
Subject: DMCA Takedown Notice - Immediate Action Required

To Whom It May Concern:

I am writing to notify you of copyright infringement pursuant to the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (17 U.S.C. § 512).

COPYRIGHTED WORK:

The copyrighted work being infringed is:
- Description: [描述你的原创内容]
- Original location (if applicable): [原始发布位置，如你的网站/社交媒体]
- Date of creation/publication: [创作日期]

INFRINGING MATERIAL:

The material that is infringing my copyright is located at:
- URL(s): 
  [URL 1]
  [URL 2]

Specific description of infringing content:
[具体描述哪部分侵权，如："The article copies my blog post verbatim" 或 "My photograph is used without permission"]

GOOD FAITH STATEMENT:

I have a good faith belief that the use of the copyrighted material described above is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.

ACCURACY STATEMENT:

I swear, under penalty of perjury, that the information in this notification is accurate and that I am the copyright owner, or am authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed.

CONTACT INFORMATION:

Name: [Your full legal name]
Address: [Your address]
Phone: [Your phone number]
Email: [Your email]

SIGNATURE:

[Your electronic or physical signature]
[Date]

---

I request that you remove or disable access to the infringing material as soon as possible.

Thank you for your prompt attention to this matter.
```

## 发送给托管商

如果网站不回应，可以投诉给他们的托管商：

1. 用 whois 查询域名信息
2. 找到托管商/注册商
3. 发送 DMCA 到他们的 abuse 邮箱

常见托管商 abuse 邮箱：
- Cloudflare: abuse@cloudflare.com
- AWS: abuse@amazonaws.com
- GoDaddy: abuse@godaddy.com
- Namecheap: abuse@namecheap.com
- DigitalOcean: abuse@digitalocean.com

## 处理时间
- 大平台：24-72 小时
- 小网站：取决于他们的响应
- 托管商：3-7 天

## 注意事项
1. DMCA 仅适用于版权，不直接适用于隐私
2. 但如果内容包含你的原创元素（照片、文字），可以使用
3. 虚假 DMCA 声明可能承担法律责任
4. 被投诉方可以提交 counter-notice（反通知）
