# GDPR 数据删除请求 (Right to Erasure)

## 适用范围
- 你是 EU/EEA 居民，或
- 数据处理发生在 EU，或
- 服务面向 EU 用户

## 法律依据
GDPR Article 17 - Right to erasure ('right to be forgotten')

## 发送对象
公司的 DPO (Data Protection Officer) 或 privacy@company.com

## 请求模板

### Subject / 主题
```
GDPR Article 17 Request - Data Erasure / Right to be Forgotten
```

### Body / 正文

```
To the Data Protection Officer,

I am writing to exercise my right to erasure under Article 17 of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

REQUESTOR INFORMATION:
- Full Name: [Your full legal name]
- Email associated with your service: [email]
- Other identifying information: [username, account ID, etc.]
- Country of residence: [EU member state]

REQUEST DETAILS:

I request the complete erasure of my personal data held by your organization. Specifically, I request deletion of:

1. The content at the following URL(s):
   - [URL 1]
   - [URL 2]

2. Any personal data you hold about me, including but not limited to:
   - [Full name]
   - [Email address]  
   - [Phone number]
   - [Physical address]
   - [Any other specific data]

LEGAL BASIS FOR ERASURE:

My request is based on the following grounds under Article 17(1):
☐ (a) The personal data is no longer necessary for the purpose it was collected
☐ (b) I withdraw my consent and there is no other legal ground for processing
☐ (c) I object to the processing under Article 21(1)
☐ (d) The personal data has been unlawfully processed
☐ (e) Erasure is required for compliance with a legal obligation

DEADLINE:

Under GDPR Article 12(3), you are required to respond to this request within one month of receipt. If you need an extension, you must inform me within the initial one-month period.

VERIFICATION:

I am happy to provide additional verification of my identity if required. Please contact me at the email address provided.

CONSEQUENCES OF NON-COMPLIANCE:

Please note that failure to comply with GDPR can result in significant fines under Article 83. I reserve the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority if this request is not handled appropriately.

Thank you for your prompt attention to this matter.

Sincerely,
[Your name]
[Date]
[Email]
[Phone - optional]
```

## 处理时间
- 法定期限: 30 天
- 可延长: 最多再 60 天（需通知你）

## 如未回应
1. 发送提醒邮件，引用原始请求
2. 联系该国的数据保护机构 (DPA)
   - 德国: BfDI
   - 法国: CNIL  
   - 英国: ICO
   - 等等
3. 考虑法律诉讼

## 注意事项
- 保存所有通信记录
- 记录发送日期作为 30 天倒计时起点
- 公司可能要求身份验证，这是合理的
