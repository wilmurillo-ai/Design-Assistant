# ASA + ASO Synergy Strategy

## Why They Must Work Together

ASA and ASO are not separate strategies — they're two sides of the same coin. Running them in isolation wastes both budget and keyword opportunities.

**The core loop:**
```
ASA Discovery Campaign
        ↓
  Finds converting search terms
        ↓
  High-converting terms → ASO keyword fields
        ↓
  Organic ranking improves
        ↓
  Lower spend needed for those keywords in ASA
        ↓
  Reinvest savings into discovering more new terms
```

---

## How ASA Data Improves ASO

### 1. Keyword Validation

Your ASO keyword field has 100 characters (iOS). These are precious. Before the ASA + ASO loop existed, you were guessing which 100 characters to use.

After running ASA Discovery for 4+ weeks, you have **actual conversion data**:
- Which exact search terms drove installs
- Which had high TTR (users recognize the relevance from your app name)
- Which had high CR (users liked what they saw on your product page)

**Action**: Replace underperforming ASO keywords with your top-converting ASA search terms. These are proven — real users searched them and installed your app.

### 2. Title and Subtitle Optimization

If your ASA Discovery campaign finds that "HIIT workout for office workers" converts at 55%, but your current subtitle is "Daily Fitness Planner" — you have a problem. You're not indexed for the phrase users are proving they want.

**Action**: Audit your app title and subtitle against your top 10 ASA converting search terms. If there's a mismatch, update ASO to include those proven terms.

### 3. Product Page Conversion Intelligence

Low CR in ASA means your product page is failing to convert taps into installs. This is the same page users see organically. Fixing the product page for ASA simultaneously improves organic conversion.

**Action**: When CR improves through product page optimization (screenshot changes, description updates), expect both ASA conversion and organic conversion to improve together.

## How ASO Improves ASA

### 1. Better ASO → Lower CPA in ASA

Apple's algorithm considers your app's organic relevance when determining your ad rank. An app with strong ASO for a keyword competes better in ASA auctions for that same keyword — often at a lower CPT than a competitor with weak ASO.

**Practical impact**: Two apps bidding the same CPT for "habit tracker" — the one with "habit tracker" in their app title gets better ad placement at the same or lower cost.

### 2. Higher Organic Ranking Reduces Need for Paid

If your ASO work (partly informed by ASA data) gets you to organic top-10 for a keyword, you can reduce ASA bids on that keyword — you're getting free installs for it. Reallocate that budget to keywords where you don't yet rank organically.

**Budget optimization rule**: Monthly, compare your organic keyword rankings against your ASA keyword list. For any keyword where you rank organically top 5, consider reducing ASA bids by 30-50%.

### 3. Strong Rating Reduces CPT

App rating affects TTR. A 4.7-star app in an ASA result gets tapped far more often than a 3.8-star app. Higher TTR = more installs per impression = effectively lower CPA at the same bid.

**Implication**: Every improvement to your review management strategy (timing of review prompts, responding to negative reviews) has a multiplier effect on ASA campaign economics.

## Practical Synergy Workflow

### Monthly Sync Process (1 hour/month)

**Step 1: Export ASA Search Terms Report**
- In ASA Advanced dashboard → Reports → Search Terms
- Filter last 30 days, sort by conversion rate descending
- Export to spreadsheet

**Step 2: Identify ASO Update Candidates**
From the search terms report, flag terms where:
- CR > 35% AND installs ≥ 10 (proven converters)
- Term is NOT currently in your app name, subtitle, or keyword field
- Term is a natural fit for your app (not a happy accident)

**Step 3: Update ASO Keyword Field**
- Remove your lowest-performing ASO keywords (check impressions in App Store Connect)
- Add the proven ASA search terms
- Submit update

**Step 4: Reduce ASA Bids for Organic Winners**
- Check App Store Connect → Organic Search Rankings
- For any keyword ranking top 5 organically, reduce ASA bid by 30%
- Reallocate saved budget to discovery

**Step 5: Product Page Alignment Check**
- Review your top 5 ASA keywords
- Are these keywords reflected in your first screenshot's headline text?
- Is the app's primary value proposition on screen 1?
- If not, schedule a screenshot update

## Common Mistakes That Break the Synergy

**Mistake 1**: Running ASA and ASO as separate responsibilities (different teams/people)
- Fix: Monthly sync meeting between whoever manages each

**Mistake 2**: Not using Search Terms report from ASA
- Fix: Export is free and available in every ASA account — schedule it monthly

**Mistake 3**: Keeping ASA on for keywords where you already rank #1 organically
- Fix: Regular comparison of ASA keyword list vs organic rankings

**Mistake 4**: Fixing product page without informing ASA budget allocation
- Fix: After product page update, increase ASA budget temporarily to capture improved CR window
