## Key BNM Requirements

### 1. Product Approval

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Product Filing | Must file with BNM before launch |
| Pricing | Actuarial sign-off required |
| Market Study | Evidence of market demand required |

### 2. Underwriting

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Age Limits | Min 30 days, Max 70 years (varies by product) |
| Medical Examination | Panel clinic network required |
| Reinsurance | Retrocession arrangements disclosed |
| Unknown Exclusions | BNM prohibition: ITOs cannot enforce conditions or exclusions not disclosed in policy terms (Dec 2025 regulatory directive) |

### 3. Medical Card / Health Insurance — 2026 Reforms 🆕

**BNM RESET Framework (active 2025–2027):**

| Strategic Thrust | Description |
|-----------------|-------------|
| Revamp MHIT Product | Redesign base Medical and Health Insurance/Takaful (MHIT) product |
| Enhance Implementation | Strengthen governance across insurers and hospitals |
| Digital Health | Expand digital health data integration |
| Cost-Effective Care | Promote affordable healthcare alternatives |
| DRG Payment System | Replace fee-for-service with Diagnosis-Related Groups (DRG) payment at hospitals |

**Base MHIT Plan — Key Details:**

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Coverage Age | Up to 85 years old |
| Enrolment Age | Capped at 70 years old |
| Pilot Timeline | **H2 2026 (voluntary basis)** |
| Full Rollout | Early 2027 (coincides with expiry of BNM interim measures) |
| Co-Payment Structure | Two-tier: in-network hospitals (no co-share); out-of-network hospitals (20% co-share, capped at RM 3,000 per disability) |
| Policyholders | Can switch to base plan with current insurer without new medicals |

**Premium Staggering Interim Measures (active through end 2026):**

| Rule | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Stagger Duration | Minimum 3 years (2024, 2025, 2026) |
| Annual Cap | At least 80% of policyholders: annual premium adjustment ≤ 10% |
| Age 60+ (min plan) | 1-year pause on premium adjustment due to medical claims inflation |
| Medical Inflation 2026 | Projected 16% (up from estimated 15% in 2025) |

**Standard MHIT Plan Indicative Premiums (monthly):**

| Age Group | Standard Plan | Standard-Plus Plan |
|-----------|--------------|-------------------|
| 31–35 | RM 80–120 | RM 50–70 |
| 61–65 | RM 280–350 | RM 220–280 |
| 75+ | RM 500–780 | RM 400–660 |

> Final premium levels will be confirmed closer to 2027 launch.

### 4. Broker Conduct — Policy Document (Effective 1 Jan 2026) 🆕

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Policy Document | BNM Policy Document on Prudent and Professional Conduct of Insurance and Takaful Brokers; effective 1 Jan 2026 |
| Applies To | Approved insurance brokers (FSA) and approved takaful brokers (IFSA) |
| Board Size | Minimum 3 directors; size commensurate with business risk |
| Independent Director | At least 1 independent director required — **effective 1 Jan 2027** |
| Takaful Brokers | At least 1 director must have understanding of Shariah concepts |
| Professional Indemnity | PI insurance / takaful cover must be maintained; amount reviewed regularly |
| Annual Report | Submit to BNM: performance against BSC framework by end of March following year |
| Financial Statements | Must be available to public (at branches or website) |

### 5. Digital Insurance / Takaful Operators (DITO) Licence 🆕

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Licence Type | New Digital Insurer and Takaful Operator (DITO) licence |
| Application Window | **2 Jan 2025 – 31 Dec 2026** (applications open now) |
| Existing Licensed ITOs | Do NOT need separate DITO licence to digitalise existing operations |
| Separate Entity | Existing ITO wishing to run digital business separately → apply for separate corporate entity |
| Core Value Propositions | Must demonstrate: Inclusion (underserved consumers), Competition (innovative products), Efficiency (seamless UX + cost savings) |
| Composite | Separate licences required for general and life/family takaful; cannot be combined |

### 6. Investment-Linked Policy (ILP)

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Protection Element | Minimum 10% of premium |
| Sales Charge | Maximum 100% first year premium |
| Switching | Unlimited free switches |

### 7. Takaful (Governed by IFSA 2013)

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Wakalah | Agency model (fee-based) |
| Mudharabah | Profit-sharing model |
| Tabarru | Contribution to risk pool; separate fund required |
| Qard | Interest-free loan for shortfall |
| Shariah Governance | SGF 2019 (Shariah Governance Framework); Shariah Committee required |
| Retakaful | Preferential use of retakaful operators before conventional reinsurance |
| Participant Funds | PRF (Participant Risk Fund) and PIF (Participant Investment Fund) must be maintained separately |

### 8. Policyholder Protection (PIDM)

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| PIDM Coverage | Life insurance: sum insured up to RM 500,000 per person per insurer |
| Disclosure | Insurer must disclose PIDM membership in policy documents |
| Assessment | Participating insurers pay annual assessment premiums to PIDM |

### 10. Claims Management (BNM directive Dec 2025) 🆕

| Directive | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Fair & Prompt Settlement | ITOs cannot unreasonably delay or deny claims without valid justification |
| Unknown Exclusions | Cannot enforce conditions or exclusions not disclosed in policy terms |
| Retrospective Investigations | BNM red line against unreasonable retrospective medical history investigation at point of claim |
| Grievance Mechanism Committee (GMC) | Industry-level body (LIAM, PIAM, MTA, MMA, APHM) to handle medical claims disputes |

## INVARIANT Declarations

```
INVARIANT 1: Unknown exclusions cannot be enforced; only disclosed policy terms apply
  Source: BNM directive Dec 2025
  Effect: Claims system must validate that any exclusion exists in policy terms before applying

INVARIANT 2: Premium repricing cannot exceed 10% annually for ≥80% of policyholders
  Source: BNM interim measures (through end 2026)
  Effect: Repricing batch must enforce staggering cap until full rollout of base MHIT plan

INVARIANT 3: Base MHIT switchers must be accepted without new medicals
  Source: BNM Base MHIT Plan design
  Effect: Reinstatement / switch workflow must suppress underwriting for eligible switchers

INVARIANT 4: Participant Risk Fund (Takaful) must be maintained separately from company funds
  Source: IFSA 2013 + SGF 2019
  Effect: Separate Tabarru/PRF fund accounting mandatory; no co-mingling with shareholder funds

INVARIANT 5: DITO applicants must carry on either general OR life/family takaful; not both in same entity
  Source: BNM DITO Policy Document
  Effect: DITO licence applies to a single category; composite digital operation requires two entities
```
