## Key Requirements

### 1. Market Entry & Licensing

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Foreign Ownership | Max 80% cap (GR14/2018); grandfathered entities cannot increase |
| Composite Insurance | NOT permitted; life and non-life must be separate entities |
| Bankruptcy | OJK is the exclusive body to submit bankruptcy petition (Law 4/2023) |
| Licensing | OJK issues all business licences; minimum paid-up capital IDR 1 trillion for new insurers |

### 2. Minimum Capital — 2026 Milestones 🆕

| Entity Type | Before 2026 | **2026** | 2028 Tier 1 | 2028 Tier 2 |
|-------------|-------------|----------|-------------|-------------|
| Conventional Insurer | Rp 150 bn | **Rp 250 bn** | Rp 500 bn | Rp 1 tn |
| Sharia Insurer | Rp 50 bn | **Rp 100 bn** | Rp 200 bn | Rp 500 bn |
| Reinsurer | Rp 300 bn | **Rp 500 bn** | Rp 1 tn | Rp 2 tn |

> 109 of 144 companies had met requirements as of March 2025.

### 3. Product Approval (POJK 8/2024)

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Prior OJK Approval | Required for most products before marketing |
| Exempt Products | Never marketed in Indonesia + no savings/cash value element + not credit/sharia financing insurance |
| Post-Market Report | Exempt products: report to OJK within 5 business days of first marketing |
| Product Committee | Mandatory internal sign-off; documented for OJK post-launch audit |
| Product Recall | OJK or insurer may initiate; action plan agreed with OJK; not an automatic sanction |

### 4. Health Insurance — POJK 33/2025 + POJK 36/2025 🆕

**POJK 33/2025 — Risk-Based Health Assessment (Effective 1 Jan 2026):**

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Scope | All insurers, surety institutions, pension funds (conventional and Sharia) |
| Risk-Based Supervision | OJK assesses: GCG, Risk Profile, Rentability, Capital/Financing |
| Self-Assessment | Companies must submit self-assessment to OJK via OJK reporting system |
| Medical Advisory Board | Required for companies providing mandatory health insurance; must have digital health data access |
| Prior OJK Approval | Insurance companies must obtain OJK approval before entering the health LoB |

**POJK 36/2025 — Health Insurance Ecosystem (Phased 2026):**

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Co-Payment | Mandatory minimum **10%** for all indemnity-based health insurance (inpatient + outpatient) |
| Co-Payment Caps | Outpatient: max **IDR 300,000** per claim; Inpatient: max **IDR 3,000,000** per claim |
| Microinsurance | Exempt from co-payment requirement |
| BPJS Coordination | Mandatory benefit coordination mechanism with BPJS Kesehatan |
| No Risk Division | Health products must NOT include risk division features |
| Auto-Renewing Policies | Existing policies must comply by **31 Dec 2026** |
| Premium Adjustment | Maximum once per year |

> **Note:** POJK 36/2025 replaces the deferred SEOJK 7/2025 (originally scheduled 1 Jan 2026, deferred July 2025 pending this regulation).

### 5. Distribution Channels

| Channel | Status | Notes |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Bancassurance | ✅ | Agent registration required |
| Agency | ✅ | OJK certification + registration mandatory (POJK 36/2024) |
| Direct | ❌ | Not permitted for broad consumer sales |
| Digital | ✅ | PSE registration + prior OJK approval for partnerships |
| e-Commerce / Embedded | ✅ | Permitted under existing conduct standards; no separate new regulation |

**Digital Insurance (POJK 36/2024 — in force):**
- Must register as PSE (Electronic System Provider) with MoCI
- IT staff: min 3 years experience
- Cannot outsource entire digital service or customer data management
- Partnership digital marketing: prior OJK approval required

### 6. Underwriting

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Non-Disclosure | Cancellation requires mutual agreement or court order (Constitutional Court 83/PUU-XXII/2024) |
| Reinsurance | Foreign reinsurers: permitted if country has bilateral/FTA agreement with Indonesia (ASEAN/RCEP qualify) |
| Non-Simple Risks | Tiered declinature before foreign placement |

### 7. Claims & Consumer Protection

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| POJK 22/2023 | Prohibits discriminatory practices; insurer accountability for policyholder losses |
| Unknown Exclusions | Cannot enforce exclusions not disclosed in policy terms |
| Fraud Detection | Mandatory anti-fraud procedures |
| Health Claims | Medical Advisory Board oversight of quality and risk management (POJK 33/2025) |

### 8. Sharia / Takaful

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Supervisory Body | OJK + DSN-MUI Sharia board |
| Models | Wakalah and Mudharabah both permitted |
| Tabarru Fund | Separate risk fund; managed independently from company funds |
| Qard | Interest-free loan to cover Tabarru shortfall |
| Sharia Audit | Annual DSN-MUI audit required |
| Capital 2026 | Sharia insurer minimum: Rp 100 billion |

### 9. Periodic Reporting (POJK 22/2024)

| Report | Frequency |
|--------|-----------|
| Monthly Operations Report | Monthly |
| Annual Audited Financial Report | Annual |
| Publication Report | Per monthly + annual cycle |
| AML / CTF Risk Assessment | Annual |
| Accountant Appointment | Per event |

### 10. ILP / Unit-Link (OJK Circular 5/SEOJK.05/2022)

| Requirement | Notes |
|-------------|-------|
| Min Protection | Minimum sum assured required per circular |
| Illustration | Insurance and investment components must be clearly separated |
| Mis-Selling Controls | Enhanced suitability assessment; product committee sign-off |

## INVARIANT Declarations

```
INVARIANT 1: Composite insurance (life + non-life) is NOT permitted
  Effect: InsureMO must be for a single-category licensed entity only

INVARIANT 2: Foreign ownership cannot exceed 80%; grandfathered entities cannot increase
  Source: GR14/2018

INVARIANT 3: Policy cancellation on non-disclosure requires mutual agreement or court order
  Source: Constitutional Court Decision No. 83/PUU-XXII/2024

INVARIANT 4: Digital marketing partnership requires prior OJK approval before activation
  Source: POJK 36/2024

INVARIANT 5: Product committee must sign off before OJK product submission
  Source: POJK 8/2024

INVARIANT 6: Customer data management cannot be fully outsourced to third-party vendors
  Source: POJK 36/2024

INVARIANT 7: Health insurance LoB requires OJK prior approval before product launch
  Source: POJK 33/2025 (effective 1 Jan 2026)

INVARIANT 8: All indemnity-based health products must include minimum 10% co-payment
  Source: POJK 36/2025; microinsurance is the only exemption
  Compliance deadline for existing auto-renewing policies: 31 Dec 2026
```
