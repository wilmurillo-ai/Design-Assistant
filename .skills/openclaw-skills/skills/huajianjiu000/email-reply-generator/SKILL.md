# Email Reply Generator

## What This Skill Does

This skill helps you craft professional email responses in seconds. Simply paste the email you received, and I'll generate an appropriate reply based on the context and your preferred tone.

## Features

- **Context-Aware Responses**: Analyzes the original email to understand the intent and provides relevant replies
- **Multiple Tone Options**: Choose from professional, friendly, neutral, or firm tones
- **Quick Turnaround**: Saves time on routine email correspondence
- **Customizable Length**: Short acknowledgment or detailed response based on your needs

## How to Use

1. **Paste the Email**: Share the email content you need to reply to
2. **Specify Your Intent**: Tell me what kind of response you want (accept, decline, acknowledge, negotiate, etc.)
3. **Choose Your Tone**: Select the appropriate tone for the situation
4. **Get Your Reply**: Receive a professionally written response ready to send

## Example Use Cases

- Accepting meeting invitations
- Politely declining requests
- Following up on pending items
- Expressing gratitude for opportunities
- Requesting more information
- Negotiating deadlines or terms

## Tips for Best Results

- Include the full email context for more accurate responses
- Mention any specific points you want to address
- Specify if there are any constraints (deadline, budget, availability)
- Let me know your relationship with the sender (colleague, client, supervisor)
