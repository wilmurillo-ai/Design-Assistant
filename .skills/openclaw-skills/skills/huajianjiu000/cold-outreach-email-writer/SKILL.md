# Cold Email Writer

## What This Skill Does

Cold emails don't have to feel cold. I write personalized, compelling outreach emails that get responses instead of landing in the trash folder.

## Features

- **Subject Lines That Open**: Catchy, relevant subject lines that boost open rates
- **Personalization Framework**: Templates that feel personal, not spammy
- **Value-First Approach**: Leads with benefits, not features
- **Clear CTA**: Specific ask that's easy to respond to
- **Multiple Variations**: A/B test different approaches

## How to Use

1. **Describe Your Offer**: What are you selling or proposing?
2. **Define Your Prospect**: Who are you reaching out to? Job title, industry?
3. **Share Your Goal**: What action do you want? Meeting, call, demo?
4. **Get Your Email**: Receive a ready-to-send cold email

## Email Types I Write

- **Introduction Emails**: First contact with potential customers
- **Partnership Proposals**: Business development outreach
- **Investor Outreach**: Pitching to VCs and angels
- **Media Pitches**: Getting journalists interested
- **Recruiting**: Reaching out to potential hires
- **Networking**: Expanding professional connections

## What Makes These Emails Work

- **Short and Scannable**: Respects busy schedules
- **Personalized Opening**: Shows you did your research
- **Clear Value Prop**: What's in it for them?
- **Social Proof**: Credibility signals when relevant
- **Easy CTA**: Simple yes/no question or specific ask

## Best For

- Sales representatives
- Business development managers
- Startup founders
- Freelancers finding clients
- Recruiters
- Anyone doing outbound outreach

## Tips for Best Results

- Share any research you have on the prospect
- Mention your company/product briefly
- Tell me if you have a specific CTA in mind
- Let me know your industry and typical deal size
