## Viral Title Patterns

### High CTR Title Characteristics

1. **Number Type**
   - Contains specific numbers (3 tips, 5 steps, 99%)
   - Example: "Master these 5 skills, double your salary!"

2. **Question Type**
   - Triggers curiosity
   - Example: "Why can't you save money?"

3. **Comparison Type**
   - Creates contrast
   - Example: "Same price, why does hers look better?"

4. **Tips Type**
   - Provides practical value
   - Example: "Most complete guide, save for later!"

5. **Emotion Type**
   - Triggers resonance
   - Example: "My genuine advice: don't care too much"

6. **Urgency Type**
   - Creates urgency
   - Example: "Limited time! Don't miss out"

## Platform-Specific Tips

### Instagram
- Use line breaks for readability
- Include 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Emojis increase engagement by 15%

### TikTok
- Use "POV:" format for relatability
- Challenges and trends work well
- Questions in captions boost comments

### Twitter/X
- Thread format for detailed content
- Hot takes drive engagement
- Numbers in headlines work

### YouTube
- Brackets for video type [Tutorial], [Vlog]
- Long-tail keywords in titles
- Numbers and adjectives ("Ultimate", "Complete")
