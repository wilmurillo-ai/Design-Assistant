# Proposal Writer

## What This Skill Does

A great proposal wins business. I help you create persuasive, professional proposals that clearly articulate the problem, your solution, and the value you'll deliver.

## Features

- **Structured Format**: Follows proven framework
- **Value-Focused**: Lead with benefits
- **Clear Pricing**: Transparent investment options
- **Risk Mitigation**: Shows you've thought of everything
- **CTA Ready**: Clear next steps

## Proposal Sections

### 1. Executive Summary
```
[Client Name],

[Your company] is excited to propose [solution] that 
will help you achieve [specific outcome]. 

Based on our understanding of your needs, we recommend 
[approach]. This will deliver [key benefit 1], [key 
benefit 2], and [key benefit 3].

Investment: [$X] over [timeline]
Expected ROI: [specific outcome]

We're confident we can deliver exceptional results for you.
```

### 2. Problem Statement
```
Challenge:
[Clear description of the problem or opportunity]

Impact:
- [Business impact 1]
- [Business impact 2]
- [Business impact 3]

Current state vs. desired state:
- [What is] → [What could be]

We understand this is important because [reason it matters to them].
```

### 3. Proposed Solution
```
Our Approach:

Phase 1: [Name] ([Timeline])
- [Deliverable 1]
- [Deliverable 2]
- [Deliverable 3]

Phase 2: [Name] ([Timeline])
- [Deliverable 1]
- [Deliverable 2]

Phase 3: [Name] ([Timeline])
- [Deliverable 1]
- [Deliverable 2]

Deliverables:
- [Specific output 1]
- [Specific output 2]
```

### 4. Investment & Pricing
```
Investment Options:

Standard Package: $[Amount]
- [What's included]
- [What's included]
Best for: [Ideal client profile]

Premium Package: $[Amount]
- [Everything in Standard]
- [Additional items]
- [Additional items]
Best for: [Ideal client profile]

Payment Terms:
- 50% upon signing
- 50% upon completion

* Custom packages available upon request
```

### 5. Why Choose Us
```
Why [Your Company]:

1. [Relevant experience 1]
   - [Specific proof or example]

2. [Relevant experience 2]
   - [Specific proof or example]

3. [Unique value proposition]
   - [What sets you apart]

Client Success Story:
"[Brief testimonial or case study]"
— [Name], [Title], [Company]
```

### 6. Terms & Next Steps
```
Timeline:
Project begins: [Date]
Estimated completion: [Date]

Terms:
- [Key terms and conditions]
- [Cancellation policy]
- [Revision policy]

Next Steps:
1. Review this proposal (this week)
2. Questions? Let's schedule a call
3. Ready to proceed? Sign below
4. We get to work!

Valid for 30 days from [date]
```

## Proposal Tips

- Research the client beforehand
- Lead with their needs, not your credentials
- Use specific numbers and results
- Keep it concise—respect their time
- Follow up within 3-5 days
