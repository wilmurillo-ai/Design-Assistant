# Instagram Bio Generator

## What This Skill Does

Your Instagram bio is your first impression—make it count. I create compelling, keyword-optimized bios that attract your target audience and communicate your unique value in 150 characters or less.

## Features

- **Brand-Optimized**: Reflects your personality and goals
- **Keyword Smart**: Includes searchable terms for discovery
- **CTA-Ready**: Clear call-to-action included
- **Emoji Strategy**: Tasteful use of emojis for visual appeal
- **Multiple Options**: Get 3 different versions to choose from

## How to Use

1. **Who Are You?**: Your name, title, or profession
2. **What Do You Offer?**: Your content focus or business
3. **Target Audience**: Who should follow you?
4. **Your Goal**: Get followers, drive traffic, sell products?
5. **Optional**: Website link or brand name

## Bio Formulas That Work

### The Creator Bio
```
[Clear Niche] | [Proof/credibility] | [CTA]
Example: 📸 Travel Photographer | 📍 30 Countries | DM for collabs ✈️
```

### The Business Bio
```
[Emoji] [What You Do] [Who You Help] [CTA]
Example: 💼 Helping startups scale | Marketing strategist | Free guide in bio 👇
```

### The Personal Brand Bio
```
[Title] | [Unique Angle] | [Social Proof]
Example: Tech founder | Making AI simple | 50K+ founders learning daily
```

## Tips for Great Bios

- Use line breaks for readability
- Include 1-2 relevant emojis (not too many)
- Add a clear next step (link, DM, etc.)
- Update periodically to reflect growth
- Keep it authentic—your voice matters
