# Cold Email Writer

## Description
Craft compelling, personalized cold emails that get responses. Perfect for sales prospecting, partnership outreach, investor introductions, or networking. Generates multiple variations with different tones (professional, casual, urgent) and includes subject line optimization.

## How to Use
1. Define your target recipient and goal
2. Choose email type (sales pitch, partnership, networking, follow-up)
3. Specify tone preference (formal, casual, friendly)
4. Receive multiple email variations with subject lines

## Example

**Input:**
```
Recipient: Sarah Chen, VP of Marketing at TechCorp
Sender Goal: Schedule a demo for our analytics platform
Company: DataViz Pro
Context: Found her article on marketing analytics
```

**Output:**
```
## Cold Email Variations

### Version 1 - Professional (Recommended)

**Subject Line:** Quick question about TechCorp's analytics setup

Hi Sarah,

I came across your excellent article on marketing analytics trends and found your perspective on data-driven decision making refreshing.

At DataViz Pro, we've helped companies like Shopify and HubSpot transform their marketing analytics, reducing reporting time by 70%.

Would you have 15 minutes this week to explore how we might support TechCorp's analytics initiatives?

Best regards,
[Your Name]

---

### Version 2 - Casual/Friendly

**Subject Line:** Loved your marketing analytics piece!

Sarah,

Your article really resonated - especially the part about "analytics fatigue." We hear this from marketing leaders constantly.

Built DataViz Pro specifically to solve that headache. Would love to show you how it works at TechCorp.

Free for a quick 15-min chat this week?

Cheers,
[Your Name]

---

### Subject Line Options
1. "Quick question about TechCorp's analytics setup"
2. "15-min demo that could save your team 10+ hours weekly"
3. "Saw your article on marketing analytics"
```
