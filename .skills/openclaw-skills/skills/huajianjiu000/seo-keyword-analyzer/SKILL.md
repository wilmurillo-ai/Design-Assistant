# SEO Keyword Analyzer

> 🔍 Unlock your content's search potential

## Skill Overview

This skill helps content creators and marketers optimize their content for search engines. It analyzes keywords, provides search insights, and offers actionable recommendations for improving SEO performance.

## Core Capabilities

- **Keyword Research**: Find relevant keywords for your topic
- **Difficulty Analysis**: Assess ranking competition
- **Content Suggestions**: Ideas for keyword integration
- **Long-tail Discovery**: Find less competitive, high-intent keywords
- **SERP Analysis**: Understand what ranks for target keywords

## Trigger Keywords

- `/analyze-keywords`
- `/seo-keywords`
- `/keyword-research`
- `/optimize-seo`

## Analysis Features

### 1. Keyword Metrics
- Search volume estimation
- Competition level (Low/Medium/High)
- Ranking difficulty score
- Trend direction

### 2. Content Recommendations
- Title optimization
- Header structure suggestions
- Content length recommendations
- Related keywords to include

### 3. Competitor Insights
- What top-ranking pages cover
- Common content patterns
- Gaps and opportunities

## Usage Examples

### Example 1: Blog Post Keyword Analysis
**Input**:
```
Topic: How to start a podcast
Target audience: Beginners
Content type: Blog post
```

**Output**:
```markdown
## Primary Keyword Analysis

**"how to start a podcast"**
- Search Volume: High (22,000+/month)
- Competition: Medium
- Difficulty Score: 65/100
- Trend: Stable

## Recommended Keywords to Target

| Keyword | Volume | Difficulty | Intent |
|---------|--------|------------|--------|
| podcast equipment for beginners | 2,400 | Low | Purchase |
| free podcast hosting | 1,900 | Medium | Research |
| how to record a podcast | 1,600 | Low | Tutorial |
| podcast name ideas | 1,300 | Low | Creative |
| podcast editing software | 990 | Medium | Purchase |

## Content Structure Suggestions

1. **Title Options**:
   - "How to Start a Podcast in 2024: Complete Beginner's Guide"
   - "Start Your Podcast Today: Step-by-Step Tutorial"

2. **Recommended H2s**:
   - Podcast Equipment You Actually Need (Under $100)
   - Choosing Your Podcast Hosting Platform
   - Recording Your First Episode
   - Editing and Launching Your Podcast

3. **Word Count**: 2,500-3,500 words recommended

## Quick Wins

- Target "podcast equipment for beginners" - lower competition
- Include comparison table for hosting platforms
- Add FAQ section with schema markup
```

### Example 2: Product Page Optimization
**Input**:
```
Product: Ergonomic office chair
Page type: Product page
Current ranking: Page 3
```

**Output**:
```markdown
## Keyword Gap Analysis

Currently targeting: "office chair"
Opportunity keywords missing from page:

1. "ergonomic office chair with lumbar support" - 1,300/mo
2. "best ergonomic chair for back pain" - 880/mo
3. "adjustable office chair" - 720/mo

## On-Page Optimization Checklist

- [ ] Update title to include "ergonomic" and "lumbar support"
- [ ] Add benefits section addressing back pain
- [ ] Include comparison with non-ergonomic chairs
- [ ] Add customer reviews mentioning comfort
- [ ] Optimize images with alt text containing keywords

## Competitor Content Gaps

Top-ranking pages are missing:
- Video demonstration of adjustability
- Comparison chart of height/weight suitability
- Detailed lumbar support explanation

## Recommended Actions

1. Expand product description by 200 words
2. Add "Benefits" section with keyword integration
3. Include customer testimonials with specific pain points addressed
```

## How to Use

1. Provide your main topic or target keyword
2. Specify content type (blog, product page, landing page)
3. Share current content if optimizing existing page
4. Receive comprehensive keyword analysis and recommendations

## Notes

- Long-tail keywords often convert better
- Consider user intent (informational, transactional, navigational)
- Update content regularly for sustained rankings
- Local keywords important for local businesses
