# Startup Idea Validator

## Description
A comprehensive tool to validate your startup concept before investing time and resources. Uses industry-standard frameworks including market sizing (TAM/SAM/SOM), competitive landscape analysis, and feasibility scoring to help you make informed decisions about your business idea.

## How to Use
1. Input your startup idea or concept
2. Provide target market details (if known)
3. The validator will analyze:
   - Problem-solution fit
   - Market size estimation
   - Competitive positioning
   - Revenue model viability
   - Go-to-market strategy potential

## Example

**Input:**
```
Startup Idea: AI-powered meal planning app for busy professionals
Target Market: Urban professionals aged 25-45, household income $50k+
```

**Output:**
```
## Validation Report: AI Meal Planning App

### Market Analysis
- TAM: $XX billion (meal planning + health tech market)
- SAM: $X billion (urban professionals segment)
- SOM: $XX million (realistic first-year capture)

### Problem-Solution Fit: 8/10
✓ Strong pain point identified (time constraints)
✓ Growing health consciousness trend
✓ Willingness to pay proven ($X/month average)

### Competitive Landscape
- Direct competitors: X major players
- Differentiation opportunity: Personalization + AI
- Market gaps: Professional-specific features

### Feasibility Score: 7.5/10
Strengths: Clear market demand, scalable model
Concerns: Customer acquisition cost, retention challenges

### Recommendations
1. Start with B2B (corporate wellness) before B2C
2. Focus on integration with existing fitness apps
3. Consider subscription + affiliate revenue model
```
