# Job Interview Answer Generator

## What This Skill Does

Interview success is preparation. I help you craft compelling, authentic answers to common interview questions so you can walk in confident and leave a lasting impression.

## Features

- **STAR Method**: Structured answers that impress
- **Customized Content**: Tailored to your experience
- **Negative-to-Positive**: Turn weaknesses into strengths
- **Industry-Specific**: Adapts to your target role
- **Follow-up Questions**: Prepares you for curveballs

## Common Questions Covered

### Tell Me About Yourself
Build a narrative arc: Present → Past → Future
```
"I'm currently [current role] at [company], where I 
[specific achievement]. Before that, I [relevant 
background]. I'm now looking to [next career goal] 
at [target company] because [specific interest]."
```

### Greatest Weakness
Choose a real weakness that's improving:
```
"One area I've been working on is [weakness]. 
I recognized this was holding me back, so I've 
[start specific action] to improve. It's been 
[effective outcome]."
```

### Why Should We Hire You?
Position yourself as the solution:
```
"You're looking for someone who can [job requirement]. 
I've demonstrated this through [specific example] at 
[company]. What sets me apart is my ability to 
[unique value]. I'm excited about this role because [enthusiasm]."
```

### Salary Expectations
Handle with confidence:
```
"I'm flexible based on the total compensation package. 
For a role at this level, I expect [market range]. 
But I'm open to discussing what's fair for both of us."
```

### Where Do You See Yourself in 5 Years?
Show ambition and stability:
```
"In 5 years, I see myself [professional growth], 
having contributed to [company goals]. I'm especially 
interested in [specific area] and would love to 
[growth opportunity]."
```

## Preparation Tips

- Research the company deeply
- Prepare 3-5 strong stories using STAR
- Practice out loud, not just in your head
- Prepare thoughtful questions for them
- Send a thank-you email within 24 hours
