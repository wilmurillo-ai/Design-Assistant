# Thank You Note Generator

## What This Skill Does

Gratitude strengthens relationships. I help you express sincere appreciation in just the right tone—for formal occasions, heartfelt personal moments, or quick professional acknowledgments.

## Features

- **Tone Matching**: Formal, casual, or warm
- **Occasion-Specific**: Interviews, gifts, help, events
- **Personal Touch**: Includes specific details
- **Length Options**: Quick notes to detailed letters
- **Follow-up Ready**: Natural next steps included

## Thank You Occasions

### After a Job Interview
```
Dear [Interviewer Name],

Thank you for taking the time to meet with me today 
about the [Position] role at [Company].

I especially enjoyed learning about [specific topic 
discussed]. Our conversation confirmed my enthusiasm 
for [aspect of the role/company].

I'm excited about the possibility of contributing to 
[company goal]. Please don't hesitate to reach out 
if you need any additional information.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

### For a Gift Received
```
Dear [Name],

You shouldn't have! But I'm so glad you did.

[Specific gift] is [how you'll use it/why you love it]. 
It's clear you [understood my interests/knew what I needed].

Your thoughtfulness means so much. Thank you for making 
[special occasion/event] even more special.

With gratitude,
[Your Name]
```

### For Help Given
```
Dear [Name],

I wanted to reach out and thank you for [specific help].

Your [advice/assistance/time] made a real difference. 
Because of your help, I was able to [positive outcome].

It's people like you who [meaningful statement about 
their character]. I'm lucky to have you in my corner.

Please let me know how I can return the favor sometime.

Warmly,
[Your Name]
```

### For a Business Deal/Partnership
```
Dear [Name],

I wanted to express my sincere appreciation for 
[the opportunity/partnership/deal].

Working with you has been [enjoyable/efficient/valuable]. 
Your [specific quality] made the process [smooth/successful].

I look forward to [future collaboration/opportunity] and 
continuing our successful partnership.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

## Quick Templates

### Text Message Length
```
Hey [Name]! Just wanted to say thank you for [reason]. 
You [made my day/really helped/super kind]. 
I owe you one! 🙏
```

### Social Media Comment
```
[Name], thank you so much! [Specific detail about 
what you appreciated]. Your [kindness/help/generosity] 
means the world to me. 💛
```

## Gratitude Tips

- Be specific about what you're thanking them for
- Mention how their action helped you
- Keep it genuine—don't overdo it
- Time matters: send within 24-48 hours
- Handwritten notes feel more personal
