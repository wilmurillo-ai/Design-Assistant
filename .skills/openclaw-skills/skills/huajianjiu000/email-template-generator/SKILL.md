# Email Template Generator

## What It Does
Generate professional, polished business emails for any situation. From cold outreach to customer service responses, get perfectly crafted emails that sound natural and achieve your goals.

## How to Use
1. Describe the type of email you need
2. Provide context (recipient info, purpose, key points)
3. Specify tone preference if needed
4. Get a ready-to-send professional email

## Email Types Covered

### Cold Outreach
- Initial contact emails
- Partnership proposals
- Sales introductions
- Guest post requests

### Follow-ups
- After no response
- After meeting/conference
- Progress updates
- Reminder emails

### Professional Communication
- Meeting requests
- Project updates
- Job applications
- Recommendation requests
- Thank you notes

### Customer Communication
- Support responses
- Complaint resolutions
- Onboarding emails
- Feedback requests

## Example

**Request**: "Cold email to a potential client about web design services"

**Generated Email**:
```
Subject: Quick Question About Your Website

Hi [Name],

I came across [Company] while researching [Industry] and noticed you're doing some exciting work in [Specific Detail].

I help businesses like yours increase conversions through strategic web design. We recently helped [Similar Company] achieve a 40% increase in leads within 3 months.

Would you be open to a brief 15-minute call this week to discuss how we might help [Company]?

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

## Tips
- Personalize with recipient's name and company
- Keep emails concise and scannable
- Always include a clear call-to-action
- Match tone to your brand voice
