# Meeting Agenda Creator

## Description

Meeting Agenda Creator is an intelligent assistant that generates structured, professional meeting agendas tailored to your specific meeting type, participants, and objectives. Transform disorganized meetings into productive sessions with clear time allocation, focused discussion points, and actionable outcomes.

## Features

- **Smart Time Allocation**: Automatically distribute time based on topic importance
- **Role Assignment**: Assign facilitator, note-taker, and timekeeper roles
- **Action Item Tracking**: Pre-populate action item templates
- **Meeting Type Templates**: Pre-built agendas for common meeting types
- **Customization Options**: Adapt agendas to your team's needs

## How to Use

1. **Define Meeting Parameters**
   - Meeting type (team standup, planning, review, brainstorming, etc.)
   - Total duration
   - Number of participants
   - Primary objectives

2. **Add Context**
   - Key topics to discuss
   - Pre-reading materials
   - Relevant documents or reports

3. **Generate Agenda**
   - Review AI-generated structure
   - Adjust time allocations as needed
   - Add or remove sections

4. **Distribute & Prepare**
   - Export agenda for distribution
   - Share pre-meeting materials
   - Set up meeting reminders

## Example

**Input**: "Create a sprint planning meeting agenda for a 90-minute session with 8 team members"

**Output**:
```

**Facilitator**: [Name]
```

## Meeting Type Templates

- **Daily Standup** (15 min)
- **Weekly Team Sync** (30 min)
- **Sprint Planning** (60-120 min)
- **Sprint Retrospective** (45-60 min)
- **Quarterly Business Review** (90 min)
- **Brainstorming Session** (60 min)
- **Project Kickoff** (90 min)
- **Client Meeting** (45 min)

## Tips

- Send agendas at least 24 hours before the meeting
- Include pre-reading materials for complex topics
- Assign roles in advance to ensure smooth facilitation
- Build in 5-minute buffers for longer meetings
