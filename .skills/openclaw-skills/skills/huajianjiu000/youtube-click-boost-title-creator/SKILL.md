# YouTube Title Optimizer

## What This Skill Does

A great video deserves a great title. I create SEO-optimized, attention-grabbing titles that make people click while maintaining authenticity. More clicks = more algorithmic love.

## Features

- **SEO Power**: Keywords that rank in search
- **Click Psychology**: Trigger curiosity and urgency
- **Platform Optimized**: Works with YouTube algorithm
- **Format Variety**: Multiple title styles
- **Character Safe**: Under 60 characters for full display

## How to Use

1. **Video Topic**: What is your video about?
2. **Target Keywords**: Words people search for
3. **Video Style**: Educational, entertainment, tutorial, vlog?
4. **Unique Angle**: What makes your video different?
5. **Audience**: Who should watch this?

## Title Formulas That Work

### The How-To Formula
```
How to [Achieve Result] in [Timeframe]
Example: How to Learn Spanish in 30 Days (Not What You Think)
```

### The List Formula
```
[Number] [Things/Facts] About [Topic] You [Verb]
Example: 7 Things About Dogs That Will Blow Your Mind
```

### The Problem-Solution
```
[Problem] SOLVED: [Solution Revealed]
Example: Bad Breath GONE: The Simple Method Dentists Don't Tell You
```

### The Curiosity Gap
```
The Truth About [Topic] (Most People Get This Wrong)
Example: The Truth About Intermittent Fasting (Science vs. Hype)
```

### The Ultimate Guide
```
The Complete Guide to [Topic] in [Year]
Example: The Complete Guide to YouTube SEO in 2024
```

## Pro Tips

- Put keywords at the beginning when possible
- Use brackets for platform compatibility [Video]
- Avoid ALL CAPS (looks spammy)
- Numbers perform well—use them
- Test variations with your audience
