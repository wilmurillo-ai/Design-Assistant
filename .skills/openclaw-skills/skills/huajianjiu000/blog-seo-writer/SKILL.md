# Blog SEO Writer

## What It Does
Create search-engine-optimized blog posts that rank higher and drive organic traffic. From keyword research to final polish, get content that's both reader-friendly and Google-approved.

## How to Use
1. Provide your target keyword/topic
2. Share any specific points to cover
3. Mention your target audience
4. Specify word count preference
5. Get a fully optimized blog post

## What You Get

### Content Structure
- SEO-friendly headline with keyword
- Compelling introduction
- Well-organized body with H2/H3 headings
- Natural keyword integration
- Smooth transitions between sections
- Strong conclusion with CTA

### SEO Elements
- Meta description (150-160 characters)
- Suggested internal/external links
- Alt text suggestions for images
- Related questions section
- Keyword density optimization

## Example

**Input**: "best coffee shops in new york"

**Generated Content Structure**:
```
Title: 15 Best Coffee Shops in New York City (2024 Guide)
Meta: Discover NYC's top coffee spots from Brooklyn to Manhattan. 
      Expert-reviewed with photos, addresses, and what makes each unique.

H2: What Makes NYC Coffee Culture Special
H2: Top 15 Coffee Shops in New York
  - Williamsburg Favorites
  - Manhattan Classics
  - Hidden Gems
H2: How to Find Quality Coffee Near You
H2: FAQ About NYC Coffee Shops
H2: Conclusion
```

## SEO Best Practices
- Target one primary keyword per post
- Include keywords in first 100 words
- Use long-tail variations naturally
- Aim for 1500+ words for competitive topics
- Add 1-2 internal links per 500 words
