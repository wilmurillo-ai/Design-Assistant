# Top Credible Research Channels

## Tier 1: Institutional-Grade (Wall Street Standard)

### Bloomberg Terminal
- **What it is**: The undisputed gold standard used by virtually every major financial institution
- **Strengths**: Real-time everything — market data, news, analyst estimates, company data, derivatives pricing, FX, fixed income. Unmatched breadth.
- **Pricing**: ~$25,000–$30,000/year per seat (subscription-only, not sold to individuals)
- **Best for**: Investment banks, hedge funds, asset managers, equity research analysts
- **Weakness**: Extremely steep learning curve; expensive

### S&P Capital IQ
- **What it is**: Platform built around the Capital IQ database — deep financial statement analytics, private company data, M&A tracking
- **Strengths**: Best-in-class for quantitative financial analysis, screening tools, transaction database. Excel add-in is industry standard.
- **Pricing**: ~$15,000–$20,000/year (institutional pricing)
- **Best for**: Value investors, equity research, M&A analysts, private equity
- **Market share**: ~6% of institutional market

### FactSet
- **What it is**: Integrated financial information and analytics platform, particularly strong in portfolio analytics and multi-asset coverage
- **Strengths**: Clean UX, strong in portfolio management, good for institutional asset managers and research houses
- **Pricing**: ~$12,000–$18,000/year
- **Best for**: Portfolio managers, research analysts, asset management firms
- **Market share**: ~4.5% of institutional market

### Refinitiv Eikon (LSEG)
- **What it is**: LSEG's answer to Bloomberg — strong real-time market data, FX, commodities, and public company analytics
- **Strengths**: Superior in real-time market data, FX, commodities; strong for traders and market watchers
- **Pricing**: ~$20,000+/year
- **Best for**: Traders, sell-side analysts, market data consumers
- **vs Bloomberg**: Eikon is stronger in real-time market data and commodities; Bloomberg is stronger in news and document search

## Tier 2: Specialized / High-Quality Alternatives

### AlphaSense
- **What it is**: AI-powered search platform for equity research, news, and industry reports
- **Strengths**: Natural language search across broker research, news, trade publications, expert calls, and internal firm content. Far superior document search vs Bloomberg.
- **Pricing**: ~$10,000–$25,000/year (institutional)
- **Best for**: Research analysts who need to search across large document corpus quickly

### MSCI
- **What it is**: Industry leader in ESG data, factor indices, and risk analytics
- **Strengths**: ESG data, factor investing, risk models, index products
- **Pricing**: High — institutional only
- **Best for**: Institutional investors with ESG mandates, risk managers

### Sentieo
- **What it is**: AI-powered financial database combining NLP search with traditional financial data
- **Strengths**: NLP document search + financial data + alternative data. Good for equity research.
- **Pricing**: Mid-range institutional

## Tier 3: Retail-Accessible (High Quality, Lower Cost)

### Koyfin
- **What it is**: freemium financial data and visualization platform
- **Strengths**: Clean UI, free tier available, good for screening and visualization
- **Pricing**: Free tier + $50/month pro tier
- **Best for**: Retail investors, small funds

### YCharts
- **What it is**: Financial data and portfolio visualization platform
- **Strengths**: Strong visualization, portfolio analytics, screening tools
- **Pricing**: ~$99–$200/month

### TradingView
- **What it is**: Charting and community-driven research platform
- **Strengths**: Best-in-class charting, large free tier, strong community
- **Pricing**: Free + $30–60/month for Pro
- **Best for**: Retail traders, technical analysts

## Selection Guidance

| Scenario | Recommended Platform |
|---------|-------------------|
| Institutional equity research | Bloomberg or Capital IQ |
| Value investing / financial statement analysis | Capital IQ |
| Real-time trading / market data | Bloomberg or Refinitiv Eikon |
| ESG / responsible investing | MSCI |
| Document-heavy research (search across broker reports) | AlphaSense |
| Budget-conscious professional | Koyfin + Capital IQ web |
| Retail investor | TradingView + Koyfin free |
