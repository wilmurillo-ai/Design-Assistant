# VaultAI DD Scanner Reference

## Core operational red flags
- customer concentration masked by group entities
- supplier concentration hidden behind distributor layers
- founder or promoter dependency
- undocumented SOPs
- plant-level bottlenecks
- key-person control over procurement or collections
- margin quality supported by temporary input pricing
- weak inventory discipline
- receivables quality issues
- integration risk in cross-border operating models

## Supply-chain red flags
- single geography dependence
- single component dependence
- long lead-time inputs with weak substitutes
- vendor lock-in without pricing protection
- undocumented secondary sourcing
- quality drift risk across sites or vendors

## Working-capital red flags
- receivable aging deterioration
- inventory build without matching sell-through
- supplier-credit dependency
- quarter-end collection distortion
- inconsistent gross-margin behavior

## Management questions
- what fails if the top supplier is delayed 30 days?
- which customer relationships are promoter-dependent?
- where is tribal knowledge concentrated?
- what process is least documented?
- what operational KPI is management most defensive about?
