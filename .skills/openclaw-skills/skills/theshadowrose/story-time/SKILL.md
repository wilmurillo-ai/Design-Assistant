# StoryTime Interactive Fiction Engine

Choose-your-own-adventure interactive fiction through your agent. Pre-built stories and a framework for building your own.

---

Choose-your-own-adventure through your agent. Play or build your own.

## Gameplay

```
Agent: "You stand at the entrance of an ancient library. Dust motes 
        float in shafts of light. A spiral staircase descends into 
        darkness. Shelves stretch into shadow. Faint humming from above.

        1. Descend the staircase
        2. Explore the shelves
        3. Follow the humming"

You: "2"

Agent: "You move between shelves. Books here are old — leather cracked, 
        pages yellowed. One book on a stand is open, pages glowing blue..."
```

## Features

- **Dynamic storytelling** — AI-generated, not pre-scripted
- **Choice tracking** — decisions shape the story
- **Character persistence** — inventory, health, relationships
- **Save/load** — pick up where you left off
- **Multiple genres** — fantasy, sci-fi, horror, mystery
- **Story builder** — create scenarios with simple templates
- **Multiplayer** — play cooperatively in group chats
