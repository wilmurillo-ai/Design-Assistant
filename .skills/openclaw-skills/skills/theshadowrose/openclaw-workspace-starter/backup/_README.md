# Backup

Emergency backup location. Your agent can copy critical files here periodically.

## What to Back Up
- MEMORY.md (curated long-term memory)
- SOUL.md (personality — took time to get right)
- USER.md (your context)
- AGENTS.md (operating rules)
- Any project files that matter

## Backup Strategy
- Have your agent refresh this folder during heartbeats (weekly is fine)
- Keep a copy off-machine too (USB, cloud, second computer)
- Timestamp your backups: `LAST_UPDATED.txt` with the date

## Why Bother
Your workspace IS your agent's brain. Losing it means starting from scratch. Five minutes of backup saves hours of reconstruction.
