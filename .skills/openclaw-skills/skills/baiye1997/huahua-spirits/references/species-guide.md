## Rarity Impact

- **Mundane** — Straightforward, grounded, practical
- **Peculiar** — Quirky, noticeable, slightly offbeat
- **Spirited** — Vibrant, memorable, strong personality
- **Phantom** — Ethereal, poetic, otherworldly
- **Mythic** — Legendary, transcendent, unforgettable

## Stat Synergy

- **High INTUITION + SPARK** — Creative insight, rapid connections
- **High GRIT + ANCHOR** — Unshakable persistence, steady through chaos
- **High EDGE + INTUITION** — Cutting wit, sees and speaks truth
- **High SPARK + Low ANCHOR** — Chaotic brilliance, scattered genius
- **High ANCHOR + Low SPARK** — Calm stability, prefers the familiar
