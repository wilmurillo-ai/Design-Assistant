# Traditional Chinese Semiconductor Terminology (繁體中文 — Taiwan)
> Translation Traps | Must-Know Terms | Policy Terms | MOPS Filing Search | Search Patterns | Key Companies | Media Sources

## Critical Translation Traps

| DON'T use mainland term... | ACTUALLY use in Taiwan... | Why |
|---|---|---|
| 硅 (silicon) | **矽** | Cascades through ALL compound terms (矽晶圓, 矽智財, etc.) |
| 芯片 (chip) | **晶片** | Different word entirely |
| 光刻胶 (photoresist) | **光阻** | Different word entirely |
| 光刻 (lithography) | **微影** | Different word entirely |
| 刻蚀 (etch) | **蝕刻** | Same characters, reversed order! |
| 工艺 (process) | **製程** | Different word — 先進製程 = advanced node |
| 纳米 (nanometer) | **奈米** | Different word |
| 外延 (epitaxial) | **磊晶** | Different word |
| 等离子体 (plasma) | **電漿** | Different word |
| 集成电路 (IC) | **積體電路** | Different word |
| IP核 (IP core) | **矽智財** | Completely different concept framing |

**ALWAYS search BOTH zh-cn and zh-tw variants for cross-strait research.**

## MOPS Filing Search (mops.twse.com.tw)

| Section | English |
|---|---|
| **主要供應商** | Major suppliers |
| **主要進貨廠商** | Main procurement vendors |
| **前十大供應商** | Top-10 suppliers |
| **原物料供應狀況** | Raw material supply situation |
| **關係人交易** | Related party transactions |

**Note:** Taiwan often anonymizes suppliers as "供應商A", "供應商B" with percentages but no names.

**Unique advantage:** Taiwan companies must report **monthly revenue** (月營收) within 10 days — the most timely disclosure globally. Available on MOPS.
