## How to Use for Supply Chain Intelligence

1. **Patent co-filing:** Search for patents co-assigned to a university + company
2. **Paper co-authorship:** Google Scholar search for papers with authors from both institutions
3. **Lens.org:** Links patent citations to scholarly articles — maps knowledge flow
4. **PhD thesis sponsors:** Corporate-sponsored theses reveal R&D priorities
5. **Conference presentations:** IEDM/VLSI/SPIE papers listing company + university affiliations
