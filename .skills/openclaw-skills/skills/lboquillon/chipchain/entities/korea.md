## Key Intelligence Notes
- **SEMES** (세메스) is Samsung's captive equipment arm — NOT publicly traded, ~KRW 2T+ revenue. Invisible outside Korea.
- **SK Group** is building a parallel captive supply chain: SK Specialty (gases), SK Siltron (wafers), SK Enpulse/SKC Solmics (CMP pads), SK Trichem (precursors).
- **KOSDAQ** is where most supply chain companies trade. KOSPI for larger diversified ones.
- **HBM demand** is reshaping the supply chain — benefiting HPSP, Hanmi Semi, Leeno, Haesung DS.
