## Key Intelligence Notes
- **Topco Scientific** (崇越科技) controls the distribution channel for foreign semiconductor materials into Taiwan fabs. Not a manufacturer but a critical node.
- **Hermes Epitek** (漢民集團) is the invisible equipment services backbone — maintains foreign equipment in Taiwan fabs. Founded by Nicky Lu (盧志遠).
- **Taiwan's monthly revenue disclosure** is unique globally — all listed companies report within 10 days. Available on MOPS (mops.twse.com.tw).
