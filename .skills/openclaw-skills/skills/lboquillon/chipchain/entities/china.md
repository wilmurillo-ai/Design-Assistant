## Entity List Exposure Risk (assess before reporting)
Companies at highest risk of future US Entity List designation:
1. SMEE — lithography is #1 target
2. AMEC — advanced etch at leading nodes
3. Naura — breadth of equipment portfolio
4. Piotech — ALD for advanced nodes
5. NSIG/Zing — enabling advanced fab capacity

## Key Intelligence Notes
- **STAR Market (688xxx) IPO prospectuses** disclose TOP-5 SUPPLIERS BY NAME with amounts. This is the richest public source for Chinese semiconductor supply chain mapping.
- **EastMoney (东方财富)** aggregates all Chinese broker reports — free PDF downloads.
- **Big Fund (大基金)** investment portfolio is partially opaque. Phase I, II, III total >$100B.
- Chinese companies frequently claim technology capabilities that exceed verified production quality — triangulate claims against actual fab qualification status.
