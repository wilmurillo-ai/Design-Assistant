# Scheduling and Frequency Recommendations

## How often to analyze

Social media content moves fast. A post that's trending today may be buried tomorrow. For best results:

* **Active campaigns:** Analyze every 1–2 days to catch fresh opportunities
* **Maintenance mode:** Analyze 2–3 times per week to stay visible
* **Launch period:** Analyze daily during your first 2 weeks

## How many posts to engage with per session

Engaging with too many posts at once can feel unnatural. Recommended per session:

| Approach | Posts per session | Best for |
|----------|-------------------|----------|
| Conservative | 3–5 | New accounts, building reputation |
| Balanced | 5–8 | Day-to-day promotion |
| Intensive | 8–10 | Established accounts with history |

## Timing your engagement

The `best_timing` field in each promotion spot tells you when to engage for maximum visibility. General principles:

* **Reddit:** Most active 8 AM – 12 PM EST on weekdays
* **YouTube:** Comments on fresh videos (< 24 hours old) get the most visibility
* **Twitter/X:** Peak engagement 9 AM – 3 PM in your target audience's timezone
* **Hacker News:** Mornings EST for US tech audience
* **Quora:** Evenings and weekends see higher read-through rates

## Pacing

Natural engagement rhythm:

1. Browse to the post and read the content (30–60 seconds)
2. Read a few existing comments for context (20–40 seconds)
3. Compose and post your reply
4. Move on to the next task after a natural pause

## Credit consumption estimates

Each analysis call (regardless of JSON or CSV format) consumes **1 credit**.

| Strategy | Frequency | Monthly credits | Monthly cost (approx) |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|----------------------|
| Conservative | Every 2 days | ~15 | S$17 (Starter × 2) |
| Balanced | Daily | ~30 | S$22.99 (Pro) |
| Intensive | Daily + re-runs | ~45–60 | S$99.99 (Ultra) |

> Exporting past analyses via `/history/{id}/csv` does **not** consume credits, so you can re-download previous results at any time.

# 频率和节奏建议

## 多久分析一次

社媒内容更新很快。今天的热帖明天可能就沉了。为获得最佳效果：

* **活跃推广期：** 每 1-2 天分析一次，抓住新机会
* **维护模式：** 每周分析 2-3 次，保持可见度
* **产品发布期：** 前 2 周每天分析

## 每次互动多少帖子

一次性互动太多帖子会显得不自然。每次推荐数量：

| 策略 | 每次帖数 | 适用场景 |
|------|---------|---------|
| 保守 | 3–5 | 新账号、积累声誉期 |
| 均衡 | 5–8 | 日常推广 |
| 密集 | 8–10 | 老号、有历史记录的账号 |

## 互动时机

每个推广位的 `best_timing` 字段告诉你最佳互动时间。通用原则：

* **Reddit：** 工作日 EST 上午 8 点 – 中午 12 点最活跃
* **YouTube：** 新视频（发布 24 小时内）的评论曝光最高
* **Twitter/X：** 目标用户时区的上午 9 点 – 下午 3 点为高峰
* **Hacker News：** EST 早晨，面向美国科技受众
* **Quora：** 晚间和周末阅读率更高

## 互动节奏

自然的互动节奏：

1. 浏览帖子，阅读内容（30–60 秒）
2. 看几条已有评论了解上下文（20–40 秒）
3. 撰写并发布回复
4. 自然停顿后进入下一个任务

## 积分消耗预估

每次分析调用（无论 JSON 还是 CSV 格式）消耗 **1 积分**。

| 策略 | 频率 | 月消耗积分 | 月费用（约） |
|------|------|-----------|------------|
| 保守 | 每 2 天 | ~15 | S$17（Starter × 2） |
| 均衡 | 每天 | ~30 | S$22.99（Pro） |
| 密集 | 每天 + 重新分析 | ~45–60 | S$99.99（Ultra） |

> 通过 `/history/{id}/csv` 导出过往分析**不消耗**积分，你可以随时重新下载之前的结果。
