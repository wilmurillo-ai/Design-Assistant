# Platform-Specific Engagement Tips

Each platform has different norms for how comments and replies are received. Following these guidelines helps ensure your engagement feels authentic and is well-received by the community.

## Reddit

* **Read before replying.** Scan existing comments to avoid duplicating what others already said.
* **Add value.** Reddit users downvote anything that looks promotional. Frame your reply as genuinely helpful — share an experience, answer a question, or add context.
* **Match the subreddit's tone.** r/technology is different from r/SideProject. Adapt formality, humor, and length accordingly.
* **Don't include links immediately.** In many subreddits, linking in your first comment gets flagged. Contribute to the discussion first; share links only when asked or when it naturally fits.
* **Timing matters.** Posts gain most traction in the first 4–6 hours. The `best_timing` field reflects this window.

## YouTube

* **Comment early on new videos.** Early comments on popular videos get pinned or upvoted to the top.
* **Reference specific content in the video.** "Great explanation at 3:42 about X" feels more genuine than generic praise.
* **Ask a follow-up question.** Questions drive engagement threads and make the creator more likely to reply.

## Twitter / X

* **Be concise.** Twitter rewards brevity and wit.
* **Quote-tweet for visibility.** If the post is relevant, a quote-tweet with your perspective reaches your followers too.
* **Use relevant hashtags sparingly.** 1–2 targeted hashtags, not a wall of them.
* **Engage in threads.** Replying to active threads gives more visibility than standalone tweets.

## Quora

* **Answer the question comprehensively.** Quora rewards detailed, well-structured answers.
* **Include your perspective.** "In my experience with [tool]..." is more credible than a generic recommendation.
* **Formatting matters.** Use bullet points, bold text, and short paragraphs. Quora readers skim.

## Product Hunt

* **Launch day is key.** Most engagement happens in the first 12 hours.
* **Ask genuine questions about the product.** Makers actively monitor and reply on launch day.
* **Share related tools.** "I've also been using X for similar purposes" positions you as knowledgeable, not spammy.

## Hacker News

* **Substance over promotion.** HN has the strictest anti-promotion culture. Focus on technical insight.
* **Engage with the ideas, not the product.** Discuss the underlying technology, market, or problem space.
* **Avoid superlatives.** "Revolutionary" and "game-changing" trigger skepticism. Use measured, specific language.
* **Show, don't tell.** Share specific data points, benchmarks, or technical details.

## General principles

* **One account, one voice.** Be consistent in your engagement style.
* **Space out your activity.** Engaging on 3–5 posts per session, with natural pauses between them, matches organic browsing behavior.
* **Respect the community.** If a platform's rules prohibit self-promotion, don't force it. Focus on adding value — people will organically check your profile.
* **Review before posting.** The `suggested_comment` from AI-CMO is a strong starting point, but always adapt it to the specific conversation context.

# 各平台互动技巧

每个平台对评论和回复有不同的规范。遵循以下指南有助于确保你的互动显得真实自然，并被社区接受。

## Reddit

* **先看再回。** 浏览已有评论，避免重复别人说过的话。
* **提供价值。** Reddit 用户会点踩任何看起来像推广的内容。把回复框架为真正有帮助的——分享经验、回答问题或补充背景。
* **匹配子版块的语气。** r/technology 和 r/SideProject 完全不同。根据情况调整正式程度、幽默感和篇幅。
* **不要立刻放链接。** 在很多子版块，首条评论放链接会被标记。先参与讨论，只在被问到或自然合适时才分享链接。
* **时机很重要。** 帖子在前 4-6 小时获得最多关注。`best_timing` 字段反映了这个窗口。

## YouTube

* **在新视频上尽早评论。** 热门视频的早期评论容易被置顶或获得高赞。
* **引用视频中的具体内容。** "3:42 处关于 X 的解释很棒" 比泛泛的夸赞更真实。
* **提出后续问题。** 问题能带动互动讨论串，也更容易让创作者回复。

## Twitter / X

* **简洁有力。** Twitter 奖励简短和机智。
* **引用转发增加曝光。** 如果帖子相关，带上你观点的引用转发能触达你的粉丝。
* **谨慎使用话题标签。** 1-2 个针对性标签即可，不要堆一堆。
* **参与讨论串。** 回复活跃的讨论串比独立发推更有曝光。

## Quora

* **全面回答问题。** Quora 奖励详细、结构清晰的回答。
* **加入你的观点。** "以我使用 [工具] 的经验来看..." 比泛泛推荐更可信。
* **排版很重要。** 使用要点、加粗和短段落。Quora 读者习惯略读。

## Product Hunt

* **发布日是关键。** 大部分互动发生在前 12 小时。
* **提出真诚的问题。** 创始人在发布日积极监控和回复。
* **分享相关工具。** "我也在用 X 做类似的事" 让你显得专业而非推销。

## Hacker News

* **内容大于推广。** HN 有最严格的反推广文化。专注于技术洞察。
* **讨论想法，而非产品。** 聊底层技术、市场或问题空间。
* **避免夸张修辞。** "颠覆性" "改变游戏规则" 会引发怀疑。用精确、具体的语言。
* **用事实说话。** 分享具体数据、基准测试或技术细节。

## 通用原则

* **一个账号，一种风格。** 保持互动风格的一致性。
* **分散活动节奏。** 每次 3-5 个帖子，帖与帖之间自然停顿，符合正常浏览行为。
* **尊重社区。** 如果平台规则禁止自我推广，不要硬推。专注于提供价值——人们会自然地查看你的个人主页。
* **发帖前审阅。** AI-CMO 的 `suggested_comment` 是很好的起点，但始终要根据具体对话上下文调整。
