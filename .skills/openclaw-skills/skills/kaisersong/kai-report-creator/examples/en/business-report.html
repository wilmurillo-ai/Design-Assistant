Conclusion & Recommendations

Q3 delivered strong results overall, with total revenue growing 12% year-over-year and overall target achievement reaching 108%. The South region's sustained outperformance is the standout result of the quarter and represents a clear opportunity for investment in Q4.

For Q4, we recommend prioritizing headcount expansion in the South region, implementing targeted competitive response strategies in the East, and accelerating onboarding of the 183 new clients acquired this quarter to maximize retention and cross-sell potential.
