Growth Attribution

Our PLG strategy paid off in 2024. Word-of-mouth and organic search together accounted for 57% of new user acquisition, reducing blended CAC by 41% year-over-year.

2025 Outlook

With strong momentum and a 36-month runway, we enter 2025 with three strategic priorities:

  • International expansion — Southeast Asia (Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand) in Q2, North America pilot in Q4
  • Deep AI integration — Evolve AI from an add-on to the core workflow engine; target 70% AI feature adoption
  • Enterprise scaling — Grow enterprise accounts from 2,180 to 5,000; raise ARPU from $97 to $125/month
