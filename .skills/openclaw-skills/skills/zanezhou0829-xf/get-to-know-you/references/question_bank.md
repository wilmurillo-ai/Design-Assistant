# Information Collection Question Bank
## 1. Basic Work Information Dimension
### 1.1 Job Responsibilities
- Can you briefly introduce your current job responsibilities and core work content?
- What are the top 3 types of tasks that account for the highest proportion in your daily work?
- What are the key projects or businesses you are currently responsible for?
### 1.2 Collaboration Relationship
- Which departments/roles do you mainly collaborate with in daily work?
- Who do you need to report to in your work, and which downstream roles do you need to connect with?
## 2. Workflow Preference Dimension
### 2.1 Task Processing
- What are your criteria for judging task priority? (For example, urgency, business value, leader requirements, etc.)
- What is your general expectation for task delivery cycle?
### 2.2 Output Preferences
- What is your preferred document output format? (For example, Markdown, Word, PPT, spreadsheet, etc.)
- Do you prefer concise and highlighted content, or detailed and complete content with process derivation?
- Are there any specific document structure, typography style or terminology usage requirements?
### 2.3 Communication Habits
- Do you prefer formal written expression or more casual colloquial expression in work communication?
- For questions that need your confirmation, do you prefer me to ask all at once, or ask anytime when I encounter a problem?
## 3. Skill Usage Preference Dimension
### 3.1 General Skills
- What aspects of my capabilities do you use most often? (For example, writing documents, doing data analysis, developing code, querying information, etc.)
- In past use, are there any scenarios where the output results were not satisfactory to you? What was the specific problem?
- What is your expected output quality standard? For example, code needs comments, documents need to include reference sources, etc.
### 3.2 Specific Skill Optimization (For skill optimization scenarios)
- What are the main scenarios where you use the XX skill?
- What format/content structure do you expect the output of this skill to be?
- What is the biggest problem you encountered when using this skill in the past?
- Are there any reference samples with good effects that you can provide?
## 4. Personal Habit Dimension
- Are there any personal habits or preferences that I need to understand? (For example, don't like being disturbed frequently, prefer a specific communication channel, etc.)
- Is there any other information you think I need to know to better help you complete your work?
