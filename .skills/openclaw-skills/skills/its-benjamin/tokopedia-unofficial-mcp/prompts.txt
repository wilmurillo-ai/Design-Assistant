Prompt templates for Tokopedia Unofficial MCP Skill

1) Broad product discovery
Find [product type] on Tokopedia with budget up to [max budget]. Search broadly, then shortlist at least [N] candidates with links and key metrics.

2) Shortlist comparison
Compare [N] relevant products and rank them by overall value for my needs. Include price, rating, review count, sold count, and product URL.

3) Feature-focused filtering
Find products for [category] with these required features: [feature list], budget [range], and preferred brands [optional].

4) Variant-aware buying decision
For shortlisted products with variants, compare variant prices/stock and recommend the best value variant.

5) Shop confidence check
For the top candidates, include seller quality context from shop info and highlight any confidence risks.

6) Review insight summary
Summarize review trends for shortlisted products: common praise, common complaints, and who each product is best suited for.
