## Meta Ecosystem

**Accounts and assets:**
- Facebook personal account
- Facebook Page
- Business Manager (BM) / Meta Business Suite
- Facebook ad account
- Instagram account
- Instagram Verified
- Threads account
- WhatsApp Business

**Common needs:**
- BM structure design and asset ownership setup
- Ad account creation within BM
- Page and ad account permission assignment
- Instagram linking to ad account
- Advertising readiness review before launch

**Common issues:**
- BM disabled or restricted
- Ad account flagged or banned
- Page not eligible for advertising
- Payment method rejected

## Advertising Infrastructure

Resources commonly required for multi-platform ad environments:

**IP Proxy**
- HTTP or Socks5 proxies
- Used for: managing multiple ad accounts, agency environments,
  geo-specific verification, multi-platform campaign management

**Virtual Cards (VCC)**
- Virtual debit or credit cards for ad billing
- Used for: accounts that don't accept local cards,
  multi-account billing separation, cross-border payment setup
- Common providers vary by region — recommend verifying
  with the user's specific platform and market
