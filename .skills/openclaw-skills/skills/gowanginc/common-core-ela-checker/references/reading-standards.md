# Reading Standards

## Literature (RL)
### Key Ideas and Details
- RL.K-12.1: Read closely, cite textual evidence
- RL.K-12.2: Determine themes/central ideas
- RL.K-12.3: Analyze character, setting, plot development

### Craft and Structure
- RL.K-12.4: Interpret words and phrases
- RL.K-12.5: Analyze text structure
- RL.K-12.6: Assess point of view

### Integration of Knowledge
- RL.K-12.7: Integrate content in diverse formats
- RL.K-12.9: Analyze how texts address similar themes

### Range of Reading
- RL.K-12.10: Read complex literary texts independently

## Informational Text (RI)
### Key Ideas and Details
- RI.K-12.1: Cite textual evidence
- RI.K-12.2: Determine central ideas
- RI.K-12.3: Analyze connections and distinctions

### Craft and Structure
- RI.K-12.4: Determine word meanings
- RI.K-12.5: Analyze text structure
- RI.K-12.6: Determine author's purpose

### Integration of Knowledge
- RI.K-12.7: Integrate and evaluate content
- RI.K-12.8: Evaluate arguments and claims
- RI.K-12.9: Analyze seminal documents

### Range of Reading
- RI.K-12.10: Read complex informational texts
