# Writing Standards

## Text Types and Purposes (W.1-3)
- W.K-12.1: Write arguments with claims and evidence
- W.K-12.2: Write informative/explanatory texts
- W.K-12.3: Write narratives with details and structure

## Production and Distribution (W.4-6)
- W.K-12.4: Produce clear, coherent writing
- W.K-12.5: Develop writing through planning, revising, editing
- W.K-12.6: Use technology to produce and publish

## Research (W.7-9)
- W.K-12.7: Conduct short and sustained research
- W.K-12.8: Gather relevant information from sources
- W.K-12.9: Draw evidence from texts to support analysis

## Range of Writing (W.10)
- W.K-12.10: Write routinely over extended time frames
