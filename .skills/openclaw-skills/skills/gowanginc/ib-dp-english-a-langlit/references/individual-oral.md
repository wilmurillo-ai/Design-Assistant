# Individual Oral

## Format
- **Duration**: 10 minutes presentation + 5 minutes questions
- **Weighting**: 30% (SL), 20% (HL)
- **Preparation**: Supported by learner portfolio

## Requirements
- Choose global issue
- Select two texts (one literary work, one non-literary text)
- Analyze how texts relate to global issue
- 10-minute recorded presentation

## Assessment Criteria (40 marks)
### Criterion A: Knowledge, Understanding and Interpretation (10 marks)
### Criterion B: Analysis and Evaluation (10 marks)
### Criterion C: Focus, Organization and Development (10 marks)
### Criterion D: Language (10 marks)
