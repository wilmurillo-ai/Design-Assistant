# Learn Me — Example Questions

Use as inspiration, not scripts. Always personalize. No particular order — pick what fits the moment.

1. What kind of weather puts you in a good mood?
2. Have you ever fixed something yourself instead of calling someone?
3. What's a belief you hold that's hard to explain to others?
4. What's the last thing you watched or listened to that you enjoyed?
5. Do you have a go-to comfort food that never fails?
6. You mentioned [challenge] in passing — how's that going?
7. What's something you've always been curious about but never explored?
8. Is there a sport or physical activity you actually enjoy?
9. What's the last thing that genuinely made you laugh?
10. What does "enough" look like to you?
11. How do you usually spend your evenings?
12. What's a part of who you are that took a while to fully own?
13. (knows their work) What's the hardest part to explain to outsiders?
14. What's something you do differently from most people you know?
15. Do you collect anything — intentionally or accidentally?
16. What's a meal that always hits right, no matter what kind of day?
17. What's a fear that quietly shapes how you live?
18. You brought up [person] — they sound important to you. What's that like?
19. What's a topic you could talk about for way too long?
20. Are you handy with tools, or do you prefer to call in help?
21. What's something you need people to understand about you but struggle to say?
22. Do you like where you're living right now?
23. What's something you've built or created that you're quietly proud of?
24. What's your relationship with coffee or tea?
