# Eye Tracking Template

Template for planning eye-tracking studies to understand visual attention and scanning patterns.

## How to Use

- Use when you need evidence of **where users look** and in what order, to complement usability and qualitative findings.
- Plan carefully for hardware, calibration, and interpretation (heatmaps, gaze plots, etc.).
