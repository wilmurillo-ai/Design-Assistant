# Expert Review Template

Template for expert reviews of products or flows conducted by experienced UX practitioners, product experts, or domain specialists.

## How to Use

- Use when time or resources are limited and you need informed feedback quickly.
- Combine with heuristic evaluation or design guidelines for more structured criteria.

## Key References for Expert Reviews

- Jakob Nielsen’s writings on expert reviews and heuristic inspections, especially within NN/g articles (`https://www.nngroup.com/articles/` – search for “expert review”).
