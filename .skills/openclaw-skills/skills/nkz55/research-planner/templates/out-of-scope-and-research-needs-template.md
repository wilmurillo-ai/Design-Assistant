# Out of Scope + Research Needs Template

Template for explicitly documenting what is **out of scope** for a research effort and what **future research needs** have been identified.

## How to Use

- Use alongside any research plan or debrief to make **boundaries and gaps explicit**.
- Helps set expectations and build a research roadmap.

## Out of Scope and Future Research Needs Template

```markdown
# Out of Scope and Future Research Needs – [Project]

Version: v[...]
Date: [...]
Owner: [...]

## 1. Out of Scope for This Study

- Topics or user groups not covered:
  - [...]
- Methods deliberately not used:
  - [...]
- Timeframes or markets excluded:
  - [...]

## 2. Rationale

- Why these areas are out of scope now:
  - [...]
- Dependencies or constraints:
  - [...]

## 3. Known Gaps

- Questions we will not answer in this study:
  - [...]
- Risks associated with these gaps:
  - [...]

## 4. Future Research Needs

- High-priority research questions for future phases:
  - [...]
- Recommended methods and rough timing:
  - [...]
```
