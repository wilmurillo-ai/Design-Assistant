# Diary Study Template

Template for planning diary studies / longitudinal logs that capture user behaviors and experiences over time in their natural context.

## How to Use

- Use when you need to understand **patterns over time**, not just single-session snapshots.
- Combine with kickoff and closing interviews for richer context.
