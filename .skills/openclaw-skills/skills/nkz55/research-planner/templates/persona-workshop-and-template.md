# Persona Workshop and Template

Template for running a persona creation or refinement workshop, plus a standard persona sheet format.

## How to Use

- Only create personas when backed by real research (interviews, surveys, analytics).
- Use the workshop section to align stakeholders; use the persona sheet as the final artifact.

## Persona Workshop Template

```markdown
# Persona Workshop – [Project / Domain]

Date: [...]
Facilitator: [...]
Participants: [...]

## 1. Workshop Objectives

- Clarify target users and segments.
- Align stakeholders on key persona attributes and needs.
- Decide how personas will be used in product decisions.

## 2. Inputs

- Existing research (interviews, surveys, analytics):
  - Links or short summaries.
- Current persona drafts (if any):
  - [...]

## 3. Workshop Agenda

- Introduction and goals (10–15 min)
- Review of evidence (20–30 min)
  - Key patterns from research.
- Group exercises (40–60 min)
  - Clustering behaviors and needs.
  - Optional: empathy mapping exercises.
- Persona drafting and naming (30–45 min)
  - Draft 1–3 personas.
- Wrap-up (10–15 min)
  - How personas will be documented and used.
```

## Key References for Personas

- Alan Cooper et al., *About Face* – foundational writing on goal-directed design and personas.
- Kim Goodwin, *Designing for the Digital Age* – detailed guidance on persona creation and usage.
- Nielsen Norman Group articles on effective personas and common pitfalls (`https://www.nngroup.com/articles/`; search for “personas”).
