# Stakeholder Kickoff Template

Template for a stakeholder kickoff document or meeting agenda used before starting a research project.

Use this to align stakeholders on goals, scope, expectations, and logistics.

## How to Use

- Use before major research projects or when working with new stakeholder groups.
- Combine this with Phase 1 (“Clarify the research need”) to ensure shared understanding.
