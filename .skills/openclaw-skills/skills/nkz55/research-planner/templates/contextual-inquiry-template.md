# Contextual Inquiry Template

Template for planning contextual inquiries / field visits to observe users in their real environments while they perform relevant tasks.

## How to Use

- Use when you need to understand **real‑world context**, not just what users say.
- Combine with interviews to clarify observations without disrupting work.
