# Literature Review Template

Template for conducting and summarizing a literature review or desk research relevant to a research topic.

## How to Use

- Use for academic or industry desk research to ground your study in existing knowledge.
- Be explicit about **scope** and **search strategy** to keep the review focused and reproducible.

## Key References for Literature Reviews

- Wayne C. Booth, Gregory G. Colomb, and Joseph M. Williams, *The Craft of Research* – practical guidance on research questions and literature review.
- Machi & McEvoy, *The Literature Review: Six Steps to Success* – structured process for conducting reviews.
- General academic writing and research methods texts covering systematic and narrative literature reviews.
