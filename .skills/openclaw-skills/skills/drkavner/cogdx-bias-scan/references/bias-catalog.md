# CogDx Detectable Bias Catalog

188+ cognitive bias patterns detectable via `/bias_scan`. Major categories:

## Judgment & Decision Biases
- `anchoring` — Over-reliance on first piece of information
- `availability` — Overweighting easily recalled examples
- `recency` — Overweighting recent events vs. base rates
- `confirmation` — Seeking evidence confirming existing beliefs
- `framing` — Response changes based on how question is framed
- `hindsight` — Believing past events were predictable
- `overconfidence` — Systematic overestimation of accuracy
- `planning_fallacy` — Underestimating time/cost/risk of tasks

## Attribution Biases
- `fundamental_attribution_error` — Over-attributing behavior to disposition vs. situation
- `self_serving` — Attributing success to self, failure to external factors
- `actor_observer` — Different attributions for own vs. others' behavior

## Social/Epistemic Biases
- `authority` — Overweighting authority figure claims
- `bandwagon` — Favoring popular positions
- `in_group` — Favoring information from perceived in-group
- `status_quo` — Preference for current state of affairs

## Memory & Representation Biases
- `representativeness` — Judging probability by resemblance to prototype
- `base_rate_neglect` — Ignoring statistical base rates
- `clustering_illusion` — Seeing patterns in random data
- `conjunction_fallacy` — Estimating A+B as more probable than A alone

## Motivated Reasoning
- `desirability` — Believing what is desired to be true
- `wishful_thinking` — Predicting what is hoped for
- `ostrich` — Ignoring negative information
