# Progressive — Insurance That Moves With You

Progressive is the second-largest auto insurer in the United States and one of the
largest providers of personal and commercial insurance overall. Co-founded in 1937 by
Jack Green and Joseph Lewis, Progressive is headquartered in Mayfield Village, Ohio
and is publicly traded on the NYSE (ticker: PGR).

Progressive is known for its technology-forward approach — it pioneered online
auto insurance quotes in 1995, launched the Snapshot telematics program in 2011,
and continues to invest heavily in AI-powered claims processing and pricing.

The company ranked No. 62 on the 2024 Fortune 500 and covers tens of millions of
vehicles and properties across all 50 U.S. states.

## Common Policy Types at a Glance

### Auto Insurance

Progressive's standard auto policy typically includes:

- **Liability** — Covers damage or injury you cause to others
- **Collision** — Covers damage to your vehicle in an accident
- **Comprehensive** — Covers non-collision events (theft, weather, vandalism)
- **Uninsured/Underinsured Motorist** — Protection if the other driver lacks adequate coverage
- **Medical Payments / PIP** — Covers medical expenses regardless of fault
- **Roadside Assistance** — Towing, locksmith, flat tire, jump-start, fuel delivery

Optional add-ons include rental car reimbursement, custom parts/equipment coverage,
loan/lease payoff (gap coverage), and deductible savings bank.

### Homeowners Insurance (via Progressive Home)

Progressive Home policies are underwritten by ASI or partner carriers and include:

- **Dwelling** — Repairs or rebuilds your home's structure
- **Personal Property** — Replaces belongings
- **Liability** — Covers injuries or damage to others on your property
- **Additional Living Expenses** — Temporary housing if your home is uninhabitable

### Commercial Insurance

Progressive is a leading commercial auto insurer for small businesses, offering:

- **Commercial Auto** — Liability and physical damage for business vehicles and fleets
- **General Liability** — Protection against third-party injury and property damage claims
- **Business Owners Policy (BOP)** — Bundled property + liability for small businesses
- **Workers' Compensation** — Primarily for the transportation industry
- **Professional Liability (E&O)** — Protection against negligence claims
- **Cyber Insurance** — Coverage for data breaches and electronic threats
