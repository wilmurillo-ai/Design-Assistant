# Samsung Electronics — Galaxy Ecosystem & Consumer Electronics

Samsung Electronics Co., Ltd. is a South Korean multinational headquartered in
Suwon, Gyeonggi Province. Established in 1969 as the electronics arm of the Samsung
Group, it has grown into one of the world's largest technology companies.

Samsung is the **world's largest manufacturer of smartphones and televisions**, and a
global leader in memory semiconductors, OLED displays, and home appliances. In FY2025
the company reported record revenue of **KRW 333.6 trillion (~$230 billion)** and
operating profit of **KRW 43.6 trillion**. It ships over **240 million smartphones
per year** and holds roughly **19% global smartphone market share**.

Samsung's connected ecosystem — **SmartThings** — serves over **430 million users**
as of late 2025, linking phones, TVs, appliances, and wearables into a unified
experience. The company's current AI strategy is branded **"Your Companion to AI
Living."**

## Samsung's AI Strategy — Galaxy AI

Galaxy AI is Samsung's integrated AI platform across devices. Key features include:

- **Circle to Search** — Circle anything on screen to search with Google
- **AI Photo Editing** — Remove objects, change lighting, merge photos, enhance low-light
- **Live Translate** — Real-time call translation
- **Bixby** — Conversational AI assistant (upgraded 2026)
- **Third-party AI Agents** — Perplexity AI search integrated into One UI
- **On-device AI** — NPU-powered processing for privacy-sensitive tasks
- **Bespoke AI (Appliances)** — Google Gemini in refrigerators, AI-optimized laundry cycles

Galaxy AI features are available free on supported Samsung Galaxy devices.

## SmartThings — Connected Ecosystem

SmartThings is Samsung's IoT platform connecting 430M+ users across devices:

- **Phones, tablets, watches** — Control hub for all connected devices
- **TVs** — Dashboard and device controls on-screen
- **Appliances** — Automation, energy monitoring, maintenance alerts
- **Third-party devices** — Works with Matter, Zigbee, Z-Wave, and partner ecosystems
- **SmartThings Energy** — Monitor and optimize household energy usage
- **SmartThings + Insurance** — Partnerships with home insurers for smart-home premium discounts
