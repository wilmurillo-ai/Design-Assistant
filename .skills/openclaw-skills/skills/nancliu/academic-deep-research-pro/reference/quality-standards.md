# Evidence and Quality Standards

## Evidence Hierarchy
1. Systematic reviews & meta-analyses
2. Randomized controlled trials
3. Cohort / longitudinal studies
4. Expert consensus / guidelines
5. Cross-sectional / observational studies
6. Expert opinion / editorials
7. Media reports / blogs

## Confidence Annotations
- [HIGH] Multiple high-quality sources agree
- [MEDIUM] Limited or mixed evidence
- [LOW] Single source or preliminary
- [SPECULATIVE] Hypothesis or emerging area

## Red Flags
- Claims without cited sources
- Single-study findings presented as fact
- Conflicts of interest not disclosed
- Outdated information
- Cherry-picked statistics
- Overgeneralization
