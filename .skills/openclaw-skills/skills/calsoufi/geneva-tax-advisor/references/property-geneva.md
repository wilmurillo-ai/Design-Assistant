# Owner-Occupied Property (Geneva)

## Valeur Locative (Imputed Rental Value)
If you own and live in your property in Geneva:
- You must declare a **theoretical rental income** (valeur locative)
- This is the rent you COULD get if you rented it out
- Geneva sends you this value annually (by March 31)
- Since 2025: values indexed annually under LEFI law (+12% for 2025 for some properties)

## Valeur Fiscale (Fortune)
- Geneva property: purchase price (automatically known by tax office)
- If you occupy it: **-4% per year of continuous occupation, up to -40%**
- This reduction applies to wealth tax only

## Maintenance Deductions
### Forfaitaire
- **ICC:** 15% of rental value after allowance (≤10yr) or 25% (>10yr)
- **IFD:** 10% of gross rental value (≤10yr) or 20% (>10yr)

### Effectifs
- Same codes as rental properties (Notice 1/2022)
- Common items: heating repairs, plumbing, painting, kitchen/bathroom renovation
- Energy-saving improvements (ECO category) are also deductible
- Keep all invoices — don't need to submit but must be available for 10 years

### Strategy
- Years with big renovation: choose effectifs
- Years with no work: choose forfait
- You can switch between forfait and effectifs each year

## IIC (Impôt Immobilier Complémentaire)
- Geneva-specific additional property tax
- Only for Geneva properties (not other cantons or foreign)
- Automatically calculated in GeTax's simplified screen
- Based on the fiscal value of the property

## PPE (Copropriété par Étages)
- If you own an apartment in a building (not a standalone house)
- PPE charges go in the simplified "Frais et taxes effectifs" screen (for Geneva properties)
- Fonds de rénovation contributions are deductible (code 9.2.3)

## Mortgage on Geneva Property
- Interest deductible in "Intérêts et dettes" section
- Remaining debt reduces taxable wealth
- No proportional allocation needed (unlike foreign property) since the property is in Geneva
