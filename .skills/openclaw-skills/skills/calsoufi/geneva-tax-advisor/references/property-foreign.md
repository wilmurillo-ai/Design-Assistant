# Foreign Property (Especially France)

## Core Principle: Exempt with Progression
- Foreign property income is **declared but NOT taxed** in Switzerland
- It raises your **tax rate** (worldwide income/wealth method for rate determination)
- Same for property value — declared in fortune, affects wealth tax rate, but not directly taxed

## What to Declare in GeTax

### Property Selection Page
- Check: "Je possédais un ou plusieurs biens immobiliers locatifs, loués ou terrain"
- Do NOT check "que j'occupais" unless you live in it
- Select "Étranger" and "France" (or relevant country)

### Property Details
- **Type:** "Immeuble loué" (≤2 units) or "Immeuble locatif" (>2 units)
- **Address, year of construction, ownership %**
- **Commune d'imposition** for property: leave as the foreign location

### Fortune (Wealth)
- **Valeur fiscale:** For foreign property, use **purchase price** (per ge.ch official guidance)
- Convert to CHF using **31.12 year-end rate**
- France doesn't issue a Swiss-style "valeur fiscale" → purchase price is the standard

### Rental Income (Loyers encaissés)
- **Enter the GROSS RENT the tenant paid** — not your net after management fees!
- Management fees are deducted separately in frais d'entretien
- Convert to CHF using **annual average rate**
- If managed by an agency (e.g., Foncia): the detailed transaction CSV shows individual rent lines

### Frais d'Entretien (Maintenance Costs)

#### ICC vs IFD
- **ICC:** Only effective costs (NO forfait for rental properties)
- **IFD:** Can choose forfait (10% if building ≤10yr, 20% if >10yr) OR effective
- **Always calculate both** — effectifs usually wins for rental properties

#### CRITICAL: Use the Detailed Screen
The simplified "Frais et taxes effectifs" screen is **ONLY for Geneva properties.**
For foreign properties → use **"Frais d'entretien effectifs"** (detailed screen with code list).

Per GeTax FAQ: "Pour de l'immobilier 'Hors canton de Genève', il faut passer par l'écran 'Frais d'entretien effectifs'"

#### Taxe Foncière Specifically
Per GeTax FAQ: search code **11.2.6** in "Liste des travaux" → "Impôts fonciers (à Genève: impôt immobilier complémentaire)". This is the correct way for properties outside Geneva.

#### Analyzing Copropriété Statements
Copro accounts often have MORE charges than what shows on the rental management account:
- **Provisions pour charges** (quarterly calls) — deductible under 9.2.1
- **Fonds de travaux** (renovation fund contributions) — deductible under 9.2.3
- **Special works** (e.g., roof repair, étanchéité) — deductible under relevant code (2.1.1 for roof)
- **Régularisation** credits — subtract from charges (reduces net deductible amount)
- **Badges, keys, parking** — generally NOT deductible (AUT)

**Always ask for the copropriété annual statement** in addition to the rental management statement!

#### GLI (Garantie Loyers Impayés)
Rent guarantee insurance paid by landlord — deductible. Include with management fees under 9.2.11.

### Documents to Collect
1. **Rental management statement** (e.g., Foncia compte de gestion) — shows rent collected, management fees
2. **Copropriété annual statement** (from syndic) — shows quarterly charges, works, régularisation
3. **Taxe foncière** (from impots.gouv.fr) — annual property tax
4. **Mortgage amortization tables** — for interest paid and debt remaining
5. **Bank statements** (for any accounts linked to the property)

### Currency Conversion
- **Fortune/debt** (balance sheet items): 31.12 year-end rate
- **Income/expenses** (P&L items): annual average rate
- Source: AFC rates via ictax.admin.ch or fidinam.com
