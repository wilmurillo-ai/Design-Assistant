## Tax Structure Reminder
- **ICC** = Impôt cantonal et communal (canton + commune)
- **IFD** = Impôt fédéral direct (federal)
- Married couples: full splitting (rate on 50% of combined income)
- Commune d'imposition = residence (NOT workplace)
