# Gotchas & Common Mistakes

The mistakes that actually cost people money, organized by section.

## Salary & Professional Expenses

1. **Field 15 on salary certs hides deductions.** Amat (assurance maternité GE), APGM, Krankentaggeld — employers list them in field 15 "Observations" but they should be added to field 9. Read field 15 on every cert.

2. **ICC meal deduction requires >2h daily commute by public transport.** This is the #1 misunderstood Geneva rule. IFD meals have no commute condition. You can split: forfaitaire ICC + effectifs IFD.

3. **Télétravail deduction is near-impossible in Geneva.** Need a dedicated room used ONLY for work AND employer must not provide a workspace. If in doubt, declare going to office every day — preserves the meal deduction on IFD.

4. **Commune in Annexe A = workplace**, not residence. Residence goes on page de garde only.

5. **Source tax for BOTH spouses.** Check field 12 on every salary certificate. Easy to miss the second spouse's source tax when entering the total in the récapitulation.

6. **Read ALL salary certificates yourself.** Don't assume details from one employer apply to another — each has different deductions, source tax rates, and field 15 items.

## Insurance & Medical

7. **ICC medical threshold is only 0.5%** of net income (vs 5% for IFD). Almost always worth claiming even small amounts. The IFD threshold is much harder to reach.

8. **Foreign medical expenses are fully deductible.** Dentist in the UK, dental insurance abroad, pharmacy in France — no territoriality restriction on frais médicaux.

9. **Foreign health insurance premiums** can be declared as medical expenses rather than in the insurance section. Simpler approach, same effect.

10. **Glasses, contacts, eye exams** are deductible as medical expenses if unreimbursed by insurance.

11. **Each spouse may have a different insurer.** Don't assume both are on the same caisse-maladie. Enter each separately in section 52.21.

## Property

12. **Foreign property is "exempt with progression."** Declared but not directly taxed in Switzerland — it only affects your tax rate.

13. **Copro statement ≠ rental management statement.** The copropriété annual account (from syndic) often shows significantly more charges than the rental agency CSV. Always request BOTH documents — missing the copro can mean thousands in lost deductions.

14. **Loyers encaissés = gross rent the tenant paid**, NOT your net after management fees. Management fees are a separate deductible expense.

15. **GeTax simplified maintenance screens** are only for Geneva properties. For foreign/hors-canton properties, use "Frais d'entretien effectifs" with the detailed code list.

16. **ICC: no maintenance forfait for rental properties.** Only effective costs. IFD allows 10% (≤10yr) or 20% (>10yr) forfait.

## Bank Accounts & Securities

17. **Bank fees (frais bancaires) are deductible.** Enter as positive values in état des titres F2. GeTax auto-deducts them from wealth income. Check every bank's annual statement.

18. **Wealth management fees** (droits de garde, custody fees, relevé fiscal fees) are deductible against securities income.

19. **DA-1 for foreign brokers, RSI for Swiss brokers.** Never check both for the same security.

20. **ictax fiscal values > market prices** for wealth declaration. Use "zu versteuernder Ertrag" for taxable income (lower than gross dividends — includes tax-free capital gains portion).

## Exchange Rates & Foreign Currency

21. **Fortune/debt uses 31.12 year-end rate. Income/interest uses annual average rate.** These are DIFFERENT numbers. Common mistake to use the same for both.

22. **Official source:** `fidinam.com/fr/blog/cours-des-devises-suisse-pour-{YEAR}` or `ictax.admin.ch`

## Other

23. **Pillar 3b / assurance-vie.** Geneva is one of few cantons allowing this deduction. Couple: CHF 3'518, single: CHF 2'345. Don't miss it.

24. **Donations to foreign charities are NOT deductible.** Must be Swiss-registered public utility organizations. International orgs with a Swiss branch (Red Cross, MSF) do count.

25. **Crypto is wealth** — declared in état des titres at AFC fiscal values (31.12). Capital gains from private trading are tax-free, but staking/mining income is taxable.

26. **Source-taxed workers (B/L permit):** If gross ≥ CHF 120k, TOU is mandatory. File full GeTax return to claim all deductions.

27. **Keep receipts 10 years** for audit. You don't need to attach them to the declaration, but must have them ready if the AFC asks.

28. **Family allowances** go in Annexe C2 (code 16.63), NOT in salary.
