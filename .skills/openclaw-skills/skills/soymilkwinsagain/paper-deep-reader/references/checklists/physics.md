# Checklist: Physics

Run this after the general checklist.

## Physical setup

- [ ] I stated the physical system and regime clearly.
- [ ] I recorded the governing equations or physical laws that matter.
- [ ] I identified the key control parameters, scales, or boundary conditions.

## Derivation quality

- [ ] I marked where approximations, linearizations, perturbative steps, or closures enter.
- [ ] I noted whether the result behaves sensibly in simple or limiting cases.
- [ ] I checked dimensional or unit consistency when relevant.
- [ ] I connected the formal result to physical intuition.

## Evidence and interpretation

- [ ] I stated how the theory connects to observables, numerics, or experiment.
- [ ] I noted any mismatch between the theoretical claim and the supporting evidence.
- [ ] I recorded the regime in which the result should and should not be trusted.

## Skepticism

- [ ] I identified at least one hidden idealization, fragile approximation, or unclear regime boundary.
- [ ] I did not treat mathematical neatness as sufficient physical validation.
