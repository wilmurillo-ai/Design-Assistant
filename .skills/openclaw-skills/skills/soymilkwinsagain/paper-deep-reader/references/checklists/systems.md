# Checklist: Systems

Run this after the general checklist.

## Systems setup

- [ ] I stated the bottleneck or systems problem precisely.
- [ ] I described the workload and deployment setting.
- [ ] I recorded the hardware / software assumptions that matter.
- [ ] I explained the design tradeoff rather than only the headline metric.

## Benchmark credibility

- [ ] I checked whether the benchmark is realistic for the claimed use case.
- [ ] I checked whether baseline implementations are fair and comparable.
- [ ] I recorded the main metrics: latency, throughput, memory, reliability, energy, or cost.
- [ ] I noted whether the gain is stable across scales, loads, or machines.

## Judgment

- [ ] I distinguished algorithmic novelty from systems engineering novelty.
- [ ] I identified what the result depends on operationally.
- [ ] I noted at least one likely deployment sensitivity or missing measurement.
