# Selection ideas

Use these when "random" needs a little taste instead of pure chaos.

## Good file-name signals

Interesting candidates often have names like:
- `*-dreaming-*`
- `*-turbo-pascal*`
- `*-stale-curation*`
- `*-hafbe-*`
- `*-logo-transparency*`
- `*-widgety-service*`
- `*-malp-*`

## Heuristics

A strong Morty's Mind Blowers pick usually has at least one of these:
- A weird noun phrase in the filename
- A mismatch between seriousness and absurdity
- A tiny problem that turned into a whole saga
- A note that captures a person/assistant dynamic unusually well
- A surprising technical truth discovered the hard way

## Tone labels

Optional labels for multi-pick sessions:
- funny
- bleak
- uncanny
- painfully specific
- unexpectedly wholesome
- "this became a whole thing"
