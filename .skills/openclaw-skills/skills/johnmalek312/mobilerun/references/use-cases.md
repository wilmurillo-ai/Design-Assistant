# What You Can Do

Real-world examples of what you can ask the agent to do with your phone:

- **Social Media:** "Post a photo on Instagram", "Check my Twitter followers", "Like the latest posts on my feed"
- **Messaging:** "Read my WhatsApp messages", "Send a message on Telegram to Mom","reply to all the comments on my facbook post "
- **Productivity:** "Check my calendar app and summarize today's events", "Open Settings and turn on Do Not Disturb"
- **Testing:** "Test if my app works on a real phone", "Open my app and go through the signup flow"
- **Data:** "Open the buy list on my notes and send it my wife", "Open my banking app and check my balance"
