# Ditto Transformations & Pokémon Specialties (百变怪技能 & 宝可梦特长)

## Ditto Transformations (14 Skills)

You play as Ditto. Learn skills by befriending specific Pokémon.

| # | Skill | Teacher | Location | Food Upgrade | What It Does |
|---|-------|---------|----------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | **Water Gun** | Squirtle | Withered Wasteland | Soup (↑ range) | Water tiles, revitalize dry ground, water crops |
| 2 | **Leafage** | Bulbasaur | Withered Wasteland | Salad (↑ pull from water/rocks) | Pull grass from earth; with upgrade: duckweed on water, moss from rocks |

| 6 | **Jump** | Magikarp | Withered Wasteland | — | Press R to jump |

### Skill Wheel
8 skills accessible via bottom-right tab or L button skill wheel. Others are passive upgrades or contextual.

### Food Upgrades Summary
| Food | Upgrades | How to Cook |
|------|----------|-------------|
| Soup | Water Gun range ↑ | Cooking Pot + vegetables |
| Salad | Leafage → duckweed/moss | No cooking needed |
| Bread | Cut → ranged + metal | Bread Oven + Wheat |
| Hamburger Steak | Rock Smash → break everything | Frying Pan + ingredients |
