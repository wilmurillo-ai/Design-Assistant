Initial release of the Photo Calorie Tracker skill.

- Recognizes food photos and logs daily calorie intake.
- Generates calorie intake reports for any specified number of days.
- Focuses on lunch, dinner, and snacks (breakfast is excluded from reports).
- Marks status as either incomplete (missing lunch or dinner) or the percent difference from the target.
- Provides a standard output format and clear meal definitions.
- Not intended for weight analysis, nutrition breakdowns, or workout planning.
