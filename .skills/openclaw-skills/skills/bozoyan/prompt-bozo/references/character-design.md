# 角色设计姿势参考

## 说明

本文档提供了72组角色设计姿势提示词，适用于2D/3D角色设计和动画制作。分为英文版和中文版。

## 姿势分类

### 基础站立姿势

**正面站立**
- 双臂自然垂放: `Make this person stand facing forward, arms relaxed at their sides`
- 单手叉腰: `Make this person stand with one hand on their hip`
- 双手背后: `Make this person stand with arms behind their back`
- 双臂张开: `Make this person stand with arms spread wide`
- T字姿势: `Make this person stand facing forward in a T-pose`

**侧面站立**
- 侧面站立双臂交叉: `Make this person stand in profile, arms crossed`
- 侧面回头: `Make this person stand turned slightly away from the viewer, glancing back`

### 坐姿

**地面坐姿**
- 盘腿坐: `Make this person sit cross-legged on the ground`
- 侧坐: `Make this person sit sideways on the floor`
- 倚靠坐: `Make this person sit on the floor leaning back on their hands`
- 抱膝坐: `Make this person sit on the ground hugging their knees`

**椅子坐姿**
- 椅子盘腿: `Make this person sit on a chair, legs crossed casually`
- 跪坐: `Make this person kneel on the ground`

### 蹲姿

**基础蹲姿**
- 普通蹲姿: `Make this person crouch down with both hands resting on the ground`
- 单膝蹲: `Make this person kneel on one knee`

### 动态姿势

**跳跃/动作**
- 跳跃: `Make this person jump high into the air`
- 高踢: `Make this person stand with one leg kicked high outward`
- 出拳: `Make this person leap forward with one fist extended`
- 战斗姿态: `Make this person stand with both fists clenched tightly`

**跑动**
- 跑步: `Make this person run sideways with arms pumping and legs stretched in motion`

### 表情与手势

**手部动作**
- 指向前方: `Make this person crouch low with one hand touching the ground and the other hand pointing forward`
- 指向上方: `Make this person stand with one hand pointing upward and the other hand resting on their hip`
- 双手合十: `Make this person stand with both hands pressed together under their cheek`
- 心形手势: `Make this person stand with both hands forming a heart shape in front of their chest`
- 和平手势: `Make this person stand with arms bent upward making a peace sign with both hands near their face`

**特殊手势**
- 手枪手势: `Make this person stand sideways with a mischievous smirk, making a finger gun gesture`
- 吃东西: `Make this person sit cross-legged on the ground, holding food with both hands near their mouth`

### 情绪表达

**开心/兴奋**
- 欢笑: `Make this person stand with both arms stretched upward in an energetic pose, expression joyful with a wide open smile`
- 飞翔: `Make this person float in the air as if flying, arms spread behind`

**悲伤/哭泣**
- 哭泣: `Make this person sit on the ground with knees pulled tightly to chest and head turned sideways, expression sad with visible tears`

**生气/愤怒**
- 生气: `Make this person stand with both fists clenched tightly, body leaning forward in a battle stance, eyes sharp and angry`

**可爱/撒娇**
- 可爱姿势: `Make this person stand with both hands pressed together under their cheek, body leaning sideways in a cute pose`
- 乞求: `Make this person kneel on one knee, both hands clasped together in front as if begging`

**迷茫/困惑**
- 困惑: `Make this person lean forward with both hands on their hips, one brow raised`

**惊讶/震撼**
- 惊讶: `Make this person stand with arms raised slightly outward, captured from a dramatic low side angle`

### 摄影参数

**镜头焦距**
- 24mm: 广角，有张力
- 35mm: 标准人文
- 50mm: 标准视角
- 85mm: 人像特写
- 135mm: 压缩背景

**光圈**
- f/2.0: 极浅景深
- f/2.8: 浅景深
- f/4.0: 中等景深
- f/5.6: 较深景深

**拍摄角度**
- 平视: 正常透视
- 俯视: 压缩人物
- 仰视: 夸张人物
- 高角度: 展现环境
- 低角度: 夸张动感

## 完整提示词格式

```
Make this person [姿势描述], photographed with [镜头] at [光圈] from [拍摄角度], expression [表情描述], background [背景描述], rendering style [渲染风格描述].
```

**中文格式**:
```
让这个人[姿势描述]，使用[镜头]以[光圈]的[拍摄角度]拍摄，表情[表情描述]，背景为[背景描述]，[渲染风格描述]。
```

## 使用建议

1. **基础姿势**: 从简单的站立、坐姿开始
2. **情绪表达**: 姿势要与表情协调
3. **动态效果**: 动态姿势注意重心和平衡
4. **摄影参数**: 根据场景选择合适的镜头和光圈
5. **渲染风格**: 2D/3D风格要明确指定
