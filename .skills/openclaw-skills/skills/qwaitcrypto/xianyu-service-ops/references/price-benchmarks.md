## Pricing Strategy Notes

1. **Never go below ¥5**: triggers risk controls and puts your account at risk
2. **¥19 is the safe floor**: the widely observed minimum psychological pricing threshold for services
3. **Packages outsell single-price listings**: giving buyers a choice reduces decision friction
4. **A premium tier has value even if it rarely sells**: the anchor effect makes the middle tier look like a better deal
5. **Pricing must match your review count**: no reviews + high price = no credibility
