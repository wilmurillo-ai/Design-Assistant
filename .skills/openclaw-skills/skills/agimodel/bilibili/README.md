# Bilibili

> A good Bilibili video does not just get watched. It gets answered by the crowd.

Bilibili is a cultural resonance engine for danmu-native video design.

## What it does

This skill helps you:
- design stronger 弹幕触发点
- create 槽点, 梗点, and 共鸣点
- improve collective audience response
- make videos feel more B站-native
- increase the chance of 名场面 and quote-worthy moments

## What it does NOT do

- guarantee virality
- replace editing execution
- act as a generic all-platform growth tool
- replace copyright or platform policy review
