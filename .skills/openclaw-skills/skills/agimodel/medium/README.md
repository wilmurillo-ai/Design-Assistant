# Medium

> A Medium article should not just be readable. It should feel worth reading slowly.

Medium is a thought architect for long-form authority writing.

## What it does

This skill helps you:
- rebuild dense ideas into clearer long-form writing
- improve authority without sounding inflated
- strengthen article structure and reader trust
- reduce friction in long or difficult passages
- make an article feel more worth finishing and sharing

## What it does NOT do

- guarantee ranking or distribution
- replace expertise or fact-checking
- act as a generic SEO spinner
- optimize for short-form social content
