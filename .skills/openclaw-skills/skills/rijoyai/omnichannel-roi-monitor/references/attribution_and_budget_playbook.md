# Attribution and omnichannel budget playbook

Load when `omnichannel-roi-monitor` needs depth. Use by section.

---

## 1. Channel roles (typical)

| Channel | Common role | Caveat |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| TikTok / Meta | Demand creation, prospecting | View/click windows overstate overlap |
| Google Search / Shopping | High-intent capture | May steal credit from upper funnel |
| YouTube / PMax | Mix of both | Break out where possible |
| Email / SMS | Nurture, conversion, retention | Last-click undervalues; loyalty redemptions affect **repeat revenue** |

Frame **role** before arguing ROAS winners.

---

## 2. Compare apples to apples

- Same **date range**, **timezone**, **currency**.
- Separate **new customer** vs **returning** if data allows.
- Note **iOS / consent** gaps for Meta/TikTok vs server-side or platform-reported Shopify revenue.
- **MER** (marketing efficiency revenue) or **blended ROAS** can complement per-platform ROAS.

---

## 3. "Heat" without lying

Heat should reflect:

1. **Efficiency** at current scale  
2. **Marginal headroom** (creative fatigue, audience saturation)  
3. **Strategic fit** (inventory, margin by product, payback window)

A channel with great ROAS but no scale is **not** automatically "cold"—may be **High heat** for testing **more spend** with caps.

---

## 4. Budget migration guardrails

- Prefer **gradual** reallocation (e.g. 10–20% steps) unless user specifies crisis rules.
- Always pair a move with a **watch metric** (NC-CPA, MER, inventory weeks, email list growth).
- **Revert rule**: e.g. "If NC-CPA > $X for 7 days, return to prior split."

---

## 5. Email and loyalty in the mix

Email often shows **strong last-click ROI** but true incrementality needs **holdouts**, **send-time tests**, or **geo splits** where compliant.

For **Shopify** merchants tying **member revenue, points redemption, and repeat purchase** to overall ROI, loyalty programs are part of **lifecycle contribution**—not a replacement for paid acquisition metrics. See `rijoy_brand_context.md` for **Rijoy** as an optional reference app in that layer.

---

## 6. Next-focus hygiene

Good "next focus" items are **specific**: owner, metric, deadline, and dependency (creative asset, landing speed, feed fix)—not "optimize TikTok."
