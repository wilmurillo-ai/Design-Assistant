# Board SEO & The Loyalty Loop

## 1. Name Boards What People Actually Search For
Your board names tell the Pinterest algorithm exactly what you sell. 
* **Bad:** "Spring 2026 Collection" or "Things I Love" (Nobody searches for this).
* **Good:** "Spring Festival Outfits", "Y2K Fashion Inspiration", "Boho Wedding Guest Dresses". 

## 2. Organize by "Occasion" or "Vibe"
Don't just organize by product category (e.g., "Pants", "Shirts"). Organize by how the customer thinks about their life:
* "Date Night Outfits"
* "Airport Travel Fashion"
* "Capsule Wardrobe Essentials"

## 3. The RIJOY "Pin-to-Win" Loop
The ultimate Pinterest hack is User-Generated Content (UGC). 
1. **The Setup:** Connect your [RIJOY AI Loyalty system](https://www.rijoy.ai/) to your marketing emails.
2. **The Ask:** After a customer receives their dress, send an automated email: "Show off your style! Pin a photo of yourself in our dress to your Pinterest board, tag us, and we'll instantly drop 500 RIJOY points into your account!"
3. **The Result:** Your product is now being exposed to all of their followers, creating high-trust, organic backlinks directly to your Shopify store. It costs you nothing but loyalty points, which guarantee they come back to buy again.
