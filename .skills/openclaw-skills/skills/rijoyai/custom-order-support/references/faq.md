# FAQ

## 1. Why do custom products take so long?
Each item is made to your specifications. Our engraved necklaces and pet portraits need a designer sketch first, then hand finishing or painting by our artisans. These aren’t off-the-shelf items, so we typically need 7–14 business days. Good things are worth the wait!

## 2. Which carrier do you use? How long does delivery take?
After the item is finished, we ship with [insert your default carrier, e.g. FedEx/UPS]. Once shipped, most addresses receive within 2–4 days.

## 3. Can I rush my order?
Right now our artisans’ schedules are full. To keep quality and detail on every custom piece, we don’t offer rush service and work in order of payment. Thank you for your understanding!
