# Custom Product Post-Purchase & Change Policy

## Changing order details (24-hour golden window)
* **Within 24 hours of order**: If you notice a typo in the engraving, want to swap the pet photo, or chose the wrong color/size, contact us to change it. This is our “golden change window.”
* **After 24 hours**: Your one-of-a-kind order has entered production (material may already be cut or engraved). We’re sorry, but we can’t change custom content at this stage. We appreciate your understanding.

## Returns & exchanges
* **Non-quality reasons**: Custom items carry your personal imprint (e.g. your name, your pet’s likeness). We can’t resell them, so we don’t support no-reason returns or exchanges (e.g. “changed my mind” or “wrong style”).
* **Quality or our error**: If we engraved the wrong name or the item was damaged in transit, send us clear photos within 48 hours of delivery. We’ll arrange a free remake and reship, or a refund.
