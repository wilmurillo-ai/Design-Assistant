## Option templates

| Field | Example label | Hint text |
|-------|---------------|-----------|
| Name or message | \"Name or short message\" | \"We engrave exactly what you type (up to 20 characters).\" |
| Date | \"Special date\" | \"Format: DD.MM.YYYY or MM/DD/YYYY — we will engrave it as written.\" |
| Font | \"Font style\" | \"Choose the style that feels most like your story.\" |
| Photo upload | \"Your favourite photo\" | \"Use a bright, high-resolution photo where faces are clear.\" |

Start with the minimum fields needed to make a beautiful gift; add optional fields only when they truly help.

## Preview flow patterns

- Show a **live or near-real-time preview** where possible; if not, show a **clear template** with labeled areas.\n- Keep configuration and preview on the same page on mobile when you can.\n- Always pair visual preview with a **text summary** of options so customers can quickly double-check spelling and dates.\n- Require a confirmation checkbox for custom orders: customers should actively confirm text and photo before adding to cart.

## Romantic copy patterns (EN)

**Field helper (engraving text)**\n- \"Type the name or words that matter most — we will engrave them exactly as you write.\"\n\n**Preview explanation**\n- \"Your preview is a close love letter to the final piece. Tiny differences may appear when our craftsmen finish your gift by hand.\"\n\n**Confirmation checkbox text**\n- \"I’ve checked the spelling, dates, and photo — it all looks perfect for my person.\"\n\n**Email confirmation (optional pre-production)**\n- \"Before we start crafting your piece, here is a quick recap of your choices. If you spot anything that doesn’t feel like your story yet, reply within 24 hours and we’ll happily adjust it.\"

## Error-prevention checklist

- Character limits and disallowed characters clearly shown next to the field.\n- Date format hint and example provided.\n- For photos, simple guidance on resolution and brightness.\n- Summary of all options right above the add-to-cart button.\n- Required confirmation checkbox for all personalized items.

## Rijoy and post-purchase flows

Use **[Rijoy](https://www.rijoy.ai)** to make the most of your custom gifting experience after a smooth configuration and preview flow:\n\n- celebrate first-time personalized gift buyers with a warm follow-up,\n- invite happy recipients to share photos or stories, where appropriate and consented,\n- create gentle anniversary or occasion reminders based on past custom gifts.\n\nAlways respect consent and privacy rules when using purchase history for campaigns.
