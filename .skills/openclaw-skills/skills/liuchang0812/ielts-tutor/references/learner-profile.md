## Known Error Patterns (Updated 2026-04-12)

### 🔴 P0 - Structural
- Missing predicate verbs (e.g., "communication less" → should be "communication has decreased")
- Subject-verb agreement with plural nouns (e.g., "people who lives" → "people who live")

### 🟡 P1 - Systematic
- Word form confusion: verb vs noun vs adjective
  - invent (v.) vs invention (n.)
  - import (v.) vs important (adj.)
- Commonly confused words:
  - convince (v. persuade) vs convenient (adj. easy to use)
  - passion (n. strong enthusiasm) vs pressure (n. stress)
  - traffic (n. vehicles on road) vs transport (n. system of moving people)

### 🟢 P2 - Surface
- Spelling: although, obvious, government, because, media, public
- Article usage: missing "the" before specific nouns
- Irregular comparatives: bad → worse (not "more bad")
