The Core Laylatul Qadr Dua

Taught by the Prophet ﷺ specifically for this blessed night

1
The Dua of 'Afw (Pardon)
اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّكَ عَفُوٌّ كَرِيمٌ تُحِبُّ الْعَفْوَ فَاعْفُ عَنِّي
Allahumma innaka 'afuwwun kareemun tuhibbul 'afwa fa'fu 'anni
O Allah, You are Pardoning and Generous. You love to pardon, so pardon me.
Tirmidhi 3513 - Sahih
When to say: Repeat 100+ times during tahajjud, especially in sujud (prostration)
Context: 'Aisha RA asked the Prophet ﷺ: "If I know which night is Laylatul Qadr, what should I say?" This was his response.
Personalize it: Add "wa 'an walidayya" (and my parents) or "wa 'an jami'il muslimeen" (and all Muslims)
Say on ALL Nights
Especially Nights 21, 23, 25, 27, 29
