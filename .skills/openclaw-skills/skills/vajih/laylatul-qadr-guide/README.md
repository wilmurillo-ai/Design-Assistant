# Laylatul Qadr Guide - OpenClaw Skill

> *A comprehensive spiritual companion for Muslims during Ramadan's last ten nights*

## 🌙 What is This?

The Laylatul Qadr Guide is an OpenClaw skill that transforms your Ramadan preparation into a deeply personalized, psychologically-informed, and spiritually-grounded experience. It combines:

- **Authentic Islamic scholarship** (Quran, Sahih Hadith, classical scholars)
- **Behavioral psychology** (habit formation, energy management, sustainable worship)
- **Sufi wisdom** (presence, sincerity, divine love)
- **Practical UX design** (personalized plans, beautiful artifacts, tracking systems)

## ✨ Key Features

### 1. Personalized Worship Plans
- Custom schedules based on your energy levels, work, family situation
- Night-by-night strategies (peak intensity on odd nights, recovery on even)
- Flexible guidance for beginners to advanced worshippers

### 2. Authenticated Islamic Content
- All duas verified from Quran and Sahih hadith
- Clear source citations (Bukhari, Muslim, Tirmidhi, etc.)
- Respects diverse madhahib (schools of thought)

### 3. Beautiful Artifacts
- Printable HTML dua books with Arabic, transliteration, translation
- Night-by-night worship schedules
- Reflection journals with prompts
- Family worship activity planners
- Interactive tracking systems

### 4. Behavioral Optimization
- Habit stacking strategies
- Implementation intentions (if-then planning)
- Energy management principles
- Compassionate self-talk (counter perfectionism)
- Social accountability structures

### 5. Holistic Family Integration
- Age-appropriate activities for children
- Couple coordination strategies
- Balance work/family/worship without guilt
