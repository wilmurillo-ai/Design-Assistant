# Personality Analysis Framework

## Signal Extraction

From profile + 5 posts, score each dimension:

### 1. Openness (开放性)

| Signal | High | Low |
|--------|------|-----|
| Content variety | Multiple themes (food + travel + fashion) | Single focused niche |
| Emoji/decoration | Rich, varied emoji use | Minimal or none |
| Caption style | Long, reflective, storytelling | Short titles, factual |
| Experimentation | Tries new things, shares firsts | Sticks to familiar topics |

### 2. Social Energy (社交能量)

| Signal | Extrovert | Introvert |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| Comment replies | Replies to most comments warmly | Few or no replies |
| Bio tone | Inviting ("找我聊天", "欢迎交流") | Boundary-setting ("别私信") |
| Post frequency | High (multiple per week) | Sparse (monthly) |
| Group content | Photos with friends, events | Solo shots, personal spaces |

### 3. Communication Style (沟通风格)

| Signal | Emotional/感性 | Rational/理性 |
|--------|--------------|--------------|
| Captions | Feelings, moods, metaphors | Facts, lists, reviews |
| Tags | Mood tags (#心情 #治愈) | Category tags (#测评 #攻略) |
| Photo style | Aesthetic, atmospheric | Informational, clear |

### 4. Engagement Personality

| Type | Signs | Best approach |
|------|-------|---------------|
| Curator (策展型) | Polished content, consistent aesthetic, few comments | Appreciate their taste, ask about curation process |
| Sharer (分享型) | Casual, frequent posts, replies to comments | Casual, relatable, share your own experience |
| Expert (专家型) | Detailed guides, tips, reviews | Ask specific questions, show you read carefully |
| Expressive (表达型) | Emotional captions, personal stories, artistic | Resonate with feelings, share similar experiences |

## Approachability Prediction

Score 0-100 based on these weighted factors (how likely they are to welcome a genuine conversation):

| Factor | Weight | High score signals | Low score signals |
|--------|--------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Comment engagement | 30% | Replies to comments, asks questions back | No replies, comments disabled |
| Bio openness | 20% | No "别加我", has contact info or inviting tone | Explicitly says don't DM |
| Follower ratio | 15% | <5k followers (approachable) | >50k (likely gets too many DMs) |
| Post recency | 15% | Active in last week | Last post >1 month ago |
| Content personality | 20% | Interactive content (questions, polls, recommendations) | Pure showcase, no interaction |

### Rating scale

- **High (70-100)**: Active, engages with comments, open bio, approachable follower count — good chance a genuine first message will be welcomed
- **Medium (40-69)**: Some engagement, neutral bio, moderate following — worth trying with a well-crafted first line
- **Low (0-39)**: No comment replies, boundary-setting bio, very high followers, or inactive — they may not see your message or prefer not to chat

### Important caveats to mention

- This is a rough estimate, not a guarantee
- The quality of your first line matters more than any prediction
- Respect if someone doesn't reply — don't double-message
