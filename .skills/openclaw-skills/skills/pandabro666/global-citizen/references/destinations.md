# Destination Structural Knowledge

This file provides **structural knowledge** about major destination regions and their general
characteristics. It does NOT contain guaranteed-accurate data — all specific numbers (prices,
processing times, requirements) must be verified through real-time queries before use.

**Every data point in this file should be treated as an approximate baseline for generating
search queries, NOT as a definitive reference.**

## Europe

### Portugal
- **Pathway types**: Golden visa (fund investment), D7 (passive income), D8 (digital nomad), skilled migration
- **Structural advantage**: Lowest residence requirement in Europe for golden visa (approximately 7-14 days/year); 5-year path to citizenship
- **Key consideration**: NHR tax regime for new residents — verify current eligibility rules as they have been tightening
- **Naturalization**: Language exam required (Portuguese A2); cumulative residence requirements apply
- **Schengen access**: Yes
- **Data to verify**: Current investment thresholds, D7/D8 income requirements, NHR eligibility criteria
- **Official source**: AIMA (aima.gov.pt)

### Greece
- **Pathway types**: Golden visa (real estate), self-employed, digital nomad
- **Structural advantage**: Real estate investment threshold historically lower than Portugal; no mandatory residence requirement for visa renewal
- **Key consideration**: 7 years to citizenship requires actual physical residence — this is a common misconception
- **Naturalization**: Must genuinely reside; language + civics exam required
- **Schengen access**: Yes
- **Data to verify**: Current real estate investment thresholds by region, flat tax regime details
- **Official source**: greekgoldenvisa.gov.gr

### Malta
- **Pathway types**: MPRP (combined investment for residency), exceptional naturalization for citizenship
- **Structural advantage**: EU member with strong passport (180+ visa-free destinations); English-speaking
- **Key consideration**: CBI (MEIN) program was ruled illegal by EU court in 2024 and abolished. Citizenship now only through exceptional naturalization — much harder and more expensive
- **Schengen access**: Yes
- **Data to verify**: MPRP investment components, exceptional naturalization criteria
- **Official source**: identitymalta.com

### Spain
- **Pathway types**: Digital nomad visa, skilled work visa, student path
- **Key consideration**: Golden visa (real estate) abolished in 2024; currently relies on work/nomad pathways
- **Naturalization**: 10 years standard (2 years for Latin American nationals); language + culture exam
- **Schengen access**: Yes
- **Data to verify**: Digital nomad income requirements, work visa salary thresholds

## Middle East

### UAE (Dubai / Abu Dhabi)
- **Pathway types**: Golden visa (real estate / business), blue card (exceptional talent), freelancer permit
- **Structural advantage**: Zero personal income tax, no capital gains tax, no inheritance tax; 10-year golden visa validity
- **Key consideration**: No realistic path to Emirati citizenship; passport has limited visa-free access
- **Residence requirement**: Golden visa requires entry every 180 days
- **Data to verify**: Current real estate thresholds for golden visa, blue card eligibility criteria
- **Official source**: u.ae (government portal)

## Southeast Asia

### Thailand
- **Pathway types**: LTR visa (4 categories), Elite Card, retirement visa, DTV (digital nomad)
- **Structural advantage**: Very low cost of living; good healthcare infrastructure; popular long-stay destination
- **Key consideration**: No realistic path to permanent residency or citizenship for foreigners; cannot own land
- **Data to verify**: LTR visa income thresholds by category, Elite Card pricing tiers, DTV requirements
- **Official source**: thaievisa.go.th

### Malaysia
- **Pathway types**: MM2H (long-term stay), DE Rantau (digital nomad)
- **Structural advantage**: English widely spoken; low cost of living; Commonwealth ties
- **Key consideration**: MM2H has undergone multiple major policy revisions with significant threshold changes
- **Data to verify**: Current MM2H financial requirements (they change frequently), DE Rantau income threshold
- **Official source**: mm2h.gov.my

### Singapore
- **Pathway types**: EP (employment pass), ONE Pass (top talent), PEP
- **Structural advantage**: Global financial center; English-speaking; rule of law; strong passport
- **Key consideration**: PR approval is opaque and competitive; no guaranteed path from EP → PR → citizenship
- **Data to verify**: EP salary thresholds, ONE Pass eligibility, current PR approval trends
- **Official source**: mom.gov.sg

## Caribbean (CBI Fast-Track Passports)

> General note: Caribbean CBI passports are primarily **visa-free travel tools and backup identities**.
> They are not designed for actual residence. The five Caribbean CBI nations harmonized their
> minimum donation thresholds in 2024 — verify current unified pricing.

### St. Kitts and Nevis
- **Distinction**: World's longest-running CBI program (est. 1984)
- **Key consideration**: St. Kitts passport does NOT qualify for US E-2 visa
- **Data to verify**: Current donation and real estate thresholds, processing time, due diligence fees

### Grenada
- **Distinction**: ONLY Caribbean CBI nation with US E-2 treaty — allows long-term US residence
- **Key consideration**: E-2 is a visa, not citizenship; must maintain substantial investment in US
- **Data to verify**: Current thresholds, E-2 application process and success rates

### Other Caribbean CBI Nations
- Antigua & Barbuda, Dominica, St. Lucia — similar structure with varying thresholds
- **Data to verify**: Each nation's current pricing, processing times, and visa-free country lists

## Americas

### Canada
- **Pathway types**: Express Entry (points-based), PNP (province-specific), student → work → PR
- **Structural advantage**: PhD holders and STEM graduates receive significant CRS points bonuses; some provinces have dedicated researcher streams
- **Naturalization**: 3 out of last 5 years physical presence required for citizenship
- **Data to verify**: Current CRS cutoff scores, PNP streams for PhD/researchers, processing times
- **Official source**: ircc.canada.ca

### Panama
- **Pathway types**: Friendly Nations visa, pensionado (retirement), qualified investor
- **Structural advantage**: US dollar economy; territorial tax system (foreign income exempt); no exchange controls
- **Key consideration**: China passport eligibility varies by visa category — verify current rules
- **Naturalization**: 5 years to permanent residency; additional years to citizenship
- **Data to verify**: Which nationalities qualify for Friendly Nations visa, current income requirements
- **Official source**: migracion.gob.pa

## Oceania

### Australia
- **Pathway types**: 189 (independent skilled), 190 (state-nominated), 482 (employer-sponsored), student pathway
- **Structural advantage**: PhD holders receive additional points; some states prioritize researchers
- **Naturalization**: 4 years on PR (including 1 year immediately before application)
- **Data to verify**: Current points test pass marks, state nomination allocations for researchers, processing times
- **Official source**: immigration.gov.au

## Greater China (HK / Macau / Taiwan)

> These are not "overseas" in the traditional sense, but represent important identity planning
> pathways for PRC nationals. Each has unique advantages in education access, tax optimization,
> and global mobility that complement traditional overseas identity options.

### Hong Kong SAR

- **Pathway types**: QMAS (points-based), Talent Admission Schemes (Top Talent, Tech Talent, GBA), investment (formerly CIES, now under reconsideration), dependent visa
- **Structural advantage**: Low tax jurisdiction (max 15% salary tax, max 16.5% profits tax); world-class financial infrastructure; HK passport ranks top 20 globally (170+ visa-free)
- **Key consideration**: 7 years of ordinary residence required for permanent residency; HK = special administrative region, NOT a separate sovereign country; HK passport does require a Home Return Permit for mainland entry
- **Talent schemes**: Multiple fast-track pathways for tech/AI researchers — verify current eligibility and quota status
- **Data to verify**: QMAS points passing scores, Top Talent Pass quota and salary thresholds, investment immigration status
- **Official source**: immigration.gov.hk

### Macau SAR

- **Pathway types**: Major Capital Investment Immigrant Residency (previously suspended, under debate for relaunch), talent attraction, specialized employment
- **Structural advantage**: Low tax (max 12% complementary tax); proximity to Guangdong-HK-Macau GBA; potential GBA integration benefits
- **Key consideration**: Macau passport has very limited visa-free access (~80 countries); economy heavily reliant on gaming/tourism; smaller professional job market than HK
- **Data to verify**: Whether major capital investment program has been officially relaunched, current talent scheme criteria
- **Official source**: fsm.gov.mo

### Taiwan (ROC)

- **Pathway types**: Employment gold card (tech professionals), investment/business, marriage, student
- **Structural advantage**: Cultural and linguistic proximity; healthcare quality; lower cost of living than HK
- **Key consideration**: Complex cross-strait political situation; PRC passport holders face restrictions; not universally recognized as a separate immigration jurisdiction
- **Data to verify**: Employment gold card eligibility for AI researchers, investment requirements, current cross-strait travel policies
- **Official source**: nat.gov.tw

## 2025-2026 Trend Patterns (Structural, Verify Details)

1. **European golden visa tightening**: Multiple countries (Spain, Ireland, Netherlands, Portugal) have closed or restricted real estate golden visa pathways. Trend continues.
2. **Middle East rising**: UAE golden visa demand increasing among high-net-worth individuals
3. **Caribbean CBI consolidation**: Five nations moved toward unified minimum pricing under MoA signed in 2024
4. **Digital nomad visa proliferation**: 50+ countries launched programs 2023-2026; varying quality and real utility
5. **AI/researcher talent competition**: Multiple countries (UK, Germany, Singapore, Canada) have introduced or expanded fast-track pathways for AI and STEM researchers
