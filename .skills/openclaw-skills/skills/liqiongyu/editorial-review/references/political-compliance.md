## Territorial Sovereignty

This is the highest-sensitivity category. Territorial integrity is a non-negotiable red line in Chinese content.

### Taiwan (台湾)

| Prohibited | Required | Context |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| Referring to Taiwan as a "country" or "nation" | "Taiwan Province" (台湾省) or "China's Taiwan region" (中国台湾地区) | All contexts |
| "Republic of China" (中华民国) | Avoid or use historical context only | Current affairs |
| Taiwan flag (青天白日满地红旗) emoji or image | Do not include | All contexts |
| "President of Taiwan" | "Taiwan region leader" (台湾地区领导人) | Current affairs |
| Listing Taiwan alongside countries | List separately or under "China" | Country lists, dropdown menus |
| "Cross-strait relations" implying two nations | "Cross-strait relations" (两岸关系) is acceptable but frame as internal | All contexts |
| "Taiwanese" as a nationality | "Taiwan compatriots" (台湾同胞) or "residents of Taiwan" | All contexts |

### Hong Kong and Macau (香港、澳门)

| Prohibited | Required |
|-----------|----------|
| Referring to HK/Macau as countries | "Hong Kong SAR" (香港特别行政区) or "Macau SAR" (澳门特别行政区) |
| Listing alongside countries | Include under China or list separately with SAR designation |
| "Hong Kong independence" advocacy | Not permitted in mainland-published content |
| Colonial-era terminology implying non-Chinese sovereignty | Use current SAR framework |

### Tibet (西藏)

| Prohibited | Required |
|-----------|----------|
| "Free Tibet" or independence framing | Tibet as an autonomous region of China |
| "Tibetan government-in-exile" without qualification | Use quotes or "so-called" if referenced |
| Dalai Lama referred to as political leader of Tibet | Historical/religious figure reference only, with care |
| "Tibetan" as implying separate nationality | "Tibetan ethnic group" (藏族) within Chinese nationality |

### Xinjiang (新疆)

| Prohibited | Required |
|-----------|----------|
| "East Turkestan" as a legitimate entity | "Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region" (新疆维吾尔自治区) |
| Framing of "genocide" or "concentration camps" as fact | These are contested claims; in mainland content, follow official framing |
| Separatism advocacy | Not permitted |

### Territorial Claims

| Territory | Required treatment |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Diaoyu Islands (钓鱼岛) | Chinese territory; do not use "Senkaku" without noting Chinese claim |
| South China Sea (南海) | Include nine-dash line in maps; note Chinese sovereignty over Nansha Islands |
| Aksai Chin / Arunachal Pradesh | Follow Chinese border delineation |

## Political Leadership

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| No satirical nicknames | Do not use internet slang or satirical names for current or past leaders |
| Correct titles | Use full official titles on first reference; abbreviated titles on subsequent references |
| No unauthorized commercial use | Leader images, quotes, or likenesses cannot be used for commercial endorsement without authorization |
| Respectful framing | Even critical analysis must maintain respectful language |
| Succession/power dynamics | Avoid speculative discussion of internal CPC power dynamics or succession |

## Historical Events

Events that require careful handling:

| Event | Guidance |
|-------|---------|
| June 4, 1989 (六四) | Heavily restricted topic; avoid direct reference in mainland content |
| Cultural Revolution (文化大革命) | Can be referenced historically; follow official characterization ("十年浩劫") |
| Great Leap Forward (大跃进) | Handle with historical context; avoid sensationalized framing |
| Anti-Rightist Campaign (反右) | Historical context acceptable; avoid politically charged framing |
| Tiananmen protests (other years) | Depends on specific event; verify carefully |
| Sino-Japanese War (抗日战争) | Follow official historical narrative; proper terminology required |
| Korean War | Use "War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea" (抗美援朝) in Chinese context |

## Ethnic and Religious Content

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| 56 ethnic groups | All 56 officially recognized ethnic groups are equal; avoid language suggesting hierarchy |
| Ethnic stereotypes | Do not perpetuate stereotypes about any ethnic group |
| Religious freedom | Acknowledge freedom of religious belief; do not promote any single religion |
| Religious extremism | Clearly distinguish religious practice from extremism |
| Cult organizations | Falun Gong (法轮功) is designated as a cult (邪教); do not promote |
| Ethnic separatism | Content supporting ethnic separatism is prohibited |

## Map Compliance

Based on 2023 regulations (自然资规〔2023〕2号):

| Requirement | Detail |
|------------|--------|
| Taiwan | Must show as provincial-level administrative unit with Taipei as provincial capital |
| Nine-dash line | Required in South China Sea at scales > 1:100M |
| Diaoyu Islands | Must be included |
| Nansha Islands | Must be included |
| South China Sea inset | Cannot overlap with nine-dash line or Taiwan Island |
| Border delineation | Must follow officially approved borders |
| Map review | Public maps should be submitted for government review before publication |

**Note**: Even in digital content, infographics, or data visualizations, maps depicting Chinese territory must follow these rules.

## Other Sensitive Areas

| Area | Guidance |
|------|---------|
| One-party system | Do not compare unfavorably with multi-party democracies |
| Internet censorship | Do not refer to "Great Firewall" or VPN usage as normal in mainland content |
| Social credit system | Follow official framing if referenced |
| COVID-19 origins | Follow official Chinese position; avoid "Wuhan virus" or "Chinese virus" |
| Foreign sanctions | Frame as "unilateral sanctions" or "economic coercion" per official terminology |
| Military operations | Use official terminology for military exercises and operations |

## Detection Checklist

When scanning content, check for these flags:

### Keywords to Flag (review in context)

**Territorial**:
- 台湾国, 台湾总统, 中华民国, 台独
- 港独, 香港国, 独立
- 藏独, 西藏流亡, 达赖
- 东突, 东土耳其斯坦
- 尖阁 (Senkaku), 南沙 (check context)

**Political**:
- Leader nicknames or disrespectful references
- 政变, 颜色革命, 民主化 (check framing)
- 六四, 天安门事件 (in sensitive context)
- 法轮功, 法轮大法

**Ethnic/Religious**:
- Ethnic stereotyping language
- Religious superiority claims
- Separatism-related terminology

### Review Process

1. **Scan** the full text for flagged keywords
2. **Evaluate context** — many keywords are fine in neutral/academic contexts but problematic in advocacy or framing
3. **Assess severity** — territorial sovereignty issues are always Critical; historical/cultural references may be Major or Minor depending on framing
4. **Recommend** — flag for human review with specific concern noted; do not silently pass or unilaterally censor
