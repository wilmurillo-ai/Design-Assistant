## [0.1.0] - 2026-03-19

### Added
- Initial release with 8-layer review pipeline.
- Chinese typography and punctuation rules (GB/T 15834, Sparanoid guidelines).
- Homophone and similar-looking character error detection.
- Six standard Chinese grammar error types and logical fallacy checks.
- Fact-checking methodology with source verification.
- Political and territorial compliance review framework.
- Advertising law (广告法) prohibited word detection.
- Style consistency and structure readability checks.
- Three review modes: Quick, Standard, Deep.
- Platform compatibility (Claude Code, Codex, Gemini, Claude.ai).
- Reference files for detailed rules and checklists.
