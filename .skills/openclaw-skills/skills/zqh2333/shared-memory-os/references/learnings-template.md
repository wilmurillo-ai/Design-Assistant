# Learnings Entry Template

Use this template when adding a reusable entry to `.learnings/`.

## Header
- ID: `LRN-YYYYMMDD-XXX`
- Type: `correction | debugging | best_practice | workflow | tool_behavior | risk | governance`
- Priority: `low | medium | high`
- Status: `applied | pending | archived`
- Area: short scope label
- Logged: ISO timestamp

## Body
### Summary
One short sentence that captures the reusable lesson.

### Trigger
What caused this learning to become visible?

### Failure or Friction
What went wrong, or what repeated effort created avoidable cost?

### Resolution
What actually worked?

### Reusable Rule
State the general lesson in a way future sessions can reuse.

### Suggested Action
What should the agent do next time by default?

## Quality bar
A good learning should be:
- reusable
- concise
- specific enough to act on
- free of secrets
- stronger than a raw log dump
