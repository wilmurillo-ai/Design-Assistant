# 名人名言人物池（參考清單）

> 不要用固定清單輪播！每天根據上下文動態選擇。
> 以下是推薦的人物和名言範例，可以自由擴充。

## 科技先驅

- **Alan Turing**: "Sometimes it is the people no one can imagine anything of who do the things no one can imagine."
  - 有時候，那些沒有人看好的人，做出了沒有人能想像的事。

- **Ada Lovelace**: "That brain of mine is something more than merely mortal; as time will show."
  - 我這顆腦袋絕非平凡之物，時間會證明一切。

- **Nikola Tesla**: "The present is theirs; the future, for which I really worked, is mine."
  - 現在屬於他們；但我真正努力的未來，屬於我。

- **Tim Berners-Lee**: "The Web does not just connect machines, it connects people."
  - 網路不只連接機器，更連接了人。

- **Grace Hopper**: "The most dangerous phrase in the language is: We've always done it this way."
  - 最危險的一句話是：「我們一直都是這樣做的。」

## 科學家

- **Albert Einstein**: "Imagination is more important than knowledge."
  - 想像力比知識更重要。

- **Richard Feynman**: "The first principle is that you must not fool yourself — and you are the easiest person to fool."
  - 第一原則是你不能欺騙自己——而你是最容易被自己欺騙的人。

- **Marie Curie**: "Nothing in life is to be feared, it is only to be understood. Now is the time to understand more, so that we may fear less."
  - 生命中沒有什麼值得恐懼的，只有需要理解的。現在是時候去理解更多，這樣我們才能少一些恐懼。

- **Carl Sagan**: "Somewhere, something incredible is waiting to be known."
  - 在某個地方，有某個不可思議的事物等待被發現。

- **Stephen Hawking**: "Intelligence is the ability to adapt to change."
  - 智慧就是適應變化的能力。

## 區塊鏈 / Web3

- **Satoshi Nakamoto**: "If you don't believe it or don't get it, I don't have the time to try to convince you."
  - 如果你不相信或不理解，我沒有時間說服你。

- **Vitalik Buterin**: "Whereas most technologies tend to automate workers on the periphery, blockchains automate away the center."
  - 多數技術傾向將邊緣的工人自動化，區塊鏈則是將中心自動化。

- **Naval Ravikant**: "Seek wealth, not money or status."
  - 追求財富，而非金錢或地位。

## 創業家

- **Steve Jobs**: "Stay hungry, stay foolish."
  - 求知若飢，虛心若愚。

- **Steve Jobs**: "The people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world are the ones who do."
  - 那些瘋狂到認為自己可以改變世界的人，才是真正改變世界的人。

- **Elon Musk**: "When something is important enough, you do it even if the odds are not in your favor."
  - 當一件事夠重要時，即使勝算不大，你也會去做。

- **Jeff Bezos**: "In the end, we are our choices."
  - 最終，我們就是我們所做的選擇。

- **Marc Andreessen**: "Software is eating the world."
  - 軟體正在吞噬世界。

- **Reid Hoffman**: "If you are not embarrassed by the first version of your product, you've launched too late."
  - 如果你不為產品的第一版感到尷尬，那你發布得太晚了。

## 投資大師

- **Warren Buffett**: "The stock market is designed to transfer money from the active to the patient."
  - 股市的設計就是把錢從急躁的人轉移到有耐心的人手中。

- **Warren Buffett**: "Risk comes from not knowing what you're doing."
  - 風險來自於不知道自己在做什麼。

- **Charlie Munger**: "The best thing a human being can do is to help another human being know more."
  - 人能做的最好的事，就是幫助另一個人知道更多。

- **Ray Dalio**: "Pain plus reflection equals progress."
  - 痛苦加上反思等於進步。

## 東方智慧

- **老子**: 「千里之行，始於足下。」
  - A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step.

- **孔子**: 「學而不思則罔，思而不學則殆。」
  - Learning without thought is labor lost; thought without learning is perilous.

- **莊子**: 「井蛙不可以語於海者，拘於虛也。」
  - You cannot discuss the ocean with a well frog — it is limited by the space it lives in.

- **王陽明**: 「知是行之始，行是知之成。」
  - Knowledge is the beginning of action; action is the completion of knowledge.

## 民主 / 領導

- **Winston Churchill**: "Success is not final, failure is not fatal: it is the courage to continue that counts."
  - 成功不是終點，失敗也不是致命的，重要的是繼續前進的勇氣。

- **Nelson Mandela**: "It always seems impossible until it's done."
  - 在完成之前，一切總看起來不可能。

- **Martin Luther King Jr.**: "The time is always right to do what is right."
  - 做對的事情，永遠是對的時機。

- **Abraham Lincoln**: "The best thing about the future is that it comes one day at a time."
  - 未來最好的一點是，它一次只來一天。

- **Mahatma Gandhi**: "Be the change you wish to see in the world."
  - 成為你想在世界上看到的改變。

## 哲學家

- **Marcus Aurelius**: "The happiness of your life depends upon the quality of your thoughts."
  - 你的人生幸福取決於你思想的品質。

- **Seneca**: "Luck is what happens when preparation meets opportunity."
  - 運氣就是當準備遇上機會時發生的事。

- **Epictetus**: "It's not what happens to you, but how you react to it that matters."
  - 重要的不是發生了什麼，而是你如何回應。

## 文藝 / 思想

- **Leonardo da Vinci**: "Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication."
  - 簡約是終極的精緻。

- **Theodore Roosevelt**: "Do what you can, with what you have, where you are."
  - 用你所擁有的，在你所在的地方，做你能做的事。
