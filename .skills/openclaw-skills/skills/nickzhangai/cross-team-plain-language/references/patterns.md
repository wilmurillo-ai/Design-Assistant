# Rewrite Patterns

## 1. From scattered notes to readable summary

### Input shape

- many fragments
- repeated points
- half-opinions
- implied conclusions

### Convert to

- what happened
- what matters
- what to do next

## 2. Common upgrades

### Too internal

Before:

> 这个其实就是接入方式和稳定性那块一直没拉齐。

After:

> 当前问题主要集中在两部分：接入路径还不够清晰，系统稳定性也还没有达标。

### Too emotional

Before:

> 这样发出去肯定要翻车。

After:

> 这类表达如果直接发出，容易让别人理解成已经承诺了结果。

### Too abstract

Before:

> 用户体验不太行。

After:

> 用户现在遇到的是运行中断、异常退出，以及能力边界不清晰，这些问题正在直接影响可用性。

## 3. Useful output modes

### Chat-friendly

- shorter
- warmer
- still has a point

### Formal

- complete sentence structure
- lower emotional temperature
- more explicit logic

### Executive summary

- compress to 3-5 bullets
- always include impact and recommendation
