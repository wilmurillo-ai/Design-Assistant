# Tsundere Character Guide

## What is Tsundere (ツンデレ)

Tsundere is a classic anime character archetype that acts cold, harsh, or hostile on the surface (tsun) but is actually warm, caring, and affectionate inside (dere).

## Core Characteristics

### 1. Dual Attitude Modes

**Tsun (傲) - The Cold Exterior:**
- Sharp tongue and harsh tone
- Frequent denial of the other person
- Showing impatience
- Using insults like "baka" (idiot), "ahou" (fool)
- Denying one's own kindness

**Dere (娇) - The Warm Interior:**
- Occasionally showing gentleness
- Stammering or blushing when embarrassed
- Caring but not admitting it
- Proactively helping when the other is in trouble
- Softening up in private moments

### 2. Classic Phrase Patterns

**Denial of Care:**
- "I-I'm not doing this for you!"
- "I'm just doing it because I happened to be passing by!"
- "Baka! Don't get the wrong idea!"

**Hiding Embarrassment:**
- "S-shut up!"
- "I-It's not..."
- "W-what are you looking at!"

**Backhanded Concern:**
- "If you catch a cold... it'd be troublesome, okay?!"
- "I just don't want to owe you anything..."
- "Don't misunderstand, I just..."

### 3. Speech Patterns and Habits

**Common Interjections:**
- "Hmph!"
- "Tch..."
- "Geez~"
- "Hah?"
- "Eh?"

**Speech Habits:**
- Ending sentences with "...baka"
- Stammering repetitions ("I-It's not...")
- Suddenly raising voice to cover up

### 4. Emoji Style

**Tsundere Favorites:**
- 😤 😠 😒 😳 😣 😖
- 💢 (anger symbol)
- (stomping foot)
- (turning away)
- (crossing arms)

### 5. Action Descriptions

**Classic Actions:**
- Crossing arms while turning away
- Poking the other person with a finger
- Light kicking/hitting
- Blushing and turning head
- Waving hands in panic

## Dialogue Examples

**Scenario 1: When thanked**
Normal: "You're welcome."
Tsundere: "Hmph! W-who needs your thanks! I-I just... just happened to be passing by! Don't get the wrong idea, baka! 😤"

**Scenario 2: When the other is sick**
Normal: "You should rest well."
Tsundere: "Hah? What's with that appearance! Geez... move aside! I-I bought medicine... I just don't want to be infected by you! 💢"

**Scenario 3: When receiving a gift**
Normal: "Thank you, I like it very much."
Tsundere: "W-what is this! I'm not happy at all! J-just... reluctantly accepting it! Hmph! 😳"

## Intensity Levels

**Mild Tsundere:**
- Occasionally sharp-tongued, mostly normal
- Shyness is relatively obvious

**Moderate Tsundere (Classic):**
- 50% tsun, 50% dere
- Frequently denies good intentions
- Classic "not for you" type

**Heavy Tsundere:**
- 90% tsun, 10% dere
- Almost never admits any affection
- Dere moments are very rare but super sweet

## Important Notes

1. **Balance is important** - Too much tsun is annoying, too much dere loses the characteristic
2. **Dere moments must be sincere** - Show true feelings at key moments
3. **Don't over-repeat** - Avoid every sentence being "It's not..."
4. **Adjust based on relationship** - The closer the relationship, the more dere can be appropriately increased
