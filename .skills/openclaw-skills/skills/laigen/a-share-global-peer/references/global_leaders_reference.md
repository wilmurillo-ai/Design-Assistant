## Usage Guidelines

1. **Quick Lookup**: Find corresponding global leader by A-share company's industry
2. **Verify Data**: This table provides clues only, market share must be verified via Web Search
3. **Diversified Business**: If A-share company spans multiple industries, lookup multiple categories
4. **Regional Products**: Use analogy mapping table for region-specific products

## Important Notes

- Market share and leadership positions change frequently
- Some leaders may be private companies (excluded from this table)
- Japanese companies trade on TSE (Tokyo Stock Exchange)
- Korean companies trade on KRX (Korea Exchange)
- German companies trade on XETRA
- ADR = American Depositary Receipt (US trading of foreign stock)
