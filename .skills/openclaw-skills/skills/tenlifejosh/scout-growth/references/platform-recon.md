# Platform Recon — Reference Guide

Researching new platforms, marketplace analysis, and distribution channel discovery.
How to evaluate whether a new channel is worth our time and effort.

---

## 1. PLATFORM EVALUATION FRAMEWORK

### The Five Platform Questions
Before investing time in any new platform:

```
Q1: AUDIENCE FIT
  Does this platform host our target audience?
  Evidence: demographic data, content categories, community discussions

Q2: PURCHASE READINESS
  Do users on this platform buy digital products?
  Evidence: competitor presence + sales signals on this platform

Q3: DISCOVERABILITY
  Can new products be found organically (not just to existing followers)?
  Evidence: search/discovery features, SEO ability, algorithmic feeds

Q4: ECONOMICS
  What % does the platform take? What are the limits?
  Research: fee structure, withdrawal options, payout timing

Q5: EFFORT REQUIRED
  What does it take to maintain a presence here?
  Research: listing requirements, customer service expectations, policies
```

## 2. KEY PLATFORMS FOR DIGITAL PRODUCTS

### Platform Comparison Matrix
```
GUMROAD:
  Category: Digital products (PDF, courses, memberships)
  Fee: 10% + payment processing (~13% total)
  Discovery: Limited organic; relies on external traffic
  Audience: Creators, entrepreneurs, broad
  Minimum effort: Low
  Best for: Quick launch, direct audience sales

AMAZON KDP:
  Category: Print books, eBooks
  Fee: 35-70% royalty (higher for $2.99-$9.99 ebooks)
  Discovery: Excellent organic search, category rankings
  Audience: Massive, general
  Minimum effort: Medium (formatting requirements)
  Best for: Books that benefit from Amazon search

ETSY:
  Category: Digital downloads, printables, planners
  Fee: 6.5% transaction + $0.20 listing + payment processing
  Discovery: Good organic search within platform
  Audience: Crafters, planners, home/family focused
  Minimum effort: Medium (keyword optimization)
  Best for: Printables, planners, journals (our sweet spot)

PAYHIP:
  Category: Digital products (like Gumroad)
  Fee: 5% (or $0 on paid plans)
  Discovery: Less than Gumroad
  Audience: Smaller but growing
  Minimum effort: Low
  Best for: Lower fees when driving own traffic

TEACHABLE / KAJABI:
  Category: Online courses, memberships
  Fee: $X/month + transaction fees
  Discovery: None (need own traffic)
  Audience: Course buyers
  Minimum effort: High
  Best for: Course-based products (not our primary model)
```

## 3. PLATFORM RECON CHECKLIST

### Before Entering Any New Platform
```
RESEARCH STEPS:
  [ ] Find 3-5 competitors already on this platform
  [ ] Check their sales signals (reviews, sales badges, engagement)
  [ ] Search for our product type - does it have a category?
  [ ] Read the platform's seller terms of service (focus: fees, restrictions, data ownership)
  [ ] Check payout options and minimum withdrawal amounts
  [ ] Look for any community/forum where sellers discuss the platform
  [ ] Check Reddit: "is [platform] worth it" "selling on [platform] experience"

PLATFORM VERDICT TEMPLATE:
  Platform: [Name]
  Audience fit: [High/Medium/Low] — [Evidence]
  Purchase readiness: [High/Medium/Low] — [Evidence]
  Discovery potential: [High/Medium/Low] — [Evidence]
  Fee structure: [Summary]
  Effort required: [Low/Medium/High]
  VERDICT: [Test now / Test when bandwidth allows / Skip]
  Rationale: [One sentence]
```

## 4. ETSY OPPORTUNITY ANALYSIS

### Why Etsy Is Particularly Relevant for TLC
```
TLC products that fit Etsy's audience:
  Budget Binder → Budget tracker printables (HUGE category on Etsy)
  Pray Deeper → Prayer journal printables (active faith community)
  Legacy Letters → Journal printables category
  Planner/workbook pages → Planner community is massive on Etsy

ETSY MARKET SIGNALS:
  Search: "budget printable" on Etsy → Look at "Best Match" results
  Check: How many reviews do top sellers have? (sales velocity signal)
  Check: Is there a "Digital Download" badge? (confirms digital product norms)
  Look for: "1000+ sales" badges on similar products (validates demand)

ETSY SEO:
  Different from Google SEO
  Tags are critical (13 tags, all relevant)
  Title should contain all main keywords
  First 40 characters = search result preview
  Etsy favors newer listings briefly — plan launch timing
```
