# 🪴 Care Card: Fiddle Leaf Fig (*Ficus lyrata*)

**💧 Watering Frequency:**
Every 7-10 days. Allow the top 2 inches of soil to dry out completely. (In winter, stretch this to 10-14 days).

**☀️ Light Requirements:**
Bright, indirect to direct light. They love sunshine! Rotate the pot a quarter turn every month so it grows straight.

**🌡️ Environment:**
- **Humidity:** Prefers 40-60%. 
- **Temperature:** 65°F - 85°F. Keep away from cold drafts and AC vents!

**🌱 Fertilizing Schedule:**
Feed once a month during the growing season (Spring and Summer) with a balanced liquid houseplant fertilizer. Pause in Fall and Winter.

**🩺 Common Issues:**
- **Dropping leaves:** Usually due to a sudden change in environment or cold drafts.
- **Brown spots in center of leaf:** Root rot from overwatering.
- **Red spots on new leaves:** Edema (inconsistent watering), usually harmless and fades over time.

**✂️ Propagation Method:**
Stem cuttings. Cut a 4-6 inch piece of stem with at least 2 leaves just below a node. Place in a jar of water in bright indirect light. Roots should appear in 3-4 weeks!
