# Example: Quiz Mode Session

This shows how Tutor Buddy Pro runs an adaptive quiz with scoring and feedback.

---

## The Interaction

**Student:**
> Quiz me on algebra — linear equations

**Tutor Buddy Pro:**
> 🎯 **Quiz Time: Linear Equations**
> 10 questions, adaptive difficulty. Let's see what you've got!
>
> ---
>
> **Question 1/10** (Difficulty: Intermediate)
>
> Solve for x: `5x - 3 = 22`
>
> What's your answer?

**Student:**
> x = 5

**Tutor Buddy Pro:**
> ✅ **Correct!** `5(5) - 3 = 25 - 3 = 22` ✓
>
> ---
>
> **Question 2/10** (Difficulty: Intermediate)
>
> Which of the following is equivalent to `3(x - 4) = 15`?
>
> A) `3x - 4 = 15`
> B) `3x - 12 = 15`
> C) `3x + 12 = 15`
> D) `x - 4 = 5`

**Student:**
> B

**Tutor Buddy Pro:**
> ✅ **Correct!** Distribute the 3: `3 × x = 3x` and `3 × (-4) = -12`, so `3x - 12 = 15`. Nice work!
>
> (Fun fact: D is also technically equivalent if you divide both sides by 3 first — good instinct if you spotted that!)
>
> ---
>
> **Question 3/10** (Difficulty: ⬆️ Advanced — you're on a streak!)
>
> Solve for x: `2(3x + 1) - 4 = 3x + 7`
>
> Show your work!

**Student:**
> 2(3x+1) - 4 = 3x + 7
> 6x + 2 - 4 = 3x + 7
> 6x - 2 = 3x + 7
> 3x = 9
> x = 3

**Tutor Buddy Pro:**
> 🎯 **Perfect!** Every step is clean and correct. I love that you showed the distributive step first, then combined like terms before moving variables. Textbook execution.
>

## What to Notice

1. **Adaptive difficulty** — difficulty increased after 2 correct answers in a row.
2. **Mixed question types** — solve, multiple choice, show-your-work.
3. **Encouragement throughout** — specific praise, not generic "good job."
4. **Detailed results** — not just a score, but specific strong/weak areas.
5. **Proficiency update** — the student sees their score improving.
6. **Next step offered** — keeps momentum going.
