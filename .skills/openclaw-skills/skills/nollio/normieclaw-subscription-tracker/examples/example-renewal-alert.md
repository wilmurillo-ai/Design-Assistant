**Agent:**

Good catch — $59.99/month is a big one. Let's get you out of it.

🚪 **HOW TO CANCEL: Adobe Creative Cloud — $59.99/mo**

1. Go to: https://account.adobe.com/plans
2. Click "Manage Plan" next to Creative Cloud
3. Click "Cancel Plan"
4. Choose a reason (pick whatever)
5. Adobe will show you retention offers — decline to continue
6. Confirm cancellation

⚠️ **Important gotcha:** Check your plan type first.
- **Month-to-month plan:** Cancels at end of current period. No fee.
- **Annual plan (paid monthly):** Has an **early termination fee of 50% of remaining months**. If you're 6 months into a 12-month plan, that's ~$180 in fees.

To check: look at your plan details on the Adobe account page. If it says "Annual plan, paid monthly" and you're mid-contract, it might be worth riding it out to the renewal date and cancelling then.

**Before cancelling:**
- Download any files from Creative Cloud storage
- Export any Adobe Fonts you're using in active projects
- Save any Lightroom presets/catalogs locally

💰 Cancelling saves you: **$59.99/month ($719.88/year)** — your biggest single subscription.
