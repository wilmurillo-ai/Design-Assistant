# Example: Overlap Detection

> Agent finds duplicate services costing the user money.

---

## Scenario

Marcus has been tracking subscriptions for 2 months. He just scanned his Amex statement and his agent merged the findings with his existing database. The agent runs overlap detection.

---

## Conversation

**Agent:**

I've merged your Amex Gold statement with your existing subscriptions from Chase. Here's your complete picture:

📊 **Full Subscription Dashboard — All Accounts**

**Monthly total: $247.91 across 14 subscriptions**
**Annual projected: $2,974.92**

I ran an overlap check and found 3 issues worth looking at:

---

⚠️ **OVERLAP #1: Streaming Video — $55.96/month ($671.52/year)**

You have 4 video streaming services:

| Service | Cost | Card |
|---------|------|------|
| Netflix (Standard) | $17.99/mo | Chase Visa |
| Hulu (No Ads) | $17.99/mo | Amex Gold |
| Disney+ | $9.99/mo | Amex Gold |
| Peacock Premium | $9.99/mo | Chase Visa |

💡 **Suggestions:**
- **Disney Bundle** (Disney+, Hulu, ESPN+) is $16.99/mo — saves $10.99/mo vs. separate Disney+ and Hulu
- **Rotate strategy:** Watch one service per month, switch next month. Most content stays.
- Netflix and Hulu have the most content overlap for drama/comedy
- Peacock is the most expendable unless you watch NBC sports or specific Peacock originals

If you dropped Peacock and switched to the Disney Bundle: **saves $20.98/month ($251.76/year)**

---

⚠️ **OVERLAP #2: Cloud Storage — $12.99/month ($155.88/year)**

You're paying for storage in 3 places:

| Service | Storage | Cost | Card |
|---------|---------|------|------|
| iCloud+ | 200GB | $2.99/mo | Chase Visa |
| Google One | 100GB | $2.99/mo | Amex Gold |
| Dropbox Plus | 2TB | $6.99/mo (annual plan) | Amex Gold |

💡 **Suggestions:**
- If you're an iPhone user, iCloud+ covers photos and device backup. Do you also need Google One?
- Dropbox Plus gives 2TB — that likely covers everything. iCloud's 200GB might be redundant.
- **Consolidate to one:** Pick your primary (Dropbox for cross-platform, iCloud for Apple ecosystem) and cancel the others.
- Dropping Google One + iCloud: **saves $5.98/month ($71.76/year)**
- Dropping Google One alone: **saves $2.99/month ($35.88/year)**

---

⚠️ **OVERLAP #3: AI Tools — $40.00/month ($480.00/year)**

| Service | Cost | Card |
|---------|------|------|
| ChatGPT Plus | $20.00/mo | Chase Visa |
| Claude Pro | $20.00/mo | Amex Gold |

💡 **Note:**
These aren't identical — ChatGPT and Claude have different strengths. But $40/month on AI chat tools is worth evaluating.
- Do you use both regularly? If one covers 90% of your needs, the other might be cuttable.
- Some users alternate months — Claude one month, ChatGPT the next.
- No pressure to cut either if you use both actively. Just flagging the combined spend.

---

📊 **Potential Savings Summary**

| Action | Monthly Savings | Annual Savings |
|--------|----------------|----------------|
| Switch to Disney Bundle + drop Peacock | $20.98 | $251.76 |
| Drop Google One | $2.99 | $35.88 |

None of these are mandatory — your call on each. Want to dig into any of them?

**Marcus:** Wow, the Disney Bundle is a no-brainer. Do that. And yeah, drop Google One — I only use Dropbox and iCloud. Keep both AI tools for now.

2. Click "Cancel membership"
