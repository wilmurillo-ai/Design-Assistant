# Handwriting Example

**Input:** A photo of messy, cursive handwritten meeting notes on a legal pad.

**Output:**
```markdown
# Q3 Strategy Meeting Notes
**Project Alpha Kickoff**

- The launch date is moved to Oct 15th
- Need to align marketing and engineering timelines by next Tuesday
- Key action items:
  1. Ask Sarah for the latest budget numbers (ASAP)
  2. Finalize the UI wireframes with the design team (Due: Friday)
  3. Send an email to the client confirming the revised scope

*Note: Budget numbers are critical. If we don't get them by Wednesday, the whole timeline shifts.*
```

*(Note: Pairs perfectly with NoteTaker Pro!)*
