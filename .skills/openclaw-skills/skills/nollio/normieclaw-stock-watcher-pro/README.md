# Stock Watcher Pro 📈

**Institutional-grade stock intelligence. Built just for you.**

Stop trading on the same surface-level news feed as everyone else. Your OpenClaw agent builds a dedicated, custom intelligence network for *your* portfolio — tracking SEC filings, earnings transcripts, and competitor signals to give you the edge retail investors never get.

🛡️ **Codex Security Verified** — Every NormieClaw skill undergoes a rigorous security audit by OpenAI's Codex before release. Your financial data stays on your machine. No data exfiltration. No phoning home.

## What You Get

- **3x Daily Briefings** — Pre-market, mid-day, and post-market intelligence delivered to your chat. Wake up smarter every trading day.
- **SEC EDGAR Integration** — Automatically monitors and summarizes 10-Ks, 10-Qs, 8-Ks, and insider trading (Form 4) the moment they drop.
- **Thesis Tracking** — Tell the agent *why* you bought a stock. It evaluates every incoming signal against your thesis and tells you if it's accelerating, holding, or weakening.
- **Source Discovery Engine** — Your agent builds a private source network for each ticker: IR pages, industry publications, competitor feeds, and more.
- **Earnings Season Mode** — Heightened monitoring during earnings with pre/post analysis and transcript summaries.
- **Conversational Analysis** — Ask your agent anything about your portfolio in plain English.

## Quick Start (3 Steps)

1. **Install:** Copy the `stock-watcher-pro/` folder into your OpenClaw workspace skills directory.
2. **Setup:** Tell your agent: *"Run the Stock Watcher Pro setup from SETUP-PROMPT.md."*
3. **Add stocks:** *"Add AAPL — 50 shares at $178.50. My thesis: spatial computing is the future growth driver."*

Your first pre-market briefing arrives the next trading morning. ☀️
