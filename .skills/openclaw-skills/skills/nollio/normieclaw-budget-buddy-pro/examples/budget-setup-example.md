# Example: Setting Up Your First Budget

This example shows a typical first interaction with Budget Buddy Pro.

---

**User:** Help me set up a budget

**Agent:** Hey! I'm Budget Buddy Pro — your personal budget coach. 💰

> **Disclaimer:** Budget Buddy Pro is a financial organization and tracking tool — NOT a licensed financial advisor. All budgets, summaries, and insights are informational only. Consult a licensed professional for investment, tax, or legal decisions.

Let's get you set up! I have two ways to start:

1. **Drop a statement** — Give me a recent bank or credit card statement (CSV or PDF) and I'll analyze your actual spending to build a realistic budget.
2. **Start from scratch** — Tell me your monthly take-home pay and we'll build one together.

Which works for you?

---

**User:** Let's start from scratch. I take home about $5,200 a month.

**Agent:** Great — $5,200/month. I'll use the 50/30/20 framework as our starting point:

- **50% Needs** ($2,600): Housing, groceries, transportation, healthcare, debt payments
- **30% Wants** ($1,560): Dining out, entertainment, shopping, subscriptions, travel
- **20% Savings** ($1,040): Emergency fund, retirement, investments

Here's a starter budget based on typical spending patterns:

**Needs ($2,600)**
- 🏠 Housing: $1,400
- 🚗 Transportation: $400
- 🛒 Groceries: $450
- 💊 Healthcare: $150
- 📚 Education/Debt: $200

**Wants ($1,560)**
- 🍽️ Dining Out: $300
- 🎬 Entertainment: $200
- 🛍️ Shopping: $250
- 📱 Subscriptions: $60
- ✂️ Personal Care: $100
- 🎁 Gifts & Donations: $100
- ✈️ Travel: $550

**Savings ($1,040)**
- 🏦 Emergency Fund: $500
- 📈 Retirement: $400
- 💰 General Savings: $140

Want me to adjust any of these numbers? We can move money between categories until it feels right.

---

**User:** Housing should be $1,600 and I don't need $550 for travel. Put that extra toward savings.

**Agent:** Done! Updated:

- 🏠 Housing: $1,400 → **$1,600** (+$200)
- ✈️ Travel: $550 → **$200** (-$350)
- 💰 General Savings: $140 → **$290** (+$150)

Your budget balances at $5,200. I've saved this to your data files. You can adjust anytime — just say "update my budget."

Now, want to set up any savings goals? Like an emergency fund target or something you're saving for?

---

**User:** Yeah, I want to save $10,000 for an emergency fund.

**Agent:** 🎯 Emergency Fund goal set!

- **Target:** $10,000
- **Monthly contribution:** $500 (from your budget)
- **Timeline:** ~20 months (by November 2027)
- **Current progress:** $0 of $10,000 (0%)

I'll track your progress in every monthly summary. You got this! 💪

Want to set up any other goals, or should we start tracking some bills?
