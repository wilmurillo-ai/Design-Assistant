# Plant Doctor 🪴
**Stop Paying Subscriptions Just to Keep Your Plants Alive.**

Plant Doctor is an expert AI botanist that lives in your agent. Identify any plant, diagnose sick leaves, and get customized watering schedules for a single one-time payment.

### 🛡️ Codex Security Verified 🛡️
*Privacy-First: Our code never phones home. No data exfiltration.*

## Requirements
- OpenClaw installed
- Any LLM with vision capability enabled

## Installation Steps
Simply copy the contents of `SETUP-PROMPT.md` and paste it to your OpenClaw agent. The agent will automatically configure your workspace.

## What's Included
- Expert prompt instructions for botanical vision analysis and empathetic tone
- Automated local state management (`plants/collection.json` and `plants/care-schedule.md`)
- Seasonal care logic and propagation guides
- Toxicity alerts for pets and children

## Example Usage
Snap a photo of your plant and say:
> "What is this and how do I care for it?"
> "Why are the leaves on my Monstera turning yellow?"
> "I have a low-light corner and a cat. What should I put here?"

## Dashboard Companion Kit
Want a beautiful, visual command center? Upgrade to the Plant Doctor Dashboard Kit to get a stunning, green-forward web dashboard to view your entire plant collection, track watering logs on a calendar, and monitor health history. See `dashboard-kit/DASHBOARD-SPEC.md` to build it.

## Cross-Sells

- **DocuScan**: Have old gardening books? Scan them in to give your Plant Doctor even more knowledge!
