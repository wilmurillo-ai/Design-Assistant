# Cuisine Categories Reference

Use this file for variety balancing during meal plan generation. Rules:
- Do NOT serve the same cuisine category for dinner on consecutive days.
- Aim for at least 3 different cuisine categories across a week's dinners.
- Anchor to the household's adventurousness — a family with lowest member at 2 should lean heavily on American, Italian, and Mexican. Adventurousness 4+ can explore freely.

---

## American / Comfort
**Example dishes:** Burgers, mac & cheese, meatloaf, grilled chicken, pot roast, BLTs, chicken pot pie, chili, pulled pork sandwiches, sloppy joes
**Key ingredients:** Ground beef, chicken breast, cheddar cheese, potatoes, corn, bread, ranch dressing, BBQ sauce
**Store sections:** Meat, Dairy, Produce, Bakery, Condiments
**Adventurousness:** 1+

## Italian
**Example dishes:** Pasta marinara, chicken parmesan, lasagna, risotto, minestrone, pizza (homemade), caprese salad, pesto pasta, Italian sausage with peppers
**Key ingredients:** Pasta, canned tomatoes, mozzarella, parmesan, basil, garlic, olive oil, Italian sausage
**Store sections:** Pantry (pasta, canned goods), Dairy, Produce, Meat
**Adventurousness:** 1+

## Mexican / Tex-Mex
**Example dishes:** Tacos, burritos, enchiladas, quesadillas, taco bowls, fajitas, chicken tortilla soup, nachos, chile verde, tamales
**Key ingredients:** Tortillas, beans, rice, avocado, salsa, cilantro, lime, cumin, chili powder, sour cream, shredded cheese
**Store sections:** Produce, Pantry, Dairy, Meat
**Adventurousness:** 1+

## Chinese / Chinese-American
**Example dishes:** Stir-fry (chicken, beef, tofu), fried rice, lo mein, orange chicken, kung pao chicken, dumplings, egg drop soup, sweet and sour pork
**Key ingredients:** Soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, rice, rice vinegar, cornstarch, scallions, bok choy, tofu
**Store sections:** Produce, Pantry, Meat, Condiments
**Adventurousness:** 2+

## Japanese
**Example dishes:** Teriyaki chicken/salmon, sushi bowls, ramen, udon, miso soup, katsu (pork/chicken), edamame, gyoza, yakitori
**Key ingredients:** Short-grain rice, soy sauce, mirin, sake, rice vinegar, miso paste, nori, sesame seeds, panko breadcrumbs, tofu
**Store sections:** Pantry, Produce, Meat/Seafood, Condiments
**Adventurousness:** 3+

## Thai
**Example dishes:** Pad thai, green/red/yellow curry, tom kha (coconut soup), larb, Thai basil chicken, mango sticky rice, satay, papaya salad
**Key ingredients:** Coconut milk, curry paste, fish sauce, lime, Thai basil, lemongrass, rice noodles, peanuts, cilantro, chili
**Store sections:** Pantry, Produce, Meat/Seafood, Condiments
**Adventurousness:** 3+

## Indian
**Example dishes:** Chicken tikka masala, butter chicken, dal (lentil curry), chana masala, saag paneer, biryani, naan, samosas, tandoori chicken
**Key ingredients:** Curry powder, garam masala, cumin, turmeric, yogurt, chickpeas, lentils, basmati rice, naan bread, coconut milk, ginger, garlic
**Store sections:** Pantry (spices, legumes), Produce, Dairy, Meat
**Adventurousness:** 3+

## Mediterranean / Greek
**Example dishes:** Gyros, souvlaki, Greek salad, hummus & pita, falafel, shawarma, stuffed grape leaves, moussaka, lemon chicken with orzo
**Key ingredients:** Olive oil, lemon, oregano, feta cheese, chickpeas, pita bread, cucumber, tomato, yogurt, tahini, lamb
**Store sections:** Produce, Dairy, Pantry, Meat, Bakery
**Adventurousness:** 2+

## Korean
**Example dishes:** Bibimbap, bulgogi, kimchi fried rice, japchae, Korean BBQ, tteokbokki, kimchi jjigae, kimbap
**Key ingredients:** Gochujang (red chili paste), gochugaru, soy sauce, sesame oil, rice, kimchi, short ribs, tofu, sweet potato starch noodles
**Store sections:** Pantry, Produce, Meat, Condiments
**Adventurousness:** 3+

## Middle Eastern
**Example dishes:** Shawarma, kebabs, fattoush, tabbouleh, baba ganoush, kibbeh, lamb kofta, shakshuka, mujaddara
**Key ingredients:** Cumin, sumac, za'atar, tahini, chickpeas, lamb, pita, pomegranate, eggplant, parsley, mint, olive oil
**Store sections:** Produce, Pantry (spices), Meat, Bakery
**Adventurousness:** 3+

## Vietnamese
**Example dishes:** Pho, banh mi, spring rolls (fresh or fried), bun cha, lemongrass chicken, vermicelli bowls, com tam (broken rice)
**Key ingredients:** Fish sauce, rice noodles, fresh herbs (mint, cilantro, Thai basil), bean sprouts, lime, hoisin sauce, sriracha, lemongrass
**Store sections:** Produce, Pantry, Meat/Seafood, Condiments
**Adventurousness:** 3+

## Latin American / Caribbean
**Example dishes:** Cuban black beans & rice, empanadas, arepas, ropa vieja, jerk chicken, plantains, ceviche, pupusas, arroz con pollo
**Key ingredients:** Black beans, plantains, yuca, citrus (lime, orange), cumin, cilantro, avocado, jerk seasoning, coconut milk
**Store sections:** Produce, Pantry, Meat, Condiments
**Adventurousness:** 3+

## French (Simplified)
**Example dishes:** Quiche, French onion soup, croque monsieur, ratatouille, chicken Dijon, beef bourguignon, salade niçoise
**Key ingredients:** Butter, Dijon mustard, cream, wine (cooking), shallots, herbs de Provence, gruyère cheese, baguette
**Store sections:** Dairy, Produce, Meat, Bakery, Condiments
**Adventurousness:** 3+

## Soul Food / Southern
**Example dishes:** Fried chicken, collard greens, cornbread, jambalaya, gumbo, shrimp and grits, biscuits and gravy, black-eyed peas, pulled pork
**Key ingredients:** Cornmeal, buttermilk, smoked meats (andouille sausage, ham hocks), okra, collard greens, hot sauce, butter
**Store sections:** Meat, Produce, Pantry, Dairy
**Adventurousness:** 2+
