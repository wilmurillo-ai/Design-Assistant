# Example: "What's in My Fridge?" Photo Mode

This shows the full flow from photo → ingredient identification → profile cross-reference → recipe suggestions.

---

## Household Context
- **Mom** — allergic to tree nuts & shellfish, dislikes mushrooms
- **Tommy** (age 7) — texture issue with "slimy", adventurousness 2
- **Lily** (age 4) — adventurousness 1, dislikes spicy food

---

## The Conversation

**User:** *[Sends a photo of their open fridge]*

**Agent:**

> Let me take a look! 📸
>
> **Here's what I see:**
> - Chicken breast (1 pack, looks like ~1.5 lbs)
> - Bell peppers — red and green
> - Cream cheese (block)
> - Eggs (about half a carton)
> - Cheddar cheese (shredded bag)
> - Tortillas (flour, large)
> - Salsa jar
> - Baby spinach (bag, looks like 5oz)
> - Butter
> - Milk
> - Leftover rice in a container
> - Sour cream
> - Carrots (bag)
>
> Did I miss anything, or get something wrong?

**User:** Spot on! There's also some frozen broccoli in the freezer.

**Agent:**

> Perfect — adding frozen broccoli to the mix! Here are 5 dinner ideas using what you already have, filtered for your household (no tree nuts, no slimy textures, kid-friendly):
>
> ---
>
> **1. 🌯 Chicken Quesadillas** — Match: 95%
> - **Uses:** Chicken breast, cheddar cheese, tortillas, bell peppers, sour cream, salsa
> - **Extra needed:** None!
> - **Time:** 20 min
> - **Family fit:** Tommy and Lily love quesadillas (simple, cheesy, no weird textures). Mom gets hers with peppers and spinach.
>
> **2. 🍳 Cheesy Chicken & Rice Skillet** — Match: 90%
> - **Uses:** Chicken breast, leftover rice, cheddar cheese, broccoli (frozen), bell peppers
> - **Extra needed:** None!
> - **Time:** 25 min
> - **Family fit:** One-pan comfort food. Broccoli goes in cheese sauce so even the kids might eat it.
>
> **3. 🥚 Breakfast-for-Dinner: Scramble & Wraps** — Match: 90%
> - **Uses:** Eggs, cheddar cheese, tortillas, bell peppers, sour cream, salsa
> - **Extra needed:** None!
> - **Time:** 15 min
> - **Family fit:** Kids love breakfast for dinner. Everyone builds their own wrap.
>
> **4. 🍝 Creamy Chicken & Spinach Tortilla Bake** — Match: 85%
> - **Uses:** Chicken breast, tortillas, cream cheese, spinach, cheddar cheese, salsa
> - **Extra needed:** Just garlic (or garlic powder if you have it in the pantry)
> - **Time:** 20 min prep, 20 min bake
> - **Family fit:** The spinach hides in the cheesy sauce. Lily won't even notice it.
>
> **5. 🥗 Chicken Burrito Bowls** — Match: 80%
> - **Uses:** Chicken breast, leftover rice, bell peppers, cheddar cheese, sour cream, salsa, spinach
> - **Extra needed:** Black beans (canned), lime
> - **Time:** 25 min
> - **Family fit:** Customizable — everyone picks their toppings. Tommy can skip anything he doesn't want.
>
> ---
>
> My top pick? **Chicken Quesadillas** — zero extra ingredients and the kids will be thrilled. Want me to give you the full recipe for any of these?
