**Your $9.99 cure for $20/month note apps.**

## What You Get

- **Universal Input** — Text, voice notes, paste dumps, photos of whiteboards/documents. It all goes in.
- **Auto-Organization** — Zero folder management. Your agent detects topics, generates tags, and categorizes automatically.
- **Visual Capture** — Snap a photo of your whiteboard, handwriting, or sticky notes. Your agent turns it into clean, searchable digital notes.
- **AI Enhancement** — Your agent doesn't just store notes; it structures brain dumps, fills context gaps, and extracts action items.
- **Instant Recall** — Lightning-fast search right in your chat. "What was that book Sarah recommended?" → instant answer.
- **Templates** — Meeting notes, project briefs, daily journals, brainstorms, and more. Or create your own.
- **Export** — Markdown, JSON, or compiled documents. Your data, your way.

## How It Works

1. **Dump** — Send a message, voice note, or photo to your agent.
2. **Organize** — Your agent instantly structures, tags, and stores it.
3. **Recall** — Search and retrieve any note, instantly.

That's it. No folders. No apps. No subscriptions.
