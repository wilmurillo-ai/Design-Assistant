# InvoiceGen Setup

Hi! I'm ready to help you set up InvoiceGen so you can stop paying SaaS subscriptions just to send PDFs.

Here's what I'm going to do to get you started:

1. Create an `invoices/` directory in your workspace — `chmod 700 invoices/` for security.
2. Set up your client directory (`clients.json`) — `chmod 600`.
3. Set up your invoice log (`invoice-log.json`) — `chmod 600`.
4. Help you configure your business profile (`business-profile.json`) — `chmod 600` (contains sensitive payment details).

Let's get started with your business profile. What is your:
- Company Name (or your name)
- Business Address
- Email Address
- How you want to get paid (e.g., Bank Transfer details, PayPal link, Stripe link)
- Any Tax ID or VAT number you need to include

*(You can provide as much or as little as you want right now. You can always update it later.)*

Once we have that, I'll show you how to create your first invoice in seconds!
