# Expense Report Pro

Stop paying $20/month for expense tracking. Turn your OpenClaw agent into a world-class expense tracker that lives right in your chat. Snap a photo of a receipt, and your agent instantly categorizes it, calculates the tip, handles multi-currency conversions, and formats it for a professional PDF report. 

Free and open-source. Zero subscriptions. 100% private.

## Features

- **Instant AI Receipt Scanning:** No manual data entry. Blurry photo? Crumpled receipt? The AI reads it perfectly, extracting vendor, date, subtotal, tax, tip, and total.
- **Multi-Currency Processing:** Detects foreign currencies and logs them correctly.
- **Employer-Ready PDF Reports:** Generate clean, formatted, IRS-compliant expense reports with one command.
- **Chat-Based Simplicity:** No clunky apps to download. Text or DM your agent the receipt in Telegram, Discord, Slack, etc.
- **Bank-Level Security:** Your financial data is privacy-first. Everything stays on your machine.
- **Bulk Processing:** Drop 20 receipts at once, and watch them get processed in parallel.
- **Natural Language Queries:** "How much did I spend on meals last month?"

## Installation

1. Copy the contents of this package to your `skills/expense-report-pro/` directory.
2. Ask your OpenClaw agent to `Read the SETUP-PROMPT.md file in the expense-report-pro skill and follow the instructions.`
3. You're ready to go!
