## 🎮 Games & Activities

**Baby Bingo (during gift opening):**
- Custom bingo cards with baby items (pacifier, onesie, diapers, etc.)
- Guests mark items as Emma opens them
- First bingo wins a $25 Target gift card
- Supplies: printed cards, markers

**Baby Word Scramble:**
- 15 scrambled baby-related words (TLETOB → BOTTLE, REPPID → DIPPER)
- 3-minute timer, most correct wins
- Prize: candle set

**Guess the Baby Food:**
- 8 jars of baby food with labels removed, numbered
- Guests taste and guess the flavor
- Most correct wins
- Supplies: baby food jars, spoons, napkins, answer sheet

**Wishes for Baby:**
- Blank cards at each table
- Guests write wishes/advice for the baby
- Collected in a keepsake box for Emma

**Diaper Raffle:**
- Every guest who brings a pack of diapers gets entered
- Drawing for a $25 Starbucks gift card
