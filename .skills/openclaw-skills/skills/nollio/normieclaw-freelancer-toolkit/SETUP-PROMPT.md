# Freelancer Toolkit — Setup Prompt

Welcome to Freelancer Toolkit. Let's get your freelance business configured in about 2 minutes.

I'll walk you through the essentials. You can always change these later in `~/.freelancer-toolkit/settings.json`.

---

## Step 1: Your Default Rate

**What's your standard hourly rate?** This is the default for new clients. Per-client rates override it.

> Example: "$85" or "75 per hour". Default: $75/hr.

---

## Step 2: Currency

**What currency do you bill in?** Default: USD ($). Say "EUR", "GBP", "CAD", etc. to change.

---

## Step 3: Your First Client

**Tell me about your main client:**

> Example: "Sarah Chen at Nova Labs, $95/hr, net 30 payment terms"

Minimum: client/company name + hourly rate (or "use default").
Optional: email, payment terms (net 15/30/45/due on receipt), notes.

---

## Step 4: Your First Project

**What are you working on for that client?**

> Example: "Website redesign, quoted $5,000, about 60 hours"

I need: project name + billing type ("fixed" or "hourly").
For fixed-price: quoted price and estimated hours.

---

## Step 5: Work Categories

Default categories: design, development, consulting, meetings, admin, writing, research.
Non-billable defaults: admin, internal.

> Want to customize? List yours. Otherwise say "defaults are fine."

---

## Step 6: Preferences

- **Week starts on:** Monday (default) or Sunday?
- **Billing period:** Monthly (default), biweekly, or weekly?
- **Time rounding:** Nearest 15 min (default), nearest 6 min, or none?
- **Weekly summary:** Every Monday? (recommended)

---

## Step 7: InvoiceGen Pro

**Have InvoiceGen Pro installed?** If yes, invoice data flows directly to it for PDF generation. If no, invoices export as JSON. Install anytime from normieclaw.ai.

---

## Done!

Your Freelancer Toolkit is ready. Here's what you can do now:

- **Log time:** "I worked 3 hours on [project] today"
- **Start a timer:** "Start tracking [client/project]"
- **Add clients:** "New client: [name], [company], [rate]"
- **Check status:** "Show my active projects"
- **Get your summary:** "How did this week look?"

The more you use it, the smarter it gets about your clients, projects, and billing patterns.

Track time by talking. Invoice by clicking. That's it.
