# Tutor Buddy Pro 🎓

**A dedicated AI tutor in your pocket. Replace $200/year subscriptions for just $9.99.**

Snap a photo of any homework problem — from middle school algebra to college calculus — and get step-by-step, interactive explanations. It doesn't just give you the answer. It teaches you how to find it.

---

## What You Get

- **📸 Photo-to-Solution Magic** — Point, shoot, learn. Works with handwritten problems, textbook pages, and screenshots.
- **🧠 Socratic Method Mode** — Guides you to the answer with hints and questions instead of just handing it over. Actually builds understanding.
- **📝 Study Plan Generator** — Tell it your exam date, and it builds a day-by-day study plan with spaced repetition and practice problems.
- **🎯 Quiz Mode** — Adaptive quizzes that get harder as you improve and easier when you're struggling. Tracks your scores over time.
- **📊 Progress Tracking** — See how you're improving across subjects and topics. Streaks, proficiency scores, weak-area identification.
- **🎨 Learning Style Adaptation** — Visual learner? Hands-on? The tutor adjusts its teaching approach to match how you learn best.
- **📚 All Subjects** — Math (Algebra through Calculus), Science, History, English, and more.

## How It Works

1. **Snap** — Take a picture of your homework problem (or type it out).
2. **Learn** — Watch as Tutor Buddy Pro breaks it down step by step.
3. **Chat** — Ask follow-up questions. "I don't get step 3" and it'll explain it a different way.

## Setup

1. Copy the `tutor-buddy-pro/` folder into your agent's skills directory.
2. Paste the contents of `SETUP-PROMPT.md` to your agent.
3. Done. Start learning.

**Time to first use: under 60 seconds.**

## Is This Cheating?

No. Tutor Buddy Pro is designed to be a *teacher*, not an answer machine. It explains the *why* and *how* rather than just giving you a number to copy down. The Socratic method forces you to think through each step. Your test scores will thank you.
