# Example: Workout Generation from Gym Photo

## Scenario
User is at a hotel gym and sends a photo of the equipment.

---

**User:** *[Sends photo of a hotel gym with dumbbells up to 50 lbs, an adjustable bench, and a cable machine]*

**Agent:** I see: dumbbells (up to 50 lbs), an adjustable bench, and a cable machine with a few attachments. Did I miss anything?

**User:** Nope, that's it. Give me a workout — I want to hit upper body.

**Agent:** Got it! Since you hit legs yesterday, here's an upper body session with what you've got:

## Upper Body Hypertrophy — March 8, 2026
**Goal:** Build muscle
**Estimated Duration:** 50 minutes
**Equipment Needed:** Dumbbells (up to 50 lbs), adjustable bench, cable machine

### 🔥 Warm-Up (5 min)
- Arm circles — 15 each direction
- Band pull-aparts (or light cable face pulls) — 2 × 15
- Light incline DB press — 1 × 12 @ 20 lbs

### 💪 Working Sets

**1. Incline DB Bench Press** — Compound
   Sets × Reps: 4 × 8-10
   Weight Target: 40 lbs each (↑ from 35 last session)
   Rest: 90 sec
   Form Cue: Keep your elbows at a 45° angle — don't flare them out to 90°.

**2. Cable Row (Seated or Standing)** — Compound
   Sets × Reps: 4 × 10-12
   Weight Target: Stack at 6 (same as last — aim for 12 reps this time)
   Rest: 90 sec
   Form Cue: Squeeze your shoulder blades together at the end of each rep like you're crushing a pencil between them.

**3. DB Lateral Raises** — Accessory
   Sets × Reps: 3 × 12-15
   Weight Target: 15 lbs
   Rest: 60 sec
   Form Cue: Lead with your elbows, not your hands — think "pouring a pitcher of water."
   ⚡ Superset with → Cable Face Pulls

**4. Cable Face Pulls** — Accessory
   Sets × Reps: 3 × 15
   Weight Target: Stack at 4
   Rest: 60 sec (after both exercises)
   Form Cue: Pull to your forehead, not your chest — external rotate at the end.

**5. DB Skullcrushers** — Isolation
   Sets × Reps: 3 × 10-12
   Weight Target: 20 lbs each
   Rest: 60 sec
   Form Cue: Only your forearms move — keep your upper arms locked in place pointing at the ceiling.

**6. DB Hammer Curls** — Isolation
   Sets × Reps: 3 × 10-12
   Weight Target: 25 lbs each
   Rest: 60 sec
   Form Cue: No swinging — if you're rocking your body, go lighter.

### 🧊 Cool-Down (5 min)
- Doorway chest stretch — 30 sec each side
- Overhead tricep stretch — 30 sec each arm
- Cross-body shoulder stretch — 30 sec each side

### 📝 Notes
- Last session you did 35 lb incline press for 10 reps — bumping to 40 lbs today. If you can't hit 8, drop back to 35 and go for 12.
- Great job hitting the hotel gym! Consistency > perfection. 💪

**User:** Perfect. Let me get after it. 🔥
