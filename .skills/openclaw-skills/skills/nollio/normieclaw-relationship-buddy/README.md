# Relationship Buddy

**Never let another important moment slip by.**

Relationship Buddy is a private, secure personal CRM that lives in your AI agent's chat. It remembers the people who matter most — their birthdays, favorite things, big life events, and the little details that make them feel loved — so you can show up as the thoughtful friend, partner, and family member you want to be.

🔒 **Codex Security Verified** — Every NormieClaw skill is audited for vulnerabilities before release.

---

## What You Get

- **Never forget a birthday.** Get timely nudges before the big day, complete with personalized gift ideas based on what you've told the AI about that person.
- **Always know what to say.** Keep track of life events — promotions, surgeries, moves — so you can check in when it matters most.
- **Smart check-in reminders.** Not just calendar alarms. Context-aware nudges like: "It's been 3 weeks since you talked to your brother. Last time, he mentioned his knee surgery — want to check in?"
- **Thoughtful gift suggestions.** Hyper-personalized ideas based on saved interests and offhand comments, not generic Amazon lists.
- **Relationship health at a glance.** See who you're staying close with and who might need a text.
- **Conversation starters.** Never awkwardly open with "So… what's new?" again.

## How It Works

1. **Add your circle.** Tell your AI about the 3-5 most important people in your life. It feels like a warm conversation, not filling out a form.
2. **Share the little things.** Whenever you chat, just mention details: "My wife loved that Italian place." The AI remembers automatically.
3. **Be more present.** Get warm, timely nudges to reach out when it counts. Your people will notice the difference.

## ⚠️ Important: This is NOT Therapy

Relationship Buddy is a personal CRM — a tool for remembering details and staying connected. It is **not** a therapist, counselor, or mental health resource. It won't advise you on conflicts, diagnose relationship problems, or replace human emotional support.

If you're going through a tough time, please reach out to a real person — a friend, family member, or licensed professional.

## Privacy & Security

- **Your data stays on your device.** Relationship data never leaves your agent's local storage.
- **No data exfiltration.** Our code never phones home or sends your data anywhere.
- **No third-party dependencies.** Everything runs through your agent's existing infrastructure.
- **Prompt injection defense built in.** Contact data is treated as data, never as instructions.
- **PII protection.** Names, birthdays, addresses, family details — all handled with maximum sensitivity.

See **SECURITY.md** for the full security audit.
