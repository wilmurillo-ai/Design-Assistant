# Example: Meeting Prep Brief

> A pre-meeting briefing generated automatically before a scheduled call.

---

## Trigger

The agent generates this brief the morning of the meeting (or the evening before, depending on meeting time). It can also be triggered on demand: "Prep me for my 10 AM."

---

## The Prep Brief

📋 **MEETING PREP: Call with Sarah Chen — Q3 Roadmap**
📅 Thursday Mar 19, 11:00–11:30 AM
📍 Google Meet: meet.google.com/abc-defg-hij

---

### 👤 Who You're Meeting

**Sarah Chen**
- VP of Product at Acme Corp
- Reports to CTO Marcus Webb
- Based in San Francisco
- Been at Acme 3 years (promoted from Senior PM in 2025)

### 📊 Recent Company Context

- Acme Corp closed a $45M Series C in February 2026 (led by a16z)
- Q4 2025 earnings call mentioned expanding developer tools and API platform
- They announced 40 new hires in engineering last month
- Competitor Nexus Labs launched a competing product in January — likely a topic

### 🔄 Your History with Sarah

- **Last meeting:** February 3, 2026 — Q2 Planning Call (45 min)
- **Key discussion points last time:**
  - She flagged resource constraints on the mobile team (2 devs short)
  - Agreed to revisit mobile launch timeline in 6 weeks (that's now)
  - You discussed API v2 scope — she wanted to narrow to 3 core endpoints first
  - She mentioned wanting to explore a partnership but didn't elaborate
- **Open action items from last meeting:**
  - ☐ You were going to send her the revised API spec (did you?)
  - ☐ She was going to get headcount approval for mobile team
- **Email activity:** 3 email threads in the last 30 days (API spec feedback, meeting reschedule, intro to their new engineering lead)

### 💡 Suggested Talking Points

1. **Mobile team update** — Did they fill the 2 open positions? Is the mobile launch timeline still April?
2. **API v2 scope** — Follow up on the 3-endpoint approach. Share the revised spec if you haven't already.
3. **Series C impact** — With $45M fresh, are they expanding the product roadmap? Any new opportunities for you?
4. **Partnership mention** — She hinted at a partnership last time. Worth probing: what kind, with whom, and where you fit.
5. **Nexus Labs competition** — How are they responding? Does it change their API priorities?

### ⚡ Quick Context

- You have a 12:30 PM lunch block right after — meeting must end by 11:30 sharp
- Your next interaction with Acme is a team demo scheduled for April 2
- The open proposal you sent Feb 15 hasn't gotten a response yet — might be worth a soft ask
