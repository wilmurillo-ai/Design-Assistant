# QSR Audit Readiness Countdown

**QSR Audit Readiness Countdown** is a 30-day audit preparation skill for restaurant and franchise operators.

It is designed for:
- EcoSure audits
- local health department inspections
- corporate brand audits
- franchise compliance reviews

Instead of treating audit prep like a last-minute scramble, this skill guides operators through a milestone-based countdown that spreads the work across 30 days. The goal is simple: make audit readiness the normal state of operations.

## What It Does

This skill acts like an audit preparation coach.

It helps operators:
- run a full baseline walkthrough 30 days out
- communicate standards and expectations to the team
- activate daily audit-level execution
- run a full mock audit one week out
- complete a final sweep before the audit window opens
- review results after the audit and track patterns over time

## Milestone Structure

The countdown is organized into five checkpoints:

- **Day -30** — Full store baseline review
- **Day -21** — Team communication and training
- **Day -14** — Daily compliance activation
- **Day -7** — Full mock audit
- **Day -1** — Final sweep

If a milestone is incomplete, open issues are carried forward to the next checkpoint.

## Why It Matters

Most stores only react when an audit is close. That usually means rushed cleaning, retraining under pressure, and avoidable misses.

This skill turns audit prep into a controlled operating process:
- earlier issue detection
- better team readiness
- fewer repeated findings
- less panic before the audit
- stronger consistency across shifts

## Best Fit

This skill is built for:
- general managers
- assistant managers
- shift leads supporting audit prep
- franchise operators
- single-location and multi-location QSR teams

## How It Works

On first run, the skill asks:
1. What type of audit is coming
2. When the audit window opens
3. Whether the operator has the checklist or criteria
4. Who owns audit prep
5. What happened on the last audit

Then it begins the 30-day countdown and generates milestone-based outputs, findings, corrective actions, and readiness checks.
