## 🏛️ Lijiang Ancient Town History Overview

### L1 Basic
> "Lijiang Ancient Town 1997 listed as World Cultural Heritage. Ancient town has 800+ years history, is Naxi ethnic settlement."

### L2 Standard
> "Lijiang Ancient Town is located at Yunnan Province Lijiang City, first built Song end Yuan early,距今 800+ years history.
>
> Lijiang Ancient Town is Naxi ethnic settlement, altitude 2,416 meters. Ancient town has Three Rooms One Screen Wall, Four Courtyards Five Skywells etc Naxi buildings.
>
> 1997, Lijiang Ancient Town listed as World Cultural Heritage."

### L3 Deep Dive
> "**History**:
> - Song end Yuan early: Lijiang Ancient Town first built
> - Ming Dynasty: Mu Tusi ruled
> - Qing Dynasty: Replacing Tusi with appointed officials
> - 1997: Listed as World Heritage
>
> "**Naxi Culture**:
> Lijiang Ancient Town is Naxi ethnic settlement:
> - Writing: Dongba script (world's only living pictographic writing)
> - Religion: Dongba religion
> - Buildings: Three Rooms One Screen Wall, Four Courtyards Five Skywells
> - Music: Naxi ancient music
>
> "**Building Features**:
> Lijiang Ancient Town building features:
> - Layout: Against mountain near water
> - Materials: Earth wood structure
> - Decoration: Dongba culture
> - Water system: Jade River water system, every family water
>
> "**Trivia**:
> - History: 800+ years
> - Altitude: 2,416 meters
> - Ethnic: Naxi
> - World Heritage: 1997"
