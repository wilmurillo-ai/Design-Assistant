# Prince Gong's Mansion Culture Stories (English)

> **Last Updated**: 2026-03

## History Overview

**Construction History**:
- 1776: Heshen built mansion
- 1799: Heshen convicted, mansion confiscated
- 1851: Given to Prince Gong Yixin
- 1988: Opened to public

**Scale**:
- Area: 60,000 square meters
- Building area: 20,000 square meters
- Rooms: 108

**Fu Character Stele**:
Kangxi imperial brush Fu character, hidden in rockery, meaning fortune arrives.

**Trivia**:
- Heshen lived here 23 years
- Prince Gong lived here 40 years
- Praised as "Half of Qing Dynasty History"
