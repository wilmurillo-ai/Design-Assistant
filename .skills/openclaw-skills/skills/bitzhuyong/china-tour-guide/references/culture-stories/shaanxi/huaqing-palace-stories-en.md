# Huaqing Palace Culture Stories (English)

> **Last Updated**: 2026-03

## History Overview

**Construction History**:
- Zhou Dynasty: First built as detached palace
- Qin Dynasty: Built Lishan Tang
- Tang Dynasty: Tang Xuanzong expanded, renamed Huaqing Palace
- 1936: Xi'an Incident location

**Yang Guifei Story**:
Tang Xuanzong and Yang Guifei bathed and played here. Bai Juyi's "Song of Everlasting Regret" "Spring cold bestowed bath at Huaqing Pool, warm spring water smooth washing creamy skin" refers to this place.

**Xi'an Incident**:
December 12, 1936, Zhang Xueliang and Yang Hucheng launched Xi'an Incident at Wujian Ting.

**Trivia**:
- Hot spring history: 3,000+ years
- Hot spring temperature: 43℃
- Water quality: Drinkable and bathable
- World Heritage: 2014 listed as Silk Road World Heritage
