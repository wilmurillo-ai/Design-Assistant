## 🏛️ Hall of Prayer for Good Harvests

### L1 Basic
> "Hall of Prayer for Good Harvests is the landmark of Temple of Heaven, 38 meters high, circular triple-eaved blue-tile hall. Emperors prayed for good harvests here."

### L2 Standard
> "Built in 1420 during Ming Dynasty, 38 meters high, 32.72 meters diameter. 28 pillars inside symbolize 28 constellations.
>
> Every first lunar month, emperor held prayer ceremony here for good weather and harvest. It's the peak of ancient Chinese architecture."

### L3 Deep Dive
> "**Construction History**:
> Built in 1420, originally named Great Sacrifice Hall. Renamed Great Enjoyment Hall in 1545. Destroyed by lightning in 1889, rebuilt in 1896.
>
> "**Architecture**:
> 38m high, 32.72m diameter, circular triple eaves. All wooden structure, no iron nails. 28 pillars symbolize 28 constellations, central 4 symbolize four seasons.
>
> "**Trivia**:
> - Height: 38 meters
> - Pillars: 28
> - No nails: All mortise-tenon joints
> - Restoration: Major repair in 1970s"

## 🏛️ Circular Mound Altar

### L1 Basic
> "Circular Mound Altar is where emperors sacrificed to Heaven, circular three-layer stone altar. Standing at center, your voice echoes loudly."

### L2 Standard
> "Built in 1530, where emperors sacrificed to Heaven on winter solstice. 5.17 meters high, three layers, all white marble.
>
> All numbers on altar are 9 or multiples of 9, because 9 is the largest yang number, symbolizing Heaven. Standing at 'Heaven Center Stone,' your voice echoes loudly."

### L3 Deep Dive
> "**Construction History**:
> Built in 1530 by Emperor Jiajing for Heaven sacrifice. Expanded by Qianlong in 1749.
>
> "**Architecture**:
> 5.17m high, top layer 23.65m diameter, middle 38.44m, bottom 54.72m. All numbers are 9 or multiples.
>
> "**Acoustic Miracle**:
> Standing at 'Heaven Center Stone,' your voice echoes loudly. This is sound wave reflection, ancient craftsmen mastered acoustics.
>
> "**Trivia**:
> - Layers: 3
> - Railings: 360 pieces, symbolizing 360 degrees
> - Steps: 9 per layer
> - Heaven Center Stone: Loud echo"

## 🏛️ Echo Wall

### L1 Basic
> "Echo Wall is the circular wall around Imperial Vault of Heaven, 61.5 meters diameter. Two people standing at east and west halls can hear each other whispering along the wall."

### L2 Standard
> "Built in 1530, circular wall, 61.5 meters diameter, 3.72 meters high. Wall is smooth, sound waves travel along it.
>
> Two people at east and west halls can hear each other whispering along the wall, even 50+ meters apart. This is a miracle of ancient Chinese acoustics."

### L3 Deep Dive
> "**Construction History**:
> Echo Wall built in 1530, wall of Imperial Vault of Heaven which stored sacrificial artifacts.
>
> "**Acoustic Principle**:
> Diameter 61.5m, height 3.72m, thickness 0.9m. Wall built with polished bricks, very smooth. Sound waves reflect continuously along wall with minimal attenuation.
>
> "**Historical Value**:
> Echo Wall is 100+ years earlier than earliest Western echo architecture, showing ancient Chinese craftsmen's wisdom.
>
> "**Trivia**:
> - Diameter: 61.5 meters
> - Height: 3.72 meters
> - Circumference: 193.2 meters
> - Best effect: Whisper along wall"
