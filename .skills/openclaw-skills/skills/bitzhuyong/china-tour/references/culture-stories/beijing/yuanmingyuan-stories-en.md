# Old Summer Palace Culture Stories (English)

> **Last Updated**: 2026-03

## History Overview

**Construction History**:
- 1709: Kangxi gave to Yongzheng
- 1722-1795: Qianlong expanded
- 1860: Anglo-French Alliance burned
- 1900: Eight-Nation Alliance destroyed again

**Scale**:
- Area: 350 hectares
- Building area: 160,000 square meters
- Scenic spots: 150+

**Dashuifa**:
Western-style building ruins landmark, originally fountain building complex, now only stone pillars remain.

**Haiyantang**:
Originally twelve zodiac animal head fountain location, now some heads lost overseas.

**Trivia**:
- Old Summer Palace consists of Yuanming/Changchun/Qichun Gardens
- Praised as "Garden of Ten Thousand Gardens"
- 1988: Listed as National Key Cultural Relics Protection Unit
