## 🏛️ Broken Bridge

### L1 Basic
> "Broken Bridge is the landmark of West Lake, famous for 'Melting Snow on Broken Bridge'. In White Snake legend, White Lady and Xu Xian met here, it's a love sanctuary."

### L2 Standard
> "Broken Bridge is located at east end of Bai Causeway, first built in Tang Dynasty, current bridge was rebuilt in 1921. Bridge is 8.8 meters long, 8.6 meters wide, single-arch stone bridge.
>
> 'Melting Snow on Broken Bridge' is one of West Lake's Ten Scenes. After winter snow, bridge snow melts, from distance looks like bridge is broken, hence name 'Broken Bridge'.
>
> In White Snake legend, White Lady and Xu Xian met here borrowing umbrella, achieved eternal love story."

### L3 Deep Dive
> "**Construction History**:
> Broken Bridge first built in Tang Dynasty, originally named Baoyou Bridge. Song Dynasty renamed Broken Bridge. Current bridge rebuilt in 1921, renovated in 2004.
>
> "**Melting Snow Scene**:
> After winter snow, sunny side snow melts first, shady side snow melts later, from distance bridge surface looks broken. This is optical illusion, actual bridge is not broken.
>
> "**White Snake Story**:
> Legend says White Lady (Bai Suzhen) and Xu Xian met at Broken Bridge. White Lady lent umbrella to Xu Xian, two fell in love and married. Later Monk Fahai separated them, pressed White Lady under Leifeng Pagoda.
>
> "**Literary Value**:
> Broken Bridge is object of literati poetry through ages. Bai Juyi, Su Shi all wrote Broken Bridge poems. 'Broken bridge not broken, heart broken' is famous line.
>
> "**Trivia**:
> - Bridge length: 8.8 meters
> - Width: 8.6 meters
> - Rebuild time: 1921
> - White Snake: One of China's four folk legends"

## 🏛️ Bai Causeway

### L1 Basic
> "Bai Causeway is 1 kilometer long, east from Broken Bridge, west to Autumn Moon on Calm Lake. Legend says Bai Juyi built it, actually it's naturally formed sand causeway."

### L2 Standard
> "Bai Causeway is about 1 kilometer long, east from Broken Bridge, west to Autumn Moon on Calm Lake, south borders Outer West Lake, north relies on Inner West Lake.
>
> Legend says Bai Causeway was built by Tang poet Bai Juyi when he was Hangzhou governor, hence name Bai Causeway. But actual Bai Causeway is naturally formed sand causeway, Bai Juyi only dredged West Lake.
>
> Bai Causeway two sides planted peach trees and willow trees, spring 'one willow one peach', scenery beautiful."

### L3 Deep Dive
> "**History**:
> Bai Causeway originally named White Sand Causeway, existed as early as Tang Dynasty. Bai Juyi was Hangzhou governor three years (822-824), dredged West Lake, later generations wrongly transmitted as Bai Juyi built, hence name Bai Causeway.
>
> "**Bai Juyi and West Lake**:
> Bai Juyi was Hangzhou governor three years, dredged West Lake, built causeways, irrigated farmland. When leaving wrote 'Spring Journey on Qiantang Lake': 'Most love lake east walk insufficient, green willow shade inside white sand causeway.'
>
> "**Plant Configuration**:
> Bai Causeway two sides planted peach trees and willow trees, forming 'one willow one peach' landscape. Spring peach blossoms bloom, willow trees sprout, scenery most beautiful.
>
> "**Trivia**:
> - Total length: About 1 kilometer
> - Width: About 10 meters
> - Peach trees: About 200
> - Willow trees: About 200"

## 🏛️ Su Causeway

### L1 Basic
> "Su Causeway is 2.8 kilometers long, Su Shi dredged West Lake using silt to build. Causeway has six bridges, spring willow trees form shade, it's 'Spring Dawn at Su Causeway'."

### L2 Standard
> "Su Causeway is 2.8 kilometers long, south from Nanping Mountain, north to Yue Temple. It was Northern Song Yuanyou period (1089) Su Shi dredged West Lake, using excavated silt to build.
>
> Causeway has six bridges: Yingbo Bridge, Suolan Bridge, Wangshan Bridge, Yadi Bridge, Dongpu Bridge, Kuahong Bridge. Each bridge has beautiful name.
>
> Su Causeway two sides planted willow trees, spring willow catkins fly, it's 'West Lake Ten Scenes' first 'Spring Dawn at Su Causeway'."

### L3 Deep Dive
> "**Construction History**:
> Northern Song Yuanyou fourth year (1089), Su Shi was Hangzhou prefect. West Lake siltation serious, Su Shi organized dredging, using excavated silt to build long causeway, namely Su Causeway.
>
> "**Six Bridges Names**:
> Su Causeway has six bridges, from south to north:
> 1. Yingbo Bridge - Reflecting waves
> 2. Suolan Bridge - Locking waves
> 3. Wangshan Bridge - Viewing mountains
> 4. Yadi Bridge - Pressing causeway
> 5. Dongpu Bridge - Eastern water
> 6. Kuahong Bridge - Crossing rainbow
>
> "**Su Shi and West Lake**:
> Su Shi twice served in Hangzhou, total five years. Dredged West Lake, built Su Causeway, left famous line 'Want compare West Lake to Xi Zi, light makeup heavy makeup always suitable.'
>
> "**Trivia**:
> - Total length: 2.8 kilometers
> - Width: 30-40 meters
> - Six bridges: Each bridge spacing about 400 meters
> - Willow trees: About 1,000"

## 🏛️ Three Pools Mirroring Moon

### L1 Basic
> "Three Pools Mirroring Moon is West Lake's largest island, island south lake has three stone towers. Mid-Autumn moon round time, light candle in tower, moonlight, lamplight, lake light shine together."

### L2 Standard
> "Three Pools Mirroring Moon also called Little Yingzhou, is West Lake's largest island, area 70,000 square meters. Island south lake has three stone towers, 2 meters high, tower body spherical, has five round holes.
>
> Mid-Autumn moon round time, light candle in three stone towers, candlelight comes out from round holes, reflects in lake, with moonlight, lake light shine together, forming 'three moons same shine' spectacle.
>
> Three Pools Mirroring Moon is one yuan RMB background pattern."

### L3 Deep Dive
> "**Construction History**:
> Three Pools Mirroring Moon first built Northern Song Yuanyou period (1089), Su Shi dredged West Lake after, in lake establish three stone towers as markers, preventing siltation.
>
> "**Stone Tower Structure**:
> Three stone towers 2 meters high, tower body spherical, has five round holes. Tower top gourd-shaped, tower base square. Shape beautiful, craft exquisite.
>
> "**Three Pools Mirroring Moon Spectacle**:
> Mid-Autumn moon round time, in three stone towers light candle, candlelight from five round holes comes out, reflects in lake. Sky one moon, water three moons, plus candlelight, total 'three moons same shine'.
>
> "**One Yuan RMB**:
> 1999 edition fifth set RMB one yuan paper money, background pattern is Three Pools Mirroring Moon. This is West Lake only scenic spot on RMB.
>
> "**Trivia**:
> - Island area: 70,000 square meters
> - Stone tower height: 2 meters
> - Round holes: Each tower 5
> - RMB: One yuan paper money background"

## 🏛️ Leifeng Pagoda

### L1 Basic
> "Leifeng Pagoda is located at West Lake south bank Xizhao Mountain. White Snake legend Fahai pressed White Lady under Leifeng Pagoda. Original pagoda 1924 collapsed, 2002 rebuilt."

### L2 Standard
> "Leifeng Pagoda is located at West Lake south bank Xizhao Mountain, first built Northern Song Taiping兴国 second year (977). Wu Yue King Qian Chu for worshipping Buddha hair relic built.
>
> 1924 September 25, Leifeng Pagoda collapsed, shocked nation. 2002 rebuilt, new pagoda 71.68 meters high, five layers eight angles, inside has elevator.
>
> Leifeng Pagoda famous because of White Snake, legend White Lady was pressed under pagoda by Fahai."

### L3 Deep Dive
> "**Construction History**:
> Leifeng Pagoda first built 977, Wu Yue King Qian Chu for worshipping Buddha hair relic built. Original name Huangfei Pagoda, because built on Leifeng, commonly called Leifeng Pagoda.
>
> "**Collapse Event**:
> 1924 September 25 afternoon 1:40, Leifeng Pagoda suddenly collapsed. Reason was pagoda base excavated empty, pagoda body tilted, plus consecutive days heavy rain, finally collapsed.
>
> "**Rebuild Project**:
> 2000 started rebuilding, 2002 October completed. New pagoda 71.68 meters high, five layers eight angles, steel structure, inside has elevator. Pagoda base preserves original pagoda ruins.
>
> "**White Snake Story**:
> Legend White Lady (Bai Suzhen) is thousand-year snake spirit, with Xu Xian fell in love married. Monk Fahai believed human demon cannot love, pressed White Lady under Leifeng Pagoda. Later her son passed imperial exam, worshipped pagoda saved mother.
>
> "**Trivia**:
> - Original height: About 40 meters
> - New height: 71.68 meters
> - Collapse time: 1924 September 25
> - Rebuild time: 2000-2002"

## 🌊 West Lake History Overview

### L1 Basic
> "West Lake is China's only lake world cultural heritage. 2011 listed as world heritage. West Lake free open, is China's first free 5A scenic area."

### L2 Standard
> "West Lake is located at Hangzhou urban west, area 6.39 square kilometers, perimeter 15 kilometers. West Lake Ten Scenes formed in Southern Song period,距今 800+ years.
>
> 2011 June 24, West Lake cultural landscape listed as world cultural heritage, is China's only lake world heritage.
>
> 2002 start, West Lake free open, is China's first free open 5A scenic area."

### L3 Deep Dive
> "**Geographic Features**:
> West Lake located at Hangzhou urban west, area 6.39 square kilometers, average water depth 2.27 meters, deepest 5 meters, water storage 14 million cubic meters.
>
> "**History**:
> - Qin Dynasty: West Lake was Qiantang River bay
> - Han Dynasty: Gradually silted into lake
> - Tang Dynasty: Bai Juyi dredged West Lake
> - Northern Song: Su Shi dredged West Lake, built Su Causeway
> - Southern Song: Capital Hangzhou, West Lake Ten Scenes formed
> - 2011: Listed as world cultural heritage
>
> "**West Lake Ten Scenes**:
> Southern Song period named,距今 800+ years:
> 1. Spring Dawn at Su Causeway 2. Breeze-ruffled Lotus at Quyuan Garden
> 3. Autumn Moon on Calm Lake 4. Melting Snow on Broken Bridge
> 5. Viewing Fish at Flower Pond 6. Oriole Singing in Willow Grove
> 7. Three Pools Mirroring Moon 8. Twin Peaks Piercing Clouds
> 9. Leifeng Pagoda in Evening Glow 10. Evening Bell at Nanping Screen
>
> "**World Heritage**:
> 2011 June 24, in Paris held 35th World Heritage Conference, West Lake cultural landscape listed as world cultural heritage.
>
> "**Trivia**:
> - Area: 6.39 square kilometers
> - Perimeter: 15 kilometers
> - Free time: 2002 start
> - Visitors: Annual average 20 million"
