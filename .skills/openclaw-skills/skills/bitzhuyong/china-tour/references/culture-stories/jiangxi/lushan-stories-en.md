# Mount Lu Culture Stories (English)

> **Last Updated**: 2026-03

## History Overview

**Development History**:
- Han Dynasty: First developed
- Tang Song: Literati gathered
- 1996: World Cultural Heritage

**Literati**:
Li Bai/Bai Juyi/Su Shi etc all visited Mount Lu, left many poems.

**Trivia**:
- Altitude: 1,474 meters
- Summer resort: Summer average 22℃
- World Heritage: 1996
- Movie: "Romance on Lushan Mountain" filming location
