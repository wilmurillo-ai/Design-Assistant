## 🏞️ Five-Color Pond (Wuhua Hai)

### L1 Basic
> "Five-Color Pond is Jiuzhaigou essence, altitude 2,472 meters. Lake water because bottom calcium deposits and water plants presents colorful colors."

### L2 Standard
> "Five-Color Pond is located at Rize Valley, altitude 2,472 meters, is Jiuzhaigou most famous lake one of.
>
> Five-Color Pond water depth 5 meters, bottom has calcium deposits and various water plants, sunlight presents blue, green, yellow, orange etc multiple colors, hence name 'Five-Color Pond'.
>
> Five-Color Pond is Jiuzhaigou photography best location."

### L3 Deep Dive
> "**Geological Formation**:
> Five-Color Pond is glacier erosion formed barrier lake. Bottom has calcium deposits and water plants, sunlight presents colorful colors.
>
> "**Color Changes**:
> Five-Color Pond colors change with seasons:
> - Spring: Blue-green main
> - Summer: Blue-green deepens
> - Autumn: Colorful (colored forest reflection)
> - Winter: Partial freezing
>
> "**Ecosystem**:
> Five-Color Pond ecosystem complete:
> - Water quality: National class one water quality
> - Water plants: Multiple aquatic plants
> - Fish: Plateau unique fish
>
> "**Trivia**:
> - Altitude: 2,472 meters
> - Depth: 5 meters
> - Colors: Colorful
> - Best photography: Autumn"

### L2 Standard

>
> Nuorilang Waterfall 270 meters wide, 24.5 meters high, is China widest calcium waterfall. Waterfall water flows from cliff down, momentum magnificent.

## 🏞️ Jiuzhaigou Valley History Overview

### L1 Basic
> "Jiuzhaigou 1992 listed as World Natural Heritage. Because valley inside has 9 Tibetan villages got name."

### L2 Standard
> "Jiuzhaigou is located at Sichuan Province Aba Tibetan Qiang Autonomous Prefecture, altitude 2,000-3,100 meters.
>
> Jiuzhaigou because valley inside has 9 Tibetan villages got name. 1992 listed as World Natural Heritage, 2017 occurred 7.0 magnitude earthquake, 2019 reopened.
>
> Jiuzhaigou has 108 lakes, praised as 'Fairy Tale World'."

### L3 Deep Dive
> "**Name Origin**:
> Jiuzhaigou because valley inside has 9 Tibetan villages got name:
> - Shuzheng Village
> - Zechawa Village
> - Heye Village
> - etc 9 villages
>
> "**Geological History**:
> - 2 million years ago: Glacier erosion
> - 10,000 years ago: Barrier lake formed
> - Modern: 108 lakes
>
> "**Protection History**:
> - 1978: Established nature reserve
> - 1992: Listed as World Natural Heritage
> - 2017: 7.0 magnitude earthquake
> - 2019: Reopened
>
> "**Lake Count**:
> Jiuzhaigou has 108 lakes:
> - Rize Valley: 56
> - Zechawa Valley: 20
> - Shuzheng Valley: 32
>
> "**Trivia**:
> - Village count: 9
> - Lake count: 108
> - World Heritage: 1992
> - Earthquake: 2017 7.0 magnitude"
