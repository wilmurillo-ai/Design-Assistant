# Giant Wild Goose Pagoda Culture Stories (English)

> **Last Updated**: 2026-03

## History Overview

**Construction History**:
- 652 AD: Tang Gaozong built pagoda for Xuanzang
- 704 AD: Wu Zetian rebuilt
- Height: 64 meters, 7 floors
- Purpose: Store Buddhist sutras Xuanzang brought from India

**Xuanzang Story**:
Xuanzang traveled west for 17 years, brought back 657 Buddhist sutras, translated sutras here for 19 years.

**Trivia**:
- Height: 64 meters
- Floors: 7
- Steps: 258 total
- World Heritage: 2014 listed as Silk Road World Heritage
