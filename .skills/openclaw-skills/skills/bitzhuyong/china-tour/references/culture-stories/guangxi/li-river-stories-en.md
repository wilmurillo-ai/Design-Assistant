## 🏞️ Li River Overview

### L1 Basic
> "Li River is the soul of Guilin scenery, total length 164 kilometers. Li River scenery famous for 'green mountains, clear water, strange caves, beautiful rocks', is typical karst landform."

### L2 Standard
> "Li River originates from Mao'er Mountain north of Guilin, flows through Guilin, Yangshuo, to Pingle County merges into Gui River, total length 164 kilometers.
>
> Li River essence section from Guilin to Yangshuo, about 83 kilometers, is typical karst landform. Two sides peaks strange, river water clear, reflection like painting, praised as 'Hundred Mile Gallery'.
>
> 20 Yuan RMB background pattern is Li River Huangbu Reflection."

### L3 Deep Dive
> "**Geographic Features**:
> Li River originates from Mao'er Mountain north of Guilin, altitude 2,141 meters. Flows through Guilin, Lingchuan, Yangshuo, to Pingle County merges into Gui River, total length 164 kilometers.
>
> "**Karst Landform**:
> Li River is typical karst landform, formed 300 million years ago. At that time this was ocean, later crust uplift, seawater retreated, limestone was dissolved by water, formed strange peaks.
>
> "**Li River Four Wonders**:
> 1. Green Mountains: Peaks emerald green, evergreen four seasons
> 2. Clear Water: River water clear, reflection like painting
> 3. Strange Caves: Caves strange, stalactites various shapes
> 4. Beautiful Rocks: Stones beautiful, various shapes
>
> "**World Heritage**:
> 2014 June 23, South China Karst (including Guilin) listed as World Natural Heritage.
>
> "**Trivia**:
> - Total length: 164 kilometers
> - Essence section: 83 kilometers (Guilin to Yangshuo)
> - Formation time: 300 million years ago
> - 20 Yuan RMB: Background is Huangbu Reflection"

## 🏞️ Nine Horse Fresco

### L1 Basic
> "Nine Horse Fresco is Li River famous scenic spot, stone wall has nine naturally formed horses. Can count nine horses people, ancient said can pass imperial exam."

### L2 Standard
> "Nine Horse Fresco is located at Li River Yangting downstream, is 100+ meters high stone wall. Stone wall has nine naturally formed horses, various shapes.
>
> Legend can count nine horses people, ancient can pass imperial exam, modern can pass university entrance exam. Actually, 'horses' on stone wall are rock textures and plants together formed.
>
> Nine Horse Fresco is Li River photography must-visit spot."

### L3 Deep Dive
> "**Formation Reason**:
> Nine Horse Fresco is limestone mountain body, 100+ meters high, 500+ meters wide. 'Horses' on stone wall are rock textures and plants together formed.
>
> Rocks contain iron, manganese and other minerals, oxidized form different colors. Plus plants grow in rock cracks, from distance look like horses.
>
> "**Nine Horses Positions**:
> 1. Lead horse: Stone wall top, head up neighing
> 2-3. Second third horses: Running side by side
> 4-5. Fourth fifth horses: Head down eating grass
> 6-7. Sixth seventh horses: Playing
> 8-9. Eighth ninth horses: Hiding behind
>
> "**Folk Legend**:
> Legend these nine horses are heavenly palace heavenly horses, descended to Li River drink water, attracted by beautiful scenery, turned into stone wall.
>
> Folk says: 'Look horse boy, look horse boy, ask you divine horses how many pairs? Count out seven pass provincial exam, count out nine pass imperial exam.'
>
> "**Trivia**:
> - Stone wall height: 100+ meters
> - Stone wall width: 500+ meters
> - Formation reason: Rock textures + plants
> - Legend: Count out nine pass imperial exam"

## 🏞️ Huangbu Reflection

### L1 Basic
> "Huangbu Reflection is Li River essence of essence, 20 Yuan RMB background pattern. River bottom has yellow stone slab, like yellow cloth spread on river bottom."

### L2 Standard
> "Huangbu Reflection is located at Li River Xingping section, is Li River most beautiful scenic spot one of.
>
> River bottom has yellow stone slab, 100+ meters long, 50+ meters wide, like yellow cloth spread on river bottom, hence name 'Huangbu'.
>
> When river water calm, two sides peaks reflect in river, form 'Huangbu Reflection' spectacle. 20 Yuan RMB background pattern is here."

### L3 Deep Dive
> "**Huangbu Formation**:
> Huangbu is river bottom yellow stone slab, 100+ meters long, 50+ meters wide. Stone slab is limestone, because contains iron oxides and presents yellow.
>
> "**Reflection Spectacle**:
> When river water calm no wind, two sides peaks reflect in river, with river bottom Huangbu reflect each other, form 'Huangbu Reflection' spectacle.
>
> "**20 Yuan RMB**:
> 1999 edition fifth set RMB 20 Yuan paper money, background pattern is Huangbu Reflection. This is Li River only scenic spot on RMB.
>
> "**Best Viewing**:
> - Time: 11 AM-12 PM, 3 PM-4 PM
> - Condition: No wind, river water calm
> - Position: Cruise ship top floor/Xingping pier
>
> "**Trivia**:
> - Huangbu length: 100+ meters
> - Huangbu width: 50+ meters
> - RMB: 20 Yuan paper money background
> - Best time: No wind time"

## 🐉 Li River History Overview

### L1 Basic
> "Li River is Guilin scenery soul, 2014 listed as World Natural Heritage. Li River cruise tourism started 1970s."

### L2 Standard
> "Li River originates from Guilin Mao'er Mountain, total length 164 kilometers. Li River scenery formed 300 million years ago, is typical karst landform.
>
> Li River cultural history long, Qin Dynasty Ling Canal connected Yangtze and Pearl River systems. Tang Dynasty Guilin became tourism destination, successive literati left大量 poems.
>
> 2014, South China Karst (including Guilin) listed as World Natural Heritage."

### L3 Deep Dive
> "**Geological History**:
> - 300 million years ago: This was ocean
> - 200 million years ago: Crust uplift, seawater retreated
> - 100 million years ago: Limestone dissolved by water, formed karst landform
> - Modern: Li River scenery formed
>
> "**Cultural History**:
> - Qin Dynasty (221 BC): Built Ling Canal, connected Yangtze and Pearl River
> - Tang Dynasty (618-907 AD): Guilin became tourism destination
> - Song Dynasty (960-1279 AD): Li River scenery best under heaven
> - Ming-Qing (1368-1911 AD): Li River cruise tourism rose
> - 1970s: Modern cruise tourism started
> - 2014: Listed as World Natural Heritage
>
> "**Literati**:
> Successive literati left大量 poems:
> - Han Yu: 'River makes green silk ribbon, mountains like jade hairpin'
> - Du Fu: 'Suitable only Guilin'
> - Fan Chengda: 'Guilin mountains strange, should be first under heaven'
>
> "**Trivia**:
> - Formation time: 300 million years
> - Ling Canal: Qin Dynasty built
> - World Heritage: 2014
> - Visitors: Annual average 10 million"
