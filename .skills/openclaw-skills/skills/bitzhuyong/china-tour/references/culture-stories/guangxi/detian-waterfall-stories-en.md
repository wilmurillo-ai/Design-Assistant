# Detian Waterfall Culture Stories (English)

> **Last Updated**: 2026-03

## History Overview

**Geographic Features**:
- Width: 200 meters
- Drop: 70 meters
- Asia's largest transnational waterfall

**Transnational Features**:
- Shared by China and Vietnam
- Guichun River as border river
- One step across two countries

**Trivia**:
- Best viewing: May-October wet season
- Movie: "Journey of Flower" filming location
- Water volume: Largest in wet season
