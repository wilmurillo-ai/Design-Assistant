## 🏔️ Guest-Greeting Pine

### L1 Basic
> "Guest-Greeting Pine is Huangshan landmark, tree age 800+ years. It grows in front of Yuping Tower, one branch extends out, like host extending arm to welcome guests."

### L2 Standard
> "Guest-Greeting Pine is located in front of Huangshan Yuping Tower, altitude 1,670 meters, tree age 800+ years. Tree 10 meters high, diameter 0.64 meters.
>
> Guest-Greeting Pine one branch extends out, like host extending arm to welcome guests, hence name 'Guest-Greeting Pine'. It is China hospitality symbol, also Huangshan landmark.
>
> Guest-Greeting Pine has special guardian, regularly checks health condition."

### L3 Deep Dive
> "**Growth Environment**:
> Guest-Greeting Pine grows on cliff in front of Yuping Tower, altitude 1,670 meters. Here wind big, soil thin, temperature difference big, growth conditions harsh.
>
> "**Tree Age History**:
> Guest-Greeting Pine tree age 800+ years, through Yuan, Ming, Qing three dynasties. It witnessed Huangshan development, also witnessed Chinese history.
>
> "**Cultural Significance**:
> Guest-Greeting Pine is China hospitality symbol. It was painted in Great Hall of People iron painting, also printed on cigarettes, stamps.
>
> "**Protection Measures**:
> Guest-Greeting Pine has special guardian, regularly checks health condition. 2000s, installed support frame, protecting branches.
>
> "**Trivia**:
> - Tree age: 800+ years
> - Tree height: 10 meters
> - Diameter: 0.64 meters
> - Altitude: 1,670 meters"

## 🏔️ Bright Summit

### L1 Basic
> "Bright Summit is Huangshan second highest peak, altitude 1,860 meters. This is best place to watch sea of clouds, sunrise."

### L2 Standard
> "Bright Summit is located at Huangshan center, altitude 1,860 meters, is Huangshan second highest peak.
>
> Bright Summit flat open, is best place to watch sea of clouds, sunrise. Top builds weather station, is Huangshan weather observation point.
>
> Bright Summit also is Huangshan front-back mountain dividing line."

### L3 Deep Dive
> "**Geographic Features**:
> Bright Summit altitude 1,860 meters, is Huangshan second highest peak. Top surface flat, area about 1 square kilometer.
>
> "**Sea of Clouds Observation**:
> Bright Summit is Huangshan sea of clouds best observation point. Sea of clouds probability: winter about 50%, summer about 30%.
>
> "**Weather Station**:
> Bright Summit builds weather station, first built 1950s. Weather station responsible for Huangshan weather forecast, providing service for tourists.
>
> "**Sunrise Viewing**:
> Bright Summit is Huangshan sunrise best viewing place. Sunrise time: summer 5:00-5:30, winter 6:30-7:00.
>
> "**Trivia**:
> - Altitude: 1,860 meters
> - Huangshan ranking: Second highest peak
> - Sea of clouds probability: Winter 50%
> - Weather station: 1950s established"

## 🏔️ Flying-Over Rock

### L1 Basic
> "Flying-Over Rock is Huangshan famous strange rock, 12 meters high, weighs about 360 tons. It stands on cliff edge, like flew from outer space."

### L2 Standard
> "Flying-Over Rock is located on way from Huangshan Bright Summit to Paiyun Pavilion, 12 meters high, weighs about 360 tons.
>
> Flying-Over Rock stands on cliff edge, bottom contact area small, like flew from outer space, hence name 'Flying-Over Rock'.
>
> Legend Flying-Over Rock is stone Nuwa left repairing sky."

### L3 Deep Dive
> "**Geological Formation**:
> Flying-Over Rock is granite spherical weathering formed. Through hundreds of millions years weathering, rock gradually formed spherical, finally stood on cliff edge.
>
> "**Size Weight**:
> Flying-Over Rock 12 meters high, diameter about 8 meters, weighs about 360 tons. Bottom contact area only few square meters.
>
> "**Folk Legend**:
> Legend Flying-Over Rock is stone Nuwa left repairing sky, flew from outer space, fell on Huangshan.
>
> Also legend, Flying-Over Rock is immortal chess piece.
>
> "**Trivia**:
> - Height: 12 meters
> - Weight: About 360 tons
> - Formation: Spherical weathering
> - Legend: Nuwa repairing sky stone"

## 🏔️ Huangshan History Overview

### L1 Basic
> "Huangshan 1990 listed as World Cultural and Natural Dual Heritage. Xu Xiake said: 'After five sacred mountains return don't watch mountains, after Huangshan return don't watch five sacred mountains'."

### L2 Standard
> "Huangshan is located at Anhui Province south, area 1,200 square kilometers. Huangshan famous for 'Strange Pines, Strange Rocks, Sea of Clouds, Hot Springs' four wonders.
>
> 1990, Huangshan listed as World Cultural and Natural Dual Heritage. 2004, rated as National 5A scenic area.
>
> Ming Dynasty traveler Xu Xiake said: 'After five sacred mountains return don't watch mountains, after Huangshan return don't watch five sacred mountains'."

### L3 Deep Dive
> "**Name Origin**:
> Huangshan originally named Yi Mountain, because mountain stones black got name. Tang Dynasty Tianbao period (747 AD), Tang Xuanzong renamed Huangshan, because legend Xuanyuan Huangdi here refined elixir.
>
> "**Geological History**:
> - 100 million years ago: Crust movement, granite formed
> - 65 million years ago: Crust uplift, peaks formed
> - Modern: Huangshan landform formed
>
> "**Development History**:
> - Qin Dynasty: Already had tourists
> - Tang Dynasty: Renamed Huangshan, built temples
> - Ming Dynasty: Xu Xiake twice toured Huangshan
> - 1990: Listed as World Heritage
> - 2004: Rated as 5A scenic area
>
> "**Huangshan Four Wonders**:
> 1. Strange Pines: Guest-Greeting Pine/Sending-Guest Pine/Linked Pines
> 2. Strange Rocks: Flying-Over Rock/Monkey Watching Sea/Immortal Pointing Way
> 3. Sea of Clouds: Winter probability highest
> 4. Hot Springs: Water temperature 42℃, drinkable bathable
>
> "**Trivia**:
> - Original name: Yi Mountain
> - Rename time: 747 AD
> - World Heritage: 1990
> - Peak count: 72 peaks"
