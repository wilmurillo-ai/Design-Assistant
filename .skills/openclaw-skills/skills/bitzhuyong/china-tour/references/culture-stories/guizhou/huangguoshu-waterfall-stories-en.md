# Huangguoshu Waterfall Culture Stories (English)

> **Last Updated**: 2026-03

## History Overview
- Height: 77.8 meters
- Width: 101 meters
- China's largest waterfall
- "Journey to the West" filming location

## Trivia
- Water Curtain Cave: Cave behind waterfall, can walk through
- Best viewing: May-October wet season
- Water volume: Maximum 2,000 cubic meters per second
