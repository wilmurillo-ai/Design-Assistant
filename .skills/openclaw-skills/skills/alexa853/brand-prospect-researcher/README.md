# Brand Prospect Researcher — ClawHub Listing

**Title:** Brand Prospect Researcher

**Tagline:** Find brands actively spending on influencer marketing — before your competitors do.

**Description:**
Stop guessing which brands to pitch. Brand Prospect Researcher scans trade press (Ad Age, BoF, WWD, Glossy, Digiday, and more), job boards, partnership announcements, and Instagram for real-time signals that a brand is actively investing in creator campaigns. Each prospect is scored on active-spender signals — new campaigns, influencer marketing hires, agency changes, product launches — and delivered as a prioritized, ready-to-pitch list with contacts where available.

Works for ANY vertical: fashion, beauty, food, fitness, tech, gaming, lifestyle, and more. Configure your niche, set the number of prospects, and get a scored list you can act on immediately.

Built for talent managers and self-managed creators who want a data-driven approach to brand outreach. Whether you run a roster of 20 or you're a creator pitching yourself, this gives you the same brand intelligence that big agencies have.
