# Pitch Follow-Up Tracker — ClawHub Listing

**Title:** Pitch Follow-Up Tracker

**Tagline:** Never let a pitch die in someone's inbox again.

**Description:**
Pitch Follow-Up Tracker monitors your outreach pipeline, checks Gmail for replies, and drafts contextual follow-up emails that actually reference your original pitch — not generic "just checking in" messages.

Connect a Google Sheet or local markdown tracker, and the skill will: flag pitches that are 3+ days old with no reply, classify them into follow-up tiers (Day 3 gentle nudge, Day 7 add-value, Day 14 final check-in), and draft personalized follow-ups ready for your review. It even searches for recent brand news to make your Day 7 "add value" emails genuinely useful.

Built for talent managers, PR professionals, agency teams, sales reps, and anyone running outbound campaigns who needs to stay on top of follow-ups without the mental overhead.
