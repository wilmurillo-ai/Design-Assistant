# Creator Casting Tool

**Find the right creators for any campaign in minutes, not days.**

Give it a brief — brand, vibe, category, budget — and it searches Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and Substack for creators who actually fit. Returns a castable shortlist with engagement data, rate estimates, content examples, and brand conflict checks.

## What It Does

- Takes a campaign brief and searches across 4 platforms
- Screens 40-60 candidates, returns your top 10-15
- Checks for competitor brand conflicts (no embarrassing overlaps)
- Estimates rates based on follower count, engagement, and niche
- Scores each creator on campaign fit (not just follower count)
- Outputs a presentation-ready casting report

## Who It's For

- Brand marketing teams casting influencer campaigns
- PR and comms agencies sourcing talent for clients
- Casting directors for events, activations, content shoots
- DTC brands building ambassador or affiliate programs
- Creative agencies staffing content productions

- web_fetch tool (required)
