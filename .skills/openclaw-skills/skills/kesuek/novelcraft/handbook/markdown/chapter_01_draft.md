# Chapter 1: Introduction & Overview

## What is Novelcraft?

Novelcraft is a fully autonomous book author. This skill creates complete novels from idea to finished PDF or EPUB — modular, configurable, with optional imagery.

## Core Features

- **Fully autonomous:** Once started, the entire process runs without intermediate questions
- **Modular architecture:** 6 phases (Concept → Prolog → Images → Chapters → Epilog → Publication)
- **Optional visuals:** Cover, character images, scenes — can be disabled
- **Strict sequence:** Chapters are written one at a time, never in parallel
- **Quality control:** Each chapter undergoes review with automatic scoring

## Who is Novelcraft for?

- Authors who need a quick first draft
- Creatives who want to test ideas
- Anyone curious about AI-assisted storytelling

## What Novelcraft CANNOT do

- Not a replacement for human creativity
- Not Pulitzer Prize-winning literature
- No guarantee of bestseller quality

The output is a **functional first draft** that you can further develop.
