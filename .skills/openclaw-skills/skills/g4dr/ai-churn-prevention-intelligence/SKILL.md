## ⚙️ The Churn Signal Intelligence Framework

```
THE 7 CHURN SIGNALS DETECTED BEFORE CANCEL:

🔴 SIGNAL 1 — Public Complaint (Highest Risk)
  G2/Trustpilot review: 1-3 stars mentioning specific frustration
  Twitter @mention with complaint or frustration
  Reddit post asking for alternatives to your product
  Window: 2-8 weeks before cancellation
  Action: Immediate executive outreach within 24 hours

🔴 SIGNAL 2 — Competitor Research (Critical)
  Visiting competitor comparison pages (detected via public activity)
  Reddit/Twitter: "looking for alternative to [your product]"
  G2 profile visit to competitor within comparison context
  Window: 1-4 weeks before cancellation
  Action: Proactive value demonstration + competitive positioning

🟠 SIGNAL 3 — Support Escalation Language
  Support tickets with language: "still not working", "I give up",
  "this is unacceptable", "thinking about switching"
  Decreasing support engagement (stopped trying to fix issues)
  Window: 3-10 weeks before cancellation
  Action: Personal CS outreach + problem resolution priority

🟠 SIGNAL 4 — Decision Maker Change
  LinkedIn: Primary contact left the company or changed role
  New stakeholder has no relationship with your product
  No onboarding done for new decision maker
  Window: 30-90 days before cancellation
  Action: New stakeholder onboarding + relationship building

🟡 SIGNAL 5 — Low Engagement Pattern
  Login frequency dropping (internal data)
  Feature adoption below baseline for their tier
  Skipping QBRs or success check-ins
  Window: 6-12 weeks before cancellation
  Action: Re-engagement campaign + success milestone creation

🟡 SIGNAL 6 — Contract and Billing Signals
  Downgrade request conversation started
  Payment method expiry not updated
  Renewal date approaching with no expansion conversations
  Window: 4-8 weeks before cancellation
  Action: Value reinforcement + expansion conversation

🟢 SIGNAL 7 — Market/Company Health Signals
  Customer company laying off staff (may affect budget)
  Funding dried up (startup customer)
  Industry downturn in their sector
  Window: Variable — may not churn but budget-driven risk
  Action: Flexible pricing conversation + value-first approach
```

## ⚙️ Full Workflow

```
INPUT: Your product name + customer list + competitor list
        ↓
STEP 1 — Public Signal Intelligence Scrape
  G2: all reviews mentioning your product in last 90 days
  Reddit: all posts mentioning your product or category
  Twitter: all @mentions + complaint language
  Trustpilot: negative reviews with churn intent language
  LinkedIn: customer contact job changes
        ↓
STEP 2 — Customer-Signal Matching
  Match public signals to your customer list
  Identify: which customer wrote which review/post
  Cross-reference: email address + company name + username patterns
  Score: how many signals per customer in last 30/60/90 days
        ↓
STEP 3 — Churn Risk Scoring
  Red (score 70-100): cancel imminent — act today
  Amber (score 40-69): elevated risk — act this week
  Green (score 0-39): healthy — monitor monthly
        ↓
STEP 4 — Competitor Intelligence
  Which competitor is each at-risk customer evaluating?
  What is the competitor's positioning against you?
  What features/price is driving the comparison?
  Your counter-positioning per competitor
        ↓
STEP 5 — Save Strategy per Customer
  Red: Executive outreach + immediate problem resolution + retention offer
  Amber: CS personal outreach + value reinforcement + success story
  Both: acknowledge their frustration specifically
        ↓
STEP 6 — GetResponse Save Sequences
  Red customers: 3-email urgent sequence + immediate phone flag
  Amber customers: 5-email value reinforcement sequence
  Green customers: monthly health check + expansion nurture
  All sequences: personalized to detected signal type
        ↓
STEP 7 — Proactive Retention Playbook
  Month 1 (Day 1-30): Onboarding success indicators
  Month 3: First value milestone confirmation
  Month 6: Expansion conversation trigger
  Month 10-11: Renewal conversation + proactive offer
  Ongoing: Signal monitoring + auto-escalation
        ↓
OUTPUT: At-risk list + churn scores + competitor intel + save sequences + retention playbook
```
