## ⚙️ The Creator Vetting Framework

```
THE 5-LAYER CREATOR SCORE (0-100):

LAYER 1 — ENGAGEMENT QUALITY (35% weight)
  Engagement rate: likes + comments + shares / followers
  Comment quality: real conversations vs "great post!" spam
  Save rate on TikTok/Instagram: highest intent signal
  Share rate: best virality predictor
  Benchmark: micro (1K-100K) should hit 3-8%+

LAYER 2 — AUDIENCE AUTHENTICITY (25% weight)
  Follower growth pattern: organic = gradual, fake = spikes
  Follower:following ratio: normal is max 10:1
  Comment-to-like ratio: below 1:10 = engagement pod suspect
  Geographic spread: concentrated fake followers in one country
  Account age vs follower count: suspicious growth speed

LAYER 3 — NICHE ALIGNMENT (20% weight)
  Content category match to your brand
  Audience demographic match to your ICP
  Brand safety: any controversial content in last 90 posts?
  Past brand deal performance: did their last promotion convert?
  Tone match: does their voice fit your brand voice?

LAYER 4 — COMMERCIAL VIABILITY (10% weight)
  Past brand deals: do they take deals? what brands?
  Response rate to brand outreach (from Reddit reports)
  Price benchmarks for their tier and niche
  Exclusivity status: how many competing brands active?

LAYER 5 — GROWTH TRAJECTORY (10% weight)
  Monthly follower growth rate
  Video view trend: growing, stable, or declining?
  Algorithm favor signals: recent viral posts
  Platform diversification: multi-platform = lower platform risk
```
