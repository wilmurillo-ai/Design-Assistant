## ⚙️ The Perfect Webinar Formula

```
THE 6-PART WEBINAR STRUCTURE:

PART 1 — THE BIG PROMISE (0-5 min)
  The ONE specific result they get from this webinar
  Formula: "By the end, you will know exactly how to [result]
  even if [main objection]"
  Credibility: why you specifically in 60 seconds

PART 2 — THE REFRAME (5-15 min)
  The reason they have failed before is not their fault
  The ONE thing they have been missing
  Destroy the old belief, install the new one

  Secret 1: The strategy shift
  Secret 2: The system
  Secret 3: The shortcut
  Teach the WHAT — the HOW is what they pay for

  Introduce your offer as the complete implementation
  Stack: core + bonus 1 + bonus 2 + bonus 3
  Total value vs price = irresistible contrast

PART 5 — THE CLOSE (55-65 min)
  Price reveal + justification
  Guarantee: removes all risk
  Urgency: real reason to decide now
  FAQ: handle top 5 objections

PART 6 — EVERGREEN (via GetResponse)
  Webinar recorded and uploaded as evergreen
  New registrants watch same recording
  GetResponse sequences run automatically after viewing
  Cart closes 48 hours after replay
```
