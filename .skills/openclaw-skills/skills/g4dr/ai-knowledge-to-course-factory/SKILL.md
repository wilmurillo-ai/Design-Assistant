## ⚙️ Full Workflow

```
INPUT: Your expertise + target student + revenue goal
        ↓
STEP 1 — Market Validation
  └─ Udemy: top courses in your niche — student count + revenue
  └─ Google: monthly searches for your topic keywords
  └─ Reddit: how urgently do people want this knowledge?
  └─ YouTube: is there demand for video content on this topic?
  └─ Verdict: GO / PIVOT / AVOID with specific reasoning
        ↓
STEP 2 — Competitor Course Teardown
  └─ Top 5 courses: curriculum structure, pricing, reviews
  └─ 1-star reviews: what's missing? what's frustrating?
  └─ 5-star reviews: what do students love most?
  └─ Gap: what does YOUR course own that nobody else has?
        ↓
STEP 3 — Curriculum Architecture
  └─ 6-8 modules, 3-6 lessons each
  └─ Each lesson: learning objective + key concepts + exercise
  └─ Quick win in Module 1 Lesson 1 — non-negotiable
  └─ Total runtime: 4-8 hours of video content
        ↓
STEP 4 — Lesson Script Writing
  └─ Full word-for-word script per lesson
  └─ Structure: hook → teach → demonstrate → exercise → bridge
  └─ Conversational tone — like explaining to a smart friend
  └─ Length: 8-15 minutes per lesson
        ↓
STEP 5 — Pictory AI Video Production
  └─ Submit each lesson script to Pictory Script-to-Video
  └─ Pictory generates: visuals + AI voiceover + captions
  └─ Customize: your brand colors + logo + end screen
  └─ Output: professional lesson video per script
  └─ Total production: 6-8 hours of content in one afternoon
        ↓
STEP 6 — Student Journey in GetResponse
  └─ Enrollment email: welcome + quick start instructions
  └─ Module unlock sequence: one module per week
  └─ Progress check-ins: are they watching? completing exercises?
  └─ Completion certificate: automated at 80%+ completion
  └─ Upsell sequence: invite to next tier at completion
        ↓
STEP 7 — Pricing & Launch Strategy
  └─ 4-tier pricing architecture
  └─ Beta launch: first cohort at 70% discount for testimonials
  └─ Full launch: email sequence + social proof + urgency
  └─ Evergreen: automated funnel selling 24/7
        ↓
OUTPUT: Validated topic + full curriculum + lesson scripts + produced videos + GetResponse setup + launch plan
```
