## ⚙️ Full Workflow

```
INPUT: Your niche + content style + rate preference
        ↓
STEP 1 — Brand Discovery
  └─ TikTok Ad Library: brands running UGC-style ads in your niche
  └─ Meta Ad Library: DTC brands with authentic-style creatives
  └─ Google: "[niche] brand UGC creator wanted"
  └─ LinkedIn: marketing managers at DTC brands in your niche
        ↓
STEP 2 — Brand Scoring
  └─ Active ad spend signals: running 5+ creatives = buying UGC
  └─ UGC history: have they worked with creators before?
  └─ Brand-creator fit: does your style match their aesthetic?
  └─ Decision maker accessibility: can you reach the right person?
        ↓
STEP 3 — Winning Script Analysis
  └─ Top 10 performing UGC ads in your niche (by engagement)
  └─ Extract: hook formula, pacing, structure, CTA
  └─ Build: your script templates from proven formulas
        ↓
STEP 4 — Rate Card Generation
  └─ Personalized rates per brand based on their size + budget signals
  └─ Package options: single video vs bundle vs retainer
  └─ Revision policy + usage rights language
        ↓
STEP 5 — Personalized Pitch Per Brand
  └─ Opens with a specific observation about their current ads
  └─ Shows you understand their audience + product
  └─ Includes rate card + demo video link
  └─ Maximum 150 words — brand managers read fast
        ↓
STEP 6 — InVideo AI Produces 5 Demo Videos
  └─ 3 niche-specific demos (your best product categories)
  └─ 1 "about me as a creator" intro video
  └─ 1 before/after style UGC demo
  └─ These are your portfolio — sent with every pitch
        ↓
STEP 7 — 30-Day Client Acquisition Calendar
  └─ Day 1-5: send first 10 pitches with demo portfolio
  └─ Day 6-10: follow up non-responders + pitch 10 more
  └─ Day 11-20: negotiate with interested brands
  └─ Day 21-30: deliver first videos, collect testimonials, upsell
        ↓
OUTPUT: 20 brand targets + scripts + pitches + rate cards + 5 demo videos + 30-day calendar
```

   - Brand-creator fit (30%): aesthetic + audience alignment
   - Decision maker accessibility (20%): reachable contact?
   - Budget signals (15%): brand size + ad spend indicators

2. UGC signals detected (specific):
   - Number of active UGC-style ads running
   - Creator program signals
   - Content gaps in their current creative

3. Rate card (personalized to brand size):
   - Per video (30s, 60s, 90s)
   - Package options
   - Retainer option
   - Usage rights pricing

4. 3 scripts per brand (different formulas):
   - Problem/Solution, Skeptic Converted, Tutorial
   - Each: hook + body + CTA + estimated length
   - Why this formula works for THIS brand's product

5. Pitch (email + Instagram DM):
   - References their SPECIFIC current ad strategy
   - Shows you've done the creative analysis
   - Includes all 3 script titles as a teaser
   - Max 150 words email

6. 5 demo video scripts (generic, reusable across brands):
   - Problem/Solution, Skeptic, Tutorial, Creator Intro, Before/After
   - Each under 60 seconds
   - Designed to show range + versatility

7. 30-day acquisition calendar (specific daily actions)

UGC SCRIPT RULES:
- Hook must create pattern interrupt in first 1.5 seconds
- Body: one specific claim + one specific proof point
- CTA: low friction ("link in bio" not "go to website and use code")
- Authentic imperfection > polished production

OUTPUT: Valid JSON only. No markdown. No preamble.
