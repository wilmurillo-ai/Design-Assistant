## ⚙️ The TikTok Virality Detection Model

```
VIRAL PRODUCT SIGNALS (scored 0-100):

📈 MOMENTUM SIGNALS
  └─ Video views growing 300%+ in last 48 hours = early viral
  └─ Multiple creators posting same product in same week = about to explode
  └─ Product hashtag growing faster than creator hashtag = product-led, not creator-led

💰 COMMERCIAL SIGNALS
  └─ High comment-to-view ratio = high purchase intent
  └─ Comments: "where to get this?" "link?" "ordering now" = buyers not viewers
  └─ Save rate high = people bookmarking to buy later

🏪 SHOP SIGNALS
  └─ TikTok Shop sales velocity: units sold per day trending up
  └─ Price point $15-45 = impulse buy zone
  └─ Product already available on TikTok Shop = fastest path to sale

⚠️ TIMING SIGNALS
  └─ Day 1-3 of trend: maximum opportunity, minimum competition
  └─ Day 4-7: still viable, move fast
  └─ Day 8+: saturation warning, margins will compress
```
