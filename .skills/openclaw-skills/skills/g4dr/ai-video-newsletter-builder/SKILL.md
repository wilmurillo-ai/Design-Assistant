## ⚙️ Full Workflow

```
INPUT: Your niche + commentary style + audience profile
        ↓
STEP 1 — Weekly Content Scan
  └─ Google News: top stories in niche — last 7 days
  └─ Reddit: top posts in relevant subreddits — what's getting upvotes?
  └─ Twitter: trending hashtags and conversations in niche
  └─ YouTube: viral videos published this week
  └─ Output: 20-30 potential stories ranked by importance + relevance
        ↓
STEP 2 — Story Selection (Top 5)
  └─ Filter by: audience relevance + timeliness + uniqueness
  └─ Balance: 2 big news stories + 2 practical insights + 1 surprising find
  └─ Prioritize: stories competitors haven't covered yet
        ↓
STEP 3 — Deep Content Extraction
  └─ Crawl full article for each story
  └─ Extract: key facts, data points, quotes, implications
  └─ Research: what's the deeper angle nobody is talking about?
        ↓
STEP 4 — Newsletter Script Writing
  └─ For each story: 150-word summary + 50-word YOUR take
  └─ Your voice: opinionated, direct, tells them what it means for THEM
  └─ Video script version: same content, conversational delivery
  └─ Weekly opinion section: 100-word hot take on the biggest story
        ↓
STEP 5 — Pictory AI Video Production
  └─ Submit newsletter script to Pictory
  └─ Pictory auto-generates: presenter-style video with visuals
  └─ Output: 10-minute professional video in your brand colors
  └─ Also generates: 3 short clips for social media teaser
        ↓
STEP 6 — GetResponse Email Build + Send
  └─ Import newsletter copy into GetResponse email builder
  └─ Embed video thumbnail with play button (links to full video)
  └─ Schedule: Tuesday 7am (optimal for B2B audiences)
  └─ Segment: send to full list OR test version to engaged segment first
        ↓
STEP 7 — Performance Tracking
  └─ GetResponse analytics: open rate, click rate, video plays
  └─ Which story got most clicks? → double down next week
  └─ Which subject line style got best open rate? → test variations
        ↓
OUTPUT: 5 curated stories + full newsletter copy + video + email campaign + performance tracking
```
