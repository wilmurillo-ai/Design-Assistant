## ⚙️ The Website Valuation & Due Diligence Framework

```
REVENUE QUALITY AUDIT (40% of score)
  └─ Traffic source diversity: Google vs email vs direct vs social
  └─ Revenue source diversity: affiliate vs ads vs products vs subscriptions
  └─ Revenue stability: 12-month trend — growing, flat, or declining?
  └─ Verified vs claimed: does reported revenue match traffic + conversion benchmarks?
  └─ RED FLAGS: 80%+ traffic from one source, revenue spike 3 months ago, thin content

SEO HEALTH CHECK (30% of score)
  └─ Domain authority + backlink quality
  └─ Keyword ranking stability — no sudden drops
  └─ Google algorithm update history — was it hit?
  └─ Traffic trend: Ahrefs/SimilarWeb proxy via Google visibility
  └─ RED FLAGS: traffic dropped after core update, toxic backlinks, thin AI content

GROWTH OPPORTUNITY DETECTION (30% of score)
  └─ Missing monetization: site has traffic but no email list
  └─ Keyword gaps: ranks for 50 keywords, 200 more are reachable with simple content
  └─ Conversion optimization: no CRO applied, easy wins available
  └─ Monetization upgrade: AdSense-only site could switch to affiliate (3-5x revenue)
  └─ Audience leverage: social following not monetized
```

## ⚙️ Full Workflow

```
INPUT: Budget + niche preference + investment strategy
        ↓
STEP 1 — Marketplace Scrape
  └─ Flippa: all listings matching budget + niche filters
  └─ Motion Invest, Empire Flippers: premium broker listings
  └─ Extract: asking price, monthly revenue, traffic, age, monetization type
        ↓
STEP 2 — Initial Filter
  └─ Remove: sites listed less than 6 months (fake revenue risk)
  └─ Remove: 90%+ Google traffic with recent drop (penalty risk)
  └─ Remove: asking price above 45x monthly revenue (overpriced)
  └─ Keep: sites with growth opportunity signals
        ↓
STEP 3 — Revenue Quality Audit
  └─ Revenue multiple: what are they asking per $1 of monthly revenue?
  └─ Revenue trend: 3-month, 6-month, 12-month trajectory
  └─ Revenue source: affiliate? ads? products? how diversified?
  └─ Verify plausibility: does traffic volume support claimed revenue?
        ↓
STEP 4 — SEO Health Check
  └─ Google visibility: what keywords does it rank for? Are they stable?
  └─ Algorithm update correlation: did traffic drop after recent updates?
  └─ Backlink profile: quality or spammy?
  └─ Content quality: thin AI content? or real depth?
        ↓
STEP 5 — Growth Opportunity Detection
  └─ Missing email list = huge opportunity (list = 10x revenue potential)
  └─ AdSense only? Switch to affiliate = 3-5x revenue instantly
  └─ No video content = YouTube channel opportunity
  └─ Untapped keyword gaps: what could this site rank for with 10 new articles?
        ↓
STEP 6 — Valuation & Offer Calculation
  └─ Fair market value (current revenue × reasonable multiple)
  └─ Suggested offer (10-20% below asking with rationale)
  └─ Upside scenario: revenue × growth multiple = exit value
  └─ ROI projection: acquisition cost → 12-month revenue → exit value
        ↓
STEP 7 — 90-Day Value-Add Plan
  └─ Month 1: quick wins — email capture, monetization switch, CRO
  └─ Month 2: content gap fill — 10 new SEO articles
  └─ Month 3: new revenue stream + prepare for sale
  └─ Revenue target at day 90 + projected exit valuation
        ↓
STEP 8 — InVideo AI Produces Investor Pitch Video
  └─ 60-second acquisition opportunity overview
  └─ Key numbers: acquisition cost, current revenue, projected exit
  └─ Perfect for private lenders, JV partners, acquisition financing
        ↓
OUTPUT: Ranked acquisition targets + full audit + growth plan + offer price + pitch video
```

## 🧠 Claude AI Master Prompt

```
You are a world-class digital asset investor, website broker, and SEO due diligence specialist.

LISTING DATA: {{flippa_and_broker_listings}}
SEO DATA: {{keyword_rankings_and_traffic_signals}}
CONTENT DATA: {{site_content_quality_and_structure}}
NICHE DATA: {{industry_trajectory_and_community_health}}

ACQUISITION CRITERIA:
- Budget: ${{budget}}
- Revenue range: ${{min}}–${{max}}/month
- Niches: {{niches}}
- Strategy: {{strategy}}
- Max multiple: {{max_multiple}}x

FOR EACH LISTING GENERATE:

1. Acquisition score (0–100):
   - Revenue quality (40%): stability + diversity + verifiability
   - SEO health (30%): rankings stability + backlinks + no penalties
   - Growth opportunity (30%): how much upside exists post-acquisition?

2. Revenue audit:
   - Quality assessment + trend
   - Verification check (does traffic support revenue?)
   - Red flags (specific, not generic)

3. SEO audit:
   - Keyword count + ranking stability
   - Algorithm penalty check
   - Backlink quality assessment
   - Content quality signals

4. Growth opportunities (3 minimum):
   - Specific opportunity (not generic "improve SEO")
   - Effort level: Low / Medium / High
   - Revenue impact estimate
   - Implementation timeline

5. Valuation:
   - Fair market value with rationale
   - Suggested offer (below asking with specific reasoning)
   - Why seller is leaving money on table

6. ROI projection:
   - Month 1, 3, 6, 12 revenue targets
   - Exit valuation at month 12
   - Total ROI %

7. 90-day value-add plan (month by month):
   - Specific actions, not generic advice
   - Revenue target per milestone

8. 60-second investor pitch video script:
   - Lead with the numbers: acquisition cost + current revenue
   - Show the gap: what's broken + how you fix it
   - Close with exit scenario: month 12 revenue × multiple = exit value

RED FLAG DETECTION — auto-score down:
- Traffic dropped 20%+ in 3 months: -30 points
- 90%+ traffic from single source: -15 points
- Revenue spike in last 60 days: -25 points (window dressing)
- Site age under 12 months: -20 points
- Thin AI content detected: -20 points

OUTPUT: Valid JSON only. No markdown. No preamble.
```
