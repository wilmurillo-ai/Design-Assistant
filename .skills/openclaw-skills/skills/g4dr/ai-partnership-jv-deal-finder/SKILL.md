## ⚙️ The JV Partnership Opportunity Matrix

```
PARTNERSHIP TYPES (ranked by revenue potential):

💎 TYPE 1 — Newsletter Swap / Sponsorship (Fastest ROI)
  └─ Partner sends your offer to their list, you send theirs to yours
  └─ 50K subscriber newsletter = 500-2,000 new leads in 24 hours
  └─ Revenue potential: $5,000–$50,000 per swap depending on offer
  └─ Best for: SaaS, courses, digital products, services

🎙️ TYPE 2 — Podcast Partnership
  └─ Appear on partner's show, they appear on yours
  └─ 10K listeners = 200-500 new leads from one episode
  └─ Revenue potential: $2,000–$20,000 per appearance (warm audience)
  └─ Best for: service businesses, coaches, consultants, SaaS

🔗 TYPE 3 — SaaS Integration Partnership
  └─ Your tool + their tool = better together
  └─ Feature in each other's app marketplace
  └─ Revenue potential: Passive leads forever
  └─ Best for: SaaS companies with overlapping users

📦 TYPE 4 — Bundle / Co-Promotion
  └─ Package your product with theirs for a limited offer
  └─ AppSumo-style: both audiences win, both businesses win
  └─ Revenue potential: $10,000–$100,000 per bundle launch
  └─ Best for: digital products, courses, SaaS tools

🏢 TYPE 5 — B2B Referral Partnership
  └─ Agency + SaaS: agency recommends your tool to clients
  └─ Consultant + service: complementary services, mutual referrals
  └─ Revenue potential: $1,000–$10,000 per referred client
  └─ Best for: B2B services, enterprise SaaS

🤝 TYPE 6 — White Label / Reseller
  └─ Partner sells your product under their brand
  └─ Revenue potential: 20-40% margin per sale × partner's volume
  └─ Best for: SaaS with agency or consultant distribution
```

## ⚙️ Full Workflow

```
INPUT: Your business + ideal partner profile + deal type preference
        ↓
STEP 1 — Partner Discovery
  └─ Google: top newsletters, podcasts, blogs in your niche
  └─ LinkedIn: complementary businesses targeting same ICP
  └─ Twitter: creators with 5K-500K engaged followers in your space
  └─ Reddit: community builders and niche authority figures
        ↓
STEP 2 — Audience Alignment Scoring
  └─ Does their audience match your ICP?
  └─ Are they selling something complementary (not competing)?
  └─ Do their existing partnerships signal openness to JV deals?
  └─ What's the estimated size of their monetizable audience?
        ↓
STEP 3 — Revenue Upside Modeling
  └─ Audience size × estimated conversion rate × your offer value
  └─ Deal type recommendation: swap / rev share / integration / bundle
  └─ Revenue estimate: conservative / base / optimistic scenario
        ↓
STEP 4 — Partner Intelligence Deep Dive
  └─ Their current offers and pricing
  └─ Past partnerships they've done (signals openness)
  └─ Their audience's pain points (your pitch angle)
  └─ Best contact person + channel
        ↓
STEP 5 — Deal Structure Design
  └─ What do THEY get? (must be clear before you pitch)
  └─ What do YOU get?
  └─ Revenue share percentage if applicable
  └─ Timeline: quick test → scale if it works
        ↓
STEP 6 — Claude AI Writes Personalized JV Pitch
  └─ Opens with a genuine observation about their business
  └─ Shows you've done the math — "here's what this could be worth to you"
  └─ Proposes a specific, low-friction first step
  └─ Never sounds like a template
        ↓
STEP 7 — InVideo AI Produces Partnership Pitch Video
  └─ 60-second "here's what I'm proposing and why" video
  └─ Increases response rate 4x vs text-only pitches
  └─ Shows face + energy — partners buy into people first
        ↓
STEP 8 — 30-Day Partnership Calendar
  └─ Week 1: Top 5 highest-potential partners (warm outreach)
  └─ Week 2: Follow up + next 5
  └─ Week 3: Negotiate terms with interested parties
  └─ Week 4: Launch first partnership, measure, optimize
        ↓
OUTPUT: 20 partner targets + revenue projections + pitches + deal structures + pitch video + calendar
```

## 🧠 Claude AI Master Prompt

```
You are a world-class business development strategist and JV partnership expert.

PARTNER DATA: {{scraped_newsletters_podcasts_businesses_creators}}
AUDIENCE DATA: {{partner_audience_size_engagement_demographics}}
OFFER DATA: {{partner_current_products_and_positioning}}
CONTACT DATA: {{decision_maker_emails_and_social}}

YOUR BUSINESS:
- Name: {{name}}
- Type: {{type}}
- Description: {{description}}
- Target customer: {{icp}}
- Price: ${{price}}/month
- Key result: {{result}}

IDEAL PARTNER PROFILE: {{partner_criteria}}

FOR EACH PARTNER GENERATE:
1. Partner score (0–100):
   - Audience alignment (40%): does their audience = your ICP?
   - Deal potential (30%): size × conversion estimate × your LTV
   - Partner openness (20%): past partnerships + communication activity
   - Accessibility (10%): can you actually reach the decision maker?

2. Tier label:
   - 💎 Dream Partner: score 85+
   - 🔥 Strong Fit: score 70-84
   - ✅ Test Partner: score 50-69

3. Revenue modeling (show all math):
   - Audience size × estimated reach × CTR × trial rate × conversion rate
   - Conservative / base / optimistic scenarios
   - LTV projection at your avg retention

4. Deal structure recommendation:
   - Which partnership type fits best (newsletter/podcast/integration/bundle/referral)
   - What THEY get (must be compelling before you pitch)
   - What YOU get
   - Revenue share % if applicable
   - Low-friction first step (test before scale)

5. Personalized pitch (email + LinkedIn DM):
   - Open with a SPECIFIC genuine observation about their business
   - Show the math — "here's what I think this is worth to you"
   - Propose one specific low-friction first step
   - Include video link placeholder
   - Maximum 200 words for email

6. 60-second pitch video script:
   - Open with their name + specific observation
   - Show the revenue model in 30 seconds
   - Close with specific ask (20-minute call)

7. 30-day partnership calendar:
   - Week-by-week outreach + follow up + negotiation + launch

PITCH RULES:
- Always show the partner what THEY earn — not just what you want
- Specific revenue numbers > vague "mutual benefit" language
- "I ran the numbers" = the most powerful phrase in a JV pitch
- Low-friction first step = test campaign, not 12-month contract

OUTPUT: Valid JSON only. No markdown. No preamble.
```
