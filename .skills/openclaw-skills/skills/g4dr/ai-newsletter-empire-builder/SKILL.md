## ⚙️ Full Workflow

```
INPUT: Your niche + expertise + content style + monetization goal
        ↓
STEP 1 — Niche & Topic Intelligence
  └─ Beehiiv/Substack: top newsletters in your space — subscriber count, open rates
  └─ Reddit: what content gaps are communities hungry for?
  └─ Twitter: what newsletter content gets shared most in your niche?
  └─ Google Trends: is audience interest growing or flat?
        ↓
STEP 2 — Competitor Newsletter Reverse Engineering
  └─ Top 5 newsletters in niche: content format, frequency, monetization
  └─ What do readers love? (replies, shares, testimonials)
  └─ What's missing? (gaps in existing newsletters = your differentiation)
  └─ Sponsorship rates: what do they charge advertisers?
        ↓
STEP 3 — Newsletter Positioning
  └─ Unique angle: what do YOU cover that nobody else does?
  └─ Format: curation vs original vs hybrid vs data-driven
  └─ Frequency: daily, 3x/week, weekly — matched to monetization goal
  └─ Name + tagline: memorable, searchable, shareable
        ↓
STEP 4 — 8 Editions Written (Full Content)
  └─ Edition 1: Launch edition — who you are and why this exists
  └─ Editions 2-7: core content formula established
  └─ Edition 8: first monetization introduction (natural, not jarring)
  └─ Each edition: hook → value section → insight → CTA
        ↓
STEP 5 — Growth Playbook (0 to 10,000)
  └─ Week 1-2: Founding subscribers via Twitter + Reddit + LinkedIn
  └─ Month 1-2: Guest posts on established newsletters (cross-promos)
  └─ Month 2-4: SparkLoop referral program + lead magnet
  └─ Month 4-6: Paid newsletter swaps with complementary audiences
        ↓
STEP 6 — Monetization Model (4 streams)
  └─ Stream 1: Newsletter sponsorships (CPM-based)
  └─ Stream 2: Paid subscriber tier (premium content)
  └─ Stream 3: Digital product (template, guide, course)
  └─ Stream 4: Consulting/service upsell to engaged readers
        ↓
STEP 7 — InVideo AI Produces Launch Video
  └─ 60-second "why I'm starting this newsletter" video
  └─ Post to Twitter + LinkedIn + YouTube Shorts on launch day
  └─ Drives first 200 subscribers from video alone
        ↓
OUTPUT: Niche intelligence + competitor map + 8 editions + growth playbook + monetization model + launch video
```
