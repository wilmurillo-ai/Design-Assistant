## ⚙️ Full Workflow

```
INPUT: Your expertise + niche + target student
        ↓
STEP 1 — Topic Validation
  └─ Udemy: top courses in your niche — student count, revenue estimate, ratings
  └─ Google: monthly search volume for your topic keywords
  └─ Reddit: how often is this question asked in relevant subreddits?
  └─ YouTube: is there free content but no structured paid course?
  └─ Verdict: GO / PIVOT / AVOID
        ↓
STEP 2 — Top Course Reverse Engineering
  └─ Top 5 courses in niche: curriculum structure, module count, lesson length
  └─ What do 1-star reviews say they're missing?
  └─ What do 5-star reviews say was transformative?
  └─ Price points + enrollment numbers = revenue estimate
        ↓
STEP 3 — Your Positioning
  └─ What's missing from existing courses?
  └─ Your unique angle: experience, method, results, audience specificity
  └─ Course title formula: [Outcome] for [Specific Person] in [Timeframe]
        ↓
STEP 4 — Complete Curriculum Design
  └─ Module structure: 5-8 modules, 3-7 lessons each
  └─ Each lesson: title + objective + key teaching points
  └─ Workbook/exercises per module
  └─ Quick wins early: student must feel progress in module 1
        ↓
STEP 5 — Full Script Writing (First 3 Modules)
  └─ Word-for-word scripts for all lessons in modules 1-3
  └─ Hook → teach → apply → next step structure per lesson
  └─ Designed for 8-15 minute lesson videos
        ↓
STEP 6 — Pricing & Platform Strategy
  └─ Platform comparison: Udemy vs Teachable vs Gumroad vs own site
  └─ 3-tier pricing recommendation with rationale
  └─ Launch price vs evergreen price
        ↓
STEP 7 — Sales Page Writing
  └─ Headline: the transformation promise
  └─ Problem agitation: they recognize themselves
  └─ Your credentials: why you specifically
  └─ Curriculum overview: what they get
  └─ Testimonial placement framework
  └─ Pricing section + guarantee
  └─ FAQ section: top 5 objections handled
        ↓
STEP 8 — 7-Email Launch Sequence
  └─ Email 1 (Day 1): The problem they know too well
  └─ Email 2 (Day 3): The hidden reason it stays unsolved
  └─ Email 3 (Day 5): The solution exists — story
  └─ Email 4 (Day 7): Proof — student result story
  └─ Email 5 (Day 8): Cart open announcement
  └─ Email 6 (Day 9): FAQ + objection handling
  └─ Email 7 (Day 10): Last chance — cart closes tonight
        ↓
STEP 9 — InVideo AI Produces Promo Video
  └─ 90-second course trailer for sales page + social
  └─ Problem → transformation → curriculum preview → CTA
  └─ Increases sales page conversion 25-40%
        ↓
OUTPUT: Validated topic + curriculum + 3 module scripts + sales page + email sequence + promo video
```
