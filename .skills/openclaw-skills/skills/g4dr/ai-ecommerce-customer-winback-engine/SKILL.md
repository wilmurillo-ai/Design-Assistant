## ⚙️ The Winback Intelligence Framework

```
THE RFM CUSTOMER SEGMENTATION MODEL:

R = RECENCY (When did they last buy?)
F = FREQUENCY (How many times did they buy?)
M = MONETARY (How much did they spend total?)

SEGMENT 1 — "SLIPPING AWAY" (R: 60-90 days, F: 2+, M: High)
  Description: Recent regulars who just went quiet
  Winback probability: 45-65%
  Best approach: Personal check-in + small incentive
  Revenue potential: Highest — they were loyal

SEGMENT 2 — "ONE AND DONE" (R: 30-180 days, F: 1, M: Medium)
  Description: Bought once, never returned
  Winback probability: 20-35%
  Best approach: Understand why + strong incentive
  Revenue potential: High volume — largest segment

SEGMENT 3 — "GONE COLD" (R: 91-180 days, F: 2+, M: Medium)
  Description: Regular customers who disappeared
  Winback probability: 25-40%
  Best approach: Remind of relationship + new arrivals
  Revenue potential: Medium — need re-education

SEGMENT 4 — "LOST" (R: 180+ days, F: Any, M: Any)
  Description: Customers who have likely moved on
  Winback probability: 8-15%
  Best approach: Big offer + last chance framing
  Revenue potential: Low per contact, high in volume

SEGMENT 5 — "VIP AT RISK" (R: 45-90 days, F: 5+, M: Very High)
  Description: Your best customers showing early churn signals
  Winback probability: 55-75% with right approach
  Best approach: White-glove personal outreach — PRIORITY
  Revenue potential: CRITICAL — protect at all cost
```

## ⚙️ Full Workflow

```
INPUT: Your store URL + customer export + competitor list
        ↓
STEP 1 — Dormant Customer Intelligence
  Import customer purchase history
  Apply RFM model: score every customer 1-5 on each dimension
  Identify: each customer's segment + winback priority score
  Flag: VIP at risk customers for immediate attention
        ↓
STEP 2 — Churn Reason Intelligence (via Apify)
  Reddit: your brand name + "stopped buying" / "switched to"
  Twitter: @yourbrand complaints + competitor switch posts
  Reviews: 1-3 star reviews mentioning switch motivation
  Google: "alternatives to [your brand]" search patterns
  Output: top 5 reasons customers leave your category
        ↓
STEP 3 — Competitor Switch Detection
  Which competitors are poaching your dormant customers?
  Their current pricing vs yours
  Their current promotions vs yours
  Their review sentiment vs yours
  Your counter-offer per competitor
        ↓
STEP 4 — Winback Strategy per Segment
  Segment 1 (Slipping Away): personal tone + surprise reward
  Segment 2 (One and Done): survey + strong first-return offer
  Segment 3 (Gone Cold): new arrivals + relationship reminder
  Segment 4 (Lost): maximum offer + last chance urgency
  Segment 5 (VIP at Risk): personal outreach + exclusive treatment
        ↓
STEP 5 — GetResponse Winback Campaigns
  5 automated sequences — one per segment
  Each sequence: 3-5 emails over 14-21 days
  Personalization: references purchase history + time since last order
  Trigger: customer passes recency threshold → enters sequence
        ↓
STEP 6 — Loyalty Prevention System
  Post-purchase sequence: ensure satisfaction after every order
  Milestone rewards: celebrate order milestones automatically
  Re-engagement triggers: alert before customer goes dormant
  VIP identification: flag high-value customers for priority treatment
        ↓
OUTPUT: RFM segments + churn reasons + competitor intel + 5 winback campaigns + loyalty system
```
