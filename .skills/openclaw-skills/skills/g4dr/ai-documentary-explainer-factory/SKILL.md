## ⚙️ The Viral Explainer Video Formula

```
THE 5-ACT STRUCTURE OF A VIRAL EXPLAINER:

🎯 ACT 1 — THE HOOK (0-60 seconds)
  └─ Open with the most shocking or counterintuitive fact
  └─ Create a question the viewer MUST have answered
  └─ Never start with "In this video I'm going to..."
  └─ Example: "In 1972, a man accidentally discovered the most 
     valuable object on earth. Nobody believed him for 40 years."

🌍 ACT 2 — THE CONTEXT (1-3 minutes)
  └─ Why does this topic matter RIGHT NOW?
  └─ The history or background that makes the main story possible
  └─ Stakes established: who is affected and how much?

🔍 ACT 3 — THE DEEP DIVE (4-8 minutes)
  └─ The main explanation — broken into 3-4 chapters
  └─ Each chapter ends with a mini-cliffhanger
  └─ Visual metaphors for complex concepts
  └─ The "I never thought of it that way" moment

💡 ACT 4 — THE REVELATION (8-10 minutes)
  └─ The insight the viewer couldn't have predicted at minute 1
  └─ Connects the hook to the conclusion
  └─ The "that's why the opening stat was true" payoff

🚀 ACT 5 — THE IMPLICATION (10-12 minutes)
  └─ What does this mean for the viewer's world?
  └─ The actionable or philosophical takeaway
  └─ Subscribe CTA embedded naturally in the conclusion

TOP-PERFORMING FORMATS BY NICHE:
  Finance: "How [Company] Actually Makes Money" — avg 2.1M views
  History: "The [Event] Nobody Talks About" — avg 1.8M views  
  Science: "Why [Common Belief] Is Completely Wrong" — avg 3.4M views
  Business: "The Rise and Fall of [Company]" — avg 1.6M views
  Tech: "How [Technology] Actually Works" — avg 2.8M views
```

```

  └─ YouTube: top 100 videos in niche by views — what topics dominate?

  └─ Google Search: "how does X work" + "why is X" volumes
  └─ News: recent events needing explanation = timely content
        ↓

  └─ How many videos exist on this topic?

  └─ What do 1-star comments say the existing videos missed?
  └─ Is the top-ranking video outdated (3+ years)?

        ↓

  └─ Top 10 videos in niche: hook type, chapter structure, length

  └─ Build: niche-specific script template from winners
        ↓
STEP 4 — 10 Topic Selection & Research
  └─ Mix: 6 evergreen + 3 trending + 1 high-competition swing
  └─ Research each: key facts, data points, narrative arc
  └─ Validate: is there enough depth for 10+ minutes?
        ↓

  └─ Hook: opening 60 seconds — most important part

  └─ Export: YouTube-ready MP4 (1080p)
        ↓
STEP 8 — Upload Calendar + Growth Strategy
  └─ Optimal posting sequence (evergreen first, build foundation)
  └─ Best upload days/times for your niche

        ↓
OUTPUT: 10 topic reports + 10 scripts + 10 produced videos + SEO packages + upload calendar
```
