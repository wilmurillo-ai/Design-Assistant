## ⚙️ The 5-Layer Contact Enrichment Model

```
LAYER 1 — CONTACT DATA (Basic)
  Full name + verified job title + seniority level
  Verified direct email (not generic)
  LinkedIn profile URL
  Phone number (where publicly available)
  Location + timezone

LAYER 2 — COMPANY INTELLIGENCE
  Headcount (exact + growth rate 6 months)
  Annual revenue estimate
  Industry + sub-industry vertical
  Founded year + stage
  HQ + number of offices

LAYER 3 — FUNDING & BUDGET SIGNALS
  Most recent funding round + amount + date
  Total capital raised to date
  Investor quality (Tier 1 VC = serious budget)
  Revenue stage estimate (pre-seed / seed / Series A-C / growth)

LAYER 4 — TECH STACK INTELLIGENCE
  CRM currently in use
  Email marketing platform
  Analytics and attribution stack
  Automation tools
  Competitor tools being used (= replacement opportunity)

LAYER 5 — INTENT SIGNALS (Highest Value)
  Recent funding: fresh budget = buying window open NOW
  Hiring for relevant role: scaling = evaluating tools NOW
  Tech stack change detected: active evaluation period
  LinkedIn activity: engaging with relevant content
  Competitor frustration mentions: ready to switch
```
