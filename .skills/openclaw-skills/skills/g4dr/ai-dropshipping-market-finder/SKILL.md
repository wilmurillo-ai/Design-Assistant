## ⚙️ The Geographic Arbitrage Framework

```
THE DROPSHIPPING GOLD RUSH STRATEGY:

🌍 HOW GEOGRAPHIC ARBITRAGE WORKS:
  └─ Product goes viral in US → 6 months later hits UK → 3 months later hits Australia
  └─ Winners launch in UK/AU/CA when US is already saturated
  └─ Same product, same supplier, 3x less competition, same profit margins

📊 MARKET READINESS SCORING:
  └─ Google Trends spike: search volume growing in target country?
  └─ Amazon presence: product available but low reviews = early market
  └─ TikTok virality: US viral product not yet viral in target market
  └─ Facebook ad library: competitors not yet running ads in target country

💎 THE ARBITRAGE WINDOW:
  └─ Too early (no demand): wait
  └─ PERFECT WINDOW (demand growing, supply not there yet): LAUNCH NOW
  └─ Too late (market saturated): find next market or next product

🏆 TOP GEOGRAPHIC ARBITRAGE MARKETS IN 2026:
  └─ United Kingdom: 6-12 month lag behind US trends
  └─ Australia/NZ: 9-15 month lag, high spending power
  └─ Canada: 3-6 month lag, similar buying behavior to US
  └─ Germany/DACH: massive market, slower to saturate
  └─ Nordics: high CPM but very high AOV and conversion
```

   - Product cost + shipping + platform fee + processing + estimated ad cost
   - Net profit + net margin %
   - Break-even ROAS

   - Top store: pricing, description style, ad angles used

   - Under 30 seconds each

   - Day by day for first 2 weeks

   - Scale triggers (when to scale, when to kill)
