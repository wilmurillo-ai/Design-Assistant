## ⚙️ The Complete Funnel Architecture

```
THE 7-STAGE HIGH-CONVERTING FUNNEL:

🎣 STAGE 1 — TRAFFIC SOURCE
  └─ Paid: Meta ads / Google ads / TikTok ads
  └─ Organic: SEO / content / social media
  └─ Warm: email list / referrals / affiliates

📄 STAGE 2 — LANDING PAGE (Lead Capture)
  └─ ONE goal: capture email address
  └─ Above the fold: headline + subhead + form + CTA
  └─ No navigation, no distractions, no escape routes
  └─ Conversion target: 30-50% opt-in rate

🎁 STAGE 3 — THANK YOU PAGE (Tripwire Offer)
  └─ Immediately after opt-in: low-ticket offer ($7-47)
  └─ "Wait — before you access your free download..."
  └─ Conversion target: 5-15% purchase rate
  └─ This pays for your ad spend

🎥 STAGE 4 — VSL PAGE (Core Offer)
  └─ Video Sales Letter: 10-20 minute presentation
  └─ Problem → agitation → solution → proof → offer → CTA
  └─ Conversion target: 1-5% of page visitors
  └─ This is where the real revenue lives

⬆️ STAGE 5 — ORDER BUMP
  └─ On the checkout page: complementary add-on
  └─ One click to add — no separate checkout
  └─ Price: 20-50% of main offer
  └─ Conversion target: 20-40% of buyers

🚀 STAGE 6 — UPSELL PAGE (OTO)
  └─ Immediately after purchase: the next logical step
  └─ "Your order is confirmed. One more thing..."
  └─ Price: 1-3x the main offer
  └─ Conversion target: 15-30% of buyers

📧 STAGE 7 — EMAIL AUTOMATION (GetResponse)
  └─ Non-buyers: nurture sequence → re-offer in 7 days
  └─ Buyers: onboarding + ascension sequence
  └─ Upsell missed: 3-email sequence for OTO non-buyers
  └─ Long-term: monthly value emails + quarterly re-offers
```

## ⚙️ Full Workflow

```
INPUT: Your product/offer + target audience + price point
        ↓
STEP 1 — Buyer Journey Mapping
  └─ Google: what does your audience search at each stage?
  └─ Reddit: what objections appear before purchase?
  └─ Competitor ads: what messaging gets them to click?
  └─ Output: complete awareness → consideration → decision map
        ↓
STEP 2 — Competitor Funnel Reverse Engineering
  └─ Top 3 competitors: opt-in page → thank you → VSL → checkout
  └─ What's their hook? Their offer structure? Their price point?
  └─ What's missing in their funnel? (your improvement angle)
        ↓
STEP 3 — Funnel Architecture Design
  └─ Lead magnet: what free thing triggers the journey?
  └─ Tripwire: what $7-47 offer converts cold traffic to buyers?
  └─ Core offer: the main product + positioning
  └─ Order bump + upsell: the revenue multipliers
        ↓
STEP 4 — Complete Copy Package
  └─ Landing page: headline + subhead + bullets + CTA
  └─ Thank you page: tripwire pitch + urgency element
  └─ VSL script: full 10-minute video sales letter
  └─ Checkout page: order bump copy
  └─ Upsell page: OTO pitch
  └─ Each page: tested headline variants (A/B)
        ↓
STEP 5 — GetResponse Automation Build
  └─ Opt-in confirmation → lead magnet delivery
  └─ Non-buyer nurture sequence (7 emails)
  └─ Buyer onboarding sequence (5 emails)
  └─ Upsell missed sequence (3 emails)
  └─ Long-term ascension sequence (monthly)
        ↓
STEP 6 — Revenue Model
  └─ Conservative: 1% conversion on core offer
  └─ Base: 2.5% conversion
  └─ Optimistic: 4% conversion
  └─ Monthly revenue at 100, 500, 1000 daily visitors
        ↓
STEP 7 — InVideo AI Produces VSL + Explainer
  └─ VSL: 10-minute video sales letter for core offer
  └─ Short explainer: 60-second funnel overview for ads
  └─ Both use persuasion structure from copy
        ↓
OUTPUT: Buyer journey map + funnel architecture + all copy + GetResponse automation + revenue model + videos
```
