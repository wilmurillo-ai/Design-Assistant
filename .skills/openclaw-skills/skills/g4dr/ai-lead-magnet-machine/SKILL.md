## ⚙️ The Lead Magnet Conversion Formula

```
THE 5 LAWS OF AN IRRESISTIBLE LEAD MAGNET:

⚡ LAW 1 — HYPER SPECIFIC (Not "Productivity Tips" → "The 3-Hour Morning System")
  └─ Specific promise = specific audience = higher conversion
  └─ "Improve your business" converts at 2%
  └─ "Get your first freelance client in 14 days" converts at 35%

🎯 LAW 2 — ONE PROBLEM, ONE SOLUTION
  └─ Solves a single burning problem completely
  └─ Not "everything about X" — "the exact solution to Y"
  └─ People subscribe for relief from one specific pain

⚡ LAW 3 — INSTANT GRATIFICATION
  └─ Deliverable in under 15 minutes
  └─ A checklist they can use today beats a 50-page guide
  └─ Time to value: immediate

💎 LAW 4 — PERCEIVED VALUE EXCEEDS $47
  └─ If you sold it, they'd pay at least $47
  └─ Templates, swipe files, calculators = high perceived value
  └─ "Free cheat sheet" < "Done-for-you template worth $97"

🔑 LAW 5 — GATEWAY TO YOUR PAID OFFER
  └─ Solves problem #1 → creates awareness of problem #2
  └─ Problem #2 = what your paid product solves
  └─ Lead magnet = the first step of your customer journey

TOP-CONVERTING FORMATS BY NICHE:
  B2B / Business: Templates, swipe files, calculators — 40-55% opt-in
  Health / Fitness: Meal plans, workout programs, quizzes — 35-50% opt-in
  Finance: Spreadsheets, checklists, calculators — 38-52% opt-in
  Marketing: Swipe files, frameworks, checklists — 42-58% opt-in
  Personal development: Quizzes, assessments, guides — 30-45% opt-in
```
