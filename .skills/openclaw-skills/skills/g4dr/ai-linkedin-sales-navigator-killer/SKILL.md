## ⚙️ Full Workflow

```
INPUT: ICP filters + your offer + personalization preferences
        ↓
STEP 1 — LinkedIn ICP Search (Replicating Sales Nav)
  Filter by: job title + seniority + industry + company size + location
  Secondary filter: recent activity (posted in last 30 days = active)
  Exclude: competitors, students, irrelevant seniority levels
  Output: 500-2,000 matching LinkedIn profiles
        ↓
STEP 2 — Decision Maker Intelligence Scrape
  Per contact: headline, summary, experience, skills, connections
  Per company: size, growth, industry, recent news, funding
  Tech stack: tools mentioned in profile or company description
        ↓
STEP 3 — Recent LinkedIn Activity Scrape
  Last 5 posts per contact: full text + engagement stats
  Recent comments: what conversations are they joining?
  Shared content: what articles/opinions do they amplify?
  This data = your personalization weapon
        ↓
STEP 4 — Buying Signal Detection
  Post about problem you solve: IMMEDIATE OUTREACH signal
  Hiring signal for relevant role: BUDGET signal
  Funding announced: BUDGET UNLOCKED signal
  Job change to new role: HONEYMOON PERIOD signal
  Competitor frustration post: SWITCH READY signal
        ↓
STEP 5 — Email Verification
  Find verified direct email per contact
  Validate: not generic, not bounced, active domain
  Fallback: LinkedIn connection + DM if no email found
        ↓
STEP 6 — Personalization Generation
  Per contact: Claude reads their latest post + generates
  a one-sentence opener referencing their specific content
  Example: "Your post about [specific topic] on [date] made
  me think of something relevant for [their role] at [company]."
  No templates — every opener is unique
        ↓
STEP 7 — GetResponse Segmentation & Sequences
  Segment A: Active buyers (post about your problem space)
  Segment B: Budget signals (funding + hiring)
  Segment C: Warm ICP (match, no signal yet)
  Each segment: different sequence, different angle, different cadence
        ↓
OUTPUT: Prospect list + activity intel + personalized openers + GetResponse campaigns live
```
