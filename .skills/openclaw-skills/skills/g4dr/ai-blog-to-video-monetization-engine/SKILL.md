## ⚙️ Full Workflow

```
INPUT: Your blog URL + niche + monetization goals
        ↓
STEP 1 — Blog Content Audit
  └─ Scrape all posts from your blog
  └─ Extract: title, word count, publish date, topic, estimated traffic
  └─ Score each post by video monetization potential
  └─ Output: ranked list of best posts to convert first
        ↓
STEP 2 — Competitor Video Gap Analysis
  └─ Identify top 5 competitor blogs in your niche
  └─ Check: which of their posts exist as YouTube videos?
  └─ Find: posts with high text traffic but no video version
  └─ These are your highest-opportunity video topics
        ↓
STEP 3 — Post-to-Video Conversion Plan
  └─ Top 10 posts selected for conversion
  └─ Per post: video format recommendation + hook + title variations
  └─ Pictory submission instructions for each
        ↓
STEP 4 — Pictory AI Video Production
  └─ Submit each blog URL to Pictory Article-to-Video
  └─ Pictory generates: visuals + AI voiceover + captions
  └─ Customize: thumbnail text + brand colors + CTA screen
  └─ Output: 5 video formats per post
        ↓
STEP 5 — Monetization Layer Per Video
  └─ YouTube description: affiliate links mapped to content
  └─ Video CTA: lead magnet offer → GetResponse landing page
  └─ Pinned comment: most relevant product/offer link
  └─ End screen: subscribe + related video + free resource
        ↓
STEP 6 — GetResponse Email Capture System
  └─ Each video links to a niche-specific lead magnet
  └─ GetResponse landing page: captures email in exchange
  └─ Welcome sequence: 5 emails, delivers value + soft pitch
  └─ Segmentation: tag subscribers by video topic = better targeting
        ↓
STEP 7 — 90-Day Distribution Calendar
  └─ Which video on which platform on which day
  └─ Cross-platform scheduling: same content, optimized per platform
  └─ Re-promotion: republish top performers every 90 days
        ↓
OUTPUT: Post ranking + 10 video production briefs + monetization map + GetResponse setup + 90-day calendar
```
