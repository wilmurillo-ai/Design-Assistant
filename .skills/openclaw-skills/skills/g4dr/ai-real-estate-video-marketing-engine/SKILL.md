## ⚙️ The Property Marketing Intelligence Framework

```
THE 5-LAYER PROPERTY MARKET ANALYSIS:

LAYER 1 — COMPARABLE MARKET ANALYSIS (via Apify Zillow Scraper)
  Last 90 days sales within 0.5 miles: price per sqft
  Active competing listings: price + days on market
  Withdrawn listings: what price didn't sell? (avoid)
  Price reduction patterns: where is the market moving?
  Absorption rate: months of supply in this micro-market

LAYER 2 — BUYER DEMAND INTELLIGENCE (via Apify Google + Reddit)
  Google search volume: "[neighborhood] homes for sale"
  Reddit r/RealEstate: are buyers discussing this area?
  Facebook groups: active buyer posts in local community
  Instagram hashtag activity: is this neighborhood trending?
  LinkedIn: any corporate offices opening nearby? (creates demand)

LAYER 3 — NEIGHBORHOOD SELLING POINTS (via Apify Google Maps)
  Schools: ratings, distance, names (top search filter for families)
  Transport: stations, bus routes, commute times
  Restaurants + cafés: walkability + lifestyle signals
  Parks + outdoor spaces: family appeal
  New developments: coffee shops, gyms, retail openings = gentrification

LAYER 4 — COMPETITIVE LISTING ANALYSIS (via Apify Realtor.com)
  How are competing listings presenting themselves?
  What photography and video do they use?
  What are their listing descriptions missing?
  Days on market vs list price: which listings are winning?
  Price cut frequency: desperation signals in competition

LAYER 5 — FUTURE VALUE SIGNALS (via Apify Twitter + Google News)
  Infrastructure announcements: new transport links = value uplift
  Business relocations: new employers = worker demand
  Gentrification signals: are premium brands moving in?
  School rating improvements: family buyer magnet
  Crime trend data: improving areas attract premium buyers
```
