## ⚙️ The Conference Lead Formula

```
THE 3-PHASE CONFERENCE LEAD SYSTEM:

📅 PHASE 1 — PRE-EVENT (1-2 weeks before)
  └─ Find every speaker: research their topic + recent posts
  └─ Find sponsors: detect their budget level + ICP alignment
  └─ Find attending companies: LinkedIn event attendees
  └─ Send pre-event outreach: "Looking forward to your session on X"
  └─ Goal: 20% book a meeting BEFORE the event even starts

🤝 PHASE 2 — AT THE EVENT (day-of)
  └─ Prioritized hit list: who to meet in what order
  └─ Conversation openers per person: based on their session/post
  └─ QR code to your networking video (instant credibility)
  └─ Goal: 30 meaningful conversations, not 100 card swaps

📬 PHASE 3 — POST-EVENT (within 24 hours)
  └─ Reference something SPECIFIC from their talk or conversation
  └─ LinkedIn connection + personal note
  └─ Email with value add (article, tool, or relevant insight)
  └─ Goal: 50%+ reply rate vs 5% with generic "great to meet you"
```

## ⚙️ Full Workflow

```
INPUT: Event name + your offer + your ICP
        ↓
STEP 1 — Event Intelligence Scrape
  └─ Event website: speaker list, agenda, sponsor list
  └─ LinkedIn: companies and people registered for event
  └─ Twitter: event hashtag — who's announced they're attending
  └─ Extract: names, titles, companies, session topics
        ↓
STEP 2 — Contact Scoring
  └─ Buying authority: C-suite / VP / Director / Manager
  └─ Company fit: does their business match your ICP?
  └─ Budget signal: company size + growth + funding
  └─ Accessibility: are they speaking? (easier to approach)
        ↓
STEP 3 — Session Research
  └─ For each speaker: what is their session about?
  └─ What's their angle? Controversial? Aligned with your offer?
  └─ Their recent LinkedIn posts: what do they care about this week?
  └─ This = your personalization weapon
        ↓
STEP 4 — Pre-Event Outreach (2 weeks before)
  └─ LinkedIn connection request per target
  └─ Personal note references their session topic
  └─ Goal: meeting booked before event = highest quality conversation
        ↓
STEP 5 — Event Day Agenda
  └─ Prioritized hit list: who to find at which session/break
  └─ Conversation starter per person
  └─ Suggested meeting slots + locations
        ↓
STEP 6 — Post-Event Follow-Up (within 24 hours)
  └─ For speakers: reference specific thing they said in their talk
  └─ For attendees: reference your conversation or shared session
  └─ Email + LinkedIn + value-add resource
        ↓
STEP 7 — InVideo AI Produces Networking Video
  └─ 60-second "who I am and what I do" video
  └─ Share as QR code at event
  └─ Send in follow-up emails = 4x response rate
        ↓
OUTPUT: 50 scored contacts + pre-event outreach + day-of agenda + post-event follow-ups + video
```
