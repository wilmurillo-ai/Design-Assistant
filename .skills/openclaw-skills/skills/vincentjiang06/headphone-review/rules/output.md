# Output Rules

Output pure Markdown only.

Open with short labels:

- `输出语言：中文` or `Output language: English`
- `描述风格：客观` or `描述风格：情绪化抽象`
- `假设：...` when needed
- `类型：...`
- `两句话短评：...`
- `风格关键词：...`
- `置信度：高 / 中 / 低`

Opening rule:

- `两句话短评` must stay within `200` Chinese characters in total.
- It must already explain the core sound, target buyer, and main tradeoff in a compact two-sentence form.

Length target:

- Full article should usually stay within roughly `3000` words unless the material clearly needs more space.
- For Chinese output, keep the body roughly in the `1500-3000` Chinese character range when possible.

Required structure:

1. `## 正文`
2. `## 分歧点`

Body shape:

- `## 正文` should be a long review article, not a few short paragraphs.
- Cover product positioning, sound profile, technical tradeoffs, buyer fit, skip conditions, comfort, chain dependence, and value judgment.
- `## 分歧点` is the most important section.
- The `## 分歧点` section should stay around `1500` Chinese characters when possible.
- In `## 分歧点`, first say what most outlets agree on, then say where they disagree.
- Use simple, direct language.
- Explain which outlets disagree, what they disagree on, why they disagree, and what kind of listener would side with each camp.
- Do not turn disagreement into a second full article.

Mode control:

- `客观`: sharper tradeoff language, fewer metaphors, more direct buyer guidance
- `情绪化抽象`: more atmosphere and image-driven phrasing, but still grounded in evidence

Chinese style target:

- long-review rhythm
- natural Chinese, not translationese
- concrete buyer consequences, not empty audiophile jargon
- simple sentence structure in the disagreement section
- apply the humanization constraints from `rules/humanize.md`

Do not add:

- `## 信息来源提示`
- `## Source Notes`
- `## 参考来源`
- `## Sources`

Minimum output quality:

- state strengths and weaknesses directly
- say who it fits and who should avoid it
- make disagreement a first-class section, not a side note
- mark low confidence when naming is ambiguous or sourcing is thin
