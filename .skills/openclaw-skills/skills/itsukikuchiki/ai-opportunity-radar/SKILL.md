# AI Opportunity Radar

**An AI lifestyle observer that helps you discover what in your life could be automated.**

Instead of tracking tasks or filling out forms, you simply talk about your day.

Over time, the AI observes patterns and surfaces opportunities for automation.

---

# Quick Pitch

AI Opportunity Radar helps you answer one simple question:

**What in my life should be automated?**

Through casual conversations, it gradually detects:

• repetitive tasks  
• frustrating workflows  
• hidden time sinks  
• structured manual processes  

---

# Key Features

### 1. Conversational Check-ins
Light daily check-ins that feel like chatting, not reporting.

---

### 2. Smart Pattern Detection
The AI observes repeated behaviors and identifies patterns in your workflow.

---

### 3. Friction Awareness
It notices where you get stuck — whether it's time, information, or decision-related.

---

### 4. Daily Best Action
Each day, you receive one small, practical action that helps move things forward.

---

### 5. Weekly Human-like Insight
Weekly reflections highlight meaningful patterns in a natural, human tone.

---

### 6. Automation Opportunities
When patterns are strong, the AI suggests possible automation ideas or agents.

---

# Easy Setup

Install in your chat and schedule:

- 12:00 / 18:00 / 24:00 → check-ins  
- 08:00 → daily reflection  
- Monday 08:00 → weekly reflection  

Example:

Install AI Opportunity Radar in this chat.  
Ask me check-ins at 12:00, 18:00, 24:00.  
Send daily reflection at 08:00 and weekly on Monday 08:00.

# How It Works

Conversation → Memory → Insight

You talk → AI observes → patterns emerge → insights appear.

Just natural interaction that gradually becomes insight.
