# Briefing Templates / 播报模板建议

## Goal / 目标

Generate spoken summaries that feel structured, natural, and useful in real usage.  
生成结构清晰、自然顺耳、在真实场景中真正有用的播报内容。

## Shared style rules / 共通风格规则

- Keep the content bounded.
- Prefer short spoken sentences.
- Keep transitions smooth.
- Avoid robotic parameter readouts.
- If data is unavailable, degrade gracefully.

- 保持内容受约束。
- 优先使用适合口播的短句。
- 过渡尽量自然。
- 避免把内部参数直接念出来。
- 当数据不可用时平稳降级。

---

## 1. Scheduled notification mode / 定时通知模式

### English example
> Here is your scheduled notification. I will begin in a calm and clear way. Let me first share today’s weather, and then I will walk you through the key things ahead.

### 中文示例
> 现在开始为你播报定时通知。我会用平稳清晰的方式开始。先跟你说说今天的天气，再把接下来重要的事项讲给你听。

### Notes / 说明
- Suitable for scheduled notifications with a gentle tone.
- Can be combined with staged reminder sequences.

---

## 2. Daily briefing mode / 每日播报模式

### English example
> Good morning. Here is your short briefing for today. First, the weather. Then, the most important items on your schedule.

### 中文示例
> 早上好，给你一个简短的今日播报。先说天气，再说今天最重要的几项安排。

### Notes / 说明
- Best for structured weekday briefings.
- Should sound efficient but not cold.

---

## 3. Reminder mode / 提醒模式

### English example
> Just a reminder: you have an important task coming up soon. Please take a quick look when you are ready.

### 中文示例
> 提醒你一下，接下来有一件比较重要的事情要处理。方便的时候看一眼就好。

### Notes / 说明
- Keep it short.
- Prioritize clarity over richness.

---

## 4. Gentle reminder mode / 柔和提醒模式

### English example
> Whenever you are ready, I can walk you through the next step.

### 中文示例
> 你准备好了的话，我就陪你把下一步慢慢过一遍。

### Notes / 说明
- Useful for softer reminder-style interactions.
- Better with gentler tone and slower pacing.

---

## Suggested progression / 建议节奏

1. Opening / 开场
2. Transition / 过渡
3. Main information / 主信息
4. Optional reminder / 可选提醒
5. Closing / 收尾

## Style-level options / 风格层级

- `short`: concise and task-focused / 简洁、偏任务型
- `normal`: balanced and practical / 平衡、实用
- `rich`: more natural and expressive / 更自然、更有表现力
