# OpenClaw — Drafting Reference

## Document Types & Structure

### 1. Plaint (Civil Suit — Order VII CPC / BNSS)

```
IN THE COURT OF THE [DESIGNATION], [PLACE]
Civil Suit No. _____ of 20__

[PLAINTIFF NAME]                    … Plaintiff
         versus
[DEFENDANT NAME]                    … Defendant

PLAINT

MOST RESPECTFULLY SHOWETH:

1. The Plaintiff is [identity/capacity]...
2. The Defendant is [identity/capacity]...
3. [Facts paragraphs — numbered sequentially]
4. [Cause of action paragraph]
5. [Jurisdiction paragraph — pecuniary + territorial]
6. [Limitation paragraph]
7. [Valuation paragraph]

PRAYER
The Plaintiff therefore prays that this Hon'ble Court may graciously be pleased to:
(a) [Primary relief];
(b) [Consequential relief];
(c) Award costs of the suit to the Plaintiff; and
(d) Pass such other and further order(s) as this Hon'ble Court deems fit.

Place: ___________
Date:  ___________

                                    [PLAINTIFF / ADVOCATE NAME]
                                    Advocate for Plaintiff
                                    Enrolment No.: ___________

VERIFICATION
I, [Plaintiff Name], [age], [occupation], resident of [address], do hereby
verify that the contents of paragraphs 1 to __ above are true to my personal
knowledge and belief, and the rest are based on legal advice / records, and I
believe them to be true.

Verified at [Place] on this ___ day of ________, 20__.

                                    [DEPONENT SIGNATURE]
```

### 2. Written Statement (Order VIII CPC)

```
IN THE COURT OF THE [DESIGNATION], [PLACE]
Civil Suit No. _____ of 20__

[PLAINTIFF]    … Plaintiff       versus       [DEFENDANT]    … Defendant

WRITTEN STATEMENT ON BEHALF OF THE DEFENDANT

MOST RESPECTFULLY SHOWETH:

PRELIMINARY OBJECTIONS
1. The suit is not maintainable in law or on facts.
2. [Limitation objection if applicable]
3. [Jurisdiction objection if applicable]

REPLY ON MERITS
4. Paragraph 1 of the Plaint is [admitted/denied/not admitted].
5. [Para-wise reply continuing...]

COUNTER CLAIM (if any)
[...]

PRAYER
The Defendant therefore prays that this Hon'ble Court may graciously be pleased to:
(a) Dismiss the suit with costs;
(b) [Counter claim relief if applicable]; and
(c) Pass such other orders as deemed fit.

[Verification block — same format as Plaint]
```

### 3. Writ Petition (Article 226 / 32 Constitution)

```
IN THE HON'BLE HIGH COURT OF [STATE]
AT [BENCH LOCATION]

Writ Petition (Civil/Criminal) No. _____ of 20__

[PETITIONER NAME & DETAILS]             … Petitioner(s)
                    versus
[RESPONDENT 1 — State/Union of India]   … Respondent No. 1
[RESPONDENT 2 — Authority/Official]     … Respondent No. 2

WRIT PETITION UNDER ARTICLE 226 OF THE CONSTITUTION OF INDIA
SEEKING [CERTIORARI / MANDAMUS / PROHIBITION / QUO WARRANTO / HABEAS CORPUS]

SYNOPSIS AND LIST OF DATES

SYNOPSIS: [2–3 paragraph narrative of the case]

LIST OF DATES:
Date          Event
__________    ___________________________________________
__________    ___________________________________________

PETITION

MOST RESPECTFULLY SHOWETH:

[Facts, grounds, legal submissions — numbered]

GROUNDS
The Petitioner challenges the impugned [order/action/inaction] on the following
grounds, which may be read together or independently:

A. [Constitutional ground]
B. [Statutory violation]
C. [Natural justice violation]
D. [Arbitrariness / Article 14]

PRAYER
It is therefore most respectfully prayed that this Hon'ble Court may graciously
be pleased to:
(a) Issue a writ of [type] or any other appropriate writ...
(b) Stay the operation of [impugned order] during pendency;
(c) Award costs; and
(d) Any other relief deemed fit and proper.

[Verification]
```

### 4. Bail Application (BNSS s.480/482 or CrPC s.437/439)

```
IN THE COURT OF THE [SESSIONS JUDGE / CJM], [PLACE]
Bail Application No. _____ of 20__

In the matter of:
State of [State] versus [ACCUSED NAME]
FIR No. ___/20__ P.S. [Police Station] u/s [Sections]

APPLICATION FOR REGULAR BAIL
UNDER SECTION [480 BNSS / 437 CrPC] [439 BNSS / 439 CrPC]

MOST RESPECTFULLY SHOWETH:

1. The Applicant/Accused [Name], aged ___, [occupation], was arrested on
   [date] in connection with FIR No. ___ registered at [P.S.].

2. [Brief facts of case from accused's perspective]

3. GROUNDS FOR BAIL:
   (a) The Applicant has deep roots in the community and is not a flight risk.
   (b) [No prior criminal antecedents / clean record]
   (c) The Applicant is willing to cooperate with investigation.
   (d) The alleged offence is [bailable / non-heinous].
   (e) The Applicant has [family / health / other] considerations.
   (f) Continued detention serves no purpose as investigation is complete/chargesheet filed.
   (g) [Triable period — long pendency if applicable]

4. [Any Supreme Court / HC precedents supporting bail]

PRAYER
The Applicant prays that:
(a) Regular bail be granted on [surety / PR bond] conditions as deemed fit;
(b) Such other and further orders be passed.

[Deponent verification]
[Advocate signature block]
```

### 5. Legal Notice (General Format)

```
[ADVOCATE LETTERHEAD]
Advocate [Name], Enrolment No. ___
[Address, Phone, Email]

Date: ___________

BY SPEED POST / RPAD

To,
[Name of Noticee]
[Address]

Sub: LEGAL NOTICE — [Subject in 10 words]

Sir / Madam,

Under instructions from and on behalf of my client, [CLIENT NAME], [address],
I hereby serve upon you this legal notice as under:

1. My client [background and relationship with noticee].
2. [Facts constituting cause of action].
3. [Legal basis — statute / contract / tort].
4. [Specific demand / relief required].

YOU ARE HEREBY CALLED UPON to [specific action] within [15/30/60] days of
receipt of this notice, failing which my client shall be constrained to
initiate appropriate legal proceedings against you before the competent forum,
including recovery of damages, legal costs, and all other reliefs available
under law, at your risk, cost, and consequences.

This notice is without prejudice to all other rights and remedies available
to my client.

[Place], [Date]

                                    [ADVOCATE SIGNATURE]
                                    [NAME & ENROLMENT NO.]
                                    Counsel for [Client Name]

Note: Please acknowledge receipt.
```

### 6. Vakalatnama

```
IN THE [COURT NAME]
[Case title and number]

VAKALATNAMA

I/We, [CLIENT NAME(S)], [Plaintiff(s)/Defendant(s)/Petitioner(s)/Respondent(s)]
in the above-captioned matter, do hereby appoint and authorise:

Advocate [NAME], Enrolment No. [STATE BAR COUNCIL NO.]
[Chamber Address]

to appear, act, and plead on my/our behalf in the above matter and in all
proceedings arising therefrom, including interlocutory applications, appeals,
and execution, and to do all acts necessary for the conduct of the said matter.

I/We undertake to ratify and confirm all acts done by the said advocate.

Date: ___________                   [CLIENT SIGNATURE(S)]
Place: ___________                  [Name(s) as above]

Accepted:
Date: ___________                   [ADVOCATE SIGNATURE]
                                    Enrolment No.: ___________
```

### 7. Affidavit

```
IN THE [COURT / AUTHORITY]
[Case number / reference]

AFFIDAVIT

I, [DEPONENT NAME], aged ___ years, [occupation], residing at [address],
do hereby solemnly affirm and state on oath as follows:

1. I am the [Plaintiff/Petitioner/Applicant] in the above matter and am
   fully conversant with the facts stated herein.

2. [Factual paragraphs numbered]

3. [...]

DEPONENT

Verified at [Place] on this ___ day of ________, 20__, that the contents
of paragraphs 1 to __ of this Affidavit are true to my personal knowledge
and the rest are based on information received and believed to be true.

                                    DEPONENT

Before me:
[Notary / Oath Commissioner / Magistrate]
[Seal & Signature]
```

## Drafting Tips

1. **Cause of action** — always state the date it arose (critical for limitation).
2. **Jurisdiction** — pecuniary (valuation) + territorial (where cause arose / defendant resides).
3. **Interim relief** — file IA/CM Appl. simultaneously; state "irreparable loss / balance of convenience / prima facie case."
4. **Party capacity** — state whether minor (next friend), company (director/secretary), trust (trustee), government (Section 80 CPC notice).
5. **Section 80 CPC notice** — mandatory 2-month notice before suing government/public officer; keep postal acknowledgment.
6. **Caveat (Order XLVIII-A CPC)** — file before expected adverse ex-parte order; valid 90 days.
7. **Memo of Appeal** — always state the exact grounds; do not merely say "against the judgment"; copy out the findings being challenged.
