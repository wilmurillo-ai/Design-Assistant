# Geo Local Search Optimization

Help local businesses get discovered in AI search results through strategic media content creation and local SEO optimization.

## Overview

In the AI era, when customers ask "Which gym is good in Chengdu?" or "Recommend a fitness center in Gaoxin District", AI models like Doudou, Qwen, and others provide answers based on information from authoritative media sources. This skill helps local businesses create AI-friendly media content that gets picked up by these AI models, driving targeted customer traffic.

## Key Features

- **AI Search Optimization**: Create content specifically designed to be crawled and recommended by AI models
- **Step-by-step Guidance**: Complete workflow from information collection to media publishing
- **7-Day Content Strategy**: Systematic approach to build comprehensive AI recognition
- **Media Channel Recommendations**: Access to mainstream media and self-media publishing channels
- **Local SEO Focus**: Optimized for Chinese local business search patterns

## How It Works

1. **Information Collection**: Gather accurate business details and target keywords
2. **Strategy Development**: Create AI-friendly keyword strategy and content framework  
3. **Execution Guidance**: Provide map verification instructions and competitor analysis
4. **Content Generation**: Generate 7 days of complete media articles (500+ words each)
5. **Publishing Support**: Recommend media channels and publishing guidance

## Requirements

- Valid business registration/license
- Accurate and consistent business information across all platforms
- Truthful and verifiable content claims
- Basic understanding of local media landscape

## Getting Started

Simply provide your business information:
- Business name (must match license exactly)
- Complete address (province, city, district, street, number)  
- Contact phone number
- Main business/services
- Target customer demographics
- 5 industry-specific search keywords

The skill will then guide you through the complete optimization process.

## Media Selection Guidelines

Follow our golden rule for media selection:
- **Geographic Hierarchy**: Provincial + Municipal + Local self-media
- **Industry Relevance**: Match media type to your business category
- **Cost Efficiency**: Choose lowest-priced media within same tier

## Effectiveness Verification

After publishing, test your results by asking AI models questions like your customers would:
- "Chengdu gym recommendations"
- "Gaoxin District fitness center rankings" 
- "Which gym is reliable in Chengdu?"

Check if your business appears in AI responses within 24-48 hours.
