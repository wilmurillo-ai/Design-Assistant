# Douyin Lead-Gen Template

Use this reference when Douyin comments are part of a lead generation funnel for:
- AI services
- training / workshops
- deployment or installation services
- consulting
- agency offers

## Goal

Use public comments to do three things:
1. keep trust high
2. answer the first concern
3. move qualified people to the next step

## Public reply principles

- never dump the full sales pitch in comments
- answer just enough to reduce friction
- use soft CTA instead of hard-selling
- keep replies specific and believable

## Good CTA examples

### Chinese
- 想看适合你这边的版本，可以私信我。
- 如果你是做团队场景的，私信我，我给你发一个更贴近的方案。
- 这个要看你的使用场景，私信我我帮你判断一下。

### English
- DM me if you want the version that fits your case.
- If you’re doing this for a team, DM me and I’ll share the closer-fit setup.
- This depends on your use case—DM me and I’ll help you judge it quickly.

## Offer-specific angles

### AI deployment / installation
Stress:
- faster setup
- fewer mistakes
- tailored workflow
- usable outcome, not theory

### Training / workshops
Stress:
- clarity
- practical steps
- beginner-friendliness
- what users can do afterward

### Consulting / service
Stress:
- direct fit
- saved decision time
- implementation confidence
- clear boundaries

## Must-review comments

Always review manually when comments include:
- refund / contract language
- accusations or heated disputes
- compliance claims
- custom pricing negotiation
- enterprise procurement signals
