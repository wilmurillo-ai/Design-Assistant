# Style Guide

## Goal
Turn spoken material into readable, publishable WeChat prose.

## Rules

### Remove spoken filler
Delete phrases like:
- 嗯
- 然后
- 就是
- 我觉得
- 其实
- 大家知道吧
unless they carry emotional force.

### Compress repetition
If the speaker repeats the same idea three times, keep the sharpest one.

### Rebuild paragraph rhythm
Spoken language is linear and repetitive. WeChat writing should have:
- hook
- build
- example
- conclusion

### Prefer explicit conclusions
Do not leave the reader to infer the main point from transcript fragments.

### Maintain voice, not raw form
Keep the original conviction and style, but not the messy spoken structure.

## Good article endings
Prefer endings that do one of these:
- sharpen the conclusion
- point to one action
- add one final strong sentence
- place QR CTA naturally if needed
