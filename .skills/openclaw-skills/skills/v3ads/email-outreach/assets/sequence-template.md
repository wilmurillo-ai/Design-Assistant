## Email 4 — Day 14 (Breakup)

**Subject:** Closing the loop  
**Preheader:** No pressure either way

---

{{first_name}},

I've reached out a few times about [topic] — completely understand if the timing's off or it's not the right fit right now.

I'll leave it here. If anything changes, you know where to find me.

[Signature]

P.S. If someone else at {{company}} handles [area], happy to reach out to them instead — just point me in the right direction.

---

*Note: Keep this one SHORT. The brevity is the point. Don't add more selling.*
