# Post Formats by Platform

## LinkedIn

**Best-performing formats:**
- **Text post (no link):** 150–300 words. Hook on line 1. "See more" break at line 3. End with question.
- **Carousel (PDF):** 8–12 slides. First slide = bold claim. Each slide = one idea. Last slide = CTA.
- **Short video:** 60–90 seconds. Subtitles mandatory. Hook in first 3 seconds.
- **Poll:** 4 options. Controversial or opinionated framing outperforms neutral questions.

**Hook formulas that work on LinkedIn:**
- "I [did thing] for [timeframe]. Here's what I learned:"
- "Unpopular opinion: [contrarian take]"
- "Most [job title]s do this wrong:" 
- "[Number] years ago I [failure]. Today [result]. The difference:"
- "Nobody talks about this, but [insight]"

**Optimal posting:** Tue–Thu, 7–9am or 12–1pm local time. 1x/day max.  
**Hashtags:** 3–5, relevant, not overstuffed.

---

## Twitter / X

**Best-performing formats:**
- **Thread:** Hook tweet → 5–10 numbered tweets → summary/CTA tweet. Each tweet standalone-readable.
- **Single take:** One sharp observation. Under 200 chars. No hashtags.
- **Quote tweet:** Add your perspective on trending content.

**Hook formulas:**
- "Thread: [bold claim] 🧵"
- "[Number] things I wish I knew about [topic]:"
- "Hot take: [controversial statement]"
- "[Thing] is dead. Here's what's replacing it:"

**Optimal posting:** 8–10am, 12–1pm, 6–8pm. Up to 3x/day.  
**Hashtags:** 0–2 max. Twitter rewards natural language.

---

## Instagram

**Best-performing formats:**
- **Carousel:** 5–10 slides. Strong visual hook. Text-heavy carousels work for B2B audiences.
- **Reel:** 15–30 seconds. Hook in first 2 seconds. Trending audio if relevant.
- **Story:** Polls, questions, quizzes for engagement. Behind-the-scenes.

**Caption structure:** Hook → Value → CTA → Hashtags (in comment or at bottom)  
**Hashtags:** 5–15, mix of broad and niche.

---

## YouTube / Long Video

**Best-performing formats:**
- **Tutorial/How-to:** "How to [achieve outcome] in [timeframe]"
- **Case study:** "How [company/person] [achieved result]"
- **List:** "[Number] ways to [do thing]"

**Title formulas:** 
- "[Keyword] Tutorial for Beginners (2026)"
- "I Tried [Thing] for [Timeframe] — Here's What Happened"
- "Why [Common Belief] Is Wrong"

**Thumbnail:** Face + emotion + bold text (max 5 words). High contrast.

---

## Blog / Long-Form

**Best-performing formats:**
- **Ultimate guide:** 2,000–5,000 words. Comprehensive. Ranks for head terms.
- **Listicle:** "[X] Best [Things] for [Audience]" — high commercial intent.
- **Comparison:** "[A] vs [B]: Which is Better?" — captures bottom-funnel buyers.
- **Case study:** Real results with data. High trust signal.

**Structure:** H1 → intro (problem + what they'll learn) → H2 sections → conclusion + CTA  
**SEO rule:** Primary keyword in H1, first 100 words, one H2, meta description.
