# Lead Scoring Rubric (1–10)

Score each lead across 4 dimensions. Sum and normalize to 1–10.

## Dimension 1: ICP Fit (0–3 points)

| Criteria | Points |
|---|---|
| Industry matches target vertical exactly | 1 |
| Company size within target range | 1 |
| Job title matches decision-maker profile | 1 |

## Dimension 2: Buying Signals (0–3 points)

| Signal | Points |
|---|---|
| Recently funded (last 6 months) | 1 |
| Actively hiring for relevant roles | 1 |
| Using a competitor / known pain point signal | 1 |
| New exec in target role (last 90 days) | +0.5 (bonus) |
| Product launch / expansion news | +0.5 (bonus) |

Cap at 3.

## Dimension 3: Reachability (0–2 points)

| Criteria | Points |
|---|---|
| Email found or confidently estimated | 1 |
| LinkedIn profile active (recent posts/activity) | 0.5 |
| Direct contact info available | 0.5 |

## Dimension 4: Timing (0–2 points)

| Criteria | Points |
|---|---|
| Q1 or Q3 (budget cycle start — buying season) | 1 |
| Trigger event < 30 days old | 1 |
| Annual contract renewal window (if known) | 1 |

Cap at 2.

---

## Score Interpretation

| Score | Action |
|---|---|
| 8–10 | 🔴 Strike now — personalized outreach within 24h |
| 6–7 | 🟡 High priority — outreach this week |
| 4–5 | 🟢 Pipeline — add to sequence, lower personalization |
| 1–3 | ⬜ Nurture or discard — not worth manual effort now |
