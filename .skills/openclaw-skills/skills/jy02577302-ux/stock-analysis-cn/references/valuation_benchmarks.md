## Valuation Zone Definitions

### For PE (Using 5-year History)
| 当前PE在历史分位 | 估值状态 | 建议 |
|-----------------|----------|------|
| 0-20% | 低估 (Undervalued) | 考虑买入，安全边际高 |
| 20-40% | 偏低 (Slightly low) | 关注，逐步建仓 |
| 40-60% | 正常 (Fair) | 持有，中性 |
| 60-80% | 偏高 (Slightly high) | 谨慎，减仓 |
| 80-100% | 高估 (Overvalued) | 避免追高，等待回调 |

### For PB (Using 5-year History)
- 0-20%: 低估
- 20-40%: 偏低
- 40-60%: 正常
- 60-80%: 偏高
- 80-100%: 高估

## Industry-Specific Adjustments

| 行业 | PE/PB Caveats |
|------|---------------|
| 金融 (银行、保险) | PE天然低（<10），不能用成长股标准。关注PB和股息率 |
| 周期 (有色、钢铁、煤炭) | PE/PB极低时可能是景气顶点，极值时需结合商品价格判断 |
| 科技/创新药 | 高PE是常态，用PEG（PE/利润增速）评估，PEG<1低估 |
| 消费/医药 | 稳定性溢价，PE 30-40可接受，需结合成长性 |
| 公用事业/环保 | 中低PE，类债券，关注股息率 |
| 军工 | 订单驱动，PE参考价值有限 |

## Quick Valuation Checklist

When analyzing any stock/ETF:

1. **Absolute level**: Is current PE/PB above or below historical median?
2. **Percentile**: Where does it stand in 5-year range?
3. **Sector relative**: How does it compare to industry peers? (premium/discount %)
4. **Growth adjustment**: Use PEG for growth stocks: `PEG = PE / (profit_growth_3y * 100)`
   - PEG < 1 → undervalued relative to growth
   - PEG > 2 → overvalued
5. **Context**: Market cycle phase (early expansion vs late cycle) affects acceptable valuation

## Limitations

- **Accounting differences**: Some industries (金融) have different earnings recognition → PE not comparable
- **Structural breaks**: Market regime changes can shift long-term averages
- **ESG & intangible assets**: Modern knowledge economy may justify higher PE
- **Interest rates**: Low rates justify higher multiples (discount rate effect)

Always combine valuation with fundamental quality and macro context.
