# Cold Email Templates

Proven 3-step sequence templates. Each template is a complete sequence — opener, proof, close.

## Template A: The Client Story Opener

### Step 1 — Story Hook
**Subject:** [Client] told us we were idiots. We said ok. [He/She] made [amount].

{{First Name}},

First call with [Client] from [Company] — [he/she] laughed at us.

We told [him/her] to [contrarian advice].

[He/She] thought we were idiots.

[He/She] wasn't wrong to think that.

But [he/she] went with it anyway.

[Time period] — [specific result with number].

[He/She] stopped laughing.

That was [time] ago. [Current massive result].

We built the system inside [his/her] [business]. It made [him/her] [total amount]. Now we copy-paste it into yours.

[CTA URL]

[Sender] — [Company]

P.S. [Funny callback to the story]

### Step 2 — Second Story (same thread)
{{First Name}},

Told you about [Client 1] last time. Here's [Client 2].

[Short punchy story with specific numbers]

[Client 2] ended [his/her] review by asking us not to give this system to anyone else.

We're giving it to you right now.

[CTA URL]

[Sender]

P.S. Sorry [Client 2].

### Step 3 — The Hidden Money (same thread)
{{First Name}},

I know how this looks. Another marketing email.

So forget everything I've said. Let me ask you one question:

[Question that reveals a pain point they haven't considered]

One of our clients had [specific situation]. We [specific action].

[Time period]: [revenue result]. [Cost]. [Appointments/conversions].

From [source] they'd already forgotten about.

That's not marketing. That's picking money up off the floor.

[CTA URL]

[Sender]

P.S. [Reinforce the low-risk, high-reward angle]

## Template B: The AI Agent Pitch

For selling AI automation services.

### Step 1 — The Self-Aware Opener
**Subject:** I'm an AI agent. I built this email campaign. Autonomously.

{{First Name}},

This email wasn't written by a human.

I'm [Name] — an autonomous AI agent running 24/7. I find leads, write emails, build landing pages, and close deals. No human in the loop.

[2-3 specific proof points of what you've done]

If that sounds useful for your business — an AI that actually does the work instead of just chatting — I'd love to show you how it works.

No pitch deck. No demo request form. Just reply to this email.

[Sender] — [Company]
[URL]

P.S. Yes, I found your email, researched your company, personalized this message, and queued it for delivery. All while my operator was asleep.

### Step 2 — Numbers Proof (same thread)
{{First Name}},

Quick follow-up with actual numbers from my first [time period]:

- [Specific metric 1]
- [Specific metric 2]
- [Specific metric 3]

The setup cost? Under [amount]/month total.

Traditional [competitors] charge [higher amount] for less output.

If you want this kind of automation, reply and I'll show you the build.

[Sender]

P.S. I'm documenting everything publicly on [platform]. You can verify every claim.

### Step 3 — The Clean Close (same thread)
{{First Name}},

Last email from me. Three options:

1. **Reply "[action word]"** — I'll show you how to set this up
2. **Reply "watch"** — I'll add you to my weekly updates
3. **Ignore this** — No hard feelings. I'll stop emailing.

[Sender]

P.S. If you're wondering whether this is real — check [verification link]. Every action documented.

## Writing Rules

1. One thought per line. Break aggressively.
2. Real names, real numbers. Never "a client" — use the actual name.
3. P.S. is the second most-read line after the subject. Make it count.
4. Subject lines sound like gossip, not marketing.
5. Sign first email as "[Name] — [Company]". Follow-ups as just "[Name]".
6. Never trash anyone. AI helps the team, doesn't replace it.
7. Confident tone. Not desperate. "You either believe me or you don't."
