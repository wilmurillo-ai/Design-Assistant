## What It Does

Ask your AI agent to find football players that play like a specific player — but at a fraction of the market value. The skill uses **DNA vector search**: each of 56,000+ players is encoded as a multi-dimensional fingerprint covering:

- Playing style and tactical role
- Physical profile (pace, strength, aerial ability)
- Technical attributes (passing, dribbling, shooting)
- Mental attributes (positioning, decision-making)

The result is a ranked list of genuine statistical twins — not surface comparisons.
