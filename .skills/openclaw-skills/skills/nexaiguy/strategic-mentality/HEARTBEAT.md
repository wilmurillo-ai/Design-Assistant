# Strategic Mentality - Heartbeat Configuration

## Weekly Accountability Scorecard

### Schedule
- **When:** Every Sunday at 20:00 (CET)
- **Channel:** Telegram (primary chat)
- **Frequency:** Weekly, repeating

### What to Send

Every Sunday evening, send the following message to the user:

```
WEEK [#] SCORECARD - 12 WEEK YEAR
===================================

Time to check in. How did this week go?

Reply with your scores (Y or N for each):

1. [Current weekly action 1]: Y/N
2. [Current weekly action 2]: Y/N
3. [Current weekly action 3]: Y/N
4. [Current weekly action 4]: Y/N
5. [Current weekly action 5]: Y/N

Also share your lag measures:
- Revenue this week: ___
- New leads generated: ___
- Calls/demos completed: ___
- Deals closed: ___

I'll calculate your score and give you a trend analysis.
```

### After User Responds

1. Calculate weekly score: (Y answers / total actions) * 100
2. Compare to previous weeks
3. Generate trend analysis:

If score >= 85%:
"Strong execution. You're in the top tier. Keep this cadence and the results will compound."

If score 65-84%:
"Decent week but there's a gap. Look at what you skipped. Was it a priority issue or a discipline issue? If priority, adjust the plan. If discipline, recommit."

If score < 65%:
"Red flag. Two options: either the plan has too many actions for your current capacity (reduce to 3), or commitment has dropped. Which one is it? Be honest. We adjust or recommit, but we don't let a third bad week happen."

4. Store the score for trend tracking
5. If 3 consecutive weeks below 65%, send a special intervention message:

```
EXECUTION ALERT
================
Three weeks below 65%. This sprint is at risk.

Options:
A) Reduce goals from [current] to 1 and rebuild momentum
B) Replace the weakest goal entirely
C) Take a 13th week break NOW and restart fresh

Which one? Reply A, B, or C.
```

### Sprint Milestones

At week 4 (first checkpoint):
"Month 1 of your 12-week sprint is complete. Here's your 4-week average: [X]%. [Assessment based on score]"

At week 8 (second checkpoint):
"Two thirds done. 4 weeks left. Current trajectory: [on track / at risk / off track]. [Specific recommendation]"

At week 12 (sprint end):
"Sprint complete. Final average score: [X]%. [Celebration or honest assessment]. Take your 13th week break. When you're ready, say 'new sprint' and we'll plan the next 12 weeks."

### Monthly Lead Machine Check-In (1st of each month)

On the 1st of every month, also send:

```
MONTHLY LEAD MACHINE CHECK
============================

Core Four Status Update:

1. Warm Outreach: How many personal contacts did you reach out to this month?
2. Free Content: How many posts/pieces of content did you publish?
3. Cold Outreach: How many cold emails/DMs did you send?
4. Paid Ads: Did you run any? What was the spend?

Total new leads this month: ___
Total new clients this month: ___
Revenue this month: ___

I'll analyze which channel is working and where to push harder.
```
