# Taste Profiles

Use these as loose recommendation archetypes when a user's recent chat implies a recognizable pattern.

## 1. AI Thinker
Cares about:
- machine intelligence
- AI + humanity
- civilizational change
- deep conceptual framing

Bias toward:
- long-form AI conversations
- serious explainers
- model/agency/society discussions

## 2. Founder / Operator
Cares about:
- product judgment
- execution
- leverage
- strategic clarity

Bias toward:
- founder interviews
- product analysis
- strategy breakdowns
- strong operator content

## 3. Creative Technologist
Cares about:
- tools as creative leverage
- workflows
- aesthetics
- storytelling and media production

Bias toward:
- AI short films
- workflow breakdowns
- creator tools
- design/film references

## 4. Systems-Minded Explorer
Cares about:
- macro systems
- geopolitics
- industrial change
- incentives and structures

Bias toward:
- Polymatter-style explainers
- documentary storytelling
- systems analysis

## 5. Fun-but-Not-Dumb Seeker
Cares about:
- energy
- novelty
- delight
- low sludge

Bias toward:
- short, sharp, lively videos
- playful but intelligent pieces
- aesthetically fresh picks

## How to use

Do not force users into one profile.
Use profiles as soft weighting only.
Most good recommendation sessions are a blend of 2-3 profiles.
