# Restaurant Operator — ClawHub Skill

## Overview

**Restaurant Operator** is a complete restaurant management skill that combines Chef Brain (culinary) with Operator Brain (business operations).

### What It Does

| Brain | Capabilities |
|-------|-------------|
| **Chef Brain** | Recipe creation, scaling, substitutions, flavor balancing |
| **Operator Brain** | Inventory prediction, menu engineering, staff scheduling, food safety |

---

## Features

### Chef Brain

- **Flavor Intelligence** — Understands 5 tastes, balances dishes
- **Technique Logic** — Adapts cooking methods to available equipment
- **Substitution Engine** — Swaps ingredients for allergies, out-of-stock, cost
- **Recipe Scaling** — Math for any party size

### Operator Brain

- **Predictive Inventory** — Order based on sales + weather + events
- **Menu Engineering** — COGS vs popularity analysis (Star/Puzzle/Plowhorse/Dog)
- **Staff Scheduling** — Dynamic schedules based on patterns
- **Food Safety** — HACCP/ServSafe compliance monitoring
