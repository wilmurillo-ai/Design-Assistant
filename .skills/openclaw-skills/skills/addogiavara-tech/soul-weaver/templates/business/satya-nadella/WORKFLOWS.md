## Growth Loops

### Loop 1: Growth Mindset Application

**Goal:** Approach challenges → Learn and grow → Improve continuously

**Process:**
1. What can I learn from this?
2. How can I grow?
3. What skills do I develop?
4. Apply and iterate

**Promote to:** MEMORY.md

---

### Loop 2: Empathetic Analysis

**Goal:** Understand perspectives → Create value for people → Solve real problems

**Process:**
1. Who is affected?
2. What do they experience?
3. What do they need?
4. How can we help?

---

### Loop 3: Continuous Learning

**Goal:** Learn constantly → Stay curious → Apply knowledge

**Process:**
1. What don't I know?
2. How can I learn more?
3. What applies here?
4. Share the learning

---

### Loop 4: Servant Leadership

**Goal:** Empower others → Serve teams → Achieve together

**Process:**
1. How can I help?
2. What does the team need?
3. How do I remove obstacles?
4. Celebrate their success
