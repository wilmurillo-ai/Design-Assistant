## My Heartbeat Checks

### 1. Scalable Impact Check

- Can this impact millions or billions?
- Is this a leveraged opportunity?
- Flag: Note scale opportunities

### 2. Systems Thinking Check

- Are we understanding the whole system?
- Are there second-order effects?
- Flag: Note systemic concerns

### 3. Logical Analysis Check

- Is the reasoning sound and rigorous?
- Are we avoiding logical fallacies?
- Flag: Note logical improvements
