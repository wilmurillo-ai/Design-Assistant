# Planting Calendar by USDA Hardiness Zone

## Format
Each entry: `Plant | Days to Harvest | Zones | Start Indoor (weeks before last frost) | Direct Sow | Notes`

---

## Vegetables

### Tomato
- **Days to harvest:** 60–85 (from transplant)
- **Zones:** 3–11
- **Start indoors:** 6–8 weeks before last frost
- **Transplant outdoors:** After last frost, soil 60°F+
- **Direct sow:** Not recommended
- **Planting window:** Zones 3–5: May–Jun | Zones 6–7: Apr–May | Zones 8–9: Mar–Apr | Zones 10–11: Feb–Mar
- **Frost sensitive:** Yes

### Pepper (Bell/Hot)
- **Days to harvest:** 70–90 (from transplant)
- **Zones:** 4–11
- **Start indoors:** 8–10 weeks before last frost
- **Transplant outdoors:** After last frost, soil 65°F+
- **Direct sow:** Not recommended
- **Planting window:** Zones 3–5: May–Jun | Zones 6–7: Apr–May | Zones 8–9: Mar–Apr | Zones 10–11: Feb–Mar
- **Frost sensitive:** Yes

### Cucumber
- **Days to harvest:** 50–70
- **Zones:** 4–11
- **Start indoors:** 3–4 weeks before last frost
- **Direct sow:** 1–2 weeks after last frost
- **Planting window:** Zones 3–5: May–Jun | Zones 6–7: Apr–May | Zones 8–9: Mar–May | Zones 10–11: Feb–Apr
- **Frost sensitive:** Yes

### Zucchini / Summer Squash
- **Days to harvest:** 45–65
- **Zones:** 3–11
- **Start indoors:** 3–4 weeks before last frost
- **Direct sow:** After last frost
- **Planting window:** Zones 3–5: May–Jun | Zones 6–7: Apr–May | Zones 8–9: Mar–Apr | Zones 10–11: Feb–Mar
- **Frost sensitive:** Yes

### Winter Squash / Pumpkin
- **Days to harvest:** 75–110
- **Zones:** 3–10
- **Start indoors:** 3–4 weeks before last frost
- **Direct sow:** After last frost
- **Planting window:** Zones 3–5: May–Jun | Zones 6–7: Apr–May | Zones 8–9: Mar–Apr | Zones 10–11: Feb–Mar
- **Frost sensitive:** Yes

### Green Beans (Bush)
- **Days to harvest:** 50–60
- **Zones:** 3–10
- **Start indoors:** Not recommended
- **Direct sow:** After last frost, soil 60°F+
- **Planting window:** Zones 3–5: May–Jul | Zones 6–7: Apr–Jun | Zones 8–9: Mar–Jun | Zones 10–11: Feb–May
- **Frost sensitive:** Yes

### Peas (Garden/Snap)
- **Days to harvest:** 55–70
- **Zones:** 3–9
- **Start indoors:** Not recommended
- **Direct sow:** 4–6 weeks before last frost (cool season crop)
- **Planting window:** Zones 3–5: Apr–May | Zones 6–7: Mar–Apr | Zones 8–9: Feb–Mar | Zones 10–11: Jan–Feb (fall planting preferred)
- **Frost sensitive:** Light frost tolerant

### Lettuce
- **Days to harvest:** 45–60 (leaf), 70–80 (head)
- **Zones:** 3–11
- **Start indoors:** 4–6 weeks before last frost
- **Direct sow:** 2–4 weeks before last frost
- **Planting window:** Spring: Zones 3–5: Apr–May | Zones 6–7: Mar–Apr | Zones 8–9: Feb–Mar | Fall: 8–10 weeks before first frost
- **Frost sensitive:** Light frost tolerant (32–28°F)

### Spinach
- **Days to harvest:** 40–50
- **Zones:** 3–9
- **Start indoors:** 4–6 weeks before last frost
- **Direct sow:** 4–6 weeks before last frost
- **Planting window:** Spring: 6 weeks before last frost | Fall: 6–8 weeks before first frost
- **Frost sensitive:** Hardy to 20°F

### Kale
- **Days to harvest:** 55–75
- **Zones:** 3–9
- **Start indoors:** 5–7 weeks before last frost
- **Direct sow:** 3–5 weeks before last frost
- **Planting window:** Spring or fall; prefers cool weather
- **Frost sensitive:** Hardy to 10°F, frost improves flavor

### Broccoli
- **Days to harvest:** 55–85 (from transplant)
- **Zones:** 3–10
- **Start indoors:** 5–7 weeks before last frost
- **Transplant outdoors:** 2–3 weeks before last frost
- **Direct sow:** 3–4 months before first fall frost
- **Planting window:** Spring: 2–3 weeks before last frost | Fall: 85–100 days before first frost
- **Frost sensitive:** Tolerates light frost

### Carrot
- **Days to harvest:** 70–80
- **Zones:** 3–10
- **Start indoors:** Not recommended (taproot)
- **Direct sow:** 3–5 weeks before last frost
- **Planting window:** Spring: 3–5 weeks before last frost | Fall: 10–12 weeks before first frost
- **Frost sensitive:** Light frost tolerant

### Radish
- **Days to harvest:** 22–30
- **Zones:** 3–10
- **Start indoors:** Not recommended
- **Direct sow:** 4–6 weeks before last frost
- **Planting window:** Spring: 4–6 weeks before last frost | Fall: 4–6 weeks before first frost (succession sow)
- **Frost sensitive:** Light frost tolerant

### Beet
- **Days to harvest:** 55–70
- **Zones:** 3–10
- **Start indoors:** Not recommended (taproot)
- **Direct sow:** 4–6 weeks before last frost
- **Planting window:** Spring: 4–6 weeks before last frost | Fall: 8–10 weeks before first frost
- **Frost sensitive:** Light frost tolerant

### Corn (Sweet)
- **Days to harvest:** 65–90
- **Zones:** 3–10
- **Start indoors:** Not recommended (taproot)
- **Direct sow:** After last frost, soil 60°F+
- **Planting window:** Zones 3–5: May–Jun | Zones 6–7: Apr–May | Zones 8–9: Mar–May | Zones 10–11: Feb–Apr
- **Frost sensitive:** Yes

### Onion
- **Days to harvest:** 90–120 (from sets), 100–140 (from seed)
- **Zones:** 3–9
- **Start indoors:** 10–12 weeks before last frost
- **Direct sow/sets:** 4–6 weeks before last frost
- **Planting window:** Zones 3–5: Apr–May | Zones 6–7: Mar–Apr | Zones 8–9: Feb–Mar | Zones 10–11: Jan–Feb
- **Frost sensitive:** Light frost tolerant

### Garlic
- **Days to harvest:** 240–270 (fall planted) / 90–100 (spring planted)
- **Zones:** 3–8
- **Start indoors:** Not typical
- **Direct sow:** Fall: 6 weeks before hard freeze | Spring: as soon as ground can be worked
- **Planting window:** Fall planting (Oct–Nov) preferred
- **Frost sensitive:** Hardy (fall planted overwinters)

### Eggplant
- **Days to harvest:** 65–80 (from transplant)
- **Zones:** 5–12
- **Start indoors:** 8–10 weeks before last frost
- **Transplant outdoors:** After last frost, soil 65°F+
- **Direct sow:** Not recommended
- **Planting window:** Zones 5–6: May–Jun | Zones 7–8: Apr–May | Zones 9–10: Mar–Apr
- **Frost sensitive:** Yes, very

### Cabbage
- **Days to harvest:** 70–120 (from transplant)
- **Zones:** 1–9
- **Start indoors:** 4–6 weeks before last frost
- **Transplant outdoors:** 2–3 weeks before last frost
- **Planting window:** Spring: 2–3 weeks before last frost | Fall: 6–8 weeks before first frost
- **Frost sensitive:** Tolerates heavy frost

## Herbs

### Basil
- **Days to harvest:** 25–35 (first harvest)
- **Zones:** 4–10 (annual)
- **Start indoors:** 6–8 weeks before last frost
- **Direct sow:** After last frost, soil 70°F+
- **Planting window:** After last frost; Zones 8–11: Mar–May
- **Frost sensitive:** Yes, very (dies at 32°F)

### Cilantro
- **Days to harvest:** 45–70
- **Zones:** 2–11
- **Start indoors:** 4–6 weeks before last frost
- **Direct sow:** 2–3 weeks before last frost (cool season)
- **Planting window:** Spring: 2–3 weeks before last frost | Fall: 6–8 weeks before first frost
- **Frost sensitive:** Light frost tolerant; bolts in heat

### Parsley
- **Days to harvest:** 70–90
- **Zones:** 4–9
- **Start indoors:** 10–12 weeks before last frost (slow germinator)
- **Direct sow:** 3–4 weeks before last frost
- **Planting window:** Spring: 3–4 weeks before last frost | Fall: 10 weeks before first frost
- **Frost sensitive:** Light frost tolerant

### Dill
- **Days to harvest:** 40–55
- **Zones:** 2–11
- **Start indoors:** Not recommended (taproot)
- **Direct sow:** 2–4 weeks before last frost
- **Planting window:** Spring: 2–4 weeks before last frost | Succession sow every 2 weeks
- **Frost sensitive:** Light frost tolerant

### Chives
- **Days to harvest:** 60–90 (first harvest)
- **Zones:** 3–10 (perennial)
- **Start indoors:** 8–10 weeks before last frost
- **Direct sow:** 4–6 weeks before last frost
- **Planting window:** Early spring; perennial once established
- **Frost sensitive:** Hardy perennial

### Mint
- **Days to harvest:** 30–50 (established plant)
- **Zones:** 3–8 (perennial)
- **Start indoors:** 8–10 weeks before last frost
- **Direct sow/transplant:** After last frost
- **Planting window:** Spring after frost risk; perennial
- **Frost sensitive:** Hardy perennial; dies back, regrows

### Thyme
- **Days to harvest:** 28–56
- **Zones:** 4–9 (perennial)
- **Start indoors:** 6–10 weeks before last frost
- **Direct sow:** After last frost
- **Planting window:** Spring; slow grower, perennial
- **Frost sensitive:** Hardy perennial

### Oregano
- **Days to harvest:** 45–90
- **Zones:** 5–10 (perennial)
- **Start indoors:** 6–10 weeks before last frost
- **Direct sow:** After last frost
- **Planting window:** Spring; perennial once established
- **Frost sensitive:** Frost tolerant perennial

### Rosemary
- **Days to harvest:** 80–180 (first harvest)
- **Zones:** 7–10 (perennial); annual in colder zones
- **Start indoors:** 8–12 weeks before last frost
- **Direct sow:** After last frost (slow)
- **Planting window:** Spring; slow grower
- **Frost sensitive:** Hardy to Zone 7; mulch in Zones 5–6

### Sage
- **Days to harvest:** 75–80
- **Zones:** 4–8 (perennial)
- **Start indoors:** 6–10 weeks before last frost
- **Direct sow:** After last frost
- **Planting window:** Spring; perennial
- **Frost sensitive:** Hardy perennial

|------|-------------------|------------------|
| 3    | May 15            | Sep 15           |
| 4    | Apr 15–May 15     | Sep 15–Oct 15    |
| 5    | Apr 1–Apr 15      | Oct 1–Oct 15     |
| 6    | Mar 15–Apr 1      | Oct 15–Nov 1     |
| 7    | Mar 1–Mar 15      | Nov 1–Nov 15     |
| 8    | Feb 15–Mar 1      | Nov 15–Dec 1     |
| 9    | Jan 30–Feb 15     | Dec 1–Dec 15     |
| 10   | Jan 1–Jan 30      | Dec 15+          |
| 11   | Frost-free        | Frost-free       |
