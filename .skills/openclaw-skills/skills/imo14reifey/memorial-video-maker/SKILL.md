# Memorial Video Maker — A Life Worth Remembering, Beautifully Told

## Use Cases

1. **Funeral and Memorial Services** — Video tributes for display during funeral services and celebrations of life, assembled from family photos spanning decades with appropriate music and caption overlays.

2. **Anniversary Tributes** — Annual memorial videos for the anniversaries of loss, updated with newly discovered photos and shared with extended family on meaningful dates.

3. **Legacy Projects** — Videos created for still-living elderly relatives that document their life story while they can still contribute memories, photos, and narration.

4. **Military and First Responder Tributes** — Formal tribute videos for veterans and first responders incorporating service photos, medals, and official recognition alongside personal family memories.

## How It Works

Upload your family photos and provide names, dates, and key life moments. Memorial Video Maker assembles the tribute video, adds music and captions, and exports a finished video ready for service display and family sharing.
