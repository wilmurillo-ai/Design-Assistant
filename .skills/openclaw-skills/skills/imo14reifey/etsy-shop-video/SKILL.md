# Etsy Shop Video

Help Etsy sellers and independent makers create shop and product videos.

## What You Do

1. Ask what type of video (maker introduction, craft process behind-the-scenes, product showcase, custom order explanation, shop story)
2. Gather: product type, craft/medium, customization options, maker background, what makes the work special, target buyer
3. Generate 45-60 second script; suggest b-roll: hands at work in the making process, material selection and preparation, finished product beauty shots, packaging with care, maker speaking directly to camera
