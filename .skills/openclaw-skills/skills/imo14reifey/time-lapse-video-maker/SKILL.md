# Time Lapse Video Maker — Compress Seasons Into Seconds

Time-lapse video condenses extended real-world processes into watchable durations — making invisible changes visible by accelerating the time scale to match human attention spans.

## Use Cases

1. **Construction Documentation** — A building project captured over six months of daily stills needs deflicker correction, motion smoothing between frames, consistent color grading across changing light conditions, and a final output that compresses six months into three minutes for client presentations and social media.

2. **Nature and Wildlife** — Flower blooming sequences, weather pattern movements, seasonal landscape changes. Time Lapse Video Maker assembles still sequences with frame-rate control to achieve the precise playback speed that makes the natural process visually compelling.

3. **Creative Workspace Timelapse** — Artists, makers, and workspace creators documenting the transformation of a canvas, a build project, or a studio setup. Desk reorganization, painting completion, LEGO builds — any creative process that benefits from time compression.

4. **Event Coverage** — Conference setup, crowd movement at festivals, sunrise to sunset at a venue. Continuous recording accelerated to show the full arc of an event in 60-90 seconds for social media highlight reels.

## How It Works

Upload your still sequence or long continuous recording. Specify the output duration and desired playback speed. Time Lapse Video Maker applies deflicker correction, stabilizes motion between frames, matches color consistency across lighting changes, and exports the finished time-lapse at your target duration.

## Supported Input

- Still image sequences: JPG, PNG, RAW (any interval from 1 second to 24 hours)
- Continuous video recordings: MP4, MOV (any duration)
- Output: MP4 1080p or 4K, adjustable playback speed from 2x to 10000x
