# Conference Highlight Video — Events That Fill Seats Next Year Too

## Use Cases
1. **Keynote Speaker Clips** — 60-90 second highlight clips from keynote presentations formatted for social media sharing by speakers, sponsors, and attendees.
2. **Attendee Experience Highlights** — Event atmosphere videos capturing networking, exhibition floor, and community moments that communicate the value of in-person attendance.
3. **Sponsor Recognition Videos** — Professional sponsor acknowledgment videos fulfilling sponsorship commitments and providing evidence of brand exposure for renewal discussions.
4. **Same-Day Social Recap** — Real-time highlight content published during and immediately after the event for live social media engagement and FOMO marketing.

## How It Works
Upload your event footage and speaker content. Conference Highlight Video assembles the highlight sequence, adds branded graphics, and exports clips optimized for each platform and the post-event email campaign.
