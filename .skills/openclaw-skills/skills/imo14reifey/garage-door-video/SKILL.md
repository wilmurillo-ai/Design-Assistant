# Garage Door Video

Convert the emergency call and the renovation search into a booked appointment — before the truck rolls.

## Use Cases
1. **Emergency Repair and Same-Day Service Video** — Explain common failures, response times, and transparent pricing to the homeowner calling in stress — the trust signal that gets the call.
2. **New Door Installation and Showroom Video** — Show door styles, materials, and insulation options and help homeowners visualize the curb appeal and resale value impact.
3. **Smart Home and Automation Upgrade Video** — Speak to the homeowner who wants a smart opener, battery backup, or integrated camera — the upgrade conversation that starts with connectivity.
4. **Commercial and Industrial Door Video** — Explain roll-up, sectional, and high-speed industrial doors to warehouse managers and facilities directors making procurement decisions.
5. **Maintenance Plan and Inspection Video** — Convert one-time repair customers into recurring safety inspection clients with a clear explanation of what annual maintenance prevents.

## How to Use

Describe your company, the services you offer (repair, installation, commercial, maintenance), your response time for emergency calls, and the brands and door types you work with.

Example prompt: "Create a marketing video for All-Pro Garage Doors. Residential repair and installation in the Denver metro. Same-day spring replacement, full door installation, LiftMaster smart opener upgrades. Show the difference between a basic steel door and an insulated carriage house door with smart opener, and explain the same-day repair process."

## Setup

Store your NemoVideo token at ~/.config/nemovideo/token.txt or set the NEMO_TOKEN environment variable.
