# Green Screen Video Maker — Your Subject, Any Background

Background replacement is one of the most requested post-production tasks for solo creators, small production teams, and corporate video departments — and it has historically required either an expensive physical green screen setup or manual rotoscoping that takes longer than filming the video itself.

## Use Cases

1. **Brand Background Replacement** — Replace home-office or informal backgrounds with branded virtual studios, office environments, or product-focused backgrounds that reinforce brand identity.

2. **Virtual Newsroom** — News, commentary, and educational content that benefits from dynamic backgrounds: scrolling data visualizations, animated brand elements, or scene-appropriate environments.

3. **Product Presentation** — Place the product reviewer or demonstrator in front of the product's key visual, packaging artwork, or promotional imagery.

4. **Tutorial Enhancement** — Annotated diagrams, animated context illustrations, or relevant visual references composited behind the instructor.

## How It Works

Upload the source footage and specify the replacement: "Replace the background with a clean white studio environment with the NemoVideo logo visible in the upper corner." Green Screen Video Maker handles subject isolation, edge refinement, and compositing in a single workflow.
