# Product Tutorial Video — Features Discovered Are Features Used

## Use Cases

1. **Help Center Videos** — Tutorial videos for every major workflow in your product, embedded in help center articles alongside written instructions for users who prefer video.

2. **YouTube Product Channel** — SEO-optimized tutorial videos for YouTube that capture search traffic from users looking for help with your product category and competitors.

3. **In-App Contextual Help** — Short tutorial clips triggered at key moments in the product flow, showing users exactly how to complete the task they are currently attempting.

4. **Partner and Reseller Training** — Tutorial video libraries for partners, resellers, and agencies who need to understand your product to sell and support it effectively.

## How It Works

Upload screen recordings of the workflow. Specify the key steps and the user goal. Product Tutorial Video adds callouts, chapter markers, and narration, then exports tutorials for each distribution channel.
