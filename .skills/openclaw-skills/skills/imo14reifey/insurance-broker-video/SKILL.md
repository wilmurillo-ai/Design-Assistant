# Insurance Broker Video

Build understanding before the claim arrives — and the client relationship that lasts for decades.

## Use Cases
1. **Coverage Explanation and Education Video** — Explain what a policy covers, what it excludes, and the specific scenarios where it matters — in plain language that clients actually retain.
2. **Lead Generation and Broker Differentiation Video** — Show prospects what an independent broker does differently, and why access to multiple markets produces better outcomes than going direct.
3. **Client Onboarding and Policy Review Video** — Walk new clients through their declarations page and key policy terms so onboarding is not entirely dependent on a call they will forget.
4. **Annual Review and Renewal Communication Video** — Explain policy changes and updated recommendations in a format clients will watch rather than file unread.
5. **Commercial Lines Specialty Video** — Industry-specific content for contractors, restaurants, healthcare, tech, and real estate explaining the coverage gaps common in each sector.

## How to Use

Describe the coverage topic, the specific client audience, and the key concepts you want to communicate. Insurance Broker Video creates educational content that builds trust and genuine understanding before the policy is signed.

Example: "Create a coverage explanation video for homeowners insurance for first-time buyers. Cover the declarations page, dwelling vs personal property coverage, the difference between ACV and replacement cost, and flood exclusion. Tone: reassuring and clear, not salesy. Target audience: buyers 28-40 closing on their first home."

## Setup

Store your NemoVideo token at ~/.config/nemovideo/token.txt or set the NEMO_TOKEN environment variable.
