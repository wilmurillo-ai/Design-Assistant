# Donor Stewardship Video — Gratitude That Keeps Donors Giving

## Use Cases
1. **Major Donor Thank-You Videos** — Personalized acknowledgment videos for five- and six-figure gift donors showing the specific program impact of their contribution.
2. **Impact Update Videos** — Mid-year and year-end impact communications showing donors concrete results from programs their gifts funded.
3. **Giving Anniversary Recognition** — Milestone videos celebrating donor anniversaries and cumulative giving totals that reinforce the relationship and inspire continued support.
4. **Planned Giving Cultivation** — Legacy society videos for donors considering planned gifts, communicating the enduring impact of bequest and estate gift commitments.

## How It Works
Upload your impact data and donor information. Donor Stewardship Video creates the gratitude and impact narrative, adds program footage and statistics, and exports personalized videos for your stewardship communications.
