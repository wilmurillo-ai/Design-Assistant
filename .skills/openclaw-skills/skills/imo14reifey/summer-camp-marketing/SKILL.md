# Summer Camp Marketing — Fill Your Sessions Before School Lets Out

## Use Cases
1. Signature experience and daily schedule videos capturing the energy and specific moments that make your camp the place kids talk about until September — the content that creates the mental picture that drives registration.
2. Counselor introduction and staff culture videos showing the adults responsible for campers — warmth, competence, and genuine investment — the trust signal families need before handing over their child for a week.
3. Session and specialty program differentiation videos showing what STEM track, arts focus, leadership program, and general camp actually look like so families can match their child to the right fit.
4. Early enrollment urgency videos communicating real capacity limits with warm, specific directness that converts January browsers into February registrants.
5. Returning camper and alumni family testimonial videos activating re-enrollment before registration opens to new families.

## How It Works
Describe your camp's age range, session structure, specialty programs, and enrollment calendar. Provide any existing footage, staff bios, or parent testimonials. Summer Camp Marketing builds the video content calendar from early-enrollment season through last-minute capacity push — each piece formatted for the platform where camp-searching parents are making their decision.

## Key Features
- Pickup-line and evening scroll optimized short-form clips for the parent mobile discovery moment
- Enrollment season campaign structure: November teaser → January early-bird → March urgency → June last-spots
- Alumni activation video formats for re-enrollment outreach before public registration opens
- Specialty program differentiation sequences for camps with multiple track offerings

## Output Formats
- Website homepage hero and session page overview videos
- Instagram Reels and Facebook short-form clips for organic parent community sharing
- Email campaign video thumbnails with registration CTA for enrollment sequence automation
- Google Business Profile clips for local "summer camp near me" search visibility
