# Metal Roofing Video — AI Video Marketing for Metal Roofing Contractors & Installers

The homeowner who gets three roofing quotes — $8,500 for asphalt, $9,200 for asphalt premium, and $28,000 for standing seam metal — makes their decision based on the story they've been told before those quotes arrive. If the metal roofing contractor told no story — if the homeowner came to the sales appointment knowing nothing about metal roofing except that it costs more — the $28,000 quote loses almost every time. If the metal roofing contractor sent a 3-minute video before the appointment that showed a 1972 standing seam roof still performing flawlessly, explained the math on 50 years vs. three asphalt replacements, and showed three neighbors comparing their energy bills — the homeowner arrives at the appointment already sold on the category. They're asking about timing and color, not whether metal is worth the price. Metal Roofing Video creates the pre-selling, objection-resolving educational content that changes who wins at the sales table.

  - Net metal roofing premium over 50 years: **$2,500** — plus energy savings, insurance discounts, and resale value

## 2. The Metal vs. Asphalt Education Video

### The Highest-Converting Video Type
The metal vs. asphalt comparison video is the single highest-converting video asset for metal roofing contractors. It addresses the primary objection (price) by reframing the decision (lifetime cost and performance), and does so at the moment the homeowner is most receptive — before the sales conversation.

### Structure That Works
**Open with the problem (0-20 sec)**: "Most homeowners replace their asphalt roof two or three times. Each replacement costs $8,000-$15,000 and takes your family out of the house for a week. There's a roof that you never have to replace."

**Visual comparison (20-60 sec)**: Side-by-side footage — a 15-year-old asphalt roof showing granule loss, curling, and UV degradation vs. a 1978 standing seam metal roof that still looks installation-fresh. The visual comparison does more persuasive work than any statistic.

**The math (60-90 sec)**: Simple 50-year cost comparison with graphics. Not complicated — just the three asphalt replacements vs. one metal installation, with current pricing.

**Performance advantages (90-120 sec)**: Hail resistance (show the dent-test comparison), wind rating (130+ mph vs. asphalt's 60-90 mph), fire resistance (Class A vs. Class C), energy savings (cool roof coatings reducing cooling loads by 15-25%).

**The ask (120-150 sec)**: "If you're thinking about your next roof, let's talk about whether metal makes sense for your home. Free estimate — we'll show you the math for your specific situation."

### Platform Distribution
- **Website homepage**: Most homeowners visit the website before calling — this video on the homepage is the first sales touchpoint
- **YouTube**: "Metal roof vs asphalt — which is better?" is a high-search-volume query; a quality video on this topic drives organic traffic to the contractor's channel for years
- **Google Business Profile**: Video on GBP increases profile engagement by 35% and is seen by homeowners actively searching for local roofers

## 3. Installation Documentation Video

### Why Project Documentation Matters
Metal roofing installation is a craft — the quality of the flashing, the precision of the seam rolling, the attention to penetrations and valleys — distinguishes a 50-year roof from one that fails at year 15. Most homeowners can't evaluate installation quality in a photo. A well-produced installation process video that shows the crew's attention to detail communicates quality that a price comparison never can.

### What to Film on Every Major Project
- **Substrate preparation**: Tear-off, inspection of deck, any repairs — shows the contractor doesn't cut corners
- **Underlayment installation**: The foundation of a proper metal installation — shows technical knowledge
- **Panel fabrication or delivery**: Custom roll-forming on-site or factory-formed panels — shows investment in proper materials
- **Seam rolling (standing seam)**: The mechanical seaming process is visually distinctive and signals professional-grade installation
- **Flashing and penetrations**: Chimney, skylights, vents — these are where inferior installations fail; showing careful execution builds confidence
- **Completed aerial view**: Drone footage of the finished roof in context with the home

### Project Video Specifications
| Format | Duration | Primary Use |
|---|---|---|
| Full documentation | 4-8 min | Website portfolio, YouTube |
| Before/after reveal | 60-90 sec | Social media, Google Business |
| Quick project reel | 30-45 sec | Instagram, TikTok |
| Drone aerial only | 30-45 sec | Instagram, Website |

## 4. Homeowner Testimonial Video

### Why Testimonials Convert in Roofing
Roofing is a high-trust purchase — homeowners are making a $20,000+ decision about a contractor they've never worked with before. The most effective trust-building mechanism is a real customer talking about their experience in their own words.

### The Testimonial Video Formula for Metal Roofing
1. **Who they are + the situation** (0-20 sec): "We've been in this house 22 years. The asphalt roof had been replaced once, and it was starting to go again."
2. **Why metal** (20-40 sec): "We started researching and figured out that we'd be replacing another asphalt roof in 15-20 years. Metal just made more sense long-term."
3. **The experience** (40-70 sec): "The crew was on time every day, they cleaned up at the end of every day, and [contractor name] walked us through everything they were doing. It took four days for our 2,800-square-foot house."
4. **The result** (70-90 sec): "Our energy bills are down noticeably — I'd say 15-20% in summer. And the roof just looks stunning. We get compliments from neighbors constantly."
5. **The endorsement** (final 10 sec): "I would absolutely recommend [contractor name] to anyone thinking about metal roofing."

## 5. Agricultural and Commercial Metal Roofing

### The Agricultural Market
Agricultural buildings — barns, equipment storage, machine sheds — are among the highest-volume metal roofing applications. Farm owners evaluate roofing on durability, minimal maintenance, and total cost of ownership — a value proposition ideally suited to metal.

Agricultural video content that converts:
- **Before/after farm building projects**: Old corrugated steel replaced with new Galvalume — the transformation is dramatic and the practical value (no more leaks, no repainting) is immediately relatable
- **Product durability demonstration**: Scratch-resistance, fade-resistance, corrosion-resistance testing footage
- **References from farm owners**: Credibility peers — "This barn was re-roofed 30 years ago with [material] and it still looks great" from a neighboring farm owner converts other farm owners

### Commercial Roofing Video
Commercial metal roofing projects (warehouses, retail, offices) require content tailored to commercial decision-makers — property managers, facility directors, CFOs:
- **Lifecycle cost analysis**: More data-driven than residential; show 30-year NPV comparison
- **Minimal maintenance documentation**: Show the ongoing maintenance requirements (essentially none for standing seam) vs. TPO or EPDM flat roofing
- **Energy code compliance**: LEED and energy code compliance capabilities for commercial projects
