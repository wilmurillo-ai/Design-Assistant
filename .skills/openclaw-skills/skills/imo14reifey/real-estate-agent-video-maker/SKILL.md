# Real Estate Agent Video Maker

Build the listing video that generates showings, the neighborhood guide that builds authority, and the brand video that wins the listing appointment.

## Use Cases
1. **Property Listing Walkthrough Video** — Show the home's flow, light, and key features — formatted for MLS, Zillow, Instagram Reels, and Facebook Marketplace.
2. **Neighborhood and Community Guide Video** — Build long-term search equity for your farm area by showing the things a relocating buyer can only learn from someone who actually works there.
3. **Market Update and Expert Analysis Video** — Monthly or quarterly content that builds an audience of buyers and sellers who are 6-18 months from a transaction and are paying attention to whoever is educating them.
4. **Agent Introduction and Personal Brand Video** — Answer the questions prospects research before calling: why you do this, who you work with, and what makes working with you different.
5. **Buyer and Seller Education Video Series** — Pre-approval, offer strategy, staging, inspection negotiation — the content that makes you a trusted advisor before the first appointment.

## How to Use

Describe your market, your specialization, and the type of content you want to build. Include your farm area, the type of clients you serve, and any specific listing or educational content you need.

Example prompt: "Create a neighborhood guide video for the Riverside Heights area of Austin, TX. I'm a buyer's agent specializing in first-time homebuyers and relocations from out of state. Cover the school districts, walkability to South Congress, the housing stock mix of bungalows and new builds, and the price range. Make it feel like a local showing the neighborhood, not a marketing brochure."

## Setup

Store your NemoVideo token at ~/.config/nemovideo/token.txt or set the NEMO_TOKEN environment variable.
