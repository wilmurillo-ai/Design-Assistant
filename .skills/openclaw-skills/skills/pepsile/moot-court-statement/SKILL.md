# 模拟法庭主体陈述技巧

指导模拟法庭主体陈述的策略和表达技巧，包括陈述策略、表达技巧、常用句式等。

## 适用场景

- 模拟法庭主体陈述环节
- 论点的有效传达
- 庭辩表达技巧训练

## 陈述策略

### 基本概念

- **A方（建构论点方）**：负责立论，掌握主动权
- **R方（反驳方）**：负责反驳，可直接重点进攻

### 发言顺序与时间分配

- 一般遵循"申请方优于被申请方"、"程序优于实体"原则
- 时间分配通常均等
- 每队需从总时长中保留 1-2 分钟用于反驳（rebuttal）和应驳（sur-rebuttal）
- 不建议双方选手发言时间相差过大或反驳时间过长

### A方陈述策略

**优势：**
- 掌握主动权，可以决定重点论证哪些部分
- 可以选择对自己最为有利的标准展开
- 可以思考不常见但合理的角度切入

**策略：**
1. 利用先手方主动权
   - 选择对自己最有利的标准
   - 从不常见但合理的角度切入
   - 预先把对 R 不利的思路或事实抛给法官

2. 预先反驳 R 方可能反驳的点
   - 重点放在有争议的要件上
   - 思考对方会就哪些薄弱点展开攻击
   - 想好回应方式
   - 可以说："the Respondent may argue xxx. However, we submit that…"

3. 不露痕迹地给 R 方挖坑，控制或影响 R 方的论述节奏和重点

### R方陈述策略

**优势：**
- 可直接在 A 方铺陈的基础上重点进攻
- 驳论天然比立论更能调动听者情绪
- 享有更多观察机会

**策略：**
1. 灵活根据 A 方内容调整
   - 庭辩稿内容要远多于 A 方
   - 涵盖所有想到的论点
   - 以小卡片形式呈现，每张对应一个争议点
   - 庭上根据 A 方论证选取卡片组合成稿件

2. 保持自己论证框架的完整性
   - 不能只回应而不顾自己的论证框架
   - 不要因为 A 方没提而放过属于自身论证义务的争议点

3. 重心放在己方论述上
   - 不需要一直复述 A 方观点
   - 最好不要直接说"A is wrong""made a mistake"等
   - 利用 A 方先发言的时间观察法官的提问节奏、偏好和兴趣点
   - 对 A 方没有回答上来的问题或被法官连续追问的问题进行回应

## 表达技巧

### 1. 眼神交流

- 与每一位法官进行真诚、轮流的互动
- 陈述关键论点或回答问题时，将目光专注于一位法官直至说完
- 切换论点时，自然地将目光转向另一位法官
- 切忌眼神飘忽、望向天花板、紧盯稿件

### 2. 肢体动作

**总原则：** 低幅度、低频率，多做多错

- 坐直身体，将手放在桌子上面
- 场下辩手也需注意坐姿
- 不宜出现大幅动作（如手举到胸部以上）
- 避免特殊手势（如 ok、比 123）
- 避免频繁使用某个手势
- 避免下意识地挥舞手中的笔
- 手势只适合作为语言表达之外强调重点的手段

### 3. 声音控制

四个方面需要持续关注：

#### （1）音量
- 强而有力、中气充足
- 不一定要"大嗓门"
- 让对手和法官都能清楚听到即可

#### （2）语气变化
- 适当的语气变化使论点更吸引人、更具交流感
- 语气不能像在读稿或背稿，要有轻重缓急
- 关键概念前后停顿半秒以示强调
- 重读某些单词
- 法庭一直没有提问时，可以多停顿一秒左右

#### （3）速度
- 整体要慢，方便法官理解
- 法庭评价的标准是他们听到了什么，而不是你说了什么
- 练习刻意降低语速，甚至比认为必要的更慢
- 紧张时会不自觉加快语速，可在一句话结束后喝口水或停顿一下来调整

#### （4）音调
- 使用平日说话时的偏中低音调
- 稍微压低音调，嗓音避免过于尖锐或漂浮
- 传递出更加沉稳的台风

### 4. 面部表情

- 保持微笑，尤其在仲裁类比赛中
- 不要过分严肃和板着脸
- 谈到重大损失或客户损害时，可以收起微笑，换上凝重的表情
- 注意与法官进行眼神接触，眼神不要飘忽、躲闪
- 不要出现吐舌头、皱眉头、翻白眼等动作

### 5. 时间管理

- 通常在最后剩余 5min、3min 和 1min 进行提示
- 一般应在剩余 5min 的时候尽力过渡到第二个大诉求
- 一旦出现"time up"的提示，立即停止陈述
- 寻求额外的补充时间（最好在 1 分钟以内，不要超过 2 分钟）
- 最好在时间完全耗尽之前寻求额外时间

**法庭同意延长时间时：**
- 不要提出新论点
- 重点强化已有论点和总结整场陈述
- 不要和法官产生重复的争论
- 迅速收尾并强化结论

**法庭不同意延长时间时：**
- 无需坚持
- 立即结束论证并感谢法庭

### 6. 察言观色

- 时刻有意识地观察法官的表情和动作
- 法官遇到困难时主动提供帮助（如笔断墨）
- 法官露出困惑或思考的神色时，稍微放慢语速，多停顿
- 引导法官翻阅案例时，观察其是否完成翻页动作
- 等待法官定位到相关位置后再开始陈述

## 常用句式与表达

### 1. 开场

#### 法院开场
```
Madam/Mr. President, your excellencies, esteemed opposing counsel. My name is ABC, together with my Co-counsel CBA. We appear before this Court on behalf of the Applicant, the Kingdom of Aglovale. Here, I will speak for xx minutes concerning the first two pleadings and My co-counsel will continue the last two pleadings for a further xx minutes. We will reserve 1 minute for rebuttal. May it please the Court.
```

#### 仲裁庭开场及自我介绍
```
Members of the tribunal, we have agreed that each counsel spends xx minutes on the main pleadings, reserving/leaving 1 minute for rebuttal and sur-rebuttal. As for the order of speaking, the Respondent will speak firstly on the procedural parts, followed by the Claimant, rebuttal and sur-rebuttal. After that, Claimant will submit on merits, followed by Respondent, rebuttal and sur-rebuttal.

May it please the tribunal, my name is Victoria. I appear here together with my co-counsel, Leonie, on behalf of Claimant, PHAR LAP ALLEVEMENTO, the seller in the case. I will proceed on the procedural parts and my co-counsel the substantive.
```

**注意：** 先发言的选手要介绍自己的庭辩队友，配"这边请"的手势，队友配合微笑点头致意。

### 2. 引入赛题或论点总结

```
Your Excellencies, this dispute is about whether there exists any violation of the Trilateral Peace Treaty obligations, concerning Ragnell's illegal use of force against the non-state actor on the territory of Balan.

Before we start, Claimant would like to point out that Claimant has fulfilled all the obligations under the contract. Beyond that, the Claimant even paid the tariff which should not be burdened by itself. Now, the Respondent refused to perform the obligation and pay the tariff, which is not acceptable.
```

**循环赛较前场次：** 第一位辩手可主动询问法庭是否需要或直接开始对事实的概况。

**加入感情色彩渲染：**
```
Your Excellencies, today Applicant appear before the Court, is to pursue justice for our loyal servant, Ms. Cross, who has devoted lifetime to the ILSA program to secure our country. However, she is now arrested by Rovinia only based on a so-called enforced disappearance. Thus, in the next 21 mins, Applicant will argue……
```

**注意：** 事实介绍不应占用太多陈述时间，一般 30 秒左右即可。

### 3. 路线图（Roadmap）

Roadmap 一定要清晰、简洁，分隔论点的关键词要有明确差异。

```
The first pleading has two parts, namely… In the first part, we will submit two points… For the first point, there are three sub-points…

In the procedural parts, we make two submissions: firstly, the tribunal has jurisdiction and powers to adapt the contract; secondly, the Claimant is entitled to submit the evidence, which is the Partial Interim Award from another arbitration.

Therefore, regarding the procedural issue, the Respondent submits that the precondition of arbitration has not been fulfilled based on two reasons: First, the Tribunal has no jurisdiction according to the res judicata rule; Second, the claimant has not exhausted its local remedies.
```

### 4. 主要陈述

#### （1）援引案例或赛题事实

**援引案例：**
```
According to xxx versus xxx case decided by XXX Court/Tribunal in 2020, page/para. xxx, a case also concerning…
```

**援引事实：**
```
May I refer you to the page xxx, paragraph xxx.
If your excellencies are with me [waiting for answer], and I quote "…" end quote. This facts shows that xxx
```

**注意：**
- 多重复几次页数和段落
- 段落过长时，指出具体行数或第几个句子
- 引用完毕后补充一段对该事实的解读
- 确认法官所用的赛题版本
- 不宜在一场庭辩中过度援引事实

#### （2）缩写

- 第一次出现时先说全称，再说 xxx for short
- 前后停顿一下给法官反应时间
- 常见缩写词当成单词读（如 ICSID 读成 /ˈɪksɪd/）
- 记得所有缩写词的全称

#### （3）论点之间的切换过渡

```
Unless I may further assist the tribunal on this issue, I will now move on to address our second submission.

Your excellencies, I notice my time is limited. May I move to my second issue…

That's actually leads to my second point…
```

#### （4）路标词（signpost）

```
Next, we will address …

Turning to our second point

Having addressed the ICCPR violation, we will now discuss …

Returning to the discussion of …

Building on our previous point that …

We would highlight that …

The most important/most determinative/most critical point is …

To briefly summarize our arguments, …
```

### 5. 与对手进行攻防

注意语气不宜过于激烈，避免"the Applicant is wrong"这类表述。

```
The Applicants have argued that in the case of xxx it was held that "...", however, we respectfully submit your excellences that they have neglected a crucial part…

The Applicants have stated that...; however, they failed to notice that...

The Respondent may argue that…; however, …
```

### 6. 表达优化

- I think/believe → submit
- so → therefore/thus
- I am sorry → my apology
- 谨慎使用 any、all、only 等表述

## 注意事项

- 不同的出场顺序将对庭辩策略的选择产生实质性影响
- 辩手应根据自身角色进行针对性设计
- A 方的陈述比 R 方要困难
- R 方更不能依赖自己的稿件，而应灵活地根据 A 方的内容进行调整
- 一个文档可能对应多个 skill
