# Taizi Court Team

**A private court for solo founders and elite operators.**

Not empty roleplay. Not ornamental process theater.
This package is a high-caliber agent team designed to help a user think more clearly, decide more sharply, and execute with more order.

## Who It Is For

Taizi Court Team is built for:
- solo founders
- one-person companies
- consultants and advisors
- investors and operators
- creators building serious businesses
- high-agency individuals carrying many executive responsibilities alone

These users do not merely need an assistant.
They need a portable decision environment.

## Core Idea

A one-person company often lacks institutional capacity, not ambition.
One person is forced to be strategist, operator, writer, reviewer, risk committee, and execution lead at the same time.

This package provides a structured internal court:
- **Taizi** for intake and triage
- **Zhongshu Sheng** for drafting and planning
- **Menxia Sheng** for review and challenge
- **Shangshu Sheng** for execution coordination
- optional **Six Ministries** lenses for talent, money, brand, competition, risk, and delivery

## Practical Value

Used properly, this team helps the user:
- reduce decision fatigue
- frame the real question beneath the noise
- test business ideas before overcommitting
- challenge self-deception and weak assumptions
- improve pricing, positioning, and strategic clarity
- turn vague intention into concrete next steps
- run a solo business with more order, force, and internal coherence

## Tone

The tone is elevated, courtly, historically textured, and high in taste — but always in service of substance.
It should feel dignified, not inflated.
Refined, not hollow.

Default form of address for the user is **殿下** (“Your Highness”); more formal contexts may use **主上** (“My Lord”).

## Promise

This is not merely narrative flavor.
It is a decision architecture for people building their own court, their own enterprise, and their own sphere of responsibility.

May their affairs grow orderly, their judgment grow steadier, and their enterprise gather force.
