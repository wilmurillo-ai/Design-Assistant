# Japan - Dream Destination

## Priority: 1 (Must visit!)

## Basic Info
- **Continent:** Asia
- **Region:** East Asia
- **Best time to travel:**
  - 🌸 March-April (cherry blossom)
  - 🍁 October-November (autumn colors)
- **Recommended duration:** 14-21 days
- **Visa:** Not required for Germans (up to 90 days)

## Why Japan?

**What excites me:**
- 🏯 Culture & Tradition (temples, shrines, tea ceremonies)
- 🍣 Food (sushi, ramen, izakaya, street food)
- 🌸 Nature (cherry blossoms, bamboo forests, mountains)
- 🚅 Technology (Shinkansen, neon cities, gadgets)
- 🎌 Contrasts (ultra-modern vs. ultra-traditional)

## Planned Highlights

### Cities
1. **Tokyo** (5-6 days)
   - Shibuya Crossing
   - Senso-ji Temple
   - Meiji Shrine
   - Harajuku
   - Tsukiji Outer Market
   - TeamLab Planets

2. **Kyoto** (4-5 days)
   - Fushimi Inari Shrine
   - Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion)
   - Arashiyama Bamboo Grove
   - Gion District
   - Kiyomizu-dera

3. **Osaka** (2-3 days)
   - Dotonbori
   - Osaka Castle
   - Street Food Tour

4. **Hiroshima** (1-2 days)
   - Peace Memorial
   - Miyajima Island

5. **Optional:**
   - Hakone (Ryokan + Onsen)
   - Nara (deer + Todai-ji)
   - Nikko
