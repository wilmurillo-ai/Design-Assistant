## Sources to Check

| Source | Website/App | Pros | Cons |
|--------|-------------|------|------|
| **Google Flights** | flights.google.com | Best price graph, flexible dates | No alerts for all airlines |
| **Skyscanner** | skyscanner.com | Compares all OTAs | Can have hidden fees |
| **Kayak** | kayak.com | Price forecasts | Sometimes outdated |
| **Momondo** | momondo.com | Finds hidden deals | Slow interface |
| **Direct Airline** | airline.com | Best customer service | Price may be higher |

## Price Trends

### What to Expect

| Route Type | Good Price | Average Price | Book When |
|------------|------------|---------------|-----------|
| **Europe Short-Haul** | <€100 | €100-200 | 2-3 months out |
| **Europe Long-Haul** | <€150 | €150-300 | 3-4 months out |
| **Intercontinental** | <€400 | €400-800 | 3-6 months out |
| **Holiday Season** | +30-50% | +50-100% | 6+ months out |

### Booking Tips
- 🎫 Tuesday/Wednesday cheapest days
- 📅 6-8 weeks before = sweet spot
- 🔄 Clear cookies or use incognito
- 💳 Check airline directly after finding price
- 🎁 Look for package deals (flight+hotel)
