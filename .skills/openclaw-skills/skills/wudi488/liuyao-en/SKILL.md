## What is Liuyao?

Liuyao is a traditional Chinese divination method using 3 coins, based on the I Ching (Book of Changes). It uses six throws to create a hexagram for analysis.

**Key Features:**
- Six lines model a situation across time
- Each line can be Yin or Yang, static or moving
- Creates primary hexagram + changed hexagram
- Five Elements and Six Relationships provide deep analysis

## 🔮 How Liuyao Works

### Step 1: Six Throws (Bottom to Top)

```
Throw 1 = Line 1 (bottom, 初爻)
Throw 2 = Line 2 (二爻)
Throw 3 = Line 3 (三爻)
Throw 4 = Line 4 (四爻)
Throw 5 = Line 5 (五爻)
Throw 6 = Line 6 (top, 上爻)
```

### Step 2: Identify Moving Lines

Moving lines (O or X) will transform:
- O (Old Yang 9) → Yin
- X (Old Yin 6) → Yang

### Step 3: Create Two Hexagrams

| Hexagram | Chinese | Shows |
|----------|---------|-------|
| Primary (本卦) | Ben Gua | Current situation |
| Changed (变卦) | Bian Gua | Future outcome |

### Step 4: Interpretation

Based on:
- Hexagram meaning
- Moving line positions
- Five Elements interactions
- Significator (用神) analysis

## ⚖️ The Role of Five Elements

Five Elements (Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water) are energy phases:

```
Generating (相生): Wood → Fire → Earth → Metal → Water → Wood
Controlling (相克): Wood → Earth → Water → Fire → Metal → Wood
```

### In Liuyao Practice

1. **Every line has a Five Element** - Shows its energy nature
2. **Six Relationships (六亲)** - Parents, Siblings, Children, Wealth, Authority
3. **Significator (用神)** - The line representing your question
4. **Element Interactions** - Determine if your position is strong or weak

### Example: Significator Analysis

> If your Significator is Wood, and it's generated by Water lines and protected from Metal control → **Favorable position**
