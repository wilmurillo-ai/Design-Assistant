## What is Liuyao?

Liuyao is a traditional Chinese divination method using 3 coins, based on the I Ching (Book of Changes). It uses six throws to create a hexagram for analysis.

## 🔮 How It Works

### Step 1: Six Throws

Each throw produces one line (bottom to top):
- Throw 1 = Line 1 (bottom)
- Throw 2 = Line 2
- ...
- Throw 6 = Line 6 (top)

### Step 2: Reading the Hexagram

```
Example:
Line 6 (top): --  Yin
Line 5:       —   Yang
Line 4:       --  Yin
Line 3:       —   Yang
Line 2:       --  Yin
Line 1:       —   Yang (bottom)

This creates a HEXAGRAM for analysis
```

### Step 3: Interpretation

Based on:
- The hexagram meaning
- Which lines are "moving" (O or X)
- The interaction between lines
