## 第一部分：客户开发话术

### 电话开发黄金话术

#### 开场白（3秒抓住注意力）

```
【标准开场】
"Hi [Name], this is [Your name] from [Company]. 
I know you're busy, so I'll be quick. 
We specialize in [product], and I noticed [Company] 
[is expanding in/doing great in] [area]. 
Do you have 30 seconds?"

【转介绍开场】
"Hi [Name], [Referrer] suggested I call you. 
They mentioned you're looking for [product type], 
and that's exactly what we excel at. 
Is this a good time?"

【价值先行开场】
"Hi [Name], this is [Your name]. 
I'm calling because we've helped [similar company] 
reduce their [pain point] by [X]%, 
and I thought you might be interested. 
Worth a brief conversation?"
```

#### 应对"没兴趣"话术

```
客户："I'm not interested."

【回应1-价值钩子】
"I understand. Most people say that initially. 
Can I ask, are you currently facing [common pain point]? 
Because that's what we specialize in solving."

【回应2-低压力试探】
"Fair enough. Can I ask what would make this interesting to you? 
Is it price, quality, or something else?"

【回应3-退而求其次】
"No problem. Would it be okay if I sent you some information 
about [specific benefit]? If it's not relevant, 
you can ignore it. But if it is, we can reconnect."
```

#### 应对"邮箱给我发资料"

```
客户："Just send me an email."

【标准回应】
"Absolutely, I'll send that right over. 
So I make sure to send something relevant, 
can I quickly ask - are you currently sourcing [product] 
from [region] or looking for alternatives?"

【深度挖掘】
"I'd be happy to. To make it most useful for you, 
what's your biggest challenge with [current supplier/situation] right now?"
```

### 客户说"价格太贵"

#### 初级应对

```
客户："Your price is too high."

【标准回应】
"I appreciate your honesty. Can you help me understand - 
is the price higher than your budget, or higher than 
what you're currently paying?

If it's the latter, I'd like to explain what makes our 
price different and why many customers find the value worth it."

【对比价值】
"I understand price is important. When comparing, 
it's important to consider [factor 1], [factor 2], and [factor 3].

Our price includes [value adds that competitors don't include].

Would it help if I broke down what's included in our price 
versus what others might charge separately?"
```

#### 进阶谈判

```
【询问预算】
"To help find a solution that works for both of us, 
can you share what price range you're looking for? 
I want to be transparent about what we can and can't do."

【探寻真实顾虑】
"When you say the price is high, is that the only concern, 
or are there other factors? Sometimes price is a reflection 
of concerns about [quality/service/timing].

If we can address those, would the price work?"

【提供选择】
"I want to work with you. Here are a few options:

Option A: [Original spec] at quoted price
Option B: [Slightly reduced spec] at [lower price]  
Option C: [Volume commitment] at [discounted price]
Option D: [Payment terms change] at [adjusted price]

Which direction interests you most?"
```

## 第三部分：谈判话术

### 价格谈判全流程

#### 阶段1：探明底牌

```
"Before we discuss numbers, can you help me understand:

1. What's your target quantity for this order?
2. Is this a one-time purchase or ongoing need?
3. What payment terms work for you?
4. What's your timeline for delivery?

This will help me give you the best possible pricing."
```

#### 阶段2：锚定价值

```
"Based on what you've shared, here's what our pricing includes:

✓ [Material/quality advantage] - worth approximately $X
✓ [Service/inclusion] - competitors typically charge $X separately
✓ [Warranty/support] - value of $X
✓ [Speed/flexibility] - reduces your risk by...

So the true value is [higher number], but we're offering it at [price]."
```

#### 阶段3：给出方案

```
"Here's what I can do:

For [quantity 1]: $[price 1]
For [quantity 2]: $[price 2] (better unit price)
For [quantity 3]: $[price 3] (best value)

The pricing at [quantity 2] is typically where we see 
the best balance of price and flexibility for our partners.

Does that work for your needs?"
```

#### 阶段4：处理异议

```
客户："Can you do [lower price]?"

【阶梯让步法】
"I understand you need to hit a certain margin. 
Let me see what I can do.

If we can agree on [condition 1], I can reduce to [price A].
If we can also [condition 2], I can get to [price B].

Which aspects are most flexible for you?"

【交换条件法】
"[Price] is quite tight for us at this quantity. 
However, if we can discuss [payment terms/larger volume/longer lead time], 
there might be room to move.

What would work for you?"

【最后防线】
"[Price] is really the best I can do for this quantity. 
I want to be honest - going lower would mean compromising 
on [quality/service] which I'm not comfortable with.

However, I can offer [alternative - payment terms, faster shipping, etc.]. 

How does that sound?"
```

### MOQ谈判话术

```
客户："Your MOQ is too high."

【标准回应】
"I understand [quantity] might be more than you're ready for. 
The MOQ is set because [reason - setup costs, material minimums, etc.].

Here are a few options:

Option 1: Start with our standard MOQ at [quantity] 
→ Best unit price, full customization

Option 2: Trial order at [lower quantity] 
→ Slightly higher unit price ($[X]), but lets you test the market

Option 3: Combine with other products 
→ If you're sourcing [related items], we can combine orders

Option 4: Check our stock items 
→ We have [quantity] units available immediately, 
  no MOQ, slightly different spec

Which direction interests you?"

【条件交换】
"Our standard MOQ is [quantity], but for partners who can 
commit to [condition - e.g., 3 orders per year, 
payment upfront, etc.], we can be flexible.

Would any of those conditions work for you?"
```

## 话术使用指南

### 调整语气的方法

| 客户类型 | 语气调整 | 示例变化 |
|---------|---------|---------|
| **直接型** | 简洁、数据驱动 | 少用寒暄，多用数字 |
| **关系型** | 热情、个人化 | 多问候，建立连接 |
| **分析型** | 详细、结构化 | 提供完整信息，数据支撑 |
| **谨慎型** | 耐心、低风险 | 强调保障，减少承诺 |

### 文化差异注意事项

- **欧美客户**：直接表达，少用"maybe"，明确Yes/No
- **日韩客户**：礼貌层级，重视书面确认
- **中东客户**：关系优先，决策慢，需耐心
- **南美客户**：热情友好，时间观念灵活
- **印度客户**：价格敏感，讨价还价是常态

### 禁忌词汇

避免使用：
- ❌ "I don't know" → ✅ "Let me find out"
- ❌ "That's not my job" → ✅ "Let me connect you with [person]"
- ❌ "I can't" → ✅ "What I can do is..."
- ❌ "You should have..." → ✅ "Going forward, let's..."
- ❌ "To be honest..." → 直接说内容，暗示之前不诚实
