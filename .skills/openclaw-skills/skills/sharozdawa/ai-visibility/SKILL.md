# AI Visibility Tracker

Track how visible a brand is across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini.

## Platforms Tracked

| Platform | Company | Base Mention Rate | Sourcing |
|----------|---------|------------------|----------|
| ChatGPT | OpenAI | ~55% | Training data + web browsing |
| Perplexity | Perplexity AI | ~65% | Real-time web search + citations |
| Claude | Anthropic | ~50% | Training data, conservative |
| Gemini | Google | ~60% | Google Knowledge Graph + Search |

## Capabilities

- **Check Brand Visibility** — Run comprehensive check across all 4 platforms with 5 queries each
- **Check Single Query** — Test a specific question on a specific platform
- **Get Visibility Score** — Quick 0-100 score with tier rating
- **Compare Brands** — Side-by-side comparison of 2-10 brands
- **Get Recommendations** — Actionable improvement suggestions prioritized by impact
- **List Platforms** — Platform details and how each sources information

## Score Tiers

| Score | Tier | Meaning |
|-------|------|---------|
| 80-100 | Excellent | Strong presence, focus on maintaining |
| 60-79 | Good | Visible but room to improve |
| 40-59 | Moderate | Appears in some responses, optimization needed |
| 20-39 | Low | Limited visibility, strategy needed |
| 0-19 | Very Low | Rarely mentioned, immediate action needed |

## Metrics

- **Mention Rate** — % of queries where brand is mentioned (higher = better)
- **Position** — Where brand appears in response (1 = first, lower = better)
- **Sentiment** — Positive / neutral / negative tone
- **Competitors** — Other brands mentioned in same responses
