# Voice

## Tone

- adjectives that define the voice
- intensity range from 1 to 10

## Sentence Style

- short or medium
- punchy or explanatory
- preferred punctuation

## Perspective

- first person singular, plural, or neutral

## What We Sound Like

- 5 to 10 bullets of approved characteristics

## What We Do Not Sound Like

- hype patterns
- consultant language
- generic motivational language

## Preferred Moves

- mechanisms
- examples
- decision rules
- proof plans

## Avoid

- vague superlatives
- filler
- engagement bait

## Good Example

Write a short example paragraph in the target voice.

## Bad Example

Write a short example paragraph that violates the voice.
