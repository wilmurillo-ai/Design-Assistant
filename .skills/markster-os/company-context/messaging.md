# Messaging

## One-Liner

One sentence.

## Short Pitch

Two to three sentences.

## Problem Framing

- what is broken today
- what it costs
- why now

## Differentiators

- specific reasons to choose this company
- do not include unverified superiority claims

## Key Claims

For each claim:

- claim
- proof status: verified, approved-example, hypothesis
- approved usage notes

## Message Variants

- founder-to-founder
- buyer-facing
- partner-facing
- investor-facing if relevant

## Banned Phrases

- list phrases that should never be used

## Required Anchors

- terms or ideas that should appear often when relevant
