# Company Identity

## One-Sentence Definition

Write one plain-language sentence that says what the company is.

Constraints:

- no jargon
- no AI hype
- no vague transformation words
- should still make sense to a stranger

## Category

State the category you want to own.

Include:

- primary category
- investor or technical framing if different
- categories you reject

## What We Do

- core job we do for customers
- what they receive
- what changes for them

## What We Do Not Do

- out-of-scope services
- rejected positioning
- activities customers should not expect

## Why We Exist

State the customer problem worth solving.

## Strategic Position

- what makes this business different
- where it sits in the market
- what tradeoffs it accepts on purpose

## Known Identity Risks

- where people misunderstand the company
- words that cause confusion
- claims that require caution
