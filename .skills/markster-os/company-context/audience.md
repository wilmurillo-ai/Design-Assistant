# Audience

## Primary ICP

- title or role
- company type
- company size
- geography if relevant
- trigger situation

## Secondary ICP

- optional second segment

## Buying Triggers

- specific events that create urgency
- signs they are likely in market

## Pain Points

- operational pains
- financial pains
- emotional pains

## Desired Outcomes

- what they want to become true
- what “better” looks like in concrete terms

## Objections

- common concerns
- skepticism patterns
- likely blockers to purchase

## Current Alternatives

- what they use today
- what they do manually
- who or what they compare us against

## Buyer Language

Write approved phrases the buyer actually uses.

Use bullets only.

## Disqualifiers

- who is not a fit
- what situations are wrong for this offer
