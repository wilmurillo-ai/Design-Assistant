# Foundation: F1-F4

Foundation is Layer 0 of ScaleOS. Four strategic prerequisites before any channel execution begins.

Companies skip Foundation because it feels like planning instead of doing. The result is campaigns that do not resonate, offers that do not close, and messages that sound like everyone else in the category.

Foundation locks your targeting, pricing logic, organizational structure, and financial architecture. It takes 3-5 hours total. It saves months.

---

## The four components

**[F1: Positioning & Differentiation](F1-positioning.md)** - Who exactly are you selling to, why do they buy, and why should they buy from you?
ICP, value proposition, competitive moat, messaging architecture. The decisions that make every downstream action either easy or impossible.

**[F2: Business Model Design](F2-business-model.md)** - How do you package and price what you deliver?
Revenue model structure, pricing tiers, unit economics. The Pricing Test: if you're not losing some deals on price, you're probably underpriced.

**[F3: Organizational Structure](F3-org-structure.md)** - Who owns what, and how does the business run without you?
GOD Ownership Model, decision rights framework, how to escape Founder Jail. One owner per pillar. No co-ownership.

**[F4: Financial Architecture](F4-financial.md)** - Do your unit economics support growth?
LTV:CAC rules, cash flow management, capital allocation priority order. Below 3:1 LTV:CAC means do not scale.

---

## How to use these files

Work through F1-F4 in order. Do not skip ahead. Each component takes 45-90 minutes done carefully.

Each file contains:
1. The question the component answers
2. Why it matters (what breaks if you skip it)
3. The framework for answering it
4. Specific questions to answer in writing
5. A summary template to document your output
6. A 5-question assessment (scored /50 each, total /200)

Your completed answers become the context that every skill and playbook uses.
