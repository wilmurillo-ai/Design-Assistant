# Stage 0: Pre-Revenue

**Revenue range:** $0 (no paying clients yet)

---

## Situation

You are building and validating. No customers. The business model exists as a hypothesis. Your goal is not to build infrastructure -- it is to get a first qualified meeting booked with someone who matches your ICP.

Every hour spent building systems, designing websites, or writing SOPs before a first meeting is an hour spent on something that has not been validated. The most expensive mistake at Stage 0 is building before anyone has confirmed they will buy.

---

## Priorities

1. **F1: Positioning & Differentiation** -- Write your ICP hypothesis. Who, buying trigger, decision-maker.
2. **G1: Find** -- Build a manual list of 50-100 prospects matching your ICP. LinkedIn or Apollo, manually filtered.
3. **G3: Book** -- Get your first 5 conversations with people who match your ICP.

---

## Skip List

Do not build any of the following at Stage 0:

- Complex automation or email sequences
- SOPs or process documentation
- Analytics dashboards or KPI frameworks
- Delivery systems or onboarding processes
- A website (unless you have paying clients to reference)
- A CRM (a spreadsheet is sufficient until you have 10+ active conversations)

The rule: if it requires more than 10 minutes of setup and does not directly result in a conversation with an ICP-match prospect, skip it.

---

## Week-by-Week Timeline

**Week 1-2: ICP Hypothesis**
- Write a one-page ICP hypothesis: who is the buyer, what pain do they feel, what outcome do you deliver, why you over alternatives
- Pull 3-5 best professional relationships that match or are adjacent to your ICP. List what they have in common.
- Pick ONE segment. Not "B2B companies." One vertical and one company size band.

**Week 3-4: First 100 Prospects**
- Build a manual list of 100 ICP-matching prospects in a Google Sheet. Not a CRM.
- Source: LinkedIn Sales Navigator or Apollo free tier. Filter by vertical, headcount, decision-maker title.
- Verify 20-30 email addresses before outreach.

**Month 1: First Meetings**
- Send 20-30 manual, personalized LinkedIn messages or emails. No templates yet.
- Goal: 3-5 conversations with real ICP-match prospects.
- These are discovery calls, not pitches. You are gathering signal, not closing deals.

---

## Success Signal

**Stage 0 is complete when:** You have booked your first qualified meeting with someone who matches your ICP definition.

A qualified meeting means the person is the decision-maker, their company fits your firmographic criteria, and they have the buying trigger you identified in F1.

---

## Common Mistake

**Building systems before validating that anyone will buy.** The instinct is to look credible: website, proposal template, onboarding process. But no amount of infrastructure compensates for an unvalidated ICP or an offer nobody wants. Get a meeting first. Build credibility through the conversation, not the materials.
