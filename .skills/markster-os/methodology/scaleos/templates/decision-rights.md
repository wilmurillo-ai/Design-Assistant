## Decision Types

| Decision Type | Who Decides | Who's Consulted | Who's Informed |
|--------------|------------|-----------------|----------------|
| Strategic (direction, markets, major investments) | Founder/CEO | Leadership team | All |
| Tactical (how to execute strategy) | Function owner | Affected parties | Team |
| Operational (day-to-day execution) | Individual contributor | Peers if needed | Manager |
