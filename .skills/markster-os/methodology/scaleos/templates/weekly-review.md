## Cadence

| Rhythm | When | Duration | Focus |
|--------|------|----------|-------|
| **Daily** | Morning + EOD | 15 min | Check metrics, top 3, blockers |
| **Weekly** | Monday + Friday | 2-3 hrs total | Review + plan + assess |
| **Monthly** | First week | Half day | Deep dive + GOD Engine scoring + next month priorities |
| **Quarterly** | First month | Full day | Strategic review + Foundation check + quarter planning |
