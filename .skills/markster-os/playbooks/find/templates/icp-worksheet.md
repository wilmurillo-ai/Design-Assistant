# ICP Worksheet

Complete this before building any list or writing any outreach. Takes 20-30 minutes.

If you cannot answer a question, that is the gap to fix -- not a reason to skip it.

## Layer 2: What triggers the purchase (situational)

A buying trigger is the specific event or condition that makes someone a buyer right now -- not eventually, now.

| Question | Your answer |
|----------|-------------|
| What event just happened that makes this urgent? | |
| What pain are they actively trying to fix this quarter? | |
| What have they tried before that did not work? | |
| What does it cost them if they do nothing for 6 more months? | |

Example buying triggers:
- Just raised a funding round and needs to build outbound
- Just hit $1M ARR and sales process is breaking
- New marketing hire who needs to prove results fast
- Competitor just launched and they are losing deals to them

## Layer 4: The one qualifying question

One question that separates a real buyer from everyone else. If they answer yes to this, they are a qualified prospect. If no, they are not worth pursuing right now.

Examples:
- "Are you currently sending more than 50 cold emails per week?"
- "Do you have a dedicated person managing client onboarding?"
- "Are you spending more than $5K/month on paid acquisition?"

Your qualifying question: ___________________________________

---

## Layer 5: Your ICP statement (complete this last)

Fill in the blanks:

"We help [title] at [company type] who [buying trigger] to [specific outcome] in [timeframe]."

Example: "We help VP Sales at B2B SaaS companies who just missed two consecutive quarters to build a repeatable outbound system in 90 days."

Your ICP statement: ___________________________________

---

## Validation check

Before building a list, confirm:
- [ ] You can name 3 real people who fit this ICP exactly
- [ ] You know where to find 100+ of them (platform + filter criteria)
- [ ] You have spoken to at least 2 of them about the problem in the past 6 months
- [ ] You can verify their email addresses before sending

If any box is unchecked, fix it before building a list.

---

## Quick ICP test

Show your ICP statement to someone who does not know your business. Ask them: "Can you name one person you know who fits this description?"

If they can name someone immediately, your ICP is specific enough.
If they say "hmm, maybe..." or "it depends," tighten the description.
