# G1: Find

**Pillar:** Growth
**North Star:** Qualified prospects identified per week

---

## What It Covers

ICP operationalization, list building, data enrichment, intent signals, and lead scoring.

G1 is the work that happens before any outreach. The quality of every downstream brick (Warm, Book) is constrained by the quality of G1. Bad lists produce bad outreach results regardless of message quality.

---

## Key Questions It Answers

- Who exactly are we targeting this week, this month?
- How do we build a list of 200 ICP-match prospects in 48 hours?
- What data quality standards must the list meet before outreach?
- How do we score and prioritize prospects so the highest-likelihood contacts get first attention?
- What intent signals indicate a prospect is in an active buying window?

## Common Failures

**Targeting too broad:** "B2B companies with 10-50 employees" is not an ICP. It is a demographic. Without a specific industry, decision-maker title, and buying trigger, every list is diluted and every outreach message is generic.

**Skipping data verification:** Sending to unverified emails burns domains and produces false signal. A 20% bounce rate destroys deliverability. Verify every list before sending.

**Volume before quality:** Pulling 5,000 contacts from Apollo without filtering is not list building -- it is noise. A list of 200 carefully filtered, verified prospects outperforms a list of 2,000 unfiltered ones every time.

**No scoring:** All prospects treated as equal. High-intent signals (recently hired, just raised funding, posted about the problem) are not actioned faster than low-intent signals.

---

## Find Done Right

G1 is working when: A named person on your team can pull a fresh list of 50-100 verified, ICP-matched prospects in under 4 hours using a repeatable process. The list has a 90%+ data accuracy rate. High-intent prospects are identified and prioritized for first contact. This runs every week without founder involvement.

## Tools

- Apollo.io: Prospect search, list building, email verification
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator: ICP filtering, intent signals (job changes, activity)
- Hunter.io / Clearbit: Email verification and enrichment
- Google Sheets: Manual list management at Stage 0-1

---

## Lead Scoring Framework (G1)

Weight prospects by:
- Firmographic fit: 30%
- Behavioral signals: 25%
- Intent signals: 25%
- Timing: 20%

Score 1-10 per dimension. Total score determines outreach priority. Contacts scoring 7+ get personalized outreach first.
