# G2: Warm

**Pillar:** Growth
**North Star:** Nurture-to-meeting conversion rate

---

## What It Covers

Content marketing, nurture sequences, social media, paid acquisition, SEO, events, and authority building.

G2 is everything that builds trust and recognition before a direct sales conversation. Warm channels do not produce immediate meetings -- they shorten sales cycles, increase reply rates on cold outreach, and generate inbound signals over time.

---

## Key Questions It Answers

- What content makes our ICP lean in and build trust before they hear from us?
- Which channels does our ICP actually use and trust?
- How do we convert content engagement into conversation signals?
- What is the minimum content output that produces meaningful warm signal?
- When does paid acquisition make sense to add?

---

## Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Engagement rate (organic social) | 2-5% |
| Email open rate (nurture) | 25-40% |
| Email click rate (nurture) | 10-20% |
| Nurture-to-meeting conversion | 5-15% |
| Content attribution (% deals touched by content) | Track trend |

---

## Common Failures

**Starting with paid:** Paid amplifies what already converts organically. Running paid before validating organic conversion rates burns budget producing no usable signal.

**Publishing without distribution:** Writing content and waiting for it to be discovered. Most B2B content gets zero distribution after the initial post. A distribution system (email list, repurposing, direct shares) is required.

**Posting frequency without point of view:** Posting three times a week with generic industry content does not warm prospects. A consistent, specific point of view that the ICP can agree or disagree with is what creates engagement.

**Events without follow-up:** Attending events, collecting business cards, doing nothing with them for two weeks. Events warm prospects only if there is a systematic follow-up sequence within 48 hours.

---

## Warm Done Right

G2 is working when: Prospects who receive cold outreach (G3) already recognize your name from content or events. Reply rates on G3 outreach are measurably higher for contacts who have engaged with G2 content. Inbound inquiries -- people who reached out because of content -- are arriving consistently, even if infrequently.
