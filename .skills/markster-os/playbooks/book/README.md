# G3: Book

**Pillar:** Growth
**North Star:** Qualified meetings booked per week

---

## What It Covers

Cold outreach sequences, inbound lead handling, BANT+ qualification, show rate optimization, and meeting scheduling.

G3 converts interest -- both outbound-generated and inbound-generated -- into qualified meetings. It is the revenue-generating motion that separates companies that talk about sales from companies that have a pipeline.

---

## Key Questions It Answers

- What outreach sequence and messaging produce the highest reply rate for our ICP?
- How do we qualify inbound inquiries quickly and book only qualified meetings?
- What does BANT+ qualification look like for our specific offer?
- How do we maintain a 80-90% show rate on booked meetings?
- At what volume does manual outreach need to be systematized?

---

## Metrics

| Metric | Cold Outreach | Warm Outreach |
|--------|--------------|---------------|
| Response rate | 5-15% | 15-30% |
| Positive response rate | 30-50% of replies | 30-50% of replies |
| Meeting book rate | 50-70% of positives | 50-70% of positives |
| Show rate | 80-90% | 80-90% |
| Qualified rate (meeting -> proposal) | 60-80% | 60-80% |

---

## Common Failures

**No qualification before booking:** Booking every person who replies, regardless of ICP fit. This fills the calendar with non-buyers and wastes the founder's or sales person's most valuable hours.

**One-touch outreach:** Sending one email and treating no reply as a no. The data consistently shows that follow-up touches 2-4 produce more replies than touch 1. Most buyers are not ignoring you -- they are busy.

**Generic openers:** Starting every email with "I help companies like yours" or "I noticed you recently..." -- pattern-matched phrases that buyers have trained themselves to ignore. The first sentence must be specific to the person.

**No show rate system:** Booking the meeting and doing nothing until the calendar invite fires. A confirmation sequence (booking confirmation, day-before, one-hour-before) is the difference between 60% and 90% show rates.

---

## Book Done Right

G3 is working when: Qualified meetings are booking consistently each week from a systematized outreach process that does not require the founder to personally write every message. Show rates are above 80%. At least 60% of meetings progress to proposal. The founder can review metrics rather than execute the motion.
