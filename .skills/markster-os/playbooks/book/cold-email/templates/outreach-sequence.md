# Outreach Sequence Templates

Standard multi-touch sequences for cold outreach campaigns. All personalized per contact.

---

## Standard 5-Touch (Cold -- ICP Match)

### Touch 1: Day 0 -- Value-First Intro

**Subject lines (rotate):**
- "Quick question about [company]'s growth"
- "[Name], how are you handling outbound?"
- "Saw [company] -- had an idea"

```
[Name],

I work with [industry] founders who are tired of being their own sales team.

[Personalized line: reference their company, recent news, or LinkedIn activity.]

We run the full growth operation -- outbound, content, SEO, campaigns -- but
everything goes through you first. Nothing touches your brand without your
approval. We call it Draft Mode.

Would it make sense to show you how it works? Takes 15 minutes.

[Your name]
[Company] -- [website]
```

### Touch 2: Day 2 -- LinkedIn Connection

```
Hi [Name] -- [Your name] from [Company]. We help [industry] founders hand off their
GTM without losing control. Thought it'd be worth connecting.
```

### Touch 3: Day 4 -- Proof Point

**Subject:** "How [similar company type] grew [result]"

```
[Name],

Quick follow-up -- wanted to share a relevant result.

We worked with a [client type, e.g. performance marketing agency] that was stuck
in founder-led sales. After setting up their growth operation (prospecting,
outbound, content, proposals), they saw:

- [Metric 1, e.g. 6x revenue growth in year one]
- [Metric 2, e.g. 3x growth in year two]
- "[Direct quote from client about time saved or result achieved]"

Their founder went from [X] hours/week on [task] to [new state, e.g. approving
AI-generated drafts overnight].

If you're spending similar time on GTM, worth a 15-minute chat?

[Your name]
```

### Touch 4: Day 8 -- Objection Pre-Handle

**Subject:** "Not another agency pitch (promise)"

```
[Name],

I know -- another email about growth services. Fair.

Here's what's different: we're not a black box agency. You see everything
we do. Every email, post, and prospect goes through you before it goes out.
[Add your key differentiator or retention proof point here.]

[your retainer fee] replaces a [equivalent full-time team cost]. And you can
leave any time.

Last ask: 15 minutes to show you Draft Mode. If it's not relevant, I'll
stop reaching out.

[Your name]
```

### Touch 5: Day 14 -- Break-Up

**Subject:** "Closing the loop"

```
[Name],

Haven't heard back, so I'll keep this short.

If offloading your outbound, content, and growth ops isn't a priority right
now, totally get it. I'll stop reaching out.

If things change, we start with a [onboarding sprint duration]-day Install
Sprint -- we learn your business and launch your first campaign. [your sprint
fee] to see exactly what it's like.

Either way, good luck with [company].

[Your name]
```

## Warm Sequence (Post-Event / Referral)

### Touch 1: Day 0 -- Personal Reference

```
[Name],

[Referral name] suggested I reach out -- [or: We met at [event]].

[Specific context from referral/meeting.]

I run [Company] -- we handle the growth operation for B2B service businesses
like [company]. Outbound, content, SEO, campaigns -- founder approves
everything through Draft Mode.

Would love to show you how it works. Free any time this week?

[Your name]
```

### Touch 2: Day 3 -- Value Add

```
[Name],

Following up on my note. Wanted to share [relevant article/insight/case study]
that relates to what [referral name] mentioned about your [situation].

Happy to chat whenever works.

[Your name]
```

### Touch 3: Day 7 -- Soft Close

```
[Name],

One more try -- [referral name] thought this would be a great fit, and I
tend to trust their judgment.

If timing isn't right, no problem. But if you're spending more time on
GTM than you'd like, it's worth 15 minutes.

[Your name]
```

## Waitlist Activation Sequence

### Touch 1: Day 0 -- You've Been Waiting

**Subject:** "You signed up -- here's what's new"

```
[Name],

You joined our waitlist [X months ago]. A lot has changed since then.

We've restructured into three clear tiers:
- [Tier 1 name] ([price range]) -- [one-line description]
- [Tier 2 name] ([price range]) -- [one-line description]
- [Tier 3 name] ([price range]) -- [one-line description]

Everything starts with a [duration]-day Install Sprint ([your sprint fee]) --
we learn your business and launch your first campaign.

What's different: Draft Mode. You see and approve everything before it
touches your brand. No black box.

Still interested? Reply and I'll set up a quick call.

[Your name]
```

### Touch 2: Day 3 -- Social Proof

```
[Name],

Quick update -- our [anchor/flagship] client just passed [X] months with us.
[Tier name] tier, [your retainer fee], growing. Their revenue grew [X]x in
year one.

[Optional: add a market observation about what's failing in your category --
positions your approach as the alternative.]

We're the opposite: transparent by default, human-in-the-loop by design.

Want to see how it works?

[Your name]
```

### Touch 3: Day 7 -- Direct Ask

```
[Name],

Last note on this -- you signed up because something resonated.

If the problem hasn't gone away (spending too much time on GTM, agencies
that don't deliver, tools that require more managing), let's talk.

15 minutes. I'll show you Draft Mode and you'll know immediately if it fits.

If not the right time, no worries -- I'll check back in a few months.

[Your name]
```
