### Key Finding

Heavy supply for startup performance marketing. Thin supply for B2B service-business growth systems with transparent pricing. That is the white space.

### AI-Native Agency Landscape

Approximately 30-120 AI-native agencies in Bay Area. Most still sell traditional retainers; few productize transparency. None currently combine all of:
- Full-stack delivery (outbound + content + SEO + CRM)
- Provider-operated (done for you, not with you)
- Transparent by default
- B2B service-business focus

## Beachhead Math

### IT/MSP Market Entry

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Bay Area IT/MSPs | 4,500+ |
| National IT services | ~180,000 |
| Average deal | $6K/mo ($72K/year) |
| Clients for $1M ARR | ~14 |
| Clients for $10M ARR | ~139 |
| Penetration needed for $10M | <0.1% of national market |

### Why This Math Works

1. Small market share needed -- even $10M ARR is <0.1% penetration
2. High referral velocity -- MSP peer groups (HTG, ConnectWise, ASCII) create network effects
3. Already budgeted -- $3-10K/mo reallocation, not new category creation
4. Short sales cycles -- technical founders, AI-positive, 1-3 month close
